# Unripe

**UNRI'PE**, _adjective_

**1.** Not ripe; not mature; not brought to a state of perfection; as _unripe_ fruit.

**2.** Not seasonable; not yet proper.

He fix'd his _unripe_ vengeance to defer.

**3.** Not prepared; not completed; as an _unripe_ scheme.

**4.** Too early; as the _unripe_ death of Dorilaus. \[Unusual.\]
