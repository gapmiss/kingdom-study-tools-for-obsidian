# Unbalanced

**UNBAL'ANCED**, _adjective_

**1.** Not balanced; not poised; not in equipoise.

Let earth unbalanc'd from her orbit fly.

**2.** Not adjusted; not settled; not brought to an equality of debt and credit; as an _unbalanced_ account.

**3.** Not restrained by equal power; as _unbalanced_ parties.
