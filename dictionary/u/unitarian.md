# Unitarian

**UNITA'RIAN**, _noun_ \[Latin unitus, unus.\] One who denies the doctrine of the trinity, and ascribes divinity to God the Father only. The Arian and Socinian are both comprehended in the term _unitarian_

**UNITA'RIAN**, _adjective_ Pertaining to Unitarians, or to the doctrine of the unity of the Godhead.
