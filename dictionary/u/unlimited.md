# Unlimited

**UNLIM'ITED**, _adjective_

**1.** Not limited; having no bounds; boundless.

**2.** Undefined; indefinite; not bounded by proper exceptions; as _unlimited_ terms.

**3.** Unconfined; not restrained.

Ascribe not to God such as _unlimited_ exercise of mercy as may destroy his justice.

Unlimited problem, is one which is capable of infinite solutions.
