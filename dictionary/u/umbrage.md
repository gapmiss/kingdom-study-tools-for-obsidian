# Umbrage

**UM'BRAGE**, _noun_ \[Latin umbra, a shade.\]

**1.** A shade; a screen of trees; as the _umbrage_ of woods.

**2.** Shadow; shade; slight appearance.

The opinion carries no show of truth nor _umbrage_ of reason on its side. \[See [[Shadow]].\]

**3.** Suspicion of injury; offense; resentment. The court of France took _umbrage_ at the conduct of spain.
