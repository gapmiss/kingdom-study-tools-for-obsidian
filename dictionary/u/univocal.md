# Univocal

**UNIV'OCAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin unus, one, and vox, word.\]

**1.** Having one meaning only. A _univocal_ word is opposed to an equivocal, which has two or more significations.

**2.** Having unison of sounds; as the octave in music and its replicates.

**3.** Certain; regular; pursuing always one tenor. \[Little used.\]
