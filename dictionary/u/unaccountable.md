# Unaccountable

**UNACCOUNT'ABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** Not to be accounted for. Such folly is _unaccountable_

**2.** Not explicable; not to be solved by reason or the light possessed; not reducible to rule. The union of soul and body is to us _unaccountable_

**3.** Not subject to account or control; not subject to answer; not responsible.
