# Utterance

**UT'TERANCE**, _noun_

**1.** The act of uttering words; pronunciation; manner of speaking; as a good or bad _utterance_

They began to speak with other tongues, as the spirit gave them _utterance_ [[Acts 2#4|Acts 2:4]].

**2.** Emission from the mouth; vocal expression; as the _utterance_ of sounds.

**3.** Extremity; furthest part. \[Not in use.\]
