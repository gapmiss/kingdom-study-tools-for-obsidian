# Unfruitful

**UNFRUITFUL**, _adjective_

**1.** Not producing fruit; barren; as an _unfruitful_ tree.

**2.** Not producing off-spring; not prolific; barren; as an _unfruitful_ female.

**3.** Not producing good effects or works; as an _unfruitful_ life.

**4.** Unproductive; not fertile; as an _unfruitful_ soil.
