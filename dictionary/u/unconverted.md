# Unconverted

**UNCONVERT'ED**, _adjective_

**1.** Not converted; not changed in opinion; not turned from one faith to another.

**2.** Not persuaded of the truth of the christian religion; as _unconverted_ pagans.

**3.** Not renewed; not regenerated; not having the natural enmity of the heart subdued, and a principle of grace implanted.

**4.** Not turned or changed from one form to another.
