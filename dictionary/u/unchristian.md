# Unchristian

**UNCHRIS'TIAN**, _adjective_

**1.** Contrary to the laws of christianity; as an _unchristian_ reflection; _unchristian_ temper or conduct.

**2.** Not evangelized; not converted to the christian faith; infidel.

**UNCHRIS'TIAN**, _verb transitive_ To deprive of the constituent qualities of christianity.
