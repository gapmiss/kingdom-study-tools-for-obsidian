# Unquiet

**UNQUIET**, _adjective_

**1.** Not quiet; not calm or tranquil; restless; uneasy; as an _unquiet_ person; an _unquiet_ mind.

**2.** Agitated; disturbed by continual motion; as the _unquiet_ ocean.

**3.** Unsatisfied; restless.

**UNQUI'ET**, _verb transitive_ To disquiet. \[Not in use.\]
