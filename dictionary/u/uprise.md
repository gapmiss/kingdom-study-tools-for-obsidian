# Uprise

**UPRI'SE**, _verb intransitive_ s as z. pret uprose; _participle passive_ uprisen.

**1.** To rise from bed or from a seat.

Uprose the virgin with the morning light.

**2.** To ascend above the horizon.

Uprose the sun.

**3.** To ascend, as a hill. _obsolete_

**UPRI'SE**, _noun_ A rising; appearance above the horizon. _obsolete_
