# Unclean

**UNCLE'AN**, _adjective_

**1.** Not clean; foul; dirty; filthy.

**2.** In the Jewish law, ceremonially impure; not cleansed by ritual practices. [[Numbers 19#7|Numbers 19:7]]. [[Leviticus 11#4|Leviticus 11:4]]. [[Romans 14#14|Romans 14:14]].

**3.** Foul with sin. [[Matthew 10#1|Matthew 10:1]].

That holy place where no _unclean_ thing shall enter.

**4.** Not in covenant with God. [[1 Corinthians 7#14|1 Corinthians 7:14]].

**5.** Lewd; unchaste.

Adultery of the heart, consisting of inordinate and _unclean_ affections.

No _unclean_ person - hath any inheritance in the kingdom of Christ and of God. [[Ephesians 5#5|Ephesians 5:5]].
