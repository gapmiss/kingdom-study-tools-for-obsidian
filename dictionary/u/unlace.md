# Unlace

**UNLA'CE**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To loose from lacing or fastening by a cord or strings passed through loops and holes; as, to _unlace_ a helmet or a garment.

**2.** To loose a woman's dress.

**3.** To divest of ornaments.

**4.** In sea language, to loose and take off a bonnet from a sail.
