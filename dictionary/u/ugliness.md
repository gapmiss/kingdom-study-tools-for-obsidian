# Ugliness

**UG'LINESS**, _noun_ \[from ugly.\]

**1.** Total want of beauty; deformity of person; as old age and _ugliness_

**2.** Turpitude of mind; moral depravity; lothesomeness.

Their dull ribaldry must be offensive to any one who does not, for the sake of the sin, pardon the _ugliness_ of its circumstances.
