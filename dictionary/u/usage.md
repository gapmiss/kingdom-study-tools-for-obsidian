# Usage

**U'SAGE**, n s as z. \[See [[Use]].\]

**1.** Treatment; an action or series of actions performed by one person towards another, or which directly affect him; as good usage; ill usage; hard _usage_ Gentle _usage_ will often effect what harsh _usage_ will not. The elephant may be governed by mild _usage_

**2.** Use, or long continued use; custom; practice. Uninterrupted _usage_ for a long time, or immemorial _usage_ constitutes prescription. Custom is a local usage; prescription is a personal _usage_ In language, _usage_ is the foundation of all rules.

Of things once received and confirmed by use, long _usage_ is a law sufficient.

**3.** Manners; behavior. _obsolete_
