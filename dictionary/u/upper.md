# Upper

**UP'PER**, _adjective_ \[_comparative_ from up.\]

**1.** Higher in place; as the _upper_ lip; the _upper_ side of a thing. An _upper_ story is a higher one; the _upper_ story is the highest. So the _upper_ deck of a ship.

**2.** Superior in rank or dignity; as the _upper_ house of a legislature.

Upper hand, advantage; superiority.

Upper-works, in a ship, the parts above water when the ship is properly balanced for a voyage; or that part which is above the main wale.
