# Ustulation

**USTULA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin ustulatus.\]

**1.** The act of burning or searing.

**2.** In metallurgy, _ustulation_ is the operation of expelling one substance from another by heat, as sulphur and arsenic from ores, in a muffle.

**3.** In pharmacy, the roasting or drying of moist substances so as to prepare them for pulverizing; also, the burning of wine.
