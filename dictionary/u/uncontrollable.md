# Uncontrollable

**UNCONTROLLABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** That cannot be controlled; ungovernable; that cannot be restrained; as an _uncontrollable_ temper; _uncontrollable_ subjects.

**2.** That cannot be resisted or diverted; as _uncontrollable_ events.

**3.** Indisputable; irrefragable; as an _uncontrollable_ maxim; the king's _uncontrollable_ title to the English throne.
