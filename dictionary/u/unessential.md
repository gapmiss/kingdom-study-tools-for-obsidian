# Unessential

**UNESSEN'TIAL**, _adjective_

**1.** Not essential; not absolutely necessary; not of prime importance.

**2.** Not constituting the essence.

**3.** Void of real being; as _unessential_ night.

**UNESSEN'TIAL**, _noun_ Something not constituting essence, or not of absolute necessity. Forms are among the unessentials of religion.
