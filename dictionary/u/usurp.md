# Usurp

**USURP**', _verb transitive_ s as z. \[Latin usurpo.\]

To seize and hold in possession by force or without right; as, to _usurp_ a throne; to _usurp_ the prerogatives of the crown; to _usurp_ power. To _usurp_ the right of a patron, is to oust or dispossess him.

Vice sometimes usurps the place of virtue.

\[Usurp is not applied to common dispossession of private property.\]
