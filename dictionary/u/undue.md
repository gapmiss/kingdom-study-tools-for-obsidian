# Undue

**UNDUE**, _adjective_

**1.** Not due; not yet demandable of right; as a debt, note or bond _undue_

**2.** Not right; not legal; improper; as an _undue_ proceeding.

**3.** Not agreeable to a rule or standard, or to duty; not proportioned; excessive; as an _undue_ regard to the externals of religion; an _undue_ attachment to forms; an _undue_ rigor in the execution of law.
