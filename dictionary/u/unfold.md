# Unfold

**UNFOLD**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To open folds; to expand; to spread out.

**2.** To open any thing covered or close; to lay open to view or contemplation; to disclose; to reveal; as, to _unfold_ one's designs; to _unfold_ the principles of a science.

**3.** To declare; to tell; to disclose.

**UNFOLD** the passion of my love.

**4.** To display; as, to _unfold_ the works of creation.

**5.** To release from a fold or pen; as, to _unfold_ sheep.
