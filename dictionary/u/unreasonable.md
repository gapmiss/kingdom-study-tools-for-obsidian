# Unreasonable

**UNRE'ASONABLE**, _adjective_ s as z.

**1.** Not agreeable to reason.

**2.** Exceeding the bounds of reason; claiming or insisting on more than is fit; as an _unreasonable_ demand.

**3.** Immoderate; exorbitant; as an _unreasonable_ love of life or of money.

**4.** Irrational. \[In this sense, see Irrational.\]
