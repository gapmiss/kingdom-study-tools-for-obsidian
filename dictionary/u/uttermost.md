# Uttermost

**UT'TERMOST**, _adjective_ \[utter and most.\] Extreme; being in the furthest, greatest or highest degree; as the _uttermost_ extent or end; the _uttermost_ distress.

**UT'TERMOST**, _noun_ The greatest. the _uttermost_ we can do is to be patient.

To the _uttermost_ in the most extensive degree; fully.

[[Hebrews 7#25|Hebrews 7:25]].
