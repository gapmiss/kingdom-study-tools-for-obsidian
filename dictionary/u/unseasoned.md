# Unseasoned

**UNSEASONED**, _adjective_ unsee'znd.

**1.** Not seasoned; not exhausted of the natural juices and hardened for use; as _unseasoned_ wood, boards, timber, etc.

**2.** Not inured; not accustomed; not fitted to endure any thing by use or habit; as, men _unseasoned_ to tropical climates are exposed to fevers.

**3.** Unformed; not qualified by use or experience; as an _unseasoned_ courtier.

**4.** Not salted; not sprinkled, filled or impregnated with any thing to give relish; as _unseasoned_ meat.

**5.** Unseasonable. \[Not in use.\]
