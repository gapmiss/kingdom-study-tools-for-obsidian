# Unable

**UNA'BLE**, _adjective_

**1.** Not able; not having sufficient strength or means; impotent; weak in power, or poor in substance. A man is _unable_ to rise when sick; he is _unable_ to labor; he is _unable_ to support his family or to purchase a farm; he is _unable_ for a particular enterprise.

**2.** Not having adequate knowledge or skill. A man is _unable_ to paint a good likeness; he is _unable_ to command a ship or an army.
