# Unimproved

**UNIMPROVED**, _adjective_

**1.** Not improved; not made better or wiser; not advanced in knowledge, manners or excellence.

**2.** Not used for a valuable purpose. How many advantages _unimproved_ have we to regret!

**3.** Not used; not employed.

**4.** Not tilled; not cultivated; as _unimproved_ land or soil; _unimproved_ lots of ground.

**5.** Uncensured; not disapproved. \[This sense, from the Latin improbo, is entirely obsolete.\]
