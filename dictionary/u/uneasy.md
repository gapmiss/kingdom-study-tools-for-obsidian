# Uneasy

**UNE'ASY**, _adjective_ s as z.

**1.** Feeling some degree of pain; restless; disturbed; unquiet. The patient is _uneasy_

**2.** Giving some pain; as an _uneasy_ garment.

**3.** Disturbed in mind; somewhat anxious; unquiet. He is _uneasy_ respecting the success of his project.

The soul, _uneasy_ and confin'd from home, rests and expatiates in a life to come.

**4.** Constraining; cramping; as _uneasy_ rules.

**5.** Constrained; stiff; not graceful; not easy; as an _uneasy_ deportment.

**6.** Giving some pain to others; disagreeable; unpleasing.

A sour, untractable nature makes him _uneasy_ to those who approach him.

**7.** Difficult.

Things - so _uneasy_ to be satisfactorily understood. \[Not in use.\]
