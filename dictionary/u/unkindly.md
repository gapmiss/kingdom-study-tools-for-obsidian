# Unkindly

**UNKINDLY**, _adjective_

**1.** Unnatural; contrary to nature; as an _unkindly_ crime.

**2.** Unfavorably; malignant; as an _unkindly_ fog.

**UNKINDLY**, _adverb_

**1.** Without kindness; without affection; as, to treat one _unkindly_

**2.** In a manner contrary to nature; unnaturally.

All works of nature, abortive, monstrous, or _unkindly_ mix'd.
