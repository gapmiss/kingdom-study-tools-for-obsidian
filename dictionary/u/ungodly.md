# Ungodly

**UNGOD'LY**, _adjective_

**1.** Wicked; impious; neglecting the fear and worship of God, or violating his commands. [[1 Peter 4#18|1 Peter 4:18]].

**2.** Sinful; contrary to the divine commands; as _ungodly_ deeds. [[Jude 1#4|Jude 1:4]].

**3.** Polluted by wickedness; as an _ungodly_ day.
