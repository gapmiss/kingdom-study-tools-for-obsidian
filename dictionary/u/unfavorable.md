# Unfavorable

**UNFA'VORABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** Not favorable; not propitious; not disposed or adapted to countenance or support. We found the minister's opinion _unfavorable_ to our project. The committee made a report _unfavorable_ to the petitioner.

**2.** Not propitious; not adapted to promote any object; as weather _unfavorable_ for harvest.

**3.** Not kind; not obliging.

**4.** Discouraging; as _unfavorable_ prospects.
