# Usurpation

**USURPA'TION**, _noun_ \[supra.\] The act of seizing or occupying and enjoying the property of another, without right; as the _usurpation_ of a throne; the _usurpation_ of the supreme power. _usurpation_ in a peculiar sense, denotes the absolute ouster and dispossession of the patron of a church, by presenting a clerk to a vacant benefice, who is thereupon admitted and instituted.
