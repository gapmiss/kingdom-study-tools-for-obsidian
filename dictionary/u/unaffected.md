# Unaffected

**UNAFFECT'ED**, _adjective_

**1.** Not affected; plain; natural; not labored or artificial; simple; as _unaffected_ ease and grace.

**2.** Real; not hypocritical; sincere; as _unaffected_ sorrow.

**3.** Not moved; not having the heart or passions touched. Men often remain _unaffected_ under all the solemn monitions of Providence.
