# Ultramarine

**ULTRAMARINE**, _adjective_ \[Latin ultra, beyond, and marinus, marine.\]

Situated or being beyond the sea.

**ULTRAMARINE**, _noun_ \[supra.\]

**1.** a beautiful and durable sky-blue; a color formed of the mineral called lapis lazuli, and consisting of little else than oxyd of iron.

**2.** Azure-stone.

**ULTRAMARINE** ashes, a pigment which is the residuum of lapis lazuli, after the _ultramarine_ has been extracted. Their appearance is that of the _ultramarine_ a little tinged with red, and diluted with white.
