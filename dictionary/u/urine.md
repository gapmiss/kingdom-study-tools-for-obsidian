# Urine

**U'RINE**, _noun_ \[Latin urina; Gr.\]

An animal fluid or liquor secreted by the kidneys, whence it is conveyed into the bladder by the ureters, and through the urethra discharged. The _urine_ of beasts is sometimes called stale.

**U'RINE**, _verb intransitive_ \[supra.\] To discharge _urine_
