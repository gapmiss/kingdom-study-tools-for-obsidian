# Ungenerous

**UNGEN'EROUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Not of a noble mind; not liberal; applied to persons; as an _ungenerous_ man or prince.

**2.** Not noble; not liberal; applied to things; as an _ungenerous_ act.

**3.** Dishonorable; ignominious.

The victor never will impose on Cato ungen'rous terms.
