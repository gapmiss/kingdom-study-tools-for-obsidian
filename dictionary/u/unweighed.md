# Unweighed

**UNWEIGHED**, _adjective_

**1.** Not weighed; not having the weight ascertained.

Solomon left all the vessels _unweighed_ [[1 Kings 7#47|1 Kings 7:47]].

**2.** Not deliberately considered and examined; as, to leave arguments or testimony _unweighed_

**3.** Not considerate; negligent; as words _unweighed_
