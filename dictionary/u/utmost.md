# Utmost

**UT'MOST**, _adjective_

**1.** Extreme; being at the furthest point or extremity; as the _utmost_ limit of North America; the _utmost_ limits of the land; the _utmost_ extent of human knowledge.

**2.** Being in the greatest or highest degree; as the _utmost_ assiduity; the _utmost_ harmony; the _utmost_ misery or happiness; the _utmost_ peril.

**UT'MOST**, _noun_ The most that can be; the greatest power, degree or effort. He has done his _utmost_ Try your _utmost_

I will be free even to the _utmost_ as I please in words.
