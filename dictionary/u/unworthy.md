# Unworthy

**UNWORTHY**, _adjective_

**1.** Not deserving; followed by of. As sinners, were are utterly _unworthy_ of the divine favor.

**2.** Not deserving; wanting merit. Receive your _unworthy_ son into favor. One great evil of government is that _unworthy_ men are elected or appointed to fill important offices.

**3.** Unbecoming; vile; base; as _unworthy_ usage or treatment.

**4.** Not suitable; inadequate. This opinion is _unworthy_ of its author.
