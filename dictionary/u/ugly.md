# Ugly

**UG'LY**, _adjective_ \[See [[Hack]].\[

Deformed; offensive to the sight; contrary to beauty; hateful; as an _ugly_ person; an _ugly_ face.

O I have pass'd a miserable night, so full of _ugly_ sights, of ghastly dreams.

Fellow, begone; I cannot bear thy sight; this news hath made thee a most _ugly_ man.
