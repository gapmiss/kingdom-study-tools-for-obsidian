# Unpassable

**UNP'ASSABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** Not admitting persons to pass; impassable; as _unpassable_ roads, rivers or mountains. \[Impassable is more generally used.\]

**2.** Not current; not received in common payments; as _unpassable_ notes or coins. \[Instead of this, uncurrent and not current are now used.\]
