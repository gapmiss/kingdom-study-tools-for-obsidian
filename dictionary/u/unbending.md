# Unbending

**UNBEND'ING**, _participle present tense_

**1.** Relaxing from any strain; remitting; taking from their yards, etc., as sails.

**2.** _adjective_ Not suffering flexure.

**3.** Unyielding; resolute; inflexible; applied to persons.

**4.** Unyielding; inflexible; firm; applied to things; as _unbending_ truth.

**5.** Devoted to relaxation.

I hope it may entertain your lordship at an _unbending_ hour.
