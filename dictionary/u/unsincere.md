# Unsincere

**UNSINCE'RE**, _adjective_

**1.** Not sincere; hypocritical. \[See [[Insincere]].\]

**2.** Not genuine; adulterated.

**3.** Not sound; not solid.

\[Obsolete in the two last significations, and for the first, insincere is generally used.\]

**INSINCERITY**, _noun_ Insincerity; cheat. \[Not used.\] \[See Insincerity.\]
