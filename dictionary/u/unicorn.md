# Unicorn

**U'NICORN**, _noun_ \[Latin unicornis; unus, one, and cornu, horn.\]

**1.** an animal with one horn; the monoceros. this name is often applied to the rhinoceros.

**2.** The sea _unicorn_ is a fish of the whale kind, called narwal, remarkable for a horn growing out at his nose.

**3.** A fowl.

fossil _unicorn_ or fossil unicorn's horn, a substance used in medicine, a terrene crustaceous spar.
