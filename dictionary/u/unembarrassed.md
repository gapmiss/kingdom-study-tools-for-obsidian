# Unembarrassed

**UNEMBAR'RASSED**, _adjective_

**1.** Not embarrassed; not perplexed in mind; not confused. The speaker appeared _unembarrassed_

**2.** Free from pecuniary difficulties or incumbrances. He or his property is _unembarrassed_

**3.** Free from perplexing connection; as, the question comes before the court _unembarrassed_ with irrelevant matter.
