# Undulate

**UN'DULATE**,

**UN'DULATE**D, _adjective_ Wavy; waved obtusely up and down, near the margin, as a leaf or corol.

**UN'DULATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin undula, a little wave; unda, a wave; Low Latin undulo.\]

To move back and forth, or up and down, as waves; to cause to vibrate.

Breath vocalized, that is, vibrated and undulated -

**UN'DULATE**, _verb intransitive_ To vibrate; to move back and forth; to wave; as undulating air.
