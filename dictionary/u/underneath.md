# Underneath

**UNDERNE'ATH**, _adverb_ \[under and neath. See [[Nether]].\]

Beneath; below; in a lower place.

Or sullen Mole that runneth _underneath_

The slate did not lie flat upon it, but left a free passage _underneath_

**UNDERNE'ATH**, _preposition_ Under; beneath.

Underneath this stone doth lie. As much beauty as could die.
