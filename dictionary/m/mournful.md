# Mournful

**MOURNFUL**, _adjective_ Intended to express sorrow, or exhibiting the appearance of grief, as a _mournful_ bell; _mournful_ music.

No funeral rites nor man in _mournful_ weeds.

**1.** Causing sorrow; sad; calamitous; as a _mournful_ death.

**2.** Sorrowful; feeling grief.

The _mournful_ fair--

Shall visit her distinguished urn.
