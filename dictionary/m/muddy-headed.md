# Muddy-headed

**MUDDY-HEADED**, _adjective_ Having a dull understanding.

**MUD'-FISH**, _noun_ A fish, a species of the cyprinus kind.

**MUD'-SILL**, _noun_ In bridges, the sill that is laid at the bottom of a river, lake, etc. \[See [[Sill]].\]

**MUD'-SUCKER**, _noun_ An aquatic fowl.

**MUD'-WALL**, _noun_ A wall composed of mud, or of materials laid in mud without mortar.

**1.** A bird, the apiaster.

**MUD'-WALLED**, _adjective_ Having a mud wall.
