# Microscopic

**MICROSCOP'IC**

**MICROSCOP'IC**AL, _adjective_ Made by the aid of a microscope; as _microscopic_ observation.

**1.** Assisted by a microscope.

Evading even the _microscopic_ eye.

**2.** Resembling a microscope; capable of seeing small objects.

Why has not man a _microscopic_ eye?

**3.** Very small; visible only by the aid of a microscope; as a _microscopic_ insect.
