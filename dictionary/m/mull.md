# Mull

**MULL**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin mollio, to soften.\]

**1.** To soften; or to heat, sweeten and enrich with spices; as, to _mull_ wine.

Drink new cider, mull'd with ginger warm.

**2.** To dispirit or deaden.

**MULL**, _noun_ In Scottish, a snuff-box, made of the small end of a horn.

**MULL**, _noun_ Dust. \[Not in use.\]
