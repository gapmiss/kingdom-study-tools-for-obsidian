# Me

**ME**, _pronoun_ pers.; the objective case of I, answering to the oblique cases of ego, in Latin. \[Latin mihi.\] Follow me; give to me; go with _me_ The phrase 'I followed _me_ close, ' is not in use. Before think, as in methinks, _me_ is properly in the dative case, and the verb is impersonal; the construction is, it appears to _me_
