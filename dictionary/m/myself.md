# Myself

**MYSELF**', _pronoun_ A compound of my and self, used after I, to express emphasis, marking emphatically the distinction between the speaker and another person; as, I _myself_ will do it; I have done it _myself_

**1.** In the objective case, the reciprocal of I. I will defend _myself_

**2.** It is sometimes used without I, particularly in poetry.

**MYSELF** shall mount the rostrum in his favor.
