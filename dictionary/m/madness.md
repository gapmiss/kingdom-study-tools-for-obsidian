# Madness

**MAD'NESS**, _noun_ \[from mad.\] Distraction; a state of disordered reason or intellect, in which the patient raves or is furious.

There are degrees of _madness_ as of folly.

**1.** Extreme folly; headstrong passion and rashness that act in opposition to reason; as the _madness_ of a mob.

**2.** Wildness of passion; fury; rage; as the _madness_ of despair.
