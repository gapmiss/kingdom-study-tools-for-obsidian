# Metallurgy

**MET'ALLURGY**, _noun_ \[Gr. metal, and work.\] The art of working metals, comprehending the whole process of separating them from other matters in the ore, smelting, refining and parting them. Gilding is also a branch of _metallurgy_ But in a more limited and usual sense, _metallurgy_ is the operation of separating metals from their ores.

The French include in _metallurgy_ the art of drawing metals from the earth.
