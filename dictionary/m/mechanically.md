# Mechanically

**MECHAN'ICALLY**, _adverb_ According to the laws of mechanism, or good workmanship.

**1.** By physical force or power.

**2.** By the laws of motion, without intelligence or design, or by the force of habit. We say, a man arrives to such perfection in playing on an instrument, that his fingers move _mechanically_

Mechanically turned or inclined, naturally or habitually disposed to use mechanical arts.
