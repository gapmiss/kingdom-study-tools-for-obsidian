# Mediation

**MEDIA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin medius, middle.\]

**1.** Interposition; intervention; agency between parties at variance, with a view to reconcile them. The contentions of individuals and families are often terminated by the _mediation_ of friends. The controversies of nations are sometimes adjusted by _mediation_ The reconciliation of sinners to God by the _mediation_ of Christ, is a glorious display of divine benevolence.

**2.** Agency interposed; intervenient power.

The soul, during its residence in the body, does all things by the _mediation_ of the passions.

**3.** Intercession; entreaty for another.
