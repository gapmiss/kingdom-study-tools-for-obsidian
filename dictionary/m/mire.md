# Mire

**MIRE**, _noun_ Deep mud; earth so wet and soft as to yield to the feet and to wheels.

**MIRE**, _verb transitive_ To plunge and fix in mire; to set or stall in mud. We say, a horse, an ox or a carriage is mired, when it has sunk deep into mud and its progress is stopped.

**1.** To soil or daub with mud or foul matter.

**MIRE**, _verb intransitive_ To sink in mud, or to sink so deep as to be unable to move forward.

**MIRE**, _noun_ An ant. \[See [[Pismire]].\]
