# Mercurial

**MERCU'RIAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin mercurialis.\]

**1.** Formed under the influence of Mercury; active; sprightly; full of fire or vigor; as a _mercurial_ youth; a _mercurial_ nation.

**2.** Pertaining to quicksilver; containing quicksilver, or consisting of mercury; as _mercurial_ preparations or medicines.
