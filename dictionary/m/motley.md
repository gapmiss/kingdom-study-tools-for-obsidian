# Motley

**MOT'LEY**, _adjective_ \[Eng.mote.\]

**1.** Variegated in color; consisting of different colors; dappled; as a _motley_ coat.

**2.** Composed of different or various parts, characters or kinds; diversified; as a _motley_ style.

And doubts of _motley_ hue.

\[This word primarily means spotted; but it may signify also striped.\]
