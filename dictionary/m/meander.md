# Meander

**MEAN'DER**, _noun_ \[the name of a winding river in Phrygia.\]

**1.** A winding course; a winding or turning in a passage; as the meanders of the veins and arteries.

While lingering rivers in meanders glide.

**2.** A maze; a labyrinth; perplexity; as the meanders of the law.

**MEAN'DER**, _verb transitive_ To wind, turn or flow round; to make flexuous.

**MEAN'DER**, _verb intransitive_ To wind or turn in a course or passage; to be intricate.
