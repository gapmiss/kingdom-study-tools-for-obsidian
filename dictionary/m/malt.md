# Malt

**MALT**, _noun_ Barley steeped in water, fermented and dried in a kiln, and thus prepared for brewing into ale or beer.

**MALT**, _verb transitive_ To make into malt; as, to _malt_ barley.

**MALT**, _verb intransitive_ To become _malt_

To house it green will make it _malt_ worse.
