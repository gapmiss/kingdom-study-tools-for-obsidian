# Metamorphic

**METAMORPH'IC**

METAMORPH'OSE, _verb transitive_ \[Gr. over, beyond, and form.\] To change into a different form; to transform; particularly, to change the form of insects, as from the larva to a winged animal. The ancients pretended that Jupiter was metamorphosed into a bull, and Lycaon into a wolf.

And earth was metamorphosed into man.
