# Mystic

**MYS'TIC**

**MYS'TIC**AL, _adjective_ \[Latin mysticus.\] Obscure; hid; secret.

**1.** Sacredly obscure or secret; remote from human comprehension.

God hath revealed a way mystical and supernatural.

**2.** Involving some secret meaning; allegorical; emblematical; as _mystic_ dance; _mystic_ Babylon.
