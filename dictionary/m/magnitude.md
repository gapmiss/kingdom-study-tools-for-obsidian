# Magnitude

**MAG'NITUDE**, _noun_ \[Latin magnitudo.\] Extent of dimensions or parts; bulk; size; applied to things that have length, breadth or thickness.

**1.** Greatness; grandeur.

With plain heroic _magnitude_ of mind.

**2.** Greatness, in reference to influence or effect; importance. In affairs or _magnitude_ disdain not to take counsel.
