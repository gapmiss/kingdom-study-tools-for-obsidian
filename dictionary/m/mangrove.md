# Mangrove

**MAN'GROVE**, _noun_ A tree of the East and West Indies, otherwise called mangle, and of the genus Rhizophora. One species, the black mangle, grows in waters on the sides of rivers. The red _mangrove_ does not grow in water. Its wood is of a deep red color, compact and heavy. The soft part of the bark of the white _mangrove_ is formed into ropes.

**1.** The name of a fish.
