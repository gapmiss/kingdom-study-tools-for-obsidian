# Malignancy

**MALIG'NANCY**, _noun_ \[See [[Malignant]].\] Extreme malevolence; bitter enmity; malice; as _malignancy_ of heart.

**1.** Unfavorableness; unpropitiousness; as the _malignancy_ of the aspect of planets.

The _malignancy_ of my fate might distemper yours.

**2.** Virulence; tendency to mortification or to a fatal issue; as the _malignancy_ of an ulcer or of a fever.
