# Mutilation

**MUTILA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin mutilatio.\] The act of mutilating; deprivation of a limb or of an essential part.

**1.** _mutilation_ is a term of very general import, applied to bodies, to statues, to buildings and to writings; but appropriately, it denotes the retrenchment of a human limb or member, and particularly of the male organs of generation.
