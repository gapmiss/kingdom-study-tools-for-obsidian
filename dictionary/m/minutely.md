# Minutely

**MINU'TELY**, _adverb_ \[from minute.\] To a small point of time, space or matter; exactly; nicely; as, to measure the length of any thing minutely; to ascertain time minutely; to relate a story _minutely_

**MINUTELY**, _adjective_ min'itly. Happening every minute.

**MIN'UTELY**, _adverb_ \[from minute.\] Every minute; with very little time intervening.

As if it were _minutely_ proclaimed in thunder from heaven.
