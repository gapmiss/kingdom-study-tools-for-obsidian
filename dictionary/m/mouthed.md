# Mouthed

**MOUTH'ED**, pp. Uttered with a full, swelling, affected voice.

**1.** Taken into the mouth; chewed.

**2.** _adjective_ Furnished with a mouth; used chiefly in composition; as well-mouthed; foul-mouthed, contumelious, reproachful or obscene; mealy-mouthed, bashful, reserved in speaking the plain truth; hard-mouthed, as a horse, not obedient to the bit, difficult to be restrained or governed by the bridle.

**3.** Borne down or overpowered by clamor.
