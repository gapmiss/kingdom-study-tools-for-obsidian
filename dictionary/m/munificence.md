# Munificence

**MUNIF'ICENCE**, _noun_ \[Latin munificentia; munus, a gift or favor, and facio, to make.\]

**1.** A giving or bestowing liberally; bounty; liberality. To constitute _munificence_ the act of conferring must be free, and proceed from generous motives.

A state of poverty obscures all the virtues of liberality and _munificence_

**2.** In Spenser, fortification or strength. \[Latin munio, to fortify.\] \[Not used.\]
