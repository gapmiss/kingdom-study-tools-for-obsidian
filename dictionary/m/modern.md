# Modern

**MOD'ERN**, _adjective_ \[Latin modo, and ern, which we find in other Latin words that have reference to time, as in hodiernus, hesternus.\]

**1.** Pertaining to the present time, or time not long past; late; recent; not ancient or remote in past time; as _modern_ days, ages or time; _modern_ authors; _modern_ fashions; _modern_ taste; _modern_ practice.

**2.** Common; mean; vulgar. \[Not used.\]
