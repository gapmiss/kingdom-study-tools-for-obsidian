# Muller

**MUL'LER**, _noun_ \[Latin molaris, from mola, a mill-stone.\]

**1.** A stone held in the hand with which colors and other matters are ground on another stone; used by painters and apothecaries.

**2.** An instrument used by glass grinders, being a piece of wood with the piece of glass to be ground cemented to one end, either convex in a basin, or concave in a sphere or bowl.
