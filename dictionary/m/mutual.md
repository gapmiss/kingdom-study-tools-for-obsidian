# Mutual

**MU'TUAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin mutuus, from muto, to change.\]

Reciprocal; interchanged , each acting in return or correspondence to the other; given and received. _mutual_ love is that which is entertained by two persons each for the other; _mutual_ advantage is that which is conferred by one person or another, and received by him in return. So we say, _mutual_ assistance, _mutual_ aversion.

_And, what should most excite a _mutual_ flame,_

Your rural cares and pleasures are the same.
