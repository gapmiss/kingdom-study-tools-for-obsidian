# Miscarry

**MISCAR'RY**, _verb intransitive_ To fail of the intended effect; not to succeed; to be unsuccessful; to suffer defeat; applied to persons or undertakings, and to things. We say, a project, scheme, design, enterprise, attempt, has miscarried.

Have you not heart of Frederick, the great soldier, who miscarried at sea?

My ships have all miscarried.

**1.** To bring forth young before the proper time; to suffer abortion.
