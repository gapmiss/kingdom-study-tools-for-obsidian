# Modulate

**MOD'ULATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin modulor, from modus, limit, measure.\]

**1.** To form sound to a certain key, or to a certain proportion.

**2.** To very or inflect sound in a natural, customary or musical manner. Thus the organs of speech _modulate_ the voice in reading or speaking.

Could any person so _modulate_ her voice as to deceive so many.
