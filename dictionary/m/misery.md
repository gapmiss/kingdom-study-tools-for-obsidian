# Misery

**MIS'ERY**, _noun_ s as z. \[Latin miseria.\]

**1.** Great unhappiness; extreme pain of body or mind. A man suffers _misery_ from the gout, or from great afflictions, distress, calamity, and other evils. _misery_ expresses somewhat less than wretchedness.

Misery is as really the fruit of vice reigning in the heart, as tares are the produce of tares sown in the field.

**2.** Calamity; misfortune; natural evils which are the cause of _misery_

And mourn the miseries of human life.

**3.** Covetousness. \[Not used.\]
