# Marvelous

**M'ARVELOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Wonderful; strange; exciting wonder or some degree of surprise.

This is the Lord's doing; it is _marvelous_ in our eyes. [[Psalms 118#1|Psalms 118:1]].

**2.** Surpassing credit; incredible.

**3.** The _marvelous_ in writings, is that which exceeds natural power, or is preternatural; opposed to probable.

**4.** Formerly used adverbially for wonderfully, exceedingly.
