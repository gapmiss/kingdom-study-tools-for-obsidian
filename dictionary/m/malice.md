# Malice

**MAL'ICE**, _noun_ \[Latin malitia, from malus, evil.\] Extreme enmity of heart, or malevolence; a disposition to injure others without cause, from mere personal gratification or from a spirit of revenge; unprovoked malignity or spite.

\--Nor set down aught in _malice_

**MAL'ICE**, _verb transitive_ To regard with extreme ill will. \[Not used.\]
