# Muck

**MUCK**, _noun_ \[Latin mucus.\]

**1.** Dung in a moist state, or a mass of dung and putrefied vegetable matter.

With fattening _muck_ besmear the roots.

**2.** Something mean, vile or filthy.

To run a _muck_ to run madly and attack all we meet.

Running a _muck_ is a phrase derived from the Malays, (in whose language amock signified to kill, ) applied to desperate persons who

intoxicate themselves with opium and then arm themselves with a dagger and attempt to kill all they meet.

**MUCK**, _verb transitive_ To manure with _muck_
