# Meteorolite

**MET'EOROLITE**

METEOROLOG'IC

METEOROLOG'ICAL, _adjective_ Pertaining to the atmosphere and its phenomena. A meteorological table or register is an account of the state of the air and its temperature, weight, dryness or moisture, winds, etc. ascertained by the barometer, thermometer, hygrometer, anemometer and other meteorological instruments.
