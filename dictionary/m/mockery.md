# Mockery

**MOCK'ERY**, _noun_ The act of deriding and exposing to contempt, by mimicking the words or actions of another.

**1.** Derision; ridicule; sportive insult or contempt; contemptuous merriment at persons or things.

Grace at meals is now generally so performed as to look more like _mockery_ upon devotion, than any solemn application of the mind to God.

**2.** Sport; subject of laughter.

Of the holy place they made a _mockery_

**3.** Vain imitation or effort; that which deceives, disappoints or frustrates.

It is as the air, invulnerable,

And our vain blows malicious _mockery_

**4.** Imitation; counterfeit appearance; false show.

And bear about the _mockery_ of woe

To midnight dances.
