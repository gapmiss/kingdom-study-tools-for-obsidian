# Machiavelian

**MACHIAVE'LIAN**, _adjective_ \[from Machiavel, an Italian writer, secretary and historiographer to the republic of Florence.\]

Pertaining to Machiavel, or denoting his principles; politically cunning; crafty; cunning in political management.

**MACHIAVE'LIAN**, _noun_ One who adopts the principles of Machiavel.
