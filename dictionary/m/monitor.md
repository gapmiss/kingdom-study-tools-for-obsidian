# Monitor

**MON'ITOR**, _noun_ \[Latin\] One who warns of faults or informs of duty; one who gives advice and instruction by way of reproof or caution.

You need not be a _monitor_ to the king.

**1.** In schools, a person authorized to look to the scholars in the absence of the instructor, or to notice the absence of faults of the scholars; or to instruct a division of class.
