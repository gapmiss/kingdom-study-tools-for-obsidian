# Mansion

**MAN'SION**, _noun_ \[Latin mansio, from maneo, to dwell.\]

**1.** Any place of residence; a house; a habitation.

Thy _mansion_ wants thee, Adam, rise.

In my Father's house are many mansions. [[John 14#2|John 14:2]].

**2.** The house of the lord of a manor.

**3.** Residence; above.

_These poets near our princes sleep,_

And in one grave their mansions keep.
