# Myrtle

**MYR'TLE**, _noun_ \[Latin myrtus.\] A plant of the genus Myrtus, of several species. The common _myrtle_ rises with a shrubby upright stem, eight or ten feet high. Its branches form a close full head, closely garnished with oval lanceolate leaves. It has numerous small, pale flowers from the axillas, singly on each footstalk.
