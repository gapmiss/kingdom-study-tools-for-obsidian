# Mist

**MIST**, _noun_ \[Latin mixtus, mistus, from misceo, to mix.\]

**1.** Water falling in very numerous, but fine and almost imperceptible drops.

A _mist_ is a multitude of small but solid globules, which therefore descend.

**2.** That which dims or darkens, and obscures or intercepts vision.

His passion cast a _mist_ before his sense.

**MIST**, _verb transitive_ To cloud; to cover with vapor.
