# Medley

**MED'LEY**, _noun_ A mixture; a mingled and confused mass of ingredients; used often or commonly with some degree of contempt.

This _medley_ of philosophy and war.

Love is a _medley_ of endearments, jars, suspicions, reconcilements, wars--then peace again.

**MED'LEY**, _adjective_ Mingled; confused. \[Little used.\]
