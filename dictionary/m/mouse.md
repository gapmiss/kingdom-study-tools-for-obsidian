# Mouse

**MOUSE**, _noun_ _plural_ mice. \[Latin mus; The Latin mus forms muris in the genitive, and the root is not obvious.\]

**1.** A small animal of the genus Mus, inhabiting houses. The name is also applied to many other species of the genus, as the field _mouse_ meadow _mouse_ rock _mouse_ etc.

**2.** Among seamen, a knob formed on a rope by spun yarn or parceling.

**MOUSE**, _verb intransitive_ . mouz. To catch mice.

**MOUSE**, _verb transitive_ mouz. To tear, as a cat devours a _mouse_

To _mouse_ a hook, with seamen, is to fasten a small line across the upper part to prevent unhooking.
