# Masculine

**M'ASCULINE**, _adjective_ \[Latin masculinus, from masculus, mas.\]

**1.** Having the qualities of a man; strong; robust; as a _masculine_ body.

**2.** Resembling man; coarse; opposed to delicate or soft; as _masculine_ features.

**3.** Bold; brave; as a _masculine_ spirit or courage.

**4.** In grammar, the _masculine_ gender of words is that which expresses a male, or something analogous to it; or it is the gender appropriated to males, though not always expressing the male sex.
