# Maritime

**MAR'ITIME**, _adjective_ \[Latin maritimus, from mare, the sea.\]

**1.** Relating or pertaining to the sea or ocean; as _maritime_ affairs.

**2.** Performed on the sea; naval; as _maritime_ service.

**3.** Bordering on the sea; as a _maritime_ coast.

**4.** Situated near the sea; as _maritime_ towns.

**5.** Having a navy and commerce by sea; as _maritime_ powers.

Maritimal is not now used.

\[Note. We never say, a _maritime_ body, a _maritime_ shell or production, a _maritime_ officer or engagement, a _maritime_ league. See [[Marine]].\]
