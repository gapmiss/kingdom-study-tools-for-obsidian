# Mound

**MOUND**, _noun_ \[Latin mons. See [[Mount]].\] Something raised as a defense or fortification, usually a bank of earth or stone; a bulwark; a rampart or fence.

God has thrown

That mountain as his garden _mound_ high raised.

To thrid the thickets or to leap the mounds.

**MOUND**, _verb transitive_ To fortify with a _mound_
