# Mankind

**MANKIND**, _noun_ \[man and kind.\] This word admits the accent either on the first or second syllable; the distinction of accent being inconsiderable.\]

**1.** The race or species of human beings.

The proper study of _mankind_ is man.

**2.** A male, or the males of the human race.

Thou shalt not lie with _mankind_ as with womankind. [[Leviticus 18#22|Leviticus 18:22]].

**MANKIND**, _adjective_ Resembling man in form, not woman.
