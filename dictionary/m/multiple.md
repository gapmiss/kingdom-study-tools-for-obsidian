# Multiple

**MUL'TIPLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin multiplex; multus, many, and plico, to fold.\]

Containing many times.

**MUL'TIPLE**, _noun_ In arithmetic, a common _multiple_ of two or more numbers contains each of them a certain number of times exactly; thus 24 is a common _multiple_ of 3 and 4. But the least common _multiple_ is the least number which will do this; thus 12 is the least common _multiple_ of 3 and 4.
