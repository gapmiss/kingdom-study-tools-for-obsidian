# Mediocrity

**MEDIOC'RITY**, _noun_ \[Latin mediocritas, from mediocris, middling; medius, middle.\]

**1.** A middle state or degree; a moderate degree or rate. A _mediocrity_ of condition is most favorable to morals and happiness. A _mediocrity_ of talents well employed will generally ensure respectability.

Men of age seldom drive business home to the full period, but content themselves with a _mediocrity_ of success.

**2.** Moderation; temperance.

We owe obedience to the law of reason, which teacheth _mediocrity_ in meats and drinks.
