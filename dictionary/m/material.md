# Material

**MATE'RIAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin materia, matter.\]

**1.** Consisting of matter; not spiritual; as _material_ substance; _material_ bodies.

**2.** Important; momentous; more or less necessary; having influence or effect.

Hold them for catholics or heretics, it is not a thing very _material_ in this question.

In the account of simple ideas, I shall set down only such as are most _material_ to our present purpose.

So we say, a _material_ point; a _material_ fault or error; a _material_ fact or consideration.

**3.** Not formal; substantial.

**4.** Furnishing materials; as _material_ men.

**MATE'RIAL**, _noun_ The substance or matter of which any thing is made; as, wool is the _material_ of cloth; rags are the _material_ of paper.
