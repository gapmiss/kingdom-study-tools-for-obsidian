# Musty

**MUS'TY**, _adjective_ \[from must.\] Moldy; sour; foul and fetid; as a _musty_ cask; _musty_ corn or straw; _musty_ books.

**1.** State; spoiled by age.

The proverb is somewhat _musty_

**2.** Having an ill flavor; as _musty_ wine.

**3.** Dull; heavy; spiritless.

That he may not grow _musty_ and unfit for conversation.
