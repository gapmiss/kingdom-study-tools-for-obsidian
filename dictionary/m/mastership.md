# Mastership

**M'ASTERSHIP**, _noun_ Dominion; rule; supreme power.

**1.** Superiority; preeminence.

Where noble youths for _mastership_ should strive.

**2.** Chief work; master-piece. \[Not used.\]

**3.** Superior skill.

**4.** Title of respect; in irony.

How now, signor Launce, what new with your _mastership_

**5.** The office of president of a college, or other institution.
