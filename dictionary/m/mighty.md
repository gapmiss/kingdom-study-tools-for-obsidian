# Mighty

**MI'GHTY**, _adjective_ Having great bodily strength or physical power; very strong or vigorous; as a _mighty_ arm.

**1.** Very strong; valiant; bold; as a _mighty_ man of valor. [[Judges 6#12|Judges 6:12]].

**2.** Very powerful; having great command.

Cush begat Nimrod; he began to be a _mighty_ one on the earth. [[Genesis 10#8|Genesis 10:8]].

**3.** Very strong in numbers; as a _mighty_ nation. [[Genesis 18#18|Genesis 18:18]].

**4.** Very strong or great in corporeal power; very able.

Wo to them that are _mighty_ to drink wine. [[Isaiah 5#15|Isaiah 5:15]].

**5.** Violent; very loud; as _mighty_ thunderings. [[Exodus 9#28|Exodus 9:28]]. [[Psalms 68#33|Psalms 68:33]].

**6.** Vehement; rushing with violence; as a _mighty_ wind or tempest. [[Exodus 10#19|Exodus 10:19]]. [[Revelation 6#13|Revelation 6:13]].

**7.** Very great; vast; as _mighty_ waters. [[Nehemiah 9#11|Nehemiah 9:11]].

**8.** Very great or strong; as _mighty_ power. [[2 Chronicles 26#12|2 Chronicles 26:12]].

**9.** Very forcible; efficacious; as, great is truth and _mighty_

**10.** Very great or eminent in intellect or acquirements; as the _mighty_ Scaliger and Selden.

**11.** Great; wonderful; performed with great power; as _mighty_ works. [[Matthew 11#20|Matthew 11:20]].

**12.** Very severe and distressing; as a _mighty_ famine. [[Luke 15#14|Luke 15:14]].

**13.** Very great, large or populous; as a _mighty_ city. [[Revelation 18#10|Revelation 18:10]].

**14.** Important; momentous.

I'll sing of heroes and of kings,

In _mighty_ numbers _mighty_ things.

**MI'GHTY**, _adverb_ In a great degree; very; as might wise; _mighty_ thoughtful. \[Colloquial.\]
