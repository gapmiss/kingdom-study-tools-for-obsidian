# Meliorate

**ME'LIORATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin melior, better.\] To make better; to improve; as, to _meliorate_ fruit by grafting, or soil by cultivation. Civilization has done much, but christianity more, to _meliorate_ the condition of men in society.

Nature by art we nobly _meliorate_

**ME'LIORATE**, _verb intransitive_ To grow better.
