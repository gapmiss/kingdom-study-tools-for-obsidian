# Mirth

**MIRTH**, _noun_ merth. Social merriment; hilarity; high excitement of pleasurable feelings in company; noisy gayety; jollity. _mirth_ differs from joy and cheerfulness, as always implying noise.

With genial joy to warm the soul,

Bright Helen mixed a mirth-inspiring bowl.

I will cause to cease the voice of _mirth_ from Judah and Jerusalem. [[Jeremiah 7#34|Jeremiah 7:34]].
