# Membranaceous

**MEMBRANA'CEOUS**, _adjective_ Belonging to a membrane; consisting of membranes; as a membraneous covering.

Birds of prey have _membranaceous_ stomachs, not muscular.

**1.** In botany, a _membranaceous_ leaf has no distinguishable pulp between the two surfaces. In general, it denotes flatted or resembling parchment.
