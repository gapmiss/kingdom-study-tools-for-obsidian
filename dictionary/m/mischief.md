# Mischief

**MIS'CHIEF**, _noun_

**1.** Harm; hurt; injury; damage; evil, whether intended or not. A new law is made to remedy the _mischief_

**2.** Intentional injury; harm or damage done by design.

Thy tongue deviseth _mischief_ [[Psalms 52#1|Psalms 52:1]].

**3.** Ill consequence; evil; vexatious affair.

The _mischief_ was, these allies would never allow that the common enemy was subdued.

**MIS'CHIEF**, _verb transitive_ To hurt; to harm; to injure.
