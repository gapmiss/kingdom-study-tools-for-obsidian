# Monkey

**MONKEY**, _noun_ The popular name of the ape and baboon. But in zoology, _monkey_ is more properly the name of those animals of the genus Simia, which have long tails. Ray distributes animals of this kind into three classes; apes which have no tails; monkeys with long tails; and baboons with short tails.

**1.** A name of contempt or of slight kindness.
