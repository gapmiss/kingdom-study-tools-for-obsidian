# Module

**MOD'ULE**, _noun_ \[Latin modulus.\] A model or representation.

**1.** In architecture, a certain measure or size taken at pleasure for regulating the proportion of columns, and the symmetry or disposition of the whole building. The usual _module_ of a column is its semidiameter at the base. This is divided into parts or minutes.

**MOD'ULE**, _verb transitive_ To model; to shape; to modulate. \[Little used.\]
