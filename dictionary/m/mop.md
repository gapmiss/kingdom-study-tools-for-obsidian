# Mop

**MOP**, _noun_ \[Latin mappa.\] A piece of cloth, or a collection of thrums or coarse yarn fastened to a handle and used for cleaning floors.

**1.** A wry mouth. \[Not used.\]

**MOP**, _verb transitive_ To rub or wipe with a _mop_

**MOP**, _verb intransitive_ To make a wry mouth. \[Not used.\]
