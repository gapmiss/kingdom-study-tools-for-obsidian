# Missing

**MISS'ING**, _participle present tense_ \[from miss.\] Failing to hit, to reach or to find; discovering to be wanting.

**1.** _adjective_ Lost; absent from the place where it was expected to be found; wanting. My horse is missing; my pen or my book is _missing_

For a time caught up to God, as once

Moses was in the mount, and _missing_ long.
