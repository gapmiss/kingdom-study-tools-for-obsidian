# Motive

**MO'TIVE**, _adjective_ \[See the Noun.\] Causing motion; having power to move or tending to move; as a _motive_ argument; _motive_ power.

**MO'TIVE**, _noun_

**1.** That which incites to action; that which determines the choice, or moves the will. Thus we speak of good motives, and bad motives; strong and weak motives. The _motive_ to continue at rest is ease or satisfaction; the _motive_ to change is uneasiness, or the prospect of good.

**2.** That which may or ought to incite to action; reason; cause.

**3.** A mover. \[Not in use.\]
