# Margin

**M'ARGIN**, _noun_ \[Latin margo.\]

**1.** A border; edge; brink; verge; as the _margin_ of a river or lake.

**2.** The edge of the leaf or page of a book, left blank or filled with notes.

**3.** The edge of a wound.

**4.** In botany, the edge of a leaf.

**M'ARGIN**, _verb transitive_ To furnish with a margin; to border.

**1.** To enter in the _margin_
