# Million

**MILLION**, _noun_ mil'yun. \[Latin mille, a thousand.\]

**1.** The number of ten hundred thousand, or a thousand thousand. It is used as a noun or an adjective; as a _million_ of men, or a _million_ men. As a noun, it has a regular plural, millions.

**2.** In common usage, a very great number, indefinitely.

There are millions of truths that men are not concerned to know.
