# Mortality

**MORTAL'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin mortalitas.\] Subjection to death or the necessity of dying.

When I saw her die,

I then did think on your _mortality_

**1.** Death.

Gladly would I meet

Mortality, my sentence.

**2.** Frequency of death; actual death of great numbers of men or beasts; as a time of great _mortality_

**3.** Human nature.

Take these tears, mortality's relief.

**4.** Power of destruction.

Mortality and mercy in Vienna,

Live in thy tongue and heart.
