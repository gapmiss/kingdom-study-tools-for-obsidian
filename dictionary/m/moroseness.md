# Moroseness

**MORO'SENESS**, _noun_ Sourness of temper; sullenness. _moroseness_ is not precisely peevishness or fretfulness, though often accompanied with it. It denotes more of silence and severity or ill humor, than the irritability or irritation which characterizes peevishness.

Learn good humor, never to oppose without just reason; abate some degrees of pride and _moroseness_
