# Ministration

**MINISTRA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin ministratio.\] The act of performing service as a subordinate agent; agency; intervention for aid or service.

\-- Because their widows were neglected in the daily ministrations. [[Acts 6#1|Acts 6:1]].

**1.** Office of a minister; service; ecclesiastical function.

As soon as the days of his _ministration_ were ended. [[Luke 1#23|Luke 1:23]].
