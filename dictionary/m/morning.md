# Morning

**MORN'ING**, _noun_

**1.** The first part of the day, beginning at twelve o'clock at night and extending to twelve at noon. Thus we say, a star rises at one o'clock in the _morning_ In a more limited sense, _morning_ is the time beginning an hour or two before sunrise, or at break of day, and extending to the hour of breakfast and of beginning the labors of the day. Among men of business in large cities, the _morning_ extends to the hour of dining.

**2.** The first or early part.

In the _morning_ of life, devote yourself to the service of the Most High.

**MORN'ING**, _adjective_ Pertaining to the first part or early part of the day; being in the early part of the day; as _morning_ dew; _morning_ light; _morning_ service.

She looks as clear

As _morning_ roses newly washed with dew.
