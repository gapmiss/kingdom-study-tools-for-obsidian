# Misuse

**MISUSE**, _verb transitive_ misyu'ze**.**

**1.** To treat or use improperly; to use to a bad purpose.

**2.** To abuse; to treat ill.

**MISUSE**, _noun_ misyu'se. Ill treatment; improper use; employment to a bad purpose; as the _misuse_ of mercies.

**1.** Abuse; ill treatment.

**2.** Wrong application; misapplication; erroneous use; as the _misuse_ of words.
