# Mica

**MI'CA**, _noun_ \[Latin _mica_ a grain or particle; mico, to shine.\] A mineral of a foliated structure, consisting of thin flexible lamels or scales, having a shining surface. The scales are sometimes parallel, sometimes interwoven, sometimes wavy or undulated, sometimes representing filaments. It is called also talck, glimmer, muscovy-glass, and glist.

Jameson subdivides _mica_ into ten subspecies, viz. _mica_ pinite, lepidolite, chlorite, green earth, talck, nacrite, potstone, steatite and figure stone.
