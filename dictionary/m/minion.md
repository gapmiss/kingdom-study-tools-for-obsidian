# Minion

**MIN'ION**, _adjective_ \[infra.\] Fine; trim; dainty. \[Not used.\]

**MINION**, _noun_ min'yon. A favorite; a darling; particularly, the favorite of a prince, on whom he lavishes his favors; one who gains favors by flattery or mean adulation.

Edward sent an army into Ireland, not for conquest, but to guard the person of his _minion_ Piers Gaviston.

The drowsy tyrant by his minions led.

**MIN'ION**, _noun_ \[Latin minor. See [[Mince]].\]

A small kind of printing types.
