# Mud

**MUD**, _noun_ \[Latin madeo.\] Moist and soft earth of any kind, such as is found in marshes and swamps, at the bottom or rivers and ponds, or in highways after rain.

**MUD**, _verb transitive_ To bury in _mud_ or slime.

**1.** To make turbid or foul with dirt; to stir the sediment in liquors.
