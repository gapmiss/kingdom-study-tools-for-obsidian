# Malignant

**MALIG'NANT**, _adjective_ \[Latin malignus, maligno, from malus, evil.\]

**1.** Malicious; having extreme malevolence or enmity; as a _malignant_ heart.

**2.** Unpropitious; exerting pernicious influence; as _malignant_ start.

**3.** Virulent; as a _malignant_ ulcer.

**4.** Dangerous to life; as a _malignant_ fever.

**5.** Extremely heinous; as the _malignant_ nature of sin.

**MALIG'NANT**, _noun_ A man of extreme enmity or evil intentions. \[Not used.\]
