# Mob

**MOB**, _noun_ \[from Latin mobilis, movable, variable.\]

**1.** A crowd or promiscuous multitude of people, rude, tumultuous and disorderly.

**2.** A disorderly assembly.

Had every Athenian citizen been a Socrates, every Athenian assembly would still have been a _mob_

**3.** A huddled dress.

**MOB**, _verb transitive_ To attack in a disorderly crowd; to harass tumultuously.

**1.** To wrap up in a cowl or vail.
