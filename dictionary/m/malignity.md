# Malignity

**MALIG'NITY**, _noun_ \[Latin malignitas.\] Extreme enmity, or evil dispositions of heart towards another; malice without provocation, or malevolence with baseness of heart; deep rooted spite.

**1.** Virulence; destructive tendency; as the _malignity_ of an ulcer or disease.

**2.** Extreme evilness of nature; as the _malignity_ of fraud.

**3.** Extreme sinfulness; enormity or heinousness; as the _malignity_ of sin.
