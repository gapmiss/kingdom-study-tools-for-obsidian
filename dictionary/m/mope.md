# Mope

**MOPE**, _verb intransitive_ \[I have not found this word, unless in the D. moppen, to pout.\]

To be very stupid; to be very dull; to drowse; to be spiritless or gloomy.

Demoniac phrensy, moping melancholy.

\--Or but a sickly part of one true sense

Could not so _mope_

**MOPE**, _verb transitive_ To make stupid or spiritless.

**MOPE**, _noun_ A stupid or low spirited person; a drone.
