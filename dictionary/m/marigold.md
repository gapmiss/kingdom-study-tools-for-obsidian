# Marigold

**MAR'IGOLD**, _noun_ A plant of the genus Calendula, bearing a yellow flower. There are several plants of different genera bearing this name; as the African _marigold_ of the genus Tagetes; corn-marigold, of the genus Chrysanthemum; fig-marigold, of the genus Mesembryanthemum; marsh-marigold, of the genus Caltha.
