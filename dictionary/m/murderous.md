# Murderous

**MUR'DEROUS**, _adjective_ Guilty of murder; as the _murderous_ king.

**1.** Consisting in murder; done with murder; bloody; cruel; as _murderous_ rapine.

**2.** Bloody; sanguinary; committing murder; as _murderous_ tyranny.

**3.** Premeditating murder; as _murderous_ intent or design.
