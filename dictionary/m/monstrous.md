# Monstrous

**MON'STROUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin monstrosus.\] Unnatural in form; out of the common course of nature; as a _monstrous_ birth or production.

**1.** Strange; very wonderful; generally expressive of dislike.

**2.** Enormous; huge; extraordinary; as a _monstrous_ highth; a _monstrous_ tree or mountain.

**3.** Shocking to the sight or other senses; hateful.

**MON'STROUS**, _adverb_ Exceedingly; very much; as _monstrous_ hard; _monstrous_ thick.

And will be _monstrous_ witty on the poor.

\[This use is colloquial and vulgar.\]
