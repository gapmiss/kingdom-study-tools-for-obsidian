# Mortise

**MORTISE**, _noun_ mor'tis. A cut or hollow place made in timber by the augur and chisel, to receive the tenon of another piece of timber.

**MOR'TISE**, _verb transitive_ To cut or make a _mortise_ in.

**1.** To join timbers by a tenon and mortise; as, to _mortise_ a beam into a post, or a joist into a girder.
