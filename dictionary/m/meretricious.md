# Meretricious

**MERETRI'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin meretricius, from meretrix, a prostitute.\]

**1.** Pertaining to prostitutes; such as in practiced by harlots; as _meretricious_ arts.

**2.** Alluring by false show; worn for disguise; having a gaudy but deceitful appearance; false; as _meretricious_ dress or ornaments.
