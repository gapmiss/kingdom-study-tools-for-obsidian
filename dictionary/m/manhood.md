# Manhood

**MAN'HOOD**, _noun_ \[man and hood.\] The state of one who is a man, of an adult male, or one who is advanced beyond puberty, boyhood or childhood; virility.

**1.** Virility; as opposed to womanhood.

**2.** Human nature; as the _manhood_ of Christ.

**3.** The qualities of a man; courage; bravery; resolution. \[Little used.\]
