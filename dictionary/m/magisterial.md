# Magisterial

**MAGISTE'RIAL**, _adjective_ \[See [[Magistrate]].\] Pertaining to a master; such as suits a master; authoritative.

**1.** Proud; lofty; arrogant; imperious; domineering.

Pretenses go a great way with men that take fair words and _magisterial_ looks for current payment.

**2.** In chemistry, pertaining to magistery, which see.
