# Martial

**M'ARTIAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin martialis; Mars, the god of war.\]

**1.** Pertaining to war; suited to war; as _martial_ equipage; _martial_ music; a _martial_ appearance.

**2.** Warlike; brave; given to war; as a _martial_ nation or people.

**3.** Suited to battle; as a _martial_ array.

**4.** Belonging to war, or to an army and navy; opposed to civil; as _martial_ law; a court _martial_

**5.** Pertaining to Mars, or borrowing the properties of that planet.

The natures of the fixed start are esteemed _martial_ or jovial, according to the colors by which they answer to those planets.

**6.** Having the properties if iron, called by the old chimists, Mars.
