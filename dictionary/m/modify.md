# Modify

**MOD'IFY**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin modificor; modus, limit, manner, and facio, to make.\]

**1.** To change the form or external qualities of a thing; to shape; to give a new form of being to; as, to _modify_ matter, light or sound.

**2.** To vary; to give a new form to any thing; as, to _modify_ the terms of a contract. A prefix modifies the sense of a verb.

**3.** To moderate; to qualify; to reduce in extent or degree.

Of his grace

He modifies his first severe decree.

**MOD'IFY**, _verb intransitive_ To extenuate.
