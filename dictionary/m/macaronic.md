# Macaronic

**MACARON'IC**, _adjective_ Pertaining to or like a macaroni; empty; trifling; vain; affected.

**1.** Consisting of a mixture or jumble of ill formed or ill connected words.

**MACARON'IC**, _noun_ A kind of burlesque poetry, in which native words are made to end in Latin terminations, or Latin words are modernized.
