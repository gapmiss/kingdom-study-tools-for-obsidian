# Manly

**MAN'LY**, _adjective_ \[man and like.\] Manlike; becoming a man; firm; brave; undaunted.

Serene and _manly_ hardened to sustain

The load of life--

**1.** Dignified; noble; stately.

He moves with _manly_ grace.

**2.** Pertaining to the adult age of man; as a _manly_ voice.

**3.** Not boyish or womanish; as a _manly_ stride.
