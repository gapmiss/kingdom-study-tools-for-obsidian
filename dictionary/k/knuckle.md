# Knuckle

**KNUCK'LE**, _noun_ nuk'l.

**1.** The joint of a finger, particularly when protuberant by the closing of the fingers.

**2.** The knee join of a calf; as a _knuckle_ of veal.

**3.** The joint of a plant. \[Not used.\]

**KNUCK'LE**, _verb intransitive_ nuk'l. To yield; to submit in contest to an antagonist.
