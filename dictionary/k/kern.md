# Kern

**KERN**, _noun_ An Irish footman or foot-soldier.

**1.** In English laws, an idle person or vagabond.

**KERN**, _noun_ A hand-mill consisting of two stones, one of which is turned by the hand; usually written quern, which see.

**1.** A churn.

**KERN**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To harden, as corn in ripening.

**2.** To take the form of corns; to granulate.

**KERN'-BABY**, _noun_ \[corn-baby.\] An image dressed with corn, and carried before reapers to their harvest-home.
