# Kernel

**KERN'EL**, _noun_

**1.** The edible substance contained in the shell of a nut.

**2.** Any thing included in a shell, husk or integument; a grain or corn; as a _kernel_ of wheat or oats.

**3.** The seed of pulpy fruit; as the _kernel_ of an apple.

**4.** The central part of any thing; a small mass around which other matter is concreted; a nucleus.

**5.** A hard concretion in the flesh.

**KERN'EL**, _verb intransitive_ To harden or ripen into kernels; as the seeds of plants.
