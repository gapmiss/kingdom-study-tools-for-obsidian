# Kite

**KITE**, _noun_ A rapacious fowl of the genus Falco or hawk, remarkable for gliding through the air without frequently moving its wings; hence called glide.

**1.** A name of reproach, denoting rapacity.

**2.** A light frame of wood and paper constructed for flying in the air for the amusement of boys.

**KITE**, _noun_ In the north of England, the belly.
