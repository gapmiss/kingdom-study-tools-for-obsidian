# Knoll

**KNOLL**, _verb transitive_ noll. \[See [[Knell]].\] To ring a bell, usually for a funeral.

**KNOLL**, _verb intransitive_ noll. To sound, as a bell.

\[This word, I believe, is not used in America.\]

**KNOLL**, _noun_ noll. The top or crown of a hill; but more generally, a little round hill or mount; a small elevation of earth.
