# Kindness

**KINDNESS**, _noun_ \[from kind, the adjective.\]

**1.** Good will; benevolence; that temper or disposition which delights in contributing to the happiness of others, which is exercised cheerfully in gratifying their wishes, supplying their wants or alleviating their distresses; benignity of nature. _kindness_ ever accompanies love.

There is no man whose _kindness_ we may not sometime want, or by whose malice we may not sometime suffer.

**2.** Act of good will; beneficence; any act of benevolence which promotes the happiness or welfare of others. Charity, hospitality, attentions to the wants of others, etc., are deemed acts of _kindness_ or kindnesses. [[Acts 28#2|Acts 28:2]].
