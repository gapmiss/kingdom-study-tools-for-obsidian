# Kit

**KIT**, _noun_ A large bottle.

**1.** A small fiddle.

**2.** A kind of fish-tub, and a milk-pail.

\[I know not that this word is used in America.\]

**KIT'-CAT**, _noun_ A term applied to a club in London, to which Addison and Steele belonged; so called from Christopher Cat, a pastry cook, who served the club with mutton pies; applied also to a portrait three fourths less than a half length, placed in the club-room.
