# Kindred

**KIN'DRED**, _noun_ \[from kin, kind.\]

**1.** Relation by birth; consanguinity.

Like her, of equal _kindred_ to the throne.

**2.** Relation by marriage; affinity.

**3.** Relatives by blood or marriage, more properly the former.

Thou shalt go unto my country and to my _kindred_ [[Genesis 24#4|Genesis 24:4]].

**4.** Relation; suit; connection in kind.

**KIN'DRED**, _adjective_ Related; congenial; of the like nature or properties; as _kindred_ souls; _kindred_ skies.
