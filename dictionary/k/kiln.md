# Kiln

**KILN**, _noun_ kil.

**1.** A large stove or oven; a fabric of brick or stone which may be heated for the purpose of hardening, burning or drying any thing; as a _kiln_ for baking or hardening earthen vessels; a _kiln_ for drying grain or meal.

**2.** A pile of brick constructed for burning or hardening; called also a brick-kiln.
