# Keenness

**KEE'NNESS**, _noun_ Eagerness; vehemence; as the _keenness_ of hunger.

**1.** Sharpness; fineness of edge; as the _keenness_ of a razor.

**2.** The quality of piercing; rigor; sharpness; as the _keenness_ of the air or of cold.

**3.** Asperity; acrimony; bitterness; as the _keenness_ of satire, invective or sarcasm.

**4.** Acuteness; sharpness; as the _keenness_ of wit.
