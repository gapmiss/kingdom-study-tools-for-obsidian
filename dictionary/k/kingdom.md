# Kingdom

**KING'DOM**, _noun_ \[king and dom, jurisdiction.\]

**1.** The territory or country subject to a king; an undivided territory under the dominion of a king or monarch. The foreign possessions of a king are not usually included in the term _kingdom_ Thus we speak of the _kingdom_ of England, of France or of Spain, without including the East or West Indies.

**2.** The inhabitants or population subject to a king. The whole _kingdom_ was alarmed.

**3.** In natural history, a division; as the animal, vegetable and mineral kingdoms.

**4.** A region; a tract; the place where any thing prevails and holds sway; as the watery _kingdom_

**5.** In Scripture, the government or universal dominion of God. [[1 Chronicles 29#11|1 Chronicles 29:11]]. [[Psalms 145#11|Psalms 145:11]].

**6.** The power of supreme administration. [[1 Samuel 18#8|1 Samuel 18:8]].

**7.** A princely nation or state.

Ye shall be unto me a _kingdom_ of priests. [[Exodus 19#6|Exodus 19:6]].

**8.** Heaven. [[Matthew 26#29|Matthew 26:29]].

**9.** State of glory in heaven. [[Matthew 5#3|Matthew 5:3]].

**10.** The reign of the Messiah. Mat 3.

**11.** Government; rule; supreme administration.
