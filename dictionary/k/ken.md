# Ken

**KEN**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin canus, white, caneo, to be white, and this with Latin cano, to sing, canto, Eng. to cant, to chant; Latin gigno.\]

**1.** To see at a distance; to descry.

We _ken_ them from afar.

**2.** To know; to understand. \[This verb is used chiefly in poetry.\]

**KEN**, _verb intransitive_ To look round.

**KEN**, _noun_ View; reach of sight.

Coasting they kept the land within their _ken_
