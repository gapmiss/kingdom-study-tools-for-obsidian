# Knee

**KNEE**, _noun_ nee. \[Latin genu.\]

**1.** In anatomy, the articulation of the thigh and leg bones.

**2.** In ship-building, a piece of timber somewhat in the shape of the human _knee_ when bent, having two branches or arms, and used to connect the beams of a ship with her sides or timbers.

**KNEE**, _verb transitive_ nee. To supplicate by kneeling. \[Not used.\]
