# Keeping

**KEE'PING**, _participle present tense_ Holding; restraining; preserving; guarding; protecting; performing.

**KEE'PING**, _noun_ A holding; restraint; custody; guard; preservation.

**1.** Feed; fodder. The cattle have good _keeping_

**2.** In painting, a representation of objects in the manner they appear to the eye at different distance from it.
