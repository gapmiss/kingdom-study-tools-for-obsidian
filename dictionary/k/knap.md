# Knap

**KNAP**, _noun_ nap. A protuberance; a swelling. \[Little used. See [[Knob]].\]

**KNAP**, _verb transitive_ nap. \[See Knab.\]

**1.** To bite; to bite off; to break short. \[Little used.\]

**2.** To strike with a sharp noise. \[Little used.\]

**KNAP**, _verb intransitive_ nap. To make a short, sharp sound.
