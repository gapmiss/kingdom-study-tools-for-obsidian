# Perspicuous

**PERSPIC'UOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin perspicuus.\]

Transparent; translucent. \[Little used.\]

**1.** Clear to the understanding; that may be clearly understood; not obscure or ambiguous. Language is _perspicuous_ when it readily presents to the reader or hearer the precise ideas which are intended to be expressed. Meaning, sense or signification if _perspicuous_ when it is clearly and easily comprehended.
