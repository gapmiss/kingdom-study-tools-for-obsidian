# Patriarchal

**PATRIARCH'AL**

PA'TRIARCHATE

PATRIARCH'IC, _adjective_ Belonging to patriarchs; possessed by patriarchs; as _patriarchal_ power or jurisdiction; a _patriarchal_ see.

**1.** Subject to a patriarch; as a _patriarchal_ church.

Patriarchal cross, in heraldry, is that where the shaft is twice crossed, the lower arms being longer than the upper ones.
