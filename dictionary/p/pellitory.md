# Pellitory

**PEL'LITORY**, _noun_ \[Latin parietaria, the wall plant, from paries.\]

The name of several plants of different genera. The _pellitory_ of the wall or common _pellitory_ is of the genus Parietaria; the bastard _pellitory_ of the genus Achillea; and the _pellitory_ of Spain is the Anthemis pyrethrum.

**PELL'-MELL**, _adverb_ With confused violence.
