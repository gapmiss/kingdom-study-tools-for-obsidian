# Polemic

**POLEM'IC**

**POLEM'IC**AL, _adjective_ \[Gr. war.\]

**1.** Controversial; disputative; intended to maintain an opinion or system in opposition to others; as a _polemic_ treatise, discourse, essay or book; _polemic_ divinity.

**2.** Engaged in supporting an opinion or system by controversy; as a _polemic_ writer.

**POLEM'IC**, _noun_ A disputant; a controvertist; one who writes in support of an opinion or system in opposition to another.
