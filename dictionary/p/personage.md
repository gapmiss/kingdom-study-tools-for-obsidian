# Personage

**PER'SONAGE**, _noun_ A man or woman of distinction; as an illustrious _personage_

**1.** Exterior appearance; stature; air; as a tall personage; a stately _personage_

**2.** Character assumed.

The Venetians, naturally grave, love to give in to the follies of such seasons, when disguised in a false _personage_

**3.** Character represented.

Some persons must be found, already known in history, whom we may make the actors and personages of this fable.
