# Purely

**PU'RELY**, _adverb_ In a pure manner; with an entire separation of heterogeneous or foul matter. [[Isaiah 1#25|Isaiah 1:25]].

**1.** Without any mixture of improper or vicious words or phrases.

**2.** Innocently; without guilt.

**3.** Merely; absolutely; without connection with any thing else; completely; totally. The meeting was _purely_ accidental.
