# Predicable

**PRED'ICABLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin proedicabilis, from proedico, to affirm; proe and dico, to say.\] That may be affirmed of something; that may be attributed to. Animal is _predicable_ of man. Intelligence is not _predicable_ of plants. More or less is not _predicable_ of a circle or of a square. Whiteness is not _predicable_ of time.

**PRED'ICABLE**, _noun_ One of the five things which can be affirmed of any thing. Genus, species, difference, property, and accident are the five predicables.
