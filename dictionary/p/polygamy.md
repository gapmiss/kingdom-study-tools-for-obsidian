# Polygamy

**POLYG'AMY**, _noun_ \[Gr. many, and marriage.\] A plurality of wives or husbands at the same time; or the having of such plurality. When a man has more wives than one, or a woman more husbands than one, at the same time, the offender is punishable for _polygamy_ Such is the fact in christian countries. But _polygamy_ is allowed in some countries, as in Turkey.
