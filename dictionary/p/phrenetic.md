# Phrenetic

**PHRENET'IC**, _adjective_ \[Gr. See [[Phrensy]].\] Subject to strong or violent sallies of imagination or excitement, which in some measure pervert the judgment and cause the person to act in a manner different from the more rational part of mankind; wild and erratic; partially mad. \[It has been sometimes written phrentic, but is now generally written frantic.\]

**PHRENET'IC**, _noun_ A person who is wild and erratic in his imagination.
