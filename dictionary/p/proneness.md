# Proneness

**PRO'NENESS**, _noun_ The state of bending downwards; opposed to the erectness of man.

**1.** The state of lying with the face downwards; contrary to supineness.

**2.** Descent; declivity; as the _proneness_ of a hill.

**3.** Inclination of mind, heart or temper; propension; disposition; as the _proneness_ of the Israelites to idolatry; _proneness_ to self-gratification or to self-justification; _proneness_ to comply with temptation; sometimes in a good sense; as ; the _proneness_ of good men to commiserate want.
