# Pectoral

**PEC'TORAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin pectoralis, from pectus, breast.\]

Pertaining to the breast; as the _pectoral_ muscles; _pectoral_ medicines.

The _pectoral_ fins of a fish are situated on the sides of the fish, behind the gills.

**PEC'TORAL**, _noun_ A breastplate.

**1.** A sacerdotal habit or vestment worn by the Jewish high priest, called in our version of the Bible, a breastplate.

**2.** A medicine adapted to cure or relieve complaints of the breast and lungs.
