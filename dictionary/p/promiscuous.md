# Promiscuous

**PROMIS'CUOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin promiscuus; pro and misceo, to mix.\]

**1.** Mingled; consisting of individuals united in a body or mass without order; confused; undistinguished; as a _promiscuous_ crowd or mass.

A wild where weeds and flow'rs _promiscuous_ shoot.

**2.** Common; indiscriminate; not restricted to an individual; as _promiscuous_ love or intercourse.
