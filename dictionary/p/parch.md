# Parch

**P'ARCH**, _verb transitive_ \[I know not from what source we have received this word. It corresponds in elements with the Italian bruciare, to burn or roast. Qu. Latin peraresco.\]

**1.** To burn the surface of a thing; to scorch; as, to _parch_ the skin; to _parch_ corn.

**2.** To dry to extremity; as, the heat of the sun's rays parches the ground; the mouth is parched with thirst.

**P'ARCH**, _verb intransitive_ To be scorched or superficially burnt; as, corn will dry and _parch_ into barley.

**1.** To become very dry.
