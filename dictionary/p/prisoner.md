# Prisoner

**PRIS'ONER**, _noun_ One who is confined in a prison by legal arrest or warrant.

**1.** A person under arrest or in custody of the sheriff, whether in prison or not; as a _prisoner_ at the bar of a court.

**2.** A captive; one taken by an enemy in war.

**3.** One whose liberty is restrained, as a bird in a cage.
