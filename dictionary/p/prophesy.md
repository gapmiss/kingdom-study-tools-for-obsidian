# Prophesy

**PROPH'ESY**, _verb transitive_ To foretell future events; to predict.

I hate him, for he doth not _prophesy_ good concerning

me, but evil. [[1 Kings 22#8|1 Kings 22:8]].

**1.** To foreshow. \[Little used.\]

**PROPH'ESY**, _verb intransitive_ To utter predictions; to make declaration of events to come. [[Jeremiah 11#21|Jeremiah 11:21]].

**1.** In Scripture, to preach; to instruct in religious doctrines; to interpret or explain Scripture or religious subjects; to exhort. [[1 Corinthians 13#9|1 Corinthians 13:9]]. [[Ezekiel 37#4|Ezekiel 37:4]].
