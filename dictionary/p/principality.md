# Principality

**PRINCIPAL'ITY**, _noun_

**1.** Sovereignty; supreme power.

**2.** A prince; one invested with sovereignty. [[Titus 3#1|Titus 3:1]].

**3.** The territory of a prince; or the country which gives title to a prince; as the _principality_ of Wales.

**4.** Superiority; predominance. \[Little used.\]

**5.** In Scripture, royal state or attire. [[Jeremiah 13#1|Jeremiah 13:1]].
