# Preceptive

**PRECEP'TIVE**, _adjective_ \[Latin proeceptivus.\] Giving precepts or commands for the regulation of moral conduct; containing precepts; as the _preceptive_ parts of the Scriptures.

**1.** Directing in moral conduct; giving rules or directions; didactic.

The lesson given us here is _preceptive_ to us.

Preceptive poetry.
