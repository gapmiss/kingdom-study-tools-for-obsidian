# Protector

**PROTECT'OR**, _noun_ One that defends or shields from injury, evil or oppression; a defender; a guardian. The king or sovereign is, or ought to be, the _protector_ of the nation; the husband is the _protector_ of his wife, and the father of his children.

**1.** In England, one who formerly had the care of the kingdom during the king's minority; a regent. Cromwell assumed the title of lord _protector_

**2.** In catholic countries, every nation and every religious order has a _protector_ residing at Rome. He is a cardinal, and called cardinal _protector_
