# Presbyterial

**PRESBYTE'RIAL**

PRESBYTE'RIAN, _adjective_ Pertaining to a presbyter, or to ecclesiastical government by presbyters.

**1.** Consisting of presbyters; as presbyterian government. The government of the church of Scotland is presbyterian.

**PRESBYTE'RIAN**, _noun_ One that maintains the validity of ordination and government by presbyters.

**1.** One that belongs to a church governed by presbyters.
