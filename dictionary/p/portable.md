# Portable

**PORTABLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin porto, to carry.\]

**1.** That may be carried by the hand or about the person, on horseback, or in a traveling vehicle; not bulky or heavy; that may be easily conveyed from place to place with one's traveling baggage; as a _portable_ bureau or secretary.

**2.** That may be carried from place to place.

**3.** That may be borne along with one.

The pleasure of the religious man is an easy and _portable_ pleasure.

**4.** Sufferable; supportable. \[Not in use.\]
