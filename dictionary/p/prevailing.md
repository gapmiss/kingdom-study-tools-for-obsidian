# Prevailing

**PREVA'ILING**, _participle present tense_ Gaining advantage, superiority or victory; having effect; persuading; succeeding.

**1.** _adjective_ Predominant; having more influence; prevalent; superior in power. The love of money and the love of power are the _prevailing_ passions of men.

**2.** Efficacious.

Saints shall assist thee with _prevailing_ prayers.

**3.** Predominant; most general; as the _prevailing_ disease of a climate; a _prevailing_ opinion. Intemperance is the _prevailing_ vice of many countries.
