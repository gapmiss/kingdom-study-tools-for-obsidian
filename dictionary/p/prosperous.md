# Prosperous

**PROS'PEROUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin prosperus.\] Advancing in the pursuit of any thing desirable; making gain or increase; thriving; successful; as a _prosperous_ trade; a _prosperous_ voyage; a _prosperous_ expedition or undertaking; a _prosperous_ man, family or nation; a _prosperous_ war.

The seed shall be prosperous; the vine shall give her fruit. [[Zechariah 8#12|Zechariah 8:12]].

**1.** Favorable; favoring success; as a _prosperous_ wind.
