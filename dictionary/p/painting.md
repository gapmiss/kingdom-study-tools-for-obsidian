# Painting

**PA'INTING**, _participle present tense_ Representing in colors; laying on colors.

**PA'INTING**, _noun_ The art of forming figures or resembling objects in colors on canvas or other material, or the art of representing to the eye by means of figures and colors, any object of sight, and sometimes the emotions of the mind.

**1.** A picture; a likeness or resemblance in colors.

**2.** Colors laid on.
