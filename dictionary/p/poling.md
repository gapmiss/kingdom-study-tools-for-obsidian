# Poling

**PO'LING**, _noun_ In gardening, the operation of dispersing the worm-casts all over the walks, with long ash poles. This destroys the worm-casts and is beneficial to the walks.

**PO'LING**, _participle present tense_ Furnishing with poles for support.

**1.** Bearing on poles.

**2.** Pushing forward with poles, as a boat.
