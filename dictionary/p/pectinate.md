# Pectinate

**PEC'TINATE**

**PEC'TINATE**D, _adjective_ \[from Latin pecten, a comb.\] Having resemblance to the teeth of a comb. In botany, a _pectinate_ leaf is a sort of pinnate leaf, in which the leaflets are toothed like a comb.

A mineral is pectinated, when it presents short filaments, crystals or branches, nearly parallel and equidistant.
