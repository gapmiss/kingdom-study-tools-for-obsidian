# Perdu

**PERDU**'

**PERDU**'E, _adverb_ \[Latin perdo.\] Close; in concealment.

The moderator, out of view,

Beneath the desk had lain perdue.

**PERDU'**, _noun_ One that is placed on the watch or in ambush.

**PERDU'**, _adjective_ Abandoned; employed on desperate purposes; accustomed to desperate purposes or enterprises.
