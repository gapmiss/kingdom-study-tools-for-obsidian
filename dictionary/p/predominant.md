# Predominant

**PREDOM'INANT**, _adjective_ \[Latin proe and dominans, dominor, to rule.\]

Prevalent over others; superior in strength, influence or authority; ascendant; ruling; controlling; as a _predominant_ color; _predominant_ beauty or excellence; a _predominant_ passion.

Those helps--were _predominant_ in the king's mind.

Foul subornation is _predominant_
