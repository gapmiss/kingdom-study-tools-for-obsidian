# Prettiness

**PRETTINESS**, _noun_ prit'tiness. \[from pretty.\]

**1.** Diminutive beauty; a pleasing form without stateliness or dignity; as the _prettiness_ of the face; the _prettiness_ of a bird or other small animal; the _prettiness_ of dress.

**2.** Neatness and taste displayed on small objects; as the _prettiness_ of a flower bed.

**3.** Decency of manners; pleasing propriety without dignity or elevation; as the _prettiness_ of a child'd behavior.
