# Potent

**PO'TENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin potens.\] Powerful; physically strong; forcible; efficacious; as a _potent_ medicine.

Moses once more his _potent_ rod extends.

**1.** Powerful, in a moral sense; having great influence; as _potent_ interest; a _potent_ argument.

**2.** Having great authority, control or dominion; as a _potent_ prince.

**PO'TENT**, _noun_ A prince; a potentate. \[Not in use.\]

**1.** A walking staff or crutch. \[Not used.\]
