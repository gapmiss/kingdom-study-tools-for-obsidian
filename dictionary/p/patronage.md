# Patronage

**PAT'RONAGE**, _noun_ Special countenance or support; favor or aid afforded to second the views of a person or to promote a design.

**1.** Guardianship, as of a saint.

**2.** Advowson; the right of presentation to a church or ecclesiastical benefice.

**PAT'RONAGE**, _verb transitive_ To patronize or support. \[Not used.\]
