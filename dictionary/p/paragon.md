# Paragon

**PAR'AGON**, _noun_ \[Latin par, equal.\]

**1.** A model or pattern; a model by way of distinction, implying superior excellence or perfection; as a _paragon_ of beauty or eloquence.

**2.** A companion; a fellow.

**3.** Emulation; a match for trial.

**PAR'AGON**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To compare; to parallel.

The picture of Pamela, in little form, he wore in a tablet, purposing to _paragon_ the little one with Artesia's length. \[Little used.\]

**2.** To equal. \[Little used.\]

**PAR'AGON**, _verb intransitive_ To pretend comparison or equality. \[Little used.\]
