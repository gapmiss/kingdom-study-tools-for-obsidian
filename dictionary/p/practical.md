# Practical

**PRAC'TICAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin practicus.\] Pertaining to practice or action.

**1.** Capable of practice or active use; opposed to speculative; as a _practical_ understanding.

**2.** That may be used in practice; that may be applied to use; as _practical_ knowledge.

**3.** That reduces his knowledge or theories to actual use; as a _practical_ man.

**4.** Derived from practice or experience; as _practical_ skill or knowledge.
