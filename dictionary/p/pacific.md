# Pacific

**PACIFIC**, _adjective_ \[Latin , to make peace. See [[Peace]].\]

**1.** Peace-making; conciliatory; suited to make or restore peace; adapted to reconcile differences; mild; appeasing; as, to offer _pacific_ propositions to a belligerent power. The measures proposed are in their nature _pacific_

**2.** Calm; tranquil; as a _pacific_ state of things.

**PACIFIC**, _noun_ The appellation given to the ocean situated between America on the west, and Asia; so called on account of its exemption from violent tempests.
