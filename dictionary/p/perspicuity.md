# Perspicuity

**PERSPICU'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin perspicuitas, from perspicio.\]

**1.** Transparency; clearness; that quality of a substance which renders objects visible through it. \[Little used.\]

**2.** Clearness to mental vision; easiness to be understood; freedom from obscurity or ambiguity; that quality of writing or language which readily presents to the mind of another the precise ideas of the author. _perspicuity_ is the first excellence of writing or speaking.
