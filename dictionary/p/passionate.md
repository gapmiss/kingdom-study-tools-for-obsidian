# Passionate

**PAS'SIONATE**, _adjective_

**1.** Easily moved to anger; easily excited or agitated by injury or insult; applied to persons.

Homer's Achilles is haughty and _passionate_

**2.** Highly excited; vehement; warm; applied to things; as _passionate_ affection; _passionate_ desire; _passionate_ concern.

**3.** Expressing strong emotion; animated; as _passionate_ eloquence.

**PAS'SIONATE**, _verb transitive_ To affect with passion; to express passionately. \[Not used.\]
