# Peccant

**PEC'CANT**, _adjective_ \[Latin peccans. See [[Peccable]].\]

**1.** Sinning; guilty of sin or transgression; criminal; as _peccant_ angels.

**2.** Morbid; bad; corrupt; not healthy; as _peccant_ humors.

**3.** Wrong; bad; defective; informal; as a _peccant_ citation. \[Not used.\]

**PEC'CANT**, _noun_ An offender. \[Not used.\]
