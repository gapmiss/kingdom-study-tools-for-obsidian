# Prejudicial

**PREJUDI'CIAL**, _adjective_ Biased or blinded by prejudices; as a _prejudicial_ eye. \[Not in use.\]

**1.** Hurtful; mischievous; injurious; disadvantageous; detrimental; tending to obstruct or impair. A high rate of interest is _prejudicial_ to trade and manufactures. Intemperance is _prejudicial_ to health.

His going away the next morning with all his troops, was most _prejudicial_ to the king's affairs.

One of the young ladies reads while the others are at work; so that the learning of the family is not at all _prejudicial_ to its manufactures.
