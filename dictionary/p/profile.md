# Profile

**PROFILE**, _noun_ pro'fil. \[Latin filum, a thread or line.\]

**1.** Primarily, an outline of contour; hence, in sculpture and painting, a head or portrait represented sidewise or in a side view; the side face or half face; as, to draw or appear in profile; the _profile_ of Pope or Addison.

**2.** In architecture, the contour or outline of a figure, building or member; also, the draught of a building, representing it as if cut down perpendicularly from the roof to the foundation.

**PRO'FILE**, _verb transitive_ To draw the outline of a head sidewise; to draw in profile; as a building.
