# Prospective

**PROSPECT'IVE**, _adjective_ Looking forward in time; regarding the future; opposed to retrospective.

The supporting of Bible societies is one of the points on which the promises, at the time of ordination, had no _prospective_ bearing.

**1.** Acting with foresight.

The French king and king of Sweden, are circumspect, industrious and _prospective_ in this affair.

**2.** Pertaining to a prospect; viewing at a distance.

**3.** Furnishing an extensive prospect.
