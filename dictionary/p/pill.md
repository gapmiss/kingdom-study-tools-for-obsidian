# Pill

**PILL**, _noun_ \[Latin pila, a ball; pilula, a little ball.\]

**1.** In pharmacy, a medicine in the form of a little ball or small round mass, to be swallowed whole.

**2.** Any thing nauseous.

**PILL**, _verb transitive_ To rob; to plunder; to pillage, that is, to peel, to strip. \[See [[Peel]], the same word in the proper English orthography.\]

**PILL**, _verb intransitive_ To be peeled; to come off in flakes.

**1.** To rob. \[See [[Peel]].\]
