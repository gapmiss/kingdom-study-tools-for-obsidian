# Piety

**PI'ETY**, _noun_ \[Latin pietas, from pius, or its root, probably a contracted word.\]

**1.** _piety_ in principle, is a compound of veneration or reverence of the Supreme Being and love of his character, or veneration accompanied with love; and _piety_ in practice, is the exercise of these affections in obedience to his will and devotion to his service.

Piety is the only proper and adequate relief of decaying man.

**2.** Reverence of parents or friends, accompanied with affection and devotion to their honor and happiness.
