# Pave

**PAVE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin pavio; Gr. to beat, to strike.\]

**1.** To lay or cover with stone or brick so as to make a level or convenient surface for horses, carriages or foot passengers; to floor with brick or stone; as, to _pave_ a street; to _pave_ a side-walk; to _pave_ a court or stable.

**2.** To prepare a passage; to facilitate the introduction of. The invention of printing paved the way for intellectual improvement.
