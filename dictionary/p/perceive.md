# Perceive

**PERCE'IVE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin percipio; per and capio, to take.\]

**1.** To have knowledge or receive impressions of external objects through the medium or instrumentality of the senses or bodily organs; as, to _perceive_ light or color; to _perceive_ the cold or ice or the taste of honey.

**2.** To know; to understand; to observe.

Till we ourselves see it with our own eyes, and _perceive_ it by our own understanding, we are in the dark.

**3.** To be affected by; to receive impressions from.

The upper regions of the air _perceive_ the collection of the matter of tempests before the air below.
