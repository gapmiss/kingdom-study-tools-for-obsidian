# Plenipotentiary

**PLENIPOTEN'TIARY**,_noun_ A person invested with full power to transact any business; usually, an embassador or envoy to a foreign court, furnished with full power to negotiate a treaty or to transact other business.

**PLENIPOTEN'TIARY**, _adjective_ Containing full power; as _plenipotentiary_ license or authority.
