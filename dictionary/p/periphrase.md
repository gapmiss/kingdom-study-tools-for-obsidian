# Periphrase

**PER'IPHRASE**, _noun_ s as z. \[Gr. about, and to speak.\] Circumlocution; a circuit of words; the use of more words than are necessary to express the idea; a figure of rhetoric employed to avoid a common and trite manner of expression.

**PER'IPHRASE**, _verb transitive_ To express by circumlocution.

**PER'IPHRASE**, _verb intransitive_ To use circumlocution.
