# Pneumatic

**PNEUMATIC**

**PNEUMATIC**AL, _adjective_ numat'ic. \[Gr. breath, spirit; to breathe or blow.\]

**1.** Consisting of air, as a thin compressible substance; opposed to dense or solid substances.

The _pneumatic_ substance being, in some bodies, the native spirit of the body.

**2.** Pertaining to air, or to the philosophy of its properties; as _pneumatic_ experiments; a _pneumatic_ engine.

**3.** Moved or played by means of air; as a _pneumatic_ instrument of music.
