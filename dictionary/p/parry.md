# Parry

**PAR'RY**, _verb transitive_

**1.** In fencing, to ward off; to stop or to put or turn by; as, to _parry_ a thrust.

**2.** To ward off; to turn aside; to prevent a blow from taking effect.

**3.** To avoid; to shift off.

The French government has parried the payment of our claims.

**PAR'RY**, _verb intransitive_ To ward off; to put by thrusts or strokes; to fence.
