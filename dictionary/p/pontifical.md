# Pontifical

**PONTIF'ICAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin pontificalis.\] Belonging to a high priest; as _pontifical_ authority; hence, belonging to the pope; popish.

**1.** Splendid; magnificent.

**2.** Bridge-building. \[Not used.\]

**PONTIF'ICAL**, _noun_ A book containing rites and ceremonies ecclesiastical.

**1.** The dress and ornaments of a priest or bishop.
