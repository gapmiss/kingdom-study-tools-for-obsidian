# Pertinent

**PER'TINENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin pertinens.\] Related to the subject or matter in

hand; just to the purpose; adapted to the end proposed; apposite; not foreign to the thing intended. We say, he used an argument not _pertinent_ to his subject or design. The discourse abounds with _pertinent_ remarks. He gave _pertinent_ answers to the questions.

**1.** Regarding; concerning; belonging. \[Little used.\]
