# Pomp

**POMP**, _noun_ \[Latin pompa; bombus; Eng. bomb, bombast.\]

**1.** A procession distinguished by ostentation of grandeur and splendor; as the _pomp_ of a Roman triumph.

**2.** Show of magnificence; parade; splendor.

Hearts formed for love, but doom'd in vain to glow

In prison'd _pomp_ and weep in splendid woe.
