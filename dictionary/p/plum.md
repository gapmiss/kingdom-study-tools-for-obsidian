# Plum

**PLUM**, _noun_

**1.** The fruit of a tree belonging to the genus Prunus. The fruit is a drupe, containing a nut or stone with prominent sutures and inclosing a kernel. The varieties of the _plum_ are numerous and well known.

**2.** A grape dried in the sun; a raisin.

**3.** The sum of f100, 000 sterling.

**4.** A kind of play.

\[Dr. Johnson remarks that this word is often written improperly plumb. This is true, not only of this word, but of all words in which b follows m, as in thumb, dumb, etc.\]
