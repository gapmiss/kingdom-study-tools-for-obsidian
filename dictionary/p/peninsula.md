# Peninsula

**PENIN'SULA**, _noun_ \[Latin pene, almost, and insula, an isle.\]

**1.** A portion of land, connected with a continent by a narrow neck or isthmus, but nearly surrounded with water. Thus Boston stands on a _peninsula_

**2.** A large extent of country joining the main land by a part narrower than the tract itself. Thus Spain and Portugal are said to be situated on a _peninsula_
