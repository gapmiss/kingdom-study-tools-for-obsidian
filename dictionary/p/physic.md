# Physic

**PHYS'IC**, _noun_ s as z. \[Gr. from nature; to produce.\]

**1.** The art of healing diseases. This is now generally called medicine.

**2.** Medicines; remedies for diseases. We desire _physic_ only for the sake of health.

**3.** In popular language, a medicine that purges; a purge; a cathartic. \[In technical and elegant language this sense is not used.\]

**PHYS'IC**, _verb transitive_ To treat with physic; to evacuate the bowels with a cathartic; to purge.

**1.** To cure.
