# Pertain

**PERTA'IN**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin pertineo, per and tenco, to hold.\]

**1.** To belong; to be the property, right or duty of.

Men hate those who affect honor by ambition, which pertaineth not to them.

He took the fortified cities which pertained to Judah. [[2 Kings 18#13|2 Kings 18:13]].

It pertains to the governor to open the ports by proclamation.

**2.** To have relation to. [[Acts 1#3|Acts 1:3]].
