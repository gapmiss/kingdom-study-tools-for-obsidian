# Prodigious

**PRODIG'IOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin prodigiosus. See [[Prodigy]].\]

**1.** Very great; huge; enormous in size, quantity, extent, etc.; as a mountain of _prodigious_ size or altitude; a _prodigious_ mass or quantity of water; an ocean or plain of _prodigious_ extent. Hence,

**2.** Wonderful; astonishing; such as may seem a prodigy; monstrous; portentous.

It is _prodigious_ to have thunder in a clear sky.

Prodigious to relate.
