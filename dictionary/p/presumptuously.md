# Presumptuously

**PRESUMP'TUOUSLY**, _adverb_ With rash confidence.

**1.** Arrogantly; insolently.

**2.** Willfully; in bold defiance of conscience or violation of known duty; as, to sin _presumptuously_ [[Numbers 15#30|Numbers 15:30]].

**3.** With groundless and vain confidence in the divine favor.
