# Pat

**PAT**, _adjective_ Fit; convenient; exactly suitable either as to time or place. \[Not an elegant word, but admissible in burlesque.\]

**PAT**, _adverb_ Fitly; conveniently.

**PAT**, _noun_ A light quick blow or stroke with the fingers or hand.

**PAT**, _verb transitive_ To strike gently with the fingers or hand; to tap.

Gay pats my shoulder and you vanish quite.
