# Prop

**PROP**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To support or prevent from falling by placing something under or against; as, to _prop_ a fence or an old building.

**2.** To support by standing under or against.

Till the bright mountains _prop_ th' incumbent sky.

**3.** To support; to sustain; in a general sense; as, to _prop_ a declining state.

I _prop_ myself upon the few supports that are left me.

**PROP**, _noun_ That which sustains an incumbent weight; that on which any thing rests for support; a support; a stay; as a _prop_ for vines; a _prop_ for an old building. An affectionate child is the _prop_ of declining age.
