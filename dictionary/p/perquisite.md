# Perquisite

**PER'QUISITE**, _noun_ s as z. \[Latin perquisitus, perquiro; per and quoero, to seek.\] A fee or pecuniary allowance to an officer for services, beyond his ordinary salary or settled wages; or a fee allowed by law to an officer for a specific service, in lieu of an annual salary. \[The latter is the common acceptation of the word in American.\]
