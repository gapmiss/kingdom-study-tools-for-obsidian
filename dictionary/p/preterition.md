# Preterition

**PRETERI'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin proeterio, to pass by.\]

**1.** The act of going past; the state of being past.

**2.** In rhetoric, a figure by which, in pretending to pass over any thing, we make a summary mention of it; as, 'I will not say, he is valiant, he is learned, he is just, ' etc. The most artful praises are those bestowed by way of _preterition_
