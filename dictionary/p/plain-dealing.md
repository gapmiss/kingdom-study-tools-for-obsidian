# Plain-dealing

**PLAIN-DE'ALING**, _adjective_ \[plain and deal.\] Dealing or communicating with frankness and sincerity; honest; open; speaking and acting without art; as a _plain-dealing_ man.

**PLAIN-DE'ALING**, _noun_ A speaking or communicating with openness and sincerity; management without art, stratagem or disguise; sincerity.
