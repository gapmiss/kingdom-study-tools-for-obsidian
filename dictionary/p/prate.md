# Prate

**PRATE**, _verb intransitive_ To talk much and without weight, or to little purpose; to be loquacious; as the vulgar express it, to run on.

To _prate_ and talk for life and honor,

And make a fool presume to _prate_ of love.

**PRATE**, _verb transitive_ To utter foolishly.

_What nonsense would the fool, thy master, _prate__

When thou, his knave, canst talk at such a rate?
