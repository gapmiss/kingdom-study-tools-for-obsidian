# Pentameter

**PENTAM'ETER**, _noun_ \[Gr. five, and measure.\] In ancient poetry, a verse of five feet. The two first feet may be either dactyls or spondees; the third is always a spondee, and the two last anapests. A _pentameter_ verse subjoined to a hexameter, constitutes what is called elegiac.

**PENTAM'ETER**, _adjective_ Having five metrical feet.
