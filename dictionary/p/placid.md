# Placid

**PLAC'ID**, _adjective_ \[Latin placidus, from placo, to appease.\]

**1.** Gentle; quiet; undisturbed; equable; as a _placid_ motion of the spirits.

**2.** Serene; mild; unruffled; indicating peace of mind; as a _placid_ countenance or smile.

**3.** Calm; tranquil; serene; not stormy; as a _placid_ sky.

**4.** Calm; quiet; unruffled; as a _placid_ stream.
