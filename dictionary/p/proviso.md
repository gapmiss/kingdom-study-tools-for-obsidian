# Proviso

**PROVI'SO**, _noun_ s as z. \[Latin provisus, ablative _proviso_ it being provided.\] An article or clause in any statute, agreement, contract, grant or other writing, by which a condition is introduced; a conditional stipulation that affects an agreement, contract, law, grant, etc. The charter of the bank contains a _proviso_ that the legislature may repeal it at their pleasure.
