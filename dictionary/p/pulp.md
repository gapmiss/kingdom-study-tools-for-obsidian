# Pulp

**PULP**, _noun_ \[Latin pulpa. This is probably allied to Latin puls, pulmentum; Gr. from softness.\]

**1.** A soft mass; in general.

**2.** The soft substance within a bone; marrow.

**3.** The soft, succulent part of fruit; as the _pulp_ of an orange.

**4.** The aril or exterior covering of a coffee-berry.

**PULP**, _verb transitive_ To deprive of the _pulp_ or integument, as the coffee-berry.

The other mode is to _pulp_ the coffee immediately as it comes from the tree. By a simple machine, a man will _pulp_ a bushel in a minute.
