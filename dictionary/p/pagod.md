# Pagod

**PA'GOD**

PAGO'DA, _noun_

**1.** A temple in the East Indies in which idols are worshiped.

**2.** An idol; an image of some supposed deity.

**PAGO'DA**, _noun_ A gold or silver coin current in Hindoostan, of different values in different parts of India, from $1.75 cts. to $2, or from 8 to 9s.sterling.
