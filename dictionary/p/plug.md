# Plug

**PLUG**, _noun_ A stopple; any piece of pointed wood or other substance used to stop a hole, but larger than a peg or spile.

Hawse-plug, in marine affairs, a _plug_ to stop a hawse-hole.

Shot-plug, a _plug_ to stop a breach made by a cannon ball in the side of a ship.

**PLUG**, _verb transitive_ To stop with a plug; to make tight by stopping a hole.
