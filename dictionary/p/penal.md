# Penal

**PE'NAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin poena; Gr. pain, punishment. See [[Pain]].\]

**1.** Enacting punishment; denouncing the punishment of offenses; as a _penal_ law or statute; the _penal_ code. _penal_ statutes must be construed strictly.

**2.** Inflicting punishment.

Adamantine chains and _penal_ fire.

**3.** Incurring punishment; subject to a penalty; as a _penal_ act of offense.
