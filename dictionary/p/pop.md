# Pop

**POP**, _noun_ A small smart quick sound or report.

**POP**, _verb intransitive_ To enter or issue forth with a quick, sudden motion.

I startled at his popping upon me unexpectedly.

**1.** To dart; to start from place to place suddenly.

**POP**, _verb transitive_ To thrust or push suddenly with a quick motion.

He popp'd a paper into his hand.

Did'st thou never pop

Thy head into a tinman's shop?

To _pop_ off, to thrust away; to shift off.

**POP**, _adverb_ Suddenly; with sudden entrance or appearance.
