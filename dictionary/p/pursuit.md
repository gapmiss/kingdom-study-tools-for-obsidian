# Pursuit

**PURSUIT**, _noun_ The act of following with a view to overtake; a following with haste, either for sport or in hostility; as the _pursuit_ of game; the _pursuit_ of an enemy.

**1.** A following with a view to reach, accomplish or obtain; endeavor to attain to or gain; as the _pursuit_ of knowledge; the _pursuit_ of happiness or pleasure; the _pursuit_ of power, of honor, of distinction, of a phantom.

**2.** Proceeding; course of business or occupation; continued employment with a view to some end; as mercantile pursuits; literary pursuits.

**3.** Prosecution; continuance of endeavor.
