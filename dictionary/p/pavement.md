# Pavement

**PA'VEMENT**, _noun_ \[Latin pavimentum.\] A floor or covering consisting of stones or bricks, laid on the earth in such a manner as to make a hard and convenient passage; as a _pavement_ of pebbles, of bricks, or of marble.

**PA'VEMENT**, _verb transitive_ To pave; to floor with stone or brick. \[Unusual.\]
