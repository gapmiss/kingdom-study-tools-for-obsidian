# Pungent

**PUN'GENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin pungens, pungo.\] Pricking; stimulating; as _pungent_ snuff.

The _pungent_ grains of titillating dust.

**1.** Acrid; affecting the tongue like small sharp points; as the sharp and _pungent_ taste of acids.

**2.** Piercing; sharp; as _pungent_ pains; _pungent_ grief.

**3.** Acrimonious; biting.
