# Pusillanimous

**PUSILLAN'IMOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Destitute of that strength and firmness of mind which constitute courage, bravery and fortitude; being of weak courage; mean spirited; cowardly; applied to persons; as a _pusillanimous_ prince.

**2.** Proceeding from weakness of mind or want of courage; feeble; as _pusillanimous_ counsels.
