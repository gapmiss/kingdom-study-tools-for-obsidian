# Polar

**PO'LAR**, _adjective_

**1.** Pertaining to the poles of the earth, north or south, or to the poles of artificial globes; situated near one of the poles; as _polar_ regions; _polar_ seas; _polar_ ice or climates.

**2.** Proceeding from one of the regions near the poles, as _polar_ winds.

**3.** Pertaining to the magnetic pole, or to the point to which the magnetic needle is directed.
