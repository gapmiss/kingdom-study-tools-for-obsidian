# Pregnant

**PREG'NANT**, _adjective_ \[Latin proegnans; supposed to be compounded of proe, before, and geno; Gr. to beget.\]

**1.** Being with young, as a female; breeding; teeming.

**2.** Fruitful; fertile; impregnating; as _pregnant_ streams.

**3.** Full of consequence; as a _pregnant_ instance of infatuation.

An egregious and _pregnant_ instance how far virtue surpasses ingenuity.

**4.** East to admit or receive.

**5.** Free; kind; ready; witty; apt. \[Not proper.\]

**6.** Plain; clear; evident; full. \[Not in use.\]
