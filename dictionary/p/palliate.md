# Palliate

**PAL'LIATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Low Latin pallio, from pallium, a cloke or robe.\]

**1.** To clothe.

**2.** To cover with excuse; to conceal the enormity of offenses by excuses and apologies; hence, to extenuate; to lessen; to soften by favorable representations; as, to _palliate_ faults, offenses, crimes or vices.

**3.** To reduce in violence; to mitigate; to lessen or abate; as, to _palliate_ a disease.

**PAL'LIATE**, _adjective_ Eased; mitigated. \[Not used.\]
