# Portray

**PORTRA'Y**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To paint or draw the likeness of any thing in colors; as, to _portray_ a king on horseback; to _portray_ a city or temple with a pencil or with chalk.

**2.** To describe in words. It belongs to the historian to _portray_ the character of Alexander of Russia. Homer portrays the character and achievements of his heroes in glowing colors.

**3.** To adorn with pictures; as shields portrayed.
