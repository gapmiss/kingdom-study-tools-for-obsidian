# Peaceable

**PE'ACEABLE**, _adjective_ Free from war, tumult or public commotion. We live in _peaceable_ times. The reformation was introduced in a _peaceable_ manner.

**1.** Free from private feuds or quarrels. The neighbors are _peaceable_ These men are _peaceable_

**2.** Quiet; undisturbed; not agitated with passion. His mind is very _peaceable_

**3.** Not violent, bloody or unnatural; as, to die a _peaceable_ death.
