# Plainness

**PLA'INNESS**, _noun_ Levelness; evenness or surface.

**1.** Want of ornament; want of artificial show.

So modest _plainness_ sets off sprightly wit.

**2.** Openness; rough, blunt or unrefined frankness.

Your _plainness_ and your shortness please me well.

**3.** Artlessness; simplicity; candor; as unthinking _plainness_

**4.** Clearness; openness; sincerity.

Seeing then we have such hope, we use great _plainness_ of speech. [[2 Corinthians 3#12|2 Corinthians 3:12]].
