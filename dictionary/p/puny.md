# Puny

**PU'NY**, _adjective_

**1.** Properly, young or younger; but in this sense not used.

**2.** Inferior; petty; of an under rate; small and feeble. This word generally includes the signification of both smallness and feebleness; as a _puny_ animal; a _puny_ subject; a _puny_ power; a _puny_ mind.

**PU'NY**, _noun_ A young inexperienced person; a novice.
