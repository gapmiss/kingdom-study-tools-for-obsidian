# Penitence

**PEN'ITENCE**

PENITENCY, _noun_ \[Latin poenitentia, from poeniteo, from poena, pain, punishment. See [[Pain]].\] Repentance; pain; sorrow or grief of heart for sins or offenses; contrition. Real _penitence_ springs from a conviction of guilt and ingratitude to God, and is followed by amendment of life.
