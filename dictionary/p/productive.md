# Productive

**PRODUC'TIVE**, _adjective_

**1.** Having the power of producing; as, _productive_ labor is that which increases the number or amount of products; opposed to unproductive labor. The labor of the farmer and mechanic is productive; the labor of officers and professional men is unproductive to the state. A tree which bears fruit, and the land which bears grass or grain, is _productive_

**2.** Fertile; producing good crops. We often denote by this word that land or plants yield large products.

**3.** Producing; bringing into being; causing to exist; efficient; as an age _productive_ of great men; a spirit _productive_ of heroic achievements.

This is turning nobility into a principle of virtue, and making it _productive_ of merit.

And kindle with thy own _productive_ fire.
