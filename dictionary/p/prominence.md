# Prominence

**PROM'INENCE**

PROM'INENCY, _noun_ \[Latin prominentia, from promineo; pro and minor, to menace, that is, to shoot forward.\] A standing out from the surface of something, or that which juts out; protuberance; as the _prominence_ of a joint; the _prominence_ of a rock or cliff; the _prominence_ of the nose. Small hills and knolls are prominences on the surface of the earth.
