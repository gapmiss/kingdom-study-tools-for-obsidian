# Prevalence

**PREV'ALENCE**

PREV'ALENCY, _noun_ Superior strength, influence or efficacy; most efficacious force in producing an effect.

The duke better knew what kind of arguments were of _prevalence_ with him.

**1.** Predominance; most general reception or practice; as the _prevalence_ of vice, or of corrupt maxims; the _prevalence_ of opinion or fashion.

**2.** Most general existence or extension; as, the _prevalence_ of a disease.

**3.** Success; as the _prevalence_ of prayer.
