# Pry

**PRY**, _verb intransitive_ \[a contracted word, the origin of which is not obvious.\]

To peep narrowly; to inspect closely; to attempt to discover something with scrutinizing curiosity, whether impertinently or not; as, to _pry_ into the mysteries of nature, or into the secrets of state.

Nor need we with a prying eye survey

The distant skies to find the milky way.

**PRY**, _noun_ Narrow inspection; impertinent peeping.

**PRY**, _verb transitive_ To raise or attempt to raise with a lever. This is the common popular pronunciation of prize, in America. The lever used is also called a _pry_
