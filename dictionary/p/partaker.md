# Partaker

**PARTA'KER**, _noun_ One who has or takes a part, share or portion in common with others; a sharer; a participator; usually followed by of.

If the Gentiles have been made partakers of their spiritual things-- [[Romans 15#27|Romans 15:27]].

Sometimes followed by in.

Wish me _partaker_ in thy happiness--

If we had been in the days of our fathers, we would not have been partakers with them in the blood of the prophets. [[Matthew 23#30|Matthew 23:30]].

**1.** An accomplice; an associate.

When thou sawest a thief, thou consentedst with him, and hast been _partaker_ with adulterers. [[Psalms 50#18|Psalms 50:18]].
