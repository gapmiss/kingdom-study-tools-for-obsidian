# Predominate

**PREDOM'INATE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin proe, before, and dominor, to rule, from dominus, lord.\] To prevail; to surpass in strength, influence or authority; to be superior; to have controlling influence. In some persons, the love of money predominates over all other passions; in others, ambition or the love of fame predominates; in most men, self-interest predominates over patriotism and philanthropy.

So much did love t'her executed lord

Predominate in this fair lady's heart.

The rays reflected least obliquely may _predominate_ over the rest.

**PREDOM'INATE**, _verb transitive_ To rule over.
