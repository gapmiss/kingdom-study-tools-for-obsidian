# Pulmonary

**PUL'MONARY**, _adjective_ \[Latin pulmonarius, from pulmo, the lungs, from pello, pulsus, pulso, to drive or beat.\] Pertaining to the lungs; affecting the lungs; as a _pulmonary_ disease or consumption; the _pulmonary_ artery.

**PUL'MONARY**, _noun_ \[Latin pulmonaria.\] A plant, lungwort.
