# Persist

**PERSIST**', _verb intransitive_ \[Latin persisto; per and sisto, to stand or be fixed.\]

To continue steadily and firmly in the pursuit of any business or course commenced; to persevere. \[Persist is nearly synonymous with persevere; but _persist_ frequently implies more obstinacy than persevere, particularly in that which is evil or injurious to others.\]

If they _persist_ in pointing their batteries against particular persons, no laws of war forbid the making reprisals.
