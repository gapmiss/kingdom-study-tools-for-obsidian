# Pureness

**PU'RENESS**, _noun_ Clearness; an unmixed state; separation or freedom from any heterogeneous or foreign matter; as the _pureness_ of water or other liquor; the _pureness_ of a metal; the _pureness_ of marl or clay; the _pureness_ of air.

**1.** Freedom from moral turpitude or guilt.

May we evermore serve thee in holiness and _pureness_ of living.

**2.** Simplicity; freedom from mixture or composition.

An essence eternal and spiritual, of absolute _pureness_ and simplicity.

**3.** Freedom from vicious or improper words, phrases or modes of speech; as _pureness_ of style.

Pure villenage, in the feudal law, is a tenure of lands by uncertain services at the will of the lord; opposed to privileged villenage.
