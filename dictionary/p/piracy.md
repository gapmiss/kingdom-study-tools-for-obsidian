# Piracy

**PI'RACY**, _noun_ \[Latin piratica, from Gr. to attempt, to dare, to enterprise, whence Latin periculum, experior; Eng. to fare.\]

**1.** The act, practice or crime of robbing on the high seas; the taking of property from others by open violence and without authority, on the sea; a crime that answers to robbery on land.

Other acts than robbery on the high seas, are declared by statute to be _piracy_ See Act of Congress, April 30, 1790.

**2.** The robbing of another by taking his writings.
