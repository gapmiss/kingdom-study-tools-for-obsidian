# Pitiful

**PIT'IFUL**, _adjective_ \[See [[Pity]].\] Full of pity; tender; compassionate; having a heart to feel sorrow and sympathy for the distressed. [[James 5#11|James 5:11]]. [[1 Peter 3#8|1 Peter 3:8]]. \[This is the proper sense of the word.\]

**1.** Miserable; moving compassion; as a sight most pitiful; a _pitiful_ condition.

**2.** To be pitied for its littleness or meanness; paltry; contemptible; despicable.

That's villainous, and shows a most _pitiful_ ambition in the fool that uses it.

**3.** Very small; insignificant.
