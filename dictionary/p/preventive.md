# Preventive

**PREVENT'IVE**, _adjective_ Tending to hinder; hindering the access of; as a medicine _preventive_ of disease.

**PREVENT'IVE**, _noun_ That which prevents; that which intercepts the access or approach of. Temperance and exercise are excellent preventives of debility and languor.

**1.** An antidote previously taken. A medicine may be taken as a _preventive_ of disease.
