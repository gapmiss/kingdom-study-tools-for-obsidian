# Pernicious

**PERNI'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin perniciosus, from pernicies; perneco, to kill; per and nex, necis, death.\]

**1.** Destructive; having the quality of killing, destroying or injuring; very injurious or mischievous. Food, drink or air may be _pernicious_ to life or health.

**2.** Destructive; tending to injure or destroy. Evil examples are _pernicious_ to morals. Intemperance is a _pernicious_ vice.

**3.** \[Latin pernix.\] Quick. \[Not used.\]
