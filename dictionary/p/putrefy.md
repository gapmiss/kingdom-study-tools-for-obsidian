# Putrefy

**PU'TREFY**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin putrefacio; putris, putrid, and facio, to make.\]

**1.** To cause to dissolve; to disorganize and reduce to the simple constituent elements, as animal or vegetable bodies; to cause to rot. Heat and moisture soon _putrefy_ dead flesh or vegetables.

**2.** To corrupt; to make foul; as, to _putrefy_ the air. \[Little used.\]

**3.** To make morbid, carious or gangrenous; as, to _putrefy_ an ulcer or wound.

**PU'TREFY**, _verb intransitive_ To dissolve and return to the original distinct elements, as animal and vegetable substances deprived of the living principle; to rot.
