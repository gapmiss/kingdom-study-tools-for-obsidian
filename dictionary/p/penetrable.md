# Penetrable

**PEN'ETRABLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin penetrabilis. See [[Penetrate]].\]

**1.** That may be penetrated, entered, or pierced by another body.

Let him try thy dart,

And pierce his only _penetrable_ part.

**2.** Susceptible of moral or intellectual impression.

_I am not made of stone,_

But _penetrable_ to your kind entreaties.
