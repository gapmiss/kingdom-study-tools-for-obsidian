# Penurious

**PENU'RIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin penuria, scarcity, want; Gr. poor; rare.\]

**1.** Excessively saving or sparing in the use of money; parsimonious to a fault; sordid; as a _penurious_ man. It expresses somewhat less than niggardly.

**2.** Scanty; affording little; as a _penurious_ spring.
