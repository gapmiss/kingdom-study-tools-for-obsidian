# Putrid

**PU'TRID**, _adjective_ \[Latin putridus, from putris, putreo.\]

**1.** In a state of dissolution or disorganization, as animal and vegetable bodies; corrupt; rotten; as _putrid_ flesh. Indicating a state of dissolution; tending to disorganize the substances composing the body; malignant; as a _putrid_ fever.

**2.** Proceeding from putrefaction or pertaining to it; as a _putrid_ scent.
