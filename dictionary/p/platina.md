# Platina

**PLATI'NA**

PLA'TING, _participle present tense_ Overlaying with plate or with a metal; beating into thin lamens.

**PLA'TING**, _noun_ The art or operation of covering any thing with plate or with a metal, particularly of overlaying a baser metal with a thin plate of silver. The coating of silver is soldered to the metal with tin or a mixture of three parts of silver with one of brass.
