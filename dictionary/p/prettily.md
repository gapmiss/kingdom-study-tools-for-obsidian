# Prettily

**PRETTILY**, _adverb_ prit'tily. \[from pretty.\] In a pretty manner; with neatness and taste; pleasingly; without magnificence or splendor; as a woman _prettily_ dressed; a parterre _prettily_ ornamented with flowers.

**1.** With decency, good manners and decorum without dignity.

Children kept out of ill company, take a price to behave themselves _prettily_
