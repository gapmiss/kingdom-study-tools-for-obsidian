# Plausible

**PLAUS'IBLE**, _adjective_ s as z. \[Latin plausibilis, from plaudo, to clap hands in token of approbation; laus, laudo; Eng. loud.\]

**1.** That may be applauded; that may gain favor or approbation; hence, superficially pleasing; apparently right; specious; popular; as a _plausible_ argument; a _plausible_ pretext; a _plausible_ doctrine.

**2.** Using specious arguments or discourse; as a _plausible_ man.
