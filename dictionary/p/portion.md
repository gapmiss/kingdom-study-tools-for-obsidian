# Portion

**PORTION**, _noun_ \[Latin portio, from partio, to divide, from pars, part. See [[Part]].\]

**1.** In general, a part of any thing separated from it. Hence,

**2.** A part, though not actually divided, but considered by itself.

These are parts of his ways, but how little a _portion_ is heard of him. [[Job 26#14|Job 26:14]].

**3.** A part assigned; an allotment; a dividend.

How small

A _portion_ to your share would fall.

The priests had a _portion_ assigned them of Pharaoh. [[Genesis 47#22|Genesis 47:22]].

**4.** The part of an estate given to a child or heir, or descending to him by law, and distributed to him in the settlement of the estate.

**5.** A wife's fortune.

**PORTION**, _verb transitive_ To divide; to parcel; to allot a share or shares.

And _portion_ to his tribes the wide domain.

**1.** To endow.

Him portion'd maids, apprentic'd orphans blest.
