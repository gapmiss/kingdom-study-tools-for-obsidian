# Propagator

**PROP'AGATOR**, _noun_ One that continues or multiplies his own species by generation.

**1.** One that continues or multiplies any species of animals or plants.

**2.** One that spreads or causes to circulate, as a report.

**3.** One that plants and establishes in a country destitute; as a _propagator_ of the gospel.

**4.** One that plants, originates or extends; one that promotes.
