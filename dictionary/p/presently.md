# Presently

**PRES'ENTLY**, _adverb_ s as z. At present; at this time.

The towns and forts you _presently_ have.

**1.** In a short time after; soon after.

Him therefore I hope to send _presently_ so soon as I shall see how it will go with me. [[Philippians 2#23|Philippians 2:23]].

And _presently_ the fig-tree withered away. [[Matthew 21#19|Matthew 21:19]].
