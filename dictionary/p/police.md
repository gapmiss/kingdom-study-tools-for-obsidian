# Police

**POLICE**, _noun_ \[Latin politia; Gr. city.\]

**1.** The government of a city or town; the administration of the laws and regulations of a city or incorporated town or borough; as the _police_ of London, of New York or Boston. The word is applied also to the government of all towns in New England which are made corporations by a general statute, for certain purposes.

**2.** The internal regulation and government of a kingdom or state.

**3.** The corporation or body of men governing a city.

**4.** In Scottish, the pleasure-ground about a gentleman's seat.
