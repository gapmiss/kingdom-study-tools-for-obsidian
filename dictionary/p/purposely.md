# Purposely

**PUR'POSELY**, _adverb_ By design; intentionally; with predetermination.

In composing this discourse, I _purposely_ declined all offensive and displeasing truths.

So much they scorn the crown, I _purposely_ declined all offensive and displeasing truths.

So much they scorn the crown, that if the throng

By chance go right, they _purposely_ go wrong.
