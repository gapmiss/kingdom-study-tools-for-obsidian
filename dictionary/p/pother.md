# Pother

**POTH'ER**, _noun_ \[This word is vulgarly pronounced bother. Its origin and affinities are not ascertained.\]

**1.** Bustle; confusion; tumult; flutter. \[Low.\]

**2.** A suffocating cloud.

**POTH'ER**, _verb intransitive_ To make a blustering ineffectual effort; to make a stir.

**POTH'ER**, _verb transitive_ To harass and perplex; to puzzle.
