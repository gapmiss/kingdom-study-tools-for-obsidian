# Participate

**PARTIC'IPATE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin participo; pars, part, and capio, to take.\]

**1.** To partake; to have a share in common with others. The heart of sensibility participates in the sufferings of a friend. It is sometimes followed by of.

He would _participate_ of their wants.

**2.** To have part of more things than one.

Few creatures _participate_ of the nature of plants and metals both.

**PARTIC'IPATE**, _verb intransitive_ To partake; to share; to receive a part of.
