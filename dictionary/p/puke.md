# Puke

**PUKE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Heb. to evacuate, to empty; Latin vacuo; to burst forth; Latin spuo, for spuco, with a prefix. The radical sense is to throw or drive.\] To vomit; to eject from the stomach.

**PUKE**, _noun_ A vomit; a medicine which excites vomiting.

**PUKE**, _adjective_ Of a color between black and russet.
