# Piragua

**PIRAGUA**, _noun_ pirau'gua. This word is variously written, periagua or pirogue. The former is the spelling of Washington and Jefferson; the latter of Charlevoix.\]

**1.** A canoe formed out of the truck of a tree, or two canoes united.

**2.** In modern usage in America, a narrow ferry boat carrying two masts and a leeboard.
