# Pose

**POSE**, _noun_ s as z. \[See the Verb.\] In heraldry, a lion, horse or other beast standing still, with all his feet on the ground.

**POSE**, _noun_ s as z. A stuffing of the head; catarrh.

**POSE**, _verb transitive_ s as z. \[Latin posui.\]

**1.** To puzzle, \[a word of the same origin; \] to set; to put to a stand or stop; to gravel.

Learning was pos'd, philosophy was set.

I design not to _pose_ them with those common enigmas of magnetism.

**2.** To puzzle or put to a stand by asking difficult questions; to set by questions; hence, to interrogate closely, or with a view to scrutiny.
