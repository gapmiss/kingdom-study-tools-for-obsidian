# Poetry

**PO'ETRY**, _noun_ \[Gr.\] Metrical composition; verse; as heroic poetry; dramatic poetry; lyric or Pindaric _poetry_

**1.** The art or practice of composing in verse. He excels in _poetry_

**2.** Poems; poetical composition. We take pleasure in reading _poetry_

**3.** This term is also applied to the language of excited imagination and feeling.
