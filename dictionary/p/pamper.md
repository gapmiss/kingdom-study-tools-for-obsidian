# Pamper

**PAM'PER**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin bibo.\]

**1.** To feed to the full; to glut; to saginate; to feed luxuriously; as, to _pamper_ the body or the appetite.

We are proud of a body fattening for worms and pampered for corruption and the grave.

**2.** To gratify to the full; to furnish with that which delights; as, to _pamper_ the imagination.
