# Pressing

**PRESS'ING**, _participle present tense_ Urging with force or weight; squeezing; constraining; crowding; embracing; distressing; forcing into service; rolling in a press.

**1.** _adjective_ Urgent; distressing.

**PRESS'ING**, _noun_ The act or operation of applying force to bodies. The _pressing_ of cloth is performed by means of the screw, or by a calendar.
