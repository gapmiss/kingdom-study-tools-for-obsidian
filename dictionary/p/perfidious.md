# Perfidious

**PERFID'IOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin perfidus; per and fidus, faithful. Per in this word signifies through, beyond, or by, aside.\]

**1.** Violating good faith or vows; false to trust or confidence reposed; treacherous; as a _perfidious_ agent; a _perfidious_ friend. \[See [[Perfidy]].\]

**2.** Proceeding from treachery, or consisting in breach of faith; as a _perfidious_ act.

**3.** Guilty of violated allegiance; as a _perfidious_ citizen; a man _perfidious_ to his country.
