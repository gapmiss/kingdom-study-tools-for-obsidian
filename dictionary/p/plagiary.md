# Plagiary

**PLA'GIARY**, _noun_ \[Latin plagium, a kidnapping, probably from plagoe, nets, toils, that which is layed or spread, from the root of Eng. lay. The Latin plaga, a stroke, is the same word differently applied, a laying on.\]

**1.** A thief in literature; one that purloins another's writings and offers them to the public as his own.

**2.** The crime of literary theft. \[Not used.\]

**PLA'GIARY**, _adjective_ Stealing men; kidnapping. \[Not used.\]

**1.** Practicing literary theft.
