# Poignant

**POIGNANT**, _adjective_ poin'ant. \[Latin pungere, pungo, to prick.\]

**1.** Sharp; stimulating the organs of taste; as _poignant_ sauce.

**2.** Pointed; keen; bitter; irritating; satirical; as _poignant_ wit.

**3.** Severe; piercing; very painful or acute; as _poignant_ pain or grief.
