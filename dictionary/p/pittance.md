# Pittance

**PIT'TANCE**, _noun_ \[The word signifies primarily, a portion of food allowed to a monk. The Spanish has pitar, to distribute allowances of meat, and pitancero, a person who distributes allowances, or a friar who lives on charity.\]

**1.** An allowance of meat in a monastery.

**2.** A very small portion allowed or assigned.

**3.** A very small quantity.
