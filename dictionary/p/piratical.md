# Piratical

**PIRAT'ICAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin piraticus.\] Robbing or plundering by open violence on the high seas; as a _piratical_ commander or ship.

**1.** Consisting in piracy; predatory; robbing; as a _piratical_ trade or occupation.

**2.** Practicing literary theft.

The errors of the press were multiplied by _piratical_ printers.
