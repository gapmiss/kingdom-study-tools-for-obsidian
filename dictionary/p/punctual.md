# Punctual

**PUNC'TUAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin punctum, a point.\]

**1.** Consisting in a point; as this _punctual_ spot. \[Little used.\]

**2.** Exact; observant of nice points; punctilious, particularly in observing time, appointments or promises. It is honorable in a man to be _punctual_ to appointments, or to appointed hours; it is just to be _punctual_ in paying debts.

**3.** Exact; as a _punctual_ correspondence between a prediction and an event.

**4.** Done at the exact time; as _punctual_ payment.
