# Parrot

**PAR'ROT**, _noun_

**1.** The name of fowls of the genus Psittacus, of numerous species. The bill is hooked and the upper mandible movable. The hooked bill of the _parrot_ is used in climbing. These fowls are found almost every where in tropical climates. They breed in hollow trees and subsist on fruits and seeds. They are also remarkable for the faculty of making indistinct articulations of words in imitation of the human voice.

**2.** A fish found among the Bahama isles, esteemed to be delicate food and remarkable for the richness of its colors.
