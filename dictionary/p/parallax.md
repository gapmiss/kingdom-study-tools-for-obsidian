# Parallax

**PAR'ALLAX**, _noun_ \[Gr. to vary, to decline or wander; beyond, and to change.\] In astronomy, the change of place in a heavenly body in consequence of being viewed from different points.

Diurnal _parallax_ the difference between the place of a celestial body, as seen from the surface, and from the center of the earth, at the same instant.

Annual _parallax_ the change of place in a heavenly body, in consequence of being viewed at opposite extremities of the earth's orbit.
