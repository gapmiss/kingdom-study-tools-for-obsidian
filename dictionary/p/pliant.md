# Pliant

**PLI'ANT**, _adjective_ That may be easily bent; readily yielding to force or pressure without breaking; flexible; flexile; lithe; limber; as a _pliant_ thread.

**1.** That may be easily formed or molded to a different shape; as _pliant_ wax.

**2.** Easily yielding to moral influence; easy to be persuaded; ductile.

The will was then more ductile and _pliant_ to right reason.
