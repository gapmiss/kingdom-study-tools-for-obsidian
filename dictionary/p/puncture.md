# Puncture

**PUNC'TURE**, _noun_ \[Latin punctura.\] The act of perforating with a pointed instrument; or a small hole made by it; as the _puncture_ of a nail, needle or pin.

A lion may perish by the _puncture_ of an asp.

**PUNC'TURE**, _verb intransitive_ To prick; to pierce with a small pointed instrument; as, to _puncture_ the skin.
