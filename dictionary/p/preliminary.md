# Preliminary

**PRELIM'INARY**, _adjective_ \[Latin proe, before, and limen, threshhold or limit.\]

Introductory; previous; proemial; that precedes the main discourse or business; as _preliminary_ observations to a discourse or book; _preliminary_ articles to a treaty; _preliminary_ measures.

**PRELIM'INARY**, _noun_ That which precedes the main discourse, work, design or business; something previous or preparatory; as the preliminaries to a negotiation or treaty; the preliminaries to a combat. The parties met to settle the preliminaries.
