# Prophet

**PROPH'ET**, _noun_ \[Latin propheta.\]

**1.** One that foretells future events; a predicter; a foreteller.

**2.** In Scripture, a person illuminated, inspired or instructed by God to announce future events; as Moses, Elijah, David, Isaiah, etc.

**3.** An interpreter; one that explains or communicates sentiments. [[Exodus 7#1|Exodus 7:1]].

**4.** One who pretends to foretell; an imposter; as a false _prophet_ [[Acts 13#6|Acts 13:6]].

_School of the prophets_, among the Israelites, a school or college in which young men were educated and qualified for public teachers. These students were called _sons of the prophets_.
