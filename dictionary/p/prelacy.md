# Prelacy

**PRE'LACY**, _noun_ \[from prelate.\] The office or dignity of a prelate.

Prelacies may be termed the greater benefices.

**1.** Episcopacy; the order of bishops.

How many are there that call themselves protestants, who put _prelacy_ and popery together as terms convertible?

**2.** Bishops, collectively.
