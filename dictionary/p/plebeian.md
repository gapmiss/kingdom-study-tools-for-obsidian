# Plebeian

**PLEBE'IAN**, _adjective_ \[Latin plebeius, from plebs, the common people.\]

**1.** Pertaining to the common people; vulgar; as _plebeian_ minds; _plebeian_ sports.

**2.** Consisting of common people; as a _plebeian_ throng.

**PLEBE'IAN**, _noun_ One of the common people or lower ranks of men. \[Usually applied to the common people of ancient Rome.\]
