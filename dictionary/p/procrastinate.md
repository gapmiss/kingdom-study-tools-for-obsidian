# Procrastinate

**PROCRAS'TINATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin procrastinor; pro and crastinus; cras, to-morrow.\] To put off from day to day; to delay; to defer to a future time; as, to _procrastinate_ repentance.

**PROCRAS'TINATE**, _verb intransitive_ To delay; to be dilatory.

I _procrastinate_ more than I did twenty years ago.
