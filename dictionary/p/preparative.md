# Preparative

**PREPAR'ATIVE**,_adjective_ Tending to prepare or make ready; having the power of preparing, qualifying or fitting for any thing; preparatory.

He spent much time in quest of knowledge _preparative_ to this work.

**PREPAR'ATIVE**, _noun_ That which has the power of preparing or previously fitting for a purpose; that which prepares.

Resolvedness in sin can with no reason be imagined a _preparative_ to remission.

**1.** That which is done to prevent an evil or secure some good.

The miseries we suffer may be _preparative_ of future blessings.

**2.** Preparation; as, to make the necessary preparatives for a voyage.
