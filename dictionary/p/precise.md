# Precise

**PRECI'SE**, _adjective_ \[Latin proecisus, from proecido, to cut off; proe and coedo; literally, cut or pared away, that is, pared to smoothness or exactness.\]

**1.** Exact; nice; definite; having determinate limitations; not loose, vague, uncertain or equivocal; as _precise_ rules of morality; _precise_ directions for life and conduct.

The law in this point is _precise_

For the hour precise

Exacts our parting.

**2.** Formal; superstitiously exact; excessively nice; punctilious in conduct or ceremony.
