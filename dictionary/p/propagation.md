# Propagation

**PROPAGA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin propagatio.\]

**1.** The act of propagating; the continuance or multiplication of the kind by generation or successive production; as the _propagation_ of animals or plants.

There is not in nature any spontaneous generation, but all come by _propagation_

**2.** The spreading or extension of any thing; as the _propagation_ of sound or of reports.

**3.** The spreading of any thing by planting and establishing in places before destitute; as the _propagation_ of the gospel among pagans.

**4.** A forward or promotion.
