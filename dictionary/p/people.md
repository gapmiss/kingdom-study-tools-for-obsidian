# People

**PEOPLE**, _noun_ \[Latin populus.\]

**1.** The body of persons who compose a community, town, city or nation. We say, the _people_ of a town; the _people_ of London or Paris; the English _people_ In this sense, the word is not used in the plural, but it comprehends all classes of inhabitants, considered as a collective body, or any portion of the inhabitants of a city or country.

**2.** The vulgar; the mass of illiterate persons.

The knowing artist may judge better than the _people_

**3.** The commonalty, as distinct from men of rank.

_Myself shall mount the rostrum in his favor,_

And strive to gain his pardon from the _people_
