# Pitching

**PITCH'ING**, _participle present tense_ Setting; planting or fixing; throwing headlong; plunging; daubing with pitch; setting, as a tune.

**1.** _adjective_ Declivous; descending; sloping; as a hill.

**PITCH'ING**, _noun_ In navigation, the rising and falling of the head and stern of a ship, as she moves over waves; or the vertical vibration of a ship about her center of gravity.

**PITCH'-ORE**, _noun_ Pitch-blend, an ore of uranium.
