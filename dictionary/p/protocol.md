# Protocol

**PRO'TOCOL**, _noun_ \[Low Latin protocollum; Gr. first, and glue; so called perhaps from the gluing together of pieces of paper, or from the spreading of it on tablets. It was formerly the upper part of a leaf of a book on which the title or name was written.\]

**1.** The original copy of any writing. \[Not now used.\]

**2.** A record or registry.
