# Peaceful

**PE'ACEFUL**, _adjective_ Quiet; undisturbed; not in a state of war or commotion; as a _peaceful_ time; a _peaceful_ country.

**1.** Pacific; mild; calm; as _peaceful_ words; a _peaceful_ temper.

**2.** Removed from noise or tumult; still; undisturbed; as the _peaceful_ cottage; the _peaceful_ scenes of rural life.
