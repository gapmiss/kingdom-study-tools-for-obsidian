# Peripatetic

**PERIPATET'IC**, _adjective_ \[Gr. to walk about.\] Pertaining to Aristotle's system of philosophy, or to the sect of his followers.

**PERIPATET'IC**, _noun_ A follower of Aristotle, so called because the founders of his philosophy taught, or his followers disputed questions, walking in the Lyceum at Athens.

**1.** It is ludicrously applied to one who is obliged to walk, or cannot afford to ride.
