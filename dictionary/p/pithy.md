# Pithy

**PITH'Y**, _adjective_ Consisting of pith; containing pith; abounding with pith; as a _pithy_ substance; a _pithy_ stem.

**1.** Containing concentrated force; forcible; energetic; as a _pithy_ word or expression.

This _pithy_ speech prevailed and all agreed.

**2.** Uttering energetic words or expressions.

In all these, Goodman Fact was very short, but _pithy_
