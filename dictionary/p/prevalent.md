# Prevalent

**PREV'ALENT**, _adjective_ Gaining advantage or superiority; victorious.

Brennus told the Roman embassadors, that _prevalent_ arms were as good as any title.

**1.** Powerful; efficacious; successful; as _prevalent_ supplications.

**2.** Predominant; most generally received or current; as a _prevalent_ opinion.

**3.** Predominant; most general; extensively existing; as a _prevalent_ disease.
