# Probe

**PROBE**, _noun_ \[Latin probo.\] A surgeon's instrument for examining the depth or other circumstances of a wound, ulcer or cavity, or the direction of a sinus, or for searching for stones in the bladder and the like.

**PROBE**, _verb transitive_ To examine a wound, ulcer or some cavity of the body, by the use of an instrument thrust into the part.

**1.** To search to the bottom; to scrutinize; to examine thoroughly into causes and circumstances.
