# Panel

**PAN'EL**, _noun_

**1.** A square piece of board, or other piece somewhat similar inserted between other pieces; as the _panel_ of a door.

**2.** A piece of parchment or schedule, containing the names of persons summoned by the sheriff. Hence more generally,

**3.** The whole jury.

**PAN'EL**, _verb transitive_ To form with panels; as, to _panel_ a wainscot.
