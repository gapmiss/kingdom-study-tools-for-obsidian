# Perpetuate

**PERPET'UATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin perpetuo.\] To make perpetual; to eternize.

**1.** To cause to endure or to be continued indefinitely; to preserve from extinction or oblivion; as, to _perpetuate_ the remembrance of a great event or of an illustrious character. The monument in London perpetuates the remembrance of the conflagration in 1666. Medals may _perpetuate_ the glories of a prince.

**2.** To continue by repetition without limitation.
