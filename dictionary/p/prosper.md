# Prosper

**PROS'PER**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin prospero, from prosperus, from the Gr. to carry to or toward; to bear.\] To favor; to render successful.

All things concur to _prosper_ our design.

**PROS'PER**, _verb intransitive_ To be successful; to succeed.

The Lord made all that he did to _prosper_ in his hand. [[Genesis 39#3|Genesis 39:3]].

He that covereth his sins, shall not _prosper_ [[Proverbs 28#13|Proverbs 28:13]].

**1.** To grow or increase; to thrive; to make gain; as, to _prosper_ in business. Our agriculture, commerce and manufactures now _prosper_
