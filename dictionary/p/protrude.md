# Protrude

**PROTRU'DE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin protrudo; pro and trudo, to thrust. See [[Thrust]].\]

**1.** To thrust forward; to drive or force along; as food protruded from the stomach into the intestine.

**2.** To thrust out, as from confinement. The contents of the abdomen are protruded in hernia.

**PROTRU'DE**, _verb intransitive_ To shoot forward; to be thrust forward.

The parts _protrude_ beyond the skin.
