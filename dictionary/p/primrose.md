# Primrose

**PRIM'ROSE**, _noun_ s as z. \[Latin primula veris; primus, first, and rose; literally, the first or an early rose in spring.\]

A plant of the genus Primula, of several varieties, as the white, the red, the yellow flowered, the cowslip, etc. Shakespeare uses the word for gay or flowery; as the _primrose_ way.
