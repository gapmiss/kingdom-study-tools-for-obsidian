# Plenteous

**PLEN'TEOUS**, _adjective_ \[from plenty.\] Abundant; copious; plentiful; sufficient for every purpose; as a _plenteous_ supply of provisions; a _plenteous_ crop.

**1.** Yielding abundance; as a _plenteous_ fountain.

The seven _plenteous_ years. [[Genesis 41#34|Genesis 41:34]].

**2.** Having an abundance.

The Lord shall make thee _plenteous_ in goods. [[Deuteronomy 28#11|Deuteronomy 28:11]].

**3.** Possessing in abundance and ready to bestow liberally. [[Psalms 86#5|Psalms 86:5]].

\[This word is less used than plentiful.\]
