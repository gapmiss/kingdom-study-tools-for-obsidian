# Prominent

**PROM'INENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin prominens.\] Standing out beyond the line or surface of something; jutting; protuberant; in high relief; as a _prominent_ figure on a vase.

**1.** Full; large; as a _prominent_ eye.

**2.** Eminent; distinguished above others; as a _prominent_ character.

**3.** Principal; most visible or striking to the eye; conspicuous. The figure of a man or of a building holds a _prominent_ place in a picture.
