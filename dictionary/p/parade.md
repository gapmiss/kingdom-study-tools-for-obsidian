# Parade

**PARA'DE**,_noun_ \[Latin paro.\]

**1.** In military affairs, the place where troops assemble for exercise, mounting guard or other purpose.

**2.** Show; ostentation; display.

Be rich, but of your wealth make no _parade_

**3.** Pompous procession.

_The rites performed, the parson paid,_

In state return'd the grand _parade_
