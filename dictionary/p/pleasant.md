# Pleasant

**PLEASANT**, _adjective_ plez'ant.

**1.** Pleasing; agreeable; grateful to the mind or to the senses; as a _pleasant_ ride; a _pleasant_ voyage; a _pleasant_ view. Light is _pleasant_ to the eye; an orange is _pleasant_ to the taste; harmony is _pleasant_ to the ear; a rose is _pleasant_ to the smell.

How good and how _pleasant_ it is for brethren to dwell together in unity! [[Psalms 133#1|Psalms 133:1]].

**2.** Cheerful; enlivening; as _pleasant_ society or company.

**3.** Gay; lively; humorous; sportive; as a _pleasant_ companion.

**4.** Trifling; adapted rather to mirth than use.

**5.** Giving pleasure; gratifying.

This word expresses less than delightful, to the mind, and delicious, to the taste.
