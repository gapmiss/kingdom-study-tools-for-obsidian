# Preferment

**PREFER'MENT**, _noun_ Advancement to a higher office, dignity or station. Change of manners and even of character often follows _preferment_ A profligate life should be considered a disqualification for _preferment_ no less than want of ability.

**1.** Superior place or office. All preferments should be given to competent men.

**2.** Preference. \[Not used.
