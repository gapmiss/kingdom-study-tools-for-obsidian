# Priming

**PRI'MING**, _participle present tense_ Putting powder in the pan of a fire-arm.

**1.** Laying on the first color.

**PRI'MING**, _noun_ The powder in the pan of a gun, or laid along the channel of a cannon for conveying fire to the charge.

**1.** Among painters, the first color laid on canvas or on a building, etc.
