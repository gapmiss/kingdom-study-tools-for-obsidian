# Parol

**PAROL**

PARO'LE, _noun_ \[Latin parabola.\]

**1.** Properly, a word; hence, in a legal sense, words or oral declaration; word of mouth. Formerly, conveyances were made by _parol_ or word of mouth only.

**2.** Pleadings in a suit; as anciently all pleadings were viva voce or ore tenus.

The _parol_ may demur.
