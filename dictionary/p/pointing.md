# Pointing

**POINT'ING**, _participle present tense_ Directing the finger; showing; directing.

**1.** Marking with points; as a writing.

**2.** Filling the joints and crevices of a wall with mortar or cement.

**POINT'ING**, _noun_ The art of making the divisions of a writing; punctuation.

**1.** The state of being pointed with marks or points.
