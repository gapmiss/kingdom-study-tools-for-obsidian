# Permanence

**PER'MANENCE**

PER'MANENCY, _noun_ \[See [[Permanent]].\] Continuance in the same state, or without a change that destroys the form or nature of a thing; duration; fixedness; as the _permanence_ of a government or state; the _permanence_ of institutions or of a system of principles.

**1.** Continuance in the same place or at rest.
