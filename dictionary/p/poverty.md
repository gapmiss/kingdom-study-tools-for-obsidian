# Poverty

**POV'ERTY**, _noun_ \[Latin paupertas. See [[Poor]].\]

**1.** Destitution of property; indigence; want of convenient means of subsistence. The consequence of _poverty_ is dependence.

The drunkard and the glutton shall come to _poverty_ [[Proverbs 23#21|Proverbs 23:21]].

**2.** Barrenness of sentiment or ornament; defect; as the _poverty_ of a composition.

**3.** Want; defect of words; as the _poverty_ of language.
