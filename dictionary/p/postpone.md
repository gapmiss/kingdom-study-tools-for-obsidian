# Postpone

**POSTPO'NE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin postpono; post, after, and pono, to put.\]

**1.** To put off; to defer to a future or later time; to delay; as, to _postpone_ the consideration of a bill or question to the afternoon, or to the following day.

**2.** To set below something else in value or importance.

All other considerations should give way and be postponed to this.
