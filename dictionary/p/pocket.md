# Pocket

**POCK'ET**, _noun_

**1.** A small bag inserted in a garment for carrying small articles.

**2.** A small bag or net to receive the balls in billiards.

**3.** A certain quantity; as a _pocket_ of hops, as in other cases we use sack. \[Not used in America.\]

**POCK'ET**, _verb transitive_ To put or conceal in the pocket; as, to _pocket_ a penknife.

**1.** To take clandestinely.

To _pocket_ an insult or affront, to receive it without resenting it, or at least without seeking redress. \[In popular use.\]
