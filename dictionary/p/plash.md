# Plash

**PLASH**, _noun_ \[Gr. superabundant moisture.\]

**1.** A small collection of standing water; a puddle.

**2.** The branch of a tree partly cut or lopped and bound to other branches.

**PLASH**, _verb intransitive_ To dabble in water; usually splash.

**PLASH**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin plico, to fold.\] To interweave branches; as, to _plash_ a hedge or quicksets. \[In New England, to splice.\]
