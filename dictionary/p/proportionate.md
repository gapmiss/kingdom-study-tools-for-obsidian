# Proportionate

**PROPORTIONATE**, _adjective_ Adjusted to something else according to a certain rate or comparative relation; proportional.

The connection between the end and means is _proportionate_

Punishment should be _proportionate_ to the transgression.

**PROPORTIONATE**, _verb transitive_ To proportion; to make proportional; to adjust according to a settled rate or to due comparative relation; as, to _proportionate_ punishments to crimes. \[This verb is less used than proportion.\]
