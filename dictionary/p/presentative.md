# Presentative

**PRESENT'ATIVE**, _adjective_ In ecclesiastical affairs, that has the right of presentation, or offering a clerk to the bishop for institution. Advowsons are _presentative_ collative or donative.

An advowson _presentative_ is where the patron hath a right of presentation to the bishop or ordinary.

**1.** That admits the presentation of a clerk; as a _presentative_ parsonage.
