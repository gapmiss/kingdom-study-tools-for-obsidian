# Preponderance

**PREPOND'ERANCE**

PREPOND'ERANCY, _noun_ \[See [[Preponderate]].\]

**1.** An outweighing; superiority of weight. The least _preponderance_ of weight on one side of a ship or boat will make it incline or heel.

**2.** Superiority of power, force or weight; in a figurative sense; as a _preponderance_ of evidence.
