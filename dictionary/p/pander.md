# Pander

**PAN'DER**, _noun_ \[Latin mango.\] A pimp, a procurer; a male bawd; a mean profligate wretch who caters for the lust of others.

**PAN'DER**, _verb transitive_ To pimp; to procure lewd women for others.

**PAN'DER**, _verb intransitive_ To act as agent for the lusts of others.

**1.** To be subservient to lust or passion.
