# Pigeon

**PIG'EON**, _noun_ A fowl of the genus Columba, of several species, as the stock dove, the ring dove, the turtle dove, and the migratory or wild _pigeon_ of America. The domestic _pigeon_ breeds in a box, often attached to a building, called a dovecot or pigeon-house. The wild _pigeon_ builds a nest on a tree in the forest.
