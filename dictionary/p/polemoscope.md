# Polemoscope

**POLEM'OSCOPE**, _noun_ \[Gr. war, and to view.\] An oblique perspective glass contrived for seeing objects that do not lie directly before the eye. It consists of a concave glass placed near a plane mirror in the end of a short round tube, and a convex glass in a hole in the side of the tube. It is called opera-glass, or diagonal opera-glass.
