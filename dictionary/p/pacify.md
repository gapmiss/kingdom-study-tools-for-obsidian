# Pacify

**PACIFY**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin , peace, and to make.\]

**1.** To appease, as wrath or other violent passion or appetite; to calm; to still; to quiet; to allay agitation or excitement; as, to _pacify_ a man when angry, or to _pacify_ his wrath or rage; the word being applied both to the person and to the passion. So we say, to _pacify_ hunger, to _pacify_ importunate demands.

**2.** To restore peace to; to tranquilize; as, to _pacify_ countries in contention.
