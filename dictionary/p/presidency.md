# Presidency

**PRES'IDENCY**, _noun_ Superintendence; inspection and care.

**1.** The office of president. Washington was elected to the _presidency_ of the United States by a unanimous vote of the electors.

**2.** The term during which a president holds his office. President J. Adams died during the _presidency_ of his son.

**3.** The jurisdiction of a president; as in the British dominions in the East Indies.

**4.** The family or suit of a president.

A worthy clergyman belonging to the _presidency_ of Fort St. George.
