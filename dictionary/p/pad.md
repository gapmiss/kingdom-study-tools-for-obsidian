# Pad

**PAD**, _noun_

**1.** A foot path; a road. \[Not now used.\]

**2.** An easy paced horse.

**3.** A robber that infests the road on foot; usually called a foot- _pad_

**PAD**, _noun_ A soft saddle, cushion or bolster stuffed with straw, hair or other soft substance.

**PAD**, _verb intransitive_ To travel slowly.

**1.** To rob on foot.

**2.** To beat a way smooth and level.
