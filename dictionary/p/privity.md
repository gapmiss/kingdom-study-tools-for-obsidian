# Privity

**PRIV'ITY**, _noun_ Privacy; secrecy; confidence.

I will to you, in _privity_ discover the drift of my purpose. \[Little used.\]

**1.** Private knowledge; joint knowledge with another of a private concern, which is often supposed to imply consent or concurrence.

All the doors were laid open for his departure, not without the _privity_ of the prince of Orange.

But it is usual to say, 'a thing is done with his _privity_ and consent; ' in which phrase, _privity_ signifies merely private knowledge.

**2.** Privities, in the plural, secret parts; the parts which modesty requires to be concealed.
