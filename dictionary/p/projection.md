# Projection

**PROJEC'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin projectio.\] The act of throwing or shooting forward.

**1.** A jutting out; extension beyond something else.

**2.** The act of scheming; plan; scheme; design of something to be executed.

**3.** Plan; delineation; the representation of something; as the _projection_ of the sphere, is a representation of the circles on the surface of the sphere. There are three principal points of projection; the stereographic, in which the eye is supposed to be placed on the surface of the sphere; the orthographic, in which the eye is supposed to be at an infinite distance; and the gnomonic, in which the eye is placed in the center of the sphere.

In perspective, _projection_ denotes the appearance or representation of an object on the perspective plane.

**4.** In alchimy, the casting of a certain powder, called powder of _projection_ into a crucible or other vessel full of some prepared metal or other matter, which is to be thereby transmuted into gold.
