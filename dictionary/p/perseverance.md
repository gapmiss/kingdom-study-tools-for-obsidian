# Perseverance

**PERSEVE'RANCE**, _noun_ \[Latin perseverantia. See [[Persevere]].\]

**1.** Persistence in any thing undertaken; continued pursuit or prosecution of any business or enterprise begun; applied alike to good or evil.

Perseverance keeps honor bright.

Patience and _perseverance_ overcome the greatest difficulties.

**2.** In theology, continuance in a state of grace to a state of glory; sometimes called final _perseverance_
