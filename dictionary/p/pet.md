# Pet

**PET**, _noun_ \[This word may be contracted from petulant, or belong to the root of that word. Peevish, which is evidently a contracted word, may be from the same root.\]

A slight fit of peevishness or fretful discontent.

Life given for noble purposes must not be thrown away in a _pet_ nor whined away in love.

**PET**, _noun_ \[formerly peat. Latin peto.\]

**1.** A cade lamb; a lamb brought up by hand.

**2.** A fondling; any little animal fondled and indulged.

**PET**, _verb transitive_ To treat as a pet; to fondle; to indulge.
