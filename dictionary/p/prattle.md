# Prattle

**PRAT'TLE**, _verb intransitive_ \[dim. of prate.\] To talk much and idly; to be loquacious on trifling subjects.

This word is particularly applied to the talk of children.

**PRAT'TLE**, _noun_ Trifling talk; loquacity on trivial subjects.

Mere _prattle_ without practice,

Is all his soldiership.
