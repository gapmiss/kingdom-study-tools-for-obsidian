# Personify

**PERSON'IFY**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin persona and facio.\] To give animation to inanimate objects; to ascribe to an inanimate being the sentiments, actions or language of a rational being or person, or to represent an inanimate being with the affections and actions of a person. Thus we say, the plants thirst for rain.

The trees said to the fig-tree, come thou, and reign over us. [[Judges 9#1|Judges 9:1]].
