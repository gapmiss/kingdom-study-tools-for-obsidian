# Procession

**PROCES'SION**, _noun_ \[Latin processio. See [[Proceed]].\]

**1.** The act of proceeding or issuing.

**2.** A train of persons walking, or riding on horseback or in vehicles, in a formal march, or moving with ceremonious solemnity; as a _procession_ of clergy and people in the Romish church; a triumphal procession; a funeral _procession_

Him all his train

Follow'd in bright _procession_
