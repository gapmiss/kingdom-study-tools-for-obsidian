# Prepense

**PREPENSE**, _adjective_ prepens'. \[Latin proepensus, proependeo; proe and pendeo, to incline to hand down.\]

Preconceived; premeditated; aforethought.

Malice _prepense_ is necessary to constitute murder.

**PREPENSE**, _verb transitive_ prepens'. \[supra.\]

To weigh or consider beforehand. \[Not used.\]

**PREPENSE**, _verb intransitive_ prepens'. To deliberate beforehand. \[Not used.\]
