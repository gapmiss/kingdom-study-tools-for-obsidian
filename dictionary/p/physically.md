# Physically

**PHYS'ICALLY**, _adverb_ According to nature; by natural power or the operation of natural laws in the material system of things, as distinguished from moral power or influence. We suppose perpetual motion to by _physically_ impossible.

I am not now treating _physically_ of light or colors.

**1.** According to the art or rules of medicine.

He that lives _physically_ must live miserably.
