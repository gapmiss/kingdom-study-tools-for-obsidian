# Prone

**PRONE**, _adjective_ \[Latin pronus.\] Bending forward; inclined; not erect.

**1.** Lying with the face downward; contrary to supine.

**2.** Headlong; precipitous; inclining in descent.

Down thither _prone_ in flight.

**3.** Sloping; declivous; inclined.

Since the floods demand

For their descent, a _prone_ and sinking land.

**4.** Inclined; propense; disposed; applied to the mind or affections, usually in an ill sense; as men _prone_ to evil, _prone_ to strife, _prone_ to intemperance, _prone_ to deny the truth, prone to change.
