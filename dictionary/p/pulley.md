# Pulley

**PULL'EY**, _noun_ _plural_ pulleys. \[Latin polus; Gr. to turn.\]

A small wheel turning on a pin in a block, with a furrow or groove in which runs the rope that turns it.

The _pulley_ is one of the mechanical powers. The word is used also in the general sense of tackle, to denote all parts of the machine for raising weights, of which the _pulley_ forms a part.
