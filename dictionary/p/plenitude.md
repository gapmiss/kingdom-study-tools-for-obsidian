# Plenitude

**PLEN'ITUDE**, _noun_ \[Latin plenitudo, from plenus, full.\]

Fullness; as the _plenitude_ of space.

**1.** Repletion; animal fullness; plethora; redundancy of blood and humors in the animal body.

**2.** Fullness; complete competence; as the _plenitude_ of the pope's power.

**3.** Completeness; as the _plenitude_ of a man's fame.
