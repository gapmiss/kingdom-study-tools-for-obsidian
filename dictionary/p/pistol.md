# Pistol

**PIS'TOL**, _noun_ A small fire-arm, or the smallest fire-arm used, differing from a musket chiefly in size. Pistols are of different lengths, and borne by horsemen in cases at the saddle bow, or by a girdle. Small pistols are carried in the pocket.

**PIS'TOL**, _verb transitive_ To shoot with a _pistol_
