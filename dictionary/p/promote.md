# Promote

**PROMO'TE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin promotus, promoveo, to move forward; pro and moveo, to move.\]

**1.** To forward; to advance; to contribute to the growth, enlargement or excellence of any thing valuable, or to the increase of any thing evil; as, to _promote_ learning, knowledge, virtue or religion; to _promote_ the interest of commerce or agriculture; to _promote_ the arts; to _promote_ civilization or refinement; to _promote_ the propagation of the gospel; to _promote_ vice and disorder.

**2.** To excite; as, to _promote_ mutiny.

**3.** To exalt; to elevate; to raise; to prefer in rank or honor.

I will _promote_ thee to very great honors. [[Numbers 22#17|Numbers 22:17]].

Exalt her, and she shall _promote_ thee. [[Proverbs 4#8|Proverbs 4:8]].
