# Protract

**PROTRACT**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin protractus, from pro and traho, to draw.\]

**1.** To draw out or lengthen in time; to continue; to prolong; as, to _protract_ an argument; to _protract_ a discussion; to _protract_ a war or a negotiation.

**2.** To delay; to defer; to put off to a distant time; as, to _protract_ the decision of a question; to _protract_ the final issue.

**PROTRACT'**, _noun_ Tedious continuance. \[Not used.\]
