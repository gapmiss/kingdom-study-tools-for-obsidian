# Posthumous

**POST'HUMOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin post, after, and humus, earth; humatus, buried.\]

**1.** Born after the death of the father, or taken from the dead body of the mother; as a _posthumous_ son or daughter.

**2.** Published after the death of the author; as _posthumous_ works.

**3.** Being after one's decease; as a _posthumous_ character.
