# Pelagic

**PEL'AGIC**, _adjective_ \[Latin pelagus, the sea.\] Pertaining to the sea; as pelagian shells.

**PELA'GIAN**, _noun_ \[from Pelagius, a native of Great Britain, who lived in the fourth century.\] A follower of Pelagius, a monk of Banchor or Bangor, who denied original sin, and asserted the doctrine of free will and the merit of good works.

**PELA'GIAN**, _adjective_ Pertaining to Pelagius and his doctrines.
