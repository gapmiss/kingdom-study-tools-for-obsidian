# Phlegmatic

**PHLEGMAT'IC**, _adjective_ \[Gr.\]

**1.** Abounding in phlegm; as _phlegmatic_ humors; a _phlegmatic_ constitution.

**2.** Generating phlegm; as _phlegmatic_ meat.

**3.** Watery.

**4.** Cold; dull; sluggish; heavy; not easily excited into action or passion; as a _phlegmatic_ temper or temperament.
