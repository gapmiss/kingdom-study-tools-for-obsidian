# Precious

**PRE'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin pretiosus, from pretium, price. See [[Praise]].\]

**1.** Of great price; costly; as a _precious_ stone.

**2.** Of great value or worth; very valuable.

She is more _precious_ than rubies. [[Proverbs 3#15|Proverbs 3:15]].

**3.** Highly valued; much esteemed.

The word of the Lord was _precious_ in those days; there was no open vision. [[1 Samuel 3#1|1 Samuel 3:1]].

**4.** Worthless; in irony and contempt.

Precious metals, gold and silver, so called on account of their value.
