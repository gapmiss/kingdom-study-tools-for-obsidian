# Ponderous

**PON'DEROUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin ponderosus.\]

**1.** Very heavy; weighty; as a _ponderous_ shield; a _ponderous_ load.

**2.** Important; momentous; as a _ponderous_ project. \[This application of the word is unusual.\]

**3.** Forcible; strongly impulsive; as a motion vehement or ponderous; a _ponderous_ blow.

Ponderous spar, heavy spar, or baryte.
