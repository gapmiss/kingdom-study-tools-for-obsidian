# Periwig

**PER'IWIG**, _noun_ A small wig; a kind of close cap formed by an intertexture of false hair, worn by men for ornament or to conceal baldness. Periwigs were in fashion in the days of Addison.

**PER'IWIG**, _verb transitive_ To dress with a _periwig_ or with false hair, or with any thing in like form.
