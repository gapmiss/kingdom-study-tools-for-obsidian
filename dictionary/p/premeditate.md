# Premeditate

**PREMED'ITATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin proemeditor; proe, before, and meditor, to mediate.\] To think on and revolve in the mind beforehand; to contrive and design previously; as, to _premeditate_ theft or robbery.

With words premeditated thus he said.

**PREMED'ITATE**, _verb intransitive_ To think, consider or revolve in the mind beforehand; to deliberate; to have formed in the mind by previous thought or meditation.

**PREMED'ITATE**, _adjective_ Contrived by previous meditation.
