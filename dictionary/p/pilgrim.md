# Pilgrim

**PIL'GRIM**, _noun_ \[Latin peregrinus. Gu. Latin peragro, to wander, palor.\]

**1.** A wanderer; a traveler; particularly, one that travels to a distance from his own country to visit a holy place, or to pay his devotion to the remains of dead saints. \[See [[Pilgrimage]].\]

**2.** In Scripture, one that has only a temporary residence on earth. [[Hebrews 11#13|Hebrews 11:13]].

**PIL'GRIM**, _verb intransitive_ To wander or ramble. \[Not used.\]
