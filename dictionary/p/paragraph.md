# Paragraph

**PAR'AGRAPH**, _noun_ \[Gr. a marginal note; to write near or beyond the text; beyond, and to write.\] A distinct part of a discourse or writing; any portion or section of a writing or chapter which relates to a particular point, whether consisting of one sentence or many sentences. A _paragraph_ is sometimes marked, but more generally, a _paragraph_ is distinguished only by a break in the composition or lines.

**PAR'AGRAPH**, _verb transitive_ To form or write paragraphs.
