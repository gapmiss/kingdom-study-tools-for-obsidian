# Protasis

**PRO'TASIS**, _noun_ \[Gr. to present.\]

**1.** A proposition; a maxim.

**2.** In the ancient drama, the first part of a comic or tragic piece, in which the several persons are shown, their characters intimated, and the subject proposed and entered on. The _protasis_ might extend to two acts, where it ended, and the epitasis commenced.
