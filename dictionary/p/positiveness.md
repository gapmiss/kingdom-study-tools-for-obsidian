# Positiveness

**POS'ITIVENESS**, _noun_ Actualness; reality of existence; not mere negation.

The _positiveness_ of sins of commission lies both in the habitude of the will and in the executed act too; the _positiveness_ of sins of omission is in the habitude of the will only.

**1.** Undoubting assurance; full confidence; peremptoriness; as, the man related the facts with _positiveness_ In matters of opinion, _positiveness_ is not an indication of prudence.
