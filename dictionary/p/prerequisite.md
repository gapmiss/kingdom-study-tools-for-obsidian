# Prerequisite

**PREREQ'UISITE**, _adjective_ s as z. \[pre and requisite.\] Previously required or necessary to something subsequent; as, certain attainments are _prerequisite_ to an admission or orders.

**PREREQ'UISITE**, _noun_ Something that is previously required or necessary to the end proposed. An acquaintance with Latin and Greek is a _prerequisite_ to the admission of a young man into a college.
