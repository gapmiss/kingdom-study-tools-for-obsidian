# Prudent

**PRU'DENT**, _adjective_ Cautious; circumspect; practically wise; careful of the consequences of enterprises, measures or actions; cautious not to act when the end is of doubtful utility, or probably impracticable.

The _prudent_ man looketh well to his going. [[Proverbs 14#8|Proverbs 14:8]].

A _prudent_ man foreseeth the evil and hideth himself. [[Proverbs 22#3|Proverbs 22:3]].

**1.** Dictated or directed by prudence; as _prudent_ behavior.

**2.** Foreseeing by instinct; as the _prudent_ crane.

**3.** Frugal; economical; as a _prudent_ woman; _prudent_ expenditure of money.

**4.** Wise; intelligent.
