# Propitiation

**PROPITIATION**, _noun_ propisia'shon.

**1.** The act of appeasing wrath and conciliating the favor of an offended person; the act of making propitious.

**2.** In theology, the atonement or atoning sacrifice offered to God to assuage his wrath and render him propitious to sinners. Christ is the _propitiation_ for the sins of men. [[Romans 3#25|Romans 3:25]]. [[1 John 2#2|1 John 2:2]].
