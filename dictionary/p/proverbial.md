# Proverbial

**PROVERB'IAL**,_adjective_ Mentioned in a proverb; as a _proverbial_ cure or remedy.

In case of excesses, I take the German _proverbial_ cure, by a hair of the same beast, to be the worst in the world.

**1.** Comprised in a proverb; used or current as a proverb; as a _proverbial_ saying or speech.

**2.** Pertaining to proverbs; resembling a proverb; suitable to a proverb; as a _proverbial_ obscurity.
