# Perjure

**PERJURE**, _verb transitive_ per'jur. \[Latin perjuro; per and juro, to swear; that is, to swear aside or beyond.\] Willfully to make a false oath when administered by lawful authority or in a court of justice; to forswear; as, the witness perjured himself.

**PER'JURE**, _noun_ A perjured person. \[Not used.\]
