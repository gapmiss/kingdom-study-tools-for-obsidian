# Pillow

**PIL'LOW**, _noun_ \[Latin pulvinar; from Latin pilus, hair, or from stuffing.\]

**1.** A long cushion to support the head of a person when reposing on a bed; a sack or case filled with feathers, down or other soft material.

**2.** In a ship, the block on which the inner end of a bowsprit is supported.

The _pillow_ of a plow, is a cross piece of wood which serves to raise or lower the beam.

**PIL'LOW**, _verb transitive_ To rest or lay on for support.
