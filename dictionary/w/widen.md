# Widen

**WIDEN**, _verb transitive_ To make wide or wider; to extend in breadth; as, to _widen_ a field; to _widen_ a breach. \[Note.--In America, females say, to _widen_ a stocking.\]

**WIDEN**, _verb intransitive_ To grow wide or wider; to enlarge; to extend itself.

And arches _widen_ and long aisles extend.
