# Why

**WHY**, _adverb_ \[Latin , for what. The original phrase is for what, for _why_ \]

**1.** For what cause or reason, interrogatively.

Turn ye, turn ye, for _why_ will ye die? [[Jeremiah 27#13|Jeremiah 27:13]].

**2.** For which reason or cause, relatively.

No ground of enmity, _why_ he should mean me ill.

**3.** For what reason or cause; for which; relatively.

Turn the discourse; I have a reason _why_ I would not have you speak so tenderly.

**4.** It is used sometimes emphatically, or rather as a expletive.

If her chill heart I cannot move, _why_ Ill enjoy the very love.
