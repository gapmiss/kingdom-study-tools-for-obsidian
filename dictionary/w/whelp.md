# Whelp

**WHELP**, _noun_ \[Latin\]

**1.** The young of the canine species, and of several other beasts of prey; a puppy; a cub; as a bear robbed of her whelps; lions whelps.

**2.** A son; in contempt.

**3.** A young man; in contempt.

**WHELP**, _verb intransitive_ To bring forth young, as the female of the canine species and some other beasts of prey.
