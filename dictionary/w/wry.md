# Wry

**WRY**, _adjective_

**1.** Twisted; turned to one side; distorted; as a _wry_ neck; a _wry_ mouth.

**2.** Deviating from the right direction; as _wry_ words.

**3.** Wrested; perverted; as, to put a _wry_ sense on an authors words.

**WRY**, _verb intransitive_ To be writhed or distorted. \[Not used.\]

**WRY**, _verb transitive_ To distort; to wrest. \[Not used.\]
