# Wretchedness

**WRETCHEDNESS**, _noun_

**1.** Extreme misery or unhappiness, either from want or sorrow; as the _wretchedness_ of poor mendicants.

We have, with the feeling, most the very memory of such _wretchedness_ as our forefathers endured--

The prodigal brought nothing to his father but his rags and _wretchedness_

**2.** Meanness; despicableness; as the _wretchedness_ of a performance.
