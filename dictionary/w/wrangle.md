# Wrangle

**WRANGLE**, _verb intransitive_ \[from the root of wring.\] To dispute angrily; to quarrel peevishly and noisily; to brawl; to altercate.

For a score of kingdoms you should _wrangle_

He did not know what it was to _wrangle_ on indifferent points.

**WRANGLE**, _verb transitive_ To involve in contention. \[Little used.\]

**WRANGLE**, _noun_ An angry dispute; a noisy quarrel.
