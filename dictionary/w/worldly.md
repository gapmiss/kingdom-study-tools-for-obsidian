# Worldly

**WORLDLY**, _adjective_

**1.** Secular; temporal; pertaining to this world or life, in contradistinction to the life to come; as _worldly_ pleasures; _worldly_ affairs; _worldly_ estate; _worldly_ honor; _worldly_ lusts. [[Titus 2#12|Titus 2:12]].

**2.** Devoted to this life and its enjoyments; bent on gain; as a _worldly_ man; a _worldly_ mind.

**3.** Human; common; belonging to the world; as _worldly_ actions; _worldly_ maxims.

**WORLDLY**, _adverb_ With relation to this life.

Subverting _worldly_ strong and _worldly_ wise by simply meek.
