# Welcome

**WELCOME**, _adjective_

**1.** Received with gladness; admitted willingly to the house, entertainment and company; as a _welcome_ guest.

**2.** Grateful; pleasing; as a _welcome_ present; _welcome_ news.

**3.** Free to have or enjoy gratuitously. You are _welcome_ to the use of my library.

To bid _welcome_ to receive with professions of kindness.

**WELCOME**, is used elliptically for you are _welcome_

**WELCOME**, great monarch, to your own.

**WELCOME** to our house, an herb.

**WELCOME**, _noun_

**1.** Salutation of a new comer.

**WELCOME** ever smiles--

**2.** Kind reception of a guest or new comer. We entered the house and found a ready _welcome_

Truth finds an entrance and a _welcome_ too.

**WELCOME**, _verb transitive_ To salute a new comer with kindness; or to receive and entertain hospitable, gratuitously and cheerfully.

Thus we salute thee with our early song, and _welcome_ thee, and wish thee long.
