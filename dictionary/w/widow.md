# Widow

**WIDOW**, _noun_ \[Latin See [[Wide]].\] A woman who has lost her husband by death. [[Luke 2#37|Luke 2:37]].

**WIDOW**s chamber, in London, the apparel and furniture of the bed-chamber of the _widow_ of a freeman, to which she is entitled.

**WIDOW**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To bereave of a husband; but rarely used except in the participle.

**2.** To endow with a widows right. \[Unusual.\]

**3.** To strip of any thing good.
