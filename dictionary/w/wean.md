# Wean

**WEAN**, _verb transitive_ \[G. See [[Wont]].\]

**1.** To accustom and reconcile, as a child or other young animal, to a want or deprivation of the breast.

And the child grew, and was weaned. [[Genesis 21#8|Genesis 21:8]].

**2.** To detach or alienate, as the affections, from any object of desire; to reconcile to the want or loss of any thing; as, to _wean_ the heart from temporal enjoyments.
