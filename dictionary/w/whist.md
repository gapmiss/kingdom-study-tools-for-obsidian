# Whist

**WHIST**, _adjective_ Silent; mute; still; not speaking; not making a noise.

_The winds with wonder _whist_ smoothly the waters kissd._

\[This adjective, like some others, always follows its noun. We never say, _whist_ wind; but the wind is _whist_ \]

**WHIST** is used for be silent. _whist_ _whist_ that is, be silent or still.

**WHIST**, _noun_ A game at cards, so called because it requires silence or close attention. It is not in America pronounced whisk.
