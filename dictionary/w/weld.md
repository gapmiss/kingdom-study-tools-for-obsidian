# Weld

**WELD**, WOLD, _noun_ A plant of the genus Reseda, used by dyers to give a yellow color, and sometimes called dyers weed. It is much cultivated in Kent for the London dyers.

**WELD**, _verb transitive_ To wield.

**WELD**, _verb transitive_ \[G., to join.\] To unite or hammer into firm union, as two pieces of iron, when heated almost to fusion.
