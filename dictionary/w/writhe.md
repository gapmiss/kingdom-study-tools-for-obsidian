# Writhe

**WRITHE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To twist; to distort.

Her mouth she writhd.

**2.** To twist with violence; as, to _writhe_ the body.

**3.** To wrest; to distort; to torture; as, to _writhe_ words.

**WRITHE**, _verb intransitive_ To twist; to be distorted; as, to _writhe_ with agony.
