# Wriggle

**WRIGGLE**, _verb intransitive_ To move the body to and fro with short motions.

Both he and his successors would often _wriggle_ in their seats, as long as the cushion lasted.

**WRIGGLE**, _verb transitive_ To put into a quick reciprocating motion; to introduce by a shifting motion.

Wriggling his body to recover his seat, and cast his right leg over.
