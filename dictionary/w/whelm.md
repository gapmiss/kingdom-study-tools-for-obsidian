# Whelm

**WHELM**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To cover with water or other fluid; to cover by immersion in something that envelops on all sides; as, to _whelm_ a person or a company in the seas; to _whelm_ a caravan in sand or dust.

**2.** To cover completely; to immerse deeply; to overburden; as, to _whelm_ one in sorrows.

**3.** To throw over so as to cover. \[Not used.\]
