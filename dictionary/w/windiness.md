# Windiness

**WINDINESS**, _noun_ \[from windey.\]

**1.** The state of being windy or tempestuous; as the _windiness_ of the weather or season.

**2.** Fullness of wind; flatulence.

**3.** Tendency to generate wind; as the _windiness_ of vegetables.

**4.** Tumor; puffiness.

The swelling _windiness_ of much knowledge.
