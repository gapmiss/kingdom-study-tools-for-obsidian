# Wreath

**WREATH**, _noun_ \[See [[Writhe]].\]

**1.** To twist; to convolve; to wind one about another; as, to _wreath_ a garland of flowers.

**2.** To interweave; to entwine; as chains of wreathed work.

**3.** To encircle, as a garland.

The flowrs that _wreath_ the sparkling bowl.

**4.** To encircle as with a garland; to dress in a garland.

And with thy winding ivy wreaths her lance.

**WREATH**, _verb intransitive_ To be interwoven or entwined; as a bower of wreathing trees.
