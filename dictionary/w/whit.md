# Whit

**WHIT**, _noun_ \[Latin\] A point; a jot; the smallest part or particle imaginable. It is used without a preposition. He is not a _whit_ the wiser for experience.

It does not me a _whit_ displease.

The regular construction would be by a _whit_ or in a _whit_ In these phrases, a _whit_ may be interpreted by in the least, in the smallest degree.
