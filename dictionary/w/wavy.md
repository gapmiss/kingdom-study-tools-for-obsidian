# Wavy

**WAVY**, _adjective_ \[from wave.\[

**1.** Rising or swelling in waves; full of waves; as the _wavy_ sea.

**2.** Playing to and fro; undulating.

Let her glad valleys smile with _wavy_ corn.

**3.** Undulating on the border or on the surface; a botanical use.

Wawes or waes, for waves. \[Not in use.\]
