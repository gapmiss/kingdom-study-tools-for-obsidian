# Wafer

**WAFER**, _noun_

**1.** A thin cake or leaf; as a _wafer_ of bread given by the Romanists in the Eucharist.

**2.** A thin leaf of paste, or a composition of flour, the white of eggs, isinglass and yeast, spread over with gumwater and dried; used in sealing letters.

**WAFER**, _verb transitive_ To seal or close with a _wafer_
