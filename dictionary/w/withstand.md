# Withstand

**WITHSTAND**, _verb transitive_ \[with and stand. See [[Stand]].\] To oppose; to resist, either with physical or moral force; as, to _withstand_ the attack of troops; to _withstand_ eloquence or arguments.

When Peter was come to Antioch, I withstood him to his face. [[Galatians 2#1|Galatians 2:1]].
