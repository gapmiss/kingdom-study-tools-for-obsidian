# Waster

**WASTER**, _noun_

**1.** One who is prodigal; one who squanders property; one who consumes extravagantly or without use.

He also that is slothful in his work, is brother to him who is a great _waster_ [[Proverbs 18#9|Proverbs 18:9]].

Sconces are great wasters of candles.

**2.** A kind of cudgel.
