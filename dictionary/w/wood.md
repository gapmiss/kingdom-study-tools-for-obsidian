# Wood

**WOOD**, _adjective_ Mad; furious.

**WOOD**, _noun_

**1.** A large and thick collection of trees; a forest.

Light thickens, and the crow makes wing to the rooky _wood_

**2.** The substance of trees; the hard substance which composes the body of a tree and its branches, and which is covered by the bark.

**3.** Trees cut or sawed for the fire. _wood_ is yet the principal fuel in the United States.

**4.** An idol. [[Habakkuk 2#19|Habakkuk 2:19]].

**WOOD**, _verb intransitive_ To supply or get supplies of _wood_
