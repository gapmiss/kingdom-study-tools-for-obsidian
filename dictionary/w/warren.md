# Warren

**WARREN**, _noun_ \[See [[Guard]], Warrant and Wary.\]

**1.** A piece of ground appropriated to the breeding and preservation of rabbits.

**2.** In law, a franchise or place privileged by prescription or grant from the king, for keeping beasts and fowls. The _warren_ is the next franchise in degree to the park; and a forest, which is the highest in dignity, comprehends a chase, a park and a free _warren_

**3.** A place for keeping fish in a river.
