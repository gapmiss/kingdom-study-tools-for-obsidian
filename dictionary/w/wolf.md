# Wolf

**WOLF**, _noun_ **WULF**. \[G., Gr.\]

**1.** An animal of the genus Canis, a beast of prey that kills sheep and other small domestic animals; called sometimes the wild dog. The _wolf_ is crafty, greedy and ravenous.

**2.** A small white worm or maggot, which infests granaries.

**3.** An eating ulcer.
