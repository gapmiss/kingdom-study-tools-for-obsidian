# Whine

**WHINE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin\] TO express murmurs by a plaintive cry; to moan with a puerile noise; to murmur meanly.

They came--with a whining accent craving liberty.

Then, if we _whine_ look pale--

**WHINE**, _noun_ A plaintive tone; the nasal puerile tone of mean complaint; mean or affected complaint.
