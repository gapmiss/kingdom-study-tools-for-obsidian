# Worsted

**WORSTED**, _participle passive_ Defeated; overthrown.

**WORSTED**, _noun_ **WUSTED**. \[The origin of this word is uncertain. It is usually supposed to take its name from a town in England or in Flanders; but in Norman, worstetz is mentioned; as lit de morstetz, a bed of _worsted_ \] Yarn spun from combed wool; a particular kind of woolen yarn.

**WORSTED**, _adjective_ Consisting of worsted; made of _worsted_ yarn; as _worsted_ stockings.
