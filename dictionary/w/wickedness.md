# Wickedness

**WICKEDNESS**, _noun_ Departure from the rules of the divine law; evil disposition or practices; immorality; crime; sin; sinfulness; corrupt manners _wickedness_ generally signifies evil practices.

What _wickedness_ is this that is done among you? [[Judges 20#3|Judges 20:3]].

But _wickedness_ expresses also the corrupt dispositions of the heart.

Their inward part is very _wickedness_ [[Psalms 5#4|Psalms 5:4]].

In heart ye work _wickedness_ [[Psalms 5#4|Psalms 5:4]]8.
