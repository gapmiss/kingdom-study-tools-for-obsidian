# Whiff

**WHIFF**, _noun_

**1.** A sudden expulsion of air from the mouth; a puff; as the _whiff_ of a smoker.

And seasons his whiffs with impertinent jokes.

**2.** In ichthyology, a species of Pleuronectes or flounder.

**WHIFF**, _verb transitive_ TO puff; to throw out in whiffs; to consume in whiffs.
