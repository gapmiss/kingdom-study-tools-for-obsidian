# Wholesale

**WHOLESALE**, _noun_ \[whole and sale.\]

**1.** Sale of goods by the piece or large quantity; as distinguished from retail. Some traders sell either by _wholesale_ or retail.

**2.** The whole mass.

Some from vanity or envy, despise a valuable book, and throw contempt upon it by _wholesale_

**WHOLESALE**, _adjective_ \[supra.\]

**1.** Buying and selling by the piece or quantity; as a _wholesale_ merchant or dealer.

**2.** Pertaining to the trade by the piece or quantity; as the _wholesale_ price.
