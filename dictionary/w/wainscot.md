# Wainscot

**WAINSCOT**, _noun_ In building, timber-work serving to line the walls of a room, being made in panels.

**WAINSCOT**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To line with boards; as, to _wainscot_ a hall.

Music sounds better in chambers wainscoted than hanged.

**2.** To line with different materials.

The other is wainscoted with looking-glass.
