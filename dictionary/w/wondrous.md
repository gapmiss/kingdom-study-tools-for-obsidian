# Wondrous

**WONDROUS**, _adjective_ Admirable; marvelous; such as may excite surprise and astonishment; strange.

That I may publish with the voice of thanksgiving, and tell of all thy _wondrous_ works. [[Psalms 26#7|Psalms 26:7]].

**WONDROUS**, _adverb_ In a wonderful or surprising degree; as a place _wondrous_ deep; you are _wondrous_ fair; _wondrous_ fond of peace. These phrases of Cowley, Dryden and Pope, are admissible only in the ludicrous and burlesque style.
