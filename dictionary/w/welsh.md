# Welsh

**WELSH**, _adjective_ \[G., foreign, strange, Celtic.\] Pertaining to the _welsh_ nation.

**WELSH**, _noun_

**1.** The language of Wales or of the _welsh_

**2.** The general name of the inhabitants of Wales. The word signifies foreigners or wanderers, and was given to this people by other nations, probably because they came from some distant country. The _welsh_ call themselves Cymry, in the plural, and a Welshman Cymro, and their country Cymra, of which the adjective is Cymreig, and the name of their language, Cymraeg. They are supposed to be the Cimbri of Jutland.
