# Waver

**WAVER**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To play or move to and fro; to move one way and the other.

**2.** To fluctuate; to be unsettled in opinion; to vacillate; to be undetermined; as, to _waver_ in opinion; to _waver_ in faith.

Let us hold fast the profession of our faith without wavering. [[Hebrews 10#23|Hebrews 10:23]].

**3.** To totter; to reel; to be in danger of falling.

**WAVER**, _noun_ A name given to a sapling or young timber tree in England.
