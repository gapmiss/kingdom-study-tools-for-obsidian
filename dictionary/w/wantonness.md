# Wantonness

**WANTONNESS**, _noun_

**1.** Sportiveness; gaiety; frolicsomeness; waggery.

\- As sad as night, only for _wantonness_

**2.** Licentiousness; negligence of restraint.

The tumults threatened to abuse all acts of grace, and turn them into _wantonness_

**3.** Lasciviousness; lewdness. [[Romans 13#13|Romans 13:13]]. [[2 Peter 2#18|2 Peter 2:18]]
