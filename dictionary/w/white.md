# White

**WHITE**, _adjective_ \[G.\]

**1.** Being in the color of pure snow; snowy; not dark; as _white_ paper; a _white_ skin.

**2.** Pale; destitute of color in the cheeks, or of the tinge of blood color; as _white_ with fear.

**3.** Having the color of purity; pure; clean; free from spot; as _white_ robed innocence.

**4.** Gray; as _white_ hair; a venerable man, _white_ with age.

**5.** Pure; unblemished.

No whiter page than Addisons remains.

**6.** In a scriptural sense, purified from sin; sanctified. [[Psalms 51#7|Psalms 51:7]].
