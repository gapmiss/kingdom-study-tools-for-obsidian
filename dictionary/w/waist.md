# Waist

**WAIST**, _noun_

**1.** That part of the human body which is immediately below the ribs or thorax; or the small part of the body between the thorax and hips.

**2.** That part of a ship which is between the quarter deck and forecastle. But in many ships now built, there is no quarter deck, and in such the _waist_ is the middle part of the ship.
