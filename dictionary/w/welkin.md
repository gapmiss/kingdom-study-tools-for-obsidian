# Welkin

**WELKIN**, _noun_ \[G., a cloud.\] The visible regions of the air; the vault of heaven. \[This is obsolete, unless in poetry.\]

**WELKIN** eye, in Shakespeare, is interpreted by Johnson, a blue eye, from _welkin_ the sky; by Todd, a rolling eye, from Sax. Wealcan, to roll; and by Entick, a languishing eye. See [[Welk]]. It is obsolete, at least in New England.
