# Workmanship

**WORKMANSHIP**, _noun_

**1.** Manufacture; something made, particularly by manual labor. [[Exodus 31#3|Exodus 31:3]].

**2.** That which is effected, made or produced. [[Ephesians 2#10|Ephesians 2:10]].

**3.** The skill of a workman; or the execution or manner of making any thing. The _workmanship_ of this cloth is admirable.

**4.** The art of working.
