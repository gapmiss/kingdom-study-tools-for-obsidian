# Wreak

**WREAK**, _verb transitive_ \[G. The sense is to drive or throw, to dash with violence.\]

**1.** To execute; to inflict; to hurl or drive; as, to _wreak_ vengeance on an enemy.

On me let death _wreak_ all his rage.

**2.** To revenge.

Come _wreak_ his loss, whom bootless ye complain.

Anothers wrongs to _wreak_ upon thyself.

\[This latter sense is nearly or quite obsolete.\]

**WREAK**, for reck. To care, is a mistake.

**WREAK**, _noun_ Revenge; vengeance; furious passion.
