# Weighty

**WEIGHTY**, _adjective_

**1.** Having great weight; heavy; ponderous; as a _weighty_ body.

**2.** Important; forcible; momentous; adapted to turn the balance in the mind, or to convince; as _weighty_ reasons; _weighty_ matters; _weighty_ considerations or arguments.

**3.** Rigorous; severe; as our weightier judgment. \[Not in use.\]
