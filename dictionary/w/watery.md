# Watery

**WATERY**, _adjective_

**1.** Resembling water; thin or transparent, as a liquid; as _watery_ humors.

The oily and _watery_ parts of the aliment.

**2.** Tasteless; insipid; vapid; spiritless; as _watery_ turneps.

**3.** Wet; abounding with water; as _watery_ land; _watery_ eyes.

**4.** Pertaining to water; as the _watery_ god.

**5.** Consisting of water; as a _watery_ desert.
