# Weakly

**WEAKLY**, _adverb_

**1.** Feebly; with little physical strength; faintly; not forcible; as a fortress _weakly_ defended.

**2.** With want of efficacy.

Was plighted faith so _weakly_ seald above?

**3.** With feebleness of mind or intellect; indiscretely; injuriously.

Beneath pretended justice _weakly_ fall.

**4.** Timorously; with little courage or fortitude.

**WEAKLY**, _adjective_ Not strong of constitution; infirm; as a _weakly_ woman; a man of a _weakly_ constitution.
