# Wheeling

**WHEELING**, _participle present tense_ Conveying on wheels or in a wheel-carriage; turning.

**WHEELING**, _noun_

**1.** The act of conveying on wheels.

**2.** The act of passing on wheels, or convenience for passing on wheels. We say, it is good _wheeling_ or bad _wheeling_ according to the state of the roads.

**3.** A turning or circular movement of troops embodied.
