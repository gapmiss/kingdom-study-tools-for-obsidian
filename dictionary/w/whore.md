# Whore

**WHORE**, _noun_ Hore. \[G. The correct orthography is hore.\] A harlot; a courtesan; a concubine; a prostitute.

**WHORE**, _verb intransitive_ \[supra.\] To have unlawful sexual commerce; to practice lewdness.

**WHORE**, _verb transitive_ To corrupt by lewd intercourse. \[Little used.\]
