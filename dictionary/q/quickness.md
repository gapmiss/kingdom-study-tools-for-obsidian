# Quickness

**QUICK'NESS**, _noun_

**1.** Speed; velocity; celerity; rapidity; as the _quickness_ of motion.

**2.** Activity; briskness; promptness, as the _quickness_ of the imagination or wit.

**3.** Acuteness of perception; keep sensibility; as _quickness_ of sensation.

**4.** Sharpness; pungency.
