# Quietly

**QUI'ETLY**, _adverb_

**1.** In a quiet state; without motion; in a state of rest; as, to lie or sit _quietly_

**2.** Without tumult, alarm, dispute or disturbance; peaceably; as, to live _quietly_

**3.** Calmly; without agitation or violent emotion; patiently. submit _quietly_ to unavoidable evils.
