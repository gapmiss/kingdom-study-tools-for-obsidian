# Quadruple

**QUAD'RUPLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin quadruplus; quadra, quatuor, and plico, to fold.\]

Fourfold; four times told; as, to make _quadruple_ restitution for trespass or theft.

**QUAD'RUPLE**, _noun_ Four times the sum or number; as, to receive _quadruple_ the amount in damages or profits.
