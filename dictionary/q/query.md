# Query

**QUE'RY**, _noun_ \[from Latin quaere, imperative of quaero; perhaps Heb. to seek, to search, to inquire.\]

A question; an inquiry to be answered or resolved.

I will conclude by proposing some queries.

**QUE'RY**, _verb intransitive_ To ask a question or questions.

Three Cambridge sophs each prompt to _query_ answer and debate.

**QUE'RY**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To seek; to inquire; as, _query_ the sum or amount; _query_ the motive or the fact.

**2.** To examine by questions.

**3.** To doubt of.
