# Queen

**QUEEN**, _noun_

**1.** The consort of a king; a _queen_ consort.

**2.** A woman who is the sovereign of a kingdom; a queen-regent; as Elizabeth, _queen_ of England; Mary, _queen_ of Scotland.

**3.** The sovereign of a swarm of bees, or the female of the hive.

A hive of bees cannot subsist without a _queen_

**QUEEN** of the meadows, meadow sweet, a plant of the genus Spiraea.

**QUEEN**, _verb intransitive_ To play the queen; to act the part or character of a _queen_
