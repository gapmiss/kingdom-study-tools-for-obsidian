# Questionable

**QUES'TIONABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** That may be questioned; doubtful; uncertain; disputable. the deed is of _questionable_ authority.

It is _questionable_ whether Galen ever saw the dissection of a human body.

**2.** Suspicious; liable to be doubted or disputed; liable to suspicion. His veracity is _questionable_

Thou com'st in such a _questionable_ shape, that I will speak to thee.
