# Quilting

**QUILT'ING**, _participle present tense_ Stitching together, as two cloths, with some soft substance between them.

**QUILT'ING**, _noun_

**1.** The act of forming a quilt.

**2.** In New England, the act of _quilting_ by a collection of females who bestow their labor gratuitously to aid a female friend, and conclude with an entertainment.
