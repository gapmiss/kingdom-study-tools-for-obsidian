# Quincunx

**QUIN'CUNX**, _noun_ \[Latin composed of quinque, five, and uncia, ounce.\]

In gardening, the _quincunx_ order is a plantation of trees disposed in a square, consisting of five trees, one at each corner and a fifth in the middle, thus : .: ; which order repeated indefinitely, forms a regular grove or wood, which viewed by an angle of the square or parallelogram, presents equal or parallel alleys.
