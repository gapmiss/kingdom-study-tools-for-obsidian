# Quietness

**QUI'ETNESS**, _noun_

**1.** A state of rest; stillness.

**2.** Calm; tranquility; as the _quietness_ of the ocean or atmosphere.

**3.** Freedom from agitation or emotion; calmness; coolness; as the _quietness_ of the mind.

**4.** Freedom from disturbance, disorder or commotion; peace; tranquility; as the _quietness_ of a city or state.
