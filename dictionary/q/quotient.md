# Quotient

**QUO'TIENT**, _noun_ \[Latin quoties, how often.\]

In arithmetic, the number resulting from the division of one number by another, and showing how often a less number is contained in a greater. Thus 3)12(4. Here 4 is the _quotient_ showing that 3 is contained 4 times in 12. Or _quotient_ is an expression denoting a certain part of a unit; as 3/4.
