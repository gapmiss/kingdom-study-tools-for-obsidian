# Quench

**QUENCH**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To extinguish; to put out; as, to _quench_ flame.

**2.** To still; to quiet; to repress; as, to _quench_ a passion or emotion.

**3.** To allay or extinguish; as, to _quench_ thirst.

**4.** To destroy.

**5.** To check; to stifle; as, to _quench_ the Spirit.

[[1 Thessalonians 5#19|1 Thessalonians 5:19]].

**QUENCH**, _verb intransitive_ To cool; to become cool.
