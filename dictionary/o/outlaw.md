# Outlaw

**OUT'LAW**, _noun_ A person excluded from the benefit of the law, or deprived of its protection. Formerly any person might kill an outlaw; but it is now held unlawful for any person to put to death an _outlaw_ except the sheriff, who has a warrant for that purpose.

**OUT'LAW**, _verb transitive_ To deprive of the benefit and protection of law; to proscribe.
