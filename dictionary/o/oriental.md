# Oriental

**ORIENT'AL**, _adjective_

**1.** Eastern; situated in the east; as _oriental_ seas or countries.

**2.** Proceeding from the east; as the _oriental_ radiations of the sun.

**ORIENT'AL**, _noun_ A native or inhabitant of some eastern part of the world. We give the appellation to the inhabitants of Asia from the Hellespont and Mediterranean to Japan.
