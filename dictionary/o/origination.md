# Origination

**ORIGINA'TION**, _noun_

1 The act of bringing or coming into existence; first production.

Descartes first introduced the fancy of making a world, and deducing the _origination_ of the universe from mechanical principles.

**2.** Mode of production or bringing into being.

This eruca is propagated by animal parents, to wit, butterflies, after the common _origination_ of all caterpillars.
