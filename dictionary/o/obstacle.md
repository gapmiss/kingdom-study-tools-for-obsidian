# Obstacle

**OB'STACLE**, _noun_ \[Latin obsto, to withstand; ob and sto.\]

That which opposes; any thing that stands in the way and hinders progress; hinderance; obstruction, either in a physical or moral sense. An army may meet with obstacles on its march; bad roads are obstacles to traveling; prejudice is an _obstacle_ to improvement; want of union is often an insuperable _obstacle_ to beneficial measures.
