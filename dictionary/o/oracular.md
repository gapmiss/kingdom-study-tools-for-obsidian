# Oracular

**ORAC'ULAR**,

**ORAC'ULAR**LY,

ORAC'ULOUS, _adjective_

**1.** Uttering oracles; as an _oracular_ tongue.

The oraculous seer.

**2.** Grace; venerable; like an oracle; as an _oracular_ shade.

They have something venerable and _oracular_ in that unadorned gravity and shortness in the expression.

**3.** Positive; authoritative; magisterial; as oraculous expressions of sentiments.

**4.** Obscure; ambiguous, like the oracles of pagan deities.
