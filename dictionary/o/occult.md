# Occult

**OCCULT**', _adjective_ \[Latin occultus, occulo; ob and celo, to conceal.\]

Hidden from the eye or understanding; invisible; secret; unknown; undiscovered; undetected; as the _occult_ qualities of matter.

The _occult_ sciences are magic, necromancy, etc.

**OCCULT** lines, in geometry, are such as are drawn with the compasses or a pencil, and are scarcely visible.
