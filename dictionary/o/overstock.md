# Overstock

**OVERSTOCK**', _noun_ Superabundance; more than is sufficient.

**OVERSTOCK'**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To fill too full; to crowd; to supply with more than is wanted. The world may be overstocked with inhabitants. The market is often overstocked with goods.

**2.** To furnish with more cattle than are wanted; as, to _overstock_ a farm.

**3.** To supply with more seed than is wanted; as, to _overstock_ land with clover.
