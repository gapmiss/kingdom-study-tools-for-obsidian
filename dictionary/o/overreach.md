# Overreach

**OVERRE'ACH**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To reach beyond in any direction; to rise above; to extend beyond.

**2.** To deceive by cunning, artifice or sagacity; to cheat.

**OVERRE'ACH**, _verb intransitive_ Applied to horses, to strike the toe of the hind foot against the heel or shoe of the fore foot.

**OVERRE'ACH**, _noun_ The act of striking the heel of the fore foot with the toe of the hind foot.
