# Operative

**OP'ERATIVE**, _adjective_

**1.** Having the power of acting; exerting force, physical or moral; having or exerting agency; active in the production of effects.

In actions of religion we should be zealous, active and _operative_ so far as prudence will permit.

It holds in all _operative_ principles, especially in morality.

**2.** Efficacious; producing the effect.
