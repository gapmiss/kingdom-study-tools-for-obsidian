# Olivin

**OL'IVIN**,

**OL'IVIN**E, _noun_ \[from olive.\] A subspecies of prismatic chrysolite of a brownish green, often inclining to a yellowish or grayish green, usually found in roundish grains in other stones; sometimes in large masses, but not crystallized. It is a constituent of many lavas and frequently occurs in basaltic rocks.
