# Officious

**OFFI'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin officiosus.\]

**1.** Kind; obliging; doing kind offices.

Yet not to earth are those bright luminaries _officious_

**2.** Excessively forward in kindness; importunately interposing services.

You are too _officious_ in her behalf that scorns your services.

**3.** Busy; intermeddling in affairs in which one has no concern.
