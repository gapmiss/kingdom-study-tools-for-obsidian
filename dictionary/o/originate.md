# Originate

**ORIG'INATE**, _verb transitive_ To cause to be; to bring into existence; to produce what is new.

The change is to be effected without a decomposition of the whole civil and political mass, for the purpose of originating a new civil order out of the elements of society.

That matter which cannot think, will, or _originate_ motion, should communicate thought, volition and motivity, is plainly impossible.

**ORIG'INATE**, _verb intransitive_ To take first existence; to have origin; to be begun. The scheme originated with the governor and council. It originated in pure benevolence.
