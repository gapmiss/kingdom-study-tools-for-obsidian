# Oyer

**OY'ER**, _noun_

**1.** In law, a hearing or trial of causes. A court of _oyer_ and terminer is constituted by a commission to inquire, hear and determine all treasons, felonies and misdemeanors.

**2.** The hearing, as of a writ, bond, note or other specialty; as when a defendant in court prays _oyer_ of a writing.
