# Outrageous

**OUTRA'GEOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Violent; furious; exorbitant; exceeding all bounds of moderation; as _outrageous_ villainies; _outrageous_ talk; _outrageous_ abuse.

**2.** Excessive; exceeding reason or decency; as _outrageous_ panegyric.

**3.** Enormous; atrocious; as _outrageous_ crimes.

**4.** Tumultuous; turbulent.
