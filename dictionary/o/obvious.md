# Obvious

**OB'VIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin obvus. See the Verb.\]

**1.** Meeting; opposed in front.

I to the evil turn my _obvious_ breast. \[Not now used.\]

**2.** Open; exposed. \[Little used.\]

**3.** Plain; evident; easily discovered, seen or understood; readily perceived by the eye or the intellect. We say, a phenomenon _obvious_ to the sight, or a truth _obvious_ to the mind.
