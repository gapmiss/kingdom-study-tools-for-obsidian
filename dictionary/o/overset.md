# Overset

**OVERSET**', _verb intransitive_

**1.** To turn from the proper position or basis; to turn upon the side, or to turn bottom upwards; as, to _overset_ a coach, a ship or a building.

**2.** To subvert; to overthrow; as, to _overset_ the constitution of a state; to _overset_ a scheme of policy.

**3.** To throw off the proper foundation.

**OVERSET'**, _verb intransitive_ To turn or be turned over; to turn or fall off the basis or bottom. A crank vessel is liable to _overset_
