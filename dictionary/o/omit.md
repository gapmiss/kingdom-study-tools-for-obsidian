# Omit

**OMIT**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin omitto; ob and mitto, to send.\]

**1.** To leave, pass by or neglect; to fail or forbear to do or to use; as, to _omit_ an opportunity of writing a letter. To _omit_ known duty is criminal.

**2.** To leave out; not to insert or mention; as, to _omit_ an important word in a deed; to _omit_ invidious comparisons; to _omit_ a passage in reading or transcribing.
