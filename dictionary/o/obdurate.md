# Obdurate

**OB'DURATE**, _adjective_ \[Latin obduro, to harden; ob and duro.\]

**1.** Hardened in heart; inflexibly hard; persisting obstinately in sin or impenitence.

**2.** Hardened against good or favor; stubborn; unyielding; inflexible.

The custom of evil makes the heart _obdurate_ against whatsoever instructions to the contrary.

**3.** Harsh; rugged; as an _obdurate_ consonant. \[Little used.\]

**OB'DURATE**, _verb transitive_ To harden. \[Not used.\]
