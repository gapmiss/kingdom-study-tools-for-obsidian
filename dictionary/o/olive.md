# Olive

**OL'IVE**, _noun_ \[Latin oliva, from olea, an _olive_ tree; Gr. See [[Oil]]\]

A plant or tree of the genus Olea. The common _olive_ tree grows in warm climates and rises to the height of twenty or thirty feet, having an upright stem with numerous branches. This tree is much cultivated in the south of Europe for its fruit, from which is expressed the _olive_ oil, and which is used also for pickles.
