# Omission

**OMIS'SION**, _noun_ \[Latin omissio, from omitto, omissus.\]

**1.** Neglect or failure to do something which a person had power to do, or which duty required to be done. _omission_ may be innocent or criminal; innocent, when no duty demands performance, but criminal when duty is neglected.

The most natural division of all offenses, is into those of _omission_ and those of commission.

**2.** A leaving out; neglect or failure; to insert or mention; as the _omission_ of a word or clause.
