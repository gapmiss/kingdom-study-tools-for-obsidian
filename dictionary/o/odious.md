# Odious

**O'DIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin odiosus, from odi, I hated, Eng. hate.\]

**1.** Hateful; deserving hatred. It expresses something less than detestable and abominable; as an _odious_ name; _odious_ vice.

All wickedness is _odious_

**2.** Offensive to the senses; disgusting; as an _odious_ sight; an _odious_ smell.

**3.** Causing hate; invidious; as, to utter _odious_ truth.

**4.** Exposed to hatred.

He rendered himself _odious_ to the parliament.
