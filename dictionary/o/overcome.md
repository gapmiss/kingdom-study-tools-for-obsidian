# Overcome

**OVERCOME**, _verb transitive_ \[See [[Come]].\]

**1.** To conquer; to vanquish; to subdue; as, to _overcome_ enemies in battle.

**2.** To surmount; to get the better of; as, to _overcome_ difficulties or obstacles.

**3.** To overflow; to surcharge. \[Not used.\]

**4.** To come upon; to invade. \[Not used.\]

**OVERCOME**, _verb intransitive_ To gain the superiority; to be victorious.
