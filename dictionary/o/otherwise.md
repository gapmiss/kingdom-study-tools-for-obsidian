# Otherwise

**OTH'ERWISE**, _adverb_ \[other and wise, manner.\]

**1.** In a different manner.

Thy father was a worthy prince, and merited, alas! a better fate; but heaven thought _otherwise_

**2.** By other causes.

Sir John Norris failed in the attempt of Lisborn, and returned with the loss, by sickness and _otherwise_ of 8000 men.

**3.** In other respects.

It is said truly, that the best men _otherwise_ are not always the best in regard to society.
