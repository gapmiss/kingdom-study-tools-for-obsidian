# Orpiment

**OR'PIMENT**, _noun_ \[Latin auripigmentum; aurum, gold, and pigmentum.\]

Sulphuret of arsenic, found native and then an ore of arsenic, or artificially composed. The native _orpiment_ appears in yellow, brilliant and seemingly talcky masses of various sizes. The red _orpiment_ is called realgar. It is more or less lively and transparent, and often crystallized in bright needles. In this form it is called ruby of arsenic.
