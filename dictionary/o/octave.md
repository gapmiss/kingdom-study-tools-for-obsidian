# Octave

**OC'TAVE**, _adjective_ \[infra.\] Denoting eight.

**OC'TAVE**, _noun_ \[Latin octavus, eighth.\]

**1.** The eighth day after a festival.

**2.** Eight days together after a festival.

**3.** In music, an eighth, or an interval of seven degrees or twelve semitones. The _octave_ is the most perfect of the chords, consisting of six full tones and two semitones major. It contains the whole diatonic scale.
