# Orrery

**OR'RERY**, _noun_ A machine so constructed as to represent by the movements of its parts, the motions and phases of the planets in their orbits. This machine was invented by George Graham, but Rowley, a workman, borrowed one from him, and made a copy for the earl of _orrery_ after whom it was named by Sir Richard Steele. similar machines are called also planetariums.
