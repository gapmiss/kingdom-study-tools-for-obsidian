# Ounce

**OUNCE**, _noun_ ouns. \[Latin uncia, the twelfth part of any thing; Gr; but the Greek is from Latin. Inch is from the same root, being the twelfth part of a foot.\]

**1.** A weight, the twelfth part of a pound troy, and the sixteenth of a pound avoirdupois. In troy weight, the _ounce_ is 20 pennyweights, each of 24 grains.

**2.** An animal of the genus Felis. \[See [[Once]].\]
