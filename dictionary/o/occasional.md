# Occasional

**OCCA'SIONAL**, _adjective_ s as z.

**1.** Incidental; casual; occurring at times, but not regular or systematic; made or happening as opportunity requires or admits. We make _occasional_ remarks on the events of the age.

**2.** Produced by accident; as the _occasional_ origin of a thing.

**3.** Produced or made on some special event; as an _occasional_ discourse.
