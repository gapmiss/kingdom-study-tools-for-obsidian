# Occurrence

**OCCUR'RENCE**, _noun_

**1.** Literally, a coming or happening; hence, any incident or accidental event; that which happens without being designed or expected; any single event. We speak of an unusual _occurrence_ or of the ordinary occurrences of life.

**2.** Occasional presentation.

Voyages detain the mind by the perpetual _occurrence_ and expectation of something new.
