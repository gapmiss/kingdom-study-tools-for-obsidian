# Overwhelm

**OVERWHELM**', _verb transitive_

**1.** To overspread or crush beneath something violent and weighty, that covers or encompasses the whole; as, to _overwhelm_ with waves.

**2.** To immerse and bear down; in a figurative sense; as, to be overwhelmed with cares, afflictions or business.

**3.** To overlook gloomily.

**4.** To put over. \[Not used.\]

**O'VERWHELM**, _noun_ The act of overwhelming.
