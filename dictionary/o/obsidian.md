# Obsidian

**OBSID'IAN**, _noun_ A mineral of two kinds, translucent and transparent. The translucent has a velvet black color; the transparent is of a dark blue. These occur massive in porphyry, gneiss or granite, generally invested with a gray opake crust.

The fracture of _obsidian_ is vitreous or pearly; hence the two varieties, vitreous _obsidian_ and pearlstone.
