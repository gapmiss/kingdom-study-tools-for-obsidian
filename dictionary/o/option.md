# Option

**OP'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin optio, from opto, to wish or desire.\]

**1.** The power of choosing; the right of choice or election; as the archbishop's _option_ in collating to a vacant benefice.

There is an _option_ left to the United States of America, whether they will be respectable and prosperous, or contemptible and miserable, as a nation.

**2.** The power of wishing; wish.

**3.** Choice; election; preference. He ought not to complain of his lot; it was his own _option_ We leave this to your own _option_
