# Only

**ONLY**, _adjective_

**1.** Single; one along; as, John was the _only_ man present.

**2.** This and no other. This is an _only_ child.

**3.** This above all others. He is the _only_ man for music.

**ONLY**, _adverb_

**1.** Singly; merely; barely; in one manner or for one purpose along.

I purpose my thoughts _only_ as conjectures.

And to be loved himself, needs _only_ to be known.

**2.** This and no other wise.

Every imagination of the thoughts of his heart was _only_ evil continually. [[Genesis 6#5|Genesis 6:5]].

**3.** Singly; without more; as only-begotten.
