# Overture

**O'VERTURE**, _noun_

**1.** Opening; disclosure; discovery. \[In this literal sense, little used.\]

**2.** Proposal; something offered for consideration, acceptance or rejection. The prince made overtures of peace, which were accepted.

**3.** The opening piece, prelude or symphony of some public act, ceremony or entertainment. The _overture_ in theatrical entertainments, is a piece of music usually ending in a fugue. The _overture_ of a jubilee is a general procession, etc.
