# Overrule

**OVERRU'LE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To influence or control by predominant power; to subject to superior authority. The law must _overrule_ all private opinions of right and wrong.

His passion and animosity overruled his conscience.

**2.** To govern with high authority.

**3.** In law, to supersede or reject; as, the plea was overruled by the court.
