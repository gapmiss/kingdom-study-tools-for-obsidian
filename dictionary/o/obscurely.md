# Obscurely

**OBSCU'RELY**, _adverb_

**1.** Darkly; not clearly; imperfectly; as an object _obscurely_ seen; _obscurely_ visible.

**2.** Out of sight; in a state not to be noticed; privately; in retirement; not conspicuously.

There live retired, content thyself to be _obscurely_ good.

**3.** Not clearly; not plainly to the mind; darkly; as future events _obscurely_ revealed.

**4.** Not plainly; indirectly; by hints or allusion.
