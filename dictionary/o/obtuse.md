# Obtuse

**OBTU'SE**, _adjective_ \[Latin obtusus, from obtundo, to beat against.\]

**1.** Blunt; not pointed or acute. Applied to angles, it denotes one that is larger than a right angle, or more than ninety degrees.

**2.** Dull; not having acute sensibility; as _obtuse_ senses.

**3.** Not sharp or shrill; dull; obscure; as _obtuse_ sound.
