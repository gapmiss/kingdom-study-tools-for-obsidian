# Observant

**OBSERV'ANT**, _adjective_ s as z.

**1.** Taking notice; attentively viewing or noticing; as an _observant_ spectator or traveler.

**2.** Obedient; adhering to in practice; with of. He is very _observant_ of the rules of his order.

We are told how _observant_ Alexander was of his master Aristotle.

**3.** Carefully attentive; submissive.

**OBSERV'ANT**, _noun_ s as z.

**1.** A slavish attendant. \[Not in use.\]

**2.** A diligent observer.
