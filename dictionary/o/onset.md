# Onset

**ON'SET**, _noun_ \[on and set.\]

**1.** A rushing or setting upon; a violent attack; assault; a storming; appropriately, the assault of an army or body of troops upon an enemy or a fort.

The shout of battle now began and rushing sound of _onset_

**2.** An attack of any kind; as the impetuous _onset_ of grief.

**ON'SET**, _verb transitive_ To assault; to begin. \[Not used.\]
