# Overtake

**OVERTA'KE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To come up with in a course, pursuit, progress or motion; to catch.

The enemy said, I will pursue, I will _overtake_ [[Exodus 15#9|Exodus 15:9]].

**2.** To come upon; to fall on afterwards. Vengeance shall _overtake_ the wicked.

**3.** To take by surprise.

Brethren, if a man be overtaken in a fault, ye who are spiritual, restore such one in the spirit of meekness. [[Galatians 6#1|Galatians 6:1]].
