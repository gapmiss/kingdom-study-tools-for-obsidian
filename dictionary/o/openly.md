# Openly

**OPENLY**, _adverb_ o'pnly.

**1.** Publicly; not in private; without secrecy; as, to avow our sins and follies _openly_

How grossly and _openly_ do many of us contradict the precepts of the gospel by our ungodliness and worldly lusts!

**2.** Plainly; evidently; without reserve or disguise.
