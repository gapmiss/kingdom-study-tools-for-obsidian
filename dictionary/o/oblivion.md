# Oblivion

**OBLIV'ION**, _noun_ \[Latin oblivio.\]

**1.** Forgetfulness; cessation of remembrance.

Among our crimes _oblivion_ may be set.

**2.** A forgetting of offenses, or remission of punishment. An act of _oblivion_ is an amnesty, or general pardon of crimes and offenses, granted by a sovereign, by which punishment is remitted.
