# Obnoxious

**OBNOX'IOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin obnoxius; ob and noxius, hurtful, from noceo.\]

**1.** Subject; answerable.

The writings of lawyers, which are tied and _obnoxious_ to their particular laws.

**2.** Liable; subject to cognizance or punishment.

We know ourselves _obnoxious_ to God's severe justice.

**3.** Liable; exposed; as friendship _obnoxious_ to jealousies.

**4.** Reprehensible; censurable; not approved; as _obnoxious_ authors.

**5.** Odious; hateful; offensive; with to; as, the minister was _obnoxious_ to the whigs.

**6.** Hurtful; noxious.
