# Obstinate

**OB'STINATE**, _adjective_ \[Latin obstinatus.\]

**1.** Stubborn; pertinaciously adhering to an opinion or purpose; fixed firmly in resolution; not yielding to reason, arguments or other means.

I have known great cures done by _obstinate_ resolutions of drinking no wine.

No ass so meek, no ass os _obstinate_

**2.** Not yielding or not easily subdued or removed; as an _obstinate_ fever; _obstinate_ obstructions; an _obstinate_ cough.
