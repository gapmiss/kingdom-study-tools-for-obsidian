# Overturn

**OVERTURN**', _verb transitive_

**1.** To overset; to turn or throw from a basis or foundation; as, to _overturn_ a carriage or a building.

**2.** To subvert; to ruin; to destroy.

**3.** To overpower; to conquer.

**O'VERTURN**, _noun_ State of being overturned or subverted; overthrow.
