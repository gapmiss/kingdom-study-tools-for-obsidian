# Onward

**ON'WARD**, _adverb_ \[Latin versus.\]

**1.** Toward the point before or in front; forward; progressively; in advance; as, to move _onward_

Not one looks backward, _onward_ still he goes.

**2.** In a state of advanced progression.

**3.** A little further or forward.

**ON'WARD**, _adjective_

**1.** Advanced or advancing; as an _onward_ course.

**2.** Increased; improved.

**3.** Conducting; leading forward to perfection.
