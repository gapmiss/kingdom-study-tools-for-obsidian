# Obloquy

**OB'LOQUY**, _noun_ \[Latin obloquor; ob and loquor, to speak.\]

**1.** Censorious speech; reproachful language; language that casts contempt on men or their actions.

Shall names that made your city the glory of the earth, be mentioned with _obloquy_ and detraction?

**2.** Cause of reproach; disgrace. \[Not used.\]
