# Obliquity

**OBLIQ'UITY**, _noun_ \[Latin obliquitas.\]

**1.** Deviation from a right line; deviation from parallelism or perpendicularity; as the _obliquity_ of the ecliptic to the equator.

**2.** Deviation from moral rectitude.

To disobey God or oppose his will in any thing imports a moral _obliquity_

**3.** Irregularity; deviation from ordinary rules.
