# Observing

**OBSERV'ING**, _participle present tense_ s as z.

**1.** Taking notice by the eye or the intellect.

**2.** Remarking.

**3.** Keeping; adhering to in practice; fulfilling.

**4.** _adjective_ Giving particular attention; habitually taking notice; attentive to what passes. He is an _observing_ man.
