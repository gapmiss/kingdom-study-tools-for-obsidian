# Ostentation

**OSTENTA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin ostentatio.\]

**1.** Outward show or appearance.

**2.** Ambitious display; vain show; display of any thing dictated by vanity, or intended to invite praise or flattery. _ostentation_ of endowments is made by boasting or self-commendation. _ostentation_ often appears in works of art and sometimes in acts of charity.

He knew that good and bountiful minds are sometimes inclined to _ostentation_

The painter is to make no _ostentation_ of the means by which he strikes the imagination.

**3.** A show or spectacle. \[Not used.\]
