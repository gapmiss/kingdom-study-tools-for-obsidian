# Orthodoxy

**OR'THODOXY**, _noun_ \[Gr. right, true, and opinion, from to think.\]

**1.** Soundness of faith; a belief in the genuine doctrines taught in the Scriptures.

Basil bears full and clear testimony to Gregory's _orthodoxy_

**2.** Consonance to genuine scriptural doctrines; as the _orthodoxy_ of a creed.
