# Truly

**TRU'LY**, _adverb_ \[from true.\] In fact; in deed; in reality.

**1.** According to truth; in agreement with fact; as, to see things truly; the facts are _truly_ represented.

**2.** Sincerely; honestly; really; faithfully; as, to be _truly_ attached to a lover. The citizens are _truly_ loyal to their prince or their country.

**3.** Exactly; justly; as, to estimate _truly_ the weight of evidence.
