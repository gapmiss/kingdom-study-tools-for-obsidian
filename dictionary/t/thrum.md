# Thrum

**THRUM**, _noun_ \[Gr. a fragment; to break.\] The ends of weaver's threads.

**1.** Any coarse yarn.

**2.** Thrums, among gardeners, the thread-like internal bushy parts of flowers; the stamens.

**THRUM**, _verb intransitive_ To play coarsely on an instrument with the fingers.

**THRUM**, _verb transitive_ To weave; to know; to twist; to fringe.

**1.** Among seamen, to insert short pieces of rope-yard or spun yard in a sail or mat.
