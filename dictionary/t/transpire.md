# Transpire

**TRANSPI'RE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin transpiro; trans and spiro.\] To emit through the pores of the skin; to send off in vapor.

**TRANSPI'RE**, _verb intransitive_ To be emitted through the pores of the skin; to exhale; to pass off in insensible perspiration; as, fluids _transpire_ from the human body.

**1.** To escape from secrecy; to become public. The proceedings of the council have not yet transpired.

**2.** To happen or come to pass.
