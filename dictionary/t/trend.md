# Trend

**TREND**, _verb intransitive_ \[This word seems to be allied to trundle, or to run.\]

To run; to stretch; to tend; to have a particular direction; as, the shore of the sea trends to the southwest.

**TREND**, _noun_ That part of the stock of an anchor from which the size is taken.

**TREND**, _verb transitive_ In rural economy, to free wool from its filth. \[Local.\]
