# Tippling-house

**TIP'PLING-HOUSE**, _noun_ \[tipple and house.\] A house in which liquors are sold in drams or small quantities, and where men are accustomed to spend their time and money in excessive drinking.

**TIP'-STAFF**, _noun_ \[tip and staff.\] An officer who bears a staff tipped with metal; a constable.

**1.** A staff tipped with metal.
