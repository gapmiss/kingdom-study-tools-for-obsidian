# Together

**TOGETH'ER**, _adverb_

**1.** In company. We walked _together_ to the wood.

**2.** In or into union.

The king joined humanity and policy _together_

**3.** In the same place; as, to live _together_ in one house.

**4.** In the same time; as, to live _together_ in the same age.

**5.** In concert; as, the allies made war upon France _together_

**6.** Into junction or a state of union; as, to sew, knit, pin or fasten two things together; to mix things _together_

Together with, in union with; in company or mixture with.

Take the bad _together_ with the good.
