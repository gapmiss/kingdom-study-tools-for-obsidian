# Tragic

**TRAGIC**

TRAG'ICAL, _adjective_ \[Latin tragicus.\]

**1.** Pertaining to tragedy; of the nature or character of tragedy; as a _tragic_ poem; _tragic_ play or representation.

**2.** Fatal to life; mournful; sorrowful; calamitous; as the _tragic_ scenes of Hayti the _tragic_ horrors of Scio and Missilonghi; the tragical fate of the Greeks.

**3.** Mournful; expressive of tragedy, the loss of life, or of sorrow.
