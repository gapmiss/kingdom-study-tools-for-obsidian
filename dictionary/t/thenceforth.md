# Thenceforth

**THENCEFORTH**, _adverb_ thens'forth. \[thence and forth.\] From that time.

If the salt hath lost its savor, it is _thenceforth_ good for nothing. [[Matthew 5#13|Matthew 5:13]].

This is also preceded by from, though not from any necessity.

And from _thenceforth_ Pilate sought to release him. [[John 19#12|John 19:12]].
