# Thesis

**THE'SIS**, _noun_ \[Latin thesis; Gr. a position, to set.\]

**1.** A position or proposition which a person advances and offers to maintain, or which is actually maintained by argument; a theme; a subject.

**2.** In logic, every proposition may be divided into _thesis_ and hypothesis. _thesis_ contains the thing affirmed or denied, and hypothesis the conditions of the affirmation or negation.
