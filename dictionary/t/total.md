# Total

**TO'TAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin totalis, totus.\]

**1.** Whole; full; complete; as _total_ darkness; a _total_ departure from the evidence; a _total_ loss; the _total_ sum or amount.

**2.** Whole; not divided.

\--Myself the _total_ crime.

**TO'TAL**, _noun_ The whole; the whole sum or amount. These sums added, make the grand _total_ of five millions.
