# Tabor

**TA'BOR**, _noun_ \[Eng. tap.\] A small drum used as an accompaniment to a pipe or fife.

**TA'BOR**, _verb intransitive_ To strike lightly and frequently.

Her maids shall lead her as with the voice of doves, taboring upon their breasts. Nahum 2.

**1.** To play on a _tabor_ or little drum.
