# Top-chain

**TOP'-CHAIN**, _noun_ In ships, a chain to sling the lower yards in time of action, to prevent their falling when the ropes by which they are hung, are shot away.

**TOP'-CLOTH**, _noun_ In ships, a piece of canvas used to cover the hammocks which are lashed to the top in action.

**TOP'-DRAINING**, _noun_ The act or practice of draining the surface of land.
