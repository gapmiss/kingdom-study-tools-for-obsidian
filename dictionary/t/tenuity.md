# Tenuity

**TENU'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin tenuitas, from tenuis, thin. See [[Thin]].\]

**1.** Thinness, smallness in diameter; exility; thinness, applied to a broad substance, and slenderness, applied to one that is long; as the _tenuity_ of paper or of a leaf; the _tenuity_ of a hair or filament.

**2.** Rarity; rareness; thinness; as of a fluid; as the _tenuity_ of the air in the higher regions of the atmosphere; the _tenuity_ of the blood.

**3.** Poverty. \[Not in use.\]
