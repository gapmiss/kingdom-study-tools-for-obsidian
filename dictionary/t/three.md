# Three

**THREE**, _adjective_ \[Latin tres.\]

**1.** Two and one.

I offer thee _three_ things. [[2 Samuel 24#12|2 Samuel 24:12]].

**2.** It is often used like other adjectives, without the noun to which it refers.

Abishai--attained not to the first _three_ [[2 Samuel 23#9|2 Samuel 23:9]].

**3.** Proverbially, a small number.

Away, thou three-inched fool. \[I believe obsolete.\]
