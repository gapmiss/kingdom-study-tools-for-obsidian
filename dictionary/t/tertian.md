# Tertian

**TER'TIAN**,_adjective_ \[Latin tertianus, from tertius, third.\]

Occurring every other day; as a _tertian_ fever.

**TER'TIAN**, _noun_ A disease or fever whose paroxysms return every other day; an intermittent occurring after intervals of about forty eight hours.

**1.** A measure of 84 gallons, the third part of a tun.
