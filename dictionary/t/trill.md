# Trill

**TRILL**, _noun_ A quaver; a shake of the voice in singing, or of the sound of an instrument. \[See [[Shake]].\]

**TRILL**, _verb transitive_ To utter with a quavering or tremulousness of voice; to shake.

The sober-suited songstress trills her lay.

**TRILL**, _verb intransitive_ To flow in a small stream, or in drops rapidly succeeding each other; to trickle.

And now and then an ample tear trill'd down

Her delicate cheek.

**1.** To shake or quaver; to play in tremulous vibrations of sound.

To judge of trilling notes and tripping feet.
