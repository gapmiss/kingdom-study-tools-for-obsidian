# Tipple

**TIP'PLE**, _verb intransitive_ To drink spiritus or strong liquors habitually; to indulge in the frequent and improper use of spiritus liquors. When a man begins to _tipple_ let his creditors secure their debts.

**TIP'PLE**, _verb transitive_ To drink, as strong liquors, in luxury or excess.

\--Himself for saving charges.

A peel'd, slic'd onion eats, and tipples verjuice.

**TIP'PLE**, _noun_ Drink; liquor taken in tippling.
