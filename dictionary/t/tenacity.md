# Tenacity

**TENAC'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin tenacitas, from teneo, to hold.\]

**1.** Adhesiveness; that quality of bodies which makes them stick or adhere to others; glutinousness; stickiness; as the _tenacity_ of oils, of glue, of tar, of starch and the like.

**2.** That quality of bodies which keeps them from parting; without considerable force; cohesiveness; the effect of attraction; opposed to brittleness or fragility.
