# Treacle

**TRE'ACLE**, _noun_ \[Latin theriaca; Gr. a wild beast.\]

**1.** The spume of sugar in sugar refineries. _treacle_ is obtained in refining sugar; molasses is the drainings of crude sugar. _treacle_ however is often used for molasses.

**2.** A saccharine fluid, consisting of the inspissated juices or decoctions of certain vegetables, as the sap of the birch, sycamore, etc.

**3.** A medicinal compound of various ingredients. \[See [[Theriaca]].\]
