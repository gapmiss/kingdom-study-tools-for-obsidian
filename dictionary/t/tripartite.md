# Tripartite

**TRIP'ARTITE**, _adjective_ \[Latin tripartitus; tres, three, and partitus, divided; partior.\]

**1.** Divided into three parts. In botany, a _tripartite_ leaf is one which is divided into three parts down to the base, but not wholly separate.

**2.** Having three corresponding parts or copies; as indentures _tripartite_
