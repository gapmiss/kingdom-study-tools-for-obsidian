# Townsman

**TOWNS'MAN**, _noun_ \[town and man.\] An inhabitant of a place; or one of the same town with another.

**1.** A selectman; an officer of the town in New England, who assists in managing the affairs of the town. \[See [[Selectmen]].\]

**TOWN'-TALK**, _noun_ \[town and talk.\] The common talk of a place, or the subject of common conversation.
