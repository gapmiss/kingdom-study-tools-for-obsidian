# Tough

**TOUGH**, _adjective_ tuf.

**1.** Having the quality of flexibility without brittleness; yielding to force without breaking. The ligaments of animals and of India rubber are remarkably _tough_ _tough_ timber, like young ash, is the most proper for the shafts and springs of a carriage.

**2.** Firm; strong; not easily broken; able to endure hardship; as an animal of a _tough_ frame.

**3.** Not easily separated; viscous; clammy; tenacious; ropy; as _tough_ phlegm.

**4.** Stiff; not flexible.
