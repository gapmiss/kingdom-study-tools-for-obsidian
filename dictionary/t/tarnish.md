# Tarnish

**T'ARNISH**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To sully; to soil by an alteration induced by the air, or by dust and the like; to diminish or destroy luster; as, to _tarnish_ a metal; to _tarnish_ gilding; to _tarnish_ the brightness or beauty of color.

**2.** To diminish or destroy the purity of; as to _tarnish_ reputation or honor.

**T'ARNISH**, _verb intransitive_ To lose luster; to become dull; as, polished substances of gilding will _tarnish_ in the course of time. Metals _tarnish_ by oxydation.
