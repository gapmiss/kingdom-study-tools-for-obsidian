# Troll

**TROLL**, _verb transitive_ To move in a circular direction; to roll; to move volubly; to turn; to drive about.

They learn to roll the eye, and _troll_ the tongue.

**TROLL** about the bridal bow.

**TROLL**, _verb intransitive_ To roll; to run about; as, to _troll_ in a coach and six.

**1.** Among anglers, to fish for pikes with a rod whose line runs on a wheel or pulley.
