# Tick

**TICK**, _noun_ Credit; trust; as, to buy upon _tick_

**TICK**, _noun_ A little animal of a livid color and globose-ovate form, that infests sheep, dogs, goats, cows, etc., a species of Acarus.

**TICK**, _noun_ \[Latin tego; Eng. to deck.\] The cover or case of a bed, which contains the feathers, wool or other material.

**TICK**, _verb intransitive_ \[from _tick_ credit.\] To run upon score.

**1.** To trust.

**TICK**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin tango, tago.\] To beat; to pat; or to make a small noise by beating or otherwise; as a watch.
