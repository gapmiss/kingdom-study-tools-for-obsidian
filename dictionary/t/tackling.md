# Tackling

**TACK'LING**, _participle present tense_ Harnessing; putting on harness; seizing; falling on.

**TACK'LING**, _noun_ Furniture of the masts and yards of a ship, as cordage, sails, etc.

**1.** Instruments of action; as fishing _tackling_

**2.** Harness; the instruments of drawing a carriage.
