# Three-parted

**THREE'-P'ARTED**, _adjective_ \[three and parted.\] Tripartite. A _three-parted_ leaf, is divided into three parts down to the base, but not entirely separate.

**THREE'-PENCE**, _noun_ thrip'ence. \[three and pence.\]

A small silver coin of three times the value of a penny.

**THREE'-PENNY**, _adjective_ thrip'enny. Worth three pence only; mean.
