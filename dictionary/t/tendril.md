# Tendril

**TEN'DRIL**, _noun_ A clasp or clasper of a vine or other climbing or creeping plant; a filiform spiral shoot, that winds round another body. Tendrils or claspers are given to plants that have weak stalks.

They are also given to creeping vines, which require support on the earth.

**TEN'DRIL**, _adjective_ Clasping; climbing; as a _tendril_
