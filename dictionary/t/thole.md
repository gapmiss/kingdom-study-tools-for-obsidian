# Thole

**THOLE**, _noun_

**1.** A pin inserted into the gunwale of a boat, to keep the oar in the row-lock, when used in rowing.

**2.** The pin or handle of a sythe-snath.

**THOLE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin tollo, tolero.\]

To bear; to endure; to undergo.

**THOLE**, _verb intransitive_ \[supra.\] To wait. \[Local.\]

**THOLE**, _noun_ \[Latin tholus.\] The roof of a temple. \[Not used or local.\]
