# Troublesome

**TROUBLESOME**, _adjective_ trub'lsome. Giving trouble or disturbance; molesting; annoying; vexatious. In warm climates, insects are very _troublesome_

**1.** Burdensome; tiresome; wearisome.

My mother will never be _troublesome_ to me.

**2.** Giving inconvenience to. I wish not to be _troublesome_ as a guest.

**3.** Teasing; importunate; as a _troublesome_ applicant.
