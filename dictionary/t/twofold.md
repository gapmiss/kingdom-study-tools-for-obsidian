# Twofold

**TWOFOLD**, _adjective_ \[two and fold.\] Two of the same kind, or two different things existing together; as _twofold_ nature; a _twofold_ sense; a _twofold_ argument.

**1.** Double; as _twofold_ strength or desire.

**2.** In botany, two and two together, growing form the same place; as _twofold_ leaves.

**TWOFOLD**, _adverb_ Doubly; in a double degree. [[Matthew 23#15|Matthew 23:15]].
