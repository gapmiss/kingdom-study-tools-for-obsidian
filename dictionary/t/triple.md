# Triple

**TRIP'LE**, _adjective_ \[Latin triplex, triplus; tres and plico, to fold.\]

**1.** Threefold; consisting of three united; as a _triple_ knot; a _triple_ tie.

By thy _triple_ shape as thou are seen--

**2.** Treble; three times repeated. \[See [[Treble]].\]

Triple time, in music, is that in which each bar is divided into three measures or equal parts, as three minims, three crotchets, three quavers, etc.

**TRIP'LE**, _verb transitive_ To treble; to make threefold or thrice as much or as many. \[Usually written treble.\]
