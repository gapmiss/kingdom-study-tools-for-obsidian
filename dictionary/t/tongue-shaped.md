# Tongue-shaped

**TONGUE-SHAPED**

TUNG'-SHAPED, _adjective_ In botany, a _tongue-shaped_ leaf, is linear and fleshy, blunt at the end, convex underneath, and having usually a cartilaginous border.

**TONGUE-TIE'-TIE**, _verb transitive_ \[tongue and tie.\] To deprive of speech or the power of speech, or of distinct articulation.
