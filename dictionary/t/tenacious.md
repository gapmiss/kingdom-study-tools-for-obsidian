# Tenacious

**TENA'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin tenax, from teneo, to hold.\]

**1.** Holding fast, or inclined to hold fast; inclined to retain what is in possession; as men _tenacious_ of their just rights. Men are usually _tenacious_ of their opinions, as well as of their property.

**2.** Retentive; apt to retain long what is committed to it; as a _tenacious_ memory.

**3.** Adhesive; apt to adhere to another substance; as oily, glutinous or viscous matter. Few substances are to _tenacious_ as tar.

**4.** Niggardly; close fisted.
