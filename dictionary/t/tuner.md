# Tuner

**TU'NER**, _noun_ One who tunes.

**1.** One whose occupation is to tune musical instruments.

**TUNG**, _noun_ A name given by the Indians to a small insect, called by the Spaniards pique, which inserts its eggs within the human skin; an insect very troublesome in the East and West Indies.

**TUNG**, _noun_ In man, the instrument of taste, and the chief instrument of speech. \[See [[Tongue]].\]
