# Tore

**TORE**, _preterit tense_ of tear. He _tore_ his robe.

**TORE**, _noun_ The dead grass that remains on mowing land in winter and spring. \[Used in New England.\]

**TORE**, _noun_ \[Latin torus.\] In architecture, a large round molding on the base of a column. It is distinguished from the astragali by its size. The bases of the Tuscan and Doric columns have only one _tore_ which is between the plinth and listel. In the Attic base there are two.
