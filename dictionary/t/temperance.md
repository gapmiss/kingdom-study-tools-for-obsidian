# Temperance

**TEM'PERANCE**, _noun_ \[Latin temperantia, from tempero.\]

**1.** Moderation; particularly, habitual moderation in regard to the indulgence of the natural appetites and passions; restrained or moderate indulgence; as _temperance_ in eating and drinking; _temperance_ in the indulgence of joy or mirth. _temperance_ in eating and drinking is opposed to gluttony and drunkenness, and in other indulgences, to excess.

**2.** Patience; calmness; sedateness; moderation of passion.

He calm'd his wrath with goodly _temperance_ \[Unusual.\]
