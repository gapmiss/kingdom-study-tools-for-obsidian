# Turbulence

**TUR'BULENCE**

TUR'BULENCY, _noun_ \[See [[Turbulent]].\] A disturbed state; tumult; confusion; as the _turbulence_ of the times; _turbulence_ in political affairs.

**1.** Disorder or tumult of the passions; as _turbulence_ of mind.

**2.** Agitation; tumultuousness; as _turbulence_ of blood.

**3.** Disposition to resist authority; insubordination; as the _turbulence_ of subjects.
