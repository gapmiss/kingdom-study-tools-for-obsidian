# Treacher

**TRE'ACHER**

TREACHEROUS, _adjective_ trech'erous. \[See [[Treachery]].\] Violating allegiance of faith pledged; faithless; traitorous to the state or sovereign; perfidious in private life; betraying a trust. A man may be treacherous to his country, or treacherous to his friend, by violating his engagements or his faith pledged.
