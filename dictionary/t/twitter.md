# Twitter

**TWIT'TER**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To make a succession of small, tremulous, intermitted noises; as, the swallow twitters.

**2.** To make the sound of a half suppressed laugh.

**TWIT'TER**, _noun_ \[from twit.\] One who twits or reproaches.

**TWIT'TER**, _noun_ A small intermitted noise, as in half suppressed laughter; or the sound of a swallow.
