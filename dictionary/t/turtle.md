# Turtle

**TUR'TLE**, _noun_ \[Latin turtur.\]

**1.** A fowl of the genus Columba; called also the _turtle_ dove, and _turtle_ pigeon. It is a wild species, frequenting the thickest parts of the woods, and its note is plaintive and tender.

**2.** The name sometimes given to the common tortoise.

**3.** The name given to the large sea-tortoise.
