# Tenderness

**TEN'DERNESS**, _noun_ The state of being tender or easily broken, bruised or injured; softness; brittleness; as the _tenderness_ of a thread; the _tenderness_ of flesh.

**1.** The state of being easily hurt; soreness; as the _tenderness_ of flesh when bruised or inflamed.

**2.** Susceptibility of the softer passions; sensibility.

Well we know your _tenderness_ of heart.

**3.** Kind attention; anxiety for the good of another, or to save him from pain.

**4.** Scrupulousness; caution; extreme care or concern not to give or to commit offense; as _tenderness_ of conscience.

**5.** Cautious care to preserve or not to injure; as a _tenderness_ of reputation.

**6.** Softness of expression; pathos.
