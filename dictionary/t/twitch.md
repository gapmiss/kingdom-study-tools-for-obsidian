# Twitch

**TWITCH**, _verb transitive_ To pull with a sudden jerk; to pluck with a short, quick motion; to snatch; as, to _twitch_ one by the sleeve; to _twitch_ a thing out of another's hand; to _twitch_ off clusters of grapes.

**TWITCH**, _noun_ A pull with a jerk; a short, sudden, quick pull; as a _twitch_ by the sleeve.

**1.** A short spasmodic contraction of the fibers or muscles; as a _twitch_ in the side; convulsive twitches.
