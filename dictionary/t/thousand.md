# Thousand

**THOU'SAND**, _adjective_ s as z.

**1.** Denoting the number of ten hundred.

**2.** Proverbially, denoting a great number indefinitely. It is a _thousand_ chances to one that you succeed.

**THOU'SAND**, _noun_ The number of ten hundred.

A _thousand_ shall fall at thy side, and ten _thousand_ at thy right hand. [[Psalms 91#7|Psalms 91:7]].

Thousand is sometimes used plurally without the plural termination, as in the passage above, ten thousand; but it often takes the plural termination. In former times, how many thousands perished by famine!
