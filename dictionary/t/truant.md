# Truant

**TRU'ANT**, _adjective_ Idle; wandering from business; loitering; as a _truant_ boy.

While _truant_ Jove, in infant price,

Play'd barefoot on Olympus'side.

**TRU'ANT**, _noun_ An idler; an idle boy.

**TRU'ANT**. _verb intransitive_ To idle away time; to loiter or be absent from employment.
