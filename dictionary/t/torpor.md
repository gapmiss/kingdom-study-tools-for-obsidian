# Torpor

**TOR'POR**, _noun_ \[Latin\] Numbness; inactivity; loss of motion, or of the power of motion. _torpor_ may amount to a total loss of sensation, or complete insensibility. It may however be applied to the state of a living body which has not lost all power of feeling and motion.

**1.** Dullness; laziness; sluggishness; stupidity.
