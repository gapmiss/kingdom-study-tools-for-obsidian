# Turbulent

**TUR'BULENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin turbulentus, from turbo, to disturb.\]

**1.** Disturbed; agitated; tumultuous; being in violent commotion; as the _turbulent_ ocean.

Calm region once,

And full of peace, now tost and _turbulent_

The _turbulent_ mirth of wine.

**2.** Restless; unquiet; refractory; disposed to insubordination and disorder; as _turbulent_ spirits.

**3.** Producing commotion.

Whose heads that _turbulent_ liquor fills with fumes.
