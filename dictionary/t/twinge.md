# Twinge

**TWINGE**, _verb transitive_ twinj.

**1.** To affect with a sharp sudden pain; to torment with pinching or sharp pains.

The gnat twinged the lion till he made him tear himself, and so he mastered him.

**2.** To pinch; to tweak; to pull with a jerk; as, to _twinge_ one by the ears and nose.

**TWINGE**, _verb transitive_ twinj. to have a sudden, sharp, local pain, like a twitch; to suffer a keen spasmodic or shooting pain; as, the side twinges. \[This is the sense in which this word is generally used within the limits of my acquaintance.

**TWINGE**, _noun_ twinj. A sudden sharp pain; a darting local pain of momentary continuance; as a _twinge_ in the arm or side.

**1.** A sharp rebuke of conscience.

**2.** A pinch; a tweak; as a _twinge_ of the ear.
