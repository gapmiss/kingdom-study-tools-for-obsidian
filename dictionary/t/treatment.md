# Treatment

**TRE'ATMENT**, _noun_ Management; manipulation; manner of mixing or combining, of decomposing and the like; as the _treatment_ of substances in chimical experiments.

**1.** Usage; manner of using; good of bad behavior towards.

Accept such _treatment_ as a swain affords.

**2.** Manner of applying remedies to cure; mode or course pursued to check and destroy; as the _treatment_ of a disease.

**3.** Manner of applying remedies to; as the _treatment_ of a patient.
