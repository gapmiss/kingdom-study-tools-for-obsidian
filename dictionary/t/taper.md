# Taper

**TA'PER**, _noun_ A small wax candle; a small lighted wax candle, or a small light.

Get me a _taper_ in my study, Lucius.

**TA'PER**, _adjective_ \[supposed to be from the form of a _taper_ \]

Regularly narrowed towards the point; becoming small towards one end; conical; pyramidical; as _taper_ fingers.

**TA'PER**, _verb intransitive_ To diminish or become gradually smaller towards one end; as, a sugar loaf tapers towards a point.

**TA'PER**, _verb transitive_ To make gradually smaller in diameter.
