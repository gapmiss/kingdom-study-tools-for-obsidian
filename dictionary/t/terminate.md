# Terminate

**TERM'INATE**, _verb transitive_ \[termino; terminus.\]

**1.** To bound; to limit; to set the extreme point or side of a thing; as, to _terminate_ a surface by a line.

**2.** To end; to put an end to; as, to _terminate_ a controversy.

**TERM'INATE**, _verb intransitive_ To be limited; to end; to come to the furthest point in space; as, a line terminates at the equator; the torrid zone terminates at the tropics.

**1.** To end; to close; to come to a limit in time. The session of congress, every second year, must _terminate_ on the third of March.

The wisdom of this world, its designs and efficacy, _terminate_ on this side heaven.
