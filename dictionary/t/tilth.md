# Tilth

**TILTH**, _noun_ That which is tilled; tillage ground. \[Not in use.\]

**1.** The state of being tilled or prepared for a crop. We say, land is in good _tilth_ when it is manured, plowed, broken and mellowed for receiving the seed. We say also, ground is in bad _tilth_ When we say, land is in _tilth_ we mean in good condition for the seed; not in _tilth_ in a bad condition.
