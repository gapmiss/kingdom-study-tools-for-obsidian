# Tint

**TINT**, _noun_ \[Latin tinctus, tingo. See [[Tinge]].\] A dye; a color, or rather a slight coloring or tincture distinct from the ground or principal color; as red with a blue _tint_ or _tint_ of blue. In painting, tints are the colors considered as more or less bright, deep or thin, by the due use and intermixture of which a picture receives its shades, softness and variety.

Or blend in beauteous _tint_ the color'd mass.

Their vigor sickens, and their tints decline.

**TINT**, _verb transitive_ To tinge; to give a slight coloring to.

**TIN'-WORM**, _noun_ \[tin and worm.\] An insect.
