# Tribulation

**TRIBULA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin tribulo, to thrash, to beat.\] Severe affliction; distresses of life; vexations. In Scripture, it often denotes the troubles and distresses which proceed from persecution.

When _tribulation_ or persecution ariseth because of the word, he is offended. [[Matthew 13#21|Matthew 13:21]].

In the world ye shall have _tribulation_ [[John 16#33|John 16:33]].
