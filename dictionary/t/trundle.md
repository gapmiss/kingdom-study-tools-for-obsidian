# Trundle

**TRUN'DLE**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To roll, as on little wheels; as, a bed trundles under another.

**2.** To roll; as a bowl.

**TRUN'DLE**, _verb transitive_ To roll, as a thing on little wheels; as, to _trundle_ a bed or a gun-carriage.

**TRUN'DLE**, _noun_ A round body; a little wheel, or a kind or low cart with small wooden wheels.
