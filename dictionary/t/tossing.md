# Tossing

**TOSS'ING**, _participle present tense_ Throwing upward with a jerk; raising suddenly; as the head.

**TOSS'ING**, _noun_ The act of throwing upward; a rising and falling suddenly; a rolling and tumbling.

Dire was the _tossing_ deep the groans.

**TOSS'-POT**, _noun_ \[toss and pot.\] A toper; one habitually given to strong drink.
