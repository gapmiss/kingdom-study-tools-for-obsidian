# Tenter

**TENT'ER**, _noun_ \[Latin tendo, tentus, to stretch.\]

A hook for stretching cloth on a frame.

To be on the tenters, to be on the stretch; to be in distress, uneasiness or suspense.

**TENT'ER**, _verb transitive_ To hang or stretch on tenters.

**TENT'ER**, _verb intransitive_ To admit extension.
