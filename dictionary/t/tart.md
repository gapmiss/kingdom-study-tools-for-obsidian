# Tart

**T'ART**, _adjective_

**1.** Acid; sharp to the taste; acidulous; as a _tart_ apple.

**2.** Sharp; keen; severe; as a _tart_ reply; _tart_ language; a _tart_ rebuke.

**T'ART**, _noun_ \[Latin tortus, twisted; and this may be the primary sense of tar, acid, sharp, and hence this noun, something acid or made of acid fruit.\] A species of pie or pastry, consisting of fruit baked on paste.
