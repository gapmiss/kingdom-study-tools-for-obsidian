# Thereupon

**THEREUPON**', _adverb_ \[there and upon.\] Upon that or this.

The remnant of the house of Judah, they shall feed _thereupon_ [[Zephaniah 2#7|Zephaniah 2:7]].

**1.** In consequence of that.

He hopes to find you forward,

And _thereupon_ he sends you this good news.

**2.** Immediately.
