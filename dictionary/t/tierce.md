# Tierce

**TIERCE**, _noun_ ters. A cask whose content is one third of a pipe, that is, forty gallons; or it may be the measure.

**1.** In Ireland, a weight by which provisions are sold. The _tierce_ of beef for the navy, is 304 lb. and for India, 336 lb.

**2.** In music, a third.

**3.** In gaming, a sequence of three cards of the same color.

**4.** A thrust in fencing.
