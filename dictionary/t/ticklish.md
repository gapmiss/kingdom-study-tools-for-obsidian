# Ticklish

**TICK'LISH**, _adjective_ Sensible to slight touches, easily tickled. The bottom of the foot is very _ticklish_ as are the sides. The palm of the hand, hardened by use, it not _ticklish_

**1.** Tottering; standing so as to be liable to totter and fall at the slightest touch; unfixed; easily moved or affected.

Ireland was a _ticklish_ and unsettled state.

**2.** Difficult; nice; critical; as, these are _ticklish_ times.
