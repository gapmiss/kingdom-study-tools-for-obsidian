# Tang

**TANG**, _noun_ \[Gr. rancor; rancid.\]

**1.** A strong taste; particularly, a taste of something extraneous to the thing itself; as, wine or cider has a _tang_ of the cask.

**2.** Relish; taste. \[Not elegant.\]

**3.** Something that leaves a sting or pain behind.

She had a tongue with a _tang_

**4.** Sound; tone. \[Not in use.\]

**TANG**, _verb intransitive_ To ring with. \[Not in use.\]

\[This may be allied to ding, dong.\]
