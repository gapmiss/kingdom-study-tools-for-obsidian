# Thievish

**THIE'VISH**, _adjective_ Given to stealing; addicted to the practice of theft; as a _thievish_ boy.

Or with a base and boist'rous sword enforce

A _thievish_ living on the common road.

**1.** Secret; sly; acting by stealth; as _thievish_ minutes.

**2.** Partaking of the nature of theft; as a _thievish_ practice.
