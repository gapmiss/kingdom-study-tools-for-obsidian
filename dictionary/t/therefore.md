# Therefore

**THEREFORE**, _adverb_ ther'fore. \[there and for.\]

**1.** For that; for that or this reason, referring to something previously stated.

I have married a wife, and _therefore_ I cannot come.

[[Luke 14#20|Luke 14:20]].

**2.** Consequently.

He blushes; _therefore_ he is guilty.

**3.** In return or recompense for this or that.

What shall we have therefore? [[Matthew 19#6|Matthew 19:6]].
