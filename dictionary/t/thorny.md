# Thorny

**THORN'Y**, _adjective_ Full of thorns or spines; rough with thorns; as a _thorny_ wood; a _thorny_ tree; a _thorny_ diadem or crown.

**1.** Troublesome; vexatious; harassing; perplexing; as _thorny_ care; the _thorny_ path of vice.

**2.** Sharp; pricking; vexatious; as _thorny_ points.

**THORN'Y REST-HARROW**, _noun_ A plant.
