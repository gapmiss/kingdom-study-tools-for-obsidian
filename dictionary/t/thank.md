# Thank

**THANK**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To express gratitude for a favor; to make acknowledgments to one for kindness bestowed.

We are bound to _thank_ God always for you. [[2 Thessalonians 1#3|2 Thessalonians 1:3]].

Joab bowed himself and thanked the king. [[2 Samuel 14#22|2 Samuel 14:22]].

**2.** It is used ironically.

Weigh the danger with the doubtful bliss,

And _thank_ yourself, if aught should fall amiss.

**THANK**
