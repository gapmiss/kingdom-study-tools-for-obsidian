# Thirsty

**THIRST'Y**, _adjective_ \[from thirst.\] Feeling a painful sensation of the throat or fauces for want of drink.

Give me a little water, for I am _thirsty_ [[Judges 4#19|Judges 4:19]].

I was _thirsty_ and ye gave me no drink. [[Matthew 25#35|Matthew 25:35]].

**1.** Very dry; having no moisture; parched.

The _thirsty_ land shall become springs of water. [[Isaiah 35#7|Isaiah 35:7]].

**2.** Having a vehement desire of any thing; as in blood-thirsty. [[Isaiah 44#1|Isaiah 44:1]].
