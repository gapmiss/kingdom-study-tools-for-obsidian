# Tinkle

**TINK'LE**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To make small quick sharp sounds, as by striking on metal; to clink.

\--And have not charity, I am become as sounding brass, or a tinkling cymbal. [[1 Corinthians 13#1|1 Corinthians 13:1]]. [[Isaiah 3#16|Isaiah 3:16]].

The sprightly horse

Moves to the music of his tinkling bells.

The moment the money tinkles in the chest, the soul mounts out of purgatory.

**2.** To hear a small sharp sound.

And his ears tinkled, and his color fled.

**TINK'LE**, _verb transitive_ To cause to clink or make sharp quick sounds.
