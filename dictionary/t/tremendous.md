# Tremendous

**TREMEN'DOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin tremendus, from tremo, to tremble.\]

**1.** Such as may excite fear or terror; terrible; dreadful. Hence,

**2.** Violent; such as may astonish by its force and violence; as a _tremendous_ wind; a _tremendous_ shower; a _tremendous_ shock or fall; a _tremendous_ noise.
