# Thither

**THITH'ER**, _adverb_ To that place; opposed to hither.

This city is near, O let me escape _thither_ [[Genesis 19#20|Genesis 19:20]].

Where I am, _thither_ ye cannot come. [[John 7#34|John 7:34]].

**1.** To that end or point.

Hither and _thither_ to this place and to that; one way and another.
