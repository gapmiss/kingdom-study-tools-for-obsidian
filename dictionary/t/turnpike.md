# Turnpike

**TURN'PIKE**, _noun_ \[turn and pike.\] Strictly, a frame consisting of two bars crossing each other at right angles, and turning on a post or pin, to hinder the passage of breasts, but admitting a person to pass between the arms.

**1.** A gate set across a road to stop travelers and carriages till toll is paid for keeping the road in repair.

**2.** A _turnpike_ road.

**3.** In military affairs, a beam filled with spikes to obstruct passage.

**TURN'PIKE**, _verb transitive_ To form, as a road, in the manner of a _turnpike_ road; to throw the path of a road into a rounded form.
