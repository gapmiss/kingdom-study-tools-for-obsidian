# Traduction

**TRADUC'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin traductio.\] Derivation from one of the same kind; propagation.

If by _traduction_ came thy mind,

Our wonder is the less to find

A soul so charming from a stock so good.

**1.** Tradition; transmission from one to another; as traditional communication and _traduction_ of truth. \[Little used.\]

**2.** Conveyance; transportation; act of transferring; as the _traduction_ of animals from Europe to America by shipping.

**3.** Transition.
