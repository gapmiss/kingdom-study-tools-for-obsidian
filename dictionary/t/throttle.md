# Throttle

**THROT'TLE**, _noun_ \[from throat.\] The windpipe or larynx.

**THROT'TLE**, _verb intransitive_ To choke; to suffocate; or to obstruct so as to endanger suffocation.

**1.** To breathe hard, as when nearly suffocated.

**THROT'TLE**, _verb transitive_ To utter with breaks and interruptions, as a person half suffocated.

Throttle their practic'd accents in their fears.
