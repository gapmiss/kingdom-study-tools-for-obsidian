# Trappings

**TRAP'PINGS**, _noun_ _plural_ \[from trap. The primary sense is that which is set, spread or put on.\]

**1.** Ornaments of horse furniture.

Caparisons and steeds,

Bases and tinsel trappings--

**2.** Ornaments; dress; external and superficial decorations.

These but the _trappings_ and the suits of woe.

Trappings of life, for ornament, not use.

Affectation is part of the _trappings_ of folly.
