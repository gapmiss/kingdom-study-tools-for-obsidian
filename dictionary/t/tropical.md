# Tropical

**TROP'ICAL**, _adjective_ Pertaining to the tropics; being within the tropics; as _tropical_ climates; _tropical_ latitudes; _tropical_ heat; _tropical_ winds.

**1.** Incident to the tropics; as _tropical_ diseases.

**2.** \[from trope.\] Figurative; rhetorically changed from its proper or original sense.

The foundation of all parables is some analogy or similitude between the _tropical_ or allusive part of the parable, and the thing intended by it.

Tropical writing or hieroglyphic, is such as represents a thing by qualities which resemble it.
