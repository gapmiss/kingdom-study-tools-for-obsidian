# Transgress

**TRANSGRESS**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin transgressus, transgredior; trans and gradior, to pass.\]

**1.** To pass over or beyond any limit; to surpass.

**2.** In a moral sense, to overpass any rule prescribed as the limit of duty; to break or violate a law, civil or moral. To _transgress_ a divine law, is sin. Legislators should not _transgress_ laws of their own making.

**TRANSGRESS'**, _verb intransitive_ To offend by violating a law; to sin.
