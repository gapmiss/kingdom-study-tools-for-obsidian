# Tidy

**TI'DY**, _adjective_ \[from tide, time, season.\]

**1.** In its primary sense, seasonable; favorable; being in proper time; as weather fair and _tidy_

**2.** Neat; dressed with neat simplicity; as a _tidy_ lass; the children are tidy; their dress is tidy; that is primarily, proper for the time or occasion.

**3.** Neat; being in good order. The apartments are well furnished and _tidy_
