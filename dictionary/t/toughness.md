# Toughness

**TOUGHNESS**, _noun_ tuf'ness. The quality of a substance which renders it in some degree flexible, without brittleness or liability to fracture; flexibility with a firm adhesion of parts; as the _toughness_ of steel.

**1.** Viscosity; tenacity; clamminess; glutinousness; as the _toughness_ of mucus.

**2.** Firmness; strength of constitution or texture.
