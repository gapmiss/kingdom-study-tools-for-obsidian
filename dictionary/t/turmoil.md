# Turmoil

**TURMOIL**', _noun_ \[I know not the origin of this word; but it is probably from the root of the Latin turba, turbo, turma, or of turn.\]

Disturbance, tumult; harassing labor; trouble; molestation by tumult.

There I'll rest, as after much turmoil

A blessed soul doth in Elysium.

**TURMOIL'**, _verb transitive_ To harass with commotion.

It is her fatal misfortune--to be miserably tossed and turmoiled with these storms of affliction.

**1.** To disquiet; to weary.

**TURMOIL'**, _verb intransitive_ To be disquieted; to be in commotion.
