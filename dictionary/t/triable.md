# Triable

**TRI'ABLE**, _adjective_ \[from try.\] That may be tried; that may be subjected to trial or test.

**1.** That may undergo a judicial examination; that may properly come under the cognizance of a court. A cause may be _triable_ before one court, which is not _triable_ in another. In England, testamentary causes are _triable_ in the ecclesiastical courts.
