# Transmission

**TRANSMIS'SION**, _noun_ \[Latin transmissio.\]

**1.** The act of sending from one place or person to another; as the _transmission_ of letters, writings, papers, news and the like, from one country to another; or the _transmission_ of rights, titles or privileges from father to son, and from one generation to another.

**2.** The passing of a substance through any body, as of light through glass.
