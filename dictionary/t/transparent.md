# Transparent

**TRANSPA'RENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin trans and pareo, to appear.\]

**1.** Having the property of transmitting rays of light so that bodies can be distinctly seen through; pervious to light; diaphanous; pellucid; as _transparent_ glass; a _transparent_ diamond; opposed to opake.

**2.** Admitting the passage of light; open porous; as a _transparent_ vail.
