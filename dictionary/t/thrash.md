# Thrash

**THRASH**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To beat out grain from the husk or pericarp with a flail; as, to _thrash_ wheat, rye or oats.

**2.** To beat corn off from the cob or spike; as, to _thrash_ maiz.

**3.** To beat soundly with a stick or whip; to drub.

**THRASH**, _verb intransitive_ To practice thrashing; to perform the business of thrashing; as a man who thrashes well.

**1.** To labor; to drudge.
