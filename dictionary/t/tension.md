# Tension

**TEN'SION**, _noun_ \[Latin tensio, tendo.\]

**1.** The act of stretching or straining; as the _tension_ of the muscles.

**2.** The state of being stretched or strained to stiffness; or the state of being bent or strained; as, different degrees of _tension_ in chords give different sounds; the greater the _tension_ the more acute the sound.

**3.** Distension.
