# Tawdry

**TAW'DRY**, _adjective_ Very fine and showy in colors without taste or elegance; having an excess of showy ornaments without grace; as a _tawdry_ dress; _tawdry_ feathers; _tawdry_ colors.

He rails from morning to night at essenced fops and _tawdry_ courtiers.

**TAW'DRY**, _noun_ A slight ornament.
