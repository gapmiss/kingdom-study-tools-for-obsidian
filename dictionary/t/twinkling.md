# Twinkling

**TWIN'KLING**, _noun_ A sparkling; a shining with intermitted light; as the _twinkling_ of the stars.

**1.** A motion of the eye.

**2.** A moment; an instant; the time of a wink.

In a moment, in the _twinkling_ of an eye, at the last trump--the dead shall be raised incorruptible. [[1 Corinthians 15#52|1 Corinthians 15:52]].

**TWIN'KLING**, _participle present tense_ Sparkling.
