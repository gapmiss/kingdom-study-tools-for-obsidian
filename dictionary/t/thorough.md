# Thorough

**THOROUGH**, _adjective_ thur'ro.

**1.** Literally, passing through or to the end; hence, complete; perfect; as a _thorough_ reformation; _thorough_ work; a _thorough_ translator; a _thorough_ poet.

**2.** Passing through; as _thorough_ lights in a house.

**THOROUGH**, _preposition_ thur'ro. From side to side, or from end to end.

**1.** By means of. \[Not now used.\] \[See [[Through]].\]

**THOROUGH**, _noun_ thur'ro. An inter-furrow between two ridges.
