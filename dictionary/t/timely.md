# Timely

**TI'MELY**, _adjective_ Seasonable; being in good time; sufficiently early. The defendant had _timely_ notice of this motion. _timely_ care will often prevent great evils.

**1.** Keeping time or measure. \[Not used.\]

**TI'MELY**, _adverb_ Early; soon; in good season.

Timely advis'd, the coming evil shun.
