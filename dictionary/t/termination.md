# Termination

**TERMINA'TION**, _noun_ The act of limiting or setting bounds; the act of ending or concluding.

**1.** Bound; limit in space or extent; as the _termination_ of a line.

**2.** End in time or existence; as the _termination_ of the year or of life; the _termination_ of happiness.

**3.** In grammar, the end or ending of a word; the syllable or letter that ends a word. Words have different terminations to express number, time and sex.

**4.** End; conclusion; result.

**5.** Last purpose.

**6.** Word; term. \[Not in use.\]
