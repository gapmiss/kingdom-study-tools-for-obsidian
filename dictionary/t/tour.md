# Tour

**TOUR**, _noun_

**1.** Literally, a going round; hence, a journey in a circuit; as the _tour_ of Europe; the _tour_ of France or England.

**2.** A turn; a revolution; as the tours of the heavenly bodies. \[Not now in use.\]

**3.** A turn; as a _tour_ of duty; a military use of the word.

**4.** A tress or circular border of hair on the head, worn sometimes by both sexes.

**5.** A tower. \[Not in use.\]
