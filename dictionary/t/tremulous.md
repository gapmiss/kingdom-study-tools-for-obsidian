# Tremulous

**TREM'ULOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin tremulus, from tremo, to tremble.\]

**1.** Trembling; affected with fear or timidity; as a trembling christian.

**2.** Shaking; shivering; quivering; as a _tremulous_ limb; a _tremulous_ motion of the hand or the lips; the _tremulous_ leaf of the poplar.
