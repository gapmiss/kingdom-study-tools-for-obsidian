# Tuft

**TUFT**, _noun_

**1.** A collection of small things in a knot or bunch; as a _tuft_ of flowers; a _tuft_ of feathers; a _tuft_ of grass or hair. A _tuft_ of feathers forms the crest of a bird..

**2.** A cluster, a clump; as a _tuft_ of trees; a _tuft_ of olives.

**3.** In botany, a head of flowers, each elevated on a partial stalk, and all forming together a dense roundish mass. The word is sometimes applied to other collections, as little bundles of leaves, hairs and the like.

**TUFT**, _verb transitive_ To separate into tufts.

**1.** To adorn with tufts or with a _tuft_
