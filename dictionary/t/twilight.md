# Twilight

**TWI'LIGHT**, _noun_

**1.** The faint light which is reflected upon the earth after sunset and before sunrise; crepuscular light. In latitudes remote from the equator, the _twilight_ is of much longer duration than at and near the equator.

**2.** Dubious or uncertain view; as the _twilight_ or probability.

**TWI'LIGHT**, _adjective_ Obscure; imperfectly illuminated; shaded.

O'er the _twilight_ groves and dusky caves.
