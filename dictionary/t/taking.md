# Taking

**TA'KING**, _participle present tense_ Receiving; catching; getting possession; apprehending.

**1.** _adjective_ Alluring; attracting.

**TA'KING**, _noun_ The act of gaining possession; a seizing; seizure; apprehension.

**1.** Agitation; distress of mind.
