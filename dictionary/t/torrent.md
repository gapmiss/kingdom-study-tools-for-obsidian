# Torrent

**TOR'RENT**, _noun_ \[Latin torrens. This is the participle of torreo, to parch; Eng. tear.\]

**1.** A violent rushing stream of water or other fluid; a stream suddenly raised and running rapidly, as down a precipice; as a _torrent_ of lava.

**2.** A violent or rapid stream; a strong current; as a _torrent_ of vices and follies; a _torrent_ of corruption.

_Erasmus, that great injur'd name,_

Stemm'd the wild _torrent_ of a barb'rous age.
