# Tithe

**TITHE**, _noun_ The tenth part of any thing; but appropriately, the tenth part of the increase annually arising from the profits of land and stock, allotted to the clergy for their support. Tithes are personal, predial, or mixed; personal, when accruing from labor, art, trade and navigation; predial, when issuing from the earth, as hay, wood and fruit; and mixed, when accruing from beasts, which are fed from the ground.

**TITHE**, _verb transitive_ To levy a tenth part on; to tax to the amount of a tenth.

When thou hast made an end of tithing all the tithes of thine increase. [[Deuteronomy 26#12|Deuteronomy 26:12]].

Ye _tithe_ mint and rue. [[Luke 11#42|Luke 11:42]].

**TITHE**, _verb intransitive_ To pay tithes.
