# Terrestrial

**TERRES'TRIAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin terrestris, from terra, the earth.\]

**1.** Pertaining to the earth; existing on the earth; as _terrestrial_ animals; bodies _terrestrial_ [[1 Corinthians 15#40|1 Corinthians 15:40]].

**2.** Consisting of earth; as the _terrestrial_ globe.

**3.** Pertaining to the world, or to the present state; sublunary. Death puts and end to all _terrestrial_ scenes.
