# Talon

**TAL'ON**, _noun_

**1.** The claw of a fowl.

**2.** In architecture, a kind of molding, concave at the bottom, and convex at the top. When the concave part is at the top, it is called an inverted _talon_ It is usually called by workmen an ogee, or O G, and by authors an upright or inverted cymatium.
