# Throughout

**THROUGHOUT**, _preposition_ thruout'. \[through and out.\] Quite through; in every part; from one extremity to the other. This is the practice _throughout_ Ireland. A general opinion prevails _throughout_ England. _throughout_ the whole course of his life, he avoided every species of vice.

**THROUGHOUT**, _adverb_ throut'. In every part. The cloth was of a piece _throughout_
