# Trephine

**TREPH'INE**, _noun_ \[See [[Trepan]].\] An instrument for trepanning, more modern than the trepan. It is a circular or cylindrical saw, with a handle like that of a gimblet, and a little sharp perforator, called the center-pin.

**TREPH'INE**, _verb transitive_ To perforate with a trephine; to trepan.
