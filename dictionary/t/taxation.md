# Taxation

**TAXA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin taxatio.\] A taxing; the act of laying a tax, or of imposing taxes on the subjects of a state by government, or on the members of a corporation or company by the proper authority. _taxation_ is probably the most difficult subject of legislation.
