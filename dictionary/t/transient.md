# Transient

**TRANSIENT**, _adjective_ tran'shent. \[Latin transiens, transeo; trans and eo.\]

**1.** Passing; not stationary; hence, of short duration; not permanent; not lasting or durable. How _transient_ are the pleasures of this life!

\--Measur'd this _transient_ world.

**2.** Hasty; momentary; imperfect; as a _transient_ view of a landscape.

**TRANSIENT** person, a person that is passing or traveling through a place; one without a settled habitation.
