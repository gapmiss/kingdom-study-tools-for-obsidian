# Truth

**TRUTH**, _noun_

**1.** Conformity to fact or reality; exact accordance with that which is, or has been, or shall be. The _truth_ of history constitutes its whole value. We rely on the _truth_ of the scriptural prophecies.

My mouth shall speak _truth_ [[Proverbs 8#7|Proverbs 8:7]].

Sanctify them through thy truth; thy word is _truth_ [[John 17#17|John 17:17]].

**2.** True state of facts or things. The duty of a court of justice is to discover the _truth_ Witnesses are sworn to declare the _truth_ the whole _truth_ and nothing but the _truth_

**3.** Conformity of words to thoughts, which is called moral _truth_

Shall _truth_ fail to keep her word?

**4.** Veracity; purity from falsehood; practice of speaking truth; habitual disposition to speak truth; as when we say, a man is a man of _truth_

**5.** Correct opinion.

**6.** Fidelity; constancy.

The thoughts of past pleasure and _truth_

**7.** Honesty; virtue.

It must appear

That malice bears down _truth_

**8.** Exactness; conformity to rule.

Plows, to go true, depend much on the _truth_ of the iron work. \[Not in use.\]

**9.** Real fact of just principle; real state of things. There are innumerable truths with which we are not acquainted.

**10.** Sincerity.

God is a spirit, and they that worship him must worship in spirit and in _truth_ [[John 4#23|John 4:23]].

**11.** The _truth_ of God, is his veracity and faithfulness. [[Psalms 71#22|Psalms 71:22]].

Or his revealed will.

I have walked in thy _truth_ [[Psalms 26#3|Psalms 26:3]].

**12.** Jesus Christ is called the _truth_ John 14.

**13.** It is sometimes used by way of concession.

She said, _truth_ Lord; yet the dogs eat of the crums-- [[Matthew 15#27|Matthew 15:27]].

That is, it is a truth; what you have said, I admit to be true.

In _truth_ in reality; in fact.

Of a _truth_ in reality; certainly.

To do _truth_ is to practice what God commands. John 3.
