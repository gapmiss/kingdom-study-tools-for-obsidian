# Transfuse

**TRANSFUSE**, _verb transitive_ transfu'ze. \[Latin transfusus, transfundo; trans and fundo.\]

**1.** To pour, as liquor, out of one vessel into another.

**2.** To transfer, as blood, from one animal to another.

**3.** To cause to pass from one to another; to cause to be instilled or imbibed; as, to _transfuse_ a spirit of patriotism from one to another; to _transfuse_ a love of letters.
