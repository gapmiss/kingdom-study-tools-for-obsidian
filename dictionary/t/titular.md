# Titular

**TIT'ULAR**, _adjective_ \[Latin titulus.\]

**1.** Existing in title or name only; nominal; having or conferring the title only; as a _titular_ king or prince.

**2.** Having the title to an office or dignity without discharging the duties of it.

Both Valerius and Austin were _titular_ bishops.
