# Trine

**TRINE**, _adjective_ Threefold; as _trine_ dimension, that is, length, breadth and thickness.

**TRINE**, _noun_ \[supra.\] In astrology, the aspect of planets distant from each other 120 degrees, forming the figure of a trigon or triangle.

**TRINE**, _verb transitive_ To put in the aspect of a _trine_
