# Thus

**THUS**, _adverb_ In this or that manner; on this wise; as, _thus_ saith the Lord; the Pharisee prayed _thus_

**THUS** did Noah, according to all that God commanded him. [[Genesis 6#22|Genesis 6:22]].

**1.** To this degree or extent; as _thus_ wise; _thus_ peaceable.

**THUS** far extend, _thus_ far thy bounds--

**2.** In the phrase, _thus_ much, it seems to be an adjective, equivalent to this much.
