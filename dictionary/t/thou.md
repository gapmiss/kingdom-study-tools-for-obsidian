# Thou

**THOU**, _pronoun_ in the obj. thee. The second personal pronoun, in the singular number; the pronoun which is used in addressing persons in the solemn style.

Art _thou_ he that should come? [[Matthew 11#3|Matthew 11:3]].

I will fear no evil, for _thou_ art with me. [[Psalms 23#4|Psalms 23:4]].

**THOU** is used only in the solemn style, unless in very familiar language, and by the Quakers.

**THOU**, _verb transitive_ To treat with familiarity.

If _thou_ thouest him some thrice, it shall not be amiss.

**THOU**, _verb intransitive_ To use _thou_ and thee in discourse.
