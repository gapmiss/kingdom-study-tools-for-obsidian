# Testoon

**TESTOON**', _noun_ A silver coin in Italy and Portugal. In Florence, the _testoon_ is worth two lire or three paoli, about seventeen pence sterling, or thirty two cents. At Lisbon, the _testoon_ as a money of account, is valued at 100 rees, about seven pence sterling, or twelve and a half cents.

**TEST'-PAPER**, _noun_ A paper impregnated with a chimical re-agent, as litmus, etc.
