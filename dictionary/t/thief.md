# Thief

**THIEF**, _noun_ _plural_ thieves.

**1.** One who secretly, unlawfully and feloniously takes the goods or personal property of another. The _thief_ takes the property of another privately; the robber by open force.

**2.** One who takes the property of another wrongfully, either secretly or by violence. [[Job 30#5|Job 30:5]].

**3.** One who seduces by false doctrine. [[John 10#1|John 10:1]].

**4.** One who makes it his business to cheat and defraud; as a den of thieves. [[Matthew 21#1|Matthew 21:1]].

**5.** An excrescence in the snuff of a candle.
