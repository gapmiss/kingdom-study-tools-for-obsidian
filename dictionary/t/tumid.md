# Tumid

**TU'MID**, _adjective_ \[Latin tumidus, from tumeo, to swell.\]

**1.** Being swelled, enlarged or distended; as a _tumid_ leg; _tumid_ flesh.

**2.** Protuberant; rising above the level.

So high as heav'd the _tumid_ hills.

**3.** Swelling in sound or sense; pompous; puffy; bombastic; falsely sublime; as a _tumid_ expression; a _tumid_ style.
