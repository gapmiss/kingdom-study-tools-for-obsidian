# Transgression

**TRANSGRES'SION**, _noun_ The act of passing over or beyond any law or rule of moral duty; the violation of a law or known principle of rectitude; breach of command.

He mourned because of the _transgression_ of them that had been carried away. [[Ezra 10#6|Ezra 10:6]].

Forgive thy people all their transgressions. [[1 Kings 8#50|1 Kings 8:50]].

**1.** Fault; offense; crime.
