# Talking

**TALKING**, _participle present tense_ tauk'ing. Conversing; speaking in familiar conversation. [[Matthew 17#3|Matthew 17:3]].

**1.** _adjective_ Given to talking; loquacious; as _talking_ age.

**TALKING**, _noun_ tauk'ing. The act of conversing familiarly; as foolish _talking_ [[Ephesians 5#4|Ephesians 5:4]].
