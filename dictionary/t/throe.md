# Throe

**THROE**, _noun_ Extreme pain; violent pang; anguish; agony. It is particularly applied to the anguish of travail in child-birth.

My throes came thicker, and my cries increas'd.

**THROE**, _verb intransitive_ To agonize; to struggle in extreme pain.

**THROE**, _verb transitive_ To put in agony.
