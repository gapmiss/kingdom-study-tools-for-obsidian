# Thence

**THENCE**, _adverb_ thens.

**1.** From that place.

When you depart _thence_ shake off the dust of your feet. [[Mark 6#1|Mark 6:1]].

It is more usual, though not necessary, to use from before _thence_

Then will I send and fetch thee from _thence_ [[Genesis 27#9|Genesis 27:9]].

**2.** From that time.

There shall be no more _thence_ an infant of days. [[Isaiah 65#20|Isaiah 65:20]].

**3.** For that reason.

Not to sit idle with so great a gift

Useless, and _thence_ ridiculous, about him.
