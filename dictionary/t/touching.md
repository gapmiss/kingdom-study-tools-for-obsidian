# Touching

**TOUCHING**, _participle present tense_ tuch'ing. Coming on contact with; hitting; striking; affecting.

**1.** Concerning; relating to; with respect to.

Now as _touching_ things offered to idols-- [[1 Corinthians 8#1|1 Corinthians 8:1]].

**2.** _adjective_ Affecting; moving; pathetic.

**TOUCHING**, _noun_ tuch'ing. Touch; the sense of feeling.
