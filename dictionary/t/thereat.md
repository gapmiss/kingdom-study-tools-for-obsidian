# Thereat

**THEREAT**', _adverb_ \[there and at.\] At that place.

Wide is the gate, and broad is the way, that leadeth to destruction, and many there are who go in _thereat_ [[Matthew 7#13|Matthew 7:13]].

**1.** At that; at that thing or event; on that account.

Every error is a stain to the beauty of nature; for which cause it blusheth _thereat_
