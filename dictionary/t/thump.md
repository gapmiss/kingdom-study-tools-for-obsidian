# Thump

**THUMP**, _noun_ A heavy blow given with any thing that is thick, as with a club or the fist, or with a heavy hammer, or with the britch of a gun.

The watchman gave so great a _thump_ at my door, that I awaked at the knock.

**THUMP**, _verb transitive_ To strike or beat with something thick or heavy.

**THUMP**, _verb intransitive_ To strike or fall on with a heavy blow.

A watchman at night thumps with his pole.
