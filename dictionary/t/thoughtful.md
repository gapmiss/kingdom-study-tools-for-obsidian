# Thoughtful

**THOUGHT'FUL**, _adjective_ Full of thought; contemplative; employed in meditation; as a man of _thoughtful_ mind.

**1.** Attentive; careful; having the mind directed to an object; as _thoughtful_ of gain.

**2.** Promoting serious thought; favorable to musing or meditation.

War, horrid war, your _thoughtful_ walks invades.

**3.** Anxious; solicitous.

Around her crowd distrust and doubt and fear,

And _thoughtful_ foresight, and tormenting care.
