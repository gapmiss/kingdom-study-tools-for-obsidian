# Frequent

**FRE'QUENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin frequens.\]

**1.** Often seen or done; often happening at short intervals; often repeated or occurring. We made _frequent_ visits to the hospital.

**2.** Used often to practice any thing. He was _frequent_ and loud in his declamations against the revolution.

**3.** Full; crowded; thronged. \[Not used.\]

**FRE'QUENT**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin frequento.\]

To visit often; to resort to often or habitually. The man who frequents a dram-shop, an ale house, or a gaming table, is in the road to poverty, disgrace and ruin.

He frequented the court of Augustus.
