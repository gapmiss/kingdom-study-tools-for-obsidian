# Festival

**FES'TIVAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin festivus, from festus, or festum or fasti. See [[Feast]].\]

Pertaining to a feast; joyous; mirthful; as a _festival_ entertainment.

**FES'TIVAL**, _noun_ The time of feasting; an anniversary day of joy, civil or religious.

The morning trumpets _festival_ proclaimed.
