# Forehanded

**FO'REHANDED**, _adjective_

**1.** Early; timely; seasonable; as a _forehanded_ care.

**2.** In America, in good circumstances as to property; free from debt and possessed of property; as a _forehanded_ farmer.

**3.** Formed in the foreparts.

A substantial true-bred beast, bravely _forehanded_
