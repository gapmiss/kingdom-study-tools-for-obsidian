# Freshness

**FRESH'NESS**, _noun_

**1.** Newness; vigor; spirit; the contrary to vapidness; as the _freshness_ of liquors or odors.

**2.** Vigor; liveliness; the contrary to a faded state; as the _freshness_ of plants or of green fields.

**3.** Newness of strength; renewed vigor; opposed to weariness or fatigue.

The Scots had the advantage both for number and _freshness_ of men.

**4.** Coolness; invigorating quality or state.

And breathe the _freshness_ of the open air.

**5.** Color of youth and health; ruddiness.

Her cheeks their _freshness_ lose and wonted grace.

**6.** Freedom from saltiness; as the _freshness_ of water or flesh.

**7.** A new or recent state or quality; rawness.

**8.** Briskness, as of wind.
