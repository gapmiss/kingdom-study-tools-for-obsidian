# Fraught

**FRAUGHT**, _adjective_ fraut.

**1.** Laden; loaded; charged; as a vessel richly _fraught_ with goods from India. This sense is used in poetry; but in common business, freighted only is used.

**2.** Filled; stored; full; as a scheme _fraught_ with mischief; the scriptures are _fraught_ with excellent precepts.

**FRAUGHT**, _noun_ A freight; a cargo. \[Not now used.\]

**FRAUGHT**, _verb transitive_ To load; to fill; to crowd. _obsolete_
