# Former

**FORM'ER**, _noun_ He that forms; a maker; an author.

**FOR'MER**, _adjective_ _comparative_ deg.

**1.** Before in time; preceding another or something else in order of time; opposed to latter.

Her _former_ husband, who sent her away, may not take her again to be his wife, after that she is defiled. [[Deuteronomy 24#4|Deuteronomy 24:4]].

**2.** Past, and frequently ancient, long past.

For inquire, I pray thee, of the _former_ age. [[Job 8#8|Job 8:8]].

**3.** Near the beginning; preceding; as the _former_ part of a discourse or argument.

**4.** Mentioned before another.

A bad author deserves better usage than a bad critic; a man may be the _former_ merely through the misfortune of want of judgment; but he cannot be the latter without both that and an ill temper.
