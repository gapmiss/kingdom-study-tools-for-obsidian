# Farrow

**FAR'ROW**, _noun_ A litter of pigs.

**FAR'ROW**, _verb transitive_ To bring forth pigs. \[Used of swine only.\]

**FAR'ROW**, _adjective_

Not producing young in a particular season or year; applied to cows only. If a cow has had a calf, but fails in a subsequent year, she is said to be _farrow_ or to go _farrow_ Such a cow may give milk through the year.
