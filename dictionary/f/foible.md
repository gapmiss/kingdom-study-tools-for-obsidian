# Foible

**FOI'BLE**, _adjective_ Weak. \[Not used.\]

**FOI'BLE**, _noun_ \[See [[Feeble]].\] A particular moral weakness; a failing. When we speak of a man's _foible_ in the singular, which is also called his weak side, we refer to a predominant failing. We use also the plural, foibles, to denote moral failings or defects. It is wise in every man to know his own foibles.
