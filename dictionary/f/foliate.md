# Foliate

**FO'LIATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin foliatus, from folium, a leaf. Gr.\]

**1.** To beat into a leaf, or thin plate or lamin.

**2.** To spread over with a thin coat of tin and quicksilver, etc.; as, to _foliate_ a looking-glass.

**FO'LIATE**, _adjective_ In botany, leafy; furnished with leaves; as a _foliate_ stalk.
