# Food

**FOOD**, _noun_ \[See [[Feed]].\]

**1.** In a general sense, whatever is eaten by animals for nourishment, and whatever supplies nutriment to plants.

**2.** Meat; aliment; flesh or vegetables eaten for sustaining human life; victuals; provisions; whatever is or may be eaten for nourishment.

Feed me with _food_ convenient for me. [[Proverbs 30#8|Proverbs 30:8]].

**3.** Whatever supplies nourishment and growth to plants, as water, carbonic acid gas, etc. Manuring substances furnish plants with _food_

**4.** Something that sustains, nourishes and augments. Flattery is the _food_ of vanity.

**FOOD**, _verb transitive_ To feed. \[Not in use.\]
