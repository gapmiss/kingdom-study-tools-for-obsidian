# Filament

**FIL'AMENT**, _noun_ \[Latin filamenta, threads, from filum.\]

A thread; a fiber. In anatomy and natural history, a fine thread of which flesh, nerves, skin, plants, roots, etc., and also some minerals, are composed. So the spider's web is composed of filaments. The threadlike part of the stamens of plants, is called the _filament_
