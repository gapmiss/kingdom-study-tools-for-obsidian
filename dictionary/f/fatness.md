# Fatness

**FAT'NESS**, _noun_ \[from fat.\]

**1.** The quality of being fat, plump, or full fed; corpulency; fullness of flesh.

Their eyes stand out with _fatness_ [[Psalms 73#7|Psalms 73:7]].

**2.** Unctuous or greasy matter.

**3.** Unctuousness; sliminess; applied to earth:hence richness; fertility; fruitfulness.

God give thee of the dew of heaven, and the _fatness_ of the earth, and plenty of corn and wine. [[Genesis 27#28|Genesis 27:28]].

**4.** That which gives fertility.

Thy paths drop _fatness_ [[Psalms 65#11|Psalms 65:11]].

The clouds drop _fatness_

**5.** The privileges and pleasures of religion; abundant blessings.

Let your soul delight itself in _fatness_ [[Isaiah 55#2|Isaiah 55:2]].
