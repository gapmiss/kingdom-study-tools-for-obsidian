# Freeness

**FREE'NESS**, _noun_

**1.** The state or quality of being free, unconstrained, unconfined, unincumbered, or unobstructed.

**2.** Openness; unreservedness; frankness; ingenuousness; candor; as the _freeness_ of a confession.

**3.** Liberality; generosity; as _freeness_ in giving.

**4.** Gratuitousness; as the _freeness_ of divine grace.
