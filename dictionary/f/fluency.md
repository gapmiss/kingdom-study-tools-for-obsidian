# Fluency

**FLU'ENCY**, _noun_ \[Latin fluens, from flue, to flow.\]

**1.** The quality of flowing, applied to speech or language; smoothness; freedom from harshness; as _fluency_ of numbers.

**2.** Readiness of utterance; facility of words; volubility; as _fluency_ of speech; a speaker of remarkable _fluency_

**3.** Affluence; abundance. _obsolete_
