# Fallacious

**FALLA'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin fallax, from fallo, to deceive. See [[Fail]].\]

**1.** Deceptive; deceiving; deceitful; wearing a false appearance; misleading; producing error or mistake; sophistical; applied to things only; as a _fallacious_ argument or proposition; a _fallacious_ appearance.

**2.** Deceitful; false; not well founded; producing disappointment; mocking expectation; as a _fallacious_ hope.
