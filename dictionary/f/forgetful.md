# Forgetful

**FORGET'FUL**, _adjective_

**1.** Apt to forget; easily losing the remembrance of. A _forgetful_ man should use helps to strengthen his memory.

**2.** Heedless; careless; neglectful; inattentive.

Be not _forgetful_ to entertain strangers. [[Hebrews 13#2|Hebrews 13:2]].

**3.** Causing to forget; inducing oblivion; oblivious; as _forgetful_ draughts.
