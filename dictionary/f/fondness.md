# Fondness

**FOND'NESS**, _noun_

**1.** Foolishness; weakness; want of sense or judgment. _obsolete_

**2.** Foolish tenderness.

**3.** Tender passion; warm affection.

Her _fondness_ for a certain earl began when I was but a girl.

**4.** Strong inclination or propensity; as a _fondness_ for vice or sin.

**5.** Strong appetite or relish; as _fondness_ for ardent spirit, or for a particular kind of food.

\[It is now used chiefly in the three latter senses.\]
