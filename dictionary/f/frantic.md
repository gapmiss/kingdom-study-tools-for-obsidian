# Frantic

**FRAN'TIC**, _adjective_ \[Latin phreneticus; Gr. from delirium or raving, from mind, the radical sense of which is to rush, to drive forward.\]

**1.** Mad; raving; furious; outrageous; wild and disorderly; distracted; as a _frantic_ person; _frantic_ with fear or grief.

**2.** Characterized by violence, fury and disorder; noisy; mad; wild; irregular; as the _frantic_ rites of Bacchus.
