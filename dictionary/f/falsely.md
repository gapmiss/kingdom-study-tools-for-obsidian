# Falsely

**FALSELY**, _adverb_ fols'ly.

**1.** In a manner contrary to truth and fact; not truly; as, to speak or swear falsely; to testify _falsely_

**2.** Treacherously; perfidiously.

Swear to me - that thou wilt not deal _falsely_ with me.

[[Genesis 21#23|Genesis 21:23]].

**3.** Erroneously; by mistake.
