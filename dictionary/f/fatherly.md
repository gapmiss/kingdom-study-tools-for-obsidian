# Fatherly

**F'ATHERLY**, _adjective_ \[father and like.\]

**1.** Like a father in affection and care; tender; paternal; protecting; careful; as _fatherly_ care or affection.

**2.** Pertaining to a father.

**F'ATHERLY**, _adverb_ In the manner of a father.

Thus Adam, _fatherly_ displeased. \[Not proper.\]
