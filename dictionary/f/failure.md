# Failure

**FA'ILURE**, _noun_ fa'ilyur.

**1.** A failing; deficience; cessation of supply, or total defect; as the _failure_ of springs or streams; _failure_ of rain; _failure_ of crops.

**2.** Omission; non-performance; as the _failure_ of a promise; a man's _failure_ in the execution of a trust.

**3.** Decay, or defect from decay; as the _failure_ of memory or of sight.

**4.** A breaking, or becoming insolvent. At the close of a war, the prices of commodities fall, and innumerable failures secceed.

**5.** A failing; a slight fault. \[Little used.\]
