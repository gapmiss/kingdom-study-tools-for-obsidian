# Forbearance

**FORBEARANCE**, _noun_

**1.** The act of avoiding, shunning or omitting; either the cessation or intermission of an act commenced, or a withholding from beginning an act. Liberty is the power of doing or forbearing an action, according as the doing or _forbearance_ has a preference in the mind. The _forbearance_ of sin is followed with satisfaction of mind.

**2.** Command of temper; restraint of passions.

**3.** The exercise of patience; long suffering; indulgence towards those who injure us; lenity; delay of resentment or punishment.

Or despisest thou the riches of his goodness, and _forbearance_ and long suffering? [[Romans 2#4|Romans 2:4]].
