# Fanciful

**FAN'CIFUL**, _adjective_ \[See [[Fancy]].\]

**1.** Guided by the imagination, rather than by reason and experience; subject to the influence of fancy; whimsical; applied to persons. A _fanciful_ man forms visionary projects.

**2.** Dictated by the imagination; full of wild images; chimerical; whimsical; ideal; visionary; applied to things; as a _fanciful_ scheme; a _fanciful_ theory.
