# Flinty

**FLINT'Y**, _adjective_

**1.** Consisting of flint; as a _flinty_ rock.

**2.** Like flint; very hard, not impressible; as a _flinty_ heart.

**3.** Cruel; unmerciful; inexorable.

**4.** Full of flint stones, as _flinty_ ground.

Flinty-slate, a mineral of two kinds, the common and the Lydian stone.
