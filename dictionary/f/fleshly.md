# Fleshly

**FLESH'LY**, _adjective_

**1.** Pertaining to the flesh; corporeal.

**2.** Carnal; worldly; lascivious.

Abstain from _fleshly_ lusts. [[1 Peter 2#11|1 Peter 2:11]].

**3.** Animal; not vegetable.

**4.** Human; not celestial; not spiritual or divine.

Vain of _fleshly_ arm.

Fleshly wisdom. [[2 Corinthians 1#12|2 Corinthians 1:12]].
