# Futility

**FUTIL'ITY**, _noun_ Talkativeness; loquaciousness; loquacity. \[In this sense, not now used.\]

**1.** Triflingness; unimportance; want of weight or effect; as, to expose the _futility_ of arguments.

**2.** The quality of producing no valuable effect, or of coming to nothing; as the _futility_ of measures or schemes.
