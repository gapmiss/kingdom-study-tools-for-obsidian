# Fervent

**FERV'ENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin fervens, from ferveo, to be hot, to boil, to glow.\]

**1.** Hot; boiling; as a _fervent_ summer; _fervent_ blood.

**2.** Hot in temper; vehement.

They are _fervent_ to dispute.

**3.** Ardent; very warm; earnest; excited; animated; glowing; as _fervent_ zeal; _fervent_ piety.

Fervent in spirit. [[Romans 12#11|Romans 12:11]].
