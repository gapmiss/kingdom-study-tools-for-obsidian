# Flattery

**FLAT'TERY**, _noun_

**1.** False praise; commendation bestowed for the purpose of gaining favor and influence, or to accomplish some purpose. Direct _flattery_ consists in praising a person himself; indirect _flattery_ consists in praising a person through his works or his connections.

Simple pride for _flattery_ makes demands.

Just praise is only a debt, but _flattery_ is a present.

**2.** Adulation; obsequiousness; wheedling.

**3.** Just commendation which gratifies self-love.
