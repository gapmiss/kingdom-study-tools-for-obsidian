# Fastigiate

**FASTIG'IATE**,

**FASTIG'IATE**D, _adjective_ \[Latin fastigiatus, pointed, from fastigio, to point, fastigium, a top or peak.\]

**1.** In botany, a _fastigiate_ stem is one whose branches are of an equal height. Peduncles are _fastigiate_ when they elevate the fructifications in a bunch, so as to be equally high, or when they form an even surface at the top.

**2.** Roofed; narrowed to the top.
