# Foreclose

**FORECLO'SE**, _verb transitive_ s as z. To shut up; to preclude; to stop; to prevent.

The embargo with Spain foreclosed this trade.

To _foreclose_ a mortgager, in law, is to cut him off from his equity of redemption, or the power of redeeming the mortgaged premises, by a judgment of court.

\[To _foreclose_ a mortgage is not technically correct, but is often used.\]
