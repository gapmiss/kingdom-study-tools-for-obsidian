# Fumble

**FUM'BLE**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To feel or grope about; to attempt awkwardly.

**2.** To grope about in perplexity; to seek awkwardly; as, to _fumble_ for an excuse.

**3.** To handle much; to play childishly; to turn over and over.

I saw him _fumble_ with the sheets, and play with flowers.

**FUM'BLE**, _verb transitive_ To manage awkwardly; to crowd or tumble together.
