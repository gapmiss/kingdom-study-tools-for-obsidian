# Fabricate

**FABRICATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin , to frame, supra.\]

**1.** To frame; to build; to construct; to form a whole by connecting its parts; as, to _fabricate_ a bridge or ship.

**2.** To form by art and labor; to manufacture; as, to _fabricate_ woolens.

**3.** To invent and form; to forge; to devise falsely; as, to _fabricate_ a lie or story.

Our books were not fabricated with an accommodation to prevailing usages.

**4.** To coin; as, to _fabricate_ money. \[Unusual.\]
