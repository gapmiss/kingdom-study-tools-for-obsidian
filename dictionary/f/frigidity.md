# Frigidity

**FRIGID'ITY**, _noun_

**1.** Coldness; want of warmth. But not applied to the air or weather.

**2.** Want of natural heat, life and vigor of body; impotency; imbecility; as the _frigidity_ of old age.

**3.** Coldness of affection.

**4.** Dullness; want of animation or intellectual fire; as the _frigidity_ of sentiments or style.
