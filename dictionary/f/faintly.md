# Faintly

**FA'INTLY**, _adverb_

**1.** In a feeble, languid manner; without vigor or activity; as, to attack or defend _faintly_

**2.** With a feeble flame; as, a torch burns _faintly_

**3.** With a feeble light; as, the candle burns _faintly_

**4.** With little force; as, to breathe _faintly_

**5.** Without force of representation; imperfectly; as, to describe _faintly_ what we have seen.

**6.** In a low tone; with a feeble voice; as, to speak _faintly_

**7.** Without spirit or courage; timorously.

He _faintly_ now declines the fatal strife.
