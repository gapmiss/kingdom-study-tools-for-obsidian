# Felonious

**FELO'NIOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Malignant; malicious; indicating or proceeding from a depraved heart or evil purpose; villainous; traitorous; perfidious; as a _felonious_ deed.

**2.** In law, proceeding from an evil heart or purpose; done with the deliberate purpose to commit a crime; as _felonious_ homicide.
