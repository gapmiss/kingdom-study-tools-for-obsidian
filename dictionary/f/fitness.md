# Fitness

**FIT'NESS**, _noun_

**1.** Suitableness; adaptedness; adaptation; as the _fitness_ of things to their use.

**2.** Propriety; meetness; justness; reasonableness; as the _fitness_ of measures or laws.

**3.** Preparation; qualification; as a student's _fitness_ for college.

**4.** Convenience; the state of being fit.
