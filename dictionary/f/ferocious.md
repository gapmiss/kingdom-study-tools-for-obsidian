# Ferocious

**FERO'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin ferox; allied to ferus, wild, fera, a wild animal.\]

**1.** Fierce; savage; wild; indicating cruelty; as a _ferocious_ look, countenance or features.

**2.** Ravenous; rapacious; as a _ferocious_ lion.

**3.** Fierce; barbarous; cruel; as _ferocious_ savages.
