# Foliaceous

**FOLIA'CEOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin foliaceus, from folium, a leaf. See [[Foil]].\]

**1.** Leafy; having leaves intermixed with flowers; as a _foliaceous_ spike. _foliaceous_ glands are those situated on leaves.

**2.** Consisting of leaves or thin lamins; having the form of a leaf or plate; as _foliaceous_ spar.
