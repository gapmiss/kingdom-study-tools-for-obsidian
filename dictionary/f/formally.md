# Formally

**FORM'ALLY**, _adverb_

**1.** According to established form, rule, order, rite or ceremony. A treaty was concluded and _formally_ ratified by both parties.

**2.** Ceremoniously; stiffly; precisely; as, to be stiff and _formally_ reserved.

**3.** In open appearance; in a visible and apparent state.

You and your followers do stand _formally_ divided against the authorized guides of the church, and the rest of the people.

**4.** Essentially; characteristically.

That which _formally_ makes this \[charity\] a christian grace, is the spring from which it flows.
