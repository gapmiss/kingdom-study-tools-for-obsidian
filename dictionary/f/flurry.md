# Flurry

**FLUR'RY**, _noun_

**1.** A sudden blast or gust, or a light temporary breeze; as a _flurry_ of wind. It is never with us applied to a storm of duration.

**2.** A sudden shower of short duration; as a _flurry_ of snow.

**3.** Agitation; commotion; bustle; hurry.

**FLUR'RY**, _verb transitive_ To put in agitation; to excite or alarm.
