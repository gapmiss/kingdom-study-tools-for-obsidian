# Florid

**FLOR'ID**, _adjective_ \[Latin floridus, from floreo, to flower.\]

**1.** Literally, flowery; covered or abounding with flowers; but in this sense little used.

**2.** Bright in color; flushed with red; of a lively red color; as a _florid_ countenance; a _florid_ cheek.

**3.** Embellished with flowers of rhetoric; enriched with lively figures; splendid; brilliant; as a _florid_ style; _florid_ eloquence.
