# Factious

**FAC'TIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin factiosus.\]

**1.** Given to faction; addicted to form parties and raise dissensions, in opposition to government; turbulent; prone to clamor against public measures of men. No state is free from _factious_ citizens.

**2.** Pertaining to faction; proceeding from faction; as _factious_ tumults; _factious_ quarrels.
