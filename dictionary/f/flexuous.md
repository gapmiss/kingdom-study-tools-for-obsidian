# Flexuous

**FLEX'UOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin flexuosus.\]

**1.** Winding; having turns or windings; as a _flexuous_ rivulet.

**2.** Bending; winding; wavering; not steady; as a _flexuous_ flame.

**3.** In botany, bending or bent; changing its direction in a curve, from joint to joint, from bud to bud, or from flower to flower.
