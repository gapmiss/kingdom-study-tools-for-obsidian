# Fighting

**FIGHTING**, _participle present tense_

**1.** Contending in battle; striving for victory or conquest.

**2.** _adjective_ Qualified for war; fit for battle.

A host of _fighting_ men. [[2 Chronicles 26#11|2 Chronicles 26:11]].

**3.** Occupied in war; being the scene of war; as a _fighting_ field.

**FIGHTING**, _noun_ Contention; strife; quarrel.

Without were fightings, within were fears. [[2 Corinthians 7#5|2 Corinthians 7:5]].
