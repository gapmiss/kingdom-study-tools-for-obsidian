# Favoritism

**FA'VORITISM**, _noun_

**1.** The act or practice of favoring, or giving a preference to one over another.

**2.** The disposition to favor, aid and promote the interest of a favorite, or of one person or family, or of one class of men, to the neglect of others having equal claims.

It has been suggested that the proceeds of the foreign bills were calculated merely to indulge a spirit of _favoritism_ to the bank of the United States.

Which consideration imposes such a necessity on the crown, as hath, in a great measure, subdued the influence of _favoritism_

**3.** Exercise of power by favorites.
