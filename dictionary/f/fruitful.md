# Fruitful

**FRUITFUL**, _adjective_

**1.** Very productive; producing fruit in abundance; as _fruitful_ soil; a _fruitful_ tree; a _fruitful_ season.

**2.** Prolific; bearing children; not barren.

Be _fruitful_ and multiply - [[Genesis 1#22|Genesis 1:22]].

**3.** Plenteous; abounding in any thing.

**4.** Productive of any thing; fertile; as _fruitful_ in expedients.

**5.** Producing in abundance; generating; as _fruitful_ in crimes.
