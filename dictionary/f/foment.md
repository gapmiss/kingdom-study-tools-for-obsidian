# Foment

**FOMENT**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin fomento, from foveo, to warm.\]

**1.** To apply warm lotions to; to bathe with warm medicated liquors, or with flannel dipped in warm water.

**2.** To cherish with heat; to encourage growth. \[Not usual.\]

**3.** To encourage; to abet; to cherish and promote by excitements; in a bad sense; as, to _foment_ ill humors.

So we say, to _foment_ troubles or disturbances; to _foment_ intestine broils.
