# Fiddle

**FID'DLE**, _noun_ \[Latin fides, fidicula.\] A stringed instrument of music; a violin.

**FID'DLE**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To play on a _fiddle_ or violin.

Themistocles said he could not _fiddle_ but he could make a small town a great city.

It is said that Nero fiddled, when Rome was in flames.

**2.** To trifle; to shift the hands often and do nothing, like a fellow that plays on a _fiddle_

Good cooks cannot abide what they call fiddling work.

**FID'DLE**, _verb transitive_ To play a tune on a _fiddle_
