# Famous

**FA'MOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin famosus. See [[Fame]].\]

**1.** Celebrated in fame or public report; renowned; much talked of and praised; distinguished in story.

Two hundred and fifty princes of the assembly, _famous_ in the congregation. [[Numbers 16#2|Numbers 16:2]].

It is followed by for. One man is _famous_ for erudition; another, for eloquence; and another, for military skill.

**2.** sometimes in a bad sense; as a _famous_ counterfeiter; a _famous_ pirate.
