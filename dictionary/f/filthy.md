# Filthy

**FILTH'Y**, _adjective_

**1.** Dirty; foul; unclean; nasty.

**2.** Polluted; defiled by sinful practices; morally impure.

He that is _filthy_ let him be _filthy_ still. [[Revelation 22#11|Revelation 22:11]].

**3.** Obtained by base and dishonest means; as _filthy_ lucre. [[Titus 1#7|Titus 1:7]].
