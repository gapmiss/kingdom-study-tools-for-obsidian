# Firmness

**FIRM'NESS**, _noun_ ferm'ness.

**1.** Closeness or denseness of texture or structure; compactness; hardness; solidity; as the _firmness_ of wood, stone, cloth or other substance.

**2.** Stability; strength; as the _firmness_ of a union, or of a confederacy.

**3.** Steadfastness; constancy; fixedness; as the _firmness_ of a purpose or resolution; the _firmness_ of a man, or of his courage; _firmness_ of mind or soul.

**4.** Certainty; soundness; as the _firmness_ of notions or opinions.
