# Fixedness

**FIX'EDNESS**, _noun_

**1.** A state of being fixed; stability; firmness; steadfastness; as a _fixedness_ in religion or politics; _fixedness_ of opinion on any subject.

**2.** The state of a body which resists evaporation or volatilization by heat; as the _fixedness_ of gold.

**3.** Firm coherence of parts; solidity.
