# Frounce

**FROUNCE**, _noun_ A distemper of hawks, in which white spittle gathers about the bill. \[See the Verb.\]

**FROUNCE**, _verb transitive_

To curl or frizzle the hair about the face.

Not tricked and frounced as she was wont.

**FROUNCE**, _noun_ A wrinkle, plait or curl; an ornament of dress.
