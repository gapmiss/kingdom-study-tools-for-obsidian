# Folding

**FOLDING**, _participle present tense_

**1.** Doubling; laying in plaits; keeping in a fold.

**2.** _adjective_ Doubling; that may close over another, or that consists of leaves which may close one over another; as a _folding_ door.

**FOLDING**, _noun_

**1.** A fold; a doubling.

**2.** Among farmers, the keeping of sheep in inclosures on arable land, etc.
