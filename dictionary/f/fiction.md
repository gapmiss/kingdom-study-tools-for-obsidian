# Fiction

**FIC'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin fictio, from fingo, to feign.\]

**1.** The act of feigning, inventing or imagining; as, by the mere _fiction_ of the mind.

**2.** That which is feigned, invented or imagined. The story is a _fiction_

So also was the _fiction_ of those golden apples kept by a dragon, taken from the serpent which tempted Eve.
