# Frosty

**FROST'Y**, _adjective_

**1.** Producing frost; having power to congeal water; as a _frosty_ night; _frosty_ weather.

**2.** Containing frost; as, the grass is _frosty_

**3.** Chill in affection; without warmth of affection or courage.

**4.** Resembling hoar-frost; white gray-haired; as a _frosty_ head.
