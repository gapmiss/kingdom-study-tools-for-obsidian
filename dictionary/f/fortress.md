# Fortress

**FOR'TRESS**, _noun_

**1.** Any fortified place; a fort; a castle; a strong hold; a place of defense or security. The English have a strong _fortress_ on the rock of Gibraltar, or that rock is a _fortress_

**2.** Defense; safety; security;

The Lord is my rock, and my _fortress_ [[Psalms 18#2|Psalms 18:2]].

**FOR'TRESS**, _verb transitive_ To furnish with fortresses; to guard; to fortify.
