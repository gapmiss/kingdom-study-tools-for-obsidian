# Feverish

**FE'VERISH**, _adjective_

**1.** Having a slight fever; as the patient is _feverish_

**2.** Diseased with fever or heat; as _feverish_ nature.

**3.** Uncertain; inconstant; fickle; now hot, now cold.

We toss and turn about our _feverish_ will.

**4.** Hot; sultry; burning; as the _feverish_ north.
