# Funeral

**FU'NERAL**, _noun_ \[Latin funus, from funale, a cord, a torch, from funis, a rope or cord, as torches were made of cords, and were used in burials among the Romans.\]

**1.** Burial; the ceremony of buying a dead body; the solemnization of interment; obsequies.

**2.** The procession of persons attending the burial of the dead.

**3.** Burial; interment.

**FU'NERAL**, _adjective_ Pertaining to burial; used at the interment of the dead; as _funeral_ rites, honors or ceremonies; a _funeral_ torch; _funeral_ feast or games; _funeral_ oration.
