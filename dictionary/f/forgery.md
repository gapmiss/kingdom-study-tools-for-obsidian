# Forgery

**FORGERY**, _noun_

**1.** The act of forging or working metal into shape. In this sense, rarely or never now used.

**2.** The act of falsifying; the crime of counterfeiting; as the _forgery_ of coin, or of bank notes, or of a bond. _forgery_ may consist in counterfeiting a writing, or in setting a false name to it, to the prejudice of another person.

**3.** That which is forged or counterfeited. Certain letters, purporting to be written by Gen. Washington, during the revolution, were forgeries.
