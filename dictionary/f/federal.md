# Federal

**FED'ERAL**, _adjective_ \[from Latin faedus, a league, allied perhaps to Eng. wed. Latin vas, vadis, vador, vadimonium. See [[Heb]]. to pledge.\]

**1.** Pertaining to a league or contract; derived from an agreement or covenant between parties, particularly between nations.

The Romans, contrary to _federal_ right, compelled them to part with Sardinia.

**2.** Consisting in a compact between parties, particularly and chiefly between states or nations; founded on alliance by contract or mutual agreement; as a _federal_ government, such as that of the United States.

**3.** Friendly to the constitution of the United States. \[See the Noun.\]
