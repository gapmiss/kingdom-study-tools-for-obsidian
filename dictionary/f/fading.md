# Fading

**FA'DING**, _participle present tense_ \[See [[Fade]].\]

**1.** Losing color; becoming less vivid; decaying; declining; withering.

**2.** _adjective_ Subject to decay; liable to lose freshness and vigor; liable to perish; not durable; transient; as a _fading_ flower.

**FA'DING**, _noun_ Decay; loss of color, freshness or vigor.
