# Feasible

**FE'ASIBLE**, _adjective_ s as z. \[Latin facere.\]

**1.** That may be done, performed, executed or effected; practicable. We say a thing is _feasible_ when it can be effected by human means or agency. A thing may be possible, but not _feasible_

**2.** That may be used or tilled, as land.

**FE'ASIBLE**, _noun_ That which is practicable; that which can be performed by human means.
