# Fearfulness

**FE'ARFULNESS**, _noun_

**1.** Timorousness; timidity.

**2.** State of being afraid; awe; dread.

A thing that makes a government despised, is _fearfulness_ of, and mean compliances with, bold popular offenders.

**3.** Terror; alarm; apprehension of evil.

Fearfulness hath surprised the hypocrites. [[Isaiah 33#14|Isaiah 33:14]].
