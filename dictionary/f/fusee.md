# Fusee

**FUSEE**', _noun_ s as z. \[Latin fusus, a spindle, from fundo, fudi, fusum.\]

The cone or conical part of a watch or clock, round which is wound the chain or cord.

**FUSEE'**, _noun_ s as z.

**1.** A small neat musket or firelock. But we now use fusil.

**2.** _fusee_ or fuse of a bomb or granade, a small pipe filled with combustible matter by which fire is communicated to the powder in the bomb; but as the matter burns slowly, time is given before the charge takes fire, for the bomb to reach its destination.

**3.** The track of a buck.
