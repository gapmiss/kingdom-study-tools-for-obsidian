# Froward

**FRO'WARD**, _adjective_ \[Latin versus:turned or looking from.\]

Perverse, that is, turning from, with aversion or reluctance; not willing to yield or comply with what is required; unyielding; ungovernable; refractory; disobedient; peevish; as a _froward_ child.

They are a very _froward_ generation, children in whom is no faith. [[Deuteronomy 32#20|Deuteronomy 32:20]].
