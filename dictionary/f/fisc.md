# Fisc

**FISC**, _noun_ \[Latin fiscus. Fiscus, signifies a basket or hanaper, probably from the twigs which composed the first baskets. Eng. whisk. The word coincides in elements with basket, and Latin fascia, twigs being the primitive bands.\]

The treasury of a prince or state; hence to confiscate to take the good of a criminal and appropriate then to the public treasure.
