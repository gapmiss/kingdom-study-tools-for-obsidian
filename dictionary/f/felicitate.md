# Felicitate

**FELIC'ITATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin felicito, from felix, happy.\]

**1.** To make very happy.

What a glorious entertainment and pleasure would fill and _felicitate_ his spirit, if he could grasp all in a single survey.

More generally,

**2.** To congratulate; to express joy or pleasure to. We _felicitate_ our friends on the acquisition of good, or an escape from evil.

**FELIC'ITATE**, _adjective_ Made very happy.
