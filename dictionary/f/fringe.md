# Fringe

**FRINGE**, _noun_ frinj. \[Latin frango, to break.\]

**1.** An ornamental appendage to the borders of garments or furniture, consisting of loose threads.

The golden _fringe_ ev'n set the ground on flame.

**2.** Something resembling fringe; an open broken border.

**FRINGE**, _verb transitive_ To adorn or border with _fringe_ or a loose edging.
