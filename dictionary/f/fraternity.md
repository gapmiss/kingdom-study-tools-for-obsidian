# Fraternity

**FRATERN'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin fraternitas.\]

**1.** The state or quality of a brother; brotherhood.

**2.** A body of men associated for their common interest or pleasure; a company; a brotherhood; a society; as the _fraternity_ of free masons.

**3.** Men of the same class, profession, occupation or character.
