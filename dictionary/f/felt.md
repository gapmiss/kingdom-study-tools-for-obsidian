# Felt

**FELT**, _preterit tense_ of feel.

**FELT**, _noun_ \[Latin pellis, Eng. fell, a skin from plucking or stripping, Latin vello, vellus, Eng. wool.\]

**1.** A cloth or stuff made of wool, or wool and hair, fulled or wrought into a compact substance by rolling and pressure with lees or size.

**2.** A hat made of wool.

**3.** Skin.

To know whether sheep are sound or not, see that the _felt_ be loose.

**FELT**, _verb transitive_ To make cloth or stuff of wool, or wool and hair, by fulling.
