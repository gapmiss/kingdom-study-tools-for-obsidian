# Forswear

**FORSWEAR**, _verb transitive_ _preterit tense_ forswore; _participle passive_ forsworn. See [[Swear and Answer]].\]

**1.** To reject or renounce upon oath.

**2.** To deny upon oath.

Like innocence, and as serenely bold as truth, how loudly he forswears thy gold.

To _forswear_ one's self, is to swear falsely; to perjure one's self.

Thou shalt not _forswear_ thyself. [[Matthew 5#33|Matthew 5:33]].

**FORSWEAR**, _verb intransitive_ To swear falsely; to commit perjury.
