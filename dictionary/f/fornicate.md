# Fornicate

**FORN'ICATE**,

**FORN'ICATE**D, _adjective_ \[Latin fornicatus, from fornix, an arch.\] Arched; vaulted like an oven or furnace.

**FORN'ICATE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin fornicor, from fornix, a brothel.\]

To commit lewdness, as an unmarried man or woman, or as a married man with an unmarried woman.

If a brahman _fornicate_ with a Nayr woman, he shall not thereby lose his cast.
