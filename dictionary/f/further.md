# Further

**FUR'THER**, _adjective_

**1.** More or most distant; as the _further_ end of the field.

**2.** Additional. We have a _further_ reason for this opinion. We have nothing _further_ to suggest.

What _further_ need have we of witnesses? [[Matthew 26#39|Matthew 26:39]].

**FUR'THER**, _adverb_ To a greater distance. He went _further_

**FUR'THER**, _verb transitive_

To help forward; to promote; to advance onward; to forward; hence, to help or assist.

This binds thee then to _further_ my design.
