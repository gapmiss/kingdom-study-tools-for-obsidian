# Fatten

**FAT'TEN**, _verb transitive_ fat'n.

**1.** To make fat; to feed for slaughter; to make fleshy, or plump with fat.

**2.** To make fertile and fruitful; to enrich; as, to _fatten_ land; to _fatten_ fields with blood.

**3.** To feed grossly; to fill.

**FAT'TEN**, _verb intransitive_ fat'n. to grow fat or corpulent; to grow plump, thick or fleshy; to be pampered.

And villains _fatten_ with the brave man's labor.

Tigers and wolves shall in the ocean breed.

The whale and dolphin _fatten_ on the mead.
