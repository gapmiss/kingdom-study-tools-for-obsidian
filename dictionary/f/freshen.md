# Freshen

**FRESH'EN**, _verb transitive_ fresh'n.

**1.** To make fresh; to dulcify; to separate, as water from saline particles; to take saltiness from any thing; as, to _freshen_ water, fish or flesh.

**2.** To refresh; to revive. \[Not used.\]

**3.** In seaman's language, to apply new service to a cable; as, to _freshen_ hawse.

**FRESH'EN**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To grow fresh; to lose salt or saltiness.

**2.** To grow brisk or strong; as, the wind freshens.
