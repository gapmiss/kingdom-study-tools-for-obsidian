# Fritter

**FRIT'TER**, _noun_ \[Latin frictus, fried.\]

**1.** A small pancake; also, a small piece of meat fried.

**2.** A fragment; a shred; a small piece.

And cut whole giants into fritters.

**FRIT'TER**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To cut meat into small pieces to be fried.

**2.** To break into small pieces or fragments.

Break all their nerves, and _fritter_ all their sense.

To _fritter_ away, is to diminish; to pare off; to reduce to nothing by taking away a little at a time.
