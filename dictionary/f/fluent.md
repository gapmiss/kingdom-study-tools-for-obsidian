# Fluent

**FLU'ENT**, _adjective_ \[See [[Fluency]].

**1.** Liquid; flowing.

**2.** Flowing; passing.

Motion being a _fluent_ thing.

**3.** Ready in the use of words; voluble; copious; having words at command and uttering them with facility and smoothness; as a _fluent_ speaker.

**4.** Flowing; voluble; smooth; as _fluent_ speech.

**FLU'ENT**, _noun_

**1.** A stream; a current of water. \[Little used.\]

**2.** The variable or flowing quantity in fluxions.
