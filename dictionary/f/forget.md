# Forget

**FORGET**', _verb transitive_ _preterit tense_ forgot. \[forgat, _obsolete_ \]

**1.** To lose the remembrance of; to let go from the memory.

Bless the Lord, O my soul, and _forget_ not all his benefits. [[Psalms 103#2|Psalms 103:2]].

**2.** To slight; to neglect.

Can a woman _forget_ her sucking child? Yea, they may _forget_ yet will I not _forget_ thee. [[Isaiah 49#15|Isaiah 49:15]].
