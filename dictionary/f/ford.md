# Ford

**FORD**, _noun_

**1.** A place in a river or other water, where it may be passed by man or beast on foot, or by wading.

**2.** A stream; a current.

Permit my ghost to pass the Stygian _ford_

**FORD**, _verb transitive_ To pass or cross a river or other water by treading or walking on the bottom; to pass through water by wading; to wade through.
