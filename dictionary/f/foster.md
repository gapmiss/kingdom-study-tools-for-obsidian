# Foster

**FOS'TER**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To feed; to nourish; to support; to being up.

Some say that ravens _foster_ forlorn children.

**2.** To cherish; to forward; to promote growth. The genial warmth of spring fosters the plants.

**3.** To cherish; to encourage; to sustain and promote; as, to _foster_ passion or genius.

**FOS'TER**, _verb intransitive_ To be nourished or trained up together.
