# Fashionable

**FASH'IONABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** Made according to the prevailing form or mode; as a _fashionable_ dress.

**2.** Established by custom or use; current; prevailing at a particular time; as the _fashionable_ philosophy; _fashionable_ opinions.

**3.** Observant of the fashion or customary mode; dressing or behaving according to the prevailing fashion; as a _fashionable_ man. Hence,

**4.** Genteel; well bred; as _fashionable_ company or society.
