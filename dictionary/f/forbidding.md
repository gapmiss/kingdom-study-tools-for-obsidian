# Forbidding

**FORBID'DING**, _participle present tense_

**1.** Prohibiting; hindering.

**2.** _adjective_ Repelling approach; repulsive; raising abhorrence, aversion or dislike; disagreeable; as a _forbidding_ aspect; a _forbidding_ formality; a _forbidding_ air.

**FORBID'DING**, _noun_ Hindrance; opposition.
