# Flexibility

**FLEXIBIL'ITY**, _noun_ \[See [[Flexible]].\]

**1.** The quality of admitting to be bent; pliancy; flexibleness; as the _flexibility_ of rays of light.

**2.** Easiness to be persuaded; the quality of yielding to arguments, persuasion or circumstances; ductility of mind; readiness to comply; facility; as _flexibility_ of temper.
