# Flimsy

**FLIM'SY**, _adjective_ s as z. \[The word is retained by the common people in New England in limsy, weak, limber, easily bending.\]

**1.** Weak; feeble; slight; vain; without strength or solid substance; as a _flimsy_ pretext; a _flimsy_ excuse; _flimsy_ objections.

**2.** Without strength or force; spiritless.

Proud of a vast extent of _flimsy_ lines.

**3.** Thin; of loose texture; as _flimsy_ cloth or stuff. \[Little used.\]
