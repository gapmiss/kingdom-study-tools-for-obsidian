# Fickle

**FICK'LE**, _adjective_ \[Latin vacillo; Gr.; Heb. to stagger.\]

**1.** Wavering; inconstant; unstable; of a changeable mind; irresolute; not firm in opinion or purpose; capricious.

They know how _fickle_ common lovers are.

**2.** Not fixed or firm; liable to change or vicissitude; as a _fickle_ state.
