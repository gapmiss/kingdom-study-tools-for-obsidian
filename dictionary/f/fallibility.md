# Fallibility

**FALLIBIL'ITY**, _noun_ \[See [[Fallible]].\]

**1.** Liableness to deceive; the quality of being fallible; uncertainty; possibility of being erroneous, or of leading to mistake; as the _fallibility_ of an argument, of reasoning or of testimony.

**2.** Liableness to err or to be deceived in one's own judgment; as the _fallibility_ of men.
