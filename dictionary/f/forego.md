# Forego

**FOREGO**', _verb transitive_ \[See [[Go]].\]

**1.** To forbear to possess or enjoy; voluntarily to avoid the enjoyment of good. Let us _forego_ the pleasures of sense, to secure immortal bliss.

**2.** To give up; to renounce; to resign. But this word is usually applied to things not possessed or enjoyed, and which cannot be resigned.

**3.** To lose.

**4.** To go before; to precede. _obsolete_
