# Famine

**FAM'INE**, _noun_ \[Latin fames.\]

**1.** Scarcity of food; dearth; a general want of provisions sufficient for the inhabitants of a country or besieged place.

There was a _famine_ in the land. [[Genesis 26#1|Genesis 26:1]].

**2.** Want; destitution; as a _famine_ of the word of life.
