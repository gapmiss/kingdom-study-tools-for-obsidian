# Fantastic

**FANTAS'TIC**,

**FANTAS'TIC**AL, _adjective_ \[Gr. vision, fancy, from to appear.\]

**1.** Fanciful; produced or existing only in imagination; imaginary; not real; chimerical.

**2.** Having the nature of phantom; apparent only.

**3.** Unsteady; irregular.

**4.** Whimsical; capricious; fanciful; indulging the vagaries of imagination; as _fantastic_ minds; a _fantastic_ mistress.

**5.** Whimsical; odd.
