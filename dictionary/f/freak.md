# Freak

**FREAK**, _noun_

**1.** Literally, a sudden starting or change of place. Hence,

**2.** A sudden causeless change or turn of the mind; a whim or fancy; a capricious prank.

She is restless and peevish, and sometimes in a _freak_ will instantly change her habitation.

**FREAK**, _verb transitive_ \[Heb. to divide.\]

To variegate; to checker.

**FREAK**ed with many a mingled hue.
