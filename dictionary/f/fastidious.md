# Fastidious

**FASTID'IOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin fastidiousus, from fastidio, to disdain from fastus, haughtiness. See [[Heb]].\]

**1.** Disdainful; squeamish; delicate to a fault; over nice; difficult to please; as a _fastidious_ mind or taste.

**2.** Squeamish; rejecting what is common or not very nice; suited with difficulty; as a _fastidious_ appetite.
