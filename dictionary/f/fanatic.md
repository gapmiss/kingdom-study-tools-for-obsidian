# Fanatic

**FANAT'IC**,

**FANAT'IC**AL, _adjective_ \[Latin fanaticus, phanaticus.\]

Wild and extravagant in opinions, particularly in religious opinions; excessively enthusiastic; possessed by a kind of frenzy. Hence we say, _fanatic_ zeal; _fanatic_ notions or opinions.
