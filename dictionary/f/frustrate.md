# Frustrate

**FRUS'TRATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin frustro.\]

**1.** Literally, to break or interrupt; hence, to defeat; to disappoint; to balk; to bring to nothing; as, to _frustrate_ a plan, design or attempt; to _frustrate_ the will or purpose.

**2.** To disappoint; applied to persons.

**3.** To make null; to nullify; to render of no effect; as, to _frustrate_ a conveyance or deed.

**FRUS'TRATE**, part. _adjective_ Vain; ineffectual; useless; unprofitable; null; void; of no effect.
