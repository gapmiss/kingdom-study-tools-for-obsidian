# Feebleness

**FEE'BLENESS**, _noun_

**1.** Weakness of body or mind, from any cause; imbecility; infirmity; want of strength, physical or intellectual; as _feebleness_ of the body or limbs; _feebleness_ of the mind or understanding.

**2.** Want of fullness or loudness; as _feebleness_ of voice.

**3.** Want of vigor or force; as _feebleness_ of exertion, or of operation.

**4.** Defect of brightness; as _feebleness_ of light or color.
