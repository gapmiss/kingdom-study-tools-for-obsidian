# Falsehood

**FALSEHOOD**, _noun_ fols'hood. \[false and hood.\]

**1.** Contrariety or inconformity to fact or truth; as the _falsehood_ of a report.

**2.** Want of truth or veracity; a lie; an untrue assertion.

**3.** Want of honesty; treachery; deceitfulness; perfidy.

But _falsehood_ is properly applied to things only. \[See [[falseness]].\]

**4.** Counterfeit; false appearance; imposture.
