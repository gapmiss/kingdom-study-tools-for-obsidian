# Foulness

**FOUL'NESS**, _noun_

**1.** The quality of being foul or filthy; filthiness; defilement.

**2.** The quality or state of containing or being covered with any thing extraneous which is noxious or offensive; as the _foulness_ of a cellar, or of a well; the _foulness_ of a musket; the _foulness_ of a ship's bottom.

**3.** Pollution; impurity.

There is not so chaste a nation as this, nor so free from all pollution or _foulness_

**4.** Hatefulness; atrociousness; as the _foulness_ of a deed.

**5.** Ugliness; deformity.

The _foulness_ of the' infernal form to hide.

**6.** Unfairness; dishonesty; want of candor.

Piety is opposed to hypocrisy and insincerity, and all falseness or _foulness_ of intentions.
