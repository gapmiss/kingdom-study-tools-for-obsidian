# Naughtiness

**NAUGHTINESS**, _noun_ Nautiness.

**1.** Badness; wickedness; evil principle or purpose.

I know thy pride and the _naughtiness_ of thy heart. [[1 Samuel 17#28|1 Samuel 17:28]].

**2.** Slight wickedness of children; perverseness; mischievousness.

, _adjective_ Nauty.

**1.** Wicked; corrupt.

A naughty person, a wicked man, walketh with a froward mouth. [[Proverbs 6#1|Proverbs 6:1]].

**2.** Bad; worthless.

The other basket had very naughty figs. [[Jeremiah 24#1|Jeremiah 24:1]].

**3.** Mischievous; perverse; froward; as a naughty child. It is now seldom used except in the latter sense, as applied to children.
