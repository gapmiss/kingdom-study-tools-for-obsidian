# Numerator

**NU'MERATOR**, _noun_ \[Latin\]

**1.** One that numbers.

**2.** In arithmetic, the number in vulgar fractions which shows how many parts of a unit are taken. Thus when a unit is divided into 9 parts, and we take 5, we express it thus, 5/9, that is, five ninths; 5 being the _numerator_ and 9 the denominator.
