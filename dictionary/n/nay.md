# Nay

**NAY**, _adverb_ \[a contracted word, to deny\]

**1.** No; a word that expresses negation.

I tell you _nay_ but except ye repent, ye shall all likewise perish. [[Luke 13#3|Luke 13:3]].

**2.** It expresses also refusal.

He that will not when he may, When he would he shall have _nay_

\[In these senses it is now rarely used; no being substituted.\]

**3.** Not only so; not this alone; intimating that something is to be added y way of amplification. He requested an answer; _nay_ he urged it.

**NAY**, _noun_ Denial, refusal.

**NAY**, _verb transitive_ To refuse. \[Not in use\]
