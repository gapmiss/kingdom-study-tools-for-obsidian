# Notch

**NOTCH**, _noun_ \[G. to crack or flaw. It seems to be the same word in origin as niche, nick.\]

**1.** A hollow cut in any thing; a nick; an indentation.

And on the stick ten equal notches makes.

**2.** An opening or narrow passge through a mountain or hill. We say, the _notch_ of a mountain.

**NOTCH**, _verb transitive_ To cut in small hollows; as, to _notch_ a stick.

**NOTCH'-WEED**, _noun_ A plant called orach.
