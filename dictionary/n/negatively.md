# Negatively

**NEGATIVELY**, _adverb_

**1.** With or by denial; as, he answered _negatively_

**2.** In the form of speech implying the absence of something; opposed to positively.

I shall show what this image of god in man is, _negatively_ by showing wherein it does not consist, and positively, by showing wherein it does consist.

**3.** _negatively_ charged or electrified. \[See [[Positively]]\]
