# Nuzzle

**NUZ'ZLE**, _verb transitive_ \[qu. from noursle.\] To nurse; to foster. \[Vulgar.\]

**NUZZLE**, _verb transitive_ \[qu. from nose or noursle.\] To hide the head, as a child in the mother's bosom.

**NUZ'ZLE**, _verb transitive_ \[qu. from noursle or nestle.\] To nestle; to house as in a nest.

**NUZ'ZLE**, _verb intransitive_ \[qu. from nose.\] To go with the nose near the ground, or thrusting the nose into the ground like a swine.
