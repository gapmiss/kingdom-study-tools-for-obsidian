# Netting

**NETTING**, _noun_

**1.** A piece of network.

**2.** A complication of ropes fastened across each other, to be stretched along the upper part of a ships quarter to contain hammocks. _netting_ is also employed to hammocks. _netting_ is also employed to hold the fore and main-top-mast sails when stowed. _netting_ is also extended along a ships gunwale in engagements, to prevent the enemy from boarding.
