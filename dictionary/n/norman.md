# Norman

**NOR'MAN**, _noun_ In seamen's language, a short wooden bar to be thrust into a hole of the windlass, on which to fasten the cable.

**NOR'MAN**, _noun_ \[north-man or nord-man.\] A Norwegian, or a native of Normandy.

**NOR'MAN**, _adjective_ Pertaining to Normandy; as the _norman_ language.
