# Nicely

**NICELY**, _adverb_

**1.** With delicate perception; as, to be _nicely_ sensible.

**2.** Accurately; exactly; with exact order or proportion; as the parts of a machine or building _nicely_ adjusted; a shape _nicely_ proportioned; a dress _nicely_ fitted tot he body; the ingredients of a medicine _nicely_ proportioned and mixed.

**3.** In colloquial language, well; cleverly; dextrously; handsomely; in the best manner; as, a feat is _nicely_ done.
