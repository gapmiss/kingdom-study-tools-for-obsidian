# Niggardly

**NIGGARDLY**, _adjective_

**1.** Meanly covetous or avaricious; sordidly parsimonious; extremely sparing of expense.

Where the owner of the house will be bountiful, it is not for the steward to be _niggardly_

**2.** Sparing; wary; cautiously avoiding profusion.

**NIGGARDLY**, _adverb_ Sparingly; with cautious parsimony.
