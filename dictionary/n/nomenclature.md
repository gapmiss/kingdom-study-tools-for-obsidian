# Nomenclature

**NOMENCLATURE**, _noun_ \[See [[Nomenclator]].\]

**1.** A list or catalogue of the more usual and important words in a language, with their significations; a vocabulary or dictionary.

**2.** The names of things in any art or science, or the whole vocabulary of names or technical terms which are appropriated to any particular branch of science; as the _nomenclature_ of botany or of chemistry; the new _nomenclature_ of Lavoisier and his associates.
