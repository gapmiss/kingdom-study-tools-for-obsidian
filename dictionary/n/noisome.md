# Noisome

**NOISOME**, _adjective_

**1.** Noxious to health; hurtful; mischievous; unwholesome; insalubrious; destructive; as _noisome_ winds; _noisome_ effluvia or miasmata; _noisome_ pestilence.

**2.** Noxious; injurious.

**3.** Offensive to the smell or other senses; disgusting; fetid.

Foul breath is _noisome_
