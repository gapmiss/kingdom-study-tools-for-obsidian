# Nurture

**NUR'TURE**, _noun_

**1.** That which nourishes; food; diet.

**2.** That which promotes growth; education; instruction. [[Ephesians 6#4|Ephesians 6:4]].

**NUR'TURE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To feed; to nourish.

**2.** To educate; to bring or train up.

He was nurtured where he was born.
