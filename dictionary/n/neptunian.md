# Neptunian

**NEPTUNIAN**, _adjective_ \[the fabled deity of the ocean.\]

**1.** Pertaining to the ocean or sea.

**2.** Formed by water or aqueous solution; as _neptunian_ rocks.

**NEPTUNIAN**, _noun_ One who adopts the theory that the whole earth was once covered with water, or rather that the substances of the globe were formed from aqueous solution; opposed to the Plutonic theory.
