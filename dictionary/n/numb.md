# Numb

**NUMB**, _adjective_

**1.** Torpid; destitute of the power of sensation and motion; as, the fingers or limbs are _numb_ with cold.

**2.** Producing numbness; benumbing; as the _numb_ cold night. \[Not used nor proper.\]

**NUMB**, _verb transitive_ To make torpid; to deprive of the power of sensation or motion; to deaden; to benumb; to stupefy.

For lazy winter numbs the laboring hand.

And numbing coldness has embraced the ear.
