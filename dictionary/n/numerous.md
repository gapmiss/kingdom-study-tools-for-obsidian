# Numerous

**NU'MEROUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin\]

**1.** Being many, or consisting of a great number of individuals; as a _numerous_ army; a _numerous_ body; a _numerous_ people.

**2.** Consisting of poetic numbers; melodious; musical. In prose, a style becomes _numerous_ by the alternate disposition or intermixture of long and short syllables, or of long and short words; or by a judicious selection and disposition of smooth flowing words, and by closing the periods with important or well sounding words.
