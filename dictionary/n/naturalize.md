# Naturalize

**NATURALIZE**, _verb transitive_ \[from natural, nature.\]

**1.** To confer on an alien the rights and privileges of a native subject or citizen; to adopt foreigners into a nation or state, and place them in the condition of natural born subjects.

**2.** To make natural; to render easy and familiar by custom and habit; as, custom naturalizes labor or study.

**3.** To adapt; to make suitable; to acclimate; as, to _naturalize_ one to a climate.

**4.** To receive or adopt as native, natural or vernacular; to make our own; as, to _naturalize_ foreign words.

**5.** To accustom; to habituate; as, to _naturalize_ the vine to a cold climate.
