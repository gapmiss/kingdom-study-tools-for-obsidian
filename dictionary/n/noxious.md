# Noxious

**NOXIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin from hurt.\]

**1.** Hurtful; harmful; baneful; pernicious; destructive; unwholesome; insalubrious; as _noxious_ air, food, climate; pernicious; corrupting to morals; as _noxious_ practices or examples; _noxious_ haunts of vice.

**2.** Guilty; criminal.

Those who are _noxious_ in the eye of the law. \[Little used.\]

**3.** Unfavorable; injurious.

Too frequent appearance in places of public resort is _noxious_ to spiritual promotion.
