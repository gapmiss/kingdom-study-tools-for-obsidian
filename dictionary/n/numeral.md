# Numeral

**NU'MERAL**, _adjective_

**1.** Pertaining to number; consisting of number.

The dependence of a long train of _numeral_ progressions.

**2.** Expressing number; representing number; standing as a substitute for figures; as _numeral_ letters; as X for 10; L for fifty; C for 100; D for 500; M for 1000.

**3.** Expressing numbers; as _numeral_ characters. The figures we now use to express numbers are 1. 2. 3. 4. 5. 6. 7. 8. 9. 0. They are said to be of Arabian origin; but the Arabians might have received them from India. This is a controverted question.
