# Niceness

**NICENESS**, _noun_

**1.** Delicacy of perception; the quality of perceiving small differences; as _niceness_ of taste.

**2.** Extreme delicacy; excess of scrupulousness or exactness.

Unlike the _niceness_ of our modern dames.

**3.** Accuracy; minute exactness; as _niceness_ of work; _niceness_ of texture or proportion.

Wheres now the labored _niceness_ in thy dress?
