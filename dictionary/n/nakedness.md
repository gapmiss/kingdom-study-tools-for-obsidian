# Nakedness

**NAKEDNESS**, _noun_

**1.** Want of covering or clothing; nudity; bareness.

**2.** Want of means of defense.

Ye are spies; to see the _nakedness_ of the land are ye come. [[Genesis 42#9|Genesis 42:9]].

**3.** Plainness; openness to view.

To uncover _nakedness_ in Scripture, is to have incestuous or unlawful commerce with a female.
