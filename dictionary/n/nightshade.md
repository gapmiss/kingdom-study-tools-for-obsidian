# Nightshade

**NIGHTSHADE**, _noun_ A plant of the genus Solanum. The deadly _nightshade_ is of the genus Atropa; the American _nightshade_ of the genus Phytolacea; the bastard _nightshade_ of the genus Rivina; the enchanter's _nightshade_ of the genus Circaea; the Malabar _nightshade_ of the genus Basella; and the three-leaved _nightshade_ of the genus Trillium.
