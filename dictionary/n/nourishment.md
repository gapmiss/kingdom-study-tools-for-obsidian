# Nourishment

**NOURISHMENT**, _noun_

**1.** That which serves to promote the growth of animals or plants, or to repair the waste of animal bodies; food; sustenance; nutriment.

**2.** Nutrition; support of animal or vegetable bodies.

**3.** Instruction, or that which promotes growth in attainments; as _nourishment_ and growth in grace.

So they may learn to seek the _nourishment_ of their souls.
