# Nundinal

**NUN'DINAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin , a fair or market, every nine days.\]

**1.** Pertaining to a fair or to a market day.

**2.** A _nundinal_ letter, among the Romans, was one of the eight first letters of the alphabet, which were repeated successively from the first to the last dya of the year. One of these always expressed the market days, which returned every nine days.

**NUN'DINAL**, _noun_ A _nundinal_ letter.
