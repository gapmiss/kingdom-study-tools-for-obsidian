# Notorious

**NOTO'RIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin known.\]

**1.** Publicly knwon; manifest to the world; evident; usually, knwon to disadvantage; hence almost always used in all ill sense; as a _notorious_ thief; a _notorious_ crime or vice; a man _notorious_ for lewdness or gaming.

**2.** In a good sense.

Your goodness, since you provoke me, shall be most _notorious_
