# Nightly

**NIGHTLY**, _adjective_

**1.** Done by night; happening in the night, or appearing in the night; as _nightly_ sports; _nightly_ dews.

**2.** Done every night. The watch goes his _nightly_ round.

**NIGHTLY**, _adverb_

**1.** By night.

Thee, Sion, and the flowery brooks beneath, _nightly_ I visit.

**2.** Every night.
