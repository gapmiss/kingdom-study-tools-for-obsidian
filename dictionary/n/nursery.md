# Nursery

**NURS'ERY**, _noun_

**1.** The place or apartment in a house appropriated to the care of children.

**2.** A place where young trees are propagated for the purpose of being transplanted; a plantation of young trees.

**3.** The place where any thing is fostered and the growth promoted.

To see fair Padua, _nursery_ of arts.

So we say , a _nursery_ of thieves or of rogues. Ale houses and dram-shops are the nurseries of intemperance.

Christian families are the nurseries of the church on earth, as she is the _nursery_ of the church in heaven.

**4.** Taht which forms and educates. Commerce is the _nursery_ of seamen.

**5.** The act of nursing. \[Little used.\]

**6.** That which is the object of a nurse's care.
