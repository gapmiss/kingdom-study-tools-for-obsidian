# Nearly

**NEARLY**, _adverb_

**1.** At no great distance; not remotely.

**2.** Closely; as two persons _nearly_ related or allied.

**3.** Intimately; pressingly; with a close relation to ones interest or happiness. It _nearly_ concerns us to preserve peace with our neighbor.

**4.** Almost; within a little. The fact is _nearly_ demonstrated.

**5.** In a parsimonious or niggardly manner.
