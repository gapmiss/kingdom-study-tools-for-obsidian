# Nuncupative

**NUNCU'PATIVE**, _adjective_ \[Latin , to declare.\]

**1.** Nominal; existing only in name.

**2.** Publicly or solemnly declaratory.

**3.** Verbal, not written. A _nuncupative_ will or testament is one which is made by the verbal declaration of the testator, and depends merely on oral testimony for proof, though afterwards reduced to writing.
