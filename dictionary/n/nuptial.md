# Nuptial

**NUP'TIAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin , to marry.\]

**1.** Pertaining to marriage; done at a wedding; as _nuptial_ rites and ceremonies; _nuptial_ torch.

**2.** Constituting marriage; as the _nuptial_ knot or band.

The Bible has mitigated the horrors of war; it has given effectual obligation to the _nuptial_ vow.
