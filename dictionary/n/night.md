# Night

**NIGHT**, _noun_ \[The sense may be dark, black, or it may be the decline of the day, from declining, departing.\]

**1.** That part of the natural day when the sun is beneath the horizon, or the time from sunset to sunrise.

**2.** The time after the close of life; death. [[John 9#4|John 9:4]].

She closed her eyes in everlasting _night_

**3.** A state of ignorance; intellectual and moral darkness; heathenish ignorance. [[Romans 13#12|Romans 13:12]].

**4.** Adversity; a state of affliction and distress. [[Isaiah 21#4|Isaiah 21:4]].

**5.** Obscurity; a state of concealment from the eye or the mind; unintelligibleness.

Nature and natures works lay hid in _night_

In the _night_ suddenly; unexpectedly. [[Luke 12#20|Luke 12:20]].

To-night, in this _night_ To-night the moon will be eclipsed.
