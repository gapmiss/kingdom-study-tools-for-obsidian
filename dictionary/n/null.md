# Null

**NULL**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin not any.\] To annul; to deprive of validity; to destroy. \[Not much used.\] \[See [[Annul]].\]

**NULL**, _adjective_ Void; of no legal or binding force or validity; of no efficacy; invalid. The contract of a minor is _null_ in law, except for necessaries.

**NULL**, _noun_ Something that has no force or meaning. A cipher is called a _null_ \[Not used.\]
