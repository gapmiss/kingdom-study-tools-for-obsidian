# Neutralize

**NEUTRALIZE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To render neutral; to reduce to a state of indifference between different parties or opinions.

**2.** In chemistry, to destroy or render inert or imperceptible the peculiar properties of a body by combining it with a different substance. Thus to _neutralize_ acids and alkalies, is to combine them in such proportions that the compound will no exhibit the qualities of either. This is called a neutral salt.

**3.** To destroy the peculiar properties or opposite dispositions of parties or other things, or reduce them to a state of indifference or inactivity; as, to _neutralize_ parties in government; to _neutralize_ opposition.
