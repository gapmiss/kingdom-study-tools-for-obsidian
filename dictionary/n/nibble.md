# Nibble

**NIBBLE**, _verb transitive_ \[from nib.\]

**1.** To bite by little at a time; to eat slowly or in small bits. So sheep are said to _nibble_ the grass.

**2.** To bite, as a fish does the bait; to carp at; just to catch by biting.

**NIBBLE**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To bite at; as, fishes _nibble_ at the bait.

**2.** To carp at; to find fault; to censure little faults.

Instead of returning a full answer to my book, he manifestly nibbles at a single passage.

**NIBBLE**, _noun_ A little bite, or seizing to bite.
