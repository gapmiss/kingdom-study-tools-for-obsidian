# Novice

**NOV'ICE**, _noun_ \[Latin from new.\]

**1.** One who is new in any business; one unacquainted or unskilled; one in the rudiments; a beginner.

I am young, a _novice_ in the trade.

**2.** One that has entered a religious house but has not taken the vow; a probationer.

**3.** One newly planted in the church, or one newly converted to the Christian faith. [[1 Timothy 3#6|1 Timothy 3:6]].
