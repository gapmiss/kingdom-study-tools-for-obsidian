# Neap

**NEAP**, _noun_ \[This word may belong to the root of neb, nib; nose\] The tongue or pole of a cart, sled or wagon.

**NEAP**, _adjective_ \[to incline, to fall\] The _neap_ tides are those which happen in the middle of the second and fourth quarters of the moon. They are low tides, and opposed to spring tides.

**NEAP**, _noun_ Low water. \[Little used.\]
