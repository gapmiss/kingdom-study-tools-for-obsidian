# News

**NEWS**, _noun_ \[ From new; This word has a plural form, but is almost always united with a verb in the singular.\]

**1.** Recent account; fresh information of something that has lately taken place at a distance, or of something before unknown; tidings. We have _news_ from Constantinople. _news_ has just arrived. This _news_ is favorable.

Evil _news_ rides fast, while good _news_ baits.

It is no _news_ for the weak and poor to be a prey to the strong and rich.

**2.** A newspaper.
