# Nut

**NUT**, _noun_ \[It seems to be allied to knot, a bunch or hard lump.\]

**1.** The fruit of certain trees and shrubs, consisting of a hard shell inclosing a kernel. A _nut_ is properly the pericarp of the fruit. Various kinds of nuts are distinguished; as walnut, chestnut, hazlenut, butternut.

**2.** In mechanics, a small cylinder or other body, with teeth or projections corresponding with the teeth or grooves of a wheel.

**3.** The projection near the eye of an anchor.

**NUT**, _verb transitive_ To gather nuts.
