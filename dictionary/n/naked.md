# Naked

**NAKED**, _adjective_ Open, discovered, to strip.

**1.** Not covered; bare; having no clothes on; as a _naked_ body or a _naked_ limb.

**2.** Unarmed; defenseless; open; exposed; having no means of defense or protection against an enemys attack, or against other injury.

Behold my bosom _naked_ to your swords.

**3.** Open to view; not concealed; manifest. [[Hebrews 4#13|Hebrews 4:13]].

**4.** Destitute of worldly goods. [[Job 1#21|Job 1:21]].

**5.** Exposed to shame and disgrace. [[Exodus 32#25|Exodus 32:25]].

**6.** Guilty and exposed to divine wrath. Revelations 3.

**7.** Plain; evident; undisguised; as the _naked_ truth.

**8.** Mere; bare; simple; wanting the necessary additions. God requires of man something besides the _naked_ belief of his being and his word.

**9.** Not inclosed in a pod or case; as _naked_ seeds of a plant.

**10.** Without leaves, fulcres or arms; as a _naked_ stem or trunk.

**11.** Not assisted by glasses; as the _naked_ eye.
