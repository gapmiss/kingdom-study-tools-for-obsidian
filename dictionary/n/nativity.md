# Nativity

**NATIVITY**, _noun_

**1.** Birth; the coming into life or the world. The feast of Christmas is observed in memory of Christs _nativity_

**2.** Time, place and manner of birth; as, to calculate ones _nativity_

**3.** State or place of being produced.

These, in their dark _nativity_ the deep Shall yield us pregnant with infernal flame.
