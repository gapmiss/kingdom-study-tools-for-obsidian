# Northerly

**NORTH'ERLY**, _adjective_ Being towards the north, or nearer towards the north than to any other cardinal point. \[We use this word and northern with considerable latitude.\]

**NORTH'ERLY**, _adverb_

**1.** Towards the north; as, to sail _northerly_

**2.** In a northern direction; as a _northerly_ course.

**3.** Proceeding from a northern point.
