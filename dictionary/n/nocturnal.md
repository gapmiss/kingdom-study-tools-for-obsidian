# Nocturnal

**NOCTURN'AL**, _adjective_ \[Latin night.\]

**1.** Pertaining to night; as _nocturnal_ darkness.

**2.** Done or happening at night; as a _nocturnal_ expedition or assault; a _nocturnal_ visit.

**3.** Nightly; done or being every night.

From gilded roofs depending lamps display _nocturnal_ beams, that emulate the day.

**NOCTURN'AL**, _noun_ An instrument, chiefly used at sea to take the altitude of stars about the pole, in order to ascertain the latitude. This may be a hemisphere, or a planisphere on the plane of the equinoctial.
