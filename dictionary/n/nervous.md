# Nervous

**NERVOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Strong; vigorous; as a _nervous_ arm.

**2.** Pertaining to the nerves; seated in or affecting the nerves; as a _nervous_ disease or fever.

**3.** Having the nerves affected; hypochondriac; a colloquial use of the word.

**4.** Possessing or manifesting vigor of mind; characterized by strength in sentiment or style; as a _nervous_ historian.
