# Youth

**YOUTH**, _noun_ Yuth. \[G.\]

**1.** The part of life that succeeds to childhood. In a general sense, _youth_ denotes the whole early part of life, from infancy to manhood; but it is not unusual to divide the stages of life into infancy, childhood, _youth_ and manhood. In this sense the word can have no plural.

Those who pass their _youth_ in vice, are justly condemned to spend their age in folly.

**2.** A young man. In this sense it has a plural.

Seven youths from Athens yearly sent--

**3.** A young person, male or female.

**4.** Young persons, collectively.

It is fit to _youth_ to read the best authors first.
