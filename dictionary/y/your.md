# Your

**YOUR**, _adjective_ Pronom. _pronoun_ yure. \[G.\]

**1.** Belonging to you; equally applicable to both numbers; as _your_ father; _your_ heart; _your_ prince; _your_ subjects.

**2.** It is used indefinitely.

**YOUR** medalist and _your_ critic are much nearer related than the world imagine.

**3.** Yours is used as a substitute for a noun in the nominative or objective. This book is yours. I have no pen; give me yours. My sword and yours are kin.
