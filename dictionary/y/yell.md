# Yell

**YELL**, _verb intransitive_ \[It agrees in elements with call.\] To cry out with a hideous noise; to cry or scream as with agony or horror. Savages _yell_ most frightfully when they are rushing to the first onset of battle.

Nor the night raven, that still deadly yells.

**YELL**, _noun_ A sharp, loud, hideous outcry.

Their hideous yells rend the dark welkin.
