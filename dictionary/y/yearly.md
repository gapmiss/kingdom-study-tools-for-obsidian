# Yearly

**YEARLY**, _adjective_

**1.** Annual; happening; accruing or coming every year; as a _yearly_ rent or income.

**2.** Lasting a year; as a _yearly_ plant.

**3.** Comprehending a year; as the _yearly_ circuit or revolution of the earth.

**YEARLY**, _adverb_ Annually; once a year; as blessings _yearly_ bestowed.
