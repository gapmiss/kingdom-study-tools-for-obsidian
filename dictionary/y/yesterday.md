# Yesterday

**YESTERDAY**, _noun_ \[See [[Yester]].\]

**1.** The day last past; the day next before the present.

All our yesterdays have lighted fools the way to dusty death.

We are but of _yesterday_ and know nothing. [[Job 8#9|Job 8:9]].

**2.** _yesterday_ is used generally without a preposition; as, I went to town _yesterday_ _yesterday_ we received letters from our friends. In this ease, a preposition is understood; as on _yesterday_ or during _yesterday_ The word may be considered as adverbially used.
