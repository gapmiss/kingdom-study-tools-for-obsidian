# Judiciary

**JUDI'CIARY** _noun_ \[Latin judiciarius.\]

**1.** Passing judgment or sentence.

**2.** Pertaining to the courts of judicature or legal tribunals.

**JUDI'CIARY**, _noun_ That branch of government which is concerned in the trial and determination of controversies between parties, and of criminal prosecutions; the system of courts of justice in a government. An independent _judiciary_ is the firmest bulwark of freedom.
