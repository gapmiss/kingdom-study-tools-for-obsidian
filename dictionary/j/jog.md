# Jog

**JOG**, _verb transitive_ \[Eng. shock, shake.\] To push or shake with the elbow or hand; to give notice or excite attention by a slight push.

Sudden I jogged Ulysses.

**JOG**, _verb intransitive_ To move by jogs or small shocks, like those of a slow trot.

_So hung his destiny, never to rot,_

While he might still _jog_ on, and keep his trot.
