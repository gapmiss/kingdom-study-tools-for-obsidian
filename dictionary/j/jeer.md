# Jeer

**JEER**, _verb intransitive_ To utter severe, sarcastic reflections; to scoff; to deride; to flout; to make a mock of; as, to _jeer_ at one in sport.

**JEER**, _verb transitive_ To treat with scoffs or derision.

**JEER**, _noun_ Railing language; scoff; taunt; biting jest; flout; jibe; mockery; derision; ridicule with scorn.

Midas exposed to all their jeers,

Had lost his art, and kept his ears.
