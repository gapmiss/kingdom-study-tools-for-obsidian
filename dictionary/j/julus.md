# Julus

**JU'LUS**, _noun_ \[Gr. a handful or bundle.\]

**1.** In botany, a catkin or ament, a species of calyx or inflorescence, consisting of chaffy scales arranged along a stalk, as in hazel, birch, willow, etc.

**2.** A genus of multiped insects, of the order of Apters, of a semi-cylindrical form, with moniliform antennae, and two articulated palpi.
