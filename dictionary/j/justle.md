# Justle

**JUS'TLE**, _verb intransitive_ jus'l. \[See [[Jostle and Just]].\] To run against; to encounter; to strike against; to clash.

The chariots shall rage in the streets; they shall _justle_ one against another in the broad ways. [[Nahum 2#4|Nahum 2:4]]

**JUS'TLE**, _verb transitive_ jus'l. To push; to drive; to force by rushing against; commonly followed by off or out; as, to _justle_ a thing off the table, or out of its place.

First occurrence in the Bible(KJV): [[Nahum 2#4|Nahum 2:4]]
