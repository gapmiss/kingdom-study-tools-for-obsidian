# Jaw

**JAW**, _noun_

**1.** The bones of the mouth in which the teeth are fixed. They resemble a horse shoe. In most animals, the under _jaw_ only is movable.

**2.** The mouth.

**3.** In vulgar language, scolding, wrangling, abusive clamor.

**JAW**, _verb intransitive_ To scold; to clamor. \[Vulgar.\]

**JAW**, _verb transitive_ To abuse by scolding. \[Vulgar.\]
