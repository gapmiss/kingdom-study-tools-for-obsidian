# Jolt

**JOLT**, _verb intransitive_ To shake with short abrupt risings and fallings; as a carriage moving on rough ground. The carriage jolts.

**JOLT**, _verb transitive_ To shake with sudden jerks, as in a carriage on rough ground, or on a high trotting horse; as the horse or carriage jolts the rider.

**JOLT**, _noun_ A shock or shake by a sudden jerk, as in a carriage.
