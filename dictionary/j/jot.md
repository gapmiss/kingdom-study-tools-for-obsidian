# Jot

**JOT**, _noun_ \[ Heb. yod.\] An iota; a point; a tittle; the least quantity assignable.

Till heaven and earth pass, one _jot_ or one title shall in no wise pass from the law till all shall be fulfilled. [[Matthew 5#18|Matthew 5:18]].

A man may read much, and acquire not a _jot_ of knowledge, or be a _jot_ the wiser.

**JOT**, _verb transitive_ To set down; to make a memorandum of.
