# Journey

**JOURNEY**, _noun_ jur'ny. \[Latin diurnus, dies.\]

**1.** The travel of a day.

**2.** Travel by land to any distance and for any time, indefinitely; as a _journey_ from London to Paris, or to Rome; a _journey_ to visit a brother; a week's journey; we made two journeys to Philadelphia.

**3.** Passage form one place to another; as a long _journey_ from the upper regions.

**4.** It may sometimes include a passing by water.

**JOURNEY**, _verb intransitive_ jur'ny. To travel form place to place; to pass from home to a distance.

Abram journeyed, going on still towards the south. [[Genesis 12#9|Genesis 12:9]].
