# Junto

**JUN'TO**, _noun_ \[Latin junctus, joined.\]

**1.** Primarily, a select council or assembly, which deliberates in secret on any affair of government. In a good sense, it is not used in English; but hence,

**2.** A cabal; a meeting or collection of men combined for secret deliberation and intrigue for party purposes; a faction; as a _junto_ of ministers.
