# Justness

**JUST'NESS**, _noun_ Accuracy; exactness; as the _justness_ of proportions.

**1.** Conformity to truth; as the _justness_ of a description or representation.

**2.** Justice; reasonableness; equity; as the _justness_ of a cause or of a demand. \[Justness is properly applied to things, and justice to persons; but the distinction is not always observed.\]
