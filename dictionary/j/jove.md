# Jove

**JOVE**, _noun_ \[Latin Jovis.\]

**1.** The name of the Supreme Deity among the Romans.

**2.** The planet jupiter.

Or ask of yonder argent fields above.

Why Jove's satellites are less than _jove_

**3.** The air or atmosphere, or the god of the air.

And _jove_ descends in showers of kindly rain.
