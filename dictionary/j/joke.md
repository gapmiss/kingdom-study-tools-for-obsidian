# Joke

**JOKE**, _noun_ \[Latin jocus.\]

**1.** A jest; something said for the sake of exciting a laugh; something witty or sportive; raillery. A jealous person will rarely bear a _joke_

**2.** An illusion; something not real, or to no purpose.

Inclose whole downs in walls, 'tis all a joke!

In _joke_ in jest; for the sake of raising a laugh; not in earnest.

**JOKE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin jocor.\] To jest; to be merry in words or actions.

**JOKE**, _verb transitive_ To rally; to cast jokes at; to make merry with.
