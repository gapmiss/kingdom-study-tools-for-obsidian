# Judicious

**JUDI'CIOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** According to sound judgment; wise; prudent; rational; adapted to obtain a good end by the best means; used of things. Nothing is more important to success in the world than a _judicious_ application of time, unless it may be a _judicious_ expenditure of money.

**2.** Acting according to sound judgment; possessing sound judgment; wise; directed by reason and wisdom; used of persons; as a _judicious_ magistrate; a _judicious_ historian.
