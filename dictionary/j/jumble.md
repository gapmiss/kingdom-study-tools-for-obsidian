# Jumble

**JUM'BLE**, _verb transitive_ To mix in a confused mass; to put or throw together without order. It is often followed by together.

One may observe how apt that is to _jumble_ together passages of Scripture.

**JUM'BLE**, _verb intransitive_ To meet, mix or unite in a confused manner.

**JUM'BLE**, _noun_ Confused mixture, mass or collection without order.
