# Refute

**REFU'TE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin refuto; re and futo, _obsolete_ The primary sense of futo, is to drive or thrust, to beat back.\]

To disprove and overthrow by argument, evidence or countervailing proof; to prove to be false or erroneous; to confute. We say, to _refute_ arguments, to _refute_ testimony, to _refute_ opinions or theories, to _refute_ a disputant.

There were so many witnesses to these two miracles, that it is impossible to _refute_ such multitudes.
