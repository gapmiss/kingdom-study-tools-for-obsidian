# Remnant

**REM'NANT**, _noun_ \[contracted from remanent. See [[Remain]].\]

**1.** Residue; that which is left after the separation, removal or destruction of a part.

The _remnant_ that are left of the captivity. [[Nehemiah 1#3|Nehemiah 1:3]].

**2.** That which remains after a part is done, performed, told or passed.

The _remnant_ of my tale is of a length to tire your patience.

Where I may think the _remnant_ of my thoughts.

**REM'NANT**, _adjective_ Remaining; yet left.

And quiet dedicate her _remnant_ life to the just duties of a humble wife. \[Little used.\]
