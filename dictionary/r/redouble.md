# Redouble

**REDOUBLE**, _verb transitive_ redub'l. \[re and double.\]

**1.** To repeat in return.

**2.** To repeat often; as, to _redouble_ blows.

**3.** To increase by repeated or continued additions.

And AEtna rages with redoubl'd heat.

**REDOUBLE**, _verb intransitive_ redub'l. To become twice as much.

The argument redoubles upon us.
