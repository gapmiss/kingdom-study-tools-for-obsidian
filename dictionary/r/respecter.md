# Respecter

**RESPECT'ER**, _noun_ One that respects; chiefly used in the phrase, _respecter_ of persons, which signifies a person who regards the external circumstances of others in his judgment, and suffers his opinion to be biased by them, to the prejudice of candor, justice and equity.

I perceive that God is no _respecter_ of persons. [[Acts 10#34|Acts 10:34]].
