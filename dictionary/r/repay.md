# Repay

**REPA'Y**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To pay back; to refund; as, to _repay_ money borrowed or advanced.

**2.** To make return or requital; in a good or bad sense; as, to _repay_ kindness; to _repay_ an injury.

Benefits which cannot be repaid - are not commonly found to increase affection.

**3.** To recompense, as for a loss.

**4.** To compensate; as false honor repaid in contempt.
