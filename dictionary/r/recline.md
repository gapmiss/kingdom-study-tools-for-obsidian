# Recline

**RECLI'NE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin reclino; re and clino, to lean.\]

To lean back; to lean to one side or sideways; as, to _recline_ the head on a pillow, or on the bosom of another, or on the arm.

The mother reclin'd her dying head upon his breast.

**RECLI'NE**, _verb intransitive_ To lean; to rest or repose; as, to _recline_ on a couch.

**RECLI'NE**, _adjective_ \[Latin reclinis.\] Leaning; being in a leaning posture.

They sat _recline_ on the soft downy bank damask'd with flowers. \[Little used.\]
