# Resolved

**RESOLV'ED**, _participle passive_

**1.** Separated into its component parts; analyzed.

**2.** Determined in purpose; as, I am _resolved_ not to keep company with gamesters. This phrase is properly, 'I have resolved; ' as we say, a person is deceased, or has deceased; he is retired, or has retired. In these phrases, the participle is rather an adjective.

**3.** Determined officially or by vote.
