# Repentant

**REPENT'ANT**, _adjective_

**1.** Sorrowful for past conduct or words.

**2.** Sorrowful for sin.

**3.** Expressing or showing sorrow for sin; as _repentant_ tears; _repentant_ ashes; _repentant_ sighs.

**REPENT'ANT**, _noun_

**1.** One who repents; a penitent.

**2.** One that expresses sorrow for sin.
