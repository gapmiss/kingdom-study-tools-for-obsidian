# Rescind

**RESCIND**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin rescindo; re and scindo; to cut.\]

**1.** To abrogate; to revoke; to annul; to vacate an act by the enacting authority or by superior authority; as, to _rescind_ a law, a resolution or a vote; to _rescind_ an edict or decree; to _rescind_ a judgment.

**2.** To cut off. \[Not used.\]
