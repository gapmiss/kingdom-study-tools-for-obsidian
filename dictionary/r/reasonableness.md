# Reasonableness

**RE'ASONABLENESS**, _noun_

**1.** The faculty of reason. \[In this sense, little used.\]

**2.** Agreeableness to reason; that state or quality of a thing which reason supports or justifies; as the _reasonableness_ of our wishes, demands or expectations.

the _reasonableness_ and excellency of charity.

**3.** Conformity to rational principles.

The whole frame and contexture of a watch carries in it a _reasonableness_ - the passive impression of the reason or intellectual idea that was in the artist. \[Unusual.\]

**4.** Moderation; as the _reasonableness_ of a demand.
