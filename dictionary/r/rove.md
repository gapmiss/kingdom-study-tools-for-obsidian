# Rove

**ROVE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin rapio.\]

To wander; to ramble; to range; to go, move or pass without certain direction in any manner, by walking, riding, flying or otherwise.

For who has power to walk, has power to _rove_

**ROVE**, _verb transitive_ To wander over; as roving a field; roving the town. This is an elliptical form of expression, for roving over, through or about the town.

**ROVE**, _verb transitive_ To draw a thread, string or cord through an eye or aperture.
