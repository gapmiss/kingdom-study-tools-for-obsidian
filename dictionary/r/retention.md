# Retention

**RETEN'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin retentio, retineo; re and teneo, to hold.\]

**1.** The act of retaining or keeping.

**2.** The power of retaining; the faculty of the mind by which it retains ideas.

**3.** In medicine, the power of retaining, or that state of contraction in the solid or vascular parts of the body, by which they hold their proper contents and prevent in voluntary evacuations; undue _retention_ of some natural discharge.

**4.** The act of withholding; restraint.

**5.** Custody; confinement. \[Not in use.\]
