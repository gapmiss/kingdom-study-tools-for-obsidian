# Reanimate

**REAN'IMATE**, _verb transitive_ \[re and animate.\]

**1.** To revive; to resuscitate; to restore to life; as a person dead or apparently dead; as, to _reanimate_ a drowned person.

**2.** To revive the spirits when dull or languid; to invigorate; to infuse new life or courage into; as, to _reanimate_ disheartened troops; to _reanimate_ drowsy senses or languid spirits.
