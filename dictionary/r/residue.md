# Residue

**RES'IDUE**, _noun_ \[Latin residuus.\]

**1.** That which remains after a part is taken, separated, removed or designated.

The locusts shall eat the _residue_ of that which has escaped. [[Exodus 10#5|Exodus 10:5]].

The _residue_ of them will I deliver to the sword. [[Jeremiah 15#9|Jeremiah 15:9]].

**2.** The balance or remainder of a debt or account.
