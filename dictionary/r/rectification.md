# Rectification

**RECTIFICA'TION**, _noun_

**1.** The act or operation of correcting, amending or setting right that which is wrong or erroneous; as the retification of errors, mistakes or abuses.

**2.** In chemistry, the process of refining or purifying any substance by repeated distiliation, which separates the grosser parts; as the _rectification_ of spirits or sulphuric acid.
