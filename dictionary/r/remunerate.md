# Remunerate

**REMU'NERATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin remunero; re and munero, from munus, a gift.\]

to reward; to recompense; to requite; in a good sense; to pay an equivalent to for any service, loss, expense or other sacrifice; as, to _remunerate_ the troops of an army for their services and sufferings; to _remunerate_ men for labor. the pious usfferer in this life will be remunerated in the life to come.
