# Roam

**ROAM**, _verb intransitive_ \[If m is radical, this word seems to be connected with ramble, Latin ramus.\]

To wander; to ramble; to rove; to walk or move about from place to place without any certain purpose or direction. The wolf and the savage _roam_ in the forest.

Daphne roaming through a thorny wood.

**ROAM**, _verb transitive_ To range; to wander over; as, to _roam_ the woods; but the phrase is elliptical.
