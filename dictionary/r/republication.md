# Republication

**REPUBLICA'TION**, _noun_ \[re and publication.\]

**1.** A second publication, or a new publication of something before published.

**2.** A second publication, as of a former will, renewal.

If there be many testaments, the last overthrows all the former; but the _republication_ of a former will, revokes one of a later date, and establishes the first.
