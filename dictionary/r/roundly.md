# Roundly

**ROUND'LY**, _adverb_

**1.** In a round form or manner.

**2.** Openly; boldly; without reserve; peremptorily.

He affirms every thing _roundly_

**3.** Plainly; fully. He gives them _roundly_ to understand that their duty is submission.

**4.** Briskly; with speed.

When the mind has brought itself to attention, it will be able to cope with difficulties and master them, and then it may go on _roundly_

**5.** Completely; to the purpose; vigorously; in earnest.
