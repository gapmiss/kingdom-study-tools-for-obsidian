# Radiance

**RA'DIANCE**,

RA'DIANCY, _noun_ \[Latin radians, radio, to beam or shoot rays. See [[Radius and Ray]].\]

Properly, brightness shooting in rays or beams; hence in general, brilliant or sparkling luster; vivid brightness; as the _radiance_ of the sun.

The Son girt with omnipotence, with _radiance_ crown'd of majesty divine.
