# Ruck

**RUCK**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin rugo, to wrinkle, to fold; ruga, a fold.\]

**1.** To cower; to bend and set close. \[Not in use.\]

**2.** To wrinkle; as, to _ruck_ up cloth or a garment.

\[In this sense, the word is still used by the common people of New England.\]

**RUCK**, _noun_ A wrinkle; a fold; a plait.
