# Raddle

**RAD'DLE**, _verb transitive_

To twist; to wind together. \[Not in use.\]

**RAD'DLE**, _noun_ \[supra.\] A long stick used in hedging; also, a hedge formed by interweaving the shoots and branches of trees or shrubs.

\[I believe the two foregoing words are not used in the United States, and probably they are local.\]
