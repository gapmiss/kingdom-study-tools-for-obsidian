# Rubric

**RU'BRIC**, _noun_ \[Latin rubrica; rubeo, to be red.\]

**1.** In the canon law, a title or article in certain ancient law books; so called because written in red letters.

**2.** Directions printed in prayer books.

The _rubric_ and the rules relating to the liturgy are established by royal authority, as well as the liturgy itself.

**RU'BRIC**, _verb transitive_ To adorn with red.
