# Reflex

**RE'FLEX**, _adjective_ \[Latin reflexus.\]

**1.** Directed back; as a _reflex_ act of the soul, the turning of the intellectual eye inward upon its own actions.

**2.** Designating the parts of a painting illuminated by light reflected from another part of the same picture.

**3.** In botany, bent back; reflected.

**REFLEX'**, _noun_ Reflection. \[Not used.\]

**REFLEX'**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To reflect.

**2.** To bend back; to turn back. \[Little used.\]
