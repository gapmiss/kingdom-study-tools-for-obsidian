# Recite

**RECI'TE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin recito; re and cito, to call or name.\]

**1.** To rehearse; to repeat the words of another or of a writing; as, to _recite_ the words of an author or of a deed or covenant.

**2.** In writing, to copy; as, the words of a deed are recited in the pleading.

**3.** To tell over; to relate; to narrate; as to _recite_ past events; to _recite_ the particulars of a voyage.

**4.** To rehearse, as a lesson to an instructor.

**5.** To enumerate.

**RECI'TE**, _verb intransitive_ To rehearse a lesson. The class will _recite_ at eleven o'clock.

**RECITE**. for recital. \[Not in use.\]
