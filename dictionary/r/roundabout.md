# Roundabout

**ROUND'ABOUT**, _adjective_ \[round and about.\]

**1.** Indirect; going round; loose.

Paraphrase is a _roundabout_ way of translating.

**2.** Ample; extensive; as _roundabout_ sense.

**3.** Encircling; encompassing.

\[In any sense, this word is inelegant.\]

**ROUND'ABOUT**, _noun_ A large strait coat.
