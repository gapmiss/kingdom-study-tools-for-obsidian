# Recusant

**RECU'SANT**, _adjective_ s as z. \[Latin recusans, recuso, to refuse; re and the root of causa, signifying to drive. The primary sense is to repel or drive back.\]

Refusing to acknowledge the supremacy of the king, or to conform to the established rites of the church; as a _recusant_ lord.

**RECU'SANT**, _noun_ \[supra.\]

**1.** In English history, a person who refuses to acknowledge the supremacy of the king in matters of religion; as a popish _recusant_ who acknowledges the supremacy of the pope.

**2.** One who refuses communion with the church of England; a non-conformist.

All that are recusants of holy rites.
