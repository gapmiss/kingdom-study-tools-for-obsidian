# Rose

**ROSE**, _noun_ s as z. \[Latin , Gr. from the root of red, ruddy. See [[Red]].\]

**1.** A plant and flower of the genus Rosa, of many species and varieties, as the wild canine or dog-rose, the white _rose_ the red _rose_ the cinnamon _rose_ the eglantine or sweet briar, etc. There are five petals; the calyx is urceolate, quinquefid, and corneous; the seeds are numerous, hispid, and fixed to the inside of the calyx.

**2.** A knot of ribbon in the form of a _rose_ used as an ornamental tie of a shoe.

Under the _rose_ in secret; privately; in a manner that forbids disclosure.

**ROSE** of Jericho, a plant growing on the plain of Jericho, the Anastatica hierochuntica.

**ROSE**, _preterit tense_ of rise.
