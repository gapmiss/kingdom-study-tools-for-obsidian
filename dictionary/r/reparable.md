# Reparable

**REP'ARABLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin reparabilis. See [[Repair]].\]

**1.** That may be repaired or restored to a sound or good state; as, a house or wall is not _reparable_

**2.** That may be retrieved or made good; as, the loss is _reparable_

**3.** That may be supplied by an equivalent; as a _reparable_ injury.
