# Risible

**RI'SIBLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin risibilis, from rideo, risi, to laugh. See [[Ridiculous]].\]

**1.** Having the faculty or power of laughing. Man is a _risible_ animal.

**2.** Laughable; capable of exciting laughter. The description of Falstaff in Shakespeare, exhibits a _risible_ scene. _risible_ differs from ludicrous, as species from genus; ludicrous expressing that which is playful and sportive; _risible_ that which may excite laughter. _risible_ differs from ridiculous, as the latter implies something mean or contemptible, and _risible_ does not.
