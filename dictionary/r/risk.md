# Risk

**RISK**, _noun_

**1.** Hazard; danger; peril; exposure to harm. He, at the _risk_ of his life, saved a drowning man.

**2.** In commerce, the hazard of loss, either of ship, goods or other property. Hence, _risk_ signifies also the degree of hazard or danger; for the premiums of insurance are calculated upon the _risk_ The underwriters now take risks at a low premium.

To run a _risk_ is to incur hazard; to encounter danger.

**RISK**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To hazard; to endanger; to expose to injury or loss; as, to _risk_ goods on board of a ship; to _risk_ one's person in battle; to _risk_ one's fame by a publication; to _risk_ life in defense of rights.

**2.** To venture; to dare to undertake; as, to _risk_ a battle or combat.
