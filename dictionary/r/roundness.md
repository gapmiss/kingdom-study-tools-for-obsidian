# Roundness

**ROUND'NESS**, _noun_

**1.** The quality of being round, circular, spherical, globular or cylindrical; circularity; sphericity; cylindrical form; rotundity; as the _roundness_ of the globe, of the orb of the sun, of a ball, of a bowl, etc.

**2.** Fullness; smoothness of flow; as the _roundness_ of a period.

**3.** Openness; plainness; boldness; positiveness; as the _roundness_ of an assertion.
