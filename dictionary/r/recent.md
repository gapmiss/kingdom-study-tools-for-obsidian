# Recent

**RE'CENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin recens.\]

**1.** New; being of late origin or existence.

The ancients believed some parts of Egypt to be _recent_ and formed by the mud discharged into the sea by the Nile.

**2.** Late; modern; as great and worthy men ancient or _recent_ \[Modern is now used.\]

**3.** Fresh; lately received; as _recent_ news or intelligence.

**4.** Late; of late occurrence; as a _recent_ event or transaction.

**5.** Fresh; not long dismissed, released or parted from; as Ulysses, _recent_ from the storms.
