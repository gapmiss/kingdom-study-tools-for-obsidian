# Revile

**REVI'LE**, _verb transitive_ \[re and vile.\]

To reproach; to treat with opprobrious and contemptuous language.

She revileth him to his face.

Thou shalt not _revile_ the gods. [[Exodus 22#28|Exodus 22:28]].

Blessed are ye when men shall _revile_ you. [[Matthew 5#11|Matthew 5:11]].

**REVI'LE**, _noun_ Reproach; contumely; contemptuous language. \[Not in use.\]
