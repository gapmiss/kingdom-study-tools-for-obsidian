# Raiment

**RA'IMENT**, _noun_ \[for arrayment. See [[Array and Ray]].\]

**1.** Clothing in general; vestments; vesture; garments.

[[Genesis 24#53|Genesis 24:53]]. [[Deuteronomy 8#4|Deuteronomy 8:4]].

Living, both food and _raiment_ she supplies.

**2.** A single garment.

\[In this sense it is rarely used, and indeed is improper.\]
