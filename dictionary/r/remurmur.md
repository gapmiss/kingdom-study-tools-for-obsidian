# Remurmur

**REMUR'MUR**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin remurmuro; re and murmuro.\]

to utter back in murmurs; to return in murmurs; to repeat in low hoarse sounds.

The trembling trees in every plain and wood, her fate _remurmur_ to the silver flood.

**REMUR'MUR**, _verb intransitive_ to murmur back; to return or echo in low rumbling sounds.

The realms of Mars remurmur'd all around.
