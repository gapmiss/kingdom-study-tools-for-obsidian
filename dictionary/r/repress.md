# Repress

**REPRESS**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin repressus, reprimo; re and premo, to press.\]

**1.** To crush; to quell; to put down; to subdue; to suppress; as, to _repress_ sedition or rebellion; to _repress_ the first risings of discontent.

**2.** To check; to restrain.

Such kings favor the innocent, _repress_ the bold.

**REPRESS'**, _noun_ The act of subduing. \[Not in use.\]
