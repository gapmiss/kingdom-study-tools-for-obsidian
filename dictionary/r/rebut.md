# Rebut

**REBUT**', _verb transitive_ \[See [[Butt and Pout]].\]

To repel; to oppose by argument, plea or countervailing proof. \[It is used by lawyers in a general sense.\]

**REBUT'**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To retire back. _obsolete_

**2.** To answer, as a plaintiff's sur-rejoinder.

The plaintiff may answer the rejoinder by a sur-rejoinder; on which the defendant may _rebut_
