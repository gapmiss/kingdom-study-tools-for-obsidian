# Republican

**REPUB'LICAN**, _adjective_

**1.** Pertaining to a republic; consisting of a commonwealth; as a _republican_ constitution or government.

**2.** Consonant to the principles of a republic; as _republican_ sentiments or opinions; _republican_ manners.

**REPUB'LICAN**, _noun_ One who favors or prefers a _republican_ form of government.
