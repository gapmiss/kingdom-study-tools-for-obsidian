# Radiant

**RA'DIANT**, _adjective_ Shooting or darting rays of light; beaming with brightness; emitting a vivid light or splendor; as the _radiant_ sun.

Mark what _radiant_ state she spreads.

Radiant in glittering arms and beamy pride.

**RA'DIANT**, _noun_ In optics, the luminous point or object from which light emanates, that falls on a mirror or lens.
