# Runner

**RUN'NER**, _noun_ \[from run.\]

**1.** One that runs; that which runs.

**2.** A racer.

**3.** A messenger.

**4.** A shooting sprig.

In every root there will be one _runner_ with little buds on it.

**5.** One of the stones of a mill.

**6.** A bird.

**7.** A thick rope used to increase the mechanical power of a tackle.
