# Rue

**RUE**, _verb transitive_ ru. \[Latin rudo, to roar, to bray.\]

To lament; to regret; to grieve for; as, to _rue_ the commission of a crime; to _rue_ the day.

Thy will chose freely what it now so justly rues.

**RUE**, _verb intransitive_ To have compassion. \[Not in use.\]

**RUE**, _noun_ Sorrow; repentance. \[Not in use.\]

**RUE**, _noun_ ru. \[Gr. Latin \]

A plant of the genus Ruta, of several species. The common garden _rue_ is medicinal, as a stimulant and detergent.
