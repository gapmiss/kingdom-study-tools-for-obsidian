# Reciprocate

**RECIP'ROCATE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin reciproco.\] To act interchangeably; to alternate.

One brawny smith the puffing bellows plies, and draws and blows reciprocating air.

**RECIP'ROCATE**, _verb transitive_ To exchange; to interchange; to give and return mutually; as, to _reciprocate_ favors.
