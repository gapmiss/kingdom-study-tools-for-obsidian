# Rivet

**RIV'ET**, _verb transitive_ \[Heb. to drive.\]

**1.** To fasten with a _rivet_ or with rivets; as, to _rivet_ two pieces of iron.

**2.** To clinch; as, to _rivet_ a pin or bolt.

**3.** To fasten firmly; to make firm, strong or immovable; as, to _rivet_ friendship or affection.

**RIV'ET**, _noun_ a pin of iron or other metal with a head, driven through a piece of timber or metal, and the point bent or spread and beat down fast, to prevent its being drawn out; or a pin or bolt clinched at both ends.
