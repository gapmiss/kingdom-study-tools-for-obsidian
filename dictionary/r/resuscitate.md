# Resuscitate

**RESUS'CITATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin resuscito; re and suscito, to raise.\]

**1.** To revivify; to revive; particularly, to recover from apparent death; as, to _resuscitate_ a drowned person; to _resuscitate_ withered plants.

**2.** To reproduce, as a mixed body from its ashes.
