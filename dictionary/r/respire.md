# Respire

**RESPI'RE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin respiro; re and spiro, to breathe.\]

**1.** To breathe; to inhale air into the lungs and exhale it, for the purpose of maintaining animal life.

**2.** To catch breath.

**3.** To rest; to take rest from toil.

**RESPI'RE**, _verb transitive_ To exhale; to breathe out; to send out in exhalations.
