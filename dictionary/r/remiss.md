# Remiss

**REMISS**', _adjective_ \[Latin remissus, supra.\]

**1.** Slack; dilatory; negligent; not performing duty or business; not complying with engagements at all, or not in due time; as to be _remiss_ in attendance on official duties; _remiss_ in payment of debts.

**2.** Slow; slack; languid.

**3.** Not intense.

These nervous, bold; those languid and _remiss_
