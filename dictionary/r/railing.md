# Railing

**RA'ILING**, _participle present tense_

**1.** Clamoring with insulting language; uttering reproachful words.

**2.** _adjective_ Expressing reproach; insulting; as a _railing_ accusation. [[2 Peter 2#11|2 Peter 2:11]].

**RA'ILING**, _noun_ Reproachful or insolent language. [[1 Peter 3#9|1 Peter 3:9]].

**RA'ILING**, _participle present tense_ Inclosing with rails.

**RA'ILING**, _noun_

**1.** A series of rails; a fence.

**2.** Rails in general; or the scantling for rails.
