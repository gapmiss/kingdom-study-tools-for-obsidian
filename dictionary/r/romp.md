# Romp

**ROMP**, _noun_ \[a different spelling of ramp. See [[Ramp and Romance]].\]

**1.** A rude girl who indulges in boisterous play.

**2.** Rude play or frolic.

**ROMP** loving miss is haul'd about in gallantry robust.

**ROMP**, _verb intransitive_ To play rudely and boisterously; to leap and frisk about in play.
