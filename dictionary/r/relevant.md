# Relevant

**REL'EVANT**, _adjective_ \[Latin relever, to relieve, to advance, to raise; re and lever, to raise.\]

**1.** Relieving; lending aid or support.

**2.** Pertinent; applicable. The testimony is not _relevant_ to the case. The argument is not _relevant_ to the question. \[This is the sense in which the word is now generally used.\]

**3.** Sufficient to support the cause.
