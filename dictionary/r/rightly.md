# Rightly

**RIGHTLY**, _adverb_

**1.** According to justice; according to the divine will or moral rectitude; as duty _rightly_ performed.

**2.** Properly; fitly; suitably; as a person _rightly_ named.

**3.** According to truth or fact; not erroneously. He has _rightly_ conjectured.

**4.** Honestly; uprightly.

**5.** Exactly.

Thou didst not _rightly_ see.

**6.** Straightly; directly. \[Not in use.\]
