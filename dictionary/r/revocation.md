# Revocation

**REVOCA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin revocatio.\]

**1.** The act of recalling or calling back; as the _revocation_ of Calvin.

**2.** State of being recalled.

**3.** Repeal; reversal; as the _revocation_ of the edict of Nantz. A law may cease to operate without an express _revocation_ so we speak of the _revocation_ of a will, of a use, of a devise, etc.
