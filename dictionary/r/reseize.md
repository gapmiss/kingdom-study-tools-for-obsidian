# Reseize

**RESE'IZE**, _verb transitive_ \[re and seize.\]

**1.** To seize again; to seize a second time.

**2.** In law, to take possession of lands and tenements which have been disseized.

Whereupon the sheriff is commanded to _reseize_ the land and all the chattels thereon, and keep the same in his custody till the arrival of the justices of assize.
