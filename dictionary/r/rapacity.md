# Rapacity

**RAPAC'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin rapacitas, from rapax, rapio.\]

**1.** Addictedness to plunder; the exercise of plunder; the act or practice of seizing by force; as the _rapacity_ of a conquering army; the _rapacity_ of pirates; the _rapacity_ of a Turkish pashaw; the _rapacity_ of extortioners.

**2.** Ravenousness; as the _rapacity_ of animals.

**3.** The act or practice of extorting or exacting by oppressive injustice.
