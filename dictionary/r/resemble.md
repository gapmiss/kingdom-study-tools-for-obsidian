# Resemble

**RESEM'BLE**, _verb transitive_ s as z. \[See [[similar]].\]

**1.** To have the likeness of; to bear the similitude of something, either in form, figure or qualities. One man may _resemble_ another in features; he may _resemble_ a third person in temper or deportment.

Each one resembled the children of a king. [[Judges 8#18|Judges 8:18]].

**2.** To liken; to compare; to represent as like something else.

The torrid parts of Africa are resembled to a libbard's skin, the distance of whose spots represents the dispersed situation of the habitations.
