# Reputable

**REP'UTABLE**, _adjective_ \[from repute.\]

**1.** Being in good repute; held in esteem; as a _reputable_ man or character; _reputable_ conduct. It expresses less than respectable and honorable, denoting the good opinion of men, without distinction or great qualities.

**2.** Consistent with reputation; not mean or disgraceful. It is evidence of extreme depravity that vice is in any case _reputable_

In the article of danger, it is as _reputable_ to elude an enemy as to defeat one.
