# Reconsider

**RECONSID'ER**, _verb transitive_ \[re and consider.\]

**1.** To consider again; to turn in the mind again; to review.

**2.** To annul; to take into consideration a second time and rescind; as, to _reconsider_ a motion in a legislative body; to _reconsider_ a vote. The vote has been reconsidered, that is, rescinded.
