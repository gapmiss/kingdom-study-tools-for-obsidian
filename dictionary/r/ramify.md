# Ramify

**RAM'IFY**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin ramus, a branch, and facio, to make.\]

To divide into branches or parts; as, to _ramify_ an art, a subject or scheme.

**RAM'IFY**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To shoot into branches, as the stem of a plant.

When the asparagus begins to _ramify_ -

**2.** To be divided or subdivided; as a main subject or scheme.
