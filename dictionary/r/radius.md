# Radius

**RA'DIUS**, _noun_ \[Latin id, a ray, a rod, a beam, a spoke, that is, a shoot; radio, to shine, that is, to dart beams. See [[Ray]].\]

**1.** In geometry, a right line drawn or extending from the center of a circle to the periphery, and hence the semidiameter of the circle. In trigonometry, the _radius_ is the whole sine, or sine of 90 degrees.

**2.** In anatomy, the exterior bone of the fore arm, descending along with the ulna from the elbow to the wrist.

**3.** In botany, a ray; the outer part or circumference of a compound radiate flower, or radiated discous flower.
