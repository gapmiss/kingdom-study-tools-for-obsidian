# Random

**RAN'DOM**, _noun_

**1.** A roving motion or course without direction; hence, want of direction, rule or method; hazard; chance; used in the phrase, at _random_ that is, without a settled point of direction; at hazard.

**2.** Course; motion; progression; distance of a body thrown; as the furthest _random_ of a missile weapon.

**RAN'DOM**, _adjective_

**1.** Done at hazard or without settled aim or purpose; left to chance; as a _random_ blow.

**2.** Uttered or done without previous calculation; as a _random_ guess.
