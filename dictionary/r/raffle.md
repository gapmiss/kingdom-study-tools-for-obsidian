# Raffle

**RAF'FLE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Heb. to strive. See [[Raff]].\]

To cast dice for a prize, for which each person concerned in the game lays down a stake, or hazards a part of the value; as, to _raffle_ for a watch.

**RAF'FLE**, _noun_ A game of chance, or lottery in which several persons deposit a part of the value of the thing, in consideration of the chance of gaining it. The successful thrower of the dice takes or sweeps the whole.
