# Repugnant

**REPUG'NANT**, _adjective_ \[Latin repugnans.\]

**1.** Opposite; contrary; inconsistent; properly followed by to. Every sin is _repugnant_ to the will of God. Every thing morally wrong, is _repugnant_ both to the honor, as well as to the interest of the offender.

**2.** Disobedient; not obsequious. \[Not in use.\]
