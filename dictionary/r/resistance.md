# Resistance

**RESIST'ANCE**, _noun_

**1.** The act of resisting; opposition. _resistance_ is passive, as that of a fixed body which interrupts the passage of a moving body; or active, as in the exertion of force to stop, repel or defeat progress or designs.

**2.** The quality of not yielding to force or external impression; that power of a body which acts in opposition to the impulse or pressure of another, or which prevents the effect of another power; as the _resistance_ of a ball which receives the force of another; the _resistance_ of wood to a cutting instrument; the _resistance_ of air to the motion of a cannon ball, or of water to the motion of a ship.
