# Reformation

**REF'ORMATION**, _noun_

**1.** The act of reforming; correction or amendment of life, manners, or of any thing vicious or corrupt; as the _reformation_ of manners; _reformation_ of the age; _reformation_ of abuses.

Satire lashes vice into _reformation_

**2.** By way of eminence, the change of religion from the corruptions of popery to its primitive purity, begun by Luther, _adjective_ D. 1517.
