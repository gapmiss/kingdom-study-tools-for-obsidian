# Reed

**REED**, _noun_

**1.** The common name of many aquatic plants; most of them large grasses, with hollow jointed stems, such as the common _reed_ of the genus Arundo, the bamboo, etc. The bur-reed is of the genus Sparganium; the Indian Flowering _reed_ of the genus Canna.

**2.** A musical pipe; reeds being anciently used for instruments of music.

**3.** A little tube through which a hautboy, bassoon or clarinet is blown.

**4.** An arrow, as made of a _reed_ headed.

**5.** Thatch.
