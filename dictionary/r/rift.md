# Rift

**RIFT**, _noun_ \[from rive.\] A cleft; a fissure; an opening made by riving or splitting.

**RIFT**, _verb transitive_ to cleave; to rive; to split; as, to _rift_ an oak or a rock.

**RIFT**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** to burst open; to split.

Timber - not apt to _rift_ with ordnance.

**2.** to belch; to break wind. \[Local.\]
