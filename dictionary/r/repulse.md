# Repulse

**REPULSE**, _noun_ repuls'. \[Latin repulsa, from repello; re and pello, to drive.\]

**1.** A being checked in advancing, or driven back by force. The enemy met with a _repulse_ and retreated.

**2.** Refusal; denial.

**REPULSE**, _verb transitive_ repuls'. \[Latin repulsus, repello.\]

To repel; to beat or drive back as, to _repulse_ an assailant or advancing enemy.
