# Rapidity

**RAPID'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin rapiditas.\]

**1.** Swiftness; celerity; velocity; as the _rapidity_ of a current; the _rapidity_ of motion of any kind.

**2.** Haste in utterance; as the _rapidity_ of speech or pronunciation.

**3.** Quickness of progression or advance; as _rapidity_ of growth or improvement.
