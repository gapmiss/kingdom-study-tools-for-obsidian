# Rapid

**RAP'ID**, _adjective_ \[Latin rapidus, from rapio, the primary sense of which is to rush.\]

**1.** Very swift or quick; moving with celerity; as a _rapid_ stream; a _rapid_ flight; a _rapid_ motion.

Part shun the goal with _rapid_ wheels.

**2.** Advancing with haste or speed; speedy in progression; as _rapid_ growth; _rapid_ improvement.

**3.** Of quick utterance of words; as a _rapid_ speaker.
