# Righteous

**RIGHTEOUS**, _adjective_ ri'chus.

**1.** Just; accordant to the divine law. Applied to persons, it denotes one who is holy in heart, and observant of the divine commands in practice; as a _righteous_ man. Applied to things, it denotes consonant to the divine will or to justice; as a _righteous_ act. It is used chiefly in theology, and applied to God, to his testimonies and to his saints.

The _righteous_ in Scripture, denote the servants of God, the saints.

**2.** Just; equitable; merited.

And I thy _righteous_ doom will bless.
