# Rashness

**RASH'NESS**, _noun_

**1.** To much haste in resolving or in undertaking a measure; precipitation; inconsiderate readiness or promptness to decide or act, implying disregard of consequences or contempt of danger; applied to persons. The failure of enterprises is often owing to _rashness_

We offend by _rashness_ which is an affirming or denying before we have sufficiently informed ourselves.

**2.** The quality of being uttered or done without due deliberation; as the _rashness_ of words or of undertakings.
