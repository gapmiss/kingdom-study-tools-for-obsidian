# Remonstrance

**REMON'STRANCE**, _noun_

**1.** Show; discovery. \[Not in use.\]

**2.** Expostulation; strong representation of reasons against a measure, either public or private, and when addressed to a public body, a prince or magistrate, it may be accompanied with a petition or supplication for the removal or prevention of some evil or inconvenience. A party aggrieved presents a _remonstrance_ to the legislature.

**3.** Pressing suggestions in opposition to a measure or act; as the remonstrances of conscience or of justice.

**4.** Expostulatory counsel or advice; reproof.
