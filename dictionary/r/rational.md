# Rational

**RA'TIONAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin rationalis.\]

**1.** Having reason or the faculty of reasoning; endowed with reason; opposed to irrational; as, man is a _rational_ being; brutes are not _rational_ animals.

It is our glory and happiness to have a _rational_ nature.

**2.** Agreeable to reason; opposed to absurd; as a _rational_ conclusion or inference; _rational_ conduct.

**3.** Agreeable to reason; not extravagant.

**4.** Acting in conformity to reason; wise; judicious; as a _rational_ man.

**RA'TIONAL**, _noun_ A _rational_ being.
