# Remediless

**REMED'ILESS**, _adjective_ \[In modern books, the accent is placed on the first syllable, which would be well if there were no derivatives; but remedilessly, remedilessness, require the accent on the second syllable.\]

**1.** Not admitting a remedy; incurable; desperate; as a _remediless_ disease.

**2.** Irreparable; as, a loss or damage is _remediless_

**3.** Not admitting change or reversal; as a _remediless_ doom.

**4.** Not admitting recovery; as a _remediless_ delusion.
