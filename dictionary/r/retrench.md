# Retrench

**RETRENCH**', _verb transitive_

**1.** To cut off; to pare away.

And thy exuberant parts _retrench_

**2.** To lessen; to abridge; to curtail; as, to _retrench_ superfluities or expenses.

**3.** To confine; to limit. \[Not proper.\]

**RETRENCH'**, _verb intransitive_ To live at less expense. It is more reputable to _retrench_ than to live embarrassed.
