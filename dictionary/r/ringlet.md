# Ringlet

**RING'LET**, _noun_

**1.** A small ring.

**2.** A curl; particularly, a curl of hair.

Her golden tresses in wanton ringlets wav'd

**3.** A circle.

To dance our ringlets in the whistling wind.

**RING'-OUSEL**, _noun_ A bird of the genus Turdus, inhabiting the hilly and mountainous parts of Great Britain.
