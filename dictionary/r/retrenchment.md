# Retrenchment

**RETRENCH'MENT**, _noun_

**1.** The act of lopping off; the act of removing what is superfluous; as the _retrenchment_ of words or lines in a writing.

**2.** The act of curtailing, lessening or abridging; diminution; as the _retrenchment_ of expenses.

**3.** In military affairs, any work raised to cover a post and fortify it against an enemy; such as fascines, gabions, sandbags and the like.
