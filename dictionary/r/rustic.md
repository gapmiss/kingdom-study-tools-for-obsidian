# Rustic

**RUST'IC**,

**RUST'IC**AL, _adjective_ \[Latin rusticus, from rus, the country.\]

**1.** Pertaining to the country; rural; as the _rustic_ gods of antiquity.

**2.** Rude; unpolished; rough; awkward; as _rustic_ manners or behavior.

**3.** Coarse; plain; simple; as _rustic_ entertainment; _rustic_ dress.

**4.** Simple; artless; unadorned.

Rustic work, in a building, is when the stones, etc., in the face of it, are hacked or pecked so as to be rough.

**RUST'IC**, _noun_ An inhabitant of the country; a clown.
