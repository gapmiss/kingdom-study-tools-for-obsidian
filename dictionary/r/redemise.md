# Redemise

**REDEMI'SE**, _verb transitive_ s as z. \[re and demise.\] To convey or transfer back, as an estate in fee simple, fee tail, for life or a term of years.

**REDEMI'SE**, _noun_ Reconveyance; the transfer of an estate back to the person who has demised it; as the demise and _redemise_ of an estate in fee simple, fee tail, or for life or years, by mutual leases.
