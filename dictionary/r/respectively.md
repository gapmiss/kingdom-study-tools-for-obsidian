# Respectively

**RESPECT'IVELY**, _adverb_

**1.** As relating to each; particularly; as each belongs to each. Let each man _respectively_ perform his duty.

The impressions from the objects of the senses do mingle _respectively_ every one with its kind.

**2.** Relatively; not absolutely.

**3.** Partially; with respect to private views. _obsolete_

**4.** With respect. _obsolete_
