# Residence

**RES'IDENCE**, _noun_

**1.** The act of abiding or dwelling in a place for some continuance of time; as the _residence_ of an American in France or Italy for a year.

The confessor had often made considerable residences in Normandy.

**2.** The place of abode; a dwelling; a habitation.

Caprea had been - the _residence_ of Tiberius for several years.

**3.** That which falls to the bottom of liquors. _obsolete_

**4.** In the canon and common law, the abode of a person or incumbent on his benefice; opposed to non-residence.
