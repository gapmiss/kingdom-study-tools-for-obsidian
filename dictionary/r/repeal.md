# Repeal

**REPE'AL**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin appello; ad and pello.\]

**1.** To recall. \[Obsolete as it respects persons.\]

**2.** To recall, as a deed, will, law or statute; to revoke; to abrogate by an authoritative act, or by the same power that made or enacted; as, the legislature may _repeal_ at one session, a law enacted at a preceding one.

**REPE'AL**, _noun_

**1.** Recall from exile. \[Not in use.\]

**2.** Revocation; abrogation; as the _repeal_ of a statute.
