# Rhetoric

**RHET'ORIC**, _noun_ \[Gr. from to speak, to flow. Eng. to read. The primary sense is to drive or send. See [[Read]].\]

**1.** The art of speaking with propriety, elegance and force.

**2.** The power of persuasion or attraction; that which allures or charms. We speak of the _rhetoric_ of the tongue, and the _rhetoric_ of the heart or eyes.

Sweet silent _rhetoric_ of persuading eyes.
