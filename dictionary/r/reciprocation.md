# Reciprocation

**RECIPROCA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin reciprocatio.\]

**1.** Interchange of acts; a mutual giving and returning; as the _reciprocation_ of kindnesses.

**2.** Alternation; as the _reciprocation_ of the sea in the flow and ebb of tides.

**3.** Regular return or alternation of two symptoms or diseases.
