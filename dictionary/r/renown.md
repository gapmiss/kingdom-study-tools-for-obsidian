# Renown

**RENOWN**', _noun_

Fame; celebrity; exalted reputation derived from the extensive praise of great achievements or accomplishments.

Giants of old, men of _renown_ [[Genesis 6#4|Genesis 6:4]]. [[Numbers 16#2|Numbers 16:2]].

**RENOWN'**, _verb transitive_ To make famous.

Soft elocution does thy style _renown_

A bard whom pilfer'd pastorals _renown_

\[This verb is nearly or quite obsolete.\]
