# Remonstrate

**REMON'STRATE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin remonstro; re and monstro, to show. See [[Muster]].\]

**1.** To exhibit or present strong reasons against an act, measure of any course of proceedings; to expostulate. Men _remonstrate_ by verbal argument, or by a written exposition of reasons.

**2.** To suggest urgent reasons in opposition to a measure. conscience remonstrates against a profligate life.

**REMON'STRATE**, _verb transitive_ To show by a strong representation of reasons.
