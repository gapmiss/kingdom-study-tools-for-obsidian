# Retail

**RETA'IL**,

RETAIL, _verb transitive_

**1.** To sell in small quantities or parcels, from the sense of cutting or dividing; opposed to selling by wholesale; as, to _retail_ cloth or groceries.

**2.** To sell at second hand.

**3.** To tell in broken parts; to tell to many; as, to _retail_ slander or idle reports.

**RE'TAIL**, _noun_ The sale of commodities in small quantities or parcels, or at second hand.
