# Reticular

**RETIC'ULAR**, _adjective_ \[supra.\] Having the form of a net or of net-work; formed with interstices; as a _reticular_ body or membrane.

In anatomy, the _reticular_ body, or rete mucosum, is the layer of the skin, intermediate between the cutis and the cuticle, the principal seat of color in man; the _reticular_ membrane is the same as the cellular membrane.
