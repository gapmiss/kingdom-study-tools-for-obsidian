# Rave

**RAVE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin rabio, to _rave_ to rage or be furious; rabies, rage.\]

**1.** To wander in mind or intellect; to be delirious; to talk irrationally; to be wild.

when men thus _rave_ we may conclude their brains are turned.

**2.** To utter furious exclamations; to be furious or raging; as a madman.

Have I not cause to _rave_ and beat my breast?

**3.** To dote; to be unreasonably fond; followed by upon; as, to _rave_ upon antiquity. \[Hardly proper.\]

**RAVE**, _noun_ the upper side-piece of timber of the body of a cart.
