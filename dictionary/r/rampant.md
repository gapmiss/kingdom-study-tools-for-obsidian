# Rampant

**RAMP'ANT**, _adjective_ \[See [[Ramp and Ramble]].\]

**1.** Overgrowing the usual bounds; rank in growth; exuberant; as _rampant_ weeds.

**2.** Overleaping restraint; as _rampant_ vice.

**3.** In heraldry, applied to the lion, leopard or other beast, _rampant_ denotes the animal reared and standing on his hind legs, in the posture of climbing. It differs from saliant, which indicates the posture of springing or making a sally.

The lion _rampant_ shakes his brinded mane.
