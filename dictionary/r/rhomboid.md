# Rhomboid

**RHOM'BOID**, _noun_ \[Gr. rhomb, and form.\]

**1.** In geometry, a figure having some resemblance to a rhomb; or a quadrilateral figure whose opposite sides and angles are equal, but which is neither equilateral nor equiangular.

**2.** _adjective_ In anatomy, the _rhomboid_ muscle is a thin, broad and obliquely square fleshy muscle, between the basis of the scapula and the spina dorsi.
