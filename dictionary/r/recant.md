# Recant

**RECANT**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin recanto; re and canto. See [[Cant]].\]

To retract; to recall; to contradict a former declaration.

How soon would ease _recant_ vows made in pain, as violent as void.

**RECANT'**, _verb intransitive_ To recall words; to revoke a declaration or proposition; to unsay what has been said. Convince me I am wrong, and I will _recant_
