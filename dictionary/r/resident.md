# Resident

**RES'IDENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin residens.\]

Dwelling or having an abode in a place for a continuance of time, but not definite; as a minister _resident_ at the court of St. James. A B is now _resident_ in South America.

**RES'IDENT**, _noun_

**1.** One who resides or dwells in a place for some time. A B is now a _resident_ in London.

**2.** A public minister who resides at a foreign court. It is usually applied to ministers of a rank inferior to that of embassadors.
