# Recourse

**RECOURSE**, _noun_ \[Latin recursus; re and cursus, curro, to run.\] Literally, a running back; a return.

**1.** Return; a new attack. \[Not in use.\]

**2.** A going to with a request or application, as for aid or protection. Children have _recourse_ to their parents for assistance.

**3.** Application of efforts, art or labor. The general had _recourse_ to stratagem to effect his purpose.

Our last _recourse_ is therefore to our art.

**4.** Access. \[Little used.\]

**5.** Frequent passage.

**RECOURSE**, _verb intransitive_ To return. \[Not used.\]
