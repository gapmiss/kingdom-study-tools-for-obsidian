# Rehearse

**REHEARSE**, _verb transitive_ rehers.'

**1.** To recite; to repeat the words of a passage or composition; to repeat the words of another.

When the words were heard which David spoke, they rehearsed them before Saul. [[1 Samuel 17#31|1 Samuel 17:31]].

**2.** To narrate or recount events or transactions.

There shall they _rehearse_ the righteous acts of the Lord. [[Judges 5#11|Judges 5:11]]. [[Acts 11#4|Acts 11:4]].

**3.** To recite or repeat in private for experiment and improvement, before a public representation; as, to _rehearse_ a tragedy.
