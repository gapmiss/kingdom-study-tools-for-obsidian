# Repine

**REPI'NE**, _verb intransitive_ \[re and pine.\]

**1.** To fret one's self; to be discontented; to feel inward discontent which preys on the spirits; with at or against. It is our duty never to _repine_ at the allotments of Providence.

**2.** To complain discontentedly; to murmur.

Multitudes _repine_ at the want of that which nothing but idleness hinders them from enjoying.

**3.** To envy.
