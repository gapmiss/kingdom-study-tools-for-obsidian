# Raft

**R'AFT**, _noun_ \[Gr. to sew that is, to fasten together, and allied to reeve; or Gr. whence a flooring. See [[Rafter and Roof]].\]

An assemblage of boards, planks or pieces of timber fastened together horizontally and floated down a stream; a float.

**R'AFT**, _participle passive_ \[Latin rapio; bereafian, to snatch away, to bereave.\]

Torn; rent; severed. _obsolete_
