# Requital

**REQUI'TAL**, _noun_ \[from requite.\]

**1.** Return for any office, good or bad; in a good sense, compensation; recompense; as the _requital_ of services; in a bad sense, retaliation or punishment, as the _requital_ of evil deeds.

**2.** Return; reciprocal action.

No merit their aversion can remove, nor ill _requital_ can efface their love.
