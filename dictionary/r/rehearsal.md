# Rehearsal

**REHEARSAL**, _noun_ rehers'al. \[from rehearse.\]

**1.** Recital; repetition of the words of another or of a written work; as the _rehearsal_ of the Lord's prayer.

**2.** Narration; a telling or recounting, as of particulars in detail; as the _rehearsal_ of a soldier's adventures.

**3.** The recital of a place before the public exhibition of it; as the _rehearsal_ of a comedy.
