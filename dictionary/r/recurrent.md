# Recurrent

**RECUR'RENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin recurrens.

**1.** Returning from time to time; as _recurrent_ pains of a disease.

**2.** In crystallography, a _recurrent_ crystal is one whose faces, being counted in annular ranges from one extremity to the other, furnish two different numbers which succeed each other times, as 4, 8, 4, 8, 4.

**3.** In anatomy, the _recurrent_ nerve is a branch of the par vagum, given off in the upper part of the thorax, which is reflected and runs up along the trachea to the larynx.
