# Resurrection

**RESURREC'TION**, _noun_ s as z. \[Latin resurrectus, resurgo; re and surgo, to rise.\]

A rising again; chiefly, the revival of the dead of the human race, or their return from the grave, particularly at the general judgment. By the _resurrection_ of Christ we have assurance of the future _resurrection_ of men. [[1 Peter 1#3|1 Peter 1:3]].

In the _resurrection_ they neither marry, nor are given in marriage. [[Matthew 22#23|Matthew 22:23]].
