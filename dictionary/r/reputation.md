# Reputation

**REPUTA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin reputatio.\]

**1.** Good name; the credit, honor or character which is derived from a favorable public opinion or esteem. _reputation_ is a valuable species of property or right, which should never be violated. With the loss of _reputation_ a man and especially a woman, loses most of the enjoyments of life.

The best evidence of _reputation_ is a man's whole life.

**2.** Character by report; in a good or bad sense; as, a man has the _reputation_ of being rich or poor, or of being a thief.
