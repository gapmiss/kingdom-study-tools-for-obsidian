# Replenish

**REPLEN'ISH**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin re and plenus, full.\]

**1.** To fill; to stock with numbers or abundance. The magazines are replenished with corn. The springs are replenished with water.

Multiply and _replenish_ the earth. [[Genesis 1#28|Genesis 1:28]].

**2.** To finish; to complete. \[Not in use.\]

**REPLEN'ISH**, _verb intransitive_ To recover former fullness.
