# Re-echo

**RE-ECH'O**, _verb intransitive_ \[re and echo.\] To echo back; to reverberate again; as, the hills _re-echo_ the roar of cannon.

**RE-ECH'O**, _verb intransitive_ \[supra.\] To echo back; to return back or be reverberated; as an echo.

And a loud groan re-echoes from the main.

**RE-ECH'O**, _noun_ The echo of an echo.
