# Rhubarb

**RHU'BARB**, _noun_

A plant of the genus Rheum, of several species; as the rhapontic, or common rhubarb; the palmated, or true Chinese rhubarb; the compact or Tartarian; the undulated or wave-leafed Chinese rhubarb; and the rubes, or currant _rhubarb_ of mount Libanus. The root is medicinal and much used as a moderate cathartic.
