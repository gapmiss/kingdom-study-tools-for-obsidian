# Recall

**RECALL**', _verb transitive_ \[re and call.\]

**1.** To call back; to take back; as, to _recall_ words or declarations.

**2.** To revoke; to annul by a subsequent act; as, to _recall_ a decree.

**3.** To call back; to revive in memory; as, to _recall_ to mind what has been forgotten.

**4.** To call back from a place or mission; as, to _recall_ a minister from a foreign court; to _recall_ troops from India.

**RECALL'**, _noun_

**1.** A calling back; revocation.

**2.** The power of calling back or revoking.
