# Ravishment

**RAV'ISHMENT**, _noun_

**1.** The act of forcing a woman to carnal connection; forcible violation of chastity.

**2.** Rapture; transport of delight; ecstasy; pleasing violence of the mind or senses.

All things joy with _ravishment_ attracted by thy beauty still to gaze.

**3.** The act of carrying away; abduction; as the _ravishment_ of children from their parents, of a ward from his guardian, or of a wife from her husband.
