# Rood

**ROOD**, _noun_ \[a different orthography of rod, which see.\]

**1.** The fourth part of an acre, or forty square rods. \[See [[Acre]].\]

**2.** A pole; a measure of five yards; a rod or perch. \[Not used in America, and probably local in England.\]

**ROOD**, _noun_ The cross; or an image of Christ, of the virgin Mary and a saint or St. John, on each side of it.
