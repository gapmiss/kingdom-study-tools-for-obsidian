# Recognize

**RECOGNIZE**, _verb transitive_ rec'onize. \[Latin recognosco; re and cognosco, to know. The g in these words has properly no sound in English.\]

**1.** To recollect or recover the knowledge of, either with an avowal of that knowledge or not. We _recognize_ a person at a distance, when we recollect that we have seen him before, or that we have formerly known him. We _recognize_ his features or his voice.

Speak, vassal; _recognize_ thy sovereign queen.

**2.** To review; to re-examine.

**REC'OGNIZE**, _verb intransitive_ To enter an obligation of record before a proper tribunal. A B recognized in the sum of twenty pounds.
