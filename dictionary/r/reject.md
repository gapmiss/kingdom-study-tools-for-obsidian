# Reject

**REJECT**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin rejicio, rejectus, re and jacio, to throw.\]

**1.** To throw away, as any thing useless or vile.

**2.** To cast off.

Have I rejected those that me ador'd?

**3.** To cast off; to forsake. [[Jeremiah 7#29|Jeremiah 7:29]].

**4.** To refuse to receive; to slight; to despise.

Because thou has rejected knowledge, I will _reject_ thee. Hosea 4. [[1 Samuel 15#23|1 Samuel 15:23]].

**5.** To refuse to grant; as, to _reject_ a prayer or request.

**6.** To refuse to accept; as, to _reject_ an offer.
