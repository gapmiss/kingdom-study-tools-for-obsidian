# Rejoice

**REJOICE**, _verb intransitive_ rejois'.

To experience joy and gladness in a high degree; to be exhilarated with lively and pleasurable sensations; to exult.

When the righteous are in authority, the people rejoice; but when the wicked beareth rule, the people mourn. [[Proverbs 29#2|Proverbs 29:2]].

I will _rejoice_ in thy salvation. [[Psalms 9#14|Psalms 9:14]].

**REJOICE**, _verb transitive_ rejois'. To make joyful; to gladden; to animate with lively pleasurable sensations; to exhilarate.

Whoso loveth wisdom rejoiceth his father. [[Proverbs 29#3|Proverbs 29:3]].

While she, great saint, rejoices heaven.
