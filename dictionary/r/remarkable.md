# Remarkable

**REM'ARKABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** Observable; worthy of notice.

'Tis _remarkable_ that they talk most, who have the least to say.

**2.** Extraordinary; unusual; that deserves particular notice, or that may excite admiration or wonder; as the _remarkable_ preservation of lives in shipwreck. The dark day in May, 1790, was a _remarkable_ phenomenon.
