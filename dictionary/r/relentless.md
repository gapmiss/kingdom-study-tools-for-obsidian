# Relentless

**RELENT'LESS**, _adjective_ Unmoved by pity unpitying; insensible to the distress of others; destitute of tenderness; as a pray to _relentless_ despotism.

For this th' avenging pow'r employs his darts,

Thus will persist _relentless_ in his ire.

Relentless thoughts, in Milton, may signify unremitted, intently fixed on disquieting objects.

\[This sense of the word is unusual and not to be countenanced.\]
