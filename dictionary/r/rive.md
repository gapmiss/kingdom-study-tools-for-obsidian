# Rive

**RIVE**, _verb transitive_ _preterit tense_ rived; _participle passive_ rived or riven. \[Latin rumpo, rupi. It may be allied to the family of Latin rapio, reap, rip.\]

To split; to cleave; to rend asunder by force; as, to _rive_ timber for rails or shingles with wedges; the riven oak; the riven clouds.

The scolding winds have riv'd the knotty oaks.

**RIVE**, _verb intransitive_ To be split or rent asunder.

Freestone rives, splits and breaks in any direction.
