# Riotous

**RI'OTOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Luxurious; wanton or licentious in festive indulgences; as _riotous_ eaters of flesh. [[Proverbs 23#20|Proverbs 23:20]].

**2.** Consisting of riot; tumultuous; partaking of the nature of an unlawful assembly; seditious.

**3.** Guilty of riot; applied to persons.
