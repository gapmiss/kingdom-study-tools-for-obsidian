# Reel

**REEL**, _noun_ \[See _reel_ to stagger.\]

**1.** A frame or machine turning on an axis, and on which yarn is extended for winding, either into skeins, or from skeins on to spools and quills. On a _reel_ also seamen wind their log-lines, etc.

**2.** A kind of dance.

**REEL**, _verb transitive_ To gather yarn from the spindle.

**REEL**, _verb intransitive_

To stagger; to incline or move in walking, first to one side and then to the other; to vacillate.

He with heavy fumes opprest, reel'd from the palace and retir'd to rest.

They _reel_ to and fro, and stagger like a drunken man.
