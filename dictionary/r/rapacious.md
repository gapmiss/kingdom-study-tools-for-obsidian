# Rapacious

**RAPA'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin rapax, from rapio, to seize. See [[Rap]].\]

**1.** Given to plunder; disposed or accustomed to seize by violence; seizing by force; as a _rapacious_ enemy.

Well may thy lord, appeas'd redeem thee quite from death's _rapacious_ claim.

**2.** Accustomed to seize for food; subsisting on prey or animals seized by violence; as a _rapacious_ tiger; a _rapacious_ fowl.
