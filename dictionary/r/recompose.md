# Recompose

**RECOMPO'SE**, _verb transitive_ s as z. \[re and compose.\]

**1.** To quiet anew; to compose or tranquilize that which is ruffled or disturbed; as, to _recompose_ the mind.

**2.** To compose anew; to form or adjust again.

We produced a lovely purple which we can destroy or _recompose_ at pleasure.
