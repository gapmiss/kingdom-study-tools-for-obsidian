# Raff

**R'AFF**, _verb transitive_ \[Heb.\]

To sweep; to snatch, draw or huddle together; to take by a promiscuous sweep. _obsolete_

Their causes and effects I thus _raff_ up together.

**R'AFF**, _noun_

**1.** The sweepings of society; the rabble; the mob. This is used chiefly in the compound or duplicate, riff-raff.

**2.** A promiscuous heap or collection; a jumble.
