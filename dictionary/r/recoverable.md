# Recoverable

**RECOVERABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** That may be regained or recovered. Goods lost or sunk in the ocean are not _recoverable_

**2.** That may be restored from sickness.

**3.** That may be brought back to a former condition.

A prodigal course is like the sun's, but not like his _recoverable_

**4.** That may be obtained from a debtor or possessor. The debt is _recoverable_
