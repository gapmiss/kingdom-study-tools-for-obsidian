# Rare

**RARE**, _adjective_ \[Latin rarus, thin.\]

**1.** Uncommon; not frequent; as a _rare_ event; a _rare_ phenomenon.

**2.** Unusually excellent; valuable to a degree seldom found.

**RARE** work, all fill'd with terror and delight.

Above the rest I judge one beauty _rare_

**3.** Thinly scattered.

**4.** Thin; porous; not dense; as a _rare_ and attenuate substance.

Water is nineteen times lighter and by consequence nineteen times rarer than gold.

**5.** Nearly raw; imperfectly roasted or boiled; as _rare_ beef or mutton; eggs roasted _rare_
