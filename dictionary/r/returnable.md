# Returnable

**RETURN'ABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** That may be returned or restored.

**2.** In law, that is legally to be returned, delivered, given or rendered; as a writ or precept _returnable_ at a certain day; a verdict _returnable_ to the court; an attachment _returnable_ to the king's bench.

**RETURN'-DAY**, _noun_ The day when the defendant is to appear in court and the sheriff is to return the writ and his proceedings.
