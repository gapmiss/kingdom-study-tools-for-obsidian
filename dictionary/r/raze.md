# Raze

**RAZE**, _noun_ A root. \[See [[Race]]-ginger, under Race.\]

**RAZE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin rasus, rado. See Rase and Erase.\]

**1.** To subvert from the foundation; to overthrow; to destroy; to demolish; as, to _raze_ a city to the ground.

The royal hand that raz'd unhappy Troy.

**2.** To erase; to efface; to obliterate.

Razing the characters of your renown.

\[In this sense, rase and erase are now used.\]

**3.** To extirpate.

And _raze_ their factions and their family.
