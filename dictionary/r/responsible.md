# Responsible

**RESPONS'IBLE**, _adjective_ \[from Latin responsus, respondeo.\]

**1.** Liable to account; accountable; answerable; as for a trust reposed, or for a debt. We are all _responsible_ for the talents entrusted to us by our Creator. A guardian is _responsible_ for the faithful discharge of his duty to his ward. The surety is _responsible_ for the debt of his principal.

**2.** Able to discharge an obligation; or having estate adequate to the payment of a debt. In taking bail, the officer will ascertain whether the proposed surety is a _responsible_ man.
