# Reveal

**REVE'AL**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin revelo; re and velo, to veil.\]

**1.** To disclose; to discover; to show; to make known something before unknown or concealed; as, to _reveal_ secrets.

**2.** To disclose, discover or make known from heaven. God has been pleased to _reveal_ his will to man.

The wrath of God is revealed from heaven against all ungodliness and unrighteousness of men. [[Romans 1#17|Romans 1:17]].

**REVE'AL**, _noun_ A revealing; disclosure. \[Not in use.\]
