# Research

**RESEARCH**, _noun_ reserch'. Diligent inquiry or examination in seeking facts or principles; laborious or continued search after truth; as researches of human wisdom.

**RESEARCH**, _verb transitive_ reserch'.

**1.** To search or examine with continued care; to seek diligently for the truth.

It is not easy to _research_ with due distinction, in the actions of eminent personages, both how much may have been blemished by the envy of others, and what was corrupted by their own felicity. \[Unusual.\]

**2.** To search again; to examine anew.
