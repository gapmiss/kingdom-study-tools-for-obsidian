# Readiness

**READINESS**, _noun_ red'iness. \[from ready.\]

**1.** Quickness; promptness; promptitude; facility; freedom from hinderance or obstruction; as _readiness_ of speech; _readiness_ of thought; _readiness_ of mind in suggesting an answer; _readiness_ of reply.

**2.** Promptitude; cheerfulness; willingness; alacrity; freedom from reluctance; as, to grant a request or assistance with _readiness_

They received the word with all _readiness_ of mind. [[Acts 17#11|Acts 17:11]].

**3.** A state of preparation; fitness of condition. The troops are in _readiness_
