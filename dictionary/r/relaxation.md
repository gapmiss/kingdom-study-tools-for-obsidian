# Relaxation

**RELAXA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin relaxatio.\]

**1.** The act of slackening or remitting tension; as a _relaxation_ of the muscles, fibers or nerves; a _relaxation_ of the whole system.

**2.** Cessation of restraint.

**3.** Remission or abatement of rigor; as a _relaxation_ of the law.

**4.** Remission of attention or application; as a _relaxation_ of mind, study or business.

**5.** An opening or loosening.
