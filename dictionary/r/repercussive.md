# Repercussive

**REPERCUSS'IVE**, _adjective_

**1.** Driving back; having the power of sending back; causing to reverberate; as _repercussive_ rocks.

**2.** Repellent; as a _repercussive_ medicine. \[Not in use.\]

**3.** Driven back; reverberated.

**REPERCUSS'IVE**, _noun_ A repellent. _obsolete_
