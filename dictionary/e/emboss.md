# Emboss

**EMBOSS**', _verb transitive_ \[en, in, and boss.\] In architecture and sculpture, to form bosses or protuberances; to fashion in relievo or raised work; to cut or form with prominent figures.

**1.** To form with bosses; to cover with protuberances.

**2.** To drive hard in hunting, till a deer foams, or a dog's knees swell.

**EMBOSS'**, _verb transitive_ To inclose as in a box; to include; to cover. \[Not used.\]

**EMBOSS'**, _verb transitive_ To inclose in a wood; to conceal in a thicket. \[Not used.\]
