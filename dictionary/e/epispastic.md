# Epispastic

**EPISPAS'TIC**, _adjective_ \[Gr. to draw.\] In medicine, drawing; attracting the humors to the skin; exciting action in the skin; blistering.

**EPISPAS'TIC**, _noun_ A topical remedy, applied to the external part of the body, for the purpose of drawing the humors to the part, or exciting action in the skin; a blister.
