# Encumbrance

**ENCUM'BRANCE**, _noun_ A load; any thing that impedes motion, or renders it difficult and laborious; clog; impediment.

**1.** Useless addition or load.

Strip from the branching Alps their piny load,

The huge _encumbrance_ of horrific wood.

**2.** Load or burden on an estate; a legal claim on an estate, for the discharge of which the estate is liable.
