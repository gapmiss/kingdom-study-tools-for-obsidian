# Exterior

**EXTE'RIOR**, _adjective_ \[Latin from exterus, foreign.\]

**1.** External; outward; applied to the outside or outer surface of a body, and opposed to interior. We speak of the _exterior_ and interior surfaces of a concavo-convex lens.

**2.** External; on the outside, with reference to a person; extrinsic. We speak of an object _exterior_ to a man, as opposed to that which is within or in his mind.

**EXTE'RIOR**, _noun_ The outward surface; that which is external.

**1.** Outward or visible deportment; appearance.
