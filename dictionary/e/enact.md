# Enact

**ENACT**', _verb transitive_ \[en and act.\] To make, as a law; to pass, as a bill into a law; to perform the last act of a legislature to a bill, giving it validity as a law; to give legislative sanction to a bill.

Shall this bill pass to be enacted?

**1.** To decree; to establish as the will of the supreme power.

**2.** To act; to perform; to effect. \[Not used.\]

**3.** To represent inaction. \[Not used.\]
