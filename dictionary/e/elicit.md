# Elicit

**ELIC'IT**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin elicio; e or ex and lacio, to allure.\]

**1.** To draw out; to bring to light; to deduce by reason or argument; as, to _elicit_ truth by discussion.

**2.** To strike out; as, to _elicit_ sparks of fire by collision.

**ELIC'IT**, _adjective_ Brought into act; brought from possibility into real existence. \[Little used.\]
