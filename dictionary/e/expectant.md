# Expectant

**EXPECT'ANT**, _adjective_ Waiting; looking for.

**1.** An _expectant_ estate, is one which is suspended till the determination of a particular estate.

**EXPECT'ANT**, _noun_ One who expects; one who waits in expectation; one held in dependence by his belief or hope of receiving some good. Those who have the gift of offices are usually surrounded by expectants.
