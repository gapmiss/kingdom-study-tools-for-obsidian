# Espalier

**ESPAL'IER**, _noun_ \[Latin palus, a stake or pole.\] A row of trees planted about a garden or in hedges, so as to inclose quarters or separate parts, and trained up to a lattice of wood-work, or fastened to stakes, forming a close hedge or shelter to protect plants against injuries from wind or weather.

**ESPAL'IER**, _verb transitive_ To form an _espalier_ or to protect by an _espalier_
