# Emulous

**EM'ULOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin oemulus.\] Desirous or eager to imitate, equal or excel another; desirous of like excellence with another; with of; as _emulous_ of another's example or virtues.

**1.** Rivaling; engaged in competition; as _emulous_ Carthage.

**2.** Factious; contentious.
