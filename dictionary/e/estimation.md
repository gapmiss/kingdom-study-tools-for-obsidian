# Estimation

**ESTIMA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin oestimatio.\] The act of estimating.

**1.** Calculation; computation; an opinion or judgment of the worth, extent or quantity of any thing, formed without using precise data. We may differ in our estimations of distance, magnitude or amount, and no less in our _estimation_ of moral qualities.

**1.** Esteem; regard; favorable opinion; honor.

I shall have _estimation_ among the multitude, and honor with the elders.
