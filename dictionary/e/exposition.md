# Exposition

**EXPOSI'TION**, _noun_ A laying open; a setting to public view.

**1.** A situation in which a thing is exposed or laid open, or in which it has an unobstructed view, or in which a free passage to it is open; as, a house has an easterly _exposition_ an _exposition_ to the south or to a southern prospect. The _exposition_ gives a free access to the air or to the sun's rays.

**2.** Explanation; interpretation; a laying open the sense or meaning of an author or of any passage in a writing.
