# Exhilarate

**EXHIL'ARATE**, _verb transitive_ egzhil'arate. \[Latin exhilaro; ex and hilaro, to make merry, hilaris, merry, jovial.\]

To make cheerful or merry; to enliven; to make glad or joyous; to gladden; to cheer. Good news exhilarates the mind, as good wine exhilarates the animal spirits.

**EXHIL'ARATE**, _verb intransitive_ To become cheerful or joyous.
