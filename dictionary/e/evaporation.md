# Evaporation

**EVAPORA'TION**, _noun_ The conversion of a fluid into vapor specifically lighter than the atmospheric air. _evaporation_ is increased by heat and is followed by cold. It is now generally considered as a solution in the atmosphere.

**1.** The act of flying off in fumes; vent; discharge.

**2.** In pharmacy, the operation of drawing off a portion of a fluid in steam, that the remainder may be of a greater consistence, or more concentrated.
