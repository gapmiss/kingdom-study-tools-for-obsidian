# Errand

**ER'RAND**, _noun_

**1.** A verbal message; a mandate or order; something to be told or done; a communication to be made to some person at a distance. The servant was sent on an errand; he told his errand; he has done the _errand_ These are the most common modes of using this word.

I have a secret _errand_ to thee, O King. [[Judges 3#19|Judges 3:19]].

**2.** Any special business to be transacted by a messenger.
