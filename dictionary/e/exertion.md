# Exertion

**EXER'TION**, _noun_ The act of exerting or straining; the act of putting into motion or action; effort; a striving or struggling; as an _exertion_ of strength or power; an _exertion_ of the limbs, of the mind or faculties. The ship was saved by great exertions of the crew. No exertions will suppress a vice which great men countenance.
