# Elegantly

**EL'EGANTLY**, _adverb_ In a manner to please; with elegance; with beauty; with pleasing propriety; as a composition _elegantly_ written.

**1.** With due symmetry; with well formed and duly proportioned parts; as a house _elegantly_ built.

**2.** Richly; with rich or handsome materials well disposed; as a room _elegantly_ furnished; a woman _elegantly_ dressed.
