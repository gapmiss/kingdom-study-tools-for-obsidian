# Exuberance

**EXU'BERANCE**

EXU'BERANCY, _noun_ \[Latin exuberans, exubero; ex and ubero, to fatten; uber, a pap or breast, that is, a swelling or mass.\]

**1.** An abundance; an overflowing quantity; richness; as an _exuberance_ of fertility or fancy.

**2.** Superfluous abundance; luxuriance.

**3.** Overgrowth; superfluous shoots, as of trees.
