# Exactor

**EXACT'OR**, _noun_ One who exacts; an officer who collects tribute, taxes or customs.

I will make thine officers peace, and thine exactors righteousness. [[Isaiah 60#17|Isaiah 60:17]].

**1.** An extortioner; one who compels another to pay more than is legal or reasonable; one who demands something without pity or regard to justice.

**2.** He that demands by authority; as an _exactor_ of oaths.

**3.** One who is unreasonably severe in his injunctions or demands.
