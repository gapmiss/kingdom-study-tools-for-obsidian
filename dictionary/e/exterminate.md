# Exterminate

**EXTERM'INATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin extermino; ex and terminus, limit.\]

Literally, to drive from within the limits or borders. Hence,

**1.** To destroy utterly; to drive away; to extirpate; as, to _exterminate_ a colony, a tribe or a nation; to _exterminate_ inhabitants or a race of men.

**2.** To eradicate; to root out; to extirpate; as, to _exterminate_ error, heresy, infidelity or atheism; to _exterminate_ vice.

**3.** To root out, as plants; to extirpate; as, to _exterminate_ weeds.

**4.** In algebra, to take away; as, to _exterminate_ surds or unknown quantities.
