# Eligible

**EL'IGIBLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin eligo, to choose or select; e and lego.\]

**1.** Fit to be chosen; worthy of choice, preferable.

In deep distress, certainty is more _eligible_ than suspense.

**2.** Suitable; proper; desirable; as, the house stands in an _eligible_ situation.

**3.** Legally qualified to be chosen; as, a man is or is not _eligible_ to an office.
