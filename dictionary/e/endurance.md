# Endurance

**ENDU'RANCE**, _noun_ \[See [[Endure]].\] Continuance; a state of lasting or duration; lastingness.

**1.** A bearing or suffering; a continuing under pain or distress without resistance, or without sinking or yielding to the pressure; sufferance; patience.

Their fortitude was most admirable in their presence and _endurance_ of all evils, of pain, and of death.

**2.** Delay; a waiting for. \[Not used.\]
