# Evasion

**EVA'SION**, _noun_ s as z. \[Latin evasio, from evado, evasi. See [[Evade]].\]

The act of eluding or avoiding, or of escaping, particularly from the pressure of an argument, from an accusation or charge, from an interrogatory and the like; excuse; subterfuge; equivocation; artifice to elude; shift. _evasion_ of a direct answer weakens the testimony of a witness.

Thou by evasions thy crime uncover'st more.
