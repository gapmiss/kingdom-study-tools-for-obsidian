# Elongate

**ELON'GATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Low Latin elongo, from longus. See [[Long]].\]

**1.** To lengthen; to extend.

**2.** To remove farther off.

**ELON'GATE**, _verb intransitive_ To depart from; to recede; to move to a greater distance; particularly, to recede apparently from the sun, as a planet in its orbit.
