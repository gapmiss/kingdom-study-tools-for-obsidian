# Eloquent

**EL'OQUENT**, _adjective_ Having the power of oratory; speaking with fluency, propriety, elegance and animation; as an _eloquent_ orator; an _eloquent_ preacher.

**1.** Composed with elegance and spirit; elegant and animated; adapted to please, affect and persuade; as an _eloquent_ address; an _eloquent_ petition or remonstrance; an _eloquent_ history.
