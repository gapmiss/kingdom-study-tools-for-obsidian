# Enclitic

**ENCLIT'IC**, _adjective_ \[Gr. inclined; to incline.\]

**1.** Leaning; inclining, or inclined. In grammar, an _enclitic_ particle or word, is one which is so closely united to another as to seem to be a part of it; as que, ne, and ve, in virumque, nonne, aliusve.

**2.** Throwing back the accent upon the foregoing syllable.

**ENCLIT'IC**, _noun_ A word which is joined to the end of another, as que, in virumque, which may vary the accent.

**1.** A particle or word that throws the accent or emphasis back upon the former syllable.
