# Emulate

**EM'ULATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin oemulor; Gr. strife, contest.\]

**1.** To strive to equal or excel, in qualities or actions; to imitate, with a view to equal or excel; to vie with; to rival. Learn early to _emulate_ the good and the great. _emulate_ the virtues and shun the vices of distinguished men.

**2.** To be equal to.

Thy eye would _emulate_ the diamond.

**3.** To imitate; to resemble. \[Unusual.\]

Convulsion emulating the motion of laughter.

**EM'ULATE**, _adjective_ Ambitious. \[Little used.\]
