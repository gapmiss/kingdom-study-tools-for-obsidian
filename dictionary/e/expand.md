# Expand

**EXPAND**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin expando; es and pando, to open or spread; Eng. span. The primary sense is to strain or stretch, and this seems to be the sense of bend, Latin pandus.\]

**1.** To open; to spread; as, a flower expands its leaves.

**2.** To spread; to enlarge a surface; to diffuse; as, a stream expands its waters over a plain.

**3.** To dilate; to enlarge in bulk; to distend; as, to _expand_ the chest by inspiration; heat expands all bodies; air is expanded by rarefaction.

**4.** To enlarge; to extend; as, to _expand_ the sphere of benevolence; to _expand_ the heart or affections.
