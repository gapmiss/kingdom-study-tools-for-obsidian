# Encouragement

**ENCOUR'AGEMENT**, _noun_ The act of giving courage, or confidence of success; incitement to action or to practice; incentive. We ought never to neglect the _encouragement_ of youth in generous deeds. The praise of good men serves as an _encouragement_ of virtue and heroism.

**1.** That which serves to incite, support, promote or advance, as favor, countenance, rewards, profit. A young man attempted the practice of law, but found little _encouragement_ The fine arts find little _encouragement_ among a rude people.
