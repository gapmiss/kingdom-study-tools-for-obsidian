# Ethereal

**ETHE'REAL**, _adjective_ Formed of ether; containing or filled with ether; as _ethereal_ space; _ethereal_ regions.

**1.** Heavenly; celestial; as _ethereal_ messenger.

**2.** Consisting of ether or spirit.

Vast chain of being, which from God began,

Natures _ethereal_ human, angel, man.
