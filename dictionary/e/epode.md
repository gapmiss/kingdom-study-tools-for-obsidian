# Epode

**EP'ODE**, _noun_ \[Gr. ode.\] In lyric poetry, the third or last part of the ode; that which follows the strophe and antistrophe; the ancient ode being divided into strophe, antistrophe and _epode_ The word is now used as the name of any little verse or verses, that follow one or more great ones. Thus a pentameter after a hexameter is an _epode_
