# Enliven

**ENLI'VEN**, _verb transitive_ enli'vn. \[from life, live.\] Literally, to give life. Hence,

**1.** To give action or motion to; to make vigorous or active; to excite; as, fresh fuel enlivens a fire.

**2.** To give spirit or vivacity to; to animate; to make sprightly. social mirth and good humor _enliven_ company; they _enliven_ the dull and gloomy.

**3.** To make cheerful, gay or joyous.
