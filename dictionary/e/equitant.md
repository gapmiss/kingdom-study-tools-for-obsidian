# Equitant

**EQ'UITANT**, _adjective_ \[Latin equitans, equito, to ride, from eques, a horseman, or equus, a horse.\]

In botany, riding, as _equitant_ leaves: a term of leafing or foliation, when two opposite leaves converge so with their edges, that one incloses the other; or when the inner leaves are inclosed by the outer ones.
