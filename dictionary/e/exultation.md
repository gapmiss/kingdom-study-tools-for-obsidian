# Exultation

**EXULTA'TION**, _noun_ The act of exulting; lively joy at success or victory, or at any advantage gained; great gladness; rapturous delight; triumph. _exultation_ usually springs from the gratification of our desire of some good; particularly of distinction or superiority, or of that which confers distinction. It often springs from the gratification of pride or ambition. But _exultation_ may be a lively joy springing from laudable causes.
