# Escalop

**ESCAL'OP**, _noun_ skal'lup. A family of bivalvular shell-fish, whose shell is regularly indented. In the center of the top of the shell is a trigonal sinus with an elastic cartilage for its hinge.

**1.** A regular curving indenture in the margin of any thing. \[See [[Scallop and Scollop]].\]
