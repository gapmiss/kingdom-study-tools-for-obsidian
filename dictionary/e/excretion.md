# Excretion

**EXCRE'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin excretio, from excerno, to separate.\]

**1.** A separation of some fluid from the blood, by means of the glands; a throwing off or discharge of animal fluids from the body.

**2.** That which is excreted; fluids separated from the body by the glands and called excrement.

The term _excretion_ is more usually applied to those secretions which are directly discharged from the body. It is also applied to the discharges from the bowels, which are called alvine excretions.
