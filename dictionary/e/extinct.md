# Extinct

**EXTINCT**', _adjective_ \[Latin extinctus. See [[Extinguish]].

**1.** Extinguished; put out; quenched; as, fire, light or a lamp is _extinct_

**2.** Being at an end; having no survivor; as, a family or race is _extinct_

**3.** Being at an end; having ceased. The enmity between the families is _extinct_

My days are _extinct_ [[Job 17#1|Job 17:1]].

**4.** Being at an end, by abolition or disuse; having no force; as, the law is _extinct_
