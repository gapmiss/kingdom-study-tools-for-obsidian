# Explain

**EXPLA'IN**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin explano; ex and planus, plain, open, smooth.\]

To make plain, manifest or intelligible; to clear of obscurity; to expound; to illustrate by discourse, or by notes. The first business of a preacher is to _explain_ his text. Notes and comments are intended to _explain_ the scriptures.

**EXPLA'IN**, _verb intransitive_ To give explanations.
