# Enervate

**ENERV'ATE**, _adjective_ \[infra.\] Weakened; weak; without strength or force.

**1.** To deprive of nerve, force or strength; to weaken; to render feeble. Idleness and voluptuous indulgences _enervate_ the body. Vices and luxury _enervate_ the strength of state.

**2.** To cut the nerves; as, to _enervate_ a horse.
