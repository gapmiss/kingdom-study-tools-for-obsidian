# Emersion

**EMER'SION**, _noun_ \[from Latin emergo. See [[Emerge]].\]

**1.** The act of rising out of a fluid or other covering or surrounding substance; opposed to immersion.

**2.** In astronomy, the reappearance of a heavenly body after an eclipse, as the _emersion_ of the moon from the shadow of the earth; also, the time of reappearance.

**3.**. The reappearance of a star, which has been hid by the effulgence of the sun's light.

**4.** Extrication.
