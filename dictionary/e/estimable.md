# Estimable

**ES'TIMABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** That is capable of being estimated or valued; as _estimable_ damage.

**2.** Valuable; worth a great price.

A pound of man's flesh, taken from a man,

Is not so _estimable_ or profitable.

**3.** Worthy of esteem or respect; deserving our good opinion or regard.

A lady said of her two companions, that one was more amiable, the other more _estimable_

**ES'TIMABLE**, _noun_ That which is worthy of regard.
