# Elementarity

**ELEMENTAR'ITY**

ELEMENT'ARY, _adjective_ Primary; simple; uncompounded; uncombined; having only one principle or constituent part; as an elementary substance. Elementary particles are those into which a body is resolved by decomposition.

**1.** Initial; rudimental; containing, teaching or discussing first principles, rules or rudiments; as an elementary treatise or disquisition.

**2.** Treating of elements; collecting, digesting or explaining principles; as an elementary writer.
