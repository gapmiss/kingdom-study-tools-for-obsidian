# Emptiness

**EMP'TINESS**, _noun_ \[from empty.\] A state of being empty; a state of containing nothing except air; destitution; absence of matter; as the _emptiness_ of a vessel.

**1.** Void space; vacuity; vacuum.

**2.** Want of solidity or substance; as the _emptiness_ of light and shade.

**3.** Unsatisfactoriness; inability to satisfy desire; as the _emptiness_ of earthly things.

**4.** Vacuity of head; want of intellect or knowledge.
