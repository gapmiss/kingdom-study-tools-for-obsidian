# Exculpate

**EXCUL'PATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin ex and culpo, to blame, culpa, fault.\]

To clear by words from a charge or imputation of fault or guilt; to excuse. How naturally are we inclined to _exculpate_ ourselves and throw the blame on others. Eve endeavored to _exculpate_ herself for eating the forbidden fruit, and throw the blame on the serpent; Adam attempted to _exculpate_ himself and throw the blame on Eve.
