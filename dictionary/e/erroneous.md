# Erroneous

**ERRO'NEOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin erroneus, from erro, to err.\]

**1.** Wandering; roving; unsettled.

They roam

Erroneous and disconsolate.

**2.** Deviating; devious; irregular; wandering from the right course.

Erroneous circulation of blood.

\[The foregoing applications of the word are less common.\]

**3.** Mistaking; misled; deviating, by mistake, from the truth. Destroy not the _erroneous_ with the malicious.

**4.** Wrong; false; mistaken; not conformable to truth; erring from truth or justice; as an _erroneous_ opinion or judgment.
