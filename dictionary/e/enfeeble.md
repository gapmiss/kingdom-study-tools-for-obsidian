# Enfeeble

**ENFEE'BLE**, _verb transitive_ \[from feeble.\] To deprive of strength; to reduce the strength or force of; to weaken; to debilitate; to enervate. Intemperance enfeebles the body, and induces premature infirmity. Excessive grief and melancholy _enfeeble_ the mind. Long wars _enfeeble_ a state.
