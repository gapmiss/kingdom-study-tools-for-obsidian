# Enormous

**ENOR'MOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin enormis; e and norma, a rule.\]

**1.** Going beyond the usual measure or rule.

Enormous in their gait.

**2.** Excursive; beyond the limits of a regular figure.

The _enormous_ part of the light in the circumference of every lucid point.

**3.** Great beyond the common measure; excessive; as _enormous_ crime or guilt.

**4.** Exceeding, in bulk or highth, the common measure; as an _enormous_ form; a man of _enormous_ size.

**5.** Irregular; confused; disordered; unusual.
