# Enterprise

**EN'TERPRISE**, _noun_ s as z. That which is undertaken, or attempted to be performed; an attempt; a project attempted; particularly, a bold, arduous or hazardous undertaking, either physical or moral. The attack on Stoney-Point was a bold, but successful _enterprise_ The attempts to evangelize the heathen are noble enterprises.

Their hands cannot perform their _enterprise_ [[Job 5#12|Job 5:12]].

**EN'TERPRISE**, _verb transitive_ To undertake; to begin and attempt to perform.

The business must be enterprised this night.
