# Ere

**ERE**, _adverb_ Before; sooner than.

**ERE** sails were spread new oceans to explore.

The nobleman saith to him, Sir, come down _ere_ my child die. [[John 4#49|John 4:49]].

In these passages, _ere_ is really a preposition, followed by a sentence, instead of a single word, as below.

**ERE**, _preposition_ Before.

Our fruitful Nile

Flow'd _ere_ the wonted season.
