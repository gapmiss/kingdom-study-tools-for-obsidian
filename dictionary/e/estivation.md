# Estivation

**ESTIVA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin oestivatio, from oestas, summer, oestivo, to pass the summer.\]

**1.** The act of passing the summer.

**2.** In botany, the disposition of the petals within the floral gem or bud; Latin convolute, when the petals are rolled together like a scroll; 2. imbricate, when they lie over each other like tiles on a roof; 3. conduplicate, when they are doubled together at the midrib; 4. valvate, when as they are about to expand they are placed like the glumes in grasses.
