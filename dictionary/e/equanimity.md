# Equanimity

**EQUANIM'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin oequanimitas; oequus and animus, an equal mind.\] Evenness of mind; that calm temper or firmness of mind which is not easily elated or depressed, which sustains prosperity without excessive joy, and adversity without violent agitation of the passions or depression of spirits. The great man bears misfortunes with _equanimity_
