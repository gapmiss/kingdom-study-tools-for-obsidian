# Extraction

**EXTRAC'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin extractio.\] The act of drawing out; as the _extraction_ of a tooth; the _extraction_ of a bone or an arrow from the body; the _extraction_ of a fetus or child in midwifery.

**1.** Descent; lineage; birth; derivation of persons from a stock or family. Hence, the stock or family from which one has descended. We say, a man is of a noble _extraction_

**2.** In pharmacy, the operation of drawing essences, tinctures, etc. from a substance.

**3.** In arithmetic and algebra, the _extraction_ of roots is the operation of finding the root of a given number of quantity; also, the method or rule by which the operation is performed.
