# Egregious

**EGRE'GIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin egregius, supposed to be from e or ex grege, from or out of or beyond the herd, select, choice.\]

**1.** Eminent; remarkable; extraordinary; distinguished; as _egregious_ exploits; an _egregious_ prince. But in this sense it is seldom applied to persons.

**2.** In a bad sense, great; extraordinary; remarkable; enormous; as an _egregious_ mistake; _egregious_ contempt. In this sense it is often applied to persons; as an _egregious_ rascal; an _egregious_ murderer.
