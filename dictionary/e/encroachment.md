# Encroachment

**ENCROACHMENT**, _noun_ The entering gradually on the rights or possessions of another, and taking possession; unlawful intrusion; advance into the territories or jurisdiction of another, by silent means, or without right.

**1.** That which is taken by encroaching on another.

**2.** In law, if a tenant owes two shillings rent service to the lord, and the lord takes three, it is an _encroachment_
