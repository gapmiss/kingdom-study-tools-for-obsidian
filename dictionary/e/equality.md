# Equality

**EQUAL'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin oequalitas.\] An agreement of things in dimensions, quantity or quality; likeness; similarity in regard to two things compared. We speak of the _equality_ of two or more tracts of land, of two bodies in length, breadth or thickness, of virtues or vices.

**1.** The same degree of dignity or claims; as the _equality_ of men in the scale of being; the _equality_ of nobles of the same rank; an _equality_ of rights.

**2.** Evenness; uniformity; sameness in state or continued course; as an _equality_ of temper or constitution.

**3.** Evenness; plainness; uniformity; as an _equality_ of surface.
