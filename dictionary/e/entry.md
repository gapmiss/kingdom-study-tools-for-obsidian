# Entry

**EN'TRY**, _noun_ The passage by which persons enter a house or other building.

**1.** The act of entering; entrance; ingress; as the _entry_ of a person into a house or city; the _entry_ of a river into the sea or a lake; the _entry_ of air into the blood; the _entry_ of a spear into the flesh.

**2.** The act of entering and taking possession of lands or other estate.

**3.** The act of committing to writing, or of recording in a book. Make an _entry_ of every sale, of every debt and credit.

**4.** The exhibition or depositing of a ship's papers at the custom house, to procure license to land goods; or the giving an account of a ship's cargo to the officer of the customs, and obtaining his permission to land the goods.
