# Extravagantly

**EXTRAV'AGANTLY**, _adverb_ In an extravagant manner; wildly; not within the limits of truth or probability. Men often write and talk _extravagantly_

**1.** Unreasonably; excessively. It is prudent not to praise or censure _extravagantly_

**2.** In a manner to use property without necessity or propriety, or to no good purpose; expensively, or profusely to an unjustifiable degree; as, to live, eat, drink, or dress _extravagantly_
