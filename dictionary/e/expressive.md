# Expressive

**EXPRESS'IVE**, _adjective_ Serving to express; serving to utter or represent; followed by of. He sent a letter couched in terms _expressive_ of his gratitude.

Each verse so swells _expressive_ of her woes.

**1.** Representing with force; emphatical.

These words are very _expressive_

**2.** Showing; representing; as an _expressive_ sign.
