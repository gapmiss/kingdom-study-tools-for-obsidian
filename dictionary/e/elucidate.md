# Elucidate

**ELU'CIDATE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Low Latin elucido, from eluceo, luceo, to shine, or from lucidus, clear, bright. See [[Light]].\]

To make clear or manifest; to explain; to remove obscurity from, and render intelligible; to illustrate. An example will _elucidate_ the subject. An argument may _elucidate_ an obscure question. A fact related by one historian may _elucidate_ an obscure passage in another's writings.
