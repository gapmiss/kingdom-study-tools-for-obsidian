# Exorbitant

**EXORB'ITANT**, _adjective_ \[Latin exorbitans.\] Literally, departing from an orbit or usual track. Hence, deviating from the usual course; going beyond the appointed rules or established limits of right or propriety; hence, excessive; extravagant; enormous. We speak of _exorbitant_ appetites and passions; _exorbitant_ demands or claims; _exorbitant_ taxes.

**1.** Anomalous; not comprehended in a settled rule or method.

The Jews were inured with causes _exorbitant_
