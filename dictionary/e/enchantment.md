# Enchantment

**ENCH'ANTMENT**, _noun_ The act of producing certain wonderful effects by the invocation or aid of demons, or the agency of certain supposed spirits; the use of magic arts, spells or charms; incantation.

The magicians of Egypt did so with their enchantments. [[Exodus 7#11|Exodus 7:11]].

**1.** Irresistible influence; overpowering influence of delight.

The warmth of fancy--which holds the heart of a reader under the strongest _enchantment_
