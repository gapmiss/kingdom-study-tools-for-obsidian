# Extraneous

**EXTRA'NEOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin extraneus.\] Foreign; not belonging to a thing; existing without; not intrinsic; as, to separate gold from _extraneous_ matter.

Relation is not contained in the real existence of things, but is _extraneous_ and superinduced.

Extraneous fossils, organic remains; exuviae of organized beings, imbedded in the strata of the earth.
