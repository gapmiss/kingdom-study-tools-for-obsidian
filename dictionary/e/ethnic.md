# Ethnic

**ETH'NIC**

**ETH'NIC**AL, _adjective_ \[Latin ethnicus; Gr. from nation from the root of G. heide, heath, woods, whence heathen. See [[Heathen]].\]

Heathen; pagan; pertaining to the gentiles or nations not converted to christianity; opposed to Jewish and Christian.

**ETH'NIC**, _noun_ A heathen; a pagan.
