# English

**ENGLISH**, _adjective_ ing'glish. \[Latin ango, from the sense of pressing, depression, laying, which gives the sense of level.\]

Belonging to England, or to its inhabitants.

**ENGLISH**, _noun_ The people of England.

**1.** The language of England or of the _english_ nation, and of their descendants in India, America and other countries.

**ENGLISH**, _verb transitive_ To translate into the _english_ language.
