# External

**EXTERN'AL**, _adjective_ \[Latin externus.\]

**1.** Outward; exterior; as the _external_ surface of a body; opposed to internal.

**2.** Outward; not intrinsic; not being within; as _external_ objects; _external_ causes or effects.

**3.** Exterior; visible; apparent; as _external_ deportment.

**4.** Foreign; relating to or connected with foreign nations; as _external_ trade or commerce; the _external_ relations of a state or kingdom.

External taxes, are duties or imposts laid on goods imported into a country.
