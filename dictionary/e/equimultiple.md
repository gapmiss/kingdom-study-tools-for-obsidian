# Equimultiple

**EQUIMUL'TIPLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin oequus and multiplico or multiplex.\]

Multiplied by the same number or quantity.

**EQUIMUL'TIPLE**, _noun_ In arithmetic and geometry, a number multiplied by the same number or quantity. Hence equimultiples are always in the same ratio to each other, as the simple numbers or quantities before multiplication. If 6 and 9 are multiplied by 4, the multiples, 24 and 36, will be to each other as 6 to 9.
