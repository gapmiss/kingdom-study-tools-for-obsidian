# Extenuation

**EXTENUA'TION**, _noun_ The act of making thin; the process of growing thin or lean; the losing of flesh.

**1.** The act representing any thing less wrong, faulty or criminal than it is in fact; palliation; opposed to aggravation; as the _extenuation_ of faults, injuries or crimes.

**2.** Mitigation; alleviation; as the _extenuation_ of punishment.

\[Not common.\]
