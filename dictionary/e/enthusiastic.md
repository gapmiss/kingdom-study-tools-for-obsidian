# Enthusiastic

**ENTHUSIAS'TIC**

**ENTHUSIAS'TIC**AL, _adjective_ Filled with enthusiasm, or the conceit of special intercourse with God or revelations from him.

**1.** Highly excited; warm and ardent; zealous in pursuit of an object; heated to animation. Our author was an _enthusiastic_ lover of poetry and admirer of Homer.

**2.** Elevated; warm; tinctured with enthusiasm. The speaker addressed the audience in _enthusiastic_ strains.
