# Extirpate

**EX'TIRPATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin extirpo; ex and stirps, root.\]

**1.** To pull or pluck up by the roots; to root out; to eradicate; to destroy totally; as, to _extirpate_ weeds or noxious plants from a field.

**2.** To eradicate; to root out; to destroy wholly; as, to _extirpate_ error or heresy; to _extirpate_ a sect.

**3.** In surgery, to cut out; to cut off; to eat out; to remove; as, to _extirpate_ a wen.
