# Expunge

**EXPUNGE**, _verb transitive_ expunj'. \[Latin expungo; ex and pungo, to thrust, to prick.\]

**1.** To blot out, as with a pen; to rub out; to efface, as words; to obliterate. We _expunge_ single words or whole lines or sentences.

**2.** To efface; to strike out; to wipe out or destroy; to annihilate; as, to _expunge_ an offense.

**EXPUNGE** the whole, or lop the excrescent parts.
