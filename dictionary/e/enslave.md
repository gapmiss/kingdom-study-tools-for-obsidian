# Enslave

**ENSLA'VE**, _verb transitive_ \[from slave.\] To reduce to slavery or bondage; to deprive of liberty and subject to the will of a master. Barbarous nations _enslave_ their prisoners of war, but civilized men barbarously and wickedly purchase men to _enslave_ them.

**1.** To reduce to servitude or subjection. Men often suffer their passions and appetites to _enslave_ them. They are enslaved to lust, to anger, to intemperance, to avarice.
