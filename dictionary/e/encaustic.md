# Encaustic

**ENCAUS'TIC**, _adjective_ \[Gr. caustic, to burn.\] Pertaining to the art of enameling, and to painting in burnt wax. _encaustic_ painting, is a method in which wax is employed to give a gloss to colors.

**ENCAUS'TIC**, _noun_ Enamel or enameling.

**1.** The method of painting in burnt wax.
