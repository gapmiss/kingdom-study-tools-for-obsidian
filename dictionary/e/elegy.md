# Elegy

**EL'EGY**, _noun_ \[Latin elegia; Gr. to speak or utter.; Latin lugeo. The verbs may have a common origin, for to speak and to cry out in wailing are only modifications of the same act, to throw out the voice with more or less vehemence.\]

**1.** A mournful or plaintive poem, or a funeral song; a poem or a song expressive of sorrow and lamentation.

**2.** A short poem without points or affected elegancies.
