# Emphatic

**EMPHAT'IC**

**EMPHAT'IC**AL, _adjective_ Forcible; strong; impressive; as an _emphatic_ voice, tone or pronunciation; emphatical reasoning.

**1.** Requiring emphasis; as an emphatical word.

**2.** Uttered with emphasis. We remonstrated in emphatical terms.

**3.** Striking to the eye; as _emphatic_ colors.
