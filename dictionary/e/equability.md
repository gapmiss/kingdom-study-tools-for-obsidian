# Equability

**EQUABIL'ITY**, _noun_ \[See [[Equable]].\] Equality in motion; continued equality, at all times, in velocity or movement; uniformity; as the _equability_ of the motion of a heavenly body, or of the blood in the arteries and veins.

**1.** Continued equality; evenness or uniformity; as the _equability_ of the temperature of the air; the _equability_ of the mind.
