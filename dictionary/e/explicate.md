# Explicate

**EX'PLICATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin explico, to unfold; ex and plico, to fold.

**1.** To unfold; to expand; to open. 'They _explicate_ the leaves.' \[In this sense, the word is not common, and hardly admissible.\]

**2.** To unfold the meaning or sense; to explain; to clear of difficulties or obscurity; to interpret.

The last verse of his last satyr is not yet sufficiently explicated.
