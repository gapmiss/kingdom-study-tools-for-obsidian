# Exorbitance

**EXORB'ITANCE**

EXORB'ITANCY, _noun_ egzorb'itance. \[Latin exorbitans, from ex and orbita, the track of a wheel, orbis, and orb.\]

Literally, a going beyond or without the tract or usual limit. Hence, enormity; extravagance; a deviation from rule or the ordinary limits or right or propriety; as the exorbitances of the tongue, or of deportment.

The reverence of my presence may be a curb to your exorbitancies.
