# Equable

**E'QUABLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin oequabilis, from oequus, equal, even, oeguo, to equal, to level.\]

**1.** Equal and uniform at all times, as motion. An _equable_ motion continues the same in degree of velocity, neither accelerated nor retarded.

**2.** Even; smooth; having a uniform surface or form; as an _equable_ glove or plain.
