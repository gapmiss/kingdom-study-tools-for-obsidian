# Excitement

**EXCI'TEMENT**, _noun_ The act of exciting; stimulation.

**1.** The state of being roused into action, or of having increased action. Stimulants are intended to produce _excitement_ in the animal system.

**2.** Agitation; a state of being roused into action; as an _excitement_ of the people.

**3.** That which excites or rouses; that which moves, stirs, or induces action; a motive.
