# Employment

**EMPLOY'MENT**, _noun_ The act of employing or using.

**1.** Occupation; business; that which engages the head or hands; as agricultural employments; mechanical employments. Men, whose _employment_ is to make sport and amusement for others, are always despised.

**2.** Office; public business or trust; agency or service for another or for the public. The secretary of the treasury has a laborious and responsible _employment_ He is in the _employment_ of government.
