# Emanate

**EM'ANATE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin emanano; e and mano, to flow.

**1.** To issue from a source; to flow from; applied to fluids; as, light emanates from the sun; perspirable matter, from animal bodies.

**2.** To proceed from a source of fountain; as, the powers of government in republics _emanate_ from the people.
