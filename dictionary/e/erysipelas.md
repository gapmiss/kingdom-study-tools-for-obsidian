# Erysipelas

**ERYSIP'ELAS**, _noun_ \[Gr.\] A disease called St.Anthony's fire; a diffused inflammation with fever of two or three days, generally with coma or delirium; an eruption of a fiery acrid humor, on some part of the body, but chiefly on the face. One species of _erysipelas_ is called shingles, or eruption with small vesicles.
