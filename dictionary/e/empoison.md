# Empoison

**EMPOIS'ON**, _verb transitive_ s as z.

**1.** To poison; to administer poison to; to destroy or endanger life by giving or causing to be taken into the stomach any noxious drug or preparation. \[In this sense, poison is generally used; but _empoison_ may be used, especially in poetry.\]

**2.** To taint with poison or venom; to render noxious or deleterious by an admixture of poisonous substance. \[This may be used, especially in poetry.\]

**3.** To embitter; to deprive of sweetness; as, to _empoison_ the joys and pleasure of life.
