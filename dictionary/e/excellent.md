# Excellent

**EX'CELLENT**, _adjective_ Being of great virtue or worth; eminent or distinguished for what is amiable, valuable or laudable; as an _excellent_ man or citizen; an _excellent_ judge or magistrate.

**1.** Being of great value or use, applied to things; remarkable for good properties; as _excellent_ timber; an _excellent_ farm; an _excellent_ horse; _excellent_ fruit.

**2.** Distinguished for superior attainments; as an _excellent_ artist.

**3.** Consummate; complete; in an ill sense.

Elizabeth was an _excellent_ hypocrite.
