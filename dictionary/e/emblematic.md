# Emblematic

**EMBLEMAT'IC**

**EMBLEMAT'IC**AL, _adjective_ Pertaining to or comprising an emblem.

**1.** Representing by some allusion or customary connection; as, a crown is _emblematic_ of royalty, a crown being worn by kings.

**2.** Representing by similar qualities; as, whiteness is _emblematic_ of purity.

**3.** Using emblems; as _emblematic_ worship.
