# Extol

**EXTOL**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin extollo; ex and tollo, to raise.\]

To raise in words or eulogy; to praise; to exalt in commendation; to magnify. We _extol_ virtues, noble exploits, and heroism. Men are too much disposed to _extol_ the rich and despise the poor.

**EXTOL** him that rideth upon the heavens by his name Jah. [[Psalms 68#4|Psalms 68:4]].
