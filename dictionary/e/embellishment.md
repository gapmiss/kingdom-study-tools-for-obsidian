# Embellishment

**EMBEL'LISHMENT**, _noun_ The act of adorning.

**1.** Ornament; decoration; any thing that adds beauty or elegance; that which renders any thing pleasing to the eye, or agreeable to the taste, in dress, furniture, manners, or in the fine arts. Rich dresses are embellishments of the person. Virtue is an _embellishment_ of the mind, and liberal arts, the embellishments of society.
