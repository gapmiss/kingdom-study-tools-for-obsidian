# Epulotic

**EPULOT'IC**, _adjective_ \[Gr. to heal, to cicatrize; a cicatrix, to be sound, whole.\] Healing; cicatrizing.

**EPULOT'IC**, _noun_ A medicament or application which tends to dry, cicatrize and heal wounds or ulcers, to repress fungous flesh and dispose the parts to recover soundness.
