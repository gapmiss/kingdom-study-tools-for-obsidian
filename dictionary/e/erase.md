# Erase

**ERA'SE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin erado, erasi; e and rado, to scrape; Heb. a graving tool.\]

**1.** To rub or scrape out, as letters or characters written, engraved or painted; to efface; as, to _erase_ a word or a name.

**2.** To obliterate; to expunge; to blot out; as with pen and ink.

**3.** To efface; to destroy; as ideas in the mind or memory.

**4.** To destroy to the foundation. \[See [[Raze]].\]
