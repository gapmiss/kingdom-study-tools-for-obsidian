# Exhale

**EXHA'LE**, _verb transitive_ egzha'le. \[Latin exhalo; ex and halo, to breathe, to send forth vapor.\]

**1.** To send out; to emit; as vapor, or minute particles of a fluid or other substance. The rose exhales a fragrant odor. The earth exhales vapor. Marshes _exhale_ noxious effluvia.

**2.** To draw out; to cause to be emitted in vapor or minute particles; to evaporate. The sun exhales the moisture of the earth.
