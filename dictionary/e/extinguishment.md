# Extinguishment

**EXTIN'GUISHMENT**, _noun_ The act of putting out or quenching; extinction; suppression; destruction; as the _extinguishment_ of fire or flame; of discord, enmity or jealousy; or of love or affection.

**1.** Abolition; nullification.

Divine laws of christian church policy may not be altered by _extinguishment_

**2.** Extinction; a putting an end to, or a coming to an end; termination; as the _extinguishment_ of a race or tribe.

**3.** The putting an end to a right or estate, by consolidation or union.

If my tenant for life makes a lease to A for life, remainder to B and his heirs, and I release to A; this release operates as an _extinguishment_ of my right to the reversion.
