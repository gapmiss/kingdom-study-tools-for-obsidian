# Expensive

**EXPENS'IVE**, _adjective_ Costly; requiring much expense; as an _expensive_ dress or equipment; an _expensive_ family. Vices are usually more _expensive_ than virtues.

**1.** Given to expense; free in the use of money; extravagant; lavish; applied to persons. Of men, some are frugal and industrious; others, idle land _expensive_

**2.** Liberal; generous in the distribution of property.

This requires an active, _expensive_ indefatigable goodness.
