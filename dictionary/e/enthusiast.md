# Enthusiast

**ENTHU'SIAST**, _noun_ enthu'ziast.

**1.** One who imagines he has special or supernatural converse with God, or special communications from him.

**2.** One whose imagination is warmed; one whose mind is highly excited with the love or in the pursuit of an object; a person of ardent zeal; as an _enthusiast_ in poetry or music.

**3.** One of elevated fancy or exalted ideas.
