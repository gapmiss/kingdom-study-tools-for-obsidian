# Excessive

**EXCESS'IVE**, _adjective_ Beyond any given degree, measure or limit, or beyond the common measure or proportion; as the _excessive_ bulk of a man; _excessive_ labor; _excessive_ wages.

**1.** Beyond the established laws of morality and religion, or beyond the bounds of justice, fitness, propriety, expedience or utility; as _excessive_ indulgence of any kind.

Excessive bail shall not be required.

**2.** Extravagant; unreasonable. His expenditures of money were _excessive_

**3.** Vehement; violent; as _excessive_ passion.
