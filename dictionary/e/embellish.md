# Embellish

**EMBEL'LISH**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin bellus, pretty.\]

**1.** To adorn; to beautify; to decorate; to make beautiful or elegant by ornaments; applied to persons or things. We _embellish_ the person with rich apparel, a garden with shrubs and flowers, and style with metaphors.

**2.** To make graceful or elegant; as, to _embellish_ manners.
