# Explosion

**EXPLO'SION**, _noun_ s as z. \[from explode.\]

**1.** A bursting with noise; a bursting or sudden expansion of any elastic fluid, with force and a loud report; as the _explosion_ of powder.

**2.** The discharge of a piece of ordnance with a loud report.

**3.** The sudden burst of sound in a volcano, etc.
