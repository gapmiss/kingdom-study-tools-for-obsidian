# Errant

**ER'RANT**, _adjective_ \[Latin errans, from erro, to err.\]

**1.** Wandering; roving; rambling; applied particularly to knights, who, in the middle ages, wandered about to seek adventures and display their heroism and generosity, called knights _errant_

**2.** Deviating from a certain course.

**3.** Itinerant.

Errant, for arrant, a false orthography. \[See [[Arrant]].\]
