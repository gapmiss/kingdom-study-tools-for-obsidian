# Equitable

**EQ'UITABLE**, _noun_ \[Latin oequitas, from oequus, equal.\]

**1.** Equal in regard to the rights of persons; distributing equal justice; giving each his due; assigning to one or more what law or justice demands; just; impartial. The judge does justice by an _equitable_ decision. The court will make an _equitable_ distribution of the estate.

**2.** Having the disposition to do justice, or doing justice; impartial; as an _equitable_ judge.

**3.** Held or exercised in equity, or with chancery powers; as the _equitable_ jurisdiction of a court.
