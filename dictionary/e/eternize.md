# Eternize

**ETER'NIZE**, _verb transitive_ \[Low Latin oeterno.\]

**1.** To make endless.

**2.** To continue the existence or duration of indefinitely; to perpetuate; as, to _eternize_ woe.

So we say, to _eternize_ fame or glory.

**3.** To make forever famous; to immortalize; as, to _eternize_ a name; to _eternize_ exploits.
