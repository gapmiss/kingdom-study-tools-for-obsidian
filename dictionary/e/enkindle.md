# Enkindle

**ENKIN'DLE**, _verb transitive_ \[from kindle.\] To kindle; to set on fire; to inflame; as, to _enkindle_ sparks into a flame. In this literal sense, kindle is generally used.

**1.** To excite; to rouse into action; to inflame; as, to _enkindle_ the passions into a flame; to _enkindle_ zeal; to _enkindle_ war or discord, or the flames of war.
