# Expedition

**EXPEDI'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin expeditio.\] Haste; speed; quickness; dispatch. The mail is conveyed with _expedition_

**1.** The march of an army, or the voyage of a fleet, to a distant place, for hostile purposes; as the _expedition_ of the French to Egypt; the _expedition_ of Xerxes into Greece.

**2.** Any enterprize, undertaking or attempt by a number of persons; or the collective body which undertakes. We say, our government sent an _expedition_ to the Pacific; the _expedition_ has arrived.
