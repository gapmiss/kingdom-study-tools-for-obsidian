# Exult

**EXULT**', _verb intransitive_ egzult'. \[Latin exulto; ex and salto, salio, to leap.\]

Properly, to leap for joy; hence, to rejoice in triumph; to rejoice exceedingly, at success or victory; to be glad above measure; to triumph. It is natural to man to _exult_ at the success of his schemes, and to _exult_ over a fallen adversary.
