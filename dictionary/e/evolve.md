# Evolve

**EVOLVE**, _verb transitive_ evolv'. \[Latin evolvo; e and volvo, to roll; Eng. to wallow.\]

**1.** To unfold; to open and expand.

The animal soul sooner evolves itself to its full orb and extent than the human soul.

**2.** To throw out; to emit.

**EVOLVE**, _verb intransitive_ To open itself; to disclose itself.
