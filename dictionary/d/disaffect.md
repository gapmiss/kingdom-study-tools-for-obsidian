# Disaffect

**DISAFFECT**, _verb transitive_ \[dis and affect.\]

**1.** To alienate affection; to make less friendly to; to make less faithful to a person, party or cause, or less zealous to support it; to make discontented or unfriendly ; as, an attempt was made to _disaffect_ the army.

**2.** To disdain, or dislike.

**3.** To throw into disorder.
