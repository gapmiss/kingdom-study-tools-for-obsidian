# Immoral

**IMMOR'AL**, _adjective_ \[in and moral.\] Inconsistent with moral rectitude; contrary to the moral or divine law; wicked; unjust; dishonest; vicious. Every action is _immoral_ which contravenes any divine precept, or which is contrary to the duties which men owe to each other.

**1.** Wicked or unjust in practice; vicious; dishonest; as an _immoral_ man. Every man who violates a divine law or a social duty, is _immoral_ but we particularly apply the term to a person who habitually violates the laws.
