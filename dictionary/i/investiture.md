# Investiture

**INVEST'ITURE**, _noun_ The action of giving possession, or livery of seizin.

The grant of land or a feud was perfected by the ceremony of corporal _investiture_ or open delivery of possession.

It was customary for princes to make _investiture_ of ecclesiastical benefices.

**1.** The right of giving possession of any manor, office or benefice.

He had refused to yield to the pope the _investiture_ of bishops.
