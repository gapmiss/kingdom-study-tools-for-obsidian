# Irreverence

**IRREV'ERENCE**, _noun_ \[Latin irreverentia; in and reverentia. See [[Reverence]].\]

**1.** Want of reverence, or want of veneration; want of due regard to the authority and character of the Supreme Being. _irreverence_ toward God is analogous to disrespect toward man.

**2.** The state of being disregarded; applied to men. But this word is appropriately applicable to the Supreme Being and to his laws and institutions.
