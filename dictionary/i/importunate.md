# Importunate

**IMPORT'UNATE**, _adjective_ \[Latin importunus. See [[Importune]].\]

**1.** Bearing on; pressing or urging in request or demand; urgent and pertinacious in solicitation; as an _importunate_ suitor or petitioner.

**2.** Pressing; urgent; as an _importunate_ demand.

**3.** Inciting urgently for gratification; as _importunate_ passions and appetites.
