# Imperative

**IMPER'ATIVE**, _adjective_ \[Latin imperativus, from impero, to command. See [[Empire]].\]

**1.** Commanding; expressive of command; containing positive command, as distinguished from advisory, or discretionary. The orders are _imperative_

**2.** In grammar, the _imperative_ mode of a verb is that which expresses command, entreaty, advice or exhortation; as, go, write, attend.
