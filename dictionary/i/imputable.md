# Imputable

**IMPU'TABLE**, _adjective_ \[See [[Impute]].\] That may be imputed or charged to a person; chargeable. Thus we say, crimes, sins, errors, trespasses are _imputable_ to those who commit them.

**1.** That may be ascribed to; in a good sense.

This favor is _imputable_ to your goodness, or to a good motive.

**2.** Accusable; chargeable with a fault. \[Not proper.\]

**3.** That may be set to the account of another. It has been a question much agitated, whether Adam's sin is _imputable_ to his posterity.
