# Individual

**INDIVID'UAL**, . \[Latin individuus; in and dividuus, from divido, to divide.\]

**1.** Not divided, or not to be divided; single; one; as an _individual_ man or city.

\--Under his great vicegerent reign abide

United, as one _individual_ soul.

**2.** Pertaining to one only; as _individual_ labor or exertions.

**INDIVID'UAL**, _noun_ A single person or human being. This is the common application of the word; as, there was not an _individual_ present.

**1.** A single animal or thing of any kind. But this word, as a noun, is rarely applied except to human beings.
