# Impure

**IMPU'RE**, _adjective_ \[Latin impurus; in and purus, pure.\]

**1.** Not pure; foul; feculent; tinctured; mixed or impregnated with extraneous substance; as _impure_ water or air; _impure_ salt or magnesia.

**2.** Obscene; as _impure_ language or ideas.

**3.** Unchaste; lewd; unclean; as _impure_ actions.

**4.** Defiled by sin or guilt; unholy; as persons.

**5.** Unhallowed; unholy; as things.

**6.** Unclean; in a legal sense; not purified according to the ceremonial law of Moses.

**IMPU'RE**, _verb transitive_ To render foul; to defile. \[Not used.\]
