# Individually

**INDIVID'UALLY**, _adverb_ Separately; by itself; to the exclusion of others. Thirty men will unitedly accomplish what each of them _individually_ cannot perform.

**1.** With separate or distinct existence.

How should that subsist solitarily by itself, which hath no substance, but _individually_ the very same whereby others subsist with it?

**2.** Inseparably; incommunicably.

Omniscience--an attribute _individually_ proper to the Godhead.
