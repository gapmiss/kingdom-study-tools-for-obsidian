# Impetuous

**IMPET'UOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin impetuosus, from impetus, impeto; in and peto, to urge, to rush. See [[Bid]].\]

**1.** Rushing with great force and violence; moving rapidly; furious; forcible; fierce; raging; as an _impetuous_ wind; an _impetuous_ torrent.

**2.** Vehement of mind; fierce; hasty; passionate; violent; as a man of _impetuous_ temper.
