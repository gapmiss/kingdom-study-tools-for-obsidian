# Inert

**INERT**', _adjective_ \[Latin iners; in and ars, art. The English sense is drawn not from art, but from the primary sense, strength or vigorous action.\]

**1.** Destitute of the power of moving itself, or of active resistance to motion impressed; as, matter is _inert_

**2.** Dull; sluggish; indisposed to move or act.
