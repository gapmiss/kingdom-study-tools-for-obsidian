# Intermediate

**INTERME'DIATE**, _adjective_ \[Latin inter and medius, middle.\] Lying or being in the middle place or degree between two extremes; intervening; interposed; as an _intermediate_ space between hills or rivers; _intermediate_ colors. Man has an _intermediate_ nature and rank between angels and brutes.

**INTERME'DIATE**, _noun_ In chemistry, a substance which is the intermedium or means of chimical affinity, as an alkali, which renders oil combinable with water.
