# Inconvenience

**INCONVE'NIENCE**

INCONVE'NIENCY, _noun_ \[Latin inconveniens; in and convenio, conveniens.\]

**1.** Unfitness; unsuitableness; inexpedience.

They plead against the _inconvenience_ not the unlawfulness of popish apparel.

**2.** That which gives trouble or uneasiness; disadvantage; any thing that disturbs quiet, impedes prosperity, or increases the difficulty of action or success. Rain and bad roads are inconveniences to the traveler; want of utensils is a great _inconvenience_ to a family; but the great _inconvenience_ of human life is the want of money and the means of obtaining it.
