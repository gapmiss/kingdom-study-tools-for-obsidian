# Impart

**IMP'ART**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin impertior; in and partio, to divide; from pars, a part.\]

**1.** To give, grant or communicate; to bestow on another a share or portion of something; as, to _impart_ a portion of provisions to the poor.

**2.** To grant; to give; to confer; as, to _impart_ honor or favor.

**3.** To communicate the knowledge of something; to make known; to show by words or tokens.

_Gentle lady,_

When first I did _impart_ my love to you--
