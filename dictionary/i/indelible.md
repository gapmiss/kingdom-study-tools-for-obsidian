# Indelible

**INDEL'IBLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin indelebilis; in and delebilis, from deleo, to blot out.\]

**1.** Not to be blotted out; that cannot be effaced or canceled; as _indelible_ letters or characters. _indelible_ ink is such as cannot be taken out of paper or cloth, or not by ordinary means.

**2.** Not to be annulled.

They are endued with _indelible_ power from above, to feed and govern this household. \[Unusual.\]

**3.** That cannot be effaced or lost; as, impressions on the mind may be indelible; reproach or stain on reputation may be _indelible_
