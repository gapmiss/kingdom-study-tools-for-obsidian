# Impracticability

**IMPRACTICABIL'ITY**

IMPRAC'TICABLE, _adjective_ \[in and practicable. See [[Practice]].\]

**1.** That cannot be done or performed; infeasible; not to be effected by human means, or by the means proposed. It is impracticable for a man to lift a ton by his unassisted strength; but not impracticable for a man aided by a mechanical power.

**2.** Untractable; unmanageable; stubborn; as a fierce, impracticable nature.

**3.** That cannot be passed or traveled; as an impracticable road; a colloquial sense.
