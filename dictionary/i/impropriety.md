# Impropriety

**IMPROPRI'ETY**, _noun_ \[Latin improprius. See [[Improper]].\]

**1.** Unfitness; unsuitableness to character, time, place or circumstances; as _impropriety_ of behavior or manners. Levity of conduct is an _impropriety_ in a religious assembly and at a funeral. Rudeness or forwardness in young persons before their superiors, is _impropriety_ Indecency and indecorum are improprieties.

**2.** Inaccuracy in language; a word or phrase not according with the established usages or principles of speaking or writing.

Many gross improprieties, however authorized by practice, ought to be discarded.
