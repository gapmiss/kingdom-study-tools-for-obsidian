# Instability

**INSTABIL'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin instabilitas, instabilis; in and stabilis, from sto, to stand.

**1.** Want of stability; want of firmness in purpose; inconstancy; fickleness; mutability of opinion or conduct. _instability_ is the characteristic of weak minds.

**2.** Changeableness; mutability; as the _instability_ of laws, plans or measures.
