# Indue

**INDUE**, _verb transitive_ indu'. \[Latin induo. This word coincides nearly in signification with endow, that is, to put on, to furnish. Dueo is evidently a contracted word.\]

**1.** To put on something; to invest; to clothe; as, to _indue_ matter with forms, or man with intelligence.

**2.** To furnish; to supply with; to endow.
