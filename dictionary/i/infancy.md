# Infancy

**IN'FANCY**, _noun_ \[Latin infantia. See [[Infant]].\]

**1.** The first part of life, beginning at the birth. In common usage, _infancy_ extends not beyond the first year or two of life, but there is not a defined limit where _infancy_ ends, and childhood begins.

**2.** In law, _infancy_ extends to the age of twenty one years.

**3.** The first age of any thing; the beginning or early period of existence; as the _infancy_ of the Roman republic; the _infancy_ of a college or of a charitable society; the _infancy_ of agriculture, of manufactures, or of commerce.
