# Insipid

**INSIP'ID**, _adjective_ \[Latin insipidus; in and sapidus, sapio, to taste.\]

**1.** Tasteless; destitute of taste; wanting the qualities which affect the organs of taste; vapid; as _insipid_ liquor.

**2.** Wanting spirit, life or animation; wanting pathos, or the power of exciting emotions; flat; dull; heavy; as an _insipid_ address; an _insipid_ composition.

**3.** Wanting power to gratify desire; as _insipid_ pleasure.
