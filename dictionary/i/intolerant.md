# Intolerant

**INTOL'ERANT**, _adjective_ \[Latin in and tolero, to endure.\]

**1.** Not enduring; not able to endure.

The powers of the human body being limited and _intolerant_ of excesses.

**2.** Not enduring difference of opinion or worship; refusing to tolerate others in the enjoyment of their opinions, rights and worship.

**INTOL'ERANT**, _noun_ One who does not favor toleration.
