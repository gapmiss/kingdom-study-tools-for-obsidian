# Implicitly

**IMPLIC'ITLY**, _adverb_ By inference deducible, but not expressed in words; virtually; in reality, but not in name.

He that denies the providence of God, _implicitly_ denies his existence.

**1.** By connection with something else; dependently; with unreserved confidence; without doubting, or without examining evidence. We are disposed to believe _implicitly_ what a man of veracity testifies.

Learn not to dispute the methods of his providence, but humbly and _implicitly_ to acquiesce in and adore them.
