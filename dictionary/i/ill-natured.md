# Ill-natured

**ILL-NA'TURED**, _adjective_ Cross, crabbed; surly; intractable; of habitual bad temper; peevish; fractious. An _ill-natured_ person may disturb the harmony of a whole parish.

**1.** That indicates ill-nature.

The _ill-natured_ task refuse.

**2.** Intractable; not yielding to culture; as _ill-natured_ land. \[Not legitimate.\]
