# Infidel

**IN'FIDEL**, _adjective_ \[Latin infidelis; in and fidelis; faithful.\]

Unbelieving; disbelieving the inspiration of the Scriptures, or the divine institution of christianity.

The _infidel_ writer is a great enemy to society.

**IN'FIDEL**, _noun_ One who disbelieves the inspiration of the Scriptures, and the divine origin of christianity.
