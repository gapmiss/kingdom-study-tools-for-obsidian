# Imminent

**IM'MINENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin imminens, from immineo, to hang over; in and minor, to threaten. See [[Menace]].\]

Literally, shooting over; hence, hanging over; impending; threatening; near; appearing as if about to fall on; used of evils; as _imminent_ danger; _imminent_ judgments, evils or death.
