# Inhospitable

**INHOS'PITABLE**, _adjective_ \[in and hospitable.\]

**1.** Not hospitable; not disposed to entertain strangers gratuitously; declining to entertain guests, or entertaining them with reluctance; as an _inhospitable_ person or people.

**2.** Affording no conveniences, subsistence or shelter to strangers; as _inhospitable_ desserts or rocks.
