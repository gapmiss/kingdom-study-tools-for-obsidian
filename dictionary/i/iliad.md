# Iliad

**IL'IAD**, _noun_ \[from Ilium, Ilion, Troy.\] An epic poem, composed by Homer, in twenty four books. The subject of this poem is the wrath of Achilles; in describing which, the poet exhibits the miserable effects of disunion and public dissensions. Hence the phrase, Ilias malorum, an _iliad_ of woes or calamities, a world of disasters.
