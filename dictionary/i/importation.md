# Importation

**IMPORTA'TION**, _noun_

**1.** The act or practice of importing, or of bringing from another country or state; opposed to exportation. Nations forbid the _importation_ of commodities which are produced or manufactured in sufficient abundance at home.

**2.** The ware or commodities imported.

The importations, this season, exceed those of the last.

**3.** Conveyance.
