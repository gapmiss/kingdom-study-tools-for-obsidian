# Inventory

**IN'VENTORY**, _noun_

**1.** An account, catalogue or schedule of all the goods and chattels of a deceased person. In some of the United States, the _inventory_ must include an account of the real as well as the personal estate of the deceased.

**2.** A catalogue of movables.

**3.** A catalogue of account of particular things. \[An indefinite use of the word.\]

**IN'VENTORY**, _verb transitive_ To make an _inventory_ of; to make a list, catalogue or schedule of; as, to _inventory_ the goods and estate of the deceased.

**1.** To insert or register in an account of goods.
