# Imperceptible

**IMPERCEP'TIBLE**, _adjective_

**1.** Not to be perceived; not to be known or discovered by the senses. We say a thing is _imperceptible_ to the touch, to the eye or sight, to the ear, to the taste or smell. Hence,

**2.** Very small; fine; minute in dimensions; or very slow in motion or progress; as, the growth of a plant or animal is imperceptible; it is too slow to be perceived by the eye.

**IMPERCEP'TIBLE**, _noun_ That which cannot be perceived by the senses on account of its smallness. \[Little used.\]
