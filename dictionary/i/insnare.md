# Insnare

**INSNA'RE**, _verb transitive_ \[in and snare.\] To catch in a snare; to entrap; to take by artificial means.

**1.** To inveigle; to seduce by artifice; to take by wiles, stratagem or deceit. The flattering tongue is apt to _insnare_ the artless youth.

**2.** To entangle; to involve in difficulties or perplexities.

\[This word is often written ensnare, but _insnare_ is the true orthography.\]
