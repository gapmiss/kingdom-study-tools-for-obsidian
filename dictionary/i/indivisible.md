# Indivisible

**INDIVIS'IBLE**, _adjective_ s as z. \[in and divisible. See [[Divide]].\]

That cannot be divided, separated or broken; not separable into parts. Perhaps the particles of matter, however small, cannot be considered as _indivisible_ The mind or soul must be _indivisible_ A mathematical point is _indivisible_

**INDIVIS'IBLE**, _noun_ In geometry, indivisibles are the elements or principles into which a body or figure may be resolved; elements infinitely small.
