# Illegitimate

**ILLEGIT'IMATE**, _adjective_ \[See [[Legitimate]].\]

**1.** Unlawfully begotten; born out of wedlock; spurious; as an _illegitimate_ son or daughter.

**2.** Unlawful; contrary to law.

**3.** Not genuine; not of genuine origin; as an _illegitimate_ inference.

**4.** Not authorized by good usage; as an _illegitimate_ word.

**ILLEGIT'IMATE**, _verb transitive_ To render illegitimate; to prove to be born out of wedlock; to bastardize.
