# Ignite

**IGNI'TE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin ignis, fire.\] To kindle, or set on fire.

**1.** More generally, to communicate fire to, or to render luminous or red by heat; as, to _ignite_ charcoal or iron. Anthracite is ignited with more difficulty than bituminous coal.

**IGNI'TE**, _verb intransitive_ To take fire; to become red with heat.
