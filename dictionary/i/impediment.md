# Impediment

**IMPED'IMENT**, _noun_ \[Latin impedimentum.\] That which hinders progress or motion; hinderance; obstruction; obstacle; applicable to every subject, physical or moral. Bad roads are impediments in marching and travelling. Idleness and dissipation are impediments to improvement. The cares of life are impediments to the progress of vital religion.

**1.** That which prevents distinct articulation; as an _impediment_ in speech.

**IMPED'IMENT**, _verb transitive_ To impede. \[Not in use.\]
