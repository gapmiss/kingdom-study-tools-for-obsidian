# Immunity

**IMMU'NITY**, _noun_ \[Latin immuinitas, from immunis, free, exempt; in and munus, charge, office, duty.\]

**1.** Freedom or exemption from obligation. To be exempted from observing the rites or duties of the church, is an _immunity_

**2.** Exemption from any charge, duty, office, tax or imposition; a particular privilege; as the immunities of the free cities of Germany; the immunities of the clergy.

**3.** Freedom; as an _immunity_ from error.
