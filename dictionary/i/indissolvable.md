# Indissolvable

**INDISSOLV'ABLE**, _adjective_ \[in and dissolvable.\]

**1.** That cannot be dissolved; not capable of being melted or liquefied.

**2.** Indissoluble; that cannot be broken; perpetually firm and binding; as an _indissolvable_ bond of union.

**3.** Not capable of separation into parts by natural process.
