# Indissoluble

**INDIS'SOLUBLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin indissolubilis; in and dissolubilis, from dissolvo; dis and solvo, to loosen.\]

**1.** Not capable of being dissolved, melted or liquefied, as by heat or water. Few substances are absolutely _indissoluble_ by heat; many are _indissoluble_ in water.

**2.** That cannot be broken or rightfully violated; perpetually binding or obligatory; as an _indissoluble_ league or covenant. The marriage covenant is _indissoluble_ except in certain specified cases.

**3.** Not to be broken; firm; stable; as _indissoluble_ friendship; _indissoluble_ bands of love.
