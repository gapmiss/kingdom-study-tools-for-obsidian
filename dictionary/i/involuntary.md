# Involuntary

**INVOL'UNTARY**, _adjective_ \[Latin in and voluntarius. See [[Voluntary]].\]

**1.** Not having will or choice; unwilling.

**2.** Independent of will or choice. The motion of the heart and arteries is _involuntary_ but not against the will.

**3.** Not proceeding from choice; not done willingly; opposed to the will. A slave and a conquered nation yield an _involuntary_ submission to a master.
