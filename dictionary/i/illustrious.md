# Illustrious

**ILLUS'TRIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin illustris.\]

**1.** Conspicuous; distinguished by the reputation of greatness; renowned; eminent; as an _illustrious_ general or magistrate; an _illustrious_ prince.

**2.** Conspicuous; renowned; conferring honor; as _illustrious_ actions.

**3.** Glorious; as an _illustrious_ display of the divine perfections.

**4.** A title of honor.
