# Insoluble

**INSOL'UBLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin insolubilis; in and solvo, to dissolve.\]

**1.** That cannot be dissolved, particularly by a liquid. We say a substance is _insoluble_ in water, when its parts will not separate and mix with that fluid.

**2.** Not to be solved or explained; not to be resolved; as a doubt or difficulty. \[Not much used.\]
