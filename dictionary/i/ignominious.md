# Ignominious

**IGNOMIN'IOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin ignominiosus. See [[Ignominy]].\]

**1.** Incurring disgrace; cowardly; of mean character.

Then with pale fear surprised,

Fled _ignominious_

**2.** Very shameful; reproachful; dishonorable; infamous. To be hanged for a crime is _ignominious_ Whipping, cropping and branding are _ignominious_ punishments.

**3.** Despicable; worthy of contempt; as an _ignominious_ projector.
