# Inalienable

**INA'LIENABLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin alieno, alienus.\]

Unalienable; that cannot be legally or justly alienated or transferred to another. The dominions of a king are _inalienable_ All men have certain natural rights which are _inalienable_ The estate of a minor is _inalienable_ without a reservation of the right of redemption, or the authority of the legislature.
