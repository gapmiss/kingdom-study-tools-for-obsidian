# Imitable

**IM'ITABLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin imitabilis. See [[Imitate]].\]

**1.** That may be imitated or copied. Let us follow our Savior in all his _imitable_ conduct and traits of character. There are some works of the ancients that are hardly _imitable_ The dignified style of Johnson is scarcely _imitable_

**2.** Worthy of imitation.
