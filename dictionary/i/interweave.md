# Interweave

**INTERWE'AVE**, _verb transitive_ _preterit tense_ interwove; _participle passive_ interwoven. \[inter and weave.\]

**1.** To weave together; to intermix or unite in texture or construction; as threads of silk and cotton interwoven.

**2.** To intermix; to set among or together; as a covert of interwoven trees.

**3.** To intermingle; to insert together; as, to _interweave_ truth with falsehood.
