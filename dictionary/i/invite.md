# Invite

**INVI'TE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin invito.\]

**1.** To ask to do some act or to go to some place; to request the company of a person; as, to _invite_ one to dine or sup; to _invite_ friends to a wedding; to _invite_ company to an entertainment; to _invite_ one to an excursion into the country.

**2.** To allure; to draw to; to tempt to come; to induce by pleasure or hope.

\--Shady groves, that easy sleep _invite_

**3.** To present should be in a situation not to _invite_ hostilities.
