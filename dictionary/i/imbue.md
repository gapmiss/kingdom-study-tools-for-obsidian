# Imbue

**IMBUE**, _verb intransitive_ imbu'. \[Latin imbuo; in and the root of Eng. buck, to buck cloth, that is, to dip, drench or steep in water.\]

**1.** To tinge deeply; to dye; as, to _imbue_ cloth.

**2.** To tincture deeply; to cause to imbibe; as, to _imbue_ the minds of youth with good principles.
