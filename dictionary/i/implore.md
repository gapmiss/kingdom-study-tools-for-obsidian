# Implore

**IMPLO'RE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin imploro; in and ploro, to cry out.\]

**1.** To call upon or for, in supplication; to beseech; to pray earnestly; to petition with urgency; to entreat; as, to _implore_ the forgiveness of sins; to _implore_ mercy.

Imploring all the gods that reign above.

**2.** To ask earnestly; to beg.

**IMPLO'RE**, _verb intransitive_ To entreat; to beg.

**IMPLO'RE**, _noun_ Earnest supplication. \[Not used.\]
