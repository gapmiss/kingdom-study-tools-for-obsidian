# Improvident

**IMPROV'IDENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin in and providens; pro and video, supra.\]

Wanting forecast; not foreseeing what will be necessary or convenient, or neglecting the measures which foresight would dictate; wanting care to make provision for future exigences. Seamen are proverbially _improvident_ It is sometimes followed by of; as _improvident_ of harm.
