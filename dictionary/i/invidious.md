# Invidious

**INVID'IOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin invidiosus, from invideo, to envy; in and video, to see. Invideo signified properly, to look against.\]

**1.** Envious; malignant.

**2.** Likely to incur ill will or hatred, or to provoke envy; hateful. \[This is the usual sense.\]

Agamemnon found it an _invidious_ affair to give the preference to any one of the Grecian heroes.
