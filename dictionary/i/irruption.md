# Irruption

**IRRUP'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin irruption; in and rumpo, to break or burst.\]

**1.** A bursting in; a breaking or sudden violent rushing into a place. Holland has been often inundated by irruptions of the sea.

**2.** A sudden invasion or incursion; a sudden, violent inroad, or entrance of invaders into a place or country; as the _irruption_ of the northern nations into France and Italy.
