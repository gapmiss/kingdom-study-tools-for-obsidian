# Investment

**INVEST'MENT**, _noun_ The action of investing.

**1.** Clothes; dress; garment; habit. \[We now use vestment.\]

**2.** The act of surrounding, blocking up or besieging by an armed force.

The capitulation was signed by the commander of the fort, within six days after its _investment_

**3.** The laying out of money in the purchase of some species

of property; literally, the clothing of money with something.

Before the _investment_ could be made, a change of the market might render it ineligible.
