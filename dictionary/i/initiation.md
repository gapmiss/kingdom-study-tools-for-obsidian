# Initiation

**INITIA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin initiatio.\] The act or process of introducing one into a new society, by instructing him in its principles, rules or ceremonies; as, too initiate a person into a christian community.

**1.** The act or process of making one acquainted with principles before unknown.

**2.** Admission by application of ceremonies or use of symbols; as, to initiate one into the visible church by baptism.
