# Indemnify

**INDEM'NIFY**, _verb transitive_ \[in and damnify; Latin damnificus; damnum, loss.\]

**1.** To save harmless; to secure against loss, damage or penalty.

**2.** To make good; to reimburse to one what he has lost. We _indemnify_ a man, by giving sufficient security to make good a future loss, or by actual reimbursement of loss, after it has occurred.
