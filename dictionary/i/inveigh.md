# Inveigh

**INVEIGH**, _verb intransitive_ inva'y. \[Latin inveho, to bear, throw or bring on or against; in and veho, to carry.\] To exclaim or rail against; to utter censorious and bitter language against any one; to reproach; with against. The author inveighed sharply against the vices of the clergy in his age. Men _inveigh_ against the follies of fashion.
