# Intendant

**INTEND'ANT**, _noun_ \[Latin intendo.\]

**1.** One who has the charge, oversight, direction or management of some public business; as an _intendant_ of marine; as _intendant_ of finance; a word much used in France, and sometimes in England and America, but we generally use in lieu of it superintendent.

**2.** In Charleston, S. Carolina, the mayor or chief municipal officer of the city.
