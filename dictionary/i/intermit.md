# Intermit

**INTERMIT**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin intermitto; inter and mitto, to send.\]

To cause to cease for a time; to interrupt; to suspend.

Pray to the gods, to _intermit_ the plague

That needs must light on this ingratitude.

**INTERMIT'**, _verb intransitive_ To cease for a time; to go off at intervals; as a fever. A tertian fever intermits every other day. The pulse sometimes intermits for a second of time.
