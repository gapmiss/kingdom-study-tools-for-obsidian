# Incurable

**INCU'RABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** That cannot be cured; not admitting of cure; beyond the power of skill or medicine; as an _incurable_ disease.

**2.** Not admitting remedy or correction; irremediable; remediless; as _incurable_ evils.

**INCU'RABLE**, _noun_ A person diseased beyond the reach of cure.
