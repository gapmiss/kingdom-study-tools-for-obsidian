# Impartial

**IMP'ARTIAL**, _adjective_ \[in and partial, from part, Latin pars.\]

**1.** Not partial; not biased in favor of one party more than another; indifferent; unprejudiced; disinterested; as an _impartial_ judge or arbitrator.

**2.** Not favoring one party more than another; equitable; just; as an _impartial_ judgment or decision; an _impartial_ opinion.
