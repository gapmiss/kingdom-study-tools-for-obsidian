# Inquisitive

**INQUIS'ITIVE**, _adjective_ s as z. Apt to ask questions; addicted to inquiry; inclined to seek information by questions; followed by about or after. He was very _inquisitive_ about or after news. Children are usually _inquisitive_

**1.** Inclined to seek knowledge by discussion, investigation or observation; given to research. He possesses an _inquisitive_ mind or disposition. We live in an _inquisitive_ age.

**INQUIS'ITIVE**, _noun_ A person who is inquisitive; one curious in research.
