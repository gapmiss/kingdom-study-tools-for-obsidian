# Inclemency

**INCLEM'ENCY**, _noun_ \[Latin inclementia. See [[Clemency]].\]

**1.** Want of clemency; want of mildness of temper; unmercifulness; harshness; severity; applied to persons.

**2.** Roughness, boisterousness; storminess; or simply raininess; severe cold, etc.; applied to the weather. We were detained by the _inclemency_ of the weather.
