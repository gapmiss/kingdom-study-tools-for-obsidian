# Insufficiency

**INSUFFI'CIENCY**, _noun_ \[in and sufficiency.\]

**1.** Inadequateness; want of sufficiency; deficiency; as an _insufficiency_ of provisions to supply the garrison.

**2.** Inadequacy of power or skill; inability; incapacity; incompetency; as the _insufficiency_ of a man for an office.

**3.** Want of the requisite strength, value or force; defect.

The _insufficiency_ of the light of nature is supplied by the light of Scripture.
