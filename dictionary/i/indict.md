# Indict

**INDICT**, _verb transitive_ indi'te. \[Latin indictus, from indico; in and dico, to speak.\] In law, to accuse or charge with a crime or misdemeanor, in writing, by a grand jury under oath. It is the peculiar province of a grand jury to _indict_ as it is of a house of representatives to impeach. It is followed by of; as indicted of treason or arson.
