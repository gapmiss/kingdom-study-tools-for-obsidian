# Insignificance

**INSIGNIF'ICANCE**

INSIGNIF'ICANCY, _noun_ \[in and significance.\]

**1.** Want of significance or meaning; as the _insignificance_ of words or phrases.

**2.** Unimportance; want of force or effect; as the _insignificance_ of human art or of ceremonies.

**3.** Want of weight; meanness.
