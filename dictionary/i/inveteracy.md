# Inveteracy

**INVET'ERACY**, _noun_ \[Latin inveteratio. See [[Inveterate]].\]

Long continuance, or the firmness or deep rooted obstinacy of any quality or state acquired by time; as the _inveteracy_ of custom and habit; usually or always applied in a bad sense; as the _inveteracy_ of prejudice, of error, or of any evil habit.
