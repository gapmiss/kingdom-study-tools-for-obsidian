# Indecisive

**INDECI'SIVE**, _adjective_ \[in and decisive.\] Not decisive; not bringing to a final close or ultimate issue; as an _indecisive_ battle or engagement; an argument _indecisive_ of the question.

**1.** Unsettled; wavering; vacillating; hesitating; as an _indecisive_ state of mind; an _indecisive_ character.
