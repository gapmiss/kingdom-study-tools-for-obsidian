# Interpret

**INTER'PRET**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin interpretor, from interpres.\]

**1.** To explain the meaning or words to a person who does not understand them; to expound; to translate unintelligible words into intelligible ones; as, to _interpret_ the Hebrew language to an Englishman.

\--Immanuel, which being interpreted, signified, God with us. [[Matthew 1#23|Matthew 1:23]].

**2.** To explain or unfold the meaning of predictions, vision, dreams or enigmas; to expound and lay open what is concealed from the understanding; as, Joseph interpreted the dream of Pharaoh.

**3.** To decipher.

**4.** To explain something not understood; as, to _interpret_ looks or signs.

**5.** To define; to explain words by other words in the same language.
