# Impertinent

**IMPER'TINENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin impertinens, supra.\]

**1.** Not pertaining to the matter in hand; of no weight; having no bearing on the subject; as an _impertinent_ remark.

**2.** Rude; intrusive; meddling with that which does not belong to the person; as an _impertinent_ coxcomb.

**3.** Trifling; foolish; negligent of the present purpose.

**IMPER'TINENT**, _noun_ An intruder; a meddler; one who interferes in what does not belong to him.
