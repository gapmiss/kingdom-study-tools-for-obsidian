# Impropriate

**IMPRO'PRIATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin in and proprius, proper.\]

**1.** To appropriate to private use; to take to one's self; as, to _impropriate_ thanks to one's self. \[Not used.\]

**2.** To annex the possessions of the church or a benefice to a layman.

**IMPRO'PRIATE**, _adjective_ Devolved into the hands of a layman.
