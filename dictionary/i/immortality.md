# Immortality

**IMMORTAL'ITY**, _noun_ The quality of never ceasing to live or exist; exemption from death and annihilation; life destined to endure without end; as the _immortality_ of the human soul.

\--Jesus Christ, who hath abolished death, and hath brought life and _immortality_ to light through the gospel. [[2 Timothy 1#10|2 Timothy 1:10]].

**1.** Exemption from oblivion.

**2.** Perpetuity; existence not limited; as the _immortality_ of a corporation.
