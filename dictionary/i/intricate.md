# Intricate

**IN'TRICATE**, _adjective_ \[Latin intricatus, from intrico, to fold; in and tricor.\] Entangled, involved; perplexed; complicated; obscure. We passed through _intricate_ windings. We found the accounts _intricate_ The case on trial is _intricate_ The plot of a tragedy may be too _intricate_ to please.

**IN'TRICATE**, _verb transitive_ To perplex; to make obscure. \[Little used.\]
