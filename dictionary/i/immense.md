# Immense

**IMMENSE**, _adjective_ immens'. \[Latin immensus; in and mensus, metior, to measure.\]

**1.** Unlimited; unbounded; infinite.

O goodness infinite! goodness immense!

**2.** Vast in extent; very great; as an _immense_ distance.

**3.** Huge in bulk; very large; as the _immense_ body of Jupiter.
