# Indigested

**INDIGEST'ED**, _adjective_ \[in and digested; Latin indigestus.\]

**1.** Not digested; not concocted in the stomach; not changed or prepared for nourishing the body; indigested; crude.

**2.** Not separated into distinct classes or orders, or into proper from; not regularly disposed and arranged. Chaos is represented as a rude or _indigested_ mass.

**3.** Not methodized; not reduced to due form; crude; as an _indigested_ scheme.

**4.** Not prepared by heat.

**5.** Not brought to suppuration, as the contents of an abscess or boil; as an _indigested_ wound.
