# Indurate

**IN'DURATE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin induro; in and duro, to harden.\]

To grow hard; to harden or become hard. Clay indurates by drying, and by extreme heat.

**IN'DURATE**, _verb transitive_ To make hard. Extreme heat indurates clay. Some fossils are indurated by exposure to the air.

**1.** To make unfeeling; to deprive of sensibility; to render obdurate; as, to _indurate_ the heart.
