# Irrecoverable

**IRRECOV'ERABLE**, _adjective_ \[in and recoverable.\]

**1.** Not to be recovered or repaired; as an _irrecoverable_ loss.

**2.** That cannot be regained. Time past is _irrecoverable_

**3.** That cannot be obtained by demand or suit; as a debt.

**4.** Not be remedied; as _irrecoverable_ misery.
