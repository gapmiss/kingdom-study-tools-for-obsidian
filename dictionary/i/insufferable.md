# Insufferable

**INSUF'FERABLE**, _adjective_ \[in and sufferable.\]

**1.** Intolerable; that cannot be borne or endured; as _insufferable_ heat, cold or pain.

**2.** That cannot be permitted or tolerated.

Our wrongs are _insufferable_

**3.** Detestable; contemptible; disgusting beyond endurance.

A multitude of scribblers who daily pester the world with their _insufferable_ stuff--
