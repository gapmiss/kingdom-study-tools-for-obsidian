# Intrinsic

**INTRIN'SIC**

**INTRIN'SIC**AL, _adjective_ \[Latin intrinsecus; intra and secus.\]

**1.** Inward; internal; hence, true; genuine; real; essential; inherent; not apparent or accidental; as the _intrinsic_ value of gold or silver; the _intrinsic_ merit of an action; the _intrinsic_ worth or goodness of a person.

**2.** Intimate; closely familiar.
