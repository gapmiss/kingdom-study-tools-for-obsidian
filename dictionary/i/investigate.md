# Investigate

**INVEST'IGATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin investigo; in and vestigo, to follow a track, to search; vestigium, a track or footstep.\]

To search into; to inquire and examine into with care and accuracy; to find out by careful disquisition; as, to _investigate_ the powers and forces of nature; to _investigate_ the causes of natural phenomena; to _investigate_ the principles of moral duty; to _investigate_ the conduct of an agent or the motives of a prince.
