# Intestate

**INTEST'ATE**, _adjective_ \[Latin intestatus; in and testatus, testor, to make a will.\]

**1.** Dying without having made a will. When a man dies _intestate_ his estate is committed for settlement to administrators.

**2.** Not devised; not disposed of by will; as an _intestate_ estate.

**INTEST'ATE**, _noun_ A person who dies without making a will.
