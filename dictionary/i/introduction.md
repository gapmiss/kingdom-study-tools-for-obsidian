# Introduction

**INTRODUC'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin introductio.\]

**1.** The action of conducting or ushering into a place; used of persons. We speak of the _introduction_ of one stranger to another; the _introduction_ of a foreign minister to a prince or court, and the _introduction_ of company to a levee.

**2.** The act of bringing into a country; as the _introduction_ of gold or bullion, or of merchandise.

**3.** The act of bringing something into notice, practice or use; as the _introduction_ of new modes of dress or of tillage.

**4.** The part of a book which precedes the main work; a preface or preliminary discourse.

**5.** The first part of an oration or discourse, in which the speaker gives some general account of his design and subject, and prepares the minds of his audience for a favorable reception of his remarks or arguments.
