# Inflection

**INFLEC'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin inflectio.\] The act of bending or turning from a direct line or course.

**1.** In optics, a property of light by which its rays, when they approach a body, are bent towards it or from it.

**2.** In grammar, the variation of nouns, etc., by declension, and verbs by conjugation.

**3.** Modulation of the voice in speaking.

More commonly _inflection_ gives significance to tones.

Point of _inflection_ in geometry, the point where a curve begins to bend the contrary way.
