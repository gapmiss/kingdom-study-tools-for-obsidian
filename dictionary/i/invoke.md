# Invoke

**INVO'KE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin invoco; in and voco, to call; vox, a word.\]

**1.** To address in prayer; to call on for assistance and protection; as, to _invoke_ the Supreme Being. Poets _invoke_ the muses for assistance.

**2.** To order; to call judicially; as, to _invoke_ depositions or evidence into a court.
