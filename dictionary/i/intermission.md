# Intermission

**INTERMIS'SION**, _noun_ \[Latin intermissio. See [[Intermit]].\]

**1.** Cessation for a time; pause; intermediate stop; as, to labor without intermission; service or business will begin after an _intermission_ of one hour.

**2.** Intervenient time.

**3.** The temporary cessation or subsidence of a fever; the space of time between the paroxysms of a disease. _intermission_ is an entire cessation, as distinguished from remission or abatement of fever.

**4.** The state of being neglected; disuse; as of words. \[Little used.\]
