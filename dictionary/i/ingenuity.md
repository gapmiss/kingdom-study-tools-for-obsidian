# Ingenuity

**INGENU'ITY**, _noun_ The quality or power of ready invention; quickness or acuteness in combining ideas, or in forming new combinations; ingeniousness; skill; used of persons. How many machines for saving labor has the _ingenuity_ of men devised and constructed.

**1.** Curiousness in design, the effect of ingenuity; as the _ingenuity_ of a plan or of mechanism.

**2.** Openness of heart; fairness; candor. \[This sense of the word was formerly common, and is found in good authors down to the age of Locke, and even later; but it is now wholly obsolete. In lieu of it, ingenousness is used.\]
