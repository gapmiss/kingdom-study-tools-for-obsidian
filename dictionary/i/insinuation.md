# Insinuation

**INSINUA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin insinuatio.\]

**1.** The act of insinuating; a creeping or winding in; a flowing into crevices.

**2.** The act of gaining on favor or affections, by gentle or artful means.

**3.** The art or power of pleasing and stealing on the affections.

He had a natural _insinuation_ and address; which made him acceptable in the best company.

**4.** A hint; a suggestion or intimation by distant allusion. Slander may be conveyed by _insinuation_
