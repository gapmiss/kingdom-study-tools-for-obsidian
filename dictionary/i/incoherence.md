# Incoherence

**INCOHE'RENCE**

INCOHE'RENCY, _noun_ \[in and coherence.\]

**1.** Want of coherence; want of cohesion or adherence; looseness or unconnected state of parts, as of a powder.

**2.** Want of connection; incongruity; inconsistency; want of agreement, or dependence of one part on another; as the _incoherence_ of arguments, facts or principles.

**3.** Inconsistency; that which does not agree with other parts of the same thing.
