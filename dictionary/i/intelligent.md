# Intelligent

**INTEL'LIGENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin intelligens.\]

**1.** Endowed with the faculty of understanding or reason. Man is an _intelligent_ being.

**2.** Knowing; understanding; well informed; skilled; as an _intelligent_ officer; an _intelligent_ young man; an _intelligent_ architect; sometimes followed by of; as _intelligent_ of seasons.

**3.** Giving information. \[Not used nor proper.\]
