# Imbibe

**IMBI'BE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin imbibo; in and bibo, to drink.\]

**1.** To drink in; to absorb; as, a dry or porous body imbibes a fluid; a sponge imbibes moisture.

**2.** To receive or admit into the mind and retain; as, to _imbibe_ principles; to _imbibe_ errors. Imbibing in the mind always implies retention, at least for a time.

**3.** To imbue, as used by Newton; but he has not been followed.
