# Inductive

**INDUCT'IVE**, _adjective_ Leading or drawing; with to.

A brutish vice,

Inductive mainly to the sin of Eve.

**1.** Tending to induce or cause.

They may be _inductive_ of credibility. \[Unusual.\]

**2.** Leading to inferences; proceeding by induction; employed in drawing conclusions from promises; as _inductive_ reasoning.
