# Irradiate

**IRRA'DIATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin irradio; in and radio, to shine. See [[Ray]].\]

**1.** To illuminate; to brighten; to make splendid; to adorn with luster.

**2.** To enlighten intellectually; to illuminate; as, to _irradiate_ the mind.

**3.** To animate by heat or light.

**4.** To decorate with shining ornaments.

**IRRA'DIATE**, _verb intransitive_ To emit rays; to shine.

**IRRA'DIATE**, _adjective_ Adorned with shining ornaments.
