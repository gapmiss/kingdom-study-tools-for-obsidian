# Interposition

**INTERPOSI'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin interpositio.\]

**1.** A being, placing or coming between; intervention; as the _interposition_ of the Baltic sea between Germany and Sweden. The _interposition_ of the moon between the earth and the sun occasions a solar eclipse.

**2.** Intervenient agency; as the _interposition_ of the magistrate in quieting sedition. How many evidences have we of divine _interposition_ in favor of good men!

**3.** Mediation; agency between parties. By the _interposition_ of a common friend, the parties have been reconciled.

**4.** Any thing interposed.
