# Invent

**INVENT**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin invenio, inventum; in and venio, to come; literally, to come to, to fall on, to meet, Eng. to find.\]

**1.** To find out something new; to devise something not before known; to contrive and produce something that did not before exist; as, to _invent_ a new instrument of music; to _invent_ a machine for spinning; to _invent_ gunpowder. \[See [[Invention]].\]

**2.** To forge; to fabricate; to contrive falsely; as, to _invent_ falsehoods.

**3.** To feign; to frame by the imagination; as, to _invent_ the machinery of a poem.

**4.** To light on; to meet with. \[This is the literal sense, but not now used.\]
