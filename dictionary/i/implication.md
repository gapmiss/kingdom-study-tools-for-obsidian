# Implication

**IMPLICA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin implicatio, supra.\]

**1.** The act of infolding or involving.

**2.** Involution; entanglement.

Three principal causes of firmness are, the grossness, the quiet contact, and the _implication_ of the component parts.

**3.** An implying, or that which is implied, but not expressed; a tacit inference, or something fairly to be understood, though not expressed in words.

The doctors are, by _implication_ of a different opinion.
