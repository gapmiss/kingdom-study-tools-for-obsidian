# Insertion

**INSER'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin insertio.\]

**1.** The act of setting or placing in or among other things; as the _insertion_ of cions in stocks; the _insertion_ of words or passages in writings; the _insertion_ of notices or essays in a public paper; the _insertion_ of vessels, tendons, etc. in other parts of the body.

**2.** The thing inserted.
