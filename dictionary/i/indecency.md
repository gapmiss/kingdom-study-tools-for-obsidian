# Indecency

**INDE'CENCY**, _noun_ \[Latin indecens, indeceo; in and deceo, to become.\]

That which is unbecoming in language or manners; any action or behavior which is deemed a violation of modesty, or an offense to delicacy, as rude or wanton actions, obscene language, and whatever tends to excite a blush in a spectator. Extreme assurance or impudence may also be deemed _indecency_ of behavior towards superiors. \[See [[Indecorum]].\]
