# Impious

**IM'PIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin impius; in and pius, pious.\]

**1.** Irreverent towards the Supreme Being; wanting in veneration for God and his authority; irreligious; profane. The scoffer at God and his authority is _impious_ The profane swear is _impious_

_When vice prevails and _impious_ men bear sway,_

The post of honor is a private station.
