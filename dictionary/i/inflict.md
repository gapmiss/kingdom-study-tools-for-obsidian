# Inflict

**INFLICT**', _verb transitive_ \[Latin inflictus, infligo; in and fligo, to strike; Eng. to flog.\]

To lay on; to throw or send on; to apply; as, to _inflict_ pain or disgrace; to _inflict_ punishment on an offender.

To _inflict_ an office, condition, knowledge, tenderness, etc. on one, as used by Chesterfield, is not an authorized use of the word.
