# Inundate

**INUN'DATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin inundo, inundatus; in and unda, a wave, or its root.\]

**1.** To overflow; to deluge; to spread over with a fluid. The low lands along the Mississippi are inundated almost every spring.

**2.** To fill with an overflowing abundance or superfluity; as, the country was once inundated with bills of credit. The presses _inundate_ the country with papers.
