# Innovate

**IN'NOVATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin innovo; in and novo, to make new, novus, new.\]

**1.** To change or alter by introducing something new.

From his attempts upon the civil power, he proceeds to _innovate_ God's worship.

**2.** To bring in something new.

**IN'NOVATE**, _verb intransitive_ To introduce novelties; to make changes in any thing established; with on. It is often dangerous to _innovate_ on the customs of a nation.
