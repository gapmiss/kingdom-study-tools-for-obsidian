# Impervious

**IMPER'VIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin impervius; in and pervius, passable; per and via, way.\]

**1.** Not to be penetrated or passed through; impenetrable; as an _impervious_ gulf; an _impervious_ forest.

**2.** Not penetrable; not to be pierced by a pointed instrument; as an _impervious_ shield.

**3.** Not penetrable by light; not permeable to fluids. Glass is pervious to light, but _impervious_ to water. Paper is _impervious_ to light. In the latter sense only, _impervious_ is synonymous with impermeable.
