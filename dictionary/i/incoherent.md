# Incoherent

**INCOHE'RENT**, _adjective_ \[in and coherent.\]

**1.** Wanting cohesion; loose; unconnected; not fixed to each other; applied to material substances.

**2.** Wanting coherence or agreement; incongruous; inconsistent; having no dependence of one part on another; as, the thoughts of a dreaming man, and the language of a madman, are _incoherent_
