# Inflammatory

**INFLAM'MATORY**, _adjective_ Inflaming; tending to excite heat or inflammation; as medicines of an _inflammatory_ nature.

**1.** Accompanied with preternatural heat and excitement of arterial action; as an _inflammatory_ fever or disease.

**2.** Tending to excite anger, animosity, tumult or sedition; as _inflammatory_ libels, writings, speeches or publications.
