# Inheritable

**INHER'ITABLE**, _adjective_ That may be inherited; transmissible or descendible from the ancestor to the heir by course of law; as an _inheritable_ estate or title.

**1.** That may be transmitted from the parent to the child; as _inheritable_ qualities or infirmities.

**2.** Capable of taking by inheritance, or of receiving by descent.

By attainder--the blood of the person attainted is so corrupted as to be rendered no longer _inheritable_
