# Indolence

**IN'DOLENCE**, _noun_ \[Latin indolentia; in and doleo, to be pained.\]

**1.** Literally, freedom from pain.

**2.** Habitual idleness; indisposition to labor; laziness; inaction or want of exertion of body or mind, proceeding from love of ease or aversion to toil. _indolence_ like laziness, implies a constitutional or habitual love of ease; idleness does not.
