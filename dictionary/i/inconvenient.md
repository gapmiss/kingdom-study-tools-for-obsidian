# Inconvenient

**INCONVE'NIENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin supra.\]

**1.** Incommodious; unsuitable; disadvantageous; giving trouble or uneasiness; increasing the difficulty of progress or success; as an _inconvenient_ dress or garment; an _inconvenient_ house; _inconvenient_ customs; an _inconvenient_ arrangement of business.

**2.** Unfit; unsuitable.
