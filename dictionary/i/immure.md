# Immure

**IMMU'RE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin in and murus, a wall.\]

**1.** To inclose within walls; to shut up; to confine; as, to _immure_ nuns in cloisters.

The student immures himself voluntarily.

**2.** To wall; to surround with walls.

Lysimachus immured it with a wall. \[Not usual.\]

**3.** To imprison.

**IMMU'RE**, _noun_ A wall. \[Not used.\]
