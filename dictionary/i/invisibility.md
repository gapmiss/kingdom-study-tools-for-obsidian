# Invisibility

**INVISIBIL'ITY**

INVIS'IBLE, _adjective_ s as z. \[Latin invisibilis; in and visibilis, viso, to see.\] That cannot be seen; imperceptible by the sight. Millions of stars, invisible to the naked eye, may be seen by the telescope.

He endured, as seeing him who is invisible. [[Hebrews 11#1|Hebrews 11:1]].
