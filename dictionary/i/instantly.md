# Instantly

**IN'STANTLY**, _adverb_ Immediately; without any intervening time; at the moment.

Lightning often kills _instantly_

**1.** With urgent importunity.

And when they came to Jesus, they besought him _instantly_ saying, that he was worthy for whom he should do this. [[Luke 7#4|Luke 7:4]].

**2.** With diligence and earnestness. [[Acts 26#7|Acts 26:7]].
