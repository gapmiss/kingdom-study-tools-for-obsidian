# Incontinent

**INCON'TINENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin incontinens.\] Not restraining the passions or appetites, particularly the sexual appetite; indulging lust without restraint or in violation of law; unchaste; lewd.

**1.** Unable to restrain discharges.

In the sense of immediate or immediately, obs.

**INCON'TINENT**, _noun_ One who is unchaste.
