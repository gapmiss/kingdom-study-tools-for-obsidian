# Indistinctly

**INDISTINCT'LY**, _adverb_ Without distinction or separation; as when parts of a thing are _indistinctly_ seen.

**1.** Confusedly; not clearly; obscurely; as when ideas are _indistinctly_ comprehended.

**2.** Not definitely; not with precise limits; as when the border of a thing is _indistinctly_ marked.
