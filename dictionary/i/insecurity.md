# Insecurity

**INSECU'RITY**, _noun_ \[in and security.\] Want of safety, or want of confidence in safety. Seamen in a tempest must be conscious of their _insecurity_

**1.** Uncertainty. With what _insecurity_ of truth we ascribe effects or unseen causes.

**2.** Want of safety; danger; hazard; exposure to destruction or loss; applied to things; as the _insecurity_ of a building exposed to fire; the _insecurity_ of a debt.
