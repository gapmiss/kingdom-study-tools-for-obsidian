# Influx

**IN'FLUX**, _noun_ \[Latin influxus, influo; in and fluo, to flow.\]

**1.** The act of flowing in; as an _influx_ of light or other fluid.

**2.** Infusion; intromission.

The _influx_ of the knowledge of God, in relation to everlasting life, is infinitely of moment.

**3.** Influence, power. \[Not used.\]

**4.** A coming in; introduction; importation in abundance; as a great _influx_ of goods into a country, or an _influx_ of gold and silver.
