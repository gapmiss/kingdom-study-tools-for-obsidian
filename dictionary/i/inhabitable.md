# Inhabitable

**INHAB'ITABLE**, _adjective_ \[from inhabit.\] Habitable; that may be inhabited; capable of affording habitation to animals. The stars may be _inhabitable_ worlds. Some regions of the earth are not _inhabitable_ by reason of cold or sterility. A building may be too old and decayed to be _inhabitable_

**1.** Not habitable. \[Latin inhabitabilis.\] \[Not in use.\]
