# Inoffensive

**INOFFENS'IVE**, _adjective_ \[in and offensive.\]

**1.** Giving no offense or provocation; as an _inoffensive_ man; an _inoffensive_ answer.

**2.** Giving no uneasiness or disturbance; as an _inoffensive_ appearance or sight.

**3.** Harmless; doing no injury or mischief.

Thy _inoffensive_ satires never bite.

**4.** Not obstructing; presenting no hinderance.

\--From hence a passage broad,

Smooth, easy, _inoffensive_ down to hell.
