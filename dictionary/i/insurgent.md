# Insurgent

**INSURG'ENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin insurgens; in and surgo, to rise.\]in opposition to lawful civil or political authority; as _insurgent_ chiefs.

**INSURG'ENT**, _noun_ A person who rises in opposition to civil or political authority; one who openly and actively resists the execution of laws. \[See [[Insurrection]].\] An _insurgent_ differs from a rebel. The _insurgent_ opposes the execution of a particular law or laws; the rebel attempts to overthrow or change the government, or he revolts and attempts to place his country under another jurisdiction. All rebels are insurgents, but all insurgents are not rebels.
