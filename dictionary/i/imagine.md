# Imagine

**IMAG'INE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin imaginor, from imago, image.\]

**1.** To form a notion or idea in the mind; to fancy. We can _imagine_ the figure of a horse's head united to a human body.

In this sense, fancy is the more proper word.

**2.** To form ideas or representations in the mind, by modifying and combining our conceptions.

**3.** To contrive in purpose; to scheme; to devise.

How long will ye _imagine_ mischief against a man? [[Psalms 62#3|Psalms 62:3]].

**IMAG'INE**, _verb intransitive_ To conceive; to have a notion or idea. I cannot _imagine_ how this should have happened.
