# Infirm

**INFIRM**, _adjective_ inferm'. \[Latin infirmus; in and firmus.\]

**1.** Not firm or sound; weak; feeble; as an _infirm_ body; an _infirm_ constitution.

**2.** Weak of mind; irresolute; as _infirm_ of purpose.

**3.** Not solid or stable.

He who fixes on false principles, treads on _infirm_ ground.

**INFIRM**, _verb transitive_ inferm'. To weaken. \[Not used.\]
