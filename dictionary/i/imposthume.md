# Imposthume

**IMPOS'THUME**, _noun_ impos'tume. \[This word is a corruption of apostem, Latin apostema; Gr. to separate, to withdraw, or to stand off; to stand.\]

An abscess; a collection of pus or purulent matter in any part of an animal body.

\[This word and its derivatives, being mere corruptions, might well be suffered to pass into oblivion.\]

**IMPOS'THUME**, _verb intransitive_ The same as imposthumate.
