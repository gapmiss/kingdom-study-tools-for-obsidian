# Infusible

**INFU'SIBLE**, _adjective_ \[from the verb.\] That may be infused. Good principles are _infusible_ into the minds of youth.

**INFU'SIBLE**, _adjective_ \[in, not, and fusible, from fuse.\] Not fusible; incapable of fusion; that cannot be dissolved or melted.

The best crucibles are made of Limoges earth, which seems absolutely _infusible_
