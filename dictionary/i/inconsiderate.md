# Inconsiderate

**INCONSID'ERATE**, _adjective_ \[Latin inconsideratus. See [[Consider]].\]

**1.** Not considerate; not attending to the circumstances which regard safety or propriety; hasty; rash; imprudent; careless; thoughtless; heedless; inattentive. The young are generally _inconsiderate_

**2.** Proceeding from heedlessness; rash; as _inconsiderate_ conduct.

**3.** Not duly regarding; with of, before the subject; as _inconsiderate_ of consequences.
