# Idiopathic

**IDIOPATH'IC**, _adjective_ \[See [[Idiopathy]].\] Pertaining to idiopathy; indicating a disease peculiar to a particular part of the body, and not arising from any preceding disease; as _idiopathic_ head-ach. The epilepsy is _idiopathic_ when it proceeds from some fault in the brain; but sympathetic, when it is the consequence of some other disorder.

The term _idiopathic_ is also applied to general as well as local diseases, as _idiopathic_ fever. It then signifies, not sympathetic or symptomatic, not arising from any previous disease.
