# Impunity

**IMPU'NITY**, _noun_ \[Latin impunitas; in and punio, to punish.\]

**1.** Exemption from punishment or penalty. No person should be permitted to violate the laws with _impunity_ _impunity_ encourages men in crimes.

**2.** Freedom or exemption from injury. Some ferocious animals are not be encountered with _impunity_
