# Insolvency

**INSOLV'ENCY**, _noun_ \[infra.\] Inability of a person to pay all his debts; or the state of wanting property sufficient for such payment; as a merchant's _insolvency_

**1.** Insufficiency to discharge all debts of the owner; as the _insolvency_ of an estate.

Act of _insolvency_ \[See [[infra]], Insolvent law.\]
