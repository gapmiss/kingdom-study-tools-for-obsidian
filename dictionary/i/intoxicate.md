# Intoxicate

**INTOX'ICATE**, _verb transitive_ \[in and Latin toxicum, which, Pliny informs us, is from taxa, a species of tree.\]

**1.** To inebriate; to make drunk; as with spirituous liquor.

As with new wine intoxicated both,

They swim in mirth--

**2.** To excite the spirits to a kind of delirium; to elate to enthusiasm, frenzy or madness. Success may sometimes _intoxicate_ a man of sobriety. An enthusiast may be intoxicated and zeal.

**INTOX'ICATE**, _adjective_ Inebriated.
