# Ignipotent

**IGNIP'OTENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin ignis, fire, and potens, powerful.\]

Presiding over fire. Vulcan is called the power _ignipotent_

**IGNIS FATUUS**, _noun_ \[Latin\] A meteor or light that appears in the night, over marshy grounds, supposed to be occasioned by phosphoric matter extricated from putrefying animal or vegetable substances, or by some inflammable gas; vulgarly called - Will with the wisp, and Jack with a lantern.
