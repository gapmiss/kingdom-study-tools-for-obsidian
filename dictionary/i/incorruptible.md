# Incorruptible

**INCORRUPT'IBLE**, _adjective_

**1.** That cannot corrupt or decay; not admitting of corruption. Thus gold, glass, mercury, etc., are _incorruptible_ Spirits are supposed to be _incorruptible_

Our bodies shall be changed into _incorruptible_ and immortal substances.

**2.** That cannot be bribed; inflexibly just and upright.
