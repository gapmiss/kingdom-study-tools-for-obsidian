# Irritable

**IR'RITABLE**, _adjective_ \[from irritate.\] Susceptible of excitement, or of heat and action, as animal bodies.

**1.** Very susceptible of anger or passion; easily inflamed or exasperated; as an _irritable_ temper.

**2.** In physiology, susceptible of contraction, in consequence of the appulse of an external body.

In general, there is nothing _irritable_ in the animal body, but the muscular fibers.
