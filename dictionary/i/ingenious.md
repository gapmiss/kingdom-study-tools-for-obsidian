# Ingenious

**INGE'NIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin ingeniosus, from ingenium; in and genius, geno, gigno, to beget.

**1.** Possessed of genius, or the faculty of invention; hence, skillful or prompt to invent; having an aptitude to contrive, or to form new combinations of ideas; as an _ingenious_ author; an _ingenious_ mechanic.

The more _ingenious_ men are, the more apt are they to trouble themselves.

**2.** Proceeding from genius or ingenuity; of curious design, structure or mechanism; as an _ingenious_ performance of any kind; an _ingenious_ scheme or plan; an _ingenious_ model or machine; _ingenious_ fabric; _ingenious_ contrivance.

**3.** Witty; well formed; well adapted; as an _ingenious_ reply.

**4.** Mental; intellectual. \[Not used.\]
