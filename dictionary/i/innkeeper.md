# Innkeeper

**INN'KEEPER**, _noun_ \[inn and keep.\] An innholder. In America, the _innkeeper_ is often a tavern keeper or taverner, as well as an _innkeeper_ the inn for furnishing lodgings and provisions being usually united with the tavern for the sale of liquors.

**INN**, _verb intransitive_ To take up lodging; to lodge.

**INN**, _verb transitive_ To house; to put under cover.
