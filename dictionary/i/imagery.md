# Imagery

**IM'AGERY**, _noun_ im'ajry. Sensible representations, pictures, statues.

Rich carvings, portraitures and _imagery_

**1.** Show; appearance.

What can thy _imagery_ and sorrow mean?

**2.** Forms of the fancy; false ideas; imaginary phantasms.

The _imagery_ of a melancholic fancy.

**3.** Representations in writing or speaking; lively descriptions which impress the images of things on the mind; figures in discourse.

I wish there may be in this poem any instance of good _imagery_

**4.** Form; make.
