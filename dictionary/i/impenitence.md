# Impenitence

**IMPEN'ITENCE**

IMPEN'ITENCY, _noun_ \[Latin in and poenitens, from poeniteo, to repent, poena, pain.\] Want of penitence or repentance; absence of contrition or sorrow for sin; obduracy; hardness of heart. Final _impenitence_ dooms the sinner to inevitable punishment.

He will advance from one degree of _impenitence_ to another.
