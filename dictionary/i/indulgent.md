# Indulgent

**INDUL'GENT**, _adjective_ Yielding to the wishes, desires, humor or appetites of those under one's care; compliant; not opposing or restraining; as an _indulgent_ parent.

**1.** Mild, favorable; not severe; as the _indulgent_ censure of posterity.

**2.** Gratifying; favoring; with of.

The feeble old, _indulgent_ of their ease.
