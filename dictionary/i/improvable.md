# Improvable

**IMPROV'ABLE**, _adjective_ \[See [[Improve]].\] Susceptible of improvement; capable of growing or being made better; that may be advanced in good qualities.

We have stock enough, and that too of an _improvable_ nature, that is capable of infinite advancement.

Man is accommodated with moral principles, improvable by the exercise of his faculties.

I have a fine spread of _improvable_ lands.

**1.** That may be used to advantage, or for the increase of any thing valuable.

The essays of weaker heads afford _improvable_ hints to better.

**2.** Capable of tillage or cultivation.

A scarcity of _improvable_ lands began to be felt in these colonies.
