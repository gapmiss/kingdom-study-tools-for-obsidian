# Intimately

**IN'TIMATELY**, _adverb_ Closely; with close intermixture and union of parts; as two fluids _intimately_ mixed.

**1.** Closely; with nearness of friendship or alliance; as two friends _intimately_ united; two families _intimately_ connected.

**2.** Familiarly; particularly; as, to be _intimately_ acquainted with facts or with a subject.
