# Indicative

**INDIC'ATIVE**,_adjective_ \[Latin indicativus.\] Showing; giving intimation or knowledge of something not visible or obvious. Reserve is not always _indicative_ of modesty; it may be _indicative_ of prudence.

**1.** In grammar, the _indicative_ mode is the form of the verb that indicates, that is, which affirms or denies; as, he writes, he is writing; they run; we misimprove advantages. It also asks questions; as, has the mail arrived?
