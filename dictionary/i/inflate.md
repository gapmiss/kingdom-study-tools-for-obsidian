# Inflate

**INFLA'TE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin inflatus, from inflo; in and flo, to blow.\]

**1.** To swell or distend by injecting air; as, to _inflate_ a bladder; to _inflate_ the lungs.

**2.** To fill with the breath; to blow in.

**3.** To swell; to puff up; to elate; as, to _inflate_ one with pride or vanity.
