# Indifferently

**INDIF'FERENTLY**, _adverb_ Without distinction or preference; as, to offer pardon _indifferently_ to all.

**1.** Equally; impartially; without favor, prejudice or bias.

\--They may truly and _indifferently_ minister justice.

**2.** In a neutral state; without concern; without wish or aversion.

_Set honor in one eye and death i'th' other,_

And I will look on death _indifferently_
