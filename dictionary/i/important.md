# Important

**IMPORT'ANT**, _adjective_ Literally, bearing on or to. Hence, weighty; momentous; of great consequence; having a bearing on some interest, measure or result by which good or ill may be produced. Truth is _important_ to happiness as well as to knowledge, but none so _important_ as religious truth. The commerce of Great Britain is _important_ to her navy, and her navy is _important_ to her independence. Men often forget the _important_ end for which they were created.

**1.** Bearing on; forcible; driving.

He fiercely at him flew,

And with _important_ outrage him assailed.

**2.** Importunate. \[Not used.\]
