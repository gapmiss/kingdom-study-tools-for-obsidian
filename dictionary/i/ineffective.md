# Ineffective

**INEFFECT'IVE**, _adjective_ \[in and effective.\] Not effective; not producing any effect, or the effect intended; inefficient; useless.

The word of God, without the spirit, is a dead and _ineffective_ letter.

**1.** Not able; not competent to the service intended; as _ineffective_ troops; _ineffective_ force.

**2.** Producing no effect.
