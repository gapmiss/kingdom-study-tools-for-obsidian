# Inexpedient

**INEXPE'DIENT**, _adjective_ \[in and expedient.\] Not expedient; not tending to promote a purpose; not tending to a good end; unfit; improper; unsuitable to time and place. Whatever tends to retard or defeat success in a good cause is _inexpedient_ What is expedient at one time, may be _inexpedient_ at another.
