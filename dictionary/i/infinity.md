# Infinity

**INFIN'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin infinitas.\]

**1.** Unlimited extent of time, space or quantity; boundlessness. We apply _infinity_ to God and his perfections; we speak of the _infinity_ of his existence, his knowledge, his power, his goodness and holiness.

**2.** Immensity; indefinite extent.

**3.** Endless or indefinite number; a hyperbolical use of the word; as an _infinity_ of beauties.
