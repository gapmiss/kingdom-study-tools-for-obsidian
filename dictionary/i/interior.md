# Interior

**INTE'RIOR**, _adjective_ \[Latin _comparative_ formed from inter or intra, in or within.\]

**1.** Internal; being within any limits, inclosure or substance; inner; opposed to exterior or superficial; as the _interior_ apartments of a house; the _interior_ ornaments; the _interior_ surface of a hollow ball, the _interior_ parts of the earth.

**2.** Inland; remote from the limits, frontier or shore; as the _interior_ parts of a country, state or kingdom.

**INTE'RIOR**, _noun_ The internal part of a thing; the inside.

**1.** The inland part of a country, state or kingdom.
