# Invalidate

**INVAL'IDATE**, _verb transitive_ \[from invalid.\]

**1.** To weaken or lessen the force of; more generally, to destroy the strength or validity of; to render of no force or effect; as, to _invalidate_ an agreement or a contract.

**2.** To overthrow; to prove to be of no force; as, to _invalidate_ an argument.
