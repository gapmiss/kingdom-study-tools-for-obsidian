# Icy

**I'CY**, _adjective_ \[from ice.\] Abounding with ice; as the _icy_ regions of the north.

**1.** Cold; frosty; as _icy_ chains.

**2.** Made of ice.

**3.** Resembling ice; chilling.

Religion lays not an _icy_ hand on the true joys of life.

**4.** Cold; frigid; destitute of affection or passion.

**5.** Indifferent; unaffected; backward.
