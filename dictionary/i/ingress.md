# Ingress

**IN'GRESS**, _noun_ \[Latin ingerssus, ingredior, supra.\]

**1.** Entrance; as the _ingress_ of air into the lungs. It is particularly applied to the entrance of the moon into the shadow of the earth in eclipses, the sun's entrance into a sign, etc.

**2.** Power of entrance; means of entering. All _ingress_ was prohibited.
