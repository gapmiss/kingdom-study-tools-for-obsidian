# Invocation

**INVOCA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin invocatio.\]

**1.** The act of addressing in prayer.

**2.** The form or act of calling for the assistance or presence of any being, particularly of some divinity; as the _invocation_ of the muses.

The whole poem is a prayer to Fortune, and the _invocation_ is divided between the two deities.

**3.** A judicial call, demand or order; as the _invocation_ of papers or evidence into a court.
