# Intellectual

**INTELLECT'UAL**, _adjective_

**1.** Relating to the intellect or understanding; belonging to the mind; performed by the understanding; mental; as _intellectual_ power or operations.

**2.** Ideal; perceived by the intellect; existing in the understanding; as an _intellectual_ scene.

**3.** Having the power of understanding; as an _intellectual_ being.

**4.** Relating to the understanding; treating of the mind; as _intellectual_ philosophy, now sometimes called mental philosophy.

**INTELLECT'UAL**, _noun_ The intellect or understanding. \[Little used.\]
