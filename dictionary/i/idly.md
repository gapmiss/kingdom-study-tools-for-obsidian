# Idly

**I'DLY**, _adverb_ In an idle manner; without employment.

**1.** Lazily; sluggishly.

**2.** Foolishly; uselessly; in a trifling way.

A shilling spent _idly_ by a fool, may be saved by a wiser person.

**3.** Carelessly; without attention.

**4.** Vainly; ineffectually; as, to reason _idly_ against truth.
