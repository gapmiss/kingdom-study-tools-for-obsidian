# Inestimable

**INES'TIMABLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin inoestimabilis. See [[Estimate]].\]

**1.** That cannot be estimated or computed; as an _inestimable_ sum of money.

**2.** Too valuable or excellent to be rated; being above all price; as _inestimable_ rights. The privileges of American citizens, civil and religious, are _inestimable_
