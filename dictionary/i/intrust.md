# Intrust

**INTRUST**', _verb transitive_ \[in and trust.\] To deliver in trust; to confide to the care of; to commit to another with confidence in his fidelity; as, to _intrust_ a servant with one's money or goods, or to _intrust_ money or goods to a servant. We _intrust_ an agent or factor with commercial business, or we _intrust_ commercial concerns to an agent.

We _intrust_ our friends with secrets, or _intrust_ secrets to them.
