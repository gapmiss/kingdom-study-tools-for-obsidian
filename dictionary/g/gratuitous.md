# Gratuitous

**GRATU'ITOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin gratuitus, from gratus.\]

**1.** Free; voluntary; not required by justice; granted without claim or merit.

We mistake the _gratuitous_ blessings of heaven for the fruits of our own industry.

**2.** Asserted or taken without proof; as a _gratuitous_ argument or affirmation.
