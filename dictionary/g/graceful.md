# Graceful

**GRA'CEFUL**, _adjective_ Beautiful with dignity; elegant; agreeable in appearance, with an expression of dignity or elevation of mind or manner; used particularly of motion, looks and speech; as a _graceful_ walk; a _graceful_ deportment; a _graceful_ speaker; a _graceful_ air.

High o'er the rest in arms the _graceful_ Turnus

rode.
