# Gun

**GUN**, _noun_ An instrument consisting of a barrel or tube of iron or other metal fixed in a stock, from which balls, shot or other deadly weapons are discharged by the explosion of gunpowder. The larger species of guns are called cannon; and the smaller species are called muskets, carbines, fowling pieces, etc. But one species of fire-arms, the pistol, is never called a _gun_

**GUN**, _verb intransitive_ To shoot.

**GUN'-BARREL**, _noun_ The barrel or tube of a _gun_
