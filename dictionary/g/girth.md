# Girth

**GIRTH**, _noun_ The band or strap by which a saddle or any burden on a horse's back is made fast, by passing under his belly.

**1.** A circular bandage.

**2.** The compass measured by a firth or inclosing bandage.

He's a lusty, jolly fellow, that lives well, at least three yards in the _girth_

**GIRTH**, _verb transitive_ To bind with a _girth_
