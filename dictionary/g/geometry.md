# Geometry

**GEOM'ETRY**, _noun_ \[Gr. the earth, and measure.\] Originally and properly, the art of measuring the earth, or any distances or dimensions on it. But _geometry_ now denotes the science of magnitude in general, comprehending the doctrine and relations of whatever is susceptible of augmentation and diminution; as the mensuration of lines, surfaces, solids, velocity, weight, etc., with their various relations.
