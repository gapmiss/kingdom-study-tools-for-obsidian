# Goodman

**GOOD'MAN**, _noun_ A familiar appellation of civility; sometimes used ironically.

With you, _goodman_ boy, if you please.

**1.** A rustic term of compliment; as old _goodman_ Dobson.

**2.** A familiar appellation of a husband; also, the master of a family. [[Proverbs 7#19|Proverbs 7:19]]. [[Matthew 24#43|Matthew 24:43]].
