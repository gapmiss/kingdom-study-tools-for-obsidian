# Going

**GO'ING**, _participle present tense_ \[from go.\] Moving; walking; traveling; turning; rolling; flying; sailing, etc.

**GO'ING**, _noun_ The act of moving in any manner.

**1.** The act of walking.

**2.** Departure.

**3.** Pregnancy.

**4.** Procedure; way; course of life; behavior; deportment; used chiefly in the plural.

His eyes are on the ways of man, and he seeeth all his goings. [[Job 34#21|Job 34:21]].

**5.** Procedure; course of providential agency or government.

They have seen thy goings, O God; even the goings of my God, my King, in the sanctuary. [[Psalms 68#24|Psalms 68:24]].
