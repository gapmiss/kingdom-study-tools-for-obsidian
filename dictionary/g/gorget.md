# Gorget

**GORG'ET**, _noun_ A piece of armor for defending the throat or neck; a kind of breast-plate like a half-moon; also, a small convex ornament worn by officers on the breast.

**1.** Formerly, a ruff worn by females.

**2.** In surgery, _gorget_ or gorgeret, is a cutting instrument used in lithotomy; also, a concave or cannulated conductor, called a blunt _gorget_
