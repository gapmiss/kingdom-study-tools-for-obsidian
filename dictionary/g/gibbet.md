# Gibbet

**GIB'BET**, _noun_ A gallows; a post or machine in form of a gallows, on which notorious malefactors are hanged in chains, and on which their bodies are suffered to remain, as spectacles in terrorem.

**1.** Any traverse beam.

**GIB'BET**, _verb transitive_ To hang and expose on a _gibbet_ or gallows.

**1.** To hang or expose on anything going travers, as the beam of a _gibbet_
