# Grossly

**GROSSLY**, _adverb_ In bulky or large parts; coarsely. This matter is _grossly_ pulverized.

**1.** Greatly; palpably; enormously; as, this affair has been _grossly_ misrepresented.

**2.** Greatly; shamefully; as _grossly_ criminal.

**3.** Coarsely; without refinement or delicacy; as language _grossly_ vulgar.

**4.** Without art or skill.
