# Glorious

**GLO'RIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin gloriosus. See [[Glory]].\]

**1.** Illustrious; of exalted excellence and splendor; resplendent in majesty and divine attributes; applied to God. [[Exodus 15#11|Exodus 15:11]].

**2.** Noble; excellent; renowned; celebrated; illustrious; very honorable; applied to men, their achievements, titles, etc.

Let us remember we are Cato's friends,

And act like men who claim that _glorious_ title.

**3.** Boastful; self-exulting; haughty; ostentatious.
