# Granulate

**GRAN'ULATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin granum.\]

**1.** To form into grains or small masses; as, to _granulate_ powder or sugar.

**2.** To raise into small asperities; to make rough on the surface.

**GRAN'ULATE**, _verb intransitive_ To collect or be formed into grains; as cane-juice granulates into sugar; melted metals _granulate_ when poured into water.
