# Generation

**GENERA'TION**, _noun_ The act of begetting; procreation, as of animals.

**1.** Production; formation; as the _generation_ of sounds or of curves or equations.

**2.** A single succession in natural descent, as the children of the same parents; hence, an age. Thus we say, the third, the fourth, or the tenth _generation_ [[Genesis 15#16|Genesis 15:16]].

**3.** The people of the same period, or living at the same time.

O faithless and perverse _generation_ [[Luke 9#41|Luke 9:41]].

**4.** Genealogy; a series of children or descendants from the same stock.

This is the book of the generations of Adam. [[Genesis 5#1|Genesis 5:1]].

**5.** A family; a race.

**6.** Progeny; offspring.
