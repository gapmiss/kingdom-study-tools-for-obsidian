# Geld

**GELD**, _noun_ Money; tribute; compensation. This word is obsolete in English, but it occurs in old laws and law books in composition; as in Danegeld, or Danegelt, a tax imposed by the Danes; Weregeld, compensation for the life of a man, etc.

**GELD**. _verb transitive_ _preterit tense_ gelded or gelt; _participle passive_ gelded or gelt.

**1.** To castrate; to emasculate.

**2.** To deprive of any essential part.

**3.** To deprive of any thing immodest or exceptionable.
