# Gibbous

**GIB'BOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin gibbus; Gr. to bend.\]

**1.** Swelling; protuberant; convex. The moon is _gibbous_ between the quarters and the full moon; the enlightened part being then convex.

The bones will rise, and make a _gibbous_ member.

**2.** Hunched; hump-backed; crook-backed.
