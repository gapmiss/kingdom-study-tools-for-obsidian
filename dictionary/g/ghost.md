# Ghost

**GHOST**, _noun_ \[See [[Ghastly]].\]

**1.** Spirit; the soul of man.

In this sense seldom used. But hence,

**2.** The soul of a deceased person; the soul or spirit separate from the body; an apparition.

The mighty ghosts of our great Harrys rose.

To give up the _ghost_ is to die; to yield up the breath or spirit; to expire.

The Holy _ghost_ is the third person in the adorable Trinity.

**GHOST**, _verb intransitive_ To die; to expire.

**GHOST**, _verb transitive_ To haunt with an apparition.
