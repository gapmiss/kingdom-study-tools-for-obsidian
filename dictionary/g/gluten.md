# Gluten

**GLUTEN**, _noun_ \[Latin See [[Glue]].\] A tough elastic substance, of a grayish color, which becomes brown and brittle by drying; found in the flour of wheat and other grain. It contributes much to the nutritive quality of flour, and gives tenacity to its paste. A similar substance is found in the juices of certain plants.

**1.** That part of the blood which gives firmness to its texture.
