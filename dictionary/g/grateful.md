# Grateful

**GRA'TEFUL**, _adjective_ \[from Latin gratus. See [[Grace]].\]

**1.** Having a due sense of benefits; kindly disposed towards one from whom a favor has been received; willing to acknowledge and repay benefits; as a _grateful_ heart.

**2.** Agreeable; pleasing; acceptable; gratifying; as a _grateful_ present; a _grateful_ offering.

**3.** Pleasing to the taste; delicious; affording pleasure; as food or drink _grateful_ offering.

Now golden fruits on loaded branches shine,

And _grateful_ clusters swell with floods of wine.
