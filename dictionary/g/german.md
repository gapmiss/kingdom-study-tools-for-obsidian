# German

**GER'MAN**, _adjective_ \[Latin germanus, a brother.\]

**1.** Cousins _german_ are the sons or daughters of brothers or sisters; first cousins.

**2.** Related.

**GER'MAN**, _adjective_ Belonging to Germany.

**GER'MAN**, _noun_ A native of Germany; and by ellipsis, the _german_ language.
