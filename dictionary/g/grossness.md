# Grossness

**GROSSNESS**, _noun_ Thickness; bulkiness; corpulence; fatness; applied to animal bodies.

**1.** Thickness; spissitude; density; as the _grossness_ of vapors.

**2.** Coarseness; rudeness; want of refinement or delicacy; vulgarity; as the _grossness_ of language; the _grossness_ of wit.

Abhor the swinish _grossness_ that delights to wound the ear of delicacy.

**3.** Greatness; enormity; as the _grossness_ of vice.
