# Galenic

**GALEN'IC**

**GALEN'IC**AL, _adjective_ Pertaining to or containing galena.

**1.** \[from Galen, the physician.\] Relating to Galen or his principles and method of treating diseases. The _galenic_ remedies consist of preparations of herbs and roots, by infusion, decoction, etc. The chimical remedies consist of preparations by means of calcination, digestion, fermentation, etc.
