# Gargle

**G'ARGLE**, _verb transitive_ \[Eng. to gurgle.\]

**1.** To wash the throat and mouth with a liquid preparation, which is kept from descending into the stomach by a gentle expiration of air.

**2.** To warble; to play in the throat. \[Unusual.\]

**G'ARBLE**, _noun_ Any liquid preparation for washing the mouth and throat.
