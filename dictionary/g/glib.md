# Glib

**GLIB**, _adjective_ \[Latin glaber, smooth; labor, to slide. This word contains the elements of slip. Qu. Latin glubo.\]

**1.** Smooth; slippery; admitting a body to slide easily on the surface; as, ice is _glib_

**2.** Smooth; voluble; easily moving; as a _glib_ tongue.

**GLIB**, _noun_ A thick curled bush of hair hanging down over the eyes. \[Not in use.\]

**GLIB**, _verb transitive_ To castrate.

**1.** To make smooth.
