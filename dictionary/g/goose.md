# Goose

**GOOSE**, _noun_ goos. _plural_ geese.

**1.** A well known aquatic fowl of the genus Anas; but the domestic _goose_ lives chiefly on land, and feeds on grass. The soft feathers are used for beds, and the quills for pens. The wild _goose_ is migratory.

**2.** A tailor's smoothing iron, so called from its handle which resembles the neck of a _goose_
