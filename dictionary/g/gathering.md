# Gathering

**GATH'ERING**, _participle present tense_ Collecting; assembling; drawing together; plaiting; wrinkling.

**GATH'ERING**, _noun_ The act of collecting or assembling.

**1.** Collection; a crowd; an assembly.

**2.** Charitable contribution. [[1 Corinthians 16#2|1 Corinthians 16:2]].

**3.** A tumor suppurated or maturated; a collection of pus; an abscess.
