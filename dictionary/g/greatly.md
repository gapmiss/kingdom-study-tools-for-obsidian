# Greatly

**GREATLY**, _adverb_ In a great degree; much.

I will _greatly_ multiply thy sorrow. [[Genesis 3#16|Genesis 3:16]].

**1.** Nobly; illustriously.

By a high fate, thou _greatly_ didst expire.

**2.** Magnanimously; generously; bravely.

He _greatly_ scorned to turn his back on his foe. He _greatly_ spurned the offered boon.
