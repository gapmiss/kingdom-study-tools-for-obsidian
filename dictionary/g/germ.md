# Germ

**GERM**, _noun_ \[Latin germen.\] In botany, the ovary or seed-bud of a plant, the rudiment of fruit yet in embryo. It is the base or lower part of the pistil, which, in the progress of vegetation, swells and becomes the seed-vessel.

**1.** Origin; first principle; that from which any thing springs; as the _germ_ of civil liberty, or of prosperity.
