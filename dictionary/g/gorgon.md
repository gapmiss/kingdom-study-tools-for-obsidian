# Gorgon

**GORG'ON**, _noun_ \[Gr.\] A fabled monster of terrific aspect, the sight of which turned the beholder to stone. The poets represent the Gorgons as three sisters, Stheno, Euryale and Medusa; but authors are not agreed in the description of them.

**1.** Any thing very ugly or horrid.

**GORG'ON**, _adjective_ Like a gorgon; very ugly or terrific; as a _gorgon_ face.
