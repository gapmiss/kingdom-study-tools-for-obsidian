# Generator

**GEN'ERATOR**, _noun_ He or that which begets, causes or produces.

**1.** In music, the principal sound or sounds by which others are produced. Thus the lowest C for the treble of the harpsichord, besides its octave, will strike an attentive ear with its twelfth above, or G in alt., and with its seventeenth above, or E in alt. Hence C is called their _generator_ the G and E its products or harmonics.

**2.** A vessel in which steam is generated.
