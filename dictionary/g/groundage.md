# Groundage

**GROUND'AGE**, _noun_ A tax paid by a ship for standing in port.

**GROUND'-ANGLING**, _noun_ Fishing without a float, with a bullet placed a few inches from the hook.

**GROUND'-ASH**, _noun_ A sapling of ash; a young shoot from the stump of an ash.

**GROUND'-BAIT**,_noun_ Bait for fish which sinks to the bottom of the water.

**GROUND'-FLOOR**, _noun_ The first or lower floor of a house. But the English call the second floor from the ground the first floor.
