# Genuine

**GEN'UINE**, _adjective_ \[Latin genuinus, from enus, or its root. See [[Gender]].\]

Native; belonging to the original stock; hence, real; natural; true; pure; not spurious, false or adulterated. The Gaels are supposed to be _genuine_ descendants of the Celts. Vices and crimes are the _genuine_ effects of depravity, as virtue and piety are the _genuine_ fruits of holiness. It is supposed we have the _genuine_ text of Homer.
