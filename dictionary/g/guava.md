# Guava

**GU'AVA**, _noun_ An American tree, and its fruit, of the genus Psidium. It is of two species, or rather varieties, the pyriferum or white _guava_ and pomiferum or red _guava_ The fruit or berry is large and oval-shaped, like a pomegranate, which it resembles in its astringent quality. The pulp is of an agreeable flavor, and of this fruit is made a delicious jelly.
