# Groundly

**GROUND'LY**, _adverb_ Upon principles; solidly. \[A bad word and not used.\]

**GROUND'-NUT**, _noun_ A plant, the Arachis, a native of South America.

**GROUND'-OAK**, _noun_ A sapling of oak.

**GROUND'-PINE**,_noun_ A plant, a species of Teucrium or germander; said to be so called from its resinous smell.

**GROUND'-PLATE**, _noun_ In architecture, the ground-plates are the outermost pieces of timber lying on or near the ground, framed into one another with mortises and tenons.
