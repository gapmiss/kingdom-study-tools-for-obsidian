# Gnash

**GNASH**, _verb transitive_ nash. To strike the teeth together, as in anger or pain; as, to _gnash_ the teeth in rage.

**GNASH**, _verb intransitive_ nash. To grind the teeth.

He shall _gnash_ with his teeth and melt away. [[Psalms 112#10|Psalms 112:10]].

**1.** To rage even to collision with the teeth; to growl.

They gnashed on me with their teeth. [[Psalms 35#16|Psalms 35:16]].
