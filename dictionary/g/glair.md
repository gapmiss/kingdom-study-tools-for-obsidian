# Glair

**GLAIR**, _noun_ \[Eng. clear, Latin clarus, and with Eng. glare, and Latin gloria; perhaps with Latin glarea, gravel, or pieces of quartz.\]

**1.** The white of an egg. It is used as a varnish for preserving paintings.

**2.** Any viscous transparent substance, resembling the white of an egg.

**3.** A kind of halbert.

**GLAIR**, _verb transitive_ To smear with the white of an egg; to varnish.
