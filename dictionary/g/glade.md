# Glade

**GLADE**, _noun_ An opening or passage made through a wood by lopping off the branches of the trees. Locally, in the U. States, a natural opening or open place in a forest.

There interspersed in lawns and opening glades.

**1.** In New England, an opening in the ice of rivers or lakes, or a place left unfrozen.

**GLADE**, _noun_ Smooth ice.
