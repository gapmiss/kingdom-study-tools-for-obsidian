# Garter

**G'ARTER**, _noun_

**1.** A string or band used to tie a stocking to the leg.

**2.** The badge of an order of knighthood in Great Britain, called the order of the _garter_ instituted by Edward III. This order is a college or corporation.

**3.** The principal king at arms.

**4.** The term in heraldry, signifying the half of a bend.

**G'ARTER**, _verb transitive_ To bind with a _garter_

**1.** To invest with the order of the _garter_
