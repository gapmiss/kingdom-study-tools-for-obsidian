# Guile

**GUILE**, _noun_ gile. Craft; cunning; artifice; duplicity; deceit; usually in a bad sense.

We may, with more successful hope, resolve

To wage by force or _guile_ eternal war.

Behold an Israelite indeed, in whom is no _guile_ [[John 1#47|John 1:47]].

**GUILE**, _verb transitive_ To disguise craftily.
