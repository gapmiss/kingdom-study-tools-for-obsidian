# Glutton

**GLUT'TON**, _noun_ glut'n. \[Low Latin gluto.\] One who indulges to excess in eating.

**1.** One eager of any thing to excess.

Gluttons in murder, wanton to destroy.

**2.** In zoology, an animal of the genus Ursus, found in the _noun_ of Europe and Siberia. It grows to the length of three feet, but has short legs and moves slowly. It is a carnivorous animal, and in order to catch its prey, it climbs a tree and from that darts down upon a deer or other animal. It is names from its voracious appetite.
