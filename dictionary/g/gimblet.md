# Gimblet

**GIMB'LET**, _noun_ A borer; small instrument with a pointed screw at the end, for boring holes in wood by turning. It is applied only to small instruments; a large instrument of the like kind is called an auger.

**GIMB'LET**, _verb transitive_ In seamen's language, to turn round an anchor by the stock; a motion resembling that of the turning of a _gimblet_
