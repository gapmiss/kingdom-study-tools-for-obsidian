# Gratify

**GRAT'IFY**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin gratificor; gratus, agreeable, and facio, to make.\]

**1.** To please; to give pleasure to; to indulge; as, to _gratify_ the taste, the appetite, the senses, the desires, the mind, etc.

**2.** To delight; to please; to humor; to soothe; to satisfy; to indulge to satisfaction.

For who would die to _gratify_ a foe?

**3.** To requite; to recompense.
