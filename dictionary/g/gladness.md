# Gladness

**GLAD'NESS**, _noun_ \[See [[Glad]].\] Joy, or a moderate degree of joy and exhilaration; pleasure of mind; cheerfulness.

They--did eat their meat with _gladness_ and singleness of heart. [[Acts 2#46|Acts 2:46]].

\[Gladness is rarely or never equivalent to mirth, merriment, gayety and triumph, and it usually expresses less than delight. It sometimes expresses great joy. [[Esther 8#16|Esther 8:16]].\]
