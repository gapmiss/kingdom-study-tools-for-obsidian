# Gown

**GOWN**, _noun_

**1.** A woman; s upper garment.

**2.** A long loose upper garment or robe, worn by professional men, as divines, lawyers, students, etc., who are called men of the _gown_ or gownmen. It is made of any kind of cloth worn over ordinary clothes, and hangs down to the ankles or nearly so.

**4.** The dress of peace, or the civil magistracy; cedant arma togoe.

He Mars deposed, and arms to gowns made yield.
