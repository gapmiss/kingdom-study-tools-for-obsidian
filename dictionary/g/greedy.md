# Greedy

**GREE'DY**, _adjective_ \[Latin gradior, and probably signifies reaching forward.\]

**1.** Having a keen appetite for food or drink; ravenous; voracious; very hungry; followed by of; as a lion that is _greedy_ of his prey. [[Psalms 17#12|Psalms 17:12]].

**2.** Having a keen desire of any thing; eager to obtain; as _greedy_ of gain.
