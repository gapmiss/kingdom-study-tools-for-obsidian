# Grope

**GROPE**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To feel along; to search or attempt to find in the dark, or as a blind person, by feeling.

We _grope_ for the wall like the blind. [[Isaiah 59#10|Isaiah 59:10]].

The dying believer leaves the weeping children of mortality to _grope_ a little longer among the miseries and sensualities of a worldly life.

**2.** To seek blindly in intellectual darkness, without a certain guide or means of knowledge.

**GROPE**, _verb transitive_ To search by feeling in the dark.

We groped our way at midnight.

But Strephon, cautious, never meant

The bottom of the pan to _grope_
