# Grub

**GRUB**, _verb intransitive_ To dig; to be occupied in digging.

**GRUB**, _verb transitive_ To dig; mostly followed by up. To _grub_ up, is to dig up by the roots with an instrument; to root out by digging, or throwing out the soil; as, to _grub_ up trees, rushes or sedge.

**GRUB**, _noun_ \[from the Verb.\] A small worm; particularly, a hexapod or six-footed worm, produced from the egg of the beetle, which is transformed into a winged insect.

**1.** A short thick man; a dwarf, in contempt.
