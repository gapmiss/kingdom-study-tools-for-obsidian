# Georgic

**GEOR'GIC**, _noun_ \[Gr. rustic; and labor.\] A rural poem; a poetical composition on the subject of husbandry, containing rules for cultivating lands, in a poetical dress; as the Georgics of Virgil.

**GEOR'GIC**, _adjective_ Relating to the doctrine of agriculture and rural affairs.

**GEORGIUM SIDUS**. \[See [[Herschel]].\]
