# Guardian

**GUARD'IAN**, _noun_

**1.** A warden; one who guards, preserves or secures; one to whom any thing is committed for preservation from injury.

**2.** In law, one who is chosen or appointed to take charge of the estate and education of an orphan who is a minor, or of any person who is not of sufficient discretion to manage his own concerns. The person committed to the care of a _guardian_ is called his ward.

Guardian of the spiritualities, the person to whom the spiritual jurisdiction of a diocese is entrusted, during the vacancy of the see.

**GUARD'IAN**, _adjective_ Protection; performing the office of a protector; as a _guardian_ angel; _guardian_ care.
