# Absolutely

**AB'SOLUTELY**, _adverb_

**1.** Completely, wholly, as a thing is _absolutely_ unintelligible.

**2.** Without dependence or relation; in a state unconnected

Absolutely we cannot discommend, we cannot _absolutely_ approve, either willingness to live, or forwardness to die.

**3.** Without restriction or limitation; as God reigns _absolutely_

**4.** Without condition, as God does not forgive _absolutely_ but upon condition of faith and repentance.

**5.** Positively, peremptorily, as command me _absolutely_ not to go.
