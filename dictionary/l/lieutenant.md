# Lieutenant

**LIEUTENANT**, _noun_ luten'ant. \[Latin tenens, holding.\]

**1.** An officer who supplies the place of a superior in his absence. Officers of this kind are civil, as the lord-lieutenant of a kingdom or county; or military, as a _lieutenant_ general, a _lieutenant_ colonel.

**2.** In military affairs, the second commissioned officer in a company of infantry cavalry or artillery.

**3.** In ships of war, the officer next in rank to the captain.
