# Ligulate

**LIG'ULATE**,

**LIG'ULATE**D, _adjective_ \[Latin ligula, a strap.\] Like a bandage or strap; as a _ligulate_ flower, a species of compound flower, the florets of which have their corollets flat, spreading out towards the end, with the base only tubular. This is the semi-floscular flower of Tournefort.
