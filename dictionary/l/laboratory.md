# Laboratory

**LAB'ORATORY**, _noun_

**1.** A house or place where operations and experiments in chemistry, pharmacy, pyrotechny, etc., are performed.

**2.** A place where arms are manufactured or repaired, or fire-works prepared; as the _laboratory_ in Springfield, in Massachusetts.

**3.** A place where work is performed, or any thing is prepared for use. Hence the stomach is called the grand _laboratory_ of the human body; the liver, the _laboratory_ of the bile.
