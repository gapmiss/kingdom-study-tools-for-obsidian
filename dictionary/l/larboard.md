# Larboard

**L'ARBOARD**, _noun_ \[Board, bord, is a side; but I know the meaning of lar. The Dutch use bakboard, and the Germans backbord.\]

The left hand side of a ship, when a person stands with his face to the head; opposed to starboard.

**L'ARBOARD**, _adjective_ Pertaining to the left hand side of a ship; as the _larboard_ quarter.
