# Licentious

**LICEN'TIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin licentiosus.\]

**1.** Using license; indulging freedom to excess; unrestrained by law or morality; loose; dissolute; as a _licentious_ man.

**2.** Exceeding the limits of law or propriety; wanton; unrestrained; as _licentious_ desires. _licentious_ thoughts precede _licentious_ conduct.
