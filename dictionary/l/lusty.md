# Lusty

**LUST'Y**, _adjective_ \[from lust.\]

**1.** Stout; vigorous; robust; healthful; able of body. this is the correct sense of the word, comprehending full health and strength; as a _lusty_ youth. But it is now used in the sense of,

**2.** Bulky; large; of great size. This sense does not always include that of vigor.

**3.** Handsome; pleasant; saucy. _obsolete_

**4.** Copious; plentiful; as a _lusty_ draught.

**5.** Pregnant; a colloquial use.
