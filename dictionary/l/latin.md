# Latin

**LAT'IN**, _adjective_ Pertaining to the Latins, a people of Latium, in Italy; Roman; as the _latin_ language.

Latin church, the western church; the christian church in Italy, France, Spain and other countries where the _latin_ language was introduced, as distinct from the Greek or eastern church.

**LAT'IN**, _noun_

**1.** The language of the ancient Romans.

**2.** An exercise in schools, consisting in turning English into _latin_
