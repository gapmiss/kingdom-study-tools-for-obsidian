# Lameness

**LA'MENESS**, _noun_

**1.** An impaired state of the body or limbs; loss of natural soundness and strength by a wound or by disease; particularly applied to the limbs, and implying a total or partial inability; as the _lameness_ of the leg or arm.

**2.** Imperfection; weakness; as the _lameness_ of an argument or of a description.
