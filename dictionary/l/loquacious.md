# Loquacious

**LOQUA'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin loquax, from loquor, to speak. Eng. to clack.\]

**1.** Talkative; given to continual talking.

Loquacious, brawling ever in the wrong.

**2.** Speaking; noisy.

Bling British bards, with volant touch, traverse _loquacious_ strings.

**3.** Apt to blab and disclose secrets.
