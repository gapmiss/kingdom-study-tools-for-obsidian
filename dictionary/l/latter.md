# Latter

**LAT'TER**, _adjective_ \[an irregular comparative of late.\]

**1.** Coming or happening after something else; opposed to former; as the former and _latter_ rain; former or _latter_ harvest.

**2.** Mentioned the last of two.

The difference between reason and revelation - and in what sense the _latter_ is superior.

**3.** Modern; lately done or past; as in these _latter_ ages.
