# Lackey

**LACK'EY**, _noun_ \[Latin lego, to send.\]

An attending servant; a footboy or footman.

**LACK'EY**, _verb transitive_ To attend servilely.

**LACK'EY**, _verb intransitive_ To act as footboy; to pay servile attendance.

Oft have I servants seen on horses ride, the free and noble _lackey_ by their side.
