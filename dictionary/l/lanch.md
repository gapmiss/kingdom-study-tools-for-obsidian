# Lanch

**L'ANCH**, _verb transitive_ \[from lance.\]

**1.** To throw, as a lance; to dart; to let fly.

See whose arm can _lanch_ the surer bolt.

**2.** To move, or cause to slide from the land into the water; as, to _lanch_ a ship.

**L'ANCH**, _verb intransitive_ To dart or fly off; to push off; as, to _lanch_ into the wide world; to _lanch_ into a wide field of discussion.

**L'ANCH**, _noun_

**1.** The sliding or movement of a ship from the land into the water, on ways prepared for the purpose.

**2.** A kind of boat, longer, lower, and more flat-bottomed than a long boat.
