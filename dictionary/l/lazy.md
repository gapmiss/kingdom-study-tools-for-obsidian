# Lazy

**LA'ZY**, _adjective_ \[Latin laxus, and it is doubtful whether this is of the same family.\]

**1.** Disinclined to action or exertion; naturally or habitually slothful; sluggish; indolent; averse to labor; heavy in motion.

Wicked men will ever live like rogues, and not fall to work, but be _lazy_ and spend victuals.

**2.** Slow; moving slowly or apparently with labor; as a _lazy_ stream.

The night-owl's _lazy_ flight.
