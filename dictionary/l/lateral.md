# Lateral

**LAT'ERAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin lateralis, from latus, a side, and broad, Gr., Eng. flat. The primary sense of these words is to extend, as in late, let.\]

**1.** Pertaining to the side; as the _lateral_ view of an object.

**2.** Proceeding from the side; as the _lateral_ branches of a tree; _lateral_ shoots.
