# Labiate

**LA'BIATE**,

**LA'BIATE**D, _adjective_ \[from Latin labium, lip.\] In botany, a _labiate_ corol is irregular, monopetalous, with two lips, or monopetalous, consisting of a narrow tube with a wide mouth, divided into two or more segments arranged in two opposite divisions or lips. a _labiate_ flower has a _labiate_ corol.
