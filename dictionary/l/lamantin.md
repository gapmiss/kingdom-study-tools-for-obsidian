# Lamantin

**LAM'ANTIN**,

LAMB, _noun_ lam.

**1.** The young of the sheep kind.

**2.** The Lamb of God, in Scripture, the Savior Jesus Christ, who was typified by the paschal lamb.

Behold the lamb of God, who taketh away the sin of the world. [[John 1#1|John 1:1]].

**LAMB**, _verb transitive_ To bring forth young, as sheep.
