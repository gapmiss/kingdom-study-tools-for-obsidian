# Lascivious

**LASCIV'IOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin lascivus, from laxus, laxo, to relax, to loosen.\]

**1.** Loose; wanton; lewd; lustful; as _lascivious_ men; _lascivious_ desires; _lascivious_ eyes.

**2.** Soft; wanton; luxurious.

He capers numbly in a lady's chamber, to the _lascivious_ pleasing of a lute.
