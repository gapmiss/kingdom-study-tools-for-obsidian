# Loosen

**LOOS'EN**, _verb transitive_ loos'n. \[from loose.\]

**1.** To free from tightness, tension, firmness or fixedness; as, to _loosen_ a string when tied, or a knot; to _loosen_ a joint; to _loosen_ a rock in the earth.

**2.** To render less dense or compact; as, to _loosen_ the earth about the roots of a tree.

**3.** To free from restraint.

It loosens his hands and assists his understanding.

**4.** To remove costiveness from; to facilitate or increase alvine discharges.

Fear looseneth the belly.

**LOOS'EN**, _verb intransitive_ To become loose; to become less tight, firm or compact.
