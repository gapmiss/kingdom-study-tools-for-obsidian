# Lucid

**LU'CID**, _adjective_ \[Latin lucidus, from luceo, to shine. See [[Light]].\]

**1.** Shining; bright; resplendent; as the _lucid_ orbs of heaven.

**2.** Clear; transparent; pellucid; as a _lucid_ stream.

**3.** Bright with the radiance of intellect; not darkened or confused by delirium or madness; marked by the regular operations of reason; as the _lucid_ intervals of a deranged man.

**4.** Clear; distinct; presenting a clear view; easily understood; as a _lucid_ order or arrangement.
