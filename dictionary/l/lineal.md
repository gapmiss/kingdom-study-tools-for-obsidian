# Lineal

**LIN'EAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin linealis, from linea, line.\]

**1.** Composed of lines; delineated; as _lineal_ designs.

**2.** In a direct line from an ancestor; as _lineal_ descent; _lineal_ succession.

**3.** Hereditary; derived from ancestors.

**4.** Allied by direct descent.

For only you are _lineal_ to the throne.

**5.** In the direction of a line; as _lineal_ measure.

Lineal measure, the measure of length.
