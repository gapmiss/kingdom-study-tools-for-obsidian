# Lothsome

**LO'THSOME**, _adjective_

**1.** Causing an extreme aversion of appetite; exciting fastidiousness. [[Numbers 11#1|Numbers 11:1]].

**2.** Exciting extreme disgust; offensive; as a _lothsome_ disease. [[Psalms 38#1|Psalms 38:1]].

**3.** Odious; exciting hatred or abhorrence; detestable; as _lothsome_ sloth.
