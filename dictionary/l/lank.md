# Lank

**LANK**, _adjective_ \[Gr. probably allied to flank.\]

**1.** Loose or lax and easily yielding to pressure; not distended; not stiff or firm by distension; not plump; as a _lank_ bladder or purse.

The clergy's bags are _lank_ and lean with thy extortions.

**2.** Thin; slender; meager; not full and firm; as a _lank_ body.

**3.** Languid; drooping. \[See [[Languish]].\]
