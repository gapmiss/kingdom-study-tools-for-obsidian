# Luster

**LUS'TER**, _noun_ \[Latin lustrum, lustro to purify.\]

**1.** Brightness; splendor; gloss; as the _luster_ of the sun or stars; the _luster_ of silk.

The sun's mild _luster_ warms the vital air.

**2.** The splendor of birth, of deeds or of fame; renown; distinction.

His ancestors continued about four hundred years, rather without obscurity than with any great share of _luster_

**3.** A sconce with lights; a branched candlestick of glass.

**4.** The space of five years. \[Latin lustrum.\]
