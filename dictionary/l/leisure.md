# Leisure

**LEISURE**, _noun_ lezh'ur or lee'zhur.

**1.** Freedom from occupation or business; vacant time; time free from employment.

The desire of _leisure_ is much more natural than of business and care.

I shall leave with him that rebuke to be considered at his _leisure_

**2.** Convenience of time.

He sigh'd, and had no _leisure_ more to say. \[Not used.\]
