# Lamely

**LA'MELY**, _adverb_ \[See [[Lame]].\]

**1.** Like a cripple; with impaired strength; in a halting manner; as, to walk _lamely_

**2.** Imperfectly; without a complete exhibition of parts; as a figure _lamely_ drawn; a scene _lamely_ described.

**3.** Weakly; poorly; unsteadily; feebly.
