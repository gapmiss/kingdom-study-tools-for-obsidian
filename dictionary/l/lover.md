# Lover

**LOV'ER**, _noun_

**1.** One who loves; one who has a tender affection, particularly for a female.

Love is blind, and lovers cannot see -

**2.** A friend; one who regards with kindness.

Your brother and his _lover_ have embraced.

**3.** One who likes or is pleased with any thing; as a _lover_ of books or of science; a _lover_ of wine; a _lover_ of religion.

Lover and loover. \[See Louver.\]
