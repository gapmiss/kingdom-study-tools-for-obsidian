# Loyal

**LOY'AL**, _adjective_ \[Latin lex, law.\]

Faithful to a prince or superior; true to plighted faith, duty or love; not treacherous; used of subjects to their prince, and of husband, wife and lovers; as a _loyal_ subject; a _loyal_ wife.

There Laodamia with Evadne moves, unhappy both! but _loyal_ in their loves.
