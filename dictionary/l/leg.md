# Leg

**LEG**, _noun_

**1.** The limb of an animal, used in supporting the body and in walking and running; properly, that part of the limb from the knee to the foot, but in a more general sense, the whole limb, including the thigh, the _leg_ and the foot.

**2.** The long or slender support of any thing; as the _leg_ of a table.

To make a _leg_ to bow; a phrase introduced probably by the practice of drawing the right _leg_ backward. \[Little used.\]

To stand on one's own legs, to support one's self; to trust to one's own strength or efforts without aid.
