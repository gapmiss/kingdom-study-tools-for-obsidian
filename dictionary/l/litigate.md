# Litigate

**LIT'IGATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin litigo, from lis, litis, a contest or debate.\]

To contest in law; to prosecute or defend by pleadings, exhibition of evidence, and judicial debate; as, to _litigate_ a cause or a question.

**LIT'IGATE**, _verb intransitive_ To dispute in law; to carry on a suit by judicial process.
