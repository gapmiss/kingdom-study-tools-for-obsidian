# Loftily

**LOFT'ILY**, _adverb_ \[from lofty.\]

**1.** On high; in an elevated place.

**2.** Proudly; haughtily.

They are corrupt and speak wickedly concerning oppression; they speak _loftily_ [[Psalms 73#8|Psalms 73:8]].

**3.** With elevation of language, diction or sentiment; sublimely.

My lowly verse may _loftily_ arise.

**4.** In an elevated attitude. A horse carries his head _loftily_
