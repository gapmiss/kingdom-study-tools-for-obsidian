# Liquor

**LIQ'UOR**, _noun_ lik'or \[Latin _liquor_ \]

A liquid or fluid substance. \[See [[Liquid]].\] _liquor_ is a word of general signification, extending to water, milk, blood, say, juice, etc.; but its most common application is to spirituous fluids, whether distilled or fermented, to decoctions, solutions, tinctures.

**LIQ'UOR**, _verb transitive_ To moisten; to drench. \[Little used.\]
