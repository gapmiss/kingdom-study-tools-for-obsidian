# Liveliness

**LI'VELINESS**, _noun_ \[from lively.\]

**1.** The quality or state of being lively or animated; sprightliness; vivacity; animation; spirit; as the _liveliness_ of youth, contrasted with the gravity of age.

**2.** An appearance of life, animation or spirit; as the _liveliness_ of the eye or countenance in a portrait.

**3.** Briskness; activity; effervescence, as of liquors.
