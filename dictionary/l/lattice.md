# Lattice

**LAT'TICE**, _noun_

Any work of wood or iron, made by crossing laths, rods or bars, and forming open squares like net-work; as the _lattice_ of a window.

The mother of Sisera looked out at a window, and cried through the _lattice_ [[Judges 5#28|Judges 5:28]].

**LAT'TICE**, _adjective_

**1.** Consisting of cross pieces; as _lattice_ work.

**2.** Furnished with _lattice_ work; as a _lattice_ window.

**LAT'TICE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To form with cross bars, and open work.

**2.** To furnish with a _lattice_
