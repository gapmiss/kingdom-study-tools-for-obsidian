# Linen

**LIN'EN**, _noun_ \[Latin linun, flax, Gr. The sense is probably long, extended or smooth. In the latter sense, it would accord with Latin linio, lenio.\]

**1.** Cloth made of flax or hemp.

**2.** An under garment.

**LIN'EN**, _adjective_ \[Latin lineus.\]

**1.** Made of flax or hemp; as _linen_ cloth; a _linen_ stocking.

**2.** Resembling _linen_ cloth; white; pale.

Fossil-linen, a kind of amianth, with soft, parallel, flexible fibers.
