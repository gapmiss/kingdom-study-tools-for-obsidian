# Lame

**LAME**, _adjective_

**1.** Crippled or disabled in a limb, or otherwise injured so as to be unsound and impaired in strength; as a _lame_ arm or leg, or a person _lame_ in one leg.

**2.** Imperfect; not satisfactory; as a _lame_ excuse.

**3.** Hobbling; not smooth; as numbers in verse.

**LAME**, _verb transitive_ To make lame; to cripple or disable; to render imperfect and unsound; as, to _lame_ an arm or a leg.
