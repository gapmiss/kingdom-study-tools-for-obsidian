# Luminary

**LU'MINARY**, _noun_ \[Latin luminare, from lumen, light.\]

**1.** Any body that gives light, but chiefly one of the celestial orbs. The sun is the principal _luminary_ in our system. the stars are inferior luminaries.

**2.** One that illustrates any subject, or enlightens mankind; as, Bacon and Newton were distinguished luminaries.
