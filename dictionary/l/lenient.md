# Lenient

**LE'NIENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin leniens, from lenio, lenis, soft, mild.

**1.** Softening; mitigating; assuasive.

Time, that on all things lays his _lenient_ hand, yet tames not this.

Sometimes with of; as _lenient_ of grief.

**2.** Laxative; emollient.

Oils relax the fibers, are _lenient_ balsamic.

**LE'NIENT**, _noun_ That which softens or assuages; an emollient.
