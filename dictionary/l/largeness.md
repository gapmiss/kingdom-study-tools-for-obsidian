# Largeness

**L'ARGENESS**, _noun_

**1.** Bigness; bulk; magnitude; as the _largeness_ of an animal.

**2.** Greatness; comprehension; as the _largeness_ of mind or of capacity.

**3.** Extent; extensiveness; as _largeness_ of views.

**4.** Extension; amplitude; liberality; as the _largeness_ of an offer; _largeness_ of heart.

**5.** Wideness; extent; as the _largeness_ of a river.
