# Legislative

**LEG'ISLATIVE**, _adjective_

**1.** Giving or enacting laws; as a _legislative_ body.

**2.** Capable of enacting laws; as _legislative_ power.

**3.** Pertaining to the enacting of laws; suitable to laws; as the _legislative_ style.

**4.** Done by enacting; as a _legislative_ act.

\[Note. In this word, and in legislator, legislatrix, legislature, the accent is nearly equal on the first and third syllables, and a, in the third, has its first or long sound.\]
