# Legitimate

**LEGIT'IMATE**, _adjective_ \[Latin legitimus, from lex, law.\]

**1.** Lawfully begotten or born; born in wedlock; as _legitimate_ heirs or children.

**2.** Genuine; real; proceeding from a pure source; not false or spurious; as _legitimate_ arguments or inferences.

**LEGIT'IMATE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To make lawful

**2.** To render legitimate; to communicate the rights of a _legitimate_ child to one that is illegitimate; to invest with the rights of a lawful heir.
