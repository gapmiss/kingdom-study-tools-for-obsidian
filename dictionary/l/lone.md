# Lone

**LONE**, _adjective_

**1.** Solitary; retired; unfrequented; having no company.

And leave you in _lone_ woods or empty walls.

**2.** Single; standing by itself; not having others in the neighborhood; as a _lone_ house.

**3.** Single; unmarried, or in widowhood.

**LONE**, _noun_ A lane. \[Local.\]
