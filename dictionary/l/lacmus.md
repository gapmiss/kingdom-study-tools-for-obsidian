# Lacmus

**LAC'MUS**, _noun_ A blue pigment, formed from archil, a species of lichen. \[See [[Archil]].\] It is prepared by bruising the archil, and adding quick lime and putrefied urine, or spirit of urine distilled from lime. The mixture, after cooling and the evaporation of the fluid, becomes a mass of the consistence of paste, which is laid on a board to dry in square lumps.
