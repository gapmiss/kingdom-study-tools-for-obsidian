# Luxurious

**LUXU'RIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin luxuriosus, from luxo, to loosen; luxor, to riot.\]

**1.** Voluptuous; indulging freely or excessively in the pleasures of the table, the gratification of appetite, or in rich and expensive dress and equipage; as a _luxurious_ life; _luxurious_ cities.

**2.** Administering to luxury; contributing to free or extravagant indulgence in diet, dress and equipage; as _luxurious_ wealth.

**3.** Furnished with luxuries; as a _luxurious_ table.

**4.** Softening by pleasure, or free indulgence in luxury; as _luxurious_ ease.

**5.** Lustful; libidinous; given to the gratification of lust; as a _luxurious_ bed.

**6.** Luxuriant; exuberant.
