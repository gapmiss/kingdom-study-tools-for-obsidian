# Leanness

**LE'ANNESS**, _noun_

**1.** Destitution of fat; want of flesh; thinness of body; meagerness; applied to animals.

**2.** Want of matter; poverty; emptiness; as the _leanness_ of a purse.

**3.** In Scripture, want of grace and spiritual comfort.

He sent _leanness_ into their soul. [[Psalms 106#15|Psalms 106:15]].
