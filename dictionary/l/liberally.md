# Liberally

**LIB'ERALLY**, _adverb_

**1.** Bountifully; freely; largely; with munificence.

If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God, who giveth to all men _liberally_ and upbraideth not. [[James 1#5|James 1:5]].

**2.** With generous and impartial regard to other interests than our own; with enlarged views; without selfishness or meanness; as, to think or judge _liberally_ of men and their actions.

**3.** Freely; not strictly; not literally.
