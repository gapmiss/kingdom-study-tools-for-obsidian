# Locality

**LOCAL'ITY**, _noun_

**1.** Existence in a place, or in a certain portion of space.

It is thought that the soul and angels are devoid of quantity and dimension, and that they have nothing to do with grosser _locality_

**2.** Limitation to a county, district or place; as _locality_ of trial.

**3.** Position; situation; place; particularly; geographical place or situation, as of a mineral or plant.
