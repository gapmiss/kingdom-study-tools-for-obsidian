# Lofty

**LOFTY**, _adjective_

**1.** Elevated in place; high; as a _lofty_ tower; a _lofty_ mountain. \[But it expresses more than high, or at least is more emphatical, poetical and elegant.\]

See _lofty_ Lebanon his head advance.

**2.** Elevated in condition or character.

Thus saith the high and _lofty_ One, that inhabiteth eternity, whose name is Holy - [[Isaiah 57#7|Isaiah 57:7]].

**3.** Proud; haughty; as _lofty_ looks. [[Isaiah 2#11|Isaiah 2:11]].

**4.** Elevated in sentiment or diction; sublime; as _lofty_ strains; _lofty_ rhyme.

**5.** Stately; dignified; as _lofty_ steps.
