# Litigious

**LITIG'IOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin litigiosus.\]

**1.** Inclined to judicial contest; given to the practice of contending in law; quarrelsome; contentious; applied to persons. a _litigious_ man is a bad neighbor and a bad citizen.

**2.** Disputable; controvertible; subject to contention; as _litigious_ right.

No fences, parted fields, nor marks nor bounds, distinguish'd acres of _litigious_ grounds.
