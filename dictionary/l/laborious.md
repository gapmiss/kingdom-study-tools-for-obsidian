# Laborious

**LABO'RIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin laboriosus.\]

**1.** Using exertion; employing labor; diligent in work or service; assiduous; used of persons; as a _laborious_ husbandman or mechanic; a _laborious_ minister or pastor.

**2.** Requiring labor; toilsome; tiresome; not easy; as _laborious_ duties or services.

**3.** Requiring labor, exertion, perseverance or sacrifices.

Dost thou love watchings, abstinence or toil, _laborious_ virtues all? Learn these from Cato.
