# Lamentable

**LAM'ENTABLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin lamentabilis.\]

**1.** To be lamented; deserving sorrow; as a _lamentable_ declension of morals.

**2.** Mournful; adapted to awaken grief; as a _lamentable_ tune.

**3.** Expressing sorrow; as _lamentable_ cries.

**4.** Miserable; pitiful; low; poor; in a sense rather ludicrous. \[Little used.\]
