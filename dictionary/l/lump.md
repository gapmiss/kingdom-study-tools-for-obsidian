# Lump

**LUMP**, _noun_

**1.** A small mass of matter of no definite shape; as a _lump_ of earth; a _lump_ of butter; a _lump_ of sugar.

**2.** A mass of things blended or thrown together without order or distinction; as copper, iron, gold, silver, lead, tin, promiscuously in one _lump_

**3.** A cluster; as a _lump_ of figs. [[2 Kings 20#7|2 Kings 20:7]].

In the _lump_ the whole together; in gross.

They may buy my papers in the _lump_

**LUMP**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To throw into a mass; to unite in a body or sum without distinction of particulars.

The expenses ought to be lumped.

**2.** To take in the gross.
