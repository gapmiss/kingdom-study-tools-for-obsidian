# Legible

**LEG'IBLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin legibilis, from lego, to read.\]

**1.** That may be read; consisting of letters or figures that may be distinguished by the eye; as a fair, _legible_ manuscript.

**2.** That may be discovered or understood by apparent marks or indications. The thoughts of men are often _legible_ in their countenances.
