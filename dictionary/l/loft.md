# Loft

**LOFT**, _noun_

**1.** Properly, an elevation; hence, in a building, the elevation of one story or floor above another; hence, a floor above another; as the second loft; third loft; fourth _loft_ Spenser seems to have used the word for the highest floor or top, and this may have been its original signification.

**2.** A high room or place.
