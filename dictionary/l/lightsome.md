# Lightsome

**LIGHTSOME**, _adjective_ li'tesome.

**1.** Luminous; not dark; not obscure.

White walls make rooms more _lightsome_ than black. \[Little used.\]

The _lightsome_ realms of love.

\[In the latter passage, the word is elegant.\]

**2.** Gay; airy; cheering; exhilarating.

That _lightsome_ affection of joy.
