# Learning

**LEARNING**, _participle present tense_ lern'ing. Gaining knowledge by instruction or reading, by study, by experience or observation; acquiring skill by practice.

**LEARNING**, _noun_ lern'ing.

**1.** The knowledge of principles or facts received by instruction or study; acquired knowledge or ideas in any branch of science or literature; erudition; literature; science. The Scaligers were men of great _learning_

\[This is the proper sense of the word.\]

**2.** Knowledge acquired by experience, experiment or observation.

**3.** Skill in anything good or bad.
