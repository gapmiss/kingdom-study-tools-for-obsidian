# Looseness

**LOOSENESS**, _noun_ loos'ness.

**1.** The state of being loose or relaxed; a state opposite to that of being tight, fast, fixed or compact; as the _looseness_ of a cord; the _looseness_ of a robe; the _looseness_ of the skin; the _looseness_ of earth, or of the texture of cloth.

**2.** The state opposite to rigor or rigidness; laxity; levity; as _looseness_ of morals or of principles.

**3.** Irregularity; habitual deviation from strict rules; as _looseness_ of life.

**4.** Habitual lewdness; unchastity.

**5.** Flux from the bowels; diarrhaea.
