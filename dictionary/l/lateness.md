# Lateness

**LA'TENESS**, _noun_

**1.** The state of being tardy, or of coming after the usual time; as the _lateness_ of spring or of harvest.

**2.** Time far advanced in any particular period; as _lateness_ of the day or night; _lateness_ in the season; _lateness_ in life.

**3.** The state of being out of time, or after the appointed time; as the _lateness_ of one's arrival.
