# Lowly

**LOWLY**, _adjective_ \[low and like.\]

**1.** Having a low esteem of one's own worth; humble; meek; free from pride.

Take my yoke upon you and learn of me, for I am meek and lovly in heart. [[Matthew 11#29|Matthew 11:29]].

He scorneth the scorners; but he giveth grace to the _lowly_ [[Proverbs 3#34|Proverbs 3:34]].

**2.** Mean; low; wanting dignity or rank.

One common right the great and _lowly_ claim.

**3.** Not lofty or sublime; humble.

these rural poems, and their _lowly_ strain.

**4.** Not high; not elevated in place.

**LOWLY**, _adverb_

**1.** Humbly; meekly; modestly.

Belowly wise.

**2.** Meanly; in a low condition; without grandeur or dignity.

I will show myself highly fed and _lowly_ taught.
