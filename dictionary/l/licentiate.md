# Licentiate

**LICEN'TIATE**, _noun_ \[from Latin licentia.\]

**1.** One who has a license; as a _licentiate_ in physic or medicine.

**2.** In Spain, one who has a degree; as a _licentiate_ in law or divinity. The officers of justice are mostly distinguished by this title.

**LICEN'TIATE**, _verb transitive_ To give license or permission.
