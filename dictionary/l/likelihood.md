# Likelihood

**LI'KELIHOOD**, _noun_ \[likely and hod.\]

**1.** Probability; verisimilitude; appearance of truth or reality. There is little _likelihood_ that an habitual drunkard will become temperate. There is little _likelihood_ that an old offender will be reformed. Prudence directs us not to undertake a design, when there is little or no _likelihood_ of success.

**2.** Appearance; show; resemblance. _obsolete_
