# Lawless

**LAW'LESS**, _adjective_

**1.** Not subject to law; unrestrained by law; as a _lawless_ tyrant; _lawless_ men.

**2.** Contrary to law; illegal; unauthorized; as a _lawless_ claim.

He needs no indirect nor _lawless_ course.

**3.** Not subject to the ordinary laws of nature; uncontrolled.

He, meteor-like, flames _lawless_ through the void.
