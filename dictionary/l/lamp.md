# Lamp

**LAMP**, _noun_ \[Latin lampas; Gr. to shine; Heb.\]

**1.** A vessel for containing oil to be burned by means of a wick; or a light, a burning wick inserted in a vessel of oil. Hence,

**2.** Figuratively, a light of any kind. The moon is called the _lamp_ of heaven.

Thy gentle eyes send forth a quickening spirit, to feed the dying _lamp_ of life within me.

**LAMP** of safety, or safety _lamp_ a _lamp_ for lighting coal mines, without exposing workmen to the explosion of inflammable air.
