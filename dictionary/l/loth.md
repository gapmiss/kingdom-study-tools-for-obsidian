# Loth

**LOTH**, _adjective_ \[In America, the primitive pronunciation of lath, that is, lawth, is retained in the adjective, which is written _loth_ The verb would be better written lothe, in analogy with cloth, clothe. See _loth_ \]

Disliking; unwilling; reluctant. He was _loth_ to leave the company. \[See _loth_ \]
