# Lyric

**LYR'IC**

**LYR'IC**AL, _adjective_ \[Latin lyricus.\] Pertaining to a lyre or harp. _lyric_ poetry is such as is sung to the harp or lyre. This was much cultivated by the ancients, among whom Anacreon, Alcoeus, Stesichorus, Sappho and Horace are distinguished as _lyric_ poets.

**LYRIC**, _noun_ A composer of _lyric_ poems.
