# Leonine

**LE'ONINE**, _adjective_ \[Latin leoninus, from leo, lion.\] Belonging to a lion; resembling a lion, or partaking of his qualities; as _leonine_ fierceness or rapacity.

Leonine verses, so named from Leo, the inventor, are those, the end of which rhymes with the middle; as,

Gloria factorum temere conceditur horum.
