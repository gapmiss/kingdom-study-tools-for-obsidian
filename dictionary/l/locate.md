# Locate

**LO'CATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin loco, locatus.\]

**1.** To place; to set in a particular spot or position.

**2.** To select, survey and settle the bounds of a particular tract of land; or to designate a portion of land by limits; as, to _locate_ a tract of a hundred acres in a particular township.

**3.** To designate and determine the place of; as, a committee was appointed to _locate_ a church or a court house.
