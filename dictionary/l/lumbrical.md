# Lumbrical

**LUM'BRICAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin lumbricus, a worm.\] Resembling a worm; as the _lumbrical_ muscles.

**LUM'BRICAL**, _adjective_ Pertaining to the loins.

**LUM'BRICAL**, _noun_ A muscle of the fingers and toes, so named from its resembling a worm. Of these muscles, there are four of the fingers and as many of the toes.
