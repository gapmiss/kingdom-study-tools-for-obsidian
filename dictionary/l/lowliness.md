# Lowliness

**LOWLINESS**, _noun_ \[from lowly.\]

**1.** Freedom from pride; humility; humbleness of mind.

Walk - with all _lowliness_ and meekness. [[Ephesians 4#2|Ephesians 4:2]]. [[Philippians 2#3|Philippians 2:3]].

**2.** Meanness; want of dignity; abject state. \[In this sense little used.\]
