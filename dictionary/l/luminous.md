# Luminous

**LU'MINOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin luminosus.\]

**1.** Shining; emitting light. The sun is a most _luminous_ body.

**2.** Light; illuminated. The moon is rendered _luminous_ by the rays of the sun.

**3.** Bright; shining; as a _luminous_ color.

**4.** Clear; as a _luminous_ essay or argument.
