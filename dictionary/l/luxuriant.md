# Luxuriant

**LUXU'RIANT**, _adjective_

**1.** Exuberant in growth; abundant; as a _luxuriant_ growth of grass.

**2.** Exuberant in plenty; superfluous in abundance.

Prune the _luxuriant_ the uncouth refine.

**3.** A _luxuriant_ flower multiplies the covers of the fructification so as to destroy the essential parts.
