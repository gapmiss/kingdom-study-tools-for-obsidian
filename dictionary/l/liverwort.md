# Liverwort

**LIV'ERWORT**, _noun_ The name of many species of plants. Several of the lichens are so called. The liverworts (Hepaticae) are a natural order of cryptogamian plants whose herbage is generally frondose, and resembling the leafy lichens, but whose seeds are contained in a distinct capsule. The noble _liverwort_ is the Anemone hepatica.
