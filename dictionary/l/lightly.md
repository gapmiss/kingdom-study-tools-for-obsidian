# Lightly

**LIGHTLY**, _adverb_ li'tely.

**1.** With little weight; as, to tread lightly; to press _lightly_

**2.** Without deep impression.

The soft ideas of the cheerful note, _lightly_ received, were easily forgot.

**3.** Easily; readily; without difficulty; of course.

**4.** Without reason, or for reasons of little weight.

Flatter not the rich, neither do thou willingly or _lightly_ appear before great personages.

**5.** Without dejection; cheerfully.

Bid that welcome which comes to punish us, and we punish it, seeming to bear it _lightly_

**6.** Not chastely; wantonly.

**7.** Nimbly; with agility; not heavily or tardily.

He led me _lightly_ o'er the stream.

**8.** Gaily; airily; with levity; without heed or care.
