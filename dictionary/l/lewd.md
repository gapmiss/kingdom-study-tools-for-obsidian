# Lewd

**LEWD**, _adjective_ \[Heb.\]

**1.** Given to the unlawful indulgence of lust; addicted to fornication or adultery; dissolute; lustful; libidinous.

[[Ezekiel 23#44|Ezekiel 23:44]].

**2.** Proceeding from unlawful lust; as _lewd_ actions.

**3.** Wicked; vile; profligate; licentious. [[Acts 27#1|Acts 27:1]].

**LEWD**, _adjective_ \[Latin gnes, from geno.\] Lay; laical; not clerical. _obsolete_
