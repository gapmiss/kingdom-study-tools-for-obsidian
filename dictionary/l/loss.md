# Loss

**LOSS**, _noun_

**1.** privation, as the _loss_ of property; _loss_ of money by gaming; _loss_ of health or reputation. every _loss_ is not a detriment. we cannot regret the _loss_ of bad company or of evil habits.

**2.** Destruction; ruin; as the _loss_ of a ship at sea; the _loss_ of an army.

**3.** Defeat; as the _loss_ of a battle.

**4.** Waste; useless application; as a _loss_ of time or labor.

**5.** Waste by leakage or escape; as a _loss_ of liquors in transportation.

To bear a _loss_ to make good; also, to sustain a _loss_ without sinking under it.

To be at a _loss_ to be puzzled; to be unable to determine; to be in a state of uncertainty.
