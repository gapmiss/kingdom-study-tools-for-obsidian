# Libation

**LIBA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin libatio, from libo, to pour out, to taste.\]

**1.** The act of pouring a liquor, usually wine, either on the ground, or on a victim in sacrifice, in honor of some deity. The Hebrews, Greeks and Romans practiced _libation_ This was a solemn act and accompanied with prayer.

**2.** The wine or other liquor poured out in honor of a deity.
