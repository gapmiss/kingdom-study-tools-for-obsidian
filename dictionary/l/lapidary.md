# Lapidary

**LAP'IDARY**, _noun_ \[Latin lapidarius, lapis, a stone.\]

**1.** An artificer who cuts precious stones.

**2.** A dealer in precious stones.

**3.** A virtuoso skilled in the nature and kinds of gems or precious stones.

**LAP'IDARY**, _adjective_ Pertaining to the art of cutting stones. The _lapidary_ style denotes that which is proper for monumental and other inscriptions.
