# Lax

**LAX**, _adjective_ \[Latin laxus.\]

**1.** Loose; flabby; soft; not tense, firm or rigid; as _lax_ flesh; a _lax_ fiber.

**2.** Slack; not tight or tense; as a _lax_ cord.

**3.** Not firly united; of loose texture; as gravel and the like laxer matter.

**4.** Not rigidly exact; as a _lax_ moral discourse.

**5.** Not strict; as _lax_ morals.

**6.** Loose in the bowels; having too frequent discharges.

**LAX**, _noun_

**1.** A looseness; diarrhea.

**2.** A species of fish or salmon. \[Not in use.\]
