# Lull

**LULL**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin lallo. The sense is to throw down, to still, to appease. Seamen say, the wind lulls, when it subsides.\]

To quiet; to compose; to cause to rest. The nation may be lulled into security.

\- To _lull_ him soft asleep.

Such sweet compulsion doth in music lie, to _lull_ the daughters of necessity.

**LULL**, _verb intransitive_ To subside; to cease; to become calm; as, the wind lulls.

**LULL**, _noun_ Power or quality of soothing.
