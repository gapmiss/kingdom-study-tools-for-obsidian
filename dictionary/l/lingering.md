# Lingering

**LIN'GERING**, _participle present tense_

**1.** Delaying; loitering.

**2.** _adjective_ Drawing out in time; remaining long; protracted; as a _lingering_ disease.

To die is the fate of man; but to die with _lingering_ anguish is generally his folly.

**LIN'GERING**, _noun_ A delaying; a remaining long; tardiness; protraction.

The lingerings of holyday customs.
