# Lordly

**LORD'LY**, _adjective_ \[lord and like.\]

**1.** Becoming a lord; pertaining to a lord.

Lordly sins require _lordly_ estates to support them.

**2.** Proud; haughty; imperious; insolent.

Every rich and _lordly_ swain, with pride would drag about her chain.

**LORD'LY**, _adverb_ Proudly; imperiously; despotically.

A famished lion, issuing from the wood, roars _lordly_ fierce.
