# Liberate

**LIB'ERATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin libero, from liber, free.\]

**1.** To free; to release from restraint or bondage; to set at liberty; as, to _liberate_ one from duress or imprisonment; to _liberate_ the mind from the shackles of prejudice.

**2.** To manumit; as, to _liberate_ a slave.
