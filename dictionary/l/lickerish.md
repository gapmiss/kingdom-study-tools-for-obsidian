# Lickerish

**LICK'ERISH**, _adjective_ \[Gr. sweet. The sense of watery, smooth, sweet, are allied; likeness is often connected with smoothness in radical sense, and sleek is probably from the root of lick, like.\]

**1.** Nice in the choice of food; dainty; as a _lickerish_ palate.

**2.** Eager; greedy to swallow; eager to taste or enjoy; having a keen relish.

**3.** Dainty; tempting the appetite; as _lickerish_ baits.
