# Sorrowful

**SOR'ROWFUL**, _adjective_

**1.** Sad; grieving for the loss of some good, or on account of some expected evil.

**2.** Deeply serious; depressed; dejected. I Sam. 1.

**3.** Producing sorrow; exciting grief; mournful; as a _sorrowful_ accident.

**4.** Expressing grief; accompanied with grief; as _sorrowful_ meat. [[Job 6#7|Job 6:7]].
