# Sewer

**SEW'ER**, _noun_ \[Latin _sicco_.\] A drain or passage to cnvey off waterunder ground; a subterraneous canal, particularly in cities; corruptly pronounced _Shore_ or _soer_.

**SEW'ER**, _noun_ An officer who serves up the feast and arranges the dishes. _Obs_.

**SEW'ER**, _noun_ One who sews, or uses the needle.
