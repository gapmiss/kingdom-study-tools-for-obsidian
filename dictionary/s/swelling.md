# Swelling

**SWELL'ING**, _participle present tense_ Growing or enlarging in its dimensions; growing tumid; inflating; growing tumid; inflating; growing or making louder.

**SWELL'ING**, _noun_ A tumor, or any morbid enlargement of the natural size; as a _swelling_ on the hand or leg.

**1.** Protuberance; prominence.

The superficies of such plates are not even, but have many cavities and swellings.

**2.** A rising or enlargement by passion; as the swellings of anger, grief or pride.
