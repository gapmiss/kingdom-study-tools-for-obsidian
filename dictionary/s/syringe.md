# Syringe

**SYRINGE**, _noun_ syr'inj. \[supra.\] An instrument for injecting liquids into animal bodies, into wounds, etc.; or an instrument in the form of a pump, serving to imbibe any fluid, and then to expel it with force.

**SYR'INGE**, _verb transitive_ To inject by means of a pipe or syringe; to wash and cleanse by injections from a _syringe_
