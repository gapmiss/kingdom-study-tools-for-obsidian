# Solemnly

**SOL'EMNLY**, _adverb_ With gravity and religious reverence. Let us _solemnly_ address the throne of grace.

**2.** With official formalities and be due authority. This question of law has been _solemnly_ decided in the highest court.

**3.** With formal state.

**4.** With formal gravity and stateliness, or with affected gravity. There in deaf murmurs _solemnly_ are wise.

**5.** With religious seriousness; as, I _solemnly_ declare myselft innocent. I do _solemnly_ assure the reader.
