# Simplify

**SIM'PLIFY**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin simplex, simple, and facio, to make.\] To make simple; to reduce what is complex to greater simplicity; to make plain or easy. The collection of duties is drawn to a point, and so far simplified. It is important in scientific pursuits, to be cautious in simplifying our deductions. This is the true way to _simplify_ the study of science.
