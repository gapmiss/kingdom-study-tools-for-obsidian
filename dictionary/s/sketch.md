# Sketch

**SKETCH**, _noun_ \[Latin scateo.\] An outline or general delineation of any thing; a first rough or incomplete draught of a plan or any design; as the _sketch_ of a building; the _sketch_ of an essay.

**SKETCH**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To draw the outline or general figure of a thing; to make a rough draught.

**2.** To plan by giving the principal points or ideas.
