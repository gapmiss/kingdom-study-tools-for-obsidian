# Sobriety

**SOBRI'ETY**, _noun_ \[Latin sobrietas, from sobrius.\]

**1.** Habitual soberness or temperance in the use of spirituous liquors; as when we say. a man of _sobriety_

**2.** Freedom from intoxication. Public _sobriety_ is a relative duty.

**3.** Habitual freedom from enthusiasm, inordinate passion or overheated imagination; calmness; coolness; as the _sobriety_ of riper years; the _sobriety_ of age.

**4.** Seriousness; gravity without sadness or melancholy. Mirth makes them not mad, nor _sobriety_ sad.
