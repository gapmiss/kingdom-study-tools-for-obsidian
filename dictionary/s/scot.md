# Scot

**SCOT**,

**SCOT**'AL,

**SCOT**'ALE, _noun_ \[scot and ale.\] In law, the keeping of an alehouse by the officer of a forest, and drawing people to spend their money for liquor, for fear of his displeasure.

**SCOTCH**, _adjective_ Pertaining to Scotland or its inhabitants. \[See [[Scotish]].\]

**SCOTCH**. \[See _scot_ the verb.\]

**SCOTCH**, _verb transitive_

To cut with shallow incisions. _obsolete_

**SCOTCH**, n A slight cut or shallow incision.
