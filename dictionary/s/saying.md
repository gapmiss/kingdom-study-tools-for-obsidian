# Saying

**SA'YING**, _participle present tense_ Uttering in articulate sounds or words; speaking; telling; relating; reciting.

**SA'YING**, _noun_

**1.** An expression; a sentence uttered; a declaration.

Moses fled at this _saying_ [[Acts 7#26|Acts 7:26]].

Cicero treasured up the sayings of Scaevola.

**2.** A proverbial expression. Many are the sayings of the wise.
