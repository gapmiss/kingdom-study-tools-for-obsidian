# Suffice

**SUFFICE**, _verb intransitive_ suffi'ze. \[Latin sufficio; sub and facio.\]

To be enough or sufficient; to be equal to the end proposed.

To recount Almighty works

What words or tongue of seraph can suffice?

**SUFFICE**, _verb transitive_ suffi'ze. To satisfy; to content; to be equal to the wants or demands of.

Let it _suffice_ thee; speak no more to me of this matter.

[[Deuteronomy 3#26|Deuteronomy 3:26]].

Lord, show us the Father, and it sufficeth us. [[John 14#1|John 14:1]].

[[Ruth 2#14|Ruth 2:14]].

**1.** To afford; to supply.

The pow'r appeas'd, with wind suffic'd the sail. \[Not in use.\]
