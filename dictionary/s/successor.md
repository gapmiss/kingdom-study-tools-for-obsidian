# Successor

**SUCCESS'OR**, _noun_ \[Latin\] One that succeeds or follows; one that takes the place which another has left, and sustains the like part or character; correlative to predecessor; as the _successor_ of a deceased king; the _successor_ of a president or governor; a man's son and _successor_

A gift to a corporation, either of lands or of chattels, without naming their successors, vests an absolute property in them so long as the corporation subsists.
