# Submultiple

**SUBMUL'TIPLE**, _noun_ \[See [[Multiply]].\] A number or quantity which is contained in another a certain number of times, or is an aliquot part of it. Thus 7 is the _submultiple_ of 56, being contained in it eight times. The word is used as an adjective also; as a _submultiple_ number; _submultiple_ ratio.
