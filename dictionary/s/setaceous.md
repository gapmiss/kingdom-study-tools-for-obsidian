# Setaceous

**SETA'CEOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin _seta_, a bristle.\]

**1.** Bristly; set with strong hairs; consisting of strong hairs; as a stiff _setaceous_ tail.

**2.** In _botany_, bristle-shaped; having the thickness and length of a bristle; as a _setaceous_ seaf or leaflet.

_Setaceous worm_, a name given to a water worm that resembles a horse hair, vulgarly supposed to be an animated hair. But this is a mistake.
