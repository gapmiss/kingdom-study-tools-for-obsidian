# Slash

**SLASH**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To cut by striking violently and at random; to cut in long cuts.

**2.** To lash.

**SLASH**, _verb intransitive_ To strike violently and at random with a sword, hanger or other edger instrument; to lay about one with blows. Hewing and slashing at their idle shades.

**SLASH**, _noun_ A long cut; a cut made at random.
