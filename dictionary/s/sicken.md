# Sicken

**SICKEN**, _verb transitive_ _sik'n_.

**1.** To make sick; to disease.

Raise this to strength, and _sicken_ that to death. _Prior_.

**2.** To make squeamish. It _sickens_ the stomach.

**3.** To disgust. It _sickens_ one to hear the fawning sycophant.

**4.** to impair. \[_Not in use_.\]

**SICK'EN**, _verb intransitive_ To become sick; to fall into disease.
