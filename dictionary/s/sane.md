# Sane

**SANE**, _adjective_ \[Latin sanus, Eng. sound. This is the Eng. sound. See [[sound]].\]

**1.** Sound; not disordered or shattered; healthy; as a _sane_ body.

**2.** Sound; not disordered; having the regular exercise of reason and other faculties of the mind; as a _sane_ person; a person of a _sane_ mind.
