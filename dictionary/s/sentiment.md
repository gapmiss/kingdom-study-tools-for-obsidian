# Sentiment

**SEN'TIMENT**, _noun_ \[from Latin _sentio_, to feel, perceive or think.\]

**1.** _Properly_. a thought prompted by passion or feeling.

**2.** In a _popular sense_, Thought; opinion; notion; judgement; the decilion of the mind formed by deliberation or reasoning. Thus in deliberative bodies, every man has the privilege of delivering his _sentiments_ upon questions, motions and bills.

**3.** The sense, thought or opinion contained in words, but considered as distinct from them. We may like the _sentiment_, when we dislike the language.

**4.** Sensibility; feeling.
