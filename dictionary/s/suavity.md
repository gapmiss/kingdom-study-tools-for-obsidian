# Suavity

**SUAVITY**, _noun_ \[Latin\]

**1.** Sweetness, in a literal sense. \[Not in use.\]

**2.** Sweetness, in a figurative sense; that which is to the mind what sweetness is to the tongue; agreeableness; softness; pleasantness; as _suavity_ of manners; _suavity_ of language, conversation or address.
