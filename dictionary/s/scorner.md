# Scorner

**SCORN'ER**, _noun_

**1.** One that scorns; a contemner; a despiser.

They are great scorners of death.

**2.** A scoffer; a derider; in Scripture, one who scoffs at religion, its ordinances and teachers, and who makes a mock of sin and the judgments and threatenings of God against sinners. [[Proverbs 1#22|Proverbs 1:22]], [[Proverbs 19#25|Proverbs 19:25]]
