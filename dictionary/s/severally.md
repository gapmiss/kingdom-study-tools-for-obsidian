# Severally

**SEV'ERALLY**, _adverb_ Separately; distinctly; apart from others. Call the men _severally_ by name.

I could not keep my eye steady on them _severally_ so as to number them.

_Newton_.

To be _jointly_ and _severally_ bound in a contract, is for each obligor to be liable to pay the whole demand, in case the other or others are not able.
