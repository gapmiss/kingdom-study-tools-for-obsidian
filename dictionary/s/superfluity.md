# Superfluity

**SUPERFLU'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin superfluitas; super and fluo, to flow.\]

**1.** Superabundance; a greater quantity than is wanted; as a _superfluity_ of water or provisions.

**2.** Something that is beyond what is wanted; something rendered unnecessary by its abundance. Among the superfluities of life we seldom number the abundance of money.
