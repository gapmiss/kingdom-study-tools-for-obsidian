# Sorrel

**SOR'REL**, _adjective_ Of a reddish color; as a sorel horse.

**SOR'REL**, _noun_ A reddish color; a faint red.

**SOR'REL**, _noun_ A plant of the genus Rumex, so named from its acid taste. The wood _sorrel_ is of the genus Oxalis. The Indian red and Indian white sorrels are of the genus Hibiscus.
