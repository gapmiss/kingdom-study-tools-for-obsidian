# Saxifrage

**SAX'IFRAGE**, _noun_ \[Latin saqxifraga; composed of saxum, a stone, and frango, to break.\]

A medicine that has the property of breaking or dissolving the stone in the bladder. But in botany, a genus of plants of many species. The burnet _saxifrage_ is of the genus Pimpinella; the golden _saxifrage_ is of the genus Chrysoplenium; the meadow _saxifrage_ is of the genus Peucedanum.
