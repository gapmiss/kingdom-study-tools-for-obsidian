# Scalp

**SCALP**, _noun_ \[Latin scalpo.\]

**1.** The skin of the top of the head; as a hairless _scalp_

**2.** The skin of the top of the head cut or torn off. A _scalp_ among the Indians of America is a trophy of victory.

**SCALP**, _verb transitive_ To deprive of the _scalp_ or integuments of the head.
