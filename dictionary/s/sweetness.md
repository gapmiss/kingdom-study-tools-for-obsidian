# Sweetness

**SWEE'TNESS**, _noun_ The quality of being sweet, in any of its senses; as gratefulness to the taste; or to the smell, fragrance; agreeableness to the ear, melody; as _sweetness_ of the voice; _sweetness_ of elocution.

**1.** Agreeableness of manners; softness; mildness; obliging civility; as _sweetness_ of behavior.

**2.** Softness; mildness; amiableness; as _sweetness_ of temper.
