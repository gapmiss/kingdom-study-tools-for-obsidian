# Shortening

**SHORT'ENING**, _participle present tense_ Making shorter; contracting.

**SHORT'ENING**, _noun_ Something used in cookery to make paste short or friable, as butter or lard.

**SHORT'-HAND**, _noun_ \[_short_ amd _hand_.\] Short writing; a compendious method of writing by substituting characters, abbreviations or symbols for words; otherwise called _stenography_.
