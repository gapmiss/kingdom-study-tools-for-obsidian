# Superb

**SUPERB**', _adjective_ \[Latin superbus, proud, from super.\]

**1.** Grand; magnificent; as a _superb_ edifice; a _superb_ colonnade.

**2.** Rich; elegant; as _superb_ furniture or decorations.

**3.** Showy; pompous; as a _superb_ exhibition.

**4.** Rich; splendid; as a _superb_ entertainment.

**5.** August; stately.
