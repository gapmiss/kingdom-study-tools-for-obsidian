# Slowly

**SLOWLY**, _adverb_

**1.** With moderate motion; not rapidly; not with velocity or celerity; as, to walk _slowly_

**2.** Not soon; not early; not in a little time; not with hasty advance; as a country that rises _slowly_ into importance.

**3.** Not hastily; not rashly; not with precipitation; as, he determines _slowly_

**4.** Not promptly; not readily; as, he learns _slowly_

**5.** Tardily; with slow progress. The building proceeds _slowly_
