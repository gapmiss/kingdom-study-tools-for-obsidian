# Slaughter

**SLAUGHTER**, _noun_ slaw'ter \[See [[Slay]].\]

**1.** In a general sense, a killing. Applied to men, _slaughter_ usually denotes great destruction of life by violent means; as the _slaughter_ of men in battle.

**2.** Applied to beasts, butchery; a killing of oxen or other beasts for market.

**SLAUGHTER**, _verb transitive_ slaw'ter.

**1.** To kill; to slay; to make great destruction of life; as , to _slaughter_ men in battle.

**2.** To butcher; to kill for the market; as beasts.
