# Situated

**SIT'UATED**, _adjective_ \[See [[Situate]].\]

**1.** Seated, placed or standing with respect to any other object; as a city _situated_ on a declivity, or in front of a lake; a town well _situated_ for trade or manufactures; an observatory well _situated_ for observation of the stars. New York is _situated_ in the forty first degree of _noun_ latitude.

**2.** Placed or being in any state or condition with regard to men or things. Observe how the executor is _situated_ with respect to the heirs.
