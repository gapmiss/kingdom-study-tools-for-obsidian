# Slouch

**SLOUCH**, _noun_ \[This word probably belongs to the root of lag, slug.\]

**1.** A hanging down; a depression of the head or of some other part of the body, an ungainly clownish gait.

**2.** An awkward, heavy, clownish fellow.

**SLOUCH**, _verb intransitive_ To hang down; to have a down cast clownish look, gait or manner.

**SLOUCH**, _verb transitive_ To depress; to cause to hang down; as, to _slouch_ the hat.
