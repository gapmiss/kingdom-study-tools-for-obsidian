# Stride

**STRIDE**, _noun_ \[Latin\] A long step.

Her voice theatrically loud, and masculine her _stride_

**STRIDE**, _verb intransitive_ _preterit tense_ strid, strode; _participle passive_ strid, stridden.

**1.** To walk with long steps.

Mars in the middle of the shining shield is gravd, and strides along the field.

**2.** To straddle.

**STRIDE**, _verb transitive_ To pass over at a step.

See [[him]] _stride_ valleys wide.
