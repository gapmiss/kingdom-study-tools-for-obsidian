# Soothe

**SOOTHE**, _verb transitive_ \[The sense of setting, allaying of softening, would give that of truth, and of sweet, that is, smooth.\]

**1.** To flatter; to please with blandishments or soft words. Can I _soothe_ tyranny? I've tried the force of every reason on him. Sooth'd and caress'd, been angry, sooth'd again

**2.** To soften; to assuage; to mollify; to calm; as, to _soothe_ one in pain or passion; or to _soothe_ pain. It is applied both to persons and things.

**3.** To gratify; to please. Sooth'd with his future fame.
