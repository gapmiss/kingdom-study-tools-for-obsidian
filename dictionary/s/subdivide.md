# Subdivide

**SUBDIVIDE**, _verb transitive_ \[sub and divide.\] To divide a part of a thing into more parts; to part into smaller divisions.

In the rise of eight in tones, are two half tones; so as if you divide the tones equally, the eight is but seven whole and equal notes; and if you _subdivide_ that into half notes, as in the stops of a lute, it makes the number thirteen.

The progenies of Cham and Japhet swarmed into colonies, and those colonies were subdivided into many others--

**SUBDIVIDE**, _verb intransitive_ To be subdivided.
