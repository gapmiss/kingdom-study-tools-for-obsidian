# Sadness

**SAD'NESS**, _noun_

**1.** Sorrowfulness; mournfulness; dejection of mind; as grief and _sadness_ at the memory of sin.

**2.** A melancholy look; gloom of countenance.

Dim _sadness_ did not spare Celestial visages.

**3.** Seriousness; sedate gravity. Let every thing in a mournful subject have an air of _sadness_
