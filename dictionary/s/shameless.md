# Shameless

**SHA'MELESS**, _adjective_ \[_shame_ and _less_.\]

**1.** Destitute of shame; wanting modesty; impudent; brazen-faced; immodest; audacious; insensible to disgrace.

Such _shameless_ bards we have. _Pope._

**2.** Done without shame; indicating want of shame; as a _shameless_ denial of truth.
