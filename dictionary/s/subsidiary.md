# Subsidiary

**SUBSID'IARY**, _adjective_ \[Latin subsidiarius. See [[Subsidy]].\]

**1.** Aiding; assistant; furnishing help. _subsidiary_ troops are troops of one nation hired by another for military service.

**2.** Furnishing additional supplies; as a _subsidiary_ stream.

**SUBSID'IARY**, _noun_ An assistant; an auxiliary; he or that which contributes aid or additional supplies.
