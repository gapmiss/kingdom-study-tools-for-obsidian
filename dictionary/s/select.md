# Select

**SELECT**', _v_. _t_. \[Latin _selectus_, from _seligo_; _se_, from, and _lego_, to pick, cull or gather.\] to choose and take from a number; to take by preference from among others; to pick out; to cull; as, to _select_ the best authors for persual; to _select_ the most interesting and virtuous men for associates.

**SELECT'**, _adjective_. Nicely chosen; taken from a number by preference; choice; whence, preferable; more valuable or excellent than others; as a body of _select_ troops; a _select_ company or society; a library consisted of _select_ authors.
