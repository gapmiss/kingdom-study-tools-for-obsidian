# Suspense

**SUSPENSE**, _noun_ suspens'. \[Latin suspensus.\] A state of uncertainty; indetermination; indecision. A man's mind is in _suspense_ when it is balancing the weight of different arguments or considerations, or when it is uncertain respecting facts unknown, or events not in his own power.

Ten days the prophet in _suspense_ remain'd.

**1.** Stop; cessation for a time.

A cool _suspense_ from pleasure or from pain.

**2.** In law, suspension; a temporary cessation of a man's right; as when the rent or other profits of land cease by unity of possession of land and rent.

**SUSPENSE**, _adjective_ suspens'. Held from proceeding. \[Little used.\]
