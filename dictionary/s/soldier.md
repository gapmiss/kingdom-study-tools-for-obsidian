# Soldier

**SOLDIER**, _noun_ soljur. \[from Latin solidus, a piece of money, the pay of a _soldier_ \]

**1.** A man engaged in military service; one whose occupation is military; a man enlisted for service in an army; a private, or noe in the ranks. There ought to be some time for sober reflection between the life of a _soldier_ and his death.

**2.** A man enrolled for service, when on duty or embodied for military discipline; a private; as a militia _soldier_

**3.** Emphatically, a brave warrior; a man of military experience and skill, or a man of distinguished valor. In this sense, an officer of any grade may be denominated a _soldier_
