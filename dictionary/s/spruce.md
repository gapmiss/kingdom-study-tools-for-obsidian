# Spruce

**SPRUCE**, _adjective_ Nice; trim; neat without elegance or dignity; formerly applied to things with a serious meaning; now applied to persons only.

He is so _spruce_ that he never can be genteel.

**SPRUCE**, _verb transitive_ To trim; to dress with great neatness.

**SPRUCE**, _verb intransitive_ To dress ones self with affected neatness.

**SPRUCE**, _noun_ The fir-tree; a name given to a species of evergreen, the Pinus nigra, which is used in families to give flavor to beer. It is used by way of decoction, or in the essence.
