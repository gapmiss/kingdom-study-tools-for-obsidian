# Shag

**SHAG**, _noun_

**1.** Coarse hair or nap, or rough wooly hair.

True Witney broadcloth, with its _shag_ unshorn. _Gay_.

**2.** A kind of cloth having a long coarse nap.

**3.** In _orinthology_, an aquatic fowl, the _Pelecanus graculus_; in the north of England called the _crave_.

**SHAG**, _adjective_ Hairy; shaggy.

**SHAG**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To make rough or hairy.

_Shag_ the green zone that bounds the boreal skies. _J. Barlow_.

**2.** To make rough or shaggy; to deform.
