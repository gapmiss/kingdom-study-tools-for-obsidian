# Sinecure

**SI'NECURE**, _noun_ \[Latin sine, without, and cura, cure, care.\] An office which has revenue without employment; in church affairs, a benefice without cure of souls. \[This is the original and proper sense of the word.\] Sine die, \[Latin without day.\] An adjournment sine die is and adjournment without fixing the time of resuming business. When a defendant is suffered to go sine die, he is dismissed the court.
