# Seminal

**SEM'INAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin _seminalis_, from _semen_, seed; from the root of _sow_.\]

**1.** Pertaining to seed, or to the elements of production.

**2.** Contained in a seed; radical; rudimental; original; as _seminal_ principles of generation; _seminal_ virtue.

_Seminal_ leaf, the same as seed-leaf.

**SEM'INAL**, _noun_ _seminal_ state.
