# Sheath

**SHEATH**, _noun_

**1.** A case for the reception of a sword or other long and slender instrument; a scabbard. A _sheath_ is that which separates, and hence a defense.

**2.** In _botany_, a membrane investing a stem or branch, as in grasses.

**3.** Any thin covering for defense; the wing-case of an insect.

**SHEATH**, _verb transitive_
