# Site

**SITE**, _noun_ \[Latin silus.\]

**1.** Situation; local position; as the _site_ of a city or of a house.

**2.** A seat or ground-plot; as a mill-site. But we usually say, mill-seat, by which we understand the place where a mill stands, or a place convenient for a mill.

**3.** The posture of a thing with respect of itself. The semblance of a lover fix'd in melancholy _site_ \[This is improper.\]
