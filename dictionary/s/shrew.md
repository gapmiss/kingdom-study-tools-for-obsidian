# Shrew

**SHREW**, _noun_

**1.** A peevish, brawling, turbulent, vexatious woman. It appears to have originally been applied to males as well as females; but is now restricted to the latter.

The man had got a _shrew_ for his wife, and there could be no quiet in the house with her. _L'Estrange_.

**2.** A shrew-mouse.

**SHREW**, _verb transitive_ To beshrew; to curse. _Obs_.
