# Securely

**SECU'RELY**, _adv_.

**1.** Without danger; safely; as, to pass a river on ice _securely_. But _safely_ is generally used.

**2.** Without fear or apprehension; carelessly; in an unguarded state; in confidence of safety.

His daring foe _securely_ him defied. _Milton_.

Devise not evil against thy neighbor, seeing he dwelleth _securely_ by thee. [[Proverbs 3#29|Proverbs 3:29]].
