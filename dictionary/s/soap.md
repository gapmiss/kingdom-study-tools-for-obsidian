# Soap

**SOAP**, _noun_ \[Latin sapo.\] A compound of oil and alkali, or oil and earth, and metallic oxyds; usually, a compound of oil and vegetable alkali or lye; used in washing and cleansing, in medicine, etc.

**SOAP**, _verb transitive_ To rub or wash over with _soap_

**SOAPHERRY TREE**, _noun_ A tree of the genus Sapindus.
