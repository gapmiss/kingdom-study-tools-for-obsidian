# Submerge

**SUBMERGE**, _verb transitive_ submerj'. \[Latin submergo; sub and mergo, to plunge.\]

**1.** To put under water; to plunge.

**2.** To cover or overflow with water; to drown.

So half my Egypt was submerg'd.

**SUBMERGE**, _verb intransitive_ submerj'. To plunge under water, as swallows.
