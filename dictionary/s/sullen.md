# Sullen

**SUL'LEN**, _adjective_ \[perhaps set, fixed, and allied to silent, sill, etc.\]

**1.** Gloomily angry and silent; cross; sour; affected with ill humor.

And _sullen_ I forsook th' imperfect feast.

**2.** Mischievous; malignant.

Such _sullen_ planets at my birth did shine.

**3.** Obstinate; intractable.

Things are as _sullen_ as we are.

**4.** Gloomy; dark; dismal.

Why are thine eyes fix'd to the _sullen_ earth?

Night with her _sullen_ wings.

No cheerful breeze this _sullen_ region knows.

**5.** Heavy; dull; sorrowful.

_Be thou the trumpet of our wrath,_

And _sullen_ presage of your own decay.
