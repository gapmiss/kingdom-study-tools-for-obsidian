# Spindle

**SPIN'DLE**, _adjective_ \[See [[Spin]].\]

**1.** The pin used in spinning wheels for twisting the thread, and on which the thread when twisted, is wound.

**2.** A slender pointed rod or pin on which any thing turn; as the _spindle_ of a vane.

**3.** The fusee of a watch.

**4.** A long slender stalk.

**5.** The lower end of a capstan, shod with iron; the pivot.

**SPIN'DLE**, _verb intransitive_ To shoot or grow in a long slender stalk or body.
