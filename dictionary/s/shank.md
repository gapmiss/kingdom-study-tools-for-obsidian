# Shank

**SHANK**, _noun_

**1.** The whole joint from the knee to the ankle. In a horse, th epart of the fore leg between the knee and footlock.

**2.** The tibia or large bone of the leg; as crooked _shanks_.

**3.** The long part of an instrument; as the _shank_ of a key.

The beam or shaft of an anchor.

**4.** A plant. \[_bryonia._\]
