# Slowness

**SLOWNESS**, _noun_

**1.** Moderate motion; want of speed or velocity. Swifness and _slowness_ are relative ideas.

**2.** Tardy advance; moderate progression; as the _slowness_ of an operation; _slowness_ of growth or improvement.

**3.** Dullness to admit conviction or affection; as _slowness_ of heart.

**4.** Want of readiness or promptness; dullness of intellect.

**5.** Deliberation; coolness; caution in deciding.

**6.** Dilatoriness; tardiness.
