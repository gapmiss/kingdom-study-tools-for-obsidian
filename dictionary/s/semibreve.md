# Semibreve

**SEM'IBREVE**, \[_semi_ and _breve_; formerly written _semibref_.\] In _music_, a note of half the duration of time of the breve. It is the measure note by which all others are regulated. It contains the time of two minims, four crotchets, eights quavers, sixteen semiquavers and thirty-two demisemiquavers.
