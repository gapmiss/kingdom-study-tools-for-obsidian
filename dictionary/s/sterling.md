# Sterling

**STERLING**, _adjective_ \[probably from Easterling.\]

**1.** An epithet by which English money of account is distinguished; as a pound sterling; a shilling sterling; a penny _sterling_ It is not now applied to the coins of England; but _sterling_ cost, _sterling_ value are used.

**2.** Genuine; pure; of excellent quality; as a work of _sterling_ merit; a man of _sterling_ wit or good sense.

**STERLING**, _noun_

**1.** English money.

And Roman wealth in English _sterling_ view.

In this use, _sterling_ may signify English coins.

**2.** Standard; rate. \[Little used in either sense.\]
