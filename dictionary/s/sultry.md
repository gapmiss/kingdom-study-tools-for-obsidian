# Sultry

**SUL'TRY**, _adjective_

**1.** Very hot, burning and oppressive; as Libya's _sultry_ deserts.

**2.** Very hot and moist, or hot, close, stagnant and unelastic; as air or the atmosphere. A _sultry_ air is usually enfeebling and oppressive to the human body.

Such as born beneath the burning sky

And _sultry_ sun, betwixt the tropics lie.
