# Secularize

**SEC'ULARIZE**, _v_. _t_.

**1.** To make secular; to convert from spiritual appropriation to scular or common use; to convert that which is regular or monastic into secular; as, the ancient regular cathedral churches were _secularized_.

At the reformation, the abbey was _secularized_. _Coxe_, _Switz_.

**2.** To make worldy.
