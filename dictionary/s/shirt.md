# Shirt

**SHIRT**, _noun_ _shurt_. \[Latin _curtus_.\] A loose garment of linen, cotton or other material, worn by men and boys next the body.

It is folly for a nation to export beef and linen, while a great part of the peaple are obliged to subsist on potatoes, and have no _shirts_ to wear. _adjective_._M_.

**SHIRT**, _verb transitive_ _shurt_.

**1.** To cover or clothe, as with a _shirt_

**2.** To change the _shirt_ and put on a clean one.
