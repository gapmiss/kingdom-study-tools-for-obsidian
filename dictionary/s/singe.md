# Singe

**SINGE**, _verb transitive_ sinj. To burn slightly or superficially; to burn the surface of a thing as the nap of cloth, or the hair of the skin; as, to _singe_ off the beard. Thus riding on his curls, he seem'd to pass A rolling fire along, and _singe_ the grass.

**SINGE**, _noun_ A burning of the surface; a slight burn.
