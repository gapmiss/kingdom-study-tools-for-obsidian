# Subterfuge

**SUB'TERFUGE**, _noun_ \[Latin subter and fugio, to flee.\] Literally, that to which a person resorts for escape or concealment; hence, a shift; an evasion; an artifice employed to escape censure or the force of an argument, or to justify opinions or conduct.

Affect not little shifts and subterfuges, to avoid the force of an argument.
