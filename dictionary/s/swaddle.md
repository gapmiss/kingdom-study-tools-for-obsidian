# Swaddle

**SWAD'DLE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To swathe; to bind, as with a bandage; to bind tight with clothes; used generally of infants; as, to _swaddle_ a child.

They swaddled me in my night-gown.

**2.** To bat; to cudgel. \[Low and not in use.\]

**SWAD'DLE**, _noun_ Clothes bound tight around the body.

They put me in bed in all my swaddles.
