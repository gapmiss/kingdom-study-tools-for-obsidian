# Suffragan

**SUF'FRAGAN**, _adjective_ \[Latin suffragans, assisting; suffragor, to vote for, to favor.\] Assisting; as a _suffragan_ bishop.

**SUF'FRAGAN**, _noun_ A bishop, considered as an assistant to his metropolitan; or rather, an assistant bishop. By 26 Hen.VIII. suffragans are to be denominated from some principal place in the diocese of the prelate whom they are to assist.
