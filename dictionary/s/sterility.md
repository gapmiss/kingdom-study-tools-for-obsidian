# Sterility

**STERILITY**, _noun_ \[Latin\]

**1.** Barrenness; unproductiveness; unfruitfulness; the quality or state of producing little or nothing; as the _sterility_ of land or soil.

**2.** Barrenness; unfruitfulness; the state of not producing young; as of animals.

**3.** Barrenness of ideas or sentiments, as in writings.

**4.** Want of fertility or the power of producing sentiment; as the _sterility_ of an author or of his mind.
