# Sparse

**SPARSE**, _adjective_ spars. \[Latin sparsus, scattered, from spargo.\]

**1.** Thinly scattered; set or planted here and there; as a _sparse_ population.

**2.** In botany, not opposite, not alternate, nor in any apparent regular order; applied to branches, leaves peduncles, etc.

**SPARSE**, _verb transitive_ spars. To disperse. \[Not in use.\]
