# Smallness

**SMALL'NESS**, _noun_ Littleness of size or extent; littleness of quantity; as the _smallness_ of a fly or of a horse; the _smallness_ of a hill.

**2.** Littleness in degree; as the _smallness_ of trouble or pain.

**3.** Littleness in force or strength; weakness; as _smallness_ of mind or intellectual powers.

**4.** Fineness; softness; melodiousness; as the _smallness_ of a female voice.

**5.** Littleness in amount of value; as the _smallness_ of a sum.

**6.** Littleness of importance; inconsideratbleness; as the _smallness_ of an affair.
