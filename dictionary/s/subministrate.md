# Subministrate

**SUBMIN'ISTRATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin subministro; sub and ministro.\]

To supply; to afford. \[Not in use.\]

**SUBMIN'ISTER**, _verb intransitive_ To subserve; to be useful to.

Our passions--subminister to the best and worst of purposes.

\[Not in use.\] \[See [[Minister and Administer]].\]
