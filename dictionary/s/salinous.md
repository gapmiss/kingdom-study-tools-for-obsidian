# Salinous

**SALI'NOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin sal, salt.\]

**1.** Consisting of salt or constituting salt; as saline particles; saline substances.

**2.** Partaking of the qualities of salt; as a saline taste.

**SALI'NE**, _noun_ A salt spring, or a place where salt water is collected in the earth; a name given to the salt springs in the United States.
