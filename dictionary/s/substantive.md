# Substantive

**SUB'STANTIVE**, _adjective_ Betokening existence; as the _substantive_ verb.

**1.** Solid; depending on itself. \[Not in use.\]

**SUB'STANTIVE**, _noun_ In grammar, a noun or name; the part of speech which expresses something that exists, either material or immaterial. Thus man, horse, city, goodness, excellence, are substantives. \[Better called name, Latin nomen, or even noun, a corruption of nomen.\]
