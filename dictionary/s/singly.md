# Singly

**SIN'GLY**, _adverb_

**1.** Individually; particularly; as, to make men _singly_ and personally good.

**2.** Only; by himself. Look thee, 'tis so, thou _singly_ honest man.

**3.** Without partners, companions or associates; as, to attack another _singly_ At ombre _singly_ to decide their doom.

**4.** Honestly; sincerely.
