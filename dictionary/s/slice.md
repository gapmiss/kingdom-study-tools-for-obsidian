# Slice

**SLICE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To cut into thin pieces, or to cut off a thin broad piece.

**2.** To cut into parts.

**3.** To cut; to divide.

**SLICE**, _noun_ A thin broad piece cut off; as a _slice_ of bacon; a _slice_ of cheese; a _slice_ of bread.

**2.** A broad piece' as a _slice_ of plaster.

**3.** A peel; a spatula; an instrument consisting of a broad plate with a handle, used by apothecaries for spreading plaster, \_ c.

**4.** In ship-building, a tapering piece of plank to be driven between the timbers before planking.
