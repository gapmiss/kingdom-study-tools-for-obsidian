# Shend

**SHEND**, _verb transitive_ ptet. and _participle passive_ _shent_.

**1.** To injure, mar or spoil. _Obs_.

That much I fear my body willbe _shent_. _Dryden_.

**2.** To blame, reproach. revile, degrade, disgrace.

The famous name of knighthood foully _shend_. _Spenser_.

**3.** To overpower or surpass.

She pass'd the rest as Cynthia doth _shend_

The lesser stars.
