# Spirited

**SPIRITED**, _participle passive_

**1.** Animated; encouraged; incited.

**2.** _adjective_ Animated; full of life; lively; full of spirit or fire; as a _spirited_ address or oration; a _spirited_ answer. It is used in composition, noting the state of the mind; as in high-spirited, low-spirited, mean-spirited.
