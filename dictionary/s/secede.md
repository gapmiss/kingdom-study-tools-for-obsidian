# Secede

**SECE'DE**, _verb intransitive_ \[**L**. _secedo; se,_ from, and _cedo_, to move. _Se_ is an inseparable preposition or prefix in Latin, but denoting departure or separation.\] To withdraw from fellowship, communion or association; to separate ones's self; as, certain ministers _seceded_ from the church of Scotland about the year 1733.
