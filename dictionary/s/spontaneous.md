# Spontaneous

**SPONTANEOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin , of free will.\]

**1.** Voluntary; acting by its own impulse or will without the incitement of any thing external; acting of its own accord; as _spontaneous_ motion.

**2.** Produced without being planted, or without human labor; as a _spontaneous_ growth of wood.

**SPONTANEOUS** combustion, a taking fire of itself. Thus oiled canvas, oiled wool, and many other combustible substances, when suffered to remain for some time in a confined state, suddenly take fire, or undergo _spontaneous_ combustion.
