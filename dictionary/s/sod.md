# Sod

**SOD**,

n. Turf; sward; that stratum of earth on the surface which is filled with the roots of grass, or any portion of that surface. It differs from clod, which may be compact mass of earth without roots; but _sod_ is formed by earth held together by roots.

**SOD**, _adjective_ Made or consisting of _sod_

**SOD**, _verb transitive_ To cover with sod; to turf.

**SOD**, _preterit tense_ of seethe; also the passive participle. \[See [[Sodden]].\]
