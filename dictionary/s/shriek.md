# Shriek

**SHRIEK**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin _ruga_, wrinkled, _rugo_, to bray.\] To utter a sharp shrill cry; to scream; as in sudden fright, in horror or anguish.

At this she _shriek'd_ aloud. _Dryden_.

It was the owl that _shriek'd_. _Shak_.

**SHRIEK**, _noun_ A sharp shrill outcry or scream, such as is produced by sudden anger or extreme anguish.

_Shrieks_, clamors, murmurs fill the frighted town. _Dryden_.
