# Sample

**SAM'PLE**, _noun_ \[Latin exemplum.\]

**1.** A specimen; a part of any thing presented for inspection or intended to be shown, as evidence of the quality of the whole; as a _sample_ of cloth or of wheat. Goods are often purchased in market by samples.

I design this as a _sample_ of what I hope more fully to discuss.

**2.** Example; instance.

**SAM'PLE**, _verb transitive_ To show something similar.
