# Sequence

**SE'QUENCE**, _noun_ \[**L**. _sequens_, _sequor_.\]

**1.** A following, or that which follows; aconsequent.

**2.** Order of succession.

How art thou king But by fair _sequence_ and succession? _Shak._

**3.** Series; arrangement; method.

**4.** In _music_, a regular alternate succession of similar chords.
