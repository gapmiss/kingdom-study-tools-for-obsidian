# Scurrilous

**SCUR'RILOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Using the low and indecent language of the meaner sort of people, or such as only the licence of buffoons can warrant; as a _scurrilous_ fellow.

**2.** Containing low indecency or abuse; mean; foul; vile; obscenely jocular; as _scurrilous_ language.
