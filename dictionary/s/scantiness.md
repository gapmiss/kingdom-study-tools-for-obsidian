# Scantiness

**SCANT'INESS**, _noun_

**1.** Narrowness; want of space or compass; as the _scantiness_ of our heroic verse.

**2.** Want of amplitude, greatness or abundance; limited extent.

Alexander was much troubled at the _scantiness_ of nature itself.

**3.** Want of fullness; want of sufficiency; as the _scantiness_ of supplies.
