# Shote

**SHOTE**, _noun_

**1.** A fish resembling the trout.

**2.** A young hog. \[See _Shoot_.\]

**SHOT'-FREE**, _adjective_ \[_shot_ and _free_.\]

**1.** Free from charge; exempted from any share of expense; scot-free.

**2.** Not to be injured by shot. \[_Not used_.\]

**3.** Unpunished. \[_Not used_.\]
