# Solve

**SOLVE**, _verb transitive_ solv. \[Latin solvo.\]

**1.** Properly, to loosen or separate the parts of any thing; hence, to explain; to resolve; to eclaircise; to unfold; to clear up; as what is obscure or difficult to be understood; as, to _solve_ questions; to _solve_ difficulties or a problem. When God shall _solve_ the dark decrees of fate.

**2.** To remove; to dissipate; as, to _solve_ doubts.
