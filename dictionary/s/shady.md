# Shady

**SHA'DY**, _adjective_ \[from _shade_.\]

**1.** Abounding with shade or shades; overspread with shade.

And Amaryllis fills the _shady_ groves. _Dryden_.

**2.** Sheltered from the glare of light or sultry heat.

Cast it also that you may have rooms _shady_ for the summer and warm for the winter. _Bacon_.
