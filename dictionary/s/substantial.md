# Substantial

**SUBSTAN'TIAL**, _adjective_ Belonging to substance; real; actually existing.

If this atheist would have his chance to be a real and _substantial_ agent, he is more stupid than the vulgar.

**1.** Real; solid; true; not seeming or imaginary.

If happiness be a _substantial_ good.

The _substantial_ ornaments of virtue.

**2.** Corporeal; material.

The rainbow appears like a _substantial_ arch in the sky.

**3.** Having substance; strong; stout; solid; as _substantial_ cloth; a _substantial_ fence or gate.

**4.** Possessed of goods or estate; responsible; moderately wealthy; as a _substantial_ freeholder or farmer; a _substantial_ citizen.
