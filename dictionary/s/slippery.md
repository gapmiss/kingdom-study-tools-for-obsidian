# Slippery

**SLIP'PERY**, _adjective_

**1.** Smooth; glib; having the quality opposite to adhesiveness; as, oily substances render things _slippery_

**2.** Not affording firm footing or confidence; as a _slippery_ promise. The slipp'ry tops of human state.

**3.** Not easily held; liable or apt to slip away. The slipp'ry god will try to loose his hold.

**4.** Not standing firm, as _slippery_ standers.

**5.** Unstable; changeable; mutable; uncertain; as the _slippery_ state of kings.

**6.** Not certain in its effect; as a _slippery_ trick.

**7.** Lubrious; wanton; unchaste.
