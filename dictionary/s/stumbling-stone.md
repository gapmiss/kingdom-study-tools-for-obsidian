# Stumbling-stone

STUMBLING-BLOCK, STUMBLING-STONE _noun_ \[stumble and block or stone.\] Any cause of stumbling; that which causes to err.

We preach Christ crucified, to the Jews a stumbling-block, and to the Greeks foolishness. [[1 Corinthians 1#1|1 Corinthians 1:1]].

This stumbling stone we hope to take away.
