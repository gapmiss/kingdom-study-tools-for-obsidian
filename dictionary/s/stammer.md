# Stammer

**STAMMER**, _verb intransitive_ Literally, to stop in uttering syllables or words; to stutter; to hesitate or falter in speaking; and hence, to speak with stops and difficulty. Demosthenes is said to have stammered in speaking, and to have overcome the difficulty by persevering efforts.

**STAMMER**, _verb transitive_ To utter or pronounce with hesitation or imperfectly.
