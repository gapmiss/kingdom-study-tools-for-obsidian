# Sequel

**SE'QUEL**, _noun_ \[**L**. _sequor_, to follow.\]

**1.** That which follows; a succeeding part; as the _sequel_ of a man's adventures or history.

**2.** Consequence; event. Lit the sun or moon cease, fail or swerve, and the _sequel_ would be ruin.

**3.** Consequence inferred; consequentialness. \[_Little used._\]
