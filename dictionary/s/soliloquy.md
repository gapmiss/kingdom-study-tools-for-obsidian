# Soliloquy

**SOLIL'OQUY**, _noun_ \[Latin solus, alone, and loquor, to speak.\]

**1.** A talking to one's self; a talking or discourse of a person alone, or not addressed to another person, even when others are persent. Lovers are always allowed the comfort of _soliloquy_

**2.** A written composition, reciting what it is supposed a person speaks to himself. The whole poem is a solioquy.
