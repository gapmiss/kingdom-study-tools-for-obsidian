# Studied

**STUDIED**, _participle passive_ \[from study.\]

**1.** Read; closely examined; read with diligence and attention; well considered. The book has been _studied_ The subject has been well _studied_

**2.** _adjective_ Learned; well versed in any branch of learning; qualified by study; as a man well _studied_ in geometry, or in law or medical science.

**3.** Having a particular inclination. \[Not in use.\]
