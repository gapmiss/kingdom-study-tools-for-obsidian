# Siren

**SIR'EN**, _noun_

**1.** A mermaid. In ancient mythology, a goddess who enticed men into her power by the charms of music, and devoured them. Hence in modern use, an enticing woman; a female rendered dangerous by her enticements. Sing, _siren_ to thyself, and I will dote.

**2.** A species of lizard in Carolina, constituting a peculiar genus, destitute of posterior extremities and pelvis.

**SIR'EN**, _adjective_ Pertaining to a _siren_ or to the dangerous enticements of music; bewitching; fascinating; as a _siren_ song.
