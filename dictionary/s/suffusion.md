# Suffusion

**SUFFU'SION**, _noun_ \[Latin suffusio.\]

**1.** The act or operation of overspreading, as with a fluid or with a color.

**2.** The state of being suffused or spread over.

To those that have the jaundice or like _suffusion_ of eyes, objects appear of that color.

**3.** That which is suffused or spread over.
