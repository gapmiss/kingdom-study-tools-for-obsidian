# Searching

**SEARCHING**, _participle passive_ _serch'ing_.

**1.** Looking into or over; exploring; examining; inquiring; seeking; investigating.

**2.** _adjective_ Penetrating; trying; close; as a _searching_ discourse.

**SEARCHING**, _noun_ _search'ing_. Exanination; severe inquisition. [[Judges 5#16|Judges 5:16]].
