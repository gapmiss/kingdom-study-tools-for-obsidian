# Stillness

**STILLNESS**, _noun_

**1.** Freedom from noise or motion; calmness; quiet; silence; as the _stillness_ of the night, the air or the sea.

**2.** Freedom from agitation or excitement; as the _stillness_ of the passions.

**3.** Habitual silence; taciturnity.

The gravity and _stillness_ of your youth, the world hath noted.
