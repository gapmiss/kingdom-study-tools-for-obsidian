# Softly

**SOFT'LY**, _adverb_

**1.** Without hardness.

**2.** Not with force or violence; gently; as, be _softly_ pressed my hand.

**3.** Not loudly; without noise; as, speak softly; walk _softly_ In this dark silence _softly_ leave the town.

**4.** Gently; placidly. She _softly_ lays him on a flowery bed.

**5.** Mildly; tenderly. The king must die; though pity _softly_ pleads within my soul.
