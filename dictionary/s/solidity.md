# Solidity

**SOLID'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin soliditas.\]

**1.** Firmness; hardness; density; compactness; that quality of bodies whcih resists impression and penetration; opposed to fluidity. That which hinders the apporach of two bodies moving ine towards another, I call _solidity_

**2.** Fullness of matter; opposed to hollowness.

**3.** Moral firmness; soundness; strength; validity; truth; certainty; as opposed to weakness or fallaciounes; as the soildity of arguments or reasonig; the _solidity_ of principles, truths or opinious.

**4.** In geometry, the solid contents of a body.
