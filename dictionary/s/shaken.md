# Shaken

**SHAKEN**, _participle passive_ _sha'kn_.

**1.** Impelled with a vacillating motion; agitated.

**2.** _adjective_ Cracked or split; as _shaken_ timber.

Nor is the wood _shaken_ nor twisted, as those about Capetown. _Barrow_.

\[Our mechanics usually pronounce this _shaky_, forming the word from _shake_, like _pithy_, from pith.\]
