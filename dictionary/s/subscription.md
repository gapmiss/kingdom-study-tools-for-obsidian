# Subscription

**SUBSCRIP'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin subscriptio.\] Any thing, particularly a paper, with names subscribed.

**1.** The act of subscribing or writing one's name underneath; name subscribed; signature.

**2.** Consent or attestation given by underwriting the name.

**3.** The act of contributing to any undertaking.

**4.** Sum subscribed; amount of sums subscribed. We speak of an individual _subscription_ or of the whole _subscription_ to a fund.

**5.** Submission; obedience. \[Not in use.\]
