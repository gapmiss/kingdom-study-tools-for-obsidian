# Strew

**STREW**, _verb transitive_ \[This verb is written straw, _strew_ or strow; straw is nearly obsolete, and strow is obsolescent. _strew_ is generally used.\]

**1.** To scatter; to spread by scattering; always applied to dry substances separable into parts or particles; as, to _strew_ seed in beds; to _strew_ sand on or over a floor; to _strew_ flowers over a grave.

**2.** To spread by being scattered over.

The snow which does the top of Pindus _strew_

Is thine alone the seed that strews the plain?

**3.** To scatter loosely.

And strewd his mangled limbs about the field.
