# Serviceable

**SERV'ICEABLE**, _adjective_

**1.** That does service; that promotes happiness, interest, advantage or any good; useful; beneficial; advantageous. Rulers may be very _serviceable_ to religion by their example. The attentions of my friends were very _serviceable_ to me when abroad. Rain and manure are _serviceable_ to land.

**2.** Active; diligent; officious.

I know thee well, a _serviceable_ villain. _Shak_. \[_Unusual_.\]
