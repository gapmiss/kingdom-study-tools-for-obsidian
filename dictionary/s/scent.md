# Scent

**SCENT**, _noun_ \[Latin sentio, to perceive.\]

**1.** Odor; smell; that substance which issuing from a body, affects the olfactory organs of animals; as the _scent_ of an orange or an apple; the _scent_ of musk. The word is applicable to any odor, agreeable or offensive.

**2.** The power of smelling; the smell; as a house of nice _scent_

**3.** chase followed by the scent; course of pursuit; track.

He travelled upon the same _scent_ into Ethiopia.

**SCENT**, _verb transitive_

**1.** to smell; to perceive by the olfactory organs; as, to _scent_ game, as a hound.

**2.** To perfume; to imbue or fill with odor, good or bad. Aromatic plants _scent_ the room. some persons _scent_ garments with musk; others _scent_ their snuff.
