# Supremacy

**SUPREM'ACY**, _noun_ \[See [[Supreme]].\] State of being supreme or in the highest station of power; highest authority or power; as the _supremacy_ of the king of Great Britain; or the _supremacy_ of parliament.

The usurped power of the pope being destroyed, the crown was restored to its _supremacy_ over spiritual men and causes.

Oath of _supremacy_ in Great Britain, an oath which acknowledges the _supremacy_ of the king in spiritual affairs, and renounces or abjures the pretended _supremacy_ of the pope.
