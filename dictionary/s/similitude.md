# Similitude

**SIMIL'ITUDE**, _noun_ \[Latin similitudo.\]

**1.** Likeness; resemblance; likeness in nature, qualities of appearance; as _similitude_ of substance. Let us make man in our image, man in our _similitude_ Fate some future bard shall join in sad _similitude_ of griefs to mine.

**2.** Comparison; simile. Tasso, in his _similitude_ never departed from the woods. \[See [[Simile]].\]
