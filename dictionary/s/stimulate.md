# Stimulate

**STIMULATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin , to prick, to goad, to excite; a goad.\] Literally, to prick or goad. Hence,

**1.** To excite, rouse or animate to action or more vigorous exertion by some pungent motive or by persuasion; as, to _stimulate_ one by the hope of reward, or by the prospect of glory.

**2.** In medicine, to excite or increase the action of the moving fibers or organs of an animal body; as, to _stimulate_ a torpid limb; or to _stimulate_ the stomach and bowels.
