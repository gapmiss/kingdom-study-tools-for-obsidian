# Soot

**SOOT**, _noun_ A black substance formed by combustion, or disengaged from fuel in the process of combustion, rising in fine particles and adhering to the sides of the chimney or pipe conveying the smoke. _soot_ consists of oil, carbon and other substances. The _soot_ of burnt pine forms lampblack.

**SOOT**, _verb transitive_ To cover or foul with _soot_
