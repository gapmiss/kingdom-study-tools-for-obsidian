# Saucy

**SAU'CY**, _adjective_ \[from sauce; Latin salsus, salt or salted. The use of this word leads to the primary sense of salt, which must be shooting forward, penetrating, pungent, for boldness is a shooting forward.\]

**1.** Impudent; bold to excess; rude; transgressing the rules of decorum; treating superiors with contempt. It expresses more than pert; as a _saucy_ boy; a _saucy_ fellow.

**2.** Expressive of impudence; as a _saucy_ eye; _saucy_ looks.
