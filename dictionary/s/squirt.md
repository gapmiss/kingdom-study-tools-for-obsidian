# Squirt

**SQUIRT**, _verb transitive_ To eject or drive out of a narrow pipe or orifice, in a stream; as, to _squirt_ water.

**SQUIRT**, _verb intransitive_ To throw out words; to let fly. \[Not in use.\]

**SQUIRT**, _noun_

**1.** An instrument with which a liquid is ejected in a stream with force.

**2.** A small quick stream.
