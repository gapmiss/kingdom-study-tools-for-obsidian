# Scarlet

**SC'ARLET**, _noun_

**1.** A beautiful bright red color, brighter than crimson.

**2.** Cloth of a _scarlet_ color.

All her household are clothed with _scarlet_ [[Proverbs 31#21|Proverbs 31:21]].

**SC'ARLET**, _adjective_ of the color called scarlet; of a bright red color; as a _scarlet_ cloth or thread; a _scarlet_ lip.
