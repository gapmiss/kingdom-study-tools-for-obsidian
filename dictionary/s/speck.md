# Speck

**SPECK**, _noun_ \[This word may be formed from peck, for peckled has been used for speckled, spotted as though pecked.\]

**1.** A spot; a stain; a small place in any thing that is discolored by foreign matter, or is of a color different from that of the main substance; as a _speck_ on paper or cloth.

**2.** A very small thing.

**SPECK**, _verb transitive_ To spot; to stain in spots or drops.
