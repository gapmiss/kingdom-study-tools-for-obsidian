# Shrill

**SHRILL**, _adjective_ \[Latin _grillus_.\]

**1.** Sharp; acute; piercing; as sound; as a _shrill_ voice; _shrill_ echoes.

**2.** Uttering an acute sound; as the cock's _shrill_ sounding throat; a _shrill_ trumpet.

\[NOTE. A _shrill_ may be tremulous of trilling; but this circumstance is not essential it, although it seems to be of the root of _trill_.\]

**SHRILL**, _verb intransitive_ To utter an acute piercing sound.

Break we out pipes that _shrill'd_ as loud as lark. _Spenser_.

**SHRILL**, _verb transitive_ To cause to make a _shrill_ sound.
