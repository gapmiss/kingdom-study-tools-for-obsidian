# Stilt

**STILT**, _noun_ \[G.\] A _stilt_ is a piece of wood with a shoulder, to support the foot in walking. Boys sometimes use stilts for raising their feet above the mud in walking, but they are rarely seen.

Men must not walk upon stilts.

**STILT**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To raise on stilts; to elevate.

**2.** To raise by unnatural means.
