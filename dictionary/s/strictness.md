# Strictness

**STRICTNESS**, _noun_

**1.** Closeness; tightness; opposed to laxity.

**2.** Exactness in the observance of rules, laws, rites and the like; rigorous accuracy; nice regularity or precision.

I could not grant too much or distrust too little to men that pretended singular piety and religious _strictness_

**3.** Rigor; severity.

These commissioners proceeded with such _strictness_ and severity as did much obscure the kings mercy.
