# Suppliant

**SUP'PLIANT**, _adjective_ \[Latin supplico, to supplicate; sub and plico, to fold. See [[Comply and Apply]].\]

**1.** Entreating; beseeching; supplicating; asking earnestly and submissively.

The rich grow _suppliant_ and the poor grow proud.

**2.** Manifesting entreaty; expressive of humble supplication.

To bow and sue for grace with _suppliant_ knee.

**SUP'PLIANT**, _noun_ A humble petitioner; one who entreats submissively.

Spare this life, and hear thy suppliant's pray'r.
