# Stupefy

**STUPEFY**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin\]

**1.** To make stupid; to make dull; to blunt the faculty of perception or understanding; to deprive of sensibility. It is a great sin to attempt to _stupefy_ the conscience.

The fumes of passion intoxicate his discerning faculties, as the fumes of drink _stupefy_ the brain.

**2.** To deprive of material motion.

It is not malleable nor fluent, but stupefied. \[Not in use.\]
