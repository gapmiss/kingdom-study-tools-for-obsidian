# Succeeding

**SUCCEE'DING**, _participle present tense_ Following in order; subsequent; coming after; as in all _succeeding_ ages. He attended to the business in every _succeeding_ stage of its progress.

**1.** Taking the place of another who has quitted the place, or is dead; as a son _succeeding_ his father; an officer _succeeding_ his predecessor.

**2.** Giving success; prospering.

**SUCCEE'DING**, _noun_ The act or state of prospering or having success. There is a good prospect of his _succeeding_
