# Sequacious

**SEQUA'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin _sequax_, from _sequor_, to follow. See _Seek_.\]

**1.** Following; attendant.

Trees uprooted left their place,

_Sequacious_ of the lyre. _Dryden_.

The fond _sequacious_ herd. _Thomson_.

**2.** Ductile; pliant.

The forge was easy, and the matter ductile and _sequacious_. \[_Little used_.\] _Ray_.
