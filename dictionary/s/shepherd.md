# Shepherd

**SHEP'HERD**, _noun_

**1.** A man employed in tending, feeding and gaurding sheep in the pasture.

**2.** A swain; a rural lover.

**3.** The pastor of a parish, church or congregation; a minister of the gospel who superintends a church or parish, and gived instruction in spiritual things. God and Christ are in Scripture dinominated _Shepherds_, as they lead, protect and govern their people, and provide for their wilfare.
