# Sty

**STY**, _noun_

**1.** A pen or inclosure for swine.

**2.** A place of bestial debauchery.

To roll with pleasure in a sensual _sty_

**3.** An inflamed tumor on the edge of the eyelid.

**STY**, _verb transitive_ To shut up in a _sty_

**STY**, _verb intransitive_ To soar; to ascend. \[Not in use.\] \[See [[Stirrup]].\]
