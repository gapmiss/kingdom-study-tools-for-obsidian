# Second-rate

**SEC'OND-RATE**, _noun_. \[_second_ and _rate_.\] The second order in size, dignity, or value.

They call it thunder of the _second-rate_. _Addison_.

So we say, a ship of the _second-rate_.

**SEC'OND-RATE**, _adjective_. Of the second size, rank, quality or value; as a _second-rate_ ship; a _second-rate_ cloth; a _second-rate_ champion.
