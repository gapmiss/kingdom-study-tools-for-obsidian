# Smut

**SMUT**, _noun_

**1.** A spot made with soot or coal; or the foul matter itself.

**2.** A foul black substance which forms on corn. Sometimes the whole ear is blasted and converted into _smut_ This is often the fact with maiz. _smut_ lessens the value of wheat.

**3.** Obscene language.

**SMUT**, _verb intransitive_ To gather smut; tobe converted into _smut_
