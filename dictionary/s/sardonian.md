# Sardonian

**SARDO'NIAN**,

SARDON'IC, _adjective_ _sardonian_ or sardonic laughter, a convulsive involuntary laughter, so called from the herba sardonia, a species of ranunculus, which is said to produce such convulsive motions in the cheeks and lips as are observed during a fit of laughter.

**SARDON'IC**, _adjective_ Denoting a kind of linen made at Colchis.
