# Symposiac

**SYMPOSIAC**, _adjective_ sympo'ziac. \[Gr. a drinking together; together, and to drink.\] Pertaining to compotations and merry-making; happening where company is drinking together; as _symposiac_ meetings.

**SYMPOSIAC** disputations. \[Not much used.\]

**SYMPO'SIAC**, _noun_ A conference or conversation of philosophers at a banquet.
