# Safflow

**SAF'FLOW**,

**SAF'FLOW**ER, _noun_ The plant, bastard saffron, of the genus Carthamus.

**SAF'FLOWER**, _noun_ A deep red fecula separated from orange-colored flowers, particularly those of the Carthamustinctorius; called also Spanish red and China lake.

The dried flowers of the Carthamustinctorius.
