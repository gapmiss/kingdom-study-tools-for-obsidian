# Sledding

**SLED'DING**, _participle present tense_ Conveying on a sled.

**SLED'DING**, _noun_ The act of transporting on a sled.

**2.** The means of conveying on sleds; snow sufficient for the running of sleds. Thus we say in America, when there is snow sufficient to run a sled, it is good _sledding_ during the winter.
