# Servitude

**SERV'ITUDE**, _noun_ \[Latin _servitudo_ or _servitus_. See _Serve_.\]

**1.** The condition of a slave; the state of involuntary subjection to a master; slavery; bondage. Such is the state of slaves in America. A large portion of the human race is in _servitude_.

**2.** The state of a servant. \[_Less common and less proper_.\]

**3.** The condition of a conquered country.

**4.** A state of slavish dependence. Some persons may be in love with splendid _servitude_.

**5.** Servants, collectively. \[_Not in use_.\]
