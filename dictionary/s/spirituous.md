# Spirituous

**SPIRITUOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Containing spirit; consisting of refined spirit; ardent; as _spirituous_ liquors. \[This might well be written spiritous.\]

**2.** Having the quality of spirit; fine; pure; active; as the _spirituous_ part of a plant.

**3.** Lively; gay; vivid; airy. \[Not in use.\]
