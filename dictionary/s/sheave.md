# Sheave

**SHEAVE**, _noun_ In _seamen's language_, a wheel in which the rope works in a block. It is made of hard wood or a metal. When made of wood, it is sometimes _bushed_, that is, has a piece or perforated brass let into its center, the better to sustain the friction of the pin.

**SHEAVE**, _verb transitive_ To bring together; to collect. \[_Not in use_.\]
