# Sententious

**SENTEN'TIOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Abounding with sentences, axioms and maxims; short and energetic; as a _sententious_ style or discourse; _sententious_ truth.

How he apes his sire,

Ambitiously _sententious_. _Addison_.

**2.** Comprising sentences; as _sententious_ marks.

\[This should be _sentential_.\]
