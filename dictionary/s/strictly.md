# Strictly

**STRICTLY**, _adverb_

**1.** Closely; tightly.

**2.** Exactly; with nice accuracy; as, patriotism _strictly_ so called , is a noble virtue.

**3.** Positively. He commanded his son _strictly_ to proceed no further.

**4.** Rigorously; severely; without remission or indulgence.

Examine thyself _strictly_ whether thou didst not best at first.
