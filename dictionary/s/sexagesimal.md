# Sexagesimal

**SEXAGES'IMAL**, _adjective_ Sixtieth; pertaining to the number sexty. _Sexagenary_ or _sexgesimal_ arithmetic, is a method of comutation by sexties, as that which is used in dividing minutes into seconds

_Sexagesimals_, or _sexagesimal_ _fractions_, are those whose denominators proceed in the ratio of sixty; as 1/60, 1/3600, 1/21600. The denominator is sixty, or its multiple. These fractions are called also astonomical fractions, because formerly there were no others used in astronomical calculations.
