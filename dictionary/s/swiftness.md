# Swiftness

**SWIFT'NESS**, _noun_ Speed; rapid motion; quickness; celerity; velocity; rapidity. _swiftness_ is a word of general import, applicable to every kind of motion, and to every thing that moves; as the _swiftness_ of a bird; the _swiftness_ of a stream; _swiftness_ of descent in a falling body; _swiftness_ of thought, etc.
