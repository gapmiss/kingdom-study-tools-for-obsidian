# Somebody

**SOMEBODY**, _noun_ \[some and body.\]

**1.** A person unknown or uncertain; a person indeterminate. Jesus said, _somebody_ hath touched me [[Luke 8#46|Luke 8:46]]. We must draw in _somebody_ that may stand 'Twixt us and danger.

**2.** A person of consideration. Before these days rose up Theudas, boasting himself to be _somebody_ [[Acts 5#36|Acts 5:36]].
