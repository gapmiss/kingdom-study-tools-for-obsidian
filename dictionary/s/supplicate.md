# Supplicate

**SUP'PLICATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin supplico; sub and plico. See [[Suppliant]].\]

**1.** To entreat for; to seek by earnest prayer; as, to _supplicate_ blessings on christian efforts to spread the gospel.

**2.** To address in prayer; as, to _supplicate_ the throne of grace.

**SUP'PLICATE**, _verb intransitive_ To entreat; to beseech; to implore; to petition with earnestness and submission.

A man cannot brook to _supplicate_ or beg.
