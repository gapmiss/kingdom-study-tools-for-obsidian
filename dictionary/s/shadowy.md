# Shadowy

**SHAD'OWY**, _adjective_

**1.** Full of shade; dark; gloomy.

This _shadowy_ desert, unfrequented woods. _Shak_.

**2.** Not brightly luminous; faintly light.

More pleasant light

_Shadowy_ sets off the face of things. _Milton_.

**3.** Faintly representative; typical; as _shadowy_ expiations.

**4.** Unsubstantial; unreal.

Milton has brought into his poems two actors of a _shadowy_ and fictitious nature, in the persons of Sin and Death. _Addison_.

**5.** Dark; obscure; opake.

By command ere yet dim night

Her _shadowy_ cloud withdraws. _Milton_.
