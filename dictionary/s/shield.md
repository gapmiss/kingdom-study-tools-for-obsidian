# Shield

**SHIELD**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To cover, as with a shield; to cover from dasnger; to defend; to protect; to secure form assault or injury.

To see the son the vanquish'd father _shield_. _Dryden_.

Hear one that comes to _shield_ his injur'd honor. _Smith._

**2.** To ward off; to defend against; as clothes _shield_ one from the cold.
