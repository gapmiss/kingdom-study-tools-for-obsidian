# Student

**STUDENT**, _noun_ \[Latin See [[Study]].\]

**1.** A person engaged in study; one who is devoted to learning, either in a seminary or in private; a scholar; as the students of an academy, of a college or university; a medical student; a law _student_

**2.** A man devoted to books; a bookish man; as a hard student; a close _student_

Keep a gamester from dice, and a good _student_ from his books.

**3.** One who studies or examines; as a _student_ of natures works.
