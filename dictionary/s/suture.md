# Suture

**SU'TURE**, _noun_ \[Latin sutura, from suo, to sew.\]

**1.** Literally, a sewing; hence, the uniting of the parts of a wound by stitching.

**2.** The seam or joint which unites the bones of the skull; or the peculiar articulation or connection of those bones; as the coronal suture; the sagittal _suture_
