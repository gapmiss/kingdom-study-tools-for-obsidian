# Sandy

**SAND'Y**, _adjective_

**1.** Abounding with sand; full of sand; covered or sprinkled with sand; as a _sandy_ desert or plain; a _sandy_ road or soil.

**2.** Consisting of sand; not firm or solid; as a _sandy_ foundation.

**3.** Of the color of sand; of a yellowish red color; as _sandy_ hair.
