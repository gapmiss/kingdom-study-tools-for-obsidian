# Sanguinary

**SAN'GUINARY**, _adjective_ \[Latin sanguinarius, from sanguis, blood.\]

**1.** Bloody; attended with much bloodshed; murderous; as a _sanguinary_ war, contest or battle.

**2.** Blood thirsty; cruel; eager to shed blood.

Passion - makes us brutal and _sanguinary_

**SAN'GUINARY**, _noun_ A plant.
