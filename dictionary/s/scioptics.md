# Scioptics

**SCIOP'TICS**, _noun_ The science of exhibiting images of external objects, received through a double convex glass into a darkened room.

**SCIRE FA'CIAS**, _noun_ \[Latin\] In law, a judicial writ summoning a person to show cause to the court why something should not be done, as to require sureties to show cause why the plaintiff should not have execution against them for debt and damages, or to require a third person to show cause why goods in his hands by replevin, should not be delivered to satisfy the execution, etc.
