# Starry

**STARRY**, _adjective_ \[from star.\]

**1.** Abounding with stars; adorned with stars.

Above the clouds, above the _starry_ sky.

**2.** Consisting of stars; stellar; stellary; proceeding from the stars; as _starry_ light; _starry_ flame.

**3.** Shining like stars; resembling stars; as _starry_ eyes.
