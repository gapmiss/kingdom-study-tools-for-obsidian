# Summary

**SUM'MARY**, _adjective_ \[Latin summa.\] Reduced into a narrow compass, or into few words; short; brief; concise; compendious; as a _summary_ statement of arguments or objections; a _summary_ proceeding or process.

**SUM'MARY**, _noun_ An abridged account; an abstract, abridgment or compendium, containing the sum or substance of a fuller account; as the comprehensive _summary_ of our duty to God in the first table of the law.
