# Swig

**SWIG**, _verb transitive_ or i. To drink by large draughts; to suck greedily.

**SWIG**, _noun_ A large draught. \[Vulgar.\]

**1.** In seamen's language, a pulley with ropes which are not parallel.

**SWIG**, _verb transitive_ To castrate, as a ram, by binding the testicles tight with a string. \[Local.\]
