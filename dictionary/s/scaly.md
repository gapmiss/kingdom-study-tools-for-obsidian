# Scaly

**SCA'LY**, _adjective_ \[from scale.\]

**1.** Covered or abounding with scales; rough; as a _scaly_ fish; the _scaly_ crocodile.

**2.** Resembling scales, lamina or layers.

**3.** In botany, composed of scales lying over each other, as a _scaly_ bulb; having scales scattered over it, as a _scaly_ stem.
