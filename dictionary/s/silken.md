# Silken

**SILKEN**, _adjective_ silk'n.

**1.** Made of silk; as _silken_ cloth; a _silken_ vail.

**2.** Like silk; soft to the touch.

**3.** Soft; delicate; tender; smooth; as mild and _silken_ language.

**4.** Dressed in silk; as a _silken_ wanton.

**SILKEN**, _verb transitive_ silk'n. To render soft or smooth.
