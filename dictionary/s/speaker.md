# Speaker

**SPE'AKER**, _noun_

**1.** One that speaks, in what ever manner.

**2.** One that proclaims or celebrates. -No other _speaker_ of my living actions.

**3.** One that utters or pronounces a discourse; usually, one that utters a speech in public. We say, a man is a good _speaker_ or a bad _speaker_

**4.** The person who presides in a deliberative assembly, preserving order and regulating the debates; as the _speaker_ of the house of commons; the _speaker_ of a house or representatives.
