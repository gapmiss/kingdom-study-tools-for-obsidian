# Shrivel

**SHRIVEL**, _verb intransitive_ _shriv'l_. \[from the root of _rivel_.\] To comtract; to draw or be drawn into wrinkles; to shrink and form corrugations; as, a leaf _shrivels_ in the hot sun; the skin _shrivels_ with age.

**SHRIV'EL**, _verb transitive_ To contract into wrinkles; to cause to shrink into corrugations. A scorching sun _shrivels_ the blades of corn.

Snd _shrivel'd_ herbs on withering stems decay. _Dryden_.
