# Sojourn

**SOJOURN**, _verb intransitive_ so'jurn. To dwell for a time; to dwell or live in a place as a temporary resident, or as a stranger, not considering the place as his permanent habitation. So Abram sojourned in Egypt. [[Genesis 12#10|Genesis 12:10]].

**SO'JOURN**, _noun_ A temporary residence, as that of a traveler in a foreign land.
