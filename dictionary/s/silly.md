# Silly

**SIL'LY**, _adjective_ \[Heb. This may be radically the same word, with a prefix. Class Sl. No. 26\]

**1.** Weak in intellect; foolish; witless; destitute of ordinary strength of mind; simple; as a _silly_ man; a _silly_ child.

**2.** Proceeding from want of understanding or common judgment; characterized by weakness of folly; unwise; as _silly_ thoughts; _silly_ actions; a _silly_ scheme; writings stupid or _silly_

**3.** Weak; helpless. After long storms- With which my _silly_ bark was toss'd
