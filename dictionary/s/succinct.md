# Succinct

**SUCCINCT**', _adjective_ \] Latin succinctus; sub and cingo, to surround.\]

**1.** Tucked up; girded up; drawn up to permit the legs to be free.

Hib habit fit for speed _succinct_ \[Little used.\]

**2.** Compressed into a narrow compass; short; brief; concise; as a _succinct_ account of the proceedings of the council.

Let all your precepts be _succinct_ and clear.
