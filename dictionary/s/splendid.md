# Splendid

**SPLENDID**, _adjective_ \[Latin , to shine. See [[Plain]].\]

**1.** Properly, shining; very bright; as a _splendid_ sun. Hence,

**2.** Showy; magnificent; sumptuous; pompous; as a _splendid_ palace; a _splendid_ procession; a _splendid_ equipage; a _splendid_ feast or entertainment.

**3.** Illustrious; heroic; brilliant; as a _splendid_ victory.

**4.** Illustrious; famous; celebrated; as a _splendid_ reputation.
