# Suppression

**SUPPRES'SION**, _noun_ \[Latin suppressio.\]

**1.** The act of suppressing, crushing or destroying; as the _suppression_ of a riot, insurrection or tumult.

**2.** The act of retaining from utterance, vent or disclosure; concealment; as the _suppression_ of truth, of reports, of evidence and the like.

**3.** The retaining of any thing from public notice; as the _suppression_ of a letter or any writing.

**4.** The stoppage, obstruction or morbid retention of discharges; as the _suppression_ of urine, of diarrhea or other discharge.

**5.** In grammar or composition, omission; as the _suppression_ of a word.
