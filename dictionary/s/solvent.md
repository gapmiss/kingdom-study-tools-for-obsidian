# Solvent

**SOLV'ENT**, _adjective_

**1.** Having the power of dissolving; as a _solvent_ body.

**2.** Able to pay all just debts. The merchant is _solvent_

**3.** Sufficient to pay all just debts. The estate is _solvent_

**SOLV'ENT**, _noun_ A fluid that dissolves any substance, is called the _solvent_
