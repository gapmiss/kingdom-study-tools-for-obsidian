# Snaky

**SNA'KY**, _adjective_

**1.** Pertaining to a snake or to snakes; resembling a snake; serpentine; winding.

**2.** Sly; cunning; insinuating; deceitful. So to the coast of Jordan he directs his easy steps, girded with _snaky_ wiles.

**3.** Having serpents; as a _snaky_ rod or want. That sanky headed gorgon shield.
