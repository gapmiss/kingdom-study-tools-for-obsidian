# Sonorous

**SONO'ROUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin sonorus, from sonus, sound.\]

**1.** Giving sound when struck. Metals are _sonorous_ bodies.

**2.** Loud sounding; giving a clear or loud sound; as a _sonorous_ voice.

**3.** Yielding sound; as, the vowels are _sonorous_

**4.** High sounding; magnificent of sound. The Italian opera, amidst all the meanness and familiarity of the thoughts, has something beautiful and _sonorous_ in the expression.
