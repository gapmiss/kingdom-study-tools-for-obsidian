# Sufficiency

**SUFFI'CIENCY**, _noun_ The state of being adequate to the end proposed.

His _sufficiency_ is such, that he bestows and possesses, his plenty being unexhausted.

**1.** Qualification for any purpose.

I am not so confident of my own _sufficiency_ as not willingly to admit the counsel of others.

**2.** Competence; adequate substance or means.

An elegant _sufficiency_ content.

**3.** Supply equal to wants; ample stock or fund.

**4.** Ability; adequate power.

Our _sufficiency_ is of God. [[2 Corinthians 3#5|2 Corinthians 3:5]].

**5.** Conceit; self-confidence. \[See [[Self-sufficiency]].\]
