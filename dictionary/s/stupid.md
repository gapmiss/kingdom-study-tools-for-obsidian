# Stupid

**STUPID**, _adjective_ \[Latin , to be stupefied, properly to stop. See [[Stop]].\]

**1.** Very dull; insensible; senseless; wanting in understanding; heavy; sluggish.

O that men should be so _stupid_ grown, as to forsake the living God.

With wild surprise, a moment _stupid_ motionless he stood.

**2.** Dull; heavy; formed without skill or genius.

Observe what loads of _stupid_ rhymes oppress us in corrupted times.
