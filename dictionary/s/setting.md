# Setting

**SET'TING**, _participle present tense_ Placing; putting; fixing; studding; appointing; sinking below the horizon, etc.

**SET'TING**, _noun_

**1.** The act of putting, placing, fixing or establishing.

**2.** The act of sinking below the horizon. The _setting_ of stars is of three kinds, _cosmical_, _acronical_, and _heliacal_. \[See these words.\]

**3.** The act or manner of taking birds by a _setting_ dog.

**4.** Inclosure; as _settings_ of stones.

**5.** The direction of a current at sea.
