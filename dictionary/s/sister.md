# Sister

**SIS'TER**, _noun_

**1.** A female born of the same patents; correlative to brother.

**2.** A woman of the same faith; a female fellow christian. If a brother or _sister_ be naked and destitute of daily food. [[James 2#15|James 2:15]].

**3.** A female of the same kind.

**4.** One of the same kind, or of the same condition; as sister-fruits.

**5.** A female of the same society; as the nuns of a convent.

**SIS'TER**, _verb transitive_ To resemble closely.

**SIS'TER**, _verb intransitive_ To be akin; to be near to. \[Little used.\]
