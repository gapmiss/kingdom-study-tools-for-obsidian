# Susceptible

**SUSCEP'TIBLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin suscipio, to take; sub and capio.\]

**1.** Capable of admitting any thing additional, or any change, affection or influence; as a body _susceptible_ of color or of alteration; a body _susceptible_ of pain; a heart _susceptible_ of love or of impression.

**2.** Tender; capable of impression; impressible. The minds of children are more _susceptible_ than those of persons more advanced in life.

**3.** Having nice sensibility; as a man of a _susceptible_ heart.
