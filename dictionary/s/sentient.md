# Sentient

**SENTIENT**, _adjective_ _sen'shent_. \[Latin _sentiens_, _sentio_.\] That perceives; having the faculty of perception. Man is a _sentient_ being; he possesses a _sentient_ principle.

**SEN'TIENT**, _noun_

**1.** A being or person that has the faculty of perception.

**2.** He that perceives.
