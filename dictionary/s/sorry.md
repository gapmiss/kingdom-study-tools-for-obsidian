# Sorry

**SOR'RY**, _adjective_

**1.** Grieved for the loss of some good; pained for some evil that has happened to one's self or friends or country. It does not ordinarily imply severe grief, but rather slight or transient regret. It may be however, and often is used to express deep grief. We are _sorry_ to lose the company of those we love; we are _sorry_ to lose friends or property; we are _sorry_ for the misfortunes of our friends or of out country. And the king was _sorry_ [[Matthew 14#9|Matthew 14:9]].

**2.** Melancholy; dismal.
