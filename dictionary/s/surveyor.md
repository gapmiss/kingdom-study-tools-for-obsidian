# Surveyor

**SURVEYOR**, _noun_ An overseer; one placed to superintend others.

**1.** One that views and examines for the purpose of ascertaining the condition, quantity, or quality of any thing; as a _surveyor_ of land; a _surveyor_ of highways; surveyors of ordnance. In the customs, a gauger; an officer who ascertains the contents of casks, and the quantity of liquors subject to duty; also in the United States, an officer who ascertains the weight and quantity of goods subject to duty.
