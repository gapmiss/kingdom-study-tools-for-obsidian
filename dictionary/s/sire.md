# Sire

**SIRE**, _noun_ \[supra.\]

**1.** A father; used in poetry. And raise his issue like a loving _sire_

**2.** The male parent of a beast; particularly used of horses; as, the horse had a good _sire_ but a bad dam.

**3.** It is used in composition; as in grandsire, for grandfather; great grandsire, great grandfather.

**SIRE**, _verb transitive_ To beget; to procreate; used of beasts.
