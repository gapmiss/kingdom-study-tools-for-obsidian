# Successive

**SUCCESS'IVE**, _adjective_

**1.** Following in order or uninterrupted course, as a series of persons or things, and either in time or place; as the _successive_ revolutions of years or ages; the _successive_ kings of Egypt. The author holds this strain of declamation through seven _successive_ pages or chapters.

Send the _successive_ ills through ages down.

**2.** Inherited by succession; as a _successive_ title; a _successive_ empire. \[Little used.\]
