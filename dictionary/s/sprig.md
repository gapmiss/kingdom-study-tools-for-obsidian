# Sprig

**SPRIG**, _noun_

**1.** A small shoot or twig of a tree or other plant; a spray; as a _sprig_ of laurel or of parsely.

**2.** A brad, or nail without a head. \[Local.\]

**3.** The representation of a small branch in embroidery.

**4.** A small eye-bolt ragged at the point.

**SPRIG**, _verb transitive_ To mark or adorn with the representation of small branches; to work with sprigs; as, to _sprig_ muslin.
