# Sneeze

**SNEEZE**, _verb intransitive_

To emit air through the nose audibly and violently, by a kind of involuntary convulsive force, occasioned by irriatation of the inner membrance of the nose. Thus snuff or any thing that tickles the nose, makes one _sneeze_

**SNEEZE**, _noun_ A sudden and violent ejection of air through the nose with an audible sound.
