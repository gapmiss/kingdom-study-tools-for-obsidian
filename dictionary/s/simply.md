# Simply

**SIM'PLY**, _adverb_

**1.** Without art; without subtilty; artlessly; plainly. Subverting worldly strong and worldly wise By _simply_ meek.

**2.** Of itself; without addition; alone. They make that good or evil, which otherwise of itself were not _simply_ the one nor the other.

**3.** Merely; solely. _simply_ the thing I am Shall make me live.

**4.** Weakly; foolishly.
