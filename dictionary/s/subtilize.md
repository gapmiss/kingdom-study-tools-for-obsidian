# Subtilize

**SUB'TILIZE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin subtilis.\]

**1.** To make thin or fine; to make less gross or coarse.

**2.** To refine; to spin into niceties; as, to _subtilize_ arguments.

**SUB'TILIZE**, _verb intransitive_ To refine in argument; to make very nice distinctions.
