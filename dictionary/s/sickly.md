# Sickly

**SICK'LY**, _adjective_

**1.** Not healthy; somewhat affected with disease; or habitually indisposed; as a _sickly_ person, or a _sickly_ constitution; a _sickly_ plant.

**2.** Producing disease extensively; marked with sickness; as a _sickly_ time; a _sickly_ autumn.

**3.** Tending to produce disease; as a _sickly_ climate.

**4.** Faint; weak; languid. The moon grows _sickly_ at the sight of day.

**SICK'LY**, _verb transitive_ To make diseased.
