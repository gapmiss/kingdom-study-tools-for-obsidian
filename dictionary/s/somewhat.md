# Somewhat

**SOMEWHAT**, _noun_ \[some and what.\]

**1.** Something, though uncertain what.

**2.** More or less; a certain quantity or degree, indeterminate. These salts have _somewhat_ of a nitrous taste.

**3.** A part, greater or less. _somewhat_ of his good sense will suffer in this transfusion, and much of the beauty of his thoughts will be lost.

**SOMEWHAT**, _adverb_ In some degree or quantity. This is _somewhat_ more or less than was expected; he is _somewhat_ aged; he is _somewhat_ disappointed; _somewhat_ disturbed.
