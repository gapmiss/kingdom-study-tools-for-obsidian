# Stickle

**STICKLE**, _verb intransitive_ \[from the practice of prize-fighters, who placed seconds with staves or sticks to interpose occasionally.\]

**1.** To take part with one side or other.

Fortune, as she wont, turnd fickle, and for the foe began to _stickle_

**2.** To contend; to contest; to altercate. Let the parties _stickle_ each for his favority doctrine.

**3.** To trim; to play fast and loose; to pass from one side to the other.

**STICKLE**, _verb transitive_ To arbitrate. \[Not in use.\]
