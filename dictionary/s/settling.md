# Settling

**SET'TLING**, _participle present tense_ Placing; fixing; establishing; regulating; adjusting; planting or colonizing; subsiding; composing; ordaining or installing; becoming the pastor of a church or parish.

**SET'TLING**, _noun_

**1.** The act of making a settlement; a planting or coloninzing.

**2.** The act of subsiding, as lees.

**3.** The adjustment of differences.

**4.** Settlings, _plural_ lees; dregs; sediment.
