# Supine

**SUPI'NE**, _adjective_ \[Latin supinus.\] Lying on the back, or with the face upward; opposed to prone.

**1.** Leaning backward; or inclining with exposure to the sun.

If the vine

On rising ground be plac'd on hills supine--

**2.** Negligent; heedless; indolent; thoughtless; inattentive.

He became pusillanimous and _supine_ and openly exposed to any temptation.

These men suffer by their _supine_ credulity.

**SU'PINE**, _noun_ \[Latin supinum.\] In grammar, a word formed from a verb, or a modification of a verb.
