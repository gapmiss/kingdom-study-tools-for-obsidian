# Spousal

**SPOUSAL**, _adjective_ \[from spouse.\] Pertaining to marriage; nuptial; matrimonial; conjugal; connubial; bridal; as _spousal_ rites; _spousal_ ornaments.

**SPOUSAL**, _noun_ \[Latin See [[Spouse]].\] Marriage; nuptials. It is now generally used in the plural; as the spousals of Hippolita.
