# Seditious

**SEDI''TIOUS**, _adjective_. \[Latin _seditiosus_.\]

**1.** Pertaining to sedition; partaking of the nature of sedition; as _seditious_ behavior; _seditious_ strife.

**2.** Tending to excite sedition; as _seditious_ words.

**3.** Disposed to excite violent or irregular opposition to law or lawful authority; turbulent; factious, or guilty of sedition; as _seditious_ citizens.
