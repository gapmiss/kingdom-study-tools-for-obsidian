# Servility

**SERVIL'ITY**,

**1.** Slavery; the condition of a slave or bondman.

To be a queen in bondage is more vile

Than is a slave in base _servility_. _Shak_.

**2.** Mean submission; baseness; slavishness.

**3.** Mean obsequiousness; slavish deference; as the common _servility_ to custom; to copy manners or opinions with _servility_.
