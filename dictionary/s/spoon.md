# Spoon

**SPOON**, _noun_

**1.** A small domestic utensil, with a bowl or concave part and a handle, for dipping liquids; as a tea spoon; a table _spoon_

**2.** An instrument consisting of a bowl or hollow iron and a long handle, used for taking earth out of holes dug for setting posts.

**SPOON**, _verb intransitive_ To put before the wind in a gale. \[I believe not now used.\]
