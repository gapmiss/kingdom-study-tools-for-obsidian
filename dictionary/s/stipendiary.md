# Stipendiary

**STIPENDIARY**, _adjective_ \[Latin\] Receiving wages or salary; performing services for a stated price or compensation.

His great _stipendiary_ prelates came with troops of evil appointed horsemen not half full.

**STIPENDIARY**, _noun_ \[supra.\] One who performs services for a settled compensation, either by the day, month or year.

If thou art become a tyrants vile stipendiary--
