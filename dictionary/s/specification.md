# Specification

**SPECIFICA'TION**, _noun_

**1.** The act of determining by a mark or limit; notation of limits. The _specification_ of limitation of the question hinders the disputers from wandering away from the precise point of inquiry.

**2.** The act of specifying; designation of particulars; particular mention; as the _specification_ of a charge against a military or naval officer.

**3.** Article or thing specified.
