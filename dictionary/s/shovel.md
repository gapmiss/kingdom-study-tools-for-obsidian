# Shovel

**SHOVEL**, _noun_ _shov'l_. An instrument consisting of a broad scoop or hollow blade with a handle; used por throwing earth or other loole substances.

**SHOVEL**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To take up and throw with a shovel; as, to _shovel_ earth into a heap or into a cart, or out of a pit.

**2.** To gather in great quantities.
