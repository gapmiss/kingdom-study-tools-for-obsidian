# Slop

**SLOP**, _verb transitive_ \[probably allied to lap.\] To drink greedily and grossly. \[Little used.\]

**SLOP**, _noun_ \[probably allied to slabber.\]

**1.** Water carelessly thrown about on a table or floor; a puddle; a soiled spot.

**2.** Mean liquor; mean liquid food.

**SLOP**, _noun_ Trowsers; a loose lower garment; drawers; hence, ready made clothes.
