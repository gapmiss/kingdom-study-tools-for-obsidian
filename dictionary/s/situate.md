# Situate

**SIT'UATE**, _adjective_ \[Latin situs, sedeo.\]

**1.** Placed, with respect to any other object; as a town _situate_ on a hill or on the sea shore.

**2.** Placed; consisting. Pleasure _situate_ in hill and dale

\[Note. In the United States, this word is less used than situated, but both are well authorized.\]
