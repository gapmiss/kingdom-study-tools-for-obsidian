# Seeming

**SEE'MING**, _ppr_.

**1.** Appearing; having the appearance or semblance, whether real or not.

**2.** _adjective_. Specious.

**SEE'MING**, _noun_.

**1.** Appearance; show; semblance.

**2.** Fair appearance.

These keep

_Seeming_ and and savor all the winter long. _Shak_.

**3.** Opinion or liking; favorable opinion.

Nothing more clear to their _seeming_. _Hooker_.

His persuasive words impregn'd

with reason to her _seeming_. _Obs_. _Milton_.
