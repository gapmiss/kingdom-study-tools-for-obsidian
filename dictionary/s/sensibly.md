# Sensibly

**SENS'IBLY**, _adverb_

**1.** In a manner to be perceived by the senses; perceptibly to the senses; as pain _sensibly_ increased; motion _sensibly_ accelerated.

**2.** With perception, either of mind or body. He feels his loss very _sensibly_.

**3.** Externally; by affecting the senses.

**4.** With quick intellectual perception.

**5.** With intelligence or good sense; judiciously. The man converses very _sensibly_ on all common topics.
