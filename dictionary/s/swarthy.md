# Swarthy

**SWARTH'Y**, _adjective_ \[See [[Swart]].\] Being of a dark hue or dusky complexion; tawny. In warm climates, the complexion of men is universally _swarthy_ or black. The Moors, Spaniards and Italians are more _swarthy_ than the French, Germans and English.

Their _swarthy_ hosts would darken all our plains.

**1.** Black; as the _swarthy_ African.
