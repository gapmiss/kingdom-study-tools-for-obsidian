# Sooty

**SOOT'Y**, _adjective_

**1.** Producing soot; as _sooty_ coal.

**2.** Consisting of soot; fuliginons; as _sooty_ matter.

**3.** Foul with soot.

**4.** Black like soot; dusky; dark; as the _sooty_ flag of Acheron.

**SOOT'Y**, _verb transitive_ To black or foul with soot. \[Not authorized.\]
