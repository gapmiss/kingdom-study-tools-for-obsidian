# Skip

**SKIP**, _verb intransitive_ To leap; to bound; to spring; as a goat or lamb.

To _skip_ over, to pass without notice; to omit.

**SKIP**, _verb transitive_ To pass over or by; to omit; to miss.

**SKIP**, _noun_ A leap; a bound; a spring.

**SKIP'-JACK**, _noun_ An upstart.

**SKIP'-KENNEL**, _noun_ A lackey; a footboy.
