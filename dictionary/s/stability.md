# Stability

**STABILITY**, _noun_ \[Latin See [[Stab]].\]

**1.** Steadiness; stableness; firmness; strength to stand without being moved or overthrown; as the _stability_ of a throne; the _stability_ of a constitution of government.

**2.** Steadiness or firmness of character; firmness of resolution or purpose; the qualities opposite to fickleness, irresolution or inconstancy. We say, a man of little _stability_ or of unusual _stability_

**3.** Fixedness; as opposed to fluidity. \[I believe not now used.\]
