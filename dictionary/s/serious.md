# Serious

**SE'RIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin _serius_.\]

**1.** Grave in manner or disposition; solemn; not light, gay or volatile; as a _serious_ man; a _serious_ habit or disposition.

**2.** Really intending what is said; being in earnest; not jesting or making false pretense. Are you _serious_, or in jest?

**3.** Important; weighty; not trijling.

The holy Sciptures bring to your ears the most _serious_ things in the world. _Young_.

**4.** Particularlyattentive to religioul concerns or one's own religious state.
