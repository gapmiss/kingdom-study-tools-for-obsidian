# Serpentine

**SER'PENTINE**, _adjective_ \[Latin _serpentinus_, from _serpens_.\]

**1.** Resembling a serpent; usually, winding and turing one way and the other, like a moving serpent; anfractuous; as a _serpentine_ road or course.

**2.** Spiral; twisted; as a _serpentine_ worm of a still.

**3.** Like a serpent; having the color or properties of a serpent.

_Serpentine tongue_, in the manege. A horse is said to have a _serpentine_ tongue, when he is constantly moving it, and sometimes passing it over the bit.

_Serpentine verse_, a verse which begins and ends with the same word.
