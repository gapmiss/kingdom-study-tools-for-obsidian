# Stout

**STOUT**, _adjective_

**1.** Strong; lusty.

A stouter champion never handled sword.

**2.** Bold; intrepid; valiant; brave.

He lost the character of a bold, _stout_ magnanimous man.

**3.** Large; bulky. \[A popular use of the word.\]

**4.** Proud; resolute; obstinate.

The lords all stand to clear their cause, most resolutely _stout_

**5.** Strong; firm; as a _stout_ vessel.

**STOUT**, _noun_ A cant name for strong beer.
