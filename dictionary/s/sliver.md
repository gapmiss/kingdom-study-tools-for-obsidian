# Sliver

**SLIV'ER**, _verb transitive_ To cut or divide into long thin pieces, or into very small pieces; to cut or rend lengthwise; as, to _sliver_ wood.

**SLIV'ER**, _noun_ A long piece cut or rent off, or a piece cut or rent lengthwise. In Scotland, it is said to signify a slice; as a _sliver_ of beef.
