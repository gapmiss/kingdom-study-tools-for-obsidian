# Superscription

**SUPERSCRIP'TION**, _noun_ The act of superscribing.

**1.** That which is written or engraved on the outside, or above something else.

The _superscription_ of his accusation was written over, THE KING OF THE JEWS. [[Mark 15#26|Mark 15:26]]. [[Luke 23#38|Luke 23:38]].

**2.** An impression of letters on coins. [[Matthew 22#20|Matthew 22:20]].
