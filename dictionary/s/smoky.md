# Smoky

**SMO'KY**, _adjective_

**1.** Emitting smoke; fumid; as _smoky_ fires.

**2.** Having the appearance or nature of smoke; as a _smoky_ fog.

**3.** Filled with smoke, or with a vapor resembling it; thick. New England in autumn frequently has a _smoky_ atmosphere.

**4.** Subject to be filled with smoke from the chimneys or fire-places; as a _smoky_ house.

**5.** Tarnished with smoke; noisome with smoke; as _smoky_ rafters; _smoky_ cells.
