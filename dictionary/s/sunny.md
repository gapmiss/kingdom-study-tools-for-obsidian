# Sunny

**SUN'NY**, _adjective_ \[from sun.\] Like the sun; bright.

**1.** Proceeding from the sun; as _sunny_ beams.

**2.** Exposed to the rays of the sun; warmed by the direct rays of the sun; as the _sunny_ side of a hill or building.

Her blooming mountains and her _sunny_ shores.

**3.** Colored by the sun.

Her _sunny_ locks

Hang on her temples like a golden fleece.
