# Scandalous

**SCAN'DALOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Giving offense.

Nothing _scandalous_ or offensive to any.

**2.** Opprobrious; disgraceful to reputation; that brings shame or infamy; as a _scandalous_ crime or vice. How perverted must be the mind that considers seduction or dueling less _scandalous_ than larceny!

**3.** Defamatory.
