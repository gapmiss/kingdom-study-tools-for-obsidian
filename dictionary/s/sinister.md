# Sinister

**SIN'ISTER**, _adjective_ \[Latin Probably the primary sense is weak, defective.\]

**1.** Left; on the left hand, or the side of the left hand; opposed to dexter or right; as the _sinister_ cheek; or the _sinister_ side of an escutcheon.

**2.** Evil; bad; corrupt; perverse; dishonest; as _sinister_ means; _sinister_ purpose. He scorns to undermine another's interest by any _sinister_ or inferior arts.

**3.** Unlucky; inauspicious.

**4.** _sinister_ aspect, in astrology, an appearance of two planets happening according to the succession of the signs; as Saturn in Aries, and Mars in the same degree of Gemini.
