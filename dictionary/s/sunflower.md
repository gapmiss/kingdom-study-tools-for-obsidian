# Sunflower

**SUN'FLOWER**, _noun_ \[sun and flower.\] A plant of the genus Helianthus; so called from the form and color of its flower, or from its habit of turning to the sun. The bastard _sunflower_ is of the genus Helenium; the dwarf _sunflower_ is of the genus Rudbeckia, and another of the genus Tetragonotheca; the little _sunflower_ is of the genus Cistus.
