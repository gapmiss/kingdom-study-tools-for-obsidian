# Sheep

**SHEEP**, _noun_ _sing_. and _plu_.

**1.** An animal of the genus Ovis, which is one of the most useful species that the Creator has bestowed upon man, and its wool constitutes a principal material of warm clothing, and its flesh is a great article of fool. The _sheep_ is remarkable for its harmless temper ant its tmidity. the varieties are numerous.

**2.** In contempt, a silly fellow.

**3.** Figuratively, God's people are called _sheep_, as being under the government and protection of Christ, the great Shepherd.
