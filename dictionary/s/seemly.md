# Seemly

**SEE'MLY**, _adjective_. Becoming; fit; suited to the object, occasion, purpose or character; suitable.

Suspense of judgement and excercise of charity were safer and _seemlier_ for christian men, than the hot pursuit of these controversies. _Hooker_.

Honor is not _seemly_ for a fool. [[Proverbs 26#1|Proverbs 26:1]].

**SEE'MLY**, _adv_. In a decent or suitable manner.
