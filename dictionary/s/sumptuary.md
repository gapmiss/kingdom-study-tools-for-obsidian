# Sumptuary

**SUMP'TUARY**, _adjective_ \[Latin sumptuarius, from sumptus, expense.\]

Relating to expense. _sumptuary_ laws or regulations are such as restrain or limit the expenses of citizens in apparel, food, furniture, etc. _sumptuary_ laws are abridgments of liberty, and of very difficult execution. They can be justified only on the ground of extreme necessity.
