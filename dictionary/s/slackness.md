# Slackness

**SLACK'NESS**, _noun_

**1.** Looseness; the state opposite to tension; not tightness or rigidness; as the _slackness_ of a cord or rope.

**2.** Remissness; negligence; inattention; as the _slackness_ of men in business or duty; _slackness_ in the performance of engagements.

**3.** Slowness; tardiness; want of tendency; as the _slackness_ of flesh to heal.

**4.** Weakness; not intenseness.
