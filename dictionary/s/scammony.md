# Scammony

**SCAM'MONY**, _noun_ \[Latin scammonia.\]

**1.** A plant of the genus convolvulus.

**2.** A gum resin, obtained from the plant of that name, of a blackish gray color, a strong nauseous smell, and a bitter and very acrid taste. The best _scammony_ comes from Aleppo, in light spungy masses, easily friable. That of Smyrna is black, ponderous, and mixed with extraneous matter.
