# Sacking

**SACK'ING**, _participle present tense_ Taking by assault and plundering or pillaging.

**SACK'ING**, _noun_ The act of taking by storm and pillaging.

**SACK'ING**, _noun_

**1.** Cloth of which sacks or bags are made.

**2.** The coarse cloth or canvas fastened to a bedstead for supporting the bed.
