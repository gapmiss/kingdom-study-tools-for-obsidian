# Swarth

**SWARTH**, sworth, _adjective_

**1.** Being of a dark hue; moderately black; tawny.

A nation strange with visage swart.

\[I believe swart and _swarth_ are never used in the United States, certainly not in New England. Swarthy is a common word.\]

**2.** Gloomy; malignant. \[Not in use.\]

**SWART**, _verb transitive_ To make tawny.
