# Speculate

**SPEC'ULATE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin speculor, to view, to contemplate, from specio, to see.\]

**1.** To meditate; to contemplate; to consider a subject by turning it in the mind and viewing it in its different aspects and relations; as, to _speculate_ on political events; to _speculate_ on the probable results of a discovery.

**2.** In commerce, to purchase land, goods, stock or other things, with the expectation of an advance in price, and of selling the articles with a profit by means of such advance; as, to _speculate_ in coffee, or in sugar, or in six percent stock, or in bank stock.

**SPEC'ULATE**, _verb transitive_ To consider attentively; as, to _speculate_ the nature of a thing. \[Not in use.\]
