# Segregate

**SEG'REGATE**, _v_. _t_. \[Latin _segrego_; _se_, from, and _grex_, flock.\] To separate from others; to set apart.

**SEG'REGATE**, _adjective_. Select. \[_Little used_.\]

_Segregate polygamy_, (_Polygamia segregata_, Linne, ) a mode of inflorescence, when several florets comprehended within a common calyx, are furnished also with their proper perianths.

_Martyn_.
