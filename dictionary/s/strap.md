# Strap

**STRAP**, _noun_ \[Latin _strap_ and strop appear to be from stripping, and perhaps stripe also; all having resemblance to a strip of bark peeled from a tree.\]

**1.** A long narrow slip of cloth or lether, of various forms and for various uses; as the _strap_ of a shoe or boot; straps for fastening trunks or other baggage, for stretching limbs in surgery, etc.

**2.** In botany, the flat part of the corollet in ligulate florets; also, an appendage to the leaf in some grasses.

**STRAP**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To beat or chastise with a _strap_

**2.** To fasten or bind with a _strap_

**3.** To rub on a _strap_ for sharpening, as a razor.
