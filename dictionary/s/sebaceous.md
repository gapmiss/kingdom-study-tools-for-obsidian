# Sebaceous

**SEBA'CEOUS**, _adjective_ \[Low **L**. _sebaceus_, from _sebum_, _sevum_, tallow.\] Made of tallow or fat; pretaining to fat.

_Sebaceous humor_, a suet-like or glutinous matter secreted by the _sebaceous_ glands, which serves to defent the skin and keep it soft. _Coxe_. _Parr_.

_Sebaceous glands_, small glands seated in the cellular membrane under the skin, which secrete the _sebaceous_ humor. _Parr_.
