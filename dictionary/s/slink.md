# Slink

**SLINK**, _verb intransitive_ _preterit tense_ and _participle passive_ slunk.

**1.** To sneak; to creep away meanly; to steal away. He would pinch the children in the dark, and then _slink_ into a corner.

**2.** To miscarry, as a beast.

**SLINK**, _verb transitive_ To cast prematurely; to miscarry of; as the female of a beast.

**SLINK**, _noun_ Produced prematurely, as the young of a beast.
