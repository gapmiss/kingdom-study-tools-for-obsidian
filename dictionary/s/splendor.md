# Splendor

**SPLENDOR**, _noun_ \[Latin See [[Plant and Planet]].\]

**1.** Great brightness; brilliant luster; as the _splendor_ of the sun.

**2.** Great show of richness and elegance; magnificence; as the _splendor_ of equipage or of royal robes.

**3.** Pomp; parade; as the _splendor_ of a procession or of ceremonies.

**4.** Brilliance; eminence; as the _splendor_ of a victory.
