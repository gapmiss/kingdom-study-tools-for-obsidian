# Sprinkling

**SPRINKLING**, _participle present tense_

**1.** Dispersing, as a liquid or as dust.

**2.** Scattering on, in fine drops or particles.

**SPRINKLING**, _noun_

**1.** The act of scattering in small drops or parcels.

**2.** A small quantity falling in distinct drops or parts, or coming moderately; as a _sprinkling_ of rain or snow.
