# Socket

**SOCK'ET**, _noun_

**1.** The little hollow tube or place in which a candle is fixed in the candlestick. And in the sockets oily bubbles dance.

**2.** Any hollow thing or place which receives and holds something else; as the sockets of the teeth or of the eyes. his eyeballs in their hollow sockets sink. Gomphosis is the connection of a tooth to its _socket_
