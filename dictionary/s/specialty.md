# Specialty

**SPECIALTY**, _noun_

**1.** Particularity. _specialty_ of rule hat been neglected.

**2.** A particular or peculiar case. Note. This word is now little used in the senses above. Its common acceptation is,

**3.** A special contract; an obligation or bond; the evidence of a debt by deed or instrument under seal. Such a debt is called a debt by _specialty_ in distinction from simple contract.
