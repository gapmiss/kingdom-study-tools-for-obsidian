# Shabby

**SHAB'BY**, _adjective_

**1.** Ragged; torn or worn to rags; as a _shabby_ coat; _shabby_ clothes.

**2.** Clothed with ragged garments.

The dean was so _shabby_\- _Swift_.

**3.** Mean; paltry; despicable; as a _shabby_ fellow; _shabby_ treatment. \[For the idea expressed by _shabby_, there is not a better word in the language.\]
