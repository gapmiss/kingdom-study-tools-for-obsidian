# Supple

**SUP'PLE**, _adjective_

**1.** Pliant; flexible; easily bent; as _supple_ joints; _supple_ fingers.

**2.** Yielding; compliant; not obstinate.

If punishment--makes not the will _supple_ it hardens the offender.

**3.** Bending to the humor of others; flattering; fawning.

**4.** That makes plaint; as _supple_ government.

**SUP'PLE**, _verb transitive_ To make soft and pliant; to render flexible; as, to _supple_ leather.

**1.** To make compliant.

A mother persisting till she had suppled the will of her daughter.

**SUP'PLE**, _verb intransitive_ To become soft and pliant; as stones suppled into softness.
