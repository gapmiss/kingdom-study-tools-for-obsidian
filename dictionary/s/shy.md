# Shy

**SHY**, _adjective_

**1.** Fearful of near approach; keeping at a distance through caution or timidity; shunning approach; as a _shy_ bird.

She is represented in a _shy_ retiring posture. _Addison_.

**2.** Reserved; not familiar; coy; avoiding freedom of intercourse.

What makes you so _shy_, my good friend? _Arbuthnot_.

**3.** Cautious; wary; careful to avoid committing one's self or adopting measures.

I am very _shy_ of using corrosive liquors in the preparation of medicines.

_Boyle_.

**4.** Suspicious; jealous.

Princes are by wisdom of state somewhat _shy_ of their successors. _Wotton_.
