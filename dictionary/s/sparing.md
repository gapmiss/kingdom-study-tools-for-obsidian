# Sparing

**SPA'RING**, _participle present tense_

**1.** Using frugally; forbearing; omitting to punish or destroy.

**2.** _adjective_ Scarce; little.

**3.** Scanty; not plentiful; not abundant; as a _sparing_ diet.

**4.** Saving; parsimonious. Virgil being so very _sparing_ of his words, and leaving so much to be imagined by the reader, can never be translated as he ought in amy modern tongue.
