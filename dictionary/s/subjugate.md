# Subjugate

**SUBJUGATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin Sub and jugo, to yoke. See [[Yoke]].\] To subdue and bring under the yoke of power or dominion; to conquer by force and compel to submit to the government or absolute control of another.

He subjugated a king, and called him his vassal.

\[Subjugate differs from subject only in implying a reduction to a more tyrannical or arbitrary sway; but they are often used as synonymous.\]
