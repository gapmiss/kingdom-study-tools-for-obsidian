# Sward

**SWARD**, _noun_

**1.** The skin of bacon. \[Local.\]

**2.** The grassy surface of land; turf; that part of the soil which is filled with the roots of grass, forming a kind of mat. When covered with green grass, it is called green _sward_

**SWARD**, _verb transitive_ To produce sward; to cover with _sward_

**SWARD'-CUTTER**, _noun_ An instrument for cutting _sward_ across the ridges.
