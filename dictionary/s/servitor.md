# Servitor

**SERV'ITOR**, _noun_ \[From Latin _servio_, to serve.\]

**1.** A servant; an attendant.

**2.** One that acts under another; a follower or adherent.

**3.** One that professes duty and obedience.

**4.** In _the university of Oxford_, a student who attends on another for his maintenance and learning; such as is called in Cambridge, a sizer.
