# Secrete

**SECRE'TE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To hide; to conceal; to remove from observation or the knowledge of others; as to _secrete_ stolen goods.

**2.** To _secrete_ one's self; to retire from notice into a private place; to abscend.

**3.** In _the animal economy_, to produce from the blood substances different from the blood itself, or from any of its constituents; as the glands. The liver _secretes_ bile; the salivary glands _secrete_ saliva.
