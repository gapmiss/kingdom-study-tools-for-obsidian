# Soundly

**SOUND'LY**, _adverb_ \[from sound, entire.\]

**1.** Healthily; heartily.

**2.** Severely; lustily; with heavy blows; smartly; as, to beat one _soundly_

**3.** Truly; without fallacy or error; as, to judge or reason _soundly_

**4.** Firmly; as a doctrine _soundly_ settled.

**5.** Fast; closely; so as not to be easily awakened; as, to sleep _soundly_
