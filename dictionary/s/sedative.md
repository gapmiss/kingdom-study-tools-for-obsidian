# Sedative

**SEDATIVE**, _adjective_ \[**L**. _sedo_, to calm.\] In _medicine_, moderating muscular action or animal energy.

**SED'ATIVE**, _noun_ A midicine that moderates muscular action or animal energy. _Se defendendo_, in defending himself; the plea of a person charged with murder, who alledges that he committed the act in his own defense.
