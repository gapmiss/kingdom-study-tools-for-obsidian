# Surely

**SU'RELY**, _adverb_ Certainly; infallibly; undoubtedly.

In the day thou eatest thereof, thou shalt _surely_ die. [[Genesis 2#17|Genesis 2:17]].

He that created something out of nothing, _surely_ can raise great things out of small.

**1.** Firmly; without danger of falling.

He that walketh uprightly, walketh _surely_ [[Proverbs 10#9|Proverbs 10:9]].
