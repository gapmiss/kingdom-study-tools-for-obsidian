# Servile

**SERV'ILE**, _adjective_ \[Latin _servilis_, from _servio_, to serve.\]

**1.** Such as pertains to a servant or slave; slavish; mean; such as proceeds from dependence; as _servile_ fear; _servile_ obedience.

**2.** Held in subjection; dependent.

Ev'n fortune rules no more a _servile_ land. _Pope_.

**3.** Cringing; fawning; meanly submissive; as _servile_ flattery.

She must bend the _servile_ knee. _Thomson_.
