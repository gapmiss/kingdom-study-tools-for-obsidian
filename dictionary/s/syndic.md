# Syndic

**SYN'DIC**, _noun_ \[Latin syndicus; Gr. with, and justice.\] An officer of government, invested with different powers in different countries; a kind of magistrate entrusted with the affairs of a city or community. In Geneva, the _syndic_ is the chief magistrate. Almost all the companies in Paris, the university, etc., have their syndics. The university of Cambridge has it syndics.
