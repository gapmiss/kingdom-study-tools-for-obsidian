# Sot

**SOT**, _noun_

**1.** A stupid person; a blockhead; a dull fellow; a dolt.

**2.** A person stupefied by excessive drinking; an habitual drunkard. What can ennoble sots?

**SOT**, _verb transitive_ To stupefy; to infatuate; to besot. I hat to see a brave bold bellow sotted. \[Not much used.\] \[See Besot.\]

**SOT**, _verb intransitive_ To tipple to stupidity. \[Little used.\]
