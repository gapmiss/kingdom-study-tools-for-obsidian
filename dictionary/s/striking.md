# Striking

**STRIKING**, _participle present tense_

**1.** Hitting with a blow; impressing; imprinting; punishing; lowering, as sails or a mast, etc.

**2.** _adjective_ Affecting with strong emotions; surprising; forcible; impressive; as a _striking_ representation or image.

**3.** Strong; exact; adapted to make impression; as a _striking_ resemblance of features.
