# Sabbatic

**SABBATIC**,

**SABBATICAL**, _adjective_

**1.** Pertaining to the sabbath.

**2.** Resembling the sabbath; enjoying or bringing an intermission of labor.

**SABBATIC**al year, in the Jewish economy, was every seventh year, in which the Israelites were commanded to suffer their fields and vineyards to rest, or lie without tillage, and the year next following every seventh sabbatical year in succession, that is, every fiftieth year, was the jubilee, which was also a year of rest to the lands, and a year of redemption or release. [[Leviticus 15#1|Leviticus 15:1]].
