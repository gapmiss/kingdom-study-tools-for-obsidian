# Subsistence

**SUBSIST'ENCE**

SUBSIST'ENCY, _noun_ Real being; as a chain of differing subsistencies.

Not only the things had _subsistence_ but the very images were of some creatures existing.

**1.** Competent provisions; means of supporting life.

His viceroy could only propose to himself a comfortable _subsistence_ out of the plunder of his province.

**2.** That which supplies the means of living; as money, pay or wages.

**3.** Inherence in something else, as the _subsistence_ of qualities in bodies.
