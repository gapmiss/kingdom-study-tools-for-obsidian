# Surplus

**SUR'PLUS**, _noun_ \[Latin id., more.\]

**1.** Overplus; that which remains when use is satisfied; excess beyond what is prescribed or wanted. In the United States, the _surplus_ of wheat and rye not required for consumption or exportation, is distilled.

**2.** In law, the residuum of an estate, after the debts and legacies are paid.
