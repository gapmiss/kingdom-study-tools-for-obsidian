# Sevenfold

**SEV'ENFOLD**, _adjective_ \[_seven_ and _fold_.\] Repeated seven times; doubled seven times; increased to seven times the size or amount; as the _sevenfold_ shield of Ajax; _sevenfold_ rage.

**SEV'ENFOLD**, _adverb_ Seven times as much or often.

Whoever slayeth Cain, vengence shall be taken on him _sevenfold_. [[Genesis 4#15|Genesis 4:15]].
