# Slaver

**SLAV'ER**, _noun_ \[the same as slabber.\] Saliva driveling from the mouth.

**SLAV'ER**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To suffer the spittle to issue from the mouth.

**2.** To be besmeared with saliva.

**SLAV'ER**, _verb transitive_ To smear with saliva issuing from the mouth; to defile with drivel.
