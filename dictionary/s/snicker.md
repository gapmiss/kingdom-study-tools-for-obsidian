# Snicker

**SNICK'ER**, SNIG'GER, _verb intransitive_ \[This can have no connection with sneer. the elements and the sense are different.\] To laugh slily; or to laugh in one's sleeve. \[It is a word in common use in New WNgland, not easily defined. It signifies to laugh with small audible catches of voice, as when persons attempt to suppress loud laughter.\]
