# Squill

**SQUILL**, _noun_ \[Latin , a _squill_ a lobster or prawn.\]

**1.** A plant of the genus Scilla. It has a large acrid bulbous root like an onion, which is used in medicine.

**2.** A fish, or rather a crustaceous animal, of the genus Cancer.

**3.** An insect, called _squill_ insect form its resemblance to the fish, having a long body covered with a crust, the head broad and squat.
