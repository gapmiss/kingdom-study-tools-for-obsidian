# Symptomatic

**SYMPTOMAT'IC**

**SYMPTOMAT'IC**AL, _adjective_ Pertaining to symptoms; happening in concurrence with something; indicating the existence of something else.

**1.** In medicine, a _symptomatic_ disease is one which proceeds from some prior disorder in some part of the body. Thus a _symptomatic_ fever may proceed from local pain or local

inflammation. It is opposed to idiopathic.

**2.** According to symptoms; as a symptomatical classification of diseases.
