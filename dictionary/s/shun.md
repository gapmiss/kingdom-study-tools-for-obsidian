# Shun

**SHUN**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To avoid; to keep clear of; not to fall on or come in contact with; as, to _shun_ rocks and shoals in navigation. In _shunning_ Scylla, take care to avoid to avoid Charybdis.

**2.** To avoid; not to mix or associate with; as, to _shun_ evil company.

**3.** To avoid; not to practice; as, to _shun_ vice.

**4.** To avoid; to escape; as, to _shun_ a blow.

**5.** To avoid; to decline; to neglect.

I haved not _shunned_ to declare the whole counsel of God. [[Acts 20#1|Acts 20:1]].
