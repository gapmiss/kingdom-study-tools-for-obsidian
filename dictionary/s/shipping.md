# Shipping

**SHIP'PING**, _participle present tense_

**1.** Putting on board of a ship or vessel; receiving on board.

**2.** _adjective_ Relating to ships; as _shipping_ concerns.

**SHIP'PING**, _noun_ Ships in general; ships or vessels of any kind for navigation. The _shipping_ of the English nation exceeds that of any other. The tunnage of _shipping_ belonging to the United States is second only to that of Great Britain.

_To take _shipping__ to embark; to enter on board a ship or vessel for conveyance or passage.

**SHIP'-SHAPE**, _adverb_ In a seamanlike manner.
