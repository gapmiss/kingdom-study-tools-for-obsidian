# Spaniel

**SPAN'IEL**, _noun_

**1.** A dog used in sports of the field, remarkable for his sagacity and obedience.

**2.** A mean, cringing, fawning person.

**SPAN'IEL**, _adjective_ Like a spaniel; mean; fawning.

**SPAN'IEL**, _verb intransitive_ TO fawn; to cringe; to be obsequious.

**SPAN'IEL**, _verb transitive_ To follow like a _spaniel_
