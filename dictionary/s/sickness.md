# Sickness

**SICK'NESS**, _noun_

**1.** Nausea; squeamishness; as _sickness_ of the stomach.

**2.** State of being diseased. I do lament the _sickness_ of the king.

**3.** Disease; malady; a morbid state of the body of an animal or plant, in which the organs do not perfectly perform their natural functions. Trust not too much your now resistless charms; Those age or _sickness_ soon or late disarms. Himself took our infirmities, and bore our sicknesses. [[Matthew 8#17|Matthew 8:17]].
