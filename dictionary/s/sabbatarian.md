# Sabbatarian

**SABBATARIAN**, _noun_ \[from sabbath.\] One who observes the seventh day of the week as the sabbath, instead of the first. A sect of baptists are called sabbatarians. They maintain that the Jewish sabbath has not been abrogated.

**SABBATARIAN**, _adjective_ Pertaining to those who keep Saturday, or the seventh day of the week, as the sabbath.
