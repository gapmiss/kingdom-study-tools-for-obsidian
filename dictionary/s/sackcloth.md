# Sackcloth

**SACK'CLOTH**, _noun_ \[sack and cloth.\] Cloth of which sacks are made; coarse cloth. This word is chiefly used in Scripture to denote a cloth or garment worn in mourning, distress or mortification.

Gird you with _sackcloth_ and mourn before Abner. [[2 Samuel 3#31|2 Samuel 3:31]].
