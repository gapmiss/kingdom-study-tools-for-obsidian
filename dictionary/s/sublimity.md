# Sublimity

**SUBLIM'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin sublimitas.\]

**1.** Elevation of place; lofty highth.

**2.** Highth in excellence; loftiness of nature or character;

moral grandeur; as God's incomprehensible _sublimity_

The _sublimity_ of the character of Christ owes nothing to his historians.

**3.** In oratory and composition, lofty conceptions, or such conceptions expressed in corresponding language; loftiness of sentiment or style.

Milton's distinguishing excellence lies in the _sublimity_ of his thoughts.
