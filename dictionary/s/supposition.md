# Supposition

**SUPPOSI'TION**, _noun_ The act of laying down, imagining or admitting as true or existing, what is known not to be true, or what is not proved.

**1.** The position of something known not to be true or not proved; hypothesis.

This is only an infallibility upon _supposition_ that if a thing be true, it is impossible to be false.

**2.** Imagination; belief without full evidence.
