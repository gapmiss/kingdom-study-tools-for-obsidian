# Hatred

**HA'TRED**, _noun_ Great dislike or aversion; hate; enmity. _hatred_ is an aversion to evil, and may spring from utter disapprobation, as the _hatred_ of vice or meanness; or it may spring from offenses or injuries done by fellow men, or from envy or jealousy, in which case it is usually accompanied with malevolence or malignity. Extreme _hatred_ is abhorrence or detestation.
