# Humorist

**HU'MORIST**, _noun_ One who conducts himself by his own inclination, or bent of mind; one who gratifies his own humor.

The _humorist_ is one that is greatly pleased or greatly displeased with little things; his actions seldom directed by the reason and nature of things.

**1.** One that indulges humor in speaking or writing; one who has a playful fancy or genius.

**2.** One who has odd conceits; also, a wag; a droll.
