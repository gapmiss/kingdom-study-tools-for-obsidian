# Hearken

**HE'ARKEN**, _verb intransitive_ h'arken.

**1.** To listen; to lend the ear; to attend to what is uttered, with eagerness or curiosity.

The furies _hearken_ and their snakes uncurl.

**2.** To attend; to regard; to give heed to what is uttered; to observe or obey.

Hearken, O Israel, to the statutes and the judgments which I teach you. [[Deuteronomy 4#1|Deuteronomy 4:1]].

**3.** To listen; ; to attend; to grant or comply with.

Hearken thou to the supplication of thy servant. [[I Kings 8#30|I Kings 8:30]].

**HE'ARKEN**, _verb transitive_ h'arken. To hear by listening. \[Little used.\]
