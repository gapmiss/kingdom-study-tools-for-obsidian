# Honey

**HON'EY**, _noun_ hun'y.

**1.** A sweet vegetable juice, collected by bees from the flowers of plants, and deposited in cells of the comb in hives. _honey_ when pure, is of a moderate consistence, of a whitish color, tinged with yellow, sweet to the taste, of an agreeable smell, soluble in water, and becoming vinous by fermentation. In medicine, it is useful as a detergent and aperient. It is supposed to consist of sugar, mucilage, and an acid.

**2.** Sweetness; lusciousness.

The king hath found

Matter against him, that forever mars

The _honey_ of his language.

**3.** A word of tenderness; sweetness; sweet one.

**HON'EY**, _verb transitive_ To talk fondly. \[Little used.\]

**1.** To sweeten.
