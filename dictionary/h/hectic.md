# Hectic

**HEC'TIC**

**HEC'TIC**AL, _adjective_ \[Gr. habit of body, to have.\] Habitual; denoting a slow, continual fever, marked by preternatural, though remitting heat, which precedes and accompanies the consumption or phthisis; as a _hectic_ fever.

**1.** Affected with _hectic_ fevers; as a _hectic_ patient.

**2.** Troubled with a morbid heat.

No _hectic_ student scares the gentle maid.

**HEC'TIC**, _noun_ A hectic; or habitual fever.
