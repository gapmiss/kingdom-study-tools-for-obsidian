# Hither

**HITH'ER**, _adverb_

**1.** To this place; used with verbs signifying motion; as, to come hither; to proceed hither; to bring _hither_

**2.** _hither_ and thither, to this place and that.

**3.** To this point; to this argument or topic; to this end. \[Little used and not to be encouraged.\]

Hither we refer whatever belongs to the highest perfection of man.

**HITH'ER**, _adjective_ Nearest; towards the person speaking; as on the _hither_ side of a hill; the _hither_ end of the building.
