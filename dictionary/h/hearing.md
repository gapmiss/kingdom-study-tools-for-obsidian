# Hearing

**HE'ARING**, _participle present tense_ Perceiving by the ear, as sound.

**1.** Listening to; attending to; obeying; observing what is commanded.

**2.** Attending to witnesses or advocates in a judicial trial; trying.

**HE'ARING**, _noun_ The faculty or sense by which sound is perceived.

**1.** Audience; attention to what is delivered; opportunity to be heard. I waited on the minister, but could not obtain a _hearing_

**2.** Judicial trial; attention to the facts, testimony and arguments in a cause between parties, with a view to a just decision.

**3.** The act of perceiving sounds; sensation or perception of sound.

I have heard of thee by the _hearing_ of the ear. [[Job 42#5|Job 42:5]].

And to the others he said in my _hearing_ [[Ezekiel 9#5|Ezekiel 9:5]].

**4.** Reach of the ear; extent within which sound may be heard. He was not within _hearing_
