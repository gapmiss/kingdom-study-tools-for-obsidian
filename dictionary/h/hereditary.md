# Hereditary

**HERED'ITARY**, _adjective_

**1.** That has descended from an ancestor. He is in possession of a large _hereditary_ estate.

**2.** That may descend from an ancestor to an heir; descendible to an heir at law. The crown of Great Britain is _hereditary_

**3.** That is or may be transmitted from a parent to a child; as _hereditary_ pride; _hereditary_ bravery; _hereditary_ disease.
