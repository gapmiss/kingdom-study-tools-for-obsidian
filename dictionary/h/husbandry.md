# Husbandry

**HUS'BANDRY**, _noun_ The business of a farmer, comprehending agriculture or tillage of the ground, the raising, managing and fattening of cattle and other domestic animals, the management of the dairy and whatever the land produces.

**1.** Frugality; domestic economy; good management; thrift. But in this sense we generally prefix good; as good _husbandry_

**2.** Care of domestic affairs.
