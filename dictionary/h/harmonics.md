# Harmonics

**HARMON'ICS**,_noun_ Harmonious sounds; consonances.

**1.** The doctrine or science of musical sounds.

**2.** Derivative sounds, generated with predominant sounds, and produced by subordinate vibrations of a chord or string, when its whole length vibrates. These shorter vibrations produce more acute sounds, and are called acute _harmonics_

**3.** Grave _harmonics_ are low sounds which accompany every perfect consonance of two sounds.
