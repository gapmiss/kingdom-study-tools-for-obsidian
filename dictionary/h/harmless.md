# Harmless

**H'ARMLESS**, _adjective_ Not hurtful or injurious; innoxious. Ceremonies are _harmless_ in themselves.

**1.** Unhurt; undamaged; uninjured; as, to give bond to save another _harmless_

**2.** Innocent; not guilty.

Who is holy, _harmless_ undefiled, separate from sinners. [[Hebrews 7#26|Hebrews 7:26]].
