# Hare

**HARE**, _noun_ A quadruped of the genus Lepus, with long ears, a short tail, soft hair, and a divided upper lip. It is a timid animal, often hunted for sport or for its flesh, which is excellent food. It moves by leaps, and is remarkable for its fecundity.

**1.** A constellation.

**HARE**, _verb transitive_ To fright, or to excite, tease and harass, or worry. \[Not used. See [[Harry]].\]
