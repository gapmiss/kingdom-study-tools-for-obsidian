# Humane

**HUMA'NE**, _adjective_ \[supra.\] Having the feelings and dispositions proper to man; having tenderness, compassion, and a disposition to treat others with kindness; particularly in relieving them when in distress, or in captivity, when they are helpless or defenseless; kind; benevolent.

**1.** Inclined to treat the lower orders of animals with tenderness.
