# Haze

**HAZE**, _noun_ \[The primary sense of this word is probably to mix, or to turn, stir and make thick.\]

Fog; a watery vapor in the air, or a dry vapor like smoke, which renders the air thick.

**HAZE**

, _verb intransitive_ To be foggy. \[A local word.\]

**HAZE**, _verb transitive_ To frighten. \[Not used.\]
