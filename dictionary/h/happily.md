# Happily

**HAP'PILY**, _adverb_ \[See [[Happy]].\] By good fortune; fortunately; luckily; with success.

Preferr'd by conquest, _happily_ o'erthrown.

**1.** In a happy state; in a state of felicity.

He lived _happily_ with his consort.

**2.** With address or dexterity; gracefully; in a manner to ensure success.

Formed by thy converse, _happily_ to steer

From grave to gay, from lively to severe.

**3.** By chance. \[See Haply.\]
