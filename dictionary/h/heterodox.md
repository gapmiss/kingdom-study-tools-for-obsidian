# Heterodox

**HET'ERODOX**, _adjective_ \[Gr. another, different, and opinion.\]

**1.** In theology, heretical; contrary to the faith and doctrines of the true church; or more precisely, contrary to the real doctrines of the Scriptures; as a _heterodox_ opinion; opposed to orthodox.

**2.** Repugnant to the doctrines or tenets of any established church.

**3.** Holding opinions repugnant to the doctrines of the Scriptures, as a _heterodox_ divine; or holding opinions contrary to those of an established church.
