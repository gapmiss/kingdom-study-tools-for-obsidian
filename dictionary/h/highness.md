# Highness

**HIGHNESS**, _noun_ hi'ness. Elevation above the surface; loftiness; altitude; highth.

**1.** Dignity; elevation in rank, character or power.

**2.** Excellence; value.

**3.** Violence; as the _highness_ of wind.

**4.** Great amount; as the _highness_ of price.

**5.** Acuteness; as the _highness_ of a note or voice.

**6.** Intenseness, as of heat.

**7.** A title of honor given to princes or other men of rank.
