# Half-penny

**H'ALF-PENNY**, _noun_ hap'penny or ha'penny. A copper coin of the value of half a penny; also, the value of half a penny. It is used in the plural.

He cheats for half-pence.

\[This coin is not current in America.\]

**H'ALF-PENNY**, _adjective_ Of the price or value of half a penny; as a _half-penny_ loaf.
