# Hypermeter

**HYPER'METER**, _noun_ \[Gr. beyond, and measure.\] Anything greater than the ordinary standard of measure.

A verse is called a _hypermeter_ when it contains a syllable more than the ordinary measure. When this is the case, the following line begins with a vowel, and the redundant syllable of the former line blends with the first of the following, and they are read as one syllable.
