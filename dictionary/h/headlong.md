# Headlong

**HEADLONG**, _adverb_ hed'long. With the head foremost; as, to fall _headlong_

**1.** Rashly; precipitately; without deliberation.

\--He hurries _headlong_ to his fate.

**2.** Hastily; without delay or respite.

**HEADLONG**, _adjective_ hed'long. Steep; precipitous.

**1.** Rash; precipitate; as _headlong_ folly.
