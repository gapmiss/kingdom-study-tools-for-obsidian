# Harlot

**H'ARLOT**, _noun_

**1.** A woman who prostitutes her body for hire; a prostitute; a common woman.

**2.** In Scripture, one who forsakes the true God and worships idols. [[Isaiah 1#21|Isaiah 1:21]].

**3.** A servant; a rogue; a cheat.

**H'ARLOT**, _adjective_ Wanton; lewd; low; base.

**H'ARLOT**, _verb intransitive_ To practice lewdness.
