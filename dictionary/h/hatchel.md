# Hatchel

**HATCH'EL**, _noun_ An instrument formed with long iron teeth set in a board, for cleaning flax or hemp from the tow, hards or coarse part. The _hatchel_ is a large species of comb.

**HATCH'EL**, _verb transitive_ To draw flax or hemp through the teeth of a _hatchel_ for separating the coarse part and broken pieces of the stalk from the fine fibrous parts.

**1.** To tease or vex, by sarcasms or reproaches; a vulgar use of the word.
