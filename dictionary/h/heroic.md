# Heroic

**HERO'IC**, _adjective_ Pertaining to a hero or heroes; as _heroic_ valor.

**1.** Becoming a hero; bold; daring; illustrious; as _heroic_ action; _heroic_ enterprises.

**2.** Brave; intrepid; magnanimous; enterprising; illustrious for valor; as Hector, the _heroic_ son of Priam; a _heroic_ race.

**3.** Productive of heroes; as a _heroic_ line in pedigree.

**4.** Reciting the achievements of heroes; as a _heroic_ poem.

**5.** Used in _heroic_ poetry or hexameter; as _heroic_ verse; a _heroic_ foot.

Heroic age, the age when the heroes, or those called the children of the gods, are supposed to have lived.
