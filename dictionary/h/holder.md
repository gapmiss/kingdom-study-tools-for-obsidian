# Holder

**HOLDER**, _noun_ One who holds or grasps in his hand, or embraces with his arms.

**1.** A tenant; one who holds land under another.

**2.** Something by which a thing is held.

**3.** One who owns or possesses; as a _holder_ of stock, or shares in a joint concern.

**4.** In ships, one who is employed in the hold.
