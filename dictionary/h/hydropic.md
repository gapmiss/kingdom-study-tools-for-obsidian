# Hydropic

**HYDROP'IC**

**HYDROP'IC**AL, _adjective_ \[Latin hydrops; Gr. dropsy; water.\]

**1.** Dropsical; diseased with extravasated water.

**2.** Containing water; caused by extravasated water; as a _hydropic_ swelling.

**3.** Resembling dropsy.

Every lust is a kind of _hydropic_ distemper, and the more we drink the more we shall thirst.
