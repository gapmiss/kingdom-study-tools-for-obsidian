# Hone

**HONE**, _noun_ A stone of a fine grit, used for sharpening instruments that require a fine edge, and particularly for setting razors. \[We never, I believe, call a _hone_ a whet-stone. The latter is a stone of coarse grit. See the word.\]

**HONE**, _verb transitive_ To rub and sharpen on a bone; as, to _hone_ a razor.

**HONE**, _verb intransitive_ To pine; to long.
