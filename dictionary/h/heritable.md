# Heritable

**HER'ITABLE**, _adjective_ \[from the root of heir, Latin haeres.\]

**1.** Capable of inheriting, or taking by descent.

By the canon law this son shall be legitimate and _heritable_

**2.** That may be inherited. \[This is the true sense.\]

**3.** Annexed to estates of inheritance. In Scot's law, _heritable_ rights are all rights that affect lands or other immovables.
