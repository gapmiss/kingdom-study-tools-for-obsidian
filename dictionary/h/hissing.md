# Hissing

**HISS'ING**, _participle present tense_ Making the noise of serpents.

**HISS'ING**, _noun_ A _hissing_ sound; an expression of scorn or contempt.

**1.** The occasion of contempt; the object of scorn and derision.

I will make this city desolate, and a _hissing_ [[Jeremiah 19#8|Jeremiah 19:8]].
