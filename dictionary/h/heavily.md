# Heavily

**HEAV'ILY**, _adverb_ hev'ily. \[from heavy.\] With great weight; as, to bear _heavily_ on a thing; to be _heavily_ loaded.

**1.** With great weight of grief; grievously; afflictively. When calamities fall _heavily_ on the christian, he finds consolation in Christ.

**2.** Sorrowfully; with grief.

_I came hither to transport the tidings,_

Which I have _heavily_ borne.
