# Hasty

**HASTY**, _adjective_ Quick; speedy; opposed to slow.

Be not _hasty_ to go out of his sight. [[Ecclesiastes 8#3|Ecclesiastes 8:3]].

**1.** Eager precipitate; rash; opposed to deliberate.

Seest thou a man that is _hasty_ in his words?

There is more hope of a fool than of him. [[Proverbs 29#20|Proverbs 29:20]].

**2.** Irritable; easily excited to wrath; passionate.

He that is _hasty_ of spirit exalteth folly. [[Proverbs 14#29|Proverbs 14:29]].

**3.** Early ripe; forward; as _hasty_ fruit. [[Isaiah 28#4|Isaiah 28:4]].
