# Harass

**HAR'ASS**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To weary; to fatigue to excess; to tire with bodily labor; as, to _harass_ an army by a long march.

**2.** To weary with importunity, care, or perplexity; to tease; to perplex.

Nature oppress'd and harrass'd out with care.

**3.** To waste or desolate.

**HAR'ASS**, _noun_ Waste; disturbance; devastation. \[Little used.\]
