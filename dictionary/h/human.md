# Human

**HU'MAN**, _adjective_ \[Latin humanus; Heb. form, species.\]

**1.** Belonging to man or mankind; pertaining or relating to the race of man; as a _human_ voice; _human_ shape; _human_ nature; _human_ knowledge; _human_ life.

**2.** Having the qualities of a man.

**3.** Profane; not sacred or divine; as a _human_ author. \[Not in use.\]
