# Harm

**H'ARM**, _noun_

**1.** Injury; hurt; damage; detriment.

Do thyself no _harm_ [[Acts 16#28|Acts 16:28]].

He shall make amends for the _harm_ he hath done in the holy thing. [[Leviticus 5#16|Leviticus 5:16]].

**2.** Moral wrong; evil; mischief; wickedness; a popular sense of the word.

**H'ARM**, _verb transitive_ To hurt; to injure; to damage; to impair soundness of body, either animal or vegetable.
