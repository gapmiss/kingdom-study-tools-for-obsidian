# Huge

**HUGE**, _adjective_

**1.** Very large or great; enormous; applied to bulk or size; as a _huge_ mountain; a _huge_ ox.

**2.** It is improperly applied to space and distance, in the sense of great, vast, immense; as a hugh space; a hugh difference. This is inelegant, or rather vulgar.

**3.** In colloquial language, very great; enormous; as a _huge_ feeder.
