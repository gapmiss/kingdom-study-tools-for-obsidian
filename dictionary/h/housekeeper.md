# Housekeeper

**HOUSEKEEPER**, _noun_ hous'keeper. One who occupies a house with his family; a man or woman who maintains a family; a man or woman who maintains a family state in a house; a householder; the master or mistress of a family.

**1.** A female servant who has the chief care of the family and superintends the other servants.

**2.** One who lives in plenty. \[Not in use.\]

**3.** One who keeps much at home. \[Not used.\]

**4.** A housedog. \[Not used.\]
