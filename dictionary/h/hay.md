# Hay

**HAY**, _noun_ Grass cut and dried for fodder; grass prepared for preservation.

Make _hay_ while the sun shines.

To dance the _hay_ to dance in a ring.

**HAY**, _verb transitive_ To dry or cure grass for preservation.

**HAY**, _noun_ A hedge.

**1.** A net which incloses the haunt of an animal.

**HAY**, _verb transitive_ To lay snares for rabbits.
