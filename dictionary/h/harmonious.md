# Harmonious

**HARMO'NIOUS**, _adjective_ Adapted to each other; having the parts proportioned to each other; symmetrical.

God hath made the intellectual world _harmonious_ and beautiful without us.

**1.** Concordant; consonant; symphonious; musical. _harmonious_ sounds are such as accord, and are agreeable to the ear.

**2.** Agreeing; living in peace and friendship; as a _harmonious_ family or society.
