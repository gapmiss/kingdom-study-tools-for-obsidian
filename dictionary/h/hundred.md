# Hundred

**HUND'RED**, _adjective_ \[Latin centum.\] Denoting the product of ten multiplied by ten, or the number of ten times ten; as a _hundred_ men.

**HUND'RED**, _noun_ A collection, body or sum, consisting of ten times ten individuals or units; the number 100.

**1.** A division or part of a county in England, supposed to have originally contained a _hundred_ families, or a _hundred_ warriors, or a _hundred_ manors. \[But as the word denotes primarily a circuit or division, it is not certin that Alfred's divisions had any reference to that number.\]
