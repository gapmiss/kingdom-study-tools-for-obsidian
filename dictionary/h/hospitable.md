# Hospitable

**HOS'PITABLE**, _adjective_ \[Latin hospitalis, from hospes, a guest.\]

**1.** Receiving and entertaining strangers, with kindness and without reward; kind to strangers and guests; disposed to treat guests with generous kindness; as a _hospitable_ man.

**2.** Proceeding from or indicating kindness to guests; manifesting generosity; as a _hospitable_ table; _hospitable_ rites.

**3.** Inviting to strangers; offering kind reception; indicating hospitality.

To where yon taper cheers the vale,

With _hospitable_ ray.
