# Hogshead

**HOGS'HEAD**, _noun_ \[the English orthography is grossly corrupt.\]

**1.** A measure of capacity, containing 63 gallons.

**2.** In America, this name is often given to a butt, a cask containing from 110 to 120 gallons; as a _hogshead_ of spirit or molasses.

**3.** A large cask, of indefinite contents.
