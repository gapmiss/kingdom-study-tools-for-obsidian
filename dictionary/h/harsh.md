# Harsh

**H'ARSH**, _adjective_

**1.** Rough to the touch; rugged; grating; as _harsh_ sand; _harsh_ cloth; opposed to smooth.

**2.** Sour; rough to the taste; as _harsh_ fruit.

**3.** Rough to the ear; grating; discordant; jarring; as a _harsh_ sound; _harsh_ notes; a _harsh_ voice.

**4.** Austere; crabbed; morose; peevish. Civilization softens the _harsh_ temper or nature of man.

**5.** Rough; rude; abusive; as _harsh_ words; a _harsh_ reflection.

**6.** Rigorous; severe.

Though _harsh_ the precept, yet the preacher charm'd.
