# Hew

**HEW**, _verb transitive_ _preterit tense_ hewed; _participle passive_ hewed or hewn.

**1.** To cut with an ax, or other like instrument, for the purpose of making an even surface or side; as, to _hew_ timber.

**2.** To chop; to cut; to hack; as, to _hew_ in pieces.

**3.** To cut with a chisel; to make smooth; as, to _hew_ stone.

**4.** To form or shape with an edged instrument; with out; as, to _hew_ out a sepulcher. [[Isaiah 22#16|Isaiah 22:16]].

**5.** To form laboriously.

I now pass my days, not studious nor idle, rather polishing old works than hewing out new ones. \[Unusual.\]

To _hew_ down, to cut down; to fell by cutting.

To _hew_ off, to cut off; to separate by a cutting instrument.
