# Hungry

**HUN'GRY**, _adjective_ Having a keen appetite; feeling pain or uneasiness from want of food. Eat only when you are _hungry_

**1.** Having an eager desire.

**2.** Lean; emaciated, as if reduced by hunger.

Cassius has a lean and _hungry_ look.

**3.** Not rich or fertile; poor; barren; requiring substances to enrich itself; as a _hungry_ soil; a _hungry_ gravel.
