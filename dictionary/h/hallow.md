# Hallow

**HAL'LOW**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin calleo, to be able.\]

**1.** To make holy; to consecrate; to set apart for holy or religious use. [[Exodus 28#38|Exodus 28:38]]. [[1 Kings 8#64|1 Kings 8:64]].

**2.** To devote to holy or religious exercises; to treat as sacred.

Hallow the sabbath day, to do no work therein. [[Jeremiah 17#22|Jeremiah 17:22]].

**3.** To reverence; to honor as sacred.

Hallowed be thy name.
