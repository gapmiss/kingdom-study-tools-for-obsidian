# Harshly

**H'ARSHLY**, _adverb_ Roughly; in a harsh manner.

**1.** Sourly; austerely.

**2.** Severely; morosely; crabbedly; as, to speak or answer _harshly_

**3.** Roughly; rudely; with violence; as, to treat a person _harshly_

**4.** Roughly; with a grating sound; unpleasantly.

It would sound _harshly_ in her ears.
