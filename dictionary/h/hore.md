# Hore

**HORE**, _noun_ A woman, married or single, who indulges unlawful sexual intercourse; also, a prostitute; a common woman; a harlot; a woman of ill fame. \[This word comprehends adultress and fornicatrix, and all lewd women whether paid for prostitution or not.\]

**HORE**, _verb intransitive_ To indulge unlawful sexual commerce, as a male or female; to be habitually lewd.
