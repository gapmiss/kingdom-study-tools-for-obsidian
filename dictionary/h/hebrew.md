# Hebrew

**HE'BREW**, _noun_ \[Heb. Eber, either a proper name, or a name denoting passage, pilgrimage, or coming from beyond the Euphrates.\]

One of the descendants of Eber, or Heber; but particularly, a descendant of Jacob, who was a descendant of Eber; an Israelite; a Jew.

**1.** The _hebrew_ language.

**HE'BREW**, _adjective_ Pertaining to the Hebrews; as the _hebrew_ language or rites.
