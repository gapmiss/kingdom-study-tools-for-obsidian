# Humility

**HUMIL'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin humilitas.\]

**1.** In ethics, freedom from pride and arrogance; humbleness of mind; a modest estimate of one's own worth. In theology, _humility_ consists in lowliness of mind; a deep sense of one's own unworthiness in the sight of God, self-abasement, penitence for sin, and submission to the divine will.

Before honor is _humility_ [[Proverbs 15#33|Proverbs 15:33]].

Serving the Lord with all _humility_ of mind. [[Acts 20#19|Acts 20:19]].

**2.** Act of submission.

With these humilities they satisfied the young king.
