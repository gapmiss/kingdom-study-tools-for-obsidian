# Hind

**HIND**, _noun_ The female of the red deer or stag.

**HIND**, _noun_ A domestic; a servant.

**1.** A peasant; a rustic; or a husbandman's servant.

**HIND**, _adjective_ Backward; pertaining to the part which follows; in opposition to the fore part; as the _hind_ legs of a quadruped; the _hind_ toes; the _hind_ shoes of a horse; the _hind_ part of an animal.
