# Happen

**HAP'PEN**, _verb intransitive_ hap'n.

**1.** To come by chance; to come without one's previous expectation; to fall out.

There shall no evil _happen_ to the just. [[Proverbs 12#21|Proverbs 12:21]].

**2.** To come; to befall.

They talked together of all those things which had happened. [[Luke 24#14|Luke 24:14]].

**3.** To light; to fall or come unexpectedly.

I have happened on some other accounts relating to mortalities.
