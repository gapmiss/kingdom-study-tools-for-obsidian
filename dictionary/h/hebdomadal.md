# Hebdomadal

**HEBDOM'ADAL**

HEBDOM'ADARY, _adjective_ Weekly; consisting of seven days, or occurring every seven days.

**HEBDOM'ADARY**, _noun_ A member of a chapter or convent, whose week it is to officiate in the choir, rehearse the anthems and prayers, and perform other services, which on extraordinary occasions are performed by the superiors.
