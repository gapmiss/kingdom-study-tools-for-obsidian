# Hypocritic

**HYPOCRIT'IC**

**HYPOCRIT'IC**AL, _adjective_ Simulating; counterfeiting a religions character; assuming a false and deceitful appearance; applied to persons.

**1.** Dissembling; concealing one's real character or motives.

**2.** Proceeding from hypocrisy, or marking hypocrisy; as a hypocritical face or look.
