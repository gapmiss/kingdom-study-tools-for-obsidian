# Herborize

**HERB'ORIZE**, _verb intransitive_ To search for plants, or to seek new species of plants, with a view to ascertain their characters and to class them.

He herborized as he traveled, and enriched the Flora Suecica with new discoveries.

**HERB'ORIZE**, _verb transitive_ To figure; to form the figures of plants in minerals. \[See [[Arborize]].\]
