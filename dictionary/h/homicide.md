# Homicide

**HOM'ICIDE**, _noun_ \[Latin homicidium; homo, man, and caedo, to strike, to kill.\]

**1.** The killing of one man or human being by another. _homicide_ is of three kinds, justifiable, excusable, and felonious; justifiable, when it proceeds from unavoidable necessity, without an intention to kill, and without negligence; excusable, when it happens from misadventure, or in self-defense; felonious, when it proceeds from malice, or is done in the prosecution of some unlawful act, or in a sudden passion. _homicide_ committed with premeditated malice, is murder. Suicide also, or self-murder, is felonious _homicide_ _homicide_ comprehends murder and manslaughter.

**1.** A person who kills another; a manslayer.
