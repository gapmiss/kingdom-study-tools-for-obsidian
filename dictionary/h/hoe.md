# Hoe

**HOE**, _noun_ ho. A farmer's instrument for cutting up weeds and loosening the earth in fields and gardens. It is in shape something like an adz, being a plate of iron, with an eye for a handle, which is set at an acute angle with the plate.

**HOE**, _verb transitive_ To cut, dig, scrape or clean with a hoe; as, to _hoe_ the earth in a garden; to _hoe_ the beds.

**1.** To clear from weeds; as, to _hoe_ maiz; to _hoe_ cabbages.

**HOE**, _verb intransitive_ To use a _hoe_
