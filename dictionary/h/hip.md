# Hip

**HIP**, _noun_ The projecting part of an animal formed by the osilium or haunch bone; the haunch, or the flesh that covers the bone and the adjacent parts; the joint of the thigh.

To have on the _hip_ to have the advantage over one; a low phrase borrowed probably from wrestlers.

**HIP** and thigh, complete overthrow or defeat. [[Judges 15#8|Judges 15:8]].

**HIP**, _verb transitive_ To sprain or dislocate the _hip_
