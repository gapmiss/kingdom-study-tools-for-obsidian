# Humorously

**HU'MOROUSLY**, _adverb_ With a wild or grotesque combination of ideas; in a manner to excite laughter or mirth; pleasantly; jocosely. Addison describes _humorously_ the manual exercise of ladies' fans.

**1.** Capriciously; whimsically; in conformity with one's humor.

We resolve by halves, rashly and _humorously_
