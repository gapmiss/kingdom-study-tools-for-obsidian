# Harmonize

**H'ARMONIZE**, _verb intransitive_ To be in concord; to agree in sounds.

**1.** To agree; to be in peace and friendship; as individuals or families.

**2.** To agree in sense or purport; as, the arguments harmonize; the facts stated by different witnesses _harmonize_

**H'ARMONIZE**, _verb transitive_ To adjust in fit proportions; to cause to agree.

**1.** To make musical; to combine according to the laws of counterpoint.
