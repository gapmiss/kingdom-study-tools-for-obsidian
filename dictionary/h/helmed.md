# Helmed

**HELM'ED**

HELM'ET, _noun_ Defensive armor for the head; a head-piece; a morion. The helmet is worn by horsemen to defend the head against the broad sword.

**1.** The part of a coat of arms that bears the crest.

**2.** The upper part of a retort.

**3.** In botany, the upper lip of a ringent corol.
