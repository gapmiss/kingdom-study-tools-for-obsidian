# Hurl

**HURL**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To throw with violence; to drive with great force; as, to _hurl_ a stone.

And _hurl_ them headlong to their fleet and main.

**2.** To utter with vehemence; as, to _hurl_ out vows. \[Not in use.\]

**3.** To play at a kind of game.

**HURL**, _noun_ The act of throwing with violence.

**1.** Tumult; riot; commotion.
