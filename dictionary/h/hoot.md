# Hoot

**HOOT**, _verb intransitive_

**1.** To cry out or shout in contempt.

Matrons and girls shall _hoot_ at thee no more.

**2.** To cry, as an owl.

The clamorous owl, that nightly hoots.

**HOOT**, _verb transitive_ To drive with cries or shouts uttered in contempt.

Partridge and his clan may _hoot_ me for a cheat.

**HOOT**, _noun_ A cry or shout in contempt.
