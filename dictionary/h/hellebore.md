# Hellebore

**HEL'LEBORE**, _noun_ \[Latin helleborus.\] The name of several plants of different genera, the most important of which are the black _hellebore_ Christmas rose, or Christmas flower, of the genus Helleborus, and the white _hellebore_ of the genus Veratrum. Both are acrid and poisonous, and are used in medicine as evacuants and alternatives.
