# Habituate

**HABIT'UATE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To accustom; to make familiar by frequent use or practice. Men may _habituate_ themselves to the taste of oil or tobacco. They _habituate_ themselves to vice. Let us _habituate_ ourselves and our children to the exercise of charity.

**2.** To settle as an inhabitant in a place.

**HABIT'UATE**, _adjective_ Inveterate by custom.

**1.** Formed by habit.
