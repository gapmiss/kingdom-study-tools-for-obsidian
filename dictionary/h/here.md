# Here

**HERE**, _adverb_

**1.** In this place; in the place where the speaker is present; opposed to there. Behold, _here_ am I. Lodge _here_ this night. Build _here_ seven altars.

**2.** In the present life or state.

Thus shall you be happy _here_ and more happy hereafter.

**3.** It is used in making an offer or attempt.

Then here's for earnest.

**4.** In drinking health.

**HERE**'s to thee, Dick.

It is neither _here_ nor there, it is neither in this place nor in that; neither in one place nor in another.

**HERE** and there, in one place and another; in a dispersed manner or condition; thinly; or irregularly.
