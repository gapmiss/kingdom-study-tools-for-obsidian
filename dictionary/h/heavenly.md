# Heavenly

**HEAV'ENLY**, _adjective_ Pertaining to heaven; celestial; as _heavenly_ regions; _heavenly_ bliss.

**1.** Resembling heaven; supremely excellent; as a _heavenly_ lyre; a _heavenly_ temper.

The love of heaven makes one _heavenly_

**2.** Inhabiting heaven; as a _heavenly_ race; the _heavenly_ throng.
