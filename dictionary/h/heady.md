# Heady

**HEADY**, _adjective_ hed'y. \[See [[Head]].\] Rash; hasty; precipitate; violent; disposed to rush forward in an enterprise without thought or deliberation; hurried on by will or passion; ungovernable.

All the talent required, is to be _heady_ to be violent on one side or the other.

**1.** Apt to affect the head; inflaming; intoxicating; strong; as spirituous liquors.

Champagne is a _heady_ wine.

**2.** Violent; ; impetuous; as a _heady_ current. \[Not usual.\]
