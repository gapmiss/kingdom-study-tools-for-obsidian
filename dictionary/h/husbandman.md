# Husbandman

**HUS'BANDMAN**, _noun_ A farmer; a cultivator or tiller of the ground; one who labors in tillage. In America, where men generally own the land on which they labor, the proprietor of a farm is also a laborer or husbandman; but the word includes the lessee and the owner.

**1.** The master of a family. \[Not in use in America.\]
