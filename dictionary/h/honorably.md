# Honorably

**HON'ORABLY**, _adverb_ With tokens of honor or respect. The man was _honorably_ received at court.

**1.** Magnanimously; generously; with a noble spirit or purpose. The prince _honorably_ interposed to prevent a rupture between the nations.

**2.** Reputably; without reproach.

Why did I not more _honorably_ starve?
