# Heft

**HEFT**, _noun_

**1.** Heaving; effort.

He cracks his gorge, his sides.

With violent hefts. \[Not used.\]

**2.** Weight; ponderousness. \[This use is common in popular language in America. And we sometimes hear it used as a verb, as, to _heft_ to lift for the purpose of feeling or judging of the weight.\]

**3.** A handle; a haft. \[Not used.\]
