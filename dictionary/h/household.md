# Household

**HOUSEHOLD**, _noun_ hous'hold. Those who dwell under the same roof and compose a family; those who belong to a family.

I baptized also the _household_ of Stephanus. [[1 Corinthians 1#16|1 Corinthians 1:16]].

**1.** Family life; domestic management.

**HOUSEHOLD**, _adjective_ hous'hold. Belonging to the house and family; domestic; as _household_ furniture; _household_ affairs.
