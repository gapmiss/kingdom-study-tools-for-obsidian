# Valley

**VAL'LEY**, _noun_ _plural_ valleys. \[Latin vallis. See [[Vale]].\]

**1.** A hollow or low tract of land between hills or mountains.

**2.** A low extended plain, usually alluvial, penetrated or washed by a river. The _valley_ of the Connecticut is remarkable for its fertility and beauty.

Ye mountains, sink; ye valleys, rise; prepare the Lord his way.

**3.** In building, a gutter over the sleepers in the roof of a building.
