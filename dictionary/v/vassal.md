# Vassal

**VAS'SAL**, _noun_

**1.** A feudatory; a tenant; one who holds land of a superior, and who vows fidelity and homage to him. A rear _vassal_ is one who holds of a lord who is himself a _vassal_

**2.** A subject; a dependant.

**3.** A servant.

**4.** In common language, a bondman; a political slave. We will never be the vassals of a foreign prince.

**VAS'SAL**, _verb transitive_ To subject to control; to enslave.
