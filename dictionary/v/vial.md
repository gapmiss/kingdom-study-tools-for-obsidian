# Vial

**VI'AL**, _noun_ \[Latin phiala.\] A phial; a small bottle of thin glass, used particularly by apothecaries and druggists.

Samuel took a _vial_ of oil, and poured it on his head.

[[1 Samuel 10#1|1 Samuel 10:1]].

Vials of God's wrath, in Scripture, are the execution of his wrath upon the wicked for their sins. [[Revelation 16#2|Revelation 16:2]].

**VI'AL**, _verb transitive_ To put in a _vial_
