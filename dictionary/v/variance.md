# Variance

**VA'RIANCE**, _noun_ \[See [[Vary]].\]

**1.** In law, an alteration of something formerly laid in a writ; or a difference between a declaration and a writ, or the deed on which it is grounded.

**2.** Any alteration or change of condition.

**3.** Difference that produces dispute or controversy; disagreement; dissension; discord. A mere _variance_ may become a war. Without a spirit of condescension, there will be an everlasting _variance_

**1.** At _variance_ in disagreement; in a state of difference or want of agreement.

**2.** In a state of dissension or controversy; in a state of enmity.
