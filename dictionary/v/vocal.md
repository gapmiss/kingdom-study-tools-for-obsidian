# Vocal

**VO'CAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin vocalis. See [[Voice]].\]

**1.** Having a voice.

To hill or valley, fountain or fresh shade, made _vocal_ by my song.

**2.** Uttered or modulated by the voice; as _vocal_ melody; _vocal_ prayer; _vocal_ praise.

Vocal music, music made by the voice, in distinction from instrumental music; hence, music or tunes set to words, to be performed by the human voice.

**VO'CAL**, _noun_ Among the Romanists, a man who has a right to vote in certain elections.
