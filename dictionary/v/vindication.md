# Vindication

**VINDICA'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin vindico.\]

**1.** The defense of any thing, or a justification against denial or censure, or against objections or accusations; as the _vindication_ of opinions or of a creed; the _vindication_ of the Scriptures against the objections and cavils of infidels.

**2.** The act of supporting by proof or legal process; the proving of any thing to be just; as the _vindication_ of a title, claim or right.

**3.** Defense by force or otherwise; as the _vindication_ of the rights of man; the _vindication_ of our liberties or the rights of conscience.
