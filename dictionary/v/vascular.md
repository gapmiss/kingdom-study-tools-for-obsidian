# Vascular

**VAS'CULAR**, _adjective_ \[Latin vasculum, a vessel, from vas, id.\]

**1.** Pertaining to the vessels of animal or vegetable bodies; as the _vascular_ functions.

**2.** Full of vessels; consisting of animal or vegetable vessels, as arteries, veins, lacteals and the like; as the _vascular_ system. Animal flesh is all _vascular_ none of it parenchymous.
