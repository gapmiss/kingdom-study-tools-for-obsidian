# Versatile

**VERS'ATILE**, _adjective_ \[Latin versatilis, from versor, to turn.\]

**1.** That may be turned round; as a _versatile_ boat or spindle.

**2.** Liable to be turned in opinion; changeable; variable; unsteady; as a man of _versatile_ disposition.

**3.** Turning with ease from one thing to another; readily applied to a new task, or to various subjects; as a man of _versatile_ genius.

**4.** In botany, a _versatile_ anther is one fixed by the middle on the point of the filament, and so poised as to turn like the needle of a compass; fixed by its side, but freely movable.
