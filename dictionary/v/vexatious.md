# Vexatious

**VEXA'TIOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Irritating; disturbing or agitating to the mind; causing disquiet; afflictive; as a _vexatious_ controversy; a _vexatious_ neighbor.

**2.** Distressing; harassing; as _vexatious_ wars.

**3.** Full of trouble and disquiet.

He leads a _vexatious_ life.

**4.** Teasing; slightly troublesome; provoking.

A _vexatious_ suit, in law, is one commenced for the purpose of giving trouble, or without cause.
