# Vermicular

**VERMIC'ULAR**, _adjective_ \[Latin vermiculus, a little worm, from vermis, a worm.\]

Pertaining to a worm; resembling a worm; particularly, resembling the motion of a worm; as the _vermicular_ motion of the intestines, called also peristaltic.

Vermicular or vermiculated work, in sculpture, a sort of ornament consisting of frets or knobs, in Mosaic pavements, winding and representing the tracks of worms.
