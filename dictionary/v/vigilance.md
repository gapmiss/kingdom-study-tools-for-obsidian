# Vigilance

**VIG'ILANCE**, _noun_ \[Latin vigilans. See [[Vigil]].\]

**1.** Forbearance of sleep; a state of being awake.

**2.** Watchfulness; circumspection; attention of the mind in discovering and guarding against danger, or providing for safety. _vigilance_ is a virtue of prime importance in a general. The _vigilance_ of the dog is no less remarkable than his fidelity.

**3.** Guard; watch.

In at this gate none pass the _vigilance_ here plac'd.
