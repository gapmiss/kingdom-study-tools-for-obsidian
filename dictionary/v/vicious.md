# Vicious

**VI'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin vitiosus.\]

**1.** Defective; imperfect; as a system of government _vicious_ and unsound.

**2.** Addicted to vice; corrupt in principles or conduct; depraved; wicked; habitually transgressing the moral law; as a _vicious_ race of men; _vicious_ parents; _vicious_ children.

**3.** Corrupt; contrary to moral principles or to rectitude; as _vicious_ examples; _vicious_ conduct.

**4.** Corrupt, in a physical sense; foul; impure; insalubrious; as _vicious_ air.

**5.** Corrupt; not genuine or pure; as _vicious_ language; _vicious_ idioms.

**6.** Unruly; refractory; not well tamed or broken; as a _vicious_ horse.
