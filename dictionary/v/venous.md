# Venous

**VE'NOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin venosus, from vena, a vein.\]

**1.** Pertaining to a vein or to veins; contained in veins; as _venous_ blood, which is distinguishable from arterial blood by its darker color.

**2.** In botany, veined. A _venous_ leaf, has vessels branching, or variously divided, over its surface.
