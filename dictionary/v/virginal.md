# Virginal

**VIR'GINAL**, _adjective_ Pertaining to a virgin; maidenly; as _virginal_ chastity.

**VIR'GINAL**, _noun_ A keyed instrument of one string, jack and quill to each note, like a spinet, but in shape resembling the forte piano; out of use.

**VIR'GINAL**, _verb intransitive_ To pat; to strike as on a _virginal_ \[A cant word.\]
