# Volition

**VOLI'TION**, _noun_ \[Latin volitio, from volo, to will. See [[Will]].\]

**1.** The act of willing the act of determining choice, or forming a purpose. There is a great difference between actual _volition_ and approbation of judgment.

Volition is the actual exercise of the power which the mind has of considering or forbearing to consider an idea.

**2.** The power of willing or determining.
