# Ventilate

**VEN'TILATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin ventilo, from ventus, wind.\]

**1.** To fan with wind; to open and expose to the free passage of air or wind; as, to _ventilate_ a room; to _ventilate_ a cellar.

**2.** To cause the air to pass through; as, to _ventilate_ a mine.

**3.** To winnow; to fan; as, to _ventilate_ wheat.

**4.** To examine; to discuss; that is, to agitate; as, to _ventilate_ questions of policy. \[Not now in use.\]
