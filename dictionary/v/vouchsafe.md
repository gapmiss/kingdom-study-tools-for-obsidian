# Vouchsafe

**VOUCHSAFE**, _verb intransitive_ \[vouch and safe; to vouch or answer for safety.\]

**1.** To permit to be done without danger.

**2.** To condescend to grant.

Shall I _vouchsafe_ your worship a word or two?

It is not said by the apostle that God vouchsafed to the heathen the means of

salvation.

**VOUCHSAFE**, _verb intransitive_ To condescend; to deign; to yield.

**VOUCHSAFE**, illustrious Ormond, to behold What powr the charms of

beauty had of old.
