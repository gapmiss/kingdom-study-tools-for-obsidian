# Vild

**VILD**,

VILE, _adjective_ \[Latin vilis. Gr.\]

**1.** Base; mean; worthless; despicable.

The inhabitants account gold a vile thing.

A man in vile raiment. [[James 2#1|James 2:1]].

Wherefore are we counted as beasts, and reputed as vile in your sight? [[Job 18#1|Job 18:1]].

**2.** Morally base or impure; sinful; depraved by sin; wicked; hateful in the sight of God and of good men. The sons of Eli made themselves vile. [[1 Samuel 3#1|1 Samuel 3:1]].

Behold I am vile; what shall I answer? [[Job 40#4|Job 40:4]].

**VI'LED**, _adjective_ Abusive; scurrilous; defamatory. \[Not in use.\]
