# Vacuum

**VAC'UUM**, _noun_ \[Latin\] Space empty or devoid of all matter or body. Whether there is such a thing as an absolute _vacuum_ in nature, is a question which has been much controverted. The Peripatetics assert that nature abhors a _vacuum_

Torricellian _vacuum_ the _vacuum_ produced by filling a tube with mercury, and allowing it to descend till it is counterbalanced by the weight of the atmosphere, as in the barometer invented by Torricelli.
