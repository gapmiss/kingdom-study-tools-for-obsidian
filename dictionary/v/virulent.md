# Virulent

**VIR'ULENT**, _adjective_ \[Latin virulentus, from virus, poison, that is, strength, from the same root as vir, vireo. See [[Venom]].\]

**1.** Extremely active in doing injury; very poisonous or venomous. No poison is more _virulent_ than that of some species of serpents.

**2.** Very bitter in enmity; malignant; as a _virulent_ invective.
