# Visual

**VIS'UAL**, _adjective_ s as z. \[Latin visus.\]

Pertaining to sight; used in sight; serving as the instrument of seeing; as the _visual_ nerve.

The air, no where so clear, sharpen'd his _visual_ ray.

Visual point, in perspective, a point in the horizontal line, in which all the ocular rays unite.

Visual rays, lines of light, imagined to come from the object to the eye.
