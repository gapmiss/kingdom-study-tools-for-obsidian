# Venerate

**VEN'ERATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin veneror.\]

To regard with respect and reverence; to reverence; to revere. We _venerate_ parents and elders; we _venerate_ men consecrated to sacred offices. We _venerate_ old age or gray hairs. We _venerate_ or ought to _venerate_ the gospel and its precepts.

And seem'd to _venerate_ the sacred shade.
