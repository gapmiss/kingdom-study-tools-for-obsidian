# Venereal

**VENE'REAL**, _adjective_ \[Latin venereus, from Venus. See [[Venus]].\]

**1.** Pertaining to the pleasures of sexual commerce. A _venereal_ person is one addicted to sexual pleasures or venery.

**2.** Proceeding from sexual intercourse; as the _venereal_ disease; _venereal_ virus or poison.

**3.** Adapted to the cure of the lues venerea; as _venereal_ medicines.

**4.** Adapted to excite _venereal_ desire; aphrodisiac; provocative.

**5.** Consisting of copper, called by chimists formerly Venus. _obsolete_
