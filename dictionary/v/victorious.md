# Victorious

**VICTO'RIOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Having conquered in battle or contest; having overcome an enemy or antagonist; conquering; vanquishing; as a _victorious_ general; _victorious_ troops; a _victorious_ admiral or navy.

**2.** That produces conquest; as a _victorious_ day.

**3.** Emblematic of conquest; indicating victory; as brows bound with _victorious_ wreaths.
