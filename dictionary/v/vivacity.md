# Vivacity

**VIVAC'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin vivacitas.\]

**1.** Liveliness; sprightliness of temper or behavior; as a lady of great _vivacity_

**2.** Air of life and activity; as _vivacity_ of countenance.

**3.** Life; animation; spirits; as the _vivacity_ of a discourse.

**4.** Power of living. \[Not used.\]

**5.** Longevity. \[Not in use.\]
