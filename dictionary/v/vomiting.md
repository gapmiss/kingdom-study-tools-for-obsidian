# Vomiting

**VOM'ITING**, _participle present tense_ Discharging from the stomach through the mouth, or ejecting from any deep place.

**VOM'ITING**, _noun_

**1.** The act of ejecting the contents of the stomach through the mouth. _vomiting_ is an inverted action of the stomach.

**2.** The act of throwing out substances with violence from a deep hollow, as a volcano, etc.
