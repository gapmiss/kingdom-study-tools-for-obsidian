# Vigorous

**VIG'OROUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Full of physical strength or active force; strong; lusty; as a _vigorous_ youth; a _vigorous_ body.

**2.** Powerful; strong; made by strength, either of body or mind; as a _vigorous_ attack; _vigorous_ exertions. The enemy expects a _vigorous_ campaign.

The beginnings of confederacies have been _vigorous_ and successful.
