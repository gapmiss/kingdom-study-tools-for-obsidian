# Virgin

**VIR'GIN**, _noun_ nearly vur'gin. \[Latin virgo.\]

**1.** A woman who has had no carnal knowledge of man.

**2.** A woman not a mother. \[Unusual.\]

**3.** The sign Virgo. \[See [[Virgo]].\]

**VIR'GIN**, _adjective_

**1.** Pure; untouched; as _virgin_ gold.

**2.** Fresh; new; unused; as _virgin_ soil.

**VIR'GIN**, _verb intransitive_ To play the virgin; a cant word.
