# Vainly

**VA'INLY**, _adverb_

**1.** Without effect; to no purpose; ineffectually; in vain.

In weak complaints you _vainly_ waste your breath.

**2.** Boastingly; with vaunting; proudly; arrogantly.

Humility teaches us not to think _vainly_ nor vauntingly of ourselves.

**3.** Idly; foolishly.

Nor _vainly_ hope to be invulnerable.
