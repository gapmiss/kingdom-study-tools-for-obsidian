# Violation

**VIOLA'TION**, _noun_

**1.** The act of violating or injuring; interruption, as of sleep or peace.

**2.** Infringement; transgression; non-observance; as the _violation_ of law or positive command; a _violation_ of covenants, engagements and promises; a _violation_ of vows.

**3.** Act of irreverence; profanation or contemptuous treatment of sacred things; as the _violation_ of a church.

**4.** Ravishment; rape.
