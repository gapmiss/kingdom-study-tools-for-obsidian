# Vicinity

**VICIN'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin vicinitas.\]

**1.** Nearness in place; as the _vicinity_ of two country seats.

**2.** Neighborhood; as a seat in the _vicinity_ of the metropolis.

**3.** Neighboring country. Vegetables produced in the _vicinity_ of the city, are daily brought to market. the _vicinity_ is full of gardens.
