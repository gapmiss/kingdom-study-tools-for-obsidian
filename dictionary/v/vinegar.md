# Vinegar

**VIN'EGAR**, _noun_

**1.** Vegetable acid; an acid liquor obtained from wine, cider, beer or other liquors, by the second or acetous fermentation. _vinegar_ may differ indefinitely in the degree of its acidity. When highly concentrated, it is called radical _vinegar_

**2.** Any thing really or metaphorically sour. \[Not in use.\]

Vinegar of lead, a liquor formed by digesting ceruse or litharge with a sufficient quantity of _vinegar_ to dissolve it.
