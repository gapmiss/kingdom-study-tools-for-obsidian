# Vitrify

**VIT'RIFY**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin vitrum, glass, and facio, to make.\]

To convert into glass by fusion or the action of heat; as, to _vitrify_ sand and alkaline salts.

**VIT'RIFY**, _verb intransitive_ To become glass; to be converted into glass.

Chimists make vessels of animal substances calcined, which will not _vitrify_ in the fire.
