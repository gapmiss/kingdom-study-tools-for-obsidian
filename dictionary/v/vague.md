# Vague

**VAGUE**, _adjective_ vag. \[Latin vagus, wandering.\]

**1.** Wandering; vagrant; vagabond; as _vague_ villains. \[In this literal sense, not used.\]

**2.** Unsettled; unfixed; undetermined; indefinite. He appears to have very _vague_ ideas of this subject.

**3.** Proceeding from no known authority; flying; uncertain; as a _vague_ report.
