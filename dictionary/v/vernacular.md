# Vernacular

**VERNAC'ULAR**, _adjective_ \[Latin vernaculus, born in one's house, from verns, a servant.\]

**1.** Native; belonging to the country of one's birth. English is our _vernacular_ language. The _vernacular_ idiom is seldom perfectly acquired by foreigners.

**2.** Native; belonging to the person by birth or nature.

A _vernacular_ disease, is one which prevails in a particular country or district; more generally called endemic.
