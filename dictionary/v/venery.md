# Venery

**VEN'ERY**, _noun_ \[from Venus.\] The pleasures of the bed.

Contentment, without the pleasure of lawful _venery_ is continence; of unlawful, chastity.

**VEN'ERY**, _noun_ \[Latin venor, to hunt, that is, to drive or rush.\]

The act or exercise of hunting; the sports of the chase.

Beasts of _venery_ and fishes.
