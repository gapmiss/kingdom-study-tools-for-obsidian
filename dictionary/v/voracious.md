# Voracious

**VORA'CIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin vorax, from voro, to devour; Heb. to clear away, to consume; Gr. food.\]

**1.** Greedy for eating; ravenous; very hungry; as a _voracious_ man or appetite.

**2.** Rapacious; eager to devour; as _voracious_ animals.

**3.** Ready to swallow up; as a _voracious_ gulf or whirlpool.
