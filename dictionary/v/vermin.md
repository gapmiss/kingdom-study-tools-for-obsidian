# Vermin

**VERM'IN**, _noun_ sing, and plu; used chiefly in the plural. \[Latin vermes, worms.\]

**1.** All sorts of small animals which are destructive to grain or other produce; all noxious little animals or insects, as squirrels, rats, mice, worms, grubs, flies, etc.

These _vermin_ do great injuries in the field.

**2.** Used of noxious human beings in contempt; as base _vermin_
