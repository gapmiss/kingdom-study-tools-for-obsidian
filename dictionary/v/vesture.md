# Vesture

**VEST'URE**, _noun_ \[See [[Vest]].\]

**1.** A garment; a robe.

There polish'd chests embroider'd _vesture_ grac'd.

**2.** Dress; garments in general; habit; clothing; vestment; as the _vesture_ of priests.

**3.** Clothing; covering.

Rocks, precipices and gulfs appareled with a _vesture_ of plants.

\- And gild the humble vestures of the plain.

**4.** In old law books, the corn with which land was covered; as the _vesture_ of an acre.

**5.** In old books, seisin; possession. _obsolete_
