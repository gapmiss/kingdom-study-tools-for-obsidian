# Very

**VER'Y**, _adjective_ \[Latin verus.\] True; real.

Whether thou be my _very_ son Esau or not. [[Genesis 27#21|Genesis 27:21]].

He that repeateth a matter, separateth _very_ friends.

[[Proverbs 17#9|Proverbs 17:9]].

**VER'Y**, _adverb_ As an adverb, or modifier of adjectives and adverbs, _very_ denotes in a great degree, an eminent or high degree, but not generally the highest; as a _very_ great mountain; a _very_ bright sun; a _very_ cold day; a _very_ pernicious war; a _very_ benevolent disposition; the river flows _very_ rapidly.
