# Volcano

**VOLCA'NO**, _noun_

**1.** In geology, an opening in the surface of the earth or in a mountain, from which smoke, flames, stones, lava or other substances are ejected. Such are seen in Etna and Vesuvius in Sicily and Italy, and Hecla in Iceland. It is vulgarly called a burning mountain. Herschel has discovered a _volcano_ in the moon.

**2.** The mountain that ejects fire, smoke, etc.
