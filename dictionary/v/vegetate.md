# Vegetate

**VEG'ETATE**, _verb intransitive_ \[Latin vegeto, vigeo, to flourish.\]

To sprout; to germinate; to grow; as plants; to grow and be enlarged by nutriment imbibed from the earth, air or water, by means of roots and leaves. Plants will not _vegetate_ without a certain degree of heat; but some plants _vegetate_ with less heat than others. Potatoes will _vegetate_ after they are pared.
