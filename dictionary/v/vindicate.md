# Vindicate

**VIN'DICATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin vindico.\]

**1.** To defend; to justify; to support or maintain as true or correct, against denial, censure or objections.

When the respondent denies any proposition, the opponent must _vindicate_ it.

Laugh where we must, be candid where we can; but _vindicate_ the ways of God to man.

**2.** To assert; to defend with success; to maintain; to prove to be just or valid; as, to _vindicate_ a claim or title.

**3.** To defend with arms, or otherwise; as, to _vindicate_ our rights.

**4.** to avenge; to punish; as a war to _vindicate_ or punish infidelity.

God is more powerful to exact subjection and to _vindicate_ rebellion.

\[This latter use is entirely obsolete.\]
