# Vacuity

**VACU'ITY**, _noun_ \[Latin vacuitas, from vacuus.\]

**1.** Emptiness; a state of being unfilled.

Hunger is such a state of _vacuity_ as to require a fresh supply.

**2.** Space unfilled or unoccupied, or occupied with an invisible fluid only.

**3.** Emptiness; void.

God only can fill every _vacuity_ of the soul.

**4.** Inanity; emptiness; want of reality.

**5.** Vacuum, which see.
