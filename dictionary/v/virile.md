# Virile

**VI'RILE**, _adjective_ \[Latin virilis, from vir, a man, vireo.\]

**1.** Pertaining to a man, in the eminent sense of the word, \[not to man, in the sense of the human race; \] belonging to the male sex; as _virile_ age.

**2.** Masculine; not puerile or feminine; as _virile_ strength or vigor.
