# Validity

**VALID'ITY**, _noun_

**1.** Strength or force to convince; justness; soundness; as the _validity_ of an argument or proof; the _validity_ of an objection.

**2.** Legal strength or force; that quality of a thing which renders it supportable in law or equity; as the _validity_ of a will; the _validity_ of a grant; the _validity_ of a claim or of a title. Certain forms and solemnities are usually requisite to give _validity_ to contracts and conveyances of rights.

**3.** Value. \[Not in use.\]
