# Version

**VER'SION**, _noun_ \[Latin versio.\]

**1.** A turning; a change or transformation; as the _version_ of air into water. \[Unusual.\]

**2.** Change of direction; as the _version_ of the beams of light. \[Unusual.\]

**3.** The act of translating; the rendering of thoughts or ideas expressed in one language, into words of like signification in another language. How long was Pope engaged in the _version_ of Homer?

**4.** Translation; that which is rendered from another language. We have a good _version_ of the Scriptures. There is a good _version_ of Pentateuch in Samaritan. The Septuagint _version_ of the Old Testament was made for the benefit of the Jews in Alexandria.
