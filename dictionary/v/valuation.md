# Valuation

**VALUA'TION**, _noun_ \[from value.\]

**1.** The act of estimating the value or worth; the act of setting a price; as the just _valuation_ of civil and religious privileges.

**2.** Apprizement; as a _valuation_ of lands for the purpose of taxation.

**3.** Value set upon a thing; estimated worth.
