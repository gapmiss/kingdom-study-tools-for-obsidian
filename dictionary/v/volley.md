# Volley

**VOL'LEY**, _noun_ _plural_ volleys. \[Latin volo.\]

**1.** A flight of shot; the discharge of many small arms at once.

**2.** A burst or emission of many things at once; as a _volley_ of words.

But rattling nonsense to full volleys breaks.

**VOL'LEY**, _verb transitive_ to discharge with a _volley_

**VOL'LEY**, _verb intransitive_ To throw out or discharge at once.
