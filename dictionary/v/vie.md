# Vie

**VIE**, _verb intransitive_ \[See [[Victor]].\]

To strive for superiority; to contend; to use effort in a race, contest, competition, rivalship or strife. How delightful it is to see children _vie_ with each other in diligence and in duties of obedience.

In a trading nation, the younger sons may be placed in a way of life to _vie_ with the best of their family.

**VIE**, _verb transitive_

**1.** To show or practice in competition; as, to _vie_ power; to _vie_ charities. \[Not legitimate.\]

**2.** To urge; to press.

She hung about my neck, and kiss and kiss she vied so fast. \[Not in use.\]
