# Voyage

**VOYAGE**, _noun_

**1.** A passing by sea or water from one place, port or country to another, especially a passing or journey by water to a distant place or country. Captain Latin made more than a hundred voyages to the West Indies. A _voyage_ over lake Superior is like a _voyage_ to Bermuda.

**2.** The practice of traveling. \[Not in use.\]

**VOYAGE**, _verb intransitive_ To sail or pass by water.

**VOYAGE**, _verb transitive_ To travel; to pass over.

I with pain Voyagd th unreal, vast, unbounded deep.
