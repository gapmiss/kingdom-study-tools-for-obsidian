# Visor

**VI'SOR**, _noun_ s as z. \[Latin visus, video; written also visard, visar, vizard.\]

**1.** A head piece or mask used to disfigure and disguise.

My weaker government since, makes you pull off the _visor_

Swarms of knaves the _visor_ quite disgrace.

**2.** A perforated part of a helmet.
