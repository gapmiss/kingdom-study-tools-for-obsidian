# Visage

**VIS'AGE**, _noun_ s as z. \[Latin visus, video.\]

The face; the countenance or look of a person, or of other animal; chiefly applied to human beings; as a wolfish _visage_

Love and beauty still that _visage_ grace.

His _visage_ was so marred, more than any man. [[Isaiah 52#14|Isaiah 52:14]].
