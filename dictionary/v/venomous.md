# Venomous

**VEN'OMOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Poisonous; noxious to animal life; as, the bite of a serpent may be _venomous_ The sack at the base of the rattlesnake's teeth, contains _venomous_ matter.

**2.** Noxious; mischievous; malignant; as a _venomous_ progeny.

**3.** Spiteful; as a _venomous_ writer.
