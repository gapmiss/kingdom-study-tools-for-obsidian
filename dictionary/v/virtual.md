# Virtual

**VIR'TUAL**, _adjective_ \[See [[Virtue]].\]

**1.** Potential; having the power of acting or of invisible efficacy without the material or sensible part.

Every kind that lives, fomented by his _virtual_ power, and warm'd.

Neither an actual nor _virtual_ intention of the mind, but only that which may be gathered from the outward acts.

**2.** Being in essence or effect, not in fact; as the _virtual_ presence of a man in his agent or substitute.
