# Virulence

**VIR'ULENCE**, 'ULENCY, _noun_ \[from virulent.\]

**1.** That renders it extremely active in doing injury; acrimony; malignancy; as the _virulence_ of poison.

**2.** Acrimony of temper; extreme bitterness or malignity; as the _virulence_ of enmity or malice; the _virulence_ of satire; to attack a man with _virulence_
