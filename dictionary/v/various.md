# Various

**VA'RIOUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin varius, See [[Vary]].\]

**1.** different; several; manifold; as men of _various_ names and _various_ occupations.

**2.** Changeable; uncertain; unfixed.

The names of mixed modes - are very _various_ and doubtful.

**3.** Unlike each other; diverse.

So many and so _various_ laws are giv'n.

**4.** Variegated; diversified.
