# Vicinage

**VIC'INAGE**, _noun_ \[from Latin vicinia, neighborhood; vicinus, near.\]

Neighborhood; the place or places adjoining or near. A jury must be of the _vicinage_ or body of the country.

In law, common because of _vicinage_ is where the inhabitants of two townships contiguous to each other, have usually intercommoned with one another; the beasts of one straying into the other's fields without molestation from either.
