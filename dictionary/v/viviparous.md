# Viviparous

**VIVIP'AROUS**, _adjective_ \[Latin vivus, alive, and pario, to bear.\]

**1.** Producing young in a living state, as all mammifers; as distinguished from oviparous, producing eggs, as fowls. If fowls were _viviparous_ it is difficult to how the female would fly during preganancy.

**2.** In botany, producing its offspring alive, either by bulbs instead of seeds, or by the seeds themselves germinating on the plant, instread of falling, as they usually do; as a _viviparous_ plant.
