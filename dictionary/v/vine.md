# Vine

**VINE**, _noun_ \[Latin vinca. See [[Wine]].\]

**1.** A plant that produces grapes, of the genus Vitis, and of a great number of varieties.

**2.** The long slender stem of any plant, that trails on the ground, or climbs and supports itself by winding round a fixed object, or by seizing any fixed thing with its tendrils or claspers. Thus we speak of the hop _vine_ the bean _vine_ the vines of melons, squashes, pumpkins, and other encurbitaceous plants.
