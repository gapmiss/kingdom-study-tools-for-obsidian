# Vivificate

**VIV'IFICATE**, _verb transitive_ \[Latin vivifico, vivus, alive, and facio, to make.\]

**1.** to give life to; to animate. \[See [[Vivify]].\]

**2.** In chemistry, to recover from such a change of form as seems to destroy the essential qualities; or to give to natural bodies new luster, force and figor.
