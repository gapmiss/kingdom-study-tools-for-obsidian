# Virtuous

**VIR'TUOUS**, _adjective_

**1.** Morally good; acting in conformity to the moral law; practicing the moral duties, and abstaining from vice; as a _virtuous_ man.

**2.** Being in conformity to the moral or divine law; as a _virtuous_ action; a _virtuous_ life.

The mere performance of _virtuous_ actions does not denominate an agent _virtuous_

**3.** Chaste; applied to women.

**4.** Efficacious by inherent qualities; as _virtuous_ herbs; _virtuous_ drugs. \[Not in use.\]

**5.** Having great or powerful properties; as _virtuous_ steel; a _virtuous_ staff; a _virtuous_ ring. \[Not in use.\]

**6.** Having medicinal qualities. \[Not used.\]
