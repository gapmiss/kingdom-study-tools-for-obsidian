# Zone

**ZONE**, _noun_ \[Latin , Gr.\]

**1.** A girdle.

An embroiderd _zone_ surrounds her waist.

**2.** In geography, a division of the earth, with respect to the temperature of different latitudes. The zones are five; the torrid _zone_ extending from tropic to tropic 46 degrees, 56 degrees, or 23 degrees 28' on each side of the equator; two temperate or variable zones, situated between the tropics and polar circles; and two frigid zones, situated between the polar circles and the poles.

**3.** Circuit; circumference.

Ciliary _zone_ in anatomy, the black impression of the ciliary processes on the vitreous humor of the eye.
