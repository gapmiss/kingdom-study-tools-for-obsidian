# Zeal

**ZEAL**, _noun_ \[Gr., Latin \] Passionate ardor in the pursuit of any thing. In general, _zeal_ is an eagerness of desire to accomplish or obtain some object, and it may be manifested either in favor of any person or thing, or in opposition to it, and in a good or bad cause.

**ZEAL**, the blind conductor of the will.

They have a _zeal_ of God, but not according to knowledge. [[Romans 10#2|Romans 10:2]].

A _zeal_ for liberty is sometimes an eagerness to subvert, with little care what shall be established.
