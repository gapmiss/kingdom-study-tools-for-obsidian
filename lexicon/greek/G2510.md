# G2510 καθάπτω

## katháptō

_(kath-ap'-to | ka-THA-ptoh | ka-THA-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G0680; to seize upon; fasten on; 

- to fit or fasten to, bind on
- to lay hold of, fasten on (hostilely)
