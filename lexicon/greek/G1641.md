# G1641 ἐλαττονέω

## elattonéō

_(el-at-ton-eh-o | eh-laht-toh-NEH-oh | ay-laht-toh-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1640; to diminish, i.e., fall short; have lack; 

- to be less, inferior, (in possessions)
- to make less, diminish
