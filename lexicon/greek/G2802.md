# G2802 Κλαύδη

## Klaúdē

_(klow'-day | KLEV-thay | KLA-thay)_

### Definition

lame; of uncertain derivation; Claude, an island near Crete; Clauda; 

- a small island nearly due west of Cape Malta on the south coast of Crete, and nearly due south of Phoenice
