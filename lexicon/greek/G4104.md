# G4104 πιστόω

## pistóō

_(pis-to'-o | pee-STOH-oh | pee-STOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G4103; to assure; assure of; 

- to make faithful, render trustworthy
  - to make firm, establish
- to be firmly persuaded of
  - to be assured of
