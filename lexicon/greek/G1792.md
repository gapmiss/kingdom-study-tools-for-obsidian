# G1792 ἐντρυφάω

## entrypháō

_(en-troo-fah'-o | en-troo-FA-oh | ane-tryoo-FA-oh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G5171; to revel in; sporting selves; 

- to live in luxury, live delicately or luxuriously, to revel in
- to take delight in
