# G1368 διϋλίζω

## diÿlízō

_(dee-oo-lid'-zo | thee-oo-LEE-zoh | thee-yoo-LEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and ὑλίζω (to filter); to strain out; strain at (probably by misprint); 

- to filter through, strain through, pour through a filter, strain out
