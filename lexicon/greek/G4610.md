# G4610 Σιλουανός

## Silouanós

_(sil-oo-an-os' | see-loo-ah-NOSE | see-loo-ah-NOSE)_

### Definition

woody; of Latin origin; "silvan"; Silvanus, a Christian; Silvanus; 

- a Roman citizen, the companion of the apostle Paul on several of his missionary journeys
