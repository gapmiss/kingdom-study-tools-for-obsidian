# G1514 εἰρηνεύω

## eirēneúō

_(i-rane-yoo'-o | ee-ray-NEV-oh | ee-ray-NAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from G1515; to be (act) peaceful; be at (have, live in) peace, live peaceably; 

- to make peace
- to cultivate or keep peace, harmony
- to be at peace, live in peace
