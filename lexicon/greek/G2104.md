# G2104 εὐγενής

## eugenḗs

_(yoog-en'-ace | ev-geh-NASE | ave-gay-NASE)_

### Definition

from G2095 and G1096; well born, i.e., (literally) high in rank, or (figuratively) generous; more noble, nobleman; 

- well born, of noble race
- noble minded
