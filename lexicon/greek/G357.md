# G357 ἀναλογίζομαι

## analogízomai

_(an-al-og-id'-zom-ahee | ah-na-loh-GEE-zoh-meh | ah-na-loh-GEE-zoh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G0356; to estimate, i.e., (figuratively) contemplate; consider; 

- to think over, consider, ponder
