# G3604 Ὀζίας

## Ozías

_(od-zee'-as | oh-ZEE-as | oh-ZEE-as)_

### Definition

strength of Jehovah; of Hebrew origin (H5818); Ozias (i.e., Uzzijah), an Israelite; Ozias; 

- son of Amaziah, king of Judah from 810–758 BC
