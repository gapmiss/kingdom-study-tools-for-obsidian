# G4508 ῥυπαρός

## rhyparós

_(rhoo-par-os' | roo-pa-ROSE | ryoo-pa-ROSE)_

### Definition

from G4509; dirty, i.e., (relatively) cheap or shabby; morally, wicked; vile; 

- to be dirty, grow filthy
- metaphorically to be defiled with iniquity
