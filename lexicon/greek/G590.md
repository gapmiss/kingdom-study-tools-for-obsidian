# G590 ἀπόδημος

## apódēmos

_(ap-od'-ay-mos | ah-POH-thay-mose | ah-POH-thay-mose)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G1218; absent from one's own people, i.e., a foreign traveller; taking a far journey; 

- away from one's people, go abroad
