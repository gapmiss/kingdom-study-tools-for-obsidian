# G4 ἀβαρής

## abarḗs

_(ab-ar-ace' | ah-va-RASE | ah-va-RASE)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G0922; weightless, i.e., (figuratively) not burdensome; from being burdensome; 

- not burdensome
- not heavy
- light without weight
