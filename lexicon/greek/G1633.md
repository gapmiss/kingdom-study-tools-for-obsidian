# G1633 ἐκχωρέω

## ekchōréō

_(ek-kho-reh'-o | ek-hoh-REH-oh | ake-hoh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G5562; to depart; depart out; 

- to depart from
- to remove from in the sense of fleeing from
