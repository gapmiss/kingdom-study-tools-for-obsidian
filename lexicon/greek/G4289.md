# G4289 πρόθυμος

## próthymos

_(proth'-oo-mos | PROH-thoo-mose | PROH-thyoo-mose)_

### Definition

from G4253 and G2372; forward in spirit, i.e., predisposed; neuter (as noun) alacrity; ready, willing; 

- ready, willing
