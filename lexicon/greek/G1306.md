# G1306 διαυγάζω

## diaugázō

_(dee-ow-gad'-zo | thee-ev-GA-zoh | thee-a-GA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G0826; to glimmer through, i.e., break (as day); dawn; 

- to shine through, to dawn
  - of daylight breaking through the darkness of night
