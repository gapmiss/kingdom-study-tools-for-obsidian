# G3851 παραβουλεύομαι

## parabouleúomai

_(par-ab-ool-yoo'-om-ahee | pa-ra-voo-LEV-oh-meh | pa-ra-voo-LAVE-oh-may)_

### Definition

from G3844 and the middle voice of G1011; to misconsult, i.e., disregard; not (to) regard(-ing); 

- to consult amiss
