# G3893 παραπικραίνω

## parapikraínō

_(par-ap-ik-rah'-ee-no | pa-ra-pee-KREH-noh | pa-ra-pee-KRAY-noh)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G4087; to embitter alongside, i.e., (figuratively) to exasperate; provoke; 

- to provoke, exasperate
- to rouse to indignation
