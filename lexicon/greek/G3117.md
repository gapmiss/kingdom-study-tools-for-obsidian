# G3117 μακρός

## makrós

_(mak-ros' | ma-KROSE | ma-KROSE)_

### Definition

from G3372; long (in place (distant) or time (neuter plural)); far, long; 

- long
  - of place: remote, distant, far off
  - of time: long, long lasting
