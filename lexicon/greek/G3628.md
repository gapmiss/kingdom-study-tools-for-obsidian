# G3628 οἰκτιρμός

## oiktirmós

_(oyk-tir-mos' | ook-teer-MOSE | ook-teer-MOSE)_

### Definition

from G3627; pity; mercy; 

- compassion, pity, mercy
  - bowels in which compassion resides, a heart of compassion
  - emotions, longings, manifestations of pity
