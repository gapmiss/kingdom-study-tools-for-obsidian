# G557 ἀπελεγμός

## apelegmós

_(ap-el-eg-mos' | ah-peh-leg-MOSE | ah-pay-lage-MOSE)_

### Definition

from a compound of G0575 and G1651; refutation, i.e., (by implication) contempt; nought; 

- censure, repudiation of a thing shown to be worthless
