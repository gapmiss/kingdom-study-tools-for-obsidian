# G2956 Κυρηναῖος

## Kyrēnaîos

_(koo-ray-nah'-yos | koo-ray-NEH-ose | kyoo-ray-NAY-ose)_

### Definition

from G2957; i.e., Cyrenaean, i.e., inhabitant of Cyrene; of Cyrene, Cyrenian; 

- a native of Cyrene
