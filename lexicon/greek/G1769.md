# G1769 ἐννεός

## enneós

_(en-neh-os' | en-neh-OSE | ane-nay-OSE)_

### Definition

from G1770; dumb (as making signs), i.e., silent from astonishment; speechless; 

- dumb, mute, destitute of power of speech
- unable to speak for terror, struck dumb, astounded
