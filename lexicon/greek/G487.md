# G487 ἀντίλυτρον

## antílytron

_(an-til'-oo-tron | an-TEE-loo-trone | an-TEE-lyoo-trone)_

### Definition

from G0473 and G3083; a redemption-price; ransom; 

- what is given in exchange for another as the price of his redemption, ransom
