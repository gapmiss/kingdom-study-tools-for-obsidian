# G1286 διασείω

## diaseíō

_(dee-as-i'-o | thee-ah-SEE-oh | thee-ah-SEE-oh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G4579; to shake thoroughly, i.e., (figuratively) to intimidate; do violence to; 

- to shake thoroughly
- to make to tremble
- to terrify
- to agitate
- to extort from one by intimidation money or other property
