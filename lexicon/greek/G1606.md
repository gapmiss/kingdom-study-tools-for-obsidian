# G1606 ἐκπνέω

## ekpnéō

_(ek-pneh'-o | ek-PNEH-oh | ake-PNAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G4154; to expire; give up the ghost; 

- to breathe out, breathe out one's life, breathe one's last, expire
