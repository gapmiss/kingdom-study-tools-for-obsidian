# G272 ἀμελέω

## ameléō

_(am-el-eh'-o | ah-meh-LEH-oh | ah-may-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G3199; to be careless of; make light of, neglect, be negligent, no regard; 

- to be careless of, to neglect
