# G2319 θεοστυγής

## theostygḗs

_(theh-os-too-gace' | theh-ose-too-GASE | thay-ose-tyoo-GASE)_

### Definition

from G2316 and the base of G4767; hateful to God, i.e., impious; hater of God; 

- hateful to God, exceptionally impious and wicked
