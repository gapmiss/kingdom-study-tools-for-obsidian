# G1427 δώδεκα

## dṓdeka

_(do'-dek-ah | THOH-theh-ka | THOH-thay-ka)_

### Definition

from G1417 and G1176; two and ten, i.e., a dozen; twelve; 

- twelve
  - the twelve apostles of Jesus, so called by way of eminence
