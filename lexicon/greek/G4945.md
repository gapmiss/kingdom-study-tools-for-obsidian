# G4945 συνωμοσία

## synōmosía

_(soon-o-mos-ee'-ah | soon-oh-moh-SEE-ah | syoon-oh-moh-SEE-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of G4862 and G3660; a swearing together, i.e., (by implication) a plot; comspiracy; 

- a swearing together
- a conspiracy
