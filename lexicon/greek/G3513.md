# G3513 νή

## nḗ

_(nay | nay | nay)_

### Definition

probably an intensive form of G3483; a particle of attestation (accompanied by the object invoked or appealed to in confirmation); as sure as; I protest by; 

- by
  - used in the affirmations of oaths
