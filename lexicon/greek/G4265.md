# G4265 προβλέπω

## problépō

_(prob-lep'-o | proh-VLEH-poh | proh-VLAY-poh)_

### Definition

from G4253 and G0991; to look out beforehand, i.e., furnish in advance; provide; 

- to foresee
- to provide
