# G5005 ταλαίπωρος

## talaípōros

_(tal-ah'-ee-po-ros | ta-LEH-poh-rose | ta-LAY-poh-rose)_

### Definition

from the base of G5007 and a derivative of the base of G3984; enduring trial, i.e., miserable; wretched; 

- enduring toils and troubles
- afflicted, wretched
