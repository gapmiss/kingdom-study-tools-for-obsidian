# G348 ἀνακόπτω

## anakóptō

_(an-ak-op'-to | ah-na-KOH-ptoh | ah-na-KOH-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G2875; to beat back, i.e., check; hinder; 

- to beat back, check (as in the course of a ship)
