# G4861 σύμψυχος

## sýmpsychos

_(soom'-psoo-khos | SOOM-psoo-hose | SYOOM-psyoo-hose)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G5590; co-spirited, i.e., similar in sentiment; like-minded; 

- of one mind, of one accord
