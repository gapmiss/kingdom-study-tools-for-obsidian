# G1211 δή

## dḗ

_(day | thay | thay)_

### Definition

probably akin to G1161; a particle of emphasis or explicitness; now, then, etc; also, and, doubtless, now, therefore; 

- now, then, verily, in truth, really, surely, certainly
- forthwith, at once
