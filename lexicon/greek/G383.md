# G383 ἀνασείω

## anaseíō

_(an-as-i'-o | ah-na-SEE-oh | ah-na-SEE-oh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G4579; figuratively, to excite; move, stir up; 

- to shake up
- to stir up, excite, rouse
