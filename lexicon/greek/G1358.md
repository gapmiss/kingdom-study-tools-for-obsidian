# G1358 διορύσσω

## diorýssō

_(dee-or-oos'-so | thee-oh-ROOS-soh | thee-oh-RYOOS-soh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G3736; to penetrate burglariously; break through (up); 

- to dig through: a house
