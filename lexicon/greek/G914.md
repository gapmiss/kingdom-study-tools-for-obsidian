# G914 Βαραχίας

## Barachías

_(bar-akh-ee'-as | va-ra-HEE-as | va-ra-HEE-as)_

### Definition

Jehovah blesses; of Hebrew origin (H1296); Barachias (i.e., Berechijah), an Israelite; Barachias; 

- said to be the father of the Zachariah slain in the temple
