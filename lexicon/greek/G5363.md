## philanthrōpía

_(fil-an-thro-pee'-ah | feel-an-throh-PEE-ah | feel-an-throh-PEE-ah)_

### Definition

from the same as G5364; fondness of mankind, i.e., benevolence ("philanthropy"); kindness, love towards man; 

- love of mankind, benevolence
