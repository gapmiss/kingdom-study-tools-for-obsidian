# G2888 κοσμοκράτωρ

## kosmokrátōr

_(kos-mok-fat'-ore | koh-smoh-KRA-tore | koh-smoh-KRA-tore)_

### Definition

from G2889 and G2902; a world-ruler, an epithet of Satan; ruler; 

- lord of the world, prince of this age
  - the devil and his demons
