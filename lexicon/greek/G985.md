# G985 βλαστάνω

## blastánō

_(blas-tan'-o | vla-STA-noh | vla-STA-noh)_

### Definition

from βλαστός (a sprout); to germinate; by implication, to yield fruit; bring forth, bud, spring (up); 

- to sprout, bud, put forth new leaves
- to produce
