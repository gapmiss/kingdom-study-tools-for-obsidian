# G647 ἀποστάσιον

## apostásion

_(ap-os-tas'-ee-on | ah-poh-STA-see-one | ah-poh-STA-see-one)_

### Definition

neuter of a (presumed) adjective from a derivative of G0868; properly, something separative, i.e., (specially) divorce; (writing of) divorcement; 

- divorce, repudiation
- a bill of divorce
