# G3909 παρατυγχάνω

## paratynchánō

_(par-at-oong-khan'-o | pa-ra-toong-HA-noh | pa-ra-tyoong-HA-noh)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G5177; to chance near, i.e., fall in with; meet with; 

- to chance to be by, to happen to be present, to meet by chance
