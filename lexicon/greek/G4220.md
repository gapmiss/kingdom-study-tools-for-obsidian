# G4220 πότερον

## póteron

_(pot'-er-on | POH-teh-rone | POH-tay-rone)_

### Definition

neuter of a comparative of the base of G4226; interrogative as adverb, which (of two), i.e., is it this or that; whether; 

- which of two
