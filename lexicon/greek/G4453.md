# G4453 πωλέω

## pōléō

_(po-leh'-o | poh-LEH-oh | poh-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

probably ultimately from πέλομαι (to be busy, to trade); to barter (as a pedlar), i.e., to sell; sell, whatever is sold; 

- to barter, to sell
- sellers
