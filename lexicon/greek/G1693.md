# G1693 ἐμμαίνομαι

## emmaínomai

_(em-mah'-ee-nom-ahee | em-MEH-noh-meh | ame-MAY-noh-may)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G3105; to rave on, i.e., rage at; be mad against; 

- to rage against one
