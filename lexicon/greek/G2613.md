# G2613 καταδικάζω

## katadikázō

_(kat-ad-ik-ad'-zo | ka-ta-thee-KA-zoh | ka-ta-thee-KA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and a derivative of G1349; to adjudge against, i.e., pronounce guilty; condemn; 

- to give judgment against (one), to pronounce guilty
- to condemn
