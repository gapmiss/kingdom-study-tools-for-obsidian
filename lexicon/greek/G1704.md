# G1704 ἐμπεριπατέω

## emperipatéō

_(em-per-ee-pat-eh'-o | em-peh-ree-pa-TEH-oh | ame-pay-ree-pa-TAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G4043; to perambulate on a place, i.e., (figuratively) to be occupied among persons; walk in; 

- to go about in, walk in
