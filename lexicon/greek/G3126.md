# G3126 μαμμωνᾶς

## mammōnâs

_(mam-mo-nas' | mahm-moh-NAHS | mahm-moh-NAHS)_

### Definition

of Chaldee origin (confidence, i.e. wealth, personified); mammonas, i.e., avarice (deified); mammon; 

- mammon
- treasure
- riches (where it is personified and opposed to God)
