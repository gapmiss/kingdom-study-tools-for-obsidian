# G2297 θαυμάσιος

## thaumásios

_(thow-mas'-ee-os | thev-MA-see-ose | tha-MA-see-ose)_

### Definition

from G2295; wondrous, i.e., (neuter as noun) a miracle; wonderful thing; 

- wonderful, marvellous
- wonderful deeds, wonders
