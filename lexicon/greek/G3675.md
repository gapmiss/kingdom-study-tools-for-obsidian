# G3675 ὁμόφρων

## homóphrōn

_(hom-of'-rone | hoh-MOH-frone | oh-MOH-frone)_

### Definition

from the base of G3674 and G5424; like-minded, i.e., harmonious; of one mind; 

- of one mind, concordant
