# G486 ἀντιλοιδορέω

## antiloidoréō

_(an-tee-loy-dor-eh'-o | an-tee-loo-thoh-REH-oh | an-tee-loo-thoh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0473 and G3058; to rail in reply; revile again; 

- to revile in turn, to retort railing
