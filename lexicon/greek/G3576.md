# G3576 νωθρός

## nōthrós

_(no-thros' | noh-THROSE | noh-THROSE)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G3541; sluggish, i.e., (literally) lazy, or (figuratively) stupid; dull, slothful; 

- slow, sluggish, indolent, dull, languid
