# G1926 ἐπιδέχομαι

## epidéchomai

_(ep-ee-dekh'-om-ahee | eh-pee-THEH-hoh-meh | ay-pee-THAY-hoh-may)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G1209; to admit (as a guest or (figuratively) teacher); receive; 

- to receive hospitality
- to admit, i.e., not to reject
