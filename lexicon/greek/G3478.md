# G3478 Ναζαρέθ

## Nazaréth

_(nad-zar-eth' | na-za-RETH | na-za-RAYTH)_

### Definition

the guarded one; of uncertain derivation; Nazareth or Nazaret, a place in Palestine; Nazareth; 

- the ordinary residence and home town of Christ
