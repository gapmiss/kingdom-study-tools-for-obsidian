# G2594 καρτερέω

## karteréō

_(kar-ter-eh'-o | kahr-teh-REH-oh | kahr-tay-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G2904 (transposed); to be strong, i.e., (figuratively) steadfast (patient); endure; 

- to be steadfast
