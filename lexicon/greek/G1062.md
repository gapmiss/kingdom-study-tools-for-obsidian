# G1062 γάμος

## gámos

_(gam'-os | GA-mose | GA-mose)_

### Definition

of uncertain affinity; nuptials; marriage, wedding; 

- a wedding or marriage festival, a wedding banquet, a wedding feast
- marriage, matrimony
