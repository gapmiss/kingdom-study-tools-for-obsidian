# G2642 καταλιθάζω

## katalitházō

_(kat-al-ith-ad'-zo | ka-ta-lee-THA-zoh | ka-ta-lee-THA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G3034; to stone down, i.e., to death; stone; 

- to overwhelm with stones, to stone
