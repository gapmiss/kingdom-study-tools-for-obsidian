# G4578 σεισμός

## seismós

_(sice-mos' | see-SMOSE | see-SMOSE)_

### Definition

from G4579; a commotion, i.e., (of the air) a gale, (of the ground) an earthquake; earthquake, tempest; 

- a shaking, a commotion
- a tempest
- an earthquake
