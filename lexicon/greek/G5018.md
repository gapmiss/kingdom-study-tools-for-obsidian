# G5018 Ταρσεύς

## Tarseús

_(tar-syoos' | tahr-SEVS | tahr-SAYFS)_

### Definition

a flat basket; from G5019; a Tarsean, i.e., native of Tarsus; of Tarsus; 

- belong to Tarsus, a native of Tarsus
