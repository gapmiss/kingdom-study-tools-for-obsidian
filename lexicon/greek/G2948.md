# G2948 κυλλός

## kyllós

_(kool-los' | kool-LOSE | kyool-LOSE)_

### Definition

from the same as G2947; rocking about, i.e., crippled (maimed, in feet or hands); maimed; 

- crooked
  - of the members of the body
  - injured, disabled
- maimed, mutilated
