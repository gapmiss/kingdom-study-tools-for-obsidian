# G1880 ἐπανέρχομαι

## epanérchomai

_(ep-an-er'-khom-ahee | ep-ah-NER-hoh-meh | ape-ah-NARE-hoh-may)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G0424; to come up on, i.e., return; come again, return; 

- to return, come back again
