# G2578 κάμπτω

## kámptō

_(kamp'-to | KAHM-ptoh | KAHM-ptoh)_

### Definition

apparently a primary verb; to bend; bow; 

- to bend, bow, the knee (the knees)
  - to one
    - in honor of one
    - in religious veneration
  - used of worshippers
- to bow one's self
