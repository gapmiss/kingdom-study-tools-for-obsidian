# G1846 ἐξορύσσω

## exorýssō

_(ex-or-oos'-so | eks-oh-ROOS-soh | ayks-oh-RYOOS-soh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G3736; to dig out, i.e., (by extension) to extract (an eye), remove (roofing); break up, pluck out; 

- to dig out, to pluck out (the eyes)
- to dig through
