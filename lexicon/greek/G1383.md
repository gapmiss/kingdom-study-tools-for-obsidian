# G1383 δοκίμιον

## dokímion

_(dok-im'-ee-on | thoh-KEE-mee-one | thoh-KEE-mee-one)_

### Definition

neuter of a presumed derivative of G1382; a testing; by implication, trustworthiness; trial, trying; 

- the proving
- that by which something is tried or proved, a test
