# G2813 κλέπτω

## kléptō

_(klep'-to | KLEH-ptoh | KLAY-ptoh)_

### Definition

a primary verb; to filch; steal; 

- to steal
  - to commit a theft
  - take away by theft i.e., take away by stealth
