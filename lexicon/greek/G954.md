# G954 Βεελζεβούλ

## Beelzeboúl

_(beh-el-zeb-ool' | veh-el-zeh-VOOL | vay-ale-zay-VOOL)_

### Definition

lord of the house; of Chaldee origin (by parody on H1176); dung-god; Beelzebul, a name of Satan; Beelzebub; 

- a name of Satan, the prince of evil spirits
