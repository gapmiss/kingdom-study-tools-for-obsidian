# G5575 ψευδομάρτυρ

## pseudomártyr

_(psyoo-dom-ar'-toor | psev-thoh-MAHR-toor | psave-thoh-MAHR-tyoor)_

### Definition

from G5571 and a kindred form of G3144; a spurious witness, i.e., bearer of untrue testimony; false witness; 

- a false witness
