# G855 ἄφαντος

## áphantos

_(af'-an-tos | AH-fahn-tose | AH-fahn-tose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G5316; non-manifested, i.e., invisible; vanished out of sight; 

- taken out of sight, made invisible
