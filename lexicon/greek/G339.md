# G339 ἀνακαθίζω

## anakathízō

_(an-ak-ath-id'-zo | ah-na-ka-THEE-zoh | ah-na-ka-THEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G2523; properly, to set up, i.e., (reflexively) to sit up; sit up; 

- to raise one's self and sit upright, to sit up, erect
