# G79 ἀδελφή

## adelphḗ

_(ad-el-fay' | ah-thel-FAY | ah-thale-FAY)_

### Definition

feminine of G0080; a sister (naturally or ecclesiastically); sister; 

- a full, own sister
- one connected by the tie of the Christian religion
