# G4257 προακούω

## proakoúō

_(pro-ak-oo'-o | proh-ah-KOO-oh | proh-ah-KOO-oh)_

### Definition

from G4253 and G0191; to hear already,i.e., anticipate; hear before; 

- to hear before
  - the hoped for salvation before its realisation
