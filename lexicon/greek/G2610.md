# G2610 καταγωνίζομαι

## katagōnízomai

_(kat-ag-o-nid'-zom-ahee | ka-ta-goh-NEE-zoh-meh | ka-ta-goh-NEE-zoh-may)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G0075; to struggle against, i.e., (by implication) to overcome; subdue; 

- to struggle against
- to overcome
