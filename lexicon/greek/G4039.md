# G4039 περιοικέω

## perioikéō

_(per-ee-oy-keh'-o | peh-ree-oo-KEH-oh | pay-ree-oo-KAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G4012 and G3611; to reside around, i.e., be a neighbor; dwell round about; 

- to dwell round about, to be one's neighbour
