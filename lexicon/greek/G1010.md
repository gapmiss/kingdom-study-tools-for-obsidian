# G1010 βουλευτής

## bouleutḗs

_(bool-yoo-tace' | voo-lev-TASE | voo-layf-TASE)_

### Definition

from G1011; an adviser, i.e., (specially) a councillor or member of the Jewish Sanhedrin; counsellor; 

- a councillor, senator
- a member of the Sanhedrin
