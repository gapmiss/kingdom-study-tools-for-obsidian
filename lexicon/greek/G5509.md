## chitṓn

_(khee-tone' | hee-TONE | hee-TONE)_

### Definition

of foreign origin (H3801); a tunic or shirt; clothes, coat, garment; 

- a tunic, an undergarment, usually worn next to the skin, a garment, a vestment
