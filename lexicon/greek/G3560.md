# G3560 νουθετέω

## nouthetéō

_(noo-thet-eh'-o | noo-theh-TEH-oh | noo-thay-TAY-oh)_

### Definition

from the same as G3559; to put in mind, i.e., (by implication) to caution or reprove gently; admonish, warn; 

- to admonish, warn, exhort
