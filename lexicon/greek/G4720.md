# G4720 Στάχυς

## Stáchys

_(stakh'-oos | STA-hoos | STA-hyoos)_

### Definition

a head of grain; the same as G4719; Stachys, a Christian; Stachys; 

- a man with a Greek name, he is said to be one of the seventy disciples, and bishop of Byzantium (Gill)
