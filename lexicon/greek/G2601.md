# G2601 καταβιβάζω

## katabibázō

_(kat-ab-ib-ad'-zo | ka-ta-vee-VA-zoh | ka-ta-vee-VA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and a derivative of the base of G0939; to cause to go down, i.e., precipitate; bring (thrust) down; 

- to cause to go down
- to bring down
- to cast down, thrust down
