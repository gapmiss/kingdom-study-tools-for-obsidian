# G3037 λίθος

## líthos

_(lee'-thos | LEE-those | LEE-those)_

### Definition

apparently a primary word; a stone (literally or figuratively); (mill-, stumbling-)stone; 

- a stone
  - of small stones
  - of building stones
  - metaphorically of Christ
