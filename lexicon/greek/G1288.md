# G1288 διασπάω

## diaspáō

_(dee-as-pah'-o | thee-ah-SPA-oh | thee-ah-SPA-oh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G4685; to draw apart, i.e., sever or dismember; pluck asunder, pull in pieces; 

- to rend asunder, break asunder
  - of a man, to tear in pieces
