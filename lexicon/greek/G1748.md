# G1748 ἐνεδρεύω

## enedreúō

_(en-ed-ryoo'-o | en-eh-THREV-oh | ane-ay-THRAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from G1747; to lurk, i.e., (figuratively) plot assassination; lay wait for; 

- to lie in wait for, to lay wait for, prepare a trap for
