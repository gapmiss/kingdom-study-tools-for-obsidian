# G5152 τρίστεγον

## trístegon

_(tris'-teg-on | TREES-teh-gone | TREES-tay-gone)_

### Definition

neuter of a compound of G5140 and G4721 as noun; a third roof (story); third loft; 

- having three roofs or stories, the third story
