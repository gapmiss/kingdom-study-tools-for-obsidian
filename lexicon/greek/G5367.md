# G5367 φίλαυτος

## phílautos

_(fil'-ow-tos | FEEL-av-tose | FEEL-af-tose)_

### Definition

from G5384 and G0846; fond of self, i.e., selfish; lover of own self; 

- loving one's self
- too intent on one's interest, selfish
