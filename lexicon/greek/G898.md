# G898 βαθμός

## bathmós

_(bath-mos' | vahth-MOSE | vahth-MOSE)_

### Definition

from the same as G0899; a step, i.e., (figuratively) grade (of dignity); degree; 

- threshold, step
  - of a grade of dignity and wholesome influence in the church
