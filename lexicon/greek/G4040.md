# G4040 περίοικος

## períoikos

_(per-ee'-oy-kos | peh-REE-oo-kose | pay-REE-oo-kose)_

### Definition

from G4012 and G3624; housed around, i.e., neighboring (used elliptically as a noun); neighbour; 

- dwelling around, a neighbour
