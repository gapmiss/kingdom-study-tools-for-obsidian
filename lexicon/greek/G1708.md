# G1708 ἐμπλοκή

## emplokḗ

_(em-plok-ay' | em-ploh-KAY | ame-ploh-KAY)_

### Definition

from G1707; elaborate braiding of the hair; plaiting; 

- an interweaving, braiding, a knot
- an elaborate gathering of one's hair into knots
