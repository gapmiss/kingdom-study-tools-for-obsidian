# G2073 ἑσπέρα

## hespéra

_(hes-per'-ah | heh-SPEH-ra | ay-SPAY-ra)_

### Definition

feminine of an adjective hesperos (evening); the eve (G5610 being implied); the evening; evening(-tide); 

- evening, eventide, nighttime
