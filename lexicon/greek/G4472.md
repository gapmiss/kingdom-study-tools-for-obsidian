# G4472 ῥαντίζω

## rhantízō

_(hran-tid'-zo | rahn-TEE-zoh | rahn-TEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of ῥαίνω (to sprinkle); to render besprinkled, i.e., asperse (ceremonially or figuratively); sprinkle; 

- to sprinkle
- to cleanse by sprinkling, hence to purify, cleanse
