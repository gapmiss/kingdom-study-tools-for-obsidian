# G2188 ἐφφαθά

## ephphathá

_(ef-fath-ah' | ef-fa-THA | afe-fa-THA)_

### Definition

of Chaldee origin (H6606); be opened!; Ephphatha; 

- be thou opened
  - receive the power of hearing, the ears of the deaf and the eyes of the blind being considered as closed
