# G2112 εὐθέως

## euthéōs

_(yoo-theh'-oce | ev-THEH-ose | afe-THAY-ose)_

### Definition

adverb from G2117; directly, i.e., at once or soon; anon, as soon as, forthwith, immediately, shortly, straightway; 

- straightway, immediately, forthwith
