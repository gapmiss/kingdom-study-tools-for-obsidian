# G2959 Κυρία

## Kyría

_(koo-ree'-ah | koo-REE-ah | kyoo-REE-ah)_

### Definition

feminine of G2962; Cyria, a Christian woman; lady; 

- a Christian woman to whom the second Epistle of John is addressed
