# G2856 κολοβόω

## kolobóō

_(kol-ob-o'-o | koh-loh-VOH-oh | koh-loh-VOH-oh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of the base of G2849; to dock, i.e., (figuratively) abridge; shorten; 

- to mutilate
- in New Testament: to shorten, abridge, curtail
