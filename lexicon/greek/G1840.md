# G1840 ἐξισχύω

## exischýō

_(ex-is-khoo'-o | eh-ksees-HOO-oh | ay-ksees-HYOO-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G2480; to have full strength, i.e., be entirely competent; be able; 

- to be eminently able, able, to have full strength
