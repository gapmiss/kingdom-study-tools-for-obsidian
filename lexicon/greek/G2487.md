# G2487 ἴχνος

## íchnos

_(ikh'-nos | EE-hnose | EE-hnose)_

### Definition

from ἰκνέομαι (to arrive; compare G2240); a track (figuratively); step; 

- a footprint, track, footstep
- in the New Testament, metaphorically of imitating the example of any one
