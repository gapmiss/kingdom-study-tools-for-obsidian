# G2713 κατέναντι

## katénanti

_(kat-en'-an-tee | ka-TEH-nahn-tee | ka-TAY-nahn-tee)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G1725; directly opposite; before, over against; 

- over against, opposite before
- metaphorically before one i.e., he being judge
