# G1962 ἐπινεύω

## epineúō

_(ep-een-yoo'-o | eh-pee-NEV-oh | ay-pee-NAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G3506; to nod at, i.e., (by implication) to assent; consent; 

- to nod to
- to express approval, to assent
