# G4354 προσλαλέω

## proslaléō

_(pros-lal-eh'-o | prose-la-LEH-oh | prose-la-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G4314 and G2980; to talk to, i.e., converse with; speak to (with); 

- to speak to
