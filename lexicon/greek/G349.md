# G349 ἀνακράζω

## anakrázō

_(an-ak-rad'-zo | ah-na-KRA-zoh | ah-na-KRA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G2896; to scream up (aloud); cry out; 

- to raise a cry from the depth of the throat, to cry out
