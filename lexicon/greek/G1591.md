# G1591 ἐκμάσσω

## ekmássō

_(ek-mas'-so | ek-MAHS-soh | ake-MAHS-soh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and the base of G3145; to knead out, i.e., (by analogy) to wipe dry; wipe; 

- to wipe off, to wipe away
