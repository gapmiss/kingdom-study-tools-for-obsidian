# G884 ἀχάριστος

## acháristos

_(ach-ar'-is-tos | ah-HA-ree-stose | ah-HA-ree-stose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G5483; thankless, i.e., ungrateful; unthankful; 

- ungracious
  - unpleasing
  - unthankful
