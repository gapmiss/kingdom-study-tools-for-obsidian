# G1684 ἐμβαίνω

## embaínō

_(em-ba'-hee-no | em-VEH-noh | ame-VAY-noh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and the base of G0939; to walk on, i.e., embark (aboard a vessel), reach (a pool); come (get) into, enter (into), go (up) into, step in, take ship; 

- to go into, step into
