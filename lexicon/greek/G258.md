# G258 ἀλώπηξ

## alṓpēx

_(al-o'-pakes | ah-LOH-payks | ah-LOH-payks)_

### Definition

of uncertain derivation; a fox, i.e., (figuratively) a cunning person; fox; 

- a fox
- metaphorically a sly or crafty man
