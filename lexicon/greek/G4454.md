# G4454 πῶλος

## pōlos

_(po'-los | POH-lose | POH-lose)_

### Definition

apparently a primary word; a "foal" or "filly", i.e., (specially), a young ass; colt; 

- a colt, the young of a horse
- a young creature
  - of a young ass
  - an ass's colt
