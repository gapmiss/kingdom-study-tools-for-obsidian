# G360 ἀναλύω

## analýō

_(an-al-oo'-o | ah-na-LOO-oh | ah-na-LYOO-oh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G3089; to break up, i.e., depart (literally or figuratively); depart, return; 

- to unloose, undo again
- to depart, break up, to depart from life, to return
