# G1018 βραβεύω

## brabeúō

_(brab-yoo'-o | vra-VEV-oh | vra-VAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from the same as G1017; to arbitrate, i.e., (genitive case) to govern (figuratively, prevail); rule; 

- to be an umpire
- to decide, determine
- to direct, control, rule
