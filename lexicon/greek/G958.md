## Beniamín

_(ben-ee-am-een' | veh-nee-ah-MEEN | vay-nee-ah-MEEN)_

### Definition

son of the right hand, son of good fortune; of Hebrew origin (H1144); Benjamin, an Israelite; Benjamin; 

- Jacob's twelfth son
- the tribe of Benjamin
