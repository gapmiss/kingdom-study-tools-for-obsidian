# G5492 χειμάζω

## cheimázō

_(khi-mad'-zo | hee-MA-zoh | hee-MA-zoh)_

### Definition

from the same as G5494; to storm, i.e., (passively) to labor under a gale; be tossed with tempest; 

- to afflict with a tempest, to toss about upon the waves
