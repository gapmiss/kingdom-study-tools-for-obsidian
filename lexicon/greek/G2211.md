# G2211 Ζηνᾶς

## Zēnâs

_(dzay-nas' | zay-NAHS | zay-NAHS)_

### Definition

Jupiter; probably contracted from a poetic form of G2203 and G1435; Jove-given; Zenas, a Christian; Zenas; 

- a teacher of the Jewish law and afterwards a Christian
