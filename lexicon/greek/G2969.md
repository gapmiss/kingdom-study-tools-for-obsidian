# G2969 κωμόπολις

## kōmópolis

_(ko-mop'-ol-is | koh-MOH-poh-lees | koh-MOH-poh-lees)_

### Definition

from G2968 and G4172; an unwalled city; town; 

- a village approximating in size and number of inhabitants to a city, a village city, a town
