# G1703 ἐμπαίκτης

## empaíktēs

_(emp-aheek-tace' | em-PEK-tase | ame-PAKE-tase)_

### Definition

from G1702; a derider, i.e., (by implication) a false teacher; mocker, scoffer; 

- a mocker, a scoffer
