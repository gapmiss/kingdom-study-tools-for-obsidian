# G3602 ὀδυρμός

## odyrmós

_(od-oor-mos' | oh-thoor-MOSE | oh-thyoor-MOSE)_

### Definition

from a derivative of the base of G1416; moaning, i.e., lamentation; mourning; 

- wailing, lamentation, mourning
