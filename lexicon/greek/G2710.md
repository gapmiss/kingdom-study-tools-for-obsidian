# G2710 καταχράομαι

## katachráomai

_(kat-akh-rah'-om-ahee | ka-ta-HRA-oh-meh | ka-ta-HRA-oh-may)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G5530; to overuse, i.e., misuse; abuse; 

- to use much or excessively or ill
- to use up, consume by use
- to use fully
