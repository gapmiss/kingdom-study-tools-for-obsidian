# G3607 ὀθόνη

## othónē

_(oth-on'-ay | oh-THOH-nay | oh-THOH-nay)_

### Definition

of uncertain affinity; a linen cloth, i.e., (especially) a sail; sheet; 

- linen (i.e., fine white linen for women's clothing)
- linen clothes (sheet or sail)
