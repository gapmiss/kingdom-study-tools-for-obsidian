# G5163 τροχιά

## trochiá

_(trokh-ee-ah' | troh-hee-AH | troh-hee-AH)_

### Definition

from G5164; a track (as a wheel-rut), i.e., (figuratively) a course of conduct; path; 

- a track of a wheel, a rut
- a track, a path
