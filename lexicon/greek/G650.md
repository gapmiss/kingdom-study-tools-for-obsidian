# G650 ἀποστερέω

## aposteréō

_(ap-os-ter-eh'-o | ah-poh-steh-REH-oh | ah-poh-stay-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and στερέω (to deprive); to despoil; defraud, destitute, kept back by fraud; 

- to defraud, rob, despoil
