# G613 ἀποκρύπτω

## apokrýptō

_(ap-ok-roop'-to | ah-poh-KROO-ptoh | ah-poh-KRYOO-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G2928; to conceal away (i.e., fully); figuratively, to keep secret; hide; 

- to hide
- concealing, keeping secret
