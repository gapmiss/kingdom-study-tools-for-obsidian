# G1188 δεξιός

## dexiós

_(dex-ee-os' | theh-ksee-OSE | thay-ksee-OSE)_

### Definition

from G1209; the right side or (feminine) hand (as that which usually takes); right (hand, side); 

- the right, the right hand
- metaphorically
  - a place of honor or authority
