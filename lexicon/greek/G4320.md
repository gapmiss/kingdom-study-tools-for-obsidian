# G4320 προσαναβαίνω

## prosanabaínō

_(pros-an-ab-ah'-ee-no | prose-ah-na-VEH-noh | prose-ah-na-VAY-noh)_

### Definition

from G4314 and G0305; to ascend farther, i.e., be promoted (take an upper (more honorable) seat); go up; 

- to go up further
- go up higher
