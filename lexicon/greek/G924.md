# G924 Βαρτιμαῖος

## Bartimaîos

_(bar-tim-ah'-yos | vahr-tee-MEH-ose | vahr-tee-MAY-ose)_

### Definition

son of Timaeus; of Chaldee origin (H1247 and H2931); son of Timaeus (or the unclean); Bar-timaeus, an Israelite; Bartimaeus; 

- a certain blind man
