# G658 ἀποτελέω

## apoteléō

_(ap-ot-el-eh'-o | ah-poh-teh-LEH-oh | ah-poh-tay-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G5055; to complete entirely, i.e., consummate; finish; 

- to perfect, to bring quite to an end
- accomplish
