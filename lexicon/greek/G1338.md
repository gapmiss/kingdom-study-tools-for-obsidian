# G1338 διϊκνέομαι

## diïknéomai

_(dee-ik-neh'-om-ahee | thee-ee-KNEH-oh-meh | thee-ee-KNAY-oh-may)_

### Definition

from G1223 and the base of G2425; to reach through, i.e., penetrate; pierce; 

- to go through, penetrate, pierce
