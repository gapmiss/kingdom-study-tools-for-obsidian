# G1369 διχάζω

## dicházō

_(dee-khad'-zo | thee-HA-zoh | thee-HA-zoh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G1364; to make apart, i.e., sunder (figuratively, alienate); set at variance; 

- to cut into two parts, cleave asunder, sever
