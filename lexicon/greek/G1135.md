# G1135 γυνή

## gynḗ

_(goo-nay' | goo-NAY | gyoo-NAY)_

### Definition

probably from the base of G1096; a woman; specially, a wife; wife, woman; 

- a woman of any age, whether a virgin, or married, or a widow
- a wife
  - of a betrothed woman
