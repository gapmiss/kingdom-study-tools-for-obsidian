# G1915 ἐπίβλημα

## epíblēma

_(ep-ib'-lay-mah | eh-PEE-vlay-ma | ay-PEE-vlay-ma)_

### Definition

from G1911; a patch; piece; 

- that which is thrown or put upon a thing, or that which is added to it
  - an addition
  - that which is sewed on to cover a rent, a patch
