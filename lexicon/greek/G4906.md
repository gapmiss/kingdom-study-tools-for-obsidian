# G4906 συνεσθίω

## synesthíō

_(soon-es-thee'-o | soon-eh-STHEE-oh | syoon-ay-STHEE-oh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G2068 (including its alternate); to take food in company with; eat with; 

- to eat with, take food together with
