# G5220 ὕπανδρος

## hýpandros

_(hoop'-an-dros | HOO-pahn-throse | YOO-pahn-throse)_

### Definition

from G5259 and G0435; in subjection under a man, i.e., a married woman; which hath an husband; 

- under i.e., subject to a man: married
