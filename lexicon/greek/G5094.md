# G5094 τιμιότης

## timiótēs

_(tim-ee-ot'-ace | tee-mee-OH-tase | tee-mee-OH-tase)_

### Definition

from G5093; expensiveness, i.e., (by implication) magnificence; costliness; 

- preciousness, costliness
  - an abundance of costly things
- metaphorically worth, excellence
