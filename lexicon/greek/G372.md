# G372 ἀνάπαυσις

## anápausis

_(an-ap'-ow-sis | ah-NA-pav-sees | ah-NA-paf-sees)_

### Definition

from G0373; intermission; by implication, recreation; rest; 

- intermission, cessation of any motion, business or labor
- rest, recreation
