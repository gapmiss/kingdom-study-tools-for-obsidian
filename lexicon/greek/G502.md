# G502 ἄντλημα

## ántlēma

_(ant'-lay-mah | AN-t-lay-ma | AN-t-lay-ma)_

### Definition

from G0501; a baling-vessel; thing to draw with; 

- what is drawn
- the act of drawing water
- a thing to draw with, e.g., bucket and rope let down into a well
