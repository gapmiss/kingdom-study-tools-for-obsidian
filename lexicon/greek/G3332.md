# G3332 μεταίρω

## metaírō

_(met-ah'-ee-ro | meh-TEH-roh | may-TAY-roh)_

### Definition

from G3326 and G0142; to betake oneself, i.e., remove (locally); depart; 

- to lift up and remove from one place to another, to transfer
- to go away, depart
