# G1191 Δέρβη

## Dérbē

_(der-bay' | THER-vay | THARE-vay)_

### Definition

tanner, tanner of skin, coverer with skin; of foreign origin; Derbe, a place in Asia Minor; Derbe; 

- a city of Lycaonia, on the confines of Isauria
