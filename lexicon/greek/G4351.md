# G4351 προσκυλίω

## proskylíō

_(pros-koo-lee'-o | prose-koo-LEE-oh | prose-kyoo-LEE-oh)_

### Definition

from G4314 and G2947; to roll towards, i.e., block against; roll (to); 

- to roll to
