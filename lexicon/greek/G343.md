# G343 ἀνακαλύπτω

## anakalýptō

_(an-ak-al-oop'-to | ah-na-ka-LOO-ptoh | ah-na-ka-LYOO-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G0303 (in the sense of reversal) and G2572; to unveil; open, (un-)taken away; 

- to unveil or uncover (by drawing back a veil)
