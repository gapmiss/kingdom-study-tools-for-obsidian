# G631 ἀπομάσσομαι

## apomássomai

_(ap-om-as'-som-ahee | ah-poh-MAHS-soh-meh | ah-poh-MAHS-soh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G0575 and μάσσω (to squeeze, knead, smear); to scrape away; wipe off; 

- to wipe off
- to wipe off one's self, to wipe of for one's self
