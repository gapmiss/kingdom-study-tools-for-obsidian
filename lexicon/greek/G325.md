# G325 ἀναδίδωμι

## anadídōmi

_(an-ad-eed'-om-ee | ah-na-THEE-thoh-mee | ah-na-THEE-thoh-mee)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G1325; to hand over; deliver; 

- to give forth, send up, so the earth producing plants, yielding fruit etc.
- to deliver up, hand over
