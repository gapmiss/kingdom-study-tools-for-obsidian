# G3441 μόνος

## mónos

_(mon'-os | MOH-nose | MOH-nose)_

### Definition

probably from G3306; remaining, i.e., sole or single; by implication, mere; alone, only, by themselves; 

- alone (without a companion), forsaken, destitute of help, alone, only, merely
