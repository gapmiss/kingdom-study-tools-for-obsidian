# G3135 μαργαρίτης

## margarítēs

_(mar-gar-ee'-tace | mahr-ga-REE-tase | mahr-ga-REE-tase)_

### Definition

from μάργαρος (a pearl-oyster); a pearl; pearl; 

- a pearl
- a proverb, i.e., a word of great value
