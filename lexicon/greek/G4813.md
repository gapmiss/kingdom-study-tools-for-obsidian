# G4813 συλάω

## syláō

_(soo-lah'-o | soo-LA-oh | syoo-LA-oh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of σύλλω (to strip; probably akin to G0138; compare G4661); to despoil; rob; 

- to rob, despoil
