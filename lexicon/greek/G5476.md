# G5476 χαμαί

## chamaí

_(kham-ah'-ee | ha-MEH | ha-MAY)_

### Definition

adverb perhaps from the base of G5490 through the idea of a fissure in the soil; earthward, i.e., prostrate; on (to) the ground; 

- on the ground, on the earth
- to the ground
