# G3838 παντελής

## pantelḗs

_(pan-tel-ace' | pahn-teh-LASE | pahn-tay-LASE)_

### Definition

from G3956 and G5056; full-ended, i.e., entire (neuter as noun, completion); + in (no) wise, uttermost; 

- all complete, perfect
- completely, perfectly, utterly
