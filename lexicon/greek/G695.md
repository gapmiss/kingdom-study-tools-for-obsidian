# G695 ἀργυροκόπος

## argyrokópos

_(ar-goo-rok-op'-os | ar-goo-roh-KOH-pose | ar-gyoo-roh-KOH-pose)_

### Definition

from G0696 and G2875; a beater (i.e., worker) of silver; silversmith; 

- a silversmith
