# G121 ἄθωος

## áthōos

_(ath'-o-os | AH-thoh-ose | AH-thoh-ose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and probably a derivative of G5087 (meaning a penalty); not guilty; innocent; 

- not guilty, innocent, unpunished
