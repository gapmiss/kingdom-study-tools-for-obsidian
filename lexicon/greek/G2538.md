# G2538 καινότης

## kainótēs

_(kahee-not'-ace | keh-NOH-tase | kay-NOH-tase)_

### Definition

from G2537; renewal (figuratively); newness; 

- newness
  - in the new state of life in which the Holy Spirit places us so as to produce a new state which is eternal life
