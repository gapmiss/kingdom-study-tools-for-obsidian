# G5275 ὑπολείπω

## hypoleípō

_(hoop-ol-i'-po | hoo-poh-LEE-poh | yoo-poh-LEE-poh)_

### Definition

from G5295 and G3007; to leave under (behind), i.e., (passively) to remain (survive); be left; 

- to leave behind
- to be left behind, left remaining
  - of a survivor
