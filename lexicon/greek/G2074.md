## Esrṓm

_(es-rome | es-ROME | ase-ROME)_

### Definition

enclosed; of Hebrew origin (H2696); Esrom (i.e., Chetsron), an Israelite; Esrom; 

- the son of Reuben, and ancestor of the Hezronites
