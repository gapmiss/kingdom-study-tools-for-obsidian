# G5388 φιλότεκνος

## philóteknos

_(fil-ot'-ek-nos | feel-OH-teh-knose | feel-OH-tay-knose)_

### Definition

from G5384 and G5043; fond of one's children, i.e., maternal; love their children; 

- loving one's offspring or children
