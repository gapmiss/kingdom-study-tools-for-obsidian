# G4867 συναθροίζω

## synathroízō

_(soon-ath-royd'-zo | soon-ah-THROO-zoh | syoon-ah-THROO-zoh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and ἀθροίζω (to hoard); to convene; call (gather) together; 

- to gather together with others
- to assemble
- to be gathered together i.e., to come together
