# G4601 σιγάω

## sigáō

_(see-gah'-o | see-GA-oh | see-GA-oh)_

### Definition

from G4602; to keep silent (transitively or intransitively); keep close (secret, silence), hold peace; 

- to keep silence, hold one's peace
- to be kept in silence, be concealed
