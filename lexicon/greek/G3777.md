# G3777 οὔτε

## oúte

_(oo'-teh | OO-teh | OO-tay)_

### Definition

from G3756 and G5037; not too, i.e., neither or nor; by analogy, not even; neither, none, nor (yet), (no, yet) not, nothing; 

- neither, and not
