# G2964 κυρόω

## kyróō

_(koo-ro'-o | koo-ROH-oh | kyoo-ROH-oh)_

### Definition

from the same as G2962; to make authoritative, i.e., ratify; confirm; 

- to make valid
- to confirm publicly or solemnly, to ratify
