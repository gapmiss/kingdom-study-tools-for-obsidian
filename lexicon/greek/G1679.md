# G1679 ἐλπίζω

## elpízō

_(el-pid'-zo | el-PEE-zoh | ale-PEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1680; to expect or confide; (have, thing) hope(-d) (for), trust; 

- to hope
  - in a religious sense, to wait for salvation with joy and full confidence
- hopefully to trust in
