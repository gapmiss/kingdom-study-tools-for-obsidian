# G2921 Κρίσπος

## Kríspos

_(kris'-pos | KREE-spose | KREE-spose)_

### Definition

curled; of Latin origin; "crisp"; Crispus, a Corinthian; Crispus; 

- the ruler of the Jewish synagogue in Corinth, baptized by Paul
