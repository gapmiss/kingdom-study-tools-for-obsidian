# G3184 μεθύω

## methýō

_(meth-oo'-o | meh-THOO-oh | may-THYOO-oh)_

### Definition

from another form of G3178; to drink to intoxication, i.e., get drunk; drink well, make (be) drunk(-en); 

- to be drunken
- metaphorically of one who has shed blood or murdered profusely
