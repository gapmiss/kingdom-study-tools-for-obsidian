# G2464 Ἰσαάκ

## Isaák

_(ee-sah-ak' | ee-sa-AK | ee-sa-AK)_

### Definition

to laugh; of Hebrew origin (H3327); Isaac (i.e., Jitschak), the son of Abraham; Isaac; 

- the son of Abraham and Sarah
