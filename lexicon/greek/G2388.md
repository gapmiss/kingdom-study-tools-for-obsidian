# G2388 Ἰαννά

## Ianná

_(ee-an-nah' | ee-an-NA | ee-an-NA)_

### Definition

flourishing; probably of Hebrew origin (compare H3238); Janna, an Israelite; Janna; 

- son of Joseph and the father of Melchi, in the genealogy of Christ
