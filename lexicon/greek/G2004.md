# G2004 ἐπιτάσσω

## epitássō

_(ep-ee-tas'-so | eh-pee-TAHS-soh | ay-pee-TAHS-soh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G5021; to arrange upon, i.e., order; charge, command, injoin; 

- to enjoin upon, order, command, charge
