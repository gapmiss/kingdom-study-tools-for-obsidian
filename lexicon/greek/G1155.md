# G1155 δανείζω

## daneízō

_(dan-ide'-zo | tha-NEE-zoh | tha-NEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1156; to loan on interest; reflexively, to borrow; borrow, lend; 

- to lend money
- to have money lent to one's self
- to take a loan, borrow
