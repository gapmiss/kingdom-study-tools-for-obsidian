# G5084 τήρησις

## tḗrēsis

_(tay'-ray-sis | TAY-ray-sees | TAY-ray-sees)_

### Definition

from G5083; a watching, i.e., (figuratively) observance, or (concretely) a prison; hold; 

- a watching
  - of prisoners
  - a place where prisoners are kept, a prison
- a keeping
  - complying with, obeying
