# G5186 τυφλόω

## typhlóō

_(toof-lo'-o | too-FLOH-oh | tyoo-FLOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G5185; to make blind, i.e., (figuratively) to obscure; blind; 

- to blind, make blind
- in the New Testament metaphorically to blunt the mental discernment, darken the mind
