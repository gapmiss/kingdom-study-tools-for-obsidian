# G48 ἁγνίζω

## hagnízō

_(hag-nid'-zo | ha-GNEE-zoh | a-GNEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G0053; to make clean, i.e., (figuratively) sanctify (ceremonially or morally); purify (self); 

- ceremonially
  - to make pure, purify, cleanse
- morally
