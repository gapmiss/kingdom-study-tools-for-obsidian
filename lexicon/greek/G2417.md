# G2417 ἱερόσυλος

## hierósylos

_(hee-er-os'-oo-los | hee-eh-ROSE-oo-lose | ee-ay-ROSE-yoo-lose)_

### Definition

from G2411 and G4813; a temple-despoiler; robber of churches; 

- guilty of sacrilege
