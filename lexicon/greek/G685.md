# G685 ἀρά

## ará

_(ar-ah' | ah-RA | ah-RA)_

### Definition

probably from G0142; properly, prayer (as lifted to Heaven), i.e., (by implication) imprecation; curse; 

- a prayer, a supplication
- an imprecation, curse, malediction
