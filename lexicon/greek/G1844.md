# G1844 ἐξορκίζω

## exorkízō

_(ex-or-kid'-zo | eks-ore-KEE-zoh | ayks-ore-KEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G3726; to exact an oath, i.e., conjure; adjure; 

- to extract an oath, to force to an oath
- to adjure
