# G3058 λοιδορέω

## loidoréō

_(loy-dor-eh'-o | loo-thoh-REH-oh | loo-thoh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G3060; to reproach, i.e., vilify; revile; 

- to reproach, rail at, revile, heap abuse upon
