# G2200 ζεστός

## zestós

_(dzes-tos' | zeh-STOSE | zay-STOSE)_

### Definition

from G2204; boiled, i.e., (by implication) calid (figuratively, fervent); hot; 

- boiling hot, hot
- metaphorically of fervour of mind an zeal
