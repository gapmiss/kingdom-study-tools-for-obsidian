# G2691 καταστρηνιάω

## katastrēniáō

_(kat-as-tray-nee-ah'-o | ka-ta-stray-nee-AH-oh | ka-ta-stray-nee-AH-oh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G4763; to become voluptuous against; begin to wax wanton against; 

- to feel the impulses of sexual desire
