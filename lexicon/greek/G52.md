# G52 ἄγνοια

## ágnoia

_(ag'-noy-ah | AH-gnoo-ah | AH-gnoo-ah)_

### Definition

from G0050; ignorance (properly, the quality); ignorance; 

- lack of knowledge, ignorance
  - especially of divine things
  - of moral blindness
