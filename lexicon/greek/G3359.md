# G3359 μέτωπον

## métōpon

_(met'-o-pon | MEH-toh-pone | MAY-toh-pone)_

### Definition

from G3326 and ops (the face); the forehead (as opposite the countenance); forehead; 

- the space between the eyes, the forehead
