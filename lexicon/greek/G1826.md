# G1826 ἔξειμι

## éxeimi

_(ex'-i-mee | EKS-ee-mee | AYKS-ee-mee)_

### Definition

from G1537 and εἶμι (to go); to issue, i.e., leave (a place), escape (to the shore); depart, get (to land), go out; 

- to go forth, go out
