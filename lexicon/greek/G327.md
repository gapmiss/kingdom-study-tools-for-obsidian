# G327 ἀναζητέω

## anazētéō

_(an-ad-zay-teh'-o | ah-na-zay-TEH-oh | ah-na-zay-TAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G2212; to search out; seek; 

- to seek out, search through, make diligent search
