# G376 ἀνάπηρος

## anápēros

_(an-ap'-ay-ros | ah-NA-pay-rose | ah-NA-pay-rose)_

### Definition

from G0303 (in the sense of intensity) and πῆρος (maimed); crippled; maimed; 

- disabled in the limbs, maimed, crippled
- injured in, or bereft of, some member of the body
