# G2598 καταβάλλω

## katabállō

_(kat-ab-al'-lo | ka-ta-VAHL-loh | ka-ta-VAHL-loh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G0906; to throw down; cast down, lay; 

- to cast down
  - to throw to the ground, prostate
- to put in a lower place
  - to lay (down) a foundation
