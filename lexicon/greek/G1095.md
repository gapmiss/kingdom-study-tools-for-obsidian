# G1095 γηράσκω

## gēráskō

_(ghay-ras'-ko | gay-RA-skoh | gay-RA-skoh)_

### Definition

from G1094; to be senescent; be (wax) old; 

- to grow old
- of things and institutions: to fail from age, be obsolescent
