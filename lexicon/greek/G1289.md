# G1289 διασπείρω

## diaspeírō

_(dee-as-pi'-ro | thee-ah-SPEE-roh | thee-ah-SPEE-roh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G4687; to sow throughout, i.e., (figuratively) distribute in foreign lands; scatter abroad; 

- to scatter abroad, disperse
