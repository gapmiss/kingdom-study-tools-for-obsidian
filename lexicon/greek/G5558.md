# G5558 χρυσόω

## chrysóō

_(khroo-so'-o | hroo-SOH-oh | hryoo-SOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G5557; to gild, i.e., bespangle with golden ornaments; deck; 

- to adorn with gold, to gild
  - of a woman ornamented with gold so profusely that she seems to be gilded
