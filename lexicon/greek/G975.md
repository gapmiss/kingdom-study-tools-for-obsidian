# G975 βιβλίον

## biblíon

_(bib-lee'-on | vee-VLEE-one | vee-VLEE-one)_

### Definition

a diminutive of G0976; a roll; bill, book, scroll, writing; 

- a small book, a scroll, a written document
- a sheet on which something has been written
  - a bill of divorcement
