# G1640 ἐλάσσων

## elássōn

_(el-as'-sone | eh-LAHS-sone | ay-LAHS-sone)_

### Definition

comparative of the same as G1646; smaller (in size, quantity, age or quality); less, under, worse, younger; 

- less
  - in age (younger)
  - in rank
  - in excellence, worse
