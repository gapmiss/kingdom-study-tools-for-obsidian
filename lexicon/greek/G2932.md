# G2932 κτάομαι

## ktáomai

_(ktah'-om-ahee | k-TA-oh-meh | k-TA-oh-may)_

### Definition

a primary verb; to get, i.e., acquire (by any means; own); obtain, possess, provide, purchase; 

- to acquire, get, or procure a thing for one's self, to possess
  - to marry a wife
