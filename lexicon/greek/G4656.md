# G4656 σκοτόω

## skotóō

_(skot-o'-o | skoh-TOH-oh | skoh-TOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G4655; to obscure or blind (literally or figuratively); be full of darkness; 

- to darken, cover with darkness
- metaphorically to darken or blind the mind
