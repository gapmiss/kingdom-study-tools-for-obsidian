# G4537 σαλπίζω

## salpízō

_(sal-pid'-zo | sahl-PEE-zoh | sahl-PEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G4536; to trumpet, i.e., sound a blast (literally or figuratively); (which are yet to) sound (a trumpet); 

- to sound a trumpet
