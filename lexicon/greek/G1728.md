# G1728 ἐνάρχομαι

## enárchomai

_(en-ar'-khom-ahee | en-AR-hoh-meh | ane-AR-hoh-may)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G0756; to commence on; rule (by mistake for G0757); 

- to begin, to make a beginning
