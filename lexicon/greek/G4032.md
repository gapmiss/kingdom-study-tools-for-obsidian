# G4032 περικρύπτω

## perikrýptō

_(per-ee-kroop'-to | peh-ree-KROO-ptoh | pay-ree-KRYOO-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G4012 and G2928; to conceal all around, i.e., entirely; hide; 

- to conceal on all sides or entirely, to hide, seclude
