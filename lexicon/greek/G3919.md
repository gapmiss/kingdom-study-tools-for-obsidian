# G3919 παρεισάγω

## pareiságō

_(par-ice-ag'-o | pa-ree-SA-goh | pa-ree-SA-goh)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G1521; to lead in aside, i.e., introduce surreptitiously; privily bring in; 

- to introduce or bring in secretly or craftily
