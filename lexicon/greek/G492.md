# G492 ἀντιπαρέρχομαι

## antiparérchomai

_(an-tee-par-er'-khom-ahee | an-tee-pa-RER-hoh-meh | an-tee-pa-RARE-hoh-may)_

### Definition

from G0473 and G3928; to go along opposite; pass by on the other side; 

- to pass by opposite to
