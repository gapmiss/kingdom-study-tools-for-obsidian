# G5589 ψιχίον

## psichíon

_(psikh-ee'-on | psee-HEE-one | psee-HEE-one)_

### Definition

diminutive from a derivative of the base of G5567 (meaning a crumb); a little bit or morsel; crumb; 

- a little morsel, a crumb
