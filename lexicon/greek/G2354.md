# G2354 θρηνέω

## thrēnéō

_(thray-neh'-o | thray-NEH-oh | thray-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G2355; to bewail; lament, mourn; 

- to mourn, to lament
  - of singers of dirges, [to wail]
- to bewail, deplore
