# G1560 ἔκδοτος

## ékdotos

_(ek'-dot-os | EK-thoh-tose | AKE-thoh-tose)_

### Definition

from G1537 and a derivative of G1325; given out or over, i.e., surrendered; delivered; 

- given over, delivered up
  - to enemies, or to the power, the will, of some one
