# G2904 κράτος

## krátos

_(krat'-os | KRA-tose | KRA-tose)_

### Definition

perhaps a primary word; vigor ("great") (literally or figuratively); dominion, might(-ily), power, strength; 

- force, strength
- power, might: mighty with great power
  - a mighty deed, a work of power
- dominion
