# G1060 γαμέω

## gaméō

_(gam-eh'-o | ga-MEH-oh | ga-MAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1062; to wed (of either sex); marry (a wife); 

- to lead in marriage, take to wife
  - to get married, to marry
  - to give one's self in marriage
- to give a daughter in marriage
