# G3949 παροργίζω

## parorgízō

_(par-org-id'-zo | pa-rore-GEE-zoh | pa-rore-GEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G3710; to anger alongside, i.e., enrage; anger, provoke to wrath; 

- to rouse to wrath, to provoke, exasperate, anger
