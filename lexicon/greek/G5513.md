# G5513 χλιαρός

## chliarós

_(khlee-ar-os' | hlee-ah-ROSE | hlee-ah-ROSE)_

### Definition

from χλίω (to warm); tepid; lukewarm; 

- tepid, lukewarm
- metaphorically of the condition of the soul wretchedly fluctuating between a torpor and a fervour of love
