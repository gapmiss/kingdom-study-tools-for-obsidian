# G3010 λειτουργικός

## leitourgikós

_(li-toorg-ik-os' | lee-toor-gee-KOSE | lee-toor-gee-KOSE)_

### Definition

from the same as G3008; functional publicly ("liturgic"); i.e., beneficient; ministering; 

- relating to the performance of service, employed in ministering
