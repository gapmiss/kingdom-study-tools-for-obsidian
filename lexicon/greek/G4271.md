# G4271 πρόδηλος

## pródēlos

_(prod'-ay-los | PROH-thay-lose | PROH-thay-lose)_

### Definition

from G4253 and G1212; plain before all men, i.e., obvious; evident, manifest (open) beforehand; 

- openly evident, known to all, manifest
