# G426 ἀνετάζω

## anetázō

_(an-et-ad'-zo | ah-neh-TA-zoh | ah-nay-TA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and ἐτάζω (to test); to investigate (judicially); (should have) examined(-d); 

- to investigate, to examine
- to examine judicially
