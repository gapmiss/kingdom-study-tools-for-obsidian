# G3044 Λίνος

## Línos

_(lee'-nos | LEE-nose | LEE-nose)_

### Definition

a net; perhaps from G3043; Linus, a Christian; Linus; 

- a Christian at Rome, known to Paul and to Timothy, 2 Timothy 4:21, who was the first bishop of Rome after the apostles (AD64)
