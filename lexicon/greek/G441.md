# G441 ἀνθρωπάρεσκος

## anthrōpáreskos

_(anth-ro-par'-es-kos | an-throh-PA-reh-skose | an-throh-PA-ray-skose)_

### Definition

from G0444 and G0700; man-courting, i.e., fawning; men-pleaser; 

- studying to please man, courting the favor of men
