# G61 ἄγρα

## ágra

_(ag'-rah | AH-gra | AH-gra)_

### Definition

from G0071; (abstractly) a catching (of fish); also (concretely) a haul (of fish); draught; 

- a catching, hunting
- that which is taken in hunting, the booty, prey
  - of birds or beasts, game
  - of fish, a draught or haul
