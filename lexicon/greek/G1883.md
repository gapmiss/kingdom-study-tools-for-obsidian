# G1883 ἐπάνω

## epánō

_(ep-an'-o | ep-AH-noh | ape-AH-noh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G0507; up above, i.e., over or on (of place, amount, rank, etc.); above, more than, (up-)on, over; 

- above
  - of place
  - of number: more than
