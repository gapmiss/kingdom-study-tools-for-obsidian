# G2427 ἱκανόω

## hikanóō

_(hik-an-o'-o | hee-ka-NOH-oh | ee-ka-NOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G2425; to enable, i.e., qualify; make able (meet); 

- to make sufficient, render fit
  - to equip one with adequate power to perform duties of one
