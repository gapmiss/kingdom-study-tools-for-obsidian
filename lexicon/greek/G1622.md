# G1622 ἐκτός

## ektós

_(ek-tos' | ek-TOSE | ake-TOSE)_

### Definition

from G1537; the exterior; figuratively (as a preposition) aside from, besides; but, except(-ed), other than, out of, outside, unless, without; 

- outside, beyond
- the outside, exterior
- outside of
- beyond, besides, except
