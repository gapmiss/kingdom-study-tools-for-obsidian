# G2029 ἐποπτεύω

## epopteúō

_(ep-opt-yoo'-o | ep-oh-PTEV-oh | ape-oh-PTAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and a derivative of G3700; to inspect, i.e., watch; behold; 

- to be an overseer
- to look upon, view attentively
- to watch
