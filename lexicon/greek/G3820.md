# G3820 παλαιός

## palaiós

_(pal-ah-yos' | pa-leh-OSE | pa-lay-OSE)_

### Definition

from G3819; antique, i.e., not recent, worn out; old; 

- old, ancient
- no longer new, worn by use, the worse for wear, old
