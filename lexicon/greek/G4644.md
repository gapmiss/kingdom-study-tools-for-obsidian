# G4644 σκληροτράχηλος

## sklērotráchēlos

_(sklay-rot-rakh'-ay-los | sklay-roh-TRA-hay-lose | sklay-roh-TRA-hay-lose)_

### Definition

from G4642 and G5137; hardnaped, i.e., (figuratively) obstinate; stiffnecked; 

- stiffnecked
- stubborn, headstrong, obstinate
