# G4586 σεμνός

## semnós

_(sem-nos' | sem-NOSE | same-NOSE)_

### Definition

from G4576; venerable, i.e., honorable; grave, honest; 

- august, venerable, reverend
- to be venerated for character, honorable
  - of persons
  - of deeds
