## Gáza

_(gad'-zah | GA-za | GA-za)_

### Definition

the fortified; the strong; of Hebrew origin (H5804); Gazah (i.e., Azzah), a place in Palestine; Gaza; 

- one of the five chief cities of the Philistines in Palestine
