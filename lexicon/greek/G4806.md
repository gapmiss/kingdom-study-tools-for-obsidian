# G4806 συζωοποιέω

## syzōopoiéō

_(sood-zo-op-oy-eh'-o | soo-zoh-oh-poo-EH-oh | syoo-zoh-oh-poo-A-oh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G2227; to reanimate conjointly with (figuratively); quicken together with; 

- to make one alive together
  - of Christians, with Christ
