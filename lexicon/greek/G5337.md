# G5337 φαῦλος

## phaûlos

_(fow'-los | FEV-lose | FA-lose)_

### Definition

apparently a primary word; "foul" or "flawy", i.e., (figuratively) wicked; evil; 

- easy, slight, ordinary, mean, worthless, of no account
- ethically, bad, base, wicked
