# G5042 τεκνογονία

## teknogonía

_(tek-nog-on-ee'-ah | teh-knoh-goh-NEE-ah | tay-knoh-goh-NEE-ah)_

### Definition

from the same as G5041; childbirth (parentage), i.e., (by implication) maternity (the performance of maternal duties); childbearing; 

- childbearing
