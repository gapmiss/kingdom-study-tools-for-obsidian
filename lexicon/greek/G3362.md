# G3362 ἐὰν μή

## eàn mḗ

_(eh-an' may | eh-AN may | ay-AN may)_

### Definition

i.e. G1437 and G3361; if not, i.e., unless; X before, but, except, if, no, (if, + whosoever) not; 

- if not, unless, whoever ... not
