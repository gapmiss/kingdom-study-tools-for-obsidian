# G606 ἀπόκειμαι

## apókeimai

_(ap-ok'-i-mahee | ah-POH-kee-meh | ah-POH-kee-may)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G2749; to be reserved; figuratively, to await; be appointed, (be) laid up; 

- to be laid away, laid by, reserved
- reserved for one, awaiting him
