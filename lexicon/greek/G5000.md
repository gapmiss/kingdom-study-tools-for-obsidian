# G5000 Ταβιθά

## Tabithá

_(tab-ee-thah' | ta-vee-THA | ta-vee-THA)_

### Definition

female gazelle; of Chaldee origin (compare H6646); the gazelle; Tabitha (i.e., Tabjetha), a Christian female; Tabitha; 

- the name of the woman that Peter raised from the dead
