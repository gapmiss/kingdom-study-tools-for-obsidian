# G2237 ἡδονή

## hēdonḗ

_(hay-don-ay' | hay-thoh-NAY | ay-thoh-NAY)_

### Definition

from ἁνδάνω (to please); sensual delight; by implication, desire; lust, pleasure; 

- pleasure
- desires for pleasure
