# G4582 σελήνη

## selḗnē

_(sel-ay'-nay | seh-LAY-nay | say-LAY-nay)_

### Definition

from σέλας (brilliancy; probably akin to the alternate of G0138, through the idea of attractiveness); the moon; moon; 

- the moon
