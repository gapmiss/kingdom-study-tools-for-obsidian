# G920 Βαριωνᾶς

## Bariōnâs

_(bar-ee-oo-nas' | va-ree-oh-NAHS | va-ree-oh-NAHS)_

### Definition

son of Jonah; of Chaldee origin (H1247 and H3124); son of Jonas (or Jonah); Bar-jonas, an Israelite; Bar-jona; 

- the surname of the apostle Peter
