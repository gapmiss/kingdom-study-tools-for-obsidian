# G1195 δεσμεύω

## desmeúō

_(des-myoo'-o | theh-SMEV-oh | thay-SMAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from a (presumed) derivative of G1196; to be a binder (captor), i.e., to enchain (a prisoner), to tie on (a load); bind; 

- to put in chains
- to bind up, bind together
