# G1138 Δαβίδ

## Dabíd

_(dab-eed' | tha-VEETH | tha-VEETH)_

### Definition

beloved; of Hebrew origin (H1732); David, the Israelite king; David; 

- second king of Israel, and ancestor of Jesus Christ

### Note

Sometimes transliterated "Dabid."
