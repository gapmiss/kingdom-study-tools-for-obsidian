# G1555 ἐκδιηγέομαι

## ekdiēgéomai

_(ek-dee-ayg-eh'-om-ahee | ek-thee-ay-GEH-oh-meh | ake-thee-ay-GAY-oh-may)_

### Definition

from G1537 and a compound of G1223 and G2233; to narrate through wholly; declare; 

- to narrate in full or wholly
- to relate, tell, declare
