# G2234 ἡδέως

## hēdéōs

_(hay-deh'-oce | hay-THEH-ose | ay-THAY-ose)_

### Definition

adverb from a derivative of the base of G2237; sweetly, i.e., (figuratively) with pleasure; gladly; 

- with pleasure, gladly
