# G1264 διαμάχομαι

## diamáchomai

_(dee-am-akh'-om-ahee | thee-ah-MA-hoh-meh | thee-ah-MA-hoh-may)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G3164; to fight fiercely (in altercation); strive; 

- to fight out
- contend fiercely
