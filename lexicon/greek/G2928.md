# G2928 κρύπτω

## krýptō

_(kroop'-to | KROO-ptoh | KRYOO-ptoh)_

### Definition

a primary verb; to conceal (properly, by covering); hide (self), keep secret, secret(-ly); 

- to hide, conceal, to be hid
- escape notice
- metaphorically to conceal (that it may not become known)
