# G5147 τρίβος

## tríbos

_(tree'-bos | TREE-vose | TREE-vose)_

### Definition

from τρίβω (to "rub"; akin to τείρω, τρύω, and the base of G5131, G5134); a rut or worn track; path; 

- a worn way, a path
