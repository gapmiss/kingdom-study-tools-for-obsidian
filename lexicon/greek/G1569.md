# G1569 ἔκθαμβος

## ékthambos

_(ek'-tham-bos | EK-thahm-vose | AKE-thahm-vose)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G2285; utterly astounded; greatly wondering; 

- quite astonished, amazed
- terrifying, dreadful
- wonder or marvel greatly
