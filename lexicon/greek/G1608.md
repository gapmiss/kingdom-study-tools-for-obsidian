# G1608 ἐκπορνεύω

## ekporneúō

_(ek-porn-yoo'-o | ek-pore-NEV-oh | ake-pore-NAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G4203; to be utterly unchaste; give self over to fornication; 

- to go a whoring, &quot;give one's self over to fornication&quot;
