# G4497 Ῥοβοάμ

## Rhoboám

_(hrob-o-am' | roh-voh-AM | roh-voh-AM)_

### Definition

enlarger of the people; of Hebrew origin (H7346); Roboam (i.e., Rechobam), an Israelite; Roboam; 

- the son of Solomon by an Ammonite princess Naamah
