# G2991 λαξευτός

## laxeutós

_(lax-yoo-tos' | la-ksev-TOSE | la-ksayf-TOSE)_

### Definition

from a compound of λᾶς (a stone) and the base of G3584 (in its original sense of scraping); rock-quarried; hewn in stone; 

- cut out of stone
