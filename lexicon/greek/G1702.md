# G1702 ἐμπαίζω

## empaízō

_(emp-aheed'-zo | em-PEH-zoh | ame-PAY-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G3815; to jeer at, i.e., deride; mock; 

- to play with, trifle with
  - to mock
  - to delude, deceive
