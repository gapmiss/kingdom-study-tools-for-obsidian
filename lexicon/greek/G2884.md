# G2884 κόρος

## kóros

_(kor'-os | KOH-rose | KOH-rose)_

### Definition

of Hebrew origin (H3734); a cor, i.e., a specific measure; measure; 

- a corus or cor, the largest Hebrew dry measure (i.e., for wheat, meal etc.) about 10 to 11 bushels (350 to 400 liters)
