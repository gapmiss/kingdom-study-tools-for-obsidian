# G134 αἰνέω

## ainéō

_(ahee-neh'-o | eh-NEH-oh | ay-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0136; to praise (God); praise; 

- to praise, extol, to sing praises in honor to God
- to allow, recommend
- to promise or vow
