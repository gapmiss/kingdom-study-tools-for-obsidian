# G3074 λύκος

## lýkos

_(loo'-kos | LOO-kose | LYOO-kose)_

### Definition

perhaps akin to the base of G3022 (from the whitish hair); a wolf; wolf; 

- a wolf
- metaphorically of cruel, greedy, rapacious, destructive men
