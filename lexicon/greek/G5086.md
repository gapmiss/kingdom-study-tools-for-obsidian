# G5086 Τιβέριος

## Tibérios

_(tib-er'-ee-os | tee-VEH-ree-ose | tee-VAY-ree-ose)_

### Definition

from the Tiber (as god-river); of Latin origin; probably pertaining to the river Tiberis or Tiber; Tiberius, a Roman emperor; Tiberius; 

- the second Roman emperor
