# G4795 συγκυρία

## synkyría

_(soong-koo-ree'-ah | soong-koo-REE-ah | syoong-kyoo-REE-ah)_

### Definition

from a comparative of G4862 and κυρέω (to light or happen; from the base of G2962); concurrence, i.e., accident; chance; 

- accident, by chance
