# G1553 ἐκδημέω

## ekdēméō

_(ek-day-meh'-o | ek-thay-MEH-oh | ake-thay-MAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G1537 and G1218; to emigrate, i.e., (figuratively) vacate or quit; be absent; 

- to go abroad
- emigrate, depart
- to be or live abroad
