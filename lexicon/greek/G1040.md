# G1040 βύσσος

## býssos

_(boos'-sos | VOOS-sose | VYOOS-sose)_

### Definition

of Hebrew origin (H0948); white linen; fine linen; 

- byssus, a species of Egyptian flax
- linen made from byssus, very costly, delicate, soft, white and also of a yellow color
