# G665 ἀποτρέπω

## apotrépō

_(ap-ot-rep'-o | ah-poh-TREH-poh | ah-poh-TRAY-poh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and the base of G5157; to deflect, i.e., (reflexively) avoid; turn away; 

- to turn one's self away from
- to shun, avoid
