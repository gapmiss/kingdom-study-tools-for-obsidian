# G1685 ἐμβάλλω

## embállō

_(em-bal'-lo | em-VAHL-loh | ame-VAHL-loh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G0906; to throw on, i.e., (figuratively) subject to (eternal punishment); cast into; 

- to throw in, cast into
