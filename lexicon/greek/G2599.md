# G2599 καταβαρέω

## katabaréō

_(kat-ab-ar-eh'-o | ka-ta-va-REH-oh | ka-ta-va-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G0916; to impose upon; burden; 

- to press down by an imposing weight
- to weigh down
- metaphorically to burden
