# G5532 χρεία

## chreía

_(khri'-ah | HREE-ah | HREE-ah)_

### Definition

from the base of G5530 or G5534; employment, i.e., an affair; also (by implication) occasion, demand, requirement or destitution; business, lack, necessary(-ity), need(-ful), use, want; 

- necessity, need
- duty, business
