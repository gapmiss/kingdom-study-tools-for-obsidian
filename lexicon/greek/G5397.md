# G5397 φλύαρος

## phlýaros

_(floo'-ar-os | FLOO-ah-rose | FLYOO-ah-rose)_

### Definition

from φλύω (to bubble); a garrulous person, i.e., prater; tattler; 

- of persons uttering or doing silly things, garrulous, babbling
- of things, foolish, trifling, vain
