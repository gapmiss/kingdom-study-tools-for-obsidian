# G3319 μέσος

## mésos

_(mes'-os | MEH-sose | MAY-sose)_

### Definition

from G3326; middle (as an adjective or (neuter) noun); among, X before them, between, + forth, mid(-day, -night), midst, way; 

- middle
- the midst
- in the midst of, amongst
