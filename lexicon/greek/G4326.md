# G4326 προσδέομαι

## prosdéomai

_(pros-deh'-om-ahee | prose-THEH-oh-meh | prose-THAY-oh-may)_

### Definition

from G4314 and G1189; to require additionally, i.e., want further; need; 

- to want besides, need in addition
