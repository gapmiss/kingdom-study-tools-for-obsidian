# G3469 μωμάομαι

## mōmáomai

_(mo-mah'-om-ahee | moh-MA-oh-meh | moh-MA-oh-may)_

### Definition

from G3470; to carp at, i.e., censure (discredit); blame; 

- to blame, find fault with, mock at
