# G706 ἀριθμός

## arithmós

_(ar-ith-mos' | ah-reeth-MOSE | ah-reeth-MOSE)_

### Definition

from G0142; a number (as reckoned up); number; 

- a fixed and definite number
- an indefinite number, a multitude
