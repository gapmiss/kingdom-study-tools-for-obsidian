# G2679 κατασκάπτω

## kataskáptō

_(kat-as-kap'-to | ka-ta-SKA-ptoh | ka-ta-SKA-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G4626; to undermine, i.e., (by implication) destroy; dig down, ruin; 

- to dig under, dig down, demolish, destroy
