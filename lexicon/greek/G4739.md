# G4739 στήκω

## stḗkō

_(stay'-ko | STAY-koh | STAY-koh)_

### Definition

from the perfect tense of G2476; to be stationary, i.e., (figuratively) to persevere; stand (fast); 

- to stand firm
- to persevere, to persist
- to keep one's standing
