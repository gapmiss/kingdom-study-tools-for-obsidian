## líbanos

_(lib'-an-os | LEE-va-nose | LEE-va-nose)_

### Definition

of foreign origin (H3828); the incense-tree, i.e., (by implication) incense itself; frankincense; 

- the frankincense tree
- the perfume, frankincense
