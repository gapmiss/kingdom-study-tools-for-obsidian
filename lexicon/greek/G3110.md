# G3110 Μακεδών

## Makedṓn

_(mak-ed'-ohn | ma-keh-THONE | ma-kay-THONE)_

### Definition

of uncertain derivation; a Macedon (Macedonian), i.e., inhabitant of Macedonia; of Macedonia, Macedonian; 

- a Macedonian
