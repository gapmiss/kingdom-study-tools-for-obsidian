# G5030 ταχέως

## tachéōs

_(takh-eh'-oce | ta-HEH-ose | ta-HAY-ose)_

### Definition

adverb from G5036; briefly, i.e., (in time) speedily, or (in manner) rapidly; hastily, quickly, shortly, soon, suddenly; 

- quickly, shortly
