# G1829 ἐξέραμα

## exérama

_(ex-er'-am-ah | eks-EH-ra-ma | ayks-A-ra-ma)_

### Definition

from a comparative of G1537 and a presumed ἐράω (to spue); vomit, i.e., food disgorged; vomit; 

- vomit
- what is cast out by vomiting
