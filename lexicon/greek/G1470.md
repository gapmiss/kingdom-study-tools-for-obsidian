# G1470 ἐγκρύπτω

## enkrýptō

_(eng-kroop'-to | eng-KROO-ptoh | ayng-KRYOO-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G2928; to conceal in, i.e., incorporate with; hid in; 

- to conceal in something
- to mingle one thing with another
