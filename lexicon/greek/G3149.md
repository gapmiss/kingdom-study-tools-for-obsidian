# G3149 μαστός

## mastós

_(mas-tos' | ma-STOSE | ma-STOSE)_

### Definition

from the base of G3145; a (properly, female) breast (as if kneaded up); pap; 

- the breasts
- the breasts (nipples) of a man
- breasts of a women
