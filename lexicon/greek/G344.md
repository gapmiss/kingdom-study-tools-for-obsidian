# G344 ἀνακάμπτω

## anakámptō

_(an-ak-amp'-to | ah-na-KAHM-ptoh | ah-na-KAHM-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G2578; to turn back; (re-)turn; 

- to bend back, to turn back
- to return
