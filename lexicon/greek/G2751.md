# G2751 κείρω

## keírō

_(ki'-ro | KEE-roh | KEE-roh)_

### Definition

a primary verb; to shear; shear(-er); 

- to sheer: a sheep
- to get or let be shorn
- of shearing or cutting short the hair of the head
