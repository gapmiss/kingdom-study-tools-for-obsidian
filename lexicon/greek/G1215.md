# G1215 δημηγορέω

## dēmēgoréō

_(day-may-gor-eh'-o | thay-may-goh-REH-oh | thay-may-goh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G1218 and G0058; to be a people-gatherer, i.e., to address a public assembly; make an oration; 

- to address a public assembly, make a speech to the people
