# G1225 διαβάλλω

## diabállō

_(dee-ab-al'-lo | thee-ah-VAHL-loh | thee-ah-VAHL-loh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G0906; (figuratively) to traduce; accuse; 

- to throw over or across, to send over
- to traduce, calumniate, slander, accuse, defame
