# G1407 δρέπανον

## drépanon

_(drep'-an-on | THREH-pa-none | THRAY-pa-none)_

### Definition

from δρέπω (to pluck); a gathering hook (especially for harvesting); sickle; 

- a sickle, a pruning-hook, a hooked vine knife, such as reapers and vinedressers use
