# G2046 ἐρέω

## eréō

_(er-eh'-o | eh-REH-oh | ay-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

probably a fuller form of G4483; an alternate for G2036 in certain tenses; to utter, i.e., speak or say; call, say, speak (of), tell; 

- to utter, speak, say
