# G2123 εὐκοπώτερος

## eukopṓteros

_(yoo-kop-o'-ter-os | ev-koh-POH-teh-rose | afe-koh-POH-tay-rose)_

### Definition

comparative of a compound of G2095 and G2873; better for toil, i.e., more facile; easier; 

- with easy labor
- easy
