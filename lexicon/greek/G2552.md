# G2552 κακοπάθεια

## kakopátheia

_(kak-op-ath'-i-ah | ka-koh-PA-thee-ah | ka-koh-PA-thee-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of G2256 and G3806; hardship; suffering affliction; 

- the suffering of evil, i.e., trouble, distress, afflicted
