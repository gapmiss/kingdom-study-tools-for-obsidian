# G1688 ἐμβιβάζω

## embibázō

_(em-bib-ad'-zo | em-vee-VA-zoh | ame-vee-VA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and βιβάζω (to mount; causative of G1684); to place on, i.e., transfer (aboard a vessel); put in; 

- to put in or on, lead in, cause to enter
