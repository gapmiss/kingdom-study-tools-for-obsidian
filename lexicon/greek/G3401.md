# G3401 μιμέομαι

## miméomai

_(mim-eh'-om-ahee | mee-MEH-oh-meh | mee-MAY-oh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from μῖμος (a "mimic"); to imitate; follow; 

- to imitate: any one
