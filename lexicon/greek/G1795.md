# G1795 ἐντυπόω

## entypóō

_(en-too-po'-o | en-too-POH-oh | ane-tyoo-POH-oh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and a derivative of G5179; to enstamp, i.e., engrave; engrave; 

- to engrave, imprint (a figure)
