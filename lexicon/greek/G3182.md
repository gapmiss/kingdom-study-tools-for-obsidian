# G3182 μεθύσκω

## methýskō

_(meth-oos'-ko | meh-THOO-skoh | may-THYOO-skoh)_

### Definition

a prolonged (transitive) form of G3184; to intoxicate; be drunk(-en); 

- to intoxicate, make drunk
- to get drunk, become intoxicated
