# G5615 ὡσαύτως

## hōsaútōs

_(ho-sow'-toce | hoh-SAV-tose | oh-SAF-tose)_

### Definition

from G5613 and an adverb from G0846; as thus, i.e., in the same way; even so, likewise, after the same (in like) manner; 

- in like manner, likewise
