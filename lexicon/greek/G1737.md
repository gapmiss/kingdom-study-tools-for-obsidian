# G1737 ἐνδιδύσκω

## endidýskō

_(en-did-oos'-ko | en-thee-THOO-skoh | ane-thee-THYOO-skoh)_

### Definition

a prolonged form of G1746; to invest (with a garment); clothe in, wear; 

- to put on, clothe, to put on one's self, be clothed in
