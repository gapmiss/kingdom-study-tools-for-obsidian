# G5332 φαρμακεύς

## pharmakeús

_(far-mak-yoos' | fahr-ma-KEVS | fahr-ma-KAYFS)_

### Definition

from φάρμακον (a drug, i.e. spell-giving potion); a druggist ("pharmacist") or poisoner, i.e., (by extension) a magician; sorcerer; 

- one who prepares or uses magical remedies
- sorcerer
