# G1235 διαγρηγορέω

## diagrēgoréō

_(dee-ag-ray-gor-eh'-o | thee-ah-gray-goh-REH-oh | thee-ah-gray-goh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G1127; to waken thoroughly; be awake; 

- to watch through
- to remain awake
- to be fully awake
