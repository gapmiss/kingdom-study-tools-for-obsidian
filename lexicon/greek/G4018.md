# G4018 περιβόλαιον

## peribólaion

_(per-ib-ol'-ah-yon | peh-ree-VOH-leh-one | pay-ree-VOH-lay-one)_

### Definition

neuter of a presumed derivative of G4016; something thrown around one, i.e., a mantle, veil; covering, vesture; 

- a covering thrown around, a wrapper
  - a mantle
  - a veil
