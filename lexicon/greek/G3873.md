# G3873 παράκειμαι

## parákeimai

_(par-ak'-i-mahee | pa-RA-kee-meh | pa-RA-kee-may)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G2749; to lie near, i.e., be at hand (figuratively, be prompt or easy); be present; 

- to lie beside, to be near
- to be present, at hand
