# G2421 Ἰεσσαί

## Iessaí

_(es-es-sah'-ee | ee-es-SEH | ee-ase-SAY)_

### Definition

wealthy; of Hebrew origin (H3448); Jessae (i.e., Jishai), an Israelite; Jesse; 

- the father of David the king
