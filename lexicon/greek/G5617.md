# G5617 Ὡσηέ

## Hōsēé

_(ho-say-eh' | hoh-say-EH | oh-say-A)_

### Definition

salvation; of Hebrew origin (H1954); Hosee (i.e., Hoshea), an Israelite; Osee; 

- the well known Hebrew prophet, son of Beeri and contemporary with Isaiah
