# G1183 δεκατόω

## dekatóō

_(dek-at-o'-o | theh-ka-TOH-oh | thay-ka-TOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G1181; to tithe, i.e., to give or take a tenth; pay (receive) tithes; 

- to exact or receive the tenth part
- to pay tithes
