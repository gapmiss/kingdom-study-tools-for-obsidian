# G3650 ὅλος

## hólos

_(hol'-os | HOH-lose | OH-lose)_

### Definition

a primary word; "whole" or "all", i.e., complete (in extent, amount, time or degree), especially (neuter) as noun or adverb; all, altogether, every whit, + throughout, whole; 

- all, whole, completely
