# G353 ἀναλαμβάνω

## analambánō

_(an-al-am-ban'-o | ah-na-lahm-VA-noh | ah-na-lahm-VA-noh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G2983; to take up; receive up, take (in, unto, up); 

- to take up, raise
- to take up (a thing in order to carry or use it)
