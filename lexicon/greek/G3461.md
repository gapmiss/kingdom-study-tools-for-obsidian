# G3461 μυριάς

## myriás

_(moo-ree'-as | moo-ree-AS | myoo-ree-AS)_

### Definition

from G3463; a ten-thousand; by extension, a "myriad" or indefinite number; ten thousand; 

- ten thousand
- an innumerable multitude, an unlimited number
- innumerable hosts
