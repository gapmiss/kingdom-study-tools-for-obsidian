## chórtasma

_(khor'-tas-mah | HORE-ta-sma | HORE-ta-sma)_

### Definition

from G5526; forage, i.e., food; sustenance; 

- feed, fodder for animals
- food, (vegetable) sustenance, whether for men or flocks
