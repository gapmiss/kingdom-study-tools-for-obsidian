# G965 Βηθλεέμ

## Bēthleém

_(bayth-leh-em' | vay-thleh-EM | vay-thlay-AME)_

### Definition

house of bread; of Hebrew origin (H1036); Bethleem (i.e., Beth-lechem), a place in Palestine; Bethlehem; 

- a village about six miles (10 km) south of Jerusalem
