# G522 ἀπαίρω

## apaírō

_(ap-ah'-ee-ro | ah-PEH-roh | ah-PAY-roh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G0142; to lift off, i.e., remove; take (away); 

- to lift off, take or carry away
- to be taken away from anyone
