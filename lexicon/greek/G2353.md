# G2353 θρέμμα

## thrémma

_(threm'-mah | THREM-ma | THRAME-ma)_

### Definition

from G5142; stock (as raised on a farm); cattle; 

- whatever is fed or nursed
  - a ward, nursling, child
- a flock, cattle, especially sheep and goats
