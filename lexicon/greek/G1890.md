# G1890 ἐπαφρίζω

## epaphrízō

_(ep-af-rid'-zo | ep-ah-FREE-zoh | ape-ah-FREE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G0875; to foam upon, i.e., (figuratively) to exhibit (a vile passion); foam out; 

- to foam up
- to cast out as foam, foam out
