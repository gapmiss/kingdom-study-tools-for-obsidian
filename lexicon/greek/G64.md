# G64 ἀγρεύω

## agreúō

_(ag-rew'-o | ah-GREV-oh | ah-GRAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from G0061; to hunt, i.e., (figuratively) to entrap; catch; 

- to hunt, to take by hunting, catch
- metaphorically to hunt after, pursue eagerly
