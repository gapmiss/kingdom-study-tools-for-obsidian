# G146 αἰσχροκερδής

## aischrokerdḗs

_(ahee-skhrok-er-dace' | esk-roh-ker-THASE | aysk-roh-kare-THASE)_

### Definition

from G0150 and κέρδος (gain); sordid; given to (greedy of) filthy lucre; 

- eager for base gain, greedy for money
