# G2231 ἡγεμονία

## hēgemonía

_(hayg-em-on-ee'-ah | hay-geh-moh-NEE-ah | ay-gay-moh-NEE-ah)_

### Definition

from G2232; government, i.e., (in time) official term; reign; 

- chief command, rule, sovereignty
  - of the reign of a Roman emperor
