# G1087 γερουσία

## gerousía

_(gher-oo-see'-ah | geh-roo-SEE-ah | gay-roo-SEE-ah)_

### Definition

from G1088; the eldership, i.e., (collectively) the Jewish Sanhedrin; senate; 

- a senate, council of elders
  - to denote the chief council of nations or cities
  - the Sanhedrin
