# G3031 λιβανωτός

## libanōtós

_(lib-an-o-tos' | lee-va-noh-TOSE | lee-va-noh-TOSE)_

### Definition

from G3030; frankincense, i.e., (by extension) a censer for burning it; censer; 

- the gum exuding from a frankincense tree
- a censer
