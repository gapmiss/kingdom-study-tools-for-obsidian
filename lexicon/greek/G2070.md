# G2070 ἐσμέν

## esmén

_(es-men' | eh-SMEN | ay-SMANE)_

### Definition

first person plural indicative of G1510; we are; are, be, have our being, X have hope, + (the gospel) was (preached unto) us; 

- first person plural of &quot;to be&quot;
