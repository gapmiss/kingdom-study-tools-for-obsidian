# G4924 συνοικέω

## synoikéō

_(soon-oy-keh'-o | soon-oo-KEH-oh | syoon-oo-KAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G3611; to reside together (as a family); dwell together; 

- to dwell together
  - of the domestic association
  - of intercourse of a husband and wife
