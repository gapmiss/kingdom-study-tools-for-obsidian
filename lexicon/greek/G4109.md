# G4109 πλάξ

## pláx

_(plax | plahks | plahks)_

### Definition

from G4111; a moulding-board, i.e., flat surface ("plate", or tablet, literally or figuratively); table; 

- a flat thing, broad tablet, plane, level surface (as of the sea)
