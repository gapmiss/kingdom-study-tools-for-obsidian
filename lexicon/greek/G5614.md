# G5614 ὡσαννά

## hōsanná

_(ho-san-nah' | hoh-sahn-NA | oh-sahn-NA)_

### Definition

of Hebrew origin (H3467 and H4994); oh save!; hosanna (i.e., hoshia-na), an exclamation of adoration; hosanna; 

- hosanna
- be propitious
