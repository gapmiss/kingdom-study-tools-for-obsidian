# G4661 σκῦλον

## skŷlon

_(skoo'-lon | SKOO-lone | SKYOO-lone)_

### Definition

neuter from G4660; something stripped (as a hide), i.e., booty; spoil; 

- a (beast's) skin stripped off, a pelt
- the weapons and valuables stripped off from an enemy, spoils
