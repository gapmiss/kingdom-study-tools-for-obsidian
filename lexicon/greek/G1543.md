# G1543 ἑκατοντάρχης

## hekatontárchēs

_(hek-at-on-tar'-khace | hek-ah-tone-TAHR-hase | ake-ah-tone-TAHR-hase)_

### Definition

from G1540 and G0757; the captain of one hundred men; centurion; 

- an officer in the Roman army
