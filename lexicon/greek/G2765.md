# G2765 κεράμιον

## kerámion

_(ker-am'-ee-on | keh-RA-mee-one | kay-RA-mee-one)_

### Definition

neuter of a presumed derivative of G2766; an earthenware vessel, i.e., jar; pitcher; 

- an earthen vessel, a pot, jar
- a jug or pitcher
- a water pitcher
