# G2002 ἐπισωρεύω

## episōreúō

_(ep-ee-so-ryoo'-o | eh-pee-soh-REV-oh | ay-pee-soh-RAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G4987; to accumulate further, i.e., (figuratively) seek additionally; heap; 

- to heap up, accumulate in piles
