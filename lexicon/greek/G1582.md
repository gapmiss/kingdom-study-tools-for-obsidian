# G1582 ἐκκρέμαμαι

## ekkrémamai

_(ek-krem'-am-ahee | ek-KREH-ma-meh | ake-KRAY-ma-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G1537 and G2910; to hang upon the lips of a speaker, i.e., listen closely; be very attentive; 

- to hang from
