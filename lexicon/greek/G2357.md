# G2357 θρησκός

## thrēskós

_(thrace'-kos | thray-SKOSE | thray-SKOSE)_

### Definition

probably from the base of G2360; ceremonious in worship (as demonstrative), i.e., pious; religious; 

- fearing or worshipping God
- to tremble
  - trembling, fearful
