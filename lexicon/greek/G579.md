# G579 ἀπόβλητος

## apóblētos

_(ap-ob'-lay-tos | ah-POH-vlay-tose | ah-POH-vlay-tose)_

### Definition

from G0577; cast off, i.e., (figuratively) such as to be rejected; be refused; 

- thrown away, to be thrown away, rejected, despised, abominated
  - as unclean
