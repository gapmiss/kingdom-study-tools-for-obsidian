# G1001 βολίζω

## bolízō

_(bol-id'-zo | voh-LEE-zoh | voh-LEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1002; to heave the lead; sound; 

- to heave the lead, take a sounding
  - a line an plummet with which mariners sound the depth of the sea
