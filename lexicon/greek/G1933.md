# G1933 ἐπιεικής

## epieikḗs

_(ep-ee-i-kace' | eh-pee-ee-KASE | ay-pee-ee-KASE)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G1503; appropriate, i.e., (by implication) mild; gentle, moderation, patient; 

- seemingly, suitable
- equitable, fair, mild, gentle
