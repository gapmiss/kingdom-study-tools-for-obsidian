# G1973 ἐπιπόθητος

## epipóthētos

_(ep-ee-poth'-ay-tos | eh-pee-POH-thay-tose | ay-pee-POH-thay-tose)_

### Definition

from G1909 and a derivative of the latter part of G1971; yearned upon, i.e., greatly loved; longed for; 

- longed for
