# G1794 ἐντυλίσσω

## entylíssō

_(en-too-lis'-so | en-too-LEES-soh | ane-tyoo-LEES-soh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and τυλίσσω (to twist; probably akin to G1507); to entwine, i.e., wind up in; wrap in (together); 

- to roll up, wrap together
