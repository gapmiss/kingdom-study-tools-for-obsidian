# G2239 ἦθος

## ēthos

_(ay'-thos | A-those | A-those)_

### Definition

a strengthened form of G1485; usage, i.e., (plural) moral habits; manners; 

- a customary abode, dwelling place, haunt, customary state
- custom, usage, morals, character
