# G4200 πορισμός

## porismós

_(por-is-mos' | poh-ree-SMOSE | poh-ree-SMOSE)_

### Definition

from a derivative of πόρος (a way, i.e. means); furnishing (procuring), i.e., (by implication) money-getting (acquisition); gain; 

- acquisition, gain
- source of gain
