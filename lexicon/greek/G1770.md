# G1770 ἐννεύω

## enneúō

_(en-nyoo'-o | en-NEV-oh | ane-NAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G3506; to nod at, i.e., beckon or communicate by gesture; make signs; 

- to nod to, signify or express by a nod or a sign
