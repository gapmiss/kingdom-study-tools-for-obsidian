# G2869 κοπάζω

## kopázō

_(kop-ad'-zo | koh-PA-zoh | koh-PA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G2873; to tire, i.e., (figuratively) to relax; cease; 

- to grow weary or tired
- to cease from violence, cease raging
