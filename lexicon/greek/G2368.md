# G2368 θυμίαμα

## thymíama

_(thoo-mee'-am-ah | thoo-MEE-ah-ma | thyoo-MEE-ah-ma)_

### Definition

from G2370; an aroma, i.e., fragrant powder burnt in religious service; by implication, the burning itself; incense, odour; 

- an aromatic substance burnt, incense
