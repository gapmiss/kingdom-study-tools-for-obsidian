# G1856 ἐξωθέω

## exōthéō

_(ex-o-theh'-o | eks-oh-THEH-oh | ayks-oh-THAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and ὠθέω (to push); to expel; by implication, to propel; drive out, thrust in; 

- to thrust out
- expel from one's abode
- to propel, drive
