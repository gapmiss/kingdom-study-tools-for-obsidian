# G5041 τεκνογονέω

## teknogonéō

_(tek-nog-on-eh'-o | teh-knoh-goh-NEH-oh | tay-knoh-goh-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G5043 and the base of G1096; to be a child-bearer, i.e., parent (mother); bear children; 

- begat or bear children
