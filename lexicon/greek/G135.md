# G135 αἴνιγμα

## aínigma

_(ah'-ee-nig-ma | EH-neeg-ma | A-neeg-ma)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G0136 (in its primary sense); an obscure saying ("enigma"), i.e., (abstractly) obscureness; X darkly; 

- an obscure saying, enigma, riddle
- an obscure thing
