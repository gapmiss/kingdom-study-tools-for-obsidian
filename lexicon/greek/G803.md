# G803 ἀσφάλεια

## aspháleia

_(as-fal'-i-ah | ah-SFA-lee-ah | ah-SFA-lee-ah)_

### Definition

from G0804; security (literally or figuratively); certainty, safety; 

- firmness, stability
- certainty, undoubted truth
- security from enemies and dangers, safety
