## prosdokía

_(pros-dok-ee'-ah | prose-thoh-KEE-ah | prose-thoh-KEE-ah)_

### Definition

from G4328; apprehension (of evil); by implication, infliction anticipated; expectation, looking after; 

- expectation (whether good or evil)
