# G1816 ἐξανατέλλω

## exanatéllō

_(ex-an-at-el'-lo | eks-ah-na-TEL-loh | ayks-ah-na-TALE-loh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G0393; to start up out of the ground, i.e., germinate; spring up; 

- to make spring up, cause to shoot forth
- to spring up
