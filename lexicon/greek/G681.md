# G681 ἅπτω

## háptō

_(hap'-to | HA-ptoh | A-ptoh)_

### Definition

a primary verb; properly, to fasten to, i.e., (specially) to set on fire; kindle, light; 

- to fasten to, adhere to
  - to fasten fire to a thing, kindle, set of fire
