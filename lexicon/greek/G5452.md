# G5452 φυτεύω

## phyteúō

_(foot-yoo'-o | foo-TEV-oh | fyoo-TAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G5453; to set out in the earth, i.e., implant; figuratively, to instil doctrine; plant; 

- to plant
