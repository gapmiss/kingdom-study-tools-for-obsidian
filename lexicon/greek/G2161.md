# G2161 Εὔτυχος

## Eútychos

_(yoo'-too-khos | EV-too-hose | AFE-tyoo-hose)_

### Definition

fortunate; from G2095 and a derivative of G5177; well- fated, i.e., fortunate; Eutychus, a young man; Eutychus; 

- a youth restored to life by Paul
