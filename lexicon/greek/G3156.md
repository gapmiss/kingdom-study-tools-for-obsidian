# G3156 Ματθαῖος

## Matthaîos

_(mat-thah'-yos | maht-THEH-ose | maht-THAY-ose)_

### Definition

gift of Jehovah; a shorter form of G3161; Matthaeus (i.e., Matthitjah), an Israelite and a Christian; Matthew; 

- son of Alphaeus, one of the 12 disciples
