# G2608 κατάγνυμι

## katágnymi

_(kat-ag'-noo-mee | ka-TA-gnoo-mee | ka-TA-gnyoo-mee)_

### Definition

from G2596 and the base of G4486; to rend in pieces, i.e., crack apart; break; 

- to break
