# G138 αἱρέομαι

## hairéomai

_(hahee-reh'-om-ahee | heh-REH-oh-meh | ay-RAY-oh-may)_

### Definition

probably akin to G0142; to take for oneself, i.e., to prefer; choose; 

- to take for oneself, to prefer, choose
- to choose by vote, elect to office
