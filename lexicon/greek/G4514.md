# G4514 Ῥωμαῖος

## Rhōmaîos

_(hro-mah'-yos | roh-MEH-ose | roh-MAY-ose)_

### Definition

from G4516; Romaean, i.e., Roman (as noun); Roman, of Rome; 

- a resident of the city of Rome, a Roman citizen
