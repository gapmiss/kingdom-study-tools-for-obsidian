# G2479 ἰσχύς

## ischýs

_(is-khoos' | ee-SKOOS | ee-SKYOOS)_

### Definition

from a derivative of ἰς (force; compare ἔσχον, a form of G2192); forcefulness (literally or figuratively); ability, might(-ily), power, strength; 

- ability, force, strength, might
