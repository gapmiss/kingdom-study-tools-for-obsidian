# G4876 συναντάω

## synantáō

_(soon-an-tah'-o | soon-an-TA-oh | syoon-an-TA-oh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and a derivative of G0473; to meet with; figuratively, to occur; befall, meet; 

- to meet with
  - of events: to happen or befall
