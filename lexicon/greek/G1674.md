# G1674 Ἑλληνίς

## Hellēnís

_(hel-lay-nis' | hel-lane-EES | ale-lane-EES)_

### Definition

feminine of G1672; a Grecian (i.e., non-Jewish) woman; Greek; 

- a Greek women
- a Gentile women, not a Jewess
