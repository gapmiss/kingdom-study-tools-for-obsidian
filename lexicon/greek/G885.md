# G885 Ἀχείμ

## Acheím

_(akh-ime' | ah-HEEM | ah-HEEM)_

### Definition

the Lord will establish; probably of Hebrew origin (compare H3137); Achim, an Israelite; Achim; 

- one of the ancestors of Christ, his name is not mentioned in the Old Testament
