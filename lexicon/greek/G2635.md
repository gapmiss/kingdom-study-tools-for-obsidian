# G2635 καταλαλέω

## katalaléō

_(kat-al-al-eh'-o | ka-ta-la-LEH-oh | ka-ta-la-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G2637; to be a traducer, i.e., to slander; speak against (evil of); 

- to speak against one, to criminate, traduce
