# G1763 ἐνιαυτός

## eniautós

_(en-ee-ow-tos' | en-ee-av-TOSE | ane-ee-af-TOSE)_

### Definition

prolongation from a primary ἔνος (a year); a year; year; 

- a year, in a wider sense, for some fixed definite period of time
