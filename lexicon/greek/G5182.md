# G5182 τυρβάζω

## tyrbázō

_(toor-bad'-zo | toor-VA-zoh | tyoor-VA-zoh)_

### Definition

from τύρβη (Latin turba, a crowd; akin to G2351); to make "turbid", i.e., disturb; trouble; 

- disturb, trouble
- to be troubled in mind, disquieted
