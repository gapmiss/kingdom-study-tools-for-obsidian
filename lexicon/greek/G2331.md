# G2331 Θεσσαλονικεύς

## Thessalonikeús

_(thes-sal-on-ik-yoos' | thes-sa-loh-nee-KEVS | thase-sa-loh-nee-KAYFS)_

### Definition

from G2332; a Thessalonican, i.e., inhabitant of Thessalonice; Thessalonian; 

- a Thessalonians
