# G2607 καταγινώσκω

## kataginṓskō

_(kat-ag-in-o'-sko | ka-ta-gee-NOH-skoh | ka-ta-gee-NOH-skoh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G1097; to note against, i.e., find fault with; blame, condemn; 

- to find fault with, blame
- to accuse, condemn
