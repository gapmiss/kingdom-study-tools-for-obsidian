# G4872 συναναβαίνω

## synanabaínō

_(soon-an-ab-ah'-ee-no | soon-ah-na-VEH-noh | syoon-ah-na-VAY-noh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G0305; to ascend in company with; come up with; 

- to ascend at the same time, come up together with to a higher place
  - with one
