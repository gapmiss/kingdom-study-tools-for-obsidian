# G5237 ὑπερείδω

## hypereídō

_(hoop-er-i'-do | hoo-per-EE-thoh | yoo-pare-EE-thoh)_

### Definition

from G5228 and G1492; to overlook, i.e., not punish; wink at; 

- to overlook, take no notice of, not attend to
