_(oo-ree'-as | oo-REE-as | oo-REE-as)_

### Definition

light of Jehovah; of Hebrew origin (H0223); Urias (i.e., Urijah), a Hittite; Urias; 

- the husband of Bathsheba, one of David's mighty men
