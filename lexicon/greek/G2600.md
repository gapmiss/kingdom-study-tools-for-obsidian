# G2600 κατάβασις

## katábasis

_(kat-ab'-as-is | ka-TA-va-sees | ka-TA-va-sees)_

### Definition

from G2597; a declivity; descent; 

- descent
  - the act of descending
  - the place of descent
    - that part of the mountain where the descent is made
