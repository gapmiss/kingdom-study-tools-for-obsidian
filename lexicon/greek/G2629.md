# G2629 κατακόπτω

## katakóptō

_(kat-ak-op'-to | ka-ta-KOH-ptoh | ka-ta-KOH-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G2875; to chop down, i.e., mangle; cut; 

- to cut up in pieces
- to slay
- to beat, bruise
- to cut, gash, mangle
