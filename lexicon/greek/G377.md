# G377 ἀναπίπτω

## anapíptō

_(an-ap-ip'-to | ah-na-PEE-ptoh | ah-na-PEE-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G4098; to fall back, i.e., lie down, lean back; lean, sit down (to meat); 

- to lie back, lie down
- to recline at a table, to sit back
