# G2162 εὐφημία

## euphēmía

_(yoo-fay-mee'-ah | ev-fay-MEE-ah | afe-fay-MEE-ah)_

### Definition

from G2163; good language ("euphemy"), i.e., praise (repute); good report; 

- the utterance of good or auspicious words
- good report, praise
