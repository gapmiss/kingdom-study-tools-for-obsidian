# G11 Ἀβραάμ

## Abraám

_(ab-rah-am' | ah-vra-AM | ah-vra-AM)_

### Definition

father of a multitude; of Hebrew origin (H0085); Abraham, the Hebrew patriarch; Abraham; 

- the son of Terah and the founder of the Jewish nation
