# G3545 νομίμως

## nomímōs

_(nom-im'-oce | noh-MEE-mose | noh-MEE-mose)_

### Definition

adverb from a derivative of G3551; legitimately (specially, agreeably to the rules of the lists); lawfully; 

- lawfully, agreeable to the law, properly
