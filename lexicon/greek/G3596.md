# G3596 ὁδοιπορέω

## hodoiporéō

_(hod-oy-por-eh'-o | hoh-thoo-poh-REH-oh | oh-thoo-poh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G3598 and G4198; to be a wayfarer, i.e., travel; go on a journey; 

- to travel, journey
