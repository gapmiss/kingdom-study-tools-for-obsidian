# G1474 ἐδαφίζω

## edaphízō

_(ed-af-id'-zo | eh-tha-FEE-zoh | ay-tha-FEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1475; to raze; lay even with the ground; 

- to throw to the ground
  - both of cities and buildings, to raze, level with the earth
  - metaphorically of men
