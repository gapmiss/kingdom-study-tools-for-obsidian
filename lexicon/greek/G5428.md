# G5428 φρόνησις

## phrónēsis

_(fron'-ay-sis | FROH-nay-sees | FROH-nay-sees)_

### Definition

from G5426; mental action or activity, i.e., intellectual or moral insight; prudence, wisdom; 

- understanding
- knowledge and holy love of the will of God
