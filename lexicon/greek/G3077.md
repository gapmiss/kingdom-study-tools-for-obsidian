# G3077 λύπη

## lýpē

_(loo'-pay | LOO-pay | LYOO-pay)_

### Definition

apparently a primary word; sadness; grief, grievous, + grudgingly, heaviness, sorrow; 

- sorrow, pain, grief, annoyance, affliction
  - of persons mourning
