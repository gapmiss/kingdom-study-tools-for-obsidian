# G249 ἄλογος

## álogos

_(al'-og-os | AH-loh-gose | AH-loh-gose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G3056; irrational; brute, unreasonable; 

- destitute of reason
- contrary to reason, absurd
