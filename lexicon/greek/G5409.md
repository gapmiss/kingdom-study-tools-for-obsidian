# G5409 φορέω

## phoréō

_(for-eh'-o | foh-REH-oh | foh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G5411; to have a burden, i.e., (by analogy) to wear as clothing or a constant accompaniment; bear, wear; 

- to bear constantly, wear
  - of clothing, garments, armour
