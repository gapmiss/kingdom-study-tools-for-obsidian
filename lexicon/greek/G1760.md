# G1760 ἐνθυμέομαι

## enthyméomai

_(en-thoo-meh'-om-ahee | en-thoo-MEH-oh-meh | ane-thyoo-MAY-oh-may)_

### Definition

from a compound of G1722 and G2372; to be inspirited, i.e., ponder; think; 

- to bring to mind, revolve in mind, ponder
- to think, to deliberate
