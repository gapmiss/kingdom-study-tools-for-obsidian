# G2296 θαυμάζω

## thaumázō

_(thou-mad'-zo | thev-MA-zoh | tha-MA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G2295; to wonder; by implication, to admire; admire, have in admiration, marvel, wonder; 

- to wonder, wonder at, marvel
- to be wondered at, to be had in admiration
