# G4525 σαίνω

## saínō

_(sah'-ee-no | SEH-noh | SAY-noh)_

### Definition

akin to G4579; to wag (as a dog its tail fawningly), i.e., (generally) to shake (figuratively, disturb); move; 

- to wag the tail
  - of dogs
- metaphorically
  - to flatter, fawn upon
  - to move (the mind of one)
    - agreeably
    - to agitate, disturb, trouble
