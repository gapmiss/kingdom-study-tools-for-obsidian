# G1486 ἔθω

## éthō

_(eth'-o | EH-thoh | A-thoh)_

### Definition

a primary verb; to be used (by habit or conventionality); neuter perfect participle usage; be custom (manner, wont); 

- to be accustomed, used, wont
- that which is wont
- usage, custom
