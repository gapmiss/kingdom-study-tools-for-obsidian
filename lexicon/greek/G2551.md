# G2551 κακολογέω

## kakologéō

_(kak-ol-og-eh'-o | ka-koh-loh-GEH-oh | ka-koh-loh-GAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G2556 and G3056; to revile; curse, speak evil of; 

- to speak evil of, revile, abuse, one
- to curse
