# G306 ἀναβάλλομαι

## anabállomai

_(an-ab-al'-lom-ahee | ah-na-VAHL-loh-meh | ah-na-VAHL-loh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G0303 and G0906; to put off (for oneself); defer; 

- to throw or toss up
- to put back or off, delay, postpone
