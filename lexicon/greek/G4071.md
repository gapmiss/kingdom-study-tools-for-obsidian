# G4071 πετεινόν

## peteinón

_(pet-i-non' | peh-tee-NONE | pay-tee-NONE)_

### Definition

neuter of a derivative of G4072; a flying animal, i.e., bird; bird, fowl; 

- flying, winged
- flying or winged animals, birds
  - the birds of the heaven, i.e., flying in the heaven (air)
