# G4723 στείρος

## steíros

_(sti'-ros | STEE-rose | STEE-rose)_

### Definition

a contraction from G4731 (as stiff and unnatural); "sterile"; barren; 

- hard, stiff
  - of men and animals
- barren
  - of woman who does not conceive
