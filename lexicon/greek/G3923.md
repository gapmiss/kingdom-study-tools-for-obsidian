# G3923 παρεισφέρω

## pareisphérō

_(par-ice-fer'-o | pa-ree-SFEH-roh | pa-ree-SFAY-roh)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G1533; to bear in alongside, i.e., introduce simultaneously; give; 

- to bring in besides
- to contribute besides to something
