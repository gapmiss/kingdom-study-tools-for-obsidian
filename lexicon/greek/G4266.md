# G4266 προγίνομαι

## progínomai

_(prog-in'-om-ahee | proh-GEE-noh-meh | proh-GEE-noh-may)_

### Definition

from G4253 and G1096; to be already, i.e., have previousy transpired; be past; 

- to arise or come before, happen before
  - of sins committed previously
