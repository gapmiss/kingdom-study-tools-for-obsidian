# G4799 σύγχυσις

## sýnchysis

_(soong'-khoo-sis | SOONG-hoo-sees | SYOONG-hyoo-sees)_

### Definition

from G4797; commixture, i.e., (figuratively) riotous disturbance; confusion; 

- confusion, disturbance
  - of riotous persons
