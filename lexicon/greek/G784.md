# G784 ἄσπιλος

## áspilos

_(as'-pee-los | AH-spee-lose | AH-spee-lose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G4695; unblemished (physically or morally); without spot, unspotted; 

- spotless
- metaphorically
  - free from censure, irreproachable
  - free from vice, unsullied
