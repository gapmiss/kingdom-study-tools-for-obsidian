## apokephalízō

_(ap-ok-ef-al-id'-zo | ah-poh-keh-fa-LEE-zoh | ah-poh-kay-fa-LEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G2776; to decapitate; behead; 

- to cut off the head, behead, decapitate
