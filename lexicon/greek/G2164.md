# G2164 εὐφορέω

## euphoréō

_(yoo-for-eh'-o | ev-foh-REH-oh | afe-foh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G2095 and G5409; to bear well, i.e., be fertile; bring forth abundantly; 

- to be fertile, bring forth plentifully
