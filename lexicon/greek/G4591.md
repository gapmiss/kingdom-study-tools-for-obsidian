# G4591 σημαίνω

## sēmaínō

_(say-mah'-ee-no | say-MEH-noh | say-MAY-noh)_

### Definition

from σῆμα (a mark; of uncertain derivation); to indicate; signify; 

- to give a sign, to signify, indicate
- to make known
