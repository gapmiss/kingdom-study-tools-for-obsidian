# G1200 δεσμοφύλαξ

## desmophýlax

_(des-mof-oo'-lax | theh-smoh-FOO-lahks | thay-smoh-FYOO-lahks)_

### Definition

from G1199 and G5441; a jailer (as guarding the prisoners); jailor, keeper of the prison; 

- a keeper of a prison, a jailor
