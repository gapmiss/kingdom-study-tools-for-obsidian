# G4978 σχίσμα

## schísma

_(skhis'-mah | SKEE-sma | SKEE-sma)_

### Definition

from G4977; a split or gap ("schism"), literally or figuratively; division, rent, schism; 

- a rent
- metaphorically a division, dissension
