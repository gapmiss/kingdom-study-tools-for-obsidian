# G5119 τότε

## tóte

_(tot'-eh | TOH-teh | TOH-tay)_

### Definition

from (the neuter of) G3588 and G3753; the when, i.e., at the time that (of the past or future, also in consecution); that time, then; 

- then
- at that time
