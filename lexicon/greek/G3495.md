# G3495 νεανίσκος

## neanískos

_(neh-an-is'-kos | neh-ah-NEE-skose | nay-ah-NEE-skose)_

### Definition

from the same as G3494; a youth (under forty); young man; 

- a young man, youth
  - used of a young attendant or servant
