# G4471 Ῥαμᾶ

## Rhamâ

_(hram-ah' | ra-MA | ra-MA)_

### Definition

a hill; of Hebrew origin (H7414); Rama (i.e., Ramah), a place in Palestine; Rama; 

- a town of the tribe of Benjamin, situated about 5 miles (8 km) north of Jerusalem on the road to Bethel
