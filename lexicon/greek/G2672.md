# G2672 καταράομαι

## kataráomai

_(kat-ar-ah'-om-ahee | ka-ta-RA-oh-meh | ka-ta-RA-oh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G2671; to execrate; by analogy, to doom; curse; 

- to curse, doom, imprecate evil upon
