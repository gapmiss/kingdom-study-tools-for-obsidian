# G4967 σφαγή

## sphagḗ

_(sfag-ay' | sfa-GAY | sfa-GAY)_

### Definition

from G4969; butchery (of animals for food or sacrifice, or (figuratively) of men (destruction)); slaughter; 

- slaughter
  - of sheep destined for slaughter
  - of day of destruction
