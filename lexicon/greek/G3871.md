# G3871 παρακαλύπτω

## parakalýptō

_(par-ak-al-oop'-to | pa-ra-ka-LOO-ptoh | pa-ra-ka-LYOO-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G2572; to cover alongside, i.e., veil (figuratively); hide; 

- to cover over, cover up, hide, conceal
