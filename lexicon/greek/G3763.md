# G3763 οὐδέποτε

## oudépote

_(oo-dep'-ot-eh | oo-THEH-poh-teh | oo-THAY-poh-tay)_

### Definition

from G3761 and G4218; not even at any time, i.e., never at all; neither at any time, never, nothing at any time; 

- never
