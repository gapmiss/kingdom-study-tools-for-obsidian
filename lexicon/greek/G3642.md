## oligópsychos

_(ol-ig-op'-soo-khos | oh-lee-GOH-psoo-hose | oh-lee-GOH-psyoo-hose)_

### Definition

from G3641 and G5590; little-spirited, i.e., faint-hearted; feebleminded; 

- fainthearted
