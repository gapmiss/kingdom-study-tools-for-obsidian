# G329 ἀναζωπυρέω

## anazōpyréō

_(an-ad-zo-poor-eh'-o | ah-na-zoh-poo-REH-oh | ah-na-zoh-pyoo-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and a compound of the base of G2226 and G4442; to re-enkindle; stir up; 

- to kindle up, inflame one's mind, strength, zeal
