# G503 ἀντοφθαλμέω

## antophthalméō

_(ant-of-thal-meh'-o | an-toh-fthahl-MEH-oh | an-toh-fthahl-MAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G0473 and G3788; to face; bear up into; 

- to look against or straight at
- metaphorically to bear up against, withstand
