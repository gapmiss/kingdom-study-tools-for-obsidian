# G3333 μετακαλέω

## metakaléō

_(met-ak-al-eh'-o | meh-ta-ka-LEH-oh | may-ta-ka-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G3326 and G2564; to call elsewhere, i.e., summon; call (for, hither); 

- to call from one place to another, to summon
- to call one's self
