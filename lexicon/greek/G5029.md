# G5029 τάχα

## tácha

_(takh'-ah | TA-ha | TA-ha)_

### Definition

as if neuter plural of G5036 (adverbially); shortly, i.e., (figuratively) possibly; peradventure(-haps); 

- hastily, quickly, soon
- peradventure, perhaps
