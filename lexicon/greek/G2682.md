# G2682 κατασκήνωσις

## kataskḗnōsis

_(kat-as-kay'-no-sis | ka-ta-SKAY-noh-sees | ka-ta-SKAY-noh-sees)_

### Definition

from G2681; an encamping, i.e., (figuratively) a perch; nest; 

- the pitching of tents, encamping
- place of tarrying, encampment, abode
  - of the nest of birds
