# G1213 δηλόω

## dēlóō

_(day-lo'-o | thay-LOH-oh | thay-LOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G1212; to make plain (by words); declare, shew, signify; 

- to make manifest
- to make known by relating, to declare
- to give one to understand, to indicate, signify
