# G3690 ὄξος

## óxos

_(oz-os | OH-ksose | OH-ksose)_

### Definition

from G3691; vinegar, i.e., sour wine; vinegar; 

- vinegar
  - the mixture of sour wine or vinegar and water which the Roman soldiers were accustomed to drink
