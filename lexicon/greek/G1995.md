# G1995 ἐπιστροφή

## epistrophḗ

_(ep-is-trof-ay' | eh-pee-stroh-FAY | ay-pee-stroh-FAY)_

### Definition

from G1994; reversion, i.e., morally, revolution; conversion; 

- conversion (of the Gentiles from idolatry to the true God)
