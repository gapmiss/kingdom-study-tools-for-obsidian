# G4432 πτωχεία

## ptōcheía

_(pto-khi'-ah | ptoh-HEE-ah | ptoh-HEE-ah)_

### Definition

from G4433; beggary, i.e., indigence (literally or figuratively); poverty; 

- beggary
- in the New Testament poverty
  - the condition of one destitute of riches and abundance
