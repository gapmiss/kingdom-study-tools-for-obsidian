# G3899 παραπορεύομαι

## paraporeúomai

_(par-ap-or-yoo'-om-ahee | pa-ra-poh-REV-oh-meh | pa-ra-poh-RAVE-oh-may)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G4198; to travel near; go, pass (by); 

- to proceed at the side, go past, pass by
