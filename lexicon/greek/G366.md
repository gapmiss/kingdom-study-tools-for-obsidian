# G366 ἀνανήφω

## ananḗphō

_(an-an-ay'-fo | ah-na-NAY-foh | ah-na-NAY-foh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G3525; to become sober again, i.e., (figuratively) regain (one's) senses; recover self; 

- to return to soberness
