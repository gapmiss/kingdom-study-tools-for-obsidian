# G2422 Ἰεφθάε

## Iephtháe

_(ee-ef-thah'-eh | ee-eh-FTHA-eh | ee-ay-FTHA-ay)_

### Definition

whom God sets free; of Hebrew origin (H3316); Jephthaë (i.e., Jiphtach), an Israelite; Jephthah; 

- son of Gilead, and a judge in Israel
