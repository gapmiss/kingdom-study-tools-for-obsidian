# G3869 παρακαθίζω

## parakathízō

_(par-ak-ath-id'-zo | pa-ra-ka-THEE-zoh | pa-ra-ka-THEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G2523; to sit down near; sit; 

- to make to sit down besides
- to set beside, place near
- sit down beside
