# G1600 ἐκπετάννυμι

## ekpetánnymi

_(ek-pet-an'-noo-mee | ek-peh-TAHN-noo-mee | ake-pay-TAHN-nyoo-mee)_

### Definition

from G1537 and a form of G4072; to fly out, i.e., (by analogy) to extend; stretch forth; 

- to spread out, stretch forth
