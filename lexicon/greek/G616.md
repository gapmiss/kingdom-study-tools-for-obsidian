# G616 ἀποκυέω

## apokyéō

_(ap-ok-oo-eh'-o | ah-poh-koo-EH-oh | ah-poh-kyoo-A-oh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and the base of G2949; to breed forth, i.e., (by transference) to generate (figuratively); beget, produce; 

- to bring forth
  - from the womb
  - give birth to
- produce
