# G5416 φραγέλλιον

## phragéllion

_(frag-el'-le-on | fra-GEL-lee-one | fra-GALE-lee-one)_

### Definition

neuter of a derivative from the base of G5417; a whip, i.e., Roman lash as a public punishment; scourge; 

- a scourge or whip
