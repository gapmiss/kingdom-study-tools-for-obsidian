# G2930 κρύσταλλος

## krýstallos

_(kroos'-tal-los | KROO-stahl-lose | KRYOO-stahl-lose)_

### Definition

from a derivative of κρύος (frost); ice, i.e., (by analogy) rock "crystal"; crystal; 

- crystal, a kind of precious stone
