# G4549 Σαούλ

## Saoúl

_(sah-ool' | sa-OOL | sa-OOL)_

### Definition

desired; of Hebrew origin (H7586); Saul (i.e., Shaul), the Jewish name of Paul; Saul; 

- the Jewish name of the apostle Paul
- the son of Kish and the first king of Israel
