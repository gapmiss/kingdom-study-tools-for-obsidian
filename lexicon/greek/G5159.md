# G5159 τροποφορέω

## tropophoréō

_(trop-of-or-eh'-o | troh-poh-foh-REH-oh | troh-poh-foh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G5158 and G5409; to endure one's habits; suffer the manners; 

- to bear one's manner, endure one's character
