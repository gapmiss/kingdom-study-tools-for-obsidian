# G3112 μακράν

## makrán

_(mak-ran' | ma-KRAHN | ma-KRAHN)_

### Definition

feminine accusative case singular of G3117 (G3598 being implied); at a distance (literally or figuratively); (a-)far (off), good (great) way off; 

- far, a great way
- far hence
