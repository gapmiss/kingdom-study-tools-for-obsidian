# G1254 διακωλύω

## diakōlýō

_(dee-ak-o-loo'-o | thee-ah-koh-LOO-oh | thee-ah-koh-LYOO-oh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G2967; to hinder altogether, i.e., utterly prohibit; forbid; 

- to hinder, prevent
