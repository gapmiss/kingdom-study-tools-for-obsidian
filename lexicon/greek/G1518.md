# G1518 εἰρηνοποιός

## eirēnopoiós

_(i-ray-nop-oy-os' | ee-ray-noh-poo-OSE | ee-ray-noh-poo-OSE)_

### Definition

from G1515 and G4160; pacificatory, i.e., (subjectively) peaceable; peacemaker; 

- a peacemaker
- pacific, loving peace
