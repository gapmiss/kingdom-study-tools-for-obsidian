# G4943 συνυπουργέω

## synypourgéō

_(soon-oop-oorg-eh'-o | soon-oo-poor-GEH-oh | syoon-yoo-poor-GAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and a derivative of a compound of G5259 and the base of G2041; to be a co-auxiliary, i.e., assist; help together; 

- to help together
