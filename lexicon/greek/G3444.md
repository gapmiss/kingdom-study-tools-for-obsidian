# G3444 μορφή

## morphḗ

_(mor-fay' | more-FAY | more-FAY)_

### Definition

perhaps from the base of G3313 (through the idea of adjustment of parts); shape; figuratively, nature; form; 

- the form by which a person or thing strikes the vision
- external appearance
