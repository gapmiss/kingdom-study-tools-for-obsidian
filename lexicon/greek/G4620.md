# G4620 σιτόμετρον

## sitómetron

_(sit-om'-et-ron | see-TOH-meh-trone | see-TOH-may-trone)_

### Definition

from G4621 and G3358; a grain-measure, i.e., (by implication) ration (allowance of food); portion of meat; 

- a measured 'portion of' grain or 'food'
