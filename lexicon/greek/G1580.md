# G1580 ἐκκομίζω

## ekkomízō

_(ek-kom-id'-zo | ek-koh-MEE-zoh | ake-koh-MEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G2865; to bear forth (to burial); carry out; 

- to carry out
  - a dead man for burial
