# G3891 παρανομέω

## paranoméō

_(par-an-om-eh'-o | pa-ra-noh-MEH-oh | pa-ra-noh-MAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G3844 and G3551; to be opposed to law, i.e., to transgress; contrary to law; 

- to act contrary to the law, to break the law
