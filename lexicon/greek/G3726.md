# G3726 ὁρκίζω

## horkízō

_(hor-kid'-zo | hore-KEE-zoh | ore-KEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G3727; to put on oath, i.e., make swear; by analogy, to solemnly enjoin; adjure, charge; 

- to force to take an oath, to administer an oath to
- to adjure (solemnly implore)
