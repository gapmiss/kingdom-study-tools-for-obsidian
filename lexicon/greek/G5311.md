# G5311 ὕψος

## hýpsos

_(hoop'-sos | HOO-psose | YOO-psose)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G5228; elevation, i.e., (abstractly) altitude, (specially), the sky, or (figuratively) dignity; be exalted, height, (on) high; 

- height
  - of measurement
  - of place, heaven
  - metaphorically rank, high station
