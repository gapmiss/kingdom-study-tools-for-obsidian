# G1037 βυθός

## bythós

_(boo-thos' | voo-THOSE | vyoo-THOSE)_

### Definition

a variation of G0899; depth, i.e., (by implication) the sea; deep; 

- the bottom or depth of the sea
- the sea itself, the deep sea
