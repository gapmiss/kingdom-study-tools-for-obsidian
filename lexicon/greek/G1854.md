# G1854 ἔξω

## éxō

_(ex'-o | EKS-oh | AYKS-oh)_

### Definition

adverb from G1537; out(-side) (of doors), literally or figuratively; away, forth, (with-)out(-ward) (of), strange; 

- without, out of doors
