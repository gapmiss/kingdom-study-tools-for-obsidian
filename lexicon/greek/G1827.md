# G1827 ἐξελέγχω

## exelénchō

_(ex-el-eng'-kho | eks-eh-LENG-hoh | ayks-ay-LAYNG-hoh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G1651; to convict fully, i.e., (by implication) to punish; convince; 

- to prove to be in the wrong, convict
