# G1092 γεωργός

## geōrgós

_(gheh-ore-gos' | geh-ore-GOSE | gay-ore-GOSE)_

### Definition

from G1093 and the base of G2041; a land-worker, i.e., farmer; husbandman; 

- a husbandman, tiller of the soil, a vine dresser
