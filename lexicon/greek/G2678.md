# G2678 κατασείω

## kataseíō

_(kat-as-i'-o | ka-ta-SEE-oh | ka-ta-SEE-oh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G4579; to sway downward, i.e., make a signal; beckon; 

- to shake down, throw down
- to shake
  - to make a sign, to signal with the hand to one
