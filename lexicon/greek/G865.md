# G865 ἀφιλάγαθος

## aphilágathos

_(af-il-ag'-ath-os | ah-fee-LA-ga-those | ah-fee-LA-ga-those)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G5358; hostile to virtue; despiser of those that are good; 

- opposed to goodness and good men
