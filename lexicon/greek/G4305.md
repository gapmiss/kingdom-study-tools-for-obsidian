# G4305 προμεριμνάω

## promerimnáō

_(prom-er-im-nah'-o | proh-meh-reem-NA-oh | proh-may-reem-NA-oh)_

### Definition

from G4253 and G3309; to care (anxiously) in advance; take thought beforehand; 

- to be anxious beforehand
