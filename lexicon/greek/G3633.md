# G3633 οἴομαι

## oíomai

_(oy'-om-ahee | OO-oh-meh | OO-oh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice apparently from G3634; to make like (oneself), i.e., imagine (be of the opinion); suppose, think; 

- to suppose, think
