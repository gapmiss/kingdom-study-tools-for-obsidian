# G1436 ἔα

## éa

_(eh'-ah | EH-ah | A-ah)_

### Definition

apparent imperative of G1439; properly, let it be, i.e., (as interjection) aha!; let alone; 

- interjection expressive of indignation, or of wonder mixed with fear, ha! ah!
