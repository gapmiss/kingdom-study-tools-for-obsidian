# G2649 καταμαρτυρέω

## katamartyréō

_(kat-am-ar-too-reh'-o | ka-ta-mahr-too-REH-oh | ka-ta-mahr-tyoo-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G3140; to testify against; witness against; 

- to bear witness against, to testify against one
