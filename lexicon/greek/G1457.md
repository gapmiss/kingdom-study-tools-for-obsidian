# G1457 ἐγκαινίζω

## enkainízō

_(eng-kahee-nid'-zo | eng-keh-NEE-zoh | ayng-kay-NEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1456; to renew, i.e., inaugurate; consecrate, dedicate; 

- to renew
- to do anew, again
- to initiate, consecrate, dedication
