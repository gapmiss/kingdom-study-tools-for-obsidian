# G1752 ἕνεκα

## héneka

_(hen'-ek-ah | HEN-eh-ka | ANE-ay-ka)_

### Definition

of uncertain affinity; on account of; because, for (cause, sake), (where-)fore, by reason of, that; 

- on account of, for the sake of, for
- for this cause, therefore
