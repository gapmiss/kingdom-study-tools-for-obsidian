# G3012 λέντιον

## léntion

_(len'-tee-on | LEN-tee-one | LANE-tee-one)_

### Definition

of Latin origin; a "linen" cloth, i.e., apron; towel; 

- a linen cloth, towel
  - of the towel or apron with which servants put on when about to work
