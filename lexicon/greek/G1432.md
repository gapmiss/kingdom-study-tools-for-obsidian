# G1432 δωρεάν

## dōreán

_(do-reh-an' | thoh-reh-AN | thoh-ray-AN)_

### Definition

accusative case of G1431 as adverb; gratuitously (literally or figuratively); without a cause, freely, for naught, in vain; 

- freely, undeservedly
