# G2558 κακουχέω

## kakouchéō

_(kak-oo-kheh'-o | ka-koo-HEH-oh | ka-koo-HAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a presumed compound of G2556 and G2192; to maltreat; which suffer adversity, torment; 

- to treat ill, oppress, plague
