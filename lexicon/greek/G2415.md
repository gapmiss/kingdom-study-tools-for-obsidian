# G2415 Ἱεροσολυμίτης

## Hierosolymítēs

_(hee-er-os-ol-oo-mee'-tace | hee-eh-rose-oh-loo-MEE-tase | ee-ay-rose-oh-lyoo-MEE-tase)_

### Definition

from G2414; a Hierosolymite, i.e., inhabitant of Hierosolyma; of Jerusalem; 

- a citizen or inhabitant of Jerusalem
