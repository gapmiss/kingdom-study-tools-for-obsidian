# G2694 κατασύρω

## katasýrō

_(kat-as-oo'-ro | ka-ta-SOO-roh | ka-ta-SYOO-roh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G4951; to drag down, i.e., arrest judicially; hale; 

- to draw down, pull down
- to draw along, drag forcibly
