# G220 ἀλέκτωρ

## aléktōr

_(al-ek'-tore | ah-LEK-tore | ah-LAKE-tore)_

### Definition

from ἀλέκω (to ward off); a cock or male fowl; cock; 

- a cock, or male of any bird, a rooster
