# G3323 Μεσσίας

## Messías

_(mes-see'-as | mes-SEE-as | mase-SEE-as)_

### Definition

anointed; of Hebrew origin (H4899); the Messias (i.e., Mashiach), or Christ; Messias; 

- the Greek form of Messiah
- a name of Christ
