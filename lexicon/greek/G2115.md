# G2115 εὔθυμος

## eúthymos

_(yoo'-thoo-mos | EV-thoo-mose | AFE-thyoo-mose)_

### Definition

from G2095 and G2372; in fine spirits, i.e., cheerful; of good cheer, the more cheerfully; 

- well disposed, kind
- of good cheer, of good courage
