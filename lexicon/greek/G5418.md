# G5418 φραγμός

## phragmós

_(frag-mos' | frahg-MOSE | frahg-MOSE)_

### Definition

from G5420; a fence, or inclosing barrier (literally or figuratively); hedge (+ round about), partition; 

- a hedge, a fence
- that which separates, prevents two from coming together
