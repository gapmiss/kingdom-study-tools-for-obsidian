# G807 ἀσχημονέω

## aschēmonéō

_(as-kay-mon-eh'-o | ah-skay-moh-NEH-oh | ah-skay-moh-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0809; to be (i.e., act) unbecoming; behave self uncomely (unseemly); 

- to act unbecomingly
