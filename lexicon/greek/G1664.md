# G1664 Ἐλιούδ

## Elioúd

_(el-ee-ood' | eh-lee-OOTH | ay-lee-OOTH)_

### Definition

God his Praise; of Hebrew origin (H0410 and H1935); God of majesty; Eliud, an Israelite; Eliud; 

- the son of Achim in the genealogy of Christ
