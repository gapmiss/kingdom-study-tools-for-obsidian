# G1244 διαιρέω

## diairéō

_(dee-ahee-reh'-o | thee-eh-REH-oh | thee-ay-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G0138; to separate, i.e., distribute; divide; 

- to divide into parts, to part, to tear, cleave or cut asunder
- to distribute
