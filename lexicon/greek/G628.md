# G628 ἀπολούω

## apoloúō

_(ap-ol-oo'-o | ah-poh-LOO-oh | ah-poh-LOO-oh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G3068; to wash fully, i.e., (figuratively) have remitted (reflexively); wash (away); 

- to wash off or away
