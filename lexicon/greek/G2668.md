# G2668 καταπλέω

## katapléō

_(kat-ap-leh'-o | ka-ta-PLEH-oh | ka-ta-PLAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G4126; to sail down upon a place, i.e., to land at; arrive; 

- to sail down from the deep sea to the land
- to put in
