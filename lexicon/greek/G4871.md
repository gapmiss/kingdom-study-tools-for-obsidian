# G4871 συναλίζω

## synalízō

_(soon-al-id'-zo | soon-ah-LEE-zoh | syoon-ah-LEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and ἁλίζω (to throng); to accumulate, i.e., convene; assemble together; 

- to gather together, assemble
- to be assembled, meet with
