# G2184 ἐφήμερος

## ephḗmeros

_(ef-ay'-mer-os | eh-FAY-meh-rose | ay-FAY-may-rose)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G2250; for a day ("ephemeral"), i.e., diurnal; daily; 

- lasting for a day
- daily
