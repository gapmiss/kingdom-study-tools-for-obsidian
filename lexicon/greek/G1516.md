# G1516 εἰρηνικός

## eirēnikós

_(i-ray-nee-kos' | ee-ray-nee-KOSE | ee-ray-nee-KOSE)_

### Definition

from G1515; pacific; by implication, salutary; peaceable; 

- relating to peace
- peaceable, pacific, loving peace
- bring peace with it, peaceful, salutary
