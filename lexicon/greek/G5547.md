# G5547 Χριστός

## Christós

_(khris-tos' | hree-STOSE | hree-STOSE)_

### Definition

anointed; from G5548; anointed, i.e., the Messiah, an epithet of Jesus; Christ; 

- Christ was the Messiah, the Son of God
- anointed
