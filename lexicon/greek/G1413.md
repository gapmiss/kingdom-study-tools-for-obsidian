# G1413 δυνάστης

## dynástēs

_(doo-nas'-tace | thoo-NA-stase | thyoo-NA-stase)_

### Definition

from G1410; a ruler or officer; of great authority, mighty, potentate; 

- a prince, a potentate
- a courtier, high officer, royal minister of great authority
