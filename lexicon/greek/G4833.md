# G4833 συμμορφόω

## symmorphóō

_(soom-mor-fo'-o | soom-more-FOH-oh | syoom-more-FOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G4832; to render like, i.e., (figuratively) to assimilate; make conformable unto; 

- to be conformed to, receive the same form as
