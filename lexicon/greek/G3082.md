# G3082 Λύστρα

## Lýstra

_(loos'-trah | LOO-stra | LYOO-stra)_

### Definition

ransoming; of uncertain origin; Lystra, a place in Asia Minor; Lystra; 

- a city in the eastern part of the great plain of Lycaonia
