# G800 ἀσύμφωνος

## asýmphōnos

_(as-oom'-fo-nos | ah-SOOM-foh-nose | ah-SYOOM-foh-nose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G4859; inharmonious (figuratively); agree not; 

- not agreeing in sound, dissonant, inharmonious, at variance
