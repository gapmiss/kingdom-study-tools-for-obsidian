# G2821 κλῆσις

## klēsis

_(klay'-sis | KLAY-sees | KLAY-sees)_

### Definition

from a shorter form of G2564; an invitation (figuratively); calling; 

- a calling, calling to
- a call, invitation
  - to a feast
  - of the divine invitation to embrace salvation of God
