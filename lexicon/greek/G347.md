# G347 ἀνακλίνω

## anaklínō

_(an-ak-lee'-no | ah-na-KLEE-noh | ah-na-KLEE-noh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G2827; to lean back; lay, (make) sit down; 

- to lean against, lean upon
  - to lay down
  - to make or bid to recline
