# G567 ἀπέχομαι

## apéchomai

_(ap-ekh'-om-ahee | ah-PEH-hoh-meh | ah-PAY-hoh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice (reflexively) of G0568; to hold oneself off, i.e., refrain; abstain; 

- to hold one's self off, refrain, abstain
