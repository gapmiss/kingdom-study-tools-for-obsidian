# G4737 στεφανόω

## stephanóō

_(stef-an-o'-o | steh-fa-NOH-oh | stay-fa-NOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G4735; to adorn with an honorary wreath (literally or figuratively); crown; 

- to encircle with a crown, to crown: the victor in a contest
- to adorn, to honor
