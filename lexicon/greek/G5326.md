# G5326 φάντασμα

## phántasma

_(fan'-tas-mah | FAHN-ta-sma | FAHN-ta-sma)_

### Definition

from G5324; (properly concrete) a (mere) show ("phantasm"), i.e., spectre; spirit; 

- an appearance
- an apparition, spectre
