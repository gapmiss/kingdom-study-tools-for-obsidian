# G1388 δόλος

## dólos

_(dol'-os | THOH-lose | THOH-lose)_

### Definition

from an obsolete primary verb, δέλλω (probably meaning to decoy; compare G1185); a trick (bait), i.e., (figuratively) wile; craft, deceit, guile, subtilty; 

- craft, deceit, guile
