# G431 ἀνεψιός

## anepsiós

_(an-eps'-ee-os | ah-neh-psee-OSE | ah-nay-psee-OSE)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a particle of union) and an obsolete νέπος (a brood); properly, akin, i.e., (specially) a cousin; sister's son; 

- a cousin
