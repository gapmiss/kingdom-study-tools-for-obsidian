# G1449 ἐγγράφω

## engráphō

_(eng-graf'-o | eng-GRA-foh | ayng-GRA-foh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G1125; to "engrave", i.e., inscribe; write (in); 

- to engrave, inscribe, write in or on
  - to record, enrol
