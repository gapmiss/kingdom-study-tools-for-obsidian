# G3119 μαλακία

## malakía

_(mal-ak-ee'-ah | ma-la-KEE-ah | ma-la-KEE-ah)_

### Definition

from G3120; softness, i.e., enervation (debility); disease; 

- softness
- in the New Testament infirmity, debility, bodily weakness, sickness
