# G5623 ὠφελέω

## ōpheléō

_(o-fel-eh'-o | oh-feh-LEH-oh | oh-fay-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from the same as G5622; to be useful, i.e., to benefit; advantage, better, prevail, profit; 

- to assist, to be useful or advantageous, to profit
