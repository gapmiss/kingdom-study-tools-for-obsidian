# G5166 τρυγάω

## trygáō

_(troo-gah'-o | troo-GA-oh | tryoo-GA-oh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of τρύγω (to dry) meaning ripe fruit (as if dry); to collect the vintage; gather; 

- to gather in ripe fruits
- to gather the harvest or vintage
  - of fruit gathered
