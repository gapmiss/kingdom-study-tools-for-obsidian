# G1786 ἐντόπιος

## entópios

_(en-top'-ee-os | en-TOH-pee-ose | ane-TOH-pee-ose)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G5117; a resident; of that place; 

- a dweller in a place
- a resident or native of a place
