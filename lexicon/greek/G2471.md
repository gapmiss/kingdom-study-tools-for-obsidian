# G2471 ἰσότης

## isótēs

_(ee-sot'-ace | ee-SOH-tase | ee-SOH-tase)_

### Definition

likeness (in condition or proportion); by implication, equity; equal(-ity); 

- equality
- equity, fairness, what is equitable
