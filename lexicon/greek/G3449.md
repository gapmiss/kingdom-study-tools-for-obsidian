# G3449 μόχθος

## móchthos

_(mokh'-thos | MOKE-those | MOKE-those)_

### Definition

from the base of G3425; toil, i.e., (by implication) sadness; painfulness, travail; 

- a hard and difficult labor, toil, travail, hardship, distress
