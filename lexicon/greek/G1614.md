# G1614 ἐκτείνω

## ekteínō

_(ek-ti'-no | ek-TEE-noh | ake-TEE-noh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and teino (to stretch); to extend; cast, put forth, stretch forth (out); 

- to stretch out, stretch forth
  - over, towards, against one
