# G5201 ὑδρία

## hydría

_(hoo-dree-ah' | hoo-THREE-ah | yoo-THREE-ah)_

### Definition

from G5204; a water-jar, i.e., receptacle for family supply; water-pot; 

- a vessel for holding water
- a water jar, a water pot
