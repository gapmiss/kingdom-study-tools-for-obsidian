# G208 ἀκυρόω

## akyróō

_(ak-oo-ro'-o | ah-koo-ROH-oh | ah-kyoo-ROH-oh)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G2964; to invalidate; disannul, make of none effect; 

- to render void, deprive of force and authority
