# G976 βίβλος

## bíblos

_(bib'-los | VEE-vlose | VEE-vlose)_

### Definition

properly, the inner bark of the papyrus plant, i.e. (by implication) a sheet or scroll of writing; a scroll; book; 

- a written book, a roll, a scroll
