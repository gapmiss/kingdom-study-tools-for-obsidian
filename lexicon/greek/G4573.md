# G4573 σεβάζομαι

## sebázomai

_(seb-ad'-zom-ahee | seh-VA-zoh-meh | say-VA-zoh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from a derivative of G4576; to venerate, i.e., adore; worship; 

- to fear, be afraid
- to honor religiously, to worship
