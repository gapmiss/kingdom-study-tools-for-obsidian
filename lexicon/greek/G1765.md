# G1765 ἐνισχύω

## enischýō

_(en-is-khoo'-o | en-ee-SKOO-oh | ane-ee-SKYOO-oh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G2480; to invigorate (transitively or reflexively); strengthen; 

- to grow strong, to receive strength
- to make strong, to strengthen
