# G1759 ἐνθάδε

## entháde

_(en-thad'-eh | en-THA-theh | ane-THA-thay)_

### Definition

from a prolonged form of G1722; properly, within, i.e., (of place) here, hither; (t-)here, hither; 

- here
- hither
