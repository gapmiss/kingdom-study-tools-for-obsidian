# G3500 νέκρωσις

## nékrōsis

_(nek'-ro-sis | NEH-kroh-sees | NAY-kroh-sees)_

### Definition

from G3499; decease; figuratively, impotency; deadness, dying; 

- putting to death, killing
- being put to death
- the dead state, utter sluggishness
  - of bodily members and organs
