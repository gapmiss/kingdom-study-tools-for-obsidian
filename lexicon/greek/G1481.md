# G1481 ἐθνάρχης

## ethnárchēs

_(eth-nar'-khace | eh-THNAHR-hase | ay-THNAHR-hase)_

### Definition

from G1484 and G0746; the governor (not king) of a district; ethnarch; 

- an ethnarch, one set over a people as ruler, but without the authority and name of a king
