# G1670 ἑλκύω

## helkýō

_(hel-koo'-o | hel-KOO-oh | ale-KYOO-oh)_

### Definition

probably akin to G0138; to drag (literally or figuratively); draw; 

- to draw, drag off
- metaphorically, to draw by inward power, lead, impel

### Note

Compare G1667.
