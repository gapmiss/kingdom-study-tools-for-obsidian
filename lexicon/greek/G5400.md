# G5400 φόβητρον

## phóbētron

_(fob'-ay-tron | FOH-vay-trone | FOH-vay-trone)_

### Definition

neuter of a derivative of G5399; a frightening thing, i.e., terrific portent; fearful sight; 

- that which strikes terror, a terror, (cause of) fright
