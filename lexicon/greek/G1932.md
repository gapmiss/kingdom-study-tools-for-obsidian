# G1932 ἐπιείκεια

## epieíkeia

_(ep-ee-i'-ki-ah | eh-pee-EE-kee-ah | ay-pee-EE-kee-ah)_

### Definition

from G1933; suitableness, i.e., (by implication) equity, mildness; clemency, gentleness; 

- mildness, gentleness, fairness
