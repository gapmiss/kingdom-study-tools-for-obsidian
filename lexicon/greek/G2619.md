# G2619 κατακαλύπτω

## katakalýptō

_(kat-ak-al-oop'-to | ka-ta-ka-LOO-ptoh | ka-ta-ka-LYOO-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G2572; to cover wholly, i.e., veil; cover, hide; 

- to cover up
- to veil or cover one's self
