# G1919 ἐπίγειος

## epígeios

_(ep-ig'-i-os | eh-PEE-gee-ose | ay-PEE-gee-ose)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G1093; worldly (physically or morally); earthly, in earth, terrestrial; 

- existing upon the earth, earthly, terrestrial
