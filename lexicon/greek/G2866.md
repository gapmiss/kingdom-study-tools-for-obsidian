# G2866 κομψότερον

## kompsóteron

_(komp-sot'-er-on | kome-PSOH-teh-rone | kome-PSOH-tay-rone)_

### Definition

neuter comparative of a derivative of the base of 2865 (meaning, properly, well dressed, i.e. nice); figuratively, convalescent; + began to amend; 

- more finely, better
