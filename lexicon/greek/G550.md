# G550 ἀπειπόμην

## apeipómēn

_(ap-i-pom'-ane | ah-pee-POH-mane | ah-pee-POH-mane)_

### Definition

reflexive past of a compound of G0575 and G2036; to say off for oneself, i.e., disown; renounce; 

- to speak out, set forth, declare
- to forbid
- to give up, renounce
