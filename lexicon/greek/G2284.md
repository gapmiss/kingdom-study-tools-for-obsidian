# G2284 θαμβέω

## thambéō

_(tham-beh'-o | thahm-VEH-oh | thahm-VAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G2285; to stupefy (with surprise), i.e., astound; amaze, astonish; 

- to be astonished
- to astonish, terrify
  - to be amazed
  - to be frightened
