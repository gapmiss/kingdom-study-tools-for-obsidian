# G4917 συνθλάω

## synthláō

_(soon-thlah'-o | soon-THLA-oh | syoon-THLA-oh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and thlao (to crush); to dash together, i.e., shatter; break; 

- to break to pieces, shatter
