# G4378 πρόσχυσις

## próschysis

_(pros'-khoo-sis | PROSE-hoo-sees | PROSE-hyoo-sees)_

### Definition

from a comparative of G4314 and χέω (to pour); a shedding forth, i.e., affusion; sprinkling; 

- a pouring or sprinkling upon, affusion
