# G1988 ἐπιστάτης

## epistátēs

_(ep-is-tat'-ace | eh-pee-STA-tase | ay-pee-STA-tase)_

### Definition

from G1909 and a presumed derivative of G2476; an appointee over, i.e., commander (teacher); master; 

- any sort of superintendent or overseer
