# G1776 ἐνοχλέω

## enochléō

_(en-okh-leh'-o | en-oh-HLEH-oh | ane-oh-HLAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G3791; to crowd in, i.e., (figuratively) to annoy; trouble; 

- to excite, disturbance, to trouble, annoy
