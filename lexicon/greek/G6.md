# G6 Ἄβελ

## Ábel

_(ab'-el | AH-vel | AH-vale)_

### Definition

vanity (i.e., transitory); of Hebrew origin (H1893); Abel, the son of Adam; Abel; 

- the second son of Adam, murdered by his brother Cain
