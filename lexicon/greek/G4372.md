# G4372 πρόσφατος

## prósphatos

_(pros'-fat-os | PROSE-fa-tose | PROSE-fa-tose)_

### Definition

from G4253 and a derivative of G4969; previously (recently) slain (fresh), i.e., (figuratively) lately made; new; 

- lately slaughtered, freshly killed
- recently made, new
