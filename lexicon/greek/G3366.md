# G3366 μηδέ

## mēdé

_(may-deh' | may-THEH | may-THAY)_

### Definition

from G3361 and G1161; but not, not even; in a continued negation, nor; neither, nor (yet), (no) not (once, so much as); 

- and not, but not, nor, not
