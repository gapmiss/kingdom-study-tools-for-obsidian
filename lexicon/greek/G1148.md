# G1148 Δαλμανουθά

## Dalmanouthá

_(dal-man-oo-thah' | thahl-ma-noo-THA | thahl-ma-noo-THA)_

### Definition

slow firebrand; probably of Chaldee origin; Dalmanutha, a place in Palestine; Dalmanutha; 

- is a town on the west side of the Sea of Galilee, near Magdala

### Verse(s)

[[Mark 8#10|Mark 8:10]]
