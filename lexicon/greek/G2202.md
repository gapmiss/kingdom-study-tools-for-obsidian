# G2202 ζευκτηρία

## zeuktēría

_(dzook-tay-ree'-ah | zevk-tay-REE-ah | zayfk-tay-REE-ah)_

### Definition

feminine of a derivative (at the second stage) from the same as G2218; a fastening (tiller-rope); band; 

- a band, fastening
