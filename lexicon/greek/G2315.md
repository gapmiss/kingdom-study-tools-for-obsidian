# G2315 θεόπνευστος

## theópneustos

_(theh-op'-nyoo-stos | theh-OH-pnev-stose | thay-OH-pnayf-stose)_

### Definition

from G2316 and a presumed derivative of G4154; divinely breathed in; given by inspiration of God; 

- inspired by God
  - the contents of the scriptures
