# G238 ἀλληγορέω

## allēgoréō

_(al-lay-gor-eh'-o | al-lay-goh-REH-oh | al-lay-goh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0243 and ἀγορέω (to harangue (compare G0058)); to allegorize; be an allegory (the Greek word itself); 

- to speak allegorically or in a figure
