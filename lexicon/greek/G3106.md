# G3106 μακαρίζω

## makarízō

_(mak-ar-id'-zo | ma-ka-REE-zoh | ma-ka-REE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G3107; to beatify, i.e., pronounce (or esteem) fortunate; call blessed, count happy; 

- to pronounce blessed
