# G5256 ὑπηρετέω

## hypēretéō

_(hoop-ay-ret-eh'-o | hoo-pay-reh-TEH-oh | yoo-pay-ray-TAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G5257; to be a subordinate, i.e., (by implication) subserve; minister (unto), serve; 

- to act as a rower, to row
- to minister, render services
