# G5436 Φύγελλος

## Phýgellos

_(foog'-el-los | FOO-gel-lose | FYOO-gale-lose)_

### Definition

a little fugitive; probably from G5343; fugitive; Phygellus, an apostate Christian; Phygellus; 

- a Christian who was with Paul and deserted him
