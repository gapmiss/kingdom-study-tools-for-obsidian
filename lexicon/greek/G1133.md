# G1133 γυναικάριον

## gynaikárion

_(goo-nahee-kar'-ee-on | goo-neh-KA-ree-one | gyoo-nay-KA-ree-one)_

### Definition

a diminutive from G1135; a little (i.e., foolish) woman; silly woman; 

- a little woman: used contemptuously
