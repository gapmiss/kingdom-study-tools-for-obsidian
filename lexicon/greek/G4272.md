# G4272 προδίδωμι

## prodídōmi

_(prod-id'-o-mee | proh-THEE-thoh-mee | proh-THEE-thoh-mee)_

### Definition

from G4253 and G1325; to give before the other party has given; first give; 

- to give before, give first
- to betray
