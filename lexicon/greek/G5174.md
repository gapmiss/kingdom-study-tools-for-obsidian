# G5174 Τρωάς

## Trōás

_(tro-as' | troh-AS | troh-AS)_

### Definition

a Trojan; from Τρός (a Trojan); the Troad (or plain of Troy), i.e., Troas, a place in Asia Minor; Troas; 

- a city near Hellespont
