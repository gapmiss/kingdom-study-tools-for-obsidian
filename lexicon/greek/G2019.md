# G2019 ἐπιφωνέω

## epiphōnéō

_(ep-ee-fo-neh'-o | eh-pee-foh-NEH-oh | ay-pee-foh-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G5455; to call at something, i.e., exclaim; cry (against), give a shout; 

- to call out to, shout
