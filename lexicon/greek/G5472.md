# G5472 χαλκηδών

## chalkēdṓn

_(khal-kay-dohn' | hahl-kay-THONE | hahl-kay-THONE)_

### Definition

from G5475 and perhaps G1491; copper-like, i.e., "chalcedony"; chalcedony; 

- chalcedony is a precious stone of misty grey color, clouded with blue, yellow, or purple
