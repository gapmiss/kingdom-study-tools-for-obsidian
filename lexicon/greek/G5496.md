# G5496 χειραγωγέω

## cheiragōgéō

_(khi-rag-ogue-eh'-o | hee-ra-goh-GEH-oh | hee-ra-goh-GAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G5497; to be a hand-leader, i.e., to guide (a blind person); lead by the hand; 

- to lead by the hand
