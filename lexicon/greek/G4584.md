# G4584 Σεμεΐ

## Semeḯ

_(sem-eh-ee' | seh-meh-EE | say-may-EE)_

### Definition

harkeners, my report; of Hebrew origin (H8096); Semei (i.e., Shimi), an Israelite; Semei; 

- the father of Mattathias in the genealogy of Christ
