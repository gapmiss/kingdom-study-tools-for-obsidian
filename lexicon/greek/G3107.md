# G3107 μακάριος

## makários

_(mak-ar'-ee-os | ma-KA-ree-ose | ma-KA-ree-ose)_

### Definition

a prolonged form of the poetical μάκαρ (meaning the same); supremely blest; by extension, fortunate, well off; blessed, happy(X -ier); 

- blessed, happy
