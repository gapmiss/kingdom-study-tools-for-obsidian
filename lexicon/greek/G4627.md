# G4627 σκάφη

## skáphē

_(skaf'-ay | SKA-fay | SKA-fay)_

### Definition

a "skiff" (as if dug out), or yawl (carried aboard a large vessel for landing); boat; 

- anything dug out, hollow vessel, trough, tray, tub
- of a boat
