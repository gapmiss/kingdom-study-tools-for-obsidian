# G4302 προλέγω

## prolégō

_(prol-eg'-o | proh-LEH-goh | proh-LAY-goh)_

### Definition

from G4253 and G3004; to say beforehand, i.e., predict, forewarn; foretell, tell before; 

- to say beforehand, to predict
