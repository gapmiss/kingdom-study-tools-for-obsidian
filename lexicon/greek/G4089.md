# G4089 πικρός

## pikrós

_(pik-ros' | pee-KROSE | pee-KROSE)_

### Definition

perhaps from G4078 (through the idea of piercing); sharp (pungent), i.e., acrid (literally or figuratively); bitter; 

- bitter
- metaphorically harsh, virulent
