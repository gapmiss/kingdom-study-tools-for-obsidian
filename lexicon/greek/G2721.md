# G2721 κατεφίστημι

## katephístēmi

_(kat-ef-is'-tay-mee | ka-teh-FEE-stay-mee | ka-tay-FEE-stay-mee)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G2186; to stand over against, i.e., rush upon (assault); make insurrection against; 

- to set up against
