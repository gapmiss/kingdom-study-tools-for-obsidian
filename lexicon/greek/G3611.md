# G3611 οἰκέω

## oikéō

_(oy-keh'-o | oo-KEH-oh | oo-KAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G3624; to occupy a house, i.e., reside (figuratively, inhabit, remain, inhere); by implication, to cohabit; dwell; 

- to dwell in
