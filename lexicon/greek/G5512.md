# G5512 χλευάζω

## chleuázō

_(khlyoo-ad'-zo | hlev-AH-zoh | hlave-AH-zoh)_

### Definition

from a derivative probably of G5491; to throw out the lip, i.e., jeer at; mock; 

- to mock, deride, jeer
