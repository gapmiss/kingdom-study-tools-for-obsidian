# G1276 διαπεράω

## diaperáō

_(dee-ap-er-ah'-o | thee-ah-peh-RA-oh | thee-ah-pay-RA-oh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and a derivative of the base of G4008; to cross entirely; go over, pass (over), sail over; 

- to pass over, cross over, i.e., a river, a lake
