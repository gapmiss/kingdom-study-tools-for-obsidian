# G3864 παραθαλάσσιος

## parathalássios

_(par-ath-al-as'-see-os | pa-ra-tha-LAHS-see-ose | pa-ra-tha-LAHS-see-ose)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G2281; along the sea, i.e., maritime (lacustrine); upon the sea coast; 

- besides the sea, by the sea
