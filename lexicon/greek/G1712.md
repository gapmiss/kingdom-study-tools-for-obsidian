# G1712 ἐμπόριον

## empórion

_(em-por'-ee-on | em-POH-ree-one | ame-POH-ree-one)_

### Definition

neuter from G1713; a mart ("emporium"); merchandise; 

- a place were trade is carried on, especially a seaport
  - a mart, an emporium
