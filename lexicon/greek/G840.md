# G840 αὐστηρός

## austērós

_(ow-stay-ros' | av-stay-ROSE | af-stay-ROSE)_

### Definition

from a (presumed) derivative of the same as G0109 (meaning blown); rough (properly as a gale), i.e., (figuratively) severe; austere; 

- of mind and manners: harsh, rough, rigid
