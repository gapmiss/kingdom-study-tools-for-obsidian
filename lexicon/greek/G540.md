# G540 ἀπάτωρ

## apátōr

_(ap-at'-ore | ah-PA-tore | ah-PA-tore)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G3962; fatherless, i.e., of unrecorded paternity; without father; 

- whose father is not recorded in the genealogies
