# G633 ἀπονίπτω

## aponíptō

_(ap-on-ip'-to | ah-poh-NEE-ptoh | ah-poh-NEE-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G3538; to wash off (reflexively, one's own hands symbolically); wash; 

- to wash off one's self, to wash off for one's self
