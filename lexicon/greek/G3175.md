# G3175 μεγιστᾶνες

## megistânes

_(meg-is-tan'-es | meh-gee-STA-nes | may-gee-STA-nase)_

### Definition

plural from G3176; grandees; great men, lords; 

- the grandees, magnates, nobles, chief men
  - of a city or a people
  - of the associates or courtiers of a king
