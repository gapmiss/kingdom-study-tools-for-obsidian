# G1576 ἐκκλείω

## ekkleíō

_(ek-kli'-o | ek-KLEE-oh | ake-KLEE-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G2808; to shut out (literally or figuratively); exclude; 

- to shut out, to turn out of doors
- to prevent the approach of one
