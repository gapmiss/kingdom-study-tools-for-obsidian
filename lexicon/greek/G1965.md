# G1965 ἐπίορκος

## epíorkos

_(ep-ee'-or-kos | eh-PEE-ore-kose | ay-PEE-ore-kose)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G3727; on oath, i.e., (falsely) a forswearer; perjured person; 

- a false swearer, a perjurer
