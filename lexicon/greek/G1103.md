# G1103 γνήσιος

## gnḗsios

_(gnay'-see-os | GNAY-see-ose | GNAY-see-ose)_

### Definition

from the same as G1077; legitimate (of birth), i.e., genuine; own, sincerity, true; 

- legitimately born, not spurious
- true, genuine, sincere
