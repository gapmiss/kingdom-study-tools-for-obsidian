# G1630 ἔκφοβος

## ékphobos

_(ek'-fob-os | EK-foh-vose | AKE-foh-vose)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G5401; frightened out of one's wits; sore afraid, exceedingly fear; 

- stricken with fear or terror, exceedingly, frightened, terrified
