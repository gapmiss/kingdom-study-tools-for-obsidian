# G304 ἀναβαθμός

## anabathmós

_(an-ab-ath-mos' | ah-na-vahth-MOSE | ah-na-vahth-MOSE)_

### Definition

from G0305 (compare G0898); a stairway; stairs; 

- an ascent
- a means of going up, a flight of stairs, stairs
