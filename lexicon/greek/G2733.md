# G2733 κατοικία

## katoikía

_(kat-oy-kee'-ah | ka-too-KEE-ah | ka-too-KEE-ah)_

### Definition

residence (properly, the condition; but by implication, the abode itself); habitation; 

- dwelling, habitation
- to cause to dwell, to send or bring into an abode
- to give a dwelling to
