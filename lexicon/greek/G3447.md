# G3447 μοσχοποιέω

## moschopoiéō

_(mos-khop-oy-eh'-o | moh-skoh-poo-EH-oh | moh-skoh-poo-A-oh)_

### Definition

from G3448 and G4160; to fabricate the image of a bullock; make a calf; 

- to make (an image of) a calf
