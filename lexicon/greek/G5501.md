# G5501 χείρων

## cheírōn

_(khi'-rone | HEE-rone | HEE-rone)_

### Definition

irregular comparative of G2556; from an obsolete equivalent χέρης (of uncertain derivation); more evil or aggravated (physically, mentally or morally); sorer, worse; 

- worse
