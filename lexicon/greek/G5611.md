# G5611 ὡραῖος

## hōraîos

_(ho-rah'-yos | hoh-REH-ose | oh-RAY-ose)_

### Definition

from G5610; belonging to the right hour or season (timely), i.e., (by implication) flourishing (beauteous (figuratively)); beautiful; 

- blooming, beautiful (used of the human body)
