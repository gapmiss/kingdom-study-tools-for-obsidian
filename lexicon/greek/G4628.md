# G4628 σκέλος

## skélos

_(skel'-os | SKEH-lose | SKAY-lose)_

### Definition

apparently from σκέλλω (to parch; through the idea of leanness); the leg (as lank); leg; 

- the leg, from the hip to the toes inclusive
