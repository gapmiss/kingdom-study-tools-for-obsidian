# G3488 Νάρκισσος

## Nárkissos

_(nar'-kis-sos | NAHR-kees-sose | NAHR-kees-sose)_

### Definition

stupidity; a flower of the same name, from νάρκη (stupefaction, as a "narcotic"); Narcissus, a Roman; Narcissus; 

- a dweller at Rome mentioned by Paul in Romans 16:11
