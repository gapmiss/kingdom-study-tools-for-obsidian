# G1751 ἔνειμι

## éneimi

_(en'-i-mee | EN-ee-mee | ANE-ee-mee)_

### Definition

from G1772 and G1510; to be within (neuter participle plural); such things as...have; 

- to be in, what is within, i.e., the soul
