# G3722 ὄρθρος

## órthros

_(or'-thros | ORE-throse | ORE-throse)_

### Definition

from the same as G3735; dawn (as sun-rise, rising of light); by extension, morn; early in the morning; 

- daybreak, dawn
- at early dawn, at daybreak, early in the morning
