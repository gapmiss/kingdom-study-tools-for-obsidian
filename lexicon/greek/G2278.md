# G2278 ἠχέω

## ēchéō

_(ay-kheh'-o | ay-HEH-oh | ay-HAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G2279; to make a loud noise, i.e., reverberate; roar, sound; 

- to sound
  - used of the roaring of the sea
