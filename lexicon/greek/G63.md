# G63 ἀγραυλέω

## agrauléō

_(ag-row-leh'-o | ah-grev-LEH-oh | ah-gra-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0068 and G0832 (in the sense of G0833); to camp out; abide in the field; 

- to live in the fields, be under the open sky, even at night
