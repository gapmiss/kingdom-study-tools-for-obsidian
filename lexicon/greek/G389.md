# G389 ἀναστενάζω

## anastenázō

_(an-as-ten-ad'-zo | ah-na-steh-NA-zoh | ah-na-stay-NA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G4727; to sigh deeply; sigh deeply; 

- to draw up deep sighs from the bottom of the breast, to sigh deeply
