# G1969 ἐπιπλήσσω

## epiplḗssō

_(ep-ee-place'-so | eh-pee-PLASE-soh | ay-pee-PLASE-soh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G4141; to chastise, i.e., (with words) to upbraid; rebuke; 

- to strike upon, beat upon
- to chastise with words, to chide, upbraid, rebuke
