# G3128 Μανασσῆς

## Manassēs

_(man-as-sace' | ma-nahs-SASE | ma-nahs-SASE)_

### Definition

forgetting; of Hebrew origin (H4519); Mannasses (i.e., Menashsheh), an Israelite; Manasses; 

- the first born son of Joseph
- the son of Hezekiah, king of Judah
