# G2042 ἐρεθίζω

## erethízō

_(er-eth-id'-zo | eh-reh-THEE-zoh | ay-ray-THEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from a presumed prolonged form of G2054; to stimulate (especially to anger); provoke; 

- to stir up, excite, stimulate, to provoke
