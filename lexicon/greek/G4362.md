# G4362 προσπήγνυμι

## prospḗgnymi

_(pros-payg'-noo-mee | prose-PAY-gnoo-mee | prose-PAY-gnyoo-mee)_

### Definition

from G4314 and G4078; to fasten to, i.e., (specially), to impale (on a cross); crucify; 

- to fasten
