# G5026 ταύτῃ

## taútēi

_(tow'-tay | TAV-tay | TAF-tay)_

### Definition

dative case, accusative case and genitive case respectively of the feminine singular of G3778; (towards or of) this; her, + hereof, it, that, + thereby, the (same), this (same); 

- this, that, the same
