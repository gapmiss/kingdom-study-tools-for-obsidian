# G2572 καλύπτω

## kalýptō

_(kal-oop'-to | ka-LOO-ptoh | ka-LYOO-ptoh)_

### Definition

akin to G2813 and G2928; to cover up (literally or figuratively); cover, hide; 

- to hide, veil
  - to hinder the knowledge of a thing
