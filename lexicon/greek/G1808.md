# G1808 ἐξαίρω

## exaírō

_(ex-ah'-ee-ro | eks-EH-roh | ayks-A-roh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G0142; to remove; put (take) away; 

- to lift up or take away out of a place
- to remove
