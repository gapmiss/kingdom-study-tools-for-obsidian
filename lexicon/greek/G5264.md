# G5264 ὑποδέχομαι

## hypodéchomai

_(hoop-od-ekh'-om-ahee | hoo-poh-THEH-hoh-meh | yoo-poh-THAY-hoh-may)_

### Definition

from G5259 and G1209; to admit under one's roof, i.e., entertain hospitably; receive; 

- to receive as a guest
