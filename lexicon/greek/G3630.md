# G3630 οἰνοπότης

## oinopótēs

_(oy-nop-ot'-ace | oo-noh-POH-tase | oo-noh-POH-tase)_

### Definition

from G3631 and a derivative of the alternate of G4095; a tippler; winebibber; 

- a winebibber, given to wine, a wino
