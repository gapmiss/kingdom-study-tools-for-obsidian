# G4541 Σαμαρείτης

## Samareítēs

_(sam-ar-i'-tace | sa-ma-REE-tase | sa-ma-REE-tase)_

### Definition

from G4540; a Samarite, i.e., inhabitant of Samaria; Samaritan; 

- a Samaritan,
  - an inhabitant of the city of Samaria
  - an inhabitant of the region of Samaria
