# G4462 ῥαββονί

## rhabboní

_(hrab-bon-ee' | rahv-voh-NEE | rahv-voh-NEE)_

### Definition

of Chaldee origin; corresponding to G4461; Lord, Rabboni; 

- master, chief, prince
- Rabboni is a title of honor Mary used to address Jesus
