# G2221 ζωγρέω

## zōgréō

_(dzogue-reh'-o | zoh-GREH-oh | zoh-GRAY-oh)_

### Definition

from the same as G2226 and G0064; to take alive (make a prisoner of war), i.e., (figuratively) to capture or ensnare; take captive, catch; 

- to take alive
- to take, catch, capture
