# G1901 ἐπεκτείνομαι

## epekteínomai

_(ep-ek-ti'-nom-ahee | ep-ek-TEE-noh-meh | ape-ake-TEE-noh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G1909 and G1614; to stretch (oneself) forward upon; reach forth; 

- to stretch out to or towards
- to stretch (one's self) forward to
