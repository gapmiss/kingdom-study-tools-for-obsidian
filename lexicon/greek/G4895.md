# G4895 σύνειμι

## sýneimi

_(soon'-i-mee | SOON-ee-mee | SYOON-ee-mee)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G1510 (including its various inflections); to be in company with, i.e., present at the time; be with; 

- to be with
