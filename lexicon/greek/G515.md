# G515 ἀξιόω

## axióō

_(ax-ee-o'-o | ah-ksee-OH-oh | ah-ksee-OH-oh)_

### Definition

from G0514; to deem entitled or fit; desire, think good, count (think) worthy; 

- to think meet, fit, right
- to judge worthy, deem, deserving
