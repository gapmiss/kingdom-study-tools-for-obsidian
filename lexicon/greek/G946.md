# G946 βδέλυγμα

## bdélygma

_(bdel'-oog-mah | v-THEH-loog-ma | v-THAY-lyoog-ma)_

### Definition

from G0948; a detestation, i.e., (specially) idolatry; abomination; 

- a foul thing, a detestable thing
  - of idols and things pertaining to idolatry
