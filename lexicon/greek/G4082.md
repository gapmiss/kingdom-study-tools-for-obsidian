# G4082 πήρα

## pḗra

_(pay'-rah | PAY-ra | PAY-ra)_

### Definition

of uncertain affinity; a wallet or leather pouch for food; scrip; 

- a wallet
- a leathern sack, in which travellers and shepherds carried their provisions
