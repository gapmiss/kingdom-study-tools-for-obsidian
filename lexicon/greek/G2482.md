## Italía

_(ee-tal-ee'-ah | ee-ta-LEE-ah | ee-ta-LEE-ah)_

### Definition

calf like; probably of foreign origin; Italia, a region of Europe; Italy; 

- the whole peninsula between the Alps and the Straits of Messina
