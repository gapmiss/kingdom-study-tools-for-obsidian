# G3433 μόλις

## mólis

_(mol'-is | MOH-lees | MOH-lees)_

### Definition

probably by variation for G3425; with difficulty; hardly, scarce(-ly), + with much work; 

- with difficulty, hardly
- not easily, i.e., scarcely, very rarely
