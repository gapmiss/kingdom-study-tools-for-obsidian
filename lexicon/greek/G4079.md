# G4079 πηδάλιον

## pēdálion

_(pay-dal'-ee-on | pay-THA-lee-one | pay-THA-lee-one)_

### Definition

neuter of a (presumed) derivative of πηδόν (the blade of an oar; from the same as G3976); a "pedal", i.e., helm; rudder; 

- a ship's rudder
