# G1137 γωνία

## gōnía

_(go-nee'-ah | goh-NEE-ah | goh-NEE-ah)_

### Definition

probably akin to G1119; an angle; corner, quarter; 

- corner
  - an external angle, a corner
  - internal corner, i.e., a secret place
