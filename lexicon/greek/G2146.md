# G2146 εὐπροσωπέω

## euprosōpéō

_(yoo-pros-o-peh'-o | ev-proh-soh-PEH-oh | afe-proh-soh-PAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G2095 and G4383; to be of good countenance, i.e., (figuratively) to make a display; make a fair show; 

- to make a fair show
- to please
