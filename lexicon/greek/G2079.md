# G2079 ἐσχάτως

## eschátōs

_(es-khat'-oce | eh-SKA-tose | ay-SKA-tose)_

### Definition

adverb from G2078; finally, i.e., (with G2192) at the extremity of life; point of death; 

- extreme, to be in the last gasp, at the point of death
