# G4775 συγκάθημαι

## synkáthēmai

_(soong-kath'-ay-mahee | soong-KA-thay-meh | syoong-KA-thay-may)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G2521; to seat oneself in company with; sit with; 

- to sit together, to sit with one another
