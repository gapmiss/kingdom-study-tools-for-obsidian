# G2697 κατάσχεσις

## katáschesis

_(kat-as'-khes-is | ka-TA-skeh-sees | ka-TA-skay-sees)_

### Definition

from G2722; a holding down, i.e., occupancy; possession; 

- a holding back, hindering
- a holding fast, possession
