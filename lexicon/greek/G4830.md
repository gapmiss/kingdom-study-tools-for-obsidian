# G4830 συμμέτοχος

## symmétochos

_(soom-met'-okh-os | soom-MEH-toh-hose | syoom-MAY-toh-hose)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G3353; a co-participant; partaker; 

- partaking together with one, a joint partaker
  - of something
