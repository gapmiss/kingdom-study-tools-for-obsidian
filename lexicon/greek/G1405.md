# G1405 δράσσομαι

## drássomai

_(dras'-som-ahee | THRAHS-soh-meh | THRAHS-soh-may)_

### Definition

perhaps akin to the base of G1404 (through the idea of capturing); to grasp, i.e., (figuratively) entrap; take; 

- to grasp with the hand, take
