# G4746 στοιβάς

## stoibás

_(stoy-bas' | stoo-VAHS | stoo-VAHS)_

### Definition

from a primary στείβω (to "step" or "stamp"); a spread (as if tramped flat) of loose materials for a couch, i.e., (by implication) a bough of a tree so employed; branch; 

- branch, leafy limb

### Verse(s)

[[Mark 11#8|Mark 11:8]]
