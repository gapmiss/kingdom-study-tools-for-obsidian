# G4161 ποίημα

## poíēma

_(poy'-ay-mah | POO-ay-ma | POO-ay-ma)_

### Definition

from G4160; a product, i.e., fabric (literally or figuratively); thing that is made, workmanship; 

- that which has been made
- a work
  - of the works of God as creator
