# G4108 πλάνος

## plános

_(plan'-os | PLA-nose | PLA-nose)_

### Definition

of uncertain affinity; roving (as a tramp), i.e., (by implication) an impostor or misleader; deceiver, seducing; 

- wandering, roving
- misleading, leading into error
  - a vagabond, &quot;tramp&quot;, imposter
  - corrupter, deceiver
