# G1424 δυσμή

## dysmḗ

_(doos-may' | thoo-SMAY | thyoo-SMAY)_

### Definition

from G1416; the sun-set, i.e., (by implication) the western region; west; 

- the setting of the sun
- the region of the sunset, the west
