# G3719 ὀρθρίζω

## orthrízō

_(or-thrid'-zo | ore-THREE-zoh | ore-THREE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G3722; to use the dawn, i.e., (by implication) to repair betimes; come early in the morning; 

- to rise early in the morning
