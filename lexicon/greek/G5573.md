# G5573 ψευδολόγος

## pseudológos

_(psyoo-dol-og'-os | psev-thoh-LOH-gose | psave-thoh-LOH-gose)_

### Definition

from G5571 and G3004; mendacious, i.e., promulgating erroneous Christian doctrine; speaking lies; 

- speaking (teaching) falsely, speaking lies
