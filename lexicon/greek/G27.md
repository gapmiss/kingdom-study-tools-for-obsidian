# G27 ἀγαπητός

## agapētós

_(ag-ap-ay-tos' | ah-ga-pay-TOSE | ah-ga-pay-TOSE)_

### Definition

from G0025; beloved; (dearly, well) beloved, dear; 

- beloved, esteemed, dear, favorite, worthy of love
