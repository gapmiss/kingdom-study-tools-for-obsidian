# G2083 ἑταῖρος

## hetaîros

_(het-ah'-ee-ros | heh-TEH-rose | ay-TAY-rose)_

### Definition

from ἔτης (a clansman); a comrade; fellow, friend; 

- a comrade, mate, partner
- in kindly address
  - friend, (my good friend)
