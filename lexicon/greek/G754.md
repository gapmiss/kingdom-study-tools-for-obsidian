# G754 ἀρχιτελώνης

## architelṓnēs

_(ar-khee-tel-o'-nace | ar-hee-teh-LOH-nase | ar-hee-tay-LOH-nase)_

### Definition

from G0746 and G5057; a principle tax-gatherer; chief among the publicans; 

- a chief of tax collectors, chief publican
