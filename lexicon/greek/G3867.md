# G3867 παραινέω

## parainéō

_(par-ahee-neh'-o | pa-reh-NEH-oh | pa-ray-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G0134; to mispraise, i.e., recommend or advise (a different course); admonish, exhort; 

- to exhort, admonish
