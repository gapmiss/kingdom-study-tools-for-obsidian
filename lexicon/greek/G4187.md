# G4187 πολυτρόπως

## polytrópōs

_(pol-oot-rop'-oce | poh-loo-TROH-pose | poh-lyoo-TROH-pose)_

### Definition

adverb from a compound of G4183 and G5158; in many ways, i.e., variously as to method or form; in divers manners; 

- in many manners
