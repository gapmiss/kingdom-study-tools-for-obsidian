# G2681 κατασκηνόω

## kataskēnóō

_(kat-as-kay-no'-o | ka-ta-skay-NOH-oh | ka-ta-skay-NOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G4637; to camp down, i.e., haunt; figuratively, to remain; lodge, rest; 

- to pitch one's tent, to fix one's abode, to dwell
