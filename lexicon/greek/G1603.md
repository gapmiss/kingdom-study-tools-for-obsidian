# G1603 ἐκπληρόω

## ekplēróō

_(ek-play-ro'-o | ek-play-ROH-oh | ake-play-ROH-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G4137; to accomplish entirely; fulfill; 

- to fill full, to fill up completely
- metaphorically to fulfil, make good
