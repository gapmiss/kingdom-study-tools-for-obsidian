# G1204 δεῦρο

## deûro

_(dyoo'-ro | THEV-roh | THAVE-roh)_

### Definition

of uncertain affinity; here; used also imperative hither!; and of time, hitherto; come (hither), hither(-to); 

- of place,
  - hither to this place
  - in urging and calling, here! come!
- of time, hither to, now
