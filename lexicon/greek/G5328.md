# G5328 Φαραώ

## Pharaṓ

_(far-ah-o' | fa-ra-OH | fa-ra-OH)_

### Definition

his nakedness; of foreign origin (H6547); Pharao (i.e., Pharoh), an Egyptian king; Pharaoh; 

- was a common title of the native kings of Egypt
