# G2132 εὐνοέω

## eunoéō

_(yoo-no-eh'-o | ev-noh-EH-oh | ave-noh-A-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G2095 and G3563; to be well- minded, i.e., reconcile; agree; 

- to wish (one) well
- to be well disposed, of a peaceable spirit
