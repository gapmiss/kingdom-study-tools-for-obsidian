# G2321 Θεόφιλος

## Theóphilos

_(theh-of'-il-os | theh-OH-fee-lose | thay-OH-fee-lose)_

### Definition

friend of God; from G2316 and G5384; friend of God; Theophilus, a Christian; Theophilus; 

- the person to whom Luke addressed his Gospel and the book of Acts
