# G1848 ἐξουθενέω

## exouthenéō

_(ex-oo-then-eh'-o | eks-oo-theh-NEH-oh | ayks-oo-thay-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

a variation of G1847 and meaning the same; to despise; contemptible, despise, least esteemed, set at nought; 

- to make of no account, despise utterly
