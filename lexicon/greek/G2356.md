# G2356 θρησκεία

## thrēskeía

_(thrace-ki'-ah | thray-SKEE-ah | thray-SKEE-ah)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G2357; ceremonial observance; religion, worshipping; 

- religious worship
  - especially external, that which consists of ceremonies
    - religious discipline, religion
