# G1026 βρέχω

## bréchō

_(brekh'-o | VREH-hoh | VRAY-hoh)_

### Definition

a primary verb; to moisten (especially by a shower); (send) rain, wash; 

- to moisten, wet, water
- to water with rain, to cause to rain, to pour the rain, to send down like rain
