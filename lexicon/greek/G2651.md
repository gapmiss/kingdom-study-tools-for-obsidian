# G2651 καταμόνας

## katamónas

_(kat-am-on'-as | ka-ta-MOH-nahs | ka-ta-MOH-nahs)_

### Definition

from G2596 and accusative case plural feminine of G3441 (with G5561 implied); according to sole places, i.e., (adverbially) separately; alone; 

- apart, alone
