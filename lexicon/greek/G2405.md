# G2405 ἱερατεία

## hierateía

_(hee-er-at-i'-ah | hee-eh-ra-TEE-ah | ee-ay-ra-TEE-ah)_

### Definition

from G2407; priestliness, i.e., the sacerdotal function; office of the priesthood, priest's office; 

- the priesthood, the office of a priest
