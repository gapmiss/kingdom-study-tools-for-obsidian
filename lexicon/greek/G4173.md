# G4173 πολιτάρχης

## politárchēs

_(pol-it-ar'-khace | poh-lee-TAHR-hase | poh-lee-TAHR-hase)_

### Definition

from G4172 and G0757; a town-officer, i.e., magistrate; ruler of the city; 

- a ruler of a city or citizens
