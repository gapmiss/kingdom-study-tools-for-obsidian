# G420 ἀνεξίκακος

## anexíkakos

_(an-ex-ik'-ak-os | ah-neh-KSEE-ka-kose | ah-nay-KSEE-ka-kose)_

### Definition

from G0430 and G2556; enduring of ill, i.e., forbearing; patient; 

- patient of ills and wrongs, forbearing
