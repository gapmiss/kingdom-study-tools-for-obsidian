# G365 ἀνανεόω

## ananeóō

_(an-an-neh-o'-o | ah-na-neh-OH-oh | ah-na-nay-OH-oh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and a derivative of G3501; to renovate, i.e., reform; renew; 

- to renew (in the mind)
