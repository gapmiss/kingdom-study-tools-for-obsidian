# G949 βέβαιος

## bébaios

_(beb'-ah-yos | VEH-veh-ose | VAY-vay-ose)_

### Definition

from the base of G0939 (through the idea of basality); stable (literally or figuratively); firm, of force, stedfast, sure; 

- stable, fast, firm
- metaphorically sure, trusty
