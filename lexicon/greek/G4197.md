# G4197 πορεία

## poreía

_(por-i'-ah | poh-REE-ah | poh-REE-ah)_

### Definition

from G4198; travel (by land); figuratively (plural) proceedings, i.e., career; journey(-ing), ways; 

- a journey
- a going, that is: purpose, pursuit, undertaking
