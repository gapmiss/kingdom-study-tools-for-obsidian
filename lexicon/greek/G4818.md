# G4818 συλλυπέω

## syllypéō

_(sool-loop-eh'-o | sool-loo-PEH-oh | syool-lyoo-PAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G3076; to afflict jointly, i.e., (passive) sorrow at (on account of) someone; be grieved; 

- to affect with grief together
- give with one's self
