# G5339 φείδομαι

## pheídomai

_(fi'-dom-ahee | FEE-thoh-meh | FEE-thoh-may)_

### Definition

of uncertain affinity; to be chary of, i.e., (subjectively) to abstain or (objectively) to treat leniently; forbear, spare; 

- to spare
- to abstain
