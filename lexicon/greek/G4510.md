# G4510 ῥυπόω

## rhypóō

_(rhoo-po'-o | roo-POH-oh | ryoo-POH-oh)_

### Definition

from G4509; to soil, i.e., (intransitively) to become dirty (morally); be filthy; 

- to make filthy, defile, soil
- to be filthy (morally)
