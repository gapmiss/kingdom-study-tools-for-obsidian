# G407 ἀνδρίζομαι

## andrízomai

_(an-drid'-zom-ahee | an-THREE-zoh-meh | an-THREE-zoh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G0435; to act manly; quit like men; 

- to make a man of or make brave
- to show one's self a man, be brave
