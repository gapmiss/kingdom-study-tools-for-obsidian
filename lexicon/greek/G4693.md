# G4693 σπήλαιον

## spḗlaion

_(spay'-lah-yon | SPAY-leh-one | SPAY-lay-one)_

### Definition

neuter of a presumed derivative of σπέος (a grotto); a cavern; by implication, a hiding-place or resort; cave, den; 

- a cave, den
