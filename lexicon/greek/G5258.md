# G5258 ὕπνος

## hýpnos

_(hoop'-nos | HOO-pnose | YOO-pnose)_

### Definition

from an obsolete primary (perhaps akin to G5259 through the idea of subsilience); sleep, i.e., (figuratively) spiritual torpor; sleep; 

- sleep
