# G5284 ὑποπλέω

## hypopléō

_(hoop-op-leh'-o | hoo-poh-PLEH-oh | yoo-poh-PLAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G5259 and G4126; to sail under the lee of; sail under; 

- to sail under, that is, to sail close by
- to the leeward of
