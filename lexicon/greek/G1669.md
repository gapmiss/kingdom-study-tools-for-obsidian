# G1669 ἑλκόω

## helkóō

_(hel-ko'-o | hel-KOH-oh | ale-KOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G1668; to cause to ulcerate, i.e., (passively) be ulcerous; full of sores; 

- to make sore, cause to ulcerate
- to be ulcerated
- full of sores
