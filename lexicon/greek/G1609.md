# G1609 ἐκπτύω

## ekptýō

_(ek-ptoo'-o | ek-PTOO-oh | ake-PTYOO-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G4429; to spit out, i.e., (figuratively) spurn; reject; 

- to spit out
- reject, spurn, loathe
