# G900 βαθύνω

## bathýnō

_(bath-oo'-no | va-THOO-noh | va-THYOO-noh)_

### Definition

from G0901; to deepen; deep; 

- 'the deep' sea (the 'high seas')
- deep, extreme poverty, the deep things of God, things hidden and above man's scrutiny, especially divine counsels
- to make deep
