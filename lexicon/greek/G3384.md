# G3384 μήτηρ

## mḗtēr

_(may'-tare | MAY-tare | MAY-tare)_

### Definition

apparently a primary word; a "mother" (literally or figuratively, immediate or remote); mother; 

- a mother
- metaphorically the source of something, the motherland
