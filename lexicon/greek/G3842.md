# G3842 πάντοτε

## pántote

_(pan'-tot-eh | PAHN-toh-teh | PAHN-toh-tay)_

### Definition

from G3956 and G3753; every when, i.e., at all times; alway(-s), ever(-more); 

- at all times, always, ever
