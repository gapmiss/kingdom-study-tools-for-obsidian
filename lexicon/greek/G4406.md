# G4406 πρώϊμος

## prṓïmos

_(pro'-ee-mos | PROH-ee-mose | PROH-ee-mose)_

### Definition

from G4404; dawning, i.e., (by analogy) autumnal (showering, the first of the rainy season); early; 

- early
  - of the early rain which fell from October on
