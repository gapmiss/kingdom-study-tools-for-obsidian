# G5090 Τιμαῖος

## Timaîos

_(tim'-ah-yos | tee-MEH-ose | tee-MAY-ose)_

### Definition

unclean, highly prized; probably of Chaldee origin (compare H2931); Timaeus (i.e., Timay), an Israelite; Timaeus; 

- the father of the blind man, Bartimaeus
