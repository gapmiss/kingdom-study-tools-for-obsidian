# G3608 ὀθόνιον

## othónion

_(oth-on'-ee-on | oh-THOH-nee-one | oh-THOH-nee-one)_

### Definition

neuter of a presumed derivative of G3607; a linen bandage; linen clothes; 

- a piece of linen, small linen cloth
- strips of linen cloth for swathing the dead
