# G2496 Ἰωράμ

## Iōrám

_(ee-o-ram' | ee-oh-RAHM | ee-oh-RAHM)_

### Definition

whom Jehovah has exalted; of Hebrew origin (H3141); Joram, an Israelite; Joram; 

- the son of Jehoshaphat, king of Judah, succeeded his father on the throne of Judah
