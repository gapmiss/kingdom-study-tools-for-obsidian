# G3578 ξενία

## xenía

_(xen-ee'-ah | kseh-NEE-ah | ksay-NEE-ah)_

### Definition

from G3581; hospitality, i.e., (by implication) a place of entertainment; lodging; 

- hospitality, hospitable reception
- a lodging place, lodgings
