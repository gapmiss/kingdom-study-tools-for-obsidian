## antílēpsis

_(an-til'-ape-sis | an-TEE-lay-psees | an-TEE-lay-psees)_

### Definition

from G0482; relief; help; 

- a laying hold of, apprehension, perception, objection of a disputant
- in New Testament, to aid, help
