# G4908 συνετός

## synetós

_(soon-et'-os | soon-eh-TOSE | syoon-ay-TOSE)_

### Definition

from G4920; mentally put (or putting) together, i.e., sagacious; prudent; 

- intelligent, having understanding, wise, learned

### Note

Compare G5429.
