# G3055 λογομαχία

## logomachía

_(log-om-akh-ee'-ah | loh-goh-ma-HEE-ah | loh-goh-ma-HEE-ah)_

### Definition

from the same as G3054; disputation about trifles ("logomachy"); strife of words; 

- to contend about words
- to wrangle about empty and trifling matters
