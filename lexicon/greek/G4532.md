# G4532 Σαλήμ

## Salḗm

_(sal-ame' | sa-LAME | sa-LAME)_

### Definition

peace; of Hebrew origin (H8004); Salem (i.e., Shalem), a place in Palestine; Salem; 

- the place where Melchizedek was king and thought to be the ancient name of Jerusalem
