# G1772 ἔννομος

## énnomos

_(en'-nom-os | EN-noh-mose | ANE-noh-mose)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G3551; (subjectively) legal, or (objectively) subject to; lawful, under law; 

- bound to the law
- bound by the law, lawful
- lawful, regular
