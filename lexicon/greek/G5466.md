# G5466 Χαλδαῖος

## Chaldaîos

_(khal-dah'-yos | hahl-THEH-ose | hahl-THAY-ose)_

### Definition

as clod breakers; probably of Hebrew or (H3778); a Chaldaean (i.e., Kasdi), or native or the region of the lower Euphrates; Chaldaean; 

- a Chaldean
