# G3348 μετέχω

## metéchō

_(met-ekh'-o | meh-TEH-hoh | may-TAY-hoh)_

### Definition

from G3326 and G2192; to share or participate; by implication, belong to, eat (or drink); be partaker, pertain, take part, use; 

- to be or become partaker
- to partake
