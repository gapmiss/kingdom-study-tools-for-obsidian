# G294 ἀμφιέννυμι

## amphiénnymi

_(am-fee-en'-noo-mee | am-fee-EN-noo-mee | am-fee-ANE-nyoo-mee)_

### Definition

from the base of G0297 and ἕννυμι (to invest); to enrobe; clothe; 

- to put on, to clothe
