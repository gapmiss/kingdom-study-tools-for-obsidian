# G839 αὔριον

## aúrion

_(ow'-ree-on | EV-ree-one | A-ree-one)_

### Definition

from a derivative of the same as G0109 (meaning a breeze, i.e. the morning air); properly, fresh, i.e., (adverb with ellipsis of G2250) to-morrow; (to-)morrow, next day; 

- tomorrow
