# G520 ἀπάγω

## apágō

_(ap-ag'-o | ah-PA-goh | ah-PA-goh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G0071; to take off (in various senses); bring, carry away, lead (away), put to death, take away; 

- to lead away
  - especially of those who are led off to trial, prison, or punishment
