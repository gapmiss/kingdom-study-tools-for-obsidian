# G1976 ἐπιῤῥάπτω

## epirrháptō

_(ep-ir-hrap'-to | eh-peer-RA-ptoh | ay-peer-RA-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and the base of G4476; to stitch upon, i.e., fasten with the needle; sew on; 

- to sew upon, sew to
