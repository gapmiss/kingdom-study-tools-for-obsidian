# G1533 εἰσφέρω

## eisphérō

_(ice-fer'-o | ees-FEH-roh | ees-FAY-roh)_

### Definition

from G1519 and G5342; to carry inward (literally or figuratively); bring (in), lead into; 

- to bring into, in or to
- to lead into
