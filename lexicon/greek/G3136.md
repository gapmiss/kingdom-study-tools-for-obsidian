# G3136 Μάρθα

## Mártha

_(mar'-thah | MAHR-tha | MAHR-tha)_

### Definition

she was rebellious; probably of Chaldee origin (meaning mistress); Martha, a Christian woman; Martha; 

- was the sister of Lazarus and Mary of Bethany
