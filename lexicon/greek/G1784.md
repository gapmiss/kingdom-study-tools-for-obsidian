# G1784 ἔντιμος

## éntimos

_(en'-tee-mos | EN-tee-mose | ANE-tee-mose)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G5092; valued (figuratively); dear, more honourable, precious, in reputation; 

- held in honor, prized, precious
