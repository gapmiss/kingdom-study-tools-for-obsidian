# G3324 μεστός

## mestós

_(mes-tos' | meh-STOSE | may-STOSE)_

### Definition

of uncertain derivation; replete (literally or figuratively); full; 

- full
  - in reference to persons, whose minds are as it were filled with thoughts and emotions, either good or bad
