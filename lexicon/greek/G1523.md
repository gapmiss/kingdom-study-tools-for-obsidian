# G1523 εἰσδέχομαι

## eisdéchomai

_(ice-dekh'-om-ahee | ees-THEH-hoh-meh | ees-THAY-hoh-may)_

### Definition

from G1519 and G1209; to take into one's favor; receive; 

- to receive kindly, to treat with favor
