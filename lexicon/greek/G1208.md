# G1208 δεύτερος

## deúteros

_(dyoo'-ter-os | THEV-teh-rose | THAYF-tay-rose)_

### Definition

as the comparative of G1417; (ordinal) second (in time, place, or rank; also adverb); afterward, again, second(-arily) (time); 

- the second, the other of two
