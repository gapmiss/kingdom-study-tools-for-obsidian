# G2981 λαλιά

## laliá

_(lal-ee-ah' | la-lee-AH | la-lee-AH)_

### Definition

from G2980; talk; saying, speech; 

- speech, i.e., a story
- dialect, mode of speech, pronunciation
  - speech which discloses the speaker's native country
