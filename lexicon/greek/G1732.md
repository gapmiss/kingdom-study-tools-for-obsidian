# G1732 ἔνδειξις

## éndeixis

_(en'-dike-sis | EN-thee-ksees | ANE-thee-ksees)_

### Definition

from G1731; indication (abstractly); declare, evident token, proof; 

- demonstration, proof
  - manifestation made by act
  - sign, evidence
