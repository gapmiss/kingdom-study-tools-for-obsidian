# G4333 προσεργάζομαι

## prosergázomai

_(pros-er-gad'-zom-ahee | prose-er-GA-zoh-meh | prose-are-GA-zoh-may)_

### Definition

from G4314 and G2038; to work additionally, i.e., (by implication) acquire besides; gain; 

- to work besides
- by working or trading to make or gain besides
