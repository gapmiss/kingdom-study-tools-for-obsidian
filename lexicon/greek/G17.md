# G17 ἀγαθοποιός

## agathopoiós

_(ag-ath-op-oy-os' | ah-ga-thoh-poo-OSE | ah-ga-thoh-poo-OSE)_

### Definition

from G0018 and G4160; a well-doer, i.e., virtuous; them that do well; 

- acting rightly, doing well, virtuous
