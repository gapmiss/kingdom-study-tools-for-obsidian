# G2615 καταδουλόω

## katadoulóō

_(kat-ad-oo-lo'-o | ka-ta-thoo-LOH-oh | ka-ta-thoo-LOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G1402; to enslave utterly; bring into bondage; 

- to bring into bondage, enslave
- to enslave to one's self, bring into bondage to one's self
