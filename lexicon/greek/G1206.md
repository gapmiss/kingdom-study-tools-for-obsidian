# G1206 δευτεραῖος

## deuteraîos

_(dyoo-ter-ah'-yos | thev-teh-REH-ose | thayf-tay-RAY-ose)_

### Definition

from G1208; secondary, i.e., (specially) on the second day; next day; 

- of or belonging to the second
- of one who comes or does a thing, on the second day
