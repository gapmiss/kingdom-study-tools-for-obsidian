# G4959 συστενάζω

## systenázō

_(soos-ten-ad'-zo | soo-steh-NA-zoh | syoo-stay-NA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G4727; to moan jointly, i.e., (figuratively) experience a common calamity; groan together; 

- to groan together
