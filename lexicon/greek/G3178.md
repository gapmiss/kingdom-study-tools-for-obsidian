# G3178 μέθη

## méthē

_(meth'-ay | MEH-thay | MAY-thay)_

### Definition

apparently a primary word; an intoxicant, i.e., (by implication) intoxication; drunkenness; 

- intoxication
- drunkenness

[[Luke 21#34|Luke 21:34]], [[Romans 13#13|Romans 13:13]], [[Galatians 5#21|Galatians 5:21]]
