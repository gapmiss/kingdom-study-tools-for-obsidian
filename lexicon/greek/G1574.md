# G1574 ἐκκεντέω

## ekkentéō

_(ek-ken-teh'-o | ek-ken-TEH-oh | ake-kane-TAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and the base of G2759; to transfix; pierce; 

- to put out, dig out
- to dig through, transfix, pierce
