# G4528 Σαλαθιήλ

## Salathiḗl

_(sal-ath-ee-ale' | sa-la-thee-ALE | sa-la-thee-ALE)_

### Definition

I have asked of God; of Hebrew origin (H7597); Salathiel (i.e., Shealtiel), an Israelite; Salathiel; 

- the son of Jeconiah in the genealogy of Christ
