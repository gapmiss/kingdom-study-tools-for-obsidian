# G5282 ὑπονοέω

## hyponoéō

_(hoop-on-o-eh'-o | hoo-poh-noh-EH-oh | yoo-poh-noh-A-oh)_

### Definition

from G5259 and G3539; to think under (privately), i.e., to surmise or conjecture; think, suppose, deem; 

- to suppose, surmise
