# G545 ἀπειθής

## apeithḗs

_(ap-i-thace' | ah-pee-THASE | ah-pee-THASE)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G3982; unpersuadable, i.e., contumacious; disobedient; 

- impersuasible, not compliant, disobedient, contumacious
