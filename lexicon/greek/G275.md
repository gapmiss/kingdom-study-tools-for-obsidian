# G275 ἀμέριμνος

## amérimnos

_(am-er'-im-nos | ah-MEH-reem-nose | ah-MAY-reem-nose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G3308; not anxious; without care(-fulness), secure; 

- free from anxiety, free from care
