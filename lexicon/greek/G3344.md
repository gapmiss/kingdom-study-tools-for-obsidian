# G3344 μεταστρέφω

## metastréphō

_(met-as-tref'-o | meh-ta-STREH-foh | may-ta-STRAY-foh)_

### Definition

from G3326 and G4762; to turn across, i.e., transmute or (figuratively) corrupt; pervert, turn; 

- to turn around, turn around
