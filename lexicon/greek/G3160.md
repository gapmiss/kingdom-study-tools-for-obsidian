# G3160 Ματταθά

## Mattathá

_(mat-tath-ah' | maht-ta-THA | maht-ta-THA)_

### Definition

givingness; probably a shortened form of G3161 (compare H4992); Mattatha (i.e., Mattithjah), an Israelite; Mattatha; 

- an ancestor of Christ
