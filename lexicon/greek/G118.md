# G118 ἀθλέω

## athléō

_(ath-leh'-o | ah-THLEH-oh | ah-THLAY-oh)_

### Definition

from ἄθλος (a contest in the public lists); to contend in the competitive games; strive; 

- to engage in a contest, contend in public games, contend for a prize
- to endure, suffer
