# G4780 συγκαλύπτω

## synkalýptō

_(soong-kal-oop'-to | soong-ka-LOO-ptoh | syoong-ka-LYOO-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G2572; to conceal altogether; cover; 

- to cover on all sides, to conceal entirely, to cover up completely
