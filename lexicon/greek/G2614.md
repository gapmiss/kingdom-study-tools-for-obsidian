# G2614 καταδιώκω

## katadiṓkō

_(kat-ad-ee-o'-ko | ka-ta-thee-OH-koh | ka-ta-thee-OH-koh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G1377; to hunt down, i.e., search for; follow after; 

- to follow after, follow up
