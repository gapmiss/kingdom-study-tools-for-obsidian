# G2154 εὔσημος

## eúsēmos

_(yoo'-say-mos | EV-say-mose | AFE-say-mose)_

### Definition

from G2095 and the base of G4591; well indicated, i.e., (figuratively) significant; easy to be understood; 

- well marked, clear and definite, distinct
