# G164 αἰχμάλωτος

## aichmálōtos

_(aheekh-mal-o-tos' | ek-MA-loh-tose | ake-MA-loh-tose)_

### Definition

from αἰχμή (a spear) and a derivative of the same as G0259; properly, a prisoner of war, i.e., (genitive case) a captive; captive; 

- a captive
