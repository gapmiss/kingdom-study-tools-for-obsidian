# G3580 ξενοδοχέω

## xenodochéō

_(xen-od-okh-eh'-o | kseh-noh-thoh-HEH-oh | ksay-noh-thoh-HAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G3581 and G1209; to be hospitable; lodge strangers; 

- to receive and entertain hospitably, to be hospitable
