# G3792 ὀχλοποιέω

## ochlopoiéō

_(okh-lop-oy-eh'-o | oh-hloh-poo-EH-oh | oh-hloh-poo-A-oh)_

### Definition

from G3793 and G4160; to make a crowd, i.e., raise a public disturbance; gather a company; 

- to collect a crowd, gather the people together
