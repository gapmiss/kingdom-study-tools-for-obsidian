# G1747 ἐνέδρα

## enédra

_(en-ed'-rah | en-EH-thra | ane-A-thra)_

### Definition

feminine from G1722 and the base of G1476; an ambuscade, i.e., (figuratively) murderous purpose; lay wait; 

- a lying in wait, an ambush
