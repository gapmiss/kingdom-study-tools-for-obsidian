# G1065 γέ

## gé

_(gheh | geh | gay)_

### Definition

a primary particle of emphasis or qualification (often used with other particles prefixed); doubtless, since; and besides, doubtless, at least, yet; 

- indeed, truly, at least
- even
- if indeed, seeing that
