# G2105 εὐδία

## eudía

_(yoo-dee'-ah | ev-THEE-ah | ave-THEE-ah)_

### Definition

feminine from G2095 and the alternate of G2203 (as the god of the weather); a clear sky, i.e., fine weather; fair weather; 

- a serene sky, fair weather
