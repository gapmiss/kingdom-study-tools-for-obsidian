# G1465 ἐγκόπτω

## enkóptō

_(eng-kop'-to | eng-KOH-ptoh | ayng-KOH-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G2875; to cut into, i.e., (figuratively) impede, detain; hinder, be tedious unto; 

- to cut into, to impede one's course by cutting off his way
- hinder
