# G1112 γογγυσμός

## gongysmós

_(gong-goos-mos' | gohng-goo-SMOSE | gohng-gyoo-SMOSE)_

### Definition

from G1111; a grumbling; grudging, murmuring; 

- a murmur, murmuring, muttering
  - a secret debate
  - a secret displeasure not openly avowed
