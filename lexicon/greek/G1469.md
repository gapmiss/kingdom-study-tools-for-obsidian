# G1469 ἐγκρίνω

## enkrínō

_(eng-kree'-no | eng-KREE-noh | ayng-KREE-noh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G2919; to judge in, i.e., count among; make of the number; 

- to reckon among, judge among
- to judge one worthy of being admitted to a certain class
