# G1924 ἐπιγράφω

## epigráphō

_(ep-ee-graf'-o | eh-pee-GRA-foh | ay-pee-GRA-foh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G1125; to inscribe (physically or mentally); inscription, write in (over, thereon); 

- to write upon, inscribe
- metaphorically to write upon the mind
