# G451 Ἄννα

## Ánna

_(an'-nah | AN-na | AN-na)_

### Definition

grace; of Hebrew origin (H2584); Anna, an Israelitess; Anna; 

- A Jewish prophetess in Jerusalem at the time of the Lord's presentation in the Temple

### Note

She was of the tribe of Asher.
