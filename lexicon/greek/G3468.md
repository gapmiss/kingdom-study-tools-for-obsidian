# G3468 μώλωψ

## mṓlōps

_(mo'-lopes | MOH-lohps | MOH-lohps)_

### Definition

from μῶλος ("moil"; probably akin to the base of G3433) and probably ὤψ (the face; from G3700); a mole ("black eye") or blow-mark; stripe; 

- a bruise, wale, wound that trickles with blood
