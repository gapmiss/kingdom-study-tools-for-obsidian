# G2138 εὐπειθής

## eupeithḗs

_(yoo-pi-thace' | ev-pee-THASE | afe-pee-THASE)_

### Definition

from G2095 and G3982; good for persuasion, i.e., (intransitively) complaint; easy to be intreated; 

- easily obeying, compliant
