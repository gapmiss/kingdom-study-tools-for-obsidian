# G1830 ἐξερευνάω

## exereunáō

_(ex-er-yoo-nah'-o | eks-eh-rev-NA-oh | ayks-ay-rave-NA-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G2045; to explore (figuratively); search diligently; 

- to search out, search anxiously and diligently
