# G4832 σύμμορφος

## sýmmorphos

_(soom-mor-fos' | SOOM-more-fose | SYOOM-more-fose)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G3444; jointly formed, i.e., (figuratively) similar; conformed to, fashioned like unto; 

- having the same form as another, similar, conformed to
