# G2544 καίτοιγε

## kaítoige

_(kah'-ee-toyg-eh | KEH-too-geh | KAY-too-gay)_

### Definition

from G2543 and G1065; and yet indeed, i.e., although really; nevertheless, though; 

- and yet, thought
