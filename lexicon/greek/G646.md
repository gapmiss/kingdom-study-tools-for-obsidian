# G646 ἀποστασία

## apostasía

_(ap-os-tas-ee'-ah | ah-poh-sta-SEE-ah | ah-poh-sta-SEE-ah)_

### Definition

feminine of the same as G0647; defection from truth (properly, the state) ("apostasy"); falling away, forsake; 

- a falling away, defection, apostasy
