# G5320 φανερῶς

## phanerōs

_(fan-er-oce' | fa-neh-ROSE | fa-nay-ROSE)_

### Definition

adverb from G5318; plainly, i.e., clearly or publicly; evidently, openly; 

- manifestly
  - plainly, clearly
  - openly
