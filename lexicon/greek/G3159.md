# G3159 Ματθίας

## Matthías

_(mat-thee'-as | maht-THEE-as | maht-THEE-as)_

### Definition

gift of God; apparently a shortened form of G3161; Matthias (i.e., Mattithjah), an Israelite; Matthias; 

- the apostle elected to fill the place of the traitor Judas
