# G3948 παροξυσμός

## paroxysmós

_(par-ox-oos-mos' | pa-roh-ksoo-SMOSE | pa-roh-ksyoo-SMOSE)_

### Definition

from G3947 ("paroxysm"); incitement (to good), or dispute (in anger); contention, provoke unto; 

- an inciting, incitement
- irritation
