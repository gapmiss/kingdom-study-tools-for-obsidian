# G528 ἀπαντάω

## apantáō

_(ap-an-tah'-o | ah-pahn-TA-oh | ah-pahn-TA-oh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and a derivative of G0473; to meet away, i.e., encounter; meet; 

- to go to meet, to meet
  - in a military sense: a hostile meeting
