# G253 ἀλυπότερος

## alypóteros

_(al-oo-pot'-er-os | ah-loo-POH-teh-rose | ah-lyoo-POH-tay-rose)_

### Definition

comparative of a compound of G0001 (as a negative particle) and G3077; more without grief; less sorrowful; 

- free from pain or grief
