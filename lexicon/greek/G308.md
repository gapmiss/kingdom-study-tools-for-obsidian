# G308 ἀναβλέπω

## anablépō

_(an-ab-lep'-o | ah-na-VLEH-poh | ah-na-VLAY-poh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G0991; to look up; by implication, to recover sight; look (up), see, receive sight; 

- to look up
- to recover (lost) sight
