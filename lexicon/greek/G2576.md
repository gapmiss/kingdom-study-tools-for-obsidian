# G2576 καμμύω

## kammýō

_(kam-moo'-o | kahm-MOO-oh | kahm-MYOO-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G2596 and the base of G3466; to shut down, i.e., close the eyes; close; 

- to shut the eyes, close the eyes
