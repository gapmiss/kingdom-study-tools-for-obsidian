# G4918 συνθλίβω

## synthlíbō

_(soon-thlee'-bo | soon-THLEE-voh | syoon-THLEE-voh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G2346; to compress, i.e., crowd on all sides; throng; 

- to press together, press on all sides
