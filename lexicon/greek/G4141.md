# G4141 πλήσσω

## plḗssō

_(place'-so | PLASE-soh | PLASE-soh)_

### Definition

apparently another form of G4111 (through the idea of flattening out); to pound, i.e., (figuratively) to inflict with (calamity); smite; 

- to strike, smite

### Note

Compare G5180.
