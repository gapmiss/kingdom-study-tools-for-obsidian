# G741 ἀρτύω

## artýō

_(ar-too'-o | ar-TOO-oh | ar-TYOO-oh)_

### Definition

from a presumed derivative of G0142; to prepare, i.e., spice (with stimulating condiments); season; 

- to prepare, arrange, with respect to food
- to season, make savory
