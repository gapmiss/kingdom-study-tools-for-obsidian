# G5137 τράχηλος

## tráchēlos

_(trakh'-ay-los | TRA-hay-lose | TRA-hay-lose)_

### Definition

probably from G5143 (through the idea of mobility); the throat (neck), i.e., (figuratively) life; neck; 

- to be ready to incur the most imminent peril to life
