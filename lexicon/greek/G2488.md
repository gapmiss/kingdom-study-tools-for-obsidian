# G2488 Ἰωάθαμ

## Iōátham

_(ee-o-ath'-am | ee-oh-AH-thahm | ee-oh-AH-thahm)_

### Definition

Jehovah is upright; of Hebrew origin (H3147); Joatham (i.e., Jotham), an Israelite; Joatham; 

- the son of King Uzziah or Azariah and Jerusha
