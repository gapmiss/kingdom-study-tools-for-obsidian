# G4008 πέραν

## péran

_(per'-an | PEH-rahn | PAY-rahn)_

### Definition

apparently accusative case of an obsolete derivative of πείρω (to "pierce"); through (as adverb or preposition), i.e., across; beyond, farther (other) side, over; 

- beyond, on the other side
