# G3810 παιδευτής

## paideutḗs

_(pahee-dyoo-tace' | peh-thev-TASE | pay-thayf-TASE)_

### Definition

from G3811; a trainer, i.e., teacher or (by implication) discipliner; which corrected, instructor; 

- an instructor, preceptor, teacher
- a chastiser
