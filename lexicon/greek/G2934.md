# G2934 κτῆνος

## ktēnos

_(ktay'-nos | k-TAY-nose | k-TAY-nose)_

### Definition

from G2932; property, i.e., (specially) a domestic animal; beast; 

- a beast
  - especially a beast of burden
  - used for four legged animals as opposed to fishes and birds
