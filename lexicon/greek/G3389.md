# G3389 μητραλῴας

## mētralṓias

_(may-tral-o'-as | may-tra-LOH-as | may-tra-LOH-as)_

### Definition

from G3384 and the base of G0257; a mother-thresher, i.e., matricide; murderer of mothers; 

- matricide, the murderer of a mother
