# G793 ἀστήρικτος

## astḗriktos

_(as-tay'-rik-tos | ah-STAY-reek-tose | ah-STAY-reek-tose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G4741; unfixed, i.e., (figuratively) vacillating; unstable; 

- unstable, unsteadfast
