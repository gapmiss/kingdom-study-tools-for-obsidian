# G5081 τηλαυγῶς

## tēlaugōs

_(tay-low-goce' | tay-lev-GOSE | tay-la-GOSE)_

### Definition

adverb from a compound of a derivative of G5056 and G0827; in a far-shining manner, i.e., plainly; clearly; 

- at a distance and clearly
