# G3404 μισέω

## miséō

_(mis-eh'-o | mee-SEH-oh | mee-SAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a primary μῖσος (hatred); to detest (especially to persecute); by extension, to love less; hate(-ful); 

- to hate, pursue with hatred, detest
- to be hated, detested
