# G3720 ὀρθρινός

## orthrinós

_(or-thrin-os' | ore-three-NOSE | ore-three-NOSE)_

### Definition

from G3722; relating to the dawn, i.e., matutinal (as an epithet of Venus, especially brilliant in the early day); morning; 

- early
