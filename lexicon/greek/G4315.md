# G4315 προσάββατον

## prosábbaton

_(pros-ab'-bat-on | prose-AV-va-tone | prose-AV-va-tone)_

### Definition

from G4253 and G4521; a fore-sabbath, i.e., the Sabbath-eve; day before the sabbath; 

- the day before the sabbath
