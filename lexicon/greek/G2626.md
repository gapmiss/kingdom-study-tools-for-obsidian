# G2626 κατακλύζω

## kataklýzō

_(kat-ak-lood'-zo | ka-ta-KLOO-zoh | ka-ta-KLYOO-zoh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and the base of G2830; to dash (wash) down, i.e., (by implication) to deluge; overflow; 

- to overwhelm with water, to submerge, deluge
