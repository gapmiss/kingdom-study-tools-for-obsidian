# G1268 διανέμω

## dianémō

_(dee-an-em'-o | thee-ah-NEH-moh | thee-ah-NAY-moh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and the base of G3551; to distribute, i.e., (of information) to disseminate; spread; 

- to distribute, divide
