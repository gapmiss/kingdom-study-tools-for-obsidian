# G2336 θήκη

## thḗkē

_(thay'-kay | THAY-kay | THAY-kay)_

### Definition

from G5087; a receptacle, i.e., scabbard; sheath; 

- that which a thing is put or laid away, a receptacle, repository, chest, box
  - used of the sheath of a sword
