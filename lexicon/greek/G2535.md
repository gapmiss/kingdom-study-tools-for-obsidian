# G2535 Κάϊν

## Káïn

_(kah'-in | KA-een | KA-een)_

### Definition

maker, fabricator (literally smith); of Hebrew origin (H7014); Cain, (i.e., Cajin), the son of Adam; Cain; 

- the first born of Adam, and slew his brother, Abel
