# G4518 σαβαχθάνι

## sabachtháni

_(sab-akh-than-ee' | sa-vahk-THA-nee | sa-vahk-THA-nee)_

### Definition

of Chaldee or (H7662 with pronominal suffix); thou hast left me; sabachthani (i.e., shebakthani), a cry of distress; sabachthani; 

- thou hast forsaken me
