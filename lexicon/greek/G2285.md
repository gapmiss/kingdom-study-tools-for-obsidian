# G2285 θάμβος

## thámbos

_(tham'-bos | THAHM-vose | THAHM-vose)_

### Definition

akin to an obsolete τάφω (to dumbfound); stupefaction (by surprise), i.e., astonishment; X amazed, + astonished, wonder; 

- to render immovable
- amazement

### Verse(s)

[[Luke 4#36|Luke 4:36]], [[Luke 5#9|Luke 5:9]], [[Acts 3#10|Acts 3:10]]
