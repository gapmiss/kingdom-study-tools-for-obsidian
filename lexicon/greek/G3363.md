# G3363 ἵνα μή

## hína mḗ

_(hin'-ah may | HEE-na may | EE-na may)_

### Definition

i.e. G2443 and G3361; in order (or so) that not; albeit not, lest, that, no(-t, -thing); 

- lest, that ... not
