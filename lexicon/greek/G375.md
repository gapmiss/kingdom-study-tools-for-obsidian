# G375 ἀναπέμπω

## anapémpō

_(an-ap-em'-po | ah-na-PEM-poh | ah-na-PAME-poh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G3992; to send up or back; send (again); 

- to send up
  - to a higher place
  - to a person higher in office, authority, or power
- to send back
