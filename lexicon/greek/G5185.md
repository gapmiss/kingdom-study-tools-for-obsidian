# G5185 τυφλός

## typhlós

_(toof-los' | too-FLOSE | tyoo-FLOSE)_

### Definition

from, G5187; opaque (as if smoky), i.e., (by analogy) blind (physically or mentally); blind; 

- blind
- mentally blind
