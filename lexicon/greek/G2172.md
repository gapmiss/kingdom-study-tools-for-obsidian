# G2172 εὔχομαι

## eúchomai

_(yoo'-khom-ahee | EV-hoh-meh | AFE-hoh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice of a primary verb; to wish; by implication, to pray to God; pray, will, wish; 

- to pray to God
- to wish, to pray, to pray for
