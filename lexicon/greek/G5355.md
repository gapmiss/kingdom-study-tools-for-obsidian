# G5355 φθόνος

## phthónos

_(fthon'-os | FTHOH-nose | FTHOH-nose)_

### Definition

probably akin to the base of G5351; ill-will (as detraction), i.e., jealousy (spite); envy; 

- envy
- for envy, i.e., prompted by envy
