# G3684 ὀνικός

## onikós

_(on-ik-os' | oh-nee-KOSE | oh-nee-KOSE)_

### Definition

from G3688; belonging to a ass, i.e., large (so as to be turned by a ass); millstone; 

- of or for an ass, turned by an ass
  - these beasts were often employed to turn millstones
