# G553 ἀπεκδέχομαι

## apekdéchomai

_(ap-ek-dekh'-om-ahee | ah-pek-THEH-hoh-meh | ah-pake-THAY-hoh-may)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G1551; to expect fully; look (wait) for; 

- assiduously and patiently waiting for
