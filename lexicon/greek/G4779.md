# G4779 συγκαλέω

## synkaléō

_(soong-kal-eh'-o | soong-ka-LEH-oh | syoong-ka-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G2564; to convoke; call together; 

- to call together, assemble
- to call together to one's self
