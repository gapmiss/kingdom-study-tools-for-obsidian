# G1175 δεισιδαιμονία

## deisidaimonía

_(dice-ee-dahee-mon-ee'-ah | thee-see-theh-moh-NEE-ah | thee-see-thay-moh-NEE-ah)_

### Definition

from the same as G1174; religion; superstition; 

- in a good sense
  - reverencing god or the gods, pious, religious
- in a bad sense
  - superstitious
- religious
