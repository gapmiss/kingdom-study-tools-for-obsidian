# G3791 ὀχλέω

## ochléō

_(okh-leh'-o | oh-HLEH-oh | oh-HLAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G3793; to mob, i.e., (by implication) to harass; vex; 

- to excite a mob against one
- to disturb, roll away
- to trouble, molest
- be in confusion, in an uproar
- to be vexed, molested, troubled: by demons
