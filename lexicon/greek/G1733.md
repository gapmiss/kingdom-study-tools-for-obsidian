# G1733 ἕνδεκα

## héndeka

_(hen'-dek-ah | HEN-theh-ka | ANE-thay-ka)_

### Definition

from (the neuter of) G1520 and G1176; one and ten, i.e., eleven; eleven; 

- eleven
  - of the eleven disciples alive after the death of Judas
