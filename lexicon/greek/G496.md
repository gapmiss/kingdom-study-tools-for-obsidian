# G496 ἀντιπίπτω

## antipíptō

_(an-tee-pip'-to | an-tee-PEE-ptoh | an-tee-PEE-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G0473 and G4098 (including its alternate); to oppose; resist; 

- to fall upon, run against
- to be adverse, oppose, strive against
