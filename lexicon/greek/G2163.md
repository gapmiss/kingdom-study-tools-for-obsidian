# G2163 εὔφημος

## eúphēmos

_(yoo'-fay-mos | EV-fay-mose | AFE-fay-mose)_

### Definition

from G2095 and G5345; well spoken of, i.e., reputable; of good report; 

- sounding well
- uttering words of good omen, speaking auspiciously
