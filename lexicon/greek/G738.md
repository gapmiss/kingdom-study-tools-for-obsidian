# G738 ἀρτιγέννητος

## artigénnētos

_(ar-teeg-en'-nay-tos | ar-tee-GEN-nay-tose | ar-tee-GANE-nay-tose)_

### Definition

from G0737 and G1084; just born, i.e., (figuratively) a young convert; new born; 

- just born, newborn
