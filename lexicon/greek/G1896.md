# G1896 ἐπεῖδον

## epeîdon

_(ep-i'-don | ep-EE-thone | ape-EE-thone)_

### Definition

and other moods and persons of the same tense; from G1909 and G1492; to regard (favorably or otherwise); behold, look upon; 

- to look upon, to regard
