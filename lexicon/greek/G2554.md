# G2554 κακοποιέω

## kakopoiéō

_(kak-op-oy-eh'-o | ka-koh-poo-EH-oh | ka-koh-poo-A-oh)_

### Definition

from G2555; to be a bad-doer, i.e., (objectively) to injure, or (genitive) to sin; do(ing) evil; 

- to do harm
- to do evil, do wrong
