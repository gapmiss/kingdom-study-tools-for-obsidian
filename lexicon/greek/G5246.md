# G5246 ὑπέρογκος

## hypéronkos

_(hoop-er'-ong-kos | hoo-PER-ohng-kose | yoo-PARE-ohng-kose)_

### Definition

from G5228 and G3591; bulging over, i.e., (figuratively) insolent; great swelling; 

- overswollen
- metaphorically immoderate, extravagant
