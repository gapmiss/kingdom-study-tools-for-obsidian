# G4640 σκιρτάω

## skirtáō

_(skeer-tah'-o | skeer-TA-oh | skeer-TA-oh)_

### Definition

akin to σκαίρω (to skip); to jump, i.e., sympathetically move (as the quickening of a fetus); leap (for joy); 

- to leap
