# G5536 χρῆμα

## chrēma

_(khray'-mah | HRAY-ma | HRAY-ma)_

### Definition

something useful or needed, i.e., wealth, price; money, riches; 

- a thing, a matter, affair, event, business
  - specifically money, riches
