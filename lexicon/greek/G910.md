# G910 Βαπτιστής

## Baptistḗs

_(bap-tis-tace' | va-ptee-STASE | va-ptee-STASE)_

### Definition

from G0907; a baptizer, as an epithet of Christ's forerunner; Baptist; 

- a baptizer
- one who administers the rite of baptism
- the surname of John, the forerunner of Christ
