# G3905 παρατείνω

## parateínō

_(par-at-i'-no | pa-ra-TEE-noh | pa-ra-TEE-noh)_

### Definition

from G3844 and teino (to stretch); to extend along, i.e., prolong (in point of time); continue; 

- to extend beside, to stretch out lengthwise, to extend
- to prolong
