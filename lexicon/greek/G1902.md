# G1902 ἐπενδύομαι

## ependýomai

_(ep-en-doo'-om-ahee | ep-en-THOO-oh-meh | ape-ane-THYOO-oh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G1909 and G1746; to invest upon oneself; be clothed upon; 

- to put on over
