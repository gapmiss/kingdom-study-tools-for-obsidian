# G2275 ἥττημα

## hḗttēma

_(hayt'-tay-mah | HATE-tay-ma | ATE-tay-ma)_

### Definition

from G2274; a deterioration, i.e., (objectively) failure or (subjectively) loss; diminishing, fault; 

- a diminution, decrease: i.e., defeat
- loss, as respects to salvation
