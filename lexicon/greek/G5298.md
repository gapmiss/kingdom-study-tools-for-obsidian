# G5298 ὑποχωρέω

## hypochōréō

_(hoop-okh-o-reh'-o | hoo-poh-hoh-REH-oh | yoo-poh-hoh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G5259 and G5562; to vacate down, i.e., retire quietly; go aside, withdraw self; 

- to go back
- withdraw
