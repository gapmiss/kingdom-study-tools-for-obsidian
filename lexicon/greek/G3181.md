# G3181 μεθόριος

## methórios

_(meth-or'-ee-os | meh-THOH-ree-ose | may-THOH-ree-ose)_

### Definition

from G3326 and G3725; bounded alongside, i.e., contiguous (neuter plural as noun, frontier); border; 

- a border, frontier
