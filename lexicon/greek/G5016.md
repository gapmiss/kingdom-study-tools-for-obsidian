# G5016 ταραχή

## tarachḗ

_(tar-akh-ay' | ta-ra-HAY | ta-ra-HAY)_

### Definition

feminine from G5015; disturbance, i.e., (of water) roiling, or (of a mob) sedition; trouble(-ing); 

- disturbance, commotion
- metaphorically a tumult, sedition
