# G5544 χρηστότης

## chrēstótēs

_(khray-stot'-ace | hray-STOH-tase | hray-STOH-tase)_

### Definition

from G5543; usefulness, i.e., morally, excellence (in character or demeanor); gentleness, good(-ness), kindness; 

- moral goodness, integrity
- benignity, kindness
