# G30 ἀγγεῖον

## angeîon

_(ang-eye'-on | ang-GEE-one | ang-GEE-one)_

### Definition

from ἄγγος (a pail, perhaps as bent; compare the base of G0043); a receptacle; vessel; 

- a vessel, receptacle, a pail, a reservoir
