# G3935 παρίημι

## paríēmi

_(par-ee'-ay-mi | pa-REE-ay-mee | pa-REE-ay-mee)_

### Definition

from G3844 and hiemi (to send); to let by, i.e., relax; hang down; 

- to let pass
  - to pass by, neglect
  - to disregard, omit
- to relax, loose, let go
- relaxed, unstrung, weakened, exhausted
