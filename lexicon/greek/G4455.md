# G4455 πώποτε

## pṓpote

_(po'-pot-e | POH-poh-teh | POH-poh-tay)_

### Definition

from G4452 and G4218; at any time, i.e., (with negative particle) at no time; at any time, + never(...to any man), + yet, never man; 

- ever, at any time
