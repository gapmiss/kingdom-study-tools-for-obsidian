# G4177 πολίτης

## polítēs

_(pol-ee'-tace | poh-LEE-tase | poh-LEE-tase)_

### Definition

from G4172; a townsman; citizen; 

- a citizen
  - the inhabitant of any city or country
  - the association of another in citizenship
    - a fellow citizen, fellow countryman
