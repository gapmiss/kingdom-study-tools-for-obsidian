# G5289 ὑποστολή

## hypostolḗ

_(hoop-os-tol-ay' | hoo-poh-stoh-LAY | yoo-poh-stoh-LAY)_

### Definition

from G5288; shrinkage (timidity), i.e., (by implication) apostasy; draw back; 

- the timidity of one stealthily retreating
