# G338 ἀναίτιος

## anaítios

_(an-ah'-ee-tee-os | ah-NEH-tee-ose | ah-NAY-tee-ose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G0159 (in the sense of G0156); innocent; blameless, guiltless; 

- guiltless, innocent
