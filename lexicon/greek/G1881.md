# G1881 ἐπανίσταμαι

## epanístamai

_(ep-an-is'-tam-ahee | ep-ah-NEE-sta-meh | ape-ah-NEE-sta-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G1909 and G0450; to stand up on, i.e., (figuratively) to attack; rise up against; 

- to cause to rise up against, to raise up against
