# G5265 ὑποδέω

## hypodéō

_(hoop-od-eh'-o | hoo-poh-THEH-oh | yoo-poh-THAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G5259 and G1210; to bind under one's feet, i.e., put on shoes or sandals; bind on, (be) shod; 

- to underbind
- to bind under one's self, bind on
