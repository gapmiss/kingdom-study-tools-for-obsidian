# G2412 ἱεροπρεπής

## hieroprepḗs

_(hee-er-op-rep-ace' | hee-eh-roh-preh-PASE | ee-ay-roh-pray-PASE)_

### Definition

from G2413 and the same as G4241; reverent; as becometh holiness; 

- befitting men, places, actions or sacred things to God
- reverent
