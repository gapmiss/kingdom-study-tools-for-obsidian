# G1634 ἐκψύχω

## ekpsýchō

_(ek-psoo'-kho | ek-PSOO-hoh | ake-PSYOO-hoh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G5594; to expire; give (yield) up the ghost; 

- to expire, to breathe out one's life
