# G1781 ἐντέλλομαι

## entéllomai

_(en-tel'-lom-ahee | en-TEL-loh-meh | ane-TALE-loh-may)_

### Definition

from G1722 and the base of G5056; to enjoin; (give) charge, (give) command(-ments), injoin; 

- to order, command to be done, enjoin
