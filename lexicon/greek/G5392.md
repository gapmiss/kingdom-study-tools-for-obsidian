# G5392 φιμόω

## phimóō

_(fee-mo'-o | fee-MOH-oh | fee-MOH-oh)_

### Definition

from φιμός (a muzzle); to muzzle; muzzle; 

- to close the mouth with a muzzle, to muzzle
- metaphorically
  - to stop the mouth, make speechless, reduce to silence
  - to become speechless
- to be kept in check
