# G424 ἀνέρχομαι

## anérchomai

_(an-erkh'-om-ahee | ah-NER-hoh-meh | ah-NARE-hoh-may)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G2064; to ascend; go up; 

- to go up
- to go up to a higher place: to Jerusalem
