# G4117 πλέγμα

## plégma

_(pleg'-mah | PLEG-ma | PLAGE-ma)_

### Definition

from G4120; a plait (of hair); broidered hair; 

- what is woven, plaited, or twisted together
- a web, plait, braid
  - of a net
  - of a basket, in which the infant Moses was laid
  - of braided hair
