# G4569 Σαῦλος

## Saûlos

_(sow'-los | SEV-lose | SA-lose)_

### Definition

desired; of Hebrew origin, the same as G4549; Saulus (i.e., Shaul), the Jewish name of Paul; Saul; 

- the Jewish name of the apostle Paul
