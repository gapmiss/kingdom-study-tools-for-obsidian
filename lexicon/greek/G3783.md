# G3783 ὀφείλημα

## opheílēma

_(of-i'-lay-mah | oh-FEE-lay-ma | oh-FEE-lay-ma)_

### Definition

from (the alternate of) G3784; something owed, i.e., (figuratively) a due; morally, a fault; debt; 

- that which is owed
  - that which is justly or legally due, a debt
- metaphorically offence, sin
