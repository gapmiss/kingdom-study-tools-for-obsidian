# G3731 ὅρμημα

## hórmēma

_(hor'-may-mah | HORE-may-ma | ORE-may-ma)_

### Definition

from G3730; an attack, i.e., (abstractly) precipitancy; violence; 

- a rush, impulse
- that which is impelled or hurried away by impulse
