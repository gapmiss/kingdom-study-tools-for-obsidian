# G2295 θαῦμα

## thaûma

_(thos'-mah | THEV-ma | THA-ma)_

### Definition

apparently from a form of G2300; wonder (properly concrete; but by implication, abstract); admiration; 

- a wonderful thing, a marvel
- wonder
  - to wonder [with great wonder i.e.,] exceedingly
