# G2383 Ἰάειρος

## Iáeiros

_(ee-ah'-i-ros | ee-AH-ee-rose | ee-AH-ee-rose)_

### Definition

whom God enlightens; of Hebrew origin (H2971); Jairus (i.e., Jair), an Israelite; Jairus; 

- a ruler of a synagogue, probably near the western shore of the Sea of Galilee
