# G3912 παραφρονέω

## paraphronéō

_(par-af-ron-eh'-o | pa-ra-froh-NEH-oh | pa-ra-froh-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G5426; to misthink, i.e., be insane (silly); as a fool; 

- to be beside one's self, out of one's senses, void of understanding, insane
