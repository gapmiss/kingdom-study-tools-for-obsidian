# G2704 καταφθείρω

## kataphtheírō

_(kat-af-thi'-ro | ka-ta-FTHEE-roh | ka-ta-FTHEE-roh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G5351; to spoil entirely, i.e., (literally) to destroy; or (figuratively) to deprave; corrupt, utterly perish; 

- to corrupt, deprave
  - corrupted in mind
- to destroy
  - to be destroyed, to perish
