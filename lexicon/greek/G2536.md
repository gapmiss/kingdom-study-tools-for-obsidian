# G2536 Καϊνάν

## Kaïnán

_(kah-ee-nan' | ka-ee-NAHN | ka-ee-NAHN)_

### Definition

their smith; of Hebrew origin (H7018); Cainan (i.e., Kenan), the name of two patriarchs; Cainan; 

- the son of Enos, Genesis 5:9
- son of Arphaxad and ancestor of Christ, Luke 3:36
