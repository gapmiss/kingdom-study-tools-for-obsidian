# G2648 καταμανθάνω

## katamanthánō

_(kat-am-an-than'-o | ka-ta-mahn-THA-noh | ka-ta-mahn-THA-noh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G3129; to learn thoroughly, i.e., (by implication) to note carefully; consider; 

- to learn thoroughly, examine carefully
- to consider well
