# G5338 φέγγος

## phéngos

_(feng'-gos | FENG-gose | FAYNG-gose)_

### Definition

probably akin to the base of G5457 (compare G5350); brilliancy; light; 

- light
  - of the moon
  - of a candle or lamp
- the bright sunshine, the beam of light
