# G763 ἀσέβεια

## asébeia

_(as-eb'-i-ah | ah-SEH-vee-ah | ah-SAY-vee-ah)_

### Definition

from G0765; impiety, i.e., (by implication) wickedness; ungodly(-liness); 

- want of reverence towards God, impiety, ungodliness
