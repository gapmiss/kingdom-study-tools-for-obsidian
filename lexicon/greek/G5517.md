# G5517 χοϊκός

## choïkós

_(kho-ik-os' | hoh-ee-KOSE | hoh-ee-KOSE)_

### Definition

from G5522; dusty or dirty (soil-like), i.e., (by implication) terrene; earthy; 

- made of earth, earthy
