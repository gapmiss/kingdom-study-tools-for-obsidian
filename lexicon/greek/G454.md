# G454 ἄνοια

## ánoia

_(an'-oy-ah | AH-noo-ah | AH-noo-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of G0001 (as a negative particle) and G3563; stupidity; by implication, rage; folly, madness; 

- want of understanding, folly
- madness expressing itself in rage
