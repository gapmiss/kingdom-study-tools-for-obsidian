# G3371 μηκέτι

## mēkéti

_(may-ket'-ee | may-KEH-tee | may-KAY-tee)_

### Definition

from G3361 and G2089; no further; any longer, (not) henceforth, hereafter, no henceforward (longer, more, soon), not any more; 

- no longer, no more, not hereafter
