# G2253 ἡμιθανής

## hēmithanḗs

_(hay-mee-than-ace' | hay-mee-tha-NASE | ay-mee-tha-NASE)_

### Definition

from a presumed compound of the base of G2255 and G2348; half dead, i.e., entirely exhausted; half dead; 

- half dead
