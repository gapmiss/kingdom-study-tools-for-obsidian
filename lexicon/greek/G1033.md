# G1033 βρῶμα

## brōma

_(bro'-mah | VROH-ma | VROH-ma)_

### Definition

from the base of G0977; food (literally or figuratively), especially (ceremonially) articles allowed or forbidden by the Jewish law; meat, victuals; 

- that which is eaten, food
