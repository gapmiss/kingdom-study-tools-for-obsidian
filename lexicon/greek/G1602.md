# G1602 ἐκπλέω

## ekpléō

_(ek-pleh'-o | ek-PLEH-oh | ake-PLAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G4126; to depart by ship; sail (away, thence); 

- to sail from, sail away, depart by ship
