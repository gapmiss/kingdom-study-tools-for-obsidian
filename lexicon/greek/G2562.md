# G2562 καλάμη

## kalámē

_(kal-am'-ay | ka-LA-may | ka-LA-may)_

### Definition

feminine of G2563; a stalk of grain, i.e., (collectively) stubble; stubble; 

- a stalk of grain or a reed, stubble,
  - the stalk left after the ears are cut off
