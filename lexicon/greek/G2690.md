# G2690 καταστρέφω

## katastréphō

_(kat-as-tref'-o | ka-ta-STREH-foh | ka-ta-STRAY-foh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G4762; to turn upside down, i.e., upset; overthrow; 

- to turn over, turn under
  - the soil with a plough
- to overturn, overthrow, throw down
