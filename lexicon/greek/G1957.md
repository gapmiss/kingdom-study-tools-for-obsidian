# G1957 ἐπιμαρτυρέω

## epimartyréō

_(ep-ee-mar-too-reh'-o | eh-pee-mahr-too-REH-oh | ay-pee-mahr-tyoo-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G3140; to attest further, i.e., corroborate; testify; 

- to bear witness to, establish by testimony
