# G4563 σαρόω

## saróō

_(sar-o'-o | sa-ROH-oh | sa-ROH-oh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of σαίρω (to brush off; akin to G4951); meaning a broom; to sweep; sweep; 

- to sweep, clean by sweeping
