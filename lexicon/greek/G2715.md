# G2715 κατεξουσιάζω

## katexousiázō

_(kat-ex-oo-see-ad'-zo | ka-teh-ksoo-see-AH-zoh | ka-tay-ksoo-see-AH-zoh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G1850; to have (wield) full privilege over; exercise authority; 

- to exercise authority, wield power
