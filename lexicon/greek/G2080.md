# G2080 ἔσω

## ésō

_(es'-o | EH-soh | A-soh)_

### Definition

from G1519; inside (as preposition or adjective); (with-)in(-ner, -to, -ward); 

- to within, into
- within
  - the internal inner man
  - the soul, conscience
