# G4629 σκέπασμα

## sképasma

_(skep'-as-mah | SKEH-pa-sma | SKAY-pa-sma)_

### Definition

from a derivative of σκέπας (a covering; perhaps akin to the base of G4649 through the idea of noticeableness); clothing; raiment; 

- a covering, spec. clothing
