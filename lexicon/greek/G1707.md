# G1707 ἐμπλέκω

## emplékō

_(em-plek'-o | em-PLEH-koh | ame-PLAY-koh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G4120; to entwine, i.e., (figuratively) involve with; entangle (in, self with); 

- of a thing: to entangle, involve in
