# G3470 μῶμος

## mōmos

_(mo'-mos | MOH-mose | MOH-mose)_

### Definition

perhaps from G3201; a flaw or blot, i.e., (figuratively) disgraceful person; blemish; 

- blemish, blot, disgrace
  - censure
  - insult
    - of men who are a disgrace to society
