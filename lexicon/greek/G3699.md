# G3699 ὅπου

## hópou

_(hop'-oo | HOH-poo | OH-poo)_

### Definition

from G3739 and G4225; what(-ever) where, i.e., at whichever spot; in what place, where(-as, -soever), whither, + whithersoever; 

- where, whereas
