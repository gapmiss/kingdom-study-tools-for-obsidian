# G1686 ἐμβάπτω

## embáptō

_(em-bap'-to | em-VA-ptoh | ame-VA-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G0911; to whelm on, i.e., wet (a part of the person, etc.) by contact with a fluid; dip; 

- to dip in
