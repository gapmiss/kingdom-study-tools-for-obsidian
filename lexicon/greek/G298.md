# G298 ἀμώμητος

## amṓmētos

_(am-o'-may-tos | ah-MOH-may-tose | ah-MOH-may-tose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G3469; unblamable; blameless; 

- that cannot be censured, blameless
