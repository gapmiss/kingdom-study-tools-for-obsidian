# G4115 πλατύνω

## platýnō

_(plat-oo'-no | pla-TOO-noh | pla-TYOO-noh)_

### Definition

from G4116; to widen (literally or figuratively); make broad, enlarge; 

- to make broad, enlarge
- be enlarged in heart, i.e., to welcome and embrace you in love
