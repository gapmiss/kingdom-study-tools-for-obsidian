# G4035 περιλείπω

## perileípō

_(per-ee-li'-po | peh-ree-LEE-poh | pay-ree-LEE-poh)_

### Definition

from G4012 and G3007; to leave all around, i.e., (passively) survive; remain; 

- to leave over
- to remain over, to survive
