# G5364 φιλανθρώπως

## philanthrṓpōs

_(fil-an-thro'-poce | feel-an-THROH-pose | feel-an-THROH-pose)_

### Definition

adverb from a compound of G5384 and G0444; fondly to man ("philanthropically"), i.e., humanely; courteously; 

- humanely, kindly
