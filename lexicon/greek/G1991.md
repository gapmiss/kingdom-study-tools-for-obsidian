# G1991 ἐπιστηρίζω

## epistērízō

_(ep-ee-stay-rid'-zo | eh-pee-stay-REE-zoh | ay-pee-stay-REE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G4741; to support further, i.e., reestablish; confirm, strengthen; 

- to establish besides, strengthen more
- to render more firm, confirm
