# G324 ἀναδέχομαι

## anadéchomai

_(an-ad-ekh'-om-ahee | ah-na-THEH-hoh-meh | ah-na-THAY-hoh-may)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G1209; to entertain (as a guest); receive; 

- to take up, take upon's self, undertake, assume
- to receive, entertain anyone hospitably
