# G1143 δάκνω

## dáknō

_(dak'-no | THA-knoh | THA-knoh)_

### Definition

a prolonged form of a primary root; to bite, i.e., (figuratively) thwart; bite; 

- to bite with the teeth
- metaphorically, to wound the soul, cut lacerate, rend with reproaches
