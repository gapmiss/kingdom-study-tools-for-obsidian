# G2699 κατατομή

## katatomḗ

_(kat-at-om-ay' | ka-ta-toh-MAY | ka-ta-toh-MAY)_

### Definition

from a compound of G2596 and τέμνω (to cut); a cutting down (off), i.e., mutilation (ironically); concision; 

- to cut up, mutilation

### Note

Compare G0609.
