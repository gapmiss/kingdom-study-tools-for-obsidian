# G4860 συμψηφίζω

## sympsēphízō

_(soom-psay-fid'-zo | soom-psay-FEE-zoh | syoom-psay-FEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G5585; to compute jointly; reckon; 

- to compute, count up
- to vote with one
