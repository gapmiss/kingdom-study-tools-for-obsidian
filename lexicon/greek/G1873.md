# G1873 ἐπακούω

## epakoúō

_(ep-ak-oo'-o | ep-ah-KOO-oh | ape-ah-KOO-oh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G0191; to hearken (favorably) to; hear; 

- to give ear to, listen to
  - to perceive by the ear
- to listen to
  - to hear with favor, grant one's request
