# G851 ἀφαιρέω

## aphairéō

_(af-ahee-reh'-o | ah-feh-REH-oh | ah-fay-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G0138; to remove (literally or figuratively); cut (smite) off, take away; 

- to take from, take away, remove, carry off
- to cut off
