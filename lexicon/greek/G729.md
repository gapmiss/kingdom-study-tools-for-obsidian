# G729 ἄῤῥαφος

## árrhaphos

_(ar'-hhraf-os | AR-ra-fose | AR-ra-fose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of the same as G4476; unsewed, i.e., of a single piece; without seam; 

- not sewed together, without a seam
