## probatikós

_(prob-at-ik-os' | proh-va-tee-KOSE | proh-va-tee-KOSE)_

### Definition

from G4263; relating to sheep, i.e., (a gate) through which they were led into Jerusalem; sheep (market); 

- pertaining to sheep
- the sheep gate
