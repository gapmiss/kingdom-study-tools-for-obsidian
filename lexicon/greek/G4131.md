# G4131 πλήκτης

## plḗktēs

_(plake'-tace | PLAKE-tase | PLAKE-tase)_

### Definition

from G4141; a smiter, i.e., pugnacious (quarrelsome); striker; 

- bruiser, ready for a blow
- a pugnacious, contentious, quarrelsome person
