# G1789 ἐντρέφω

## entréphō

_(en-tref'-o | en-TREH-foh | ane-TRAY-foh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G5142; (figuratively) to educate; nourish up in; 

- to nourish in: a person or a thing
- metaphorically to educate, form the mind
