# G4391 προϋπάρχω

## proÿpárchō

_(pro-oop-ar'-kho | proh-oo-PAHR-hoh | proh-yoo-PAHR-hoh)_

### Definition

from G4253 and G5225; to exist before, i.e., (adverbially) to be or do something previously; + be before(-time); 

- to be before, exist previously
