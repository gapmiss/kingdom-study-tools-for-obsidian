# G1610 ἐκριζόω

## ekrizóō

_(ek-rid-zo'-o | ek-ree-ZOH-oh | ake-ree-ZOH-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G4492; to uproot; pluck up by the root, root up; 

- to root out, pluck up by the roots
