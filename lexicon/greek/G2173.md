# G2173 εὔχρηστος

## eúchrēstos

_(yoo'-khrays-tos | EV-hray-stose | AFE-hray-stose)_

### Definition

from G2095 and G5543; easily used, i.e., useful; profitable, meet for use; 

- easy to make use of, useful
