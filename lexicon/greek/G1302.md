# G1302 διατί

## diatí

_(dee-at-ee' | thee-ah-TEE | thee-ah-TEE)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G5101; through what cause ?, i.e., why?; wherefore, why; 

- through, by, with, because of, for the sake of
