# G5285 ὑποπνέω

## hypopnéō

_(hoop-op-neh'-o | hoo-poh-PNEH-oh | yoo-poh-PNAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G5259 and G4154; to breathe gently, i.e., breeze; blow softly; 

- to blow underneath
- to blow softly
