# G2760 κεντυρίων

## kentyríōn

_(ken-too-ree'-ohn | ken-too-REE-one | kane-tyoo-REE-one)_

### Definition

of Latin origin; a centurion, i.e., captain of one hundred soldiers; centurion; 

- centurion, an officer in the Roman army
