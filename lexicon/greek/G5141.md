# G5141 τρέμω

## trémō

_(trem'-o | TREH-moh | TRAY-moh)_

### Definition

strengthened from a primary τρέω (to "dread", "terrify"); to "tremble" or fear; be afraid, trembling; 

- tremble
- to fear, be afraid

### Verse(s)

[[Mark 5#33|Mark 5:33]], [[Luke 8#47|Luke 8:47]], [[2 Peter 2#10|2 Peter 2:10]]
