# G183 ἀκατάσχετος

## akatáschetos

_(ak-at-as'-khet-os | ah-ka-TA-skeh-tose | ah-ka-TA-skay-tose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G2722; unrestrainable; unruly; 

- that can not be restrained
