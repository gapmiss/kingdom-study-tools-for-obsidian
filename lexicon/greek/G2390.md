# G2390 ἰάομαι

## iáomai

_(ee-ah'-om-ahee | ee-AH-oh-meh | ee-AH-oh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice of apparently a primary verb; to cure (literally or figuratively); heal, make whole; 

- to cure, heal
- to make whole
  - to free from errors and sins, to bring about (one's) salvation
