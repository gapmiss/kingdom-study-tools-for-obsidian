# G2489 Ἰωάννα

## Iōánna

_(ee-o-an'-nah | ee-oh-AN-na | ee-oh-AN-na)_

### Definition

Jehovah is a gracious giver; feminine of the same as G2491; Joanna, a Christian; Joanna; 

- the wife of Chuza, Herod's steward, and a follower of Jesus
