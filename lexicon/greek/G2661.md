## kataxióō

_(kat-ax-ee-o'-o | ka-ta-ksee-OH-oh | ka-ta-ksee-OH-oh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G0515; to deem entirely deserving; (ac-)count worthy; 

- to account worthy, judge worthy
