# G4888 συνδοξάζω

## syndoxázō

_(soon-dox-ad'-zo | soon-thoh-KSA-zoh | syoon-thoh-KSA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G1392; to exalt to dignity in company (i.e., similarly) with; glorify together; 

- to approve together, to join in approving
- to glorify together
