# G1258 διάλεκτος

## diálektos

_(dee-al'-ek-tos | thee-AH-lek-tose | thee-AH-lake-tose)_

### Definition

from G1256; a (mode of) discourse, i.e., "dialect"; language, tongue; 

- conversation, speech, discourse, language
- the tongue or language peculiar to any people
