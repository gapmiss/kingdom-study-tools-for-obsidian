# G1257 διαλείπω

## dialeípō

_(dee-al-i'-po | thee-ah-LEE-poh | thee-ah-LEE-poh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G3007; to leave off in the middle, i.e., intermit; cease; 

- to interpose a delay, to intermit, leave off for a time something already begun
