# G159 αἴτιος

## aítios

_(ah'-ee-tee-os | EH-tee-ose | A-tee-ose)_

### Definition

from the same as G0154; causative, i.e., (concretely) a causer; author; 

- that which is the cause of anything resides, causative, causing
  - the author
    - of a cause
    - of crime or offence
