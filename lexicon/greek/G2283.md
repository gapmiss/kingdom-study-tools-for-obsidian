# G2283 Θάμαρ

## Thámar

_(tham'-ar | THA-mahr | THA-mahr)_

### Definition

palm tree; of Hebrew origin (H8559); Thamar (i.e., Tamar), an Israelitess; Thamar; 

- Thamar (or Tamar), the wife of the two sons of Judah, Er and Onan
