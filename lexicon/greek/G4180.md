## polylogía

_(pol-oo-log-ee'-ah | poh-loo-loh-GEE-ah | poh-lyoo-loh-GEE-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of G4183 and G3056; loquacity, i.e., prolixity; much speaking; 

- much speaking
