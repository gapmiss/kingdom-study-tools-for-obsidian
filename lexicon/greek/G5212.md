# G5212 ὑμέτερος

## hyméteros

_(hoo-met'-er-os | hoo-MEH-teh-rose | yoo-MAY-tay-rose)_

### Definition

from G5210; yours, i.e., pertaining to you; your (own); 

- you, yours
  - to be possessed by you
  - to be allocated by you
  - proceeding from you
