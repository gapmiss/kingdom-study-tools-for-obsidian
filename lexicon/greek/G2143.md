# G2143 εὐπρέπεια

## euprépeia

_(yoo-prep'-i-ah | ev-PREH-pee-ah | afe-PRAY-pee-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of G2095 and G4241; good suitableness, i.e., gracefulness; grace; 

- goodly appearance, shapeliness, beauty, comeliness
