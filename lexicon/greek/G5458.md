# G5458 φωστήρ

## phōstḗr

_(foce-tare' | fose-TARE | fose-TARE)_

### Definition

from G5457; an illuminator, i.e., (concretely) a luminary, or (abstractly) brilliancy; light; 

- that which gives light, an illuminator
  - of the stars, sun and moon
- light, brightness
