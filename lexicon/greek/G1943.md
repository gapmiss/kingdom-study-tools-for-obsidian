# G1943 ἐπικαλύπτω

## epikalýptō

_(ep-ee-kal-oop'-to | eh-pee-ka-LOO-ptoh | ay-pee-ka-LYOO-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G2572; to conceal, i.e., (figuratively) forgive; cover; 

- to cover over
