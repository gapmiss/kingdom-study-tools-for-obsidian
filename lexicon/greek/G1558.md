# G1558 ἔκδικος

## ékdikos

_(ek'-dik-os | EK-thee-kose | AKE-thee-kose)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G1349; carrying justice out, i.e., a punisher; a (re-)venger; 

- without law and justice
- exacting penalty from one
  - an avenger, punisher
