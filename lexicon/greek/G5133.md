# G5133 τραπεζίτης

## trapezítēs

_(trap-ed-zee'-tace | tra-peh-ZEE-tase | tra-pay-ZEE-tase)_

### Definition

from G5132; a money-broker or banker; exchanger; 

- a money changer, broker, banker
- one who exchanges money for a fee, and pays interest on deposits
