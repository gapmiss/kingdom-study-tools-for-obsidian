# G5327 φάραγξ

## pháranx

_(far'-anx | FA-rahng-ks | FA-rahng-ks)_

### Definition

properly, strengthened from the base of G4008 or rather of G4486; a gap or chasm, i.e., ravine (winter-torrent); valley; 

- a valley shut off by cliffs and precipices
- a ravine
