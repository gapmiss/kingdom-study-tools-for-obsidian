# G4112 πλαστός

## plastós

_(plas-tos' | pla-STOSE | pla-STOSE)_

### Definition

from G4111; moulded, i.e., (by implication) artificial or (figuratively) fictitious (false); feigned; 

- moulded, formed, as from clay, wax, or stone
- feigned
