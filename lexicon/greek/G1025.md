# G1025 βρέφος

## bréphos

_(bref'-os | VREH-fose | VRAY-fose)_

### Definition

of uncertain affinity; an infant (properly, unborn) literally or figuratively; babe, (young) child, infant; 

- an unborn child, embryo, a foetus
- a new-born child, an infant, a babe
