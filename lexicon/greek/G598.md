# G598 ἀποθλίβω

## apothlíbō

_(ap-oth-lee'-bo | ah-poh-THLEE-voh | ah-poh-THLEE-voh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G2346; to crowd (from every side); press; 

- to press on all sides, squeeze, press hard
  - of pressing out grapes and olives
