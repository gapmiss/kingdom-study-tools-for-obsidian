# G641 ἀποῤῥίπτω

## aporrhíptō

_(ap-or-hrip'-to | ah-pore-REE-ptoh | ah-pore-REE-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G4496; to hurl off, i.e., precipitate (oneself); cast; 

- to throw away, cast down
- to cast one's self down
