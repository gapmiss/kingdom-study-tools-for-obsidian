# G2739 καυματίζω

## kaumatízō

_(kow-mat-id'-zo | kev-ma-TEE-zoh | ka-ma-TEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G2738; to burn; scorch; 

- to burn with heat, to scorch
- to be tortured with intense heat
