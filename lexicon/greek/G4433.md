# G4433 πτωχεύω

## ptōcheúō

_(pto-khyoo'-o | ptoh-HEV-oh | ptoh-HAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from G4434; to be a beggar, i.e., (by implication) to become indigent (figuratively); become poor; 

- to be a beggar, to beg, to be poor
