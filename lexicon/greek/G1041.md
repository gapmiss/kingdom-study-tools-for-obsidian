# G1041 βωμός

## bōmós

_(bo'-mos | voh-MOSE | voh-MOSE)_

### Definition

from the base of G0939; properly, a stand, i.e., (specially) an altar; altar; 

- an elevated place
  - very often a raised place on which to offer a sacrifice, an altar
