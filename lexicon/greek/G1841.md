# G1841 ἔξοδος

## éxodos

_(ex'-od-os | EKS-oh-those | AYKS-oh-those)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G3598; an exit, i.e., (figuratively) death; decease, departing; 

- exit i.e., departure
- the close of one's career, one's final fate
- departure from life, decease
