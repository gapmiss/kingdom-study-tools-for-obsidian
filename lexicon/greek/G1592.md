# G1592 ἐκμυκτηρίζω

## ekmyktērízō

_(ek-mook-ter-id'-zo | ek-mook-tay-REE-zoh | ake-myook-tay-REE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G3456; to sneer outright at; deride; 

- to deride by turning up the nose, to sneer at, to scoff at
