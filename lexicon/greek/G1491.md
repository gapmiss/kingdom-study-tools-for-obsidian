# G1491 εἶδος

## eîdos

_(i'-dos | EE-those | EE-those)_

### Definition

from G1492; a view, i.e., form (literally or figuratively); appearance, fashion, shape, sight; 

- the external or outward appearance, form figure, shape
- form, kind
