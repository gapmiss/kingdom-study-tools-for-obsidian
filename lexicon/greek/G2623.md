# G2623 κατακλείω

## katakleíō

_(kat-ak-li'-o | ka-ta-KLEE-oh | ka-ta-KLEE-oh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G2808; to shut down (in a dungeon), i.e., incarcerate; shut up; 

- to shut up, confine
