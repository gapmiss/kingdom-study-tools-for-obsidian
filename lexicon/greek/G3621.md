# G3621 οἰκονομέω

## oikonoméō

_(oy-kon-om-eh'-o | oo-koh-noh-MEH-oh | oo-koh-noh-MAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G3623; to manage (a house, i.e., an estate); be steward; 

- to be a steward
- to manage the affairs of a household
- to manage, dispense, order, regulate
