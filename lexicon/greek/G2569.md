# G2569 καλοποιέω

## kalopoiéō

_(kal-op-oy-eh'-o | ka-loh-poo-EH-oh | ka-loh-poo-A-oh)_

### Definition

from G2570 and G4160; to do well, i.e., live virtuously; well doing; 

- to do well, act uprightly
