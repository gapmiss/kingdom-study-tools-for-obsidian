# G1151 δάμαλις

## dámalis

_(dam'-al-is | THA-ma-lees | THA-ma-lees)_

### Definition

probably from the base of G1150; a heifer (as tame); heifer; 

- a young cow, heifer
  - of the red heifer with whose ashes, by the Mosaic law, those were to be sprinkled who had become defiled
