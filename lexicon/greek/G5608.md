# G5608 ὠνέομαι

## ōnéomai

_(o-neh'-om-ahee | oh-NEH-oh-meh | oh-NAY-oh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from an apparently primary ὦνος (a sum or price); to purchase (synonymous with the earlier G4092); buy; 

- to buy
