# G345 ἀνακεῖμαι

## anakeîmai

_(an-ak-i'-mahee | ah-na-KEE-meh | ah-na-KEE-may)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G2749; to recline (as a corpse or at a meal); guest, lean, lie, sit (down, at meat), at the table; 

- to lie at a table, eat together, dine
