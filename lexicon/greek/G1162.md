# G1162 δέησις

## déēsis

_(deh'-ay-sis | THEH-ay-sees | THAY-ay-sees)_

### Definition

from G1189; a petition; prayer, request, supplication; 

- need, indigence, want, privation, penury
- a seeking, asking, entreating, entreaty to God or to man
