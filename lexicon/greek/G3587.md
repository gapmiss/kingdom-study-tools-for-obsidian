# G3587 ξυράω

## xyráō

_(xoo-rah'-o | ksoo-RA-oh | ksyoo-RA-oh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of the same as G3586 (meaning a razor); to shave or "shear" the hair; shave; 

- to shear, shave
- to get one's self shaved
