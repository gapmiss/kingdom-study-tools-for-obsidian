# G5565 χωρίς

## chōrís

_(kho-rece' | hoh-REES | hoh-REES)_

### Definition

adverb from G5561; at a space, i.e., separately or apart from (often as preposition); beside, by itself, without; 

- separate, apart
  - without any
  - besides
