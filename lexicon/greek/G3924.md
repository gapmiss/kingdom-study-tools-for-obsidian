# G3924 παρεκτός

## parektós

_(par-ek-tos' | pa-rek-TOSE | pa-rake-TOSE)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G1622; near outside, i.e., besides; except, saving, without; 

- except, with the exception of (a thing)
- besides
