# G2174 εὐψυχέω

## eupsychéō

_(yoo-psoo-kheh'-o | ev-psoo-HEH-oh | afe-psyoo-HAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G2095 and G5590; to be in good spirits, i.e., feel encouraged; be of good comfort; 

- to be of good courage, to be of a cheerful spirit
