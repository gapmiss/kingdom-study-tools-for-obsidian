# G3600 ὀδυνάω

## odynáō

_(od-oo-nah'-o | oh-thoo-NA-oh | oh-thyoo-NA-oh)_

### Definition

from G3601; to grieve; sorrow, torment; 

- to cause intense pain
- to be in anguish, be tormented
- to torment or distress one's self
