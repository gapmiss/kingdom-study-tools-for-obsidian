# G1324 Δίδυμος

## Dídymos

_(did'-oo-mos | THEE-thoo-mose | THEE-thyoo-mose)_

### Definition

two fold, twain; prolongation from G1364; double, i.e., twin; Didymus, a Christian; Didymus; 

- a surname for the apostle Thomas
