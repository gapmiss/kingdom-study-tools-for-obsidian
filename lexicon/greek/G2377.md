# G2377 θυρωρός

## thyrōrós

_(thoo-ro-ros' | thoo-roh-ROSE | thyoo-roh-ROSE)_

### Definition

from G2374 and οὖρος (a watcher); a gate- warden; that kept the door, porter; 

- a doorkeeper, porter
  - a male or female janitor
