# G2213 ζήτημα

## zḗtēma

_(dzay'-tay-mah | ZAY-tay-ma | ZAY-tay-ma)_

### Definition

from G2212; a search (properly concretely), i.e., (in words) a debate; question; 

- a question, debate
  - about the law
