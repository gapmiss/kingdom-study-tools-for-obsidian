# G638 ἀποπνίγω

## apopnígō

_(ap-op-nee'-go | ah-poh-PNEE-goh | ah-poh-PNEE-goh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G4155; to stifle (by drowning or overgrowth); choke; 

- to choke
  - to suffocate with water, drown
