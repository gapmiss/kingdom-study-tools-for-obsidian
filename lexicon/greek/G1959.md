# G1959 ἐπιμελέομαι

## epimeléomai

_(ep-ee-mel-eh'-om-ahee | eh-pee-meh-LEH-oh-meh | ay-pee-may-LAY-oh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G1909 and the same as G3199; to care for (physically or otherwise); take care of; 

- to take care of a person or thing
