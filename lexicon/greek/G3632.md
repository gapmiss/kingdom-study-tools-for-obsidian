# G3632 οἰνοφλυγία

## oinophlygía

_(oy-nof-loog-ee'-ah | oo-noh-floo-GEE-ah | oo-noh-flyoo-GEE-ah)_

### Definition

from G3631 and a form of the base of G5397; an overflow (or surplus) of wine, i.e., vinolency (drunkenness); excess of wine; 

- drunkenness
