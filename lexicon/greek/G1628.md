# G1628 ἐκφεύγω

## ekpheúgō

_(ek-fyoo'-go | ek-FEV-goh | ake-FAVE-goh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G5343; to flee out; escape, flee; 

- to flee out of, flee away
  - to seek safety in flight
  - to escape
