## Boóz

_(bo-oz' | voh-OZE | voh-OZE)_

### Definition

in him is strength; of Hebrew origin (H1162); Booz, (i.e., Boaz), an Israelite; Booz; 

- a kinsman of Ruth, afterwards her second husband
