## athanasía

_(ath-an-as-ee'-ah | ah-tha-na-SEE-ah | ah-tha-na-SEE-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of G0001 (as a negative particle) and G2288; deathlessness; immortality; 

- undying, immortality, everlasting
