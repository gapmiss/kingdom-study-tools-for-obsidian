# G3510 νεφρός

## nephrós

_(nef-ros' | neh-FROSE | nay-FROSE)_

### Definition

of uncertain affinity; a kidney (plural), i.e., (figuratively) the inmost mind; reins; 

- a kidney
- kidneys, loins
  - used of the inmost thoughts, feelings, purposes, of the soul
