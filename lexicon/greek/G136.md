# G136 αἶνος

## aînos

_(ah'-ee-nos | EH-nose | A-nose)_

### Definition

apparently a prime word; properly, a story, but used in the sense of G1868; praise (of God); praise; 

- a saying, proverb
- praise, laudatory discourse
