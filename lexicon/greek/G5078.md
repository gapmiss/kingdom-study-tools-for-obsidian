# G5078 τέχνη

## téchnē

_(tekh'-nay | TEH-hnay | TAY-hnay)_

### Definition

from the base of G5088; art (as productive), i.e., (specially), a trade, or (generally) skill; art, craft, occupation; 

- of the plastic art
- of a trade
