# G310 ἀναβοάω

## anaboáō

_(an-ab-o-ah'-o | ah-na-voh-AH-oh | ah-na-voh-AH-oh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and G0994; to halloo; cry (aloud, out); 

- to raise a cry, to cry out
