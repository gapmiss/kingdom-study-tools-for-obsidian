# G1462 ἔγκλημα

## énklēma

_(eng'-klay-mah | ENG-klay-ma | AYNG-klay-ma)_

### Definition

from G1458; an accusation, i.e., offence alleged; crime laid against, laid to charge; 

- accusation: the crime of which one is accused
