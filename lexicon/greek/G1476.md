# G1476 ἑδραῖος

## hedraîos

_(hed-rah'-yos | heh-THREH-ose | ay-THRAY-ose)_

### Definition

from a derivative of ἕζομαι (to sit); sedentary, i.e., (by implication) immovable; settled, stedfast; 

- sitting, sedentary
- firm, immovable, steadfast
