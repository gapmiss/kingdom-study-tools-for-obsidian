# G1309 διαφεύγω

## diapheúgō

_(dee-af-yoo'-go | thee-ah-FEV-goh | thee-ah-FAVE-goh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G5343; to flee through, i.e., escape; escape; 

- to flee through danger, to escape
