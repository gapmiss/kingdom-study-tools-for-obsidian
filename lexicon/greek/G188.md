# G188 ἀκμήν

## akmḗn

_(ak-mane' | ak-MANE | ak-MANE)_

### Definition

accusative case of a noun ("acme") akin to ἀκή (a point) and meaning the same; adverbially, just now, i.e., still; yet; 

- a point
- extremity, climax, acme, highest degree
- the present time
