# G558 ἀπελεύθερος

## apeleútheros

_(ap-el-yoo'-ther-os | ah-peh-LEV-theh-rose | ah-pay-LAYF-thay-rose)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G1658; one freed away, i.e., a freedman; freeman; 

- a slave that has been released from servitude, a freeman
