# G4986 Σώπατρος

## Sṓpatros

_(so'-pat-ros | SOH-pa-trose | SOH-pa-trose)_

### Definition

saviour of his father; from the base of G4982 and G3962; of a safe father; Sopatrus, a Christian; Sopater; 

- the son of Pyrhus of Berea, a companion of Paul

### Note

Compare G4989.
