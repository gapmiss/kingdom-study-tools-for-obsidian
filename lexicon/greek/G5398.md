# G5398 φοβερός

## phoberós

_(fob-er-os' | foh-veh-ROSE | foh-vay-ROSE)_

### Definition

from G5401; frightful, i.e., (objectively) formidable; fearful, terrible; 

- inspiring fear, terrible, formidable
- affected with fear, timid
