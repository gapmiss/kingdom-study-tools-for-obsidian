# G5366 φιλάργυρος

## philárgyros

_(fil-ar'-goo-ros | feel-AR-goo-rose | feel-AR-gyoo-rose)_

### Definition

from G5384 and G0696; fond of silver (money), i.e., avaricious; covetous; 

- loving money, avarice
