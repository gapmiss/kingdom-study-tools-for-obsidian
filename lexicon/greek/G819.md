# G819 ἀτιμία

## atimía

_(at-ee-mee'-ah | ah-tee-MEE-ah | ah-tee-MEE-ah)_

### Definition

from G0820; infamy, i.e., (subjectively) comparative indignity, (objectively) disgrace; dishonour, reproach, shame, vile; 

- dishonor, ignominy, disgrace
