# G476 ἀντίδικος

## antídikos

_(an-tid'-ee-kos | an-TEE-thee-kose | an-TEE-thee-kose)_

### Definition

from G0473 and G1349; an opponent (in a lawsuit); specially, Satan (as the arch-enemy); adversary; 

- opponent
  - an opponent in a suit of law
  - an adversary, enemy
