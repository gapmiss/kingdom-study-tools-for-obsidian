# G3121 Μαλελεήλ

## Maleleḗl

_(mal-el-eh-ale' | ma-leh-leh-ALE | ma-lay-lay-ALE)_

### Definition

praise of God; of Hebrew origin (H4111); Maleleel (i.e., Mahalalel), an antediluvian; Maleleel; 

- the fourth in descent from Adam, son of Cainan
