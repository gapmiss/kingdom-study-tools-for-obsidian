# G3733 ὄρνις

## órnis

_(or'-nis | ORE-nees | ORE-nees)_

### Definition

probably from a prolonged form of the base of G3735; a bird (as rising in the air), i.e., (specially), a hen (or female domestic fowl); hen; 

- a bird
- a cock, a hen
