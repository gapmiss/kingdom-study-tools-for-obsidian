# G3704 ὅπως

## hópōs

_(hop'-oce | HOH-pose | OH-pose)_

### Definition

from G3739 and G4459; what(-ever) how, i.e., in the manner that (as adverb or conjunction of coincidence, intentional or actual); because, how, (so) that, to, when; 

- how, that
