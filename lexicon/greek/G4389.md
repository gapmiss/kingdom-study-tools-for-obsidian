# G4389 προτρέπομαι

## protrépomai

_(prot-rep'-om-ahee | proh-TREH-poh-meh | proh-TRAY-poh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G4253 and the base of G5157; to turn forward for oneself, i.e., encourage; exhort; 

- to urge forwards, exhort, encourage
