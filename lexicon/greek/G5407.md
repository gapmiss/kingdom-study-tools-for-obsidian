# G5407 φονεύω

## phoneúō

_(fon-yoo'-o | foh-NEV-oh | foh-NAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from G5406; to be a murderer (of); kill, do murder, slay; 

- to kill, slay, murder
- to commit murder
