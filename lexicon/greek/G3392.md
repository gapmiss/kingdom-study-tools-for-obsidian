# G3392 μιαίνω

## miaínō

_(me-ah'-ee-no | mee-EH-noh | mee-A-noh)_

### Definition

perhaps a primary verb; to sully or taint, i.e., contaminate (ceremonially or morally); defile; 

- to dye with another color, to stain
- to defile, pollute, sully, contaminate, soil
  - to defile with sins
