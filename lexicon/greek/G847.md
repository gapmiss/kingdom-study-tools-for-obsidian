# G847 αὐτοῦ

## autoû

_(ow-too' | av-TOO | af-TOO)_

### Definition

genitive (i.e. possessive) of G0846, used as an adverb of location; properly, belonging to the same spot, i.e., in this (or that) place; (t-)here; 

- in that place, there, here
