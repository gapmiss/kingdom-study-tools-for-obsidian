# G1507 εἱλίσσω

## heilíssō

_(hi-lis'-so | hee-LEES-soh | ee-LEES-soh)_

### Definition

a prolonged form of a primary but defective verb εἵλω (of the same meaning); to coil or wrap; roll together; 

- to roll up or together
