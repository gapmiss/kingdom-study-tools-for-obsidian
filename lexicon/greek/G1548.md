# G1548 ἐκγαμίσκω

## ekgamískō

_(ek-gam-is'-ko | ek-ga-MEE-skoh | ake-ga-MEE-skoh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G1061; the same as 1547; give in marriage; 

- to give away in marriage: a daughter
- to marry, to be given in marriage
