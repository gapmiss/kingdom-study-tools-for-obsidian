# G4696 σπίλος

## spílos

_(spee'-los | SPEE-lose | SPEE-lose)_

### Definition

of uncertain derivation; a stain or blemish, i.e., (figuratively) defect, disgrace; spot; 

- a spot
- a fault, moral blemish
  - of base and gluttonous men
