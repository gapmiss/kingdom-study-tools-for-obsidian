# G872 ἀφοράω

## aphoráō

_(af-or-ah'-o | ah-foh-RA-oh | ah-foh-RA-oh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G3708; to consider attentively; look; 

- to turn the eyes away from other things and fix them on something
