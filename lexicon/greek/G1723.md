# G1723 ἐναγκαλίζομαι

## enankalízomai

_(en-ang-kal-id'-zom-ahee | en-ang-ka-LEE-zoh-meh | ane-ang-ka-LEE-zoh-may)_

### Definition

from G1722 and a derivative of G0043; to take in one's arms, i.e., embrace; take up in arms; 

- to take into one's arms, embrace
