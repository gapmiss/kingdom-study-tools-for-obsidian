# G1265 διαμένω

## diaménō

_(dee-am-en'-o | thee-ah-MEH-noh | thee-ah-MAY-noh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G3306; to stay constantly (in being or relation); continue, remain; 

- to stay permanently, remain permanently, continue
