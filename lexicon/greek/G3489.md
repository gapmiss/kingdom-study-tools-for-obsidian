# G3489 ναυαγέω

## nauagéō

_(now-ag-eh'-o | nev-ah-GEH-oh | na-ah-GAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G3491 and G0071; to be shipwrecked (stranded, "navigate"), literally or figuratively; make (suffer) shipwreck; 

- to suffer shipwreck
