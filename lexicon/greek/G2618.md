# G2618 κατακαίω

## katakaíō

_(kat-ak-ah'-ee-o | ka-ta-KEH-oh | ka-ta-KAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G2545; to burn down (to the ground), i.e., consume wholly; burn (up, utterly); 

- to burn up, consume by fire
