# G4075 πετρώδης

## petrṓdēs

_(pet-ro'-dace | peh-TROH-thase | pay-TROH-thase)_

### Definition

from G4073 and G1491; rock-like, i.e., rocky; stony; 

- rocky, stony
  - of a ground full of rocks
