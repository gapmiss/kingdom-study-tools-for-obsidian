# G2217 ζόφος

## zóphos

_(dzof'-os | ZOH-fose | ZOH-fose)_

### Definition

akin to the base of G3509; gloom (as shrouding like a cloud); blackness, darkness, mist; 

- darkness, blackness
  - used of the darkness of the nether world
