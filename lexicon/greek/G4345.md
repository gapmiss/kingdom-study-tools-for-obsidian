# G4345 προσκληρόω

## prosklēróō

_(pros-klay-ro'-o | prose-klay-ROH-oh | prose-klay-ROH-oh)_

### Definition

from G4314 and G2820; to give a common lot to, i.e., (figuratively) to associate with; consort with; 

- to add or assign by lot, to allot
