# G1205 δεῦτε

## deûte

_(dyoo'-teh | THEV-teh | THAYF-tay)_

### Definition

from G1204 and an imperative form of εἶμι (to go); come hither!; come, X follow; 

- come hither, come here, come
- interjection, come!, come now!
