# G3535 Νινευΐ

## Nineuḯ

_(nin-yoo-ee' | nee-nev-EE | nee-nave-EE)_

### Definition

offspring of ease, offspring abiding; of Hebrew origin (H5210); Ninevi (i.e., Nineveh), the capital of Assyria; Nineve; 

- the capital of the ancient kingdom of Assyria
