# G2337 θηλάζω

## thēlázō

_(thay-lad'-zo | thay-LA-zoh | thay-LA-zoh)_

### Definition

from θηλή (the nipple); to suckle, (by implication) to suck; (give) suck(-ling); 

- to give the breast, give suck, to suckle
- to suck
