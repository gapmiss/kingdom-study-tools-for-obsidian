# G5245 ὑπερνικάω

## hypernikáō

_(hoop-er-nik-ah'-o | hoo-per-nee-KA-oh | yoo-pare-nee-KA-oh)_

### Definition

from G5228 and G3528; to vanquish beyond, i.e., gain a decisive victory; more than conquer; 

- to be more than a conqueror, to gain a surpassing victory
