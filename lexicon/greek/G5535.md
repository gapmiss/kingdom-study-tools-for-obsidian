# G5535 χρῄζω

## chrḗizō

_(khrade'-zo | HRAY-zoh | HRAY-zoh)_

### Definition

from G5532; to make (i.e., have) necessity, i.e., be in want of; (have) need; 

- to have need of, to be in want of
