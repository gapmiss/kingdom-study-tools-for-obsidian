# G2027 ἐποκέλλω

## epokéllō

_(ep-ok-el'-lo | ep-oh-KEL-loh | ape-oh-KALE-loh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and ὀκέλλω (to urge); to drive upon the shore, i.e., to beach a vessel; run aground; 

- to drive upon, strike against
  - to run a ship aground
