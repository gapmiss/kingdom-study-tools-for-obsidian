# G3979 πεζῇ

## pezēi

_(ped-zay' | peh-ZAY | pay-ZAY)_

### Definition

dative case feminine of a derivative of G4228 (as adverb); foot-wise, i.e., by walking; a- (on) foot; 

- on foot (as opposed to riding)
- by land (as opposed to going by sea)
