# G3978 πεζεύω

## pezeúō

_(ped-zyoo'-o | peh-ZEV-oh | pay-ZAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from the same as G3979; to foot a journey, i.e., travel by land; go afoot; 

- to travel on foot (not on horseback or in carriage), or (if opposed to going by sea) by land
