# G5541 χρηστεύομαι

## chrēsteúomai

_(khraste-yoo'-om-ahee | hray-STEV-oh-meh | hray-STAVE-oh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from G5543; to show oneself useful, i.e., act benevolently; be kind; 

- to show one's self mild, to be kind, use kindness
