# G596 ἀποθήκη

## apothḗkē

_(ap-oth-ay'-kay | ah-poh-THAY-kay | ah-poh-THAY-kay)_

### Definition

from G0659; a repository, i.e., granary; barn, garner; 

- a place in which anything is laid by or up
- a storehouse, granary
