# G3499 νεκρόω

## nekróō

_(nek-ro'-o | neh-KROH-oh | nay-KROH-oh)_

### Definition

from G3498; to deaden, i.e., (figuratively) to subdue; be dead, mortify; 

- to make dead, to put to death, slay
- worn out
  - of an impotent old man
- to deprive of power, destroy the strength of
