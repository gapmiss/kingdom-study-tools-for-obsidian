# G805 ἀσφαλίζω

## asphalízō

_(as-fal-id'-zo | ah-sfa-LEE-zoh | ah-sfa-LEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G0804; to render secure; make fast (sure); 

- to make firm, to make secure (against harm)
- to be made secure
