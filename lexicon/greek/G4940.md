# G4940 συντυγχάνω

## syntynchánō

_(soon-toong-khan'-o | soon-toong-HA-noh | syoon-tyoong-HA-noh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G5177; to chance together, i.e., meet with (reach); come at; 

- to meet with, come
