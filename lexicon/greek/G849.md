# G849 αὐτόχειρ

## autócheir

_(ow-tokh'-ire | av-TOH-heer | af-TOH-heer)_

### Definition

from G0846 and G5495; self-handed, i.e., doing personally; with...own hands; 

- doing a thing with one's own hands (Often in the tragedians and Attic orators)
