# G5431 φροντίζω

## phrontízō

_(fron-tid'-zo | frone-TEE-zoh | frone-TEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G5424; to exercise thought, i.e., be anxious; be careful; 

- to think, to be careful
- to be thoughtful or anxious
