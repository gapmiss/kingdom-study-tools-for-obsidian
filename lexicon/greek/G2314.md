# G2314 θεομάχος

## theomáchos

_(theh-om'-akh-os | theh-oh-MA-hose | thay-oh-MA-hose)_

### Definition

from G2316 and G3164; an opponent of deity; to fight against God; 

- fighting against God, resisting God
