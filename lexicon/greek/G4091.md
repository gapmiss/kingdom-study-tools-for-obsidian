# G4091 Πιλᾶτος

## Pilâtos

_(pil-at'-os | pee-LA-tose | pee-LA-tose)_

### Definition

armed with a spear; of Latin origin; close-pressed, i.e., firm; Pilatus, a Roman; Pilate; 

- the sixth Roman procurator of Judah and Samaria who ordered Christ to be crucified
