# G307 ἀναβιβάζω

## anabibázō

_(an-ab-ee-bad'-zo | ah-na-vee-VA-zoh | ah-na-vee-VA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G0303 and a derivative of the base of G0939; to cause to go up, i.e., haul (a net); draw; 

- to cause to go up or ascend, to draw up
