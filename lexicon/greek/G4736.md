# G4736 Στέφανος

## Stéphanos

_(stef'-an-os | STEH-fa-nose | STAY-fa-nose)_

### Definition

crowned; the same as G4735; Stephanus, a Christian; Stephen; 

- one of the seven deacons in Jerusalem and the first Christian martyr
