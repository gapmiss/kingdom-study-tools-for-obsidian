# G623 Ἀπολλύων

## Apollýōn

_(ap-ol-loo'-ohn | ah-pole-LOO-one | ah-pole-LYOO-one)_

### Definition

Destroyer; active participle of G0622; a destroyer (i.e., Satan); Apollyon; 

- the angel of the bottomless pit, the Destroyer
