# G1948 ἐπικρίνω

## epikrínō

_(ep-ee-kree'-no | eh-pee-KREE-noh | ay-pee-KREE-noh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G2919; to adjudge; give sentence; 

- to adjudge, approve by one's decision, decree, give sentence
