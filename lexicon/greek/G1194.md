# G1194 δέρω

## dérō

_(der'-o | THEH-roh | THAY-roh)_

### Definition

a primary verb; properly, to flay, i.e., (by implication) to scourge, or (by analogy) to thrash; beat, smite; 

- to flay, skin
- to beat, thrash, smite
