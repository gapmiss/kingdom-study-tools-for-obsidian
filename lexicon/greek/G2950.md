# G2950 κύμβαλον

## kýmbalon

_(koom'-bal-on | KOOM-va-lone | KYOOM-va-lone)_

### Definition

from a derivative of the base of G2949; a "cymbal" (as hollow); cymbal; 

- a cymbal, i.e., a hollow basin of brass, producing when struck together a musical sound
