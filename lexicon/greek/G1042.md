# G1042 γαββαθά

## gabbathá

_(gab-bath-ah' | gahv-va-THA | gahv-va-THA)_

### Definition

elevated, a platform; of Chaldee origin (compare H1355); the knoll; gabbatha, a vernacular term for the Roman tribunal in Jerusalem; Gabbatha; 

- a raised place, elevation
