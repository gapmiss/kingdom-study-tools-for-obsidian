# G4593 σημειόω

## sēmeióō

_(say-mi-o'-o | say-mee-OH-oh | say-mee-OH-oh)_

### Definition

from G4592; to distinguish, i.e., mark (for avoidance); note; 

- to mark, to note, distinguish by marking
- to mark or note for one's self
