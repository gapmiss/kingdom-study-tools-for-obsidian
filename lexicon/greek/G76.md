# G76 Ἀδάμ

## Adám

_(ad-am' | ah-THAHM | ah-THAHM)_

### Definition

the red earth; of Hebrew origin (H0121); Adam, the first man; typically (of Jesus) man (as his representative); Adam; 

- Adam, the first man, the parent of the whole human race
