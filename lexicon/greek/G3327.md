# G3327 μεταβαίνω

## metabaínō

_(met-ab-ah'-ee-no | meh-ta-VEH-noh | may-ta-VAY-noh)_

### Definition

from G3326 and the base of G0939; to change place; depart, go, pass, remove; 

- to pass over from one place to another, to remove, depart
