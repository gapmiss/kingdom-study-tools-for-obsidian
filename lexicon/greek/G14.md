## agathoergéō

_(ag-ath-er-gheh'-o | ah-ga-thoh-er-GEH-oh | ah-ga-thoh-are-GAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0018 and G2041; to work good; do good; 

- to work good, to do good, to do well, act rightly
