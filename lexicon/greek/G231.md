# G231 ἁλιεύς

## halieús

_(hal-ee-yoos' | ha-lee-EVS | a-lee-AYFS)_

### Definition

from G0251; a sailor (as engaged on the salt water), i.e., (by implication) a fisher; fisher(-man); 

- a fisherman, fisher
