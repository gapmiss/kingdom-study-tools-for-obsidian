# G1638 ἐλαιών

## elaiṓn

_(el-ah-yone' | eh-leh-ONE | ay-lay-ONE)_

### Definition

from G1636; an olive-orchard, i.e., (specially) the Mount of Olives; Olivet; 

- an olive orchard, a place planted with olive trees
  - Mount of Olives
