# G1201 δεσμωτήριον

## desmōtḗrion

_(des-mo-tay'-ree-on | theh-smoh-TAY-ree-one | thay-smoh-TAY-ree-one)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G1199 (equivalent to G1196); a place of bondage, i.e., a dungeon; prison; 

- a prison, a jail
