# G1836 ἑξῆς

## hexēs

_(hex-ace' | heks-ASE | ayks-ASE)_

### Definition

from G2192 (in the sense of taking hold of, i.e. adjoining); successive; after, following, X morrow, next; 

- successively in order
- the next following, the next in succession
