# G1374 δίψυχος

## dípsychos

_(dip'-soo-khos | THEE-psoo-hose | THEE-psyoo-hose)_

### Definition

from G1364 and G5590; two-spirited, i.e., vacillating (in opinion or purpose); double minded; 

- double minded
  - wavering, uncertain, doubting
  - divided in interest
