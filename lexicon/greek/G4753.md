# G4753 στράτευμα

## stráteuma

_(strat'-yoo-mah | STRA-tev-ma | STRA-tave-ma)_

### Definition

from G4754; an armament, i.e., (by implication) a body of troops (more or less extensive or systematic); army, soldier, man of war; 

- an army
- a band of soldiers
- bodyguard, guards men
