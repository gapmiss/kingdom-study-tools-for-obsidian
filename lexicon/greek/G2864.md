# G2864 κόμη

## kómē

_(kom'-ay | KOH-may | KOH-may)_

### Definition

apparently from the same as G2865; the hair of the head (locks, as ornamental, and thus differing from G2359; which properly denotes merely the scalp); hair; 

- hair, head of hair
