# G4702 σπόριμος

## spórimos

_(spor'-ee-mos | SPOH-ree-mose | SPOH-ree-mose)_

### Definition

from G4703; sown, i.e., (neuter plural) a planted field; corn(-field); 

- fit for sowing, sown
- sown fields, growing crops
