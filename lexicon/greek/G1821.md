# G1821 ἐξαποστέλλω

## exapostéllō

_(ex-ap-os-tel'-lo | eks-ah-poh-STEL-loh | ayks-ah-poh-STALE-loh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G0649; to send away forth, i.e., (on a mission) to despatch, or (peremptorily) to dismiss; send (away, forth, out); 

- to send forth
- to send away
