# G4445 πυρέσσω

## pyréssō

_(poo-res'-so | poo-RES-soh | pyoo-RASE-soh)_

### Definition

from G4443; to be on fire, i.e., (specially), to have a fever; be sick of a fever; 

- to be sick with a fever
