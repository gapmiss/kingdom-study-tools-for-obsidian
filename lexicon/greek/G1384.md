# G1384 δόκιμος

## dókimos

_(dok'-ee-mos | THOH-kee-mose | THOH-kee-mose)_

### Definition

from G1380; properly, acceptable (current after assayal), i.e., approved; approved, tried; 

- accepted, particularly of coins and money
- accepted, pleasing, acceptable
