# G153 αἰσχύνομαι

## aischýnomai

_(ahee-skhoo'-nom-ahee | eh-SKOO-noh-meh | ay-SKYOO-noh-may)_

### Definition

from αἶσχος (disfigurement, i.e. disgrace); to feel shame (for oneself); be ashamed; 

- to disfigure
- to dishonor
- to suffuse with shame, make ashamed, be ashamed
