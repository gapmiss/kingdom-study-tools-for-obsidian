# G2025 ἐπιχρίω

## epichríō

_(ep-ee-khree'-o | eh-pee-HREE-oh | ay-pee-HREE-oh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G5548; to smear over; anoint; 

- to spread on, anoint anything upon anything
