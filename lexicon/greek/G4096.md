# G4096 πιότης

## piótēs

_(pee-ot'-ace | pee-OH-tase | pee-OH-tase)_

### Definition

from πίων (fat; perhaps akin to the alternate of G4095 through the idea of repletion); plumpness, i.e., (by implication) richness (oiliness); fatness; 

- fatness
