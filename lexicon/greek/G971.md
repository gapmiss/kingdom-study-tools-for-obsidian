# G971 βιάζω

## biázō

_(bee-ad'-zo | vee-AH-zoh | vee-AH-zoh)_

### Definition

from G0970; to force, i.e., (reflexively) to crowd oneself (into), or (passively) to be seized; press, suffer violence; 

- to use force, to apply force
- to force, inflict violence on
