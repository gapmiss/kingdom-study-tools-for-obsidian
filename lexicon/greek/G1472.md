# G1472 ἐγχρίω

## enchríō

_(eng-khree'-o | eng-HREE-oh | ayng-HREE-oh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G5548; to rub in (oil), i.e., besmear; anoint; 

- to rub in, besmirch, anoint
- to anoint one's self
