# G744 ἀρχαῖος

## archaîos

_(ar-khah'-yos | ar-HEH-ose | ar-HAY-ose)_

### Definition

from G0746; original or primeval; (them of) old (time); 

- that has been from the beginning, original, primal, old ancient
  - of men, things, times, conditions
