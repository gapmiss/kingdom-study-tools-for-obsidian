# G5370 φίλημα

## phílēma

_(fil'-ay-mah | FEEL-ay-ma | FEEL-ay-ma)_

### Definition

from G5368; a kiss; kiss; 

- a kiss
- the kiss with which, as a sign of fraternal affection, Christians were accustomed to welcome or dismiss their companions in the faith
