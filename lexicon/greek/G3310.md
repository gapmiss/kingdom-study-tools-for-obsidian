# G3310 μερίς

## merís

_(mer-ece' | meh-REES | may-REES)_

### Definition

feminine of G3313; a portion, i.e., province, share or (abstractly) participation; part (X -akers); 

- a part as distinct from the whole
- an assigned part, a portion, share
