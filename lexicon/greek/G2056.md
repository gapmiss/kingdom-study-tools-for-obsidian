# G2056 ἔριφος

## ériphos

_(er'-if-os | EH-ree-fose | A-ree-fose)_

### Definition

perhaps from the same as G2053 (through the idea of hairiness); a kid or (genitive case) goat; goat, kid; 

- a kid, a young goat
