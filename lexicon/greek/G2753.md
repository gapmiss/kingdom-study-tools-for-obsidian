# G2753 κελεύω

## keleúō

_(kel-yoo'-o | keh-LEV-oh | kay-LAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from a primary κέλλω (to urge on); "hail"; to incite by word, i.e., order; bid, (at, give) command(-ment); 

- to command, to order
