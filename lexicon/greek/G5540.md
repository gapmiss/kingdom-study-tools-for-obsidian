# G5540 χρῆσις

## chrēsis

_(khray'-sis | HRAY-sees | HRAY-sees)_

### Definition

from G5530; employment, i.e., (specially), sexual intercourse (as an occupation of the body); use; 

- use
  - of the sexual use of a woman
