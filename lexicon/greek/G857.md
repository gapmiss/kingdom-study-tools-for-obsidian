# G857 ἀφειδία

## apheidía

_(af-i-dee'-ah | ah-fee-THEE-ah | ah-fee-THEE-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of G0001 (as a negative particle) and G5339; unsparingness, i.e., austerity (asceticism); neglecting; 

- unsparingly severity
