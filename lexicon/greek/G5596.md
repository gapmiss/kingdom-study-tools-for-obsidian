# G5596 ψωμίον

## psōmíon

_(pso-mee'-on | psoh-MEE-one | psoh-MEE-one)_

### Definition

diminutive from a derivative of the base of G5597; a crumb or morsel (as if rubbed off), i.e., a mouthful; sop; 

- a fragment, bit, morsel
