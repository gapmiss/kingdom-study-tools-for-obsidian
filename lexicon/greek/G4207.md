# G4207 πόῤῥωθεν

## pórrhōthen

_(por'-rho-then | PORE-roh-then | PORE-roh-thane)_

### Definition

from G4206 with adverbial enclitic of source; from far, or (by implication) at a distance, i.e., distantly; afar off; 

- from afar, afar off
