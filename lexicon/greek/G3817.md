# G3817 παίω

## paíō

_(pah'-yo | PEH-oh | PAY-oh)_

### Definition

a primary verb; to hit (as if by a single blow and less violently than G5180); specially, to sting (as a scorpion); smite, strike; 

- to strike, smite
- to sting (to strike or wound with a sting)
