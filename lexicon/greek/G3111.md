# G3111 μάκελλον

## mákellon

_(mak'-el-lon | MA-kel-lone | MA-kale-lone)_

### Definition

of Latin origin (macellum); a butcher's stall, meat market or provision-shop; shambles; 

- a place where meat and other articles of food are sold, meat market
