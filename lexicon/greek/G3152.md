# G3152 μάταιος

## mátaios

_(mat'-ah-yos | MA-teh-ose | MA-tay-ose)_

### Definition

from the base of G3155; empty, i.e., (literally) profitless, or (specially), an idol; vain, vanity; 

- devoid of force, truth, success, result
- useless, of no purpose
