# G4777 συγκακοπαθέω

## synkakopathéō

_(soong-kak-op-ath-eh'-o | soong-ka-koh-pa-THEH-oh | syoong-ka-koh-pa-THAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G2553; to suffer hardship in company with; be partaker of afflictions; 

- to suffer hardship together with one
