# G4164 ποικίλος

## poikílos

_(poy-kee'-los | poo-KEE-lose | poo-KEE-lose)_

### Definition

of uncertain derivation; motley, i.e., various in character; divers, manifold; 

- a various colors, variegated
- of various sorts
