# G4344 προσκεφάλαιον

## proskephálaion

_(pros-kef-al'-ahee-on | prose-keh-FA-leh-one | prose-kay-FA-lay-one)_

### Definition

neuter of a presumed compound of G4314 and G2776; something for the head, i.e., a cushion; pillow; 

- a pillow, cushion
