# G1406 δραχμή

## drachmḗ

_(drakh-may' | thrahk-MAY | thrahk-MAY)_

### Definition

from G1405; a drachma or (silver) coin (as handled); piece (of silver); 

- a drachma, a Greek silver coin about the same weight as a Roman denarius
