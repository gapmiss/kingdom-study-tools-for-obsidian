# G1719 ἔμφοβος

## émphobos

_(em'-fob-os | EM-foh-vose | AME-foh-vose)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G5401; in fear, i.e., alarmed; affrighted, afraid, tremble; 

- thrown into fear, terrified, affrighted
