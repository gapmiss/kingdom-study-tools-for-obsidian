# G3815 παίζω

## paízō

_(paheed'-zo | PEH-zoh | PAY-zoh)_

### Definition

from G3816; to sport (as a boy); play; 

- to play like a child
- to play, sport, jest
- to give way to hilarity, especially by joking singing, dancing
