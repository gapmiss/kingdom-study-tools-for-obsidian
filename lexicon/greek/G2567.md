_(kal-od-id-as'-kal-os | ka-loh-thee-THA-ska-lose | ka-loh-thee-THA-ska-lose)_

### Definition

from G2570 and G1320; a teacher of the right; teacher of good things; 

- teaching that which is good, a teacher of goodness
