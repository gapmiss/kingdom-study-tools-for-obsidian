# G1130 γυμνητεύω

## gymnēteúō

_(goom-nayt-yoo'-o | goom-nay-TEV-oh | gyoom-nay-TAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G1131; to strip, i.e., (reflexively) go poorly clad; be naked; 

- to be lightly or poorly clad
- to be a light armed soldier
