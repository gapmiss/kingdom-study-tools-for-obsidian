# G2215 ζιζάνιον

## zizánion

_(dziz-an'-ee-on | zee-ZA-nee-one | zee-ZA-nee-one)_

### Definition

of uncertain origin; darnel or false grain; tares; 

- a kind of darnel, resembling wheat except the grains are black
