# G1076 γενεαλογία

## genealogía

_(ghen-eh-al-og-ee'-ah | geh-neh-ah-loh-GEE-ah | gay-nay-ah-loh-GEE-ah)_

### Definition

from the same as G1075; tracing by generations, i.e., "genealogy"; genealogy; 

- a genealogy, a record of descent or lineage
