# G3735 ὄρος

## óros

_(or'-os | OH-rose | OH-rose)_

### Definition

probably from an obsolete ὄρω (to rise or "rear"; perhaps akin to G0142; compare G3733); a mountain (as lifting itself above the plain); hill, mount(-ain); 

- a mountain
