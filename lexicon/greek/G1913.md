# G1913 ἐπιβιβάζω

## epibibázō

_(ep-ee-bee-bad'-zo | eh-pee-vee-VA-zoh | ay-pee-vee-VA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and a reduplicated derivative of the base of G0939 (compare G0307); to cause to mount (an animal); set on; 

- to cause to mount
- to place upon
