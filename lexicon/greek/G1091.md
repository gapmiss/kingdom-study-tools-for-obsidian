# G1091 γεώργιον

## geṓrgion

_(gheh-ore'-ghee-on | geh-ORE-gee-one | gay-ORE-gee-one)_

### Definition

neuter of a (presumed) derivative of G1092; cultivable, i.e., a farm; husbandry; 

- a cultivated field, husbandry, tillage
