# G2171 εὐχή

## euchḗ

_(yoo-khay' | ev-HAY | afe-HAY)_

### Definition

from G2172; properly, a wish, expressed as a petition to God, or in votive obligation; prayer, vow; 

- a prayer to God
- a vow
