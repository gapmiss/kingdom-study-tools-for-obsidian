# G2998 λατομέω

## latoméō

_(lat-om-eh'-o | la-toh-MEH-oh | la-toh-MAY-oh)_

### Definition

from the same as the first part of G2991 and the base of G5114; to quarry; hew; 

- to cut stones, to hew out stones
