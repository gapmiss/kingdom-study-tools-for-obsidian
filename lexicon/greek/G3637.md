# G3637 ὀκταήμερος

## oktaḗmeros

_(ok-tah-ay'-mer-os | oke-ta-A-meh-rose | oke-ta-A-may-rose)_

### Definition

from G3638 and G2250; an eight-day old person or act; the eighth day; 

- eight days old
- passing the eighth day
