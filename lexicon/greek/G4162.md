# G4162 ποίησις

## poíēsis

_(poy'-ay-sis | POO-ay-sees | POO-ay-sees)_

### Definition

from G4160; action, i.e., performance (of the law); deed; 

- a making
- a doing or performing
  - in his doing i.e., in the obedience he renders to the law
