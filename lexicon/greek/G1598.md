# G1598 ἐκπειράζω

## ekpeirázō

_(ek-pi-rad'-zo | ek-pee-RA-zoh | ake-pee-RA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G3985; to test thoroughly; tempt; 

- to prove, test, thoroughly
- to put to proof God's character and power
