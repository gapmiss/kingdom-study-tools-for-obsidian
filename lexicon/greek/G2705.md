# G2705 καταφιλέω

## kataphiléō

_(kat-af-ee-leh'-o | ka-ta-fee-LEH-oh | ka-ta-fee-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from 2596 and 5368; to kiss earnestly; kiss; 

- to kiss much, kiss again and again, kiss tenderly
