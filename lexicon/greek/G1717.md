# G1717 ἐμφανής

## emphanḗs

_(em-fan-ace' | em-fa-NASE | ame-fa-NASE)_

### Definition

from a compound of G1722 and G5316; apparent in self; manifest, openly; 

- manifest
  - figurative for God giving proofs of his saving grace and thus manifesting himself
