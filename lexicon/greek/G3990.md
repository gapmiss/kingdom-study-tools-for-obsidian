# G3990 πελεκίζω

## pelekízō

_(pel-ek-id'-zo | peh-leh-KEE-zoh | pay-lay-KEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G4141 (meaning an axe); to chop off (the head), i.e., truncate; behead; 

- to cut off with an axe, to behead
