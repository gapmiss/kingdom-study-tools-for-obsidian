# G764 ἀσεβέω

## asebéō

_(as-eb-eh'-o | ah-seh-VEH-oh | ah-say-VAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G0765; to be (by implied act) impious or wicked; commit (live, that after should live) ungodly; 

- to be ungodly, act impiously
