# G4814 συλλαλέω

## syllaléō

_(sool-lal-eh'-o | sool-la-LEH-oh | syool-la-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G2980; to talk together, i.e., converse; commune (confer, talk) with, speak among; 

- to talk with
