# G895 ἄψυχος

## ápsychos

_(ap'-soo-khos | AH-psoo-hose | AH-psyoo-hose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G5590; lifeless, i.e., inanimate (mechanical); without life; 

- without a soul, lifeless
