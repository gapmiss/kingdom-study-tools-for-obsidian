# G2193 ἕως

## héōs

_(heh'-oce | HEH-ose | AY-ose)_

### Definition

of uncertain affinity; a conjunction, preposition and adverb of continuance, until (of time and place); even (until, unto), (as) far (as), how long, (un-)til(-l), (hither-, un-, up) to, while(-s); 

- till, until
