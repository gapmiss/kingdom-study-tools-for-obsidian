# G4485 ῥῆγμα

## rhēgma

_(hrayg'-mah | RAGE-ma | RAGE-ma)_

### Definition

from G4486; something torn, i.e., a fragment (by implication and abstractly, a fall); ruin; 

- that which has been broken or rent asunder
  - a fracture, breach, cleft
  - rent clothes
  - fall, ruin
