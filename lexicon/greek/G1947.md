# G1947 ἐπικουρία

## epikouría

_(ep-ee-koo-ree'-ah | eh-pee-koo-REE-ah | ay-pee-koo-REE-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of G1909 and a (prolonged) form of the base of G2877 (in the sense of servant); assistance; help; 

- aid, succour, help
