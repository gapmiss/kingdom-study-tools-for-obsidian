# G3673 ὁμότεχνος

## homótechnos

_(hom-ot'-ekh-nos | hoh-MOH-teh-hnose | oh-MOH-tay-hnose)_

### Definition

from the base of G3674 and G5078; a fellow-artificer; of the same craft; 

- practising the same trade or craft, of the same trade
