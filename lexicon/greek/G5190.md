# G5190 Τυχικός

## Tychikós

_(too-khee-kos' | too-hee-KOSE | tyoo-hee-KOSE)_

### Definition

fateful; from a derivative of G5177; fortuitous, i.e., fortunate; Tychicus, a Christian; Tychicus; 

- an Asiatic Christian, friend and companion of the apostle Paul
