# G1612 ἐκστρέφω

## ekstréphō

_(ek-stref'-o | ek-STREH-foh | ake-STRAY-foh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G4762; to pervert (figuratively); subvert; 

- to turn or twist out, tear up
- to turn inside out, invert
- to change for the worse, pervert, corrupt
