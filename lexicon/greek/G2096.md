# G2096 Εὖα

## Eûa

_(yoo'-ah | EV-ah | AVE-ah)_

### Definition

life; of Hebrew origin (H2332); Eve (or Eua or Eva, i.e., Chavvah), the first woman; Eve; 

- first woman in the scriptures, mother of the human race
