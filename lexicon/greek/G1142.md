# G1142 δαίμων

## daímōn

_(dah'-ee-mown | THEH-mone | THAY-mone)_

### Definition

from δαίω (to distribute fortunes); a daemon or supernatural spirit (of a bad nature); devil; 

- a god, a goddess
  - an inferior deity, whether good or bad
- in the New Testament, an evil spirit
