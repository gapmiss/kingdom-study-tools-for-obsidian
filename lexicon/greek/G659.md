# G659 ἀποτίθημι

## apotíthēmi

_(ap-ot-eeth'-ay-mee | ah-poh-TEE-thay-mee | ah-poh-TEE-thay-mee)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G5087; to put away (literally or figuratively); cast off, lay apart (aside, down), put away (off); 

- to put off or aside or away
