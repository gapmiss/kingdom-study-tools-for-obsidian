# G2764 κεραμικός

## keramikós

_(ker-am-ik-os' | keh-ra-mee-KOSE | kay-ra-mee-KOSE)_

### Definition

from G2766; made of clay, i.e., earthen; of a potter; 

- of or belonging to a potter
- made of clay, earthen
