# G1300 διατελέω

## diateléō

_(dee-at-el-eh'-o | thee-ah-teh-LEH-oh | thee-ah-tay-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G5055; to accomplish thoroughly, i.e., (subjectively) to persist; continue; 

- to bring thoroughly to an end, accomplish
