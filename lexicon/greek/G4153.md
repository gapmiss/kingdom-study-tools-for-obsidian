# G4153 πνευματικῶς

## pneumatikōs

_(pnyoo-mat-ik-oce' | pnev-ma-tee-KOSE | pnave-ma-tee-KOSE)_

### Definition

adverb from G4152; non-physically, i.e., divinely, figuratively; spiritually; 

- spiritually: i.e., by the aid of the Holy Spirit
