# G4088 πικρία

## pikría

_(pik-ree'-ah | pee-KREE-ah | pee-KREE-ah)_

### Definition

from G4089; acridity (especially poison), literally or figuratively; bitterness; 

- bitter gall
  - extreme wickedness
  - a bitter root, and so producing a bitter fruit
  - metaphorically bitterness, bitter hatred
