# G3643 ὀλιγωρέω

## oligōréō

_(ol-ig-o-reh'-o | oh-lee-goh-REH-oh | oh-lee-goh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G3641 and ὤρα ("care"); to have little regard for, i.e., to disesteem; despise; 

- to care little for, regard lightly, make small account
