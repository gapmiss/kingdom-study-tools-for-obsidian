# G3617 οἰκοδεσπότης

## oikodespótēs

_(oy-kod-es-pot'-ace | oo-koh-theh-SPOH-tase | oo-koh-thay-SPOH-tase)_

### Definition

from G3624 and G1203; the head of a family; goodman (of the house), householder, master of the house; 

- master of the house, householder
