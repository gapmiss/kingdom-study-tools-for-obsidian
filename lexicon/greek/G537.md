# G537 ἅπας

## hápas

_(hap'-as | HA-pahs | A-pahs)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a particle of union) and G3956; absolutely all or (singular) every one; all (things), every (one), whole; 

- quite, all, the whole, all together, all
