# G4015 περιαστράπτω

## periastráptō

_(per-ee-as-trap'-to | peh-ree-ah-STRA-ptoh | pay-ree-ah-STRA-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G4012 and G0797; to flash all around, i.e., envelop in light; shine round (about); 

- to flash around, shine about
