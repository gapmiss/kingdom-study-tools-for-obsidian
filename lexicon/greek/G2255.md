# G2255 ἥμισυ

## hḗmisy

_(hay'-mee-soo | HAY-mee-soo | AY-mee-syoo)_

### Definition

neuter of a derivative from an inseparable prefix akin to G0260 (through the idea of partition involved in connection) and meaning semi-; (as noun) half; half; 

- half
