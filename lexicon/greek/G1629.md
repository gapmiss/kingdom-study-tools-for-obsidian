# G1629 ἐκφοβέω

## ekphobéō

_(ek-fob-eh'-o | ek-foh-VEH-oh | ake-foh-VAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G5399; to frighten utterly; terrify; 

- to frighten away, to terrify
- to throw into violent flight
