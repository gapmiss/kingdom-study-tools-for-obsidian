# G2942 κυβερνήτης

## kybernḗtēs

_(koo-ber-nay'-tace | koo-ver-NAY-tase | kyoo-vare-NAY-tase)_

### Definition

from the same as G2941; helmsman, i.e., (by implication) captain; (ship) master; 

- steersman, helmsman, sailing master
