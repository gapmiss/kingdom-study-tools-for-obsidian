# G2335 θεωρία

## theōría

_(theh-o-ree'-ah | theh-oh-REE-ah | thay-oh-REE-ah)_

### Definition

from the same as G2334; spectatorship, i.e., (concretely) a spectacle; sight; 

- a viewing, beholding
- that which is viewed, a spectacle, sight
