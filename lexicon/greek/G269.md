# G269 ἄμαχος

## ámachos

_(am'-akh-os | AH-ma-hose | AH-ma-hose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G3163; peaceable; not a brawler; 

- not to be withstood, invincible
- not contentious
- abstaining from fighting
