# G163 αἰχμαλωτίζω

## aichmalōtízō

_(aheekh-mal-o-tid'-zo | ek-ma-loh-TEE-zoh | ake-ma-loh-TEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G0164; to make captive; lead away captive, bring into captivity; 

- to lead away captive
- metaphorically to capture ones mind, captivate
