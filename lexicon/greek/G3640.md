# G3640 ὀλιγόπιστος

## oligópistos

_(ol-ig-op'-is-tos | oh-lee-GOH-pee-stose | oh-lee-GOH-pee-stose)_

### Definition

from G3641 and G4102; incredulous, i.e., lacking confidence (in Christ); of little faith; 

- of little faith, trusting too little
