## chreōpheilétēs

_(khreh-o-fi-let'-ace | hreh-oh-fee-LEH-tase | hray-oh-fee-LAY-tase)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G5531 and G3781; a loan-ower, i.e., indebted person; debtor; 

- a debtor
