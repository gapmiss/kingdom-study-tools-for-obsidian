# G4831 συμμιμητής

## symmimētḗs

_(soom-mim-ay-tace' | soom-mee-may-TASE | syoom-mee-may-TASE)_

### Definition

from a presumed compound of G4862 and G3401; a co-imitator, i.e., fellow votary; follower together; 

- an imitator of others
