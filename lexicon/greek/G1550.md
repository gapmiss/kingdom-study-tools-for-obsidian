# G1550 ἐκδαπανάω

## ekdapanáō

_(ek-dap-an-ah'-o | ek-tha-pa-NA-oh | ake-tha-pa-NA-oh)_

### Definition

from G1537 and G1159; to expend (wholly), i.e., (figuratively) exhaust; spend; 

- to exhaust by expending, to spend wholly, use up,
- to spend one's self wholly
