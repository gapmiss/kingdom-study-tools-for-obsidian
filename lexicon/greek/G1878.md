# G1878 ἐπαναμιμνήσκω

## epanamimnḗskō

_(ep-an-ah-mim-nace'-ko | ep-ah-na-meem-NAY-skoh | ape-ah-na-meem-NAY-skoh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G0363; to remind of; put in mind; 

- to recall to mind again
- reminding one
