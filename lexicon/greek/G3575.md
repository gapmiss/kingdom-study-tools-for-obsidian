# G3575 Νῶε

## Nōe

_(no'-eh | NOH-eh | NOH-ay)_

### Definition

rest; of Hebrew origin (H5146); Noë, (i.e., Noäch), a patriarch; Noe; 

- the tenth in descent from Adam, second father of the human race
