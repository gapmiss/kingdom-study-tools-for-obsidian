# G198 ἀκριβόω

## akribóō

_(ak-ree-bo'-o | ah-kree-VOH-oh | ah-kree-VOH-oh)_

### Definition

from the same as G0196; to be exact, i.e., ascertain; enquire diligently; 

- to know accurately, to do exactly
- to investigate diligently
