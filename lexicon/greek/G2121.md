# G2121 εὔκαιρος

## eúkairos

_(yoo'-kahee-ros | EV-keh-rose | AFE-kay-rose)_

### Definition

from G2095 and G2540; well-timed, i.e., opportune; convenient, in time of need; 

- seasonable, timely, opportune
