# G3314 μεσημβρία

## mesēmbría

_(mes-ame-bree'-ah | meh-same-VREE-ah | may-same-VREE-ah)_

### Definition

from G3319 and G2250; midday; by implication, the south; noon, south; 

- as respects to time: noon
- as respects to locality
  - the south
