# G1221 δήποτε

## dḗpote

_(day'-pot-eh | THAY-poh-teh | THAY-poh-tay)_

### Definition

from G1211 and G4218; a particle of generalization; indeed, at any time; (what-)soever; 

- now at length
- at any time
- at last
- just exactly
