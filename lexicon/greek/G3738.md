# G3738 ὀρχέομαι

## orchéomai

_(or-kheh'-om-ahee | ore-HEH-oh-meh | ore-HAY-oh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from ὄρχος (a row or ring); to dance (from the ranklike or regular motion); dance; 

- to dance
