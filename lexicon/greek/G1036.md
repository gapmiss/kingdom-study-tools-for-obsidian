# G1036 βυθίζω

## bythízō

_(boo-thid'-zo | voo-THEE-zoh | vyoo-THEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1037; to sink; by implication, to drown; begin to sink, drown; 

- to plunge into the deep, to sink
