# G724 ἁρπαγή

## harpagḗ

_(har-pag-ay' | hahr-pa-GAY | ahr-pa-GAY)_

### Definition

from G0726; pillage (properly abstract); extortion, ravening, spoiling; 

- the act of plundering, robbery
- plunder spoil
