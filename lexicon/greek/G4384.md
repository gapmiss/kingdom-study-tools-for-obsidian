# G4384 προτάσσω

## protássō

_(prot-as'-so | proh-TAHS-soh | proh-TAHS-soh)_

### Definition

from G4253 and G5021; to pre-arrange, i.e., prescribe; before appoint; 

- to place before
- to appoint before, define beforehand
