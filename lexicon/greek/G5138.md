# G5138 τραχύς

## trachýs

_(trakh-oos' | tra-HOOS | tra-HYOOS)_

### Definition

perhaps strengthened from the base of G4486 (as if jagged by rents); uneven, rocky (reefy); rock, rough; 

- rough, rocky
  - of places of the sea
