# G3799 ὄψις

## ópsis

_(op'-sis | OH-psees | OH-psees)_

### Definition

from G3700; properly, sight (the act), i.e., (by implication) the visage, an external show; appearance, countenance, face; 

- seeing, sight
- face, countenance
- the outward appearance, look
