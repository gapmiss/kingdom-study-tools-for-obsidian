# G2281 θάλασσα

## thálassa

_(thal'-as-sah | THA-lahs-sa | THA-lahs-sa)_

### Definition

probably prolonged from G0251; the sea (genitive case or specially); sea; 

- the sea
  - used of the sea in general
  - used specifically of the Mediterranean Sea or the Red Sea
