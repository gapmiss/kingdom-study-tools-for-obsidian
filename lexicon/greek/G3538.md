# G3538 νίπτω

## níptō

_(nip'-to | NEE-ptoh | NEE-ptoh)_

### Definition

to cleanse (especially the hands or the feet or the face); ceremonially, to perform ablution; wash; 

- to wash
- to wash one's self

### Note

Compare G3068.
