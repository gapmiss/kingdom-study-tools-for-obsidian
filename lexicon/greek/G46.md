# G46 ἄγναφος

## ágnaphos

_(ag'-naf-os | AH-gna-fose | AH-gna-fose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and the same as G1102; properly, unfulled, i.e., (by implication) new (cloth); new; 

- unmilled, unfulled, undressed, unprocessed, new
