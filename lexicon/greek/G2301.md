# G2301 θεατρίζω

## theatrízō

_(theh-at-rid'-zo | theh-ah-TREE-zoh | thay-ah-TREE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G2302; to expose as a spectacle; make a gazing stock; 

- to bring upon the stage
- to set forth as a spectacle, expose to contempt
