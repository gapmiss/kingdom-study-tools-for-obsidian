# G5266 ὑπόδημα

## hypódēma

_(hoop-od'-ay-mah | hoo-POH-thay-ma | yoo-POH-thay-ma)_

### Definition

from G5265; something bound under the feet, i.e., a shoe or sandal; shoe; 

- what is bound under, a sandal, a sole fastened to the foot with thongs
