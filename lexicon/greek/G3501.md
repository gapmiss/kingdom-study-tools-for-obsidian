# G3501 νέος

## néos

_(neh'-os | NEH-ose | NAY-ose)_

### Definition

including the comparative νεότερος; a primary word; "new", i.e., (of persons) youthful, or (of things) fresh; figuratively, regenerate; new, young; 

- recently born, young, youthful
- new
