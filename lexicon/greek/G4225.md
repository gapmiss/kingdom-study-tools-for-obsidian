# G4225 πού

## poú

_(poo | poo | poo)_

### Definition

genitive case of an indefinite pronoun πός (some) otherwise obsolete (compare G4214); as adverb of place, somewhere, i.e., nearly; about, a certain place; 

- where?, in what place?
