# G5191 ὑακίνθινος

## hyakínthinos

_(hoo-ak-in'-thee-nos | hoo-ah-KEEN-thee-nose | yoo-ah-KEEN-thee-nose)_

### Definition

from G5192; "hyacinthine" or "jacinthine", i.e., deep blue; jacinth; 

- hyacinth, of the color of hyacinth
  - of a red color bordering on black
