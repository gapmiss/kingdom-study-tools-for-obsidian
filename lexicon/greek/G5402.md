# G5402 Φοίβη

## Phoíbē

_(foy'-bay | FOO-vay | FOO-vay)_

### Definition

radiant; feminine of φοῖβος (bright; probably akin to the base of G5457); Phoebe, a Christian woman; Phebe; 

- a servant of the church at Cenchrea, near Corinth
