## epikályma

_(ep-ee-kal'-oo-mah | eh-pee-KA-loo-ma | ay-pee-KA-lyoo-ma)_

### Definition

from G1943; a covering, i.e., (figuratively) pretext; cloke; 

- a covering, veil
- metaphorically a pretext, cloak
