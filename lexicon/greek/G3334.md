# G3334 μετακινέω

## metakinéō

_(met-ak-ee-neh'-o | meh-ta-kee-NEH-oh | may-ta-kee-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G3326 and G2795; to stir to a place elsewhere, i.e., remove (figuratively); move away; 

- to move from a place, to move away
