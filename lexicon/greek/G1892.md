# G1892 ἐπεγείρω

## epegeírō

_(ep-eg-i'-ro | ep-eh-GEE-roh | ape-ay-GEE-roh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G1453; to rouse upon, i.e., (figuratively) to excite against; raise, stir up; 

- to raise or excite against
