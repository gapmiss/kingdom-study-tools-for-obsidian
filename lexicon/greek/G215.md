# G215 ἀλάλητος

## alálētos

_(al-al'-ay-tos | ah-LA-lay-tose | ah-LA-lay-tose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G2980; unspeakable; unutterable, which cannot be uttered; 

- not to be uttered, not expressed in words
