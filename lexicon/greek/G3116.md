# G3116 μακροθυμώς

## makrothymṓs

_(mak-roth-oo-moce' | ma-kroh-thoo-MOSE | ma-kroh-thyoo-MOSE)_

### Definition

adverb of a compound of G3117 and G2372; with long (enduring) temper, i.e., leniently; patiently; 

- with longanimity, i.e., patiently
