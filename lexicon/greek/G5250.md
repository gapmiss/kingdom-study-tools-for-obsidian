# G5250 ὑπερπλεονάζω

## hyperpleonázō

_(hoop-er-pleh-on-ad'-zo | hoo-per-pleh-oh-NA-zoh | yoo-pare-play-oh-NA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G5228 and G4121; to superabound; be exceeding abundant; 

- to be exceedingly abundant
- to overflow
- to possess in excess
