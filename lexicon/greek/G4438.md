# G4438 πυκτέω

## pyktéō

_(pook-teh'-o | pook-TEH-oh | pyook-TAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of the same as G4435; to box (with the fist), i.e., contend (as a boxer) at the games (figuratively); fight; 

- to be a boxer, to box
