# G4677 Σουσάννα

## Sousánna

_(soo-san'-nah | soo-SAHN-na | soo-SAHN-na)_

### Definition

a lily; of Hebrew origin (H7799 feminine); lily; Susannah (i.e., Shoshannah), an Israelitess; Susanna; 

- one of the women who ministered to Christ
