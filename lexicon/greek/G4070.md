# G4070 πέρυσι

## pérysi

_(per'-oo-si | PEH-roo-see | PAY-ryoo-see)_

### Definition

adverb from G4009; the by-gone, i.e., (as noun) last year; + a year ago; 

- last year
- the year just past
  - for a year past, a year ago
