# G4414 πρωτοστάτης

## prōtostátēs

_(pro-tos-tat'-ace | proh-toh-STA-tase | proh-toh-STA-tase)_

### Definition

from G4413 and G2476; one standing first in the ranks, i.e., a captain (champion); ringleader; 

- one who stands in the front rank
  - a leader, chief, champion
