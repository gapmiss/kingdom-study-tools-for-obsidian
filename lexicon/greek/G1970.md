# G1970 ἐπιπνίγω

## epipnígō

_(ep-ee-pnee'-go | eh-pee-PNEE-goh | ay-pee-PNEE-goh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G4155; to throttle upon, i.e., (figuratively) overgrow; choke; 

- to throttle upon, to overgrow, to choke
