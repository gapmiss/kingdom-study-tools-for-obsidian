# G4170 πολεμέω

## poleméō

_(pol-em-eh'-o | poh-leh-MEH-oh | poh-lay-MAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G4171; to be (engaged) in warfare, i.e., to battle (literally or figuratively); fight, (make) war; 

- to war, carry on war
- to fight
