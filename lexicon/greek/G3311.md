# G3311 μερισμός

## merismós

_(mer-is-mos' | meh-rees-MOSE | may-rees-MOSE)_

### Definition

from G3307; a separation or distribution; dividing asunder, gift; 

- a division, partition
  - distribution (of various kinds)
- a separation
  - so far as to cleave asunder or separate
