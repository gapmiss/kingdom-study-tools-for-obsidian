# G3562 νουνεχῶς

## nounechōs

_(noon-ekh-oce' | noo-neh-HOSE | noo-nay-HOSE)_

### Definition

adverb from a comparative of the accusative case of G3563 and G2192; in a mind-having way, i.e., prudently; discreetly; 

- wisely, discreetly, prudently
