# G2406 ἱεράτευμα

## hieráteuma

_(hee-er-at'-yoo-mah | hee-eh-RA-tev-ma | ee-ay-RA-tave-ma)_

### Definition

from G2407; the priestly fraternity, i.e., sacerdotal order (figuratively); priesthood; 

- the office of a priest
- the order or body of priests
