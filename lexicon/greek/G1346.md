# G1346 δικαίως

## dikaíōs

_(dik-ah'-yoce | thee-KEH-ose | thee-KAY-ose)_

### Definition

adverb from G1342; equitably; justly, (to) righteously(-ness); 

- just, agreeably to right
- properly, as is right
- uprightly, agreeable to the law of rectitude
