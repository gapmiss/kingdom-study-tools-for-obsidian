## ploutízō

_(ploo-tid'-zo | ploo-TEE-zoh | ploo-TEE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G4149; to make wealthy (figuratively); en- (make) rich; 

- to make rich, enrich
  - of spiritual riches
- to be richly furnished
