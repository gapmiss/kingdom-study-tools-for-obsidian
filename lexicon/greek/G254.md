# G254 ἅλυσις

## hálysis

_(hal'-oo-sis | HA-loo-sees | A-lyoo-sees)_

### Definition

of uncertain derivation; a fetter or manacle; bonds, chain; 

- a chain, bond by which the body or any part of it (hands, feet) is bound
