# G2084 ἑτερόγλωσσος

## heteróglōssos

_(het-er-og'-loce-sos | heh-teh-ROH-glose-sose | ay-tay-ROH-glose-sose)_

### Definition

from G2087 and G1100; other- tongued, i.e., a foreigner; man of other tongue; 

- one who speaks a foreign language
