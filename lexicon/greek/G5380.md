# G5380 φιλόνεικος

## philóneikos

_(fil-on'-i-kos | feel-OH-nee-kose | feel-OH-nee-kose)_

### Definition

from G5384 and νεῖκος (a quarrel; probably akin to G3534); fond of strife, i.e., disputatious; contentious; 

- fond of strife, contentious
  - in a good sense, emulous
