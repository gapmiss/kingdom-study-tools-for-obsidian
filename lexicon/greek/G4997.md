# G4997 σωφροσύνη

## sōphrosýnē

_(so-fros-oo'-nay | soh-froh-SOO-nay | soh-froh-SYOO-nay)_

### Definition

from G4998; soundness of mind, i.e., (literally) sanity or (figuratively) self-control; soberness, sobriety; 

- soundness of mind
- self-control, sobriety
