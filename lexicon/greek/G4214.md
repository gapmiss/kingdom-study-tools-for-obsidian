# G4214 πόσος

## pósos

_(pos'-os | POH-sose | POH-sose)_

### Definition

from an absolute πός (who, what) and G3739; interrogative pronoun (of amount) how much (large, long or (plural) many); how great (long, many), what; 

- how great
- how much
- how many
