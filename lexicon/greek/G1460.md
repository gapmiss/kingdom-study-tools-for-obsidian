# G1460 ἐγκατοικέω

## enkatoikéō

_(eng-kat-oy-keh'-o | eng-ka-too-KEH-oh | ayng-ka-too-KAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G2730; to settle down in a place, i.e., reside; dwell among; 

- to dwell among
