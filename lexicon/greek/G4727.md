# G4727 στενάζω

## stenázō

_(sten-ad'-zo | steh-NA-zoh | stay-NA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G4728; to make (intransitively, be) in straits, i.e., (by implication) to sigh, murmur, pray inaudibly; with grief, groan, grudge, sigh; 

- a sigh, to groan
