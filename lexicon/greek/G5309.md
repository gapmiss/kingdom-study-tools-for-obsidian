# G5309 ὑψηλοφρονέω

## hypsēlophronéō

_(hoop-say-lo-fron-eh'-o | hoo-psay-loh-froh-NEH-oh | yoo-psay-loh-froh-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of G5308 and G5424; to be lofty in mind, i.e., arrogant; be highminded; 

- to be high minded, proud
