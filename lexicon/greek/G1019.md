# G1019 βραδύνω

## bradýnō

_(brad-oo'-no | vra-THOO-noh | vra-THYOO-noh)_

### Definition

from G1021; to delay; be slack, tarry; 

- to delay, be slow
  - to render slowly, retard
  - to be long, to tarry, loiter
