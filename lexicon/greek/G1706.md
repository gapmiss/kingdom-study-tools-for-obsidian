# G1706 ἐμπίπτω

## empíptō

_(em-pip'-to | em-PEE-ptoh | ame-PEE-ptoh)_

### Definition

from G1722 and G4098; to fall on, i.e., (literally) to be entrapped by, or (figuratively) be overwhelmed with; fall among (into); 

- to fall into
  - to fall among robbers
  - fall into one's power
