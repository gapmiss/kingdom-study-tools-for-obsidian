# G2017 ἐπιφαύω

## epiphaúō

_(ep-ee-fow'-o | eh-pee-FEV-oh | ay-pee-FA-oh)_

### Definition

a form of G2014; to illuminate (figuratively); give light; 

- to shine upon
  - Christ will pour upon you the divine truth as the sun gives light to men aroused from sleep
