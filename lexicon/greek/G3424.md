# G3424 μογιλάλος

## mogilálos

_(mog-il-al'-os | moh-gee-LA-lose | moh-gee-LA-lose)_

### Definition

from G3425 and G2980; hardly talking, i.e., dumb (tongue-tied); having an impediment in his speech; 

- speaking with difficulty
