# G5597 ψώχω

## psṓchō

_(pso'-kho | PSOH-hoh | PSOH-hoh)_

### Definition

prolongation from the same base as G5567; to triturate, i.e., (by analogy) to rub out (kernels from husks with the fingers or hand); rub; 

- to rub, to rub to pieces
