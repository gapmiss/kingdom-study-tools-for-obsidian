# G2706 καταφρονέω

## kataphronéō

_(kat-af-ron-eh'-o | ka-ta-froh-NEH-oh | ka-ta-froh-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G5426; to think against, i.e., disesteem; despise; 

- to contemn, despise, disdain, think little or nothing of
