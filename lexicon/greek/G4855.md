# G4855 συμφύω

## symphýō

_(soom-foo'-o | soom-FOO-oh | syoom-FYOO-oh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G5453; passive, to grow jointly; spring up with; 

- to cause to grow together
- to grow together, grow with
