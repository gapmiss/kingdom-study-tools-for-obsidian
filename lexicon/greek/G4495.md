# G4495 ῥιπτέω

## rhiptéō

_(hrip-teh'-o | ree-PTEH-oh | ree-PTAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a derivative of G4496; to toss up; cast off; 

- to cast, throw
- throw down
- to cast forward or before
- to set down (with the suggestion of haste and want of care)
- to throw to the ground, prostrate
