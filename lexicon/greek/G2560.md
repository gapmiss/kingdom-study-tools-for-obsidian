# G2560 κακῶς

## kakōs

_(kak-oce' | ka-KOSE | ka-KOSE)_

### Definition

from G2556; badly (physically or morally); amiss, diseased, evil, grievously, miserably, sick, sore; 

- miserable, to be ill
- improperly, wrongly
- to speak ill of, revile, one
