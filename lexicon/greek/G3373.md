# G3373 μηκύνω

## mēkýnō

_(may-koo'-no | may-KOO-noh | may-KYOO-noh)_

### Definition

from G3372; to lengthen, i.e., (middle voice) to enlarge; grow up; 

- to make long, to lengthen
- in the Bible twice of plants, to cause to grow, increase
