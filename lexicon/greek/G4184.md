# G4184 πολύσπλαγχνος

## polýsplanchnos

_(pol-oo'-splankh-nos | poh-LOO-splahng-hnose | poh-LYOO-splahng-hnose)_

### Definition

from G4183 and G4698 (figuratively); extremely compassionate; very pitiful; 

- full of pity, very kind
