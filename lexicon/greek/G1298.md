# G1298 διαταράσσω

## diatarássō

_(dee-at-ar-as'-so | thee-ah-ta-RAHS-soh | thee-ah-ta-RAHS-soh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G5015; to disturb wholly, i.e., agitate (with alarm); trouble; 

- to agitate greatly, trouble greatly
