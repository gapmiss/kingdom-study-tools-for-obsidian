# G1245 διακαθαρίζω

## diakatharízō

_(dee-ak-ath-ar-id'-zo | thee-ah-ka-tha-REE-zoh | thee-ah-ka-tha-REE-zoh)_

### Definition

from G1223 and G2511; to cleanse perfectly, i.e., (specially) winnow; thoroughly purge; 

- to cleanse thoroughly
