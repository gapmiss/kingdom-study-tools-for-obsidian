# G65 ἀγριέλαιος

## agriélaios

_(ag-ree-el'-ah-yos | ah-gree-EH-leh-ose | ah-gree-A-lay-ose)_

### Definition

from G0066 and G1636; an oleaster; olive tree (which is) wild; 

- of or belonging to the oleaster or wild olive
- the oleaster, wild olive tree
