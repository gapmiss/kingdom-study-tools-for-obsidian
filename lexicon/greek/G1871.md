# G1871 ἐπαιτέω

## epaitéō

_(ep-ahee-teh'-o | ep-eh-TEH-oh | ape-ay-TAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G1909 and G0154; to ask for; beg; 

- to ask besides, ask for more
- to ask again and again, importunately
- to beg, to ask alms
