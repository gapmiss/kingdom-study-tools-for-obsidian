# G3714 ὀρεινός

## oreinós

_(or-i-nos | oh-ree-NOSE | oh-ree-NOSE)_

### Definition

from G3735; mountainous, i.e., (feminine by implication, of G5561) the Highlands (of Judaea); hill country; 

- mountainous, hilly
- the mountain district, hill country
