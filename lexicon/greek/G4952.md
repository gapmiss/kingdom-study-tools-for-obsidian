# G4952 συσπαράσσω

## sysparássō

_(soos-par-as'-so | soo-spa-RAHS-soh | syoo-spa-RAHS-soh)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G4682; to rend completely, i.e., (by analogy) to convulse violently; throw down; 

- to convulse completely
