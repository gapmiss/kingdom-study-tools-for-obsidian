# G1636 ἐλαία

## elaía

_(el-ah'-yah | eh-LEH-ah | ay-LAY-ah)_

### Definition

feminine of a presumed derivative from an obsolete primary; an olive (the tree or the fruit); olive (berry, tree); 

- an olive tree
- an olive, the fruit of an olive tree
