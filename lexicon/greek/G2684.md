# G2684 κατασκοπέω

## kataskopéō

_(kat-as-kop-eh'-o | ka-ta-skoh-PEH-oh | ka-ta-skoh-PAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G2685; to be a sentinel, i.e., to inspect insidiously; spy out; 

- to inspect, view closely, in order to spy out and plot against
