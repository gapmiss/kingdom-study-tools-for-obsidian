# G3926 παρενοχλέω

## parenochléō

_(par-en-okh-leh'-o | pa-reh-noh-HLEH-oh | pa-ray-noh-HLAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G3844 and G1776; to harass further, i.e., annoy; trouble; 

- to cause trouble in a matter, to trouble, annoy
