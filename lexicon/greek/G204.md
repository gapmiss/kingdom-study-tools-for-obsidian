# G204 ἀκρογωνιαῖος

## akrogōniaîos

_(ak-rog-o-nee-ah'-yos | ah-kroh-goh-nee-EH-ose | ah-kroh-goh-nee-A-ose)_

### Definition

from G0206 and G1137; belonging to the extreme corner; chief corner; 

- placed at an extreme corner, the corner foundation stone
