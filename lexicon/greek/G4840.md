# G4840 συμπάρειμι

## sympáreimi

_(soom-par'-i-mee | soom-PA-ree-mee | syoom-PA-ree-mee)_

### Definition

from G4862 and G3918; to be at hand together, i.e., now present; be here present with; 

- to be present together
