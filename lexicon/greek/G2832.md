# G2832 Κλωπᾶς

## Klōpâs

_(klo-pas' | kloh-PAHS | kloh-PAHS)_

### Definition

my exchanges; of Chaldee origin (corresponding to H0256); Clopas, an Israelite; Cleophas; 

- the father of James the less, the husband of Mary the sister of the mother of Jesus
