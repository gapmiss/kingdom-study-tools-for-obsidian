# G4324 προσαπειλέω

## prosapeiléō

_(pros-ap-i-leh'-o | prose-ah-pee-LEH-oh | prose-ah-pee-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G4314 and G0546; to menace additionally; i.e., threaten further; 

- to add threats, threaten further
