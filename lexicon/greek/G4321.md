# G4321 προσαναλίσκω

## prosanalískō

_(pros-an-al-is'-ko | prose-ah-na-LEE-skoh | prose-ah-na-LEE-skoh)_

### Definition

from G4314 and G0355; to expend further; spend; 

- to expend besides (i.e., upon physicians)
