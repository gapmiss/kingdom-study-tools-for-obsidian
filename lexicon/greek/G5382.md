# G5382 φιλόξενος

## philóxenos

_(fil-ox'-en-os | feel-OH-kseh-nose | feel-OH-ksay-nose)_

### Definition

from G5384 and G3581; fond of guests, i.e., hospitable; given to (lover of, use) hospitality; 

- hospitable, generous to guests
