# G3996 πενθέω

## penthéō

_(pen-theh'-o | pen-THEH-oh | pane-THAY-oh)_

### Definition

from G3997; to grieve (the feeling or the act); mourn, (be-)wail; 

- to mourn
- to mourn for, lament one
