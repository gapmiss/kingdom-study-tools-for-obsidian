# G2703 καταφεύγω

## katapheúgō

_(kat-af-yoo'-go | ka-ta-FEV-goh | ka-ta-FAVE-goh)_

### Definition

from G2596 and G5343; to flee down (away); flee; 

- to flee away, flee for refuge
