# H6403 פָּלַט / פלט

## pâlaṭ

_(paw-lat' | paw-LAHT | pa-LAHT)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to slip out, i.e., escape; causatively, to deliver; calve, carry away safe, deliver, (cause to) escape; v

- to escape, save, deliver, slip away
  - (Qal) to escape
  - (Piel)
    - to bring into security, deliver
    - to cause to escape, cast forth
    - to be delivered
    - to slip away
  - (Hiphil) to bring into security, bring to safety
