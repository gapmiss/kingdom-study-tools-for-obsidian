# H5207 נִיחוֹחַ / ניחוח

## nîychôwach

_(nee-kho'-akh | nee-HOH-ah | nee-HOH-ak)_

## Definition

or נִיחֹחַ; from H5117; properly, restful, i.e., pleasant; abstractly, delight; sweet (odour); n-m

- soothing, quieting, tranquillising
