# H8302 שִׁרְיוֹן / שריון

## shiryôwn

_(shir-yone' | shir-YONE | sheer-YONE)_

## Definition

or שִׁרְיֹן; and שִׁרְיָן; also (feminine) שִׁרְיָה; and שִׁרְיֹנָה; from H8281 in the original sense of turning; a corslet (as if twisted); breastplate, coat of mail, habergeon, harness; n

- body armour
- a weapon
  - perhaps a lance, javelin
