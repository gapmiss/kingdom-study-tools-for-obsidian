# H4600 מָעַךְ / מעך

## mâʻak

_(maw-ak' | maw-AK | ma-AK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to press, i.e., to pierce, emasculate, handle; bruised, stuck, be pressed; v

- to press, squeeze
  - (Qal) to be crushed
  - (Pual) to be handled, be pressed
