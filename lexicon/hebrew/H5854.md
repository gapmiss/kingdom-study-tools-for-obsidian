# H5854 עַטְרוֹת בֵּית יוֹאָב / עטרות בית יואב

## ʻAṭrôwth bêyth Yôwʼâb

_(at-roth' bayth yoawb' | at-ROTE bate yoh-AWB | at-ROTE bate yoh-AV)_

## Definition

the crowns of; from the same as H5852 and H1004 and H3097; crowns of the house of Joab; Atroth-beth-Joab, a place in Palestine; Ataroth the house of Joab; n-pr-loc

- the house of Joab, a place in Judah
