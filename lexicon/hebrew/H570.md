# H570 אֶמֶשׁ / אמש

## ʼemesh

_(eh'-mesh | eh-MESH | eh-MESH)_

## Definition

time past, i.e; yesterday or last night; former time, yesterday(-night); adv

- yesterday, last night
- recently (figuratively)
