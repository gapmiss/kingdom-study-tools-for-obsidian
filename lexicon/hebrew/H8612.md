# H8612 תֹּפֶת / תפת

## Tôpheth

_(to'-feth | toh-PET | toh-FET)_

## Definition

place of fire; the same as H8611; Topheth, a place near Jerusalem; Tophet, Topheth; n-pr-loc

- a place in the southeast end of the valley of the son of Hinnom south of Jerusalem. Same as H8613
