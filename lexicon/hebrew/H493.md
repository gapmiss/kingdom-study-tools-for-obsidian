# H493 אֶלְנַעַם / אלנעם

## ʼElnaʻam

_(el-nah'-am | el-na-AM | el-na-AM)_

## Definition

God is delight, God is pleasantness; from H0410 and H5276; God (is his) delight; Elnaam, an Israelite; Elnaam; n-pr-m

- the father of two of David's mighty warriors
