# H7097 קָצֶה / קצה

## qâtseh

_(kaw-tseh' | kaw-TSEH | ka-TSEH)_

## Definition

or (negative only) קֵצֶה; from H7096; (used in a great variety of applications and idioms; compare H7093); an extremity; × after, border, brim, brink, edge, end, (in-)finite, frontier, outmost coast, quarter, shore, (out-)side, × some, ut(-ter-)most (part); n-m

- end, extremity
  - end, mouth, extremity
  - border, outskirts
  - the whole (condensed term for what is included within extremities)
  - at the end of (a certain time)
