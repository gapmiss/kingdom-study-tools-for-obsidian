# H8599 תַּפּוּחַ / תפוח

## Tappûwach

_(tap-poo'-akh | tahp-POO-ah | ta-POO-ak)_

## Definition

the apple city; the same as H8598; Tappuach, the name of two places in Palestine; Tappuah; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a Judaite of the family of Caleb, son of Hebron
- (n pr loc) a town in the lowland of Judah
- a town on the border in Ephraim
- a territory within the boundaries of Manasseh
