# H6438 פִּנָּה / פנה

## pinnâh

_(pin-naw' | pin-NAW | pee-NA)_

## Definition

feminine of H6434; an angle; by implication, a pinnacle; figuratively, a chieftain; bulwark, chief, corner, stay, tower; n-f

- corner
  - corner (of square objects)
  - corner (figurative for ruler or chief)
