# H6244 עָשֵׁשׁ / עשש

## ʻâshêsh

_(aw-shaysh' | aw-SHAYSH | ah-SHAYSH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; probably to shrink, i.e., fail; be consumed; v

- (Qal) to waste away, fail
