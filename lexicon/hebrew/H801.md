# H801 אִשָּׁה / אשה

## ʼishshâh

_(ish-shaw' | ish-SHAW | ee-SHA)_

## Definition

the same as H0800, but used in a liturgical sense; properly, a burnt-offering; but occasionally of any sacrifice; (offering, sacrifice), (made) by fire; n-m

- burnt-offering, offering made by fire, fire offering
