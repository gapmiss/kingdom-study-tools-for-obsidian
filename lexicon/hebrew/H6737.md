# H6737 צָיַר / ציר

## tsâyar

_(tsaw-yar' | tsaw-YAHR | tsa-YAHR)_

## Definition

a denominative from H6735 in the sense of ambassador; to make an errand, i.e., betake oneself; make as if...had been ambassador; v

- (Hithpael) to supply oneself with provisions, take as one's provision
- to act as an ambassador
