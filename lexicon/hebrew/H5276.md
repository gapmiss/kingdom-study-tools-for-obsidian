# H5276 נָעֵם / נעם

## nâʻêm

_(naw-ame' | naw-AME | na-AME)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be agreeable (literally or figuratively); pass in beauty, be delight, be pleasant, be sweet; v

- (Qal) to be pleasant, be beautiful, be sweet, be delightful, be lovely
