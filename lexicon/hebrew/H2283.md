# H2283 חָגָא / חגא

## châgâʼ

_(khaw-gaw' | haw-ɡAW | ha-ɡA)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to revolve (compare H2287); properly, vertigo, i.e., (figuratively) fear; terror; n-f

- terror, a reeling (in terror)
