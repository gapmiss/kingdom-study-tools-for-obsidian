# H7477 רָעַל / רעל

## râʻal

_(raw-al' | raw-AL | ra-AL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to reel, i.e., (figuratively) to brandish; terribly shake; v

- to quiver, shake, reel
  - (Hophal) to be made to quiver or shake
