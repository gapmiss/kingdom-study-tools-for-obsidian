# H6467 פֹּעַל / פעל

## pôʻal

_(po'-al | poh-AL | poh-AL)_

## Definition

from H6466; an act or work (concretely); act, deed, do, getting, maker, work; n-m

- work, deed, doing
  - deed, thing done
  - work, thing made
  - wages of work
  - acquisition (of treasure)
