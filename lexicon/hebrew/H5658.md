# H5658 עַבְדוֹן / עבדון

## ʻAbdôwn

_(ab-dohn' | ab-DONE | av-DONE)_

## Definition

servile; from H5647; servitude; Abdon, the name of a place in Palestine and of four Israelites; Abdon; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a judge of Israel in the time of the judges; perhaps the same as 'Bedan'
- a Benjamite, son of Shashak
- a Gibeonite, son of Jehiel and a grandson of Gideon
- son of Micah in the time of king Josiah of Judah; also 'Achbor'
- (n pr loc) a city in Asher allotted to the Gershonite Levites; located 10 miles (16 km) north of Accho; modern 'Abdeh'

Compare H5683.
