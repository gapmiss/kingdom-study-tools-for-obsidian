# H7420 רֹמַח / רמח

## rômach

_(ro'-makh | roh-MA | roh-MAHK)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to hurl; a lance (as thrown); especially the iron point; buckler, javelin, lancet, spear; n-m

- spear, lance
