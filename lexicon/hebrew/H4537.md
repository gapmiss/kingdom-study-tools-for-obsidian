# H4537 מָסַךְ / מסך

## mâçak

_(maw-sak' | maw-SAHK | ma-SAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to mix, especially wine (with spices); mingle; v

- to mix, mingle, produce by mixing
  - (Qal) to pour, mix
