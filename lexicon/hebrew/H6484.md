# H6484 פָּצַר / פצר

## pâtsar

_(paw-tsar' | paw-TSAHR | pa-TSAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to peck at, i.e., (figuratively) stun or dull; press, urge, stubbornness; v

- to press, push
  - (Qal) to push, press
  - (Hiphil) be insolent, display pushing (arrogance, presumption)
