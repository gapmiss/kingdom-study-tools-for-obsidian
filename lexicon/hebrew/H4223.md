# H4223 מְחָא / מחא

## mᵉchâʼ

_(mekh-aw' | meh-HAW | meh-HA)_

## Definition

corresponding to H4222; to strike in pieces; also to arrest; specifically to impale; hang, smite, stay; v

- to strike, smite, kill
  - (P'al) to strike
  - (Pael) to hinder
  - (Ithp'al) to allow to be stricken
