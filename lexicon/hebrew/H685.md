# H685 אֶצְעָדָה / אצעדה

## ʼetsʻâdâh

_(ets-aw-daw' | ets-aw-DAW | ets-ah-DA)_

## Definition

a variation from H6807; properly, a step-chain; by analogy, a bracelet; bracelet, chain; n-f

- armlet, bracelet, ankle chain
