# H530 אֱמוּנָה / אמונה

## ʼĕmûwnâh

_(em-oo-naw' | eh-moo-NAW | ay-moo-NA)_

## Definition

or (shortened) אֱמֻנָה; feminine of H0529; literally firmness; figuratively security; morally fidelity; faith(-ful (man), -ly, -ness), set office, stability, steady, truly, truth, verily; n-f

- firmness, fidelity, steadfastness, steadiness
