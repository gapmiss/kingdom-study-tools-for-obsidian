# H8231 שָׁפַר / שפר

## shâphar

_(shaw-far' | shaw-PAHR | sha-FAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to glisten, i.e., (figuratively) be (causatively, make) fair; × goodly; v

- to be pleasing, be beautiful, be fair, be comely, be bright, glisten
  - (Qal) to be beautiful
