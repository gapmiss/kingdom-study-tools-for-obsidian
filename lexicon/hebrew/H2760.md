# H2760 חָרַךְ / חרך

## chârak

_(khaw-rak' | haw-RAHK | ha-RAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to braid (i.e., to entangle or snare) or catch (game) in a net; roast; v

- (Qal) to set in motion, start
- to roast
