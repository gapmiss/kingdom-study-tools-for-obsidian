# H5141 נֶזֶם / נזם

## nezem

_(neh'-zem | neh-DZEM | neh-ZEM)_

## Definition

from an unused root of uncertain meaning; a nose-ring; earring, jewel; n-m

- ring, nose ring, earring
  - nose ring (woman's ornament)
  - earring (ornament of men or women)
