# H7520 רָצַד / רצד

## râtsad

_(raw-tsad' | raw-TSAHD | ra-TSAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; probably to look askant, i.e., (figuratively) be jealous; leap; v

- (Piel) to watch stealthily or with envious hostility, watch with enmity, watch with envy
