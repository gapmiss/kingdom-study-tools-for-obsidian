# H5129 נוֹעַדְיָה / נועדיה

## Nôwʻadyâh

_(no-ad-yaw' | noh-ad-YAW | noh-ad-YA)_

## Definition

meeting with Jehovah; from H3259 and H3050; convened of Jah; Noadjah, the name of an Israelite, and a false prophetess; Noadiah; n-pr-m n-pr-f

- (n pr m) a Levite, son of Binnui, who weighed the vessels of gold and silver belonging to the temple which were brought back from Babylon
- (n pr f) a prophetess who joined Sanballat and Tobiah in their attempt to intimidate Nehemiah
