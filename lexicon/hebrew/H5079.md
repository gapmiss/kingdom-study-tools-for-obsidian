# H5079 נִדָּה / נדה

## niddâh

_(nid-daw' | nid-DAW | nee-DA)_

## Definition

from H5074; properly, rejection; by implication, impurity, especially personal (menstruation) or moral (idolatry, incest); × far, filthiness, × flowers, menstruous (woman), put apart, × removed (woman), separation, set apart, unclean(-ness, thing, with filthiness); n-f

- impurity, filthiness, menstruous, set apart
  - impurity
    - of ceremonial impurity
    - of menstruation
  - impure thing (figuratively)
    - of idolatry, immorality
