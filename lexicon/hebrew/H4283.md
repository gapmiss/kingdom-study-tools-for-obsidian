# H4283 מׇחֳרָת / מחרת

## mochŏrâth

_(mokh-or-awth' | moh-hoh-RAWT | moh-hoh-RAHT)_

## Definition

or מׇחֳרָתָם; (1 Samuel 30:17), feminine from the same as H4279; the morrow or (adverbially) tomorrow; morrow, next day; n-f

- the morrow, the day after
