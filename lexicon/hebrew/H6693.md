# H6693 צוּק / צוק

## tsûwq

_(tsook | tsook | tsook)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to compress, i.e., (figuratively) oppress, distress; constrain, distress, lie sore, (op-)press(-or), straiten; v

- (Hiphil) to constrain, press, bring into straits, straiten, oppress
