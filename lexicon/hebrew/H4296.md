# H4296 מִטָּה / מטה

## miṭṭâh

_(mit-taw' | mit-TAW | mee-TA)_

## Definition

from H5186; a bed (as extended) for sleeping or eating; by analogy, a sofa, litter or bier; bed(-chamber), bier; n-f

- couch, bed, bier
