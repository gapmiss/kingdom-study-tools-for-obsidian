# H1085 בִּלְדַּד / בלדד

## Bildad

_(bil-dad' | bil-DAHD | beel-DAHD)_

## Definition

confusing (by mingling) love; of uncertain derivation; Bildad, one of Job's friends; Bildad; n-pr-m

- the second friend of Job
