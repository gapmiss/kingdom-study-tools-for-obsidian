# H7833 שָׁחַק / שחק

## shâchaq

_(shaw-khak' | shaw-HAHK | sha-HAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to comminate (by trituration or attrition); beat, wear; v

- to rub away, beat fine, pulverise
  - (Qal)
    - to rub away
      - of incense, stones
      - of enemies (figuratively)
