# H4207 מַזְלֵג / מזלג

## mazlêg

_(maz-layg' | mahdz-LAɡE | mahz-LAɡE)_

## Definition

or (feminine) מִזְלָגָה; from an unused root meaning to draw up; a fork; fleshhook; n-m

- three-pronged fork
  - a sacrificial implement
