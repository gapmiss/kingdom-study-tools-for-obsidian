# H6043 עֲנָיָה / עניה

## ʻĂnâyâh

_(an-aw-yaw' | uh-naw-YAW | uh-na-YA)_

## Definition

Jehovah has answered; from H6030; Jah has answered; Anajah, the name of two Israelites; Anaiah; n-pr-m

- a priest who helped Ezra and signed the covenant with Nehemiah
