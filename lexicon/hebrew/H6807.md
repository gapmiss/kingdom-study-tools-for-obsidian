## tsᵉʻâdâh

_(tseh-aw-daw' | tseh-aw-DAW | tseh-ah-DA)_

## Definition

feminine of H6806; a march; (concretely) an (ornamental) ankle-chain; going, ornament of the legs; n-f

- marching
- armlet, anklet, stepping chains
