# H5605 סָפַף / ספף

## çâphaph

_(saw-faf' | saw-PAHP | sa-FAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to snatch away, i.e., terminate; but used only as denominative from H5592 (in the sense of a vestibule); to wait at the threshold; be a doorkeeper; v

- (Hithpoel) to stand at or guard the threshold
