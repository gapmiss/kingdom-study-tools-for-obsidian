# H4242 מְחִיר / מחיר

## mᵉchîyr

_(mekk-eer' | meh-HEER | meh-HEER)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to buy; price, payment, wages; gain, hire, price, sold, worth; n-m

- price, hire
  - price
  - hire, reward, gain
