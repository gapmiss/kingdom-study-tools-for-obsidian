# H6385 פָּלַג / פלג

## pâlag

_(paw-lag' | paw-LAHɡ | pa-LAHɡ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to split (literally or figuratively); divide; v

- to divide, split
  - (Niphal) to be split, be divided
  - (Piel)
    - to split, cleave
    - to divide
