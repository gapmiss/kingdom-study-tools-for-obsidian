# H1779 דִּין / דין

## dîyn

_(deen | deen | deen)_

## Definition

or (Job 19:29) דּוּן; from H1777; judgement (the suit, justice, sentence or tribunal); by implication also strife; cause, judgement, plea, strife; n-m

- judgment
  - judgment
  - cause, plea
  - condemnation, judgment
  - dispute, legal suit, strife
  - government
