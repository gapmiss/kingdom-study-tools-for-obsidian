# H5413 נָתַךְ / נתך

## nâthak

_(naw-thak' | naw-TAHK | na-TAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to flow forth (literally or figuratively); by implication, to liquify; drop, gather (together), melt, pour (forth, out); v

- to pour out or forth, drop (or rain), be poured, be poured out, be melted, be molten
  - (Qal) to pour forth
  - (Niphal) to be poured, be poured out
  - (Hiphil) to pour out, melt
  - (Hophal) to be melted
