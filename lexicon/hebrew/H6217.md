# H6217 עָשׁוּק / עשוק

## ʻâshûwq

_(aw-shook' | aw-SHOOK | ah-SHOOK)_

## Definition

or עָשֻׁק; passive participle of H6231; used in plural masculine as abstractly, tyranny; oppressed(-ion); n-m

- oppression, extortion

## Note

Could be translated as "burdened."
