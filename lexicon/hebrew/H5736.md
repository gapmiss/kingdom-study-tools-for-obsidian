# H5736 עֲדַף / עדף

## ʻădaph

_(aw-daf' | uh-DAHP | uh-DAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be (causatively have) redundant; be more, odd number, be (have) over (and above), overplus, remain; v

- to remain over, be in excess
  - (Qal)
    - to be in excess
      - excess (subst)
      - surplus (participle)
  - (Hiphil) to have a surplus
