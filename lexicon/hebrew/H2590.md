# H2590 חָנַט / חנט

## chânaṭ

_(khaw-nat' | haw-NAHT | ha-NAHT)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to spice; by implication, to embalm; also to ripen; embalm, put forth; v

- (v) to embalm, spice, make spicy
  - (Qal)
    - to spice, make spicy
    - to embalm
- to mature (i.e., to ripen)
- (n m pl abst) embalming
