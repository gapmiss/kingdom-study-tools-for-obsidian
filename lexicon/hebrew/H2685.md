# H2685 חֲצַף / חצף

## chătsaph

_(khats-af' | huh-TSAHP | huh-TSAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to shear or cut close; figuratively, to be severe; hasty, be urgent; v

- (Aphel) to be urgent, harsh, show insolence
