# H6429 פְּלֶשֶׁת / פלשת

## Pᵉlesheth

_(pel-eh'-sheth | peh-leh-SHET | peh-leh-SHET)_

## Definition

land of sojourners; from H6428; rolling, i.e., migratory; Pelesheth, a region of Syria; Palestina, Palestine, Philistia, Philistines; n

- the general territory on the west coast of Canaan or the entire country of Palestine
