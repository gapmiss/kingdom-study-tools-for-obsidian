# H6284 פָּאָה / פאה

## pâʼâh

_(paw-aw' | paw-AW | pa-AH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to puff, i.e., blow away; scatter into corners; v

- to cleave in pieces, break into pieces, shatter
  - (Hiphil) to cleave in pieces, dash to pieces
