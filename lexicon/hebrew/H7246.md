# H7246 רָבַךְ / רבך

## râbak

_(raw-bak' | raw-BAHK | ra-VAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to soak (bread in oil); baken, (that which is) fried; v

- to mix, stir
  - (Hophal) to be mixed
