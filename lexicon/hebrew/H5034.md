# H5034 נָבֵל / נבל

## nâbêl

_(naw-bale' | naw-BALE | na-VALE)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to wilt; generally, to fall away, fail, faint; disgrace, dishounour, lightly esteem, fade (away, -ing), fall (down, -ling, off), do foolishly, come to nought, × surely, make vile, wither; v

- to be senseless, be foolish
  - (Qal) to be foolish
  - (Piel)
    - to regard or treat as foolish
    - to treat with contempt
- to sink or drop down, languish, wither and fall, fade
  - (Qal)
    - to sink or drop down
    - to fall, wither and fall, fade
    - to droop
