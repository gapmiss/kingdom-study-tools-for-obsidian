# H1711 דָּגָה / דגה

## dâgâh

_(daw-gaw' | daw-ɡAW | da-ɡA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to move rapidly; used only as a denominative from H1709; to spawn, i.e., become numerous; grow; v

- (Qal) to multiply, increase
