# H908 בָּדָא / בדא

## bâdâʼ

_(baw-daw' | baw-DAW | ba-DA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; (figuratively) to invent; devise, feign; v

- to devise, contrive, invent (bad sense)
  - (Qal)
    - to devise
    - to contrive
