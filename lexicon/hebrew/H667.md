# H667 אֶפְרֹחַ / אפרח

## ʼephrôach

_(ef-ro'-akh | ep-ROH-ah | ef-ROH-ak)_

## Definition

from H6524 (in the sense of bursting the shell); the brood of a bird; young (one); n-m

- young, young one (of birds)
- young birds
