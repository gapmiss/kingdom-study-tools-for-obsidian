# H6322 פּוּל / פול

## Pûwl

_(pool | pool | pool)_

## Definition

distinguishing; of foreign origin; Pul, the name of an Assyrian king and of an Ethiopian tribe; Pul; n-pr-m

- the Babylonian name for Tiglath-pileser III, king of Assyria
