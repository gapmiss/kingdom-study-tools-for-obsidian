# H582 אֱנוֹשׁ / אנוש

## ʼĕnôwsh

_(en-oshe' | eh-NOHSH | ay-NOHSH)_

## Definition

from H0605; properly, a mortal (and thus differing from the more dignified H0120); hence, a man in general (singly or collectively); another, × (blood-)thirsty, certain, chap(-man); divers, fellow, × in the flower of their age, husband, (certain, mortal) man, people, person, servant, some (× of them), + stranger, those, + their trade; n-m

- man, mortal man, person, mankind
  - of an individual
  - men (collective)
  - man, mankind

## Note

It is often unexpressed in the English versions, especially when used in apposition with another word. Compare H0376.
