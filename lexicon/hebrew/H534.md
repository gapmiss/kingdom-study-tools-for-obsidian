# H534 אָמִיר / אמיר

## ʼâmîyr

_(aw-meer' | aw-MEER | ah-MEER)_

## Definition

apparently from H0559 (in the sense of self-exaltation); a summit (of a tree or mountain); bough, branch; n-m

- top, summit
  - of tree
  - of mountain
