# H6464 פָּעוּ / פעו

## Pâʻûw

_(paw-oo' | paw-OO | pa-OO)_

## Definition

bleating; or פָּעִי; from H6463; screaming; Pau or Pai, a place in Edom; Pai, Pau; n-pr-loc

- the capital of king Hadar of Edom; site uncertain
