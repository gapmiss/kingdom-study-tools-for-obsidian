# H162 אֲהָהּ / אהה

## ʼăhâhh

_(a-haw' | uh-HAW | uh-HA)_

## Definition

apparently a primitive word expressing pain exclamatorily; Oh!; ah, alas; inj

- alas!, oh!, ah!
