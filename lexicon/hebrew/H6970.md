# H6970 קוֹעַ / קוע

## Qôwaʻ

_(ko'-ah | KOH-ah | KOH-ah)_

## Definition

he-camel; probably from H6972 in the original sense of cutting off; curtailment; Koa, a region of Babylon; Koa; n-pr-loc

- a territory in Mesopotamia, east of the Tigris, on border of Elam and Media
