# H6255 עַשְׁתְּרֹת קַרְנַיִם / עשתרת קרנים

## ʻAshtᵉrôth Qarnayim

_(ash-ter-oth' kar-nah'-yim | ash-teh-ROTE kahr-na-YIM | ash-teh-ROTE kahr-na-YEEM)_

## Definition

Ashtoreth of the two horns, peaks; from H6252 and the dual of H7161; Ashtaroth of (the) double horns (a symbol of the deity); Ashteroth-Karnaim, a place East of the Jordan; Ashtoreth Karnaim; n-pr-loc

- a city in Bashan east of the Jordan given to Manasseh
  - same as H6252
