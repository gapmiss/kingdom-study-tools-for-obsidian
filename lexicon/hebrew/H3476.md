## yôsher

_(yo'-sher | yoh-SHER | yoh-SHER)_

## Definition

from H3474; the right; equity, meet, right, upright(-ness); n-m

- straightness, uprightness
  - straightness, evenness (moral implications)
  - rightness, uprightness
  - what is right, what is due
