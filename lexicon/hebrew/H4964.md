# H4964 מֶתֶג / מתג

## metheg

_(meh-theg | meh-TEɡ | meh-TEɡ)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to curb; a bit; bit, bridle; n-m

- bridle
  - bridle (for animals)
  - control, authority (figuratively)
