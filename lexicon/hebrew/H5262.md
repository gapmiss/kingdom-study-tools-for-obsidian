# H5262 נֶסֶךְ / נסך

## neçek

_(neh'-sek | neh-SEK | neh-SEK)_

## Definition

or נֵסֶךְ; from H5258; a libation; also a cast idol; cover, drink offering, molten image; n-m

- drink offering, libation, molten image, something poured out
  - drink offering
  - molten images
