# H7685 שָׂגָה / שגה

## sâgâh

_(saw-gaw' | saw-ɡAW | sa-ɡA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to enlarge (especially upward, also figuratively); grow (up), increase; v

- to grow, increase
  - (Qal) to grow (great)
  - (Hiphil) to increase
