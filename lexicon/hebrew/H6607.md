# H6607 פֶּתַח / פתח

## pethach

_(peh'-thakh | peh-TA | peh-TAHK)_

## Definition

from H6605; an opening (literally), i.e., door (gate) or entrance way; door, entering (in), entrance (-ry), gate, opening, place; n-m

- opening, doorway, entrance
