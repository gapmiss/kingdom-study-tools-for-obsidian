# H4643 מַעֲשֵׂר / מעשר

## maʻăsêr

_(mah-as-ayr' | ma-uh-SARE | ma-uh-SARE)_

## Definition

or מַעֲשַׂר; and (in plural) feminine מַעַשְׂרָה; from H6240; a tenth; especially a tithe; tenth (part), tithe(-ing); n-m

- tithe, tenth part
  - tenth part
  - tithe, payment of a tenth part
