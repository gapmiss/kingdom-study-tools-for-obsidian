# H1985 הִלֵּל / הלל

## Hillêl

_(hil-layl' | hil-LALE | hee-LALE)_

## Definition

praising; from H1984; praising (namely God); Hillel, an Israelite; Hillel; n-pr-m

- a native of Pirathon in Mount Ephraim, the father of Abdon the judge
