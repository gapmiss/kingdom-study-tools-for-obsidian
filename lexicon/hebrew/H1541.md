# H1541 גְּלָה / גלה

## gᵉlâh

_(ghel-aw' | ɡeh-LAW | ɡeh-LA)_

## Definition

or גְּלָא; (Aramaic), corresponding to H1540; to reveal, to carry away; bring over, carry away, reveal; v

- to reveal
  - (P'al) to reveal (secrets), be revealed
  - (Aphel) to take into exile
