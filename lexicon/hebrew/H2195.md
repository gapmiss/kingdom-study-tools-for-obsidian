# H2195 זַעַם / זעם

## zaʻam

_(zah'-am | dza-AM | za-AM)_

## Definition

from H2194; strictly froth at the mouth, i.e., (figuratively) fury (especially of God's displeasure with sin); angry, indignation, rage; n-m

- anger, indignation
