# H5740 עֵדֶר / עדר

## ʻÊder

_(ay'-der | ay-DER | ay-DER)_

## Definition

a flock; the same as H5739; Eder, the name of two places in Palestine; Edar, Eder; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a Merarite Levite in the time of David
- (n pr loc) a town in the extreme south of Judah on the border of Edom; site unknown
