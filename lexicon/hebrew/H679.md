# H679 אַצִּיל / אציל

## ʼatstsîyl

_(ats-tseel' | ats-SEEL | ah-TSEEL)_

## Definition

from H0680 (in its primary sense of uniting); a joint of the hand (i.e., knuckle); also (according to some) a party-wall (Ezekiel 41:8); (arm) hole, great; n-f

- joining, joint (such as elbow etc)
