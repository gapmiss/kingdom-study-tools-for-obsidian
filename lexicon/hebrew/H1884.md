## dᵉthâbâr

_(deth-aw-bawr' | deh-taw-BAWR | deh-ta-VAHR)_

## Definition

of Persian origin; meaning one skilled in law; a judge; counsellor; n-m

- lawyer, interpreter of decrees, judge
