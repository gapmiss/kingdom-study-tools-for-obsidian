# H771 אׇרְנָן / ארנן

## ʼOrnân

_(or-nawn' | ore-NAWN | ore-NAHN)_

## Definition

light was perpetuated, their fir trees; probably from H0766; strong; Ornan, a Jebusite; Ornan; n-pr-m

- a Jebusite who sold, to David, a threshing floor for an altar
