# H5727 עָדַן / עדן

## ʻâdan

_(aw-dan' | aw-DAHN | ah-DAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be soft or pleasant; figuratively and reflexively, to live voluptuously; delight self; v

- (Hithpael) to luxuriate, delight oneself
