# H283 אַחְיוֹ / אחיו

## ʼAchyôw

_(akh-yo' | ah-YOH | ak-YOH)_

## Definition

brotherly, fraternal; prolonged from H0251; brotherly; Achio, the name of three Israelites; Ahio; n-pr-m

- son of Abinadab who sheltered the ark
- a Benjamite
- another Benjamite of Saul's ancestry
