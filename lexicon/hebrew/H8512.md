# H8512 תֵּל אָבִיב / תל אביב

## Têl ʼÂbîyb

_(tale aw-beeb' | tale aw-BEEB | tale ah-VEEV)_

## Definition

mound of the flood; from H8510 and H0024; mound of green growth; Tel-Abib, a place in Chaldaea; Tel-abib; n-pr-loc

- a city in Babylon, the home of the prophet Ezekiel, located on the river Chebar which was probably a branch of the Euphrates
