# H860 אָתוֹן / אתון

## ʼâthôwn

_(aw-thone' | aw-TONE | ah-TONE)_

## Definition

probably from the same as H0386 (in the sense of patience); a female donkey (from its docility); (she) ass; n-f

- she-ass, she-donkey
