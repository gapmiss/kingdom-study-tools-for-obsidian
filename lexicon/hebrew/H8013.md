# H8013 שְׁלֹמוֹת / שלמות

## Shᵉlômôwth

_(shel-o-moth' | sheh-loh-MOTE | sheh-loh-MOTE)_

## Definition

peaceful; feminine plural of H7965; pacifications; Shelomoth, the name of two Israelites; Shelomith (from the margin), Shelomoth; n-pr-m

- a Gershonite Levite, son of Shimei in the time of David
- a Kohathite Levite of the sons of Izhar in the time of David
- a Gershonite Levite, son of Zichri in the time of David

Compare H8019.
