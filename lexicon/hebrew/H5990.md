# H5990 עַמִּיזָבָד / עמיזבד

## ʻAmmîyzâbâd

_(am-mee-zaw-bawd' | am-mee-dzaw-BAWD | ah-mee-za-VAHD)_

## Definition

my people have bestowed; from H5971 and H2064; people of endowment; Ammizabad, an Israelite; Ammizabad; n-pr-m

- son of Benaiah, who commanded the 3rd division of David's army
