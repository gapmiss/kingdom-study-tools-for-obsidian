# H3849 לֹג / לג

## lôg

_(lohg | loɡe | loɡe)_

## Definition

from an unused root apparently meaning to deepen or hollow (like H3537); a log or measure for liquids; log (of oil); n-m

- log
  - a liquid measure equal to about one half litre
