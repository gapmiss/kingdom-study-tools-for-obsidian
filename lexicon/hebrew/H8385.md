# H8385 תַּאֲנָה / תאנה

## taʼănâh

_(tah-an-aw' | ta-uh-NAW | ta-uh-NA)_

## Definition

or תֹּאֲנָה; from H0579; an opportunity or (subjectively) purpose; occasion; n-f

- occasion, time of heat or oestrous or copulation, sexual drive (of animal)
- occasion, opportunity (for a quarrel)
