# H7713 שְׂדֵרָה / שדרה

## sᵉdêrâh

_(sed-ay-raw' | seh-day-RAW | seh-day-RA)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to regulate; a row, i.e., rank (of soldiers), story (of rooms); board, range; n-f

- row, rank (of soldiers)
  - rows, ranks
  - technical term of building
    - meaning unknown
