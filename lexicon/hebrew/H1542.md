# H1542 גִּלֹה / גלה

## Gilôh

_(ghee-lo' | ɡih-LOH | ɡee-LOH)_

## Definition

exile; or (fully) גִּילֹה; from H1540; open; Giloh, a place in Palestine; Giloh; n-pr-loc

- a city in the mountains of Judah, home town of Ahithophel
