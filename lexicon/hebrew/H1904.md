# H1904 הָגָר / הגר

## Hâgâr

_(haw-gawr' | haw-ɡAWR | ha-ɡAHR)_

## Definition

flight; of uncertain (perhaps foreign) derivation; Hagar, the mother of Ishmael; Hagar; n-pr-f

- Sarah's Egyptian slave girl, Abraham's concubine, Ishmael's mother
