# H2348 חוֹף / חוף

## chôwph

_(khofe | hope | hofe)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to cover; a cove (as a sheltered bay); coast (of the sea), haven, shore, (sea-)side; n-m

- seashore, coast, shore
