# H6731 צִיץ / ציץ

## tsîyts

_(tseets | tseets | tseets)_

## Definition

or צִץ; from H6692; properly, glistening, i.e., a burnished plate; a wing (as gleaming in the air); blossom, flower, plate, wing; n-m

- flower, bloom
  - flower, bloom
  - shining thing (of gold plate on high priest's mitre) (metaphorically)
- feather, wing
  - meaning dubious
