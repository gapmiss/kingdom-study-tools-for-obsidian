# H7819 שָׁחַט / שחט

## shâchaṭ

_(shaw-khat' | shaw-HAHT | sha-HAHT)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to slaughter (in sacrifice or massacre); kill, offer, shoot out, slay, slaughter; v

- (v) to kill, slaughter, beat
  - (Qal)
    - to slaughter
      - beast for food
      - sacrifice
      - person in human sacrifice
      - beaten, hammered (of shekels)
  - (Niphal) to be slaughtered, be slain (of food or sacrifice)
- (n f) slaughtering
  - word doubtful
