# H7964 שִׁלּוּחַ / שלוח

## shillûwach

_(shil-loo'-akh | shil-LOO-ah | shee-LOO-ak)_

## Definition

or שִׁלֻּחַ; from H7971; (only in plural) a dismissal, i.e., (of a wife) divorce (especially the document); also (of a daughter) dower; presents, have sent back; n-m

- sending away, parting gift
  - sending away
  - parting gift
