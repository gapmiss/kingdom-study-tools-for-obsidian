# H8197 שְׁפוּפָם / שפופם

## Shᵉphûwphâm

_(shef-oo-fawm' | sheh-poo-PAWM | sheh-foo-FAHM)_

## Definition

serpent; or שְׁפוּפָן; from the same as H8207; serpent-like; Shephuphan, an Israelite; Shephuphan, Shupham; n-pr-m

- son of Bela and grandson of Benjamin
