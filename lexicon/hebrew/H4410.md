# H4410 מְלוּכָה / מלוכה

## mᵉlûwkâh

_(mel-oo-kaw' | meh-loo-KAW | meh-loo-HA)_

## Definition

feminine passive participle of H4427; something ruled, i.e., a realm; kingsom, king's, × royal; n-f

- kingship, royalty, kingly office
