# H7214 רְאֵם / ראם

## rᵉʼêm

_(reh-ame' | reh-AME | reh-AME)_

## Definition

or רְאֵים; or רֵים; or רֵם; from H7213; a wild bull (from its conspicuousness); unicorn; n-m

- probably the great aurochs or wild bulls which are now extinct. The exact meaning is not known.
