# H7845 שַׁחַת / שחת

## shachath

_(shakh'-ath | sha-HAHT | sha-HAHT)_

## Definition

from H7743; a pit (especially as a trap); figuratively, destruction; corruption, destruction, ditch, grave, pit; n-f

- pit, destruction, grave
  - pit (for catching lions)
  - pit (of Sheol)
