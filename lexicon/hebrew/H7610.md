# H7610 שְׁאָר יָשׁוּב / שאר ישוב

## Shᵉʼâr Yâshûwb

_(sheh-awr'yaw-shoob' | sheh-AWR yaw-SHOOB | sheh-AR ya-SHOOV)_

## Definition

a remnant shall return; from H7605 and H7725; a remnant will return; Shear-Jashub, the symbolic name of one of Isaiah's sons; Shear-jashub; n-pr-m

- a symbolical name for the son of Isaiah the prophet
