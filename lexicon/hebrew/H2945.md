# H2945 טַף / טף

## ṭaph

_(taf | tahp | tahf)_

## Definition

from H2952 (perhaps referring to the tripping gait of children); a family (mostly used collectively in the singular); (little) children (ones), families; n-m

- children, little children, little ones
