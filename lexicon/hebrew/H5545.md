# H5545 סָלַח / סלח

## çâlach

_(saw-lakh' | saw-LA | sa-LAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to forgive; forgive, pardon, spare; v

- to forgive, pardon
  - (Qal) to forgive, pardon
  - (Niphal) to be forgiven
