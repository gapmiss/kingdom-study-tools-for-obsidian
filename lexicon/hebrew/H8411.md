# H8411 תְּדִירָא / תדירא

## tᵉdîyrâʼ

_(ted-ee-raw' | teh-dee-RAW | teh-dee-RA)_

## Definition

from H1753 in the original sense of enduring; permanence, i.e., (adverb) constantly; continually; n-f

- continuance, continuity, perpetuity
  - constantly (as adverb)
