# H5998 עָמַל / עמל

## ʻâmal

_(aw-mal' | aw-MAHL | ah-MAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to toil, i.e., work severely and with irksomeness; (take) labour (in); v

- to labor, toil
  - (Qal) to labor
