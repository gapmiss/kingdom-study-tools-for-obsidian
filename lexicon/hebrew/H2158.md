# H2158 זָמִיר / זמיר

## zâmîyr

_(zaw-meer' | dzaw-MEER | za-MEER)_

## Definition

or זָמִר; and (feminine) זְמִרָה; from H2167; a song to be accompanied with instrumental music; psalm(-ist), singing, song; n-m

- song, psalm
