# H188 אוֹי / אוי

## ʼôwy

_(o'-ee | oy | oy)_

## Definition

probably from H0183 (in the sense of crying out after); lamentation; also interjectionally Oh!; alas, woe; inj

- woe!, alas!, oh!
  - passionate cry of grief or despair
