# H5215 נִיר / ניר

## nîyr

_(neer | neer | neer)_

## Definition

or נִר; from H5214; properly, plowing, i.e., (concretely) freshly plowed land; fallow ground, plowing, tillage; n-m

- tillable or untilled or fallow ground
