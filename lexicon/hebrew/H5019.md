# H5019 נְבוּכַדְנֶאצַּר / נבוכדנאצר

## Nᵉbûwkadneʼtstsar

_(neb-oo-kad-nets-tsar' | neh-boo-kahd-nets-SAHR | neh-voo-hahd-neh-TSAHR)_

## Definition

may Nebo protect the crown; or נְבֻּכַדְנֶאצַּר; (2 Kings 24:1,10), or נְבוּכַדְנֶצַּר; (Esther 2:6; Daniel 1:18), or נְבוּכַדְרֶאצַּר; or נְבוּכַדְרֶאצּוֹר; (Ezra 2:1; Jeremiah 49:28), or foreign derivation; Nebukadnetstsar (or Nebukadretsts(-ar, or)), king of Babylon; Nebuchadnezzar, Nebuchadrezzar; n-pr-m

- the great king of Babylon who captured Jerusalem and carried Judah captive
