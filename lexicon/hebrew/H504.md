# H504 אֶלֶף / אלף

## ʼeleph

_(eh'-lef | eh-LEP | eh-LEF)_

## Definition

from H0502; a family; also (from the sense of yoking or taming) an ox or cow; family, kine, oxen; n-m

- cattle, oxen
  - in farming
  - as a possession
