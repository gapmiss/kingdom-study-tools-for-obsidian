# H1508 גִּזְרָה / גזרה

## gizrâh

_(ghiz-raw' | ɡidz-RAW | ɡeez-RA)_

## Definition

feminine of H1506; the figure or person (as if cut out); also an inclosure (as separated); polishing, separate place; n-f

- a cutting, polishing, separation
  - cutting, polishing
  - separation, separate place
