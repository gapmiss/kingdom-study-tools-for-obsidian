# H7381 רֵיחַ / ריח

## rêyach

_(ray'-akh | RAY-ah | RAY-ak)_

## Definition

from H7306; odor (as if blown); savour, scent, smell; n-m

- scent, fragrance, aroma, odor
  - scent, odor
  - odor of soothing (technical term for sacrifice to God)
