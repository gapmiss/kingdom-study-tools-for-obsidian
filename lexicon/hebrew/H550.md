# H550 אַמְנוֹן / אמנון

## ʼAmnôwn

_(am-nohn' | am-NONE | am-NONE)_

## Definition

faithful; or אֲמִינוֹן; from H0539; faithful; Amnon (or Aminon), a son of David; Amnon; n-m

- oldest son of David, rapist of Tamar, slain by Absalom
- a son of Shimon (of Caleb's clan)
