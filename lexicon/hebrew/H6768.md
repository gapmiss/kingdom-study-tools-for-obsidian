# H6768 צֶלֶק / צלק

## Tseleq

_(tseh'-lek | tseh-LEK | tseh-LEK)_

## Definition

fissure; from an unused root meaning to split; fissure; Tselek, an Israelite; Zelek; n-pr-m

- an Ammonite and a warrior for David
