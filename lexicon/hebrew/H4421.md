# H4421 מִלְחָמָה / מלחמה

## milchâmâh

_(mil-khaw-maw' | mil-haw-MAW | meel-ha-MA)_

## Definition

from H3898 (in the sense of fighting); a battle (i.e., the engagement); generally, war (i.e., war-fare); battle, fight(-ing), war(-rior); n-f

- battle, war
