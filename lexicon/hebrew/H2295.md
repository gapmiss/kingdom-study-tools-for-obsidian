# H2295 חׇגְלָה / חגלה

## Choglâh

_(khog-law' | hoɡe-LAW | hoɡe-LA)_

## Definition

partridge; of uncertain derivation; probably a partridge; Choglah, an Israelitess; Hoglah; n-pr-f

- 3rd of 5 daughters of Zelophehad, a descendant of Manasseh
