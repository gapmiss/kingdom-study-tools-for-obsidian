# H4611 מַעֲלָל / מעלל

## maʻălâl

_(mah-al-awl' | ma-uh-LAWL | ma-uh-LAHL)_

## Definition

from H5953; an act (good or bad); doing, endeavour, invention, work; n-m

- deed, practice
  - practice (usually bad)
  - deeds
  - acts
