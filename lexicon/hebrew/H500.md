# H500 אֶלְעָלֵא / אלעלא

## ʼElʻâlêʼ

_(el-aw-lay' | el-aw-LAY | el-ah-LAY)_

## Definition

God is ascending; or (more properly) אֶלְעָלֵה; from H0410 and H5927; God (is) going up; Elale or Elaleh, a place east of the Jordan; Elealeh; n-pr-loc

- a Reubenite village near Heshbon (in ruins)
