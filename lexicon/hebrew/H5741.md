# H5741 עַדְרִיאֵל / עדריאל

## ʻAdrîyʼêl

_(ad-ree-ale' | ad-ree-ALE | ad-ree-ALE)_

## Definition

flock of God; from H5739 and H0410; flock of God; Adriel, an Israelite; Adriel; n-pr-m

- son of Barzillai, and the man to whom Saul gave his daughter Merab in marriage although she was already promised to David

[[1 Samuel 18#19|1 Samuel 18:19]], [[2 Samuel 21#8|2 Samuel 21:8]]
