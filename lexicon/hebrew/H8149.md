# H8149 שְׁנִיר / שניר

## Shᵉnîyr

_(shen-eer' | sheh-NEER | sheh-NEER)_

## Definition

snow mountain; or שְׂנִיר; from an unused root meaning to be pointed; peak; Shenir or Senir, a summit of Lebanon; Senir, Shenir; n-pr

- the Amorite name for Mount Hermon
