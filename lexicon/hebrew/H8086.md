# H8086 שְׁמַע / שמע

## shᵉmaʻ

_(shem-ah' | sheh-MA | sheh-MA)_

## Definition

corresponding to H8085; to hear intelligently (often with implication of attention, obedience, etc.; causatively, to tell, etc.); hear, obey; v

- to hear
  - (P'al) to hear, have a sense of hearing
  - (Ithpael) to show oneself obedient
