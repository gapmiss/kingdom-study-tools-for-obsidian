# H6997 קָטָן / קטן

## Qâṭân

_(kaw-tawn' | kaw-TAWN | ka-TAHN)_

## Definition

the small; the same as H6996; small; Katan, an Israelite; Hakkatan (including the article); n-pr-m

- father of Johanan, the chief of the Bene-Azgad who returned from exile with Ezra
