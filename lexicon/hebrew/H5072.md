# H5072 נְדַבְיָה / נדביה

## Nᵉdabyâh

_(ned-ab-yaw' | neh-dahb-YAW | neh-dahv-YA)_

## Definition

whom Jehovah impels; from H5068 and H3050; largess of Jah; Nedabjah, an Israelite; Nedabiah; n-pr-m

- one of the sons of king Jehoiachin (Jeconiah) of Judah
