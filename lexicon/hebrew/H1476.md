# H1476 גּוּנִי / גוני

## Gûwnîy

_(goo-nee' | ɡoo-NEE | ɡoo-NEE)_

## Definition

my defender(?); probably from H1598; protected; Guni, the name of two Israelites; Guni; n-pr-m

- a son of Naphtali and founder of the family of Gunites
- a descendant of Gad
