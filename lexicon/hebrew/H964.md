## bizyôwthᵉyâh

_(biz-yo-the-yaw' | bidz-yoh-teh-YAW | beez-yoh-teh-YA)_

## Definition

contempt of Jehovah; from H0959 and H3050; contempts of Jah; Bizjothjah, a place in Palestine; Bizjothjah; n-pr-loc
