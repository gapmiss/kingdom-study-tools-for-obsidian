# H1373 גַּבַּי / גבי

## Gabbay

_(gab-bah'ee | ɡahb-BAI | ɡa-BAI)_

## Definition

my back; from the same as H1354; collective; Gabbai, an Israelite; Gabbai; n-pr-m

- the head of an important Benjamite family living in Jerusalem in the time of Nehemiah
