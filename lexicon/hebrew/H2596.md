# H2596 חָנַךְ / חנך

## chânak

_(khaw-nak' | haw-NAHK | ha-NAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; (compare H2614); properly, to narrow; figuratively, to initiate or discipline; dedicate, train up; v

- to train, dedicate, inaugurate
  - (Qal)
    - to train, train up
    - to dedicate
