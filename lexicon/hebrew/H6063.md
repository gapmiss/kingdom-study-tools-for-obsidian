# H6063 עָנֵר / ענר

## ʻÂnêr

_(aw-nare' | aw-NARE | ah-NARE)_

## Definition

boy; probably for H5288; Aner, a Amorite, also a place in Palestine; Aner; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) one of the Amorite chiefs who aided Abraham in the pursuit of the 4 invading kings
- (n pr loc) a Levitical city west of the Jordan in Manasseh allotted to the Kohathite Levites
