# H3252 יִסְכָּה / יסכה

## Yiçkâh

_(yis-kaw' | yis-KAW | yees-KA)_

## Definition

one who looks forth; from an unused root meaning to watch; observant; Jiskah, sister of Lot; Iscah; n-pr-f

- daughter of Haran, niece of Abraham, sister of Lot and Milcah
