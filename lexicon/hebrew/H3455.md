# H3455 יָשַׂם / ישם

## yâsam

_(yaw-sam' | yaw-SAHM | ya-SAHM)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to place; intransitively, to be placed; be put (set); v

- to put, place, set, appoint, make
  - (Qal) to put, place
