# H7994 שָׁלָךְ / שלך

## shâlâk

_(shaw-lawk' | shaw-LOKE | sha-LOKE)_

## Definition

from H7993; bird of prey, usually thought to be the pelican (from casting itself into the sea); cormorant; n-m

- bird of prey
  - probably the cormorant
