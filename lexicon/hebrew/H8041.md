# H8041 שָׂמַאל / שמאל

## sâmaʼl

_(saw-mal' | saw-MAHL | sa-MAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root (denominative from H8040); to use the left hand or pass in that direction; (go, turn) (on the, to the) left; v

- to take the left, go to the left
  - (Hiphil)
    - to go to the left
    - to turn (aside) to the left (figuratively)
    - to use the left hand
