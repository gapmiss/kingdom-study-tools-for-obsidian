# H7286 רָדַד / רדד

## râdad

_(raw-dad' | raw-DAHD | ra-DAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to tread in pieces, i.e., (figuratively) to conquer, or (specifically) to overlay; spend, spread, subdue; v

- to beat down, beat out, subdue
  - (Qal) to beat down (figuratively)
  - (Hiphil) to beat out
