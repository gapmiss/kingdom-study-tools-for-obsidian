# H7786 שׂוּר / שור

## sûwr

_(soor | soor | soor)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to vanquish; by implication, to rule (causatively, crown); make princes, have power, reign; v

- to be or act as prince, rule, contend, have power, prevail over
  - (Qal) to rule over, govern
  - (Hiphil) to make rulers, play the prince
