# H2290 חֲגוֹר / חגור

## chăgôwr

_(khag-ore' | huh-ɡORE | huh-ɡORE)_

or חֲגֹר; and (feminine) חֲגוֹרָה; or חֲגֹרָה; from H2296; a belt (for the waist); apron, armour, gird(-le); n-pr-m

- girdle, belt
- girdle, loin-covering, belt, loin-cloth, armour
