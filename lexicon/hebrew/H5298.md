# H5298 נֶפֶג / נפג

## Nepheg

_(neh'-feg | neh-PEɡ | neh-FEɡ)_

## Definition

sprout; from an unused root probably meaning to spring forth; a sprout; Nepheg, the name of two Israelites; Nepheg; n-pr-m

- one of sons of Izhar and grandson of Kohath of the tribe of Levi in the time of the exodus
- one of the sons of David born to him at Jerusalem
