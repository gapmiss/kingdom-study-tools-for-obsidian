# H1694 דֶּבֶק / דבק

## debeq

_(deh'-bek | deh-BEK | deh-VEK)_

## Definition

from H1692; a joint; by implication, solder; joint, solder; n-m

- joints, soldering, joining, appendage
  - joining, soldering, riveting
  - appendage, open joints of a breastplate
