# H6078 עׇפְנִי / עפני

## ʻOphnîy

_(of-nee' | ope-NEE | ofe-NEE)_

## Definition

mouldy; from an unused noun (denoting a place in Palestine; from an unused root of uncertain meaning); an Ophnite (collectively) or inhabitants of Ophen; Ophni; n-pr-loc

- a town of Benjamin located 2.5 (4.5 km) miles northwest of Bethel; modern 'Jifna'
