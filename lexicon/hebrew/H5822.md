# H5822 עׇזְנִיָּה / עזניה

## ʻoznîyâh

_(oz-nee-yaw' | ohdz-nih-YAW | oze-nee-YA)_

## Definition

probably feminine of H5797; probably the sea-eagle (from its strength); ospray; n-f

- an unclean bird of prey
  - osprey, black eagle, buzzard
  - perhaps an extinct bird, exact meaning unknown
