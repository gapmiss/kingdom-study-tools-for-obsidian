# H6497 פֶּקַע / פקע

## peqaʻ

_(peh'-kah | peh-KA | peh-KA)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to burst; only used as an architectural term of an ornament similar to H6498; a semi-globe; knop; n-m

- knob-shaped or ball-shaped or gourd-shaped carved wood or metal ornament
