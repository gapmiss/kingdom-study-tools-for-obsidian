# H3295 יַעֲרָה / יערה

## yaʻărâh

_(yah-ar-aw' | ya-uh-RAW | ya-uh-RA)_

## Definition

feminine of H3293, and meaning the same; a copse of bushes; hence, a forest; hence, honey in the comb (as hived in trees); (honey-)comb, forest; n-f

- forest
  - honeycomb (metonymy)
