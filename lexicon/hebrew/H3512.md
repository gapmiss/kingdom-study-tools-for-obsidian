# H3512 כָּאָה / כאה

## kâʼâh

_(kaw-aw' | kaw-AW | ka-AH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to despond; causatively, to deject; broken, be grieved, make sad; v

- to be sad, be disheartened, be cowed
  - (Niphal) to be disheartened, be cowed
  - (Hiphil) to make sad
