# H753 אֹרֶךְ / ארך

## ʼôrek

_(o'rek' | oh-REK | oh-REK)_

## Definition

from H0748; length; + forever, length, long; n-m

- length
  - physical length
  - of time
- forbearance, self-restraint (of patience)
