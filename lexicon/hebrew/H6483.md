# H6483 פִּצֵּץ / פצץ

## Pitstsêts

_(pits-tsates' | pits-SAYTS | pee-TSAYTS)_

## Definition

to break; from an unused root meaning to dissever; dispersive; Pitstsets, a priest; Apses (including the article); n-pr-m

- a priest, chief of the 18th course in the service of the temple in the time of David
