# H3978 מַאֲכָל / מאכל

_(mah-ak-awl' | ma-uh-KAWL | ma-uh-HAHL)_

## Definition

from H0398; an eatable (including provender, flesh and fruit); food, fruit, (bake-)meat(-s), victual; n-m

- food, fruit, meat
