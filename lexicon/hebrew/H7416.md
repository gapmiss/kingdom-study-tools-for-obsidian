# H7416 רִמּוֹן / רמון

## rimmôwn

_(rim-mone' | rim-MONE | ree-MONE)_

## Definition

or רִמֹּן; from H7426; a pomegranate, the tree (from its upright growth) or the fruit (also an artificial ornament); pomegranate; n-m

- pomegranate
  - as tree
  - as fruit
  - as pomegranate shaped ornaments in temple
