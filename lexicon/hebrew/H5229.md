# H5229 נְכֹחָה / נכחה

## nᵉkôchâh

_(nek-o-khaw' | neh-koh-HAW | neh-hoh-HA)_

## Definition

feminine of H5228; properly, straightforwardness, i.e., (figuratively) integrity, or (concretely) a truth; equity, right (thing), uprightness; a-f

- straight in front, be in front of, straight, right, straightness
  - true things (of prophecy)
  - rectitude (subst)
