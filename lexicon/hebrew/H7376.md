## râṭâsh

_(raw-tash' | raw-TAHSH | ra-TAHSH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to dash down; dash (in pieces); v

- to dash to pieces
  - (Piel) to dash in pieces
  - (Pual) to be dashed in pieces
