# H7733 שׁוֹבֵק / שובק

## Shôwbêq

_(sho-bake' | shoh-BAKE | shoh-VAKE)_

## Definition

free; active participle from a primitive root meaning to leave (compare H7662); forsaking; Shobek, an Israelite; Shobek; n-pr-m

- one of the heads of the people who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
