# H899 בֶּגֶד / בגד

## beged

_(behg'-ed | beh-ɡED | beh-ɡED)_

## Definition

from H0898; a covering, i.e., clothing; apparel, cloth(-es, -ing), garment, lap, rag, raiment, robe, × very (treacherously), vesture, wardrobe; n-m

- treachery, deceit
- garment, clothing
