# H6741 צִלָּה / צלה

## Tsillâh

_(tsil-law' | tsil-LAW | tsee-LA)_

## Definition

shade; feminine of H6738; Tsillah, an antediluvian woman; Zillah; n-pr-f

- the 2nd wife of Lamech and mother of Tubal-cain, an instructor of every craftsman in bronze and iron
