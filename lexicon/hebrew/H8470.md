_(takh-an-ee' | ta-huh-NEE | ta-huh-NEE)_

## Definition

thou will decline; patronymically from H8465; a Tachanite (collectively) or descendants of Tachan; Tahanites; a

- descendants of Tachan, son of Telah, an Ephraimite
