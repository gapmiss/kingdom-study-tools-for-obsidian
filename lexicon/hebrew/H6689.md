# H6689 צוּף / צוף

## Tsûwph

_(tsoof | tsoop | tsoof)_

## Definition

honeycomb; or צוֹפַי; or צִיף; from H6688; honey-comb; Tsuph or Tsophai or Tsiph, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine; Zophai, Zuph; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a Kohathite Levite, ancestor of Elkanah and Samuel
- (n pr loc) a district northwest of Jerusalem where Saul encountered Samuel
