# H4102 מָהַהּ / מהה

## mâhahh

_(maw-hah' | maw-HA | ma-HA)_

## Definition

apparently a denominative from H4100; properly, to question or hesitate, i.e., (by implication) to be reluctant; delay, linger, stay selves, tarry; v

- (Hithpalpel) to linger, tarry, wait, delay
