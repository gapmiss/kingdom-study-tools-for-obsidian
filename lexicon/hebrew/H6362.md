# H6362 פָּטַר / פטר

## pâṭar

_(paw-tar' | paw-TAHR | pa-TAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to cleave or burst through, i.e., (causatively) to emit, whether literal or figurative (gape); dismiss, free, let (shoot) out, slip away; v

- to separate, set free, remove, open, escape, burst through
  - (Qal)
    - to remove (oneself), escape
    - to set free, let out
