# H4120 מְהֵרָה / מהרה

## mᵉhêrâh

_(meh-hay-raw' | meh-hay-RAW | meh-hay-RA)_

## Definition

feminine of H4118; properly, a hurry; hence (adverbially) promptly; hastily, quickly, shortly, soon, make (with) speed(-ily), swiftly; n-f

- haste, speed
  - hastily, speedily (as adverb)
  - in haste (with prep)
