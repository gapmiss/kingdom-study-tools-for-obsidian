# H4113 מַהֲמֹרָה / מהמרה

## mahămôrâh

_(mah-ham-o-raw' | ma-huh-moh-RAW | ma-huh-moh-RA)_

## Definition

from an unused root of uncertain meaning; perhaps an abyss; deep pit; n-f

- a flood, a pit of water, watery pit
