# H7783 שׁוּק / שוק

## shûwq

_(shook | shook | shook)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to run after or over, i.e., overflow; overflow, water; v

- to be abundant (meaning probable)
  - (Polel) to give abundance to
  - (Hiphil) to overflow
