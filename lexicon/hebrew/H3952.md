# H3952 לָקַק / לקק

## lâqaq

_(law-kak' | law-KAHK | la-KAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to lick or lap; lap, lick; v

- to lap, lick, lap up
  - (Qal) to lap, lap up
  - (Piel) to lap up
