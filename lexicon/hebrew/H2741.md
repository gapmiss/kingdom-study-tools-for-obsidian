# H2741 חֲרוּפִי / חרופי

## Chărûwphîy

_(khar-oo-fee' | huh-roo-PEE | huh-roo-FEE)_

## Definition

native of Haruph; a patrial from (probably) a collateral form of H2756; a Charuphite or inhabitant of Charuph (or Chariph); Haruphite; n-m

- an inhabitant of Hariph
