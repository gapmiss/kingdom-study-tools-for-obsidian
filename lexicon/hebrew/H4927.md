# H4927 מִשְׁמָע / משמע

## Mishmâʻ

_(mish-maw' | mish-MAW | meesh-MA)_

## Definition

a hearing; the same as H4926; Mishma, the name of a son of Ishmael, and of an Israelite; Mishma; n-pr-m

- one of the 12 sons of Ishmael
- son of Mibsam of the family of Simeon
