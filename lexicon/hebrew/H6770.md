# H6770 צָמֵא / צמא

## tsâmêʼ

_(tsaw-may' | tsaw-MAY | tsa-MAY)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to thirst (literally or figuratively); (suffer) (be a-)thirst(-y); v

- (Qal) to be thirsty
