# H6589 פָּשַׂק / פשק

## pâsaq

_(paw-sak' | paw-SAHK | pa-SAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to dispart (the feet or lips), i.e., become licentious; open (wide); v

- to part, open wide
  - (Qal)
    - to open wide
    - to be talkative (of the mouth)
  - (Piel) to open wide
