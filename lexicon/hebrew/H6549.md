# H6549 פַּרְעֹה נְכֹה / פרעה נכה

## Parʻôh Nᵉkôh

_(par-o' nek-o' | pahr-OH neh-KOH | pahr-OH neh-HOH)_

## Definition

great house-he is smitten; or פַּרְעֹה נְכוֹ; of Egyptian derivation; Paroh-Nekoh (or Paroh-Neko), an Egyptian king; Pharaoh-necho, Pharaohnechoh; n-pr-m

- the Pharaoh of Egypt who fought king Josiah of Judah at Megiddo and killed him
