# H5104 נָהָר / נהר

## nâhâr

_(naw-hawr' | naw-HAWR | na-HAHR)_

## Definition

from H5102; a stream (including the sea; expectation the Nile, Euphrates, etc.); figuratively, prosperity; flood, river; n-m

- stream, river
  - stream, river
  - (underground) streams
