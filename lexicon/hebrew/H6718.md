# H6718 צַיִד / ציד

## tsayid

_(tsah'-yid | tsa-YID | tsa-YEED)_

## Definition

from a form of H6679 and meaning the same; (generally) lunch (especially for a journey); × catcheth, food, × hunter, (that which he took in) hunting, venison, victuals; n-m

- hunting, game
  - hunting
  - game hunted
- provision, food
  - provision, food, food-supply
