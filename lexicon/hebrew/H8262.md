# H8262 שָׁקַץ / שקץ

## shâqats

_(shaw-kats' | shaw-KAHTS | sha-KAHTS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be filthy, i.e., (intensively) to loathe, pollute; abhor, make abominable, have in abomination, detest, × utterly; v

- (Piel) to detest, make abominable, count filthy, make detestable
  - to detest
  - to make detestable
