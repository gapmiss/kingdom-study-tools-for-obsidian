# H451 אַלְיָה / אליה

## ʼalyâh

_(al-yaw' | al-YAW | al-YA)_

## Definition

from H0422 (in the original sense of strength); the stout part, i.e., the fat tail of the Middle Eastern sheep; rump; n-f

- tail, fat-tail (of sheep—an Eastern delicacy)
