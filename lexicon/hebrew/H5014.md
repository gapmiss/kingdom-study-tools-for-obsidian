# H5014 נָבַב / נבב

## nâbab

_(naw-bab' | naw-BAHB | na-VAHV)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to pierce; to be hollow, or (figuratively) foolish; hollow, vain; v

- (Qal) to hollow out
  - hollowed (participle)
