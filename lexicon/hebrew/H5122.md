# H5122 נְוָלוּ / נולו

## nᵉvâlûw

_(nev-aw-loo' | neh-waw-LOO | neh-va-LOO)_

## Definition

or נְוָלִי; (Aramaic), from an unused root probably meaning to be foul; a sink; dunghill; n-f

- refuse-heap, dunghill, outhouse
