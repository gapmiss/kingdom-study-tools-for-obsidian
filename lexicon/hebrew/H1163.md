# H1163 בָּעַט / בעט

## bâʻaṭ

_(baw-at' | baw-AT | ba-AT)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to trample down, i.e., (figuratively) despise; kick; v

- to kick, kick at
  - (Qal)
    - to kick
    - to kick at
    - to desire (figuratively)
