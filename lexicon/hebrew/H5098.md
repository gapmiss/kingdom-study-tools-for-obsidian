# H5098 נָהַם / נהם

## nâham

_(naw-ham' | naw-HAHM | na-HAHM)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to growl; mourn, roar(-ing); v

- to growl, groan
  - (Qal)
    - to growl (of lion)
    - to groan (of sufferer)
