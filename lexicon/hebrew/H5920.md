# H5920 עַל / על

## ʻal

_(al | al | al)_

## Definition

from H5927; properly, the top; specifically, the Highest (i.e., God); also (adverb) aloft, to Jehovah; above, high, most High; n-m

- (subst) height
- (adv) above, upwards, on high
