# H7953 שָׁלָה / שלה

## shâlâh

_(shaw-law' | shaw-LAW | sha-LA)_

## Definition

a primitive root (rather cognate (by contraction) to the base of H5394, H7997 and their congeners through the idea of extracting); to draw out or off, i.e., remove (the soul by death); take away; v

- (Qal) to draw out, extract
