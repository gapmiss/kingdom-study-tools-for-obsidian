# H5566 סֶמֶל / סמל

## çemel

_(seh'-mel | seh-MEL | seh-MEL)_

## Definition

or סֵמֶל; from an unused root meaning to resemble; a likeness; figure, idol, image; n-m

- image, statue, idol
