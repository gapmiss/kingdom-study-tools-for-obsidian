# H7336 רָזַן / רזן

## râzan

_(raw-zan' | raw-DZAHN | ra-ZAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; probably to be heavy, i.e., (figuratively) honorable; prince, ruler; v

- (Qal) to be weighty, be judicious, be commanding
