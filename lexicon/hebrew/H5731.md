# H5731 עֵדֶן / עדן

## ʻÊden

_(ay'-den | ay-DEN | ay-DEN)_

## Definition

pleasure; the same as H5730 (masculine); Eden, the region of Adam's home; Eden; n-pr-m

- (n pr m loc) the first habitat of man after the creation; site unknown
- (n pr m) a Gershonite Levite, son of Joah in the days of king Hezekiah of Judah
