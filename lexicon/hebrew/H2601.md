## Chănamʼêl

_(khan-am-ale' | huh-nahm-ALE | huh-nahm-ALE)_

## Definition

God is gracious; probably by orthographical variation for H2606; Chanamel, an Israelite; Hanameel; n-pr-m

- son of Shallum and cousin of Jeremiah
