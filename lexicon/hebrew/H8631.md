# H8631 תְּקֵף / תקף

## tᵉqêph

_(tek-afe' | teh-KAPE | teh-KAFE)_

## Definition

corresponding to H8630; to become (causatively, make) mighty or (figuratively) obstinate; make firm, harden, be(-come) strong; v

- to grow strong, be hardened
  - (P'al)
    - to grow strong, be hardened
    - to grow arrogant (figuratively)
  - (Pael) to make strong, make firm, make stringent
