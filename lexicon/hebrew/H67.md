# H67 אָבֵל מִצְרַיִם / אבל מצרים

## ʼÂbêl Mitsrayim

_(aw-bale' mits-rah'-yim | aw-BALE mits-ra-YIM | ah-VALE meets-ra-YEEM)_

## Definition

meadow of Egypt; from H0058 and H4714; meadow of Egypt; Abel-Mitsrajim, a place in Palestine; Abel-mizraim; n-pr-loc

- a place east of the Jordan (perhaps &quot;as fertile as Egypt&quot;)
