# H733 אָרַח / ארח

## ʼÂrach

_(aw-rakh' | aw-RA | ah-RAHK)_

## Definition

traveller; from H0732; way faring; Arach, the name of three Israelites; Arah; n-pr-m

- a man of Asher
- the prominent head of a family of returning exiles
- another returned exile
