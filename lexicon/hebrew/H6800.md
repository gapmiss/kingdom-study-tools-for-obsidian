# H6800 צָנַע / צנע

## tsânaʻ

_(tsaw-nah' | tsaw-NA | tsa-NA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to humiliate; humbly, lowly; v

- to be humble, be modest, be lowly
  - (Qal) to show humility
- (Hiphil) modest
