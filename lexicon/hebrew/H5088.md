# H5088 נֶדֶר / נדר

## neder

_(neh'-der | neh-DER | neh-DER)_

## Definition

or נֵדֶר; from H5087; a promise (to God); also (concretely) a thing promised; vow(-ed); n-m

- vow, votive offering
