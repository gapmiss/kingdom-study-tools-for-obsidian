# H7488 רַעֲנָן / רענן

## raʻănân

_(rah-an-awn' | ra-uh-NAWN | ra-uh-NAHN)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to be green; verdant; by analogy, new; figuratively, prosperous; green, flourishing; a

- (v) (Palel) to be or grow luxuriant or fresh or green
- (adj) luxuriant, fresh
