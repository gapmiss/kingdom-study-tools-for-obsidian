# H697 אַרְבֶּה / ארבה

## ʼarbeh

_(ar-beh' | ar-BEH | ar-BEH)_

## Definition

from H7235; a locust (from its rapid increase); grasshopper, locust; n-m

- a kind of locust, locust swarm (collective)

## Note

Perhaps the gryllus gregarius species.
