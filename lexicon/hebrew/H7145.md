# H7145 קׇרְחִי / קרחי

## Qorchîy

_(kor-khee' | kore-HEE | kore-HEE)_

## Definition

one of Korah; patronymic from H7141; a Korchite (collectively) or descendants of Korach; Korahite, Korathite, sons of Kore, Korhite; n-pr-m

- descendants of Korah the son of Levi
