# H5517 סִיעָא / סיעא

## Çîyʻâʼ

_(see-ah' | see-AW | see-AH)_

## Definition

departing; or סִיעֲהָא; from an unused root meaning to converse; congregation; Sia or Siaha, one of the Nethinim; Sia, Siaha; n-pr-m

- ancestor of a family of temple slaves who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
