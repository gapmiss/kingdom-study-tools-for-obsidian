# H1538 גֻּלְגֹּלֶת / גלגלת

## gulgôleth

_(gul-go'-leth | ɡool-ɡoh-LET | ɡool-ɡoh-LET)_

## Definition

by reduplication from H1556; a skull (as round); by implication, a head (in enumeration of persons); head, every man, poll, skull; n-f

- head, poll, skull
  - skull
  - head, poll (of census)
