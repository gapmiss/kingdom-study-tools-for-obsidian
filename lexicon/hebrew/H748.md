# H748 אָרַךְ / ארך

## ʼârak

_(aw-rak' | aw-RAHK | ah-RAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be (causative, make) long (literally or figuratively); defer, draw out, lengthen, (be, become, make, pro-)long, (out-, over-)live, tarry (long); v

- to be long, prolong
  - (Qal) to be long
  - (Hiphil)
    - to prolong (days)
    - to make long (tent cords)
    - to grow long, continue long
