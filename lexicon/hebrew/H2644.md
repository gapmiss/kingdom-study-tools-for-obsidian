# H2644 חָפָא / חפא

## châphâʼ

_(khaw-faw' | haw-PAW | ha-FA)_

## Definition

an orthographical variation of H2645; properly, to cover, i.e., (in a sinister sense) to act covertly; do secretly; v

- (Piel) to cover, do secretly
