_(naw-ko'-akh | naw-KOH-ah | na-HOH-ak)_

## Definition

from the same as H5226; straightforward, i.e., (figuratively), equitable, correct, or (abstractly), integrity; plain, right, uprightness; a

- straight, right, straightness, be in front of
