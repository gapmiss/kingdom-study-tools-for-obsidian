# H399 אֲכַל / אכל

## ʼăkal

_(ak-al' | uh-KAHL | uh-HAHL)_

## Definition

corresponding to H0398; to eat; + accuse, devour, eat; v

- to eat, devour
  - (P'al)
    - to eat (of beasts)
    - to devour
    - eat their pieces (in a phrase, that is, slander them)
