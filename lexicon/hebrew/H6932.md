# H6932 קְדֵמוֹת / קדמות

## Qᵉdêmôwth

_(ked-ay-mothe' | keh-day-MOTE | keh-day-MOTE)_

## Definition

eastern; from H6923; beginnings; Kedemoth, a place in eastern Palestine; Kedemoth; n-pr-loc

- one of the towns in the district east of the Dead Sea allotted to the tribe of Reuben and given to the Merarite Levites
