# H5637 סָרַר / סרר

## çârar

_(saw-rar' | saw-RAHR | sa-RAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to turn away, i.e., (morally) be refractory; × away, backsliding, rebellious, revolter(-ing), slide back, stubborn, withdrew; v

- to rebel, be stubborn, be rebellious, be refractory
  - (Qal)
    - to be stubborn
    - stubborn, rebel (subst)
