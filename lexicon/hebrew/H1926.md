# H1926 הָדָר / הדר

## hâdâr

_(haw-dawr' | haw-DAWR | ha-DAHR)_

## Definition

from H1921; magnificence, i.e., ornament or splendor; beauty, comeliness, excellency, glorious, glory, goodly, honour, majesty; n-m

- ornament, splendor, honor
  - ornament
  - splendor, majesty
  - honor, glory
