# H7848 שִׁטָּה / שטה

## shiṭṭâh

_(shit-taw' | shit-TAW | shee-TA)_

## Definition

feminine of a derivative (only in the plural שִׁטִּים; meaning the sticks of wood) from the same as H7850; the acacia (from its scourging thorns); shittah, shittim; n-f

- acacia tree, acacia wood
