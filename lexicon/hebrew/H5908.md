# H5908 עַכָּבִישׁ / עכביש

## ʻakkâbîysh

_(ak-kaw-beesh' | ak-kaw-BEESH | ah-ka-VEESH)_

## Definition

probably from an unused root in the literal sense of entangling; a spider (as weaving a network); spider; n-m

- spider
  - spider
  - web (house of spider)
