# H7346 רְחַבְעָם / רחבעם

## Rᵉchabʻâm

_(rekh-ab-awm' | reh-hahb-AWM | reh-hahv-AM)_

## Definition

a people has enlarged; from H7337 and H5971; a people has enlarged; Rechabam, an Israelite king; Rehoboam; n-pr-m

- son of Solomon and the 1st king of Judah after the split up of the kingdom of Israel
