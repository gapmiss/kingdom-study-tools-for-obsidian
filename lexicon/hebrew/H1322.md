# H1322 בֹּשֶׁת / בשת

## bôsheth

_(bo'-sheth | boh-SHET | boh-SHET)_

## Definition

from H0954; shame (the feeling and the condition, as well as its cause); by implication (specifically) an idol; ashamed, confusion, + greatly, (put to) shame(-ful thing); n-f

- shame

  - shameful thing
