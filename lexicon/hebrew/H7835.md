# H7835 שָׁחַר / שחר

## shâchar

_(shaw-khar' | shaw-HAHR | sha-HAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root (identical with H7836 through the idea of the duskiness of early dawn); to be dim or dark (in color); be black; v

- to be black
  - (Qal) to be black (of skin)
