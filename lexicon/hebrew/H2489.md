# H2489 חֵלְכָא / חלכא

## chêlᵉkâʼ

_(khay-lek-aw' | hay-leh-KAW | hay-leh-HA)_

## Definition

or חֵלְכָה; apparently from an unused root probably meaning to be dark or (figuratively) unhappy; a wretch, i.e., unfortunate; poor; a

- hapless, poor, unfortunate person
