# H2864 חָתַר / חתר

## châthar

_(khaw-thar' | haw-TAHR | ha-TAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to force a passage, as by burglary; figuratively, with oars; dig (through), row; v

- to dig, row
  - (Qal)
    - to dig (into houses—of burglary)
    - to row (in water)
