# H4888 מִשְׁחָה / משחה

## mishchâh

_(meesh-khaw' | mish-HAW | meesh-HA)_

## Definition

or מׇשְׁחָה; from H4886; unction (the act); by implication, a consecratory gift; (to be) anointed(-ing), ointment; n-f

- consecrated portion, anointing oil, portion, ointment, anointing portion
  - ointment (used to consecrate by anointing)
  - anointing portion
