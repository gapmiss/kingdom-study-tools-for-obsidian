# H6822 צָפָה / צפה

## tsâphâh

_(tsaw-faw' | tsaw-PAW | tsa-FA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to lean forward, i.e., to peer into the distance; by implication, to observe, await; behold, espy, look up (well), wait for, (keep the) watch(-man); v

- to look out or about, spy, keep watch, observe, watch
  - (Qal) to keep watch, spy
  - (Piel) to watch, watch closely
