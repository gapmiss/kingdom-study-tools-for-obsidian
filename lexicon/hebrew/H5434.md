# H5434 סְבָא / סבא

## Çᵉbâʼ

_(seb-aw' | seh-BAW | seh-VA)_

## Definition

drink thou; of foreign origin; Seba, a son of Cush, and the country settled by him; Seba; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a son of Cush
- (n pr loc) a nation south of Palestine, perhaps Ethiopia
