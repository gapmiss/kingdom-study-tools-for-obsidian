# H6964 קוֹלָיָה / קוליה

## Qôwlâyâh

_(ko-law-yaw' | koh-law-YAW | koh-la-YA)_

## Definition

voice of Jehovah; from H6963 and H3050; voice of Jah; Kolajah, the name of two Israelites; Kolaiah; n-pr-m

- father of the false prophet Ahab
- a Benjamite whose descendants settled in Jerusalem after the return from captivity
- a Levite in the time of Ezra

[[Nehemiah 11#7|Nehemiah 11:7]], [[Jeremiah 29#21|Jeremiah 29:21]]
