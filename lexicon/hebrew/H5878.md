# H5878 עֵין חֲרֹד / עין חרד

## ʻÊyn Chărôd

_(ane khar-ode' | ane huh-RODE | ane huh-RODE)_

## Definition

spring of Herod; from H5869 and a derivative of H2729; fountain of trembling; En-Charod, a place in Palestine; well of Harod; n-pr-loc

- a camping place of Gideon and Israel near the hill of Moreh
