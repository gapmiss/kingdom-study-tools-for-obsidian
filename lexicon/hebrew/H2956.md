# H2956 טָרַד / טרד

## ṭârad

_(taw-rad' | taw-RAHD | ta-RAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to drive on; figuratively, to follow close; continual; v

- to pursue, chase, be continuous
  - (Qal) dripping (participle)
