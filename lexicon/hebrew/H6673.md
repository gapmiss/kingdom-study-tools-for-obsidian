# H6673 צַו / צו

## tsav

_(tsav | tsa | tsahv)_

## Definition

or צָו; from H6680; an injunction; commandment, precept; n-m

- command, ordinance, oracle (meaning dubious)
  - used in mocking mimicry of Isaiah's words and thus not a true divine command
