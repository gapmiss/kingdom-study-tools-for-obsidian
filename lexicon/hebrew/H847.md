# H847 אֶשְׁתָּאֹל / אשתאל

## ʼEshtâʼôl

_(esh-taw-ole' | esh-taw-OLE | esh-ta-OLE)_

## Definition

entreaty; or אֶשְׁתָּאוֹל; probably from H7592; intreaty; Eshtaol, a place in Palestine; Eshtaol; n-pr-loc

- a Danite city located within Judah
