# H2427 חִיל / חיל

## chîyl

_(kheel | heel | heel)_

## Definition

and (feminine) חִילָה; from H2342; a throe (expectant of childbirth); pain, pang, sorrow; n-m

- pain, agony, sorrow, a writhing, anguish
  - writhing (of fear)
  - anguish
