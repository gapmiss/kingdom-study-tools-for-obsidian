# H1223 בׇּצְרָה / בצרה

## botsrâh

_(bots-raw' | bohts-RAW | bohts-RA)_

## Definition

sheepfold; feminine from H1219; an enclosure, i.e., sheep fold; Bozrah; n-f

- enclosure, fold, sheepfold
- (n pr) Bozrah
  - a chief city of the Edomites, a fortified place
