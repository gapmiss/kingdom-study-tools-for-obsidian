# H7613 שְׂאֵת / שאת

## sᵉʼêth

_(seh-ayth' | seh-ATE | seh-ATE)_

## Definition

from H5375; an elevation or leprous scab; figuratively, elation or cheerfulness; exaltation in rank or character; be accepted, dignity, excellency, highness, raise up self, rising; n-f

- elevation, exaltation, dignity, swelling, uprising
  - dignity, exaltation, loftiness
  - swelling
  - uprising
