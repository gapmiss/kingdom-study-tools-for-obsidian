# H5399 נֶשֶׁף / נשף

## nesheph

_(neh'-shef | neh-SHEP | neh-SHEF)_

## Definition

from H5398; properly, a breeze, i.e., (by implication) dusk (when the evening breeze prevails); dark, dawning of the day (morning), night, twilight; n-m

- twilight
  - evening twilight
  - morning twilight
