# H853 אֵת / את

## ʼêth

_(ayth | ate | ate)_

## Definition

apparent contracted from H0226 in the demonstrative sense of entity; properly, self (but generally used to point out more definitely the object of a verb or preposition, even or namely); [as such unrepresented in English]; prt

- sign of the definite direct object, not translated in English but generally preceding and indicating the accusative
