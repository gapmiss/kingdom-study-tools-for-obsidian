# H2333 חַוָּה / חוה

## chavvâh

_(khav-vaw' | ha-WAW | ha-WA)_

## Definition

properly, the same as H2332 (lifegiving, i.e., living-place); by implication, an encampment or village; (small) town; n-f

- village, town, tent village
