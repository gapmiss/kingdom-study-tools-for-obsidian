# H4798 מַרְזֵחַ / מרזח

## marzêach

_(mar-zay'-akh | mahr-DZAY-ah | mahr-ZAY-ak)_

## Definition

formed like H4797; a cry, i.e., (of grief) a lamentation; mourning; n-m

- cry, cry of joy, revelry
  - mourning cry
    - perhaps, feast cry
  - cry of revelry
