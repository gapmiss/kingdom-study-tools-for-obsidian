# H6927 קַדְמָה / קדמה

## qadmâh

_(kad-maw' | kahd-MAW | kahd-MA)_

## Definition

from H6923; priority (in time); also used adverbially (before); afore, antiquity, former (old) estate; n-f

- antiquity, former state or estate or situation, before, origin
  - antiquity, beginning, origin
  - former state or situation
  - (conj) before
