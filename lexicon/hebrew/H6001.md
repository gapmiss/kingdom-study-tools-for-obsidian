# H6001 עָמֵל / עמל

## ʻâmêl

_(aw-male' | aw-MALE | ah-MALE)_

## Definition

from H5998; toiling; concretely, a laborer; figuratively, sorrowful; that laboureth, that is a misery, had taken (labour), wicked, workman; a

- (n m) laborer, sufferer, wretched one
  - laborer, workman
  - sufferer
- (adj v) toiling
