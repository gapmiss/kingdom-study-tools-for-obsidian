# H6620 פֶּתֶן / פתן

## pethen

_(peh'-then | peh-TEN | peh-TEN)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to twist; an asp (from its contortions); adder; n-m

- a snake, venomous serpent
  - perhaps the cobra, adder, or viper
