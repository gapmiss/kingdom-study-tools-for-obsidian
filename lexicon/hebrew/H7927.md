# H7927 שְׁכֶם / שכם

## Shᵉkem

_(shek-em' | sheh-KEM | sheh-HEM)_

## Definition

back, shoulder; the same as H7926; ridge; Shekem, a place in Palestine; Shechem; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) son of Hamor, the chieftain of the Hivites at Shechem at the time of Jacob's arrival
- (n pr loc) a city in Manasseh; located in a valley between Mount Ebal and Mount Gerizim, 34 miles (54 km) north of Jerusalem and 7 miles (10.5 km) south-east of Samaria
