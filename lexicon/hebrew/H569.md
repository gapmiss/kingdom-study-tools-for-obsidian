# H569 אַמְרָפֶל / אמרפל

## ʼAmrâphel

_(am-raw-fel' | am-raw-PEL | am-ra-FEL)_

## Definition

sayer of darkness, fall of the sayer; of uncertain (perhaps foreign) derivation; Amraphel, a king of Shinar; Amraphel; n-pr-m

- the king of Shinar (Babylon) (perhaps Hammurabi, circa 2100 BC)
