# H1865 דְּרוֹר / דרור

## dᵉrôwr

_(der-ore' | deh-RORE | deh-RORE)_

## Definition

from an unused root (meaning to move rapidly); freedom; hence, spontaneity of outflow, and so clear; liberty, pure; n-m

- a flowing, free run, liberty
  - flowing (of myrrh)
  - liberty
