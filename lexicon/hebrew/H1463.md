# H1463 גּוֹג / גוג

## Gôwg

_(gohg | ɡoɡe | ɡoɡe)_

## Definition

mountain; of uncertain derivation; Gog, the name of an Israelite, also of some nothern nation; Gog; n-pr-m

- a Reubenite, son of Shemaiah
- the prophetic prince of Rosh, Meshech and Tubal, and Magog
