# H811 אֶשְׁכּוֹל / אשכול

## ʼeshkôwl

_(esh-kole' | esh-KOLE | esh-KOLE)_

## Definition

or אֶשְׁכֹּל; probably prolonged from H0810; a bunch of grapes or other fruit; cluster (of grapes); n-m

- cluster
  - of grapes
  - of flowers (metaphor of lover)
