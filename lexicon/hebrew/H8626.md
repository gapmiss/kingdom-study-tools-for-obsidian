# H8626 תָּקַן / תקן

## tâqan

_(taw-kan' | taw-KAHN | ta-KAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to equalize, i.e., straighten (intransitive or transitive); figuratively, to compose; set in order, make straight; v

- to equalise, make straight, become straight
  - (Qal) to become straight
  - (Piel) to make straight, put straight, arrange in order, set in order
