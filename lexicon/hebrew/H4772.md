# H4772 מַרְגְלָה / מרגלה

## margᵉlâh

_(mar-ghel-aw' | mahr-ɡeh-LAW | mahr-ɡeh-LA)_

## Definition

denominative from H7272; (plural for collective) a footpiece, i.e., (adverbially) at the foot, or (direct.) the foot itself; feet; n-f

- place of the feet, feet

## Note

Compare H4763.
