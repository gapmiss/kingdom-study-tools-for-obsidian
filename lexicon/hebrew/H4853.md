# H4853 מַשָּׂא / משא

## massâʼ

_(mas-saw' | mahs-SAW | ma-SA)_

## Definition

burden; from H5375; a burden; figuratively, an utterance, chiefly a doom, especially singing; mental, desire; burden, carry away, prophecy, × they set, song, tribute; n-m n-pr-m

- (n m) load, bearing, tribute, burden, lifting
  - load, burden
  - lifting, uplifting, that to which the soul lifts itself up
  - bearing, carrying
  - tribute, that which is carried or brought or borne
- utterance, oracle, burden
- (n pr m) a son of Ishmael
