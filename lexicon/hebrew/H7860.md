# H7860 שֹׁטֵר / שטר

## shôṭêr

_(sho-tare' | shoh-TARE | shoh-TARE)_

## Definition

active participle of an otherwise unused root probably meaning to write; properly, a scribe, i.e., (by analogy or implication) an official superintendent or magistrate; officer, overseer, ruler; n-m

- (Qal) official, officer
