# H1281 בָּרִיחַ / בריח

## bârîyach

_(baw-ree'-akh | baw-REE-ah | ba-REE-ak)_

## Definition

or בָּרִחַ; (shortened) from H1272; a fugitive, i.e., the serpent (as fleeing), and the constellation by that name; crooked, noble, piercing; n-m

- (adj) fleeing
- (subst) fugitive
