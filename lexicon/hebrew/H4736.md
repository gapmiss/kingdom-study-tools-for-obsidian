# H4736 מִקְנָה / מקנה

## miqnâh

_(mik-naw' | mik-NAW | meek-NA)_

## Definition

feminine of H4735; properly, a buying, i.e., acquisition; concretely, a piece of property (land or living); also the sum paid; (he that is) bought, possession, piece, purchase; n-f

- purchase
  - purchase
  - purchase-price
  - possession (gained by purchase)
