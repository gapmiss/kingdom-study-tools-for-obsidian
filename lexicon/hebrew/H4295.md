# H4295 מַטָּה / מטה

## maṭṭâh

_(mat'-taw | maht-TAW | ma-TA)_

## Definition

from H5786 with directive enclitic appended; downward, below or beneath; often adverbially with or without prefixes; beneath, down(-ward), less, very low, under(-neath); adv

- downwards, below
  - downwards
  - under (of age)
  - beneath
