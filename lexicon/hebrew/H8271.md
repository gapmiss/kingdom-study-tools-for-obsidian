# H8271 שְׁרֵא / שרא

## shᵉrêʼ

_(sher-ay' | sheh-RAY | sheh-RAY)_

## Definition

a root corresponding to that of H8293; to free, separate; figuratively, to unravel, commence; by implication (of unloading beasts) to reside; begin, dissolve, dwell, loose; v

- to loosen, abide, begin
  - (P'al)
    - to loosen
    - to abide (from loosing girths for camp)
  - (Pael) to begin, open
  - (Ithpael) to be loosened
