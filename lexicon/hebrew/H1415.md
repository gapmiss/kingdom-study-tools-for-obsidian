# H1415 גָּדָה / גדה

## gâdâh

_(gaw-daw' | ɡaw-DAW | ɡa-DA)_

## Definition

from an unused root (meaning to cut off); a border of a river (as cut into by the stream); bank; n-f

- a river bank
