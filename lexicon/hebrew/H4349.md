# H4349 מָכוֹן / מכון

## mâkôwn

_(maw-kone' | maw-KONE | ma-HONE)_

## Definition

from H3559; properly, a fixture, i.e., a basis; generally a place, especially as an abode; foundation, habitation, (settled) (dwelling-)place; n-m

- fixed or established place, foundation
  - fixed place
  - foundation
