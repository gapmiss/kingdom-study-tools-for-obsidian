# H5913 עָכַס / עכס

## ʻâkaç

_(aw-kas' | aw-KAHS | ah-HAHS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to tie, specifically, with fetters; but used only as denominative from H5914; to put on anklets; make a tinkling ornament; v

- (Piel) to shake bangles, rattle, tinkle
