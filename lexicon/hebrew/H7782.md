# H7782 שׁוֹפָר / שופר

## shôwphâr

_(sho-far' | shoh-PAWR | shoh-FAHR)_

## Definition

or שֹׁפָר; from H8231 in the original sense of incising; a cornet (as giving a clear sound) or curved horn; cornet, trumpet; n-m

- horn, ram's horn
