# H692 אַרְאֵלִי / אראלי

## ʼArʼêlîy

_(ar-ay-lee' | ar-ay-LEE | ar-ay-LEE)_

## Definition

lion of God; from H0691; heroic; Areli (or an Arelite, collectively), an Israelite and his descendants; Areli, Arelites; n-pr-m

- a son of Gad
- those of the family of Areli (pater adj—Arelites)
