# H5248 נִמְרוֹד / נמרוד

## Nimrôwd

_(nim-rode' | nim-RODE | neem-RODE)_

## Definition

rebellion, the valiant; or נִמְרֹד; probably of foreign origin; Nimrod, a son of Cush; Nimrod; n-pr-m

- the son of Cush, grandson of Ham, and great grandson of Noah; a mighty hunter, he established an empire in the area of Babylon and Assyria
