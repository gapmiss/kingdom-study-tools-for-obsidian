# H424 אֵלָה / אלה

## ʼêlâh

_(ay-law' | ay-LAW | ay-LA)_

## Definition

feminine of H0352; an oak or other strong tree; elm, oak, teil-tree; n-f

- terebinth, terebinth tree
- valley where David killed Goliath
