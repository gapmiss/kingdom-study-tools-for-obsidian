# H6612 פְּתִי / פתי

## pᵉthîy

_(peth-ee' | peh-TEE | peh-TEE)_

## Definition

or פֶּתִי; or פְּתָאִי; from H6601; silly (i.e., seducible); foolish, simple(-icity) (one); a

- (n f) simplicity, naivete
- (adj) simple, foolish, open-minded
