# H7498 רָפָא / רפא

## Râphâʼ

_(raw-faw' | raw-PAW | ra-FA)_

## Definition

tall; or רָפָה; probably the same as H7497; giant; Rapha or Raphah, the name of two Israelites; Rapha; n-pr-m

- son of Binea and a descendant of Saul
- son of Benjamin
