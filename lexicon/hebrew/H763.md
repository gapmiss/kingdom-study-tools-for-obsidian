# H763 אֲרַם נַהֲרַיִם / ארם נהרים

## ʼĂram Nahărayim

_(ar-am' nah-har-ah'-yim | uh-RAHM na-huh-ra-YIM | uh-RAHM na-huh-ra-YEEM)_

## Definition

Aram of the two rivers; from H0758 and the dual of H5104; Aram of (the) two rivers (Euphrates and Tigris) or Mesopotamia; Aram-naharaim, Mesopotamia; n-pr-loc

- Mesopotamia
