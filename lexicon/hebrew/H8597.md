# H8597 תִּפְאָרָה / תפארה

## tiphʼârâh

_(tif-aw-raw' | tip-aw-RAW | teef-ah-RA)_

## Definition

or תִּפְאֶרֶת; from H6286; ornament (abstractly or concretely, literally or figuratively); beauty(-iful), bravery, comely, fair, glory(-ious), honour, majesty; n-f

- beauty, splendor, glory
  - beauty, finery (of garments, jewels)
  - glory
    - of rank, renown
    - as attribute of God
  - honor (or nation Israel)
  - glorying, boasting (of individual)
