# H1814 דָּלַק / דלק

## dâlaq

_(daw-lak' | daw-LAHK | da-LAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to flame (literally or figuratively); burning, chase, inflame, kindle, persecute(-or), pursue hotly; v

- to burn, hotly pursue
  - (Qal)
    - to burn
    - to hotly pursue
  - (Hiphil)
    - to kindle
    - to inflame
