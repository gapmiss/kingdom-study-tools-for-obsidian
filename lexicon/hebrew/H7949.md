# H7949 שָׁלַג / שלג

## shâlag

_(shaw-lag' | shaw-LAHɡ | sha-LAHɡ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, meaning to be white; used only as denominative from H7950; to be snow-white (with the linen clothing of the slain); be as snow; v

- (Hiphil) to snow, be as snow
