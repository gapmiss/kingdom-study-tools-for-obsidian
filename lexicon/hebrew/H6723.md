# H6723 צִיָּה / ציה

## tsîyâh

_(tsee-yaw' | tsih-YAW | tsee-YA)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to parch; aridity; concretely, a desert; barren, drought, dry (land, place), solitary place, wilderness; n-f

- dryness, drought, desert
