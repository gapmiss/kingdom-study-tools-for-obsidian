# H7799 שׁוּשַׁן / שושן

## shûwshan

_(shoo-shan' | shoo-SHAHN | shoo-SHAHN)_

## Definition

or שׁוֹשָׁן; or שֹׁשָׁן; and (feminine) שׁוֹשַׁנָּה; from H7797; a lily (from its whiteness), as a flower of architectural ornament; also a (straight) trumpet (from the tubular shape); lily, Shoshannim; n-m

- lily
  - probably any lily-like flower
  - Shoshannim, in title of Psalm 45
