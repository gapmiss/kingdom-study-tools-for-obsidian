# H739 אֲרִיאֵל / אריאל

## ʼărîyʼêl

_(ar-ee-ale' | uh-ree-ALE | uh-ree-ALE)_

## Definition

or אֲרִאֵל; from H0738 and H0410; lion of God; i.e., heroic; lionlike men; n-m

- lion-like
- two sons of Ariel of Moab

## Note

The meaning is considered uncertain.
