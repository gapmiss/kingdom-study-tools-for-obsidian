# H8464 תַּחְמָס / תחמס

## tachmâç

_(takh-mawce' | ta-MAWS | tahk-MAHS)_

## Definition

from H2554; a species of unclean bird (from its violence), perhaps an owl; night hawk; n-m

- a ceremonially unclean bird
  - a name of the male ostrich (known for its unusual cruelty toward its young)
  - perhaps an extinct bird, exact meaning unknown
