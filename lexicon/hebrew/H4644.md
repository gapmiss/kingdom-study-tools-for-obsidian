# H4644 מֹף / מף

## Môph

_(mofe | mope | mofe)_

## Definition

haven of the good; of Egyptian origin; Moph, the capital of Lower Egypt; Memphis; n-pr-loc

- a capital of Lower Egypt located on the western bank of the Nile about 9 miles (15 km) south of Cairo
