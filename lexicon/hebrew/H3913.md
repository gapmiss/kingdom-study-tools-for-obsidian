# H3913 לָטַשׁ / לטש

## lâṭash

_(law-tash' | law-TAHSH | la-TAHSH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to hammer out (an edge), i.e., to sharpen; instructer, sharp(-en), whet; v

- to sharpen, hammer, whet
  - (Qal)
    - to hammer
    - to sharpen (sword)
    - hammerer (participle)
  - (Pual) to be sharpened
