# H6856 צִפֹּרֶן / צפרן

## tsippôren

_(tsip-po'-ren | tsip-poh-REN | tsee-poh-REN)_

## Definition

from H6852 (in the denominative sense (from H6833) of scratching); properly, a claw, i.e., (human) nail; also the point of a style (or pen, tipped with adamant); nail, point; n-m

- fingernail, stylus point
  - finger-nail
  - point (of a diamond)
