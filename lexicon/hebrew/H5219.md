# H5219 נְכֹאת / נכאת

## nᵉkôʼth

_(nek-ohth' | neh-KOTE | neh-HOTE)_

## Definition

from H5218; properly, a smiting, i.e., (concretely) an aromatic gum (perhaps styrax) (as powdered); spicery(-ces); n-f

- a spice
  - perhaps tragacanth gum
