# H8311 שָׂרַע / שרע

## sâraʻ

_(saw-rah' | saw-RA | sa-RA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to prolong, i.e., (reflex) be deformed by excess of members; stretch out self, (have any) superfluous thing; v

- to extend, stretch out
  - (Qal) extended (participle)
  - (Hithpael) to stretch oneself
