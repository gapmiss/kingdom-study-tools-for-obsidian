# H1441 גִּדְעֹנִי / גדעני

## Gidʻônîy

_(ghid-o-nee' | ɡid-oh-NEE | ɡeed-oh-NEE)_

## Definition

my hewer; from H1438; warlike (compare H1439); Gidoni, an Israelite; Gideoni; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite, father of Abidan
