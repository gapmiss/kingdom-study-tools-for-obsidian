# H5423 נָתַק / נתק

## nâthaq

_(naw-thak' | naw-TAHK | na-TAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to tear off; break (off), burst, draw (away), lift up, pluck (away, off), pull (out), root out; v

- to pull or tear or draw off or away or apart, draw out, pluck up, break, lift, root out
  - (Qal)
    - to draw away
    - to draw or pull off
    - to pull or tear away
  - (Niphal)
    - to be drawn away, be drawn out
    - to be torn apart or in two, be snapped
    - to be separated
  - (Piel)
    - to tear apart, snap
    - to tear out, tear up, tear away
  - (Hiphil)
    - to draw away
    - to drag away
  - (Hophal) to be drawn away
