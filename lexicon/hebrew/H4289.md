# H4289 מַחְתָּה / מחתה

## machtâh

_(makh-taw' | ma-TAW | mahk-TA)_

## Definition

the same as H4288 in the sense of removal; a pan for live coals; censer, firepan, snuffdish; n-f

- fire-holder, censer, firepan, snuff dish, tray
  - snuff-dish
  - fire-pans
  - censer
