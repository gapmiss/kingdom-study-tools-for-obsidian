# H72 אֶבֶן הָעֵזֶר / אבן העזר

## ʼEben hâ-ʻÊzer

_(eh'-ben haw-e'-zer | eh-BEN haw-ay-DZER | eh-VEN ha-ay-ZER)_

## Definition

stone of help; from H0068 and H5828 with the article inserted; stone of the help; Eben-ha-Ezer, a place in Palestine; Ebenezer; n

- memorial stone erected by Samuel to mark where God helped Israel to defeat the Philistines—north of Jerusalem
