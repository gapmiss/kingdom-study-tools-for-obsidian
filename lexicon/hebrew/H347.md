# H347 אִיּוֹב / איוב

## ʼÎyôwb

_(ee-yobe' | ih-YOBE | ee-YOVE)_

## Definition

hated; from H0340; hated (i.e., persecuted); Ijob, the patriarch famous for his patience; Job; n-pr-m

- a patriarch, the subject of the book of Job
