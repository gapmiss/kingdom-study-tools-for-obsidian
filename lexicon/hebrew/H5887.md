# H5887 עֵין תַּפּוּחַ / עין תפוח

## ʻÊyn Tappûwach

_(ane tap-poo'-akh | ane tahp-POO-ah | ane ta-POO-ak)_

## Definition

fountain of the apple-city; from H5869 and H8598; fountain of an apple-tree; En-Tappuach, a place in Palestine; En-tappuah; n-pr-loc

- a place on the border between Ephraim and Manasseh
