# H892 בָּבָה / בבה

## bâbâh

_(baw-baw' | baw-BAW | ba-VA)_

## Definition

feminine active participle of an unused root meaning to hollow out; something hollowed (as a gate), i.e., pupil of the eye; apple (of the eye); n-f

- the apple (pupil) of the eye
