# H4448 מָלַל / מלל

## mâlal

_(maw-lal' | maw-LAHL | ma-LAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to speak (mostly poetical) or say; say, speak, utter; v

- to speak, utter, say
  - (Qal) to speak
  - (Piel) to say, utter
