# H2191 זְעֵיר / זעיר

## zᵉʻêyr

_(zeh-ayr' | dzeh-ARE | zeh-ARE)_

## Definition

from an unused root (akin (by permutation) to H6819), meaning to dwindle; small; little; n-m

- a little
  - of quantity
  - of time
