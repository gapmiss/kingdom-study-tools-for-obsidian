# H8155 שָׁסַס / שסס

## shâçaç

_(shaw-sas' | shaw-SAHS | sha-SAHS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to plunder; rifle, spoil; v

- to plunder, spoil
  - (Qal) to plunder
  - (Niphal) to be plundered, be rifled
