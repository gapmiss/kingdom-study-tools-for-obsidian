# H7809 שָׁחַד / שחד

## shâchad

_(shaw-khad' | shaw-HAHD | sha-HAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to donate, i.e., bribe; hire, give a reward; v

- (Qal) to give a present, bribe, ransom, offer gifts
