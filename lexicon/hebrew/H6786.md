# H6786 צְמָרִי / צמרי

## Tsᵉmârîy

_(tsem-aw-ree' | tseh-maw-REE | tseh-ma-REE)_

## Definition

double woolens; patrial from an unused name of a place in Palestine; a Tsemarite or branch of the Canaanites; Zemarite; a

- one of the Hamite tribes descended from Canaan and inhabitants of an unknown Canaanite city
