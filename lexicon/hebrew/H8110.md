# H8110 שִׁמְרוֹן / שמרון

## Shimrôwn

_(shim-rone' | shim-RONE | sheem-RONE)_

## Definition

watch-height; from H8105 in its original sense; guardianship; Shimron, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine; Shimron; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) 4th son of Issachar and the progenitor of the family of Shimronites
- (n pr loc) a Canaanite royal city allotted to Zebulun
