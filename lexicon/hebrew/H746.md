# H746 אֲרְיוֹךְ / אריוך

## ʼĂryôwk

_(ar-yoke' | ur-YOKE | ur-YOKE)_

## Definition

lion-like; of foreign origin; Arjok, the name of two Babylonians; Arioch; n-pr-m

- the ancient king of Ellasar, ally of Chedorlaomer
- the chief of the executioners for Nebuchadnezzar
