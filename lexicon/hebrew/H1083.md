# H1083 בִּלְגַה / בלגה

## Bilgah

_(bil-gaw' | bil-ɡA | beel-ɡA)_

## Definition

cheerfulness; from H1082; desistance; Bilgah, the name of two Israelites; Bilgah; n-pr-m

- a priest of the 15th course, in David's time
- a priest accompanying Zerubbabel
