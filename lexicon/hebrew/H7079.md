# H7079 קְנָת / קנת

## Qᵉnâth

_(ken-awth' | keh-NAWT | keh-NAHT)_

## Definition

possession; from H7069; possession; Kenath, a place East of the Jordan; Kenath; n-pr-loc

- a city in Gilead east of the Jordan taken possession of by a certain Nobah and renamed in his own honor
