# H5369 נֵר / נר

## Nêr

_(nare | nare | nare)_

## Definition

lamp; the same as H5216; lamp; Ner, an Israelite; Ner; n-pr-m

- son of Jehiel, father of Kish, and grandfather of king Saul
- father of Abner, the uncle of Saul
  - perhaps the same as 1
