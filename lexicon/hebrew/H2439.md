# H2439 חִישׁ / חיש

## chîysh

_(kheesh | heesh | heesh)_

## Definition

another form of H2363; to hurry; make haste; v

- to haste, make haste, hurry
  - (Qal) to make haste
  - (Hiph)
    - to show haste, act quickly, hasten, come quickly
    - to enjoy, be excited
