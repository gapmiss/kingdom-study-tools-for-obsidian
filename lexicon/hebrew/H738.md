# H738 אֲרִי / ארי

## ʼărîy

_(ar-ee' | uh-REE | uh-REE)_

## Definition

or (prolonged) אַרְיֵה; from H0717 (in the sense of violence); a lion; (young) lion, + pierce (from the margin); n-m

- lion
  - pictures or images of lions
