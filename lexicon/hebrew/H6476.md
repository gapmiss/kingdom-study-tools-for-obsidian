# H6476 פָּצַח / פצח

## pâtsach

_(paw-tsakh' | paw-TSA | pa-TSAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to break out (in joyful sound); break (forth, forth into joy), make a loud noise; v

- to cause to break or burst forth, break forth with, break out
  - (Qal) to break out, break forth with
  - (Piel) to break up
