# H4962 מַת / מת

## math

_(math | maht | maht)_

## Definition

from the same as H4970; properly, an adult (as of full length); by implication, a man (only in the plural); + few, × friends, men, persons, × small; n-m

- male, man
  - males, men
  - few men (in prose)
    - less emphasis on sex
  - men (poetic)
    - less emphasis on sex
