# H3852 לֶהָבָה / להבה

## lehâbâh

_(leh-aw-baw' | leh-haw-BAW | leh-ha-VA)_

## Definition

or לַהֶבֶת; feminine of H3851, and meaning the same; flame; flame(-ming), head (of a spear); n-f

- flame
- tip of weapon, point, head of spear
