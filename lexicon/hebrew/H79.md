# H79 אָבַק / אבק

## ʼâbaq

_(aw-bak' | aw-BAHK | ah-VAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root, probably to float away (as vapor), but used only as denominative from H0080; to bedust, i.e., grapple; wrestle; v

- (Niphal) to wrestle, grapple (get dusty), bedust
