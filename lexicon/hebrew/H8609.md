# H8609 תָּפַר / תפר

## tâphar

_(taw-far' | taw-PAHR | ta-FAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to sew; (women that) sew (together); v

- to sew together
  - (Qal) to sew, sew together
  - (Piel)
    - to sew
    - sewing (participle)
