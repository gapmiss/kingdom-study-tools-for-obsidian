# H5513 סִינִי / סיני

## Çîynîy

_(see-nee' | see-NEE | see-NEE)_

## Definition

thorn, clay; from an otherwise unknown name of a man; a Sinite, or descendant of one of the sons of Canaan; Sinite; a

- a tribe of the Canaanites descended from Canaan inhabiting the northern part of the Lebanon district
