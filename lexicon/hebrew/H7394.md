# H7394 רֵכָב / רכב

## Rêkâb

_(ray-kawb' | ray-KAWB | ray-HAHV)_

## Definition

rider; from H7392; rider; Rekab, the name of two Arabs and of two Israelites; Rechab; n-pr-m a

- (n pr m) father of Jehonadab in the time of king Jehu of the northern kingdom of Israel
- father of Malchijah, a leader of the district of Beth Haccerem and repairer of the wall of Jerusalem in the time of Nehemiah
- one of the 2 captains whom Ishbosheth took into his service and who conspired to murder him
- (adj pr) descendants of Rechab
