# H6313 פּוּג / פוג

## pûwg

_(poog | pooɡ | pooɡ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be sluggish; cease, be feeble, faint, be slacked; v

- to grow numb, be feeble, be benumbed
  - (Qal) to be feeble
  - (Niphal) to be benumbed
