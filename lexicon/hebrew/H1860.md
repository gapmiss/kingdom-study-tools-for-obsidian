# H1860 דְּרָאוֹן / דראון

## dᵉrâʼôwn

_(der-aw-one' | deh-raw-ONE | deh-ra-ONE)_

## Definition

or דֵּרָאוֹן; from an unused root (meaning to repulse); an object of aversion; abhorring, contempt; n-m

- aversion, abhorrence
