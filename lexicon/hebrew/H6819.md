# H6819 צָעַר / צער

## tsâʻar

_(tsaw-ar' | tsaw-AR | tsa-AR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be small, i.e., (figuratively) ignoble; be brought low, little one, be small; v

- to be or grow insignificant, grow small
  - (Qal) to ge insignificant
