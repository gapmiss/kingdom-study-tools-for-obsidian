# H7540 רָקַד / רקד

## râqad

_(raw-kad' | raw-KAHD | ra-KAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to stamp, i.e., to spring about (wildly or for joy); dance, jump, leap, skip; v

- to skip about
  - (Qal) to skip about
  - (Piel) to dance, leap
  - (Hiphil) to make to skip
