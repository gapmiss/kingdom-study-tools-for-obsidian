# H3654 כֵּן / כן

## kên

_(kane | kane | kane)_

## Definition

from H3661 in the sense of fastening; (from infixing its sting; used only in plural (and irregular in Exodus 8:17,18; Hebrews 13:14)); a gnat; lice, × manner; n-m

- gnat, gnats, gnat-swarm
