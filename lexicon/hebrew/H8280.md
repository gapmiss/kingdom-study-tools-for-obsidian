# H8280 שָׂרָה / שרה

## sârâh

_(saw-raw' | saw-RAW | sa-RA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to prevail; have power (as a prince); v

- contend, have power, contend with, persist, exert oneself, persevere
  - (Qal) to persevere, contend with
