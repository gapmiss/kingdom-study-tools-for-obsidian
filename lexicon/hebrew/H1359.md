# H1359 גֹּב / גב

## Gôb

_(gobe | ɡobe | ɡove)_

## Definition

cistern; or (fully) גּוֹב; from H1461; pit; Gob, a place in Palestine; Gob; n-pr-loc

- a place which was the scene of two encounters between David's warriors and the Philistines (also &quot;Gezer&quot;)
