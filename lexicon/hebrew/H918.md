# H918 בָּדַק / בדק

## bâdaq

_(baw-dak' | baw-DAHK | ba-DAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to gap open; used only as a denominative from H0919; to mend a breach; repair; v

- (Qal) to mend, repair (a building)
