# H6070 עַנְתֹתִיָּה / ענתתיה

## ʻAnthôthîyâh

_(an-tho-thee-yaw' | an-toh-tih-YAW | an-toh-tee-YA)_

## Definition

Jehovah's answer; from the same as H6068 and H3050; answers of Jah; Anthothijah, an Israelite; Antothijah; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite, one of the sons of Jeroham
