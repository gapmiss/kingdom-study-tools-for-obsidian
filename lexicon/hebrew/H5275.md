# H5275 נַעַל / נעל

## naʻal

_(nah'-al | na-AL | na-AL)_

## Definition

or (feminine) נַעֲלָה; from H5274; properly, a sandal tongue; by extension a sandal or slipper (sometimes as a symbol of occupancy, a refusal to marry, or of something valueless); dryshod, (pair of) shoe(-latchet, -s); n-f

- sandal, shoe
