# H5091 נָהָה / נהה

## nâhâh

_(naw-haw' | naw-HAW | na-HA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to groan, i.e., bewail; hence (through the idea of crying aloud), to assemble (as if on proclamation); lament, wail; v

- to wail, lament
  - (Qal) to wail, lament
  - (Niphal) to go mourning after
