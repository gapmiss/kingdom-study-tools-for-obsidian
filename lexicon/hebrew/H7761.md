# H7761 שׂוּם / שום

## sûwm

_(soom | soom | soom)_

## Definition

corresponding to H7760; to put (used in a great variety of applications, literal, figurative, inferentially, and elliptically); + command, give, lay, make, + name, + regard, set; v

- to set, make, appoint
  - (P'al)
    - to make, make decree, set out (decree)
    - to make, appoint
    - to set, fix
  - (Ithp'al) to be made, be set, be laid
