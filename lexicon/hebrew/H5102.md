# H5102 נָהַר / נהר

## nâhar

_(naw-har' | naw-HAHR | na-HAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to sparkle, i.e., (figuratively) be cheerful; flow (together), be lightened; v

- to shine, beam, light, burn
  - (Qal) to beam, be radiant
- to flow, stream
  - (Qal) to flow, stream
