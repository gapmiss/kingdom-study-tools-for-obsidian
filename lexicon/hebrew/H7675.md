# H7675 שֶׁבֶת / שבת

## shebeth

_(sheh'-beth | sheh-BET | sheh-VET)_

## Definition

infinitive of H3427; properly, session; but used also concretely, an abode or locality; place, seat; n-f

- (Qal) seat, dwelling, place

## Note

Compare H3429.
