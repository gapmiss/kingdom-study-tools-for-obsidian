# H7696 שָׁגַע / שגע

## shâgaʻ

_(shaw-gah' | shaw-ɡA | sha-ɡA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to rave through insanity; (be, play the) mad (man); v

- to be mad
  - (Pual)
    - to be mad
    - to be maddened (participle)
  - (Hithpael) to show madness
