# H1280 בְּרִיחַ / בריח

## bᵉrîyach

_(ber-ee'-akh | beh-REE-ah | beh-REE-ak)_

## Definition

from H1272; a bolt; bar, fugitive; n-m

- bar
  - of wood
  - of city gates
- of tribulation, a fortress, of the earth as a prison (figuratively)
