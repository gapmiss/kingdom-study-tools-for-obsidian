# H774 אַרְפָּד / ארפד

## ʼArpâd

_(ar-pawd' | ar-PAWD | ar-PAHD)_

## Definition

I shall be spread out (or supported); from H7502; spread out; Arpad, a place in Syria; Arpad, Arphad; n-pr-loc

- a city in northern Syria cited as an example of the Assyrian conquest
