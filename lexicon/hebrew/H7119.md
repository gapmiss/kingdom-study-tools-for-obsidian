# H7119 קַר / קר

## qar

_(kar | kahr | kahr)_

## Definition

contracted from an unused root meaning to chill; cool; figuratively, quiet; cold, excellent (from the margin); a

- cool
  - cool, calm, self-possessed (of spirit)
