# H757 אַרְכִּי / ארכי

## ʼArkîy

_(ar-kee' | ar-KEE | ar-KEE)_

## Definition

lengthy; patrial from another place (in Palestine) of similar name with H0751; an Arkite or native of Erek; Archi, Archite; a

- referring to the home city of Hushai
