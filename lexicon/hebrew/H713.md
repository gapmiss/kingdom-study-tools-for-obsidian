# H713 אַרְגָּמָן / ארגמן

## ʼargâmân

_(ar-gaw-mawn' | ar-ɡaw-MAWN | ar-ɡa-MAHN)_

## Definition

of foreign origin; purple (the color or the dyed stuff); purple; n-m

- purple, red-purple
