# H5312 נְפַק / נפק

## nᵉphaq

_(nef-ak' | neh-PAHK | neh-FAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to issue; causatively, to bring out; come (go, take) forth (out); v

- to go or come out, bring or come forth
  - (P'al) to go out or forth
  - (Aphel) to bring forth
