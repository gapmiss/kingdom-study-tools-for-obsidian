# H402 אׇכְלָה / אכלה

## ʼoklâh

_(ok-law' | oke-LAW | oke-LA)_

## Definition

feminine of H0401; food; consume, devour, eat, food, meat; n-f

- food
  - food, eating
  - object of devouring, consuming
    - by wild beasts (figurative)
    - in fire
    - of judgment (figurative)
