# H1503 גֶּזַע / גזע

## gezaʻ

_(geh'-zah | ɡeh-DZA | ɡeh-ZA)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to cut down (trees); the trunk or stump of a tree (as felled or as planted); stem, stock; n-m

- stem, trunk, stock (of trees)
