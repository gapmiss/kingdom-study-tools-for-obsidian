# H2240 זַתּוּא / זתוא

## Zattûwʼ

_(zat-too' | dzaht-TOO | za-TOO)_

## Definition

brightness of him; of uncertain derivation; Zattu, an Israelite; Zattu; n-pr-m

- a family of exiles who returned with Zerubbabel
