# H6154 עֵרֶב / ערב

## ʻêreb

_(ay'-reb | ay-REB | ay-REV)_

## Definition

or עֶרֶב; (1 Kings 10:15), (with the article prefix), from H6148; the web (or transverse threads of cloth); also a mixture, (or mongrel race); Arabia, mingled people, mixed (multitude), woof; n-m

- woof
  - as mixed, interwoven
  - knitted material
- mixture, mixed people, mixed company
