# H8401 תֶּבֶן / תבן

## teben

_(teh'-ben | teh-BEN | teh-VEN)_

## Definition

probably from H1129; properly, material, i.e., (specifically) refuse haum or stalks of grain (as chopped in threshing and used for fodder); chaff, straw, stubble; n-m

- straw, stubble
  - as building material
  - as fodder for livestock
