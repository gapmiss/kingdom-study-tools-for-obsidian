# H4407 מִלּוֹא / מלוא

## millôwʼ

_(mil-lo' | mil-LOH | mee-LOH)_

## Definition

rampart, mound; or מִלֹּא; (2 Kings 12:20), from H4390; a rampart (as filled in), i.e., the citadel; Millo; n-pr-m

- a place near Shechem; site unknown
- a part of the fortifications of Jerusalem
