# H792 אֶשְׁבַּעַל / אשבעל

## ʼEshbaʻal

_(esh-bah'-al | esh-ba-AL | esh-ba-AL)_

## Definition

a man of Baal; from H0376 and H1168; man of Baal; Eshbaal (or Ishbosheth), a son of Saul; Eshbaal; n-pr-m

- the fourth son of Saul, also called Ishbosheth
