# H4731 מַקֵּל / מקל

## maqqêl

_(mak-kale | mahk-KALE | ma-KALE)_

## Definition

or (feminine) מַקְּלָה; from an unused root meaning apparently to germinate; a shoot, i.e., stick (with leaves on, or for walking, striking, guiding, divining); rod, (hand-)staff; n-m

- rod, staff
  - rod, stick
  - staff (in travel)
  - wand (of diviner)
