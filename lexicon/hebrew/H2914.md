# H2914 טְחֹר / טחר

## ṭᵉchôr

_(tekh-ore' | teh-HORE | teh-HORE)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to burn; a boil or ulcer (from the inflammation), especially a tumor in the anus or pudenda (the piles); emerod; n-m

- tumours, haemorrhoids
