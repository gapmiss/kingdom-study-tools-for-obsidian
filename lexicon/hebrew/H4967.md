# H4967 מְתוּשָׁאֵל / מתושאל

## Mᵉthûwshâʼêl

_(meth-oo-shaw-ale' | meh-too-shaw-ALE | meh-too-sha-ALE)_

## Definition

who is of God; from H4962 and H0410, with the relative interposed; man who (is) of God; Methusael, an antediluvian patriarch; Methusael; n-pr-m

- son of Mehujael, 4th in descent from Cain, and father of Lamech
