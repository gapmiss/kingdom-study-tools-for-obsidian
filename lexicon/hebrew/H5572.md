# H5572 סְנֶה / סנה

## çᵉneh

_(sen-eh' | seh-NEH | seh-NEH)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to prick; a bramble; bush; n-m

- a bush, thorny bush
  - the burning bush of Moses
  - perhaps a blackberry bush
