# H4024 מִגְדּוֹל / מגדול

## Migdôwl

_(mig-dole' | miɡ-DOLE | meeɡ-DOLE)_

## Definition

tower; or מִגְדֹּל; probably of Egyptian origin; Migdol, a place in Egypt; Migdol, tower; n-pr-m n-m

- (n pr m) a fortified city on the Egyptian border
- (n m) tower
