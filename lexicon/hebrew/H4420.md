# H4420 מְלֵחָה / מלחה

## mᵉlêchâh

_(mel-ay-khaw' | meh-lay-HAW | meh-lay-HA)_

## Definition

from H4414 (in its denominative sense); understood as land (H0776); properly, salted (i.e., land), i.e., a desert; barren land(-ness), salt (land); n-f

- saltness, barrenness, saltiness
