# H1502 גַּזָּם / גזם

## Gazzâm

_(gaz-zawm' | ɡahdz-ZAWM | ɡa-ZAHM)_

## Definition

devouring; from the same as H1501; devourer; Gazzam, one of the Nethinim; Gazzam; n-pr-m

- the head of a family of temple slaves returning from exile with Zerubbabel
