# H7345 רְחַבְיָה / רחביה

## Rᵉchabyâh

_(rekh-ab-yaw' | reh-hahb-YAW | reh-hahv-YA)_

## Definition

Jehovah has enlarged; or רְחַבְיָהוּ; from H7337 and H3050; Jah has enlarged; Rechabjah, an Israelite; Rehabiah; n-pr-m

- the only son of Eliezer and grandson of Moses
