# H2529 חֶמְאָה / חמאה

## chemʼâh

_(khem-aw' | hem-AW | hem-AH)_

## Definition

or (shortened) חֵמָה; from the same root as H2346; curdled milk or cheese; butter; n-f

- curd, butter
