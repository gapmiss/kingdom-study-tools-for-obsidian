# H620 אׇסְנַפַּר / אסנפר

## ʼOçnappar

_(os-nap-par' | ose-nahp-PAHR | ose-na-PAHR)_

## Definition

horned bull, thorn abolished; of foreign derivation; Osnappar, an Assyrian king; Asnapper; n-pr-m

- an Assyrian king who transported other peoples into Samaria—probably the king, Ashurbanipal
