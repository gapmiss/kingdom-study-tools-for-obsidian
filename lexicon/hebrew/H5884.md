# H5884 עֵין רִמּוֹן / עין רמון

## ʻÊyn Rimmôwn

_(ane rim-mone' | ane rim-MONE | ane ree-MONE)_

## Definition

fount of the pomegranate; from H5869 and H7416; fountain of a pomegranate; En-Rimmon, a place in Palestine; En-rimmon; n-pr-loc

- one of the places that the returning exiles reinhabited in Judah
