# H1060 בְּכוֹר / בכור

## bᵉkôwr

_(bek-ore' | beh-KORE | beh-HORE)_

## Definition

from H1069; first-born; hence, chief; eldest (son), firstborn(-ling); n-m

- firstborn, firstling
  - of men and women
  - of animals
  - noun of relation (figuratively)
