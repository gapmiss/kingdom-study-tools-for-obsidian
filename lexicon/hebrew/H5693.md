# H5693 עָגוּר / עגור

## ʻâgûwr

_(aw-goor' | aw-ɡOOR | ah-ɡOOR)_

## Definition

passive part (but with active sense) of an unused root meaning to twitter; probably the swallow; swallow; n-m

- name of a bird
  - perhaps a thrush, swallow, crane
