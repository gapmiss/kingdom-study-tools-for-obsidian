# H8462 תְּחִלָּה / תחלה

## tᵉchillâh

_(tekh-il-law' | teh-hil-LAW | teh-hee-LA)_

## Definition

from H2490 in the sense of opening; a commencement; relatively original (adverb, -ly); begin(-ning), first (time); n-f

- beginning, first
  - the first time
  - from the beginning, in the beginning (with prep)
