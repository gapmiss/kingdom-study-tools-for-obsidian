# H7036 קָלוֹן / קלון

## qâlôwn

_(kaw-lone' | kaw-LONE | ka-LONE)_

## Definition

from H7034; disgrace; (by implication) the pudenda; confusion, dishonour, ignominy, reproach, shame; n-m

- shame, disgrace, dishonor, ignominy
  - ignominy (of nation)
  - dishonor, disgrace (personal)
