# H1642 גְּרָר / גרר

## Gᵉrâr

_(gher-awr' | ɡeh-RAWR | ɡeh-RAHR)_

## Definition

a lodging place; probably from H1641; a rolling country; Gerar, a Philistine city; Gerar; n-pr-loc

- a Philistine town south of Gaza, modern 'Umm'
