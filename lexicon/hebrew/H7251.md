# H7251 רָבַע / רבע

## râbaʻ

_(raw-bah' | raw-BA | ra-VA)_

## Definition

a primitive root (rather identical with H7250 through the idea of sprawling 'at all fours' (or possibly the reverse is the order of deriv.); compare H0702); properly, to be four (sided); used only as denominative of H7253; to be quadrate; (four-)square(-d); v

- to square, be squared
  - (Qal) square (participle)
  - (Pual) square (participle)
