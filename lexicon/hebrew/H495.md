# H495 אֶלָּסָר / אלסר

## ʼEllâçâr

_(el-law-sawr' | el-law-SAWR | eh-la-SAHR)_

## Definition

God is chastener; probably of foreign derivation; Ellasar, an early country of Asia; Ellasar; n-pr-loc

- a town in Babylonia, about 28 miles (50 km) east of Ur
