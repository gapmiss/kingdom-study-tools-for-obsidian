# H8168 שֹׁעַל / שעל

## shôʻal

_(sho'-al | shoh-AL | shoh-AL)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to hollow out; the palm; by extension, a handful; handful, hollow of the hand; n-m

- hollow hand, hollow of hand, handful
  - hollow hand, hollow of hand
  - handful
