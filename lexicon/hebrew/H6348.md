# H6348 פָּחַז / פחז

## pâchaz

_(paw-khaz' | paw-HAHDZ | pa-HAHZ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to bubble up or froth (as boiling water), i.e., (figuratively) to be unimportant; light; v

- (Qal) to be wanton, be reckless, be frothy
