# H1712 דָּגוֹן / דגון

## Dâgôwn

_(daw-gohn' | daw-ɡONE | da-ɡONE)_

## Definition

a fish; from H1709; the fish-god; Dagon, a Philistine deity; Dagon; n-pr-m

- a Philistine deity of fertility; represented with the face and hands of a man and the tail of a fish
