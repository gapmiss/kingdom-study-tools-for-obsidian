# H7998 שָׁלָל / שלל

## shâlâl

_(shaw-lawl' | shaw-LAWL | sha-LAHL)_

## Definition

from H7997; booty; prey, spoil; n-m

- prey, plunder, spoil, booty
  - prey
  - booty, spoil, plunder (of war)
  - plunder (private)
  - gain (meaning dubious)
