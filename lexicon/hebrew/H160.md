# H160 אַהֲבָה / אהבה

## ʼahăbâh

_(a-hab-aw | ah-huh-BAW | ah-huh-VA)_

## Definition

feminine of H0158 and meaning the same; love; love; n-f

- love
  - human love for human object
    - of man toward man
    - of man toward himself
    - between man and woman
    - sexual desire
- God's love to His people
