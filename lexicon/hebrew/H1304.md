# H1304 בָּרֶקֶת / ברקת

## bâreqeth

_(baw-reh'-keth | baw-reh-KET | ba-reh-KET)_

## Definition

or בָּרְקַת; from H1300; a gem (as flashing), perhaps the emerald; carbuncle; n-f

- a gem, precious stone, emerald (Josephus)
