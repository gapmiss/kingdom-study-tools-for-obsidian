# H3161 יָחַד / יחד

## yâchad

_(yaw-khad' | yaw-HAHD | ya-HAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be (or become) one; join, unite; v

- to join, unite, be joined, be united
  - (Qal) to be united
  - (Piel) to unite
