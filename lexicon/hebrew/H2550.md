# H2550 חָמַל / חמל

## châmal

_(khaw-mal' | haw-MAHL | ha-MAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to commiserate; by implication, to spare; have compassion, (have) pity, spare; v

- (Qal) to spare, pity, have compassion on
