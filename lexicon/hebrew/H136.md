# H136 אֲדֹנָי / אדני

## ʼĂdônây

_(ad-o-noy' | uh-doh-NAI | uh-doh-NAI)_

## Definition

an emphatic form of H0113; the Lord (used as a proper name of God only); (my) Lord; n-m

- my lord, lord
  - of men
  - of God
- Lord—title, spoken in place of Jehovah in Jewish display of reverence
