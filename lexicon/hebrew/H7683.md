# H7683 שָׁגַג / שגג

## shâgag

_(shaw-gag' | shaw-ɡAHɡ | sha-ɡAHɡ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to stray, i.e., (figuratively) sin (with more or less apology); × also for that, deceived, err, go astray, sin ignorantly; v

- to go astray, err, commit sin or error
  - (Qal)
    - to err (mentally)
    - to sin (ignorantly or inadvertently)
