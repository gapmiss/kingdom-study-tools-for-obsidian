# H4478 מָן / מן

## mân

_(mawn | mawn | mahn)_

## Definition

from H4100; literally a whatness (so to speak), i.e., manna (so called from the question about it); manna; n-m

- manna
  - the bread from Heaven that fed the Israelites for 40 years of wilderness wanderings
  - means 'What is it?'
