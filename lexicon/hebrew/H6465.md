# H6465 פְּעוֹר / פעור

## Pᵉʻôwr

_(peh-ore' | peh-ORE | peh-ORE)_

## Definition

cleft; from H6473; a gap; (also H1187); Peor, a mountain East of Jordan in Moab; Baal Peor, (for H1187) a deity worshipped there; Peor; n-pr

- (n pr loc) a mountain peak in Moab belonging to the Abarim range and near Pisgah
- (n pr deity) a false god worshipped in Moab; corresponds to Baal

## Note

Through the counsel of Balaam, the Moabites seduced the children of Israel into committing grievous sins with the women of Moab in the worship of Baal-Peor. Therefore, Peor is associated with this sin. See Numbers 25.
