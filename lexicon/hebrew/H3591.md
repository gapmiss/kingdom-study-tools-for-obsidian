# H3591 כִּידוֹן / כידון

_(kee-dohn' | kee-DONE | kee-DONE)_

## Definition

from the same as H3589; (perhaps smaller than H2595); properly, something to strike with, i.e., a dart; lance, shield, spear, target; n-m

- javelin, short sword, dart
