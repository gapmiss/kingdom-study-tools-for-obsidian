# H6953 קֹהֶלֶת / קהלת

## qôheleth

_(ko-heh'-leth | koh-heh-LET | koh-heh-LET)_

## Definition

feminine of active participle from H6950; a (female) assembler (i.e., lecturer); abstractly, preaching (used as a 'nom de plume', Koheleth); preacher; n-m

- collector (of sentences), preacher, public speaker, speaker in an assembly, Qoheleth
