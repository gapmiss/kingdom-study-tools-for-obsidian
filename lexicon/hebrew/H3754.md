# H3754 כֶּרֶם / כרם

## kerem

_(keh'-rem | keh-REM | keh-REM)_

## Definition

from an unused root of uncertain meaning; a garden or vineyard; vines, (increase of the) vineyard(-s), vintage; n-m

- vineyard
