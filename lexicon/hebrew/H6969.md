# H6969 קוּן / קון

## qûwn

_(koon | koon | koon)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to strike a musical note, i.e., chant or wail (at a funeral); lament, mourning woman; v

- to chant a dirge, chant, wail, lament
  - (Polel) to lament
