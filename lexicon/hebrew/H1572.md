# H1572 גָּמָא / גמא

## gâmâʼ

_(gaw-maw' | ɡaw-MAW | ɡa-MA)_

## Definition

a primitive root (literally or figuratively) to absorb; to absorb; swallow, drink; v

- to swallow (liquids)
  - (Piel) to swallow
  - (Hiphil) to drink
