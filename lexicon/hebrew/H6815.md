# H6815 צַעֲנַנִּים / צעננים

## Tsaʻănannîym

_(tsah-an-an-neem' | tsa-uh-nahn-NEEM | tsa-uh-na-NEEM)_

## Definition

removings; or (dual) צַעֲנַיִם; plural from H6813; removals; Tsaanannim or Tsaanajim, a place in Palestine; Zaannannim, Zaanaim; n-pr-loc

- a place in Naphtali near Kedesh
