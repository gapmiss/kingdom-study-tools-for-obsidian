# H7023 קִיר / קיר

## qîyr

_(keer | keer | keer)_

## Definition

or קִר; (Isaiah 22:5), or (feminine) קִירָה; from H6979; a wall (as built in a trench); + mason, side, town, × very, wall; n-m

- wall, side
  - wall (of house or chamber)
  - the sides (of the altar)
