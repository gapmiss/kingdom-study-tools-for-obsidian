# H4760 מֻרְאָה / מראה

## murʼâh

_(moor-aw' | moor-AW | moor-AH)_

## Definition

apparently feminine passive causative participle of H7200; something conspicuous, i.e., the craw of a bird (from its prominence); crop; n-f

- crop or craw (of a bird), alimentary canal (of a bird)
