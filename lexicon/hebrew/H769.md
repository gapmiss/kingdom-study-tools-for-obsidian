# H769 אַרְנוֹן / ארנון

## ʼArnôwn

_(ar-nohn' | ar-NONE | ar-NONE)_

## Definition

rushing stream; or אַרְנֹן; from H7442; a brawling stream; the Arnon, a river east of the Jordan, also its territory; Arnon; n-pr-loc

- a river and surrounding valley in south Palestine, forms the border between Moab and the Amorites
