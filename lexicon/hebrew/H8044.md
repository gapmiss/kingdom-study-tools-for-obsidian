# H8044 שַׁמְגַּר / שמגר

## Shamgar

_(sham-gar' | shahm-ɡAHR | shahm-ɡAHR)_

## Definition

sword; of uncertain derivation; Shamgar, an Israelite judge; Shamgar; n-pr-m

- son of Anath and a judge of Israel; with an ox-goad he slew 600 Philistines and delivered Israel
