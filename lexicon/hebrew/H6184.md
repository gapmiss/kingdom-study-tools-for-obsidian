# H6184 עָרִיץ / עריץ

## ʻârîyts

_(aw-reets' | aw-REETS | ah-REETS)_

## Definition

from H6206; fearful, i.e., powerful or tyrannical; mighty, oppressor, in great power, strong, terrible, violent; a

- awe-inspiring, terror-striking, awesome, terrifying, ruthless, mighty
