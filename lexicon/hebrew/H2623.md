# H2623 חָסִיד / חסיד

## châçîyd

_(khaw-seed' | haw-SEED | ha-SEED)_

## Definition

from H2616; properly, kind, i.e., (religiously) pious (a saint); godly (man), good, holy (one), merciful, saint, (un-)godly; a

- faithful, kind, godly, holy one, saint, pious
  - kind
  - pious, godly
  - faithful ones (subst)
