# H7643 שְׂבָם / שבם

## Sᵉbâm

_(seb-awm' | seh-BAWM | seh-VAHM)_

## Definition

fragrance; or (feminine) שִׂבְמָה; probably from H1313; spice; Sebam or Sibmah, a place in Moab; Shebam, Shibmah, Sibmah; n-pr-loc

- one of the towns in the pastoral district on the east of the Jordan in Moab; allotted to the tribes of Reuben and Gad
