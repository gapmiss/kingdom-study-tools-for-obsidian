# H7458 רָעָב / רעב

## râʻâb

_(raw-awb' | raw-AWB | ra-AV)_

## Definition

from H7456; hunger (more or less extensive); dearth, famine, + famished, hunger; n-m

- famine, hunger
  - famine (in land, nation)
    - of Jehovah's word (figuratively)
  - hunger (of individuals)
