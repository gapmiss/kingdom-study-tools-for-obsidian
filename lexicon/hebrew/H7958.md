# H7958 שְׂלָו / שלו

## sᵉlâv

_(sel-awv' | seh-LAW | seh-LAHV)_

## Definition

or שְׂלָיו; by orthographical variation from H7951 through the idea of sluggishness; the quail collectively (as slow in flight from its weight); quails; n-f

- quail
