# H6728 צִיִּי / ציי

## tsîyîy

_(tsee-ee' | tsih-YEE | tsee-YEE)_

## Definition

from the same as H6723; a desert-dweller, i.e., nomad or wild beast; wild beast of the desert, that dwell in (inhabiting) the wilderness; n-m

- a wild beast, desert-dweller, crier, yelper
  - a specific wild beast but not certainly identified
