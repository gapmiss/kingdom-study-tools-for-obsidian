# H7741 שָׁוֵה קִרְיָתַיִם / שוה קריתים

## Shâvêh Qiryâthayim

_(shaw-vay' kir-yawthah'-yim | shaw-WAY kir-yaw-ta-YIM | sha-VAY keer-ya-ta-YEEM)_

## Definition

plain of the double, two city; from the same as H7740 and the dual of H7151; plain of a double city; Shaveh-Kirjathajim, a place East of the Jordan; Shaveh Kiriathaim; n-pr-m

- the place attacked by Chedorlaomer
