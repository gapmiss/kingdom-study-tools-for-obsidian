# H1752 דּוּר / דור

## dûwr

_(dure | door | door)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to gyrate (or move in a circle), i.e., to remain; dwell; v

- to heap up, pile
- to dwell
  - (Qal)
    - to dwell
    - heap up (imp.)
