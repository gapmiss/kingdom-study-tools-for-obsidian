# H5391 נָשַׁךְ / נשך

## nâshak

_(naw-shak' | naw-SHAHK | na-SHAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to strike with a sting (as a serpent); figuratively, to oppress with interest on a loan; bite, lend upon usury; v

- to bite
  - (Qal) to bite
  - (Piel) to bite
- to pay, give interest, lend for interest or usury
  - (Qal) to give interest
  - (Hiphil) to make to give interest
