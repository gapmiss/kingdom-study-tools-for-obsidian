# H7098 קָצָה / קצה

## qâtsâh

_(kaw-tsaw' | kaw-TSAW | ka-TSA)_

## Definition

feminine of H7097; (used like H7097); a termination; coast, corner, (selv-)edge, lowest, (uttermost) participle; n

- end, extremity
  - end
  - from the whole of, from among (of what is included between extremities)
