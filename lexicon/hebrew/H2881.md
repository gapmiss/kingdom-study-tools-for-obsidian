# H2881 טָבַל / טבל

## ṭâbal

_(taw-bal' | taw-BAHL | ta-VAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to dip, to immerse; dip, plunge; v

- to dip, dip into, plunge
  - (Qal)
    - to dip in or into
    - to dip oneself
  - (Niphal) to be dipped
