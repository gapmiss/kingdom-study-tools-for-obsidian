# H3448 יִשַׁי / ישי

## Yishay

_(yee-shah'-ee | yih-SHAI | yee-SHAI)_

## Definition

I possess; by Aramaic אִישַׁי; from the same as H3426; extant; Jishai, David's father; Jesse; n-pr-m

- son of Boaz and the father of king David
