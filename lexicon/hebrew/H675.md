# H675 אֶצְבּוֹן / אצבו

## ʼEtsbôwn

_(ets-bone' | ets-BONE | ets-BONE)_

## Definition

hasting to discern, I will be enlargement; or אֶצְבֹּן; of uncertain derivation; Etsbon, the name of two Israelites; Ezbon; n-pr-m

- a son of Gad
- grandson of Benjamin
