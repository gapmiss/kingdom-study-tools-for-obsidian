# H8095 שִׁמְעוֹן / שמעון

## Shimʻôwn

_(shim-one' | shim-ONE | sheem-ONE)_

## Definition

heard; from H8085; hearing; Shimon, one of Jacob's sons, also the tribe descended from him; Simeon; n-pr-m

- the 2nd son of Jacob by his wife Leah and progenitor of the tribe of Simeon
- an Israelite of the sons of Bani who had a foreign wife in the time of Ezra
