# H516 אַל תַּשְׁחֵת / אל תשחת

## ʼAl tashchêth

_(al tash-kayth' | al tahsh-HATE | al tahsh-HATE)_

## Definition

do not destroy; from H0408 and H7843; 'Thou must not destroy'; probably the opening words to a popular song; Al-taschith; n-m

- (Hiphil) a command to the chief musician, or perhaps the title of a melody used for several Psalms
