# H8528 תֵּל מֶלַח / תל מלח

## Têl Melach

_(tale meh'-lakh | tale meh-LA | tale meh-LAHK)_

## Definition

mound of salt; from H8510 and H4417; mound of salt; Tel-Melach, a place in Babylonia; Tel-melah; n-pr-loc

- a place in Babylon from which some exiles of unknown parentage came; returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel
