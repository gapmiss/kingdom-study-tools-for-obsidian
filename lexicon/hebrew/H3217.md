# H3217 יַלֶּפֶת / ילפת

## yallepheth

_(yal-leh'-feth | yahl-leh-PET | ya-leh-FET)_

## Definition

from an unused root apparently meaning to stick or scrape; scurf or tetter; scabbed; n-f

- scab, skin sore, scales, scurf, an eruptive disease
