# H8256 שִׁקְמָה / שקמה

## shiqmâh

_(shik-maw' | shik-MAW | sheek-MA)_

## Definition

or (feminine) שִׁקְמָה; of uncertain derivation; a sycamore (usually the tree); sycamore (fruit, tree); n-f

- sycamore tree (bearing figs)
