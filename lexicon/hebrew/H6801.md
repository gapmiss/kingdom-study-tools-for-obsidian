# H6801 צָנַף / צנף

## tsânaph

_(tsaw-naf' | tsaw-NAHP | tsa-NAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to wrap, i.e., roll or dress; be attired, × surely, violently turn; v

- (Qal) to wrap, wrap or wind up together, wind around
