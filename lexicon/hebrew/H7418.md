# H7418 רָמוֹת / רמות

## Râmôwth

_(raw-moth | raw-MOTE | ra-MOTE)_

## Definition

heights; or רָמַת נֶגֶב; from the plural or construct form of H7413 and H5045; heights (or height) of the South; Ramoth-Negeb or Ramath-Negeb, a place in Palestine; south Ramoth, Ramath of the south; n-pr-loc

- a place in Judah which received presents from David
  - site dubious
