# H6635 צָבָא / צבא

## tsâbâʼ

_(tsaw-baw' | tsaw-BAW | tsa-VA)_

## Definition

or (feminine) צְבָאָה; from H6633; a mass of persons (or figuratively, things), especially reg. organized for war (an army); by implication, a campaign, literally or figuratively (specifically, hardship, worship); appointed time, (+) army, (+) battle, company, host, service, soldiers, waiting upon, war(-fare); n-m

- that which goes forth, army, war, warfare, host
  - army, host
    - host (of organised army)
    - host (of angels)
    - of sun, moon, and stars
    - of whole creation
  - war, warfare, service, go out to war
  - service
