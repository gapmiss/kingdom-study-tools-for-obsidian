# H2406 חִטָּה / חטה

## chiṭṭâh

_(khit-taw' | hit-TAW | hee-TA)_

## Definition

of uncertain derivation; wheat, whether the grain or the plant; wheat(-en); n-f

- wheat
  - wheat (plant)
  - wheat flour
