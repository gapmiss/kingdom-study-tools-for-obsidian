# H7666 שָׁבַר / שבר

## shâbar

_(shaw-bar' | shaw-BAHR | sha-VAHR)_

## Definition

denominative from H7668; to deal in grain; buy, sell; v

- to buy or purchase grain
  - (Qal) to buy grain
  - (Hiphil) to sell grain
