# H4308 מַטְרֵד / מטרד

## Maṭrêd

_(mat-rade' | maht-RADE | maht-RADE)_

## Definition

pushing forward; from H2956; propulsive; Matred, an Edomitess; Matred; n-pr-f

- daughter of Mezahab and mother of Mehetabel, the wife of Hadar (Hadad) of Pau, king of Edom
