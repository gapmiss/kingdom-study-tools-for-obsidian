# H5703 עַד / עד

## ʻad

_(ad | ad | ad)_

## Definition

from H5710; properly, a (peremptory) terminus, i.e., (by implication) duration, in the sense of advance or perpetuity (substantially as a noun, either with or without a preposition); eternity, ever(-lasting, -more), old, perpetually, + world without end; n-m

- perpetuity, for ever, continuing future
  - ancient (of past time)
  - for ever (of future time)
    - of continuous existence
  - for ever (of God's existence)
