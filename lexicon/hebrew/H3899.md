# H3899 לֶחֶם / לחם

## lechem

_(lekh'-em | leh-HEM | leh-HEM)_

## Definition

from H3898; See also H1036; food (for man or beast), especially bread, or grain (for making it); (shew-)bread, × eat, food, fruit, loaf, meat, victuals; n-m

- bread, food, grain
  - bread
    - bread
    - bread-corn
  - food (generally)
