# H4095 מַדְרֵגָה / מדרגה

## madrêgâh

_(mad-ray-gaw' | mahd-ray-ɡAW | mahd-ray-ɡA)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to step; properly, a step; by implication, a steep or inaccessible place; stair, steep place; n-f

- steep place, steep
