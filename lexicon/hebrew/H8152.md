# H8152 שִׁנְעָר / שנער

## Shinʻâr

_(shin-awr' | shin-AWR | sheen-AR)_

## Definition

country of two rivers; probably of foreign derivation; Shinar, a plain in Babylonia; Shinar; n-pr-loc

- the ancient name for the territory later known as Babylonia or Chaldea
