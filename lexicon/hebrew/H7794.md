# H7794 שׁוֹר / שור

## shôwr

_(shore | shore | shore)_

## Definition

from H7788; a bullock (as a traveller); bull(-ock), cow, ox, wall (by mistake for H7791); n-m

- ox, bull, a head of cattle
  - for plowing, for food, as sacrifice
