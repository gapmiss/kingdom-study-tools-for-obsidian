# H5675 עֲבַר / עבר

## ʻăbar

_(ab-ar' | uh-BAHR | uh-VAHR)_

## Definition

corresponding to H5676; properly, a region across; but used only adverbially (with or without a preposition) on the opposite side (especially of the Jordan; ususally meaning the east); beyond, this side; n-m

- region beyond or across
