# H4452 מָלַץ / מלץ

## mâlats

_(maw-lats' | maw-LAHTS | ma-LAHTS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be smooth, i.e., (figuratively) pleasant; be sweet; v

- to be smooth, be slippery
  - (Niphal) to be smooth (agreeable, pleasant)
