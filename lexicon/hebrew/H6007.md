# H6007 עֲמַסְיָה / עמסיה

## ʻĂmaçyâh

_(am-as-yaw' | uh-mahs-YAW | uh-mahs-YA)_

## Definition

Jehovah is strength; from H6006 and H3050; Jah has loaded; Amasjah, an Israelite; Amasiah; n-pr-m

- son of Zichri and captain of 200,000 warriors of Judah in the reign of king Jehoshaphat of Judah
