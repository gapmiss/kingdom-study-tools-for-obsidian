# H284 אֲחִיחֻד / אחיחד

## ʼĂchîychud

_(akh-ee-khood' | uh-hee-HOOD | uh-hee-HOOD)_

## Definition

brother of mystery; from H0251 and H2330; brother of a riddle (i.e., mysterious); Achichud, an Israelite; Ahihud; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite, son of Ehud
