# H2426 חֵיל / חיל

## chêyl

_(khale | hale | hale)_

## Definition

or (shortened) חֵל; a collateral form of H2428; an army; also (by analogy,) an intrenchment; army, bulwark, host, + poor, rampart, trench, wall; n-m

- rampart, fortress, wall
  - rampart
  - fortress
