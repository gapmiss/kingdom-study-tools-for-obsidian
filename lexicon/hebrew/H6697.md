# H6697 צוּר / צור

## tsûwr

_(tsoor | tsoor | tsoor)_

## Definition

or צֻר; from H6696; properly, a cliff (or sharp rock, as compressed); generally, a rock or boulder; figuratively, a refuge; also an edge (as precipitous); edge, × (mighty) God (one), rock, × sharp, stone, × strength, × strong; n-m

- rock, cliff
  - rocky wall, cliff
  - rock (with flat surface)
  - block of stone, boulder
  - rock (specifically)
  - rock (of God)
  - rock (of heathen gods)
  - (n pr dei) Rock
