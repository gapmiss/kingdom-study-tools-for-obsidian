# H6957 קַו / קו

## qav

_(kav | ka | kahv)_

## Definition

or קָו; from H6960 (compare H6961); a cord (as connecting), especially for measuring; figuratively, a rule; also a rim, a musical string or accord; line; n-m

- cord, line, measuring-line
  - cord
  - measuring-line
- onomatapoetic mimicry of Isaiah's words, perhaps senseless

## Note

Compare H6978.
