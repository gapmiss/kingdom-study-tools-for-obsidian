# H6005 עִמָּנוּאֵל / עמנואל

## ʻImmânûwʼêl

_(im-maw-noo-ale' | im-maw-noo-ALE | ee-ma-noo-ALE)_

## Definition

God with us, with us is God; from H5973 and with a pronominal suffix inserted; with us (is) God; Immanuel; Immanuel; n-pr-m

- symbolic and prophetic name of the Messiah, the Christ, prophesying that He would be born of a virgin and would be 'God with us'
