# H1927 הֲדָרָה / הדרה

## hădârâh

_(had-aw-raw' | huh-daw-RAW | huh-da-RA)_

## Definition

feminine of H1926; decoration; beauty, honour; n-f

- adornment, glory
  - holy adornment (of public worship)
  - glory (of the king)
