# H5785 עוֹר / עור

## ʻôwr

_(ore | ore | ore)_

## Definition

from H5783; skin (as naked); by implication, hide, leather; hide, leather, skin; n-m

- skin, hide
  - skin (of men)
  - hide (of animals)
