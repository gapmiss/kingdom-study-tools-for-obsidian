# H5502 סָחַף / סחף

## çâchaph

_(saw-khaf' | saw-HAHP | sa-HAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to scrape off; sweep (away); v

- to prostrate, beat down
  - (Qal) prostrating (participle)
  - (Niphal) to be prostrated
