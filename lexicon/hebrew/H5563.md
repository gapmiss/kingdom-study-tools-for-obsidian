# H5563 סְמָדַר / סמדר

## çᵉmâdar

_(sem-aw-dar' | seh-maw-DAHR | seh-ma-DAHR)_

## Definition

of uncertain derivation; a vine blossom; used also adverbially, abloom; tender grape; n-m

- grape blossom, grape bud
