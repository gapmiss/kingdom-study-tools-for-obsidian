# H7727 שׁוֹבָב / שובב

## Shôwbâb

_(sho-bawb' | shoh-BAWB | shoh-VAHV)_

## Definition

rebellious; the same as H7726; rebellious; Shobab, the name of two Israelites; Shobab; n-pr-m

- son of David by Bathsheba
- a Judaite, son of Caleb by his wife Azubah and grandson of Hezron
