# H2179 זָנַב / זנב

## zânab

_(zaw-nab' | dzaw-NAHB | za-NAHV)_

## Definition

a primitive root meaning to wag; used only as a denominative from H2180; to curtail, i.e., cut off the rear; smite the hindmost; v

- to cut off
  - (Piel) to attack the rear, smite the tail
