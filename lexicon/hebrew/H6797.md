# H6797 צָנִיף / צניף

## tsânîyph

_(tsaw-neef' | tsaw-NEEP | tsa-NEEF)_

## Definition

or צָנוֹף; or (feminine) צָנִיפָה; from H6801; a head-dress (i.e., piece of cloth wrapped around); diadem, hood, mitre; n-m

- turban, headdress
