# H4532 מַסָּה / מסה

## Maççâh

_(mas-saw' | mahs-SAW | ma-SA)_

## Definition

temptation; the same as H4531; Massah, a place in the Desert; Massah; n-pr-loc

- the place in the wilderness where the Israelites tested Jehovah
