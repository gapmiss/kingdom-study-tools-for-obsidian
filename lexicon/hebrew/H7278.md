## Regem Melek

_(reh'-gem meh'-lek | reh-ɡEM meh-LEK | reh-ɡEM meh-LEK)_

## Definition

king's heap; from H7276 and H4428; king's heap; Regem-Melek, an Israelite; Regem-melech; n-pr-m

- a man sent by some of the captivity to make enquiries at the temple concerning fasting
