# H812 אֶשְׁכֹּל / אשכל

## ʼEshkôl

_(esh-kole' | esh-KOLE | esh-KOLE)_

## Definition

cluster; the same as H0811; Eshcol, the name of an Amorite, also of a valley in Palestine; Eshcol; n-pr

- an area of Hebron, the valley of Eshcol
- an Amorite, the brother of Mamre, dwelling in Hebron
