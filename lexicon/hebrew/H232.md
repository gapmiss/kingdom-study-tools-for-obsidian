# H232 אֵזוֹר / אזור

## ʼêzôwr

_(ay-zore' | ay-DZORE | ay-ZORE)_

## Definition

from H0246; something girt; a belt, also a band; girdle; n-m

- waist-cloth, the innermost piece of clothing
  - of God's power over kings (figuratively)
  - of faithfulness (metaphorically)
- waistband
