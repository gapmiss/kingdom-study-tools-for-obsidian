# H7789 שׁוּר / שור

## shûwr

_(shoor | shoor | shoor)_

## Definition

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of going round for inspection); to spy out, i.e., (generally) survey, (for evil) lurk for, (for good) care for; behold, lay wait, look, observe, perceive, regard, see; v

- to see, behold, observe, regard
  - (Qal)
    - to behold, observe
    - to regard
    - to watch, watch (stealthily), lie in wait
