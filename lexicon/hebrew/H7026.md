# H7026 קֵירֹס / קירס

## Qêyrôç

_(kay-roce' | kay-ROSE | kay-ROSE)_

## Definition

ankle; or קֵרֹס; from the same as H7166; ankled; Keros, one of the Nethinim; Keros; n-pr-m

- head of a family of temple slaves who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
