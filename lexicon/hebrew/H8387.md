# H8387 תַּאֲנַת שִׁלֹה / תאנת שלה

## Taʼănath Shilôh

_(tah-an-ath' shee-lo' | ta-uh-NAHT shih-LOH | ta-uh-NAHT shee-LOH)_

## Definition

approach to Shiloh; from H8385 and H7887; approach of Shiloh; Taanath-Shiloh, a place in Palestine; Taanath-shiloh; n-pr-loc

- a landmark on the boundary of Ephraim
