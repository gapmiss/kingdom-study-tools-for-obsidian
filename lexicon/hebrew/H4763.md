# H4763 מְרַאֲשָׁה / מראשה

## mᵉraʼăshâh

_(mer-ah-ash-aw' | meh-ra-uh-SHAW | meh-ra-uh-SHA)_

## Definition

formed like H4761; properly, a headpiece, i.e., (plural for adverbial) at (or as) the head-rest (or pillow); bolster, head, pillow; n-f

- (n f) place at the head, dominion, head place
- (adv) at head place

## Note

Compare H4772.
