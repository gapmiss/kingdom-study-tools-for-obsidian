# H6991 קָטַל / קטל

## qâṭal

_(kaw-tal' | kaw-TAHL | ka-TAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to cut off, i.e., (figuratively) put to death; kill, slay; v

- (Qal) to slay, kill
