# H6034 עֲנָה / ענה

## ʻĂnâh

_(an-aw' | uh-NAW | uh-NA)_

## Definition

answer; probably from H6030; an answer; Anah, the name of two Edomites and one Edomitess; Anah; n-pr-m n-pr-f

- (n pr m) son of Zibeon and father of Aholibamah, a wife of Esau
- a Horite chief
- (n pr f) daughter of Zibeon
