# H382 אִישׁ־טוֹב / אישטוב

## ʼÎysh-Ṭôwb

_(eesh-tobe' | eesh tobe | eesh tove)_

## Definition

man of Tob; from H0376 and H2897; man of Tob; Ish-Tob, a place in Palestine; Ish-tob; n-pr-loc

- a region beyond Jordan, north or northeast of Gilead
