# H3163 יַחְדוֹ / יחדו

## Yachdôw

_(yakh-doe' | ya-DOH | yahk-DOH)_

## Definition

His unity; from H3162 with pronominal suffix; his unity, i.e., (adverb) together; Jachdo, an Israelite; Jahdo; n-pr-m

- a Gileadite, son of Buz and father of Jeshishai
