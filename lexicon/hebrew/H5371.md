# H5371 נֵרְגַּל שַׁרְאֶצֶר / נרגל שראצר

## Nêrᵉgal Sharʼetser

_(nare-gal' shar-eh'-tser | nay-reg-GAHL shahr-eh-TSER | nay-reh-ɡAHL shahr-eh-TSER)_

## Definition

prince of fire; from H5370 and H8272; Nergal-Sharetser, the name of two Babylonians; Nergal-sharezer; n-pr-m

- chief soothsayer and a ruler in the army of Nebuchadnezzar
