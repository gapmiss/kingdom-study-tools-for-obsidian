# H2715 חֹר / חר

## chôr

_(khore | hore | hore)_

## Definition

or (fully) חוֹר; from H2787; (compare H2751); properly, white or pure (from the cleansing or shining power of fire; hence (figuratively) noble (in rank); noble; n-m

- a noble, freeborn one
