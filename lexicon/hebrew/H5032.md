# H5032 נְבָיוֹת / נביות

## Nᵉbâyôwth

_(neb-aw-yoth' | neh-baw-YOTE | neh-va-YOTE)_

## Definition

heights; or נְבָיֹת; feminine plural from H5107; fruitfulnesses; Nebajoth, a son of Ismael, and the country settled by him; Nebaioth, Nebajoth; n-pr-f

- a son of Ishmael
- people descended from 1 called the Nabateans with their capital at Petra
