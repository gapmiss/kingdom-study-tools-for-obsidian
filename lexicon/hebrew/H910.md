# H910 בָּדָד / בדד

## bâdâd

_(baw-dawd' | baw-DAWD | ba-DAHD)_

## Definition

from H0909; separate; adverb, separately; alone, desolate, only, solitary; n-m

- (n m) isolation, withdrawal, separation
  - an isolated city
- (adv) alone
  - security (figuratively)
