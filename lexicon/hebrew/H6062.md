# H6062 עֲנָקִי / ענקי

## ʻĂnâqîy

_(an-aw-kee' | uh-naw-KEE | uh-na-KEE)_

## Definition

long-necked; patronymically from H6061; an Anakite or descendant of Anak; Anakim; a

- a race of giants, descendants of Anak, which dwelled in southern Canaan
