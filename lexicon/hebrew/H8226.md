# H8226 שָׂפַן / שפן

## sâphan

_(saw-fan' | saw-PAHN | sa-FAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to conceal (as a valuable); treasure; v

- to cover, cover in, panel, hide, treasure up
  - (Qal)
    - covered, panelled, treasures (participle)
