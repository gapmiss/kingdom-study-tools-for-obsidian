# H341 אֹיֵב / איב

## ʼôyêb

_(o-yabe' | oh-YABE | oh-YAVE)_

## Definition

or (fully) אוֹיֵב; active participle of H0340; hating; an adversary; enemy, foe; n-m

- (Qal) enemy
  - personal
  - national
