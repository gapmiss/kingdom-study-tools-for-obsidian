# H61 אֲבָל / אבל

## ʼăbâl

_(ab-awl' | uh-BAWL | uh-VAHL)_

## Definition

apparently from H0056 through the idea of negation; nay, i.e., truly or yet; but, indeed, nevertheless, verily; adv

- truly, verily, surely
- but, however, howbeit
- contrariwise, nay rather (negative)
