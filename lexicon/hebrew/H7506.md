# H7506 רֶפַח / רפח

## Rephach

_(reh'-fakh | reh-PA | reh-FAHK)_

## Definition

healing of the breath; from an unused root apparently meaning to sustain; support; Rephach, an Israelite; Rephah; n-pr-m

- son of Ephraim and a ancestor of Joshua
