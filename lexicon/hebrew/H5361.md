# H5361 נָקַע / נקע

## nâqaʻ

_(naw-kah' | naw-KA | na-KA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to feel aversion; be alienated; v

- (Qal) to be estranged, be alienated
  - to be severed (figuratively)
