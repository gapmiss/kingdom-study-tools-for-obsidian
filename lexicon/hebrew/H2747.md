# H2747 חֶרֶט / חרט

## chereṭ

_(kheh'-ret | heh-RET | heh-RET)_

## Definition

from a primitive root meaning to engrave; a chisel or graver; also a style for writing; graving tool, pen; n-m

- an engraving tool, stylus, chisel, graving tool
  - graving tool
  - stylus
