# H3577 כָּזָב / כזב

## kâzâb

_(kaw-zawb' | kaw-DZAWB | ka-ZAHV)_

## Definition

from H3576; falsehood; literally (untruth) or figuratively (idol); deceitful, false, leasing, liar, lie, lying; n-m

- a lie, untruth, falsehood, deceptive thing
