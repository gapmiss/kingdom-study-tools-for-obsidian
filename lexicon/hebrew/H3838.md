# H3838 לְבָנָא / לבנא

## Lᵉbânâʼ

_(leb-aw-naw' | leh-baw-NAW | leh-va-NA)_

## Definition

moon-white; or לְבָנָה; the same as H3842; Lebana or Lebanah, one of the Nethinim; Lebana, Lebanah; n-pr-m

- head of a family of exiles who returned with Zerubbabel
