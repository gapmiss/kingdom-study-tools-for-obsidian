# H8100 שִׁמְעַת / שמעת

## Shimʻath

_(shim-awth' | shim-AT | sheem-AT)_

## Definition

report; feminine of H8088; annunciation; Shimath, an Ammonitess; Shimath; n-pr-f

- an Ammonitess, mother of Jozachar or Zabad, one of the assassins of king Joash of Judah
