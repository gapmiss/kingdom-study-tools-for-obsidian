# H5096 נַהֲלָל / נהלל

## Nahălâl

_(nah-hal-awl' | na-huh-LAWL | na-huh-LAHL)_

## Definition

pasture; or נַהֲלֹל; the same as H5097; Nahalal or Nahalol, a place in Palestine; Nahalal, Nahallal, Nahalol; n-pr-loc

- one of the cities of Zebulun given to the Merarite Levites
  - site uncertain
  - maybe modern 'Malul', a village in the plain of Esdraelon
