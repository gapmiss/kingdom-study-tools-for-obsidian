# H816 אָשַׁם / אשם

## ʼâsham

_(aw-sham' | aw-SHAHM | ah-SHAHM)_

## Definition

or אָשֵׁם; a primitive root; to be guilty; by implication to be punished or perish; × certainly, be(-come) (made) desolate, destroy, × greatly, be(-come) (found, hold) guilty, offend (acknowledge offence), trespass; v

- to offend, be guilty, trespass
  - (Qal)
    - to do wrong, offend, trespass, commit an offense, do injury
    - to be or become guilty
    - to be held guilty
    - to be incriminated
  - (Niphal) to suffer punishment
  - (Hiphil) to declare guilty
- to be desolate, acknowledge offense
