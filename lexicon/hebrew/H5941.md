# H5941 עֵלִי / עלי

## ʻÊlîy

_(ay-lee' | ay-LEE | ay-LEE)_

## Definition

ascension; from H5927; lofty; Eli, an Israelite highpriest; Eli; n-pr-m

- descendant of Aaron through Ithamar and high priest and judge of Israel when Samuel entered service as a child
