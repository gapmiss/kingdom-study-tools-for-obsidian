# H671 אֲפַרְסְכַי / אפרסכי

## ʼĂpharçᵉkay

_(af-ar-sek-ah'ee | uh-pahr-seh-KAI | uh-fahr-seh-HAI)_

## Definition

as causers of division(?), I will divide the deceivers(?); or אֲפַרְסַתְכַי; (Aramaic), of foreign origin (only in the plural); an Apharsekite or Apharsathkite, an unknown Assyrian tribe; Apharsachites, Apharasthchites; n-pr-m

- a group of people (perhaps Assyrians)
- perhaps an office or an official

## Note

Apharsachites might mean "as causers of division," and Apharsathchites might mean "I will divide the deceivers."
