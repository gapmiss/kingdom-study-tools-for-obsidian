# H8077 שְׁמָמָה / שממה

_(shem-aw-maw' | sheh-maw-MAW | sheh-ma-MA)_

## Definition

or שִׁמָמָה; feminine of H8076; devastation; figuratively, astonishment; (laid, × most) desolate(-ion), waste; n-f

- devastation, waste, desolation
