# H8518 תָּלָה / תלה

## tâlâh

_(taw-law' | taw-LAW | ta-LA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to suspend (especially to gibbet); hang (up); v

- to hang
  - (Qal)
    - to hang
      - to hang up (any object)
      - to put to death by hanging
  - (Niphal) to be hanged
  - (Piel) to hang, hang up (for display)
