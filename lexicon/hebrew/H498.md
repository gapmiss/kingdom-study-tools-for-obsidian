# H498 אֶלְעוּזַי / אלעוזי

## ʼElʻûwzay

_(el-oo-zah'ee | el-oo-DZAI | el-oo-ZAI)_

## Definition

God is my strength; from H0410 and H5756 (in the sense of H5797); God (is) defensive; Eluzai, an Israelite; Eluzai; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite warrior who joined David
