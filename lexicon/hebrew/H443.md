# H443 אֶלְזָבָד / אלזבד

## ʼElzâbâd

_(el-zaw-bawd' | el-dzaw-BAWD | el-za-VAHD)_

## Definition

God has given; from H0410 and H2064; God has bestowed; Elzabad, the name of two Israelites; Elzabad; n-pr-m

- a Gadite warrior who aided David
- a Korhite Levite
