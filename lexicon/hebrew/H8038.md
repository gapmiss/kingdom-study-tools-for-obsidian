# H8038 שֶׁמְאֵבֶר / שמאבר

## Shemʼêber

_(shem-ay'-ber | shem-ay-BER | shem-ay-VER)_

## Definition

lofty flight; apparently from H8034 and H0083; name of pinion, i.e., illustrious; Shemeber, a king of Zeboim; Shemeber; n-pr-m

- king of Zeboim and an ally of the king of Sodom
