# H548 אֲמָנָה / אמנה

## ʼămânâh

_(am-aw-naw' | uh-maw-NAW | uh-ma-NA)_

## Definition

feminine of H0543; something fixed, i.e., a covenant. an allowance; certain portion, sure; n-f

- faith, support, sure, certain
  - of a covenant
  - of financial support
