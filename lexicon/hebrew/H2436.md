# H2436 חֵיק / חיק

## chêyq

_(khake | hake | hake)_

## Definition

or חֵק; and חוֹק; from an unused root, apparently meaning to inclose; the bosom (literally or figuratively); bosom, bottom, lap, midst, within; n-m

- bosom, hollow, bottom, midst
