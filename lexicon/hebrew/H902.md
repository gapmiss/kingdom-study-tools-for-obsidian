# H902 בִּגְוַי / בגוי

## Bigvay

_(big-vah'ee | biɡ-WAI | beeɡ-VAI)_

## Definition

in my bodies; probably of foreign origin; Bigvai, an Israelite; Bigvai; n-pr-m

- an exile who returned under Zerubbabel, perhaps the head of a family
