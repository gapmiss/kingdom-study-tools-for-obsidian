# H258 אָחַד / אחד

## ʼâchad

_(aw-khad' | aw-HAHD | ah-HAHD)_

## Definition

perhaps a primitive root; to unify, i.e., (figuratively) collect (one's thoughts); go one way or other; v

- (Hithpael) to go one way or another, to be sharp
