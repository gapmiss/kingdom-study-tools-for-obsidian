# H5523 סֻכּוֹת / סכות

## Çukkôwth

_(sook-kohth' | sook-KOTE | soo-KOTE)_

## Definition

booths; or סֻכֹּת; plural of H5521; booths; Succoth, the name of a place in Egypt and of three in Palestine; Succoth; n

- the site where Jacob put up booths for his cattle and built a house for himself; apparently east of the Jordan near the ford of the torrent Jabbok and later allotted to the tribe of Gad
- the 1st stopping place of the Israelites when they left Egypt
