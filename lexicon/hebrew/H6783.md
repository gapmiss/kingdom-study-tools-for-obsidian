# H6783 צְמִיתֻת / צמיתת

## tsᵉmîythuth

_(tsem-ee-thooth' | tseh-mee-TOOT | tseh-mee-TOOT)_

## Definition

or צְמִתֻת; from H6789; excision, i.e., destruction; used only (adverbially) with prepositional prefix to extinction, i.e., perpetually; ever; n-f

- completion, finality, in perpetuity
