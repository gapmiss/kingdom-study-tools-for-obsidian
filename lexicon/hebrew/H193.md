# H193 אוּל / אול

## ʼûwl

_(ool | ool | ool)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to twist, i.e., (by implication) be strong; the body (as being rolled together); also powerful; strength; n-m

- prominence
  - body, belly (contemptuous)
  - nobles, wealthy men
