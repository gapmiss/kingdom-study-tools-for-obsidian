# H104 אִגְּרָא / אגרא

## ʼiggᵉrâʼ

_(ig-er-aw' | ig-geh-RAW | ee-ɡeh-RA)_

## Definition

of Persian origin; an epistle (as carried by a state courier or postman); letter; n-f

- letter, missive (Aramaic loan-word used in last Old Testament books)
