# H4908 מִשְׁכָּן / משכן

## mishkân

_(mish-kawn' | mish-KAWN | meesh-KAHN)_

## Definition

from H7931; a residence (including a shepherd's hut, the lair of animals, figuratively, the grave; also the Temple); specifically, the Tabernacle (properly, its wooden walls); dwelleth, dwelling (place), habitation, tabernacle, tent; n-m

- dwelling place, tabernacle
  - dwelling-place
  - dwellings
