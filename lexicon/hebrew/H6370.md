# H6370 פִּילֶגֶשׁ / פילגש

## pîylegesh

_(pee-leh'-ghesh | pee-leh-ɡESH | pee-leh-ɡESH)_

## Definition

or פִּלֶגֶשׁ; of uncertain derivation; a concubine; also (masculine) a paramour; concubine, paramour; n-f

- concubine, paramour
  - concubine
  - paramour
