# H425 אֵלָה / אלה

## ʼÊlâh

_(ay-law' | ay-LAW | ay-LA)_

## Definition

An oak; the same as H0424; Elah, the name of an Edomite, of four Israelites, and also of a place in Palestine; Elah; n-pr

- an Edomite chief
- a king of Israel for two years, son of Baasha
- the father of king Hoshea of Israel
- a son of Caleb
- son of Uzzi
