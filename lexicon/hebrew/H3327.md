# H3327 יִצְחָק / יצחק

## Yitschâq

_(yits-khawk' | yits-HAWK | yeets-HAHK)_

## Definition

he laughs; from H6711; laughter (i.e., mochery); Jitschak (or Isaac), son of Abraham; Isaac; n-pr-m

- son of Abraham by Sarah his wife and father of Jacob and Esau

## Note

Compare H3446.
