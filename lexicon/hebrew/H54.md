# H54 אֶבְיָתָר / אביתר

## ʼEbyâthâr

_(ab-yaw-thawr' | eb-yaw-TAWR | ev-ya-TAHR)_

## Definition

my father is great; contracted from H0001 and H3498; father of abundance (i.e., liberal); Ebjathar, an Israelite; Abiathar; n-pr-m

- priest, son of Ahitub (Ahimelech), faithful to David, but later rebelled with Adonijah
