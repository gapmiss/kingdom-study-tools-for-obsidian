# H7353 רָחֵל / רחל

## râchêl

_(raw-kale' | raw-HALE | ra-HALE)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to journey; a ewe [the females being the predominant element of a flock] (as a good traveller); ewe, sheep; n-f

- ewe
