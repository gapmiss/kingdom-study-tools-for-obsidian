# H7718 שֹׁהַם / שהם

## shôham

_(sho'-ham | shoh-HAHM | shoh-HAHM)_

## Definition

from an unused root probably mean to blanch; a gem, probably the beryl (from its pale green color); onyx; n-m

- a precious stone or gem
  - probably onyx, chrysoprasus, beryl, malachite
