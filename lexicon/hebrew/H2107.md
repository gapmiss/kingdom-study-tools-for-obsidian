# H2107 זוּל / זול

## zûwl

_(zool | dzool | zool)_

## Definition

a primitive root (compare H2151); probably to shake out, i.e., (by implication) to scatter profusely; figuratively, to treat lightly; lavish, despise; v

- (Qal) to pour out, lavish
- (Hiphil) despise
