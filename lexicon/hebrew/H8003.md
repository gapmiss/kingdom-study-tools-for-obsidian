# H8003 שָׁלֵם / שלם

## shâlêm

_(shaw-lame' | shaw-LAME | sha-LAME)_

## Definition

from H7999; complete (literally or figuratively); especially friendly; full, just, made ready, peaceable, perfect(-ed), quiet, Shalem (by mistake for a name), whole; a

- complete, safe, peaceful, perfect, whole, full, at peace
  - complete
    - full, perfect
    - finished
  - safe, unharmed
  - peace (of covenant of peace, mind)
    - perfect, complete (of keeping covenant relation)
