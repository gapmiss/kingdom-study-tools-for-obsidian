# H8657 תֶּרֶשׁ / תרש

## Teresh

_(teh'-resh | teh-RESH | teh-RESH)_

## Definition

strictness; of foreign derivation; Teresh, a eunuch of Xerxes; Teresh; n-pr-m

- one of the 2 eunuchs who plotted to kill king Ahasuerus of Persia but whose plot was discovered by Mordecai
