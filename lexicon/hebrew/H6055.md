# H6055 עֲנַנְיָה / ענניה

## ʻĂnanyâh

_(an-an-yaw' | uh-nahn-YAW | uh-nahn-YA)_

## Definition

Jehovah clouds; from H6049 and H3050; Jah has covered; Ananjah, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine; Ananiah; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) ancestor of Azariah who assisted in rebuilding the city wall in the time of Nehemiah
- (n pr loc) a city northwest of Jerusalem to which some Benjamite exiles returned
