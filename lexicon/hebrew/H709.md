# H709 אַרְגֹּב / ארגב

## ʼArgôb

_(ar-gobe' | ar-ɡOBE | ar-ɡOVE)_

## Definition

heap of clods; from the same as H7263; stony; Argob, a district of Palestine; Argob; n-pr-loc n-pr-m

- (n pr loc) a district or area in Bashan
- (n pr m) one of king Pekah's officers
