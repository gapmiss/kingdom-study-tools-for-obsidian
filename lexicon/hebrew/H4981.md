# H4981 מִתְנִי / מתני

## Mithnîy

_(mith-nee' | mit-NEE | meet-NEE)_

## Definition

athlete; probably patrial from an unused noun meaning slenderness; a Mithnite, or inhabitant of Methen; Mithnite; a

- unknown derivation
  - used in description of Joshaphat, one of David's guards
