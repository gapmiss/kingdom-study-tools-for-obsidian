# H6072 עָסַס / עסס

## ʻâçaç

_(aw-sas' | aw-SAHS | ah-SAHS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to squeeze out juice; figuratively, to trample; tread down; v

- to press, crush, press by treading, tread down or out, press (grapes)
  - (Qal) to press
