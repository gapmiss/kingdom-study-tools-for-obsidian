# H5876 עֵין חַדָּה / עין חדה

## ʻÊyn Chaddâh

_(ane khad-daw' | ane hahd-DAW | ane ha-DA)_

## Definition

swift fountain; from H5869 and the feminine of a derivative from H2300; fountain of sharpness; En-Chaddah, a place in Palestine; En-haddah; n-pr-loc

- one of the cities on the border of Issachar
