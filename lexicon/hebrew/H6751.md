# H6751 צָלַל / צלל

## tsâlal

_(tsaw-lal' | tsaw-LAHL | tsa-LAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of hovering over (compare H6754)); to shade, as twilight or an opaque object; begin to be dark, shadowing; v

- to be or become or grow dark
  - (Qal) to become or grow dark
  - (Hiphil) to shadow
