# H7876 שָׁיָה / שיה

## shâyâh

_(shaw-yaw' | shaw-YAW | sha-YA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to keep in memory; be unmindful; v

- (Qal) to forget, deprive

## Note

Render Deuteronomy 32:18, "A Rock bore thee, thou must recollect; and (yet) thou hast forgotten," etc.
