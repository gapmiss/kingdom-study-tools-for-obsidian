# H1951 הוּן / הון

## hûwn

_(hoon | hoon | hoon)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to be naught, i.e., (figuratively) to be (causatively, act) light; be ready; v

- to be easy, be ready
  - (Hiphil) to consider as easy, make light of
