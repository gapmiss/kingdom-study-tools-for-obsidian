# H4940 מִשְׁפָּחָה / משפחה

## mishpâchâh

_(mish-paw-khaw' | mish-paw-HAW | meesh-pa-HA)_

## Definition

from H8192 (compare H8198); a family, i.e., circle of relatives; figuratively, a class (of persons), a species (of animals) or sort (of things); by extension a tribe or people; family, kind(-red); n-f

- clan, family
  - clan
    - family
    - tribe
    - people, nation
  - guild
  - species, kind
  - aristocrats
