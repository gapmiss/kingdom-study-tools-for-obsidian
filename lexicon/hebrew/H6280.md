# H6280 עָתַר / עתר

## ʻâthar

_(aw-thar' | aw-TAHR | ah-TAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be (causatively, make) abundant; deceitful, multiply; v

- to be abundant
  - (Niphal) to be plentiful
  - (Hiphil) to multiply, become abundant
