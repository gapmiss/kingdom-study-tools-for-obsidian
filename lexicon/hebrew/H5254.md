# H5254 נָסָה / נסה

## nâçâh

_(naw-saw' | naw-SAW | na-SA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to test; by implication, to attempt; adventure, assay, prove, tempt, try; v

- to test, try, prove, tempt, assay, put to the proof or test
  - (Piel)
    - to test, try
    - to attempt, assay, try
    - to test, try, prove, tempt
