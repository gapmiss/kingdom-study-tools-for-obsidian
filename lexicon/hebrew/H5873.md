# H5873 עֵין גַּנִּים / עין גנים

## ʻÊyn Gannîym

_(ane gan-neem' | ane ɡahn-NEEM | ane ɡa-NEEM)_

## Definition

fountain of the garden; from H5869 and the plural of H1588; fountain of gardens; En-Gannim, a place in Palestine; En-gannim; n-pr-loc

- a city in the low country of Judah
- a city on the border of Issachar and allotted to the Gershonite Levites
