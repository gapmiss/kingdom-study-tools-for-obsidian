# H7105 קָצִיר / קציר

## qâtsîyr

_(kaw-tseer' | kaw-TSEER | ka-TSEER)_

## Definition

from H7114; severed, a limb (of a tree, or simply foliage); bough, branch, harvest (man); n-m

- harvest, harvesting
  - process of harvesting
  - crop, what is harvested or reaped
  - time of harvest
- boughs, branches
