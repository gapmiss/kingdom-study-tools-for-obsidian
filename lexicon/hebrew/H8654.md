# H8654 תִּרְעָתִי / תרעתי

## Tirʻâthîy

_(teer-aw-thee' | tir-aw-TEE | teer-ah-TEE)_

## Definition

men of the gate; patrial from an unused name meaning gate; a Tirathite or inhabitant of an unknown Tirah; Tirathite; n-pr-m

- one of the 3 families of Kenite scribes living at Jabez
