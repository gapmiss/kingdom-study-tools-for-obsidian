# H3684 כְּסִיל / כסיל

## kᵉçîyl

_(kes-eel' | keh-SEEL | keh-SEEL)_

## Definition

from H3688; properly, fat, i.e., (figuratively) stupid or silly; fool(-ish); n-m

- fool, stupid fellow, dullard, simpleton, arrogant one
