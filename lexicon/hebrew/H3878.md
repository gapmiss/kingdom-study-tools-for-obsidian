# H3878 לֵוִי / לוי

## Lêvîy

_(lay-vee' | lay-WEE | lay-VEE)_

## Definition

joined to; from H3867; attached; Levi, a son of Jacob; Levi; n-pr-m

- the 3rd son of Jacob by Leah and progenitor of tribe of Levites
