# H1811 דָּלַף / דלף

## dâlaph

_(daw-laf' | daw-LAHP | da-LAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to drip; by implication, to weep; drop through, melt, pour out; v

- to drop, drip
  - (Qal)
    - to leak
    - to weep (of tears)
