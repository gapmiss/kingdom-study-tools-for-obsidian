# H1933 הָוָא / הוא

## hâvâʼ

_(haw-vaw' | haw-WAW | ha-VA)_

## Definition

or הָוָה; a primitive root (compare H0183, H1961) supposed to mean properly, to breathe; to be (in the sense of existence); be, × have; v

- Qal)
  - to fall
  - to be, become, exist, happen
