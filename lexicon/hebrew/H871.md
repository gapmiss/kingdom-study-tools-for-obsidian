# H871 אֲתָרִים / אתרים

## ʼĂthârîym

_(ath-aw-reem' | uh-taw-REEM | uh-ta-REEM)_

## Definition

plural from an unused root (probably meaning to step); places; Atharim, a place near Palestine; spies; n-pr-loc

- meaning uncertain—perhaps mountain pass or caravan route
