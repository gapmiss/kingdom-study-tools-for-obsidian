# H1250 בָּר / בר

## bâr

_(bawr | bawr | bahr)_

## Definition

or בַּר; from H1305 (in the sense of winnowing); grain of any kind (even while standing in the field); by extension the open country; corn, wheat; n-m

- corn, grain
