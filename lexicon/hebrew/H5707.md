# H5707 עֵד / עד

## ʻêd

_(ayd | ade | ade)_

## Definition

contracted from H5749; concretely, a witness; abstractly, testimony; specifically, a recorder, i.e., prince; witness; n-m

- witness
  - witness, testimony, evidence (of things)
  - witness (of people)
