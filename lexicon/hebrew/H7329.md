# H7329 רָזָה / רזה

## râzâh

_(raw-zaw' | raw-DZAW | ra-ZA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to emaciate, i.e., make (become) thin (literally or figuratively); famish, wax lean; v

- to be or become or grow lean
  - (Qal) to make lean
  - (Niphal) to be made lean
