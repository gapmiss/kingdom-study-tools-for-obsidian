# H5140 נָזַל / נזל

## nâzal

_(naw-zal' | naw-DZAHL | na-ZAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to drip, or shed by trickling; distil, drop, flood, (cause to) flow(-ing), gush out, melt, pour (down), running water, stream; v

- to flow, distil, flow forth or down, trickle, drop
  - (Qal)
    - to flow
      - streams, floods (participle)
    - to distil
  - (Hiphil) to cause to flow
