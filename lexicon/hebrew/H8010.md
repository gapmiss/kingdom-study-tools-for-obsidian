# H8010 שְׁלֹמֹה / שלמה

## Shᵉlômôh

_(shel-o-mo' | sheh-loh-MOH | sheh-loh-MOH)_

## Definition

peace; from H7965; peaceful; Shelomah, David's successor; Solomon; n-pr-m

- son of David by Bathsheba and 3rd king of Israel; author of Proverbs and Song of Songs
