# H970 בָּחוּר / בחור

## bâchûwr

_(baw-khoor' | baw-HOOR | ba-HOOR)_

## Definition

or בָּחֻר; participle passive of H0977; properly, selected, i.e., a youth (often collective); (choice) young (man), chosen, × hole; n-m

- youth, young man
