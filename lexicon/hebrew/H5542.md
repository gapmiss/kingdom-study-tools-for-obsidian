# H5542 סֶלָה / סלה

## çelâh

_(seh'-law | seh-LAW | seh-LA)_

## Definition

from H5541; suspension (of music), i.e., pause; Selah; v

- to lift up, exalt
  - Selah
    - a technical musical term probably showing accentuation, pause, interruption
