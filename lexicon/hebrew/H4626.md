# H4626 מַעַר / מער

## maʻar

_(mah'-ar | ma-AR | ma-AR)_

## Definition

from H6168; a nude place, i.e., (literally) the pudenda, or (figuratively) a vacant space; nakedness, proportion; n-m

- bare, naked place, nakedness
  - bare place
  - nakedness, pudenda (exposed in punishment)
