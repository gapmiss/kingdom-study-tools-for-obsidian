# H7101 קָצִין / קצין

## qâtsîyn

_(kaw-tseen' | kaw-TSEEN | ka-TSEEN)_

## Definition

from H7096 in the sense of determining; a magistrate (as deciding) or other leader; captain, guide, prince, ruler; n-m

- chief, ruler, commander
  - chief, commander (in war)
  - dictator
  - ruler (of one in authority)

## Note

Compare H6278.
