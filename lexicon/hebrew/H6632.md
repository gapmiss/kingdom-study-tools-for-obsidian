# H6632 צָב / צב

## tsâb

_(tsawb | tsawb | tsahv)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to establish; a species of lizard (probably as clinging fast); covered, litter, tortoise; n-m

- a carrying vehicle, litter, covered wagon
- an unclean lizard
  - perhaps a tortoise
  - perhaps an extinct animal, exact meaning unknown
