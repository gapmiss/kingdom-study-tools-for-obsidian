# H5898 עִיר הַמֶּלַח / עיר המלח

## ʻÎyr ham-Melach

_(eer ham-meh'-lakh | eer hahm-meh-LA | eer ha-meh-LAHK)_

## Definition

the city of salt; from H5892 and H4417 with the article of substance interp.; city of (the) salt; Irham-Melach, a place near Palestine; the city of salt; n-pr-loc

- a place in the desert of Judah
