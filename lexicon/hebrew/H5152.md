# H5152 נָחוֹר / נחור

## Nâchôwr

_(naw-khore' | naw-HORE | na-HORE)_

## Definition

snorting; from the same as H5170; snorer; Nachor, the name of the grandfather and a brother of Abraham; Nahor; n-pr-m

- son of Serug, father of Terah, and grandfather of Abraham
- son of Terah and brother of Abraham
