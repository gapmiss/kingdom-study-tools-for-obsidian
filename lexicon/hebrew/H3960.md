# H3960 לָשַׁן / לשן

## lâshan

_(law-shan' | law-SHAHN | la-SHAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to lick; but used only as a denominative from H3956; to wag the tongue, i.e., to calumniate; accuse, slander; v

- to use the tongue, slander
  - (Poel) to slander
  - (Piel) to slander
  - (Hiphil) to accuse
