# H6658 צָדָה / צדה

## tsâdâh

_(tsaw-daw' | tsaw-DAW | tsa-DA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to chase; by implication, to desolate; destroy, hunt, lie in wait; v

- (Qal) to lie in wait
- to lay waste
  - (Niphal) to be laid waste
