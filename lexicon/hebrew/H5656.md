# H5656 עֲבֹדָה / עבדה

## ʻăbôdâh

_(ab-o-daw' | uh-boh-DAW | uh-voh-DA)_

## Definition

or עֲבוֹדָה; from H5647; work of any kind; act, bondage, + bondservant, effect, labour, ministering(-try), office, service(-ile, -itude), tillage, use, work, × wrought; n-f

- labor, service
  - labor, work
  - labor (of servant or slave)
  - labor, service (of captives or subjects)
  - service (of God)
