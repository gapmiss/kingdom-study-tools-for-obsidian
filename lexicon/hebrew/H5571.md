# H5571 סַנְבַלַּט / סנבלט

## Çanballaṭ

_(san-bal-lat' | sahn-bahl-LAHT | sahn-va-LAHT)_

## Definition

strength; of foreign origin; Sanballat, a Persian satrap of Samaria; Sanballat; n-pr-m

- a Moabite of Horonaim and the opponent of Nehemiah and the returned exiles in their goal of rebuilding the walls of Jerusalem
