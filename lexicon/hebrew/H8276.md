# H8276 שָׂרַג / שרג

## sârag

_(saw-rag' | saw-RAHɡ | sa-RAHɡ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to intwine; wrap together, wreath; v

- to be intertwined
  - (Pual) to be intertwined
  - (Hithpael) to intertwine oneself
