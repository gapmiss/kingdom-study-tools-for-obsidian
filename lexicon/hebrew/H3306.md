# H3306 יָפַח / יפח

## yâphach

_(yaw-fakh' | yaw-PA | ya-FAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to breathe hard, i.e., (by implication) to sigh; bewail self; v

- to breathe, breathe hard, puff
  - (Hithpael) to gasp for breath
