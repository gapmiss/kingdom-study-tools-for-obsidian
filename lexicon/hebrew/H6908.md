# H6908 קָבַץ / קבץ

## qâbats

_(kaw-bats' | kaw-BAHTS | ka-VAHTS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to grasp, i.e., collect; assemble (selves), gather (bring) (together, selves together, up), heap, resort, × surely, take up; v

- to gather, assemble
  - (Qal) to gather, collect, assemble
  - (Niphal)
    - to assemble, gather
    - to be gathered
  - (Piel) to gather, gather together, take away
  - (Pual) to be gathered together
  - (Hithpael) to gather together, be gathered together
