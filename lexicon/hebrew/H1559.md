# H1559 גָּלָל / גלל

## Gâlâl

_(gaw-lawl' | ɡaw-LAWL | ɡa-LAHL)_

## Definition

influential; from H1556, in the sense of H1560; great; Galal, the name of two Israelites; Galal; n-pr-m

- a Levite, one of the sons of Asaph
- a third Levite, son of Jeduthun
