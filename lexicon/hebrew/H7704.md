# H7704 שָׂדֶה / שדה

## sâdeh

_(saw-deh' | saw-DEH | sa-DEH)_

## Definition

or שָׂדַי; from an unused root meaning to spread out; a field (as flat); country, field, ground, land, soil, × wild; n-m

- field, land
  - cultivated field
  - of home of wild beasts
  - plain (opposed to mountain)
  - land (opposed to sea)
