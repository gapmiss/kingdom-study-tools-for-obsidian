# H2708 חֻקָּה / חקה

## chuqqâh

_(khook-kaw' | hook-KAW | hoo-KA)_

## Definition

feminine of H2706, and meaning substantially the same; a statute; appointed, custom, manner, ordinance, site, statute; n-f

- statute, ordinance, limit, enactment, something prescribed
  - statute
