# H5533 סָכַן / סכן

## çâkan

_(saw-kan' | saw-KAHN | sa-HAHN)_

## Definition

probably a denominative from H7915; properly, to cut, i.e., damage; also to grow (causatively, make) poor; endanger, impoverish; v

- to incur danger
  - (Niphal) to endanger oneself
- to be poor
  - (Pual) to be impoverished
