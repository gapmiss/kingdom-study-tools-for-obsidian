# H7064 קֵן / קן

## qên

_(kane | kane | kane)_

## Definition

contracted from H7077; a nest (as fixed), sometimes including the nestlings; figuratively, a chamber or dwelling; nest, room; n-m

- nest
  - nest (of bird)
  - cells (like nests in Noah's ark)
