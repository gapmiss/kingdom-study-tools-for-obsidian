# H6292 פִּגּוּל / פגול

## piggûwl

_(pig-gool' | pig-GOOL | pee-ɡOOL)_

## Definition

or פִּגֻּל; from an unused root meaning to stink; properly, fetid, i.e., (figuratively) unclean (ceremonially); abominable(-tion) (thing); n-m

- foul thing, refuse
  - unclean sacrificial flesh (only use)
