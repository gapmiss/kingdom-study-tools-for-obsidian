# H6048 עֲנַמֶּלֶךְ / ענמלך

## ʻĂnammelek

_(an-am-meh'-lek | uh-nahm-meh-LEK | uh-na-meh-LEK)_

## Definition

image of the king; of foreign origin; Anammelek, an Assyrian deity; Anammelech; n-pr

- an Assyrian false god introduced to Israel during the monarchy; worshipped with rites resembling those of Molech; companion god of 'Adrammelech'
