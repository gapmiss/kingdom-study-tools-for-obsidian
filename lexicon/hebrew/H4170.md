# H4170 מוֹקֵשׁ / מוקש

## môwqêsh

_(mo-kashe' | moh-KAYSH | moh-KAYSH)_

## Definition

or מֹקֵשׁ; from H3369; a noose (for catching animals) (literally or figuratively); by implication, a hook (for the nose); be ensnared, gin, (is) snare(-d), trap; n-m

- bait, lure, snare
