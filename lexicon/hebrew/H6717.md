# H6717 צִיבָא / ציבא

## Tsîybâʼ

_(tsee-baw' | tsee-BAW | tsee-VA)_

## Definition

statue; from the same as H6678; station; Tsiba, an Israelite; Ziba; n-pr-m

- a servant of Saul whom David made steward of Jonathan's son Mephibosheth
