# H584 אָנַח / אנח

## ʼânach

_(aw-nakh' | aw-NA | ah-NAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to sigh; groan, mourn, sigh; v

- (Niphal) sigh, groan (in pain or grief), gasp
  - moan (of cattle)
