# H90 אֲגַג / אגג

## ʼĂgag

_(ag-ag' | uh-ɡAHɡ | uh-ɡAHɡ)_

## Definition

I will overtop; or אֲגָג; of uncertain derivation (compare H0089); flame; Agag, a title of Amalekitish kings; Agag; n-pr-m

- king of Amalek, spared by Saul but slain by Samuel
