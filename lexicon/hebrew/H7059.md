# H7059 קָמַט / קמט

## qâmaṭ

_(kaw-mat' | kaw-MAHT | ka-MAHT)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to pluck, i.e., destroy; cut down, fill with wrinkles; v

- to seize
  - (Qal) to seize
  - (Pual) to be snatched away prematurely
