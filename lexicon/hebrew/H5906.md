# H5906 עַיִשׁ / עיש

## ʻAyish

_(ah'-yish | ah-YISH | ah-YEESH)_

## Definition

or עָשׁ; from H5789; the constellation of the Great Bear (perhaps from its migration through the heavens); Arcturus; n-f

- a constellation
  - Great Bear, Ursa Major
  - Arcturus
