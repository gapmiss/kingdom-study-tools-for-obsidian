# H8565 תַּן / תן

## tan

_(tan | tahn | tahn)_

## Definition

from an unused root probably meaning to elongate; a monster (as preternaturally formed), i.e., a sea-serpent (or other huge marine animal); also a jackal (or other hideous land animal); dragon, whale; n-m

- dragon, maybe the extinct dinosaur the plesiosaurus, whale

Compare H8577.
