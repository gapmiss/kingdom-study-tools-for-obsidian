# H2921 טָלָא / טלא

## ṭâlâʼ

_(taw-law' | taw-LAW | ta-LA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to cover with pieces; i.e., (by implication) to spot or variegate (as tapestry); clouted, with divers colours, spotted; v

- to patch, spot, be spotted, be colored
  - (Qal)
    - to spot
    - spotted (participle)
  - (Pual) to be patched
