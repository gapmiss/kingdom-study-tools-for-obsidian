# H7619 שְׁבוּאֵל / שבואל

## Shᵉbûwʼêl

_(sheb-oo-ale' | sheh-boo-ALE | sheh-voo-ALE)_

## Definition

captive of God; or שׁוּבָאֵל; from H7617 (abbreviated form) or H7725 and H0410; captive (or returned) of God; Shebuel or Shubael, the name of two Israelites; Shebuel, Shubael; n-pr-m

- son of Gershom and grandson of Moses
- one of the 14 sons of Heman the musician
