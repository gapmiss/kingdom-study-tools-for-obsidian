_(keh'-ren hap-pook' | keh-REN hahp-POOK | keh-REN ha-POOK)_

horn of antimony; from H7161 and H6320; horn of cosmetic; Keren-hap-Puk, one of Job's daughters; Keren-happuch; n-pr-f
