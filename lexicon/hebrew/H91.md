_(ag-aw-ghee' | uh-ɡaw-ɡEE | uh-ɡa-ɡEE)_

## Definition

I will overtop; patrial or patronymic from H0090; an Agagite or descendent (subject) of Agag; Agagite; a

- said of Haman, Haman the Agagite
