# H8075 שְׁמַם / שמם

## shᵉmam

_(shem-am' | sheh-MAHM | sheh-MAHM)_

## Definition

corresponding to H8074; to stun (or intransitively, grow numb), i.e., devastate or (figuratively) stupefy (both usually in a passive sense); be astonied; v

- (Ithpolel) to be appalled, be astonished
