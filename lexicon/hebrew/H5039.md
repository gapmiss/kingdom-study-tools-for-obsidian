# H5039 נְבָלָה / נבלה

## nᵉbâlâh

_(neb-aw-law' | neh-baw-LAW | neh-va-LA)_

## Definition

feminine of H5036; foolishness, i.e., (morally) wickedness; concretely, a crime; by extension, punishment; folly, vile, villany; n-f

- senselessness, folly
  - disgraceful folly
    - of immorality, profane actions
  - disgrace, contumely disgrace
