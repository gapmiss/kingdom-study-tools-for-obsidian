# H6951 קָהָל / קהל

## qâhâl

_(kaw-hawl' | kaw-HAWL | ka-HAHL)_

## Definition

from H6950; assemblage (usually concretely); assembly, company, congregation, multitude; n-m

- assembly, company, congregation, convocation
  - assembly
    - for evil counsel, war or invasion, religious purposes
  - company (of returning exiles)
  - congregation
    - as organised body
