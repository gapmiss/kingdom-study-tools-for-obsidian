# H5035 נֶבֶל / נבל

## nebel

_(neh'-bel | neh-BEL | neh-VEL)_

## Definition

or נֵבֶל; from H5034; a skin-bag for liquids (from collapsing when empty); also a lyre (as having a body of like form); bottle, pitcher, psaltery, vessel, viol; n-m

- a skin-bag, jar, pitcher
  - skin-bottle, skin
  - jar, pitcher (earthen)
- harp, lute, guitar, musical instrument
