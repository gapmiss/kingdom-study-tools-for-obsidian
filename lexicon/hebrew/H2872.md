# H2872 טַבּוּר / טבור

## ṭabbûwr

_(tab-boor' | tahb-BOOR | ta-BOOR)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to pile up; properly, accumulated; i.e., (by implication) a summit; middle, midst; n-m

- center, midst, navel, highest part
