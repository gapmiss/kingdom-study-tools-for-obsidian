# H729 אָרַז / ארז

## ʼâraz

_(aw-raz' | aw-RAHDZ | ah-RAHZ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be firm; used only in the passive participle as a denominative from H0730; of cedar; made of cedar; a

- (v) to make oneself firm or compact
- (adj) made of cedar
- firm, strong
