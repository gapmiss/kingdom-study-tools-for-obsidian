# H5306 נֹפֶךְ / נפך

## nôphek

_(no'-fek | noh-PEK | noh-FEK)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to glisten; shining; a gem, probably the garnet; emerald; n-m

- a precious stone in the high priest's breastplate
  - perhaps an emerald, turquoise, ruby, or carbuncle
- jewels imported from Tyre
