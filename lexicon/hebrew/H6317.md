# H6317 פּוּטִיאֵל / פוטיאל

## Pûwṭîyʼêl

_(poo-tee-ale' | poo-tee-ALE | poo-tee-ALE)_

## Definition

afflicted of God; from an unused root (probably meaning to disparage) and H0410; contempt of God; Putiel, an Israelite; Putiel; n-pr-m

- father of the wife of Eleazar, the son of Aaron
