# H6439 פְּנוּאֵל / פנואל

## Pᵉnûwʼêl

_(pen-oo-ale' | peh-noo-ALE | peh-noo-ALE)_

## Definition

facing God; or (more properly,) פְּנִיאֵל; from H6437 and H0410; face of God; Penuel or Peniel, a place East of Jordan; also (as Penuel) the name of two Israelites; Peniel, Penuel; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a Benjamite, son of Shashak, brother of Iphedeiah of the family of Saul
- son of Hur, father of Gedor, and a descendant of Judah
- (n pr loc) the place named by Jacob when he wrestled with God and located on the north bank of the Jabbok close to the Jordan
