# H8321 שֹׂרֵק / שרק

## sôrêq

_(so-rake' | soh-RAKE | soh-RAKE)_

## Definition

or שׂוֹרֵק; and (feminine) שֹׂרֵקָה; from H8319 in the sense of redness (compare H8320); a vine stock (properly, one yielding purple grapes, the richest variety); choice(-st, noble) wine; n-m

- choice species of vine, choice grapes

## Note

Compare H8291.
