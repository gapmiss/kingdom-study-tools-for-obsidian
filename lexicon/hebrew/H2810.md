# H2810 חִשָּׁבוֹן / חשבון

## chishshâbôwn

_(khish-shaw-bone' | hish-shaw-BONE | hee-sha-VONE)_

## Definition

from H2803; a contrivance, i.e., actual (a warlike machine) or mental (a machination); engine, invention; n-m

- device, invention
