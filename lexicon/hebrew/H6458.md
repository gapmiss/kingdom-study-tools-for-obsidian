# H6458 פָּסַל / פסל

## pâçal

_(paw-sal' | paw-SAHL | pa-SAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to carve, whether wood or stone; grave, hew; v

- to cut, hew, hew into shape
  - (Qal) to hew, hew out, quarry
