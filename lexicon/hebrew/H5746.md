# H5746 עוּג / עוג

## ʻûwg

_(oog | ooɡ | ooɡ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to gyrate; but used only as a denominative from H5692; to bake (round cakes on the hearth); bake; v

- (Qal) to bake, bake a cake
