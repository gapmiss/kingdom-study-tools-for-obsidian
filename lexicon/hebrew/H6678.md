# H6678 צוֹבָא / צובא

## Tsôwbâʼ

_(tso-baw' | tsoh-BAW | tsoh-VA)_

## Definition

station; or צוֹבָה; or צֹבָה; from an unused root meaning to station; a station; Zoba or Zobah, a region of Syria; Zoba, Zobah; n-pr-loc

- the name of a portion of Syria which formed a separate kingdom in the times of Saul, David, and Solomon; located northeast of Damascus
