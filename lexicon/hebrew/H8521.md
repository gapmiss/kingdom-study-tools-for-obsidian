# H8521 תֵּל חַרְשָׁא / תל חרשא

## Têl Charshâʼ

_(tale khar-shaw' | tale hahr-SHAW | tale hahr-SHA)_

## Definition

mound of the deaf mute; from H8510 and the feminine of H2798; mound of workmanship; Tel-Charsha, a place in Babylonia; Tel-haresha, Tel-harsa; n-pr-loc

- a town of Babylon
  - probably located in the low country near the sea
