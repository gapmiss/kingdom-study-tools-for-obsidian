# H6621 פֶּתַע / פתע

## pethaʻ

_(peh'-thah | peh-TA | peh-TA)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to open (the eyes); (compare H6597); a wink, i.e., moment (used only [with or without preposition] adverbially, quickly or unexpectedly); at an instant, suddenly, × very; n-m

- suddenness, in an instant
