# H7822 שְׁחִין / שחין

## shᵉchîyn

_(shekh-een' | sheh-HEEN | sheh-HEEN)_

## Definition

from an unused root probably meaning to burn; inflammation, i.e., an ulcer; boil, botch; n-m

- boil, inflamed spot, inflammation, eruption
  - of man, leprosy, of man and beast
