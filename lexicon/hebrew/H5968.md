# H5968 עָלַף / עלף

## ʻâlaph

_(aw-laf' | aw-LAHP | ah-LAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to veil or cover; figuratively, to be languid; faint, overlaid, wrap self; v

- to cover
  - (Pual) covered, encrusted (participle)
  - (Hithpael) to enwrap oneself, disguise oneself, faint
