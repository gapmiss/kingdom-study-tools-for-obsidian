# H7701 שֹׁד / שד

## shôd

_(shode | shode | shode)_

## Definition

or שׁוֹד; (Job 5:21), from H7736; violence, ravage; desolation, destruction, oppression, robbery, spoil(-ed, -er, -ing), wasting; n-m

- havoc, violence, destruction, devastation, ruin
  - violence, havoc (as social sin)
  - devastation, ruin
