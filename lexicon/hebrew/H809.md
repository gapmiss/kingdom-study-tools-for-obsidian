# H809 אֲשִׁישָׁה / אשישה

## ʼăshîyshâh

_(ash-ee-shaw' | uh-shee-SHAW | uh-shee-SHA)_

## Definition

feminine of H0808; something closely pressed together, i.e., a cake of raisins or other comfits; flagon; n-f

- raisin-cake, used in sacrificial feasts
  - consistently translated in AV as &quot;flagon of wine&quot;
