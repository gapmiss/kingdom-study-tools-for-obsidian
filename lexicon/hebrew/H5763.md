# H5763 עוּל / עול

## ʻûwl

_(ool | ool | ool)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to suckle, i.e., give milk; milch, (ewe great) with young; v

- to give suck, suckle, nurse
  - (Qal) giving suck (participle)
