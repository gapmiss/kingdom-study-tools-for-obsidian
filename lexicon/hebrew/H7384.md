# H7384 רִיפַת / ריפת

## Rîyphath

_(ree-fath' | ree-PAHT | ree-FAHT)_

## Definition

spoken; or (probably by orthographical error) דִּיפַת; of foreign origin; Riphath, a grandson of Japheth and his descendants; Riphath; n-pr-m

- the 2nd son of Gomer
