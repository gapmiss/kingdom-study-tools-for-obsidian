# H8553 תִּמְנָה / תמנה

## Timnâh

_(tim-naw' | tim-NAW | teem-NA)_

## Definition

portion; from H4487; a portion assigned; Timnah, the name of two places in Palestine; Timnah, Timnath, Thimnathah; n-pr-loc

- a town on the northern boundary of Judah later assigned to Dan
- a town in the hill country of Judah
