# H8070 שְׁמִירָמוֹת / שמירמות

## Shᵉmîyrâmôwth

_(shem-ee-raw-moth' | sheh-mee-raw-MOTE | sheh-mee-ra-MOTE)_

## Definition

name of heights; or שְׁמָרִימוֹת; probably from H8034 and plural of H7413; name of heights; Shemiramoth, the name of two Israelites; Shemiramoth; n-pr-m

- a Merarite Levite in the second rank in the choir of David
- a Levite in the time of king Jehoshaphat of Judah
