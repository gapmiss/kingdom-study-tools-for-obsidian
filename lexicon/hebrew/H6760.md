# H6760 צָלַע / צלע

## tsâlaʻ

_(tsaw-lah' | tsaw-LA | tsa-LA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; probably to curve; used only as denominative from H6763; to limp (as if one-sided); halt; v

- to limp, be lame
  - (Qal)
    - to limp
    - to be lame
