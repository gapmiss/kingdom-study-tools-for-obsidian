# H917 בְּדָן / בדן

## Bᵉdân

_(bed-awn' | beh-DAWN | beh-DAHN)_

## Definition

in judging; probably shortened for H5658; servile; Bedan, the name of two Israelites; Bedan; n-pr-m

- a judge who followed Gideon
- a Manassite
