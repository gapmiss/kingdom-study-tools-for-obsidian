# H7543 רָקַח / רקח

## râqach

_(raw-kakh' | raw-KA | ra-KAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to perfume; apothecary, compound, make (ointment), prepare, spice; v

- to mix, compound
  - (Qal)
    - to mix, compound
    - compounder, mixer, perfumer (participle) (subst)
  - (Pual) mixed (participle)
  - (Hiphil) to mix
