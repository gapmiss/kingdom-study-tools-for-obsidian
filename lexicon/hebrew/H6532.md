# H6532 פֹּרֶכֶת / פרכת

## pôreketh

_(po-reh'-keth | poh-reh-KET | poh-reh-HET)_

## Definition

feminine active participle of the same as H6531; a separatrix, i.e., (the sacred) screen; vail; n-f

- curtain, veil
