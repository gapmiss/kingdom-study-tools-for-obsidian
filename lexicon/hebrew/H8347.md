# H8347 שֵׁשַׁךְ / ששך

## Shêshak

_(shay-shak' | shay-SHAHK | shay-SHAHK)_

## Definition

thy fine linen; of foreign derivation; Sheshak, a symbolic name of Babylon; Sheshach; n-pr-loc

- another name for Babylon apparently taken from the goddess 'Shach'
