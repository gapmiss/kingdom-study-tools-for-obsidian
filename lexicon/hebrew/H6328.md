# H6328 פּוּק / פוק

## pûwq

_(pook | pook | pook)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to waver; stumble, move; v

- to reel, totter, stumble
  - (Qal) to reel
  - (Hiphil)
    - to totter, wobble
    - to cause tottering
