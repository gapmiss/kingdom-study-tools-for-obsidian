# H6150 עָרַב / ערב

## ʻârab

_(aw-rab' | aw-RAHB | ah-RAHV)_

## Definition

a primitive root (rather identical with H6148 through the idea of covering with a texture); to grow dusky at sundown; be darkened, (toward) evening; v

- to become evening, grow dark
  - (Qal) to become evening, grow dark
  - (Hiphil) to spend the evening, do at evening
