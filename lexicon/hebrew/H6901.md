# H6901 קָבַל / קבל

## qâbal

_(kaw-bal' | kaw-BAHL | ka-VAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to admit, i.e., take (literally or figuratively); choose, (take) hold, receive, (under-)take; v

- to take, receive, be before
  - (Piel)
    - to take, choose, receive
    - to accept
    - to accept, assume
  - (Hiphil)
    - to show oppositeness
    - to correspond, receive one to the other
