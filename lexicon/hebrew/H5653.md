# H5653 עַבְדָּא / עבדא

## ʻAbdâʼ

_(ab-daw' | ab-DAW | av-DA)_

## Definition

servant of Jehovah; from H5647; work; Abda, the name of two Israelites; Abda; n-pr-m

- father of Adoniram
- a Levite, son of Shammua; also called 'Obadiah'
