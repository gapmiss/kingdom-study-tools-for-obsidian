# H359 אֵילוֹת / אילות

## ʼÊylôwth

_(ay-loth' | ay-LOTE | ay-LOTE)_

## Definition

grove of lofty trees; or אֵילַת ; from H0352; trees or a grove (i.e., palms); Eloth or Elath, a place on the Red Sea; Elath, Eloth; n-pr-loc

- a port on the north-east arm of the Red Sea
