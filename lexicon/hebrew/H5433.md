# H5433 סָבָא / סבא

## çâbâʼ

_(saw-baw' | saw-BAW | sa-VA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to quaff to satiety, i.e., become tipsy; drunkard, fill self, Sabean, (wine-)bibber; v

- to drink heavily or largely, imbibe
  - (Qal)
    - to imbibe
    - wine-bibber, drunkard (participle) (subst)
