# H631 אָסַר / אסר

## ʼâçar

_(aw-sar' | aw-SAHR | ah-SAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to yoke or hitch; by analogy, to fasten in any sense, to join battle; bind, fast, gird, harness, hold, keep, make ready, order, prepare, prison(-er), put in bonds, set in array, tie; v

- to tie, bind, imprison
  - (Qal)
    - to tie, bind
    - to tie, harness
    - to bind (with cords)
    - to gird (rare and late)
    - to begin the battle, make the attack
    - of obligation of oath (figurative)
  - (Niphal) to be imprisoned, bound
  - (Pual) to be taken prisoner
