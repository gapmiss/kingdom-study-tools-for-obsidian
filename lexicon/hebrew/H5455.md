# H5455 סַבְתְּכָא / סבתכא

## Çabtᵉkâʼ

_(sab-tek-aw' | sahb-teh-KAW | sahv-teh-HA)_

## Definition

striking; probably of foreign derivation; Sabteca, the name of a son of Cush, and the region settled by him; Sabtecha, Sabtechah; n-pr-m

- the 5th son of Cush
