# H5212 נִיסָן / ניסן

## Nîyçân

_(nee-sawn' | nee-SAWN | nee-SAHN)_

## Definition

their flight; probably of foreign origin; Nisan, the first month of the Jewish sacred year; Nisan; n

- the 1st month of the Jewish calendar corresponding to Mar or Apr
