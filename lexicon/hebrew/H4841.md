# H4841 מֶרְקָחָה / מרקחה

## merqâchâh

_(mer-kaw-khaw' | mer-kaw-HAW | mer-ka-HA)_

## Definition

feminine of H4840; abstractly, a seasoning (with spicery); concretely, an unguentkettle (for preparing spiced oil); pot of ointment, × well; n-f

- a seasoning, compounding, spice-seasoning
- ointment pot
