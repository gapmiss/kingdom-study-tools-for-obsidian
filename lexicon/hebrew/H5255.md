# H5255 נָסַח / נסח

## nâçach

_(naw-sakh' | naw-SA | na-SAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to tear away; destroy, pluck, root; v

- to pull or tear away
  - (Qal) to tear away, tear down
  - (Niphal) to be torn away
