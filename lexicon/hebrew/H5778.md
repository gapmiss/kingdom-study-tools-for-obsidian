# H5778 עוֹפַי / עופי

## ʻÔwphay

_(o-fah'-ee | oh-PAI | oh-FAI)_

## Definition

gloomy; from H5775; birdlike; Ephai, an Israelite; Ephai (from margin); n-pr-m

- a Netophathite, whose sons were among the captains of the forces left in Judah after the deportation to Babylon
