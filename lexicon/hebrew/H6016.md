# H6016 עֹמֶר / עמר

## ʻômer

_(o'-mer | oh-MER | oh-MER)_

## Definition

from H6014; properly, a heap, i.e., a sheaf; also an omer, as a dry measure; omer, sheaf; n-m

- omer
  - a dry measure of 1/10 ephah (about 2 liters)
- sheaf
