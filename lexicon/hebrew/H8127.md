# H8127 שֵׁן / שן

## shên

_(shane | shane | shane)_

## Definition

from H8150; compare H8143; a tooth (as sharp); specifically ivory; figuratively, a cliff; crag, × forefront, ivory, × sharp, tooth; n-f

- tooth, ivory
  - tooth
    - of man, lex talionis, beast
  - tooth, tine (of fork)
  - ivory
    - as material
    - of commerce
  - sharp pointed rock
