# H4574 מַעֲדָן / מעדן

## maʻădân

_(mah-ad-awn' | ma-uh-DAWN | ma-uh-DAHN)_

## Definition

or (feminine) מַעֲדַנָּה; from H5727; a delicacy or (abstractly) pleasure (adverbially, cheerfully); dainty, delicately, delight; n-m

- dainty (food), delight
