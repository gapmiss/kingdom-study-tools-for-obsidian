# H7100 קֶצַח / קצח

## qetsach

_(keh'-tsakh | keh-TSA | keh-TSAHK)_

## Definition

from an unused root apparently meaning to incise; fennel-flower (from its pungency); fitches; n-m

- black cummin
  - a plant with small black acrid seeds used as a condiment
