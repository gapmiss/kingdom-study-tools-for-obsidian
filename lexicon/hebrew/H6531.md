# H6531 פֶּרֶךְ / פרך

## perek

_(peh'-rek | peh-REK | peh-REK)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to break apart; fracture, i.e., severity; cruelty, rigour; n-m

- harshness, severity, cruelty
