# H5945 עֶלְיוֹן / עליון

## ʻelyôwn

_(el-yone' | el-YONE | el-YONE)_

## Definition

from H5927; an elevation, i.e., (adjectively) lofty (comparatively); as title, the Supreme; (Most, on) high(-er, -est), upper(-most); n-m

- (adj) high, upper
  - of Davidic king exalted above monarchs
- (n m) Highest, Most High
  - name of God
  - of rulers, either monarchs or angel-princes
