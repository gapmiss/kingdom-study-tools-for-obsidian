# H5182 נְחַת / נחת

## nᵉchath

_(nekh-ath' | neh-HAHT | neh-HAHT)_

## Definition

corresponding to H5181; to descend; causatively, to bring away, deposit, depose; carry, come down, depose, lay up, place; v

- to descend
  - (P'al) to descend
  - (Aphel) to deposit
  - (Hophal) to be deposed, be cast down
