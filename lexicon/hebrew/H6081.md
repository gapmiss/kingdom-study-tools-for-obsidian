# H6081 עֵפֶר / עפר

## ʻÊpher

_(ay'-fer | ay-PER | ay-FER)_

## Definition

a calf; probably a variation of H6082; gazelle; Epher, the name of an Arabian and of two Israelites; Epher; n-pr-m

- the 2nd son of Midian
- a son of Ezra, among the descendants of Judah
- one of the heads of the families of Manasseh on the east of the Jordan
