# H5974 עִם / עם

## ʻim

_(eem | im | eem)_

## Definition

corresponding to H5973; adverb or preposition, with (i.e., in conjunction with), in varied applications; specifically, equally with; often with prepositional prefix (and then usually unrepresented in English); by, from, like, to(-ward), with; prep

- with
  - together with, with
  - with, during
