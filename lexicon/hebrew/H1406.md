# H1406 גָּג / גג

## gâg

_(gawg | ɡawɡ | ɡahɡ)_

## Definition

probably by reduplication from H1342; a roof; by analogy, the top of an altar; roof (of the house), (house) top (of the house); n-m

- roof, top, housetop
  - roof (of house)
  - top (of altar of incense)
