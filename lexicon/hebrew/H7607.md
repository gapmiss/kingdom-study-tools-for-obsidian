# H7607 שְׁאֵר / שאר

## shᵉʼêr

_(sheh-ayr' | sheh-ARE | sheh-ARE)_

## Definition

from H7604; flesh (as swelling out), as living or for food; generally food of any kind; figuratively, kindred by blood; body, flesh, food, (near) kin(-sman, -swoman), near (nigh) (of kin); n-m

- flesh, food, body, near kin, near kinswoman
  - flesh
    - as food
    - for physical power (figuratively)
  - flesh relation, blood relation
  - self
