# H44 אֲבִיעֶזֶר / אביעזר

## ʼĂbîyʻezer

_(ab-ee-ay'-zer | uh-bee-eh-DZER | uh-vee-eh-ZER)_

## Definition

my father is help; from H0001 and H5829; father of help (i.e., helpful); Abiezer, the name of two Israelites; Abiezer; n-pr-m

- a Manassite, called &quot;son&quot; of Gilead, also son of Gilead's sister
- a Benjamite, warrior of David
