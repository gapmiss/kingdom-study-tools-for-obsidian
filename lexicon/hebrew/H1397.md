# H1397 גֶּבֶר / גבר

## geber

_(gheh'-ber | ɡeh-BER | ɡeh-VER)_

## Definition

from H1396; properly, a valiant man or warrior; generally, a person simply; every one, man, × mighty; n-m

- man, strong man, warrior (emphasising strength or ability to fight)
