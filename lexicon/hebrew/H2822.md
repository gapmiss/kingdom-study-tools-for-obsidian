# H2822 חֹשֶׁךְ / חשך

## chôshek

_(kho-shek' | hoh-SHEK | hoh-SHEK)_

## Definition

from H2821; the dark; hence (literally) darkness; figuratively, misery, destruction, death, ignorance, sorrow, wickedness; dark(-ness), night, obscurity; n-m

- darkness, obscurity
  - darkness
  - secret place
