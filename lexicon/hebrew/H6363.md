# H6363 פֶּטֶר / פטר

## peṭer

_(peh'-ter | peh-TER | peh-TER)_

## Definition

or פִּטְרָה; from H6362; a fissure, i.e., (concretely) firstling (as opening the matrix); firstling, openeth, such as open; n-m

- firstborn, firstling, that which separates or first opens
