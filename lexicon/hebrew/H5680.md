# H5680 עִבְרִי / עברי

## ʻIbrîy

_(ib-ree' | ib-REE | eev-REE)_

## Definition

one from beyond; patronymic from H5677; an Eberite (i.e., Hebrew) or descendant of Eber; Hebrew(-ess) (woman); n-pr

- (n pr) a designation of the patriarchs and the Israelites
- (adj) a designation of the patriarchs and the Israelites
