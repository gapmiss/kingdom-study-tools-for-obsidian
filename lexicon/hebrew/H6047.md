# H6047 עֲנָמִים / ענמים

## ʻĂnâmîym

_(an-aw-meem' | uh-naw-MEEM | uh-na-MEEM)_

## Definition

affliction of the waters; as if plural of some Egyptian word; Anamim, a son of Mizraim and his descendants, with their country; Anamim; n

- a tribe of Egyptians
