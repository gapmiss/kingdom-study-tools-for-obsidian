# H5879 עֵינַיִם / עינים

## ʻÊynayim

_(ay-nah'-yim | ay-na-YIM | ay-na-YEEM)_

## Definition

double spring; or עֵינָם; dual of H5869; double fountain; Enajim or Enam, a place in Palestine; Enaim, openly (Genesis 38:21); n-pr-loc

- a city in the lowlands of Judah
