# H1573 גֹּמֶא / גמא

## gômeʼ

_(go'-meh | ɡoh-MEH | ɡoh-MEH)_

## Definition

from H1572; properly, an absorbent, i.e., the bulrush (from its porosity); specifically the papyrus; (bul-)rush; n-m

- rush, reed, papyrus
