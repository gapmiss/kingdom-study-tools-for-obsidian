# H5243 נָמַל / נמל

## nâmal

_(naw-mal' | naw-MAHL | na-MAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to become clipped or (specifically) circumcised; (branch to) be cut down (off), circumcise; v

- (Qal) to circumcise, become clipped, be circumcised, be cut off
