# H3874 לוּט / לוט

## lûwṭ

_(loot | loot | loot)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to wrap up; cast, wrap; v

- to wrap closely or tightly, enwrap, envelop
  - (Qal) to wrap tightly
  - (Hiphil) to envelop, wrap
