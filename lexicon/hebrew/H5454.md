# H5454 סַבְתָּא / סבתא

## Çabtâʼ

_(sab-taw' | sahb-TAW | sahv-TA)_

## Definition

striking; or סַבְתָּה; probably of foreign derivation; Sabta or Sabtah, the name of a son of Cush, and the country occupied by his posterity; Sabta, Sabtah; n-pr-m

- the 3rd son of Cush
