# H7824 שָׁחִיף / שחיף

## shâchîyph

_(shaw-kheef' | shaw-HEEP | sha-HEEF)_

## Definition

from the same as H7828; a board (as chipped thin); cieled with; a

- (adj) panelled or wainscotted with wood
  - meaning doubtful
- (n) a wainscot of wood
  - meaning doubtful
