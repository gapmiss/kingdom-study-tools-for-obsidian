# H6051 עָנָן / ענן

## ʻânân

_(aw-nawn' | aw-NAWN | ah-NAHN)_

## Definition

from H6049; a cloud (as covering the sky), i.e., the nimbus or thunder-cloud; cloud(-y); n-m

- cloud, cloudy, cloud-mass
  - cloud-mass (of theophanic cloud)
  - cloud
