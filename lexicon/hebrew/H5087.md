# H5087 נָדַר / נדר

## nâdar

_(naw-dar' | naw-DAHR | na-DAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to promise (pos., to do or give something to God); (make a) vow; v

- to vow, make a vow
  - (Qal) to vow a vow
