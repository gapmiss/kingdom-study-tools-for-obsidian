# H5125 נוּן / נון

## nûwn

_(noon | noon | noon)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to resprout, i.e., propagate by shoots; figuratively, to be perpetual; be continued; v

- (Niphal) continue, to increase, propagate
- (Hiphil) continue, to increase, propagate
