# H2205 זָקֵן / זקן

## zâqên

_(zaw-kane' | dzaw-KANE | za-KANE)_

## Definition

from H2204; old; aged, ancient (man), elder(-est), old (man, men and...women), senator; a

- old
  - old (of humans)
  - elder (of those having authority)
