# H4291 מְטָא / מטא

## mᵉṭâʼ

_(met-aw' | meh-TAW | meh-TA)_

## Definition

or מְטָה; (Aramaic), apparently corresponding to H4672 in the intransitive sense of being found present; to arrive, extend or happen; come, reach; v

- to reach, come upon, attain
  - (P'al)
    - to reach, come to
    - to reach, extend
    - to come upon
