# H8047 שַׁמָּה / שמה

## shammâh

_(sham-maw' | shahm-MAW | sha-MA)_

## Definition

from H8074; ruin; by implication, consternation; astonishment, desolate(-ion), waste, wonderful thing; n-f

- waste, horror, appalment
  - a waste (of land, city, etc)
  - appalment, horror
