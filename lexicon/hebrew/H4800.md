# H4800 מֶרְחָב / מרחב

## merchâb

_(mer-khawb' | mer-HAWB | mer-HAHV)_

## Definition

from H7337; enlargement, either literally (an open space, usually in a good sense), or figuratively (liberty); breadth, large place (room); n-m

- broad or roomy place, wide, expanses
