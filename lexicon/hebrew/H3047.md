# H3047 יָדָע / ידע

## Yâdâʻ

_(yaw-daw' | yaw-DAW | ya-DA)_

## Definition

He knows; from H3045; knowing; Jada, an Israelite; Jada; n-pr-m

- a Judahite, son of Onam and brother of Shammai, in the genealogy of the sons of Jerahmeel by his wife Atarah
