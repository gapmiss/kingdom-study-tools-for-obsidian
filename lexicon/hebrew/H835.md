## ʼesher

_(eh'-sher | eh-SHER | eh-SHER)_

## Definition

from H0833; happiness; only in masculine plural construction as interjection, how happy!; blessed, happy; n-m

- happiness, blessedness
  - often used as interjection
  - blessed are
