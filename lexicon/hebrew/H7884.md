# H7884 שִׁיחוֹר לִבְנָת / שיחור לבנת

## Shîychôwr Libnâth

_(shee-khore' lib-nawth' | shee-HORE lib-NAWT | shee-HORE leev-NAHT)_

## Definition

blackness of whiteness; from the same as H7883 and H3835; darkish whiteness; Shichor-Libnath, a stream of Palestine; Shihor-libnath; n-pr-m

- a stream in Asher
