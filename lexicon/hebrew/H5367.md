# H5367 נָקַשׁ / נקש

## nâqash

_(naw-kash' | naw-KAHSH | na-KAHSH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to entrap (with a noose), literally or figuratively; catch (lay a) snare; v

- to knock, strike, bring down
  - (Qal) to strike down
  - (Niphal) to be thrust after, be impelled
  - (Piel) to strike at, take aim at
  - (Hithpael) to be struck
- to ensnare
  - (Qal) to ensnare
  - (Niphal) to be ensnared
  - (Piel) to lay snares
  - (Hithpael) to lay a snare
