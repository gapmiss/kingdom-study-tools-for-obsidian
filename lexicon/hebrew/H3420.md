# H3420 יֵרָקוֹן / ירקון

## yêrâqôwn

_(yay-raw-kone' | yay-raw-KONE | yay-ra-KONE)_

## Definition

from H3418; paleness, whether of persons (from fright), or of plants (from drought); greenish, yellow; n-m

- mildew, paleness, lividness
  - mildew, rust
  - paleness
