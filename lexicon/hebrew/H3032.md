# H3032 יָדַד / ידד

## yâdad

_(yaw-dad' | yaw-DAHD | ya-DAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; (compare H3034); properly, to handle, i.e., to throw, e.g., lots; cast; v

- (v) (Qal) to throw lots, cast lots
- (n) love, loving-one, friend
