# H8287 שָׁרוּחֶן / שרוחן

## Shârûwchen

_(shaw-roo-khen' | shaw-roo-HEN | sha-roo-HEN)_

## Definition

refuge of grace; probably from H8281 (in the sense of dwelling (compare H8271) and H2580; abode of pleasure; Sharuchen, a place in Palestine; Sharuhen; n-pr-loc

- a city in Judah allotted to Simeon
