# H684 אֹצֶם / אצם

## ʼÔtsem

_(o'-tsem | oh-TSEM | oh-TSEM)_

## Definition

I shall hasten them; from an unused root probably meaning to be strong; strength (i.e., strong); Otsem, the name of two Israelites; Ozem; n-pr-m

- older brother of David
- a descendant of Judah
