# H4696 מֵצַח / מצח

## mêtsach

_(may'-tsakh | may-TSA | may-TSAHK)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to be clear, i.e., conspicuous; the forehead (as open and prominent); brow, forehead, impudent; n-m

- brow, forehead
