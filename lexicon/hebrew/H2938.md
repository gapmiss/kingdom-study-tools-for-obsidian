# H2938 טָעַם / טעם

## ṭâʻam

_(taw-am' | taw-AM | ta-AM)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to taste; figuratively, to perceive; × but, perceive, taste; v

- to taste, perceive, eat
  - (Qal) to taste
