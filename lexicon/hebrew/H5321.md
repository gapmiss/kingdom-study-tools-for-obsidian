# H5321 נַפְתָּלִי / נפתלי

## Naphtâlîy

_(naf-taw-lee' | nahp-taw-LEE | nahf-ta-LEE)_

## Definition

wrestling; from H6617; my wrestling; Naphtali, a son of Jacob, with the tribe descended from him, and its territory; Naphtali; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) the 5th son of Jacob and the 2nd by Bilhah the handmaid of Rachel
- the tribe descended from Naphtali the son of Jacob
- (n pr loc) the territory assigned to the tribe of Naphtali
