# H4749 מִקְשָׁה / מקשה

## miqshâh

_(mik-shaw' | mik-SHAW | meek-SHA)_

## Definition

feminine of H4748; rounded work, i.e., moulded by hammering (repousse); beaten (out of one piece, work), upright, whole piece; n-f

- hammered work, finely decorated cultic objects of gold or silver
