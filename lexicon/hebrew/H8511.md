# H8511 תָּלָא / תלא

## tâlâʼ

_(taw-law' | taw-LAW | ta-LA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to suspend; figuratively (through hesitation) to be uncertain; by implication (of mental dependence) to habituate; be bent, hang (in doubt); v

- (Qal) to hang, hang to, cling to
