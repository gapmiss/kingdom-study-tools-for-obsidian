# H7568 רֶשֶׁת / רשת

## resheth

_(reh'-sheth | reh-SHET | reh-SHET)_

## Definition

from H3423; a net (as catching animals); net(-work); n-f

- net
  - net
    - for catching
    - of judgment (figuratively)
    - of leaders leading people to sin
    - as a trap for man
  - network (brazen—for altar of tabernacle)
