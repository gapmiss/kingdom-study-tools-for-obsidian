# H2435 חִיצוֹן / חיצון

## chîytsôwn

_(khee-tsone' | hee-TSONE | hee-TSONE)_

## Definition

from H2434; properly, the (outer) wall side; hence, exterior; figuratively, secular (as opposed to sacred); outer, outward, utter, without; a

- outer, external, outward
