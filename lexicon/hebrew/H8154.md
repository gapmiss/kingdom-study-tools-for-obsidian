# H8154 שָׁסָה / שסה

## shâçâh

_(shaw-saw' | shaw-SAW | sha-SA)_

## Definition

or שָׁשָׂה; (Isaiah 10:13), a primitive root; to plunder; destroyer, rob, spoil(-er); v

- to spoil, plunder, take spoil
  - (Qal)
    - to plunder
    - plunderers (participle)
  - (Poel) to plunder
