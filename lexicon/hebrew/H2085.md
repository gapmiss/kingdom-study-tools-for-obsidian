# H2085 זָג / זג

## zâg

_(zawg | dzawɡ | zahɡ)_

## Definition

from an unused root probably meaning to inclose; the skin of a grape; husk; n-m

- (meaning uncertain)
  - name of some insignificant product of the vine, forbidden to Nazarites, perhaps the stem or skin of the grape
