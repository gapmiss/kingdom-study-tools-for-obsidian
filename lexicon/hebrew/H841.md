# H841 אֲשַׂרְאֵלָה / אשראלה

## ʼĂsarʼêlâh

_(as-ar-ale'-aw | uh-sahr-ay-LAW | uh-sahr-ay-LA)_

## Definition

God holds; from the same as H0840; right toward God; Asarelah, an Israelite; Asarelah; n-pr-m

- a son of Asaph, appointed sanctuary musician by David
