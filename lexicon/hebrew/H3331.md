# H3331 יַצַע / יצע

## yatsaʻ

_(yaw-tsah' | ya-TSA | ya-TSA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to strew as a surface; make (one's) bed, × lie, spread; v

- to spread out, make a bed
  - (Hiphil) to lay, spread out
  - (Hophal) to be laid, be spread out
