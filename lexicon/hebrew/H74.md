# H74 אַבְנֵר / אבנר

## ʼAbnêr

_(ab-nare' | ab-NARE | av-NARE)_

## Definition

my father is a lamp; or (fully) אֲבִינֵר ; from H0001 and H5216; father of light (i.e., enlightening); Abner, an Israelite; Abner; n-pr-m

- Saul's cousin and army captain, treacherously slain by Joab
