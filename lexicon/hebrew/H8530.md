# H8530 תַּלְפִּיָּה / תלפיה

## talpîyâh

_(tal-pee-yaw' | tahl-pih-YAW | tahl-pee-YA)_

## Definition

feminine from an unused root meaning to tower; something tall, i.e., (plural collective) slenderness; armoury; n-f

- weapons, armoury, armaments
  - meaning doubtful
