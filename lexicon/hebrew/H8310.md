# H8310 שַׂרְסְכִים / שרסכים

## Sarçᵉkîym

_(sar-seh-keem' | sahr-seh-KEEM | sahr-seh-HEEM)_

## Definition

prince of the eunuchs; of foreign derivation; Sarsekim, a Babylonian general; Sarsechim; n-pr-m

- a ruler or general of Nebuchadnezzar at the taking of Jerusalem

[[Jeremiah 39#3|Jeremiah 39:3]]
