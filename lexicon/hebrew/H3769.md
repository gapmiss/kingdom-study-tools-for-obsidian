# H3769 כָּרַר / כרר

## kârar

_(kaw-rar' | kaw-RAHR | ka-RAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to dance (i.e., whirl); dance(-ing); v

- (Pilpel) to whirl, dance
  - whirling, dancing (participle)
