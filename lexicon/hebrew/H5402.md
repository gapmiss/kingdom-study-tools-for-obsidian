# H5402 נֶשֶׁק / נשק

## nesheq

_(neh'-shek | neh-SHEK | neh-SHEK)_

## Definition

or נֵשֶׁק; from H5401; military equipment, i.e., (collectively) arms (offensive or defensive), or (concretely) an arsenal; armed men, armour(-y), battle, harness, weapon; n-m

- equipment, weapons, armoury
  - equipment, weapons
  - armoury
