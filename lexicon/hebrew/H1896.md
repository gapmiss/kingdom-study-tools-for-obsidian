# H1896 הֵגֵא / הגא

## Hêgêʼ

_(hay-gay' | hay-ɡAY | hay-ɡAY)_

## Definition

eunuch; or (by permutation) הֵגַי; probably of Persian origin; Hege or Hegai, a eunuch of Xerxes; Hegai, Hege; n-pr-m

- one of the eunuchs of the court of Ahasuerus
