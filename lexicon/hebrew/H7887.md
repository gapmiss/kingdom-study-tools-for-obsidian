# H7887 שִׁילֹה / שילה

## Shîylôh

_(shee-lo' | shee-LOH | shee-LOH)_

## Definition

place of rest; or שִׁלֹה; or שִׁילוֹ; or שִׁלוֹ; from the same as H7886; Shiloh, a place in Palestine; Shiloh; n-pr-loc

- a city in Ephraim and temporary home of the Ark of the Covenant and the Tabernacle, the place where Samuel grew up
