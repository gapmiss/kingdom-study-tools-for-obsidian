# H6904 קֹבֶל / קבל

## qôbel

_(ko'-bel | koh-BEL | koh-VEL)_

## Definition

from H6901 in the sense of confronting (as standing opposite in order to receive); a battering-ram; war; n-m

- something in front, attacking engine, something before
  - before
  - siege-engine, battering-ram
