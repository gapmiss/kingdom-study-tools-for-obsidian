# H6686 צוּעָר / צוער

## Tsûwʻâr

_(tsoo-awr' | tsoo-AWR | tsoo-AR)_

## Definition

littleness; from H6819; small; Tsuar, an Israelite; Zuar; n-pr-m

- father of Nethaneel, the chief of the tribe of Issachar at the time of the exodus and an assistant of Moses
