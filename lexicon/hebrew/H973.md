# H973 בָּחַל / בחל

## bâchal

_(baw-khal' | baw-HAHL | ba-HAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to loath; abhor, get hastily (from the margin for H0926); v

- to loathe, abhor, feel loathing
  - (Qal)
    - to loathe
    - to gain by greed, gain hastily
  - (Pual) obtained by greed
