# H7436 רָמָתַיִם צוֹפִים / רמתים צופים

## Râmâthayim Tsôwphîym

_(raw-maw-thah'-yim tso-feem' | raw-maw-ta-YIM tsoh-PEEM | ra-ma-ta-YEEM tsoh-FEEM)_

## Definition

double height of the watchers; from the dual of H7413 and the plural of the active participle of H6822; double height of watchers; Ramathajim-Tsophim, a place in Palestine; Ramathaimzophim; n-pr-loc

- the home place of Samuel located in the hill country of Ephraim
  - the same as H7414
