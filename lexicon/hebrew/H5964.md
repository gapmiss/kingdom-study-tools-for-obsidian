# H5964 עָלֶמֶת / עלמת

## ʻÂlemeth

_(aw-leh'-meth | aw-leh-MET | ah-leh-MET)_

## Definition

covering; from H5956; a covering; Alemeth, the name of a place in Palestine and of two Israelites; Alameth, Alemeth; n-pr-m

- (n pr m) a Benjamite, son of Jehoadah or Jerah and descended from Saul through Jonathan
- a town in Benjamin
