# H6182 עֲרִיסָה / עריסה

## ʻărîyçâh

_(ar-ee-saw' | uh-ree-SAW | uh-ree-SA)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to comminute; meal; dough; n-f

- dough, meal, coarse meal, kneading trough
  - meaning dubious
