# H5803 עַזְגָּד / עזגד

## ʻAzgâd

_(az-gawd' | adz-ɡAWD | az-ɡAHD)_

## Definition

Gad is mighty; from H5794 and H1409; stern troop; Azgad, an Israelite; Azgad; n-pr-m

- ancestor of a family of laymen who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
- a man who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
