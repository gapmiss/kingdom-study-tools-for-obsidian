# H4171 מוּר / מור

## mûwr

_(moor | moor | moor)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to alter; by implication, to barter, to dispose of; × at all, (ex-)change, remove; v

- to change, exchange
  - (Niphal) to be changed
  - (Hiphil)
    - to change, alter
    - to exchange
