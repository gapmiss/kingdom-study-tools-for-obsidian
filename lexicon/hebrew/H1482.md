# H1482 גּוּר / גור

## gûwr

_(goor | ɡoor | ɡoor)_

## Definition

or (shortened) גֻּר; perhaps from H1481; a cub (as still abiding in the lair), especially of the lion; whelp, young one; n-m

- cub, whelp, young
