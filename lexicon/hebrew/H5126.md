# H5126 נוּן / נון

## Nûwn

_(noon | noon | noon)_

## Definition

fish, posterity; or נוֹן; (1 Chronicles 7:27), from H5125; perpetuity; Nun or Non, the father of Joshua; Non, Nun; n-pr-m

- father of Joshua the successor of Moses
