# H6996 קָטָן / קטן

## qâṭân

_(kaw-tawn' | kaw-TAWN | ka-TAHN)_

## Definition

or קָטֹן; from H6962; abbreviated, i.e., diminutive, literally (in quantity, size or number) or figuratively (in age or importance); least, less(-er), little (one), small(-est) (one, quantity, thing), young(-er, -est); a

- young, small, insignificant, unimportant
  - small
  - insignificant
  - young
  - unimportant
