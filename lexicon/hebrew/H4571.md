# H4571 מָעַד / מעד

## mâʻad

_(maw-ad' | maw-AD | ma-AD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to waver; make to shake, slide, slip; v

- to slip, slide, totter, shake
  - (Qal) to slip
  - (Hiphil) to cause or make to shake or totter
