# H5440 סָבַךְ / סבך

## çâbak

_(saw-bak' | saw-BAHK | sa-VAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to entwine; fold together, wrap; v

- to interweave
  - (Qal)
    - to interweave
    - interwoven (participle)
  - (Pual) to be interwoven
