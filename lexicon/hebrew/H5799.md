# H5799 עֲזָאזֵל / עזאזל

## ʻăzâʼzêl

_(az-aw-zale' | uh-dzaw-DZALE | uh-za-ZALE)_

## Definition

from H5795 and H0235; goat of departure; the scapegoat; scapegoat; n-m

- entire removal, scapegoat
  - refers to the goat used for sacrifice for the sins of the people
  - meaning dubious
