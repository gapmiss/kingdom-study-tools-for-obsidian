# H5160 נַחֲלִיאֵל / נחליאל

## Nachălîyʼêl

_(nakh-al-ee-ale' | na-huh-lee-ALE | na-huh-lee-ALE)_

## Definition

torrents of God; from H5158 and H0410; valley of God; Nachaliel, a place in the Desert; Nahaliel; n-pr-loc

- a station of Israel in the wilderness; located north of the Arnon
