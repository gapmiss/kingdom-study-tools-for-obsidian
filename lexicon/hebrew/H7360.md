# H7360 רָחָם / רחם

## râchâm

_(raw-khawm' | raw-HAWM | ra-HAHM)_

## Definition

or (feminine) רָחָמָה; from H7355; a kind of vulture (supposed to be tender towards its young); gier-eagle; n-m

- carrion vulture
  - perhaps an extinct bird, exact meaning unknown
