# H7490 רְעַע / רעע

## rᵉʻaʻ

_(reh-ah' | reh-AH | reh-AH)_

## Definition

corresponding to H7489; properly, to spoil (literally, by breaking to pieces); figuratively, to make (or be) good for nothing, i.e., bad (physically, socially or morally); break, bruise; v

- (P'al) to crush, break, shatter
