# H385 אִיתָמָר / איתמר

## ʼÎythâmâr

_(eeth-aw-mawr' | ee-taw-MAWR | ee-ta-MAHR)_

## Definition

coast of palms; from H0339 and H8558; coast of the palm-tree; Ithamar, a son of Aaron; Ithamar; n-pr-m

- fourth and youngest son of Aaron
