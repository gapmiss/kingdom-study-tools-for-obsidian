# H7042 קְלִיטָא / קליטא

## Qᵉlîyṭâʼ

_(kel-ee-taw' | keh-lee-TAW | keh-lee-TA)_

## Definition

crippled; from H7038; maiming; Kelita, the name of three Israelites; Kelita; n-pr-m

- a Levite who had a foreign wife in the time of Ezra
  - also 'Kelaiah'
