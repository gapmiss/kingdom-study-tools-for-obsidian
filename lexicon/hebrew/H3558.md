# H3558 כּוּמָז / כומז

## kûwmâz

_(koo-mawz' | koo-MAWDZ | koo-MAHZ)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to store away; a jewel (probably gold beads); tablet; n-m

- ornaments, golden ornament
  - maybe—armlets of gold
- tablet
