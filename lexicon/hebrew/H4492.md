# H4492 מִנְהָרָה / מנהרה

## minhârâh

_(min-haw-raw' | min-haw-RAW | meen-ha-RA)_

## Definition

from H5102; properly, a channel or fissure, i.e., (by implication) a cavern; den; n-f

- crevices, ravines, mountain clefts, den, dugout holes
  - meaning dubious
