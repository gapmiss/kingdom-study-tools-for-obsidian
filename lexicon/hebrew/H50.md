# H50 אֲבִישׁוּעַ / אבישוע

## ʼĂbîyshûwaʻ

_(ab-ee-shoo'-ah | uh-bee-SHOO-ah | uh-vee-SHOO-ah)_

## Definition

my father is rescue (safety), is opulence; from H0001 and H7771; father of plenty (i.e., prosperous); Abishua, the name of two Israelites; Abishua; n-pr-m

- son of Phinehas, grandson of Aaron
