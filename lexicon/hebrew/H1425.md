# H1425 גָּדִי / גדי

## Gâdîy

_(gaw-dee' | ɡaw-DEE | ɡa-DEE)_

## Definition

an invader, a troop, fortune; patronymically from H1410; a Gadite (collectively) or descendants of Gad; Gadites, children of Gad; a

- one of the tribe descended from Gad
