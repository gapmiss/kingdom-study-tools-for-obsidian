# H7191 קִשְׁיוֹן / קשיון

## Qishyôwn

_(kish-yone' | kish-YONE | keesh-YONE)_

## Definition

hard; from H7190; hard ground; Kishjon, a place in Palestine; Kishion, Keshon; n-pr-loc

- a town on the boundary of Issachar allotted to the Gershonite Levites
