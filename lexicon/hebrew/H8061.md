# H8061 שְׁמִידָע / שמידע

## Shᵉmîydâʻ

_(shem-ee-daw' | sheh-mee-DAW | sheh-mee-DA)_

## Definition

wise; apparently from H8034 and H3045; name of knowing; Shemida, an Israelite; Shemida, Shemidah; n-pr-m

- a son of Gilead, grandson of Manasseh, and progenitor of a family in Manasseh
