# H6657 צְדָד / צדד

## Tsᵉdâd

_(tsed-awd' | tseh-DAWD | tseh-DAHD)_

## Definition

mountain side; from the same as H6654; a siding; Tsedad, a place near Palestine; Zedad; n-pr-loc

- one of the landmarks on the northern border of Israel as promised by Moses and restated by Ezekiel

## Verse(s)

[[Numbers 34#8|Numbers 34:8]], [[Ezekiel 47#15|Ezekiel 47:15]]
