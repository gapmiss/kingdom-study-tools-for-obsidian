# H7480 רְעֵלָיָה / רעליה

## Rᵉʻêlâyâh

_(reh-ay-law-yaw' | reh-ay-law-YAW | reh-ay-la-YA)_

## Definition

bearer of Jehovah; from H7477 and H3050; made to tremble (i.e., fearful) of Jah; Reelajah, an Israelite; Reeliah; n-pr-m

- one who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
  - also 'Raamiah'
