# H6237 עָשַׂר / עשר

## ʻâsar

_(aw-sar' | aw-SAHR | ah-SAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root (identical with H6238); to accumulate; but used only as denominative from H6235; to tithe, i.e., to take or give a tenth; × surely, give (take) the tenth, (have, take) tithe(-ing, -s), × truly; v

- to tithe, take the tenth part of, give a tithe, take a tithe
  - (Qal) to tithe
  - (Piel) to give a tithe
  - (Hiphil) to take a tithe
