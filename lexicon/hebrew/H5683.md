## ʻEbrôn

_(eb-rone' | eb-RONE | ev-RONE)_

## Definition

alliance; from H5676; transitional; Ebron, a place in Palestine; Hebron; n-pr-loc

- a city of Judah located in the mountains 20 (32 km) miles south of Jerusalem and 20 (32 km) miles north of Beersheba
