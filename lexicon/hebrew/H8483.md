# H8483 תַּחְתִּים חׇדְשִׁי / תחתים חדשי

## Tachtîym Chodshîy

_(takh-teem' khod-shee' | ta-TEEM hode-SHEE | tahk-TEEM hode-SHEE)_

## Definition

lowest-moon; apparently from the plural masculine of H8482 or H8478 and H2320; lower (ones) monthly; Tachtim-Chodshi, a place in Palestine; Tahtim-hodshi; n-pr-loc

- a place near Gilead on the east of the Jordan
