## Mâshâl

_(maw-shawl' | maw-SHAWL | ma-SHAHL)_

## Definition

entreaty; for H4861; Mashal, a place in Palestine; Mashal; n-pr-loc

- a town in the territory of Asher allotted to the Gershonite Levites
  - also 'Mishal' and 'Misheal'
