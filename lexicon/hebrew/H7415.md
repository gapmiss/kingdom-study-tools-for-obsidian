# H7415 רִמָּה / רמה

## rimmâh

_(rim-maw' | rim-MAW | ree-MA)_

## Definition

from H7426 in the sense of breeding (compare H7311); a maggot (as rapidly bred), literally or figuratively; worm; n-f

- maggot, worm (as cause and sign of decay)
