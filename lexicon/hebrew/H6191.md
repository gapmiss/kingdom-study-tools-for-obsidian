# H6191 עָרַם / ערם

## ʻâram

_(aw-ram' | aw-RAHM | ah-RAHM)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to be (or make) bare; but used only in the derivative sense (through the idea perhaps of smoothness) to be cunning (usually in a bad sense); × very, beware, take crafty (counsel), be prudent, deal subtilly; v

- to be subtle, be shrewd, be crafty, beware, take crafty counsel, be prudent
  - (Qal) to be crafty, be subtle
  - (Hiphil) to be crafty, be or become shrewd
