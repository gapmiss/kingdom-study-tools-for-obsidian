# H6536 פָּרַס / פרס

## pâraç

_(paw-ras' | paw-RAHS | pa-RAHS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to break in pieces, i.e., (usually without violence) to split, distribute; deal, divide, have hoofs, part, tear; v

- to divide, break in two
  - (Qal) to break, break up
  - (Hiphil) to be divided (of hoofs)
