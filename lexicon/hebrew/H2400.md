# H2400 חַטָּא / חטא

## chaṭṭâʼ

_(khat-taw' | haht-TAW | ha-TA)_

## Definition

intensively from H2398; a criminal, or one accounted guilty; offender, sinful, sinner; n-m

- (n m) sinners
- (adj) sinful
- exposed to condemnation, reckoned as offenders
