# H4979 מַתָּנָה / מתנה

## mattânâh

_(mat-taw-naw' | maht-taw-NAW | ma-ta-NA)_

## Definition

feminine of H4976; a present; specifically (in a good sense), a sacrificial offering, (in a bad sense) a bribe; gift; n-f

- gift
