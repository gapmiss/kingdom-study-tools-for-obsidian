# H637 אַף / אף

## ʼaph

_(af | ap | af)_

## Definition

a primitive particle; meaning accession (used as an adverb or conjunction); also or yea; adversatively though; also, + although, and (furthermore, yet), but, even, + how much less (more, rather than), moreover, with, yea; prt

- (conj) (denoting addition, especially of something greater) also, yea, though, so much the more
- (adv) furthermore, indeed
