# H8181 שֵׂעָר / שער

## sêʻâr

_(say-awr' | say-AWR | say-AR)_

## Definition

or שַׂעַר; (Isaiah 7:20), from H8175 in the sense of dishevelling; hair (as if tossed or bristling); hair(-y), × rough; n-m

- hair
  - hair (of animals, man)
  - hair (of garment made of hair)
