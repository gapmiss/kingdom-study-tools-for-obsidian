# H8429 תְּוַהּ / תוה

## tᵉvahh

_(tev-ah' | teh-WA | teh-VA)_

## Definition

corresponding to H8539 or perhaps to H7582 through the idea of sweeping to ruin (compare H8428); to amaze, i.e., (reflexive by implication) take alarm; be astonied; v

- (P'al) to be startled, be alarmed
