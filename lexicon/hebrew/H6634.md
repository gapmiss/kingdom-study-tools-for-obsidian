# H6634 צְבָא / צבא

## tsᵉbâʼ

_(tseb-aw' | tseh-BAW | tseh-VA)_

## Definition

corresponding to H6623 in the figurative sense of summoning one's wishes; to please; will, would; v

- to desire, be inclined, be willing, be pleased
  - (P'al)
    - to desire
    - to be pleased
    - to will (without hindrance) (of God)
