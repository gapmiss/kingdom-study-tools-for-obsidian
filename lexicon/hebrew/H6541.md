# H6541 פַּרְסָה / פרסה

## parçâh

_(par-saw' | pahr-SAW | pahr-SA)_

## Definition

feminine of H6538; a claw or split hoof; claw, (cloven-)footed, hoof; n-f

- hoof
  - of ruminants
  - of horses (undivided hooves)
