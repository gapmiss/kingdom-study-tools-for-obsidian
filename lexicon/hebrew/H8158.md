# H8158 שָׁסַף / שסף

## shâçaph

_(shaw-saf' | shaw-SAHP | sha-SAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to cut in pieces, i.e., slaughter; hew in pieces; v

- (Piel) to hew in pieces, cut in pieces
  - meaning dubious
