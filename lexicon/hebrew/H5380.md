# H5380 נָשַׁב / נשב

## nâshab

_(naw-shab' | naw-SHAHB | na-SHAHV)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to blow; by implication, to disperse; (cause to) blow, drive away; v

- to blow
  - (Qal) to blow
  - (Hiphil)
    - to cause to blow
    - to disperse, to drive away
