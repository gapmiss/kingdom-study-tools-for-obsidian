# H7552 רֶקֶם / רקם

## Reqem

_(reh'-kem | reh-KEM | reh-KEM)_

## Definition

variegation; from H7551; versi-color; Rekem, the name of a place in Palestine, also of a Midianite and an Israelite; Rekem; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) one of the 5 kings of Midian killed by the Israelites
- one of the 4 sons of Hebron and the father of Shammai
- a Manassite, son of Machir by Maachah his wife
- (n pr loc) a town in Benjamin; site unknown
