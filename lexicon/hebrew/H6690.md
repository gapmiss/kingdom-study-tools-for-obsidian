# H6690 צוֹפַח / צופח

## Tsôwphach

_(tso-fakh' | tsoh-PA | tsoh-FAHK)_

## Definition

a cruse; from an unused root meaning to expand, breadth; Tsophach, an Israelite; Zophah; n-pr-m

- an Asherite, son of Helem and father of Suah, Harnepher, Shual, Beri, and Imrah
