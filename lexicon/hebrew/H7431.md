# H7431 רֶמֶשׂ / רמש

## remes

_(reh'-mes | reh-MES | reh-MES)_

## Definition

from H7430; a reptile or any other rapidly moving animal; that creepeth, creeping (moving) thing; n-m

- creeping things, moving things, creeping organism
  - creeping things
  - gliding things (of sea animals)
  - moving things (of all animals)
