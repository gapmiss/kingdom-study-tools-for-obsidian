# H8426 תּוֹדָה / תודה

## tôwdâh

_(to-daw' | toh-DAW | toh-DA)_

## Definition

from H3034; properly, an extension of the hand, i.e., (by implication) avowal, or (usually) adoration; specifically, a choir of worshippers; confession, (sacrifice of) praise, thanks(-giving) (offering); n-f

- confession, praise, thanksgiving
  - give praise to God
  - thanksgiving in songs of liturgical worship, hymn of praise
  - thanksgiving choir or procession or line or company
  - thank-offering, sacrifice of thanksgiving
  - confession
