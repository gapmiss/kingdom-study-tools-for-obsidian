# H8585 תְּעָלָה / תעלה

## tᵉʻâlâh

_(teh-aw-law' | teh-aw-LAW | teh-ah-LA)_

## Definition

from H5927; a bandage or plaster (as placed upon a wound); conduit, cured, healing, little river, trench, watercourse; n-f

- conduit, water course, trench
- healing (of new flesh and skin forming over wound)
  - a plaister (to put on a wound), bandage
