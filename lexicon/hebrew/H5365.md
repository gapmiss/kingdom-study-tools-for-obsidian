# H5365 נָקַר / נקר

## nâqar

_(naw-kar' | naw-KAHR | na-KAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to bore (penetrate, quarry); dig, pick out, pierce, put (thrust) out; v

- to bore, pick, dig, pick out
  - (Qal) to bore, pick, dig
  - (Piel) to bore out
  - (Pual) to be dug out
