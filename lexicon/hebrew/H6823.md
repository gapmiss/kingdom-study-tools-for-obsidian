# H6823 צָפָה / צפה

## tsâphâh

_(tsaw-faw' | tsaw-PAW | tsa-FA)_

## Definition

a primitive root (probably identical with H6822 through the idea of expansion in outlook, transferring to action); to sheet over (especially with metal); cover, overlay; v

- to lay out, lay over, overlay, cover
  - (Piel) to overlay, plate, stud
  - (Pual) to be laid over
