# H8392 תֵּבָה / תבה

## têbâh

_(tay-baw' | tay-BAW | tay-VA)_

## Definition

perhaps of foreign derivation; a box; ark; n-f

- ark
  - vessel which Noah built
  - basket vessel in which Moses was placed
