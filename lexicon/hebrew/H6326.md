# H6326 פּוּעָה / פועה

## Pûwʻâh

_(poo-aw' | poo-AW | poo-AH)_

## Definition

splendid; from an unused root meaning to glitter; brilliancy; Puah, an Israelitess; Puah; n-pr-f

- one of the 2 midwives of the Hebrews ordered by Pharaoh to kill all the male children born to Israel; time of Moses
