# H8498 תְּכוּנָה / תכונה

## tᵉkûwnâh

_(tek-oo-naw' | teh-koo-NAW | teh-hoo-NA)_

## Definition

feminine passive participle of H8505; adjustment, i.e., structure; by implication, equipage; fashion, store; n-f

- arrangement, preparation, fixed place
  - arrangement, disposition
  - preparation
  - fixed place
