# H3213 יָלַל / ילל

## yâlal

_(yaw-lal' | yaw-LAHL | ya-LAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to howl (with a wailing tone) or yell (with a boisterous one); (make to) howl, be howling; v

- (Hiphil) to howl, wail, make a howling
