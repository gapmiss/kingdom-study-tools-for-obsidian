# H8207 שְׁפִיפֹן / שפיפן

## shᵉphîyphôn

_(shef-ee-fone' | sheh-pee-PONE | sheh-fee-FONE)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning the same as H7779; a kind of serpent (as snapping), probably the cerastes or horned adder; adder; n-m

- horned snake
- adder (marked with black and white spots)
