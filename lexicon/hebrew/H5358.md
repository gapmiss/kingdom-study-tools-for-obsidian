# H5358 נָקַם / נקם

## nâqam

_(naw-kam' | naw-KAHM | na-KAHM)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to grudge, i.e., avenge or punish; avenge(-r) (self), punish, revenge (self), × surely, take vengeance; v

- to avenge, take vengeance, revenge, avenge oneself, be avenged, be punished
  - (Qal)
    - to avenge, take vengeance
    - to entertain revengeful feelings
  - (Niphal)
    - to avenge oneself
    - to suffer vengeance
  - (Piel) to avenge
  - (Hophal) to be avenged, vengeance be taken (for blood)
  - (Hithpael) to avenge oneself
