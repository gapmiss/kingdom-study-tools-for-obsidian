# H3955 לְשַׁד / לשד

## lᵉshad

_(lesh-ad' | leh-SHAHD | leh-SHAHD)_

## Definition

from an unused root of uncertain meaning; apparently juice, i.e; (figuratively) vigor; also a sweet or fat cake; fresh, moisture; n-m

- juice, juicy bit, dainty bit
