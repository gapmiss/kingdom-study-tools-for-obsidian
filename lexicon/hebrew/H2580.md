# H2580 חֵן / חן

## chên

_(khane | hane | hane)_

## Definition

from H2603; graciousness, i.e., subjective (kindness, favor) or objective (beauty); favour, grace(-ious), pleasant, precious, (well-)favoured; n-m

- favor, grace, charm
  - favor, grace, elegance
  - favor, acceptance
