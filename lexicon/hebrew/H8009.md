# H8009 שַׂלְמָה / שלמה

## Salmâh

_(sal-maw' | sahl-MAW | sahl-MA)_

## Definition

garment; the same as H8008; clothing; Salmah, an Israelite; Salmon; n-pr-m

- father of Boaz, father-in-law of Ruth, and grandfather of David
