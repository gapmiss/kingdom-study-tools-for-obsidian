# H3229 יִמְלָא / ימלא

## Yimlâʼ

_(yeem-law' | yim-LAW | yeem-LA)_

## Definition

whom God will fill up; or יִמְלָה; from H4390; full; Jimla or Jimlah, an Israelite; Imla, Imlah; n-pr-m

- father of Michaiah the prophet
