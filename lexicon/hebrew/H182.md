# H182 אוֹדוֹת / אודות

## ʼôwdôwth

_(o-doth' | oh-DOTE | oh-DOTE)_

## Definition

or (shortened) אֹדוֹת (only thus in the plural); from the same as H0181; turnings (i.e., occasions); (adverb) on account of; (be-)cause, concerning, sake; n-f

- cause
  - cause, reason for
  - the occasion of
