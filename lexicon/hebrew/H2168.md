# H2168 זָמַר / זמר

## zâmar

_(zaw-mar' | dzaw-MAHR | za-MAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root (compare H2167, H5568, H6785); to trim (a vine); prune; v

- to trim, prune
  - (Qal) to trim, prune
  - (Niphal) to be pruned
