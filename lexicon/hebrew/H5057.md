# H5057 נָגִיד / נגיד

## nâgîyd

_(naw-gheed' | naw-ɡEED | na-ɡEED)_

## Definition

or נָגִד; from H5046; a commander (as occupying the front), civil, military or religious; generally (abstractly, plural), honorable themes; captain, chief, excellent thing, (chief) governor, leader, noble, prince, (chief) ruler; n-m

- leader, ruler, captain, prince
  - ruler, prince
  - prince-overseer
  - ruler (in other capacities)
  - princely things
