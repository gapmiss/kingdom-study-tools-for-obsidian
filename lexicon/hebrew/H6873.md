# H6873 צָרַח / צרח

## tsârach

_(tsaw-rakh' | tsaw-RA | tsa-RAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be clear (in tone, i.e., shrill), i.e., to whoop; cry, roar; v

- to cry, roar, make a shrill or clear sound
  - (Qal) to roar
  - (Hiphil) to utter a roar, cry out (a battle cry)
