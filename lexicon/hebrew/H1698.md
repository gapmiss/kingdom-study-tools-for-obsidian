# H1698 דֶּבֶר / דבר

## deber

_(deh'-ber | deh-BER | deh-VER)_

## Definition

from H1696 (in the sense of destroying); a pestilence; murrain, pestilence, plague; n-m

- pestilence, plague
- murrain, cattle disease, cattle-plague
