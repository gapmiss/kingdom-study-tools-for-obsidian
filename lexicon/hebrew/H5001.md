# H5001 נָאַם / נאם

## nâʼam

_(naw-am' | naw-AM | na-AM)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to whisper, i.e., (by implication) to utter as an oracle; say; v

- to prophesy, utter a prophecy, speak as prophet, say
  - (Qal) to utter a prophecy, speak as prophet
