# H656 אָפֵס / אפס

## ʼâphêç

_(aw-face' | aw-PASE | ah-FASE)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to disappear, i.e., cease; be clean gone (at an end, brought to nought), fail; v

- (Qal) to cease, break, come to an end
