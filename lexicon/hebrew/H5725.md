# H5725 עֲדֻלָּם / עדלם

## ʻĂdullâm

_(ad-ool-lawm' | uh-dool-LAWM | uh-doo-LAHM)_

## Definition

justice of the people; probably from the passive participle of the same as H5724; Adullam, a place in Palestine; Adullam; n-pr-loc

- a town of the Canaanites allotted to Judah and lying in the lowlands; site of the cave where David hid
