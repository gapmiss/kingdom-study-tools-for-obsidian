# H6285 פֵּאָה / פאה

## pêʼâh

_(pay-aw' | pay-AW | pay-AH)_

## Definition

feminine of H6311; properly, mouth in a figurative sense, i.e., direction, region, extremity; corner, end, quarter, side; n-f

- corner, edge, side, quarter, extremity
  - corner
  - side
