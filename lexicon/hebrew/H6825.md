# H6825 צְפוֹ / צפו

## Tsᵉphôw

_(tsef-o' | tseh-POH | tseh-FOH)_

## Definition

watch-tower; or צְפִי; from H6822; observant; Tsepho or Tsephi, an Idumaean; Zephi, Zepho; n-pr-m

- son of Eliphaz and grandson of Esau and one of the chiefs of the Edomites
