# H3618 כַּלָּה / כלה

## kallâh

_(kal-law' | kahl-LAW | ka-LA)_

## Definition

from H3634; a bride (as if perfect); hence, a son's wife; bride, daughter-in-law, spouse; n-f

- bride, daughter-in-law
  - daughter-in-law
  - bride, young wife
