# H8492 תִּירוֹשׁ / תירוש

## tîyrôwsh

_(tee-roshe' | tee-ROHSH | tee-ROHSH)_

## Definition

or תִּירֹשׁ; from H3423 in the sense of expulsion; must or fresh grape-juice (as just squeezed out); by implication (rarely) fermented wine; (new, sweet) wine; n-m

- wine, fresh or new wine, must, freshly pressed wine
