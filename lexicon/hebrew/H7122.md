# H7122 קָרָא / קרא

## qârâʼ

_(kaw-raw' | kaw-RAW | ka-RA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to encounter, whether accidentally or in a hostile manner; befall, (by) chance, (cause to) come (upon), fall out, happen, meet; v

- to encounter, befall, meet
  - (Qal)
    - to meet, encounter
    - to befall (figuratively)
  - (Niphal) to meet, meet unexpectedly
  - (Hiphil) to cause to meet
