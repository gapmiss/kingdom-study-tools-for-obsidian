# H7806 שָׁזַר / שזר

## shâzar

_(shaw-zar' | shaw-DZAHR | sha-ZAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to twist (a thread of straw); twine; v

- to twist, be twisted
  - (Hophal)
    - to be twisted
    - twisted (participle)
