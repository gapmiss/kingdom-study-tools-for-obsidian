# H6935 קַדְמֹנִי / קדמני

## Qadmônîy

_(kad-mo-nee' | kahd-moh-NEE | kahd-moh-NEE)_

## Definition

easterners; the same as H6931; ancient, i.e., indigenous; Kadmonite (collectively), the name of a tribe in Palestine; Kadmonites; a

- a people who occupied the land of Canaan when God promised it to Abram
