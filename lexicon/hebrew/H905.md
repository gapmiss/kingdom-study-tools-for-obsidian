# H905 בַּד / בד

## bad

_(bad | bahd | bahd)_

## Definition

from H0909; properly, separation; by implication, a part of the body, branch of a tree, bar for carrying; figuratively, chief of a city; especially (with prepositional prefix) as an adverb, apart, only, besides; alone, apart, bar, besides, branch, by self, of each alike, except, only, part, staff, strength; n-m

- alone, by itself, besides, a part, separation, being alone
  - separation, alone, by itself
    - only (adv)
    - apart from, besides (prep)
  - part
  - parts (eg limbs, shoots), bars
