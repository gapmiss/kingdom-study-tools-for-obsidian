# H5780 עוּץ / עוץ

## ʻÛwts

_(oots | oots | oots)_

## Definition

wooded; apparently from H5779; consultation; Uts, a son of Aram, also a Seirite, and the regions settled by them; Uz; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) son of Aram and grandson of Seth
- son of Nahor by Milcah
- an Edomite, son of Dishan and grandson of Seir
- (n pr loc) the country of Job; probably east and southeast of Palestine somewhere in the Arabian desert
