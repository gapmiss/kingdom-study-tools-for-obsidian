# H6805 צָעַד / צעד

## tsâʻad

_(tsaw-ad' | tsaw-AD | tsa-AD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to pace, i.e., step regularly; (upward) to mount; (along) to march; (down and causatively) to hurl; bring, go, march (through), run over; v

- to step, march, stride
  - (Qal) to step, march
  - (Hiphil) to cause to march
