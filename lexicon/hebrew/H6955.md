# H6955 קְהָת / קהת

## Qᵉhâth

_(keh-hawth' | keh-HAWT | keh-HAHT)_

## Definition

assembly; from an unused root meaning to ally oneself; allied; Kehath, an Israelite; Kohath; n-pr-m

- the 2nd of the 3 sons of Levi and progenitor of a family in the tribe of Levi
