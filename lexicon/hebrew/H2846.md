# H2846 חָתָה / חתה

## châthâh

_(khaw-thaw' | haw-TAW | ha-TA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to lay hold of; especially to pick up fire; heap, take (away); v

- (Qal) to take hold of, seize, take away, pile up, snatch up (coals)
