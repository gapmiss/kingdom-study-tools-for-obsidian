# H6937 קָדַר / קדר

## qâdar

_(kaw-dar' | kaw-DAHR | ka-DAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be ashy, i.e., dark-colored; by implication, to mourn (in sackcloth or sordid garments); be black(-ish), be (make) dark(-en), × heavily, (cause to) mourn; v

- to mourn, be dark
  - (Qal) to be dark
  - (Hiphil)
    - to darken
    - to cause to mourn
  - (Hithpael) to grow dark
