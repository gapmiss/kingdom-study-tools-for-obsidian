# H8547 תֶּמַח / תמח

## Temach

_(teh'-makh | teh-MA | teh-MAHK)_

## Definition

laughter; of uncertain derivation; Temach, one of the Nethinim; Tamah, Thamah; n-pr-m

- a head of a family of temple slaves who returned from exile with Zerubbabel

[[Ezra 2#53|Ezra 2:53]], [[Nehemiah 7#55|Nehemiah 7:55]]
