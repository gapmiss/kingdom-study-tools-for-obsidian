# H7140 קֶרַח / קרח

## qerach

_(keh'-rakh | keh-RA | keh-RAHK)_

## Definition

or קֹרַח; from H7139; ice (as if bald, i.e., smooth); hence, hail; by resemblance, rock crystal; crystal, frost, ice; n-m

- frost, ice, ice crystal
  - frost (of night)
  - ice
