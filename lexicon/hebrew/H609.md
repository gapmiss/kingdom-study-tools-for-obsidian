# H609 אָסָא / אסא

## ʼÂçâʼ

_(aw-saw' | aw-SAW | ah-SA)_

## Definition

healer, injurious(?); of uncertain derivation; Asa, the name of a king and of a Levite; Asa; n-pr-m

- king of Judah, son of Abijam, father of Jehoshaphat
- a Levite
