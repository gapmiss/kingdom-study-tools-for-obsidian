# H6149 עָרֵב / ערב

## ʻârêb

_(aw-rabe' | aw-RABE | ah-RAVE)_

## Definition

a primitive root (rather identical with H6148 through the idea of close association); to be agreeable; be pleasant(-ing), take pleasure in, be sweet; v

- (Qal) to be pleasant, be sweet, be pleasing
  - sweet, pleasant
