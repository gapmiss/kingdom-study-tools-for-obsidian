# H2366 חוּשִׁים / חושים

## Chûwshîym

_(khoo-sheem' | hoo-SHEEM | hoo-SHEEM)_

## Definition

who makes haste; or חֻשִׁים; or חֻשִׁם; plural from H2363; hasters; Chushim, the name of three Israelites; Hushim; n-pr-f n-pr-m

- (n pr f) one of the two wives of Shaharaim
- (n pr m) the sons of Aher, a Benjamite
- descendants of Dan
