# H85 אַבְרָהָם / אברהם

## ʼAbrâhâm

_(ab-raw-hawm' | ab-raw-HAWM | av-ra-HAHM)_

## Definition

father of a multitude, chief of multitude; contracted from H0001 and an unused root (probably meaning to be populous); father of a multitude; Abraham, the later name of Abram; Abraham; n-pr-m

- friend of God and founder of Hebrew nation via God's elective covenant
