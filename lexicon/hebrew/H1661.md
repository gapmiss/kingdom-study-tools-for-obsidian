# H1661 גַּת / גת

## Gath

_(gath | ɡaht | ɡaht)_

## Definition

winepress; the same as H1660; Gath, a Philistine city; Gath; n-pr-loc

- one of the five royal or chief cities of the Philistines and the native city of Goliath
