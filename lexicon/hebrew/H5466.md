# H5466 סָדִין / סדין

## çâdîyn

_(saw-deen' | saw-DEEN | sa-DEEN)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to envelop; a wrapper, i.e., shirt; fine linen, sheet; n-m

- linen wrapper
  - rectangular piece of fine linen worn as outer, or at night, as a sole garment
