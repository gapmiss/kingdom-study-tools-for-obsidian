# H58 אָבֵל / אבל

## ʼâbêl

_(aw-bale' | aw-BALE | ah-VALE)_

## Definition

from an unused root (meaning to be grassy); a meadow; plain; n-f

- meadow (from verb—to grow green, to withstand)

## Note

Compare also the proper names beginning with Abel-.
