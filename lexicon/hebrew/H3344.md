# H3344 יָקַד / יקד

## yâqad

_(yaw-kad' | yaw-KAHD | ya-KAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to burn; (be) burn(-ing), × from the hearth, kindle; v

- to burn, kindle, be kindled
  - (Qal) to be kindled
  - (Hophal) to burn, be burning,
