# H653 אֲפֵלָה / אפלה

## ʼăphêlâh

_(af-ay-law' | uh-pay-LAW | uh-fay-LA)_

## Definition

feminine of H0651; duskiness, figuratively, misfortune; concrete, concealment; dark, darkness, gloominess, × thick; n-f

- darkness, gloominess, calamity
- wickedness (figuratively)
