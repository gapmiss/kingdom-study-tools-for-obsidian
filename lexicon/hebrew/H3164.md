## Yachdîyʼêl

_(yakh-dee-ale' | ya-dee-ALE | yahk-dee-ALE)_

## Definition

my unity is God; from H3162 and H0410; unity of God; Jachdiel, an Israelite; Jahdiel; n-pr-m
