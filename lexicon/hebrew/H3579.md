# H3579 כֹּזְבִי / כזבי

## Kôzᵉbîy

_(koz-bee' | koh-dzeh-BEE | koh-zeh-VEE)_

## Definition

my lie; from H3576; false; Cozbi, a Midianitess; Cozbi; n-pr-f

- a daughter of Zur, a chief of the Midianites, who was slain by Phinehas because of her adultery
