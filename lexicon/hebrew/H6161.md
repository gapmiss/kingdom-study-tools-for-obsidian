# H6161 עֲרֻבָּה / ערבה

## ʻărubbâh

_(ar-oob-baw' | uh-roob-BAW | uh-roo-BA)_

## Definition

feminine passive participle of H6048 in the sense of a bargain or exchange; something given as security, i.e., (literally) a token (of safety) or (metaphorically) a bondsman; pledge, surety; n-f

- pledge, token, bond, surety, thing exchanged
