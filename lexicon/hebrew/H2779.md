# H2779 חֹרֶף / חרף

## chôreph

_(kho'-ref | hoh-REP | hoh-REF)_

## Definition

from H2778; properly, the crop gathered, i.e., (by implication) the autumn (and winter) season; figuratively, ripeness of age; cold, winter(-house), youth; n-m

- harvest time, autumn
- winter
