# H4083 מְדִינָה / מדינה

## mᵉdîynâh

_(med-ee-naw' | meh-dee-NAW | meh-dee-NA)_

## Definition

corresponding to H4082; properly, a judgeship, i.e., jurisdiction; by implication, a district (as ruled by a judge); generally, a region; province; n-f

- district, province
