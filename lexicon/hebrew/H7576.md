# H7576 רָתַק / רתק

## râthaq

_(raw-thak' | raw-TAHK | ra-TAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to fasten; bind; v

- to bind
  - (Niphal) to be snapped, be broken
  - (Pual) to be bound
