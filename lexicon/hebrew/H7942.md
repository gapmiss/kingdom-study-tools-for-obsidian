# H7942 שִׁכְּרוֹן / שכרון

## Shikkᵉrôwn

_(shik-ker-one' | shik-keh-RONE | shee-keh-RONE)_

## Definition

drunkenness; for H7943; drunkenness, Shikkeron, a place in Palestine; Shicron; n-pr-loc

- a landmark on the northwestern boundary of Judah lying between Ekron and Jabneel
