# H5400 נָשַׂק / נשק

## nâsaq

_(naw-sak' | naw-SAHK | na-SAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to catch fire; burn, kindle; v

- to kindle, burn
  - (Niphal) to be kindled
  - (Hiphil) to make a fire, burn
