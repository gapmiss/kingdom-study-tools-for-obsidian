# H4928 מִשְׁמַעַת / משמעת

## mishmaʻath

_(mish-mah'-ath | mish-ma-AT | meesh-ma-AT)_

## Definition

feminine of H4926; audience, i.e., the royal court; also obedience, i.e., (concretely) a subject; bidding, guard, obey; n-f

- subjects, body of subjects, bodyguard, listeners, obedient ones
  - bodyguard
  - subjects
