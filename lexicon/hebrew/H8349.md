# H8349 שָׁשַׁק / ששק

## Shâshaq

_(shaw-shak' | shaw-SHAHK | sha-SHAHK)_

## Definition

longing; probably from the base of H7785; pedestrian; Shashak, an Israelite; Shashak; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite, son of Beriah and father of Iphedeiah and Penuel
