# H8366 שָׁתַן / שתן

## shâthan

_(shaw-than' | shaw-TAHN | sha-TAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; (causatively) to make water, i.e., urinate; piss; v

- (Hiphil) to urinate
  - one who urinates (used as a designation of a male)
