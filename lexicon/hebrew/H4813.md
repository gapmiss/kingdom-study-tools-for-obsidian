# H4813 מִרְיָם / מרים

## Miryâm

_(meer-yawm' | mir-YAWM | meer-YAHM)_

## Definition

rebellion; from H4805; rebelliously; Mirjam, the name of two Israelitesses; Miriam; n-pr-f

- elder sister of Moses and Aaron
- a woman of Judah
