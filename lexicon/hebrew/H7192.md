# H7192 קְשִׂיטָה / קשיטה

## qᵉsîyṭâh

_(kes-ee-taw' | keh-see-TAW | keh-see-TA)_

## Definition

from an unused root (probably meaning to weigh out); an ingot (as definitely estimated and stamped for a coin); piece of money (silver); n-f

- a unit of unknown value
  - perhaps weight, money
