# H5115 נָוָה / נוה

## nâvâh

_(naw-vaw' | naw-WAW | na-VA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; (compare H5116); to rest (as at home); causatively (through the implied idea of beauty), to celebrate (with praises); keep at home, prepare an habitation; v

- to beautify
  - (Hiphil) to beautify, adorn
- to dwell
  - (Qal) to dwell, abide, keep at home
- (Hophal) rest
