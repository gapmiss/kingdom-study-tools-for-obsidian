# H3834 לָבִיבָה / לביבה

## lâbîybâh

_(law-bee-baw' | law-bee-BAW | la-vee-VA)_

## Definition

or rather לְבִבָה; from H3823 in its original sense of fatness (or perhaps of folding); a cake (either as fried or turned); cake; n-f

- cakes, bread
