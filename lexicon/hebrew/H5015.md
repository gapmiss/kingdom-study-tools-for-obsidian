# H5015 נְבוֹ / נבו

## Nᵉbôw

_(neb-o' | neh-BOH | neh-VOH)_

## Definition

prophet; probably of foreign derivation; Nebo, the name of a Babylonian deity; Nebo; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a Babylonian deity who presided over learning and letters; corresponds to Greek Hermes, Latin Mercury, and Egyptian Thoth
- (n pr loc) a city in Moab and at one time assigned to Reuben; probably located on or near Mount Nebo
- a city in Judah (maybe Benjamin) from which the families of some exiles, who returned from Babylon with Zerubbabel, originally came
- the mountain where Moses died; located east of the Jordan opposite Jericho; site uncertain
