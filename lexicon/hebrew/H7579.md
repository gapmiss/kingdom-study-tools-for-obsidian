# H7579 שָׁאַב / שאב

## shâʼab

_(sahw-ab' | shaw-AB | sha-AV)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to bale up water; (woman to) draw(-er) (water); v

- to draw (water)
  - (Qal)
    - to draw (water)
    - water-drawing women (participle)
