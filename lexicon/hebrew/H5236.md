# H5236 נֵכָר / נכר

## nêkâr

_(nay-kawr' | nay-KAWR | nay-HAHR)_

## Definition

' from H5234; foreign, or (concretely) a foreigner, or (abstractly) heathendom; alien, strange, + stranger; n-m

- foreign, alien, foreignness, that which is foreign
  - foreignness, foreign gods
  - alien, foreigner
  - foreign (vanities)
