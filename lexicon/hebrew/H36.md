# H36 אֲבִיטוּב / אביטוב

## ʼĂbîyṭûwb

_(ab-ee-toob' | uh-bee-TOOB | uh-vee-TOOV)_

## Definition

my father is good(-ness); from H0001 and H2898; father of goodness (i.e., good); Abitub, an Israelite; Abitub; n-pr-m

- son of Shaharaim, a Benjamite
