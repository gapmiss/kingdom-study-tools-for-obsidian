# H825 אַשָּׁף / אשף

## ʼashshâph

_(ash-shawf' | ash-SHAWP | ah-SHAHF)_

## Definition

from an unused root (probably meaning to lisp, i.e., practice enchantment); a conjurer; astrologer; n-m

- necromancer, conjurer, enchanter, magician
- astrologer
