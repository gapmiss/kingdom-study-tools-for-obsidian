# H5408 נָתַח / נתח

## nâthach

_(naw-thakh' | naw-TA | na-TAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to dismember; cut (in pieces), divide, hew in pieces; v

- to cut, cut up, cut in pieces, divide
  - (Piel) to cut up, cut in pieces, divide by joints
