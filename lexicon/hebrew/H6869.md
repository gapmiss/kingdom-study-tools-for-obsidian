# H6869 צָרָה / צרה

## tsârâh

_(tsaw-raw' | tsaw-RAW | tsa-RA)_

## Definition

feminine of H6862; transitively, a female rival; adversary, adversity, affliction, anguish, distress, tribulation, trouble; n-f

- straits, distress, trouble
- vexer, rival wife
