# H5747 עוֹג / עוג

## ʻÔwg

_(ogue | oɡe | oɡe)_

## Definition

long-necked; probably from H5746; round; Og, a king of Bashan; Og; n-pr-m

- the Amorite king of Bashan and one of the last representatives of the giant race of Rephaim
