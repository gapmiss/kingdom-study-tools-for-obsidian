# H3322 יָצַג / יצג

## yâtsag

_(yaw-tsag' | yaw-TSAHɡ | ya-TSAHɡ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to place permanently; establish, leave, make, present, put, set, stay; v

- to place, set, establish
  - (Hiphil)
    - to set, place
    - to present
    - to exhibit
  - (Hophal) to be stopped, be stayed, be detained
