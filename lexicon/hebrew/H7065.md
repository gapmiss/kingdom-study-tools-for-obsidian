# H7065 קָנָא / קנא

## qânâʼ

_(kaw-naw' | kaw-NAW | ka-NA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be (causatively, make) zealous, i.e., (in a bad sense) jealous or envious; (be) envy(-ious), be (move to, provoke to) jealous(-y), × very, (be) zeal(-ous); v

- to envy, be jealous, be envious, be zealous
  - (Piel)
    - to be jealous of
    - to be envious of
    - to be zealous for
    - to excite to jealous anger
  - (Hiphil) to provoke to jealous anger, cause jealousy
