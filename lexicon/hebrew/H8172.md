# H8172 שָׁעַן / שען

## shâʻan

_(shaw-an' | shaw-AN | sha-AN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to support one's self; lean, lie, rely, rest (on, self), stay; v

- to lean on, trust in, support
  - (Niphal) to lean, lean upon, support oneself
    - of trust in God (figuratively)
