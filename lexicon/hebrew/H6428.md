# H6428 פָּלַשׁ / פלש

## pâlash

_(paw-lash' | paw-LAHSH | pa-LAHSH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to roll (in dust); roll (wallow) self; v

- to roll
  - (Hithpael) to roll in ashes or dust (as an act of mourning)
