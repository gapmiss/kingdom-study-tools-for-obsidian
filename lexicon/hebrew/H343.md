# H343 אֵיד / איד

## ʼêyd

_(ade | ade | ade)_

## Definition

from the same as H0181 (in the sense of bending down); oppression; by implication misfortune, ruin; calamity, destruction; n-m

- distress, burden, calamity
  - burden (of the righteous)
  - calamity (of nation)
  - disaster (of wicked)
  - day of calamity
