# H198 אוּלָם / אולם

## ʼÛwlâm

_(oo-lawm' | oo-LAWM | oo-LAHM)_

## Definition

their leader, vestibule; appar, from H0481 (in the sense of dumbness); solitary; Ulam, the name of two Israelites; Ulam; n-pr-m

- a Manassite
- a Benjamite of Saul's family
