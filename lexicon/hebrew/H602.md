# H602 אָנַק / אנק

## ʼânaq

_(aw-nak' | aw-NAHK | ah-NAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to shriek; cry, groan; v

- to cry, groan
  - (Qal) to cry, lament
  - (Niphal) to make lamentation
