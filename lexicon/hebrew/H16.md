# H16 אֵבֶה / אבה

## ʼêbeh

_(ay-beh' | ay-BEH | ay-VEH)_

## Definition

from H0014 (in the sense of bending toward); the papyrus; swift; n-m

- reed, papyrus

## Note

Probably ships made of reeds or papyrus were the clipper sailing ships of those days because of their speed.
