# H2835 חָשִׂף / חשף

## châsiph

_(khaw-seef' | haw-SIP | ha-SEEF)_

## Definition

from H2834; properly, drawn off, i.e., separated; hence, a small company (as divided from the rest); little flock; n-m

- small flocks, little flocks
