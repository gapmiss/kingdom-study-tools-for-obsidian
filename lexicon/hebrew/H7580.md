# H7580 שָׁאַג / שאג

## shâʼag

_(shaw-ag' | shaw-Aɡ | sha-Aɡ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to rumble or moan; × mightily, roar; v

- (Qal) to roar
  - of lion, conqueror, Jehovah, cry of distress
