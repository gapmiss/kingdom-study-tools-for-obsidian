# H2737 חָרוּז / חרוז

## chârûwz

_(khaw-rooz' | haw-ROODZ | ha-ROOZ)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to perforate; properly, pierced, i.e., a bead of pearl, gems or jewels (as strung); chain; n-m

- a beaded necklace, string of beads
