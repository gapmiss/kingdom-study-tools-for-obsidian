# H361 אֵילָם / אילם

## ʼêylâm

_(ay-lawm' | ay-LAWM | ay-LAHM)_

## Definition

or (shortened) אֵלָם ; or (feminine) אֵלַמָּה ; probably from H0352; a pillar-space (or colonnade), i.e., a pale (or portico); arch; n-m

- porch, vestibule, portico
