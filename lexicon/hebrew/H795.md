# H795 אַשְׁדּוֹד / אשדוד

## ʼAshdôwd

_(ash-dode' | ash-DODE | ash-DODE)_

## Definition

powerful; from H7703; ravager; Ashdod, a place in Palestine; Ahdod; n-pr-loc

- a major Philistine city on the Mediterranean Sea west from Jerusalem, modern Esdud
