# H4453 מֶלְצָר / מלצר

## meltsâr

_(mel-tsawr' | mel-TSAWR | mel-TSAHR)_

## Definition

of Persian derivation; the butler or other officer in the Babylonian court; Melzar; n-m

- guardian, an officer of the court
  - meaning dubious
