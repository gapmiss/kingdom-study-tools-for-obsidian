# H59 אָבֵל / אבל

## ʼÂbêl

_(aw-bale' | aw-BALE | ah-VALE)_

## Definition

from H0058; a meadow; Abel, the name of two places in Palestine; Abel; n-pr-loc

- city in northern Israel near Bethmaachah
- the place where the ark rested in the field of Joshua at Bethshemesh
