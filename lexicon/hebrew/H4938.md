# H4938 מִשְׁעֵנָה / משענה

_(mish-ay-naw' | mish-ay-NAW | meesh-ay-NA)_

## Definition

or מִשְׁעֶנֶת; feminine of H4937; support (abstractly), i.e., (figuratively) sustenance or (concretely) a walking-stick; staff; n-f
