# H6249 עַשְׁתֵּי / עשתי

## ʻashtêy

_(ash-tay' | ash-TAY | ash-TAY)_

## Definition

apparently masculine plural construction of H6247 in the sense of an afterthought (used only in connection with H6240 in lieu of H0259); eleven or (ordinal) eleventh; eleven(-th); n

- one, eleven, eleventh
  - one as combined with ten (H6240)
