# H5905 עִיר שֶׁמֶשׁ / עיר שמש

## ʻÎyr Shemesh

_(eer sheh'-mesh | eer sheh-MESH | eer sheh-MESH)_

## Definition

city of the sun-god; from H5892 and H8121; city of the sun; Ir-Shemesh, a place in Palestine; Irshemesh; n-pr-loc

- a city in Dan; probably the same as 'Beth-shemesh'
