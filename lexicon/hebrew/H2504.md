# H2504 חָלָץ / חלץ

## châlâts

_(khaw-lawts' | haw-LAWTS | ha-LAHTS)_

## Definition

from H2502 (in the sense of strength); only in the dual; the loins (as the seat of vigor); loins, reins; n-f

- loins
  - as seat of virility
  - as girded
  - as seat of pain (woman's travail)
