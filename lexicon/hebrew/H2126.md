# H2126 זִינָא / זינא

## Zîynâʼ

_(zee-naw' | dzee-NAW | zee-NA)_

## Definition

well-fed; from H2109; well-fed; or perhaps an orthographical error for H2124; Zina, an Israelite; Zina; n-pr-m

- a Gershonite Levite, second son of Shimei; also called 'Zizah'
