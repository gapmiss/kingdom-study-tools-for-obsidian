# H5984 עַמּוֹנִי / עמוני

## ʻAmmôwnîy

_(am-mo-nee' | am-moh-NEE | ah-moh-NEE)_

## Definition

tribal; patronymically from H5983; an Ammonite or (the adjective) Ammonitish; Ammonite(-s); a

- descendants of Ammon and inhabitants of Ammon
