# H1325 בַּת / בת

## bath

_(bath | baht | baht)_

## Definition

corresponding to H1324; a bath or Hebrew measure (as a means of division) of liquids; bath; n-m

- bath, a unit of liquid measure, 6 gallons or 22.7 liters, equal to dry measure ephah
