# H7090 קִפּוֹד / קפוד

## qippôwd

_(kip-pode' | kip-PODE | kee-PODE)_

## Definition

or קִפֹּד; from H7088; a species of bird, perhaps the bittern (from its contracted form); bittern; n-m

- porcupine, hedgehog
  - a shrinking animal
