# H260 אָחוּ / אחו

## ʼâchûw

_(aw'-khoo | aw-HOO | ah-HOO)_

## Definition

of uncertain (perhaps Egyptian) derivation; a bulrush or any marshy grass (particularly that along the Nile); flag, meadow; n-m

- reeds, marsh plants, rushes
