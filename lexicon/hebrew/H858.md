# H858 אָתָה / אתה

## ʼâthâh

_(aw-thaw' | aw-TAW | ah-TA)_

## Definition

or אָתָא; (Aramaic), corresponding to H0857; to come, to bring; (be-)come, bring; v

- to come, arrive
  - (P'al) to come
  - (Aphel) to bring
  - (Hophal) to be brought

## Note

This word is used in the Greek New Testament in the word "maranatha" (meaning "Lord come").
