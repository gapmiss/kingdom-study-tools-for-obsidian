# H4173 מוֹרַג / מורג

## môwrag

_(mo-rag' | moh-RAHɡ | moh-RAHɡ)_

## Definition

or מֹרַג; from an unused root meaning to triturate; a threshing sledge; threshing instrument; n-m

- thresher, threshing-sledge
