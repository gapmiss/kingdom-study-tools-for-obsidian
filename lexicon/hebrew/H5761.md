# H5761 עַוִּים / עוים

## ʻAvvîym

_(av-veem' | ah-WEEM | ah-WEEM)_

## Definition

ruins; plural of H5757; Avvim (as inhabited by Avvites), a place in Palestine (with the article prefix); Avim; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a people among the early inhabitants of Palestine located in the southwest corner of the seacoast
- (n pr loc) a city in Benjamin
