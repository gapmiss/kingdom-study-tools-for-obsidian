# H7402 רָכַל / רכל

## râkal

_(raw-kal' | raw-KAHL | ra-HAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to travel for trading; (spice) merchant; v

- to go about (meaning dubious)
  - (Qal) trafficker, trader (participle) (subst)
