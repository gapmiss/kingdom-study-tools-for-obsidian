# H2932 טֻמְאָה / טמאה

## ṭumʼâh

_(toom-aw' | toom-AW | toom-AH)_

## Definition

from H2930; religious impurity; filthiness, unclean(-ness); n-f

- uncleanness
  - sexual
  - of filthy mass
  - ethical and religious
  - ritual
  - local (of nations)
