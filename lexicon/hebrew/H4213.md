# H4213 מִזְעָר / מזער

## mizʻâr

_(miz-awr' | midz-AWR | meez-AR)_

## Definition

from the same as H2191; fewness; by implication, as superlative diminutiveness; few, × very; n-m

- a little, a trifle, a few
  - a trifle, a little (of time)
  - a remnant, a very few (of number)
