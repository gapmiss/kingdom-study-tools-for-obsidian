# H6282 עָתָר / עתר

## ʻâthâr

_(aw-thawr' | aw-TAWR | ah-TAHR)_

## Definition

from H6280; also compare H6279; incense (as increasing to a volume of smoke); hence a worshipper; suppliant, thick; n-m

- suppliant, worshipper
- odor, incense (odoriferous smoke)
