# H244 אׇזְנִי / אזני

## ʼOznîy

_(oz-nee' | ohdz-NEE | oze-NEE)_

## Definition

my ear [hearing]; from H0241; having (quick) ears; Ozni, an Israelite; also an Oznite (collectively), his descendant; Ozni; n-pr-m

- son of Gad
- pertaining to Ozni and his descendants
