# H297 אֲחִירָם / אחירם

## ʼĂchîyrâm

_(akh-ee-rawm' | uh-hee-RAWM | uh-hee-RAHM)_

## Definition

my brother is exalted, brother, [the] lofty; from H0251 and H7311; brother of height (i.e., high); Achiram, an Israelite; Ahiram; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite, son of Benjamin
