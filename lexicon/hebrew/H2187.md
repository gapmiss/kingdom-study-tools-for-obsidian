# H2187 זָנַק / זנק

## zânaq

_(zaw-nak' | dzaw-NAHK | za-NAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to draw together the feet (as an animal about to dart upon its prey), i.e., to spring forward; leap; v

- (Piel) to leap, spring
