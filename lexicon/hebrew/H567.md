# H567 אֱמֹרִי / אמרי

## ʼĔmôrîy

_(em-o-ree' | eh-moh-REE | ay-moh-REE)_

## Definition

a sayer; probably a patronymic from an unused name derived from H0559 in the sense of publicity, i.e., prominence; thus, a mountaineer; an Emorite, one of the Canaanitish tribes; Amorite; n-m

- one of the peoples of east Canaan and beyond the Jordan, dispossessed by the Israelite incursion from Egypt
