# H5949 עֲלִילָה / עלילה

## ʻălîylâh

_(al-ee-law' | uh-lee-LAW | uh-lee-LA)_

## Definition

or עֲלִלָה; from H5953 in the sense of effecting; an exploit (of God), or a performance (of man, often in a bad sense); by implication, an opportunity; act(-ion), deed, doing, invention, occasion, work; n-f

- wantonness, deed, doing
  - wantonness
  - deed
  - practices, evil deeds
