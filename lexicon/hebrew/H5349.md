# H5349 נֹקֵד / נקד

## nôqêd

_(no-kade' | noh-KADE | noh-KADE)_

## Definition

active participle from the same as H5348; a spotter (of sheep or cattle), i.e., the owner or tender (who thus marks them); herdman, sheepmaster; n-m

- sheep-raiser, sheep-dealer, sheep-tender
