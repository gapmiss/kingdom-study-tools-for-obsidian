# H7759 שׁוּלַמִּית / שולמית

## Shûwlammîyth

_(shoo-lam-meeth' | shoo-lahm-MEET | shoo-la-MEET)_

## Definition

the perfect, the peaceful; from H7999; peaceful (with the article always prefixed, making it a pet name); the Shulammith, an epithet of Solomon's queen; Shulamite; a

- the heroine lover of the Song of Songs (of Solomon)
