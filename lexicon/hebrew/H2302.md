# H2302 חָדָה / חדה

## châdâh

_(khaw-daw' | haw-DAW | ha-DA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to rejoice; make glad, be joined, rejoice; v

- to rejoice
  - (Qal) to rejoice
  - (Piel) to make glad, gladden
