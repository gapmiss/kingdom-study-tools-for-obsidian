# H923 בַּהַט / בהט

## bahaṭ

_(bah'-hat | ba-HAHT | ba-HAHT)_

## Definition

from an unused root (probably meaning to glisten); white marble or perhaps alabaster; red (marble); n-m

- a costly stone (perhaps porphyry), red marble
