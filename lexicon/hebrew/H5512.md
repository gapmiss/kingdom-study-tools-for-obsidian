# H5512 סִין / סין

## Çîyn

_(seen | seen | seen)_

## Definition

thorn, clay; of uncertain derivation; Sin the name of an Egyptian town and (probably) desert adjoining; Sin; n-pr-loc

- a town in eastern Egypt
- the tract of wilderness between Elim and Sinai
