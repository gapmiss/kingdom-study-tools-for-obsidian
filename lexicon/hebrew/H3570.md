# H3570 כּוּשִׁי / כושי

## Kûwshîy

_(koo-shee' | koo-SHEE | koo-SHEE)_

## Definition

their blackness; the same as H3569; Cushi, the name of two Israelites; Cushi; n-pr-m

- an ancestor of Jehudi (Jeremiah 36:14)
- the father of the prophet Zephaniah (Zephaniah 1:1)
