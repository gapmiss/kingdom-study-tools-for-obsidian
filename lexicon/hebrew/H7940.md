# H7940 שָׂכָר / שכר

## Sâkâr

_(saw-kar' | saw-KAWR | sa-HAHR)_

## Definition

wages; the same as H7939; recompense; Sakar, the name of two Israelites; Sacar; n-pr-m

- father of Ahiam, one of David's mighty warriors
- a Korahite Levite gatekeeper, the 4th son of Obed-edom in the time of David
