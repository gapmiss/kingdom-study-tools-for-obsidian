## Tsᵉmârayim

_(tsem-aw-rah'-yim | tseh-maw-ra-YIM | tseh-ma-ra-YEEM)_

## Definition

double fleece of wool; dual of H6785; double fleece; Tsemarajim, a place in Palestine; Zemaraim; n-pr-loc

- a city in northern Benjamin near Bethel
- a hill in Ephraim
