# H5285 נַעֲצוּץ / נעצוץ

## naʻătsûwts

_(nah-ats-oots' | na-uh-TSOOTS | na-uh-TSOOTS)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to prick; probably a brier; by implication, a thicket of thorny bushes; thorn; n-m

- thornbush
