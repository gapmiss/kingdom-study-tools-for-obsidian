# H73 אַבְנֵט / אבנט

## ʼabnêṭ

_(ab-nate' | ab-NATE | av-NATE)_

## Definition

of uncertain derivation; a belt; girdle; n-m

- girdle, sash
  - of high priest
  - of other priests
  - of high official
