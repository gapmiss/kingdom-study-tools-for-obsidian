# H6637 צֹבֵבָה / צבבה

## Tsôbêbâh

_(tso-bay-baw' | tsoh-bay-BAW | tsoh-vay-VA)_

## Definition

the slow; feminine active participle of the same as H6632; the canopier (with the article); Tsobebah, an Israelitess; Zobebah; n-pr-m

- son of Coz of the tribe of Judah
