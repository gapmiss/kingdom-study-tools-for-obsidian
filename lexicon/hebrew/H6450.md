# H6450 פַּס דַּמִּים / פס דמים

## Paç Dammîym

_(pas dam-meem' | pahs dahm-MEEM | pahs da-MEEM)_

## Definition

boundary of blood; from H6446 and the plural of H1818; palm (i.e., dell) of bloodshed; Pas-Dammim, a place in Palestine; Pas-dammim; n-pr-loc

- a place in western Judah

Compare H0658.
