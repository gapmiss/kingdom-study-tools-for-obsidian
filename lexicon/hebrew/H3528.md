# H3528 כְּבָר / כבר

## kᵉbâr

_(keb-awr' | keh-BAWR | keh-VAHR)_

## Definition

from H3527; properly, extent of time, i.e., a great while; hence, long ago, formerly, hitherto; already, (seeing that which), now; adv

- already, long ago, a great while
