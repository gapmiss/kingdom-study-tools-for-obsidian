# H5875 עֵין הַקּוֹרֵא / עין הקורא

## ʻÊyn haq-Qôwrêʼ

_(ane-hak-ko-ray' | ane hahk-koh-RAY | ane ha-koh-RAY)_

## Definition

spring of One calling; from H5869 and the active participle of H7121; fountain of One calling; En-hak-Kore, a place near Palestine; En-hakhore; n-pr-loc

- the spring which the Lord brought forth in response to Samson's complaint of being thirsty after he had just killed 1000 men with the jawbone of an ass
