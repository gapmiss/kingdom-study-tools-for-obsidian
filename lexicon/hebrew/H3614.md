_(kaw-lib-bo' | kaw-lib-BOH | ka-lee-BOH)_

## Definition

dog; probably by erroneous transcription for כָּלֵבִי; patronymically from H3612; a Calebite or descendant of Caleb; of the house of Caleb; a
