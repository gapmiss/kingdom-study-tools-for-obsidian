# H588 אֲנָחֲרָת / אנחרת

## ʼĂnâchărâth

_(an-aw-kha-rawth' | uh-naw-huh-RAWT | uh-na-huh-RAHT)_

## Definition

the groaning of fear; probably from the same root as H5170; a gorge or narrow pass; Anacharath, a place in Palestine; Anaharath; n-pr-loc

- city in Issachar
