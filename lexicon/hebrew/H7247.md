# H7247 רִבְלָה / רבלה

## Riblâh

_(rib-law' | rib-LAW | reev-LA)_

## Definition

fertility; from an unused root meaning to be fruitful; fertile; Riblah, a place in Syria; Riblah; n-pr-loc

- a place on the eastern boundary of Israel
- a town in the land of Hamath on the great road between Babylon and Palestine
