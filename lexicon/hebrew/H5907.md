# H5907 עַכְבּוֹר / עכבור

## ʻAkbôwr

_(ak-bore' | ak-BORE | ak-BORE)_

## Definition

mouse; probably for H5909; Akbor, the name of an Idumaean and of two Israelites; Achbor; n-pr-m

- father of king Baal-hanan of Edom
- son of Michaiah and contemporary of king Josiah of Judah
