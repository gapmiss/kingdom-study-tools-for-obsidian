# H8144 שָׁנִי / שני

## shânîy

_(shaw-nee' | shaw-NEE | sha-NEE)_

## Definition

of uncertain derivation; crimson, properly, the insect or its color, also stuff dyed with it; crimson, scarlet (thread); n-m

- scarlet, crimson
  - properly, the insect 'coccus ilicis', the dried body of the female yielding coloring matter from which is made the dye used for cloth to color it scarlet or crimson
