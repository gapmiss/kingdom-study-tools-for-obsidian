# H5910 עַכּוֹ / עכו

## ʻAkkôw

_(ak-ko' | ak-KOH | ah-KOH)_

## Definition

his straitness; apparently from an unused root meaning to hem in; Akko (from its situation on a bay); Accho; n-pr-loc

- a seaport town in Asher north of Mount Carmel and south or Tyre
