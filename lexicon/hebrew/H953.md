# H953 בּוֹר / בור

## bôwr

_(bore | bore | bore)_

## Definition

from H0952 (in the sense of H0877); a pit hole (especially one used as a cistern or a prison); cistern, dungeon, fountain, pit, well; n-m

- pit, well, cistern
