# H5594 סָפַד / ספד

## çâphad

_(saw-fad' | saw-PAHD | sa-FAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to tear the hair and beat the breasts (as Middle Easterners do in grief); generally to lament; by implication, to wail; lament, mourn(-er), wail; v

- to wail, lament, mourn
  - (Qal)
    - to wail, lament
    - wailers (participle)
  - (Niphal) to be lamented, be bewailed
