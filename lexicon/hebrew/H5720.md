# H5720 עָדִין / עדין

## ʻÂdîyn

_(aw-deen' | aw-DEEN | ah-DEEN)_

## Definition

dainty, delicate; the same as H5719; Adin, the name of two Israelites; Adin; n-pr-m

- ancestor of a family of exiles who returned with Zerubbabel
- a chief among the returned exiles who signed the covenant with Nehemiah
