# H7977 שִׁלְחִי / שלחי

## Shilchîy

_(shil-khee' | shil-HEE | sheel-HEE)_

## Definition

armed; from H7973; missive, i.e., armed; Shilchi, an Israelite; Shilhi; n-pr-m

- father of Azubah, the mother of Jehoshaphat
