# H6535 פַּרְנַךְ / פרנך

## Parnak

_(par-nak' | pahr-NAHK | pahr-NAHK)_

## Definition

delicate; of uncertain derivation; Parnak, an Israelite; Parnach; n-pr-m

- father of Elizaphan, prince of the tribe of Zebulun chosen to help apportion the promised land between the tribes
