# H4971 מַתְכֹנֶת / מתכנת

## mathkôneth

_(math-ko'-neth | maht-koh-NET | maht-hoh-NET)_

## Definition

or מַתְכֻנֶת; from H8505 in the transferred sense of measuring; proportion (in size, number or ingredients); composition, measure, state, tale; n-f

- measurement, proportion, tale
  - tale, tally (of bricks)
  - measurement, proportion
