# H893 בֵּבַי / בבי

## Bêbay

_(bay-bah'ee | bay-BAI | bay-VAI)_

## Definition

my cavities; probably of foreign origin; Bebai, an Israelite; Bebai; n-pr-m

- a leader of the returning exiles
