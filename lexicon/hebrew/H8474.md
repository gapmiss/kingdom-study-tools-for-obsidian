# H8474 תַּחָרָה / תחרה

## tachârâh

_(takh-aw-raw' | ta-haw-RAW | ta-ha-RA)_

## Definition

a facitious root from H2734 through the idea of the heat of jealousy; to vie with a rival; close, contend; v

- to burn, be kindled (of anger)
  - (Tiphel) to heat oneself in vexation
