# H8080 שָׁמַן / שמן

## shâman

_(shaw-man' | shaw-MAHN | sha-MAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to shine, i.e., (by analogy) be (causatively, make) oily or gross; become (make, wax) fat; v

- to be or become fat, grow fat
  - (Qal) fat (subst)
  - (Hiphil) to make fat, show fatness
