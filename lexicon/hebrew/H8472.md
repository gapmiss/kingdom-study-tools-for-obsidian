# H8472 תַּחְפְּנֵיס / תחפניס

## Tachpᵉnêyç

_(takh-pen-ace' | ta-peh-NASE | tahk-peh-NASE)_

## Definition

wife of the king; of Egyptian derivation; Tachpenes, an Egyptian woman; Tahpenes; n-pr-f

- an Egyptian queen in the times of David and Solomon
