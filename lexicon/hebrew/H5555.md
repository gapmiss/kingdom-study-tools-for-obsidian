# H5555 סֶלַע הַמַּחְלְקוֹת / סלע המחלקות

## Çelaʻ ham-machlᵉqôwth

_(seh'-lah hammakh-lek-oth' | seh-LA hahm-ma-leh-KOTE | seh-LA ha-mahk-leh-KOTE)_

## Definition

the cliff of escapes, the cliff of divisions; from H5553 and the plural of H4256 with the article interposed; rock of the divisions; Sela-ham-Machlekoth, a place in Palestine; Sela-hammalekoth; n-pr-loc

- a rock or cliff in the wilderness of Maon, southeast of Hebron, the scene of an escape of David from Saul
