# H7133 קׇרְבָּן / קרבן

## qorbân

_(kor-bawn' | kore-BAWN | kore-BAHN)_

## Definition

or קֻרְבָּן; from H7126; something brought near the altar, i.e., a sacrificial present; oblation, that is offered, offering; n-m

- offering, oblation
