# H4735 מִקְנֶה / מקנה

## miqneh

_(mik-neh' | mik-NEH | meek-NEH)_

## Definition

from H7069; something bought, i.e., property, but only live stock; abstractly, acquisition; cattle, flock, herd, possession, purchase, substance; n-m

- cattle, livestock
  - cattle, livestock
    - in general of a purchasable domestic animal
  - cows, sheep, goats (in herds and flocks)
