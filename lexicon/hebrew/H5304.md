# H5304 נְפִיסִים / נפיסים

## Nᵉphîyçîym

_(nef-ee-seem' | neh-pee-SEEM | neh-fee-SEEM)_

## Definition

scattered spices; plural from an unused root meaning to scatter; expansions; Nephisim, a Temple-servant; Nephusim (from the margin); n-pr

- a family of exiles who returned with Zerubbabel
