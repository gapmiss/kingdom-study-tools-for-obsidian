# H175 אַהֲרוֹן / אהרון

## ʼAhărôwn

_(a-har-one' | ah-huh-RONE | ah-huh-RONE)_

## Definition

light bringer; of uncertain derivation; Aharon, the brother of Moses; Aaron; n-pr-m

- brother of Moses, a Levite and the first high priest
