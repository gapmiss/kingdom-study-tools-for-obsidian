# H2630 חָסַן / חסן

## châçan

_(khaw-san' | haw-SAHN | ha-SAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to (be) compact; by implication, to hoard; lay up; v

- (Niphal) to be treasured up, be hoarded, be laid up, be stored
