# H7651 שֶׁבַע / שבע

## shebaʻ

_(sheh'-bah | sheh-BA | sheh-VA)_

## Definition

or (masculine) (שִׁבְעָה); from H7650; a primitive cardinal number; seven (as the sacred full one); also (adverbially) seven times; by implication, a week; by extension, an indefinite number; (+ by) seven(-fold),-s, (-teen, -teenth), -th, times); n

- seven (cardinal number)
  - as ordinal number
  - in combination—17, 700, etc.

## Note

Compare H7658.
