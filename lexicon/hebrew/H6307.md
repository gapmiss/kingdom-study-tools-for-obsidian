# H6307 פַּדָּן / פדן

## Paddân

_(pad-dawn' | pahd-DAWN | pa-DAHN)_

## Definition

field; from an unused root meaning to extend; a plateau; or פַּדַּן אֲרָם; from the same and H0758; the table-land of Aram; Paddan or Paddan-Aram, a region of Syria; Padan, Padan-aram; n-pr-loc

- a plain or tableland in northern Mesopotamia in Aram, a region of Syria
