# H728 אֲרַוְנָה / ארונה

## ʼĂravnâh

_(ar-av-naw' | uh-ra-NAW | uh-rahv-NA)_

## Definition

I shall shout for joy, make ye to shine, joyful shouting of Jah (Jehovah); or (by transposition) אוֹרְנָה; or אַרְנִיָּה; all by orthographical variation for H0771; Aravnah (or Arnijah or Ornah), a Jebusite; Araunah; n-pr-m

- a Jebusite who sold David the site for an altar
