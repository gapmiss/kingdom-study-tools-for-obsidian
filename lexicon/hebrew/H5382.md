# H5382 נָשָׁה / נשה

## nâshâh

_(naw-shaw' | naw-SHAW | na-SHA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to forget; figuratively, to neglect; causatively, to remit, remove; forget, deprive, exact; v

- to forget, deprive
  - (Qal) to forget
  - (Niphal) to be forgotten
  - (Piel) to cause to forget
  - (Hiphil) to cause to forget, allow to be forgotten
