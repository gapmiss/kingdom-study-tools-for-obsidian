# H2111 זוּעַ / זוע

## zûwaʻ

_(zoo'-ah | DZOO-ah | ZOO-ah)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to shake off, i.e., (figuratively) to agitate (as with fear); move, tremble, vex; v

- to tremble, quiver, quake, be in terror
  - (Qal) to tremble, quake
  - (Pilpel)
    - to shake violently
    - to cause to tremble
