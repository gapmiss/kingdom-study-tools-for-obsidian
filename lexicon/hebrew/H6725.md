# H6725 צִיּוּן / ציון

## tsîyûwn

_(tsee-yoon' | tsih-YOON | tsee-YOON)_

## Definition

from the same as H6723 in the sense of conspicuousness (compare H5329); a monumental or guiding pillar; sign, title, waymark; n-m

- signpost, monument, market
