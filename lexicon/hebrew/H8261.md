# H8261 שָׁקֻף / שקף

## shâquph

_(shaw-koof' | shaw-KOOP | sha-KOOF)_

## Definition

passive participle of H8259; (compare H8260); an embrasure or opening with bevelled jam; light, window; n-m

- frame, casing (of windows), beams laid over, window frames
