# H338 אִי / אי

## ʼîy

_(ee | ee | ee)_

## Definition

probably identical with H0337 (through the idea of a doleful sound); a howler (used only in the plural), i.e., any solitary wild creature; wild beast of the islands; n-m

- howling beast, jackal
