# H6753 צְלֶלְפּוֹנִי / צללפוני

## Tsᵉlelpôwnîy

_(tsel-el-po-nee' | tseh-lel-poh-NEE | tseh-lel-poh-NEE)_

## Definition

facing the shade; from H6752 and the active participle of H6437; shade-facing; Tselelponi, an Israelitess; Hazelelponi (including the article); n-pr-f

- sister of the sons of Etam in the genealogies of Judah
