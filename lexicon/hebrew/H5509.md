# H5509 סִיג / סיג

## çîyg

_(seeg | seeɡ | seeɡ)_

## Definition

or סוּג; (Ezekiel 22:18), from H5472 in the sense of refuse; scoria; dross; n-m

- a moving back or away
- dross (usually of silver)
