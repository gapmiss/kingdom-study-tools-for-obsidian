# H1468 גּוּז / גוז

## gûwz

_(gooz | ɡoodz | ɡooz)_

## Definition

a primitive root (compare H1494); properly, to shear off; but used only in the (figuratively) sense of passing rapidly; bring, cut off; v

- to pass over, pass away
  - (Qal) to pass away (of life)
- to bring, cut off
