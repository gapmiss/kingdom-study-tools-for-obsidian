# H5525 סֻכִּי / סכי

## Çukkîy

_(sook-kee' | sook-KEE | soo-KEE)_

## Definition

booth dwellers; patrial from an unknown name (perhaps H5520); a Sukkite, or inhabitant of some place near Egypt (i.e., hut-dwellers); Sukkiims; n-pr-m

- a people who supplied warriors for the Egyptian army under Shishak
