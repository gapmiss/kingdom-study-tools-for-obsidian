# H492 אַלְמֹנִי / אלמני

## ʼalmônîy

_(al-mo-nee' | al-moh-NEE | al-moh-NEE)_

## Definition

from H0489 in the sense of concealment; some one (i.e., so and so, without giving the name of the person or place); one, and such; a

- someone, a certain one
