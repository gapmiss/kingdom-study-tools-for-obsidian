# H8412 תַּדְמֹר / תדמר

## Tadmôr

_(tad-more' | tahd-MORE | tahd-MORE)_

## Definition

palm tree; or תַּמֹּר; (1 Kings 9:18), apparently from H8558; palm-city; Tadmor, a place near Palestine; Tadmor; n-pr-loc

- a city built by Solomon after his conquest of Hamath-zobah
