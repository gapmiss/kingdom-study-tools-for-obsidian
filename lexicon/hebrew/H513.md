# H513 אֶלְתּוֹלַד / אלתולד

## ʼEltôwlad

_(el-to-lad' | el-toh-LAHD | el-toh-LAHD)_

## Definition

God's generations; probably from H0410 and a masculine form of H8435 (compare H8434); God (is) generator; Eltolad, a place in Palestine; Eltolad; n-pr-loc

- a city in southern Judah
