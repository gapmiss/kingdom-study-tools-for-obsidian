# H672 אֶפְרָת / אפרת

## ʼEphrâth

_(ef-rawth' | ep-RAWT | ef-RAHT)_

## Definition

ash-heap, place of fruitfulness; or אֶפְרָתָה; from H6509; fruitfulness; Ephrath, another name for Bethlehem; once (Psalm 132:6) perhaps for Ephraim; also of an Israelitish woman; Ephrath, Ephratah; n-pr

- (n pr loc) a place near Bethel where Rachel died and was buried
- another name for Bethlehem
- (n pr f) wife of Caleb
