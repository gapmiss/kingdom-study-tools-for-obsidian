# H4166 מוּצָקָה / מוצקה

## mûwtsâqâh

_(moo-tsaw-kaw' | moo-tsaw-KAW | moo-tsa-KA)_

## Definition

or מֻצָקָה; from H3332; properly, something poured out, i.e., a casting (of metal); by implication, a tube (as cast); when it was cast, pipe; n-f

- pipe
- a casting
