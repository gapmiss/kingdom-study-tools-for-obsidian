# H2950 טָפַל / טפל

## ṭâphal

_(taw-fal' | taw-PAHL | ta-FAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to stick on as a patch; figuratively, to impute falsely; forge(-r), sew up; v

- (Qal) to smear, plaster over, stick, glue
