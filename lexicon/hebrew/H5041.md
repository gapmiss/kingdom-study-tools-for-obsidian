## Nᵉballâṭ

_(neb-al-lawt' | neh-bahl-LAWT | neh-va-LAHT)_

## Definition

hidden folly; apparently from H5036 and H3909; foolish secrecy; Neballat, a place in Palestine; Neballat; n-pr-loc
