# H8112 שִׁמְרוֹן מְראוֹן / שמרון מראון

## Shimrôwn Mᵉrʼôwn

_(shim-rone' mer-one' | shim-RONE mer-ONE | sheem-RONE mer-ONE)_

## Definition

watch-height of Meron; from H8110 and a derivative of H4754; guard of lashing; Shimron-Meron, a place in Palestine; Shimon-meron; n-pr-loc

- a Canaanite royal city allotted to Zebulun
  - probably the same as 'Shimron'
