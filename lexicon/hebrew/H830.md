# H830 אַשְׁפֹּת / אשפת

## ʼashpôth

_(ash-pohth' | ash-POTE | ash-POTE)_

## Definition

or אַשְׁפּוֹת; or שְׁפֹת; (contraction) plural of a noun of the same form as H0827, from H8192 (in the sense of scraping); a heap of rubbish or filth; dung (hill); n-m

- ash heap, refuse heap, dung-hill
