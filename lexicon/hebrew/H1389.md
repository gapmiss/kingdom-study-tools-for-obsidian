# H1389 גִּבְעָה / גבעה

## gibʻâh

_(ghib-aw' | ɡib-AW | ɡeev-AH)_

## Definition

feminine from the same as H1387; a hillock; hill, little hill; n-f

- hill
  - hill (lower than a mountain)
  - as a place of illicit worship
  - poetic for mountain
  - used in place names
