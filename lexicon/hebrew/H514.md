# H514 אֶלְתְּקֵא / אלתקא

## ʼEltᵉqêʼ

_(el-te-kay' | el-teh-KAY | el-teh-KAY)_

## Definition

let God spue thee out; or (more properly) אֶלְתְּקֵה; of uncertain derivation; Eltekeh or Elteke, a place in Palestine; Eltekeh; n-pr-loc

- Levitical city in the tribe of Dan, between Ekron and Timna

[[Joshua 19#44|Joshua 19:44]], [[Joshua 21#23|Joshua 21:23]]
