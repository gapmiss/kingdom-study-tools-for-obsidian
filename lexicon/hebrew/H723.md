# H723 אֻרְוָה / ארוה

## ʼurvâh

_(oor-vaw' | oor-WAW | oor-VA)_

## Definition

or אֲרָיָה; from H0717 (in the sense of feeding); a herding-place for an animal; stall; n-f

- manger, crib, stall (for animals)
