# H4806 מְרִיא / מריא

## mᵉrîyʼ

_(mer-ee' | meh-REE | meh-REE)_

## Definition

from H4754 in the sense of grossness, through the idea of domineering (compare H4756); stall-fed; often (as noun) a beeve; fat (fed) beast (cattle, -ling); n-m

- well-fed, fatling
