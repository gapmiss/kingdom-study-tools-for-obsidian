# H5055 נָגַח / נגח

## nâgach

_(naw-gakh' | naw-ɡA | na-ɡAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to butt with the horns; figuratively, to war against; gore, push (down, -ing); v

- to push, thrust, gore
  - (Qal) to gore
  - (Piel) to push or thrust at
  - (Hithpael) to engage in thrusting, wage war
