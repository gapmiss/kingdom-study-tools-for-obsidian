# H66 אַבֵל מַיִם / אבל מים

## ʼAbêl Mayim

_(aw-bale' mah'-yim | ah-BALE ma-YIM | ah-VALE ma-YEEM)_

## Definition

meadow of waters; from H0058 and H4325; meadow of water; Abel-Majim, a place in Palestine; Abel-maim; n-pr-loc

- a city in northern Israel (perhaps Abel Beth Maachah)
