# H6958 קוֹא / קוא

## qôwʼ

_(ko | koh | koh)_

## Definition

or קָיָה; (Jeremiah 25:27), a primitive root; to vomit; spue (out), vomit (out, up, up again); v

- to vomit up, spue out, disgorge
  - (Qal) to vomit
  - (Hiphil) to vomit up
    - of land spewing out its inhabitants (figuratively)
    - vomiting, disgorging
