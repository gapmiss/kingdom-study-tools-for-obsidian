# H7485 רַעַמְיָה / רעמיה

## Raʻamyâh

_(rah-am-yaw' | ra-am-YAW | ra-am-YA)_

## Definition

thunder of Jehovah; from H7481 and H3050; Jah has shaken; Raamjah, an Israelite; Raamiah; n-pr-m

- one who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
  - also 'Reelaiah'
