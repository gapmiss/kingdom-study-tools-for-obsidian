# H5826 עָזַר / עזר

## ʻâzar

_(aw-zar' | aw-DZAHR | ah-ZAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to surround, i.e., protect or aid; help, succour; v

- to help, succour, support
  - (Qal) to help
  - (Niphal) to be helped
  - (Hiphil) to help
