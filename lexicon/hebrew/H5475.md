# H5475 סוֹד / סוד

## çôwd

_(sode | sode | sode)_

## Definition

from H3245; a session, i.e., company of persons (in close deliberation); by implication, intimacy, consultation, a secret; assembly, consel, inward, secret (counsel); n-m

- council, counsel, assembly
  - council (of familiar conversation)
    - divan, circle (of familiar friends)
    - assembly, company
  - counsel
    - counsel (itself)
    - secret counsel
    - familiar converse, intimacy (with God)
