# H7281 רֶגַע / רגע

## regaʻ

_(reh'-gah | reh-ɡA | reh-ɡA)_

## Definition

from H7280; a wink (of the eyes), i.e., a very short space of time; instant, moment, space, suddenly; n-m

- (n m) a moment
- (adv) for a moment, at one moment...at another moment
- (with prep) in a moment
