# H2204 זָקֵן / זקן

## zâqên

_(zaw-kane' | dzaw-KANE | za-KANE)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be old; aged man, be (wax) old (man); v

- to be old, become old
  - (Qal) to be old, become old
  - (Hiphil) to grow old, show age
