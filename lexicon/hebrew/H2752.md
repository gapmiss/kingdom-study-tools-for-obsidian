# H2752 חֹרִי / חרי

## Chôrîy

_(kho-ree' | hoh-REE | hoh-REE)_

## Definition

cave dweller; from H2356; cave-dweller or troglodyte; a Chorite or indigenous Idumaean; Horims, Horites; n-m

- the inhabitants of Mount Seir
- the inhabitants of Edom (in later times)
