# H5775 עוֹף / עוף

## ʻôwph

_(ofe | ope | ofe)_

## Definition

from H5774; a bird (as covered with feathers, or rather as covering with wings), often collectively; bird, that flieth, flying, fowl; n-m

- flying creatures, fowl, insects, birds
  - fowl, birds
  - winged insects
