# H3411 יְרֵכָה / ירכה

## yᵉrêkâh

_(yer-ay-kaw' | yeh-ray-KAW | yeh-ray-HA)_

## Definition

feminine of H3409; properly, the flank; but used only figuratively, the rear or recess; border, coast, part, quarter, side; n-f

- flank, side, extreme parts, recesses
  - side
  - sides, recesses (dual)
