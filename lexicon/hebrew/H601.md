# H601 אֲנָפָה / אנפה

## ʼănâphâh

_(an-aw-faw' | uh-naw-PAW | uh-na-FA)_

## Definition

from H0599; an unclean bird, perhaps the parrot (from its irascibility); heron; n-f

- an unclean bird
  - perhaps an heron
