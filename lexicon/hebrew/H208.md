# H208 אוֹנָם / אונם

## ʼÔwnâm

_(o-nawm' | oh-NAWM | oh-NAHM)_

## Definition

vigorous; a variation of H0209; strong; Onam, the name of an Edomite and of an Israelite; Onam; n-pr-m

- a Horite chief, grandson of Seir
- son Jerahmeel, a chief of Judah
