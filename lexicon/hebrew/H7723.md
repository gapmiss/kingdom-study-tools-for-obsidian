# H7723 שָׁוְא / שוא

## shâvᵉʼ

_(shawv | shoh | shove)_

## Definition

or שַׁו; from the same as H7722 in the sense of desolating; evil (as destructive), literally (ruin) or morally (especially guile); figuratively idolatry (as false, subjective), uselessness (as deceptive, objective; also adverbially, in vain); false(-ly), lie, lying, vain, vanity; n-m

- emptiness, vanity, falsehood
  - emptiness, nothingness, vanity
  - emptiness of speech, lying
  - worthlessness (of conduct)
