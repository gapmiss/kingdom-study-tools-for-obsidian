# H3099 יוֹאָחָז / יואחז

## Yôwʼâchâz

_(yo-aw-khawz' | yoh-aw-HAWDZ | yoh-ah-HAHZ)_

## Definition

Jehovah has grasped; a form of H3059; Joachaz, the name of two Israelites; Jehoahaz, Joahaz; n-pr-m

- the father of Joah, Josiah's chronicler
