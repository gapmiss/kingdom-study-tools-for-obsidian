# H1092 בִּלְהָן / בלהן

## Bilhân

_(bil-hawn' | bil-HAWN | beel-HAHN)_

## Definition

their decrepitude; from H1089; timid; Bilhan, the name of an Edomite and of an Israelite; Bilhan; n-pr-m

- a descendant of Esau
- a Benjamite
