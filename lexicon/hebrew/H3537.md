# H3537 כַּד / כד

## kad

_(kad | kahd | kahd)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to deepen; properly, a pail; but generally of earthenware; a jar for domestic purposes; barrel, pitcher; n-f

- jar, large jar (portable)
