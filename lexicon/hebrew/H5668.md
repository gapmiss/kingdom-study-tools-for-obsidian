# H5668 עָבוּר / עבור

## ʻâbûwr

_(aw-boor' | aw-BOOR | ah-VOOR)_

## Definition

or עָבֻר; passive participle of H5674; properly, crossed, i.e., (abstractly) transit; used only adverbially, on account of, in order that; because of, for (...'s sake), (intent) that, to; prep

- (prep) for the sake of, on account of, because of, in order to
- (conj) in order that
