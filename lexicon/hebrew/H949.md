# H949 בּוֹצֵץ / בוצץ

## Bôwtsêts

_(bo-tsates' | boh-TSAYTS | boh-TSAYTS)_

## Definition

surpassing white, glistening; from the same as H0948; shining; Botsets, a rock near Michmash; Bozez; n-pr-loc

- a rock formation near Michmash by which Jonathan approached the Philistine troop
