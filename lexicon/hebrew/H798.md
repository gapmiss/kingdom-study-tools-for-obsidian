# H798 אַשְׁדּוֹת הַפִּסְגָּה / אשדות הפסגה

## ʼAshdôwth hap-Piçgâh

_(ash-doth' hap-pisgaw' | ash-DOTE hahp-pis-ɡAW | ash-DOTE ha-pees-ɡA)_

## Definition

slopes of Pisgah; from the plural of H0794 and H6449 with the article interposed; ravines of the Pisgah; Ashdoth-Pisgah, a place east of the Jordan; Ashdoth-pisgah; n-pr-loc

- the mountains of Pisgah, which include Matthew Nebo
