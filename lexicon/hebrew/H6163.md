# H6163 עֲרָבִי / ערבי

## ʻĂrâbîy

_(ar-aw-bee' | uh-raw-BEE | uh-ra-VEE)_

## Definition

mixed; or עַרְבִי; patrial from H6152; an Arabian or inhabitant of Arab (i.e., Arabia); Arabian; a

- an inhabitant of Arabia
  - steppe-dweller
