# H5937 עָלַז / עלז

## ʻâlaz

_(aw-laz' | aw-LAHDZ | ah-LAHZ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to jump for joy, i.e., exult; be joyful, rejoice, triumph; v

- (Qal) to exult, rejoice, triumph
