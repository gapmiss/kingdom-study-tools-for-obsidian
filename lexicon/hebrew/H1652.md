# H1652 גָּשַׁם / גשם

## gâsham

_(gaw-sham' | ɡaw-SHAHM | ɡa-SHAHM)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to shower violently; (cause to) rain; v

- to rain
  - (Pual) to be rained on
  - (Hiphil) to cause rain, send rain
