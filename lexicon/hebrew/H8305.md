# H8305 שְׂרִיקָה / שריקה

## sᵉrîyqâh

_(ser-ee-kaw' | seh-ree-KAW | seh-ree-KA)_

## Definition

from the same as H8321 in the original sense of piercing; hetchelling (or combing flax), i.e., (concretely) tow (by extension, linen cloth); fine; a

- carded, combed, fine (of flax)
