# H5534 סָכַר / סכר

## çâkar

_(saw-kar' | saw-KAHR | sa-HAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to shut up; by implication, to surrender; stop, give over; v

- to shut up, stop up
  - (Niphal) to be stopped, be shut up
  - (Piel) to shut up, give over
