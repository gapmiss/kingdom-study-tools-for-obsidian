# H2152 זַלְעָפָה / זלעפה

## zalʻâphâh

_(zal-aw-faw' | dzahl-aw-PAW | zahl-ah-FA)_

## Definition

or זִלְעָפָה; from H2196; a glow (of wind or anger); also a famine (as consuming); horrible, horror, terrible; n-f

- burning heat, raging heat
