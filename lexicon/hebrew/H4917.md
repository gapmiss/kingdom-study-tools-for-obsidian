# H4917 מִשְׁלַחַת / משלחת

## mishlachath

_(mish-lakh'-ath | mish-la-HAHT | meesh-la-HAHT)_

## Definition

feminine of H4916; a mission, i.e., (abstractly and favorable) release, or (concretely and unfavorable) an army; discharge, sending; n-f

- discharge, sending, sending away, deputation
  - discharge
  - deputation
