# H1581 גָּמָל / גמל

## gâmâl

_(gaw-mawl' | ɡaw-MAWL | ɡa-MAHL)_

## Definition

apparently from H1580 (in the sense of labor or burden-bearing); a camel; camel; n

- camel
  - as property, as beast of burden, for riding, forbidden for food
