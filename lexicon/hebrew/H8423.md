# H8423 תּוּבַל קַיִן / תובל קין

## Tûwbal Qayin

_(too-bal' kah'-yin | too-BAHL ka-YIN | too-VAHL ka-YEEN)_

## Definition

thou will be brought of Cain; apparently from H2986 (compare H2981) and H7014; offspring of Cain; Tubal-Kajin, an antidiluvian patriarch; Tubal-cain; n-pr-m

- son of Lamech by his wife Zillah and the first worker in metal
