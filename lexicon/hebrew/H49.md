# H49 אֲבִישַׁג / אבישג

## ʼĂbîyshag

_(ab-ee-shag' | uh-bee-SHAHɡ | uh-vee-SHAHɡ)_

## Definition

my father is a wanderer; from H0001 and H7686; father of error (i.e., blundering); Abishag, a concubine of David; Abishag; n-pr-f

- David's beautiful young nurse
