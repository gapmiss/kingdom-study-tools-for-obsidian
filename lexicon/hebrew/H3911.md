# H3911 לְטָאָה / לטאה

## lᵉṭâʼâh

_(let-aw-aw' | leh-taw-AW | leh-ta-AH)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to hide; a kind of lizard (from its covert habits); lizard; n-f

- a kind of lizard
  - named as being unclean
  - perhaps an extinct animal, exact meaning is unknown
