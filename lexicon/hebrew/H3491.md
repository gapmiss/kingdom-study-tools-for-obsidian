# H3491 יָתוּר / יתור

## yâthûwr

_(yaw-thoor' | yaw-TOOR | ya-TOOR)_

## Definition

passive participle of H3498; properly, what is left, i.e., (by implication) a gleaning; range; v

- to remain over, leave, range over
- a searching (n m)
