# H6286 פָּאַר / פאר

## pâʼar

_(paw-ar' | paw-AR | pa-AR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; also denominative from H6288; to gleam, i.e., (causatively) embellish; figuratively, to boast; also to explain (i.e., make clear) oneself; to shake a tree; beautify, boast self, go over the boughs, glorify (self), glory, vaunt self; v

- to glorify, beautify, adorn
  - (Piel) to glorify, beautify
  - (Hithpael)
    - to glorify oneself
    - to get glory to oneself, be glorified
- (Piel) to go over the boughs
