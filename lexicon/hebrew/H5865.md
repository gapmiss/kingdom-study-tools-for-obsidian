# H5865 עֵילוֹם / עילום

## ʻêylôwm

_(ay-lome' | ay-LOME | ay-LOME)_

## Definition

for H5769; concealed, i.e., the vanishing point; generally, time out of mind (past or future), i.e., (practically) eternity; frequentatively, adverbial (especially with prepositional prefix) always; ever; n-m

- for ever, ever, everlasting, evermore, perpetual, old, ancient, world
