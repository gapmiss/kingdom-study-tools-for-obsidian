# H7952 שָׁלָה / שלה

## shâlâh

_(shaw-law' | shaw-LAW | sha-LA)_

## Definition

a primitive root (probably identical with H7953 through the idea of educing); to mislead; deceive, be negligent; v

- (Niphal) to be negligent
- (Hiphil) to mislead
