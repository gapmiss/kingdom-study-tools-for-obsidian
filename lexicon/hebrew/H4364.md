# H4364 מַכְמָר / מכמר

## makmâr

_(mak-mawr' | mahk-MAWR | mahk-MAHR)_

## Definition

or מִכְמֹר; from H3648 in the sense of blackening by heat; a (hunter's) net (as dark from concealment); net; n-m

- net, snare
