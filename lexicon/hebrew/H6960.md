# H6960 קָוָה / קוה

## qâvâh

_(kaw-vaw' | kaw-WAW | ka-VA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to bind together (perhaps by twisting), i.e., collect; (figuratively) to expect; gather (together), look, patiently, tarry, wait (for, on, upon); v

- to wait, look for, hope, expect
  - (Qal) waiting (participle)
  - (Piel)
    - to wait or look eagerly for
    - to lie in wait for
    - to wait for, linger for
- to collect, bind together
  - (Niphal) to be collected
