# H1566 גָּלַע / גלע

## gâlaʻ

_(gaw-lah' | ɡaw-LA | ɡa-LA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be obstinate; (inter-)meddle (with); v

- to expose, lay bare
  - (Hithpael)
    - to disclose oneself, break out
    - to break out (in contention)
