# H6320 פּוּךְ / פוך

## pûwk

_(pook | pook | pook)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to paint; dye (specifically, stibium for the eyes); fair colours, glistering, paint(-ed, -ing); n-m

- antimony, stibium, black paint
  - eye cosmetic
