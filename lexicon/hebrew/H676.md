# H676 אֶצְבַּע / אצבע

## ʼetsbaʻ

_(ets-bah' | ets-BA | ets-BA)_

## Definition

from the same as H6648 (in the sense of grasping); something to sieze with, i.e., a finger; by analogy, a toe; finger, toe; n-f

- finger, toe
