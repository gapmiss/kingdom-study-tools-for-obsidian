# H6547 פַּרְעֹה / פרעה

## Parʻôh

_(par-o' | pahr-OH | pahr-OH)_

## Definition

great house; of Egyptian derivation; Paroh, a general title of Egyptian kings; Pharaoh; n-m

- the common title of the king of Egypt
