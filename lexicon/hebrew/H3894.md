# H3894 לָחוּם / לחום

## lâchûwm

_(law-khoom' | law-HOOM | la-HOOM)_

## Definition

or לָחֻם; passive participle of H3898; properly, eaten, i.e., food; also flesh, i.e., body; while...is eating, flesh; n-m

- food, that which is eaten
- flesh, body
