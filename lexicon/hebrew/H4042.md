# H4042 מָגַן / מגן

## mâgan

_(maw-gan' | maw-ɡAHN | ma-ɡAHN)_

## Definition

a denominative from H4043; properly, to shield; encompass with; figuratively, to rescue, to hand safely over (i.e., surrender); deliver; v

- (Piel) to deliver up, give, deliver
