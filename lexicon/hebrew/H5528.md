# H5528 סָכַל / סכל

## çâkal

_(saw-kal' | saw-KAHL | sa-HAHL)_

## Definition

for H3688; to be silly; do (make, play the, turn into) fool(-ish, -ishly, -ishness); v

- to be foolish, be a fool
  - (Piel) to make foolish, turn into foolishness
  - (Niphal) to act or do foolishly
  - (Hiphil) to do foolishly, play the fool
