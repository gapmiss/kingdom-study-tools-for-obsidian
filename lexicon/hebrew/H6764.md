# H6764 צָלָף / צלף

## Tsâlâph

_(tsaw-lawf' | tsaw-LAWP | tsa-LAHF)_

## Definition

wound; from an unused root of unknown meaning; Tsalaph, an Israelite; Zalaph; n-pr-m

- father of Hanun who assisted in rebuilding the city wall of Jerusalem after the return from exile

## Verse(s)

[[Nehemiah 3#30|Nehemiah 3:30]]
