# H6649 צִבְעוֹן / צבעון

## Tsibʻôwn

_(tsib-one' | tsib-ONE | tseev-ONE)_

## Definition

colored; from the same as H6648; variegated; Tsibon, an Idumaean; Zibeon; n-pr-m

- father of Anah, whose daughter Aholibamah married Esau
- son of Seir the Horite
  - maybe same as 1
