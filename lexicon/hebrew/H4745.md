# H4745 מִקְרֶה / מקרה

## miqreh

_(mik-reh' | mik-REH | meek-REH)_

## Definition

from H7136; something met with, i.e., an accident or fortune; something befallen, befalleth, chance, event, hap(-peneth); n-m

- unforeseen meeting or event, accident, happening, chance, fortune
  - accident, chance
  - fortune, fate
