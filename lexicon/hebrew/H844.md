# H844 אַשְׂרִיאֵל / אשריאל

## ʼAsrîyʼêl

_(as-ree-ale' | as-ree-ALE | as-ree-ALE)_

## Definition

I shall be prince of God; an orthographical variation for H0840; Asriel, the name of two Israelites; Ashriel, Asriel; n-pr-m

- a great-grandson of Manasseh, and son of Gilead
- a son of Manasseh
