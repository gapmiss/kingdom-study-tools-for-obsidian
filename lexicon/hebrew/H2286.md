# H2286 חֲגָבָא / חגבא

## Chăgâbâʼ

_(khag-aw-baw' | huh-ɡaw-BAW | huh-ɡa-VA)_

## Definition

locust; or חֲגָבָה; feminine of H2285; locust; Chagaba or Chagabah, one of the Nethinim; Hagaba, Hagabah; n-pr-m

- head of a family of exiles who returned with Zerubbabel
