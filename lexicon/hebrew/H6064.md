# H6064 עָנַשׁ / ענש

## ʻânash

_(aw-nash' | aw-NAHSH | ah-NAHSH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to urge; by implication, to inflict a penalty, specifically, to fine; amerce, condemn, punish, × surely; v

- to fine, amerce, punish, condemn, mulct
  - (Qal) to fine, punish
  - (Niphal) to be fined, be punished, be mulcted
