# H8422 תּוּבַל / תובל

## Tûwbal

_(too-bal' | too-BAHL | too-VAHL)_

## Definition

thou shall be brought; or תֻּבַל; probably of foreign derivation; Tubal, a postdiluvian patriarch and his posterity; Tubal; n-pr-m

- (n pr m) son of Japheth and grandson of Noah
- (n pr terr) a region in east Asia Minor
  - perhaps nearly equal to Cappadocia
