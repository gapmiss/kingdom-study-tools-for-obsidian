## Mishrâʻîy

_(mish-raw-ee' | mish-raw-EE | meesh-ra-EE)_

## Definition

touching evil; patrial from an unused noun from an unused root; probably meaning to stretch out; extension; a Mishraite, or inhabitant (collectively) of Mishra; Mishraites; a

- the 4th of the 4 families of Kirjath-jearim
