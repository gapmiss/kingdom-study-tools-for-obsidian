# H8350 שָׁשַׁר / ששר

## shâshar

_(shaw-shar' | shaw-SHAHR | sha-SHAHR)_

## Definition

perhaps from the base of H8324 in the sense of that of H8320; red ochre (from its piercing color); vermillion; n-m

- red color, vermilion
  - formerly gained from 'kermes' insect
