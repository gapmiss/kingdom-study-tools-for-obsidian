# H7687 שְׂגוּב / שגוב

## Sᵉgûwb

_(seg-oob' | seh-ɡOOB | seh-ɡOOV)_

## Definition

exalted; from H7682; aloft; Segub, the name of two Israelites; Segub; n-pr-m

- the youngest son of Hiel the Bethelite who rebuilt Jericho
- a Judaite, son of Hezron
