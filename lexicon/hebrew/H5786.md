# H5786 עָוַר / עור

## ʻâvar

_(aw-var' | aw-WAHR | ah-VAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root (rather denominatively from H5785 through the idea of a film over the eyes); to blind; blind, put out; v

- (Piel) to blind, make blind, put out the eyes of
