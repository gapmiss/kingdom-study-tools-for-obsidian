# H7081 קֶסֶם / קסם

## qeçem

_(keh'-sem | keh-SEM | keh-SEM)_

## Definition

from H7080; a lot; also divination (including its fee), oracle; (reward of) divination, divine sentence, witchcraft; n-m

- divination, witchcraft
  - of the nations, Balaam
  - of false prophets
  - in a good sense (king's lips as oracles)
