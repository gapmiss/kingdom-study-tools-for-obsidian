# H3415 יָרַע / ירע

## yâraʻ

_(yaw-rah' | yaw-RA | ya-RA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to be broken up (with any violent action) i.e., (figuratively) to fear; be grevious (only Isaiah 15:4; the rest belong to H7489); v

- (Qal) to tremble, quiver
