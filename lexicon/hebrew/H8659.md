# H8659 תַּרְשִׁישׁ / תרשיש

## Tarshîysh

_(tar-sheesh' | tahr-SHEESH | tahr-SHEESH)_

## Definition

yellow jasper; probably the same as H8658 (as the region of the stone, or the reverse); Tarshish, a place on the Mediterranean, hence, the ephithet of a merchant vessel (as if for or from that port); also the name of a Persian and of an Israelite; Tarshish, Tharshish; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) son of Javan
  - site unknown but perhaps in Cyprus or Spain
- a Benjamite, son of Bilhan
- one of the wise men close to king Ahasuerus of Persia
- (n pr loc) a city of the Phoenicians in a distant part of the Mediterranean Sea to which the prophet Jonah was trying to flee
- a city somewhere near and accessible to the Red Sea to which ships constructed at Ezion-geber on the Elanitic Gulf on the Red Sea were to sail
