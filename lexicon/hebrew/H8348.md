## Shêshân

_(shay-shawn' | shay-SHAWN | shay-SHAHN)_

## Definition

noble; perhaps for H7799; lily; Sheshan, an Israelite; Sheshan; n-pr-m

- a Judaite of the families of Hezron and Jerahmeel, son of Ishi and father of Ahlai
