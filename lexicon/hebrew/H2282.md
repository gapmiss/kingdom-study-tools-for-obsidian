# H2282 חַג / חג

## chag

_(khag | hahɡ | hahɡ)_

## Definition

or חָג; from H2287; a festival, or a victim therefor; (solemn) feast (day), sacrifice, solemnity; n-m

- festival, feast, festival-gathering, pilgrim-feast
  - feast
  - festival sacrifice
