# H7279 רָגַן / רגן

## râgan

_(raw-gan' | raw-ɡAHN | ra-ɡAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to grumble, i.e., rebel; murmur; v

- to murmur, whisper
  - (Qal) murmurer (participle)
  - (Niphal)
    - to murmur
    - backbiter (participle)
