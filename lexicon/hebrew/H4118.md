# H4118 מַהֵר / מהר

## mahêr

_(mah-hare' | ma-HARE | ma-HARE)_

## Definition

from H4116; properly, hurrying; hence (adverbially) in a hurry; hasteth, hastily, at once, quickly, soon, speedily, suddenly; a

- (adj) hurrying, speedy, swift, hastening
- (adv) quickly, speedily
