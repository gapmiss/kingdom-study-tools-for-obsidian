# H7077 קָנַן / קנן

## qânan

_(kaw-nan' | kaw-NAHN | ka-NAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to erect; but used only as denominative from H7064; to nestle, i.e., build or occupy as a nest; make...nest; v

- to make a nest
  - (Piel) to make a nest
  - (Pual) to be nested
