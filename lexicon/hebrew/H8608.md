# H8608 תָּפַף / תפף

## tâphaph

_(taw-faf' | taw-PAHP | ta-FAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to drum, i.e., play (as) on the tambourine; taber, play with timbrels; v

- to play or sound the timbrel, beat, play upon, drum (on a timbrel or other object)
  - (Qal) playing (participle)
  - (Poel) beating (participle)
