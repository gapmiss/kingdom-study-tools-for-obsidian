# H8441 תּוֹעֵבַה / תועבה

## tôwʻêbah

_(to-ay-baw' | toh-ay-BA | toh-ay-VA)_

## Definition

or תֹּעֵבַה; feminine active participle of H8581; properly, something disgusting (morally), i.e., (as noun) an abhorrence; especially idolatry or (concretely) an idol; abominable (custom, thing), abomination; n-f

- a disgusting thing, abomination, abominable
  - in ritual sense (of unclean food, idols, mixed marriages)
  - in ethical sense (of wickedness etc)
