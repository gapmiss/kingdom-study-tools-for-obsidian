# H345 אַיָּה / איה

## ʼAyâh

_(ah-yaw' | ah-YAW | ah-YA)_

## Definition

falcon; the same as H0344; Ajah, the name of two Israelites; Aiah, Ajah; n-pr-m

- a Horite, son of Zibeon
- father of Rizpah, Saul's concubine
