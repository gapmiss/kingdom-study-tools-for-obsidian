# H6638 צָבָה / צבה

## tsâbâh

_(tsaw-baw' | tsaw-BAW | tsa-VA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to amass, i.e., grow turgid; specifically, to array an army against; fight swell; v

- to swell, swell up
  - (Qal) to swell (of adulteress' belly)
  - (Hiphil) to cause to swell (of adulteress' belly)
