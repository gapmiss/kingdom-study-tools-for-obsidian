# H1706 דְּבַשׁ / דבש

## dᵉbash

_(deb-ash' | deh-BAHSH | deh-VAHSH)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to be gummy; honey (from its stickiness); by analogy, syrup; honey(-comb); n-m

- honey
