# H1650 גְּשׁוּר / גשור

## Gᵉshûwr

_(ghesh-oor' | ɡeh-SHOOR | ɡeh-SHOOR)_

## Definition

proud beholder; from an unused root (meaning to join); bridge; Geshur, a district of Syria; Geshur, Geshurite; n-pr-m

- a people
- a land in north Transjordania
