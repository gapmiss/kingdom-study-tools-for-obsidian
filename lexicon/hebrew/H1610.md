# H1610 גַּף / גף

## gaph

_(gaf | ɡahp | ɡahf)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to arch; the back; by extension the body or self; + highest places, himself; n-m

- body, self (only in phrase, eg by himself)
- height, elevation
