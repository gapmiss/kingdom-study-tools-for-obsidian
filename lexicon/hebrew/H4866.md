# H4866 מִשְׁבֵּר / משבר

## mishbêr

_(mish-bare' | mish-BARE | meesh-BARE)_

## Definition

from H7665; the orifice of the womb (from which the fetus breaks forth); birth, breaking forth; n-m

- place of breaking forth, place of breach, opening
  - of womb
