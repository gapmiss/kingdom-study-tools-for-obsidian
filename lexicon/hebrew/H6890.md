# H6890 צֶרֶת הַשַּׁחַר / צרת השחר

## Tsereth hash-Shachar

_(tseh'-reth hash-shakh'-ar | tseh-RET hahsh-sha-HAHR | tseh-RET ha-sha-HAHR)_

## Definition

splendor of the dawn; from the same as H6889 and H7837 with the article interposed; splendor of the dawn; Tsereth-hash-Shachar, a place in Palestine; Zareth-shahar; n-pr-loc

- a town in Reuben
