# H1959 הֵידָד / הידד

## hêydâd

_(hay-dawd' | hay-DAWD | hay-DAHD)_

## Definition

from an unused root (meaning to shout); acclamation; shout(-ing); n-m

- a shout, cheer, shouting
  - shouting
  - shout (of the foe)
