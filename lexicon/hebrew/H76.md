# H76 אֲבַעְבֻּעָה / אבעבעה

## ʼăbaʻbuʻâh

_(ab-ah-boo-aw' | uh-ba-boo-AW | uh-va-boo-AH)_

## Definition

(by reduplication) from an unused root (meaning to belch forth); an inflammatory pustule (as eruption); blains; n-f

- blisters, boils (from root, to swell up)
