# H4303 מַטְעַם / מטעם

## maṭʻam

_(mat-am' | maht-AM | maht-AM)_

## Definition

or (feminine) מַטְעַמָּה; from H2938; a delicacy; dainty (meat), savoury meat; n-m

- tasty or savory food, delectable food, dainties
