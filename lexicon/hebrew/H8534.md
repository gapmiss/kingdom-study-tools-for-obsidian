# H8534 תַּלְתַּל / תלתל

## taltal

_(tal-tal' | tahl-TAHL | tahl-TAHL)_

## Definition

by reduplication, from H8524 through the idea of vibration; a trailing bough (as pendulous); bushy; n-f

- wavy, branches of palms, branchy (figuratively describing hair)

## Note

The exact meaning is considered uncertain.
