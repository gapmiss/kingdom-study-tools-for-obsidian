## Zᵉbul

_(zeb-ool' | dzeh-BOOL | zeh-VOOL)_

## Definition

exalted; the same as H2073; dwelling; Zebul, an Israelite; Zebul; n-pr-m

- chief man of the city of Shechem at the time of the contest between Abimelech and the native Canaanites
