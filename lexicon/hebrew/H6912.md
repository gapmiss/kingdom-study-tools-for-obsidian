# H6912 קָבַר / קבר

## qâbar

_(kaw-bar' | kaw-BAHR | ka-VAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to inter; × in any wise, bury(-ier); v

- to bury
  - (Qal) to bury
  - (Niphal) to be buried
  - (Piel) to bury, bury (in masses)
  - (Pual) to be buried
