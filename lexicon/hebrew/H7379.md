# H7379 רִיב / ריב

## rîyb

_(reeb | reeb | reev)_

## Definition

or רִב; from H7378; a contest (personal or legal); + adversary, cause, chiding, contend(-tion), controversy, multitude (from the margin), pleading, strife, strive(-ing), suit; n-m

- strife, controversy, dispute
  - strife, quarrel
  - dispute, controversy, case at law
