# H4454 מָלַק / מלק

## mâlaq

_(maw-lak' | maw-LAHK | ma-LAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to crack a joint; by implication, to wring the neck of a fowl (without separating it); wring off; v

- (Qal) to nip, nip off (head of a bird)
