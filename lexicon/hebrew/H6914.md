# H6914 קִבְרוֹת הַתַּאֲוָה / קברות התאוה

## Qibrôwth hat-Taʼă-vâh

_(kib-roth' hat-tahav-aw' | kib-ROTE haht-ta-uh-WAW | keev-ROTE ha-ta-uh-VA)_

## Definition

graves of lust; from the feminine plural of H6913 and H8378 with the article interposed; graves of the longing; Kibroth-hat-Taavh, a place in the Desert; Kibroth-hattaavah; n-pr

- a station of Israel in the wilderness 3 campsites away from Sinai near the gulf of Akabah
