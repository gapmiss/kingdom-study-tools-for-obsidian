# H6316 פּוּט / פוט

## Pûwṭ

_(poot | poot | poot)_

## Definition

a bow; of foreign origin; Put, a son of Ham, also the name of his descendants or their region, and of a Persian tribe; Phut, Put; n-pr

- a nation and people of northern Africa; probably Libyans
