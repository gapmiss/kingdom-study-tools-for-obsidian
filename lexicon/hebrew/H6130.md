# H6130 עָקָן / עקן

## ʻÂqân

_(aw-kawn' | aw-KAWN | ah-KAHN)_

## Definition

sharp-sighted; from an unused root meaning to twist; tortuous; Akan, an Idummaean; Akan; n-pr-m

- son of Ezer, descendant of Seir, and one of the chiefs of the Horites of Edom

Compare H3292.
