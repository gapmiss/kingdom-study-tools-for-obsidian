# H5641 סָתַר / סתר

## çâthar

_(saw-thar' | saw-TAHR | sa-TAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to hide (by covering), literally or figuratively; be absent, keep close, conceal, hide (self), (keep) secret, × surely; v

- to hide, conceal
  - (Niphal)
    - to hide oneself
    - to be hidden, be concealed
  - (Piel) to hide carefully
  - (Pual) to be hidden carefully, be concealed
  - (Hiphil) to conceal, hide
  - (Hithpael) to hide oneself carefully
