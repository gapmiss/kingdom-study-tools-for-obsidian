# H3728 כָּפַשׁ / כפש

## kâphash

_(kaw-fash' | kaw-PAHSH | ka-FAHSH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to tread down; figuratively, to humiliate; cover; v

- (Hiphil) to make bent, press or bend together, to cover over
- to cause to bend down
  - (Hiphil) to humble
