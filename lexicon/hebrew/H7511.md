# H7511 רָפַס / רפס

## râphaç

_(raw-fas' | raw-PAHS | ra-FAHS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to trample, i.e., prostrate; humble self, submit self; v

- (Hithpael)
  - to stamp oneself down, humble oneself
  - stamping or tramping down (participle)
