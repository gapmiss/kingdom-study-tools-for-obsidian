# H4748 מִקְשֶׁה / מקשה

## miqsheh

_(mik-sheh' | mik-SHEH | meek-SHEH)_

## Definition

from H7185 in the sense of knotting up round and hard; something turned (rounded), i.e., a curl (of tresses); × well (set) hair; n-m

- turned work, well-dressed hair, well-set hair, turner's work
  - meaning uncertain; possible referring to artistry of the hairdo
