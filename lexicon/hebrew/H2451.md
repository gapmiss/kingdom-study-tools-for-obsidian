# H2451 חׇכְמָה / חכמה

## chokmâh

_(khok-maw' | hoke-MAW | hoke-MA)_

## Definition

from H2449; wisdom (in a good sense); skilful, wisdom, wisely, wit; n-f

- wisdom
  - skill (in war)
  - wisdom (in administration)
  - shrewdness, wisdom
  - wisdom, prudence (in religious affairs)
  - wisdom (ethical and religious)
