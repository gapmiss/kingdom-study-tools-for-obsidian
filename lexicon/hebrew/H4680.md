# H4680 מָצָה / מצה

## mâtsâh

_(maw-tsaw' | maw-TSAW | ma-TSA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to suck out; by implication, to drain, to squeeze out; suck, wring (out); v

- to drain, drain out
  - (Qal) to drain, drain out
  - (Niphal) to be drained out
