# H5503 סָחַר / סחר

## çâchar

_(saw-khar' | saw-HAHR | sa-HAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to travel round (specifically as a pedlar); intensively, to palpitate; go about, merchant(-man), occupy with, pant, trade, traffick; v

- to go around, go about, travel about in, go about in trade
  - (Qal)
    - to go about to and fro (in business)
    - trader, trafficker (participle)
  - (Pilpel) to palpitate
