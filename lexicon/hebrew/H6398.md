# H6398 פָּלַח / פלח

## pâlach

_(paw-lakh' | paw-LA | pa-LAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to slice, i.e., break open or pierce; bring forth, cleave, cut, shred, strike through; v

- to cleave, slice
  - (Qal) to cleave, plow
  - (Piel)
    - to cleave open or through
    - to cause to cleave open
    - to slice, pierce
