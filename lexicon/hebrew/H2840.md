# H2840 חִשֻּׁר / חשר

## chishshur

_(khish-shoor' | hish-SHOOR | hee-SHOOR)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to bind together; combined, i.e., the nave or hub of a wheel (as holding the spokes together); spoke; n-m

- hub, hub of a wheel, nave
