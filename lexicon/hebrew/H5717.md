# H5717 עֲדִיאֵל / עדיאל

## ʻĂdîyʼêl

_(ad-ee-ale' | uh-dee-ALE | uh-dee-ALE)_

## Definition

ornament of God; from H5716 and H0410; ornament of God; Adiel, the name of three Israelites; Adiel; n-pr-m

- a prince of the Simeon of the family of Shimei in the time of king Hezekiah of Judah
- a priest, ancestor of Maasiai
- ancestor of Azmaveth, the treasurer of David
