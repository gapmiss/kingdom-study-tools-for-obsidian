# H8485 תֵּימָא / תימא

## Têymâʼ

_(tay-maw' | tay-MAW | tay-MA)_

## Definition

desert; or תֵּמָא; probably of foreign derivation; Tema, a son of Ishmael, and the region settled by him; Tema; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) the 9th son of Ishmael
- (n pr loc) the land settled by Tema the son of Ishmael
