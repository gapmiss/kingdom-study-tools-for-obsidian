# H8264 שָׁקַק / שקק

## shâqaq

_(shaw-kak' | shaw-KAHK | sha-KAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to course (like a beast of prey); by implication, to seek greedily; have appetite, justle one against another, long, range, run (to and fro); v

- to run, run about, rush, run to and fro, be eager or greedy or thirsty
  - (Qal) roving, ranging, longing, rush out upon (participle)
  - (Hithpalpel) to rush to and fro, rush back and forth
