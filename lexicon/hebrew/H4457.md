# H4457 מֶלְקָח / מלקח

## melqâch

_(mel-kawkh' | mel-KAW | mel-KAHK)_

## Definition

or מַלְקָח; from H3947; (only in dual) tweezers; snuffers, tongs; n-m

- snuffers, tongs
  - tongs (for altar use)
  - snuffers (for lamps in temple or tabernacle)
