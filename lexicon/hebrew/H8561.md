# H8561 תִּמֹּר / תמר

## timmôr

_(tim-more' | tim-MORE | tee-MORE)_

## Definition

(plural or (feminine) תִּמֹּרָה; (singular and plural), from the same root as H8558; (architectural) a palm-like pilaster (i.e., umbellate); palm tree; n-f

- palm tree figure (as ornament)
