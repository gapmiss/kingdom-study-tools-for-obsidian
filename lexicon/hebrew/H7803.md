# H7803 שׁוּתֶלַח / שותלח

## Shûwthelach

_(shoo-theh'-lakh | shoo-teh-LA | shoo-teh-LAHK)_

## Definition

noise of breaking; probably from H7582 and the same as H8520; crash of breakage; Shuthelach, the name of two Israelites; Shuthelah; n-pr-m

- son of Ephraim, ancestor of Joshua, and progenitor of family of Ephraim
