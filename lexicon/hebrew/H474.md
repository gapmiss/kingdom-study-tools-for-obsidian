# H474 אֱלִישׁוּעַ / אלישוע

## ʼĔlîyshûwaʻ

_(el-ee-shoo'-ah | eh-lee-SHOO-ah | ay-lee-SHOO-ah)_

## Definition

my God is wealth, God is salvation; from H0410 and H7769; God of supplication (or of riches); Elishua, the son of King David; Elishua; n-pr-m

- a son of David
