# H6622 פָּתַר / פתר

## pâthar

_(paw-thar' | paw-TAHR | pa-TAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to open up, i.e., (figuratively) interpret (a dream); interpret(-ation, -er); v

- (Qal) to interpret (dreams)
