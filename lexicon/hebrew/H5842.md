# H5842 עֵט / עט

## ʻêṭ

_(ate | ate | ate)_

## Definition

from H5860 (contracted) in the sense of swooping, i.e., side-long stroke; a stylus or marking stick; pen; n-m

- stylus
  - iron stylus with diamond tip used to write on stone or metal
  - reed pen used on a roll
