# H7621 שְׁבוּעָה / שבועה

## shᵉbûwʻâh

_(sheb-oo-aw' | sheh-boo-AW | sheh-voo-AH)_

## Definition

feminine passive participle of H7650; properly, something sworn, i.e., an oath; curse, oath, × sworn; n-f

- oath, curse
  - oath
    - attesting of innocence
    - curse
  - oath (of Jehovah)
