# H5556 סׇלְעָם / סלעם

## çolʻâm

_(sol-awm' | sole-AWM | sole-AM)_

## Definition

apparently from the same as H5553 in the sense of crushing as with a rock, i.e., consuming; a kind of locust (from its destructiveness); bald locust; n-m

- locust
  - an edible winged locust
