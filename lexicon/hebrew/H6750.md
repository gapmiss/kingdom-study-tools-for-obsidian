# H6750 צָלַל / צלל

## tsâlal

_(tsaw-lal' | tsaw-LAHL | tsa-LAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of vibration); to tinkle, i.e., rattle together (as the ears in reddening with shame, or the teeth in chattering with fear); quiver, tingle; v

- to tingle, quiver
  - (Qal)
    - to tingle (of ears)
    - to quiver (of fear)
