# H1360 גֶּבֶא / גבא

## gebeʼ

_(geh'-beh | ɡeh-BEH | ɡeh-VEH)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning probably to collect; a reservoir; by analogy, a marsh; marish, pit; n-m

- cistern, pool
  - cistern
  - pool, marsh
