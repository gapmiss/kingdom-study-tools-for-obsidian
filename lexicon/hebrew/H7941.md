# H7941 שֵׁכָר / שכר

## shêkâr

_(shay-kawr' | shay-KAWR | shay-HAHR)_

## Definition

from H7937; an intoxicant, i.e., intensely alcoholic liquor; strong drink, + drunkard, strong wine; n-m

- strong drink, intoxicating drink, fermented or intoxicating liquor
