# H8566 תָּנָה / תנה

## tânâh

_(taw-naw' | taw-NAW | ta-NA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to present (a mercenary inducement), i.e., bargain with (a harlot); hire; v

- to hire
  - (Qal) to hire
  - (Hiphil) to hire (a prostitute), give amorous favors
