# H7802 שׁוּשַׁן עֵדוּת / שושן עדות

## Shûwshan ʻÊdûwth

_(shoo-shan' ay-dooth' | shoo-SHAHN ay-DOOT | shoo-SHAHN ay-DOOT)_

## Definition

lilies; or (plural of former) שׁוֹשַׁנִּים עֵדוּת; from H7799 and H5715; lily (or trumpet) of assemblage; Shushan-Eduth or Shoshannim-Eduth, the title of a popular song; Shoshannim-Eduth, Shushan-eduth; n-m

- instruction in the title of Psalm 60 and Psalm 80
  - meaning uncertain
