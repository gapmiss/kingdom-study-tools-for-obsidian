# H7797 שׂוּשׂ / שוש

## sûws

_(soos | soos | soos)_

## Definition

or שִׂישׂ; a primitive root; to be bright, i.e., cheerful; be glad, × greatly, joy, make mirth, rejoice; v

- to exult, rejoice
  - (Qal) to exult, display joy
