# H929 בְּהֵמָה / בהמה

## bᵉhêmâh

_(be-hay-maw' | beh-hay-MAW | beh-hay-MA)_

## Definition

from an unused root (probably meaning to be mute); properly, a dumb beast; especially any large quadruped or animal (often collective); beast, cattle; n-f

- beast, cattle, animal
  - beasts (coll of all animals)
  - cattle, livestock (of domestic animals)
  - wild beasts
