# H7693 שָׁגַל / שגל

## shâgal

_(shaw-gal' | shaw-ɡAHL | sha-ɡAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to copulate with; lie with, ravish; v

- to violate, ravish
  - (Qal) to violate, ravish
  - (Niphal) to be ravished
  - (Pual) to be ravished
