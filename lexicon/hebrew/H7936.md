# H7936 שָׂכַר / שכר

## sâkar

_(saw-kar' | saw-KAHR | sa-HAHR)_

## Definition

or (by permutation) סָכַר; (Ezra 4:5), a primitive root (apparently akin (by prosthesis) to H3739 through the idea of temporary purchase; compare H7937); to hire; earn wages, hire (out self), reward, × surely; v

- to hire
  - (Qal) to hire
  - (Niphal) to hire oneself out
  - (Hithpael) to earn wages
