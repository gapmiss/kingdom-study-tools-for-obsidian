# H1744 דּוּכִיפַת / דוכיפת

## dûwkîyphath

_(doo-kee-fath' | doo-kee-PAHT | doo-hee-FAHT)_

## Definition

of uncertain derivation; the hoopoe or else the grouse; lapwing; n-f

- unclean bird (probably hoopoe)
