# H4331 מֵישָׁא / מישא

## Mêyshâʼ

_(may-shaw' | may-SHAW | may-SHA)_

## Definition

freedom; from H4185; departure; Mesha, a place in Arabia; also an Israelite; Mesha; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite, son of Shaharaim by his wife Hodesh who bore him in the land of Moab
