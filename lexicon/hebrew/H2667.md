## Chôphesh

_(kho'-fesh | hoh-PESH | hoh-FESH)_

## Definition

from H2666; something spread loosely, i.e., a carpet; precious; n-m

- widespread, fabric for horseback riding, saddle-blanket, saddle-cloths; meaning dubious
