# H1756 דּוֹר / דור

## Dôwr

_(dore | dore | dore)_

## Definition

generation; or (by permutation) דֹּאר; (Joshua 17:11; 1 Kings 4:11), from H1755; dwelling; Dor, a place in Palestine; Dor; n-pr-loc

- a coastal city in Manasseh, south of Carmel
