# H8661 תַּרְתָּן / תרתן

## Tartân

_(tar-tawn' | tahr-TAWN | tahr-TAHN)_

## Definition

of foreign derivation; Tartan, an Assyrian; Tartan; n-m

- Tartan—field marshal, general, or commander
  - a title used by the Assyrian military
