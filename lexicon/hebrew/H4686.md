# H4686 מָצוּד / מצוד

## mâtsûwd

_(maw-tsood' | maw-TSOOD | ma-TSOOD)_

## Definition

or (feminine) מְצוּדָה; or מְצֻדָה; for H4685; a fastness; castle, defense, fort(-ress), (strong) hold, be hunted, net, snare, strong place; n-f

- net, prey, net prey
  - net
  - prey
- fastness, stronghold
