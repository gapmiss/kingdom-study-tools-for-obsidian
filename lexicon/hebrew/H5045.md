# H5045 נֶגֶב / נגב

## negeb

_(neh'-gheb | neh-ɡEB | neh-ɡEV)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to be parched; the south (from its drought); specifically, the Negeb or southern district of Judah, occasionally, Egypt (as south to Palestine); south (country, side, -ward); n-m

- south-country, Nekeb, south
  - south-country
    - region of southern Judah, boundaries not specific
  - south
