# H1204 בָּעַת / בעת

## bâʻath

_(baw-ath' | baw-AT | ba-AT)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to fear; affright, be (make) afraid, terrify, trouble; v

- to terrify, startle, fall upon, dismay, be overtaken by sudden terror
  - (Niphal) to be terrified
  - (Piel)
    - to fall upon, overwhelm
    - to terrify
