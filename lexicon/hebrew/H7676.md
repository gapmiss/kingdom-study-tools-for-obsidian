# H7676 שַׁבָּת / שבת

## shabbâth

_(shab-bawth' | shahb-BAWT | sha-BAHT)_

## Definition

intensive from H7673; intermission, i.e (specifically) the Sabbath; (+ every) sabbath; n

- Sabbath
  - sabbath
  - day of atonement
  - sabbath year
  - week
  - produce (in sabbath year)
