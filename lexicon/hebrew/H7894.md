# H7894 שִׁישָׁא / שישא

## Shîyshâʼ

_(shee-shaw' | shee-SHAW | shee-SHA)_

## Definition

Jehovah contends; from the same as H7893; whiteness; Shisha, an Israelite; Shisha; n-pr-m

- father of Elihoreph and Ahiah, the royal secretaries in the reign of Solomon
  - apparently the same as 'Shavsha' who held the same position under David
