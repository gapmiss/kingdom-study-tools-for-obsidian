# H6552 פִּרְעָתוֹן / פרעתון

## Pirʻâthôwn

_(pir-aw-thone' | pir-aw-TONE | peer-ah-TONE)_

## Definition

princely; from H6546; chieftaincy; Pirathon, a place in Palestine; Pirathon; n-pr-loc

- a town in Ephraim in the mount of the Amalekite
  - perhaps modern 'Ferata' located 1.5 miles (2.5 km) south from Jaffa on the road to Nablus
