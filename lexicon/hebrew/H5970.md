# H5970 עָלַץ / עלץ

## ʻâlats

_(aw-lats' | aw-LAHTS | ah-LAHTS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to jump for joy, i.e., exult; be joyful, rejoice, triumph; v

- to rejoice, exult
  - (Qal) to exult
