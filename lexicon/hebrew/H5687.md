# H5687 עָבֹת / עבת

## ʻâbôth

_(aw-both' | aw-BOTE | ah-VOTE)_

## Definition

or עָבוֹת; from H5686; intwined, i.e., dense; thick; a

- having interwoven foliage, leafy, dense with foliage
