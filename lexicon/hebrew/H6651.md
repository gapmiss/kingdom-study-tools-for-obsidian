# H6651 צָבַר / צבר

## tsâbar

_(tsaw-bar' | tsaw-BAHR | tsa-VAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to aggregate; gather (together), heap (up), lay up; v

- (Qal) to heap up, pile up
  - of grain, dust, silver
