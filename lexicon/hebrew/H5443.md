# H5443 סַבְּכָא / סבכא

## çabbᵉkâʼ

_(sab-bek-aw' | sahb-beh-KAW | sa-beh-HA)_

## Definition

or שַׂבְּכָא; (Aramaic), from a root corresponding to H5440; a lyre; sackbut; n-f

- trigon, musical instrument
  - a triangular musical instrument with four strings, similar to a lyre
