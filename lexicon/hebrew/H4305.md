# H4305 מָטַר / מטר

## mâṭar

_(maw-tar' | maw-TAHR | ma-TAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to rain; (cause to) rain (upon); v

- to rain
  - (Niphal) to be rained on or upon
  - (Hiphil)
    - to rain, send rain
    - to rain hail, send hail
