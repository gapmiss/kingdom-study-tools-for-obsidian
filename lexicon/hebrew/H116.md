# H116 אֱדַיִן / אדין

## ʼĕdayin

_(ed-ah'-yin | eh-da-YIN | ay-da-YEEN)_

## Definition

of uncertain derivation; then (of time); now, that time, then; adv

- then, afterwards, thereupon, from that time
