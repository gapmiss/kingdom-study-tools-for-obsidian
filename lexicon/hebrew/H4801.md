# H4801 מֶרְחָק / מרחק

## merchâq

_(mer-khawk' | mer-HAWK | mer-HAHK)_

## Definition

 from H7368; remoteness, i.e., (concretely) a distant place; often (adverbially) from afar; (dwell in, very) (a-)far (country, off); n-m

- distant place, distance, far country
