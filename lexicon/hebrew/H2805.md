# H2805 חֵשֶׁב / חשב

## chêsheb

_(khay'-sheb | hay-SHEB | hay-SHEV)_

## Definition

from H2803; a belt or strap (as being interlaced); curious girdle; n-m

- girdle, band, ingenious work
  - ingenious work—the name of the girdle or band of the ephod
