# H5849 עָטַר / עטר

## ʻâṭar

_(aw-tar' | aw-TAHR | ah-TAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to encircle (for attack or protection); especially to crown (literally or figuratively); compass, crown; v

- to surround
  - (Qal) to surround
- to crown, give a crown
  - (Piel) to crown
  - (Hiphil) crown-bestower (participle)
