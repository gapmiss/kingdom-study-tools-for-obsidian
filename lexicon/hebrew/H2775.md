# H2775 חֶרֶס / חרס

## chereç

_(kheh'-res | heh-RES | heh-RES)_

## Definition

or (with a directive enclitic) חַרְסָה; from an unused root meaning to scrape; also perhaps from the mediating idea of H2777; the itch; sun; n-m

- itch, an eruptive disease
- sun
