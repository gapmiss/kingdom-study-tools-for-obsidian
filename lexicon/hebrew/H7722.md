# H7722 שׁוֹא / שוא

## shôwʼ

_(sho | shoh | shoh)_

## Definition

or (feminine) שׁוֹאָה; or שֹׁאָה; from an unused root meaning to rush over; a tempest; by implication, devastation; desolate(-ion), destroy, destruction, storm, wasteness; n-m

- (n m) ravage
- (n f) devastation, ruin, waste
  - devastation, ruin
  - ruin, waste (of land)
