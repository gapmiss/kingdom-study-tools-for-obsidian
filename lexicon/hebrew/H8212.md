# H8212 שׇׁפְכָה / שפכה

## shophkâh

_(shof-kaw' | shope-KAW | shofe-HA)_

## Definition

feminine of a derivative from H8210; a pipe (for pouring forth, e.g., wine), i.e., the penis; privy member; n-f

- penis, urethra, male organ
  - as fluid duct
