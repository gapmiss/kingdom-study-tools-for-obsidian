# H2314 חָדַר / חדר

## châdar

_(khaw-dar' | haw-DAHR | ha-DAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to inclose (as a room), i.e., (by analogy,) to beset (as in a siege); enter a privy chamber; v

- (Qal) to encompass, surround, enclose
