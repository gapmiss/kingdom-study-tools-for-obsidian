# H7779 שׁוּף / שוף

## shûwph

_(shoof | shoop | shoof)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to gape, i.e., snap at; figuratively, to overwhelm; break, bruise, cover; v

- to bruise, crush, gape upon, desire?, seize?, strike out?
  - (Qal) to fall upon, bruise
