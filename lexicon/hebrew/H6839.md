# H6839 צֹפִים / צפים

## Tsôphîym

_(tso-feem' | tsoh-PEEM | tsoh-FEEM)_

## Definition

watchers; plural of active participle of H6822; watchers; Tsophim, a place East of the Jordan; Zophim; n-pr-loc

- a spot on or near the top of Pisgah, the site of Balaam's oracles
