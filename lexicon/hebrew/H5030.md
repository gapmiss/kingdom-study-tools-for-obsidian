# H5030 נָבִיא / נביא

## nâbîyʼ

_(naw-bee' | naw-BEE | na-VEE)_

## Definition

from H5012; a prophet or (generally) inspired man; prophecy, that prophesy, prophet; n-m

- spokesman, speaker, prophet
  - prophet
  - false prophet
  - heathen prophet
