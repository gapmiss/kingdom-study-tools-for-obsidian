# H7387 רֵיקָם / ריקם

## rêyqâm

_(ray-kawm' | ray-KAWM | ray-KAHM)_

## Definition

from H7386; emptily; figuratively (objective) ineffectually, (subjective) undeservedly; without cause, empty, in vain, void; adv

- vainly, emptily
  - in empty condition, empty, emptily
  - in vain, without effect, vainly
