# H6394 פִּלְדָּשׁ / פלדש

## Pildâsh

_(pil-dawsh' | pil-DOHSH | peel-DOHSH)_

## Definition

flame of fire; of uncertain derivation; Pildash, a relative of Abraham; Pildash; n-pr-m

- one of the 8 sons of Nahor, the brother of Abraham, by Milcah his wife or niece
