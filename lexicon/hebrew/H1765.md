# H1765 דָּחַף / דחף

## dâchaph

_(daw-khaf' | daw-HAHP | da-HAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to urge, i.e., hasten; (be) haste(-ned), pressed on; v

- to drive on, hurry, hasten
  - (Qal) to hasten
  - (Niphal) to hasten oneself, hurry
