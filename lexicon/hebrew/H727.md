# H727 אָרוֹן / ארון

## ʼârôwn

_(aw-rone' | aw-RONE | ah-RONE)_

## Definition

or אָרֹן; from H0717 (in the sense of gathering); a box; ark, chest, coffin; n-m

- chest, ark
  - money chest
  - Ark of the Covenant
- coffin
