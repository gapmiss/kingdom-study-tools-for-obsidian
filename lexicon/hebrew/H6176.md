# H6176 עֲרוֹעֵר / ערוער

## ʻărôwʻêr

_(ar-o-ayr' | uh-roh-ARE | uh-roh-ARE)_

## Definition

or עַרְעָר; from H6209 reduplicated; a juniper; health; n-m

- naked, stripped, destitute
- tree or bush
  - probably juniper or cypress (because of its stripped appearance)
