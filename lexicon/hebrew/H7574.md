# H7574 רֶתֶם / רתם

## rethem

_(reh'-them | reh-TEM | reh-TEM)_

## Definition

or רֹתֶם; from H7573; the Spanish broom (from its pole-like stems); juniper (tree); n-m

- broom-plant, retem
  - a kind of broom plant
