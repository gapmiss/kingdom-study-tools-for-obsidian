# H7154 קִרְיַת בַּעַל / קרית בעל

## Qiryath Baʻal

_(keer-yath' bah'-al | kir-YAHT ba-AL | keer-YAHT ba-AL)_

## Definition

city of Baal; from H7151 and H1168; city of Baal; Kirjath-Baal, a place in Palestine; Kirjathbaal; n-pr-loc

- a city on the northern boundary of Judah and on the western and southern boundaries of Benjamin
  - also 'Kirjath-jearim' and 'Baalah'
