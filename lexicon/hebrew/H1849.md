# H1849 דָּפַק / דפק

## dâphaq

_(daw-fak' | daw-PAHK | da-FAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to knock; by analogy, to press severely; beat, knock, overdrive; v

- to beat, knock
  - (Qal)
    - to beat, drive severely
    - to knock
  - (Hithpael) to beat violently
