# H6354 פַּחַת / פחת

## pachath

_(pakh'-ath | pa-HAHT | pa-HAHT)_

## Definition

probably from an unused root apparently meaning to dig; a pit, especially for catching animals; hole, pit, snare; n-m

- pit, hole
