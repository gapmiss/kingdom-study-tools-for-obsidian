# H6946 קָדֵשׁ / קדש

## Qâdêsh

_(kaw-dashe' | kaw-DAYSH | ka-DAYSH)_

## Definition

holy; the same as H6945; sanctuary; Kadesh, a place in the Desert; Kadesh; n-pr-loc

- a city in the extreme south of Judah
  - same as 'Kedesh' and 'Kadesh-barnea'

## Note

Compare H6947.
