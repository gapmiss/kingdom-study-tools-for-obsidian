# H6424 פָּלַס / פלס

## pâlaç

_(paw-las' | paw-LAHS | pa-LAHS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to roll flat, i.e., prepare (a road); also to revolve, i.e., weigh (mentally); make, ponder, weigh; v

- (Piel) to weigh, make level, balance
  - to weigh out
  - to make level or smooth
