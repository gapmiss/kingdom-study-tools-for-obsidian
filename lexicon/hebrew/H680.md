# H680 אָצַל / אצל

## ʼâtsal

_(aw-tsal' | aw-TSAHL | ah-TSAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to join; used only as a denominative from H0681; to separate; hence, to select, refuse, contract; keep, reserve, straiten, take; v

- to lay aside, reserve, withdraw, withhold
  - (Qal) to reserve, set apart, withhold
  - (Niphal) to be withdrawn
  - (Hiphil) to withdraw
