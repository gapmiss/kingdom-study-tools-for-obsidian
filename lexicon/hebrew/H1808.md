_(daw-lee-yaw' | daw-lih-YAW | da-lee-YA)_

## Definition

from H1802; something dangling, i.e., a bough; branch; n-f

- branch, bough
  - of Israel (figuratively) (used with olive tree, vine, cedar tree)
