# H300 אֲחִישַׁחַר / אחישחר

## ʼĂchîyshachar

_(akh-ee-shakh'-ar | uh-hee-sha-HAHR | uh-hee-sha-HAHR)_

## Definition

my brother is dawn, brother of the dawn; from H0251 and H7837; brother of (the) dawn; Achishachar, an Israelite; Ahishar; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite
