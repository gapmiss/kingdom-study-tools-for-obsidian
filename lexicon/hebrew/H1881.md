# H1881 דָּת / דת

## dâth

_(dawth | dawt | daht)_

## Definition

of uncertain (perhaps foreign) derivation; a royal edict or statute; commandment, commission, decree, law, manner; n-f

- decree, law, edict, regulation, usage
  - decree, edict, commission
  - law, rule
