# H1787 דִּישׁוֹן / דישון

## Dîyshôwn

_(dee-shone' | dee-SHONE | dee-SHONE)_

## Definition

thresher; Diyshon, the same as H1788; Dishon, the name of two Edomites; Dishon; n-pr-m

- the fifth son of Seir
- the son of Anah and grandson of Seir
