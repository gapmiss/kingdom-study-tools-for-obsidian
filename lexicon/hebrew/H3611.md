# H3611 כֶּלֶב / כלב

## keleb

_(keh'-leb | keh-LEB | keh-LEV)_

## Definition

from an unused root means. to yelp, or else to attack; a dog; hence (by euphemism) a male prostitute; dog; n-m

- dog
  - dog (literally)
  - contempt or abasement (figuratively)
  - of pagan sacrifice
  - of male cult prostitute (figuratively)
