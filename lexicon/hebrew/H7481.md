# H7481 רָעַם / רעם

## râʻam

_(raw-am' | raw-AM | ra-AM)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to tumble, i.e., be violently agitated; specifically, to crash (of thunder); figuratively, to irritate (with anger); make to fret, roar, thunder, trouble; v

- to thunder
  - (Qal)
    - to make the sound of thunder, thunder
    - to rage
  - (Hiphil)
    - to thunder, cause thunder
    - to tremble, cause to tremble (by extension)
