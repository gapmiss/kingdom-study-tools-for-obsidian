# H1618 גָּרָב / גרב

## gârâb

_(gaw-rawb' | ɡaw-RAWB | ɡa-RAHV)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to scratch; scurf (from itching); scab, scurvy; n-m

- itch, scab
