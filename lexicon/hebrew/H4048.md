# H4048 מָגַר / מגר

## mâgar

_(maw-gar' | maw-ɡAHR | ma-ɡAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to yield up; intensively, to precipitate; cast down, terror; v

- to throw, cast, toss
  - (Qal) to be thrown
  - (Piel) to hurl
