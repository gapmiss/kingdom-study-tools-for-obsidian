# H5133 נוֹצָה / נוצה

## nôwtsâh

_(no-tsaw' | noh-TSAW | noh-TSA)_

## Definition

or נֹצָה; feminine active participle of H5327 in the sense of flying; a pinion (or wing feather); often (collectively) plumage; feather(-s), ostrich; n-f

- plumage, feathers
