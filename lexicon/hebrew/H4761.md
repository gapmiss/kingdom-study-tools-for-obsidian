# H4761 מַרְאָשָׁה / מראשה

## marʼâshâh

_(mar-aw-shaw' | mahr-aw-SHAW | mahr-ah-SHA)_

## Definition

denominative from H7218; properly, headship, i.e., (plural for collective) dominion; principality; n-f

- (n f) place at the head, dominion, head place
- (adv) at head place
