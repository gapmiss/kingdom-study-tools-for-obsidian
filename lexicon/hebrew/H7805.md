# H7805 שָׁזַף / שזף

## shâzaph

_(shaw-zaf' | shaw-DZAHP | sha-ZAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to tan (by sunburning); figuratively (as if by a piercing ray) to scan; look up, see; v

- (Qal) to catch sight of, look on
