# H528 אָמוֹן / אמון

## ʼÂmôwn

_(aw-mone' | aw-MONE | ah-MONE)_

## Definition

to nourish, to be faithful; of Egyptian derivation; used only as an adjunct of H4996; Amon (i.e., Ammon or Amn), a deity of Egypt; multitude, populous; n-pr-m

- an Egyptian god, originally the local god of Thebes, later head of the Egyptian pantheon
