# H6931 קַדְמוֹנִי / קדמוני

## qadmôwnîy

_(kad-mo-nee' | kahd-moh-NEE | kahd-moh-NEE)_

## Definition

or קַדְמֹנִי; from H6930; (of time) anterior or (of place) oriental; ancient, they that went before, east, (thing of) old; a

- former, ancient, eastern
  - former, ancient
  - eastern
