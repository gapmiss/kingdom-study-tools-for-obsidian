# H8576 תַּנְחֻמֶת / תנחמת

## Tanchumeth

_(tan-khoo'-meth | tahn-hoo-MET | tahn-hoo-MET)_

## Definition

consolation; for H8575 (feminine); Tanchumeth, an Israelite; Tanhumeth; n-pr-m

- father of Seraiah, a leader who joined Gedaliah after Jerusalem's fall
