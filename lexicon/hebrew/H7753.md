# H7753 שׂוּךְ / שוך

## sûwk

_(sook | sook | sook)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to entwine, i.e., shut in (for formation, protection or restraint); fence, (make an) hedge (up); v

- to hedge or fence up or about
  - (Qal) to fence about, hedge up
