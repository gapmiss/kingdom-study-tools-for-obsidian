# H618 אָסָם / אסם

## ʼâçâm

_(aw-sawm' | aw-SAWM | ah-SAHM)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to heap together; a storehouse (only in the plural); barn, storehouse; n-m

- storehouse, barn
