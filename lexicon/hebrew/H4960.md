# H4960 מִשְׁתֶּה / משתה

## mishteh

_(mish-teh' | mish-TEH | meesh-TEH)_

## Definition

from H8354; drink, by implication, drinking (the act); also (by implication) a banquet or (generally) feast; banquet, drank, drink, feast(-ed, -ing); n-m

- feast, drink, banquet
  - feast, banquet
  - drink
