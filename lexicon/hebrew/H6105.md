# H6105 עָצַם / עצם

## ʻâtsam

_(aw-tsam' | aw-TSAHM | ah-TSAHM)_

## Definition

a primitive root; also denominatively (from H6106); to bind fast, i.e., close (the eyes); intransitively, to be (causatively, make) powerful or numerous; to crunch the bones; break the bones, close, be great, be increased, be (wax) mighty(-ier), be more, shut, be(-come, make) strong(-er); v

- to be vast, be numerous, be mighty
  - (Qal)
    - to be mighty
    - to be numerous
  - (Hiphil) to make strong, make mighty
- to shut (the eyes), close (the eyes)
  - (Qal) to shut (the eyes)
  - (Piel) to shut tightly (the eyes)
