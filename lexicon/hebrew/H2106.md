# H2106 זָוִית / זוית

## zâvîyth

_(zaw-veeth' | dzaw-WEET | za-VEET)_

## Definition

apparently from the same root as H2099 (in the sense of prominence); an angle (as projecting), i.e., (by implication) a corner-column (or anta); corner(stone); n-f

- corner
