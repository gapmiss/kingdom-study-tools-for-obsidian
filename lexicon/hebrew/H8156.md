# H8156 שָׁסַע / שסע

## shâçaʻ

_(shaw-sah' | shaw-SA | sha-SA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to split or tear; figuratively, to upbraid; cleave, (be) cloven (footed), rend, stay; v

- to divide, cleave, part, split
  - (Qal) cleaving, cleft (participle)
  - (Piel) to tear in two, cleave in two
