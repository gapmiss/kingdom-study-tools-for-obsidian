# H312 אַחֵר / אחר

## ʼachêr

_(akh-air' | ah-HARE | ah-HARE)_

## Definition

from H0309; properly, hinder; generally, next, other, etc; (an-)other man, following, next, strange; a

- another, other, following
  - following, further
  - other, different
