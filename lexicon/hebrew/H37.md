# H37 אֲבִיטָל / אביטל

## ʼĂbîyṭâl

_(ab-ee-tal' | uh-bee-TAWL | uh-vee-TAHL)_

## Definition

my father is [the] dew; from H0001 and H2919; father of dew (i.e., fresh); Abital, a wife of King David; Abital; n-pr-f

- a wife of David
