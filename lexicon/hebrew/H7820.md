# H7820 שָׁחַט / שחט

## shâchaṭ

_(shaw-khat' | shaw-HAHT | sha-HAHT)_

## Definition

a primitive root (identical with H7819 through the idea of striking); to hammer out; beat; v

- to beat, hammer
  - (Qal) beaten (participle)
- to kill, slay
