# H8445 תּוֹקַהַת / תוקהת

## Tôwqahath

_(to-kah'-ath | toh-ka-HAHT | toh-ka-HAHT)_

## Definition

hope; from the same as H3349; obedience; Tokahath, an Israelite; Tikvath (by correction for H8616); n-pr-m

- father of Shallum, the husband of the prophetess Huldah in the time of king Josiah of Judah
