# H7652 שֶׁבַע / שבע

## shebaʻ

_(sheh'-bah | sheh-BA | sheh-VA)_

## Definition

seven; the same as H7651; seven; Sheba, the name of a place in Palestine, and of two Israelites; Sheba; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) Benjamite, son of Bichri and one who led a rebellion against David
- Gadite, brother of Michael, Meshullam, Jorai, Jachan, Zia, and Heber
- (n pr loc) a town in Simeon
