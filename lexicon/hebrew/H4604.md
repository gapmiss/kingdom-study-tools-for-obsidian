# H4604 מַעַל / מעל

## maʻal

_(mah'-al | ma-AL | ma-AL)_

## Definition

from H4603; treachery, i.e., sin; falsehood, grievously, sore, transgression, trespass, × very; n-m

- unfaithful or treacherous act, trespass
  - against man
  - against God
