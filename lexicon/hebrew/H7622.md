# H7622 שְׁבוּת / שבות

## shᵉbûwth

_(sheb-ooth' | sheh-BOOT | sheh-VOOT)_

## Definition

or שְׁבִית; from H7617; exile, concretely, prisoners; figuratively, a former state of prosperity; captive(-ity); n-f

- captivity, captives
