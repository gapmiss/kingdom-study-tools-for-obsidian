# H1469 גּוֹזָל / גוזל

_(go-zawl' | ɡoh-DZAWL | ɡoh-ZAHL)_

## Definition

or (shortened) גֹּזָל; from H1497; a nestling (as being comparatively nude of feathers); young (pigeon); n-m

- a nestling, young (of birds)
