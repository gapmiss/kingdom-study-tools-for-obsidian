# H2636 חַסְפַּס / חספס

## chaçpaç

_(khas-pas' | hahs-PAHS | hahs-PAHS)_

## Definition

reduplicated from an unused root meaning apparently to peel; a shred or scale; round thing; v

- (Pual) to peel, flake off (only in the participle)
  - scale-like, flake-like, scaled-off
