# H1066 בֹּכִים / בכים

## Bôkîym

_(bo-keem' | boh-KEEM | boh-HEEM)_

## Definition

weeping; plural active participle of H1058; (with the article) the weepers; Bo-kim, a place in Palestine; Bochim; n-pr-loc

- a place near Gilgal (or Bethel), where sons of Israel wept
