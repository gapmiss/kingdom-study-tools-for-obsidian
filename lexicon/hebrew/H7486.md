# H7486 רַעְמְסֵס / רעמסס

## Raʻmᵉçêç

_(rah-mes-ace' | ra-meh-SASE | ra-meh-SASE)_

## Definition

child of the sun; or רַעַמְסֵס; of Egyptian origin; Rameses or Raamses, a place in Egypt; Raamses, Rameses; n-pr-loc

- a city in lower Egypt built by Hebrew slaves; probably in Goshen
