# H8646 תֶּרַח / תרח

## Terach

_(teh'-rakh | teh-RA | teh-RAHK)_

## Definition

station, delay; of uncertain derivation; Terach, the father of Abraham; also a place in the Desert; Tarah, Terah; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) the father of Abraham
- (n pr loc) a station of Israel in the wilderness

## Note

Terah means "station," and Tarah means "delay."
