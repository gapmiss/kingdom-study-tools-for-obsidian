# H2042 הָרָר / הרר

## hârâr

_(haw-rawr' | haw-RAWR | ha-RAHR)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to loom up; a mountain; hill, mount(-ain); n-m

- mountain, hill, hill country, mount
