# H6679 צוּד / צוד

## tsûwd

_(tsood | tsood | tsood)_

## Definition

a denominative from H6718; to victual (for a journey); chase, hunt, sore, take (provision); v

- to hunt
  - (Qal) to hunt
  - (Poel) to hunt, hunt eagerly or keenly
  - (Hithpael) take provision
