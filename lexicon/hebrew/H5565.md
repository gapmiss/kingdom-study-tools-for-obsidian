# H5565 סְמַכְיָהוּ / סמכיהו

## Çᵉmakyâhûw

_(sem-ak-yaw'-hoo | seh-mahk-yaw-HOO | seh-mahk-ya-HOO)_

## Definition

Jehovah has sustained; from H5564 and H3050; supported of Jah; Semakjah, an Israelite; Semachiah; n-pr-m

- a Korahite Levite, one of the sons of Shemaiah
