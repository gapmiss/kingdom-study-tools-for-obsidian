# H6010 עֵמֶק / עמק

## ʻêmeq

_(ay'-mek | ay-MEK | ay-MEK)_

## Definition

from H6009; a vale (i.e., broad depression); dale, vale, valley (often used as a part of proper names); n-m

- valley, vale, lowland, open country
