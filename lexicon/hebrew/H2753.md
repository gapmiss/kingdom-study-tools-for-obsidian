# H2753 חֹרִי / חרי

## Chôrîy

_(kho-ree' | hoh-REE | hoh-REE)_

## Definition

cave dweller; or חוֹרִי; the same as H2752; Chori, the name of two men; Hori; n-pr-m

- a Horite, son of Lotan, the son of Seir
- a Simeonite, father of Shaphat
