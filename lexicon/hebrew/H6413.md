# H6413 פְּלֵיטָה / פליטה

## pᵉlêyṭâh

_(pel-ay-taw' | peh-lay-TAW | peh-lay-TA)_

## Definition

or פְּלֵטָה; feminine of H6412; deliverance; concretely, an escaped portion; deliverance, (that is) escape(-d), remnant; n-f

- escape, deliverance
  - escape, deliverance
  - escaped remnant
