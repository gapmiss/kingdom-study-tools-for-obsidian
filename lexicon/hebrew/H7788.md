# H7788 שׁוּר / שור

## shûwr

_(shoor | shoor | shoor)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to turn, i.e., travel about (as a harlot or a merchant); go, singular See also H7891; v

- (Qal) to travel, journey, go
  - traveller (participle)
  - meaning dubious
