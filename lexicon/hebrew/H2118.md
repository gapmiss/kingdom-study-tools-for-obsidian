# H2118 זָחַח / זחח

## zâchach

_(zaw-khakh' | dzaw-HA | za-HAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to shove or displace; loose; v

- to remove, displace
  - (Niphal) to be removed, be loosened, be displaced
