# H5603 סָפַן / ספן

## çâphan

_(saw-fan' | saw-PAHN | sa-FAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to hide by covering; specifically, to roof (passive participle as noun, a roof) or wainscot; figuratively, to reserve; cieled, cover, seated; v

- to cover, cover in, wainscotted, covered with boards or panelling
  - (Qal)
    - to cover in, panel
    - covered, panelled (participle)
