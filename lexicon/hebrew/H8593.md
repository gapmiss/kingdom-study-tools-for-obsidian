# H8593 תַּעַר / תער

## taʻar

_(tah'-ar | ta-AR | ta-AR)_

## Definition

from H6168; a knife or razor (as making bare); also a scabbard (as being bare, i.e., empty); (pen-)knife, razor, scabbard, shave, sheath; n

- razor, sheath (making naked)
  - razor
  - sheath (of sword)
