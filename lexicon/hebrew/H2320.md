# H2320 חֹדֶשׁ / חדש

## chôdesh

_(kho'-desh | hoh-DESH | hoh-DESH)_

## Definition

from H2318; the new moon; by implication, a month; month(-ly), new moon; n-m

- the new moon, month, monthly
  - the first day of the month
  - the lunar month
