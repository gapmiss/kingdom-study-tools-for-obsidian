# H2479 חַלְחָלָה / חלחלה

## chalchâlâh

_(khal-khaw-law' | hahl-haw-LAW | hahl-ha-LA)_

## Definition

feminine from the same as H2478; writhing (in childbirth); by implication, terror; (great, much) pain; n-f

- pain, trembling, terror, writhing, anguish
