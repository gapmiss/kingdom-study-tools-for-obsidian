# H6168 עָרָה / ערה

## ʻârâh

_(aw-raw' | aw-RAW | ah-RA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be (causatively, make) bare; hence, to empty, pour out, demolish; leave destitute, discover, empty, make naked, pour (out), rase, spread self, uncover; v

- to be bare, be nude, uncover, leave destitute, discover, empty, raze, pour out
  - (Piel)
    - to bare, lay bare
    - to lay bare by emptying, empty
    - to pour out
  - (Hiphil)
    - to make naked, strip bare (of sexual offences)
    - to pour out
  - (Niphal) to be poured out, be exposed
  - (Hithpael)
    - to expose oneself, make oneself naked
    - pouring oneself, spreading oneself (participle)
