# H2288 חֲגָו / חגו

## chăgâv

_(khag-awv' | huh-ɡAW | huh-ɡAHV)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to take refuge; a rift in rocks; cleft; n-m

- clefts, places of concealment, retreats
