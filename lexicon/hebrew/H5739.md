# H5739 עֵדֶר / עדר

## ʻêder

_(ay'-der | ay-DER | ay-DER)_

## Definition

from H5737; an arrangement, i.e., muster (of animals); drove, flock, herd; n-m

- flock, herd
  - flock
  - herds, flocks and herds
