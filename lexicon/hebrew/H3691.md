# H3691 כִּסְלֵו / כסלו

## Kiçlêv

_(kis-lave' | kis-LAY | kees-LAVE)_

## Definition

his confidence; probably of foreign origin; Kisleu, the 9th Hebrew month; Chisleu; n-pr-m

- the 9th month of the Hebrew calendar corresponding to November or December
