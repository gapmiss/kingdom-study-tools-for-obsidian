# H444 אָלַח / אלח

## ʼâlach

_(aw-lakh' | aw-LA | ah-LAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to muddle, i.e., (figuratively and intransitive) to turn (morally) corrupt; become filthy; v

- (Niphal) to be corrupt morally, tainted
