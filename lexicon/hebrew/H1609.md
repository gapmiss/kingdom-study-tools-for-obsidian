# H1609 גַּעְתָּם / געתם

## Gaʻtâm

_(gah-tawm' | ɡa-TAWM | ɡa-TAHM)_

## Definition

a burnt valley; of uncertain derivation; Gatam, an Edomite; Gatam; n-pr-m

- fourth son of Eliphaz and grandson of Esau and one of the dukes of Eliphaz
