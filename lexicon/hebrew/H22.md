# H22 אֲבִיאֵל / אביאל

## ʼĂbîyʼêl

_(ab-ee-ale' | uh-bee-ALE | uh-vee-ALE)_

## Definition

El (God) is [my] father; from H0001 and H0410; father (i.e., possessor) of God; Abiel, the name of two Israelites; Abiel; n-pr-m

- Saul's grandfather
