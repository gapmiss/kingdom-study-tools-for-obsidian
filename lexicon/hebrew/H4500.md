# H4500 מָנוֹר / מנור

## mânôwr

_(maw-nore' | maw-NORE | ma-NORE)_

## Definition

from H5214; a yoke (properly, for plowing), i.e., the frame of a loom; beam; n-m

- beam
  - beam (carrying the heddles in a loom)
