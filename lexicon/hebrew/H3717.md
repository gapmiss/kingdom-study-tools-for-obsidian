# H3717 כָּפַל / כפל

## kâphal

_(kaw-fal' | kaw-PAHL | ka-FAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to fold together; figuratively, to repeat; double; v

- to double, fold double, double over
  - (Qal) to double over
  - (Niphal) to be doubled
