# H7378 רִיב / ריב

## rîyb

_(reeb | reeb | reev)_

## Definition

or רוּב; a primitive root; properly, to toss, i.e., grapple; mostly figuratively, to wrangle, i.e., hold a controversy; (by implication) to defend; adversary, chide, complain, contend, debate, × ever, × lay wait, plead, rebuke, strive, × thoroughly; v

- to strive, contend
  - (Qal)
    - to strive
      - physically
      - with words
    - to conduct a case or suit (legal), sue
    - to make complaint
    - to quarrel
  - (Hiphil) to contend against
