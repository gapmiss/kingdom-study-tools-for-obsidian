# H7397 רֵכָה / רכה

## Rêkâh

_(ray-kaw' | ray-KAW | ray-HA)_

## Definition

uttermost part; probably feminine from H7401; softness; Rekah, a place in Palestine; Rechah; n-pr-loc

- a place in Judah
  - perhaps the village of 'Rashiah' 3 miles (5 km) south of Jerusalem
