# H1675 דָּאָה / דאה

## dâʼâh

_(daw-aw' | daw-AW | da-AH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to dart, i.e., fly rapidly; fly; v

- to fly fast, fly swiftly, dart through the air
  - (Qal) to fly swiftly, dart
