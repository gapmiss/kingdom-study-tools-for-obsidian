# H8035 שֵׁם / שם

## Shêm

_(shame | shame | shame)_

## Definition

name; the same as H8034; name; Shem, a son of Noah (often including his posterity); Sem, Shem; n-pr-m

- the eldest son of Noah and progenitor of the Semitic tribes
