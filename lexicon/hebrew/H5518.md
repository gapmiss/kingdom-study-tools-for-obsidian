# H5518 סִיר / סיר

## çîyr

_(seer | seer | seer)_

## Definition

or (feminine) סִירָה; or סִרָה; (Jeremiah 52:18), from a primitive root meaning to boil up; a thorn (as springing up rapidly); by implication, a hook; caldron, fishhook, pan, (wash-)pot, thorn; n-m

- pot
  - pot (household utensil)
  - pot (of temple)
- thorn, hook, brier
  - thorns
  - hooks
