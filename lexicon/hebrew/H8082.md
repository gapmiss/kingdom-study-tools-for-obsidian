# H8082 שָׁמֵן / שמן

## shâmên

_(shaw-mane' | shaw-MANE | sha-MANE)_

## Definition

from H8080; greasy, i.e., gross; figuratively, rich; fat, lusty, plenteous; a

- fat, rich, robust
  - fat, rich (of food)
  - stout, robust (of men)
