# H7426 רָמַם / רמם

## râmam

_(raw-mam' | raw-MAHM | ra-MAHM)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to rise (literally or figuratively); exalt, get (oneself) up, lift up (self), mount up; v

- to be exalted, be lifted up
  - (Qal) to be exalted
  - (Niphal) to lift oneself
