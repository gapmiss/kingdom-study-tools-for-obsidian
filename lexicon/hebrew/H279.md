# H279 אֲחִיאָם / אחיאם

## ʼĂchîyʼâm

_(akh-ee-awm' | uh-hee-AWM | uh-hee-AM)_

## Definition

mother's brother; from H0251 and H0517; brother of the mother (i.e., uncle); Achiam, an Israelite; Ahiam; n-pr-m

- one of David's warriors (heroes)
