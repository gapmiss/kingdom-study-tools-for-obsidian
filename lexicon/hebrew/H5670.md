# H5670 עָבַט / עבט

## ʻâbaṭ

_(aw-bat' | aw-BAHT | ah-VAHT)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to pawn; causatively, to lend (on security); figuratively, to entangle; borrow, break (ranks), fetch (a pledge), lend, × surely; v

- to take a pledge, give a pledge (for a debt)
  - (Qal) to take a pledge, give a pledge (for a debt)
  - (Piel) to interchange
  - (Hiphil) to cause to give a pledge
