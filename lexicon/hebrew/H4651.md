## mappâl

_(map-pawl' | mahp-PAWL | ma-PAHL)_

## Definition

from H5307; a falling off, i.e., chaff; also something pendulous, i.e., a flap; flake, refuse; n-m

- refuse, hanging parts
  - fallings, refuse
  - hanging parts (of a crocodile)
