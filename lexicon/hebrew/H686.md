# H686 אָצַר / אצר

## ʼâtsar

_(aw-tsar' | aw-TSAHR | ah-TSAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to store up; (lay up in) store, (make) treasure(-r); v

- to store up, save, lay up
  - (Qal) to store up, make treasurer
  - (Niphal) treasured
