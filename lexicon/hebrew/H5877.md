# H5877 עֵין חָצוֹר / עין חצור

## ʻÊyn Châtsôwr

_(ane khaw-tsore' | ane haw-TSORE | ane ha-TSORE)_

## Definition

fount of Hazor; from H5869 and the same as H2674; fountain of a village; En-Chatsor, a place in Palestine; En-hazor; n-pr-loc

- one of the fenced cities in the territory of Naphtali
