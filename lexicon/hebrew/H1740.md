# H1740 דּוּחַ / דוח

## dûwach

_(doo'-akh | DOO-ah | DOO-ak)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to thrust away; figuratively, to cleanse; cast out, purge, wash; v

- to rinse, cleanse away by rinsing, cast out, purge, wash, washing
  - (Hiphil)
    - to rinse
    - to cleanse (by washing) (figuratively)
