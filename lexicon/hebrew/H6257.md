# H6257 עָתַד / עתד

## ʻâthad

_(aw-thad' | aw-TAHD | ah-TAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to prepare; make fit, be ready to become; v

- to be ready, make ready, prepare
  - (Piel) to make ready
  - (Hithpael) to be prepared
