# H8130 שָׂנֵא / שנא

## sânêʼ

_(saw-nay' | saw-NAY | sa-NAY)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to hate (personally); enemy, foe, (be) hate(-ful, -r), odious, × utterly; v

- to hate, be hateful
  - (Qal) to hate
    - of man
    - of God
    - hater, one hating, enemy (participle) (subst)
  - (Niphal) to be hated
  - (Piel) hater (participle)
    - of persons, nations, God, wisdom
