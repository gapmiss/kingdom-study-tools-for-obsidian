# H884 בְּאֵר שֶׁבַע / באר שבע

## Bᵉʼêr Shebaʻ

_(be-ayr' sheh'-bah | beh-ARE sheh-BA | beh-ARE sheh-VA)_

## Definition

well of the sevenfold oath; from H0875 and H7651 (in the sense of H7650); well of an oath; Beer-Sheba, a place in Palestine; Beer-shebah; n-pr-loc

- a city at the south edge of Israel
