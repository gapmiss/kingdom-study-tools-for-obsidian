# H673 אֶפְרָתִי / אפרתי

## ʼEphrâthîy

_(ef-rawth-ee' | ep-raw-TEE | ef-ra-TEE)_

## Definition

ashiness, fruitfulness; patrial from H0672; an Ephrathite or an Ephraimite; Ephraimite, Ephrathite; a

- an inhabitant or descendant of Ephraim
- an inhabitant of Bethlehem
