# H6278 עֵת קָצִין / עת קצין

## ʻÊth Qâtsîyn

_(ayth kaw-tseen' | ate kaw-TSEEN | ate ka-TSEEN)_

## Definition

time of the judge; from H6256 and H7011; time of a judge; Eth-Katsin, a place in Palestine; Ittahkazin (by including directive enclitic); n-pr-loc

- one of the landmarks of the boundary of Zebulun; site unknown
