# H6984 קוּשָׁיָהוּ / קושיהו

## qûwshâyâhûw

_(koo-shaw-yaw'-hoo | koo-shaw-yaw-HOO | koo-sha-ya-HOO)_

## Definition

bow of Jehovah; from the passive participle of H6983 and H3050; entrapped of Jah; Kushajah, an Israelite; Kushaiah; n-pr-m

- father of Ethan the Merarite
  - same as 'Kish' or 'Kishi'
