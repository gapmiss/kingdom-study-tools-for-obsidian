# H1150 בִּנְעָא / בנעא

## Binʻâʼ

_(bin-aw' | bin-AW | been-AH)_

## Definition

fountain; or בִּנְעָה; of uncertain derivation; Bina or Binah, an Israelite; Binea, Bineah; n-pr-m

- a descendant of Jonathan
