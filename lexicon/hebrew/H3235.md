# H3235 יָמַר / ימר

## yâmar

_(yaw-mar' | yaw-MAHR | ya-MAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to exchange; by implication, to change places; boast selves, change; v

- to exchange
  - (Hiphil) to exchange
  - (Hithpael) to enter into, be changed into
