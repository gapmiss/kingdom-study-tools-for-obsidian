# H143 אֲדָר / אדר

## ʼĂdâr

_(ad-awr' | uh-DAWR | uh-DAHR)_

## Definition

glorious; probably of foreign derivation; perhaps meaning fire; Adar, the 12th Hebrew month; Adar; n

- twelfth month, corresponding to modern March or April
