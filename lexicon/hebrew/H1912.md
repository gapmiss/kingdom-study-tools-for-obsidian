# H1912 הֹדוּ / הדו

## Hôdûw

_(ho'-doo | hoh-DOO | hoh-DOO)_

## Definition

flee away, give ye thanks; of foreign origin; Hodu (i.e., Hindustan); India; n-pr-loc

- the country surrounding the Indus, mentioned as the eastern border of the empire of Ahasuerus
