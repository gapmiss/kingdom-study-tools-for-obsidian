# H7502 רָפַד / רפד

## râphad

_(raw-fad' | raw-PAHD | ra-FAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to spread (a bed); by implication, to refresh; comfort, make (a bed), spread; v

- to spread
  - (Qal) to spread
  - (Piel) to spread out, support
