# H6265 עֲתָיָה / עתיה

## ʻĂthâyâh

_(ath-aw-yaw' | uh-taw-YAW | uh-ta-YA)_

## Definition

Jehovah has helped; from H5790 and H3050; Jah has helped; Athajah, an Israelite; Athaiah; n-pr-m

- a descendant of Judah through Perez; dwelt at Jerusalem after the return from exile in Babylon
