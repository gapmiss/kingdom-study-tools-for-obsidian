# H3957 לִשְׁכָּה / לשכה

## lishkâh

_(lish-kaw' | lish-KAW | leesh-KA)_

## Definition

from an unused root of uncertain meaning; a room in a building (whether for storage, eating, or lodging); chamber, parlour; n-f

- room, chamber, hall, cell

Compare H5393.
