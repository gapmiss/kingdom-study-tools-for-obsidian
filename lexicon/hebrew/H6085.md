# H6085 עֶפְרוֹן / עפרון

## ʻEphrôwn

_(ef-rone' | ep-RONE | ef-RONE)_

## Definition

fawn-like; from the same as H6081; fawn-like; Ephron, the name of a Canaanite and of two places in Palestine; Ephron, Ephrain (from the margin); n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a Hittite, son of Zohar and the one from whom Abraham bought the field and cave of Machpelah
- (n pr loc) a city on the borders of Benjamin
- a mountain on the northern border of Judah
