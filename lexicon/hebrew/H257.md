# H257 אַחְבָן / אחבן

## ʼAchbân

_(akh-bawn' | ah-BAWN | ak-VAHN)_

## Definition

brother of an understanding (intelligent) one; from H0251 and H0995; brother (i.e., possessor) of understanding; Achban, an Israelite; Ahban; n-pr-m

- son of Abishur of Judah
