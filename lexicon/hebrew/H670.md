# H670 אֲפָרְסַי / אפרסי

## ʼĂphârᵉçay

_(af-aw-re-sah'ee | uh-pore-SAI | uh-fore-SAI)_

## Definition

causers of division(?); of foreign origin (only in the plural); an Apherasite or inhabitant of an unknown region of Assyria; Apharsite; n-pr-m

- a group of people (perhaps Persians) who were brought into Samaria
- perhaps secretaries
