_(yek-ale' | yeh-KALE | yeh-HALE)_

or יְכִיל; (Aramaic), to H3201; to be able, literally (can, could) or morally (may, might); be able, can, couldest, prevail; v

- to be able
  - (P'al)
    - to be able
    - to prevail
