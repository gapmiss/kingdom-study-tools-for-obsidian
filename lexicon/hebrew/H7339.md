# H7339 רְחֹב / רחב

## rᵉchôb

_(rekh-obe' | reh-HOBE | reh-HOVE)_

## Definition

or רְחוֹב; from H7337; a width, i.e., (concretely) avenue or area; broad place (way), street; n-f

- broad or open place or plaza
