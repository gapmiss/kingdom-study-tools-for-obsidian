# H8319 שָׁרַק / שרק

## shâraq

_(shaw-rak' | shaw-RAHK | sha-RAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to be shrill, i.e., to whistle or hiss (as a call or in scorn); hiss; v

- to hiss, whistle, pipe
  - (Qal) to hiss (as a signal)
