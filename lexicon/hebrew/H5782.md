# H5782 עוּר / עור

## ʻûwr

_(oor | oor | oor)_

## Definition

a primitive root (rather identical with through the idea of opening the eyes); to wake (literally or figuratively); (a-)wake(-n) (up), lift up (self), × master, raise (up), stir up (self); v

- to rouse oneself, awake, awaken, incite
  - (Qal) to rouse oneself, awake
  - (Niphal) to be roused
  - (Polel) to stir up, rouse, incite
  - (Hithpolel) to be excited, be triumphant
  - (Hiphil)
    - to rouse, stir up
    - to act in an aroused manner, awake
