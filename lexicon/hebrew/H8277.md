# H8277 שָׂרַד / שרד

## sârad

_(saw-rad' | saw-RAHD | sa-RAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; (compare H8279); properly, to puncture, i.e., (figuratively through the idea of slipping out) to escape or survive; remain; v

- (Qal) to escape, survive
