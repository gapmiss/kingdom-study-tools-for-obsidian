# H6032 עֲנָה / ענה

## ʻănâh

_(an-aw' | uh-NAW | uh-NA)_

## Definition

corresponding to H6030; properly, to eye or (generally) to heed, i.e., pay attention; by implication, to respond; by extension to begin to speak; specifically to sing, shout, testify, announce; answer, speak; v

- to answer, respond
  - (P'al)
    - to answer, make reply
    - to respond
