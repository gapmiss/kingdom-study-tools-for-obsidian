# H6714 צֹחַר / צחר

## Tsôchar

_(tso'-khar | tsoh-HAHR | tsoh-HAHR)_

## Definition

tawny; from the same as H6713; whiteness; Tsochar, the name of a Hittite and of an Israelite; Zohar; n-pr-m

- a Judaite, son of Ashur by wife Helah
- father of Ephron the Hittite
- one of the sons of Simeon; also 'Zerah'

## Note

Compare H3328.
