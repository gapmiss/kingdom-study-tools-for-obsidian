# H2241 זֵתָם / זתם

## Zêthâm

_(zay-thawm' | dzay-TAWM | zay-TAHM)_

## Definition

olive; apparently a variation for H2133; Zetham, an Israelite; Zetham; n-pr-m

- a Gershonite Levite, the son of Jehieli and grandson of Laadan
