# H6311 פֹּה / פה

## pôh

_(po | poh | poh)_

## Definition

or פֹּא; (Job 38:11), or פּוֹ; probably from a primitive inseparable particle 'p' (of demonstrative force) and H1931; this place (French ici), i.e., here or hence; here, hither, the one (other, this, that) side; adv

- here, from here, hither
  - here
  - hither
