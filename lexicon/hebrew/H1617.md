# H1617 גֵּרָא / גרא

## Gêrâʼ

_(gay-raw' | ɡay-RAW | ɡay-RA)_

## Definition

a grain; perhaps from H1626; a grain; Gera, the name of six Israelites; Gera; n-pr-m

- a son of Benjamin
- a son of Bela and grandson of Benjamin
- any member of the Gera family of the Benjamite tribe
