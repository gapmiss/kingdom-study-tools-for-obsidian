# H1364 גָּבֹהַּ / גבה

## gâbôahh

_(gaw-bo'-ah | ɡaw-BOH-ah | ɡa-VOH-ah)_

## Definition

or גָּבוֹהַּ; (fully) from H1361; elevated (or elated), powerful, arrogant; haughty, height, high(-er), lofty, proud, × exceeding proudly; a

- high, exalted
  - high, tall
  - high (in position)
  - proud, haughty
- (n m) loftiness
