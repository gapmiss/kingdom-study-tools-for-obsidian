# H6002 עֲמָלֵק / עמלק

## ʻĂmâlêq

_(am-aw-lake' | uh-maw-LAKE | uh-ma-LAKE)_

## Definition

dweller in a valley; probably of foreign origin; Amalek, a descendant of Esau; also his posterity and their country; Amalek; n-pr-m

- son of Eliphaz by his concubine Timnah, grandson of Esau, and progenitor of a race of people in southern Canaan
- descendants of Amalek
