# H5511 סִיחוֹן / סיחון

## Çîychôwn

_(see-khone' | see-HONE | see-HONE)_

## Definition

warrior; or סִיחֹן; from the same as H5477; tempestuous; Sichon, an Amoritish king; Sihon; n-pr-m

- king of the Amorites at the time of the conquest and defeated by Moses in Transjordan
