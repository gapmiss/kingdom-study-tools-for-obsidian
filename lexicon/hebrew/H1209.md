# H1209 בֵּצַי / בצי

## Bêtsay

_(bay-tsah'-ee | bay-TSAI | bay-TSAI)_

## Definition

conqueror; perhaps the same as H1153; Betsai, the name of two Israelites; Bezai; n-pr-m

- a chief of the returning exiles in the time of Ezra
