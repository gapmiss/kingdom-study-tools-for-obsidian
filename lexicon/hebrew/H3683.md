# H3683 כָּסַח / כסח

## kâçach

_(kaw-sakh' | kaw-SA | ka-SAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to cut off; cut down (up); v

- to cut down, cut away, cut off (of plants)
  - (Qal) cut away (pass participle)
    - of judgment (figuratively)
