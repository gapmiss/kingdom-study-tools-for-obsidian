# H2404 חָטַב / חטב

## châṭab

_(khaw-tab' | haw-TAHB | ha-TAHV)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to chop or carve wood; cut down, hew(-er), polish; v

- to cut, gather
  - (Qal) to cut, gather (wood)
  - (Pual) to cut, carve
