# H7849 שָׁטַח / שטח

## shâṭach

_(shaw-takh' | shaw-TA | sha-TAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to expand; all abroad, enlarge, spread, stretch out; v

- to spread, spread abroad, stretch out
  - (Qal)
    - to spread
    - spreading, expanding (participle)
  - (Piel) to spread out
