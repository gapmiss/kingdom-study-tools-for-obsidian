# H2629 חָסַם / חסם

## châçam

_(khaw-sam' | haw-SAHM | ha-SAHM)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to muzzle; by analogy, to stop the nose; muzzle, stop; v

- (Qal) to stop up, muzzle
