# H5405 נָשַׁת / נשת

## nâshath

_(naw-shath' | naw-SHAHT | na-SHAHT)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to eliminate, i.e., (intransitively) to dry up; fail; v

- to be dry, be parched
  - (Qal) to be dry, be parched
  - (Niphal) to be dried up
