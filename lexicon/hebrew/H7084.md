# H7084 קְעִילָה / קעילה

## Qᵉʻîylâh

_(keh-ee-law' | keh-ee-LAW | keh-ee-LA)_

## Definition

fortress; perhaps from H7049 in the sense of enclosing; citadel; Keilah, a place in Palestine; Keilah; n-pr-loc

- a city in the lowlands of Judah northwest of Hebron
