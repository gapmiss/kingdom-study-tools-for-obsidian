# H8176 שָׁעַר / שער

## shâʻar

_(shaw-ar' | shaw-AR | sha-AR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to split or open, i.e., (literally, but only as denominative from H8179); compare see H7778; to act as gatekeeper; (figuratively) to estimate; think; v

- to split open, reason out, calculate, reckon, estimate
  - (Qal) to calculate Proverbs 23:7
