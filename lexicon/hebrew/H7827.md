# H7827 שְׁחֵלֶת / שחלת

## shᵉchêleth

_(shekh-ay'-leth | sheh-hay-LET | sheh-hay-LET)_

## Definition

apparently from the same as H7826 through some obscure idea, perhaps that of peeling off by concussion of sound; a scale or shell, i.e., the aromatic mussel; onycha; n-f

- onycha
  - an ingredient used in the holy incense
