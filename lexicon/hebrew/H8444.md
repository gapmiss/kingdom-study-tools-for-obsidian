# H8444 תּוֹצָאָה / תוצאה

## tôwtsâʼâh

_(to-tsaw-aw' | toh-tsaw-AW | toh-tsa-AH)_

## Definition

or תֹּצָאָה; from H3318; (only in plural collective) exit, i.e., (geographical) boundary, or (figuratively) deliverance, (actively) source; border(-s), going(-s) forth (out), issues, outgoings; n-f

- outgoing, border, a going out, extremity, end, source, escape
  - outgoing, extremity (of border)
  - source (of life)
  - escape (from death)
