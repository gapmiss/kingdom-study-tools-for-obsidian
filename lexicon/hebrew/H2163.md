# H2163 זָמַן / זמן

## zâman

_(zaw-man' | dzaw-MAHN | za-MAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to fix (a time); appoint; v

- to appoint a time, be fixed, be appointed
  - (Pual)
    - to be fixed, be appointed
    - appointed times (participle)
