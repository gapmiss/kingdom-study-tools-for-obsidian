# H308 אֲחַסְבַּי / אחסבי

## ʼĂchaçbay

_(akh-as-bah'ee | uh-hahs-BAI | uh-hahs-BAI)_

## Definition

brother of my encompassers; of uncertain derivation; Achasbai, an Israelite; Ahasbai; n-pr-f

- father of Eliphelet, one of David's heroes
