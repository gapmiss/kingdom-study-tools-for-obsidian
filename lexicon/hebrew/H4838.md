# H4838 מָרַק / מרק

## mâraq

_(maw-rak' | maw-RAHK | ma-RAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to polish; by implication, to sharpen; also to rinse; bright, furbish, scour; v

- to scour, polish
  - (Qal) to scour, polish
  - (Pual) to be scoured
