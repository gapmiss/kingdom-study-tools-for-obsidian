# H7386 רֵיק / ריק

## rêyq

_(rake | rake | rake)_

## Definition

or (shorter) רֵק; from H7324; empty; figuratively, worthless; emptied(-ty), vain (fellow, man); a

- empty, vain
  - empty (of vessels)
  - empty, idle, worthless (ethically)
