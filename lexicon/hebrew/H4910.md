# H4910 מָשַׁל / משל

## mâshal

_(maw-shal' | maw-SHAHL | ma-SHAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to rule; (have, make to have) dominion, governor, × indeed, reign, (bear, cause to, have) rule(-ing, -r), have power; v

- to rule, have dominion, reign
  - (Qal) to rule, have dominion
  - (Hiphil)
    - to cause to rule
    - to exercise dominion
