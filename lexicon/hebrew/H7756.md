# H7756 שׂוּכָתִי / שוכתי

## Sûwkâthîy

_(soo-kaw-thee' | soo-kaw-TEE | soo-ha-TEE)_

## Definition

bush-men; probably patronymic from a name corresponding to H7754 (feminine); a Sukathite or descendant of an unknown Israelite named Sukah; Suchathite; a

- a family of scribes at Jabez and descendants of Judah through Caleb
