# H5106 נוּא / נוא

## nûwʼ

_(noo | noo | noo)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to refuse, forbid, dissuade, or neutralize; break, disallow, discourage, make of none effect; v

- to hinder, hold back, forbid, disallow, restrain, frustrate
  - (Qal) to hinder, hold back
  - (Hiphil)
    - to restrain, forbid, frustrate
    - to restrain, make averse, discourage
