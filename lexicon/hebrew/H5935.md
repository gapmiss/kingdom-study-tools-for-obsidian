# H5935 עַלְוָן / עלון

## ʻAlvân

_(al-vawn' | al-WAWN | al-VAHN)_

## Definition

tall; or עַלְיָן; from H5927; lofty; Alvan or Aljan, an Idumaean; Alian, Alvan; n-pr-m

- a Horite, son of Shobal
