# H5562 סַמְגַּר נְבוֹ / נבו

## Çamgar Nᵉbôw

_(sam-gar' neb-o' | sahm-ɡAHR neh-BOH | sahm-ɡAHR neh-VOH)_

## Definition

sword of Nebo; of foreign origin; Samgar-Nebo, a Babylonian general; Samgar-nebo; n-pr-m

- one of the princes or generals of king Nebuchadnezzar of Babylon
