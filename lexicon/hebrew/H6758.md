## Tsalmônâh

_(tsal-mo-naw' | tsahl-moh-NAW | tsahl-moh-NA)_

## Definition

shady; feminine of H6757; shadiness; Tsalmonah, a place in the Desert; Zalmonah; n-pr-loc
