# H8002 שֶׁלֶם / שלם

## shelem

_(sheh'-lem | sheh-LEM | sheh-LEM)_

## Definition

from H7999; properly, requital, i.e., a (voluntary) sacrifice in thanks; peace offering; n-m

- peace offering, requital, sacrifice for alliance or friendship
  - voluntary sacrifice of thanks
