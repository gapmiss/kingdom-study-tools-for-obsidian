# H6020 עַמְרָמִי / עמרמי

## ʻAmrâmîy

_(am-raw-mee' | am-raw-MEE | am-ra-MEE)_

## Definition

exalted; from H6019; an Amramite or descendant of Amram; Amramite; a

- a branch of the Kohathite family of the tribe of Levi and descendants of Amram, the father of Moses
