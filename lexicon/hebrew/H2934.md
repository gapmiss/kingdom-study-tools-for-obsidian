# H2934 טָמַן / טמן

## ṭâman

_(taw-man' | taw-MAHN | ta-MAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to hide (by covering over); hide, lay privily, in secret; v

- to hide, conceal, bury
  - (Qal)
    - to hide
    - hiding, concealing, secretly laying (participle)
    - darkness (participle)
  - (Niphal) to hide oneself
  - (Hiphil) to hide
