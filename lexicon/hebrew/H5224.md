# H5224 נְכוֹ / נכו

## Nᵉkôw

_(nek-o' | neh-KOH | neh-HOH)_

## Definition

lame; probably of Egyptian origin; Neko, an Egyptian king; Necho; n-pr-m

- the Pharaoh of Egypt in the time of king Josiah of Judah
