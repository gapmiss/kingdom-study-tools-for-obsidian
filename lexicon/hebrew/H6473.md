# H6473 פָּעַר / פער

## pâʻar

_(paw-ar' | paw-AR | pa-AR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to yawn, i.e., open wide (literally or figuratively); gape, open (wide); v

- to open wide, gape
  - (Qal) to gape
