# H7679 שָׂגָא / שגא

## sâgâʼ

_(saw-gaw' | saw-ɡAW | sa-ɡA)_

## Definition

 a primitive root; to grow, i.e., (causatively) to enlarge, (figuratively) laud; increase, magnify; v

- to increase, grow, magnify, grow great
  - (Hiphil)
    - to make great
    - to magnify, laud
