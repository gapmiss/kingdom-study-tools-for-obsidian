# H7522 רָצוֹן / רצון

## râtsôwn

_(raw-tsone' | raw-TSONE | ra-TSONE)_

## Definition

or רָצֹן; from H7521; delight (especially as shown); (be) acceptable(-ance, -ed), delight, desire, favour, (good) pleasure, (own, self, voluntary) will, as...(what) would; n-m

- pleasure, delight, favor, goodwill, acceptance, will
  - goodwill, favor
  - acceptance
  - will, desire, pleasure, self-will
