# H5802 עַזְבּוּק / עזבוק

## ʻAzbûwq

_(az-book' | adz-BOOK | az-BOOK)_

## Definition

strong devastation; from H5794 and the root of H0950; stern depopulator; Azbuk, an Israelite; Azbuk; n-pr-m

- father of Nehemiah, leader of half the district of Beth Zur, and a repairer of the walls of Jerusalem under another Nehemiah
