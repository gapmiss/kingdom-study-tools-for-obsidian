# H3095 יַהֲלֹם / יהלם

## yahălôm

_(yah-hal-ome' | ya-huh-LOME | ya-huh-LOME)_

## Definition

from H1986 (in the sense of hardness); a precious stone, probably onyx; diamond; n-m

- a precious stone (known for its hardness)
  - perhaps the jasper, onyx, or diamond
