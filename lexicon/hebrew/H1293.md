# H1293 בְּרָכָה / ברכה

## Bᵉrâkâh

_(ber-aw-kaw' | beh-raw-KAW | beh-ra-HA)_

## Definition

from H1288; benediction; by implication prosperity; blessing, liberal, pool, present; n-f

- blessing
- (source of) blessing
- blessing, prosperity
- blessing, praise of God
- a gift, present
- treaty of peace
