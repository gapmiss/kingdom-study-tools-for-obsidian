# H628 אֲסְפְּסֻף / אספסף

## ʼăçpᵉçuph

_(as-pes-oof' | us-peh-SOOP | us-peh-SOOF)_

## Definition

by reduplication from H0624; gathered up together, i.e., a promiscuous assemblage (of people); mixt multitude; n-m

- collection, rabble, collected multitude
