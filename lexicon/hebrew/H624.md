# H624 אָסֻף / אסף

## ʼâçuph

_(aw-soof' | aw-SOOP | ah-SOOF)_

## Definition

passive participle of H0622; collected (only in the plural), i.e., a collection of offerings); threshold, Asuppim; n-m

- what is gathered, store, storing, storehouse
- threshold (metonymy)
