# H2145 זָכָר / זכר

## zâkâr

_(zaw-kawr' | dzaw-KAWR | za-HAHR)_

## Definition

from H2142; properly, remembered, i.e., a male (of man or animals, as being the most noteworthy sex); × him, male, man(child, -kind); n-m

- (n m) male (of humans and animals)
- (adj) male (of humans)
