# H1110 בָּלַק / בלק

## bâlaq

_(baw-lak' | baw-LAHK | ba-LAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to annihilate; (make) waste; v

- to waste, lay waste, devastate
  - (Poel) to make waste
  - (Pual) devastated (participle)
