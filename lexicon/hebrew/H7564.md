# H7564 רִשְׁעָה / רשעה

## rishʻâh

_(rish-aw' | rish-AW | reesh-AH)_

## Definition

feminine of H7562; wrong (especially moral); fault, wickedly(-ness); n-f

- wickedness, guilt
  - wickedness (in civil relations)
  - wickedness (of enemies)
  - wickedness (ethical and religious)
