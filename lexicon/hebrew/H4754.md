# H4754 מָרָא / מרא

## mârâʼ

_(maw-raw' | maw-RAW | ma-RA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to rebel; hence (through the idea of maltreating) to whip, i.e., lash (self with wings, as the ostrich in running); be filthy, lift up self; v

- (Hiphil) to beat (the air), flap (the wings)
  - meaning dubious
- (Qal) filthy
