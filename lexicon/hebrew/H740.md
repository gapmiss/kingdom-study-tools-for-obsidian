# H740 אֲרִיאֵל / אריאל

## ʼĂrîyʼêl

_(ar-ee-ale' | uh-ree-ALE | uh-ree-ALE)_

## Definition

lion of God, lioness of God; the same as H0739; Ariel, a symbolical name for Jerusalem, also the name of an Israelite; Ariel; n-pr

- a name applied to Jerusalem
- the name of a chief of the returning exiles
