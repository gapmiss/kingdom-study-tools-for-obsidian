# H7891 שִׁיר / שיר

## shîyr

_(sheer | sheer | sheer)_

## Definition

or (the original form) שׁוּר; (1 Samuel 18:6), a primitive root (rather identical with H7788 through the idea of strolling minstrelsy); to sing; behold (by mistake for H7789), sing(-er, -ing man, -ing woman); v

- to sing
  - (Qal)
    - to sing
    - singer, songstresses (participle)
  - (Polel)
    - to sing
    - singer, songstress (participle)
  - (Hophal) to be sung
