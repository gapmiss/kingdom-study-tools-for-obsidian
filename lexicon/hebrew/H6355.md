# H6355 פַּחַת מוֹאָב / פחת מואב

## Pachath Môwʼâb

_(pakh'-ath mo-awb' | pa-HAHT moh-AWB | pa-HAHT moh-AV)_

## Definition

pit of Moab; from H6354 and H4124; pit of Moab; Pachath-Moab, an Israelite; Pahathmoab; n-pr-m

- ancestor of one or two families of Israel who returned from exile in Babylon
- a leader of the people, father of the wall rebuilder Hashub, and one who signed the covenant with Nehemiah
