# H4807 מְרִיב בַּעַל / מריב בעל

## Mᵉrîyb Baʻal

_(mer-eeb' bah'-al | meh-REEB ba-AL | meh-REEV ba-AL)_

## Definition

Baal is my advocate; from H7378 and H1168; quarreller of Baal, Merib-Baal, an epithet of Gideon; Merib-baal; n-pr-m

- another name for Mephibosheth
  - son of Jonathan and grandson of Saul

## Note

Compare H4810.
