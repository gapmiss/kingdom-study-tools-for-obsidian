# H737 אֲרֻחָה / ארחה

## ʼăruchâh

_(ar-oo-khaw' | uh-roo-HAW | uh-roo-HA)_

## Definition

feminine passive participle of H0732 (in the sense of appointing); a ration of food; allowance, diet, dinner, victuals; n-f

- meal, allowance, ration
  - food
  - diet
