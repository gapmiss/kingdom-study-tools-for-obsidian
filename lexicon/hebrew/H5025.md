# H5025 נֹבַח / נבח

## Nôbach

_(no'-bach | noh-BA | noh-VAHK)_

## Definition

barking; from H5024; a bark; Nobach, the name of an Israelite, and of a place East of the Jordan; Nobah; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a warrior of Manasseh who during the conquest of the territory on the east of the Jordan possessed himself of the town of Kenath and surrounding villages
- (n pr loc) a place in Gilead which comprised the towns of Kenath and its surrounding villages captured by 1 above and renamed after himself; area eventually regained its original identity
