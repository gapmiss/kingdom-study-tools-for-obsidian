# H7107 קָצַף / קצף

## qâtsaph

_(kaw-tsaf' | kaw-TSAHP | ka-TSAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to crack off, i.e., (figuratively) burst out in rage; (be) anger(-ry), displease, fret self, (provoke to) wrath (come), be wroth; v

- to be displeased, be angry, fret oneself, be wroth
  - (Qal) to be wroth
  - (Hiphil) to provoke to wrath or anger
  - (Hithpael) to put oneself in a rage, anger oneself
