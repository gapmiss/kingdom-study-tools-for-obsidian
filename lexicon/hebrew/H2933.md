# H2933 טָמָה / טמה

## ṭâmâh

_(taw-maw' | taw-MAW | ta-MA)_

## Definition

a collateral form of H2930; to be impure in a religious sense; be defiled, be reputed vile; v

- (Niphal) to be stopped up
  - to be regarded as unclean (metaplastic)
