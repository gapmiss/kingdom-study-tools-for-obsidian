# H5372 נִרְגָּן / נרגן

## nirgân

_(neer-gawn' | nir-ɡAWN | neer-ɡAHN)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to roll to pieces; a slanderer; talebearer, whisperer; v

- to murmur, whisper
  - murmurers
  - backbite, slander, talebearer, backbiter
