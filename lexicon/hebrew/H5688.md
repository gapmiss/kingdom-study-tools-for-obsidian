# H5688 עֲבֹת / עבת

## ʻăbôth

_(ab-oth' | uh-BOTE | uh-VOTE)_

## Definition

or עֲבוֹת; or (feminine) עֲבֹתָה; the same as H5687; something intwined, i.e., a string, wreath or foliage; band, cord, rope, thick bough (branch), wreathen (chain); n

- cord, rope, cordage, foliage, interwoven foliage
  - cord, rope, cordage, chain
  - interwoven foliage
