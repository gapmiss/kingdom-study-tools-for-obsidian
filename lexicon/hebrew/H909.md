# H909 בָּדַד / בדד

## bâdad

_(baw-dad' | baw-DAHD | ba-DAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to divide, i.e., (reflexive) be solitary; alone; v

- to withdraw, be separate, be isolated
  - (Qal) an army straggler (part.)
    - of Ephraim (metaphorically)
- alone
