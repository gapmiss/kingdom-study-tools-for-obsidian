# H4714 מִצְרַיִם / מצרים

## Mitsrayim

_(mits-rah'-yim | mits-ra-YIM | meets-ra-YEEM)_

## Definition

land of the Copts (Egyptians), double straits; dual of H4693; Mitsrajim, i.e., Upper and Lower Egypt; Egypt, Egyptians, Mizraim; n-pr

- (n pr loc) a country at the northeastern section of Africa, adjacent to Palestine, and through which the Nile flows
- (adj) the inhabitants or natives of Egypt
