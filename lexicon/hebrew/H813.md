# H813 אַשְׁכְּנַז / אשכנז

## ʼAshkᵉnaz

_(ash-ken-az' | ash-keh-NAHDZ | ash-keh-NAHZ)_

## Definition

a man as sprinkled, fire as scattered; of foreign origin; Ashkenaz, a Japhethite, also his descendants; Ashkenaz; n-pr-m

- a descendant of Japheth
- a northern people, perhaps of Bithynia
