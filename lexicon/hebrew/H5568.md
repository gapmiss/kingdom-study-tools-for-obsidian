# H5568 סָמַר / סמר

## çâmar

_(saw-mar' | saw-MAHR | sa-MAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be erect, i.e., bristle as hair; stand up, tremble; v

- to bristle up, shiver, stand up
  - (Qal) to bristle up, shiver
  - (Piel) to bristle up, stand up
