# H5132 נוּץ / נוץ

## nûwts

_(noots | noots | noots)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to flash; hence, to blossom (from the brilliancy of color); flee away, bud (forth); v

- (Qal) to fly, flee
- (Hiphil) to bloom, blossom
