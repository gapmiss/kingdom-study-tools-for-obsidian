# H8446 תּוּר / תור

## tûwr

_(toor | toor | toor)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to meander (causatively, guide) about, especially for trade or reconnoitring; chap(-man), sent to descry, be excellent, merchant(-man), search (out), seek, (e-)spy (out); v

- to seek, search out, spy out, explore
  - (Qal)
    - to seek out, select, find out how to do something
    - to spy out, explore
      - explorers, spies (participle)
    - to go about
      - merchant, trader (participle)
  - (Hiphil) to make a search, make a reconnaissance
