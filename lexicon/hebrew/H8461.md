# H8461 תַּחְכְּמֹנִי / תחכמני

## Tachkᵉmônîy

_(takh-kem-o-nee' | ta-keh-moh-NEE | tahk-keh-moh-NEE)_

## Definition

thou will make me wise; probably for H2453; sagacious; Tachkemoni, an Israelite; Tachmonite; n-pr-m

- descriptive designation of Josheb-basshebeth, one of David's mighty warriors
