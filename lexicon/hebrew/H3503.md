# H3503 יִתְרוֹ / יתרו

## Yithrôw

_(yith-ro' | yit-ROH | yeet-ROH)_

## Definition

his abundance; from H3499 with pron. suffix; his excellence; Jethro, Moses' father-in-law; Jethro; n-pr-m

- father-in-law of Moses; also 'Jether'

## Note

Compare H3500.
