# H1588 גַּן / גן

## gan

_(gan | ɡahn | ɡahn)_

## Definition

from H1598; a garden (as fenced); garden; n

- (n m/f) garden, enclosure
  - enclosed garden
    - (figurative for a bride)
  - garden (of plants)
  - (n pr loc) Garden of Eden
