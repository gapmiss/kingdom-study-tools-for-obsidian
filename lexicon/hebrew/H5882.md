_(ane eg-lah'-yim | ane eɡ-la-YIM | ane eɡ-la-YEEM)_

## Definition

fountain of the two calves; עַיִן and the dual of H5695; fountain of two calves; En-Eglajim, a place in Palestine; En-eglaim; n-pr-loc

- a place on the Dead Sea; site uncertain
