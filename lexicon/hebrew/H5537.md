# H5537 סָלָא / סלא

## çâlâʼ

_(saw-law' | saw-LAW | sa-LA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to suspend in a balance, i.e., weigh; compare; v

- to weigh, compare
  - (Pual)
    - to be weighed
    - weighed (participle)
