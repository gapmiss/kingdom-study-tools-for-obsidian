# H5880 עֵין מִשְׁפָּט / עין משפט

## ʻÊyn Mishpâṭ

_(ane mish-pawt' | ane mish-PAWT | ane meesh-PAHT)_

## Definition

spring of judgment; from H5869 and H4941; fountain of judgment; En-Mishpat, a place near Palestine; En-mishpat; n-pr-loc

- a place near southern Palestine coincident or the same place as Kadesh
