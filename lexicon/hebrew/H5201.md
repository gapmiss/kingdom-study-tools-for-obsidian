# H5201 נָטַר / נטר

## nâṭar

_(naw-tar' | naw-TAHR | na-TAHR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to guard; figuratively, to cherish (anger); bear grudge, keep(-er), reserve; v

- to keep, keep guard, reserve, maintain
  - (Qal)
    - to keep, maintain
    - to keep, guard
