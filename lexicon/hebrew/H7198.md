# H7198 קֶשֶׁת / קשת

## qesheth

_(keh'-sheth | keh-SHET | keh-SHET)_

## Definition

from H7185 in the original sense (of H6983) of bending; a bow, for shooting (hence, figuratively, strength) or the iris; × arch(-er), + arrow, bow(-man, -shot); n-f

- bow
  - bow (for hunting, battle)
  - bowmen, archers
  - bow (figurative for might)
  - rainbow
