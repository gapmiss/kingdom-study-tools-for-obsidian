# H6871 צְרוּעָה / צרועה

## Tsᵉrûwʻâh

_(tser-oo-aw' | tseh-roo-AW | tseh-roo-AH)_

## Definition

full breasted; feminine passive participle of H6879; leprous; Tseruah, an Israelitess; Zeruah; n-pr-f

- wife of Nebat and mother of king Jeroboam I of the northern kingdom of Israel
