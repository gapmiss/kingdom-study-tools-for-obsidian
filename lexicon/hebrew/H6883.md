# H6883 צָרַעַת / צרעת

## tsâraʻath

_(tsaw-rah'-ath | tsaw-ra-AT | tsa-ra-AT)_

## Definition

from H6879; leprosy; leprosy; n-f

- leprosy
  - in people, malignant skin disease (Leviticus 13–14)
  - in clothing, a mildew or mould (Leviticus 13:47–52)
  - in buildings, a mildew or mould (Leviticus 14:34–53)
