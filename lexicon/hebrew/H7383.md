# H7383 רִיפָה / ריפה

## rîyphâh

_(ree-faw' | ree-PAW | ree-FA)_

## Definition

or רִפָה; from H7322; (only plural), grits (as pounded); ground corn, wheat; n-f

- a grain or fruit (for grinding)
  - meaning dubious
