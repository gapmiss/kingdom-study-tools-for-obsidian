## Chăkalyâh

_(khak-al-yaw' | huh-kahl-YAW | huh-hahl-YA)_

## Definition

whom Jehovah enlightens; from the base of H2447 and H3050; darkness of Jah; Chakaljah, an Israelite; Hachaliah; n-pr-m

- the father of Nehemiah
