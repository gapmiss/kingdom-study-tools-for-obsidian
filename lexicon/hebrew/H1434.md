# H1434 גְּדִל / גדל

## gᵉdil

_(ghed-eel' | ɡeh-DIL | ɡeh-DEEL)_

## Definition

from H1431 (in the sense of twisting); thread, i.e., a tassel or festoon; fringe, wreath; n-m

- twisted threads, tassels, festoons
  - tassels (on clothes)
  - festoons (on capitals of columns)
