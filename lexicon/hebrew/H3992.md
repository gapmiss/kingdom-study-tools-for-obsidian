# H3992 מָאַר / מאר

## mâʼar

_(maw-ar' | maw-AR | ma-AR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be bitter or (causatively) to embitter, i.e., be painful; fretting, picking; v

- (Hiphil) to pain, prick, irritate, be in pain
