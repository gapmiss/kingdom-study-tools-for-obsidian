# H3196 יַיִן / יין

## yayin

_(yah'-yin | ya-YIN | ya-YEEN)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to effervesce; wine (as fermented); by implication, intoxication; banqueting, wine, wine(-bibber); n-m

- wine
