# H8564 תַּמְרוּר / תמרור

## tamrûwr

_(tam-roor' | tahm-ROOR | tahm-ROOR)_

## Definition

from the same root as H8558; an erection, i.e., pillar (probably for a guide-board); high heap; n-m

- guideposts, markers, signposts, high heap
  - meaning doubtful
