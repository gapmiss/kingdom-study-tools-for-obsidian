# H6556 פֶּרֶץ / פרץ

## perets

_(peh'-rets | peh-RETS | peh-RETS)_

## Definition

from H6555; a break (literally or figuratively); breach, breaking forth (in), × forth, gap; n-m

- breach, gap, bursting forth
  - bursting forth, outburst
  - breach
  - broken wall
  - outburst (figurative for God's wrath)
