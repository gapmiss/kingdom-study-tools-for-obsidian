# H4494 מָנוֹחַ / מנוח

## mânôwach

_(maw-no'-akh | maw-NOH-ah | ma-NOH-ak)_

## Definition

from H5117; quiet, i.e., (concretely) a settled spot, or (figuratively) a home; (place of) rest; n-m

- resting place, state or condition of rest, place
  - resting place
  - rest, repose, condition of rest
  - coming to rest
