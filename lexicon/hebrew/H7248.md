# H7248 רַב־מָג / רבמג

## Rab-Mâg

_(rab-mawg' | rahb mawɡ | rahv mahɡ)_

## Definition

from H7227 and a foreign word for a Magian; chief Magian; Rab-Mag, a Babylonian official; Rab-mag; n-m

- soothsayer, magician, chief soothsayer
  - Rab-mag, chief soothsayer, or chief of princes, an official of Babylonia
