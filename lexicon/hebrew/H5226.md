# H5226 נֵכַח / נכח

## nêkach

_(nay'-kakh | nay-KA | nay-HAHK)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to be straightforward; properly, the fore part; used adverbially, opposite; before, over against; adv

- be in front of
- (adv) in front of, opposite to, in the sight of, before, to the front, right on
- (prep) towards the front of, in front of, on behalf of, as far as in front of
