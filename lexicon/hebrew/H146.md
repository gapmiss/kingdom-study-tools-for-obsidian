# H146 אַדָּר / אדר

## ʼAddâr

_(ad-dawr' | ad-DAWR | ah-DAHR)_

## Definition

exceeding glorious; intensive from H0142; ample; Addar, a place in Palestine; also an Israelite; Addar; n-pr-m

- son of Bela and Benjamin's grandson
- city in Judah near Edom
