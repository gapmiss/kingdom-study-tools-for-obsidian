# H5071 נְדָבָה / נדבה

## nᵉdâbâh

_(ned-aw-baw' | neh-daw-BAW | neh-da-VA)_

## Definition

from H5068; properly (abstractly) spontaneity, or (adjectively) spontaneous; also (concretely) a spontaneous or (by inference, in plural) abundant gift; free(-will) offering, freely, plentiful, voluntary(-ily, offering), willing(-ly), offering); n-f

- voluntariness, free-will offering
  - voluntariness
  - freewill, voluntary, offering
