# H7434 רָמַת הַמִּצְפֶּה / רמת המצפה

## Râmath ham-Mit-speh

_(raw-math' ham-mitspeh' | raw-MAHT hahm-mits-PEH | ra-MAHT ha-meets-PEH)_

## Definition

high place of the watch tower; from H7413 and H4707 with the article interpolated; height of the watch-tower; Ramath-ham-Mitspeh, a place in Palestine; Ramath-mizpeh; n-pr-loc

- a place in Gilead on the northern border of Gad
