# H8504 תְּכֵלֶת / תכלת

## tᵉkêleth

_(tek-ay'-leth | teh-kay-LET | teh-hay-LET)_

## Definition

probably for H7827; the cerulean mussel, i.e., the color (violet) obtained therefrom or stuff dyed therewith; blue; n-f

- violet, violet stuff
  - violet thread
  - violet stuff or fabric
- blue
