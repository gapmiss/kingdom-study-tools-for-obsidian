# H7125 קִרְאָה / קראה

## qirʼâh

_(keer-aw' | kir-AW | keer-AH)_

## Definition

from H7122; an encountering, accidental, friendly or hostile (also adverbially, opposite); × against (he come), help, meet, seek, × to, × in the way; n-m

- (n m) to encounter, befall, meet
  - (Qal)
    - to meet, encounter
    - to befall (figuratively)
