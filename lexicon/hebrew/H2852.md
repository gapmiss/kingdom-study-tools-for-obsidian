# H2852 חָתַךְ / חתך

## châthak

_(khaw-thak' | haw-TAHK | ha-TAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to cut off, i.e., (figuratively) to decree; determine; v

- to divide, determine
  - (Niphal) to be determined, be decreed, be settled, be marked out
