# H6514 פְּרוּדָא / פרודא

## Pᵉrûwdâʼ

_(per-oo-daw' | peh-roo-DAW | peh-roo-DA)_

## Definition

grain, kernel; or פְּרִידָא; from H6504; dispersion; Peruda or Perida, one of 'Solomon's servants'; Perida, Peruda; n-pr-m

- an ancestor of a family of temple slaves who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
