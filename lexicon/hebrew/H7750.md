# H7750 שׂוּט / שוט

## sûwṭ

_(soot | soot | soot)_

## Definition

or (by permutation) סוּט; a primitive root; to detrude, i.e., (intransitively and figuratively) become derelict (wrongly practise; namely, idolatry); turn aside to; v

- to swerve, fall away
  - (Qal)
    - to swerve, fall away
    - those falling away (participle)
