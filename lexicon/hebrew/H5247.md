# H5247 נִמְרָה / נמרה

## Nimrâh

_(nim-raw' | nim-RAW | neem-RA)_

## Definition

limpid, pure; from the same as H5246; clear water; Nimrah, a place East of the Jordan; Nimrah; n-pr-loc

- a place on the east of the Jordan, 10 miles (16 km) north of the Dead Sea and 3 miles (5 km) east of the Jordan
