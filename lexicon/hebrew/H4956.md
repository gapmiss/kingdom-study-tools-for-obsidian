# H4956 מִשְׂרְפוֹת מַיִם / משרפות מים

## Misrᵉphôwth mayim

_(mis-ref-ohth' mah'-yim | mis-reh-POTE ma-YIM | mees-reh-FOTE ma-YEEM)_

## Definition

burnings of water; from the plural of H4955 and H4325; burnings of water; Misrephoth-Majim, a place in Palestine; Misrephoth-mayim; n-pr-loc

- a place in northern Palestine near Sidon
