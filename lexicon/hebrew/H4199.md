# H4199 מִזָּה / מזה

## Mizzâh

_(miz-zaw' | midz-ZAW | mee-ZA)_

## Definition

fear; probably from an unused root meaning to faint with fear; terror; Mizzah, an Edomite; Mizzah; n-pr-m

- son of Reuel and grandson of Esau
