# H4968 מְתוּשֶׁלַח / מתושלח

## Mᵉthûwshelach

_(meth-oo-sheh'-lakh | meh-too-sheh-LA | meh-too-sheh-LAHK)_

## Definition

when he dies judgment, when he dies it will be sent, man of the spear(?); from H4962 and H7973; man of a dart; Methushelach, an antediluvian patriarch; Methuselah; n-pr-m

- son of Enoch, 6th in descent from Seth, and father of Lamech and other sons and daughters

## Note

Methuselah was the oldest person in recorded history dieing at the age of 969, shortly before Noah's flood.
