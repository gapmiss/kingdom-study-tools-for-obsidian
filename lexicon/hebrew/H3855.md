# H3855 לַהַד / להד

## Lahad

_(lah'-had | la-HAHD | la-HAHD)_

## Definition

oppression; from an unused root meaning to glow (compare H3851) or else to be earnest (compare H3854); Lahad, an Israelite; Lahad; n-pr-m

- son of Jahath, a descendant of Judah
