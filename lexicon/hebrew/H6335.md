# H6335 פּוּשׁ / פוש

## pûwsh

_(poosh | poosh | poosh)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to spread; figuratively, act proudly; grow up, be grown fat, spread selves, be scattered; v

- to spring about
  - (Qal) to frisk, act proudly (figuratively)
- (Niphal) to be scattered, be spread
