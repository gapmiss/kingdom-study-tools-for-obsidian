# H8273 שָׁרָב / שרב

## shârâb

_(shaw-rawb' | shaw-RAWB | sha-RAHV)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to glare; quivering glow (of the air), especially the mirage; heat, parched ground; n-m

- burning or scorching heat, parched ground
