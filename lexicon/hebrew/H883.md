# H883 בְּאֵר לַחַי רֹאִי / באר לחי ראי

## Bᵉʼêr la-Chay Rôʼîy

_(be-ayr' lakh-ah'ee ro-ee' | beh-ARE la-HAI roh-EE | beh-ARE la-HAI roh-EE)_

## Definition

well of the Living One seeing me; from H0875 and H2416 (with prefix) and H7203; well of a living (One) my Seer; Beer-Lachai-Roi, a place in the Desert; Beer-lahai-roi; n-pr-loc

- a well west of Kadesh, south of Israel
