# H7906 שֵׂכוּ / שכו

## Sêkûw

_(say'-koo | say-KOO | say-HOO)_

## Definition

the watch-tower; from an unused root apparently meaning to surmount; an observatory (with the article); Seku, a place in Palestine; Sechu; n-pr-loc

- a place near Ramah with a great well
