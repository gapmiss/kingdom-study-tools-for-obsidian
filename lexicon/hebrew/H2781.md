# H2781 חֶרְפָּה / חרפה

## cherpâh

_(kher-paw' | her-PAW | her-PA)_

## Definition

from H2778; contumely, disgrace, the pudenda; rebuke, reproach(-fully), shame; n-f

- reproach, scorn
  - taunt, scorn (upon enemy)
  - reproach (resting upon condition of shame, disgrace)
  - a reproach (an object)
