# H7650 שָׁבַע / שבע

## shâbaʻ

_(shaw-bah' | shaw-BA | sha-VA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly to be complete, but used only as a denominative from H7651; to seven oneself, i.e., swear (as if by repeating a declaration seven times); adjure, charge (by an oath, with an oath), feed to the full (by mistake for H7646), take an oath, × straitly, (cause to, make to) swear; v

- to swear, adjure
  - (Qal) sworn (participle)
  - (Niphal)
    - to swear, take an oath
    - to swear (of Jehovah by Himself)
    - to curse
  - (Hiphil)
    - to cause to take an oath
    - to adjure
