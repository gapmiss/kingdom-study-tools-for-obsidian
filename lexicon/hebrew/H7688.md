# H7688 שָׁגַח / שגח

## shâgach

_(shaw-gakh' | shaw-ɡA | sha-ɡAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to peep, i.e., glance sharply at; look (narrowly); v

- (Hiphil) to gaze, stare
