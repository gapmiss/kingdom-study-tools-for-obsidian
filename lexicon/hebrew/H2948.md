# H2948 טֹפַח / טפח

## ṭôphach

_(to'-fakh | toh-PA | toh-FAHK)_

## Definition

from H2946 (the same as H2947); a spread of the hand, i.e., a palm-breadth (not 'span' of the fingers); architecturally, a corbel (as a supporting palm); hand-breadth (broad); n-m

- span, hand breadth
