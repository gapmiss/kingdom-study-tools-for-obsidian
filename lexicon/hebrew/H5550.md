# H5550 סֹלְלָה / סללה

## çôlᵉlâh

_(so-lel-aw' | soh-leh-LAW | soh-leh-LA)_

## Definition

or סוֹלְלָה; active participle feminine of H5549, but used passively; a military mound, i.e., rampart of besiegers; bank, mount; n-f

- mound
