# H1659 גָּשַׁשׁ / גשש

## gâshash

_(gaw-shash' | ɡaw-SHAHSH | ɡa-SHAHSH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; apparently; to feel about; grope; v

- to feel with the hand, grope, stroke, feel
  - (Piel) to grope, grope for, feel with the hand
