# H8658 תַּרְשִׁישׁ / תרשיש

## tarshîysh

_(tar-sheesh' | tahr-SHEESH | tahr-SHEESH)_

## Definition

probably of foreign derivation (compare H8659); a gem, perhaps the topaz; beryl; n-m

- a precious stone or semi-precious gem
  - perhaps a chrysolite, yellow jasper, or other yellow colored stone
