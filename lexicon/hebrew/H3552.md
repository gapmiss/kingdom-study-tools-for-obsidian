# H3552 כּוּב / כוב

## Kûwb

_(koob | koob | koov)_

## Definition

of foreign derivation; Kub, a country near Egypt; Chub; n-pr-loc

- the name of a people in alliance with Nebuchadnezzar and probably located in northern Africa; maybe same as 'Lybia' or 'Lub'
