# H3940 לַפִּיד / לפיד

## lappîyd

_(lap-peed' | lahp-PEED | la-PEED)_

## Definition

or לַפִּד; from an unused root probably meaning to shine; a flambeau, lamp or flame; (fire-)brand, (burning) lamp, lightning, torch; n-m

- torch
