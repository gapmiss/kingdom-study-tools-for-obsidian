# H4628 מַעֲרָב / מערב

## maʻărâb

_(mah-ar-awb' | ma-uh-RAWB | ma-uh-RAHV)_

## Definition

or (feminine) מַעֲרָבָה; from H6150, in the sense of shading; the west (as a region of the evening sun); west; n-m

- setting place, west, westward
