# H683 אֲצַלְיָהוּ / אצליהו

## ʼĂtsalyâhûw

_(ats-al-yaw'-hoo | uh-tsahl-yaw-HOO | uh-tsahl-ya-HOO)_

## Definition

Jehovah has reserved (set apart); from H0680 and H3050 prolonged; Jah has reserved; Atsaljah, an Israelite; Azaliah; n-pr-m

- the father of Shaphan the scribe
