# H94 אָגוּר / אגור

## ʼÂgûwr

_(aw-goor' | aw-ɡOOR | ah-ɡOOR)_

## Definition

gathered; passive participle of H0103; gathered (i.e., received among the sages); Agur, possibly a symbolic name for Solomon; Agur; n-pr-m

- son of Jakeh, an author or compiler of Proverbs 30, possibly Solomon
