# H3471 יָשְׁפֵה / ישפה

## yâshᵉphêh

_(yaw-shef-ay' | yohsh-PAY | yohsh-FAY)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to polish; a gem supposed to be jasper (from the resemblance in name); jasper; n-m

- jasper (a precious stone)
