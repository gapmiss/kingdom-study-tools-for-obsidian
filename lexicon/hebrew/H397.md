# H397 אֲכִישׁ / אכיש

## ʼĂkîysh

_(aw-keesh' | uh-KEESH | uh-HEESH)_

## Definition

I will blacken (or terrify), only a man; of uncertain derivation; Akish, a Philistine king; Achish; n-pr-m

- Philistine king of Gath
