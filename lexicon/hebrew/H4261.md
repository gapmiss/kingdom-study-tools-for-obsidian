# H4261 מַחְמָד / מחמד

## machmâd

_(makh-mawd' | ma-MAWD | mahk-MAHD)_

## Definition

from H2530; delightful; hence, a delight, i.e., object of affection or desire; beloved, desire, goodly, lovely, pleasant (thing); n-m

- desire, desirable thing, pleasant thing
