# H3135 יוֹעָשׁ / יועש

## Yôwʻâsh

_(yo-awsh' | yoh-OHSH | yoh-OHSH)_

## Definition

Jehovah hastens; from H3068 and H5789; Jehovahhastened; Joash, the name of two Israelites; Joash; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite, son of Becher
- one of the officers in David's court
