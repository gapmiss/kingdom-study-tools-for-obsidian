# H759 אַרְמוֹן / ארמון

## ʼarmôwn

_(ar-mone' | ar-MONE | ar-MONE)_

## Definition

from an unused root (meaning to be elevated); a citadel (from its height); castle, palace; n-m

- citadel, palace, fortress
