# H5038 נְבֵלָה / נבלה

## nᵉbêlâh

_(neb-ay-law' | neh-bay-LAW | neh-vay-LA)_

## Definition

from H5034; a flabby thing, i.e., a carcase or carrion (human or bestial, often collectively); figuratively, an idol; (dead) body, (dead) carcase, dead of itself, which died, (beast) that (which) dieth of itself; n-f

- carcass, corpse
  - of humans, idols, animals
