# H5062 נָגַף / נגף

## nâgaph

_(naw-gaf' | naw-ɡAHP | na-ɡAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to push, gore, defeat, stub (the toe), inflict (a disease); beat, dash, hurt, plague, slay, smite (down), strike, stumble, × surely, put to the worse; v

- to strike, smite
  - (Qal) to strike, smite
  - (Niphal) to be stricken, be smitten
  - (Hithpael) to stumble
