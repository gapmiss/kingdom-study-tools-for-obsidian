# H714 אַרְדְּ / ארד

## ʼArd

_(ard | ah-RED | ah-RED)_

## Definition

I shall subdue; from an unused root probably meaning to wander; fugitive; Ard, the name of two Israelites; Ard; n-pr-m

- son of Benjamin
- grandson of Benjamin
