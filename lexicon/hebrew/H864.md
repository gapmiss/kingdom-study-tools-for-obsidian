# H864 אֵתָם / אתם

## ʼÊthâm

_(ay-thawm' | ay-TAWM | ay-TAHM)_

## Definition

with them, their plowshare; of Egyptian derivation; Etham, a place in the Desert; Etham; n-pr-loc

- a stopping place for Israel during the Exodus
