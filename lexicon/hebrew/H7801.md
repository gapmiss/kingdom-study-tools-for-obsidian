# H7801 שׁוּשַׁנְכִי / שושנכי

## Shûwshankîy

_(shoo-shan-kee' | shoo-shahn-KEE | shoo-shahn-HEE)_

## Definition

lily; of foreign origin; a Shushankite (collectively) or inhabitants of some unknown place in Assyrian; Susanchites; n-pr

- the inhabitants of the city of Shushan or Susa

[[Ezra 4#9|Ezra 4:9]]
