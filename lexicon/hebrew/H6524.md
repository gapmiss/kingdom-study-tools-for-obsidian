# H6524 פָּרַח / פרח

## pârach

_(paw-rakh' | paw-RA | pa-RAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to break forth as a bud, i.e., bloom; generally, to spread; specifically, to fly (as extending the wings); figuratively, to flourish; × abroad, × abundantly, blossom, break forth (out), bud, flourish, make fly, grow, spread, spring (up); v

- to bud, sprout, shoot, bloom
  - (Qal) to bud, sprout, send out shoots, blossom
  - (Hiphil)
    - to cause to bud or sprout
    - to show buds or sprouts
- (Qal) to break out (of leprosy)
- (Qal) to fly
