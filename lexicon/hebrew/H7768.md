# H7768 שָׁוַע / שוע

## shâvaʻ

_(shaw-vah' | shaw-WA | sha-VA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to be free; but used only causatively and reflexively, to halloo (for help, i.e., freedom from some trouble); cry (aloud, out), shout; v

- (Piel) to cry out (for help), shout
