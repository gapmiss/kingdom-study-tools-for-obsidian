# H8550 תֻּמִּים / תמים

## Tummîym

_(toom-meem' | toom-MEEM | too-MEEM)_

## Definition

perfection; plural of H8537; perfections, i.e., (technically) one of the epithets of the objects in the high-priest's breastplate as an emblem of complete Truth; Thummim; n-pr-m

- stones provided for the means of achieving a sacred lot
  - used with the Urim, the will of God was revealed
