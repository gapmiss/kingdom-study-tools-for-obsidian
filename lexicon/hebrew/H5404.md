# H5404 נֶשֶׁר / נשר

## nesher

_(neh'-sher | neh-SHER | neh-SHER)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to lacerate; the eagle (or other large bird of prey); eagle; n-m

- eagle, vulture, griffon-vulture
