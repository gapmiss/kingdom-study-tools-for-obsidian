# H4355 מָכַךְ / מכך

## mâkak

_(maw-kak' | maw-KAHK | ma-HAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to tumble (in ruins); figuratively, to perish; be brought low, decay; v

- to be low, be humiliated
  - (Qal) to be low, be humiliated
  - (Niphal) to sink
  - (Hophal) to be brought low
