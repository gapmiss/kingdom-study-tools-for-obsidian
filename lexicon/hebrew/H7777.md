# H7777 שׁוּעָל / שועל

## Shûwʻâl

_(shoo-awl' | shoo-AWL | shoo-AL)_

## Definition

jackal; the same as H7776; Shual, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine; Shual; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) as Asherite, son of Zophah
- (n pr loc) a district in Benjamin probably north of Michmash
