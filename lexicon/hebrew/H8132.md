# H8132 שָׁנָא / שנא

## shânâʼ

_(shaw-naw' | shaw-NAW | sha-NA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to alter; change; v

- to change, alter
  - (Qal) to change
  - (Piel) to change, alter
  - (Pual) to be changed
