# H1071 בֶכֶר / בכר

## Beker

_(beh'-ker | beh-KER | veh-HER)_

## Definition

young camel; the same as H1070; Beker, the name of two Israelites; Becher; n-pr-m

- a son of Ephraim
- a son of Benjamin
