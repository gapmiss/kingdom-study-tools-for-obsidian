# H5467 סְדֹם / סדם

## Çᵉdôm

_(sed-ome' | seh-DOME | seh-DOME)_

## Definition

burning; from an unused root meaning to scorch; burnt (i.e., volcanic or bituminous) district; Sedom, a place near the Dead Sea; Sodom; n-pr-loc

- a Canaanite city, usually paired with Gomorrah, located in the area of the Dead Sea and the Jordan river; both cities destroyed by God in judgment
