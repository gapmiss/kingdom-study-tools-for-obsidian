# H6352 פֶּחָם / פחם

## pechâm

_(peh-khawm' | peh-HAWM | peh-HAHM)_

## Definition

perhaps from an unused root probably meaning to be black; a coal, whether charred or live; coals; n-m

- coal, charcoal, ember
