# H8056 שָׂמֵחַ / שמח

## sâmêach

_(saw-may'-akh | saw-MAY-ah | sa-MAY-ak)_

## Definition

from H8055; blithe or gleeful; (be) glad, joyful, (making) merry(-hearted, -ily), rejoice(-ing); a

- joyful, merry, glad
  - joyful, showing joy
  - you who rejoice (subst)
