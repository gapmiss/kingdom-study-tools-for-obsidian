# H7158 קִרְיַת סַנָּה / קרית סנה

## Qiryath Çannâh

_(keer-yath' san-naw' | kir-YAHT sahn-NAW | keer-YAHT sa-NA)_

## Definition

city of the book; or קִרְיַת סֵפֶר; from H7151 and a simpler feminine from the same as H5577, or (for the latter name) H5612; city of branches, or of a book; Kirjath-Sannah or Kirjath-Sepher, a place in Palestine; Kirjath-sannah, Kirjath-sepher; n-pr-loc

- a town in the mountains of Judah to the west of Hebron
