# H3472 יִשְׁפָּה / ישפה

## Yishpâh

_(yish-paw' | yish-PAW | yeesh-PA)_

## Definition

He is bare; perhaps from H8192; he will scratch; Jishpah, an Israelite; Ispah; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite of the family of Beriah and one of the heads of his tribe
