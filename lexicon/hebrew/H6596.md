# H6596 פֹּת / פת

## pôth

_(pohth | pote | pote)_

## Definition

or פֹּתָה; (Ezekiel 13:19), from an unused root meaning to open; a hole, i.e., hinge or the female pudenda; hinge, secret participle; n-f

- sockets, hinges, secret parts (meaning dubious)
  - sockets
  - secret parts
