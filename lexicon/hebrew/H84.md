# H84 אֶבְרָה / אברה

## ʼebrâh

_(eb-raw' | eb-RAW | ev-RA)_

## Definition

feminine of H0083; wing; feather, wing; n-f

- pinion, wing
  - of bird (ostrich, eagle, dove)
  - of God (metaphorically)
