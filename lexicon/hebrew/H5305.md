# H5305 נָפִישׁ / נפיש

## Nâphîysh

_(naw-feesh' | naw-PEESH | na-FEESH)_

## Definition

refreshment; from H5314; refreshed; Naphish, a son of Ishmael, and his posterity; Naphish; n-pr-m

- the next to last son of Ishmael
- an Arabian tribe
