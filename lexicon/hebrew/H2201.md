# H2201 זַעַק / זעק

## zaʻaq

_(zah'-ak | dza-AK | za-AK)_

## Definition

and (feminine) זְעָקָה; from H2199; a shriek or outcry; cry(-ing); n-f

- cry, outcry
  - outcry
  - cry of distress
  - outcry, clamour
