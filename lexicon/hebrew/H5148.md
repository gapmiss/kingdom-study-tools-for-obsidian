# H5148 נָחָה / נחה

## nâchâh

_(naw-khaw' | naw-HAW | na-HA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to guide; by implication, to transport (into exile, or as colonists); bestow, bring, govern, guide, lead (forth), put, straiten; v

- to lead, guide
  - (Qal) to lead, bring
  - (Hiphil) to lead, guide
