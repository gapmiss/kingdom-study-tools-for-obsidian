# H5268 נִסְרֹךְ / נסרך

## Niçrôk

_(nis-roke' | nis-ROKE | nees-ROKE)_

## Definition

the great eagle; of foreign origin; Nisrok, a Babylonian idol; Nisroch; n-pr-m

- an idol of Nineveh worshipped by Sennacherib; symbolised by the eagle-headed human figure
