# H6696 צוּר / צור

## tsûwr

_(tsoor | tsoor | tsoor)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to cramp, i.e., confine (in many applications, literally and figuratively, formative or hostile); adversary, assault, beset, besiege, bind (up), cast, distress, fashion, fortify, inclose, lay siege, put up in bags; v

- to bind, besiege, confine, cramp
  - (Qal)
    - to confine, secure
    - to shut in, beseige
    - to shut up, enclose
- (Qal) to show hostility to, be an adversary, treat as foe
- (Qal) to form, fashion, delineate
