# H8469 תַּחֲנוּן / תחנון

## tachănûwn

_(takh-an-oon' | ta-huh-NOON | ta-huh-NOON)_

## Definition

or (feminine) תַּחֲנוּנָה; from H2603; earnest prayer; intreaty, supplication; n-m

- supplication, supplication for favor
  - to man
  - to God
