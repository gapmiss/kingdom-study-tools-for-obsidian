# H8121 שֶׁמֶשׁ / שמש

## shemesh

_(sheh'-mesh | sheh-MESH | sheh-MESH)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to be brilliant; the sun; by implication, the east; figuratively, a ray, i.e., (architectural) a notched battlement; + east side(-ward), sun (rising), + west(-ward), window; n

- sun
  - sun
  - sunrise, sun-rising, east, sun-setting, west (of direction)
  - sun (as object of illicit worship)
  - openly, publicly (in other phrases)
  - pinnacles, battlements, shields (as glittering or shining)
