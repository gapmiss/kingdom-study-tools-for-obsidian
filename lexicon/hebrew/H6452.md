# H6452 פָּסַח / פסח

## pâçach

_(paw-sakh' | paw-SA | pa-SAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to hop, i.e., (figuratively) skip over (or spare); by implication, to hesitate; also (literally) to limp, to dance; halt, become lame, leap, pass over; v

- to pass over, spring over
  - (Qal) to pass over
  - (Piel) to skip, pass over
- to limp
  - (Qal) to limp
  - (Niphal) to be lame
  - (Piel) to limp
