# H4017 מִבְשָׂם / מבשם

## Mibsâm

_(mib-sawm' | mib-SAWM | meev-SAHM)_

## Definition

sweet odor; from the same as H1314; fragrant; Mibsam, the name of an Ishmaelite and of an Israelite; Mibsam; n-pr-m

- a son of Ishmael
- a descendant of Simeon
