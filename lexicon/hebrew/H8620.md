# H8620 תְּקוֹעַ / תקוע

## Tᵉqôwaʻ

_(tek-o'-ah | teh-KOH-ah | teh-KOH-ah)_

## Definition

a stockade; a form of H8619; Tekoa, a place in Palestine; Tekoa, Tekoah; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a Judaite, son of Ashur and grandson of Hezron
- (n pr loc) a town in the hill country of Judah near Hebron built by king Rehoboam of Judah; birthplace of Amos
- a wilderness area where king Jehoshaphat of Judah defeated the people of Moab, Ammon, and Mount Seir
