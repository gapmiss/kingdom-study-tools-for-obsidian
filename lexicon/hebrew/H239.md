# H239 אָזַן / אזן

## ʼâzan

_(aw-zan' | aw-DZAHN | ah-ZAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root (rather identical with H0238 through the idea of scales as if two ears); to weigh, i.e., (figuratively) ponder; give good heed; v

- (Piel) weigh, test, prove, consider
