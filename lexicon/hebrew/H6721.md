# H6721 צִידוֹן / צידון

## Tsîydôwn

_(tsee-done' | tsee-DONE | tsee-DONE)_

## Definition

hunting; or צִידֹן; from H6679 in the sense of catching fish; fishery; Tsidon, the name of a son of Canaan, and of a place in Palestine; Sidon, Zidon; n-pr-loc

- ancient Phoenician city, on Mediterranean coast north of Tyre
