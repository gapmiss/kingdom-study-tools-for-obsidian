# H5570 סְנָאָה / סנאה

## Çᵉnâʼâh

_(sen-aw-aw' | seh-naw-AW | seh-na-AH)_

## Definition

thorny; from an unused root meaning to prick; thorny; Senaah, a place in Palestine; Senaah, Hassenaah (with the article); n-pr-m

- ancestor of a family who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
