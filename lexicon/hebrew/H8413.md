# H8413 תִּדְעָל / תדעל

## Tidʻâl

_(tid-awl' | tid-AWL | teed-AL)_

## Definition

great son; perhaps from H1763; fearfulness; Tidal, a Canaanite; Tidal; n-pr-m

- chief of various nomadic tribes and an ally of Chedorlaomer
