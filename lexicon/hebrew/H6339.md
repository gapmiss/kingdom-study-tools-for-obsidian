# H6339 פָּזַז / פזז

## pâzaz

_(paw-zaz' | paw-DZAHDZ | pa-ZAHZ)_

## Definition

a primitive root (identical with H6338); to solidify (as if by refining); also to spring (as if separating the limbs); leap, be made strong; v

- to bound, be agile, be supple
  - (Qal) to be nimble
  - (Piel) to leap, show agility
