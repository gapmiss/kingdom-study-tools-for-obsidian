# H6694 צוּק / צוק

## tsûwq

_(tsook | tsook | tsook)_

## Definition

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of narrowness (of orifice)); to pour out, i.e., (figuratively) smelt, utter; be molten, pour; v

- (Qal) to pour out, melt
