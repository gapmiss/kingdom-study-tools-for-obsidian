# H1672 דָּאַג / דאג

## dâʼag

_(daw-ag' | daw-Aɡ | da-Aɡ)_

## Definition

a primitive root; be anxious; be afraid (careful, sorry), sorrow, take thought; v

- to fear, be anxious, be concerned, be afraid, be careful
  - (Qal)
    - to be anxious, be concerned
    - to fear, dread
