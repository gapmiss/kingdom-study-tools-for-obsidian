# H5963 עַלְמֹן דִּבְלָתָיְמָה / עלמן דבלתימה

## ʻAlmôn Diblâthâyᵉmâh

_(al-mone' dib-lawthaw'-yem-aw | al-MONE dib-law-tai-MAW | al-MONE deev-la-tai-MA)_

## Definition

concealing the two cakes; from the same as H5960 and the dual of H1960 (compare H1015) with enclitic of direction; Almon towards Diblathajim; Almon-Diblathajemah, a place in Moab; Almondilathaim; n-pr-loc

- a station of the Israelites in the wilderness of Moab
