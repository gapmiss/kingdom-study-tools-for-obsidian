# H8428 תָּוָה / תוה

## tâvâh

_(taw-vaw' | taw-WAW | ta-VA)_

## Definition

a primitive root (or perhaps identical with H8427 through a similar idea from scraping to pieces); to grieve; limit (by confusion with H8427); v

- (Hiphil) to pain, wound, trouble, cause pain
  - meaning probable
