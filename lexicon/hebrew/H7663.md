# H7663 שָׂבַר / שבר

## sâbar

_(saw-bar' | saw-BAHR | sa-VAHR)_

## Definition

erroneously שָׁבַר; (Nehemiah 2:13,15), a primitive root; to scrutinize; by implication (of watching) to expect (with hope and patience); hope, tarry, view, wait; v

- to inspect, examine, wait, hope, wait upon
  - (Qal) examined (participle)
  - (Piel)
    - to wait (for), wait upon
    - to hope (for)
