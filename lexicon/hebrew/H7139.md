# H7139 קָרַח / קרח

## qârach

_(kaw-rakh' | kaw-RA | ka-RAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to depilate; make (self) bald; v

- to be bald, make bald
  - (Qal) to make bald, make a baldness
  - (Niphal) to make oneself bald
  - (Hiphil) to make oneself bald
  - (Hophal) to make bald
