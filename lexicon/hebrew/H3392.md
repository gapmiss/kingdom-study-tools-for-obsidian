# H3392 יֶרַח / ירח

## Yerach

_(yeh'-rakh | yeh-RA | yeh-RAHK)_

## Definition

new moon; the same as H3391; Jerach, an Arabian patriarch; Jerah; n-pr-m

- a 4th son of Joktan and a progenitor of an Arabian tribe
