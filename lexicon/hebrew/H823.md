# H823 אַשְׁנָה / אשנה

## ʼAshnâh

_(ash-naw' | ash-NAW | ash-NA)_

## Definition

I will cause change; probably a variation for H3466; Ashnah, the name of two places in Palestine; Ashnah; n-pr-loc

- a town near Dan
- a town in Judah
