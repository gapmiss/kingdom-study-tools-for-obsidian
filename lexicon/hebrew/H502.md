# H502 אָלַף / אלף

## ʼâlaph

_(aw-lof' | aw-LAHP | ah-LAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root, to associate with; hence, to learn (and causatively to teach); learn, teach, utter; v

- to learn
  - (Qal) to learn
  - (Piel) to teach
