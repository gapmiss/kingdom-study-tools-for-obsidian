# H3812 לֵאָה / לאה

## Lêʼâh

_(lay-aw' | lay-AW | lay-AH)_

## Definition

weary; from H3811; weary; Leah, a wife of Jacob; Leah; n-pr-f

- daughter of Laban, first wife of Jacob, and mother of Reuben, Simeon, Levi, Judah, Issachar, Zebulun, and Dinah
