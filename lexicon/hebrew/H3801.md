# H3801 כְּתֹנֶת / כתנת

## kᵉthôneth

_(keth-o'-neth | keh-toh-NET | keh-toh-NET)_

## Definition

or כֻּתֹּנֶת; from an unused root meaning to cover (compare H3802); a shirt; coat, garment, robe; n-f

- tunic, under-garment
  - a long shirt-like garment usually of linen
