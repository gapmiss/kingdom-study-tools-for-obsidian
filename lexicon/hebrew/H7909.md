# H7909 שַׁכּוּל / שכול

## shakkûwl

_(shak-kool' | shahk-KOOL | sha-KOOL)_

## Definition

or שַׁכֻּל; from H7921; bereaved; barren, bereaved (robbed) of children (whelps); a

- childless (through bereavement)
- bereaved, robbed of offspring
