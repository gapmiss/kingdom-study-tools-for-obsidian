# H6453 פֶּסַח / פסח

## peçach

_(peh'-sakh | peh-SA | peh-SAHK)_

## Definition

from H6452; a pretermission, i.e., exemption; used only techically of the Jewish Passover (the festival or the victim); passover (offering); n-m

- passover
  - sacrifice of passover
  - animal victim of the passover
  - festival of the passover
