# H5318 נֶפְתּוֹחַ / נפתוח

## Nephtôwach

_(nef-to'-akh | nep-TOH-ah | nef-TOH-ak)_

## Definition

opening; from H6605; opened, i.e., a spring; Nephtoach, a place in Palestine; Neptoah; n-pr-loc

- a spring or source of water on the boundary of the territories of Judah and Benjamin; located northwest of Jerusalem
