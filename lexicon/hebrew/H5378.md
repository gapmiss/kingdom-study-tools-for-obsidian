# H5378 נָשָׁא / נשא

## nâshâʼ

_(naw-shaw' | naw-SHAW | na-SHA)_

## Definition

a primitive root (perhaps identical with H5377, through the idea of imposition); to lend on interest; by implication, to dun for debt; × debt, exact, giver of usury; v

- to lend on interest or usury, be a creditor
  - (Qal) creditor (participle)
  - (Hiphil) to act as a creditor
