# H817 אָשָׁם / אשם

## ʼâshâm

_(aw-shawm' | aw-SHAWM | ah-SHAHM)_

## Definition

from H0816; guilt; by implication, a fault; also a sin-offering; guiltiness, (offering for) sin, trespass (offering); n-m

- guilt, offense, guiltiness
  - offense, trespass, fault
  - guilt, guiltiness
  - compensation (for offense)
  - trespass offering, guilt offering
