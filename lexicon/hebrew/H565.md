# H565 אִמְרָה / אמרה

## ʼimrâh

_(im-raw' | im-RAW | eem-RA)_

## Definition

or אֶמְרָה; feminine of H0561, and meaning the same; an utterance; commandment, speech, word; n-f

- utterance, speech, word
  - word of God, the Torah
