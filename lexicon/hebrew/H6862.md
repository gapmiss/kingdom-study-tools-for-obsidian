# H6862 צַר / צר

## tsar

_(tsar | tsahr | tsahr)_

## Definition

or צָר; from H6887; a pebble (as in H6864); adversary, afflicted(-tion), anguish, close, distress, enemy, flint, foe, narrow, small, sorrow, strait, tribulation, trouble; a

- narrow, tight
- straits, distress
- adversary, foe, enemy, oppressor
- hard pebble, flint
