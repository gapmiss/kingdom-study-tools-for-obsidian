# H8295 שָׂרַט / שרט

## sâraṭ

_(saw-rat' | saw-RAHT | sa-RAHT)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to gash; cut in pieces, make (cuttings) pieces; v

- to incise, scratch, tattoo, cut
  - (Qal) to make incision
  - (Niphal) to be scratched or lacerated, be cut
