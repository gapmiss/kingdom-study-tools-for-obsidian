# H4439 מַלְכִּיאֵל / מלכיאל

## Malkîyʼêl

_(mal-kee-ale' | mahl-kee-ALE | mahl-kee-ALE)_

## Definition

my king is God; from H4428 and H0410; king of (i.e., appointed by) God; Malkiel, an Israelite; Malchiel; n-pr-m

- the son of Beriah and grandson of Asher
