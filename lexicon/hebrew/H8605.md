# H8605 תְּפִלָּה / תפלה

_(tef-il-law' | teh-pil-LAW | teh-fee-LA)_

## Definition

from H6419; intercession, supplication; by implication, a hymn; prayer; n-f

- prayer
  - prayer
  - pray a prayer
  - house of prayer
  - hear prayer
  - in Psalm titles (of poetic or liturgical prayer)
