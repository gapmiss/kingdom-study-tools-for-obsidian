# H7583 שָׁאָה / שאה

## shâʼâh

_(shaw-aw' | shaw-AW | sha-AH)_

## Definition

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of whirling to giddiness); to stun, i.e., (intransitively) be astonished; wonder; v

- (Hithpael)
  - to gaze
  - gazing (participle)
