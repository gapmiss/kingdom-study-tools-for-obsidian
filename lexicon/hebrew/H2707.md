# H2707 חָקָה / חקה

## châqâh

_(khaw-kaw' | haw-KAW | ha-KA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to carve; by implication, to delineate; also to entrench; carved work, portrayed, set a print; v

- to cut, carve, cut in
  - (Pual) carved (participle)
  - (Hithpael) to engrave, cut a limit
