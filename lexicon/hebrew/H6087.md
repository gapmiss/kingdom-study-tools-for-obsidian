# H6087 עָצַב / עצב

## ʻâtsab

_(aw-tsab' | aw-TSAHB | ah-TSAHV)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to carve, i.e., fabricate or fashion; displease, grieve, hurt, make, be sorry, vex, worship, wrest; v

- to hurt, pain, grieve, displease, vex, wrest
  - (Qal) to hurt, pain
  - (Niphal) to be in pain, be pained, be grieved
  - (Piel) to vex, torture
  - (Hiphil) to cause pain
  - (Hithpael) to feel grieved, be vexed
- to shape, fashion, make, form, stretch into shape, worship
  - (Piel) to shape, form
  - (Hiphil) to form, fashion
