# H7137 קָרֶה / קרה

## qâreh

_(kaw-reh' | kaw-REH | ka-REH)_

## Definition

from H7136; an (unfortunate) occurrence, i.e., some accidental (ceremonial) disqualification; uncleanness that chanceth; n-m

- chance, accident
  - of nocturnal pollution requiring temporary exodus from camp
