# H663 אֲפֵק / אפק

## ʼĂphêq

_(af-ake' | uh-PAKE | uh-FAKE)_

## Definition

enclosure; or אֲפִיק; from H0662 (in the sense of strength); fortress; Aphek (or Aphik), the name of three places in Palestine; Aphek, Aphik; n-pr-loc

- a Canaanite city near Jezreel
- a city in territory of Asher
- a city northeast of Beirut in Transjordan
