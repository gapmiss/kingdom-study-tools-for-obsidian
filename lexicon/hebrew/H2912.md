# H2912 טָחַן / טחן

## ṭâchan

_(taw-khan' | taw-HAHN | ta-HAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to grind meal; hence, to be a concubine (that being their employment); grind(-er); v

- (Qal) to grind, crush
