# H7609 שֶׁאֱרָה / שארה

## Sheʼĕrâh

_(sheh-er-aw' | sheh-eh-RAW | sheh-ay-RA)_

## Definition

kinswoman; the same as H7608; Sheerah, an Israelitess; Sherah; n-pr-f

- daughter of Ephraim and foundress of the Beth-horons and of a town called after her Uzzen-sherah
