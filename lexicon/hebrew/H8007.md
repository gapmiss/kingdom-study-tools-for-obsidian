# H8007 שַׂלְמָא / שלמא

## Salmâʼ

_(sal-maw' | sahl-MAW | sahl-MA)_

## Definition

garment; probably for H8008; clothing; Salma, the name of two Israelites; Salma; n-pr-m

- father of Boaz, father-in-law of Ruth, and grandfather of David
  - also 'Salmon', 'Salmah'
