# H7205 רְאוּבֵן / ראובן

## Rᵉʼûwbên

_(reh-oo-bane' | reh-oo-BANE | reh-oo-VANE)_

## Definition

behold a son; from the imperative of H7200 and H1121; see ye a son; Reuben, a son of Jacob; Reuben; n-pr-m

- the eldest son of Jacob by Leah
- the tribe descended from Reuben
- the territory inhabited by the tribe of Reuben
