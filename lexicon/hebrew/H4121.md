# H4121 מַהֲרַי / מהרי

## Mahăray

_(mah-har-ah'-ee | ma-huh-RAI | ma-huh-RAI)_

## Definition

impetuous; from H4116; hasty; Maharai, an Israelite; Maharai; n-pr-m

- an inhabitant of Netophah in the tribe of Judah and one of David's mighty warriors
