# H5766 עֶוֶל / עול

## ʻevel

_(eh'-vel | eh-WEL | eh-VEL)_

## Definition

or עָוֶל; and (feminine) עַוְלָה; or עוֹלָה; or עֹלָה; from H5765; (moral) evil; iniquity, perverseness, unjust(-ly), unrighteousness(-ly); wicked(-ness); n

- injustice, unrighteousness, wrong
  - violent deeds of injustice
  - injustice (of speech)
  - injustice (generally)
