# H4810 מְרִי בַעַל / מרי בעל

## Mᵉrîy Baʻal

_(mer-ee' bah'-al | meh-REE ba-AL | meh-REE va-AL)_

## Definition

Baal is my advocate; from H4805 and H1168; rebellion of (i.e., against) Baal; Meri-Baal, an epithet of Gideon; Meri-baal; n-pr-m

- another name for Mephibosheth
  - son of Jonathan and grandson of Saul

## Note

Compare H4807.
