# H1513 גֶּחֶל / גחל

## gechel

_(geh'-khel | ɡeh-HEL | ɡeh-HEL)_

## Definition

or (feminine) גַּחֶלֶת; from an unused root meaning to glow or kindle; an ember; (burning) coal; n-f

- coal, burning coal, coals of fire, hot coals
