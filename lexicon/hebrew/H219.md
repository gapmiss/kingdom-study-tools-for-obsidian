# H219 אוֹרָה / אורה

## ʼôwrâh

_(o-raw' | oh-RAW | oh-RA)_

## Definition

feminine of H0216; luminousness, i.e., (figuratively) prosperity; also a plant (as being bright); light; n-f

- light
- light of joy and happiness (figuratively)
