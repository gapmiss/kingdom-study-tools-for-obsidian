# H1371 גִּבֵּחַ / גבח

## gibbêach

_(ghib-bay'-akh | ɡib-BAY-ah | ɡee-BAY-ak)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to be high (in the forehead); bald in the forehead; forehead bald; a

- to be high, bald (in the forehead), having a bald forehead
