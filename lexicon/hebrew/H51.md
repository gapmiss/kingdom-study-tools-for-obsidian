# H51 אֲבִישׁוּר / אבישור

## ʼĂbîyshûwr

_(ab-ee-shoor' | uh-bee-SHOOR | uh-vee-SHOOR)_

## Definition

my father is a wall; from H0001 and H7791; father of (the) wall (i.e., perhaps mason); Abishur, an Israelite; Abishur; n-pr-m

- son of Shammai, descendant of Judah
