# H8539 תָּמַהּ / תמה

## tâmahh

_(taw-mah' | taw-MA | ta-MA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be in consternation; be amazed, be astonished, marvel(-lously), wonder; v

- to be astounded, be stunned, be amazed, be dumbfounded
  - (Qal) to be astounded
  - (Hithpael) to astonish yourself, be astounded, be astonished at one another
