# H2651 חֹפֶן / חפן

## chôphen

_(kho'-fen | hoh-PEN | hoh-FEN)_

## Definition

from an unused root of uncertain signification; a fist (only in the dual); fists, (both) hands, hand(-ful); n-m

- handfuls, hollow of the hand
