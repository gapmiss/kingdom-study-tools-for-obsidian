# H658 אֶפֶס דַּמִּים / אפס דמים

## ʼEpheç Dammîym

_(eh'-fes dam-meem' | eh-PES dahm-MEEM | eh-FES da-MEEM)_

## Definition

edge of blood; from H0657 and the plural of H1818; boundary of blood-drops; Ephes-Dammim, a place in Palestine; Ephes-dammim; n-pr-loc

- a place approximately 16 miles (25 km) southwest of Jerusalem
