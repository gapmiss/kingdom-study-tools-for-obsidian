# H894 בָּבֶל / בבל

## Bâbel

_(baw-bel' | baw-BEL | ba-VEL)_

## Definition

confusion (by mixing); from H1101; confusion; Babel (i.e., Babylon), including Babylonia and the Babylonian empire; Babel, Babylon; n-pr-loc

- Babel or Babylon, the ancient site and/or capital of Babylonia (modern Hillah) situated on the Euphrates
