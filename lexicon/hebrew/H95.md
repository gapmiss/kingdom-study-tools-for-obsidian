# H95 אֲגוֹרָה / אגורה

## ʼăgôwrâh

_(ag-o-raw' | uh-ɡoh-RAW | uh-ɡoh-RA)_

## Definition

from the same as H0094; properly, something gathered, i.e., perhaps a grain or berry; used only of a small (silver) coin; piece (of) silver; n-f

- payment, piece, coin
