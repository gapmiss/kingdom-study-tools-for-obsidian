# H7484 רַעְמָה / רעמה

## Raʻmâh

_(rah-maw' | ra-MAW | ra-MA)_

## Definition

horse's mane; the same as H7483; Ramah, the name of a grandson of Ham, and of a place (perhaps founded by him); Raamah; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) son of Cush and father of Sheba and Dedan
- (n pr loc) a home of traders
