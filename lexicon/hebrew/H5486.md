# H5486 סוּף / סוף

## çûwph

_(soof | soop | soof)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to snatch away, i.e., terminate; consume, have an end, perish, × be utterly; v

- to cease, come to an end
  - (Qal) to come to an end
  - (Hiphil) to make an end
