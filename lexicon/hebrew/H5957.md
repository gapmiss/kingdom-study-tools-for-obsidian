## ʻâlam

_(aw-lam' | aw-LAHM | ah-LAHM)_

## Definition

corresponding to H5769; remote time, i.e., the future or past indefinitely; often adverb, forever; for (n-)ever (lasting), old; n-m

- perpetuity, antiquity, for ever
