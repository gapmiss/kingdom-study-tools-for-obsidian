# H5835 עֲזָרָה / עזרה

## ʻăzârâh

_(az-aw-raw' | uh-dzaw-RAW | uh-za-RA)_

## Definition

from H5826 in its original meaning of surrounding; an inclosure; also a border; court, settle; n-f

- enclosure
  - ledge (surrounding Ezekiel's altar)
  - court (outer one of temple)
