# H3401 יָרִיב / יריב

## yârîyb

_(yaw-rebe' | yaw-REEB | ya-REEV)_

## Definition

from H7378; literally he will contend; properly, adjective contentious; used as noun, an adversary; that content(-eth), that strive; n-m

- contender, opponent, adversary
