# H8088 שֵׁמַע / שמע

## shêmaʻ

_(shay'-mah | shay-MA | shay-MA)_

## Definition

from H8085; something heard, i.e., a sound, rumor, announcement; abstractly, audience; bruit, fame, hear(-ing), loud, report, speech, tidings; n-m

- report, a hearing
