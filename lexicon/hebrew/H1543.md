# H1543 גֻּלָּה / גלה

## gullâh

_(gool-law' | ɡool-LAW | ɡoo-LA)_

## Definition

feminine from H1556; a fountain, bowl or globe (all as round); bowl, pommel, spring; n-f

- bowl, spring, basin
  - basin
  - bowl
    - of a lamp
    - of bowl shaped portion of capitals of pillars of the temple
