# H7130 קֶרֶב / קרב

## qereb

_(keh'-reb | keh-REB | keh-REV)_

## Definition

from H7126; properly, the nearest part, i.e., the center, whether literal, figurative or adverbial (especially with preposition); × among, × before, bowels, × unto charge, + eat (up), × heart, × him, × in, inward(-s) (part, thought), x inwardly, midst, + out of, purtenance, × therein, × through, × within self; n-m

- midst, among, inner part, middle
  - inward part
    - physical sense
    - as seat of thought and emotion
    - as faculty of thought and emotion
  - in the midst, among, from among (of a number of persons)
  - entrails (of sacrificial animals)
