# H8195 שְׁפוֹ / שפו

## Shᵉphôw

_(shef-o' | sheh-POH | sheh-FOH)_

## Definition

bold; or שְׁפִי; from H8192; baldness (compare H8205); Shepho or Shephi, an Idumaean; Shephi, Shepho; n-pr-m

- an Edomite, son of Shobal of the sons of Seir
