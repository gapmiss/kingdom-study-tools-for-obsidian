# H3953 לָקַשׁ / לקש

## lâqash

_(law-kash' | law-KAHSH | la-KAHSH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to gather the after crop; gather; v

- to glean, gather, take the aftermath, take everything
  - (Piel) to despoil, gather everything from, strip
