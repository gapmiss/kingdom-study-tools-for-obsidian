# H719 אַרְוַד / ארוד

## ʼArvad

_(ar-vad' | ar-WAHD | ar-VAHD)_

## Definition

I shall break loose; probably from H7300; a refuge for the roving; Arvad, an island-city of Palestine; Arvad; n-pr-loc

- a city-island near Sidon
