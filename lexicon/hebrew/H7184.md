# H7184 קָשָׂה / קשה

## qâsâh

_(kaw-saw' | kaw-SAW | ka-SA)_

## Definition

or קַשְׂוָה; from an unused root meaning to be round; a jug (from its shape); cover, cup; n-f

- a kind of jug, jar
