# H4526 מִסְגֶּרֶת / מסגרת

## miçgereth

_(mis-gheh'-reth | mis-ɡeh-RET | mees-ɡeh-RET)_

## Definition

from H5462; something enclosing, i.e., a margin (of a region, of a panel); concretely, a stronghold; border, close place, hole; n-f

- border, fastness, rim
  - border, rim
  - fastness
