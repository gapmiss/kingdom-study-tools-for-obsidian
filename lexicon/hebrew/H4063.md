# H4063 מֶדֶו / מדו

## medev

_(meh'-dev | meh-DEH | meh-DEV)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to stretch; properly, extent, i.e., measure; by implication, a dress (as measured); garment; n-m

- garment
