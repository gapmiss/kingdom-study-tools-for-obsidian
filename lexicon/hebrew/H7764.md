# H7764 שׁוּנִי / שוני

## Shûwnîy

_(shoo-nee' | shoo-NEE | shoo-NEE)_

## Definition

fortunate; from an unused root meaning to rest; quiet; Shuni, an Israelite; Shuni; n-pr-m

- son of Gad and progenitor of the family of Shunites
