# H5488 סוּף / סוף

## çûwph

_(soof | soop | soof)_

## Definition

probably of Egyptian origin; a reed, especially the papyrus; flag, Red (sea), weed; n-m

- reed, rush, water plant
  - rushes
  - sea of rushes
    - of Red Sea
    - of arms of Red Sea
    - of Gulf of Suez
    - of sea from straits to Gulf of Akaba

## Note

Compare H5489.
