# H6597 פִּתְאוֹם / פתאום

## pithʼôwm

_(pith-ome' | pit-OME | peet-OME)_

## Definition

or פִּתְאֹם; from H6621; instantly; straightway, sudden(-ly); adv

- (adv) suddenly, surprisingly
- (subst) suddenness
