# H5821 עַזָּן / עזן

## ʻAzzân

_(az-zawn' | adz-ZAWN | ah-ZAHN)_

## Definition

very strong; from H5794; strong one; Azzan, an Israelite; Azzan; n-pr-m

- father of Paltiel prince of the tribe of Issachar who represented his tribe in the division of the promised land
