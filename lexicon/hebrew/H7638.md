# H7638 שָׂבָךְ / שבך

## sâbâk

_(saw-bawk' | saw-BOKE | sa-VOKE)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to intwine; a netting (ornament to the capital of a column); net; n-m

- network, lattice-work, net, netting
  - lattice
  - net-ornament (on pillars)
  - network, toils (for catching animals)
