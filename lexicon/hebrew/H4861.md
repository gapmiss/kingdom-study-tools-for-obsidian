# H4861 מִשְׁאָל / משאל

## Mishʼâl

_(mish-awl' | mish-AWL | meesh-AL)_

## Definition

entreaty; from H7592; request; Mishal, a place in Palestine; Mishal, Misheal; n-pr-loc

- a town in the territory of Asher allotted to the Gershonite Levites
