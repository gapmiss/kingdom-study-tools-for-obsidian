# H5394 נָשַׁל / נשל

## nâshal

_(naw-shal' | naw-SHAHL | na-SHAHL)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to pluck off, i.e., divest, eject or drop; cast (out), drive, loose, put off (out), slip; v

- to slip off, drop off, clear away, draw off
  - (Qal)
    - to slip or drop off
    - to draw off
    - to clear away
  - (Piel) to clear out completely, clear entirely
