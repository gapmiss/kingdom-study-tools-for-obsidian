# H8442 תּוֹעָה / תועה

## tôwʻâh

_(to-aw' | toh-AW | toh-AH)_

## Definition

feminine active participle of H8582; mistake, i.e., (morally) impiety, or (political) injury; error, hinder; n-f

- error, wandering, impiety, perversions
  - error (in morals and religion)
  - confusion, disturbance
