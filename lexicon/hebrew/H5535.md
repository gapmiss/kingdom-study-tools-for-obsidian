# H5535 סָכַת / סכת

## çâkath

_(saw-kath' | saw-KAHT | sa-HAHT)_

## Definition

a primitive root to be silent; by implication, to observe quietly; take heed; v

- to be silent
  - (Hiphil) to keep silence, show silence
