# H5077 נָדָה / נדה

## nâdâh

_(naw-daw' | naw-DAW | na-DA)_

## Definition

or נָדָא; (2 Kings 17:21), a primitive root; properly, to toss; figuratively, to exclude, i.e., banish, postpone, prohibit; cast out, drive, put far away; v

- (Hiphil) exclude, drive away, thrust aside
- (Piel) cast out, put away
