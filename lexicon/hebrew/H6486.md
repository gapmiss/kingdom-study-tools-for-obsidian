# H6486 פְּקֻדָּה / פקדה

## pᵉquddâh

_(pek-ood-daw' | peh-kood-DAW | peh-koo-DA)_

## Definition

feminine passive participle of H6485; visitation (in many senses, chiefly official); account, (that have the) charge, custody, that which...laid up, numbers, office(-r), ordering, oversight, + prison, reckoning, visitation; n-f

- oversight, care, custody, mustering, visitation, store
  - visitation, punishment
  - oversight, charge, office, overseer, class of officers
  - mustering
  - store
