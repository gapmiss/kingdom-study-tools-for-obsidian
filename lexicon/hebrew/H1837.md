# H1837 דַּנָּה / דנה

## Dannâh

_(dan-naw' | dahn-NAW | da-NA)_

## Definition

thou hast judged, judgement; of uncertain derivation; Dannah, a place in Palestine; Dannah; n-pr-loc

- a town in the hill country of Judah, probably south or southwest of Hebron
