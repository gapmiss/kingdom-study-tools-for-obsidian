# H304 אַחְלַי / אחלי

## ʼAchlay

_(akh-lah'ee | ah-LAI | ak-LAI)_

## Definition

O! would that!; the same as H0305; wishful; Achlai, the name of an Israelitess and of an Israelite; Ahlai; n-pr-m

- daughter of Sheshan
- father of one of David's heroes (mighty men, warriors)
