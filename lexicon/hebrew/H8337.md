# H8337 שֵׁשׁ / שש

## shêsh

_(shaysh | shaysh | shaysh)_

## Definition

masculine שִׁשָּׁה; a primitive number; six (as an overplus beyond five or the fingers of the hand); as ordinal, sixth; six(-teen, -teenth), sixth; n

- six
  - six (cardinal number)
  - sixth (ordinal number)
  - in combination with other numbers
