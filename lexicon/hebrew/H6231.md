# H6231 עָשַׁק / עשק

## ʻâshaq

_(aw-shak' | aw-SHAHK | ah-SHAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root (compare H6229); to press upon, i.e., oppress, defraud, violate, overflow; get deceitfully, deceive, defraud, drink up, (use) oppress(-ion, -or), do violence (wrong); v

- to press upon, oppress, violate, defraud, do violence, get deceitfully, wrong, extort
  - (Qal)
    - to oppress, wrong, extort
    - to oppress
  - (Pual) to be exploited, be crushed
