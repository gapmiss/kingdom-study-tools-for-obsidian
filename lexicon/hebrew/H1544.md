# H1544 גִּלּוּל / גלול

## gillûwl

_(ghil-lool' | ɡil-LOOL | ɡee-LOOL)_

## Definition

or (shortened) גִּלֻּל; from H1556; properly, a log (as round); by implication, an idol; idol; n-m

- idols
