# H6203 עֹרֶף / ערף

## ʻôreph

_(o-ref' | oh-REP | oh-REF)_

## Definition

from H6202; the nape or back of the neck (as declining); hence, the back generally (whether literal or figurative); back, (stiff-)neck(-ed); n-m

- neck, back of the neck, back
  - back of the neck
    - of fleeing foe
    - of apostasy (figuratively)
  - stiff of neck, obstinate (figuratively)
