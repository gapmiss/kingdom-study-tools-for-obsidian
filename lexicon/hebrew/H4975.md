# H4975 מֹתֶן / מתן

## môthen

_(mo'-then | moh-TEN | moh-TEN)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to be slender; properly, the waist or small of the back; only in plural the loins; + greyhound, loins, side; n-m

- loins, hips
  - used with H2223 in Proverbs 30:31; perhaps an extinct animal, exact meaning unknown
