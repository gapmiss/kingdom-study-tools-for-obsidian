# H7939 שָׂכָר / שכר

## sâkâr

_(saw-kawr' | saw-KAWR | sa-HAHR)_

## Definition

from H7936; payment of contract; concretely, salary, fare, maintenance; by implication, compensation, benefit; hire, price, reward(-ed), wages, worth; n-m

- hire, wages
  - wages
  - reward, pay
  - fare, fee, passage-money
