# H6421 פְּלַלְיָה / פלליה

## Pᵉlalyâh

_(pel-al-yaw' | peh-lahl-YAW | peh-lahl-YA)_

## Definition

Jehovah has judged; from H6419 and H3050; Jah has judged; Pelaljah, an Israelite; Pelaliah; n-pr-m

- son of Amzi and ancestor of Adaiah the priest in the time of Nehemiah
