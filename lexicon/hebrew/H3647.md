# H3647 כָּמַס / כמס

## kâmaç

_(kaw-mas' | kaw-MAHS | ka-MAHS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to store away, i.e., (figuratively) in the memory; lay up in store; v

- to store up, save
  - (Qal) stored up (participle)
