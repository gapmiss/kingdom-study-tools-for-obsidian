# H4529 מָסָה / מסה

## mâçâh

_(maw-saw' | maw-SAW | ma-SA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to dissolve; make to consume away, (make to) melt, water; v

- to melt, dissolve, be liquefied
  - (Hiphil)
    - to melt, cause to dissolve, consume, cause to vanish
    - to intimidate (figuratively)
