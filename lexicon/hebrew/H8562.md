# H8562 תַּמְרוּק / תמרוק

## tamrûwq

_(tam-rook' | tahm-ROOK | tahm-ROOK)_

## Definition

or תַּמְרֻק; or תַּמְרִיק; from H4838; properly, a scouring, i.e., soap or perfumery for the bath; figuratively, a detergent; × cleanse, (thing for) purification, (thing for) purifying; n-m

- a scraping, rubbing
- remedy (figuratively), cleansing (figuratively)
