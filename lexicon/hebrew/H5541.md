# H5541 סָלָה / סלה

## çâlâh

_(saw-law' | saw-LAW | sa-LA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to hang up, i.e., weigh, or (figuratively) contemn; tread down (under foot), value; v

- to make light of, toss aside
  - (Qal) to make light of
  - (Piel) to flout, reject
- to weigh, balance
  - (Pual) to be weighed
