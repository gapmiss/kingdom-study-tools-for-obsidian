## Tsûwrîyʼêl

_(tsoo-ree-ale' | tsoo-ree-ALE | tsoo-ree-ALE)_

## Definition

my rock is God; from H6697 and H0410; rock of God; Tsuriel, an Israelite; Zuriel; n-pr-m

- son of Abihail and chief of the Merarite Levites at the time of the exodus
