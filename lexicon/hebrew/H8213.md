# H8213 שָׁפֵל / שפל

## shâphêl

_(shaw-fale' | shaw-PALE | sha-FALE)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to depress or sink (especially figuratively, to humiliate, intransitive or transitive); abase, bring (cast, put) down, debase, humble (self), be (bring, lay, make, put) low(-er); v

- to be or become low, sink, be humbled, be abased
  - (Qal) to be or become low
  - (Hiphil)
    - to lay or bring low, humiliate
    - to set in a lower place, show abasement
    - to make low, sit down
