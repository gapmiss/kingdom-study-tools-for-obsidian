# H2365 חוּשַׁי / חישי

## Chûwshay

_(khoo-shah'-ee | hoo-SHAI | hoo-SHAI)_

## Definition

hasting; from H2363; hasty; Chushai, an Israelite; Hushai; n-pr-m

- an Archite, a friend of David
- the father of Baana, an officer of Solomon; probably the same as 1
