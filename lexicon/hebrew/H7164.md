# H7164 קָרַס / קרס

## qâraç

_(kaw-ras' | kaw-RAHS | ka-RAHS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to protrude; used only as denominative from H7165 (for alliteration with H7167); to hunch, i.e., be hump-backed; stoop; v

- (Qal) to bend down, stoop, crouch
