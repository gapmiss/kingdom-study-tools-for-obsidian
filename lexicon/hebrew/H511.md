# H511 אֶלְקָנָה / אלקנה

## ʼElqânâh

_(el-kaw-naw' | el-kaw-NAW | el-ka-NA)_

## Definition

God has possessed, God has created; from H0410 and H7069; God has obtained; Elkanah, the name of several Israelites; Elkanah; n-pr-m

- Samuel's father
- a ruler in Jerusalem in the time of king Ahaz
- one of David's mighty warriors
- son of Korah
- several Levites
