# H5411 נָתִין / נתין

## Nâthîyn

_(naw-theen' | naw-TEEN | na-TEEN)_

## Definition

or נָתוּן (Ezra 8:17), (the proper form as passive participle), from H5414; one given, i.e., (in the plural only); the Nethinim, or Temple-servants (as given to that duty); Nethinims; n-m

- Nethinims
  - temple slaves assigned to the Levites and priests for service in the sanctuary
