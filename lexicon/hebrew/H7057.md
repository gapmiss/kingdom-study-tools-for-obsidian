# H7057 קִמּוֹשׁ / קמוש

## qimmôwsh

_(kim-moshe' | kim-MOHSH | kee-MOHSH)_

## Definition

or קִימוֹשׁ; from an unused root meaning to sting; a prickly plant; nettle; n-m

- thistles or nettles, a thorny or useless plant
