# H484 אַלְמֻגִּים / אלמגים

## ʼalmuggîym

_(al-moog-gheem' | al-moog-GEEM | al-moo-ɡEEM)_

## Definition

probably of foreign derivation (used thus only in the plural); almug (i.e., probably sandle-wood) sticks; almug trees; n-m

- a tree from Lebanon, almug trees (perhaps sandalwood), almug wood

## Note

Compare H0418.
