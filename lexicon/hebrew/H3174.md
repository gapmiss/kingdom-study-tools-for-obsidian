# H3174 יְחִיָּה / יחיה

## Yᵉchîyâh

_(yekh-ee-yaw' | yeh-hih-YAW | yeh-hee-YA)_

## Definition

Jehovah lives; from H2421 and H3050; Jah will live; Jechijah, an Israelite; Jehiah; n-pr-m

- doorkeeper of the ark at the time of its establishment in Jerusalem
