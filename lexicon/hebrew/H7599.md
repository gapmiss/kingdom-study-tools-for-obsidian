# H7599 שָׁאַן / שאן

## shâʼan

_(shaw-an' | shaw-AN | sha-AN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to loll, i.e., be peaceful; be at ease, be quiet, rest; v

- (Pilel) to be at ease, be at peace, rest, rest securely, be quiet
