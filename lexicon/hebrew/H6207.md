# H6207 עָרַק / ערק

## ʻâraq

_(aw-rak' | aw-RAHK | ah-RAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to gnaw, i.e., (figuratively) eat (by hyberbole); also (participle) a pain; fleeing, sinew; v

- (Qal) to gnaw, chew
