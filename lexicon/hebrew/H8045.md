# H8045 שָׁמַד / שמד

## shâmad

_(shaw-mad' | shaw-MAHD | sha-MAHD)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to desolate; destory(-uction), bring to nought, overthrow, perish, pluck down, × utterly; v

- to destroy, exterminate, be destroyed, be exterminated
  - (Niphal)
    - to be annihilated, be exterminated
    - to be destroyed, be devastated
  - (Hiphil)
    - to annihilate, exterminate
    - to destroy
