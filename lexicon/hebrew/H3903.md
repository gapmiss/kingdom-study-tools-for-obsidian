# H3903 לַחְמָס / לחמס

## Lachmâç

_(lakh-maws' | la-MAWS | lahk-MAHS)_

## Definition

provisions; probably by erroneous transcription for לַחְמָם; from H3899; food-like; Lachmam or Lachmas, a place in Palestine; Lahmam; n-pr-loc

- a town in the lowland of Judah
