# H3532 כֶּבֶשׂ / כבש

## kebes

_(keh-bes' | keh-BES | keh-VES)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to dominate; a ram (just old enough to butt); lamb, sheep; n-m

- lamb, sheep, young ram
