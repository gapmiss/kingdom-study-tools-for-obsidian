# H579 אָנָה / אנה

## ʼânâh

_(aw-naw' | aw-NAW | ah-NA)_

## Definition

a primitive root (perhaps rather identical with H0578 through the idea of contraction in anguish); to approach; hence, to meet in various senses; befall, deliver, happen, seek a quarrel; v

- to meet, encounter, approach, be opportune
  - (Piel) to allow to meet, cause to meet
  - (Pual) to be sent, be allowed to meet
  - (Hithpael) to seek occasion (quarrel), cause oneself to meet
