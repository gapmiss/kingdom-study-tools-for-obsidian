# H7324 רוּק / רוק

## rûwq

_(rook | rook | rook)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to pour out (literally or figuratively), i.e., empty; × arm, cast out, draw (out), (make) empty, pour forth (out); v

- to make empty, empty out
  - (Hiphil)
    - to empty, keep empty or hungry
    - to pour out or down
    - to empty out
  - (Hophal) to be emptied out
