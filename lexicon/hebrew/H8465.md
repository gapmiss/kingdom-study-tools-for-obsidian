# H8465 תַּחַן / תחן

## Tachan

_(takh'-an | ta-HAHN | ta-HAHN)_

## Definition

camp; probably from H2583; station; Tachan, the name of two Israelites; Tahan; n-pr-m

- an Ephraimite, son of Telah and progenitor of a family of Ephraim
