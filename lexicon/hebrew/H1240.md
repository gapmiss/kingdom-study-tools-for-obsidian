# H1240 בְּקַר / בקר

## bᵉqar

_(bek-ar' | beh-KAHR | beh-KAHR)_

## Definition

corresponding to H1239; properly, to plough, or (generally) break forth, i.e., (figuratively) to inspect, admire, care for, consider; inquire, make search; v

- to seek, enquire
  - (Pael) to enquire
  - (Ithpael) to let search be made
