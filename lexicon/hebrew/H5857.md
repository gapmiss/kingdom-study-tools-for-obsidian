# H5857 עַי / עי

## ʻAy

_(ah'ee | ai | ai)_

## Definition

heap of ruins; or (feminine) עַיָּא; (Nehemiah 11:31), or עַיָּת; (Isaiah 10:28), for H5856; Ai, Aja or Ajath, a place in Palestine; Ai, Aija, Aijath, Hai; n-pr-loc

- a city lying east of Bethel and beside Bethaven near Jericho and the 2nd city taken on the invasion of Canaan
- a city of the Ammonites on the east of the Jordan and apparently attached to Heshbon
