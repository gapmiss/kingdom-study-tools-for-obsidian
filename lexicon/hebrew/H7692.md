_(shig-gaw-yone' | shig-gaw-YONE | shee-ɡa-YONE)_

or שִׁגָּיֹנָה; from H7686; properly, aberration, i.e., (technically) a dithyramb or rambling poem; Shiggaion, Shigionoth; n-pr-m

- song?
