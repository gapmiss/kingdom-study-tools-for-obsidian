# H1598 גָּנַן / גנן

## gânan

_(gaw-nan' | ɡaw-NAHN | ɡa-NAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to hedge about, i.e., (generally) protect; defend; v

- to defend, cover, surround
  - (Qal) to defend
  - (Hiphil) to defend
