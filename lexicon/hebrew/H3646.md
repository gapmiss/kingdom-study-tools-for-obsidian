# H3646 כַּמֹּן / כמן

## kammôn

_(kam-mone' | kahm-MONE | ka-MONE)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to store up or preserve; 'cummin' (from its use as a condiment); cummin; n-m

- cummin, cumin
  - a seed used as a condiment
