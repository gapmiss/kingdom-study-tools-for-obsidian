# H980 בַּחֻרִים / בחרים

## Bachurîym

_(bakh-oo-reem' | ba-hoo-REEM | ba-hoo-REEM)_

## Definition

young men's village; or בַּחוּרִים; masculine plural of H0970; young men; Bachurim, a place in Palestine; Bahurim; n-pr-loc

- a town in Benjamin between Jerusalem and Jericho beyond the Mount of Olives on the way to Jericho
