# H5695 עֵגֶל / עגל

## ʻêgel

_(ay-ghel | ay-ɡEL | ay-ɡEL)_

## Definition

from the same as H5696; a (male) calf (as frisking round), especially one nearly grown (i.e., a steer); bullock, calf; n-m

- calf, bull-calf
