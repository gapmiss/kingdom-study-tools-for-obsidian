# H8318 שֶׁרֶץ / שרץ

## sherets

_(sheh'-rets | sheh-RETS | sheh-RETS)_

## Definition

from H8317; a swarm, i.e., active mass of minute animals; creep(-ing thing), move(-ing creature); n-m

- teeming or swarming things, creepers, swarmers
  - of insects, animals, small reptiles, quadrupeds
