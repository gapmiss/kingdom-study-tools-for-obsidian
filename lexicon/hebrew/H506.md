# H506 אֲלַף / אלף

## ʼălaph

_(al-af' | uh-LAHP | uh-LAHF)_

## Definition

or אֶלֶף; (Aramaic), corresponding to H0505; hence (the ox's head being the first letter of the alphabet, and this eventually used as a numeral) a thousand; thousand; n-m

- a thousand, 1000

## Verse(s)

[[Daniel 5#1|Daniel 5:1]], [[Daniel 7#10|Daniel 7:10]]
