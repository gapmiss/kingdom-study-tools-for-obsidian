# H6847 צׇפְנַת פַּעְנֵחַ / צפנת פענח

## Tsophnath Paʻnêach

_(tsof-nath' pah-nay'-akh | tsope-NAHT pa-NAY-ah | tsofe-NAHT pa-NAY-ak)_

## Definition

treasury of the glorious rest; of Egyptian derivation; Tsophnath-Paneach, Joseph's Egyptian name; Zaphnath-paaneah; n-pr-m

- a name given by Pharaoh to Joseph
