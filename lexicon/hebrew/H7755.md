# H7755 שׂוֹכֹה / שוכה

## Sôwkôh

_(so-ko' | soh-KOH | soh-HOH)_

## Definition

bushy; or שֹׂכֹה; or שׂוֹכוֹ; from H7753; Sokoh or Soko, the name of two places in Palestine; Shocho, Shochoh, Sochoh, Soco, Socoh; n-pr-loc

- a town in the lowlands of Judah
- a town in the mountain district of Judah
