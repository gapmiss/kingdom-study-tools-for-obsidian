# H3763 כְּרָן / כרן

## Kᵉrân

_(ker-awn' | keh-RAWN | keh-RAHN)_

## Definition

lyre; of uncertain derivation; Keran, an indigenous Idumaean; Cheran; n-pr-m

- an Edomite, one of the sons of Dishon, the Horite duke
