# H5733 עַדְנָא / עדנא

## ʻAdnâʼ

_(ad-naw' | ad-NAW | ad-NA)_

## Definition

rest, pleasure; from H5727; pleasure; Adna, the name of two Israelites; Adna; n-pr-m

- one of the family of Pahath-moab who returned with Ezra and married a foreign wife
- a priest, descendant of Harim in the days of Joiakim, the son of Jeshua

[[Ezra 10#30|Ezra 10:30]], [[Nehemiah 12#15|Nehemiah 12:15]]
