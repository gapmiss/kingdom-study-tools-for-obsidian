# H8480 תַּחַת / תחת

## Tachath

_(takh'-ath | ta-HAHT | ta-HAHT)_

## Definition

station; the same as H8478; Tachath, the name of a place in the Desert, also of three Israelites; Tahath; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a Kohathite Levite, son of Assir and father of Uriel
- a Kohathite Levite, son of Assir and father of Zephaniah
  - maybe the same as 1
- (n pr loc) a station of Israel in the wilderness
- son of Eladah, grandson of 3, and father Zabad
