# H2123 זִיז / זיז

## zîyz

_(zeez | dzeedz | zeez)_

## Definition

from an unused root apparently meaning to be conspicuous; fulness of the breast; abundance, wild beast; n-m

- (n m coll) moving creatures, moving things
- (n m) abundance, fullness
