# H8377 תְּאוֹ / תאו

_(teh-o' | teh-OH | teh-OH)_

## Definition

and תּוֹא; (the original form), from H8376; a species of antelope (probably from the white stripe on the cheek); wild bull (ox); n-m

- wild ox, antelope, oryx
  - perhaps an extinct animal, exact meaning unknown
