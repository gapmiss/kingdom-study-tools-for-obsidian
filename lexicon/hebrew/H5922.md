# H5922 עַל / על

## ʻal

_(al | al | al)_

## Definition

corresponding to H5921; above, over, upon, or against (yet always in this last relation with a downward aspect) in a great variety of applications; about, against, concerning, for, (there-)fore, from, in, × more, of, (there-, up-)on, (in-)to, + why with; prep

- upon, over, on account of, above, to, against
  - upon, over, on account of, regarding, concerning, on behalf of
  - over (with verbs of ruling)
  - above, beyond (in comparison)
  - to, against (of direction)
