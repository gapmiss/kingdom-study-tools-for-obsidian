# H5886 עֵין תַּנִּים / עין תנים

## ʻÊyn Tannîym

_(ane tan-neem' | ane tahn-NEEM | ane ta-NEEM)_

## Definition

fountain of the jackal; from H5869 and the plural of H8565; fountain of jackals; En-Tannim, a pool near Jerusalem; dragon well; n-pr-loc

- a spring near Jerusalem
