# H62 אָבֵל בֵּית־מֲעַכָה / אבל ביתמעכה

## ʼÂbêl Bêyth-Măʻakâh

_(aw-bale' bayth ma-akaw' | aw-BALE bate muh-ah-KAW | ah-VALE bate muh-ah-HA)_

## Definition

meadow of the house of Maachah; from H0058 and H1004 and H4601; meadow of Beth-Maakah; Abel of Beth-maakah, a place in Palestine; Abel-beth-maachah, Abel of Beth-maachah; n-pr-loc

- city in northern Israel near Beth Maachah
