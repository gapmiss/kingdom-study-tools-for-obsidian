# H433 אֱלוֹהַּ / אלוה

## ʼĕlôwahh

_(el-o'-ah | eh-LOH-ah | ay-LOH-ah)_

## Definition

rarely (shortened) אֱלֹהַּ; probably prolonged (emphatic) from H0410; a deity or the Deity; God, god; n-m

- God
- false god
