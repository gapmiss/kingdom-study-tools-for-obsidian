# H2510 חָלָק / חלק

## Châlâq

_(khaw-lawk' | haw-LAWK | ha-LAHK)_

## Definition

smooth; the same as H2509; bare; Chalak, a mountain of Idumaea; Halak; n-pr-loc

- a mountain in southern Judah which is the southern limit of Joshua's conquest; site unknown
