# H6818 צַעֲקָה / צעקה

## tsaʻăqâh

_(tsah-ak-aw' | tsa-uh-KAW | tsa-uh-KA)_

## Definition

from H6817; a shriek; cry(-ing); n-f

- cry, outcry
  - outcry (against)
  - cry of distress (especially as heard by God)
