# H5050 נָגַהּ / נגה

## nâgahh

_(naw-gah' | naw-ɡA | na-ɡA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to glitter; causatively, to illuminate; (en-)lighten, (cause to) shine; v

- to shine
  - (Qal) to shine
  - (Hiphil)
    - to enlighten
    - to cause to shine
