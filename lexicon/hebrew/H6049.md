# H6049 עָנַן / ענן

## ʻânan

_(aw-nan' | aw-NAHN | ah-NAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to cover; used only as a denominative from H6051, to cloud over; figuratively, to act covertly, i.e., practise magic; × bring, enchanter, Meonemin, observe(-r of) times, soothsayer, sorcerer; v

- (Piel) to make appear, produce, bring (clouds)
- (Poel) to practise soothsaying, conjure
  - to observe times, practice soothsaying or spiritism or magic or augury or witchcraft
  - soothsayer, enchanter, sorceress, diviner, fortuneteller, barbarian, Meonenim (participle)
