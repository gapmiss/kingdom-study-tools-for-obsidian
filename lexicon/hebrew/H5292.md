# H5292 נַעֲרָה / נערה

## Naʻărâh

_(nah-ar-aw' | na-uh-RAW | na-uh-RA)_

## Definition

maiden; the same as H5291; Naarah, the name of an Israelitess; Naarah, Naarath; n-pr

- (n pr f) the 2nd wife of Ashur of the tribe of Judah
- (n pr loc) one of the landmarks on the southern boundary of the territory of Ephraim
