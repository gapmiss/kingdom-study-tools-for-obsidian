# H842 אֲשֵׁרָה / אשרה

## ʼăshêrâh

_(ash-ay-raw' | uh-shay-RAW | uh-shay-RA)_

## Definition

groves (for idol worship); or אֲשֵׁירָה; from H0833; happy; Asherah (or Astarte) a Phoenician goddess; also an image of the same; grove; n-pr-f

- a Babylonian and Canaanite goddess (of fortune and happiness), the goddess Astarte (in Babylonian), the supposed consort of Baal
  - the goddess, goddesses
  - her images
  - sacred trees or poles set up near an altar

Compare H6253.
