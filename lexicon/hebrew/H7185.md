# H7185 קָשָׁה / קשה

## qâshâh

_(kaw-shaw' | kaw-SHAW | ka-SHA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to be dense, i.e., tough or severe (in various applications); be cruel, be fiercer, make grievous, be ((ask a), be in, have, seem, would) hard(-en, (labour), -ly, thing), be sore, (be, make) stiff(-en, -necked); v

- to be hard, be severe, be fierce, be harsh
  - (Qal)
    - to be hard, be difficult
    - to be hard, be severe
  - (Niphal)
    - to be ill-treated
    - to be hard pressed
  - (Piel) to have severe labor (of women)
  - (Hiphil)
    - to make difficult, make difficulty
    - to make severe, make burdensome
    - to make hard, make stiff, make stubborn
      - of obstinacy (figuratively)
    - to show stubbornness
