# H836 אָשֵׁר / אשר

## ʼÂshêr

_(aw-share' | aw-SHARE | ah-SHARE)_

## Definition

happy; from H0833, happy; Asher, a son of Jacob, and the tribe descended from him, with its territory; also a place in Palestine; Asher; n-pr

- son of Jacob and Zilpah
- the tribe descended from Asher
- a city east of Shechem in Manasseh
