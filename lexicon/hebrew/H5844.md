# H5844 עָטָה / עטה

## ʻâṭâh

_(aw-taw' | aw-TAW | ah-TA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to wrap, i.e., cover, veil, clothe, or roll; array self, be clad, (put a) cover (-ing) (self), fill, put on, × surely, turn aside; v

- to cover, enwrap, wrap oneself, envelop oneself
  - (Qal)
    - to wrap, envelop oneself
    - to cover (the beard in mourning)
    - mourner, one who covers (participle)
  - (Hiphil) to cover, enwrap, envelop
- to grasp
  - (Qal) to grasp
