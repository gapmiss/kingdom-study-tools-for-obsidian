# H2347 חוּס / חוס

## chûwç

_(khoos | hoos | hoos)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to cover, i.e., (figuratively) to compassionate; pity, regard, spare; v

- (Qal) to pity, have compassion, spare, look upon with compassion
