# H1375 גְּבִיעַ / גביע

## gᵉbîyaʻ

_(gheb-ee'-ah | ɡeh-BEE-ah | ɡeh-VEE-ah)_

## Definition

from an unused root (meaning to be convex); a goblet; by analogy, the calyx of a flower; house, cup, pot; n-m

- cup, bowl
