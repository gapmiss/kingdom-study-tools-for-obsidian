## ʼâmôts

_(aw-mohts' | aw-MOHTS | ah-MOHTS)_

## Definition

probably from H0553; of a strong color, i.e., red (others fleet); bay; a

- strong
- bay, dappled, piebald (of color), spotted with two different colors (especially white and black)
