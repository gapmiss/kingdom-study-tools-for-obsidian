# H6981 קוֹרֵא / קורא

## Qôwrêʼ

_(ko-ray' | koh-RAY | koh-RAY)_

## Definition

crier; or קֹרֵא; (1 Chronicles 26:1), active participle of H7121; crier; Kore, the name of two Israelites; Kore; n-pr-m

- a Korahite Levite, ancestor of Shallum and Meshelemiah, chief porters in the reign of David
- a Levite, son of Imnah, in the reign of king Hezekiah of Judah
