## Migdal-Gâd

_(migdal-gawd' | miɡ-DAHL ɡawd | meeɡ-DAHL ɡahd)_

## Definition

tower of God; from H4026 and H1408; tower of Fortune; Migdal-Gad, a place in Palestine; Migdal-gad; n-pr-loc

- one of the fortified cities of Judah
