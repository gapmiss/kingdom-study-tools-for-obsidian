# H63 אָבֵל הַשִּׁטִּים / אבל השטים

## ʼÂbêl hash-Shiṭṭîym

_(aw-bale' hash-shit-teem' | aw-BALE hahsh-shit-TEEM | ah-VALE ha-shee-TEEM)_

## Definition

meadow of acacias; from H0058 and the plural of H7848, with the article inserted; meadow of the acacias; Abel hash-Shittim, a place in Palestine; Abelshittim; n-pr-loc

- place in lowlands of Moab
