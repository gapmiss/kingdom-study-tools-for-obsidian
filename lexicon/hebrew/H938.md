# H938 בּוּז / בוז

## Bûwz

_(booz | boodz | booz)_

## Definition

contempt; the same as H0937; Buz, the name of a son of Nahor, and of an Israelite; Buz; n-pr-m

- second son of Nahor, Abraham's nephew
- a Gadite
