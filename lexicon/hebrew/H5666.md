# H5666 עָבָה / עבה

## ʻâbâh

_(aw-baw' | aw-BAW | ah-VA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be dense; be (grow) thick(-er); v

- to be thick, be fat, be gross
  - (Qal) to be fat, be thick, be gross
