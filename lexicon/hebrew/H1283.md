# H1283 בְּרִיעָה / בריעה

## Bᵉrîyʻâh

_(ber-ee'-aw | beh-ree-AW | beh-ree-AH)_

## Definition

with a friend; apparently from the feminine of H7451 with a prepositional prefix; in trouble; Beriah, the name of four Israelites; Beriah; n-pr-m

- a son of Asher
- a son of Ephraim
- a Benjamite
- a Levite
