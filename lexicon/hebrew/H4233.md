## Machăvîym

_(makh-av-eem' | ma-huh-WEEM | ma-huh-VEEM)_

## Definition

propagators; apparently a patrial, but from an unknown place (in the plural only for a singular); a Machavite or inhabitant of some place named Machaveh; Mahavite; a

- apparently, an inhabitant of a place called 'Macheweh'
  - the designation of Eliel, one of David's mighty warriors
