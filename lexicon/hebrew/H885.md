# H885 בְּאֵרֹת בְּנֵי־יַעֲקַן / בארת בנייעקן

## Bᵉʼêrôth Bᵉnêy-Yaʻăqan

_(be-ay-roth' be-nay' yah-a-can' | beh-ay-ROTE beh-NAY ya-uh-KAHN | beh-ay-ROTE beh-NAY ya-uh-KAHN)_

## Definition

wells, wells of the sons of Jaakan; from the feminine plural of H0875, and the plural contraction of H1121, and H3292; wells of (the) sons of Jaakan; Beeroth-Bene-Jaakan, a place in the Desert; Beeroth of the children of Jaakan; n-pr-loc

- an encampment for Israel in the wilderness probably in the country of the Horites
