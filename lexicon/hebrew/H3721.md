# H3721 כָּפַף / כפף

## kâphaph

_(kaw-faf' | kaw-PAHP | ka-FAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to curve; bow down (self); v

- to bend, bend down, bow down, be bent, be bowed
  - (Qal) to bend down, bow down
  - (Niphal) to bow oneself down
