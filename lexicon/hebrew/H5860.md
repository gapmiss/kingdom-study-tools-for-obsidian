# H5860 עִיט / עיט

## ʻîyṭ

_(eet | eet | eet)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to swoop down upon (literally or figuratively); fly, rail; v

- to scream, shriek
  - (Qal) to scream
- to dart greedily, swoop upon, rush upon
  - (Qal) to dart greedily
