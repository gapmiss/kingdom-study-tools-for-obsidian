# H6850 צָפַף / צפף

## tsâphaph

_(tsaw-faf' | tsaw-PAHP | tsa-FAHF)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to coo or chirp (as a bird); chatter, peep, whisper; v

- (Pilpel) to chirp, peep
  - of birds
  - of ghosts
