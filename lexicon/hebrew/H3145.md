_(yo-shav-yaw' | yoh-sha-YAW | yoh-shahv-YA)_

## Definition

Jehovah makes equal; from H3068 and H7737; Jehovah-set; Joshavjah, an Israelite; Joshaviah; n-pr-m

- son of Elnaam and one of David's mighty warriors
