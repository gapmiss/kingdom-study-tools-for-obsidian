# H4762 מַרְאֵשָׁה / מראשה

## Marʼêshâh

_(mar-ay-shaw' | mahr-ay-SHAW | mahr-ay-SHA)_

## Definition

crest of a hill; or מַרֵשָׁה; formed like H4761; summit; Mareshah, the name of two Israelites and of a place in Palestine; Mareshah; n-pr-loc

- one of the cities in the lowlands of Judah
