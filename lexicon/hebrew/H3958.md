# H3958 לֶשֶׁם / לשם

## leshem

_(leh'-shem | leh-SHEM | leh-SHEM)_

## Definition

from an unused root of uncertain meaning; a gem, perhaps the jacinth; ligure; n-m

- a precious stone
  - a precious stone in the high priest's breast-plate
    - probably jacinth or ligure
