# H2115 זוּר / זור

## zûwr

_(zoor | dzoor | zoor)_

## Definition

a primitive root (compare H6695); to press together, tighten; close, rush, thrust together; v

- (Qal) to press, squeeze, crush, press down and out
  - to close up (of a wound)
