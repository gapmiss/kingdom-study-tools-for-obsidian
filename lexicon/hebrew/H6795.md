# H6795 צָנַח / צנח

## tsânach

_(tsaw-nakh' | tsaw-NA | tsa-NAHK)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to alight; (transitive) to cause to descend, i.e., drive down; fasten, light (from off); v

- (Qal) to go down, descend
