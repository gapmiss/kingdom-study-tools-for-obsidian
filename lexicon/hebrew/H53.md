# H53 אֲבִישָׁלוֹם / אבישלום

## ʼĂbîyshâlôwm

_(ab-ee-shaw-lome' | uh-bee-shaw-LOME | uh-vee-sha-LOME)_

## Definition

my father is peace; or (shortened) אַבְשָׁלוֹם ; from H0001 and H7965; father of peace (i.e., friendly); Abshalom, a son of David; also (the fuller form) a later Israelite; Abishalom, Absalom; n-pr-m

- father-in-law of Rehoboam
- third son of David, killer of first-born son Amnon, also leader of revolt against his father, David
