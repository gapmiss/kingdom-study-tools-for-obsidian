# H2767 חׇרְמָה / חרמה

## Chormâh

_(khor-maw' | hore-MAW | hore-MA)_

## Definition

devotion; from H2763; devoted; Chormah, a place in Palestine; Hormah; n-pr-loc

- a town of the Canaanites, conquered by Joshua, allotted to Judah, and located in the south of Judah
