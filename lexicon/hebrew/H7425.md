# H7425 רְמַלְיָהוּ / רמליהו

## Rᵉmalyâhûw

_(rem-al-yaw'-hoo | reh-mahl-yaw-HOO | reh-mahl-ya-HOO)_

## Definition

protected by Jehovah; from an unused root and H3050 (perhaps meaning to deck); Jah has bedecked; Remaljah, an Israelite; Remaliah; n-pr-m

- father of king Pekah of the northern kingdom of Israel
