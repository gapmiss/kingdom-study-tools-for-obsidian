# H6166 עֲרָד / ערד

## ʻĂrâd

_(ar-awd' | uh-RAWD | uh-RAHD)_

## Definition

a wild ass; from an unused root meaning to sequester itself; fugitive; Arad, the name of a Canaanite and an Israelite; Arad; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a Benjamite, son of Beriah, who drove out the inhabitants of Gath
- (n pr loc) a royal city of the Canaanites north of the wilderness of Judah
