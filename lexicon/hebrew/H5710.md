# H5710 עָדָה / עדה

## ʻâdâh

_(aw-daw' | aw-DAW | ah-DA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to advance, i.e., pass on or continue; causatively, to remove; specifically, to bedeck (i.e., bring an ornament upon); adorn, deck (self), pass by, take away; v

- to pass on, advance, go on, pass by, remove
  - (Qal) to pass on, put on
  - (Hiphil) to remove
