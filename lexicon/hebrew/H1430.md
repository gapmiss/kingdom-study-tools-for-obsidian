# H1430 גָּדִישׁ / גדיש

## gâdîysh

_(gaw-deesh' | ɡaw-DEESH | ɡa-DEESH)_

## Definition

from an unused root (meaning to heap up); a stack of sheaves; by analogy, a tomb; shock (stack) (of corn); n-m

- heap, stack, pile
- tomb
