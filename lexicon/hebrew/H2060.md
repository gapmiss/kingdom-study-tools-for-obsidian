## Vashtîy

_(vash-tee' | wahsh-TEE | vahsh-TEE)_

## Definition

beautiful; of Persian origin; Vashti, the queen of Xerxes; Vashti; n-pr-f

- the queen, wife of Ahasuerus, whom he divorced for disobeying his orders
