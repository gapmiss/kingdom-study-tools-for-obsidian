# H990 בֶּטֶן / בטן

## beṭen

_(beh'-ten | beh-TEN | beh-TEN)_

## Definition

from an unused root probably meaning to be hollow; the belly, especially the womb; also the bosom or body of anything; belly, body, + as they be born, + within, womb; n-f

- belly, womb, body
  - belly, abdomen
    - as seat of hunger, as seat of mental faculties, of depth of Sheol (figuratively)
  - womb
