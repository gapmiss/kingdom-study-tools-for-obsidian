# H8490 תִּימָרָה / תימרה

## tîymârâh

_(tee-maw-raw' | tee-maw-RAW | tee-ma-RA)_

## Definition

or תִּמָרָה; from the same as H8558; a column, i.e., cloud; pillar; n-f

- pillar, column
  - palm-like spreading at top
