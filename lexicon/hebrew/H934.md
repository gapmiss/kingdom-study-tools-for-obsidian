# H934 בֹּהֶרֶת / בהרת

## bôhereth

_(bo-heh'-reth | boh-heh-RET | boh-heh-RET)_

## Definition

feminine active participle of the same as H0925; a whitish spot on the skin; bright spot; n-f

- white patch of skin, brightness, bright spot (on skin) (possible sign of leprosy)
