# H7698 שֶׁגֶר / שגר

## sheger

_(sheh'-ger | sheh-ɡER | sheh-ɡER)_

## Definition

from an unused root probably meaning to eject; the fœtus (as finally expelled); that cometh of, increase; n-f

- offspring, young or offspring of beasts
