# H3800 כֶּתֶם / כתם

## kethem

_(keh'-them | keh-TEM | keh-TEM)_

## Definition

from H3799; properly, something carved out, i.e., ore; hence, gold (pure as originally mined); ((most) fine, pure) gold(-en wedge); n-m

- gold, pure gold
