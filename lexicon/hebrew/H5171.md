# H5171 נַחֲרַי / נחרי

## Nachăray

_(nakh-ar-ah'-ee | na-huh-RAI | na-huh-RAI)_

## Definition

snorter; or נַחְרַי; from the same as H5170; snorer; Nacharai or Nachrai, an Israelite; Naharai, Nahari; n-pr-m

- one of David's mighty warriors and the armour bearer of Joab
