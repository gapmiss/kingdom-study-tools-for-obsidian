# H730 אֶרֶז / ארז

## ʼerez

_(eh-rez' | eh-REDZ | eh-REZ)_

## Definition

from H0729; a cedar tree (from the tenacity of its roots); cedar (tree); n-m

- cedar
  - cedar tree
  - cedar timber, cedar wood (in building)
  - cedar wood (in purifications)
