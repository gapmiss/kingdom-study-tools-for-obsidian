# H4834 מָרַץ / מרץ

## mârats

_(maw-rats' | maw-RAHTS | ma-RAHTS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to press, i.e., (figuratively) to be pungent or vehement; to irritate; embolden, be forcible, grievous, sore; v

- to be or make sick
  - (Niphal) to be grievous or painful
  - (Hiphil) to make sick
