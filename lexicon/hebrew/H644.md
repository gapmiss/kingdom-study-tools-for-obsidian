# H644 אָפָה / אפה

## ʼâphâh

_(aw-faw' | aw-PAW | ah-FA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to cook, especially to bake; bake(-r, -meats); v

- to bake
  - (Qal)
    - to bake
    - (subst) baker
  - (Niphal) to be baked, baked
