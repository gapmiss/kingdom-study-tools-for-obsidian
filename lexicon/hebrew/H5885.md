# H5885 עֵין שֶׁמֶשׁ / עין שמש

## ʻÊyn Shemesh

_(ane sheh'-mesh | ane sheh-MESH | ane sheh-MESH)_

## Definition

fountain of the sun; from H5869 and H8121; fountain of the sun; En-Shemesh, a place in Palestine; Enshemesh; n-pr-loc

- a spring on the boundary of Judah on the south and Benjamin on the north
