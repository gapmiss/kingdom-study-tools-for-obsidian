# H1838 דִּנְהָבָה / דנהבה

## Dinhâbâh

_(din-haw-baw' | din-haw-BAW | deen-ha-VA)_

## Definition

give thou judgement; of uncertain derivation; Dinhabah, an Edomitish town; Dinhaban; n-pr-loc

- capital city of Bela, king of Edom
