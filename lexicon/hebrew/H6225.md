# H6225 עָשַׁן / עשן

## ʻâshan

_(aw-shan' | aw-SHAHN | ah-SHAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to smoke, whether literal or figurative; be angry (be on a) smoke; v

- to smoke, be angry, be wroth
  - (Qal)
    - to smoke
    - to fume, be wroth, be furious
