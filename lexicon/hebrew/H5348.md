# H5348 נָקֹד / נקד

## nâqôd

_(naw-kode' | naw-KODE | na-KODE)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to mark (by puncturing or branding); spotted; speckled; a

- speckled, marked with points
  - of sheep and goats
