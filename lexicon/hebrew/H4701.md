# H4701 מִצְנֶפֶת / מצנפת

## mitsnepheth

_(mits-neh'-feth | mits-neh-PET | meets-neh-FET)_

## Definition

from H6801; a tiara, i.e., official turban (of a king or high priest); diadem, mitre; n-f

- turban (of the high priest)
