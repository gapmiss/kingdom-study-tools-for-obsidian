# H6780 צֶמַח / צמח

## tsemach

_(tseh'-makh | tseh-MA | tseh-MAHK)_

## Definition

from H6779; a sprout (usually concrete), literal or figurative; branch, bud, that which (where) grew (upon), spring(-ing); n-m

- sprout, growth, branch
  - sprouting, growth, sprout
  - growth (of process)
  - sprout, shoot (of Messiah from Davidic tree)
