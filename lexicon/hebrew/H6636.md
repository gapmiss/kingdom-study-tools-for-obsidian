# H6636 צְבֹאִים / צבאים

## Tsᵉbôʼîym

_(tseb-o-eem' | tseh-boh-EEM | tseh-voh-EEM)_

## Definition

gazelles; or (more correctly) צְבִיִּים; or צְבִיִּם; plural of H6643; gazelles; Tseboim or Tsebijim, a place in Palestine; Zeboiim, Zeboim; n-pr-loc

- one of the 5 cities in the plain which included Sodom and Gomorrah
