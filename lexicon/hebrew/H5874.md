# H5874 עֵין־דֹּאר / עיןדאר

## ʻÊyn-Dôʼr

_(ane-dore' | ane dore | ane dore)_

## Definition

fountain of Dor; or עֵין דּוֹר; or עֵין־דֹּר; from H5869 and H1755; fountain of dwelling; En-Dor, a place in Palestine; En-dor; n-pr-loc

- a place in the territory of Issachar yet possessed by Manasseh; located 4 miles (6.5 km) north of Tabor
  - place of residence of the spiritist consulted by king Saul
