# H7707 שְׁדֵיאוּר / שדיאור

## Shᵉdêyʼûwr

_(shed-ay-oor' | sheh-day-OOR | sheh-day-OOR)_

## Definition

darter of light; from the same as H7704 and H0217; spreader of light; Shedejur, an Israelite; Shedeur; n-pr-m

- father of Elizur, chief of the tribe of Reuben at the time of the exodus
