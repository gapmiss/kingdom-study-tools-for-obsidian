# H7300 רוּד / רוד

## rûwd

_(rood | rood | rood)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to tramp about, i.e., ramble (free or disconsolate); have the dominion, be lord, mourn, rule; v

- to wander restlessly, roam
  - (Qal) to roam
  - (Hiphil) to be restless, show restlessness
