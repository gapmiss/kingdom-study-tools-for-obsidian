# H7565 רֶשֶׁף / רשף

## resheph

_(reh'-shef | reh-SHEP | reh-SHEF)_

## Definition

from H8313; a live coal; by analogy lightning; figuratively, an arrow, (as flashing through the air); specifically, fever; arrow, (burning) coal, burning heat, + spark, hot thunderbolt; n-m

- flame, firebolt, spark
  - flame
  - fire-bolt
