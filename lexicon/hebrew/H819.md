# H819 אַשְׁמָה / אשמה

## ʼashmâh

_(ash-maw' | ash-MAW | ash-MA)_

## Definition

feminine of H0817; guiltiness, a fault, the presentation of a sin-offering; offend, sin, (cause of) trespass(-ing) (offering); n-f

- guiltiness, guilt, offense, sin, wrong-doing
  - doing wrong, committing a trespass or offense
  - becoming guilty, guilt
  - bringing a guilt-offering
