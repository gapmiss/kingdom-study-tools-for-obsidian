# H8303 שִׁרְיוֹן / שריון

## Shiryôwn

_(shir-yone' | shir-YONE | sheer-YONE)_

## Definition

breastplate; and שִׂרְיֹן; the same as H8304 (i.e., sheeted with snow); Shirjon or Sirjon, a peak of the Lebanon; Sirion; n-pr

- one of the names of Mount Hermon this one used by the Sidonians
