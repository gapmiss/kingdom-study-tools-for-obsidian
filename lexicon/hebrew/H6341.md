# H6341 פַּח / פח

## pach

_(pakh | pa | pahk)_

## Definition

from H6351; a (metallic) sheet (as pounded thin); gin, (thin) plate, snare; n-m

- bird trap, trap, snare
  - bird trap (literally)
  - of calamities, plots, source or agent of calamity (figuratively)
- plate (of metal)
