# H7853 שָׂטַן / שטן

## sâṭan

_(saw-tan' | saw-TAHN | sa-TAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to attack, (figuratively) accuse; (be an) adversary, resist; v

- (Qal) to be or act as an adversary, resist, oppose
