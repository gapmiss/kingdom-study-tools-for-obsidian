# H8669 תְּשׁוּקָה / תשוקה

## tᵉshûwqâh

_(tesh-oo-kaw' | teh-shoo-KAW | teh-shoo-KA)_

## Definition

from H7783 in the original sense of stretching out after; a longing; desire; n-f

- desire, longing, craving
  - of man for woman
  - of woman for man
  - of beast to devour
