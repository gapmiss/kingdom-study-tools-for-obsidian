# H8093 שִׁמְעָה / שמעה

## Shimʻâh

_(shim-aw' | shim-AW | sheem-AH)_

## Definition

fame; feminine of H8088; annunciation; Shimah, an Israelite; Shimeah; n-pr-m

- brother of David, the 3rd son of Jesse
  - also 'Shammah', and 'Shimma'
