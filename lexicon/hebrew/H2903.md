# H2903 טוֹפָפָה / טופפה

## ṭôwphâphâh

_(to-faw-faw' | toh-paw-PAW | toh-fa-FA)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to go around or bind; a fillet for the forehead; frontlet; n-f

- bands, phylacteries, frontlets, marks
