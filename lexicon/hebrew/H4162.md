# H4162 מוֹצָא / מוצא

## môwtsâʼ

_(mo-tsaw' | moh-TSAW | moh-TSA)_

## Definition

fountain; the same as H4161; Motsa, the name of two Israelites; Moza; n-pr-m

- son of Caleb by Ephah his concubine
- son of Zimri and a descendant of Saul
