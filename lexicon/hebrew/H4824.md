## Mêrônôthîy

_(may-ro-no-thee' | may-roh-noh-TEE | may-roh-noh-TEE)_

## Definition

joyful shouter; patrial from an unused noun; a Meronothite, or inhabitant of some (otherwise unknown) Meronoth; Meronothite; a

- a native of a place probably called 'Meronoth'
  - Jehdeiah
  - Jadon
