# H3872 לוּחִית / לוחית

## Lûwchîyth

_(loo-kheeth' | loo-HEET | loo-HEET)_

## Definition

tablets; or לֻחוֹת; (Jeremiah 48:5), from the same as H3871; floored; Luchith, a place East of the Jordan; Luhith; n-pr-loc

- a town of Moab, south of the Arnon River
