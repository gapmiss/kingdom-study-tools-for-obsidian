# H4222 מָחָא / מחא

## mâchâʼ

_(maw-khaw' | maw-HAW | ma-HA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to rub or strike the hands together (in exultation); clap; v

- to strike, clap (the hands)
  - (Qal) to clap (of joy)
  - (Piel) to clap (of exultation)
