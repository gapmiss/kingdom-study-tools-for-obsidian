# H406 אִכָּר / אכר

## ʼikkâr

_(ik-kawr' | ik-KAWR | ee-KAHR)_

## Definition

from an unused root meaning to dig; a farmer; husbandman, ploughman; n-m

- plowman, husbandman, farmer
  - working the land, yet not owning any of it
