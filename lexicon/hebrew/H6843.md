_(tsef-ee-raw' | tseh-pee-RAW | tseh-fee-RA)_

## Definition

feminine formed like H6842; a crown (as encircling the head); also a turn of affairs (i.e., mishap); diadem, morning; n-f

- plait, chaplet, wreath, crown
