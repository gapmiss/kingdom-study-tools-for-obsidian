# H6900 קְבוּרָה / קבורה

## qᵉbûwrâh

_(keb-oo-raw' | keh-boo-RAW | keh-voo-RA)_

## Definition

or קְבֻרָה; feminine passive participle of H6912; sepulture; (concretely) a sepulcher; burial, burying place, grave, sepulchre; n-f

- grave, burial, burial site
  - grave
  - burial
