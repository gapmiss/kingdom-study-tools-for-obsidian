# H6560 פֶּרֶץ עֻזָּא / פרץ עזא

## Perets ʻUzzâʼ

_(peh'-rets ooz-zaw' | peh-RETS oodz-ZAW | peh-RETS oo-ZA)_

## Definition

breach of Uzza; from H6556 and H5798; break of Uzza; Perets-Uzza, a place in Palestine; Perezuzza; n-pr-loc

- the place where Uzzah was slain by God for touching the Ark; located close to Jerusalem

[[2 Samuel 6#8|2 Samuel 6:8]], [[1 Chronicles 13#11|1 Chronicles 13:11]]
