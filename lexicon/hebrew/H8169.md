# H8169 שַׁעַלְבִים / שעלבים

## Shaʻalbîym

_(shah-al-beem' | sha-al-BEEM | sha-al-VEEM)_

## Definition

place of foxes; or שַׁעֲלַבִּין; plural from H7776; fox-holes; Shaalbim or Shaalabbin, a place in Palestine; Shaalabbin, Shaalbim; n-pr-loc

- a town in Dan occupied by the Amorites
