# H7157 קִרְיַת יְעָרִים / קרית יערים

## Qiryath Yᵉʻârîym

_(keer-yath' yeh-aw-reem' | kir-YAHT yeh-aw-REEM | keer-YAHT yeh-ah-REEM)_

## Definition

city of forests; or (Jeremiah 26:20) with the article interposed; or (Joshua 18:28) simply the former part of the word; or קִרְיַת עָרִים; from H7151 and the plural of H3293 or H5892; city of forests, or city of towns; Kirjath-Jearim or Kirjath-Arim, a place in Palestine; Kirjath, Kirjath-jearim, Kirjath-arim; n-pr-loc

- a city on the northern boundary of Judah and on the western and southern boundaries of Benjamin
  - also 'Kirjath-baal' and 'Baalah'
