# H5278 נֹעַם / נעם

## nôʻam

_(no'-am | noh-AM | noh-AM)_

## Definition

from H5276; agreeableness, i.e., delight, suitableness, splendor or grace; beauty, pleasant(-ness); n-m

- kindness, pleasantness, delightfulness, beauty, favor
  - delightfulness
  - symbolic name of one of two staves
  - pleasantness (of genitals)
