# H5059 נָגַן / נגן

## nâgan

_(naw-gan' | naw-ɡAHN | na-ɡAHN)_

## Definition

a primitive root; properly, to thrum, i.e., beat a tune with the fingers; expectation. to play on a stringed instrument; hence (generally), to make music; player on instruments, sing to the stringed instruments, melody, ministrel, play(-er, -ing); v

- to play or strike strings, play a stringed instrument
  - (Qal)
    - player (participle)
  - (Piel)
    - to play
      - player, minstrel (participle)
