# H3391 יֶרַח / ירח

## yerach

_(yeh'-rakh | yeh-RA | yeh-RAHK)_

## Definition

from a unused root of uncertain signification; a lunation, i.e., month; month, moon; n-m

- month (lunar cycle), moon
  - month
  - calendar month
