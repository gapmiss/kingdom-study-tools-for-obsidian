# H3850 לֹד / לד

## Lôd

_(lode | lode | lode)_

## Definition

travail; from an unused root of uncertain signification; Lod, a place in Palestine; Lod; n-pr-loc n-pr-m

- (n pr loc) a city approximately 11 miles (17 km) southeast of Joppa in the plain of Sharon in the territory of Benjamin
- (n pr m) head of a family of returning exiles
