# H7814 שְׂחוֹק / שחוק

## sᵉchôwq

_(sekh-oke' | seh-HOKE | seh-HOKE)_

## Definition

or שְׂחֹק; from H7832; laughter (in merriment or defiance); derision, laughter(-ed to scorn, -ing), mocked, sport; n-m

- laughter, laughing stock, mocking, derision
  - laughter
    - joyful
    - hollow
  - derision (of object)
  - sport
