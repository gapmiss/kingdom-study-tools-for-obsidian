# H7567 רָשַׁשׁ / רשש

## râshash

_(raw-shash' | raw-SHAHSH | ra-SHAHSH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to demolish; impoverish; v

- to beat down, shatter
  - (Poel) to beat down, shatter
  - (Pual) to be beaten down
