# H6976 קוֹץ / קוץ

## Qôwts

_(kotse | kohts | kohts)_

## Definition

thorn; the same as H6975; Kots, the name of two Israelites; Koz, Hakkoz (including the article); n-pr-m

- a descendant of Judah
- a priest who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
