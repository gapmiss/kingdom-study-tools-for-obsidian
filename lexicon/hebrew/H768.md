# H768 אַרְנֶבֶת / ארנבת

## ʼarnebeth

_(ar-neh'-beth | ar-neh-BET | ar-neh-VET)_

## Definition

of uncertain derivation; the hare; hare; n-f

- hare
  - probably an extinct animal because no known hare chews its cud, exact meaning is unknown, and best left untranslated as &quot;arnebeth&quot;
