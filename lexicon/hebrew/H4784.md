# H4784 מָרָה / מרה

## mârâh

_(maw-raw' | maw-RAW | ma-RA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to be (causatively, make) bitter (or unpleasant); (figuratively) to rebel (or resist; causatively, to provoke); bitter, change, be disobedient, disobey, grievously, provocation, provoke(-ing), (be) rebel (against, -lious); v

- to be contentious, be rebellious, be refractory, be disobedient towards, be rebellious against
  - (Qal) to be disobedient, be rebellious
    - towards father
    - towards God
  - (Hiphil) to show rebelliousness, show disobedience, disobey
