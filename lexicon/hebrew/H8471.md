# H8471 תַּחְפַּנְחֵס / תחפנחס

## Tachpanchêç

_(takh-pan-khace' | ta-pahn-HASE | tahk-pahn-HASE)_

## Definition

thou will fill hands with pity; or תְּחַפְנְחֵס; (Ezekiel 30:18), or תַּחְפְּנֵס; (Jeremiah 2:16), of Egyptian derivation; Tachpanches, Techaphneches or Tachpenes, a place in Egypt; Tahapanes, Tahpanhes, Tehaphnehes; n-pr-loc

- a city in Egypt; modern 'Tel Defenneh' or 'Tel Defneh' located approximately 18 miles (29 km) east southeast from Tanis
