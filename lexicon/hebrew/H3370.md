# H3370 יׇקְשָׁן / יקשן

## Yoqshân

_(yok-shawn' | yoke-SHAWN | yoke-SHAHN)_

## Definition

snarer; from H3369; insidious; Jokshan, an Arabian patriarch; Jokshan; n-pr-m

- a son of Abraham by Keturah and father of Sheba and Dedan
