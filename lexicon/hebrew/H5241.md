# H5241 נְמוּאֵל / נמואל

## Nᵉmûwʼêl

_(nem-oo-ale' | neh-moo-ALE | neh-moo-ALE)_

## Definition

day of God; apparently for H3223; Nemuel, the name of two Israelites; Nemuel; n-pr-m

- the eldest son of Simeon
- a Reubenite, son of Eliab and brother of Dathan and Abiram in the time of the exodus
