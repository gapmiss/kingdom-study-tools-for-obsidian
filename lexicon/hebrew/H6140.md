# H6140 עָקַשׁ / עקש

## ʻâqash

_(aw-kash' | aw-KAHSH | ah-KAHSH)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to knot or distort; figuratively, to pervert (act or declare perverse); make crooked, (prove, that is) perverse(-rt); v

- to be perverse, twist, pervert, make crooked, prove perverse, declare perverse
  - (Niphal) to be crooked
  - (Piel) to twist, distort, pervert, make crooked
  - (Hiphil) to declare crooked
  - (Qal) perverse
