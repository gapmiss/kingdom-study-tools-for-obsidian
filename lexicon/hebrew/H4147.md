# H4147 מוֹסֵר / מוסר

## môwçêr

_(mo-sare' | moh-SARE | moh-SARE)_

## Definition

also (in plural) feminine מוֹסֵרָה; or מֹסְרָה; from H3256; properly, chastisement, i.e., (by implication) a halter; figuratively, restraint; band, bond; n-m

- band, bond
