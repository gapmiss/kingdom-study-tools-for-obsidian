# H874 בָּאַר / באר

## bâʼar

_(baw-ar' | baw-AR | ba-AR)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to dig; by analogy, to engrave; figuratively, to explain; declare, (make) plain(-ly); v

- (Piel)
  - to make plain, distinct
  - to make clear
  - to declare
  - letters on a tablet
