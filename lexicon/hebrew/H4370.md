# H4370 מִכְנָס / מכנס

## miknâç

_(mik-nawce' | mik-NAWS | meek-NAHS)_

## Definition

from H3647 in the sense of hiding; (only in dual) drawers (from concealing the private parts); breeches; n-m

- underwear, drawers, trousers
  - a priestly undergarment of linen
