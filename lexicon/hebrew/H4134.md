# H4134 מוּךְ / מוך

## mûwk

_(mook | mook | mook)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to become thin, i.e., (figuratively) be impoverished; be (waxen) poor(-er); v

- (Qal) to be low, grow poor, be depressed, be poor
