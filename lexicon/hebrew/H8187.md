## Shᵉʻaryâh

_(sheh-ar-yaw' | sheh-ar-YAW | sheh-ar-YA)_

## Definition

valued by Jehovah; from H8176 and H3050; Jah has stormed; Shearjah, an Israelite; Sheariah; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite of the family of Saul, one of the 6 sons of Azel
