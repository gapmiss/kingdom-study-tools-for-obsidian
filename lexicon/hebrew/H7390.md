# H7390 רַךְ / רך

## rak

_(rak | rahk | rahk)_

## Definition

from H7401; tender (literally or figuratively); by implication, weak; faint((-hearted), soft, tender(-hearted) (one), weak; a

- tender, soft, delicate, weak
  - tender, delicate (of flesh)
  - weak of heart, timid
  - soft (of words)
    - gentle words (subst)
