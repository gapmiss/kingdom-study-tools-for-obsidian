# H2931 טָמֵא / טמא

## ṭâmêʼ

_(taw-may' | taw-MAY | ta-MAY)_

## Definition

from H2930; foul in a religious sense; defiled, + infamous, polluted(-tion), unclean; a

- unclean, impure
  - ethically and religiously
  - ritually
  - of places
