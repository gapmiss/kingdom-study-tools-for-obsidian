# H4263 מַחְמָל / מחמל

## machmâl

_(makh-mawl' | ma-MAWL | mahk-MAHL)_

## Definition

from H2550; (by paronomasia with H4261) delight; properly, sympathy; pitieth; n-m

- object of compassion or pity, thing pitied
- object of love or favor, a desired object
