# H4041 מְגַמָּה / מגמה

## mᵉgammâh

_(meg-am-maw' | meh-ɡahm-MAW | meh-ɡa-MA)_

## Definition

from the same as H1571; properly, accumulation, i.e., impulse or direction; sup up; n-f

- meaning uncertain; perhaps hordes, accumulation, assembling, eagerness (of Babylonians)
