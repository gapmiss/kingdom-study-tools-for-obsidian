# H7046 קָלַס / קלס

## qâlaç

_(kaw-las' | kaw-LAHS | ka-LAHS)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to disparage, i.e., ridicule; mock, scoff, scorn; v

- to mock, scoff
  - (Piel) to scoff at
  - (Hithpael) to mock, deride, make fun of
