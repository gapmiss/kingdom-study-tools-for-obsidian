# H5161 נֶחֱלָמִי / נחלמי

## Nechĕlâmîy

_(nekh-el-aw-mee' | neh-heh-law-MEE | neh-hay-la-MEE)_

## Definition

he of the dream; apparently a patronymic from an unused name (apparently passive participle of H2492); dreamed; a Nechelamite, or descendant of Nechlam; Nehelamite; a

- the designation of a false prophet Shemaiah taken into captivity to Babylon; name is formed from his native place or his progenitor
