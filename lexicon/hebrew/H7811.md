# H7811 שָׂחָה / שחה

## sâchâh

_(saw-khaw' | saw-HAW | sa-HA)_

## Definition

a primitive root; to swim; causatively, to inundate; (make to) swim; v

- to swim
  - (Qal) swim (participle)
  - (Hiphil) to make to swim
