# H1969 הִין / הין

## hîyn

_(heen | heen | heen)_

## Definition

probably of Egyptian origin; a hin or liquid measure; hin; n-m

- hin
  - a unit of measure, about 5 quarts (6 l)
  - a vessel holding a hin of liquid
