# H4781 מְרֹדָךְ / מרדך

## Mᵉrôdâk

_(mer-o-dawk' | meh-roh-DOKE | meh-roh-DOKE)_

## Definition

thy rebellion; of foreign derivation; Merodak, a Babylonian idol; Merodach; n-pr-m

- the chief deity of the Babylonians in the time of Nebuchadnezzar
