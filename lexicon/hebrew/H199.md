# H199 אוּלָם / אולם

## ʼûwlâm

_(oo-lawm' | oo-LAWM | oo-LAHM)_

## Definition

apparently a variation of H0194; however or on the contrary; as for, but, howbeit, in very deed, surely, truly, wherefore; adv

- but, but indeed (a strong adversative)
- however, nevertheless
