# H1332 בִּתְיָה / בתיה

## Bithyâh

_(bith-yaw' | bit-YAW | beet-YA)_

## Definition

daughter of Jehovah; from H1323 and H3050; daughter (i.e., worshipper) of Jah; Bithjah, an Egyptian woman; Bithiah; n-pr-f

- a daughter of a Pharaoh and wife of Mered of Judah
